# § 621. Omitted

##### § 621. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1949, ch. 425, § 1](/us/act/1949-08-13/ch425/s1), [63 Stat. 604](/us/stat/63/604), which provided for portions of tribal lands to be held in trust by the United States and for the remainder to become part of the public domain, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
