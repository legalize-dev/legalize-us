Section, added [July 18, 1956, ch. 629](/us/act/1956-07-18/ch629), title I, § 104(a), [70 Stat. 570](/us/stat/70/570); amended Oct. 27, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–513, title III, § 1102(g)(1)](/us/pl/91/513/tIII/s1102/g/1), [84 Stat. 1292](/us/stat/84/1292), set forth additional authority for Bureau of Customs with respect to firearms, warrants, etc.
