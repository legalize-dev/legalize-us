# § 446. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–133](/us/pl/86/133), Aug. 4, 1959, [73 Stat. 279](/us/stat/73/279)

##### § 446. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–133](/us/pl/86/133), Aug. 4, 1959, [73 Stat. 279](/us/stat/73/279)

Section, [act July 3, 1956, ch. 512, § 5](/us/act/1956-07-03/ch512/s5), [70 Stat. 492](/us/stat/70/492), prescribed three years following July 3, 1956, as expiration date for availability of grain under sections 667f to 667f–3 of Title 16, Conservation.
