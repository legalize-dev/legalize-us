##### §§ 5701 to 5709. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–31, div. E, title VI, § 631(a)](/us/pl/115/31/dE/tVI/s631/a), May 5, 2017, [131 Stat. 376](/us/stat/131/376)

Section 5704, [Pub. L. 102–281, title IV, § 425](/us/pl/102/281/tIV/s425), May 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 143](/us/stat/106/143), related to stipends.

Section 5705, [Pub. L. 102–281, title IV, § 426](/us/pl/102/281/tIV/s426), May 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 143](/us/stat/106/143); [Pub. L. 111–8, div. D, title VI, § 623(1)](/us/pl/111/8/dD/tVI/s623/1), Mar. 11, 2009, [123 Stat. 678](/us/stat/123/678), related to Christopher Columbus Fellowship Fund.

Section 5706, [Pub. L. 102–281, title IV, § 427](/us/pl/102/281/tIV/s427), May 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 144](/us/stat/106/144), related to audits of the activities of the Foundation.

Section 5709, [Pub. L. 102–281, title IV, § 430](/us/pl/102/281/tIV/s430), as added [Pub. L. 111–8, div. D, title VI, § 623(2)](/us/pl/111/8/dD/tVI/s623/2), Mar. 11, 2009, [123 Stat. 678](/us/stat/123/678), authorized appropriations.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 115–31, div. E, title VI, § 631](/us/pl/115/31/dE/tVI/s631), May 5, 2017, [131 Stat. 376](/us/stat/131/376), provided that: “(a) Effective one year after the date of the enactment of this Act [May 5, 2017], subtitle B [§§ 421–430] of title IV of [Public Law 102–281](/us/pl/102/281) [enacting this chapter] is repealed. “(b) On the day before the date of the repeal under subsection (a), the Secretary of the Treasury shall transfer the amounts in the fund described in section 408(a) of subtitle A of title IV of such Public Law [listed in a table of Commemorative Coins under [section 5112 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s5112), Money and Finance] into the general fund of the Treasury.”

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 102–281, title IV, § 400](/us/pl/102/281/tIV/s400), May 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 139](/us/stat/106/139), provided that: “This title [enacting this chapter and provisions set out as a note under [section 5112 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s5112), Money and Finance] may be cited as the ‘Frank Annunzio Act’.”

[Pub. L. 102–281, title IV, § 421](/us/pl/102/281/tIV/s421), May 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 142](/us/stat/106/142), which provided that subtitle B (§§ 421–430) of title IV of [Pub. L. 102–281](/us/pl/102/281), enacting this chapter, could be cited as the “Christopher Columbus Fellowship Act”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 115–31, div. E, title VI, § 631(a)](/us/pl/115/31/dE/tVI/s631/a), May 5, 2017, [131 Stat. 376](/us/stat/131/376).
