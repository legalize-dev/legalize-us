# § 10250. Termination of Office

##### § 10250. Termination of Office

The Office shall cease to exist not later than 30 days after the date 7 years after December 22, 1987.

*([Pub. L. 97–425, title IV, § 410](/us/pl/97/425/tIV/s410), as added [Pub. L. 100–202, § 101(d) [title III]](/us/pl/100/202/s101/d/tIII), Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1329–104](/us/stat/101/1329-104), 1329–121; [Pub. L. 100–203, title V, § 5041](/us/pl/100/203/tV/s5041), Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–247](/us/stat/101/1330-247); amended [Pub.
