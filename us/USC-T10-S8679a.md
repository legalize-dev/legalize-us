# § 8679a. Contracting with shipyards controlled by a foreign adversary country: prohibition

##### § 8679a. Contracting with shipyards controlled by a foreign adversary country: prohibition

The Secretary of Defense may not enter into any contract or other agreement with a shipyard determined by the Secretary of Defense to be under the ownership, control, or influence of a covered nation, as defined in [section 4872(f) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s4872/f).

*(Added [Pub. L. 118–159, div. A, title X, § 1025](/us/pl/118/159/dA/tX/s1025), Dec. 23, 2024, [138 Stat. 2055](/us/stat/138/2055); amended [Pub. L. 119–60, div. A, title XVII, § 1701(a)(36)](/us/pl/119/60/dA/tXVII/s1701/a/36), Dec. 18, 2025, [139 Stat. 1209](/us/stat/139/1209).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2025—[Pub. L. 119–60](/us/pl/119/60) substituted “a covered nation, as defined in [section 4872(f) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s4872/f)” for “a foreign adversary country (as defined in [section 4872(d)(2) of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s4872/d/2), United States Code)” in text.
