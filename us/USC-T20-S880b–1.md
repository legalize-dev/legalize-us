# § 880b–1. Omitted

##### § 880b–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 880b–1, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 703](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s703), as added and amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 105(a)(1)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s105/a/1), (b), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 504](/us/stat/88/504), 512, which made general provisions for the bilingual education program, was omitted in the general revision of title VII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII](/us/pl/89/10/tVII), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 105(a)(1)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s105/a/1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 511](/us/stat/88/511), by [Pub. L. 95–561, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/95/561/tVII/s701), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2268](/us/stat/92/2268).

A prior section 880b–1, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 703](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s703), as added [Pub. L. 90–247, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/90/247/tVII/s702), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 816](/us/stat/81/816); amended [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 151](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s151), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 151](/us/stat/84/151), authorized appropriation of 15, 30, 40, 80, 100, and 135 million dollars for fiscal years ending June 30, 1968 through 1973, respectively, and for distribution of funds.
