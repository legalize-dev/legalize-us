# § 5112. Indian corporations; appropriation for organizing

##### § 5112. Indian corporations; appropriation for organizing

There is authorized to be appropriated, out of any funds in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, such sums as may be necessary, but not to exceed $250,000 in any fiscal year, to be expended at the order of the Secretary of the Interior, in defraying the expenses of organizing Indian chartered corporations or other organizations created under this Act.

*([June 18, 1934, ch. 576, § 9](/us/act/1934-06-18/ch576/s9), [48 Stat. 986](/us/stat/48/986).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, is [act June 18, 1934, ch. 576](/us/act/1934-06-18/ch576), [48 Stat. 984](/us/stat/48/984), popularly known as the Indian Reorganization Act, which is classified generally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 5101 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5101) and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 469 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s469) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
