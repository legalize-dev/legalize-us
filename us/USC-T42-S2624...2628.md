# §§ 2624 to 2628. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VII, § 714](/us/pl/93/203/tVII/s714), formerly title VI, § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883); renumbered title VII, § 714, [Pub. L. 93–567, title I, § 101](/us/pl/93/567/tI/s101), Dec. 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 1845](/us/stat/88/1845)

##### §§ 2624 to 2628. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VII, § 714](/us/pl/93/203/tVII/s714), formerly title VI, § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883); renumbered title VII, § 714, [Pub. L. 93–567, title I, § 101](/us/pl/93/567/tI/s101), Dec. 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 1845](/us/stat/88/1845)

Section 2624, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 501](/us/pl/87/415/tV/s501), as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13](/us/pl/90/636/s13), Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1355](/us/stat/82/1355), set out Congressional declaration of purpose in providing for supplementary State programs.

Section 2625, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 502](/us/pl/87/415/tV/s502), as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13](/us/pl/90/636/s13), Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1356](/us/stat/82/1356), provided authorization for grants for supplementary State programs.

Section 2626, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 503](/us/pl/87/415/tV/s503), as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13](/us/pl/90/636/s13), Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1356](/us/stat/82/1356), provided for applications for supplementary State programs and conditions for such programs.

Section 2627, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 504](/us/pl/87/415/tV/s504), as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13](/us/pl/90/636/s13), Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1356](/us/stat/82/1356), provided for promulgation of rules and regulations.

Section 2628, [Pub. L. 87–415, title V, § 505](/us/pl/87/415/tV/s505), as added [Pub. L. 90–636, § 13](/us/pl/90/636/s13), Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1356](/us/stat/82/1356), authorized appropriations for supplementary State programs.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 93–203, title VII, § 714](/us/pl/93/203/tVII/s714), formerly title VI, § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883); renumbered title VII, § 714, [Pub. L. 93–567, title I, § 101](/us/pl/93/567/tI/s101), Dec. 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 1845](/us/stat/88/1845), provided that the repeal is effective with respect to fiscal years after June 30, 1974.
