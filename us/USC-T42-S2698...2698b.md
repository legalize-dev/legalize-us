# §§ 2698 to 2698b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, § 662(4)](/us/pl/91/230/tVI/s662/4), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 188](/us/stat/84/188)

##### §§ 2698 to 2698b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, § 662(4)](/us/pl/91/230/tVI/s662/4), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 188](/us/stat/84/188)

Section 2698, [Pub. L. 88–164, title V, § 501](/us/pl/88/164/tV/s501), as added [Pub. L. 90–170, § 7](/us/pl/90/170/s7), Dec. 4, 1967, [81 Stat. 530](/us/stat/81/530), related to appropriations and grants for training of personnel.

Section 2698a, [Pub. L. 88–164, title V, § 502](/us/pl/88/164/tV/s502), as added [Pub. L. 90–170, § 7](/us/pl/90/170/s7), Dec. 4, 1967, [81 Stat. 530](/us/stat/81/530), related to grants for research and demonstration projects, payment thereof, and evaluation of such projects.

Section 2698b, [Pub. L. 88–164, title V, § 503](/us/pl/88/164/tV/s503), as added [Pub. L. 90–170, § 7](/us/pl/90/170/s7), Dec. 4, 1967, [81 Stat. 531](/us/stat/81/531), related to appointment of an advisory committee and compensation of its members and appointees.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, § 662](/us/pl/91/230/tVI/s662), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 188](/us/stat/84/188), provided that the repeal by that section is effective July 1, 1971.
