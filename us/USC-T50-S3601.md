# § 3601. Short title

##### § 3601. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “National Security Agency Act of 1959”.

*([Pub. L. 86–36, § 1](/us/pl/86/36/s1), as added [Pub. L. 96–450, title IV, § 402(a)(2)](/us/pl/96/450/tIV/s402/a/2), Oct. 14, 1980, [94 Stat. 1978](/us/stat/94/1978).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified in a note under [section 402 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s402) prior to editorial reclassification as this section.
