# §§ 2861 to 2864. Omitted

##### §§ 2861 to 2864. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 2861, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1456](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1456), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 198](/us/stat/102/198), authorized rural assistance programs.

Section 2862, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1457](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1457), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 198](/us/stat/102/198), related to application priority requirements.

Section 2863, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1458](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1458), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 198](/us/stat/102/198), related to coordination, dissemination, and report.

Section 2864, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1459](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1459), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 199](/us/stat/102/199), authorized appropriations.
