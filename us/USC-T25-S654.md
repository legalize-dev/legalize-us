#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 18, 1928, ch. 624, § 4](/us/act/1928-05-18/ch624/s4), [45 Stat. 602](/us/stat/45/602), which related to the filing and verification of claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
