# § 1143. Omitted

##### § 1143. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–93, § 3](/us/pl/90/93/s3), Sept. 27, 1967, [81 Stat. 229](/us/stat/81/229), which related to assistance of the Secretary of the Interior in preparing membership rolls and eligibility for enrollment, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
