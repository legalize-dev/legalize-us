# § 805. Judicial review

##### § 805. Judicial review

No determination, finding, action, or omission under this chapter shall be subject to judicial review.

*(Added [Pub. L. 104–121, title II, § 251](/us/pl/104/121/tII/s251), Mar. 29, 1996, [110 Stat. 873](/us/stat/110/873).)*
