# § 6501. Purposes

##### § 6501. Purposes

(1) to establish national standards governing the marketing of certain agricultural products as organically produced products;

(2) to assure consumers that organically produced products meet a consistent standard; and

(3) to facilitate interstate commerce in fresh and processed food that is organically produced.

*([Pub. L. 101–624, title XXI, § 2102](/us/pl/101/624/tXXI/s2102), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3935](/us/stat/104/3935).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 101–624, title XXI, § 2101](/us/pl/101/624/tXXI/s2101), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3935](/us/stat/104/3935), provided that: “This title [enacting this chapter] may be cited as the ‘Organic Foods Production Act of 1990’.”
