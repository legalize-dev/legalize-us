# §§ 121 to 125. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

##### §§ 121 to 125. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

Section 121, acts [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443, § 1](/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443/s1), [33 Stat. 1009](/us/stat/33/1009); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for establishment in perpetuity of a hospital station and laboratory at Molokai, Hawaii.

Section 122, [act Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443, § 3](/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443/s3), [33 Stat. 1009](/us/stat/33/1009), provided for admission and treatment of leper patients. See [section 255 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s255).

Section 123, acts [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443, § 4](/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443/s4), [33 Stat. 1009](/us/stat/33/1009); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for detail of officers and employees for leprosy work. See [section 215 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s215).

Section 124, acts [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443, § 6](/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443/s6), [33 Stat. 1010](/us/stat/33/1010); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided regulations for administration of hospital station and laboratory.

Section 125, acts [Mar. 3, 1905, ch. 1443, § 7](/us/act/1905-03-03/ch1443/s7), [33 Stat. 1010](/us/stat/33/1010); [Mar. 4, 1911, ch. 285, § 1](/us/act/1911-03-04/ch285/s1), [36 Stat. 1394](/us/stat/36/1394); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for additional pay and allowances to officers detailed on leprosy duty. See [section 210 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s210).
