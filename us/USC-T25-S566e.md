# § 566e. Omitted

##### § 566e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 99–398, § 7](/us/pl/99/398/s7), Aug. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 850](/us/stat/100/850), which related to criminal and civil jurisdiction within the reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
