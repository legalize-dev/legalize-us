# § 12859. Termination

##### § 12859. Termination

The Trust shall terminate September 30, 1994.

*([Pub. L. 101–625, title III, § 310](/us/pl/101/625/tIII/s310), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 4133](/us/stat/104/4133); [Pub. L. 102–550, title I, § 182(a)](/us/pl/102/550/tI/s182/a), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 3736](/us/stat/106/3736).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1992—[Pub. L. 102–550](/us/pl/102/550) substituted “September 30, 1994” for “on September 30, 1993”.
