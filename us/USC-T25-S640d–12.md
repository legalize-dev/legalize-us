# § 640d–12. Omitted

##### § 640d–12. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 13](/us/pl/93/531/s13), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1717](/us/stat/88/1717); [Pub. L. 96–305, § 6](/us/pl/96/305/s6), July 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 932](/us/stat/94/932); [Pub. L. 100–666, § 4(d)](/us/pl/100/666/s4/d), Nov. 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 3931](/us/stat/102/3931); [Pub. L. 101–121, title I, § 120](/us/pl/101/121/tI/s120), Oct. 23, 1989, [103 Stat. 722](/us/stat/103/722), which directed the Commissioner of the Office of Navajo and Hopi Indian Relocation to prepare a report for Congress concerning relocation of households and members of each tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
