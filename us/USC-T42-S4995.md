# § 4995. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title II, § 2132](/us/pl/111/13/tII/s2132), Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1584](/us/stat/123/1584)

##### § 4995. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title II, § 2132](/us/pl/111/13/tII/s2132), Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1584](/us/stat/123/1584)

Section, [Pub. L. 93–113, title I, § 124](/us/pl/93/113/tI/s124), formerly § 125, as added [Pub. L. 102–73, title VII, § 701(a)(1)](/us/pl/102/73/tVII/s701/a/1), July 25, 1991, [105 Stat. 358](/us/stat/105/358); renumbered § 124 and amended [Pub. L. 103–82, title III, § 333(2)](/us/pl/103/82/tIII/s333/2), title IV, § 405(a)(5), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 903](/us/stat/107/903), 920, authorized the Director to award literacy challenge grants to eligible public agencies and private organizations.

A prior [section 124 of Pub. L. 93–113](/us/pl/93/113/s124) was classified to [section 4994 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4994) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–82](/us/pl/103/82).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 2009, see [section 6101(a) of Pub. L. 111–13](/us/pl/111/13/s6101/a), set out as an Effective Date of 2009 Amendment note under [section 4950 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4950).
