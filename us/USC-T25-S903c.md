##### § 903c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–197, § 5](/us/pl/93/197/s5), Dec. 22, 1973, [87 Stat. 772](/us/stat/87/772), which related to tribal constitution and bylaws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
