# § 1300c–1. Omitted

##### § 1300c–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–468, § 2](/us/pl/92/468/s2), Oct. 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 782](/us/stat/86/782), which provided for the withholding of a portion of the judgment funds for expert witnesses or other programing needs in certain claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
