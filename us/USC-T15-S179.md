# § 179. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–826, § 3](/us/pl/87/826/s3), Oct. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 953](/us/stat/76/953)

##### § 179. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–826, § 3](/us/pl/87/826/s3), Oct. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 953](/us/stat/76/953)

Section, R.S. § 339; acts [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 129, § 1](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch129/s1), [18 Stat. 352](/us/stat/18/352); [Feb. 14, 1903, ch. 552, § 10](/us/act/1903-02-14/ch552/s10), [32 Stat. 829](/us/stat/32/829); [Aug. 23, 1912, ch. 350, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-23/ch350/s1), [37 Stat. 407](/us/stat/37/407); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1), [37 Stat. 736](/us/stat/37/736); [Mar. 1, 1919, ch. 86](/us/act/1919-03-01/ch86), [40 Stat. 1256](/us/stat/40/1256), required quarterly reports of exports and imports. See section 301 et seq. of Title 13, Census.

See [section 3 of Pub. L. 87–826](/us/pl/87/826/s3), set out as a note under sections 173 and 174 of this title.
