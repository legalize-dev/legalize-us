# §§ 402 to 405. Repealed. [Aug. 13, 1953, ch. 434, § 2](/us/act/1953-08-13/ch434/s2), [67 Stat. 577](/us/stat/67/577)

##### §§ 402 to 405. Repealed. [Aug. 13, 1953, ch. 434, § 2](/us/act/1953-08-13/ch434/s2), [67 Stat. 577](/us/stat/67/577)

Section 402, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 2](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s2), [40 Stat. 224](/us/stat/40/224), related to issuance of warrant for detention of seized property.

Section 403, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 3](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s3), [40 Stat. 224](/us/stat/40/224), related to filing petition for restoration of seized property.

Section 404, acts [June 15, 1917, ch. 30](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30), title VI, § 4, [40 Stat. 224](/us/stat/40/224); [Mar. 1, 1929, ch. 420](/us/act/1929-03-01/ch420), [45 Stat. 1423](/us/stat/45/1423), related to institution of libel proceedings and sale of seized property.

Section 405, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 5](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s5), [40 Stat. 224](/us/stat/40/224), related to method of trial and bond for redelivery.

For subject matter of sections 402 to 405 of this title, see [section 401 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s401).
