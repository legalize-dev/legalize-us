# § 83. Omitted

##### § 83. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 18, 1894, ch. 301, § 1](/us/act/1894-08-18/ch301/s1), [28 Stat. 384](/us/stat/28/384), which required that a detailed report of expenses on account of the National Zoological Park be made to Congress at the beginning of each regular session, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance. See, also, page 192 of House Document No. 103–7.
