# § 8901. Definition of Administrator

##### § 8901. Definition of Administrator

In this chapter, the term “Administrator” means the Administrator of the Environmental Protection Agency.

*([Pub. L. 116–92, div. F, title LXXIII, § 7302](/us/pl/116/92/dF/tLXXIII/s7302), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2275](/us/stat/133/2275).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title LXXIII of [Pub. L. 116–92, div. F](/us/pl/116/92/dF), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2275](/us/stat/133/2275), known as the PFAS Act of 2019, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out below and Tables.
