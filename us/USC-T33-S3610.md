# § 3610. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 3610. Authorization of appropriations

(1) $48,000,000 for fiscal year 2021;

(2) $50,000,000 for fiscal year 2022;

(3) $52,000,000 for fiscal year 2023;

(4) $54,000,000 for fiscal year 2024; and

(5) $56,000,000 for fiscal year 2025.

*([Pub. L. 111–11, title XII, § 12311](/us/pl/111/11/tXII/s12311), Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1436](/us/stat/123/1436); [Pub. L. 116–271, title I, § 108](/us/pl/116/271/tI/s108), Dec. 31, 2020, [134 Stat. 3341](/us/stat/134/3341).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2020—[Pub. L. 116–271](/us/pl/116/271) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section related to authorization of appropriations for fiscal years 2009 to 2013.
