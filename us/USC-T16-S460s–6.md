# § 460s–6. Taxing power

##### § 460s–6. Taxing power

Nothing in this subchapter shall be construed as prohibiting governmental jurisdiction in the State of Michigan from assessing taxes upon any interest in real estate retained under the provisions of [section 460s–10 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s460s–10) to the owner of such interest.

*([Pub. L. 89–668, § 7](/us/pl/89/668/s7), Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 923](/us/stat/80/923).)*
