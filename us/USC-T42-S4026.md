# § 4026. Expiration of program

No new contract for flood insurance under this chapter shall be entered into after September 30, 2023.11 See Extension of Program note below.

L. 90–448](/us/pl/90/448), Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 572](/us/stat/82/572), known as the National Flood Insurance Act of 1968, which is classified principally to this chapter.
