# § 178. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–325, title VI, § 602(f)(5)(B)](/us/pl/103/325/tVI/s602/f/5/B), Sept. 23, 1994, [108 Stat. 2293](/us/stat/108/2293)

##### § 178. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–325, title VI, § 602(f)(5)(B)](/us/pl/103/325/tVI/s602/f/5/B), Sept. 23, 1994, [108 Stat. 2293](/us/stat/108/2293)

Section, acts [July 12, 1882, ch. 290, § 9](/us/act/1882-07-12/ch290/s9), [22 Stat. 164](/us/stat/22/164); [Mar. 14, 1900, ch. 41, § 12](/us/act/1900-03-14/ch41/s12), [31 Stat. 49](/us/stat/31/49); [Mar. 4, 1907, ch. 2913, § 4](/us/act/1907-03-04/ch2913/s4), [34 Stat. 1290](/us/stat/34/1290), authorized national banking associations desiring to withdraw circulating notes to deposit money with Treasurer of United States and withdraw bonds or other securities securing such notes.
