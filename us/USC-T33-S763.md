#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 763 consisted mostly of provisions from section 6 of act June 20, 1918, as amended, with one additional proviso from act Nov. 4, 1918, and was restated at former [section 2532 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s2532), Coast Guard. Section 6 of act June 20, 1918, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8510(a), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4757](/us/stat/134/4757). The proviso from act Nov. 4, 1918, was repealed by [Pub. L. 117–263, div. K, title CXVIII, § 11808(a)(13)](/us/pl/117/263/dK/tCXVIII/s11808/a/13), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 4165](/us/stat/136/4165).
