# § 894b. Rights of the victim of a sexual assault

##### § 894b. Rights of the victim of a sexual assault

A victim of a sexual assault covered by the comprehensive policy developed under [section 894a(a) of this title](/us/usc/t33/s894a/a) has the right to be reasonably protected from the accused.

*([Pub. L. 114–328, div. C, title XXXV, § 3543](/us/pl/114/328/dC/tXXXV/s3543), Dec. 23, 2016, [130 Stat. 2803](/us/stat/130/2803).)*
