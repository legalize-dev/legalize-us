# § 22904. Membership

##### § 22904. Membership

Eligibility for membership in the corporation and the rights and privileges of members are as provided in the bylaws.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1310](/us/stat/112/1310).)*
