# § 7110. Regulations

##### § 7110. Regulations

The Secretary may issue such regulations as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 103–407, § 11](/us/pl/103/407/s11), Oct. 22, 1994, [108 Stat. 4226](/us/stat/108/4226).)*
