# § 11924. Definitions

##### § 11924. Definitions

(1) Controlled substance The term “controlled substance” has the meaning given such term in [section 802 of title 21](/us/usc/t21/s802).

(2) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Housing and Urban Development.

*([Pub. L. 100–690, title V, § 5145](/us/pl/100/690/tV/s5145), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4304](/us/stat/102/4304).)*
