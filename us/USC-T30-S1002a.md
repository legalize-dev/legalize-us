# § 1002a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–214, § 7(16)](/us/pl/97/214/s7/16), July 12, 1982, [96 Stat. 174](/us/stat/96/174)

##### § 1002a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–214, § 7(16)](/us/pl/97/214/s7/16), July 12, 1982, [96 Stat. 174](/us/stat/96/174)

Section, [Pub. L. 95–356, title VIII, § 803(a)](/us/pl/95/356/tVIII/s803/a), (b), Sept. 8, 1978, [92 Stat. 585](/us/stat/92/585); [Pub. L. 96–125, title VIII, § 802(2)](/us/pl/96/125/tVIII/s802/2), Nov. 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 948](/us/stat/93/948); [Pub. L. 97–99, title IX, § 908](/us/pl/97/99/tIX/s908), Dec. 23, 1981, [95 Stat. 1385](/us/stat/95/1385), related to development of geothermal energy sources on military lands, contracts for provision and operation of production facilities and energy purchases, and terms, conditions and prerequisites of such contracts. See sections 2917 and 2922a of Title 10, Armed Forces.

[Pub. L. 95–356, title VIII, § 803(c)](/us/pl/95/356/tVIII/s803/c), Sept. 8, 1978, [92 Stat. 585](/us/stat/92/585), which provided that this section take effect Oct. 1, 1978, was repealed by [Pub. L. 97–214, § 7(16)](/us/pl/97/214/s7/16), July 12, 1982, [96 Stat. 174](/us/stat/96/174).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1982, and applicable to military construction projects, and to construction and acquisition of military family housing before, on, or after such date, see [section 12(a) of Pub. L. 97–214](/us/pl/97/214/s12/a), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 2801 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s2801), Armed Forces.
