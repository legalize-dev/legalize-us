# §§ 300ll to 300ll–9. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–240, title VI, § 642(a)](/us/pl/112/240/tVI/s642/a), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2358](/us/stat/126/2358)

##### §§ 300ll to 300ll–9. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–240, title VI, § 642(a)](/us/pl/112/240/tVI/s642/a), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2358](/us/stat/126/2358)

Section 300*ll*, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3201](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3201), as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 828](/us/stat/124/828), established the purpose of this subchapter.

Section 300*ll*–1, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3202](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3202), as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 828](/us/stat/124/828), set out definitions.

Section 300*ll*–2, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3203](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3203), as added and amended [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), title X, § 10801(a)(1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 830](/us/stat/124/830), 1015, required the Secretary to develop at least 3 actuarially sound benefit plans as alternatives for consideration for designation by the Secretary as the CLASS Independence Benefit Plan.

Section 300*ll*–3, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3204](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3204), as added and amended [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), title X, § 10801(a)(2), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 834](/us/stat/124/834), 1015, related to enrollment and disenrollment requirements.

Section 300*ll*–4, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3205](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3205), as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 836](/us/stat/124/836), related to benefits and determination of eligibility.

Section 300*ll*–5, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3206](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3206), as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 842](/us/stat/124/842), related to the CLASS Independence Fund.

Section 300*ll*–6, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3207](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3207), as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 844](/us/stat/124/844), created the CLASS Independence Advisory Council.

Section 300*ll*–7, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3208](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3208), as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 845](/us/stat/124/845), related to solvency and fiscal independence of the CLASS program and required regulations and annual reports.

Section 300*ll*–8, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3209](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3209), as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 845](/us/stat/124/845), required the Inspector General of the Department of Health and Human Services to submit an annual report on CLASS program progress and waste, fraud, and abuse.

Section 300*ll*–9, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXXII, § 3210](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXXII/s3210), as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/a/1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 846](/us/stat/124/846), provided that the CLASS program would be treated for tax purposes as a qualified long-term care insurance contract for qualified long-term care services.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(e)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/e), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 847](/us/stat/124/847), which provided that the amendments made by section 8002(a), (b), and (d) (enacting this subchapter, amending [section 1396a of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1396a), and amending provisions set out as a note under [section 1396p of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1396p)) were effective on Jan. 1, 2011, was repealed by [Pub. L. 112–240, title VI, § 642(b)(1)](/us/pl/112/240/tVI/s642/b/1), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2358](/us/stat/126/2358).

#### Construction

[Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(f)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/f), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 847](/us/stat/124/847), which provided that nothing in title VIII of [Pub. L. 111–148](/us/pl/111/148) (enacting this subchapter, amending [section 1396a of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1396a), enacting provisions set out as notes under this section and [section 201 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s201), and amending provisions set out as a note under [section 1396p of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1396p)) was intended to replace or displace public or private disability insurance benefits, including such benefits for income replacement, was repealed by [Pub. L. 112–240, title VI, § 642(b)(1)](/us/pl/112/240/tVI/s642/b/1), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2358](/us/stat/126/2358).

#### Personal Care Attendants Workforce Advisory Panel

[Pub. L. 111–148, title VIII, § 8002(c)](/us/pl/111/148/tVIII/s8002/c), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 846](/us/stat/124/846), which required the Secretary of Health and Human Services to establish a Personal Care Attendants Workforce Advisory Panel for the purpose of examining and advising the Secretary and Congress on workforce issues related to personal care attendant workers and which set out membership requirements for the Panel, was repealed by [Pub. L. 112–240, title VI, § 642(b)(1)](/us/pl/112/240/tVI/s642/b/1), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2358](/us/stat/126/2358).
