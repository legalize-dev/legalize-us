# § 329. Omitted

##### § 329. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 85–469, title I, § 101](/us/pl/85/469/tI/s101), June 25, 1958, [72 Stat. 234](/us/stat/72/234); [Pub. L. 86–88, title I, § 101](/us/pl/86/88/tI/s101), July 13, 1959, [73 Stat. 207](/us/stat/73/207), which prescribed the maximum base rate of pay for employees conducting meteorological investigations in the Arctic region, was from an appropriation act and was omitted in view of [section 327 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s327) which authorizes the Secretary of Commerce to establish the rates of compensation for such personnel. Provisions of this section were repeated in [Pub. L. 86–451, title I, § 101](/us/pl/86/451/tI/s101), May 13, 1960, [74 Stat. 99](/us/stat/74/99).
