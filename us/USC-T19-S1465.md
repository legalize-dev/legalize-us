# § 1465. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–182, title VI, § 690(b)(7)](/us/pl/103/182/tVI/s690/b/7), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2223](/us/stat/107/2223)

##### § 1465. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–182, title VI, § 690(b)(7)](/us/pl/103/182/tVI/s690/b/7), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2223](/us/stat/107/2223)

Section, [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497, title IV, § 465](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497/tIV/s465), [46 Stat. 718](/us/stat/46/718), required master of any vessel engaged in certain foreign and coasting trade and conductor of any railway car to file, upon arrival from foreign contiguous country, a list of all supplies or other merchandise purchased in such foreign country.
