##### §§ 2995 to 2995c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 683(a)](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s683/a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 519](/us/stat/95/519)

Section 2995, [Pub. L. 88–452, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/88/452/tIX/s901), as added [Pub. L. 92–424, § 27(a)](/us/pl/92/424/s27/a), Sept. 19, 1972, [86 Stat. 704](/us/stat/86/704); amended [Pub. L. 93–644, § 12](/us/pl/93/644/s12), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2328](/us/stat/88/2328); [Pub. L. 95–568, § 17(a)(42)](/us/pl/95/568/s17/a/42), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2443](/us/stat/92/2443), related to program and project evaluation.

Section 2995a, [Pub. L. 88–452, title IX, § 902](/us/pl/88/452/tIX/s902), as added [Pub. L. 92–424, § 27(a)](/us/pl/92/424/s27/a), Sept. 19, 1972, [86 Stat. 704](/us/stat/86/704); amended [Pub. L. 93–644, § 12](/us/pl/93/644/s12), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2329](/us/stat/88/2329); [Pub. L. 95–568, § 17(a)(43)](/us/pl/95/568/s17/a/43), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2443](/us/stat/92/2443), related to cooperation and consultation with other Federal agencies.

Section 2995b, [Pub. L. 88–452, title IX, § 903](/us/pl/88/452/tIX/s903), as added [Pub. L. 92–424, § 27(a)](/us/pl/92/424/s27/a), Sept. 19, 1972, [86 Stat. 704](/us/stat/86/704); amended [Pub. L. 93–644, § 12](/us/pl/93/644/s12), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2329](/us/stat/88/2329), related to evaluation by other Federal agencies.

Section 2995c, [Pub. L. 88–452, title IX, § 904](/us/pl/88/452/tIX/s904), as added [Pub. L. 95–568, § 16](/us/pl/95/568/s16), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2439](/us/stat/92/2439), set forth authorization of appropriations.

A prior section 2995c, [Pub. L. 88–452, title IX, § 904](/us/pl/88/452/tIX/s904), as added [Pub. L. 92–424, § 27(a)](/us/pl/92/424/s27/a), Sept. 19, 1972, [86 Stat. 705](/us/stat/86/705), which provided for the publication of summaries of evaluations of the results of research, was omitted as superseded in the general reorganization and amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 93–644, § 12](/us/pl/93/644/s12), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2327](/us/stat/88/2327).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 9912(a) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s9912/a), prior to the general amendment of chapter 106 (§ 9901 et seq.) of this title by [Pub. L. 105–285](/us/pl/105/285).
