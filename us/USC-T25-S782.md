# § 782. Omitted

##### § 782. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 1, 1955, ch. 444, § 3](/us/act/1955-08-01/ch444/s3), [69 Stat. 432](/us/stat/69/432), which related to payments to heirs or legatees, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
