# § 2692. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 2692. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out the purposes of this subchapter such sums as may be necessary.

*([Pub. L. 94–469, title IV, § 412](/us/pl/94/469/tIV/s412), as added [Pub. L. 102–550, title X, § 1021(a)](/us/pl/102/550/tX/s1021/a), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 3923](/us/stat/106/3923).)*
