# § 114. Leif Erikson Day

##### § 114. Leif Erikson Day

The President may issue each year a proclamation designating October 9 as Leif Erikson Day.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1257](/us/stat/112/1257).)*
