# § 8079. Appointment of chiropractors in the Medical Service Corps

##### § 8079. Appointment of chiropractors in the Medical Service Corps

Chiropractors who are qualified under regulations prescribed by the Secretary of the Navy may be appointed as commissioned officers in the Medical Service Corps of the Navy.

*(Added [Pub. L. 102–484, div. A, title V, § 505(b)(1)](/us/pl/102/484/dA/tV/s505/b/1), Oct. 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 2404](/us/stat/106/2404), § 5139; renumbered § 8079, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(a)(2)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/a/2), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/132/1834).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

Prior sections 8079 and 8080 were repealed by [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI](/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI), §§ 1661(a)(3)(A), 1691, Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2980](/us/stat/108/2980), 3026, effective Dec. 1, 1994.

Section 8079, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 496](/us/stat/70A/496), related to status of Air National Guard of United States when not in Federal service. See [section 10113 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s10113).
