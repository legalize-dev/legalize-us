# § 888. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–185, § 2(c)(3)](/us/pl/106/185/s2/c/3), Apr. 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 210](/us/stat/114/210)

##### § 888. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–185, § 2(c)(3)](/us/pl/106/185/s2/c/3), Apr. 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 210](/us/stat/114/210)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–513, title II, § 518](/us/pl/91/513/tII/s518), formerly § 511A, as added [Pub. L. 100–690, title VI, § 6080(a)](/us/pl/100/690/tVI/s6080/a), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4326](/us/stat/102/4326); renumbered § 518, [Pub. L. 101–647, title X, § 1002(h)(1)](/us/pl/101/647/tX/s1002/h/1), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4828](/us/stat/104/4828), related to expedited procedures for seized conveyances.

Section was classified to [section 881–1 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s881–1) prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 101–647](/us/pl/101/647).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to any forfeiture proceeding commenced on or after the date that is 120 days after Apr. 25, 2000, see [section 21 of Pub. L. 106–185](/us/pl/106/185/s21), set out as an Effective Date of 2000 Amendment note under [section 1324 of Title 8](/us/usc/t8/s1324), Aliens and Nationality.
