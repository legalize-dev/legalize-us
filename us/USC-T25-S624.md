# § 624. Omitted

##### § 624. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–231, § 10](/us/pl/87/231/s10), Sept. 14, 1961, [75 Stat. 505](/us/stat/75/505), which related to exchange of Pueblo lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
