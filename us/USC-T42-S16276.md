# § 16276. Security of nuclear facilities

##### § 16276. Security of nuclear facilities

(1) the safety of nuclear facilities from natural phenomena; and

(2) the security of nuclear facilities from deliberate attacks.

*([Pub. L. 109–58, title IX, § 956](/us/pl/109/58/tIX/s956), Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 888](/us/stat/119/888); [Pub. L. 115–248, § 2(f)](/us/pl/115/248/s2/f), Sept. 28, 2018, [132 Stat. 3157](/us/stat/132/3157).)*
