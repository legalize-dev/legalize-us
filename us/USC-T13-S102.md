# § 102. Religion

##### § 102. Religion

The Secretary may collect decennially statistics relating to religious bodies.

*([Aug. 31, 1954, ch. 1158](/us/act/1954-08-31/ch1158), [68 Stat. 1018](/us/stat/68/1018).)*
