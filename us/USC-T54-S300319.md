# § 300319. Tribal land

##### § 300319. Tribal land

(1) all land within the exterior boundaries of any Indian reservation; and

(2) all dependent Indian communities.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3191](/us/stat/128/3191).)*
