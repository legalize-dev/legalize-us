# § 1082. Omitted

##### § 1082. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–655, § 2](/us/pl/89/655/s2), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 905](/us/stat/80/905), which directed the preparation of membership rolls for the Quileute and Hoh Tribes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
