# § 1200e. Omitted

##### § 1200e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 6, 1954, ch. 463, § 8](/us/act/1954-07-06/ch463/s8), [68 Stat. 453](/us/stat/68/453), which authorized appropriations for relocation of the members of the Yankton Sioux Tribe, South Dakota, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
