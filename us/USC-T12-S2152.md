# § 2152. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–233, title II, § 207(a)(1)](/us/pl/100/233/tII/s207/a/1), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1607](/us/stat/101/1607)

##### § 2152. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–233, title II, § 207(a)(1)](/us/pl/100/233/tII/s207/a/1), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1607](/us/stat/101/1607)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).1, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 104](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s104), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1687](/us/stat/99/1687), contained requirements for purchase of stock and payment of assessments and contribution of capital to Capital Corporation.

A prior section 2152, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).1, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 609](/us/stat/85/609), related to revolving funds and government deposits, prior to repeal, effective thirty days after Dec. 23, 1985, by [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 101(2)](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s101/2), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1678](/us/stat/99/1678).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 100–233, title II, § 207(b)](/us/pl/100/233/tII/s207/b), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1607](/us/stat/101/1607), provided that: “The repeals made by subsection (a) [repealing this section and sections 2216 to 2216k, and 2252(a)(8) of this title] shall take effect 15 days after the date of the enactment of this Act [Jan. 6, 1988].”
