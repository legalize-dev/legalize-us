##### § 300ff–31. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–345, title II, § 207(1)](/us/pl/106/345/tII/s207/1), Oct. 20, 2000, [114 Stat. 1337](/us/stat/114/1337)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXVI, § 2621](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXVI/s2621), as added [Pub. L. 104–146, § 3(c)(7)](/us/pl/104/146/s3/c/7), May 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1356](/us/stat/110/1356), related to coordination of planning and implementation of Federal HIV programs to facilitate the local development of a complete continuum of HIV-related services for individuals with HIV disease and those at risk of such disease and required a biennial report to Congress on coordination efforts.
