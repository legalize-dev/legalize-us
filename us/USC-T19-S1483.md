# § 1483. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–446, title II, § 201(c)](/us/pl/97/446/tII/s201/c), Jan. 12, 1983, [96 Stat. 2349](/us/stat/96/2349)

##### § 1483. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–446, title II, § 201(c)](/us/pl/97/446/tII/s201/c), Jan. 12, 1983, [96 Stat. 2349](/us/stat/96/2349)

Section, acts [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 483, [46 Stat. 721](/us/stat/46/721); Oct. 3, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–410, title II, § 207](/us/pl/95/410/tII/s207), [92 Stat. 901](/us/stat/92/901), provided that for specified purposes the consignee of merchandise be deemed the owner.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective with respect to merchandise entered on and after 30th day after Jan. 12, 1983, see [section 201(g) of Pub. L. 97–446](/us/pl/97/446/s201/g), set out as an Effective Date of 1983 Amendment note under [section 1484 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1484).
