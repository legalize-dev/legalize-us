# §§ 1070a–41 to 1070a–43. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/105/244/tIV/s405), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/112/1664)

##### §§ 1070a–41 to 1070a–43. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/105/244/tIV/s405), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/112/1664)

Section 1070a–41, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 408A](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s408A), as added [Pub. L. 102–325, title IV, § 402(a)(4)](/us/pl/102/325/tIV/s402/a/4), July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 500](/us/stat/106/500), authorized grants to develop model programs.

Section 1070a–42, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 408B](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s408B), as added [Pub. L. 102–325, title IV, § 402(a)(4)](/us/pl/102/325/tIV/s402/a/4), July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 500](/us/stat/106/500), related to collection and dissemination of information about programs.

Section 1070a–43, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 408C](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s408C), as added [Pub. L. 102–325, title IV, § 402(a)(4)](/us/pl/102/325/tIV/s402/a/4), July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 501](/us/stat/106/501), authorized appropriations to carry out this division.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1998, except as otherwise provided in [Pub. L. 105–244](/us/pl/105/244), see [section 3 of Pub. L. 105–244](/us/pl/105/244/s3), set out as an Effective Date of 1998 Amendment note under [section 1001 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1001).
