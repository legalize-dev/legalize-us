# §§ 1411 to 1417. Repealed. [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), title V, § 542(a)(2), (3), [68 Stat. 861](/us/stat/68/861)

##### §§ 1411 to 1417. Repealed. [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), title V, § 542(a)(2), (3), [68 Stat. 861](/us/stat/68/861)

Section 1411, acts [May 31, 1947, ch. 90, § 1](/us/act/1947-05-31/ch90/s1), [61 Stat. 125](/us/stat/61/125); [Dec. 17, 1947, ch. 520, § 16(a)](/us/act/1947-12-17/ch520/s16/a), [61 Stat. 939](/us/stat/61/939); [Apr. 5, 1952, ch. 159, § 1](/us/act/1952-04-05/ch159/s1), [66 Stat. 43](/us/stat/66/43), related to appropriation; uses; establishment of relief distribution missions; limitations on amounts; and advances by R.F.C.

Section 1412, [act May 31, 1947, ch. 90, § 2](/us/act/1947-05-31/ch90/s2), [61 Stat. 125](/us/stat/61/125), related to relief assistance. See sections 2354, 2385, and 2392 of this title.

Section 1413, [act May 31, 1947, ch. 90, § 3](/us/act/1947-05-31/ch90/s3), [61 Stat. 126](/us/stat/61/126), prescribed conditions governing relief assistance. See section 2171 et seq. of this title.

Section 1414, [act May 31, 1947, ch. 90, § 4](/us/act/1947-05-31/ch90/s4), [61 Stat. 127](/us/stat/61/127), related to supervision of relief supplies in recipient countries; appointment, compensation, and duties of field administrator; and delegation of President’s authority. See sections 2381 and 2385 of this title.

Section 1415, [act May 31, 1947, ch. 90, § 5](/us/act/1947-05-31/ch90/s5), [61 Stat. 127](/us/stat/61/127), related to termination of relief by President or Congress. See [section 2367 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2367).

Section 1416, [act May 31, 1947, ch. 90, § 6](/us/act/1947-05-31/ch90/s6), [61 Stat. 128](/us/stat/61/128), related to sale of relief supplies by recipient country; establishment of special account as revolving fund; termination; and disposition of balance.

Section 1417, [act May 31, 1947, ch. 90, § 7](/us/act/1947-05-31/ch90/s7), [61 Stat. 128](/us/stat/61/128), required the President to submit quarterly reports to Congress. See [section 2394 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2394).

See section 2151 et seq. of this title.

Foreign Aid Act of 1947, [act Dec. 17, 1947, ch. 520](/us/act/1947-12-17/ch520), §§ 1–18, [61 Stat. 934](/us/stat/61/934), which provided immediate aid urgently needed by peoples of Austria, China, France, and Italy and which was formerly set out as a note under former [section 1411 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1411), was repealed by act Aug. 26, 1954, § 542(a)(3).
