# §§ 615kkkk to 615kkkk–6. Omitted

##### §§ 615kkkk to 615kkkk–6. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 615kkkk, [Pub. L. 94–228, title I, § 101](/us/pl/94/228/tI/s101), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 205](/us/stat/90/205), authorized construction, operation, and maintenance of Polecat Bench area, Shoshone extensions unit, Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin program, Wyoming, and described principal features of this area.

Section 615kkkk–1, [Pub. L. 94–228, title I, § 102](/us/pl/94/228/tI/s102), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 205](/us/stat/90/205), related to conservation and development of fish and wildlife and enhancement of recreation opportunities in connection with this area.

Section 615kkkk–2, [Pub. L. 94–228, title I, § 103](/us/pl/94/228/tI/s103), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 205](/us/stat/90/205), related to integration of this area with other Federal works.

Section 615kkkk–3, [Pub. L. 94–228, title I, § 104](/us/pl/94/228/tI/s104), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 206](/us/stat/90/206), related to lands held in single ownership.

Section 615kkkk–4, [Pub. L. 94–228, title I, § 105](/us/pl/94/228/tI/s105), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 206](/us/stat/90/206), related to newly irrigated lands.

Section 615kkkk–5, [Pub. L. 94–228, title I, § 106](/us/pl/94/228/tI/s106), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 206](/us/stat/90/206), related to interest rates.

Section 615kkkk–6, [Pub. L. 94–228, title I, § 107](/us/pl/94/228/tI/s107), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 206](/us/stat/90/206), authorized appropriations for this area.
