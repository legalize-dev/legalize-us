# § 3751. Termination of program

##### § 3751. Termination of program

The Secretary may not make commitments for financial assistance under this subchapter after September 30, 1986.

*(Added [Pub. L. 97–72, title III, § 302(a)](/us/pl/97/72/tIII/s302/a), Nov. 3, 1981, [95 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/95/1059), § 1851; amended [Pub. L. 101–237, title III, § 313(b)(1)](/us/pl/101/237/tIII/s313/b/1), Dec. 18, 1989, [103 Stat. 2077](/us/stat/103/2077); renumbered § 3751, [Pub. L. 102–83, § 5(a)](/us/pl/102/83/s5/a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406](/us/stat/105/406).)*
