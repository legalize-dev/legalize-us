# §§ 351 to 355. Repealed. [Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656), title III, § 307, [69 Stat. 589](/us/stat/69/589)

##### §§ 351 to 355. Repealed. [Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656), title III, § 307, [69 Stat. 589](/us/stat/69/589)

Section 351, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title IV, § 401](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIV/s401), as added [Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 102](/us/stat/60/102), defined terms for purposes of this chapter.

A prior section 351, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title IV, § 401](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIV/s401), as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150](/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150), [58 Stat. 147](/us/stat/58/147), authorized appropriations for purposes of this chapter and was repealed by [act Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 96](/us/stat/60/96).

Section 352, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title IV, § 402](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIV/s402), as added [Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 102](/us/stat/60/102); amended [Sept. 29, 1950, ch. 1112, § 2](/us/act/1950-09-29/ch1112/s2), [64 Stat. 1083](/us/stat/64/1083), related to free postage.

A prior section 352, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title IV, § 402](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIV/s402), as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150](/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150), [58 Stat. 147](/us/stat/58/147), related to free postage and was repealed by [act Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 96](/us/stat/60/96).

Section 353, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title IV, § 403](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIV/s403), as added [Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 103](/us/stat/60/103), related to administration of this chapter.

A prior section 353, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title IV, § 403](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIV/s403), as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150](/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150), [58 Stat. 148](/us/stat/58/148), defined terms for purposes of this chapter and was repealed by [act Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 96](/us/stat/60/96).

Section 354, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title IV, § 404](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIV/s404), as added [Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 103](/us/stat/60/103), related to separability of provisions.

A prior section 354, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title IV, § 404](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIV/s404), as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150](/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150), [58 Stat. 148](/us/stat/58/148), related to separability of provisions and was repealed by [act Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 96](/us/stat/60/96).

Section 355, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title IV, § 405](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIV/s405), as added [Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 103](/us/stat/60/103), related to construction of this chapter.
