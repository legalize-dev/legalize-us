# § 2290. Flood control in Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands

##### § 2290. Flood control in Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands

The Secretary is authorized to use the authority contained in section 205 of the Flood Control Act of 1948 ([33 U.S.C. 701s](/us/usc/t33/s701s)), section 2 of the Flood Control Act of August 28, 1937 ([33 U.S.C. 701g](/us/usc/t33/s701g)), section 14 of the Flood Control Act of 1946 ([33 U.S.C. 701r](/us/usc/t33/s701r)), section 107 of the River and Harbor Act of 1960 ([33 U.S.C. 577](/us/usc/t33/s577)), section 3 of the Act entitled “An Act authorizing Federal participation in the cost of protecting the shores of publicly owned property”, approved August 13, 1946 ([33 U.S.C. 426g](/us/usc/t33/s426g)), and section 111 of the River and Harbor Act of 1968 ([33 U.S.C. 426i](/us/usc/t33/s426i)) in the Trust Territory of the Pacific Islands.

*([Pub. L. 99–662, title IX, § 915(h)](/us/pl/99/662/tIX/s915/h), Nov. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 4191](/us/stat/100/4191).)*
