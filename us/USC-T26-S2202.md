##### [§ 2202. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1902(a)(8)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1902/a/8), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1805](/us/stat/90/1805)]

Section, acts [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 401](/us/stat/68A/401); June 25, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–70, § 22(a)](/us/pl/86/70/s22/a), [73 Stat. 146](/us/stat/73/146); July 12, 1960, [Pub. Repeal applicable to estates of decedents dying after Oct. 4, 1976, see [section 1902(c)(1) of Pub. L. 94–455](/us/pl/94/455/s1902/c/1), set out as an Effective Date of 1976 Amendment note under [section 2012 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s2012).
