# § 1780h. Omitted

##### § 1780h. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 109–286, § 10](/us/pl/109/286/s10), Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1225](/us/stat/120/1225), which related to management of lands prior to conveyance, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
