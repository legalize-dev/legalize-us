##### § 1456a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–164, title III, § 306(b)](/us/pl/95/164/tIII/s306/b), Nov. 9, 1977, [91 Stat. 1322](/us/stat/91/1322)

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 120 days after Nov. 9, 1977, see [section 307 of Pub. L. 95–164](/us/pl/95/164/s307), set out as an Effective Date of 1977 Amendment note under [section 801 of Title 30](/us/usc/t30/s801), Mineral Lands and Mining.
