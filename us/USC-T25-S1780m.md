# § 1780m. Omitted

##### § 1780m. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 109–286, § 15](/us/pl/109/286/s15), Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1229](/us/stat/120/1229), which provided for rule of construction and judicial review of the Settlement and Los Alamos Agreements, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
