# § 11183. Withholding

##### § 11183. Withholding

(1) the program or activity for which the grant or contract involved was made has been so changed that it no longer complies with this subchapter; or

(2) in the operation of such program or activity there is failure to comply substantially with any provision of this subchapter;

*([Pub. L. 93–415, title II, § 299B](/us/pl/93/415/tII/s299B), formerly § 293, as added [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII, § 7266(4)](/us/pl/100/690/tVII/s7266/4), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4449](/us/stat/102/4449); renumbered § 299B, [Pub. L. 102–586, § 2(i)(1)(B)](/us/pl/102/586/s2/i/1/B), Nov. 4, 1992, [106 Stat. 5006](/us/stat/106/5006).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 5673 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s5673), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Effective Date

Section effective Oct. 1, 1988, see [section 7296(a) of Pub. L. 100–690](/us/pl/100/690/s7296/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 11101 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s11101).
