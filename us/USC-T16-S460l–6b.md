##### § 460l–6b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–203, title V, § 5201(d)(2)](/us/pl/100/203/tV/s5201/d/2), Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–267](/us/stat/101/1330-267)
