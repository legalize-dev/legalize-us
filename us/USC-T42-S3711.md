# § 3711. Transferred

##### § 3711. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 3711 was editorially reclassified as [section 10101 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10101), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of 1994 Amendment

[Pub. L. 103–322, title XXXII, § 320701](/us/pl/103/322/tXXXII/s320701), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2121](/us/stat/108/2121), provided that subtitle G of title XXXII of [Pub. L. 103–322](/us/pl/103/322), which amended former [section 3760 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3760) and enacted provisions formerly set out as a note under former [section 3760 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3760), could be cited as the “Safer Streets and Neighborhoods Act of 1994”.
