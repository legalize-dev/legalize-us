# §§ 8771 to 8780. Omitted

##### §§ 8771 to 8780. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 8771 to 8780 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under [section 8791 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s8791).

Section 8771, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 171](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s171), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 673](/us/stat/94/673), related to general powers of Corporation.

Section 8772, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 172](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s172), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 674](/us/stat/94/674), related to coordination by Corporation with Federal entities.

Section 8773, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 173](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s173), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 675](/us/stat/94/675), related to patents.

Section 8774, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 174](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s174), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 676](/us/stat/94/676), related to utilization of small and disadvantaged businesses.

Section 8775, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 175](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s175), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 676](/us/stat/94/676); [Pub. L. 98–426, § 27(d)(2)](/us/pl/98/426/s27/d/2), Sept. 28, 1984, [98 Stat. 1654](/us/stat/98/1654), provided relationship to other Federal laws.

Section 8776, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 176](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s176), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 678](/us/stat/94/678), provided for severability of provisions.

Section 8777, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 177](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s177), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 678](/us/stat/94/678), specified Corporation’s fiscal year and provided for audits and reports.

Section 8778, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 178](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s178), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 679](/us/stat/94/679), related to water rights.

Section 8779, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 179](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s179), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 679](/us/stat/94/679), related to Western Hemisphere projects.

Section 8780, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 180](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s180), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 680](/us/stat/94/680), related to a lender financial protection and completion guarantee study.
