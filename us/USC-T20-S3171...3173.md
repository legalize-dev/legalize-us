# §§ 3171 to 3173. Omitted

##### §§ 3171 to 3173. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 3171, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5101](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s5101), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 252](/us/stat/102/252), provided that this subchapter could be cited as the “Drug-Free Schools and Communities Act of 1986”.

A prior section 3171, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 531](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s531), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title V, § 501](/us/pl/95/561/tV/s501), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2249](/us/stat/92/2249); amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 301(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/88/tIII/s301/a/1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677](/us/stat/93/677), 692, related to national and State councils on quality in education, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s587/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480](/us/stat/95/480), eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

Section 3172, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5102](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s5102), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 252](/us/stat/102/252), stated findings of Congress.

Section 3173, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5103](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s5103), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 252](/us/stat/102/252), stated purpose of this subchapter.
