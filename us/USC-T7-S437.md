# § 437. Omitted

##### § 437. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Apr. 21, 1948, ch. 224, § 2](/us/act/1948-04-21/ch224/s2), [62 Stat. 197](/us/stat/62/197), authorized Secretary of Agriculture to receive the property transferred by former [section 436 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s436) and directed the Secretary to administer it to best advance the livestock and agricultural interests of the United States, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Authorization of Appropriations; Abolition of Army Remount Program

[Act Apr. 21, 1948, ch. 224, § 4](/us/act/1948-04-21/ch224/s4), [62 Stat. 198](/us/stat/62/198), authorized appropriations to carry out former sections 436 to 438 of this title and abolished authority of Department of the Army to conduct a remount breeding program.
