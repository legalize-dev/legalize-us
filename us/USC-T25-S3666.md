# § 3666. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 3666. Authorization of appropriations

For purposes of carrying out the activities under this subchapter, there are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary for fiscal years 2011 through 2015.

*([Pub. L. 106–559, title I, § 107](/us/pl/106/559/tI/s107), formerly § 106, Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2781](/us/stat/114/2781); renumbered § 107 and amended [Pub. L. 111–211, title II](/us/pl/111/211/tII), §§ 214(a)(2)(A), 242(b)(3)(A), July 29, 2010, [124 Stat. 2270](/us/stat/124/2270), 2292.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2010—[Pub. L. 111–211, § 242(b)(3)(A)](/us/pl/111/211/s242/b/3/A), substituted “2011 through 2015” for “2000 through 2004”.
