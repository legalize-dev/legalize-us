# § 3889. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–415, title IV, § 402(b)](/us/pl/93/415/tIV/s402/b), Sept. 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 1133](/us/stat/88/1133)

##### § 3889. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–415, title IV, § 402(b)](/us/pl/93/415/tIV/s402/b), Sept. 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 1133](/us/stat/88/1133)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV, § 409](/us/pl/90/445/tIV/s409), as added [Pub. L. 92–381, § 1](/us/pl/92/381/s1), Aug. 14, 1972, [86 Stat. 537](/us/stat/86/537), related to preparation and submission of a report to the President by the Interdepartmental Council concerning all Federal activities in the field of juvenile delinquency.

A prior section 3889, [Pub. L. 90–445, title IV, § 409](/us/pl/90/445/tIV/s409), July 31, 1968, [82 Stat. 473](/us/stat/82/473), related to appointment of advisory committees by the Secretary in respect to activities under prior chapter 47, prior to the general amendment of [Pub. L. 90–445](/us/pl/90/445) by [section 1 of Pub. L. 92–381](/us/pl/92/381/s1).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Repeals

Title IV of [Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415), which repealed this section, was repealed by [Pub. L. 95–115, § 10](/us/pl/95/115/s10), Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1061](/us/stat/91/1061), and [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title II, § 12221(a)(4)](/us/pl/107/273/dC/tII/s12221/a/4), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1894](/us/stat/116/1894).

Repeal by [Pub. L. 95–115](/us/pl/95/115) effective Oct. 1, 1977, see former [section 263(c) of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s263/c), as added by [Pub. L. 95–115](/us/pl/95/115), which is classified as an Effective Date of 1977 Amendment note under [section 11101 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s11101), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
