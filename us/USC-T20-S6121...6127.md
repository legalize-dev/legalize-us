# §§ 6121 to 6127. Omitted

##### §§ 6121 to 6127. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 6121 to 6127 were omitted pursuant to [section 6251 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6251) which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001.

Section 6121, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 201](/us/pl/103/239/tII/s201), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 578](/us/stat/108/578), stated purpose of this part.

Section 6122, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 202](/us/pl/103/239/tII/s202), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 578](/us/stat/108/578), authorized grants to States and territories.

Section 6123, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 203](/us/pl/103/239/tII/s203), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 579](/us/stat/108/579), related to application for grant.

Section 6124, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 204](/us/pl/103/239/tII/s204), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 580](/us/stat/108/580), related to approval of application.

Section 6125, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 205](/us/pl/103/239/tII/s205), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 580](/us/stat/108/580), related to use of amounts from grant.

Section 6126, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 206](/us/pl/103/239/tII/s206), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 581](/us/stat/108/581), related to maintenance of effort by State.

Section 6127, [Pub. L. 103–239, title II, § 207](/us/pl/103/239/tII/s207), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 582](/us/stat/108/582), required reports by State.
