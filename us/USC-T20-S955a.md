# § 955a. Omitted

##### § 955a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–146, title II](/us/pl/98/146/tII), Nov. 4, 1983, [97 Stat. 949](/us/stat/97/949), which provided that persons serving on National Council on the Arts continue until their successors are qualified for office, was omitted as superseded. See [section 955(c) of this title](/us/usc/t20/s955/c) as amended by [Pub. L. 98–306](/us/pl/98/306). Similar provisions were contained in [Pub. L. 97–394, title II](/us/pl/97/394/tII), Dec. 30, 1982, [96 Stat. 1994](/us/stat/96/1994).
