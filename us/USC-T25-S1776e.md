# § 1776e. Omitted

##### § 1776e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–444, § 7](/us/pl/103/444/s7), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4640](/us/stat/108/4640), which related to effect of Federal recognition of the tribe on eligibility of a tribe or a member of a tribe for other Federal services, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
