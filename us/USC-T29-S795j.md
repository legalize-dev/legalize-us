# § 795j. Eligibility

##### § 795j. Eligibility

(1) the individual is eligible for vocational rehabilitation services under subchapter I;

(2) the individual is determined to be an individual with a most significant disability;

(3) for purposes of activities carried out with funds described in [section 795h(d) of this title](/us/usc/t29/s795h/d), the individual is a youth with a disability, as defined in [section 705(42) of this title](/us/usc/t29/s705/42); 11 See References in Text note below. and

(4) a comprehensive assessment of the rehabilitation needs of the individual described in [section 705(2)(B) of this title](/us/usc/t29/s705/2/B), including an evaluation of rehabilitation, career, and job needs, identifies supported employment as the appropriate employment outcome for the individual.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 705(42) of this title](/us/usc/t29/s705/42), referred to in par. (3), was in the original “section (7)(42)”, and was translated as meaning section 7(42) of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973, which is classified to [section 705(42) of this title](/us/usc/t29/s705/42), to reflect the probable intent of Congress.

L. 105–220](/us/pl/105/220). See [section 795g of this title](/us/usc/t29/s795g).
