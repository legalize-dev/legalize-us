# [§ 14. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–473, § 2(a)](/us/pl/89/473/s2/a), June 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 221](/us/stat/80/221)]

##### [§ 14. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–473, § 2(a)](/us/pl/89/473/s2/a), June 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 221](/us/stat/80/221)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 87–489, § 1(a)](/us/pl/87/489/s1/a), June 19, 1962, [76 Stat. 104](/us/stat/76/104), provided for reimbursement between appropriations. See [section 1534 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1534), Money and Finance.
