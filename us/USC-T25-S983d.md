# § 983d. Omitted

##### § 983d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–484, § 6](/us/pl/101/484/s6), Oct. 31, 1990, [104 Stat. 1168](/us/stat/104/1168), which provided for governance of the tribe by an Interim Council until a constitution would be adopted and tribal officials would be elected, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
