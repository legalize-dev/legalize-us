# § 3813. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 3813. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 96–468, § 13](/us/pl/96/468/s13), formerly § 14, Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2234](/us/stat/94/2234); renumbered § 13, [Pub. L. 104–127, title IX, § 914(b)(2)](/us/pl/104/127/tIX/s914/b/2), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1186](/us/stat/110/1186).)*
