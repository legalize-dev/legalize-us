# [§§ 572a to 574. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII, § 7608(a)(2)(A)](/us/pl/100/690/tVII/s7608/a/2/A), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4514](/us/stat/102/4514)]

##### [§§ 572a to 574. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII, § 7608(a)(2)(A)](/us/pl/100/690/tVII/s7608/a/2/A), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4514](/us/stat/102/4514)]

Section 572a, added [Pub. L. 97–258, § 2(g)(3)(B)](/us/pl/97/258/s2/g/3/B), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1060](/us/stat/96/1060), related to depositing of public moneys. See [section 566(f) of this title](/us/usc/t28/s566/f).

Section 573, added [Pub. L. 89–554, § 4(c)](/us/pl/89/554/s4/c), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 621](/us/stat/80/621), related to delivery of prisoners to a successor. See [section 566(g)(1) of this title](/us/usc/t28/s566/g/1).

Section 574, added [Pub. L. 89–554, § 4(c)](/us/pl/89/554/s4/c), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 621](/us/stat/80/621), related to delivery of unserved process to a successor. See [section 566(g)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t28/s566/g/2).
