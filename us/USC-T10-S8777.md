# § 8777. Quarters: Nurse Corps officers; assignment in hospitals

##### § 8777. Quarters: Nurse Corps officers; assignment in hospitals

Under such regulations as the Secretary of the Navy prescribes, officers in the Nurse Corps may be assigned quarters in naval hospitals.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 469](/us/stat/70A/469), § 7577; renumbered § 8777, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(d)(9)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/d/9), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1837](/us/stat/132/1837).)*
