# § 2200j. Definitions

##### § 2200j. Definitions

(1) The term “program” means the Defense Civilian Training Corps program established under section 2200g.

(2) The term “member of the program” means a student at an accredited civilian educational institution who is enrolled in the program.

*(Added [Pub. L. 116–92, div. A, title VIII, § 860(a)](/us/pl/116/92/dA/tVIII/s860/a), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 1514](/us/stat/133/1514); amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title X, § 1081(a)(4)(A)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tX/s1081/a/4/A), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3870](/us/stat/134/3870).)*
