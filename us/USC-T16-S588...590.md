# §§ 588 to 590. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 648](/us/stat/80/648)

##### §§ 588 to 590. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 648](/us/stat/80/648)

Sections, [act Mar. 31, 1933, ch. 17](/us/act/1933-03-31/ch17), §§ 4–6, [48 Stat. 23](/us/stat/48/23), related to appropriations, loans and duration of authority for the period of employment in forestation.

[Act Apr. 8, 1935, ch. 48, § 14](/us/act/1935-04-08/ch48/s14), [49 Stat. 119](/us/stat/49/119), formerly classified to [section 590 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s590), was repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 649](/us/stat/80/649).
