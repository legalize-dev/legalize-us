# § 7908. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 7908. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 101–240, title VII, § 738](/us/pl/101/240/tVII/s738), as added [Pub. L. 109–58, title XVI, § 1611](/us/pl/109/58/tXVI/s1611), Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 1117](/us/stat/119/1117).)*
