# § 4691. Transmission of reports

##### § 4691. Transmission of reports

Each report issued by a rapid response labor panel constituted in accordance with Annex 31–A of the USMCA shall be immediately submitted to the appropriate congressional committees, the Labor Advisory Committee established under [section 2155(c)(1) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2155/c/1) (or successor advisory committee), and, as appropriate, the petitioner submitting information pursuant to [section 4646 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4646). The Trade Representative shall also make each such report publicly available in a timely manner.

*([Pub. L. 116–113, title VII, § 751](/us/pl/116/113/tVII/s751), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 89](/us/stat/134/89).)*
