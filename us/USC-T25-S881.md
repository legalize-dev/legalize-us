# § 881. Omitted

##### § 881. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–401, § 1](/us/pl/91/401/s1), Sept. 16, 1970, [84 Stat. 838](/us/stat/84/838), which related to disposition of the judgment fund of the Potawatomi Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
