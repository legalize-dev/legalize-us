# § 3663. Appellate review

##### § 3663. Appellate review

A person, including the Postal Service, adversely affected or aggrieved by a final order or decision of the Postal Regulatory Commission may, within 30 days after such order or decision becomes final, institute proceedings for review thereof by filing a petition in the United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia. The court shall review the order or decision in accordance with [section 706 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s706), and chapter 158 and [section 2112 of title 28](/us/usc/t28/s2112), on the basis of the record before the Commission.

*(Added [Pub. L. 109–435, title II, § 205](/us/pl/109/435/tII/s205), Dec. 20, 2006, [120 Stat. 3217](/us/stat/120/3217).)*
