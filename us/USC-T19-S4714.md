# § 4714. Enforcement actions

##### § 4714. Enforcement actions

(1) may request the Trade Representative to, within a reasonable period of time, request consultations under—

(A) article 24.29 of the USMCA (relating to environment consultations) with respect to the USMCA country; or

(B) articles 31.4 and 31.6 of the USMCA (relating to dispute settlement consultations) with respect to the USMCA country; or

(2) may request the heads of other Federal agencies described in [section 4715 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4715) to initiate monitoring or enforcement actions with respect to the USMCA country under the provisions of law described in [section 4715 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4715).

*([Pub. L. 116–113, title VIII, § 814](/us/pl/116/113/tVIII/s814), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 93](/us/stat/134/93).)*
