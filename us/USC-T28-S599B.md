# § 599B. Personnel Management demonstration 11 So in original. Probably should be capitalized. project 1

##### § 599B. Personnel Management demonstration 11 So in original. Probably should be capitalized. project 1

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, the Personnel Management Demonstration Project established under section 102 of title I of division C of the Omnibus Consolidated and Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Act for Fiscal Year 1999 ([Public Law 105–277](/us/pl/105/277); 122 22 So in original. Probably should be “112”. Stat. 2681–585) shall be transferred to the Attorney General of the United States for continued use by the Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms, and Explosives, Department of Justice, and the Secretary of the Treasury for continued use by the Tax and Trade Bureau.

*(Added and amended [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI, § 1187(b)](/us/pl/109/162/tXI/s1187/b), (c)(2), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3127](/us/stat/119/3127), 3128.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Section 102 of title I of division C of the Omnibus Consolidated and Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Act for Fiscal Year 1999, referred to in text, probably means section 102 of title I of div. C of the Omnibus Consolidated and Emergency Supplemental Appropriations Act, 1999, [Pub. L. 105–277](/us/pl/105/277), which amended [section 122 of Pub. L. 105–119](/us/pl/105/119/s122), classified as a note under [section 3104 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s3104), Government Organization and Employees.

#### Codification

The text of [section 1115 of Pub. L. 107–296](/us/pl/107/296/s1115), formerly classified as [section 533 of Title 6](/us/usc/t6/s533), Domestic Security, which was transferred to this chapter, redesignated as this section, and amended by [Pub. L. 109–162, § 1187(b)](/us/pl/109/162/s1187/b), (c)(2), was based on [Pub. L. 107–296, title XI, § 1115](/us/pl/107/296/tXI/s1115), Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2280](/us/stat/116/2280).

#### Amendments

2006—[Pub. L. 109–162](/us/pl/109/162) transferred [section 1115 of Pub. L. 107–296](/us/pl/107/296/s1115) to this chapter, redesignated it as this section, and substituted “demonstration project” for “Demonstration Project” in the section catchline. See Codification note above.
