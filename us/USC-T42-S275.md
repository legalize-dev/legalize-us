# § 275. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–43, title I, § 121(a)](/us/pl/103/43/tI/s121/a), June 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 133](/us/stat/107/133)

##### § 275. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–43, title I, § 121(a)](/us/pl/103/43/tI/s121/a), June 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 133](/us/stat/107/133)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III, § 381](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIII/s381), as added Nov. 20, 1985, [Pub. L. 99–158, § 11](/us/pl/99/158/s11), [99 Stat. 883](/us/stat/99/883); amended Nov. 4, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–607, title I, § 157(a)](/us/pl/100/607/tI/s157/a), [102 Stat. 3059](/us/stat/102/3059), established the Biomedical Ethics Board and provided for its membership, functions, reports to Congress, etc., and provided for appointment of a Biomedical Ethics Advisory Committee to assist the Biomedical Ethics Board.

A prior section 275, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III, § 381](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIII/s381), formerly § 371, as added [Aug. 3, 1956, ch. 907, § 1](/us/act/1956-08-03/ch907/s1), [70 Stat. 960](/us/stat/70/960); renumbered § 381, Mar. 13, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–212, § 10(a)(3)](/us/pl/91/212/s10/a/3), [84 Stat. 66](/us/stat/84/66), established a National Library of Medicine in the Public Health Service and stated the congressional purposes for such establishment, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 99–158, § 3(b)](/us/pl/99/158/s3/b), Nov. 20, 1985, [99 Stat. 879](/us/stat/99/879).
