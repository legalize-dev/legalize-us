##### § 1138e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VI, § 65, [48 Stat. 269](/us/stat/48/269); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title I, § 110(a), [69 Stat. 662](/us/stat/69/662); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(p), [70 Stat. 666](/us/stat/70/666), provided for receivership and voluntary liquidation for production credit associations and banks for cooperatives. See [section 2183 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2183).
