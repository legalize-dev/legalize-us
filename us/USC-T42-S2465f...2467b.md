# §§ 2465f to 2467b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

##### §§ 2465f to 2467b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

Section 2465f, [Pub. L. 101–611, title II, § 206](/us/pl/101/611/tII/s206), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3207](/us/stat/104/3207); [Pub. L. 105–303, title II, § 203(4)](/us/pl/105/303/tII/s203/4), Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2855](/us/stat/112/2855), related to conditions for launching commercial payloads on the space shuttle. See [section 70103(b) of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s70103/b), National and Commercial Space Programs.

Section 2466, [Pub. L. 99–170, title II, § 201](/us/pl/99/170/tII/s201), Dec. 5, 1985, [99 Stat. 1017](/us/stat/99/1017), related to shuttle pricing policy and congressional findings and declaration of purpose.

Section 2466a, [Pub. L. 99–170, title II, § 202](/us/pl/99/170/tII/s202), Dec. 5, 1985, [99 Stat. 1017](/us/stat/99/1017), related to goals of the reimbursement pricing policy for the Space Transportation System.

Section 2466b, [Pub. L. 99–170, title II, § 203](/us/pl/99/170/tII/s203), Dec. 5, 1985, [99 Stat. 1017](/us/stat/99/1017), defined “Administrator” and “additive cost”.

Section 2466c, [Pub. L. 99–170, title II, § 204](/us/pl/99/170/tII/s204), Dec. 5, 1985, [99 Stat. 1017](/us/stat/99/1017); [Pub. L. 103–437, § 15(c)(5)](/us/pl/103/437/s15/c/5), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4592](/us/stat/108/4592), related to duties of Administrator.

Section 2467, [Pub. L. 100–404, title II](/us/pl/100/404/tII), Aug. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 1028](/us/stat/102/1028); [Pub. L. 103–327, title III](/us/pl/103/327/tIII), Sept. 28, 1994, [108 Stat. 2328](/us/stat/108/2328), related to Science, Space, and Technology Education Trust Fund and Administrator’s annual report to Congress. See [section 40901 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s40901).

Section 2467a, [Pub. L. 102–195, § 20](/us/pl/102/195/s20), Dec. 9, 1991, [105 Stat. 1615](/us/stat/105/1615), related to National Aeronautics and Space Administration Endeavor Teacher Fellowship Trust Fund. See [section 40902 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s40902).

Section 2467b, [Pub. L. 102–588, title III, § 304](/us/pl/102/588/tIII/s304), Nov. 4, 1992, [106 Stat. 5120](/us/stat/106/5120), related to requirements for merit grant competition. See section 40903(a) to (c) of Title 51.
