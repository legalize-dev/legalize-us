# § 1145. Omitted

##### § 1145. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–93, § 5](/us/pl/90/93/s5), Sept. 27, 1967, [81 Stat. 229](/us/stat/81/229), which related to disposition of apportioned shares, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
