# § 558g. Liability insurance

##### § 558g. Liability insurance

The Secretaries shall not require a cooperator or volunteer (as those terms are used in [section 558e of this title](/us/usc/t16/s558e)) to have liability insurance to provide the volunteer services authorized under this Act.

*([Pub. L. 92–300, § 8](/us/pl/92/300/s8), as added [Pub. L. 118–234, title III, § 341](/us/pl/118/234/tIII/s341), Jan. 4, 2025, [138 Stat. 2914](/us/stat/138/2914).)*
