# § 2306. Special provisions

##### § 2306. Special provisions

(a) In general Nothing in this chapter provides an exemption from any limitation on the acquisition of land or interest in land under any Federal law in effect on July 25, 2000.

(b) Other law This chapter shall not apply to land eligible for sale under—

(1) [Public Law 96–586](/us/pl/96/586) (commonly known as the “Santini-Burton Act”) ([94 Stat. 3381](/us/stat/94/3381));

(2) the Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act of 1998 ([Public Law 105–263](/us/pl/105/263); [112 Stat. 2343](/us/stat/112/2343));

(3) the White Pine County Conservation, Recreation, and Development Act of 2006 ([Public Law 109–432](/us/pl/109/432); [120 Stat. 3028](/us/stat/120/3028));

(4) the Lincoln County Conservation, Recreation, and Development Act of 2004 ([Public Law 108–424](/us/pl/108/424); [118 Stat. 2403](/us/stat/118/2403));

(5) subtitle F of title I of the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009 ([16 U.S.C. 1132](/us/usc/t16/s1132) note; [Public Law 111–11](/us/pl/111/11));

(6) subtitle O of title I of the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009 ([16 U.S.C. 460www](/us/usc/t16/s460www) note, 1132 note; [Public Law 111–11](/us/pl/111/11));

(7) section 2601 of the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009 ([Public Law 111–11](/us/pl/111/11); [123 Stat. 1108](/us/stat/123/1108)); or

(8) section 2606 of the Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009 ([Public Law 111–11](/us/pl/111/11); [123 Stat. 1121](/us/stat/123/1121)).

(c) Exchanges Nothing in this chapter precludes, preempts, or limits the authority to exchange land under authorities providing for the exchange of Federal lands, including but not limited to—

(1) the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 ([43 U.S.C. 1701](/us/usc/t43/s1701) et seq.); or

(2) the Federal Land Exchange Facilitation Act of 1988 ([102 Stat. 1086](/us/stat/102/1086)) or the amendments made by that Act.

(d) No new right or benefit Nothing in this chapter 11 See References in Text note below. creates a right or benefit, substantive or procedural, enforceable at law or in equity by a party against the United States, its agencies, its officers, or any other person.

*([Pub. L. 106–248, title II, § 207](/us/pl/106/248/tII/s207), July 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 617](/us/stat/114/617); [Pub. L. 115–141, div. O, title III, § 302(8)](/us/pl/115/141/dO/tIII/s302/8), Mar. 23, 2018, [132 Stat. 1076](/us/stat/132/1076).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Public Law 96–586](/us/pl/96/586) (commonly known as the “Santini-Burton Act”), referred to in subsec. (b)(1), is [Pub. L. 96–586](/us/pl/96/586), Dec. 23, 1980, [94 Stat. 3381](/us/stat/94/3381). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

The Southern Nevada Public Land Management Act of 1998, referred to in subsec. (b)(2), is [Pub. L. 105–263](/us/pl/105/263), Oct. 19, 1998, [112 Stat. 2343](/us/stat/112/2343), which amended [section 460ccc–1 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s460ccc–1), Conservation, and [section 6901 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6901), Money and Finance, and enacted provisions set out as a note under [section 6901 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6901). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title of 1998 Amendment note set out under [section 6901 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6901) and Tables.

The White Pine County Conservation, Recreation, and Development Act of 2006, referred to in subsec. (b)(3), is title III of div. C of [Pub. L. 109–432](/us/pl/109/432), Dec. 20, 2006, [120 Stat. 3028](/us/stat/120/3028), which enacted provisions set out as notes under sections 1241 and 1244 of Title 16, Conservation, and enacted and amended provisions listed in a table of Wilderness Areas set out under [section 1132 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s1132). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title of 2006 Amendment note set out under [section 1241 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s1241) and Tables.

The Lincoln County Conservation, Recreation, and Development Act of 2004, referred to in subsec. (b)(4), is [Pub. L. 108–424](/us/pl/108/424), Nov. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2403](/us/stat/118/2403), which enacted provisions set out as notes under sections 1241 and 1244 of Title 16, Conservation, and provisions listed in a table of Wilderness Areas set out under [section 1132 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s1132) and amended provisions listed in a table of National Wildlife Conservation Areas set out under [section 668dd of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s668dd). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title of 2004 Amendment note set out under [section 1241 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s1241) and Tables.

The Omnibus Public Land Management Act of 2009, referred to in subsec. (b)(5) to (8), is [Pub. L. 111–11](/us/pl/111/11), Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 991](/us/stat/123/991). Subtitle F (§ 1501 et seq.) of title I of the Act enacted provisions listed in a table of Wilderness Areas set out under [section 1132 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s1132), Conservation, and amended [section 1274 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s1274). Subtitle O (§ 1971 et seq.) of title I of the Act enacted [section 346a–6 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s346a–6), amended sections 460www, 460xxx, and 1274 of Title 16, and enacted provisions set out as notes under sections 460www and 1274 of Title 16 and provisions listed in a table of Wilderness Areas set out under [section 1132 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s1132). Section 2601 of the Act is not classified to the Code. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

The Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976, referred to in subsec. (c)(1), is [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2743](/us/stat/90/2743), which is classified principally to chapter 35 (§ 1701 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1701) and Tables.

The Federal Land Exchange Facilitation Act of 1988, referred to in subsec. (c)(2), is [Pub. L. 100–409](/us/pl/100/409), Aug. 20, 1988, [102 Stat. 1086](/us/stat/102/1086), which enacted [section 1723 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1723), amended [section 1716 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1716) and sections 505a, 505b, and 521b of Title 16, Conservation, and enacted provisions set out as notes under sections 751 and 1716 of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title of 1988 Amendment note set out under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1701) and Tables.

This chapter, referred to in subsec. (d), was in the original “this Act” and was translated as reading “this title”, meaning title II of [Pub. L. 106–248](/us/pl/106/248), which enacted this chapter, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
