# § 20987. Consultation with Standards Board and Board of Advisors

##### § 20987. Consultation with Standards Board and Board of Advisors

The Commission shall carry out its duties under this part in consultation with the Standards Board and the Board of Advisors.

*([Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 247](/us/pl/107/252/tII/s247), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1692](/us/stat/116/1692).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 15387 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s15387), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
