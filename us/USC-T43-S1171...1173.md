# §§ 1171 to 1173. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

##### §§ 1171 to 1173. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

Section 1171, R.S. § 2455; acts [Feb. 26, 1895, ch. 133](/us/act/1895-02-26/ch133), [28 Stat. 687](/us/stat/28/687); [June 27, 1906, ch. 3554](/us/act/1906-06-27/ch3554), [34 Stat. 517](/us/stat/34/517); [Mar. 28, 1912, ch. 67](/us/act/1912-03-28/ch67), [37 Stat. 77](/us/stat/37/77); [Mar. 9, 1928, ch. 164](/us/act/1928-03-09/ch164), [45 Stat. 253](/us/stat/45/253); [June 28, 1934, ch. 865, § 14](/us/act/1934-06-28/ch865/s14), [48 Stat. 1274](/us/stat/48/1274); [July 30, 1947, ch. 383](/us/act/1947-07-30/ch383), [61 Stat. 630](/us/stat/61/630), set forth provisions relating to sale of isolated or disconnected tracts by Secretary of the Interior.

Section 1171a, [act Apr. 24, 1928, ch. 428](/us/act/1928-04-24/ch428), [45 Stat. 457](/us/stat/45/457), provided for applicability of [section 1171 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1171) to certain lands in Oklahoma.

Section 1171b, [act May 23, 1930, ch. 313](/us/act/1930-05-23/ch313), [46 Stat. 377](/us/stat/46/377), provided for applicability of [section 1171 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1171) to certain lands in Alabama.

Section 1172, [act Feb. 4, 1919, ch. 13](/us/act/1919-02-04/ch13), [40 Stat. 1055](/us/stat/40/1055), provided for applicability of [section 1171 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1171) to ceded Chippewa Indian lands in Minnesota.

Section 1173, [act May 10, 1920, ch. 178](/us/act/1920-05-10/ch178), [41 Stat. 595](/us/stat/41/595), provided for applicability of [section 1171 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1171) to sale of tracts in Fort Berthold Indian Reservation, North Dakota.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789), provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

#### Savings Provision

Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579) not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579/s701), set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1701).
