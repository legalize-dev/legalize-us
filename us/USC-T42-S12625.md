# § 12625. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title I, § 1515](/us/pl/111/13/tI/s1515), Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1528](/us/stat/123/1528)

##### § 12625. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title I, § 1515](/us/pl/111/13/tI/s1515), Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1528](/us/stat/123/1528)

Section, [Pub. L. 101–610, title I, § 165](/us/pl/101/610/tI/s165), formerly § 195N, as added [Pub. L. 102–484, div. A, title X, § 1092(a)(1)](/us/pl/102/484/dA/tX/s1092/a/1), Oct. 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 2532](/us/stat/106/2532); renumbered § 165 and amended [Pub. L. 103–82, title I, § 104(b)](/us/pl/103/82/tI/s104/b), title IV, § 402(b)(1), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 840](/us/stat/107/840), 918, directed the Corporation to ensure that no amounts appropriated under [section 12681 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12681) be utilized to carry out this division.

Section was formerly classified to [section 12653n of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12653n) prior to renumbering by [section 104(b) of Pub. L. 103–82](/us/pl/103/82/s104/b).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 2009, see [section 6101(a) of Pub. L. 111–13](/us/pl/111/13/s6101/a), set out as an Effective Date of 2009 Amendment note under [section 4950 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4950).
