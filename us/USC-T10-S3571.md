# § 3571. Simplified acquisition threshold

##### § 3571. Simplified acquisition threshold

(a) Simplified Acquisition Threshold.— For purposes of acquisitions by agencies named in [section 3063 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s3063), the simplified acquisition threshold is as specified in [section 134 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s134).

(b) Inapplicable Laws.— No law properly listed in the Federal Acquisition Regulation pursuant to [section 1905 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s1905) shall apply to or with respect to a contract or subcontract that is not greater than the simplified acquisition threshold.

L. 111–350, § 5(b)(9)](/us/pl/111/350/s5/b/9), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3843](/us/stat/124/3843); renumbered § 3571 and amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div.
