# §§ 1221f to 1221h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–297, title V, § 5352(4)](/us/pl/100/297/tV/s5352/4), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 414](/us/stat/102/414)

##### §§ 1221f to 1221h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–297, title V, § 5352(4)](/us/pl/100/297/tV/s5352/4), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 414](/us/stat/102/414)

Section 1221f, [Pub. L. 92–318, title IV, § 441](/us/pl/92/318/tIV/s441), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 343](/us/stat/86/343), established Office of Indian Education. See [section 2641 of Title 25](/us/usc/t25/s2641), Indians.

Section 1221g, [Pub. L. 92–318, title IV, § 442](/us/pl/92/318/tIV/s442), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 343](/us/stat/86/343); [Pub. L. 93–380, title V, § 505(a)(2)](/us/pl/93/380/tV/s505/a/2), title VIII, § 845(d), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 562](/us/stat/88/562), 612; [Pub. L. 94–273](/us/pl/94/273), §§ 3(11), 13(1), Apr. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 376](/us/stat/90/376), 378; [Pub. L. 95–561, title XI, § 1141(c)(3)](/us/pl/95/561/tXI/s1141/c/3), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2329](/us/stat/92/2329); [Pub. L. 98–511, title V, § 513(b)(5)](/us/pl/98/511/tV/s513/b/5), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2400](/us/stat/98/2400), established National Advisory Council on Indian Education. See [section 2642 of Title 25](/us/usc/t25/s2642).

Section 1221h, [Pub. L. 92–318, title IV, § 453](/us/pl/92/318/tIV/s453), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 345](/us/stat/86/345); [Pub. L. 95–561, title XI](/us/pl/95/561/tXI), §§ 1147, 1148, 1151, Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2330](/us/stat/92/2330), 2331, 2333; [Pub. L. 96–46, § 7](/us/pl/96/46/s7), Aug. 6, 1979, [93 Stat. 343](/us/stat/93/343), defined “Indian” for purposes of the Indian Education Act. See [section 2651 of Title 25](/us/usc/t25/s2651).
