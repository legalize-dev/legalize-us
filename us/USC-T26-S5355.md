# § 5355. General provisions relating to bonds

##### § 5355. General provisions relating to bonds

The provisions of section 5551 (relating to bonds) shall be applicable to the bonds required under section 5354.

*(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201](/us/pl/85/859/tII/s201), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/72/1379).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 5355, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 664](/us/stat/68A/664), consisted of provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859](/us/pl/85/859).
