# § 460ccc. Definitions

##### § 460ccc. Definitions

(a) “conservation area” means the Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area established pursuant to [section 460ccc–1 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s460ccc–1);

(b) “public lands” has the meaning stated in [section 1702(e) of title 43](/us/usc/t43/s1702/e); and

(c) “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

*([Pub. L. 101–621, § 2](/us/pl/101/621/s2), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3342](/us/stat/104/3342).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 101–621, § 1](/us/pl/101/621/s1), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3342](/us/stat/104/3342), provided that: “This Act [enacting this subchapter] may be cited as the ‘Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area Establishment Act of 1990’.”
