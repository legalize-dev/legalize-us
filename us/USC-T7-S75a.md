##### § 75a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–354, title II, § 293(a)(2)](/us/pl/103/354/tII/s293/a/2), Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3237](/us/stat/108/3237)

Section, [act Aug. 11, 1916, ch. 313](/us/act/1916-08-11/ch313), pt. B, § 3A, as added Oct. 21, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–582, § 4](/us/pl/94/582/s4), [90 Stat. 2868](/us/stat/90/2868); amended Sept. 29, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–113, title XVI, § 1604(b)](/us/pl/95/113/tXVI/s1604/b), [91 Stat. 1026](/us/stat/91/1026); Nov. 24, 1993, [Pub. L. 103–156, § 15](/us/pl/103/156/s15), [107 Stat. 1530](/us/stat/107/1530), established Federal Grain Inspection Service in Department of Agriculture and provided for cost containment plan to make the Service more efficient.
