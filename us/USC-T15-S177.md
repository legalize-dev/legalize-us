# § 177. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–826, § 3](/us/pl/87/826/s3), Oct. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 953](/us/stat/76/953)

##### § 177. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–826, § 3](/us/pl/87/826/s3), Oct. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 953](/us/stat/76/953)

Section, acts [July 16, 1892, ch. 196, § 1](/us/act/1892-07-16/ch196/s1), [27 Stat. 197](/us/stat/27/197); [Mar. 3, 1893, ch. 211, § 1](/us/act/1893-03-03/ch211/s1), [27 Stat. 689](/us/stat/27/689); [Feb. 14, 1903, ch. 552, § 10](/us/act/1903-02-14/ch552/s10), [32 Stat. 829](/us/stat/32/829); [Aug. 23, 1912, ch. 350, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-23/ch350/s1), [37 Stat. 407](/us/stat/37/407); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1), [37 Stat. 736](/us/stat/37/736), related to returns of exports to foreign countries leaving the United States by rail. See section 301 et seq. of Title 13, Census.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 180 days after Oct. 15, 1962, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 87–826](/us/pl/87/826/s4), set out as a note under [section 301 of Title 13](/us/usc/t13/s301), Census.

#### Savings Provision

See [section 3 of Pub. L. 87–826](/us/pl/87/826/s3), set out as a note under sections 173 and 174 of this title.
