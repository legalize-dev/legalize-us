# § 58b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–137, § 2](/us/pl/100/137/s2), Oct. 21, 1987, [101 Stat. 819](/us/stat/101/819)

##### § 58b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–137, § 2](/us/pl/100/137/s2), Oct. 21, 1987, [101 Stat. 819](/us/stat/101/819)

Section, [Pub. L. 97–12, title I, § 110](/us/pl/97/12/tI/s110), June 5, 1981, [95 Stat. 62](/us/stat/95/62); [Pub. L. 97–51, § 125](/us/pl/97/51/s125), Oct. 1, 1981, [95 Stat. 965](/us/stat/95/965); [Pub. L. 98–367, title I, § 11(a)](/us/pl/98/367/tI/s11/a), July 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 476](/us/stat/98/476); [Pub. L. 99–349, title I, § 2(a)](/us/pl/99/349/tI/s2/a), (b), July 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 741](/us/stat/100/741), 742, provided for transfer to a Senator’s Official Office Expense Account of that Senator’s clerk hire allowance funds remaining at end of fiscal year. See [section 6313 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6313).

[Pub. L. 100–137, § 2](/us/pl/100/137/s2), Oct. 21, 1987, [101 Stat. 819](/us/stat/101/819), provided that the repeal is effective Jan. 1, 1988.
