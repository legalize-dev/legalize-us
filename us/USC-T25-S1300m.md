# § 1300m. Omitted

##### § 1300m. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–454, title III, § 302](/us/pl/103/454/tIII/s302), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4793](/us/stat/108/4793), which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 103–454, title III, § 301](/us/pl/103/454/tIII/s301), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4793](/us/stat/108/4793), provided that title III of [Pub. L. 103–454](/us/pl/103/454), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Paskenta Band Restoration Act”.
