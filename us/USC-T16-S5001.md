# § 5001. Purpose

##### § 5001. Purpose

It is the purpose of this chapter to implement the Convention for the Conservation of Anadromous Stocks in the North Pacific Ocean, signed in Moscow, February 11, 1992.

*([Pub. L. 102–567, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/102/567/tVIII/s802), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4309](/us/stat/106/4309); [Pub. L. 102–587, title VIII, § 8002](/us/pl/102/587/tVIII/s8002), Nov. 4, 1992, [106 Stat. 5098](/us/stat/106/5098); [Pub. L. 106–562, title III, § 304(a)](/us/pl/106/562/tIII/s304/a), Dec. 23, 2000, [114 Stat. 2806](/us/stat/114/2806).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title VIII of [Pub. L. 102–567](/us/pl/102/567), which is classified generally to this chapter. For complete classification of title VIII to the Code, see Short Title note below and Tables.

#### Codification

Title VIII of [Pub. L. 102–567](/us/pl/102/567) and [Pub. L. 102–587](/us/pl/102/587) enacted identical sections. Title VIII of [Pub. L. 102–587](/us/pl/102/587) was repealed by [Pub. L. 106–562](/us/pl/106/562).
