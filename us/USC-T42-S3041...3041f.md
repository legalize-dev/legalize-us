# §§ 3041 to 3041f. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–478, title V, § 501(a)](/us/pl/95/478/tV/s501/a), Oct. 18, 1978, [92 Stat. 1558](/us/stat/92/1558)

##### §§ 3041 to 3041f. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–478, title V, § 501(a)](/us/pl/95/478/tV/s501/a), Oct. 18, 1978, [92 Stat. 1558](/us/stat/92/1558)

Section 3041, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V, § 501](/us/pl/89/73/tV/s501), as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V, § 501](/us/pl/93/29/tV/s501), May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 50](/us/stat/87/50), related to provisions for grants or contracts. See sections 3028 and 3030d(b)(1) of this title.

A prior section 3041, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V, § 501](/us/pl/89/73/tV/s501), July 14, 1965, [79 Stat. 224](/us/stat/79/224); [Pub. L. 91–69, § 8](/us/pl/91/69/s8), Sept. 17, 1969, [83 Stat. 111](/us/stat/83/111), which related to training project grants and contracts, training programs, studies, preparation and dissemination of materials, and conferences, was repealed by [Pub. L. 93–29, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/93/29/tIV/s401), May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 45](/us/stat/87/45).

Section 3041a, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V, § 502](/us/pl/89/73/tV/s502), as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V, § 501](/us/pl/93/29/tV/s501), May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 50](/us/stat/87/50), related to requirements for approval of applications, preferred areas, and agency cooperation.

Section 3041b, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V](/us/pl/89/73/tV) § 503, as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V, § 501](/us/pl/93/29/tV/s501), May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 51](/us/stat/87/51), provided for payments, reservation from appropriations amount of grant or contract, amendments, advances, reimbursement, and installments.

Section 3041c, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V, § 504](/us/pl/89/73/tV/s504), as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V, § 501](/us/pl/93/29/tV/s501), May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 51](/us/stat/87/51), provided for recapture of payments. See [section 3030b of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3030b).

Section 3041d, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V, § 505](/us/pl/89/73/tV/s505), as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V, § 501](/us/pl/93/29/tV/s501), May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 51](/us/stat/87/51); amended [Pub. L. 94–135, title I, § 112(e)](/us/pl/94/135/tI/s112/e), Nov. 28, 1975, [89 Stat. 720](/us/stat/89/720), provided for authorization of appropriations. See [section 3023 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3023).

Section 3041e, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V, § 506](/us/pl/89/73/tV/s506), as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V, § 501](/us/pl/93/29/tV/s501), May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 51](/us/stat/87/51), related to mortgage insurance for Multipurpose Senior Centers.

Section 3041f, [Pub. L. 89–73, title V, § 507](/us/pl/89/73/tV/s507), as added [Pub. L. 93–29, title V, § 501](/us/pl/93/29/tV/s501), May 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 54](/us/stat/87/54); amended [Pub. L. 94–135, title I, § 114(g)](/us/pl/94/135/tI/s114/g), Nov. 28, 1975, [89 Stat. 725](/us/stat/89/725), related to annual interest grants.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 95–478, title V, § 501(a)](/us/pl/95/478/tV/s501/a), Oct. 18, 1978, [92 Stat. 1558](/us/stat/92/1558), provided that the repeal by that section is effective at the close of Sept. 30, 1978.
