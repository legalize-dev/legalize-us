##### § 17733. Repealed or Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 110–422, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/110/422/tIV/s405), Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4789](/us/stat/122/4789), which related to exploration technology development, was repealed in part and transferred in part. Subsec. (b) was repealed and reenacted as [section 70506 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s70506), National and Commercial Space Programs, by [Pub. L. 111–314](/us/pl/111/314), §§ 3, 6, Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3328](/us/stat/124/3328), 3444, which Act enacted Title 51. Subsec. (a) was transferred and is set out as a note under [section 70506 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s70506).
