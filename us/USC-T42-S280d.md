# § 280d. Transferred

##### § 280d. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III, § 399D](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIII/s399D), as added [Pub. L. 102–321, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/102/321/tIV/s401/a), July 10, 1992, [106 Stat. 419](/us/stat/106/419), and amended, which related to grants for services for children of substance abusers, was renumbered section 399A of title III of act July 1, 1944 by [Pub. L. 106–310, div. A, title V, § 502(1)](/us/pl/106/310/dA/tV/s502/1), Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1115](/us/stat/114/1115). Subsequently, section 399D was renumbered section 519 of title V of act July 1, 1944, without reference to its prior renumbering as 399A, by [Pub. L. 106–310, div. B, title XXXI, § 3106(m)](/us/pl/106/310/dB/tXXXI/s3106/m), Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1179](/us/stat/114/1179). Section was transferred to [section 290bb–25 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290bb–25).
