# § 1773. District of Columbia credit unions; conversion to Federal status

##### § 1773. District of Columbia credit unions; conversion to Federal status

Any credit union organized under the District of Columbia Credit Unions Act, as amended, may apply for conversion into a Federal credit union by filing with the National Credit Union Administration Board (in sections 1773 to 1775 of this title referred to as the Board), pursuant to a resolution adopted by a majority of its directors, an organization certificate meeting the requirements of [section 1753 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1753).

*([Pub. L. 88–395, § 1](/us/pl/88/395/s1), Aug. 1, 1964, [78 Stat. 377](/us/stat/78/377); [Pub. L. 91–206, § 3](/us/pl/91/206/s3), Mar. 10, 1970, [84 Stat. 49](/us/stat/84/49); [Pub. L. 95–630, title V, § 501](/us/pl/95/630/tV/s501), Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3680](/us/stat/92/3680).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The District of Columbia Credit Unions Act, referred to in text, was repealed by [Pub. L. 88–395, § 4](/us/pl/88/395/s4), Aug. 1, 1964, [78 Stat. 377](/us/stat/78/377).

#### Codification

Section was not enacted as part of the Federal Credit Union Act which comprises this chapter.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Transfer of Functions

“National Credit Union Administration Board” and “Board” substituted in text for “Director of the Bureau of Federal Credit Unions” and “Director”, respectively, pursuant to [section 3 of Pub. L. 91–206](/us/pl/91/206/s3) and [section 501 of Pub. L. 95–630](/us/pl/95/630/s501) [[12 U.S.C. 1752a](/us/usc/t12/s1752a)] which transferred functions of Bureau of Federal Credit Unions, and Director thereof, to National Credit Union Administration and vested authority for management of Administration in National Credit Union Administration Board.

#### Repeals; Revocation of Organization Certificates Issued Under District of Columbia Credit Unions Act

[Pub. L. 88–395, § 4](/us/pl/88/395/s4), Aug. 1, 1964, [78 Stat. 377](/us/stat/78/377), provided that: “Effective thirty days after enactment of this Act [Aug. 1, 1964], the District of Columbia Credit Unions Act ([47 Stat. 326](/us/stat/47/326)), as amended, is repealed and all organization certificates issued thereunder and still in force are revoked.”
