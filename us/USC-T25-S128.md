# § 128. Repealed. [Pub. L. 117–317, § 2(3)](/us/pl/117/317/s2/3), Dec. 27, 2022, [136 Stat. 4419](/us/stat/136/4419)

##### § 128. Repealed. [Pub. L. 117–317, § 2(3)](/us/pl/117/317/s2/3), Dec. 27, 2022, [136 Stat. 4419](/us/stat/136/4419)

Section, [act Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 132, § 2](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch132/s2), [18 Stat. 449](/us/stat/18/449), prohibited payment of appropriations made for the Indian Service to Indians at war with the United States.
