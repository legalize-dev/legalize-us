# § 2910. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 2910. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated for purposes of making reimbursements under [section 2905 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s2905) to States for the development and implementation of conservation plans and for administration of this chapter under [section 2907 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s2907) not to exceed $5,000,000 for each of fiscal years 1992, 1993, 1994, 1995, 1996, and 1997.

*([Pub. L. 96–366, § 11](/us/pl/96/366/s11), Sept. 29, 1980, [94 Stat. 1329](/us/stat/94/1329); [Pub. L. 99–375](/us/pl/99/375), Aug. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 803](/us/stat/100/803); [Pub. L. 100–653, title VIII, § 801](/us/pl/100/653/tVIII/s801), Nov. 14, 1988, [102 Stat. 3833](/us/stat/102/3833); [Pub. L. 101–593, title I, § 106](/us/pl/101/593/tI/s106), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2955](/us/stat/104/2955); [Pub. L. 102–440, title III, § 301](/us/pl/102/440/tIII/s301), Oct. 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 2234](/us/stat/106/2234).)*
