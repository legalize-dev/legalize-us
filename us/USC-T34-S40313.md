# § 40313. Enactment and consent of the United States

##### § 40313. Enactment and consent of the United States

The National Crime Prevention and Privacy Compact, as set forth in [section 40316 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s40316), is enacted into law and entered into by the Federal Government. The consent of Congress is given to States to enter into the Compact.

*([Pub. L. 105–251, title II, § 214](/us/pl/105/251/tII/s214), Oct. 9, 1998, [112 Stat. 1875](/us/stat/112/1875).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 14613 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s14613), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
