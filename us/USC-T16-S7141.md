# § 7141. Definitions

##### § 7141. Definitions

(1) County funds The term “county funds” means all funds an eligible county elects under [section 7112(d) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7112/d) to reserve for expenditure in accordance with this subchapter.

(2) Participating county The term “participating county” means an eligible county that elects under [section 7112(d) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7112/d) to expend a portion of the Federal funds received under [section 7112 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7112) in accordance with this subchapter.
