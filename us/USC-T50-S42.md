# § 42. Transferred

##### § 42. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title XIII, § 4](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tXIII/s4), [40 Stat. 231](/us/stat/40/231), related to savings provisions and is set out as a Separability note under [section 195 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s195).

Section was formerly classified to [section 536 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s536) prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, by [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645), [62 Stat. 683](/us/stat/62/683).
