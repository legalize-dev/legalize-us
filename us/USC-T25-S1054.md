# § 1054. Omitted

##### § 1054. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 88–506, § 4](/us/pl/88/506/s4), Aug. 30, 1964, [78 Stat. 639](/us/stat/78/639), which provided for payment of costs incurred in preparation of rolls and distribution of payment of pro rata shares, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
