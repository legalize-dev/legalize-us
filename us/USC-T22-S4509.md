##### § 4509. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–138, title II, § 209](/us/pl/102/138/tII/s209), Oct. 28, 1991, [105 Stat. 694](/us/stat/105/694)

Section, [Pub. L. 98–164, title VIII, § 810](/us/pl/98/164/tVIII/s810), Nov. 22, 1983, [97 Stat. 1051](/us/stat/97/1051), provided that provisions of this chapter were to cease to be effective at end of ten-year period beginning on Nov. 22, 1983.
