# § 1795h. Audit of financial transactions

##### § 1795h. Audit of financial transactions

The Comptroller General of the United States shall audit the Central Liquidity Facility under such rules and regulations as the Comptroller may prescribe.

*([June 26, 1934, ch. 750](/us/act/1934-06-26/ch750), title III, formerly subch. III, § 309, as added [Pub. L. 95–630, title XVIII, § 1802](/us/pl/95/630/tXVIII/s1802), Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3723](/us/stat/92/3723); amended [Pub. L. 96–221, title III, § 309(b)(1)](/us/pl/96/221/tIII/s309/b/1), Mar. 31, 1980, [94 Stat. 149](/us/stat/94/149).)*
