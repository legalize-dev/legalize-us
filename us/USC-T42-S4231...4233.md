# §§ 4231 to 4233. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

##### §§ 4231 to 4233. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

Section 4231, [Pub. L. 90–577, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/90/577/tIV/s401), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1103](/us/stat/82/1103), set out the declaration of a development assistance policy. See section 6506(a)–(e) of Title 31, Money and Finance.

Section 4232, [Pub. L. 90–577, title IV, § 402](/us/pl/90/577/tIV/s402), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1104](/us/stat/82/1104), related to the favoring of units of general local government in the provision of loans or grants-in-aid. See [section 6506(f) of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6506/f).

Section 4233, [Pub. L. 90–577, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/90/577/tIV/s403), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1104](/us/stat/82/1104); 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 102, eff. July 1, 1970, 35 F.R. 7959, [84 Stat. 2085](/us/stat/84/2085), related to rules and regulations for the effective administration of this subchapter. See [section 6506(g) of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6506/g).
