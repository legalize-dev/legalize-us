# § 1300l–3. Omitted

##### § 1300l–3. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–434, title II, § 205](/us/pl/103/434/tII/s205), Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4534](/us/stat/108/4534), which related to tribal membership rolls, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
