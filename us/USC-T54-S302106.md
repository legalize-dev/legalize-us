# § 302106. Retention of name

##### § 302106. Retention of name

Notwithstanding [section 43(c) of the Act of July 5, 1946](/us/act/1946-07-05/s43/c) (known as the Trademark Act of 1946) ([15 U.S.C. 1125(c)](/us/usc/t15/s1125/c)), buildings and structures on or eligible for inclusion on the National Register (either individually or as part of a historic district), or designated as an individual landmark or as a contributing building in a historic district by a unit of State or local government, may retain the name historically associated with the building or structure.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3193](/us/stat/128/3193).)*
