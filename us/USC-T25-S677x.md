# § 677x. Omitted

##### § 677x. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 25](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s25), [68 Stat. 877](/us/stat/68/877), which provided that nothing in this subchapter would affect the status of the members of the tribe as citizens of the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
