# § 1154. Omitted

##### § 1154. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–114, § 4](/us/pl/90/114/s4), Oct. 24, 1967, [81 Stat. 335](/us/stat/81/335), which exempted funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
