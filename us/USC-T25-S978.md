# § 978. Omitted

##### § 978. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–629, § 8](/us/pl/87/629/s8), Sept. 5, 1962, [76 Stat. 430](/us/stat/76/430), which related to taxation of property distributed under this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
