# § 941f. Omitted

##### § 941f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–116, § 8](/us/pl/103/116/s8), Oct. 27, 1993, [107 Stat. 1125](/us/stat/107/1125), which related to transitional and provisional tribal government, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
