##### § 10. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 645](/us/stat/80/645)

Section, [act May 24, 1924, ch. 182, § 14](/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182/s14), formerly § 8, [43 Stat. 142](/us/stat/43/142); renumbered § 14 and amended [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276, § 7](/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276/s7), [46 Stat. 1208](/us/stat/46/1208), abolished grade of consular assistant and provided against reduction of salaries of certain consuls.
