# § 78ii. Omitted

##### § 78ii. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 6, 1934, ch. 404, title II, § 210](/us/act/1934-06-06/ch404/tII/s210), [48 Stat. 908](/us/stat/48/908), transferred the powers, duties and functions of the Federal Trade Commission under subchapter I of chapter 2A of this title to the Securities and Exchange Commission. Pending proceedings before the Federal Trade Commission were continued before the Securities and Exchange Commission.
