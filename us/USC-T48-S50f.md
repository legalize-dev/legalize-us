# § 50f. Disposal of miscellaneous revenues from schools, hospitals, and other Indian Service facilities

##### § 50f. Disposal of miscellaneous revenues from schools, hospitals, and other Indian Service facilities

After February 20, 1942, miscellaneous revenues derived from schools, hospitals, and other facilities maintained and operated by the Indian Service for the benefit of Indians and natives of Alaska shall be covered into the Treasury of the United States under the provisions of [section 155 of title 25](/us/usc/t25/s155).

*([Feb. 20, 1942, ch. 98](/us/act/1942-02-20/ch98), [56 Stat. 95](/us/stat/56/95).)*
