# § 18f–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

##### § 18f–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

Section, [act July 1, 1955, ch. 259, § 3](/us/act/1955-07-01/ch259/s3), as added [Pub. L. 104–333, div. I, title VIII, § 804(b)](/us/pl/104/333/dI/tVIII/s804/b), Nov. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 4188](/us/stat/110/4188), related to application and definitions. Subsec. (a), which made certain authorities available to the Secretary of the Interior, was repealed as obsolete. Subsec. (b) was repealed and restated in [section 102502 of Title 54](/us/usc/t54/s102502), National Park Service and Related Programs.
