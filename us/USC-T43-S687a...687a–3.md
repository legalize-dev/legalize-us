# §§ 687a to 687a–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

##### §§ 687a to 687a–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

Section 687a, acts [May 14, 1898, ch. 299, § 10](/us/act/1898-05-14/ch299/s10), [30 Stat. 413](/us/stat/30/413); [Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 323](/us/act/1927-03-03/ch323), [44 Stat. 1364](/us/stat/44/1364); [May 26, 1934, ch. 357](/us/act/1934-05-26/ch357), [48 Stat. 809](/us/stat/48/809); Aug. 23, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–725, § 3](/us/pl/85/725/s3), [72 Stat. 730](/us/stat/72/730), related to purchase rights, price and limits of acreage, and access to waterfront.

Section 687a–1, [act Apr. 29, 1950, ch. 137, § 5](/us/act/1950-04-29/ch137/s5), [64 Stat. 95](/us/stat/64/95), related to filing of notice of claim, and effect of failure to file.

Section 687a–2, acts [May 14, 1898, ch. 299, § 10](/us/act/1898-05-14/ch299/s10), [30 Stat. 413](/us/stat/30/413); [Aug. 3, 1955, ch. 496, § 2](/us/act/1955-08-03/ch496/s2), [69 Stat. 444](/us/stat/69/444), related to entry on lands abutting on navigable waters.

Section 687a–3, [act May 14, 1898, ch. 299, § 10](/us/act/1898-05-14/ch299/s10), [30 Stat. 413](/us/stat/30/413), related to multiple claimants of the same tract.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789), provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after tenth anniversary of date of approval of this Act, Oct. 21, 1976.

#### Savings Provision

Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579) not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579/s701), set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1701).
