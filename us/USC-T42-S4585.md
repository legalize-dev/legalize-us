# § 4585. Transferred

##### § 4585. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–616, title V, § 501](/us/pl/91/616/tV/s501), as added [Pub. L. 94–371, § 7](/us/pl/94/371/s7), July 26, 1976, [90 Stat. 1038](/us/stat/90/1038); amended [Pub. L. 95–622, title II, § 268(c)](/us/pl/95/622/tII/s268/c), (d), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3437](/us/stat/92/3437); [Pub. L. 96–180, § 14](/us/pl/96/180/s14), Jan. 2, 1980, [93 Stat. 1305](/us/stat/93/1305), which directed Secretary to take certain steps to encourage research, was redesignated section 510 of the Public Health Service Act by [Pub. L. 98–24, § 2(b)(9)](/us/pl/98/24/s2/b/9), Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 179](/us/stat/97/179), and is classified to [section 290bb of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290bb).

A prior [section 501 of Pub. L. 91–616](/us/pl/91/616/s501), title V, Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1854](/us/stat/84/1854), was renumbered 601 by [section 7 of Pub. L. 94–371](/us/pl/94/371/s7), and is classified to [section 4591 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4591).
