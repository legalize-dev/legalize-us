# § 2211. Governing body of tribe; construction of chapter as not vesting with authority not authorized by tribal constitution or by-laws

##### § 2211. Governing body of tribe; construction of chapter as not vesting with authority not authorized by tribal constitution or by-laws

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as vesting the governing body of an Indian tribe with any authority which is not authorized by the constitution and by-laws or other organizational document of such tribe.

*([Pub. L. 97–459, title II, § 212](/us/pl/97/459/tII/s212), as added [Pub. L. 98–608, § 1(5)](/us/pl/98/608/s1/5), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 3173](/us/stat/98/3173).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, which was translated as reading “this title”, meaning title II of [Pub. L. 97–459](/us/pl/97/459), to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
