# § 2202e. Waiver of mediation rights by borrowers

##### § 2202e. Waiver of mediation rights by borrowers

No System institution may make a loan secured by a mortgage or lien on agricultural property to a borrower on the condition that the borrower waive any right under the mediation program of any State.

*([Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).14E, as added [Pub. L. 100–233, title V, § 511](/us/pl/100/233/tV/s511), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/101/1664); amended [Pub. L. 103–354, title II, § 282(f)(2)](/us/pl/103/354/tII/s282/f/2), Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3235](/us/stat/108/3235).)*
