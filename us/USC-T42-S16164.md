# § 16164. Cost sharing

##### § 16164. Cost sharing

The costs of carrying out projects and activities under this subchapter shall be shared in accordance with [section 16352 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s16352).

*([Pub. L. 109–58, title VIII, § 820](/us/pl/109/58/tVIII/s820), formerly § 815, Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 855](/us/stat/119/855); renumbered § 820, [Pub. L. 117–58, div. D, title III, § 40314(1)](/us/pl/117/58/dD/tIII/s40314/1), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 1008](/us/stat/135/1008).)*
