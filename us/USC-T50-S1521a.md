# § 1521a. Destruction of existing stockpile of lethal chemical agents and munitions

##### § 1521a. Destruction of existing stockpile of lethal chemical agents and munitions

(a) Program management The Secretary of Defense shall ensure that the program for destruction of the United States stockpile of lethal chemical agents and munitions is managed as a major defense acquisition program (as defined in [section 4201 of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s4201)) in accordance with the essential elements of such programs as may be determined by the Secretary.

(b) Requirement for Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) annual certification Beginning with respect to the budget request for fiscal year 2004, the Under Secretary of Defense (Comptroller) shall submit to the congressional defense committees on an annual basis a certification that the budget request for the chemical agents and munitions destruction program has been submitted in accordance with the requirements of [section 1521 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s1521).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was enacted as part of the Bob Stump National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2003, and not as part of [Pub. L. 91–121, title IV, § 409](/us/pl/91/121/tIV/s409), Nov. 19, 1969, [83 Stat. 209](/us/stat/83/209), which comprises this chapter.

#### Amendments

2021—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283) and [Pub. L. 117–81](/us/pl/117/81) amended subsec. (a) identically, substituting “section 4201” for “section 2430”.
