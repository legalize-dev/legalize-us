##### § 640d–7. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 8](/us/pl/93/531/s8), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1715](/us/stat/88/1715); [Pub. L. 96–305, § 2](/us/pl/96/305/s2), July 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 929](/us/stat/94/929); [Pub. L. 100–666, § 9](/us/pl/100/666/s9), Nov. 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 3933](/us/stat/102/3933), which related to determination of tribal rights and interests in land, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
