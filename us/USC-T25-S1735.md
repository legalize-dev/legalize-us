# § 1735. Omitted

##### § 1735. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–420, § 16](/us/pl/96/420/s16), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1797](/us/stat/94/1797), which related to construction of this subchapter and construction in the State of Maine of Federal laws enacted after Oct. 10, 1980, for the benefit of Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
