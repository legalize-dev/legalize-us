# § 3105. Safeguards

##### § 3105. Safeguards

(1) that records in the custody of the agency are not to be alienated or destroyed except in accordance with sections 3301–3314 of this title, and

(2) the penalties provided by law for the unlawful removal or destruction of records.

*([Pub. L. 90–620](/us/pl/90/620), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1298](/us/stat/82/1298); [Pub. L. 98–497, title I, § 107(b)(22)](/us/pl/98/497/tI/s107/b/22), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2290](/us/stat/98/2290); [Pub. L. 113–187, § 8(11)](/us/pl/113/187/s8/11), Nov. 26, 2014, [128 Stat. 2012](/us/stat/128/2012).)*
