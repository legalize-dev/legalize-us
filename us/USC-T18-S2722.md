# § 2722. Additional unlawful acts

##### § 2722. Additional unlawful acts

(a) Procurement for Unlawful Purpose.— It shall be unlawful for any person knowingly to obtain or disclose personal information, from a motor vehicle record, for any use not permitted under [section 2721(b) of this title](/us/usc/t18/s2721/b).

(b) False Representation.— It shall be unlawful for any person to make false representation to obtain any personal information from an individual’s motor vehicle record.

*(Added [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXX, § 300002(a)](/us/pl/103/322/tXXX/s300002/a), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2101](/us/stat/108/2101).)*
