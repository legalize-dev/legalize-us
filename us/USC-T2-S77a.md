# § 77a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(22)(A)(iii)](/us/pl/104/186/tII/s204/22/A/iii), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1733](/us/stat/110/1733)

##### § 77a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(22)(A)(iii)](/us/pl/104/186/tII/s204/22/A/iii), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1733](/us/stat/110/1733)

Section, based on H. Res. No. 8, par. (3), Ninety-fifth Congress, Jan. 4, 1977, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 95–94, title I, § 115](/us/pl/95/94/tI/s115), Aug. 5, 1977, [91 Stat. 668](/us/stat/91/668), related to compensation of Sergeant at Arms of House.

A prior section 77a, based on H. Res. No. 890, Ninety-second Congress, Oct. 4, 1972, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 92–607](/us/pl/92/607), ch. V, Oct. 31, 1972, [86 Stat. 1509](/us/stat/86/1509), set forth the compensation of the Sergeant at Arms at equal to the annual rate of basic pay fixed for level IV of the Executive Schedule under [section 5315 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s5315), Government Organization and Employees.
