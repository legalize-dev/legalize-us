# §§ 1691 to 1709. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 199(b)(2)](/us/pl/105/220/tI/s199/b/2), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/112/1059)

##### §§ 1691 to 1709. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 199(b)(2)](/us/pl/105/220/tI/s199/b/2), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/112/1059)

Section 1691, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 421](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s421), Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1370](/us/stat/96/1370), stated purpose of Job Corps program.

Section 1692, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 422](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s422), Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1370](/us/stat/96/1370), established the Job Corps.

Section 1693, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 423](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s423), Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1370](/us/stat/96/1370); [Pub. L. 102–367, title I, § 103(b)(4)](/us/pl/102/367/tI/s103/b/4), title IV, § 402(a), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1026](/us/stat/106/1026), 1076, related to eligibility of individuals for the Job Corps.

Section 1694, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 424](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s424), Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1371](/us/stat/96/1371), contained general provisions relating to screening and selection of applicants.

Section 1695, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 425](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s425), Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1372](/us/stat/96/1372); [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 231](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s231), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2031](/us/stat/98/2031), contained special limitations relating to screening and selection of applicants.

Section 1696, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 426](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s426), Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1372](/us/stat/96/1372); [Pub. L. 102–367, title IV, § 402(b)](/us/pl/102/367/tIV/s402/b), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1076](/us/stat/106/1076), related to enrollment and assignment.

L. 98–524, § 4(a)(4)](/us/pl/98/524/s4/a/4), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2487](/us/stat/98/2487); [Pub. L. 102–367, title IV, § 402(c)](/us/pl/102/367/tIV/s402/c), (d), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1076](/us/stat/106/1076), 1077, related to Job Corps centers.

Section 1698, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 428](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s428), Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1373](/us/stat/96/1373); [Pub. L. 102–367, title IV, § 402(e)](/us/pl/102/367/tIV/s402/e), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1077](/us/stat/106/1077), related to program activities.

Section 1703a, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 433A](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s433A), as added [Pub.
