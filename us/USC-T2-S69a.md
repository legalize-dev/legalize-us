# § 69a. Transferred

##### § 69a. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 69a was editorially reclassified as [section 6514 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6514).

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 69a, [Pub. L. 95–94, title I, § 105](/us/pl/95/94/tI/s105), Aug. 5, 1977, [91 Stat. 661](/us/stat/91/661), provided for expenditure of $1,000 during any fiscal year to conduct orientation seminars for new Senators and their staffs, prior to repeal, effective July 1, 1979, by [Pub. L. 96–38, title I, § 107(b)](/us/pl/96/38/tI/s107/b), July 25, 1979, [93 Stat. 113](/us/stat/93/113).
