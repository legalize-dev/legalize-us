# §§ 701 to 709. Transferred

##### §§ 701 to 709. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 701 to 709, comprising title I of the Ethics in Government Act of 1978, [Pub. L. 95–521](/us/pl/95/521), was amended generally by [Pub. L. 101–194, title II, § 202](/us/pl/101/194/tII/s202), Nov. 30, 1989, [103 Stat. 1724](/us/stat/103/1724), effective Jan. 1, 1991, and was transferred to section 101 et seq. of the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
