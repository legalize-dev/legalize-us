# § 1320c–11. Exemptions for religious nonmedical health care institutions

##### § 1320c–11. Exemptions for religious nonmedical health care institutions

The provisions of this part shall not apply with respect to a religious nonmedical health care institution (as defined in [section 1395x(ss)(1) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1395x/ss/1)).

*([Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531), title XI, § 1162, as added [Pub. L. 97–248, title I, § 143](/us/pl/97/248/tI/s143), Sept. 3, 1982, [96 Stat. 393](/us/stat/96/393); amended [Pub. L. 105–33, title IV, § 4454(c)(2)](/us/pl/105/33/tIV/s4454/c/2), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 431](/us/stat/111/431).)*
