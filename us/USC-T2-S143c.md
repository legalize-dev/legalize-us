# § 143c. Use of other library funds to make payments

##### § 143c. Use of other library funds to make payments

In addition to amounts transferred pursuant to [section 182b(e)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t2/s182b/e/2), the Librarian of Congress may transfer amounts made available for salaries and expenses of the Library of Congress during a fiscal year to the applicable appropriations accounts of the United States Capitol Police in order to reimburse the Capitol Police for services provided in connection with a special event or program described in [section 182b(a)(4) of this title](/us/usc/t2/s182b/a/4).

*([Pub. L. 110–161, div. H, title I, § 1004(f)(2)](/us/pl/110/161/dH/tI/s1004/f/2), Dec. 26, 2007, [121 Stat. 2236](/us/stat/121/2236); [Pub. L. 110–178, § 6(b)](/us/pl/110/178/s6/b), Jan. 7, 2008, [121 Stat. 2554](/us/stat/121/2554); [Pub. L. 111–145, § 6(d)(1)](/us/pl/111/145/s6/d/1), Mar. 4, 2010, [124 Stat. 54](/us/stat/124/54).)*
