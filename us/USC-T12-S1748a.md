# § 1748a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–117, title XI, § 1108(aa)](/us/pl/89/117/tXI/s1108/aa), Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 507](/us/stat/79/507)

##### § 1748a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–117, title XI, § 1108(aa)](/us/pl/89/117/tXI/s1108/aa), Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 507](/us/stat/79/507)

Section, [act June 27, 1934, ch. 847, title VIII, § 802](/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847/tVIII/s802), as added [Aug. 8, 1949, ch. 403, § 1](/us/act/1949-08-08/ch403/s1), [63 Stat. 571](/us/stat/63/571); amended [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 783](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch783), title IV, § 401, [69 Stat. 646](/us/stat/69/646), created the Armed Services Housing Insurance Fund.

For establishment of the General Insurance Fund, see [section 1735c of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1735c).
