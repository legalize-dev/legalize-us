# § 2231. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–394, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/105/394/tIV/s401), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3661](/us/stat/112/3661)

##### § 2231. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–394, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/105/394/tIV/s401), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3661](/us/stat/112/3661)

Section, [Pub. L. 100–407, title II, § 201](/us/pl/100/407/tII/s201), as added [Pub. L. 103–218, title II, § 201](/us/pl/103/218/tII/s201), Mar. 9, 1994, [108 Stat. 85](/us/stat/108/85), related to classification system for assistive technology devices and assistive technology services.

A prior section 2231, [Pub. L. 100–407, title II, § 201](/us/pl/100/407/tII/s201), Aug. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/102/1059), directed National Council on the Handicapped to study implementation, acquisition or financing assistive technology devices and services for individuals with disabilities, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–218, § 201](/us/pl/103/218/s201).
