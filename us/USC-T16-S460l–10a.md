##### § 460l–10a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

Section, [Pub. L. 88–578, title I, § 9](/us/pl/88/578/tI/s9), formerly § 8, as added [Pub. L. 90–401, § 4](/us/pl/90/401/s4), July 15, 1968, [82 Stat. 355](/us/stat/82/355); amended [Pub. L. 91–308, § 3](/us/pl/91/308/s3), July 7, 1970, [84 Stat. 410](/us/stat/84/410); renumbered § 9, [Pub. L. 92–347, § 2](/us/pl/92/347/s2), July 11, 1972, [86 Stat. 459](/us/stat/86/459), and amended [Pub. L. 93–303, § 3](/us/pl/93/303/s3), June 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 194](/us/stat/88/194), related to contracts for acquisition of lands and waters. See [section 200308 of Title 54](/us/usc/t54/s200308), National Park Service and Related Programs.

A prior [section 9 of Pub. L. 88–578](/us/pl/88/578/s9) was renumbered section 10 and was classified to section 460*l*–10b of this title prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 113–287](/us/pl/113/287).
