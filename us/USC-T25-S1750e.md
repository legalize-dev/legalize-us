#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 105–83, title VII, § 707](/us/pl/105/83/tVII/s707), Nov. 14, 1997, [111 Stat. 1626](/us/stat/111/1626), which set forth provisions relating to eligibility for Federal services or benefits and taxation of payments and conveyances of land, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
