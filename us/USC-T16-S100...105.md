# §§ 100 to 105. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 100 to 105. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 100, acts [June 30, 1916, ch. 197, § 6](/us/act/1916-06-30/ch197/s6), [39 Stat. 245](/us/stat/39/245); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213), related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 101, [act June 30, 1916, ch. 197, § 7](/us/act/1916-06-30/ch197/s7), [39 Stat. 245](/us/stat/39/245), related to arrest and bail by commissioner [now magistrate judge].

Section 102, [act June 30, 1916, ch. 197, § 8](/us/act/1916-06-30/ch197/s8), [39 Stat. 245](/us/stat/39/245), related to issuance of process.

Section 103, acts [June 30, 1916, ch. 197, § 9](/us/act/1916-06-30/ch197/s9), [39 Stat. 246](/us/stat/39/246); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213), related to salary of commissioner [now magistrate judge].

Section 104, [act June 30, 1916, ch. 197, § 11](/us/act/1916-06-30/ch197/s11), [39 Stat. 246](/us/stat/39/246), related to disposition of fines and costs.

Section 105, [act June 30, 1916, ch. 197, § 10](/us/act/1916-06-30/ch197/s10), [39 Stat. 246](/us/stat/39/246), related to fees, costs, and expenses chargeable to the United States.
