Section, acts [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 9](/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s9), [36 Stat. 538](/us/stat/36/538); [July 27, 1939, ch. 388, § 1](/us/act/1939-07-27/ch388/s1), [53 Stat. 1130](/us/stat/53/1130), related to employment of temporary draftsmen.
