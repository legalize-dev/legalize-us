# § 564r. Omitted

##### § 564r. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 19](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s19), [68 Stat. 722](/us/stat/68/722), which related to termination of Federal powers over tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
