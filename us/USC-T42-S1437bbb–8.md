# § 1437bbb–8. Definitions

##### § 1437bbb–8. Definitions

(1) Jurisdiction The term “jurisdiction” means—

(A) a unit of general local government (as such term is defined in [section 12704 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12704)) that has boundaries, for purposes of carrying out this subchapter, that—

(i) wholly contain the area within which a public housing agency is authorized to operate; and

(ii) do not contain any areas contained within the boundaries of any other participating jurisdiction; and

(B) a consortia of such units of general local government, organized for purposes of this subchapter.

(2) Participating jurisdiction The term “participating jurisdiction” means, with respect to a period for which such an agreement is made, a jurisdiction that has entered into an agreement under [section 1437bbb–5(b)(3) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1437bbb–5/b/3) to receive assistance pursuant to this subchapter for such fiscal year.
