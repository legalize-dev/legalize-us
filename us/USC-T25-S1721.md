# § 1721. Omitted

##### § 1721. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–420, § 2](/us/pl/96/420/s2), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1785](/us/stat/94/1785), which set out Congressional findings and declaration of policy, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 96–420, § 1](/us/pl/96/420/s1), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1785](/us/stat/94/1785), provided that [Pub. L. 96–420](/us/pl/96/420), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Maine Indian Claims Settlement Act of 1980”.
