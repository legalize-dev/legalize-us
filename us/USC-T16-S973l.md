# § 973l. Repealed. [Pub. L. 119–60, div. H, title LXXXIV, § 8422](/us/pl/119/60/dH/tLXXXIV/s8422), Dec. 18, 2025, [139 Stat. 1918](/us/stat/139/1918)

##### § 973l. Repealed. [Pub. L. 119–60, div. H, title LXXXIV, § 8422](/us/pl/119/60/dH/tLXXXIV/s8422), Dec. 18, 2025, [139 Stat. 1918](/us/stat/139/1918)

Section, [Pub. L. 100–330, § 14](/us/pl/100/330/s14), June 7, 1988, [102 Stat. 599](/us/stat/102/599), required the crew of a vessel to allow and assist any observer identified under the Treaty by the Pacific Island Parties.
