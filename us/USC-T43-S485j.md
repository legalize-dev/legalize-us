# § 485j. Effect on existing laws

##### § 485j. Effect on existing laws

The provisions of previous Acts of Congress not inconsistent with the provisions of this subchapter shall remain in full force and effect.

*([Aug. 4, 1939, ch. 418, § 16](/us/act/1939-08-04/ch418/s16), [53 Stat. 1198](/us/stat/53/1198).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Construction With Other Laws

[Act Aug. 4, 1939, ch. 418, § 18](/us/act/1939-08-04/ch418/s18), [53 Stat. 1198](/us/stat/53/1198), provided that: “Nothing in this Act [see [section 485k of this title](/us/usc/t43/s485k)] shall be construed to amend the Boulder Canyon Project Act ([45 Stat. 1057](/us/stat/45/1057)), as amended [section 617 et seq. of this title].”
