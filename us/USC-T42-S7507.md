# § 7507. New motor vehicle emission standards in nonattainment areas

##### § 7507. New motor vehicle emission standards in nonattainment areas

(1) such standards are identical to the California standards for which a waiver has been granted for such model year, and

(2) California and such State adopt such standards at least two years before commencement of such model year (as determined by regulations of the Administrator).

*([July 14, 1955, ch. 360](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360), title I, § 177, as added [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 129(b)](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s129/b), Aug. 7, 1977, [91 Stat. 750](/us/stat/91/750); amended [Pub. L. 101–549, title II, § 232](/us/pl/101/549/tII/s232), Nov. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 2529](/us/stat/104/2529).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1990—[Pub. L. 101–549](/us/pl/101/549) added sentence at end prohibiting States from limiting or prohibiting sale or manufacture of new vehicles or engines certified in California as having met California standards and from taking any actions where effect of those actions would be to create a “third vehicle”.
