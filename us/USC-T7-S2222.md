# § 2222. Details of law clerks

##### § 2222. Details of law clerks

Law clerks may be detailed by the Secretary of Agriculture for service in or out of Washington.

*([Mar. 4, 1911, ch. 238](/us/act/1911-03-04/ch238), [36 Stat. 1236](/us/stat/36/1236).)*
