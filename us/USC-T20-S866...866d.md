# §§ 866 to 866d. Omitted

##### §§ 866 to 866d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general revision of title V of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V](/us/pl/89/10/tV), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 47](/us/stat/79/47), by [Pub. L. 95–561, title V, § 501](/us/pl/95/561/tV/s501), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2240](/us/stat/92/2240).

Section 866, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 521](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s521), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(D)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 142](/us/stat/84/142); amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 104(b)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s104/b), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 503](/us/stat/88/503); [Pub. L. 95–112, § 2(e)(2)](/us/pl/95/112/s2/e/2), Sept. 24, 1977, [91 Stat. 911](/us/stat/91/911), authorized appropriations for grants to local educational agencies for fiscal years ending prior to Oct. 1, 1979.

A prior section 866, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 506](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s506), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 51](/us/stat/79/51), provided for payments to States: installments; advances or reimbursement; and adjustments, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(A)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/A), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 142](/us/stat/84/142).

Section 866a, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 522](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s522), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(D)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 143](/us/stat/84/143); amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title VIII, § 843(a)(2)](/us/pl/93/380/tVIII/s843/a/2), (3), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 611](/us/stat/88/611), related to the apportionment of grant funds among States.

Section 866b, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 523](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s523), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(D)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 143](/us/stat/84/143), related to grants from apportioned funds and to the programs and activities covered.

Section 866c, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 524](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s524), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(D)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 144](/us/stat/84/144), related to the approval of applications by the Commissioner.

Section 866d, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 525](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s525), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(D)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 144](/us/stat/84/144), related to the approval of applications by State educational agencies.
