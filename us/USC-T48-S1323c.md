# § 1323c. Omitted

##### § 1323c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, acts [Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 371, § 6](/us/act/1916-08-21/ch371/s6), [39 Stat. 528](/us/stat/39/528); [Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 370, § 11](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch370/s11), [42 Stat. 1008](/us/stat/42/1008); [Feb. 16, 1933, ch. 89, § 2](/us/act/1933-02-16/ch89/s2), [47 Stat. 812](/us/stat/47/812); [June 13, 1940, ch. 358, § 2](/us/act/1940-06-13/ch358/s2), [54 Stat. 389](/us/stat/54/389), related to interest rate on postal-savings certificates and was omitted as not of general application, and as covered by the Canal Zone Code. The Canal Zone Code was subsequently redesignated the Panama Canal Code by [Pub. L. 96–70, § 3303(b)](/us/pl/96/70/s3303/b), and repealed by [Pub. L. 104–201, § 3549](/us/pl/104/201/s3549).
