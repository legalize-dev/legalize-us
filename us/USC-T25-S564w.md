# § 564w. Omitted

##### § 564w. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 26](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s26), [68 Stat. 723](/us/stat/68/723), which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to implement education and training programs for tribe members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
