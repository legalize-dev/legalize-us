# § 940h. Bonding requirements

##### § 940h. Bonding requirements

(1) the interests of the Secretary are adequately protected by product warranties; or

(2) the costs or conditions associated with a bond exceed the benefit of the bond.

*([May 20, 1936, ch. 432](/us/act/1936-05-20/ch432), title III, § 318, as added [Pub. L. 110–234, title VI, § 6109](/us/pl/110/234/tVI/s6109), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1198](/us/stat/122/1198), and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title VI, § 6109, June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 1960.)*
