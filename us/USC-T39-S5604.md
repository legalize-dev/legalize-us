# § 5604. Fines on ocean carriers

##### § 5604. Fines on ocean carriers

(1) unreasonable or unnecessary delay to the mails; and

(2) other delinquencies in the transportation of mail.

*([Pub. L. 91–375](/us/pl/91/375), Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 773](/us/stat/84/773).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective July 1, 1971, pursuant to Resolution No. 71–9 of the Board of Governors. See [section 15(a) of Pub. L. 91–375](/us/pl/91/375/s15/a), set out as a note preceding [section 101 of this title](/us/usc/t39/s101).
