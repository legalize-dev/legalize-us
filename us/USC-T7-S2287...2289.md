# §§ 2287 to 2289. Omitted

##### §§ 2287 to 2289. Omitted

Sections 2287 to 2289 of this title were omitted in the general amendment of this chapter by [Pub. L. 97–98, title XI, § 1116(a)](/us/pl/97/98/tXI/s1116/a), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1270](/us/stat/95/1270).

Section 2287, [Pub. L. 95–113, title XVIII, § 1807](/us/pl/95/113/tXVIII/s1807), Sept. 29, 1977, [91 Stat. 1043](/us/stat/91/1043), related to transmission of an annual report. See [section 2284 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2284).

Section 2288, [Pub. L. 95–113, title XVIII, § 1808](/us/pl/95/113/tXVIII/s1808), Sept. 29, 1977, [91 Stat. 1044](/us/stat/91/1044), related to budget prohibitions. See [section 2285 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2285).

Section 2289, [Pub. L. 95–113, title XVIII, § 1809](/us/pl/95/113/tXVIII/s1809), Sept. 29, 1977, [91 Stat. 1044](/us/stat/91/1044), related to termination of advisory committees. See [section 2286 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2286).
