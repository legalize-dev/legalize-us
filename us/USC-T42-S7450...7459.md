# §§ 7450 to 7459. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–549, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/101/549/tVI/s601), Nov. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 2648](/us/stat/104/2648)

##### §§ 7450 to 7459. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–549, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/101/549/tVI/s601), Nov. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 2648](/us/stat/104/2648)

Section 7450, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 150](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s150), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 725](/us/stat/91/725), set forth Congressional declaration of purpose.

Section 7451, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 151](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s151), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 726](/us/stat/91/726), set forth Congressional findings.

Section 7452, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 152](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s152), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 726](/us/stat/91/726), set forth definitions applicable to this part.

Section 7453, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 153](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s153), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 726](/us/stat/91/726), related to studies by Environmental Protection Agency.

Section 7454, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 154](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s154), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 728](/us/stat/91/728); amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)](/us/pl/96/88/tV/s509/b), Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695](/us/stat/93/695), related to research and monitoring activities by Federal agencies.

Section 7455, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 155](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s155), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 729](/us/stat/91/729), related to reports on progress of regulation.

Section 7456, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 156](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s156), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 729](/us/stat/91/729), authorized President to enter into international agreements to foster cooperative research.

Section 7457, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 157](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s157), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 729](/us/stat/91/729), related to promulgation of regulations.

Section 7458, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 158](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s158), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 730](/us/stat/91/730), set forth other provisions of law that would be unaffected by this part.

Section 7459, [act July 14, 1955, ch. 360, title I, § 159](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360/tI/s159), as added Aug. 7, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–95, title I, § 126](/us/pl/95/95/tI/s126), [91 Stat. 730](/us/stat/91/730), related to authority of States to protect the stratosphere.
