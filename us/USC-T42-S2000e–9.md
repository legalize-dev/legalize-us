# § 2000e–9. Conduct of hearings and investigations pursuant to [section 161 of title 29](/us/usc/t29/s161)

##### § 2000e–9. Conduct of hearings and investigations pursuant to [section 161 of title 29](/us/usc/t29/s161)

For the purpose of all hearings and investigations conducted by the Commission or its duly authorized agents or agencies, [section 161 of title 29](/us/usc/t29/s161) shall apply.

*([Pub. L. 88–352, title VII, § 710](/us/pl/88/352/tVII/s710), July 2, 1964, [78 Stat. 264](/us/stat/78/264); [Pub. L. 92–261, § 7](/us/pl/92/261/s7), Mar. 24, 1972, [86 Stat. 109](/us/stat/86/109).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1972—[Pub. L. 92–261](/us/pl/92/261) substituted provisions making applicable [section 161 of title 29](/us/usc/t29/s161) to all hearings and investigations conducted by the Commission or its authorized agents or agencies, for provisions enumerating the investigatory powers of the Commission and the procedure for their enforcement.
