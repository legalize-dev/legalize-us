# § 2164. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–424, title I, § 102(g)(1)(A)](/us/pl/95/424/tI/s102/g/1/A), Oct. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 942](/us/stat/92/942)

##### § 2164. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–424, title I, § 102(g)(1)(A)](/us/pl/95/424/tI/s102/g/1/A), Oct. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 942](/us/stat/92/942)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. I, § 204, Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 427](/us/stat/75/427), related to the establishment, duties and appointment of officers of the Development Loan Committee. The provisions of this section were redesignated as subsec. (e) of [section 2151t of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2151t) by section 102(d)(1), (2) of [Pub. L. 95–424](/us/pl/95/424).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 605 of Pub. L. 95–424](/us/pl/95/424/s605), set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 2151 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2151).
