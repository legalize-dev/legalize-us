# § 3907. Omitted

##### § 3907. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–448, title IV, § 408](/us/pl/90/448/tIV/s408), Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 516](/us/stat/82/516); [Pub. L. 91–609, title III, § 303(c)](/us/pl/91/609/tIII/s303/c), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1780](/us/stat/84/1780), which related to incontestability of guarantees was omitted pursuant to [section 4528 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4528), which terminated authority to guarantee bonds, debentures, notes, or other obligations under this chapter after Dec. 31, 1970, with exceptions now inapplicable.
