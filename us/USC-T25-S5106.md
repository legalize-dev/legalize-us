# § 5106. Use of funds appropriated under section 5108

##### § 5106. Use of funds appropriated under section 5108

For the purpose of carrying into effect the land-purchase provision of this Act, the Secretary of the Interior is authorized to use so much as may be necessary of any funds heretofore or hereafter appropriated pursuant to [section 5108 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5108).

*([Aug. 10, 1939, ch. 662, § 4](/us/act/1939-08-10/ch662/s4), [53 Stat. 1351](/us/stat/53/1351).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, is [act Aug. 10, 1939, ch. 662](/us/act/1939-08-10/ch662), [53 Stat. 1351](/us/stat/53/1351), which is classified principally to sections 5104 to 5106 of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 463g of this title](/us/usc/t25/s463g) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
