# § 3391. Rule of construction

##### § 3391. Rule of construction

(1) to restore diplomatic relations with the Republic of China; or

(2) to alter the United States Government’s position with respect to the international status of the Republic of China.

*([Pub. L. 117–263, div. E, title LV, § 5539](/us/pl/117/263/dE/tLV/s5539), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 3334](/us/stat/136/3334).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this subtitle”, meaning subtitle A (§§ 5501–5540) of [Pub. L. 117–263, div. E, title LV](/us/pl/117/263/dE/tLV), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 3292](/us/stat/136/3292), known as the Taiwan Enhanced Resilience Act, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 3351 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3351) and Tables.
