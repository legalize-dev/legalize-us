# § 9521. Definition

##### § 9521. Definition

In this part, the term “MBDA Business Center Program” means the program established under section ___113. 11 So in original. See References in Text note below.

*([Pub. L. 117–58, div. K, title I, § 100111](/us/pl/117/58/dK/tI/s100111), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 1451](/us/stat/135/1451).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Section ___113, referred to in text, probably means [section 100113 of Pub. L. 117–58](/us/pl/117/58/s100113), which is classified to [section 9523 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s9523).
