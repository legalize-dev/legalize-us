# § 1128. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–403, title III, § 305(a)(1)](/us/pl/110/403/tIII/s305/a/1), Oct. 13, 2008, [122 Stat. 4270](/us/stat/122/4270)

##### § 1128. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–403, title III, § 305(a)(1)](/us/pl/110/403/tIII/s305/a/1), Oct. 13, 2008, [122 Stat. 4270](/us/stat/122/4270)

Section, [Pub. L. 106–58, title VI, § 653](/us/pl/106/58/tVI/s653), Sept. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 480](/us/stat/113/480); [Pub. L. 106–113, div. B, § 1000(a)(9) [title IV, § 4741(b)(1)]](/us/pl/106/113/dB/s1000/a/9/tIV/s4741/b/1), Nov. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 1536](/us/stat/113/1536), 1501A–586; [Pub. L. 108–447, div. B, title II, § 210](/us/pl/108/447/dB/tII/s210), Dec. 8, 2004, [118 Stat. 2884](/us/stat/118/2884), established the National Intellectual Property Law Enforcement Coordination Council.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 110–403, title III, § 305(a)(1)](/us/pl/110/403/tIII/s305/a/1), Oct. 13, 2008, [122 Stat. 4270](/us/stat/122/4270), provided that the repeal of this section is effective upon confirmation of the Intellectual Property Enforcement Coordinator by the Senate and publication of such appointment in the Congressional Record. The Senate confirmed the first Intellectual Property Enforcement Coordinator on Dec. 3, 2009, as reflected in that day’s Congressional Record. See 155 Cong. Rec. 29389 (2009).
