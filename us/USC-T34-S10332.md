# § 10332. Description of grant program

##### § 10332. Description of grant program

The Director is authorized to make grants to provide equipment and personnel training for the closed-circuit televising and video taping of the testimony of children in criminal proceedings for the violation of laws relating to the abuse of children.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 1402](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s1402), as added [Pub. L. 101–647, title II, § 241(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/647/tII/s241/a/2), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4810](/us/stat/104/4810); amended [Pub. L. 103–322, title IV, § 40156(c)(2)](/us/pl/103/322/tIV/s40156/c/2), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1923](/us/stat/108/1923).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3796aa–1 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796aa–1), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

1994—[Pub. L. 103–322](/us/pl/103/322) struck out “to States, for the use of States and units of local government in the States” after “make grants”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**
