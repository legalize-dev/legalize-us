# § 1977. Termination of authority

##### § 1977. Termination of authority

The prize program will terminate after 5 prize competition cycles have been completed.

*([Pub. L. 109–449, § 127](/us/pl/109/449/s127), formerly [Pub. L. 116–224, title I, § 127](/us/pl/116/224/tI/s127), Dec. 18, 2020, [134 Stat. 1083](/us/stat/134/1083); renumbered [Pub.
