# § 958. Omitted

##### § 958. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 86–339, § 8](/us/pl/86/339/s8), Sept. 21, 1959, [73 Stat. 605](/us/stat/73/605), which authorized band to organize a legal entity and to request the Secretary of the Interior to transfer to such entity title to lands in the reserves established by former [section 953 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s953), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
