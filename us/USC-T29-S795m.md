# § 795m. Savings provision

##### § 795m. Savings provision

(a) Supported employment services Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to prohibit a State from providing supported employment services in accordance with the State plan submitted under [section 721 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s721) by using funds made available through a State allotment under [section 730 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s730).

(b) Postemployment services Nothing in this subchapter shall be construed to prohibit a State from providing discrete postemployment services in accordance with the State plan submitted under [section 721 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s721) by using funds made available through a State allotment under [section 730 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s730) to an individual who is eligible under this subchapter.
