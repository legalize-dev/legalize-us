# § 1643h. Applicability of administrative provisions of subchapter I

##### § 1643h. Applicability of administrative provisions of subchapter I

To the extent they are not inconsistent with the provisions of this subchapter, the following provisions of subchapter I of this chapter shall be applicable to this subchapter: Subsections (b), (c), (d) (e), (h), and (j) of [section 1623 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1623); subsection (f) of [section 1626 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1626).
