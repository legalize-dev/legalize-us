# § 9239. Enlisted members: officers not to use as servants

##### § 9239. Enlisted members: officers not to use as servants

No officer of the Air Force or the Space Force may use an enlisted member of the Air Force or the Space Force as a servant.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 533](/us/stat/70A/533), § 8639; renumbered § 9239, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 806(b)(8)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s806/b/8), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1833](/us/stat/132/1833); amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title IX, § 923(c)(8)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tIX/s923/c/8), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3811](/us/stat/134/3811).)*

The words “in any case whatever” are omitted as surplusage.
