# § 30525. Apportionment of losses

##### § 30525. Apportionment of losses

(1) all claimants shall be paid in proportion to their respective losses out of the amount determined under [section 30523 of this title](/us/usc/t46/s30523); and

(2) personal injury and death claimants, if any, shall be paid an additional amount in proportion to their respective losses out of the additional amount determined under [section 30524(b) of this title](/us/usc/t46/s30524/b).

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 6(c)](/us/pl/109/304/s6/c), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1513](/us/stat/120/1513), § 30507; renumbered § 30525 and amended [Pub. L. 117–263, div. K, title CXV, § 11503(a)(3)](/us/pl/117/263/dK/tCXV/s11503/a/3), (f)(4), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 4130](/us/stat/136/4130), 4131.)*

This section is substituted for 46 App. U.S.C. 183(b) (last sentence) and 184 (words before semicolon) for clarity and consistency and to eliminate unnecessary words. The text of 46 App. U.S.C. 184 (words after semicolon) is omitted as unnecessary. See G. Gilmore & C. Black, *The Law of Admiralty*, § 10–8 (2d ed. 1975).
