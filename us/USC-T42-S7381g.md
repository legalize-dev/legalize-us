# § 7381g. Definitions

##### § 7381g. Definitions

(1) Director The term “Director” means the Director of Science, Engineering, and Mathematics Education.

(2) National Laboratory The term “National Laboratory” has the meaning given the term in [section 15801 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s15801).

*([Pub. L. 101–510, div. C, title XXXI, § 3170](/us/pl/101/510/dC/tXXXI/s3170), as added [Pub. L. 110–69, title V, § 5003(d)(3)](/us/pl/110/69/tV/s5003/d/3), Aug. 9, 2007, [121 Stat. 603](/us/stat/121/603).)*
