# § 5012. Disposition of property

##### § 5012. Disposition of property

The Secretary shall dispose of any United States property held by the International North Pacific Fisheries Commission on the date of its termination in a manner that would further the purposes of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 102–567, title VIII, § 813](/us/pl/102/567/tVIII/s813), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4316](/us/stat/106/4316); [Pub. L. 102–587, title VIII, § 8013](/us/pl/102/587/tVIII/s8013), Nov. 4, 1992, [106 Stat. 5106](/us/stat/106/5106); [Pub. L. 106–562, title III, § 304(a)](/us/pl/106/562/tIII/s304/a), Dec. 23, 2000, [114 Stat. 2806](/us/stat/114/2806).)*
