# §§ 1131d to 1131g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 1131d to 1131g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 1131d, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title II, § 20, [48 Stat. 259](/us/stat/48/259); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(f), [70 Stat. 665](/us/stat/70/665), authorized organization of production credit associations and provided for charters, bylaws, powers of governor, and other powers of such associations. See [section 2093 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2093).

Section 1131e, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title II, § 21, [48 Stat. 260](/us/stat/48/260); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title II, § 201, [69 Stat. 663](/us/stat/69/663); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(g), [70 Stat. 665](/us/stat/70/665), provided for capital stock of production credit associations and its value, classes, voting rights, limitations on transfer, exchange, and dividends. See [section 2094 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2094).

Section 1131e–1, acts [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, § 16](/us/act/1953-08-06/ch335/s16), [67 Stat. 399](/us/stat/67/399); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 107(b), [70 Stat. 666](/us/stat/70/666); Oct. 17, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–582, § 2(b)](/us/pl/90/582/s2/b), [82 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/82/1145), provided for issuance of class C stock for production credit associations and conditions, privileges, restrictions, limitations, and qualifications placed on such stock.

Section 1131f, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title II, § 22, [48 Stat. 261](/us/stat/48/261); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title II, § 202, [69 Stat. 663](/us/stat/69/663); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(h), [70 Stat. 665](/us/stat/70/665); Oct. 3, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–343, § 2(2)](/us/pl/87/343/s2/2), [75 Stat. 758](/us/stat/75/758); Oct. 4, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–237, § 2(a)](/us/pl/89/237/s2/a), [78 Stat. 924](/us/stat/78/924), covered application of earnings of production credit associations and restoration of capital, impairment, surplus account, distribution of earnings, and allocation on a patronage basis. See [section 2095 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2095).

Section 1131g, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title II, § 23, [48 Stat. 261](/us/stat/48/261); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title II, § 204, [69 Stat. 663](/us/stat/69/663); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(i), [70 Stat. 665](/us/stat/70/665); Oct. 4, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–237, § 2(b)](/us/pl/89/237/s2/b), [79 Stat. 924](/us/stat/79/924); Dec. 15, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–204, § 3(a)](/us/pl/90/204/s3/a), [81 Stat. 612](/us/stat/81/612); June 18, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–345, § 2](/us/pl/90/345/s2), [82 Stat. 183](/us/stat/82/183), authorized loans to farmers by production credit associations, terms and conditions, rates of interest, security, limitations on indebtedness, ownership of stock, and investments in equity reserve. See [section 2096 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2096).
