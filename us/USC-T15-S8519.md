# § 8519. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 8519. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated to the Office of Oceanic and Atmospheric Research to carry out this subchapter—

(1) $136,516,000 for fiscal year 2019, of which—

(A) $85,758,000 is authorized for weather laboratories and cooperative institutes;

(B) $30,758,000 is authorized for weather and air chemistry research programs; and

(C) $20,000,000 is authorized for the joint technology transfer initiative described in [section 8512(b)(4) of this title](/us/usc/t15/s8512/b/4);

(2) $148,154,000 for fiscal year 2020, of which—

(A) $87,258,000 is authorized for weather laboratories and cooperative institutes;

(B) $40,896,000 is authorized for weather and air chemistry research programs; and

(C) $20,000,000 is authorized for the joint technology transfer initiative described in [section 8512(b)(4) of this title](/us/usc/t15/s8512/b/4);

(3) $150,154,000 for fiscal year 2021, of which—

(A) $88,758,000 is authorized for weather laboratories and cooperative institutes;

(B) $41,396,000 is authorized for weather and air chemistry research programs; and

(C) $20,000,000 is authorized for the joint technology transfer initiative described in [section 8512(b)(4) of this title](/us/usc/t15/s8512/b/4);

(4) $152,154,000 for fiscal year 2022, of which—

(A) $90,258,000 is authorized for weather laboratories and cooperative institutes;

(B) $41,896,000 is authorized for weather and air chemistry research programs; and

(C) $20,000,000 is authorized for the joint technology transfer initiative described in [section 8512(b)(4) of this title](/us/usc/t15/s8512/b/4); and

(5) $154,154,000 for fiscal year 2023, of which—

(A) $91,758,000 is authorized for weather laboratories and cooperative institutes;

(B) $42,396,000 is authorized for weather and air chemistry research programs; and

(C) $20,000,000 is authorized for the joint technology transfer initiative described in [section 8512(b)(4) of this title](/us/usc/t15/s8512/b/4).

(b) Limitation No additional funds are authorized to carry out this subchapter and the amendments made by this title.11 See References in Text note below.

*([Pub. L. 115–25, title I, § 110](/us/pl/115/25/tI/s110), Apr. 18, 2017, [131 Stat. 98](/us/stat/131/98); [Pub. L. 115–423, § 3(b)](/us/pl/115/423/s3/b), Jan. 7, 2019, [132 Stat. 5455](/us/stat/132/5455).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 115–25](/us/pl/115/25), which enacted this subchapter and amended provisions formerly set out as a note under [section 313 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s313), which is now classified to [section 8520 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s8520). For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Tables.

The amendments made by this title, referred to in subsec. (b), mean the amendments made by title I of [Pub. L. 115–25](/us/pl/115/25), which amended provisions formerly set out as a note under [section 313 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s313) and which is now classified to [section 8520 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s8520).

#### Amendments

2019—[Pub. L. 115–423](/us/pl/115/423) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section related to authorization of appropriations for fiscal years 2017 and 2018.
