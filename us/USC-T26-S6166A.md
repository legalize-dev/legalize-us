# [§ 6166A. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–34, title IV, § 422(d)](/us/pl/97/34/tIV/s422/d), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 315](/us/stat/95/315)]

##### [§ 6166A. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–34, title IV, § 422(d)](/us/pl/97/34/tIV/s422/d), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 315](/us/stat/95/315)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 85–866, title II, § 206(a)](/us/pl/85/866/tII/s206/a), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1681](/us/stat/72/1681), § 6166; amended [Pub. L. 93–625, § 7(d)(2)](/us/pl/93/625/s7/d/2), (3), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2115](/us/stat/88/2115); renumbered § 6166A and amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1906/b/13/A), title XX, § 2004(a), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/90/1834), 1862, provided for an extension of time for payment of estate tax where estate consists largely of interest in closely held business.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to estates of decedents dying after Dec. 31, 1981, see [section 422(f)(1) of Pub. L. 97–34](/us/pl/97/34/s422/f/1), set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under [section 6166 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s6166).
