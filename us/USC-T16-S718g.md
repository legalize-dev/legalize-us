# § 718g. Violations

##### § 718g. Violations

Any person that violates or fails to comply with any provision of this subchapter (including a regulation promulgated under this subchapter) shall be subject to the penalties described in [section 707 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s707).

*([Mar. 16, 1934, ch. 71, § 7](/us/act/1934-03-16/ch71/s7), as added [Pub. L. 109–266, § 10(h)](/us/pl/109/266/s10/h), Aug. 3, 2006, [120 Stat. 678](/us/stat/120/678).)*
