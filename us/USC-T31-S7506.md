# § 7506. Effective date

##### § 7506. Effective date

This chapter shall apply to any non-Federal entity with respect to any of its fiscal years which begin after June 30, 1996.

*(Added [Pub. L. 98–502, § 2(a)](/us/pl/98/502/s2/a), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2334](/us/stat/98/2334), § 7507; amended [Pub. L. 104–156, § 2](/us/pl/104/156/s2), July 5, 1996, [110 Stat. 1404](/us/stat/110/1404); renumbered § 7506, [Pub. L. 114–301, § 2(a)(1)(B)](/us/pl/114/301/s2/a/1/B), Dec. 16, 2016, [130 Stat. 1514](/us/stat/130/1514).)*

#### Amendments

2016—[Pub. L. 114–301](/us/pl/114/301) renumbered [section 7507 of this title](/us/usc/t31/s7507) as this section.

1996—[Pub. L. 104–156](/us/pl/104/156) struck out “; report” after “Effective date” in section catchline and amended text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows:

“(a) This chapter shall apply to any State or local government with respect to any of its fiscal years which begin after December 31, 1984.

“(b) The Director, on or before May 1, 1987, and annually thereafter, shall submit to each House of Congress a report on operations under this chapter. Each such report shall specifically identify each Federal agency or State or local government which is failing to comply with this chapter.”
