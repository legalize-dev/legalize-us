##### § 1706a. Repealed. [June 3, 1939, ch. 175, § 3](/us/act/1939-06-03/ch175/s3), [53 Stat. 805](/us/stat/53/805)

Section, [act June 27, 1934, ch. 847, title I, § 6](/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847/tI/s6), as added [act Apr. 17, 1936, ch. 234, § 3](/us/act/1936-04-17/ch234/s3), [49 Stat. 1233](/us/stat/49/1233); amended [act Apr. 22, 1937, ch. 121, § 1](/us/act/1937-04-22/ch121/s1), [50 Stat. 70](/us/stat/50/70), related to insurance of financial institutions financing rehabilitation of property damaged by fires, floods, storms, etc.
