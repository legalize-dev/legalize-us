# § 100301. Establishment

##### § 100301. Establishment

There is in the Department of the Interior a service called the National Park Service.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3097](/us/stat/128/3097).)*
