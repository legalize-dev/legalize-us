# § 4772. Effective date of grant provisions

##### § 4772. Effective date of grant provisions

Grant provisions of this chapter shall become effective one hundred and eighty days following January 5, 1971.

*([Pub. L. 91–648, title V, § 513](/us/pl/91/648/tV/s513), Jan. 5, 1971, [84 Stat. 1929](/us/stat/84/1929).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, means the provisions of subchapters I, II, III, and IV of this chapter. See [section 4761 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4761).
