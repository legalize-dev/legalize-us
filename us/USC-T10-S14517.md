# § 14517. Entitlement of officers discharged under this chapter to separation pay

##### § 14517. Entitlement of officers discharged under this chapter to separation pay

An officer who is discharged under section 14513, 14514, or 14515 of this title is entitled to separation pay under [section 1174 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1174) if otherwise eligible under that section.

*(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1611](/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI/s1611), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2954](/us/stat/108/2954).)*
