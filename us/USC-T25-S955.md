#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 86–339, § 5](/us/pl/86/339/s5), Sept. 21, 1959, [73 Stat. 604](/us/stat/73/604); [Pub. L. 100–581, title II, § 216](/us/pl/100/581/tII/s216), Nov. 1, 1988, [102 Stat. 2941](/us/stat/102/2941), which related to tax exemptions for equalization allotments and cash payments received in lieu thereof, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
