# § 4522. General provision

##### § 4522. General provision

An award under this subchapter is in addition to the basic pay of the recipient.

*(Added [Pub. L. 101–509, title V, § 529 [title IV, § 408(a)]](/us/pl/101/509/tV/s529/tIV/s408/a), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1427](/us/stat/104/1427), 1467.)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**
