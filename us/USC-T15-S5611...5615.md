Section 5615, [Pub. L. 102–555, title I, § 105](/us/pl/102/555/tI/s105), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 4170](/us/stat/106/4170), related to data policy for Landsat 7. See [section 60113 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s60113).
