# § 2799aa–2. “Nuclear explosive device” defined

##### § 2799aa–2. “Nuclear explosive device” defined

As used in this subchapter, the term “nuclear explosive device” has the meaning given that term in [section 6305(4) of this title](/us/usc/t22/s6305/4).

*([Pub. L. 90–629](/us/pl/90/629), ch. 10, § 103, as added [Pub. L. 103–236, title VIII, § 826(a)](/us/pl/103/236/tVIII/s826/a), Apr. 30, 1994, [108 Stat. 519](/us/stat/108/519).)*
