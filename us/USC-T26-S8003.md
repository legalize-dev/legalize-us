# § 8003. Election of chairman and vice chairman

##### § 8003. Election of chairman and vice chairman

The Joint Committee shall elect a chairman and vice chairman from among its members.

*([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 926](/us/stat/68A/926).)*
