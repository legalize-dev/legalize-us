# § 1300c. Omitted

##### § 1300c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–468, § 1](/us/pl/92/468/s1), Oct. 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 782](/us/stat/86/782), which related to distribution of judgment funds and payment of attorney fees and expenses, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
