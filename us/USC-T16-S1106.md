# § 1106. Employees

##### § 1106. Employees

The Commission may employ both United States and Canadian citizens.

*([Pub. L. 88–363, § 7](/us/pl/88/363/s7), July 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 300](/us/stat/78/300).)*
