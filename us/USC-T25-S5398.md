# § 5398. Appeals

##### § 5398. Appeals

(1) the validity of the grounds for the decision made; and

(2) that the decision is fully consistent with provisions and policies of this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 93–638, title V, § 518](/us/pl/93/638/tV/s518), as added [Pub. L. 106–260, § 4](/us/pl/106/260/s4), Aug. 18, 2000, [114 Stat. 730](/us/stat/114/730).)*
