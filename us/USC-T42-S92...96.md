# §§ 92 to 96. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

##### §§ 92 to 96. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

Section 92, acts [Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 3](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s3), [27 Stat. 450](/us/stat/27/450); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for enforcement of quarantine regulations. See sections 264 to 267 of this title.

Section 92a, acts [Apr. 29, 1878, ch. 66, § 5](/us/act/1878-04-29/ch66/s5), [20 Stat. 38](/us/stat/20/38); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); Aug. 14, 1912, § 1, [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for a national quarantine system. See sections 264 to 267 of this title.

Section 93, acts [Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 4](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s4), [27 Stat. 451](/us/stat/27/451); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for duties of Surgeon General relating to quarantine. See sections 264 to 270 of this title.

Section 94, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 5](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s5), [27 Stat. 451](/us/stat/27/451), provided for regulations to secure sanitary conditions of vessels. See [section 269 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s269).

Section 94a, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 14](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s14), as added [Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409, § 1](/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409/s1), [46 Stat. 1492](/us/stat/46/1492), provided for hours of inspection at quarantine stations. See [section 267 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s267).

Section 94b, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 16](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s16), as added [Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409, § 1](/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409/s1), [46 Stat. 1492](/us/stat/46/1492), provided for schedule of charges and payment of them. See [section 269 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s269).

Section 94c, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 15](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s15), as added [Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409, § 1](/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409/s1), [46 Stat. 1492](/us/stat/46/1492), provided for procurement of health certificates. See [section 269 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s269).

Section 94d, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 17](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s17), as added [Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409, § 1](/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409/s1), [46 Stat. 1492](/us/stat/46/1492), provided medical and hospital benefits to officers and employees of national quarantine service. See [section 253 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s253).

Section 94e, [act Mar. 3, 1931, ch. 409, § 3](/us/act/1931-03-03/ch409/s3), [46 Stat. 1492](/us/stat/46/1492), provided for extended quarantine service. See [section 267 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s267).

Section 95, acts [Mar. 27, 1890, ch. 51, § 1](/us/act/1890-03-27/ch51/s1), [26 Stat. 31](/us/stat/26/31); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), related to regulations to prevent spread of communicable diseases. See [section 264 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s264).

Section 96, [act June 19, 1906, ch. 3433, § 6](/us/act/1906-06-19/ch3433/s6), [34 Stat. 301](/us/stat/34/301), provided that jurisdiction over established station acquired by the United States be ceded before payment of compensation.
