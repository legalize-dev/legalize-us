# § 7451. Regulations

##### § 7451. Regulations

The Secretary may issue such regulations as are necessary to carry out this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 104–127, title V, § 542](/us/pl/104/127/tV/s542), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1063](/us/stat/110/1063).)*
