# § 1847. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–212, § 6(a)](/us/pl/97/212/s6/a), June 30, 1982, [96 Stat. 147](/us/stat/96/147)

##### § 1847. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–212, § 6(a)](/us/pl/97/212/s6/a), June 30, 1982, [96 Stat. 147](/us/stat/96/147)

Section, [Pub. L. 95–372, title IV, § 407](/us/pl/95/372/tIV/s407), Sept. 18, 1978, [92 Stat. 690](/us/stat/92/690), related to survey of obstructions on Outer Continental Shelf and development of charts for commercial fishermen.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective June 30, 1982, and applicable with respect to claims for damages filed on or after such date, with the Secretary of Commerce under [section 1845(a) of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1845/a), see [section 9(a) of Pub. L. 97–212](/us/pl/97/212/s9/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1982 Amendment note under section 1841.
