# § 1300l–4. Omitted

##### § 1300l–4. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–434, title II, § 206](/us/pl/103/434/tII/s206), Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4535](/us/stat/108/4535); [Pub. L. 104–109, § 8(b)](/us/pl/104/109/s8/b), Feb. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 765](/us/stat/110/765), which provided for governance of the tribe by an Interim Council until adoption of a new tribal constitution and bylaws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
