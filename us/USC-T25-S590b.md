# § 590b. Omitted

##### § 590b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–442, § 2](/us/pl/92/442/s2), Sept. 29, 1972, [86 Stat. 744](/us/stat/86/744), which related to disposition of funds credited to the Shoshone-Bannock Tribes of the Fort Hall Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
