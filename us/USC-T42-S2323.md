# § 2323. Basis of appraisal

##### § 2323. Basis of appraisal

Except for lots sold pursuant to the provisions of [section 2347(a) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2347/a), the appraised value shall be the current fair market value of the Government’s interest in the property.

*([Aug. 4, 1955, ch. 543](/us/act/1955-08-04/ch543), ch. 3, § 33, [69 Stat. 474](/us/stat/69/474).)*
