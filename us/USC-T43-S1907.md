# § 1907. National Grasslands; exemptions

##### § 1907. National Grasslands; exemptions

All National Grasslands are exempted from the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 95–514, § 11](/us/pl/95/514/s11), Oct. 25, 1978, [92 Stat. 1808](/us/stat/92/1808).)*
