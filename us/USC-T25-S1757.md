# § 1757. Omitted

##### § 1757. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–134, § 8](/us/pl/98/134/s8), Oct. 18, 1983, [97 Stat. 855](/us/stat/97/855), which provided for a restriction against alienation of lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
