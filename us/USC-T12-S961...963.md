# §§ 961 to 963. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 961 to 963. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 961, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 29, [39 Stat. 381](/us/stat/39/381); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 5(a)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s5/a), [50 Stat. 704](/us/stat/50/704); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), covered institution and conduct of receiverships for land bank associations and duties and powers of receivers.

Section 962, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 29](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s29), [39 Stat. 381](/us/stat/39/381); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, covered the disposition of moneys collected by receivers and reports to be made thereon.

Section 963, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 29, [39 Stat. 381](/us/stat/39/381); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for institution and conduct of receiverships for federal and joint-stock land banks.
