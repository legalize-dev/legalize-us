# §§ 111 to 113. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### §§ 111 to 113. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section 111, [act Apr. 19, 1918, ch. 58, § 1](/us/act/1918-04-19/ch58/s1), [40 Stat. 533](/us/stat/40/533), related to prohibited acts affecting homing pigeons owned by United States. See [section 45 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s45), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 112, [act Apr. 19, 1918, ch. 58, § 2](/us/act/1918-04-19/ch58/s2), [40 Stat. 533](/us/stat/40/533), related to possession of pigeons as evidence of violation of law. See [section 45 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s45).

Section 113, [act Apr. 19, 1918, ch. 58, § 3](/us/act/1918-04-19/ch58/s3), [40 Stat. 533](/us/stat/40/533), related to punishment. See [section 45 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s45).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal of sections 111 to 113 effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s1), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
