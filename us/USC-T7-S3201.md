# § 3201. Funds appropriated or otherwise made available pursuant to other provisions of law

##### § 3201. Funds appropriated or otherwise made available pursuant to other provisions of law

The sums appropriated and allocated to States and eligible institutions under subsection (c) of [section 3195 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s3195) to carry out subsection (a) of such section or [section 3196 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s3196), as applicable, shall be in addition to, and not in substitution for, sums appropriated or otherwise made available to such States and institutions pursuant to other provisions of law.

*([Pub. L. 95–113, title XIV, § 1439](/us/pl/95/113/tXIV/s1439), Sept. 29, 1977, [91 Stat. 1005](/us/stat/91/1005); [Pub. L. 113–79, title VII, § 7111(b)(3)(F)](/us/pl/113/79/tVII/s7111/b/3/F), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 874](/us/stat/128/874).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2014—[Pub. L. 113–79](/us/pl/113/79) substituted “under subsection (c) of [section 3195 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s3195) to carry out subsection (a) of such section or [section 3196 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s3196), as applicable,” for “under this subchapter”.
