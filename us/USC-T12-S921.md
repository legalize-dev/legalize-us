# § 921. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### § 921. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 25, [39 Stat. 380](/us/stat/39/380); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title III, § 310, [42 Stat. 1477](/us/stat/42/1477); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for notice to indorsing association in event of a default on a mortgage held by a land bank and for making good of such default by association.
