# § 6572. Purchases of stationery and materials for folding

##### § 6572. Purchases of stationery and materials for folding

Purchases of stationery and materials for folding shall be made in accordance with [section 4104 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s4104).

All contracts and bonds for purchases made under the authority of this section shall be filed with the Committee on Rules and Administration of the Senate.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 112 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s112) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2014—[Pub. L. 113–235](/us/pl/113/235) substituted “[section 4104 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s4104)” for “sections 4104 and 6569 to 6571 of this title”.

1996—[Pub. L. 104–186](/us/pl/104/186) struck out “or the Committee on Accounts of the House of Representatives respectively” before period at end.

1946—Act Aug. 2, 1946, substituted “Committee on Rules and Administration” for “Committee to Audit and Control the Contingent Expenses”.
