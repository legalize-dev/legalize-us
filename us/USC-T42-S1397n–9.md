# § 1397n–9. Availability of funds

##### § 1397n–9. Availability of funds

Amounts made available to carry out this division shall remain available until 10 years after February 9, 2018.

*([Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531), title XX, § 2060, as added [Pub. L. 115–123, div. E, title VIII, § 50802(2)](/us/pl/115/123/dE/tVIII/s50802/2), Feb. 9, 2018, [132 Stat. 281](/us/stat/132/281).)*
