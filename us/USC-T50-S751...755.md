# §§ 751 to 755. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1570](/us/stat/72/1570)

##### §§ 751 to 755. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1570](/us/stat/72/1570)

Section 751, [act July 24, 1956, ch. 689, § 1](/us/act/1956-07-24/ch689/s1), [70 Stat. 630](/us/stat/70/630), related to representation of members of the Armed Forces before foreign judicial tribunals and employment of counsel.

Section 752, [act July 24, 1956, ch. 689, § 2](/us/act/1956-07-24/ch689/s2), [70 Stat. 630](/us/stat/70/630), provided for enactment of regulations.

Section 753, [act July 24, 1956, ch. 689, § 3](/us/act/1956-07-24/ch689/s3), [70 Stat. 630](/us/stat/70/630), provided that sections 189 and 365 of the Revised Statutes not apply to action taken under this act.

Section 754, [act July 24, 1956, ch. 689, § 4](/us/act/1956-07-24/ch689/s4), [70 Stat. 630](/us/stat/70/630), related to claims for reimbursement.

Section 755, [act July 24, 1956, ch. 689, § 5](/us/act/1956-07-24/ch689/s5), [70 Stat. 630](/us/stat/70/630), related to appropriations.
