# § 985a. Cancellation of bonds issued under section 985

##### § 985a. Cancellation of bonds issued under section 985

Notwithstanding any other provision of law, any bond issued under [section 985 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s985), is hereby canceled together with the obligation to pay such bond.

*([Pub. L. 97–369, title III, § 311](/us/pl/97/369/tIII/s311), Dec. 18, 1982, [96 Stat. 1782](/us/stat/96/1782).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was enacted as part of the Department of Transportation and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 1983, and not as part of [act May 13, 1954, ch. 201](/us/act/1954-05-13/ch201), [68 Stat. 903](/us/stat/68/903), which comprises this chapter.

Section consists of a part of [section 311 of Pub. L. 97–369](/us/pl/97/369/s311). The remainder of [section 311 of Pub. L. 97–369](/us/pl/97/369/s311) amended sections 984 and 988 of this title.
