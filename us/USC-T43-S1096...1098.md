# §§ 1096 to 1098. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

##### §§ 1096 to 1098. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

Section 1096, [act May 2, 1890, ch. 182, § 24](/us/act/1890-05-02/ch182/s24), [26 Stat. 92](/us/stat/26/92), related to fraudulent settlement of open lands.

Section 1097, [act May 2, 1890, ch. 182, § 27](/us/act/1890-05-02/ch182/s27), [26 Stat. 93](/us/stat/26/93), related to rights of occupants of lands prior to May 2, 1890.

Section 1098, [act Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 543, § 16](/us/act/1891-03-03/ch543/s16), [26 Stat. 1026](/us/stat/26/1026), provided that all lands in Oklahoma be deemed agricultural lands for purposes of entry.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789), provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

#### Savings Provision

Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579) not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579/s701), set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1701).
