#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–364, § 3](/us/pl/91/364/s3), July 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 688](/us/stat/84/688), which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
