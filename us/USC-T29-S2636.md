# § 2636. Termination

##### § 2636. Termination

The Commission shall terminate 30 days after the date of the submission of the report of the Commission to Congress.

*([Pub. L. 103–3, title III, § 306](/us/pl/103/3/tIII/s306), Feb. 5, 1993, [107 Stat. 25](/us/stat/107/25).)*
