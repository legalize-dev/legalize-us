# § 8574. Savings provision

##### § 8574. Savings provision

No additional Federal funds are authorized to carry out the requirements of this Act and the activities authorized by this Act are subject to the availability of appropriations made in advance for such purposes.

*([Pub. L. 118–234, title III, § 355](/us/pl/118/234/tIII/s355), Jan. 4, 2025, [138 Stat. 2917](/us/stat/138/2917).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 118–234](/us/pl/118/234), Jan. 4, 2025, [138 Stat. 2836](/us/stat/138/2836), known as the Expanding Public Lands Outdoor Recreation Experiences Act and also as the EXPLORE Act, which enacted this chapter and enacted, amended, and repealed numerous other sections and notes in the Code. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 8401 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s8401) and Tables.
