# § 291j–5. Limitation on amounts of loans guaranteed or directly made

##### § 291j–5. Limitation on amounts of loans guaranteed or directly made

(1) such limitations as may be specified in appropriations Acts, or

(2) in the case of loans covered by allotments for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1971, $500,000,000; for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1972, $1,000,000,000; and for each of the fiscal years ending June 30, 1973, and June 30, 1974, $1,500,000,000.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title VI, § 625, as added [Pub. L. 91–296, title II, § 201](/us/pl/91/296/tII/s201), June 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 347](/us/stat/84/347); amended [Pub. L. 93–45, title I, § 108(b)(2)](/us/pl/93/45/tI/s108/b/2), June 18, 1973, [87 Stat. 93](/us/stat/87/93).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1973—[Pub. L. 93–45](/us/pl/93/45) provided for a limitation of $1,500,000,000 on amount of loans outstanding in the case of loans covered by allotments for fiscal year ending June 30, 1974.
