# §§ 601 to 606. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### §§ 601 to 606. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 601, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 118](/us/stat/64/118), related to charges and specifications. See [section 830 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s830), Armed Forces.

Section 602, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 118](/us/stat/64/118), prohibited compulsory self-incrimination. See [section 831 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s831).

Section 603, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 118](/us/stat/64/118), related to investigations. See [section 832 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s832).

Section 604, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 119](/us/stat/64/119), provided for forwarding of charges. See [section 833 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s833).

Section 605, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 119](/us/stat/64/119), related to advice of staff judge advocate and reference for trial. See [section 834 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s834).

Section 606, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 119](/us/stat/64/119), provided for service of charges. See [section 835 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s835).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1957, see [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53) footnote, [70A Stat. 680](/us/stat/70A/680).
