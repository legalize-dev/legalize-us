# § 2349aa–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–164, title I, § 121(c)](/us/pl/104/164/tI/s121/c), July 21, 1996, [110 Stat. 1428](/us/stat/110/1428)

##### § 2349aa–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–164, title I, § 121(c)](/us/pl/104/164/tI/s121/c), July 21, 1996, [110 Stat. 1428](/us/stat/110/1428)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. II, § 574, as added [Pub. L. 98–151, § 101(b)(2)](/us/pl/98/151/s101/b/2), Nov. 14, 1983, [97 Stat. 972](/us/stat/97/972), directed President to transmit to Congress, not less than 30 days before providing assistance to foreign country under this part, a report which specified terms and objectives of such assistance, and required annual presentation to Congress of information on all countries that received assistance under this part for each fiscal year.
