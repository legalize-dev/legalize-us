# § 754. Action by the Comptroller General

##### § 754. Action by the Comptroller General

When the Comptroller General has authority, the Comptroller General promptly shall carry out action the General Accounting Office Personnel Appeals Board 11 See Change of Name note below. orders under [section 753 of this title](/us/usc/t31/s753).

*([Pub. L. 97–258](/us/pl/97/258), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 902](/us/stat/96/902).)*

The words “to do so” and “corrective” are omitted as surplus. The words “under [section 753 of this title](/us/usc/t31/s753)” are added for clarity.
