# § 410oo–6. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 410oo–6. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated not to exceed $12,000,000 to carry out land acquisition and development as authorized in this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 100–479, § 7](/us/pl/100/479/s7), Oct. 7, 1988, [102 Stat. 2327](/us/stat/102/2327); [Pub. L. 115–31, div. G, title I, § 119](/us/pl/115/31/dG/tI/s119), May 5, 2017, [131 Stat. 462](/us/stat/131/462).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2017—[Pub. L. 115–31](/us/pl/115/31) inserted “land acquisition and development as authorized in” after “carry out”.
