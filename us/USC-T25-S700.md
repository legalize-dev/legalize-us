# § 700. Omitted

##### § 700. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 733, § 10](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch733/s10), [68 Stat. 726](/us/stat/68/726), which related to transfer of property to minors, persons non compos mentis, and other members needing assistance, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
