# § 1962c–5. “State” defined

##### § 1962c–5. “State” defined

For the purpose of this subchapter the term “State” means a State, the District of Columbia, Puerto Rico, the Virgin Islands or Guam.

*([Pub. L. 89–80, title III, § 306](/us/pl/89/80/tIII/s306), July 22, 1965, [79 Stat. 253](/us/stat/79/253); [Pub. L. 94–285, § 2](/us/pl/94/285/s2), May 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 516](/us/stat/90/516).)*
