# §§ 145 to 147. Repealed. [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 651(a)(1), [46 Stat. 762](/us/stat/46/762), eff. June 18, 1930

##### §§ 145 to 147. Repealed. [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 651(a)(1), [46 Stat. 762](/us/stat/46/762), eff. June 18, 1930

Section 145, acts [June 26, 1884, ch. 121, § 16](/us/act/1884-06-26/ch121/s16), [23 Stat. 57](/us/stat/23/57); [June 19, 1886, ch. 421, § 15](/us/act/1886-06-19/ch421/s15), [24 Stat. 82](/us/stat/24/82); [July 24, 1897, ch. 11, § 14](/us/act/1897-07-24/ch11/s14), [30 Stat. 207](/us/stat/30/207), related to supplies for vessels in foreign trade or trade between Atlantic and Pacific. See [section 1309 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1309).

Section 146, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356, title III, § 309](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356/tIII/s309), [42 Stat. 938](/us/stat/42/938), related to supplies to war vessels free of duty. See [section 1309 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1309).

Section 147, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356, title III, § 310](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356/tIII/s310), [42 Stat. 938](/us/stat/42/938), related to admission free of duty of merchandise of sunken and abandoned vessels. See [section 1310 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1310).
