# § 1466. Omitted

##### § 1466. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 15, 1949, ch. 338, title I, § 115](/us/act/1949-07-15/ch338/tI/s115), as added Aug. 10, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–117, title I, § 106(a)](/us/pl/89/117/tI/s106/a), [79 Stat. 457](/us/stat/79/457); amended May 25, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–19, § 6(b)](/us/pl/90/19/s6/b), [81 Stat. 21](/us/stat/81/21); Aug. 1, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–448, title V, § 503](/us/pl/90/448/tV/s503), [82 Stat. 521](/us/stat/82/521); Dec. 24, 1969, [Pub. L. 91–152, title II, § 205](/us/pl/91/152/tII/s205), [83 Stat. 387](/us/stat/83/387), which related to rehabilitation grants, was omitted pursuant to [section 5316 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5316) which terminated authority to make grants or loans under this subchapter after Jan. 1, 1975.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Amendment of Contracts Executed Prior to Enactment of Section

[Pub. L. 89–117, title I, § 106(b)](/us/pl/89/117/tI/s106/b), Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 458](/us/stat/79/458), provided that any contract with a local public agency executed under this subchapter before Aug. 10, 1965, could be amended to provide for grants authorized by this section.
