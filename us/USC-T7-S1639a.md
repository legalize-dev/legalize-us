# § 1639a. Applicability

##### § 1639a. Applicability

(a) In general This subchapter shall apply to any claim in a disclosure that a food bears that indicates that the food is a bioengineered food.

(b) Application of definition The definition of the term “bioengineering” under [section 1639 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1639) shall not affect any other definition, program, rule, or regulation of the Federal Government.

(c) Application to foods This subchapter shall apply only to a food subject to—

(1) the labeling requirements under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ([21 U.S.C. 301](/us/usc/t21/s301) et seq.); or

(2) the labeling requirements under the Federal Meat Inspection Act ([21 U.S.C. 601](/us/usc/t21/s601) et seq.), the Poultry Products Inspection Act ([21 U.S.C. 451](/us/usc/t21/s451) et seq.), or the Egg Products Inspection Act ([21 U.S.C. 1031](/us/usc/t21/s1031) et seq.) only if—

(A) the most predominant ingredient of the food would independently be subject to the labeling requirements under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ([21 U.S.C. 301](/us/usc/t21/s301) et seq.); or

(B)

(i) the most predominant ingredient of the food is broth, stock, water, or a similar solution; and

(ii) the second-most predominant ingredient of the food would independently be subject to the labeling requirements under the Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act ([21 U.S.C. 301](/us/usc/t21/s301) et seq.).

*([Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 966](/us/act/1946-08-14/ch966), title II, § 292, as added [Pub. L. 114–216, § 1](/us/pl/114/216/s1), July 29, 2016, [130 Stat. 834](/us/stat/130/834).)*

#### References in Text

The Federal Food, Drug, and Cosmetic Act, referred to in subsec. (c)(1), (2)(A), (B)(ii), is [act June 25, 1938, ch. 675](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch675), [52 Stat. 1040](/us/stat/52/1040), which is classified generally to chapter 9 (§ 301 et seq.) of Title 21, Food and Drugs. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 301 of Title 21](/us/usc/t21/s301) and Tables.

The Federal Meat Inspection Act, referred to in subsec. (c)(2), is titles I to IV of [act Mar. 4, 1907, ch. 2907](/us/act/1907-03-04/ch2907), as added [Pub. L. 90–201](/us/pl/90/201), Dec. 15, 1967, [81 Stat. 584](/us/stat/81/584), which are classified generally to subchapters I to IV (§ 601 et seq.) of Title 21, Food and Drugs. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 601 of Title 21](/us/usc/t21/s601) and Tables.

The Poultry Products Inspection Act, referred to in subsec. (c)(2), is [Pub. L. 85–172](/us/pl/85/172), Aug. 28, 1957, [71 Stat. 441](/us/stat/71/441), which is classified generally to chapter 10 (§ 451 et seq.) of Title 21, Food and Drugs. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 451 of Title 21](/us/usc/t21/s451) and Tables.

The Egg Products Inspection Act, referred to in subsec. (c)(2), is [Pub. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1031 of Title 21](/us/usc/t21/s1031) and Tables.
