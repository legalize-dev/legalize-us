# § 293j. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–392, title I, § 102(2)](/us/pl/105/392/tI/s102/2), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3537](/us/stat/112/3537)

##### § 293j. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–392, title I, § 102(2)](/us/pl/105/392/tI/s102/2), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3537](/us/stat/112/3537)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title VII, § 746](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tVII/s746), as added [Pub. L. 102–408, title I, § 102](/us/pl/102/408/tI/s102), Oct. 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 2034](/us/stat/106/2034); amended [Pub. L. 102–531, title III, § 313(a)(2)](/us/pl/102/531/tIII/s313/a/2), Oct. 27, 1992, [106 Stat. 3507](/us/stat/106/3507); [Pub. L. 103–43, title XX, § 2008(i)(3)](/us/pl/103/43/tXX/s2008/i/3), June 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 213](/us/stat/107/213), related to area health education center programs.

A prior section 746 of act July 1, 1944, was classified to [section 294q–2 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s294q–2) prior to the general revision of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 102–408](/us/pl/102/408).
