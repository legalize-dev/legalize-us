# § 17202. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 17202. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary to carry out the programs under this part $170,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2021 through 2025.

*([Pub. L. 110–140, title VI, § 623](/us/pl/110/140/tVI/s623), Dec. 19, 2007, [121 Stat. 1684](/us/stat/121/1684); [Pub. L. 116–260, div. Z, title III, § 3002(j)](/us/pl/116/260/dZ/tIII/s3002/j), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2495](/us/stat/134/2495).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2020—[Pub. L. 116–260](/us/pl/116/260) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section related to authorization of appropriations for fiscal years 2008 to 2012.
