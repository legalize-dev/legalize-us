# [§ 491. Renumbered § 431]

##### [§ 491. Renumbered § 431]

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Section 491 of this title](/us/usc/t37/s491), which related to benefits for certain members assigned to the Defense Intelligence Agency, was originally [section 431 of this title](/us/usc/t37/s431) and was transferred to chapter 8 of this title by [Pub. L. 112–81, div. A, title VI, § 631(d)(2)](/us/pl/112/81/dA/tVI/s631/d/2), Dec. 31, 2011, [125 Stat. 1460](/us/stat/125/1460), and renumbered [section 491 of this title](/us/usc/t37/s491) by [Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title X, § 1076(h)(2)](/us/pl/112/239/dA/tX/s1076/h/2), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 1955](/us/stat/126/1955). Section 491 was repealed by [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title VI, § 604(a)](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tVI/s604/a), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 1767](/us/stat/135/1767). The section was revived to read as it did immediately before its repeal and renumbered [section 431 of this title](/us/usc/t37/s431) by [Pub. L. 117–263, div. A, title VI, § 615(a)](/us/pl/117/263/dA/tVI/s615/a), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 2624](/us/stat/136/2624). The section was transferred to its original location in Chapter 7 of this title to reflect the probable intent of Congress.

#### Prior Provisions

Provisions similar to section 491 were contained in [section 192 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s192), Armed Forces, prior to enactment of section 491 by [Pub. L. 99–145](/us/pl/99/145).
