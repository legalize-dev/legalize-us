# § 942–7. Military park, Indian or other reservation

##### § 942–7. Military park, Indian or other reservation

Sections 687a, 687a–2 to 687a–5,11 See References in Text note below. and 942–1 to 942–9 of this title and sections 607a and 615a of title 16 shall not apply to any lands within the limits of any military park, Indian, or other reservation unless such right of way shall be provided for by Act of Congress.

*([May 14, 1898, ch. 299, § 7](/us/act/1898-05-14/ch299/s7), [30 Stat. 412](/us/stat/30/412).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Sections 687a and 687a–2 to 687a–5 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII](/us/pl/94/579/tVII), §§ 703(a), 704(a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789), 2792.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 417 of Title 48](/us/usc/t48/s417), Territories and Insular Possessions.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**
