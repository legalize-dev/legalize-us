# § 300ii–4. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 300ii–4. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter, $10,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2020 through fiscal year 2024.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XXIX, § 2905, as added [Pub. L. 109–442, § 2](/us/pl/109/442/s2), Dec. 21, 2006, [120 Stat. 3296](/us/stat/120/3296); amended [Pub. L. 116–324, § 2(b)](/us/pl/116/324/s2/b), Jan. 5, 2021, [134 Stat. 5085](/us/stat/134/5085).)*
