# § 554. Forest supervisors and rangers

##### § 554. Forest supervisors and rangers

Forest supervisors and rangers shall be selected, when practicable, from qualified citizens of the States or Territories in which the national forests, respectively, are situated.

*([Feb. 1, 1905, ch. 288, § 3](/us/act/1905-02-01/ch288/s3), [33 Stat. 628](/us/stat/33/628).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

“National forests” substituted in text for “reserves” on authority of [act Mar. 4, 1907, ch. 2907](/us/act/1907-03-04/ch2907), [34 Stat. 1269](/us/stat/34/1269), which provided that forest reserves shall hereafter be known as national forests.
