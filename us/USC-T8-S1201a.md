# § 1201a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–653, § 5(b)](/us/pl/99/653/s5/b), formerly § 5(a)(d), Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3656](/us/stat/100/3656), renumbered § 5(b), [Pub. L. 100–525, § 8(d)(2)](/us/pl/100/525/s8/d/2), Oct. 24, 1988, [102 Stat. 2617](/us/stat/102/2617)

##### § 1201a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–653, § 5(b)](/us/pl/99/653/s5/b), formerly § 5(a)(d), Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3656](/us/stat/100/3656), renumbered § 5(b), [Pub. L. 100–525, § 8(d)(2)](/us/pl/100/525/s8/d/2), Oct. 24, 1988, [102 Stat. 2617](/us/stat/102/2617)

Section, [Pub. L. 85–316, § 8](/us/pl/85/316/s8), Sept. 11, 1957, [71 Stat. 641](/us/stat/71/641), related to waiver of fingerprinting requirements for nonimmigrant aliens.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to applications for immigrant visas made, and visas issued, on or after Nov. 14, 1986, see [section 23(b) of Pub. L. 99–653](/us/pl/99/653/s23/b), set out as an Effective Date of 1986 Amendment note under [section 1201 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1201).
