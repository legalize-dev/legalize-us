# § 906a. Use of funds outside the United States or its territories prohibited

##### § 906a. Use of funds outside the United States or its territories prohibited

No funds provided under the Rural Electrification Act of 1936, as amended [[7 U.S.C. 901](/us/usc/t7/s901) et seq.], shall be used outside the United States or any of its territories.

*([Pub. L. 93–32, § 10](/us/pl/93/32/s10), May 11, 1973, [87 Stat. 71](/us/stat/87/71).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Rural Electrification Act of 1936, referred to in text, is [act May 20, 1936, ch. 432](/us/act/1936-05-20/ch432), [49 Stat. 1363](/us/stat/49/1363), which is classified generally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 901 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s901) and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was not enacted as part of the Rural Electrification Act of 1936 which comprises this chapter.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective May 11, 1973, see [section 12 of Pub. L. 93–32](/us/pl/93/32/s12), set out as a note under [section 930 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s930).
