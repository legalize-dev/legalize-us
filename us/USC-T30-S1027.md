# § 1027. Land subject to prohibition on leasing

##### § 1027. Land subject to prohibition on leasing

The Secretary shall not issue any lease under this chapter on those lands subject to the prohibition provided under [section 226–3 of this title](/us/usc/t30/s226–3).

*([Pub. L. 91–581, § 29](/us/pl/91/581/s29), as added [Pub. L. 100–443, § 5(d)](/us/pl/100/443/s5/d), Sept. 22, 1988, [102 Stat. 1769](/us/stat/102/1769); amended [Pub. L. 109–58, title II, § 236(27)](/us/pl/109/58/tII/s236/27), Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 673](/us/stat/119/673).)*
