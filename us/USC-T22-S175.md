# § 175. Omitted

##### § 175. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Mar. 2, 1901, ch. 802](/us/act/1901-03-02/ch802), [31 Stat. 893](/us/stat/31/893), which appropriated funds for feeding and keeping of prisoners, was repeated in subsequent appropriation acts down to and including [act Mar. 3, 1917, ch. 161](/us/act/1917-03-03/ch161), [39 Stat. 1058](/us/stat/39/1058), but was not repeated thereafter.
