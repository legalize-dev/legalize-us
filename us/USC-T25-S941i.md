##### § 941i. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–116, § 11](/us/pl/103/116/s11), Oct. 27, 1993, [107 Stat. 1127](/us/stat/107/1127), which related to tribal trust funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
