# § 9601. Definitions

##### § 9601. Definitions

(1) In general The terms “local educational agency” and “State educational agency” have the meanings given those terms in [section 7801 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7801).

(2) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Education.

*([Pub. L. 107–279, title II, § 202](/us/pl/107/279/tII/s202), Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1975](/us/stat/116/1975); [Pub. L. 114–95, title IX, § 9215(gg)(1)](/us/pl/114/95/tIX/s9215/gg/1), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2174](/us/stat/129/2174).)*
