# § 1397n–13. Funding

##### § 1397n–13. Funding

Out of any money in the Treasury of the United States not otherwise appropriated, there is hereby appropriated $100,000,000 for fiscal year 2018 to carry out this division.

*([Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531), title XX, § 2064, as added [Pub. L. 115–123, div. E, title VIII, § 50802(2)](/us/pl/115/123/dE/tVIII/s50802/2), Feb. 9, 2018, [132 Stat. 282](/us/stat/132/282).)*
