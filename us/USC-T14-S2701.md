# § 2701. Procurement of personnel

##### § 2701. Procurement of personnel

(1) obtain recruits for the Service and cadet applicants; and

(2) gain support of recruiting objectives from those who may assist in the recruiting effort.

*([Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393), [63 Stat. 531](/us/stat/63/531), § 468; [Pub. L. 104–324, title II, § 206(b)](/us/pl/104/324/tII/s206/b), Oct. 19, 1996, [110 Stat. 3908](/us/stat/110/3908); renumbered § 2701, [Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 116(b)](/us/pl/115/282/tI/s116/b), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4226](/us/stat/132/4226); [Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8513(a)(4), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4760](/us/stat/134/4760).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on the following language contained in the Coast Guard appropriation act for 1949, “Pay and Allowances” and preceding years: “expenses of recruiting for the Coast Guard; advertising for and obtaining enlisted personnel and applicants for appointment as cadets;” ([June 19, 1948, ch. 558](/us/act/1948-06-19/ch558), [62 Stat. 562](/us/stat/62/562)).

Changes were made in phraseology. 81st Congress, House Report No. 557.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 2701 was renumbered [section 4901 of this title](/us/usc/t14/s4901).

#### Amendments

2021—[Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283), in introductory provisions, substituted “operations and support” for “operating expense” and struck out “but not limited to” after “including” and “in order” after “entertainment,”.

2018—[Pub. L. 115–282](/us/pl/115/282) renumbered [section 468 of this title](/us/usc/t14/s468) as this section.

1996—[Pub. L. 104–324](/us/pl/104/324) amended text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “The Coast Guard may make expenditures as necessary in order to obtain recruits for the service and cadet applicants, including advertising.”
