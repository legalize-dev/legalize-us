# § 2104. Publication of list of works of art, historical objects, and exhibits

##### § 2104. Publication of list of works of art, historical objects, and exhibits

The Commission shall, from time to time, but at least once every ten years, publish as a Senate document a list of all works of art, historical objects, and exhibits currently within the Senate wing of the Capitol and the Senate Office Buildings, together with their description, location, and with such notes as may be pertinent to their history.

*([Pub. L. 100–696, title IX, § 901(a)](/us/pl/100/696/tIX/s901/a), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4610](/us/stat/102/4610).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was classified to section 188b–3 of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by [Pub. L. 107–217, § 1](/us/pl/107/217/s1), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1062](/us/stat/116/1062).

Section is based on section 4 of Senate Resolution No. 382, Ninetieth Congress, Oct. 1, 1968, which was enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 100–696](/us/pl/100/696).
