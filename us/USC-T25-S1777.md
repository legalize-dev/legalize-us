# § 1777. Omitted

##### § 1777. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 106–425, § 2](/us/pl/106/425/s2), Nov. 1, 2000, [114 Stat. 1890](/us/stat/114/1890), which set out congressional findings and purposes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 106–425, § 1](/us/pl/106/425/s1), Nov. 1, 2000, [114 Stat. 1890](/us/stat/114/1890), provided that [Pub. L. 106–425](/us/pl/106/425), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Santo Domingo Pueblo Claims Settlement Act of 2000”.
