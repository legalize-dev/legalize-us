#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 15, 1922, ch. 313](/us/act/1922-09-15/ch313), [42 Stat. 844](/us/stat/42/844), which related to marking of anchorage grounds by the Commissioner of Lighthouses, was transferred to the end of subchapter III of chapter 5 of Title 14, Coast Guard, and redesignated as section 548 of that title by [Pub. L. 117–263, div. K, title CXVIII, § 11808(c)](/us/pl/117/263/dK/tCXVIII/s11808/c), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 4166](/us/stat/136/4166).
