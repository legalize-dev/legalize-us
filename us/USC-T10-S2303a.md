# [§ 2303a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–577, title III, § 302(c)(1)](/us/pl/98/577/tIII/s302/c/1), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 3077](/us/stat/98/3077)]

##### [§ 2303a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–577, title III, § 302(c)(1)](/us/pl/98/577/tIII/s302/c/1), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 3077](/us/stat/98/3077)]

Section, [Pub. L. 98–525, title XII, § 1212(a)](/us/pl/98/525/tXII/s1212/a), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2590](/us/stat/98/2590), related to publication of proposed regulations.

Section, pursuant to [section 1212(b) of Pub. L. 98–525](/us/pl/98/525/s1212/b), was to have taken effect with respect to procurement policies, regulations, procedures, or forms first proposed to be issued by an agency on or after the date which was 30 days after the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 98–525](/us/pl/98/525). [Pub. L. 98–525](/us/pl/98/525) was approved Oct. 19, 1984. However, before that effective date, the section was repealed by [Pub. L. 98–577](/us/pl/98/577).
