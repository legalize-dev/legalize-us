##### § 606. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–306, title III, § 311](/us/pl/97/306/tIII/s311), Oct. 14, 1982, [96 Stat. 1442](/us/stat/96/1442); [Pub. L. 97–375, title I, § 110(a)](/us/pl/97/375/tI/s110/a), Dec. 21, 1982, [96 Stat. 1820](/us/stat/96/1820)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–83, § 6(f)](/us/pl/90/83/s6/f), Sept. 11, 1967, [81 Stat. 221](/us/stat/81/221), directed Secretary of Labor to report to Congress not later than Jan. 15 of each year the number of cases reviewed under this chapter and the number of certificates issued.
