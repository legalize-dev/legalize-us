# § 3702. Unlawful sports gambling

##### § 3702. Unlawful sports gambling

(1) a governmental entity to sponsor, operate, advertise, promote, license, or authorize by law or compact, or

(2) a person to sponsor, operate, advertise, or promote, pursuant to the law or compact of a governmental entity,

*(Added [Pub. L. 102–559, § 2(a)](/us/pl/102/559/s2/a), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 4228](/us/stat/106/4228).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Constitutionality

For information regarding constitutionality of par. (1) of this section, as added by [section 2(a) of Pub. L. 102–559](/us/pl/102/559/s2/a), see note under [section 3701 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s3701).
