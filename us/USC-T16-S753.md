# § 753. Cooperative work

##### § 753. Cooperative work

On and after July 2, 1942, cooperative work conducted by the United States Fish and Wildlife Service shall be subject to the provisions of the Act of July 24, 1919 [[7 U.S.C. 2220](/us/usc/t7/s2220), 2279i].

*([July 2, 1942, ch. 473, § 1](/us/act/1942-07-02/ch473/s1), [56 Stat. 558](/us/stat/56/558); [Aug. 8, 1956, ch. 1036, § 3](/us/act/1956-08-08/ch1036/s3), [70 Stat. 1120](/us/stat/70/1120).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Act of July 24, 1919, referred to in text, was formerly classified to sections 563 and 564 of Title 5 prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).

#### Prior Provisions

Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [act June 28, 1941, ch. 259, § 1](/us/act/1941-06-28/ch259/s1), [55 Stat. 357](/us/stat/55/357).

#### Transfer of Functions

Plan No. 3 of 1950, §§ 1, 2, eff. May 24, 1950, 15 F.R. 3174, [64 Stat. 1262](/us/stat/64/1262), set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
