# §§ 83 to 104. Transferred

##### §§ 83 to 104. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 83, R.S. § 1715, which related to certification of invoices, was transferred to [section 4200 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4200).

Section 84, [act Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 9](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s9), [34 Stat. 101](/us/stat/34/101), which related to fees for certification of invoices, was transferred to [section 4201 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4201).

Section 85, R.S. § 1716, which related to exaction of excessive fees for verification of invoices, was transferred to [section 4202 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4202).

Section 86, [act Feb. 24, 1903, ch. 753](/us/act/1903-02-24/ch753), [32 Stat. 854](/us/stat/32/854), which related to destruction of old invoices, was transferred to [section 4203 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4203).

Section 87, R.S. § 1717, which related to granting of a certificate for goods shipped from countries adjacent to the United States, was transferred to [section 4204 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4204).

Section 88, R.S. § 1718, which related to retention of papers of American vessels until payment of demands and wages, was transferred to [section 4205 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4205).

Section 89, [act June 26, 1884, ch. 121, § 12](/us/act/1884-06-26/ch121/s12), [23 Stat. 56](/us/stat/23/56), which related to fees for official services to American vessels or seamen, was transferred to [section 4206 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4206).

Section 90, R.S. § 1719, which related to profits from dealings with discharged seamen, was transferred to [section 4207 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4207).

Section 91, R.S. § 1722, which related to valuation of foreign coins in payment of fees, was transferred to [section 4208 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4208).

Section 92, R.S. § 1723, which related to exaction of excessive fees generally, was transferred to [section 4209 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4209).

Section 93, R.S. § 1724, which related to liability for uncollected fees, was transferred to [section 4210 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4210).

Section 94, R.S. § 1725, which related to returns as to fees by officers compensated by fees, was transferred to [section 4211 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4211).

Section 95, R.S. §§ 1726, 1727, which related to receipt for fees, was transferred to [section 4212 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4212).

Section 96, R.S. § 1727, which related to registry of fees, was transferred to [section 4213 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4213).

Section 97, R.S. § 1728, which related to account of fees, was transferred to [section 4214 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4214).

Section 98, [act Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 7](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s7), [34 Stat. 101](/us/stat/34/101), which related to notarial acts, oaths, affirmations, affidavits, and depositions, was transferred to [section 4215 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4215).

Section 99, acts [Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 8](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s8), [34 Stat. 101](/us/stat/34/101); [Feb. 5, 1915, ch. 23](/us/act/1915-02-05/ch23), §§ 3, 6, [38 Stat. 805](/us/stat/38/805), 806; [May 24, 1924, ch. 182, § 11](/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182/s11), [43 Stat. 142](/us/stat/43/142); [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title XI, § 1131(26), [60 Stat. 1037](/us/stat/60/1037), which related to general duty to account for fees, was transferred to [section 4223 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4223).

Section 100, [act Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 10](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s10), [34 Stat. 102](/us/stat/34/102), which related to use of official fee stamps on documents involved in performance of any consular or notarial act, was transferred to [section 1196 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1196) and was subsequently repealed by [act June 28, 1955, ch. 196](/us/act/1955-06-28/ch196), [69 Stat. 187](/us/stat/69/187).

Section 101, R.S. § 1731, which related to posting rates of fees, was transferred to [section 4216 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4216).

Section 102, R.S. § 1734, which related to embezzlement of fees or of effects of American citizens, was transferred to [section 4217 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4217).

Section 103, R.S. §§ 1735, 1736, which related to liability for neglect of duty or malfeasance, was transferred to [section 1199 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1199).

Section 104, R.S. § 1737, which related to false certificates as to ownership of property, was transferred to [section 4218 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4218).
