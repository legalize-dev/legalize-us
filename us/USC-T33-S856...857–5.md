# §§ 856 to 857–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–372, title II, § 271(3)](/us/pl/107/372/tII/s271/3), (4), Dec. 19, 2002, [116 Stat. 3094](/us/stat/116/3094)

##### §§ 856 to 857–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–372, title II, § 271(3)](/us/pl/107/372/tII/s271/3), (4), Dec. 19, 2002, [116 Stat. 3094](/us/stat/116/3094)

Section 856, acts [May 22, 1917, ch. 20, § 16](/us/act/1917-05-22/ch20/s16), [40 Stat. 88](/us/stat/40/88); [June 21, 1955, ch. 172, § 2(b)](/us/act/1955-06-21/ch172/s2/b), [69 Stat. 169](/us/stat/69/169); 1965 Reorg. Plan No. 2, eff. July 13, 1965, 30 F.R. 8819, [79 Stat. 1318](/us/stat/79/1318); 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 4, eff. Oct. 3, 1970, 35 F.R. 15627, [84 Stat. 2090](/us/stat/84/2090), related to relative rank of National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration officers when serving with Army, Navy, or Air Force. See [section 3062 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3062).

Section 857, acts [May 22, 1917, ch. 20, § 16](/us/act/1917-05-22/ch20/s16), [40 Stat. 88](/us/stat/40/88); [Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 837](/us/act/1956-08-01/ch837), title V, § 501(d)(1), [70 Stat. 883](/us/stat/70/883); 1965 Reorg. Plan No. 2, eff. July 13, 1965, 30 F.R. 8819, [79 Stat. 1318](/us/stat/79/1318); 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 4, eff. Oct. 3, 1970, 35 F.R. 15627, [84 Stat. 2090](/us/stat/84/2090); [Pub. L. 102–54, § 13(m)(2)](/us/pl/102/54/s13/m/2), June 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 277](/us/stat/105/277), related to service of commissioned officers of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration for purposes of servicemen’s and veterans’ benefits. See [section 3072 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3072).

Section 857–1, [Pub. L. 91–621, § 1](/us/pl/91/621/s1), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1863](/us/stat/84/1863), related to applicable definitions. See [section 3002 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3002).

Section 857–2, [Pub. L. 91–621, § 2](/us/pl/91/621/s2), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1863](/us/stat/84/1863), related to retirement grade and pay. See [section 3046 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3046).

Section 857–3, [Pub. L. 91–621, § 3](/us/pl/91/621/s3), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1863](/us/stat/84/1863); [Pub. L. 98–498, title III, § 310(a)](/us/pl/98/498/tIII/s310/a), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2306](/us/stat/98/2306); [Pub. L. 102–54, § 13(m)(3)](/us/pl/102/54/s13/m/3), June 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 277](/us/stat/105/277), related to service for purposes of certain statutory rights, privileges, immunities, and benefits and provision of medical and dental care. See sections 3072 and 3073 of this title.

Section 857–4, [Pub. L. 91–621, § 4](/us/pl/91/621/s4), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1863](/us/stat/84/1863), related to commissary privileges. See [section 3074 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3074).

Section 857–5, [Pub. L. 91–621, § 5](/us/pl/91/621/s5), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1864](/us/stat/84/1864), related to applicability of statutes that had applied to commissioned officers of the Coast and Geodetic Survey and the Environmental Science Services Administration.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Repeals

[Pub. L. 91–621, § 8](/us/pl/91/621/s8), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1867](/us/stat/84/1867), provided that all provisions of law inconsistent with [Pub. L. 91–621](/us/pl/91/621) were repealed, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 107–372, title II, § 271(3)](/us/pl/107/372/tII/s271/3), Dec. 19, 2002, [116 Stat. 3094](/us/stat/116/3094).
