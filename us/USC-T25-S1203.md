# § 1203. Omitted

##### § 1203. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–283, § 3](/us/pl/91/283/s3), June 19, 1970, [84 Stat. 313](/us/stat/84/313), which related to payment of shares to minors and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
