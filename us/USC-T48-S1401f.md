# § 1401f. Omitted

##### § 1401f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, act Oct. 5, 1992, [Pub. L. 102–381, title I](/us/pl/102/381/tI), [106 Stat. 1392](/us/stat/106/1392), which authorized Territorial and local governments of Virgin Islands to make purchases through General Services Administration, was from the Department of the Interior and Related Agencies Appropriations Act, 1993, and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts.
