# § 1490. Maturity of loans

##### § 1490. Maturity of loans

The maturity of any loan guaranteed or insured hereunder shall not exceed thirty years.

*([Pub. L. 93–262, title II, § 210](/us/pl/93/262/tII/s210), Apr. 12, 1974, [88 Stat. 80](/us/stat/88/80).)*
