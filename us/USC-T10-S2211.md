# § 2211. Reimbursement for equipment, material, or services furnished members of the United Nations

##### § 2211. Reimbursement for equipment, material, or services furnished members of the United Nations

Amounts paid by members of the United Nations for equipment or materials furnished, or services performed, in joint military operations shall be credited to appropriate appropriations of the Department of Defense in the manner authorized by section 632(d) of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961 ([22 U.S.C. 2392(d)](/us/usc/t22/s2392/d)).

*(Added [Pub. L. 87–651, title II, § 207(a)](/us/pl/87/651/tII/s207/a), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 522](/us/stat/76/522); amended [Pub. L. 96–513, title V, § 511(73)](/us/pl/96/513/tV/s511/73), Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2926](/us/stat/94/2926).)*

The reference to [section 2392(d) of title 22](/us/usc/t22/s2392/d) is substituted for the reference to section 1574(b) of that title to reflect [section 542(b) of the Act of August 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937/s542/b) ([68 Stat. 861](/us/stat/68/861)) and section 642(a)(2) and (b) of the Act of September 4, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195) ([75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460)).
