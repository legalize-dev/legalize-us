# § 10497. Reports

##### § 10497. Reports

(1) program descriptions;

(2) the number of staff employed to administer programs;

(3) the number of individuals who participated in programs; and

(4) an evaluation of the effectiveness of grant programs.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2307](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2307), as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXI, § 210201(a)(3)](/us/pl/103/322/tXXI/s210201/a/3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2064](/us/stat/108/2064).)*
