# § 2002. Liability for payment

##### § 2002. Liability for payment

The tax imposed by this chapter shall be paid by the executor.

*([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 374](/us/stat/68A/374); [Pub. L. 98–369, div. A, title V, § 544(b)(1)](/us/pl/98/369/dA/tV/s544/b/1), July 18, 1984, [98 Stat. 894](/us/stat/98/894); [Pub. L. 101–239, title VII, § 7304(b)(2)(A)](/us/pl/101/239/tVII/s7304/b/2/A), Dec. 19, 1989, [103 Stat. 2353](/us/stat/103/2353).)*
