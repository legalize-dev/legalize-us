# § 94. Omitted

##### § 94. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 19, 1919, ch. 24](/us/act/1919-07-19/ch24), [41 Stat. 182](/us/stat/41/182), providing that the disbursing clerk of the Treasury Department should act in a similar capacity for the Commission, was a proviso repeated in successive appropriation acts but which has not been repeated in recent years.
