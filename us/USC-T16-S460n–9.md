# § 460n–9. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 460n–9. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated not more than $7,100,000 for the acquisition of land and interests in land pursuant to [section 460n–1 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s460n–1).

*([Pub. L. 88–639, § 10](/us/pl/88/639/s10), Oct. 8, 1964, [78 Stat. 1041](/us/stat/78/1041); [Pub. L. 93–477, title I, § 101(12)](/us/pl/93/477/tI/s101/12), Oct. 26, 1974, [88 Stat. 1445](/us/stat/88/1445).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1974—[Pub. L. 93–477](/us/pl/93/477) substituted “$7,100,000” for “$1,200,000”.
