# § 11713. Compliance with Budget Act

##### § 11713. Compliance with Budget Act

Any new spending authority (described in subsection (c)(2)(A) or (B) of section 651 11 See References in Text note below. of title 2) which is provided under this chapter shall be effective for any fiscal year only to such extent or in such amounts as are provided in appropriation Acts.

*([Pub. L. 100–579, § 15](/us/pl/100/579/s15), as added [Pub. L. 102–396, title IX, § 9168](/us/pl/102/396/tIX/s9168), Oct. 6, 1992, [106 Stat. 1948](/us/stat/106/1948).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 651 of title 2](/us/usc/t2/s651), referred to in text, was amended by [Pub. L. 105–33, title X, § 10116(a)(3)](/us/pl/105/33/tX/s10116/a/3), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 691](/us/stat/111/691), by striking out subsec. (c) and redesignating former subsec. (d) as (c).

#### Codification

Section enacted by section 1 of S. 2681, One Hundred Second Congress, as passed by the Senate on Aug. 7, 1992, which was enacted into law by [section 9168 of Pub. L. 102–396](/us/pl/102/396/s9168). Section 9168, which referred to S. 2681, as passed by the Senate on “September 12, 1992”, has been treated as referring to S. 2681, as passed by the Senate on Aug. 7, 1992, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
