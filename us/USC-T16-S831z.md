# § 831z. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 831z. Authorization of appropriations

All appropriations necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter are authorized.

*([May 18, 1933, ch. 32, § 27](/us/act/1933-05-18/ch32/s27), [48 Stat. 71](/us/stat/48/71).)*
