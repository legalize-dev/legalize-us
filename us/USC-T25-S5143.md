# § 5143. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 5143. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out sections 5136 to 5143 of this title $8,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1991 through 1995.

*([Pub. L. 91–229, § 6](/us/pl/91/229/s6), as added [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVIII, § 1854(b)](/us/pl/101/624/tXVIII/s1854/b), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3837](/us/stat/104/3837).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Another [section 6 of Pub. L. 91–229](/us/pl/91/229/s6) was added by [Pub. L. 101–82, title III, § 303](/us/pl/101/82/tIII/s303), Aug. 14, 1989, [103 Stat. 583](/us/stat/103/583), and is classified to [section 5142 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5142).
