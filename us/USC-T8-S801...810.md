# §§ 801 to 810. Repealed. [June 27, 1952, ch. 477](/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477), title IV, § 403(a)(42), [66 Stat. 280](/us/stat/66/280), eff. Dec. 24, 1952

##### §§ 801 to 810. Repealed. [June 27, 1952, ch. 477](/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477), title IV, § 403(a)(42), [66 Stat. 280](/us/stat/66/280), eff. Dec. 24, 1952

Section 801, acts [Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876), title I, subchap. IV, § 401, [54 Stat. 1168](/us/stat/54/1168); [Jan. 20, 1944, ch. 2, § 1](/us/act/1944-01-20/ch2/s1), [58 Stat. 4](/us/stat/58/4); [July 1, 1944, ch. 368, § 1](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch368/s1), [58 Stat. 677](/us/stat/58/677); [Sept. 27, 1944, ch. 418, § 1](/us/act/1944-09-27/ch418/s1), [58 Stat. 746](/us/stat/58/746), related to general means of losing United States nationality. See [section 1481(a) of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1481/a).

Section 802, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title I](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tI), subchap. IV, § 402, [54 Stat. 1169](/us/stat/54/1169), related to presumption of expatriation. See [section 1482 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1482).

Section 803, acts [Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876), title I, subchap. IV, § 403, [54 Stat. 1169](/us/stat/54/1169); [July 1, 1944, ch. 368, § 2](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch368/s2), [58 Stat. 677](/us/stat/58/677), related to restrictions on expatriation. See sections 1482 and 1483(b) of this title.

Section 804, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title I](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tI), subchap. IV, § 404, [54 Stat. 1170](/us/stat/54/1170), related to expatriation of naturalized nationals by residence abroad. See [section 1484(a) of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1484/a).

Section 805, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title I](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tI), subchap. IV, § 405, [54 Stat. 1170](/us/stat/54/1170), related to exceptions in the case of persons employed or compensated by United States while residing abroad. See section 1485(1), (2) of this title.

Section 806, acts [Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876), title I, subchap. IV, § 406, [54 Stat. 1170](/us/stat/54/1170); [Dec. 8, 1942, ch. 696](/us/act/1942-12-08/ch696), [56 Stat. 1043](/us/stat/56/1043); [Dec. 24, 1942, ch. 819](/us/act/1942-12-24/ch819), [56 Stat. 1085](/us/stat/56/1085), related to additional exceptions. See section 1485(3)–(9) of this title.

Section 807, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title I](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tI), subchap. IV, § 407, [54 Stat. 1170](/us/stat/54/1170), related to minor children of naturalized nationals losing nationality by foreign residence. See [section 1487 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1487).

Section 808, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title I](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tI), subchap. IV, § 408, [54 Stat. 1171](/us/stat/54/1171), related to exclusiveness of means of losing nationality. See [section 1488 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1488).

Section 809, acts [Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876), title I, subchap. IV, § 409, [54 Stat. 1171](/us/stat/54/1171); [Oct. 16, 1941, ch. 446](/us/act/1941-10-16/ch446), [55 Stat. 743](/us/stat/55/743); [Oct. 9, 1942, ch. 585](/us/act/1942-10-09/ch585), [56 Stat. 779](/us/stat/56/779); [Sept. 27, 1944, ch. 419](/us/act/1944-09-27/ch419), [58 Stat. 747](/us/stat/58/747); [Oct. 11, 1945, ch. 410](/us/act/1945-10-11/ch410), [59 Stat. 544](/us/stat/59/544), related to nationality not lost under sections 804 or 807 until October, 1942. See [section 1487 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1487).

Section 810, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title I](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tI), subchap. IV, § 410, [54 Stat. 1171](/us/stat/54/1171), related to chapter being inapplicable where it contravened convention of treaties. See [section 1489 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1489).
