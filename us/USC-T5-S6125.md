# § 6125. Flexible schedules; time-recording devices

##### § 6125. Flexible schedules; time-recording devices

Notwithstanding [section 6106 of this title](/us/usc/t5/s6106), the Office of Personnel Management or any agency may use recording clocks as part of programs under [section 6122 of this title](/us/usc/t5/s6122).

*(Added [Pub. L. 97–221, § 2(a)(2)](/us/pl/97/221/s2/a/2), July 23, 1982, [96 Stat. 229](/us/stat/96/229).)*
