# § 861. Omitted

##### § 861. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–281, § 1](/us/pl/95/281/s1), May 15, 1978, [92 Stat. 246](/us/stat/92/246), which related to Federal recognition of the Wyandotte, Ottawa, and Peoria Tribes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
