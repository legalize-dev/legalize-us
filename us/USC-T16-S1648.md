Section, [Pub. L. 95–307, § 9](/us/pl/95/307/s9), as added [Pub. L. 101–624, title XII, § 1241(b)](/us/pl/101/624/tXII/s1241/b), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3544](/us/stat/104/3544), related to recycling research.

A prior [section 9 of Pub. L. 95–307](/us/pl/95/307/s9), June 30, 1978, [92 Stat. 357](/us/stat/92/357), provided the effective date for [Pub. L. 95–307](/us/pl/95/307) and was set out as a note under [section 1641 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s1641), prior to general amendment by [Pub. L. 101–624](/us/pl/101/624).
