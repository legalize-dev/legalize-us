# § 5028a. Prohibition

##### § 5028a. Prohibition

The Director may not reduce the activities, projects, or volunteers funded under the other parts of this subchapter in order to support projects under this part.

*([Pub. L. 93–113, title II, § 232](/us/pl/93/113/tII/s232), as added [Pub. L. 103–82, title III, § 351](/us/pl/103/82/tIII/s351), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 907](/us/stat/107/907).)*
