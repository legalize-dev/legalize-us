##### § 1200d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 6, 1954, ch. 463, § 7](/us/act/1954-07-06/ch463/s7), [68 Stat. 453](/us/stat/68/453), which related to construction of former sections 1198 to 1200e of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
