# § 6112. Regulations

##### § 6112. Regulations

The Secretary may issue such regulations as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 101–624, title XIX, § 1933](/us/pl/101/624/tXIX/s1933), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3865](/us/stat/104/3865).)*
