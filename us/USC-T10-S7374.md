# § 7374. Expert accountant for Inspector General

##### § 7374. Expert accountant for Inspector General

The Secretary of the Army shall appoint an expert accountant to perform duties under the Inspector General.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 234](/us/stat/70A/234), § 4024; renumbered § 7374, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(b)(15)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/b/15), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1839](/us/stat/132/1839).)*

The words “in case of vacancy” are omitted as surplusage.
