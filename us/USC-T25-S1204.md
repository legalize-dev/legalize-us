# § 1204. Omitted

##### § 1204. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–283, § 4](/us/pl/91/283/s4), June 19, 1970, [84 Stat. 313](/us/stat/84/313), which exempted funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
