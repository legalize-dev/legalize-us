# § 3057l. Administration

##### § 3057l. Administration

In establishing regulations for the purpose of part A the Assistant Secretary shall consult with the Secretary of the Interior.

*([Pub. L. 89–73, title VI, § 641](/us/pl/89/73/tVI/s641), formerly § 631, as added [Pub. L. 106–501, title VI, § 604(2)](/us/pl/106/501/tVI/s604/2), Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2288](/us/stat/114/2288).)*
