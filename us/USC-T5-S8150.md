# § 8150. Effect on other statutes

##### § 8150. Effect on other statutes

(a) This subchapter does not affect the maritime rights and remedies of a master or member of the crew of a vessel.

(b) [Section 8141 of this title](/us/usc/t5/s8141) and [section 9491 of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s9491) do not confer military or veteran status on any individual.

*([Pub. L. 89–554](/us/pl/89/554), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 555](/us/stat/80/555); [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 809(c)(4)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s809/c/4), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1841](/us/stat/132/1841).)*

Standard changes are made to conform with the definitions applicable and the style of this title as outlined in the preface to the report.
