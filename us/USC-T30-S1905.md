# § 1905. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1905. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 91–631, title II, § 205](/us/pl/91/631/tII/s205), as added [Pub. L. 104–325, § 2(3)](/us/pl/104/325/s2/3), Oct. 19, 1996, [110 Stat. 3999](/us/stat/110/3999).)*
