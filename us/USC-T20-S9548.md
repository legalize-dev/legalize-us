# § 9548. State defined

##### § 9548. State defined

In this part, the term “State” means each of the 50 States, the District of Columbia, and the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.

*([Pub. L. 107–279, title I, § 158](/us/pl/107/279/tI/s158), Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1962](/us/stat/116/1962).)*
