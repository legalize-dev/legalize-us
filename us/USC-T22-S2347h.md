# § 2347h. Human rights report

##### § 2347h. Human rights report

(a) In general Not later than March 1 of each year, the Secretary of State shall submit to the Speaker of the House of Representatives and the Committee on Foreign Relations of the Senate a report describing, to the extent practicable, any involvement of a foreign military or defense ministry civilian participant in education and training activities under this part in a violation of internationally recognized human rights reported under [section 2151n(d) of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2151n/d) subsequent to such participation.

(b) Form The report described in subsection (a) shall be in unclassified form, but may include a classified annex.
