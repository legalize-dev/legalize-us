# §§ 678 to 682. Omitted

##### §§ 678 to 682. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were stricken by [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 7(b)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s7/b), [50 Stat. 707](/us/stat/50/707), and former [section 677a of this title](/us/usc/t12/s677a) was substituted in lieu thereof.

Section 678, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 363](/us/stat/39/363); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title III, § 304, [42 Stat. 1474](/us/stat/42/1474); [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VII, § 70a(a)(1), [48 Stat. 269](/us/stat/48/269), related to number and qualifications of directors.

Section 678a, [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VII, § 70a(b)](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98/tVII/s70a/b), [48 Stat. 270](/us/stat/48/270), related to successors of directors.

Section 679, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 363](/us/stat/39/363); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title III, § 304, [42 Stat. 1474](/us/stat/42/1474); [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VII, § 70a(a)(2), [48 Stat. 270](/us/stat/48/270), related to nomination of candidates for local directors.

Section 680, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 363](/us/stat/39/363); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title III, § 304, [42 Stat. 1474](/us/stat/42/1474); [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VII, § 70a(a)(3), [48 Stat. 270](/us/stat/48/270), related to election of local directors.

Section 681, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 363](/us/stat/39/363); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title III, § 304, [42 Stat. 1474](/us/stat/42/1474); [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VII, § 70a(a)(4), [48 Stat. 270](/us/stat/48/270), related to appointment and election of district directors and director at large, and filling of vacancies.

Section 682, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 363](/us/stat/39/363); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title III, § 304, [42 Stat. 1474](/us/stat/42/1474), related to qualifications of directors and limitation on engagement in other business.
