# § 3908. Vessel deemed public vessel

##### § 3908. Vessel deemed public vessel

While assigned to authorized Coast Guard duty, any motorboat or yacht shall be deemed to be a public vessel of the United States and a vessel of the Coast Guard within the meaning of sections 937 and 938 of this title and other applicable provisions of law.

*([Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393), [63 Stat. 555](/us/stat/63/555), § 827; [Pub. L. 104–324, title IV, § 406](/us/pl/104/324/tIV/s406), Oct. 19, 1996, [110 Stat. 3924](/us/stat/110/3924); renumbered § 3908 and amended [Pub. L. 115–282, title I](/us/pl/115/282/tI), §§ 119(b), 123(b)(2), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4236](/us/stat/132/4236), 4240.)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on title 14, U.S.C., 1946 ed., § 266 ([Feb. 19, 1941, ch. 8](/us/act/1941-02-19/ch8), title I, § 7, [55 Stat. 10](/us/stat/55/10)).

Changes were made in phraseology. 81st Congress, House Report No. 557.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–282, § 123(b)(2)](/us/pl/115/282/s123/b/2), substituted “sections 937 and 938” for “sections 646 and 647”.

[Pub. L. 115–282, § 119(b)](/us/pl/115/282/s119/b), renumbered [section 827 of this title](/us/usc/t14/s827) as this section.

1996—[Pub. L. 104–324](/us/pl/104/324) reenacted section catchline without change and amended text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “Any motorboat or yacht, while assigned to authorized Coast Guard duty shall be deemed to be a public vessel of the United States, and within the meaning of [section 646 of this title](/us/usc/t14/s646) shall be deemed to be a vessel of the Coast Guard.”
