# § 4586. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–24, § 2(c)(1)](/us/pl/98/24/s2/c/1), Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 182](/us/stat/97/182)

##### § 4586. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–24, § 2(c)(1)](/us/pl/98/24/s2/c/1), Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 182](/us/stat/97/182)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–616, title V, § 502](/us/pl/91/616/tV/s502), as added [Pub. L. 94–371, § 7](/us/pl/94/371/s7), July 26, 1976, [90 Stat. 1039](/us/stat/90/1039), provided for scientific peer review of grants and contracts. See [section 290aa–5 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290aa–5).

A prior [section 502 of Pub. L. 91–616](/us/pl/91/616/s502), title V, Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1854](/us/stat/84/1854), was renumbered 602 by [section 7 of Pub. L. 94–371](/us/pl/94/371/s7), and is classified to [section 4592 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4592).
