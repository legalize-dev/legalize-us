# § 336c. Omitted

##### § 336c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 30, 1956, ch. 778, § 3](/us/act/1956-07-30/ch778/s3), [70 Stat. 716](/us/stat/70/716), provided that property rights of an entryman making an election under [section 336a of this title](/us/usc/t43/s336a) or whose entry is allowed under [section 336b of this title](/us/usc/t43/s336b) was a personal right, inheritable but not assignable.
