# § 1300i–6. Omitted

##### § 1300i–6. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–580, § 7](/us/pl/100/580/s7), Oct. 31, 1988, [102 Stat. 2931](/us/stat/102/2931), which related to division of remaining funds after Settlement Fund payments have been made, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
