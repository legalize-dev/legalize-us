# § 586. Omitted

##### § 586. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–206, § 6](/us/pl/92/206/s6), Dec. 18, 1971, [85 Stat. 738](/us/stat/85/738), which related to distribution of funds to members of the Northwestern Band of Shoshone Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
