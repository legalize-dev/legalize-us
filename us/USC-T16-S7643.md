# § 7643. Engagement of United States diplomatic missions

##### § 7643. Engagement of United States diplomatic missions

As soon as practicable but not later than 2 years after October 7, 2016, each chief of mission to a focus country should begin to implement the recommendations contained in the strategic plan required under [section 7631(a)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7631/a/2), among other goals, for the country.

*([Pub. L. 114–231, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/114/231/tIV/s403), Oct. 7, 2016, [130 Stat. 955](/us/stat/130/955).)*
