# § 1036. Omitted

##### § 1036. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 88–457, § 6](/us/pl/88/457/s6), Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 556](/us/stat/78/556), which exempted funds distributed in accordance with this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
