Section 1784, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 499A](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s499A), as added [Pub. L. 102–367, title IV, § 408](/us/pl/102/367/tIV/s408), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1094](/us/stat/106/1094), authorized disaster relief employment assistance.
