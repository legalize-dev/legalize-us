# § 3505. Effect on other laws

##### § 3505. Effect on other laws

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to supersede or alter the existing authorities of any Federal agency with respect to ocean and coastal mapping.

*([Pub. L. 111–11, title XII, § 12208](/us/pl/111/11/tXII/s12208), formerly § 12206, Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1426](/us/stat/123/1426); renumbered § 12208, [Pub. L. 117–263, div. J, title CIII, § 10307(e)(1)](/us/pl/117/263/dJ/tCIII/s10307/e/1), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 3978](/us/stat/136/3978).)*
