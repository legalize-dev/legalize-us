# § 10595. Reports

##### § 10595. Reports

(1) is based on evidence-based data; and

(2) uses the methods and measurements described in the application of that entity for purposes of evaluating that program.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2925](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2925), as added [Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 113](/us/pl/110/199/tI/s113), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 676](/us/stat/122/676).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3797s–4 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3797s–4), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
