# §§ 14731 to 14735. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

##### §§ 14731 to 14735. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

Section 14731, [Pub. L. 105–303, title II, § 201](/us/pl/105/303/tII/s201), Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2854](/us/stat/112/2854), related to requirement to procure commercial space transportation services. See [section 50131 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s50131), National and Commercial Space Programs.

Section 14732, [Pub. L. 105–303, title II, § 202](/us/pl/105/303/tII/s202), Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2855](/us/stat/112/2855), related to acquisition of commercial space transportation services. See [section 50132 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s50132).

Section 14733, [Pub. L. 105–303, title II, § 204](/us/pl/105/303/tII/s204), Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2856](/us/stat/112/2856), related to potential privatization of the Space Shuttle program. Subsec. (a) was repealed and reenacted as [section 50133 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s50133). Subsec. (b), requiring feasibility study, and subsec. (c), requiring reports to congressional committees within 60 days after Oct. 28, 1998, were repealed as obsolete.

Section 14734, [Pub. L. 105–303, title II, § 205](/us/pl/105/303/tII/s205), Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2857](/us/stat/112/2857); [Pub. L. 106–65, div. A, title X, § 1067(21)](/us/pl/106/65/dA/tX/s1067/21), Oct. 5, 1999, [113 Stat. 775](/us/stat/113/775), related to use of excess intercontinental ballistic missiles. See [section 50134 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s50134).

Section 14735, [Pub. L. 105–303, title II, § 206](/us/pl/105/303/tII/s206), Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2857](/us/stat/112/2857), required report to certain congressional committees regarding national launch capability no later than 180 days after Oct. 28, 1998.
