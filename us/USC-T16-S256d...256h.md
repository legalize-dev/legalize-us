# §§ 256d to 256h. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 256d to 256h. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 256d, acts [Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151, § 5](/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151/s5), [56 Stat. 137](/us/stat/56/137); [Apr. 21, 1948, ch. 223, § 4](/us/act/1948-04-21/ch223/s4), [62 Stat. 197](/us/stat/62/197), related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 256e, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151, § 6](/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151/s6), [56 Stat. 137](/us/stat/56/137), related to issuance of process. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

Section 256f, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151, § 7](/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151/s7), [56 Stat. 137](/us/stat/56/137), related to commissioner’s [now magistrate judge’s] salary.

Section 256g, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151, § 8](/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151/s8), [56 Stat. 137](/us/stat/56/137), related to certification and payment of fees, costs, and expenses.

Section 256h, [act Mar. 6, 1942, ch. 151, § 9](/us/act/1942-03-06/ch151/s9), [56 Stat. 137](/us/stat/56/137), related to disposition of fines and costs.
