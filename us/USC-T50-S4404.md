# § 4404. Repealed or Transferred

##### § 4404. Repealed or Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was comprised of section 8 of [act Mar. 8, 1946, ch. 82](/us/act/1946-03-08/ch82), [60 Stat. 45](/us/stat/60/45), as amended. Subsections (a) to (c) of section 8 of act Mar. 8, 1946, were repealed by [Pub. L. 101–225, title III, § 307(12)](/us/pl/101/225/tIII/s307/12), Dec. 12, 1989, [103 Stat. 1925](/us/stat/103/1925). Subsection (d) of section 8 of act Mar. 8, 1946, was redesignated as and transferred to [section 56308 of Title 46](/us/usc/t46/s56308), Shipping, by [Pub. L. 115–91, div. C, title XXXV, § 3502(a)(2)](/us/pl/115/91/dC/tXXXV/s3502/a/2), Dec. 12, 2017, [131 Stat. 1910](/us/stat/131/1910).

Section was formerly classified to section 1741 of the former Appendix to this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
