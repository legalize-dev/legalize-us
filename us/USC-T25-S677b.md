#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 3](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s3), [68 Stat. 868](/us/stat/68/868), which related to method of determining Ute Indian blood, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
