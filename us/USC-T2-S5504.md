# § 5504. Officers of Senate

##### § 5504. Officers of Senate

[Section 5503 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s5503) shall not apply to officers of the Senate.

*([Pub. L. 95–521, title VII, § 714(d)](/us/pl/95/521/tVII/s714/d), Oct. 26, 1978, [92 Stat. 1884](/us/stat/92/1884).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 118a of this title](/us/usc/t2/s118a) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective Jan. 3, 1979, see [section 717 of Pub. L. 95–521](/us/pl/95/521/s717), set out as a note under [section 288 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s288).
