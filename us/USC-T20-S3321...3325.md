# §§ 3321 to 3325. Omitted

##### §§ 3321 to 3325. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 3321, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7041](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7041), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 289](/us/stat/102/289), related to use of funds for training and technical assistance.

Section 3322, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7042](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7042), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 290](/us/stat/102/290), related to multifunctional resource centers.

Section 3323, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7043](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7043), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 291](/us/stat/102/291), related to fellowships.

Section 3324, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7044](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7044), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 291](/us/stat/102/291), related to priorities in making grants and contracts.

Section 3325, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7045](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7045), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 291](/us/stat/102/291), related to stipends.
