# § 1801. Applicability of the immigration laws

##### § 1801. Applicability of the immigration laws

(a) Applicability The fees under this subtitle shall apply to aliens in the circumstances described in this subtitle.

(b) Terms The terms used under this subtitle shall have the meanings given such terms in section 101 of the Immigration and Nationality Act ([8 U.S.C. 1101](/us/usc/t8/s1101)).

(c) References to Immigration and Nationality Act Except as otherwise expressly provided, any reference in this subtitle to a section or other provision shall be considered to be to a section or other provision of the Immigration and Nationality Act ([8 U.S.C. 1101](/us/usc/t8/s1101) et seq.).

*([Pub. L. 119–21, title X, § 100001](/us/pl/119/21/tX/s100001), July 4, 2025, [139 Stat. 364](/us/stat/139/364).)*
