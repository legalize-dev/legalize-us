# § 558. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 558. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums,11 So in original. The comma probably should not appear. as may be necessary to carry out the provisions and purposes of this chapter.

*([Aug. 31, 1954, ch. 1156, § 8](/us/act/1954-08-31/ch1156/s8), [68 Stat. 1011](/us/stat/68/1011); [Pub. L. 102–486, title XXV, § 2504(d)(3)](/us/pl/102/486/tXXV/s2504/d/3), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 3106](/us/stat/106/3106).)*

#### Amendments
