# §§ 197 to 197d. Repealed. [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title XI, § 1131(54), [60 Stat. 1039](/us/stat/60/1039)

##### §§ 197 to 197d. Repealed. [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title XI, § 1131(54), [60 Stat. 1039](/us/stat/60/1039)

Section 197, [act Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 365, § 1](/us/act/1927-03-03/ch365/s1), [44 Stat. 1394](/us/stat/44/1394), related to establishment of Foreign Commerce Service.

Section 197a, [act Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 365, § 2](/us/act/1927-03-03/ch365/s2), [44 Stat. 1394](/us/stat/44/1394), related to duties of officers.

Section 197b, acts [Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 365, § 3](/us/act/1927-03-03/ch365/s3), [44 Stat. 1394](/us/stat/44/1394); [Apr. 12, 1930, ch. 142](/us/act/1930-04-12/ch142), [46 Stat. 163](/us/stat/46/163), related to appointment of officers, assignments for duty, and allowances.

Section 197c, [act Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 365, § 4](/us/act/1927-03-03/ch365/s4), [44 Stat. 1395](/us/stat/44/1395), related to employment of clerks and assistants.

Section 197d, [act Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 365, § 5](/us/act/1927-03-03/ch365/s5), [44 Stat. 1396](/us/stat/44/1396), related to status of officer while serving abroad.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective three months from Aug. 13, 1946, see section 1141 of act Aug. 13, 1946.
