# § 7911. Prohibition on nationwide database

##### § 7911. Prohibition on nationwide database

Nothing in this chapter (other than [section 6398(b) of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6398/b)) shall be construed to authorize the development of a nationwide database of personally identifiable information on individuals involved in studies or other collections of data under this chapter.
