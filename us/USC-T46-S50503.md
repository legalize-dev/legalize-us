# § 50503. Oceanographic research vessels

##### § 50503. Oceanographic research vessels

An oceanographic research vessel (as defined in [section 2101 of this title](/us/usc/t46/s2101)) is deemed not to be engaged in trade or commerce.

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 8(b)](/us/pl/109/304/s8/b), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1567](/us/stat/120/1567).)*

The definitions of “oceanographic research vessel” and “scientific personnel” in 46 App. U.S.C. 441 are omitted because substantially the same definitions are already in [46 U.S.C. 2101](/us/usc/t46/s2101).

The text of 46 App. U.S.C. 444 is omitted because [section 10101(3) of title 46](/us/usc/t46/s10101/3), which defines “seaman” for purposes of part G of subtitle II of title 46, already contains an exception for scientific personnel. Title 53 of the Revised Statutes, referred to [in] 46 App. U.S.C. 444, was previously codified principally in part G of subtitle II of title 46.
