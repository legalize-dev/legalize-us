# § 1262. Omitted

##### § 1262. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–254, § 2](/us/pl/92/254/s2), Mar. 18, 1972, [86 Stat. 64](/us/stat/86/64), which related to membership roll and per capita distributions to tribal members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
