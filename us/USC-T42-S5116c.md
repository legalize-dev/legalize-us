##### § 5116c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–36, title I, § 124](/us/pl/108/36/tI/s124), June 25, 2003, [117 Stat. 815](/us/stat/117/815)

Section, [Pub. L. 93–247, title II, § 204](/us/pl/93/247/tII/s204), as added [Pub. L. 104–235, title I, § 121](/us/pl/104/235/tI/s121), Oct. 3, 1996, [110 Stat. 3083](/us/stat/110/3083); amended [Pub. L. 106–400, § 2](/us/pl/106/400/s2), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1675](/us/stat/114/1675), related to continuation of grants and agreements in effect on October 3, 1996.

A prior section 5116c, [Pub. L. 93–247, title II, § 204](/us/pl/93/247/tII/s204), formerly [Pub. L. 98–473, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/98/473/tIV/s405), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2198](/us/stat/98/2198); renumbered [§ 204 of Pub. L. 93–247](/us/pl/93/247/s204), and amended [Pub. L. 101–126](/us/pl/101/126), §§ 2(a), 3(a)(3), (c)(1), 4(d), Oct. 25, 1989, [103 Stat. 764](/us/stat/103/764), 766, 767; [Pub. L. 102–295, title I, § 123](/us/pl/102/295/tI/s123), May 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 198](/us/stat/106/198), established requirement for State grant eligibility, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 103–252, § 401(a)](/us/pl/103/252/s401/a).
