# §§ 1323d to 1323h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–845, § 26(b)](/us/pl/87/845/s26/b), Oct. 18, 1962, [76A Stat. 701](/us/stat/76A/701)

##### §§ 1323d to 1323h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–845, § 26(b)](/us/pl/87/845/s26/b), Oct. 18, 1962, [76A Stat. 701](/us/stat/76A/701)

Section 1323d, [act June 13, 1940, ch. 358, § 2](/us/act/1940-06-13/ch358/s2), [54 Stat. 389](/us/stat/54/389), related to faith of United States pledged to payment of deposits.

Section 1323e, [act June 13, 1940, ch. 358, § 2](/us/act/1940-06-13/ch358/s2), [54 Stat. 389](/us/stat/54/389), related to control of money-order and postal-savings funds.

Section 1323f, [act June 13, 1940, ch. 358, § 2](/us/act/1940-06-13/ch358/s2), [54 Stat. 389](/us/stat/54/389), related to deposit of money-order and postal-savings funds in United States treasury.

Section 1323g, [act June 13, 1940, ch. 358, § 2](/us/act/1940-06-13/ch358/s2), [54 Stat. 389](/us/stat/54/389), related to deposit of money-order and postal-savings funds in banks.

Section 1323h, [act June 13, 1940, ch. 358, § 2](/us/act/1940-06-13/ch358/s2), [54 Stat. 389](/us/stat/54/389), related to investment of money-order and postal-savings funds in securities of the United States.
