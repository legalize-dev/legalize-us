# § 967. Omitted

##### § 967. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–235, § 7](/us/pl/87/235/s7), Sept. 14, 1961, [75 Stat. 509](/us/stat/75/509), which authorized the prescription of rules and regulations, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
