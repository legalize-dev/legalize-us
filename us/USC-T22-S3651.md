# § 3651. Definitions

##### § 3651. Definitions

(1) “agency” means—

(A) the Commission, and

(B) any other Executive agency or the Smithsonian Institution, to the extent of any election in effect under [section 3652(b) of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3652/b);

(2) “position” means a civilian position in the Commission, or in any other agency if a substantial portion of the duties and responsibilities are performed in the Republic of Panama; and

(3) “employee” means an individual serving in a position.

*([Pub. L. 96–70, title I, § 1211](/us/pl/96/70/tI/s1211), Sept. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 463](/us/stat/93/463); [Pub. L. 104–201, div. C, title XXXV](/us/pl/104/201/dC/tXXXV), §§ 3529, 3548(b)(1), Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2862](/us/stat/110/2862), 2869.)*
