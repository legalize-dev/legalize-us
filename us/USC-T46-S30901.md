# § 30901. Short title

##### § 30901. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “Suits in Admiralty Act”.

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 6(c)](/us/pl/109/304/s6/c), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1517](/us/stat/120/1517).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Act Mar. 9, 1920, ch. 95](/us/act/1920-03-09/ch95), [41 Stat. 525](/us/stat/41/525), which enacted chapter 20 (§ 741 et seq.) of the former Appendix to this title, was popularly known as the “Suits in Admiralty Act”, prior to being repealed and restated in this chapter by [Pub. L. 109–304](/us/pl/109/304), §§ 6(c), 19, Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1509](/us/stat/120/1509), 1710.
