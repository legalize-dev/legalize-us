# § 7385a. Subrogation of the United States

##### § 7385a. Subrogation of the United States

Upon payment of compensation under this subchapter, the United States is subrogated for the amount of the payment to a right or claim that the individual to whom the payment was made may have against any person on account of injuries referred to in [section 7385 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s7385).

*([Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [div. C, title XXXVI, § 3642]](/us/pl/106/398/s1/dC/tXXXVI/s3642), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1654](/us/stat/114/1654), 1654A–509; [Pub. L. 108–375, div. C, title XXXI, § 3162(b)](/us/pl/108/375/dC/tXXXI/s3162/b), Oct. 28, 2004, [118 Stat. 2186](/us/stat/118/2186).)*
