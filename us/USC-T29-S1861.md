# § 1861. Rules and regulations

##### § 1861. Rules and regulations

The Secretary may issue such rules and regulations as are necessary to carry out this chapter, consistent with the requirements of chapter 5 of title 5.

*([Pub. L. 97–470, title V, § 511](/us/pl/97/470/tV/s511), Jan. 14, 1983, [96 Stat. 2598](/us/stat/96/2598).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective 90 days from Jan. 14, 1983, see [section 524 of Pub. L. 97–470](/us/pl/97/470/s524), set out as a note under [section 1801 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s1801).
