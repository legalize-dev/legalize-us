# § 526. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–17, title I, § 135(e)](/us/pl/100/17/tI/s135/e), Apr. 2, 1987, [101 Stat. 174](/us/stat/101/174)

##### § 526. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–17, title I, § 135(e)](/us/pl/100/17/tI/s135/e), Apr. 2, 1987, [101 Stat. 174](/us/stat/101/174)

Section, acts [Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753), title V, § 503, [60 Stat. 847](/us/stat/60/847); Jan. 12, 1983, [Pub. L. 97–449, § 2(d)(1)](/us/pl/97/449/s2/d/1), [96 Stat. 2440](/us/stat/96/2440), provided that tolls charged for transit over any interstate bridge be just and reasonable and authorized Secretary of Transportation to prescribe reasonable rates of toll for such transit, which rates were to be legal rates demanded and received. See [section 508 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s508).
