# § 49c–5. Omitted

##### § 49c–5. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 8, 1947, ch. 210, title I](/us/act/1947-07-08/ch210/tI), [61 Stat. 263](/us/stat/61/263), which related to a joint budget, was from the Department of Labor Appropriation Act, 1948, and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts. Similar provisions were contained in [act July 26, 1946, ch. 672, title I, § 101](/us/act/1946-07-26/ch672/tI/s101), [60 Stat. 686](/us/stat/60/686).
