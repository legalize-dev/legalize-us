# § 6823. Criminal penalty

##### § 6823. Criminal penalty

(a) In general Whoever knowingly and intentionally violates, or knowingly and intentionally attempts to violate, [section 6821 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s6821) shall be fined in accordance with title 18 or imprisoned for not more than 5 years, or both.

(b) Enhanced penalty for aggravated cases Whoever violates, or attempts to violate, [section 6821 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s6821) while violating another law of the United States or as part of a pattern of any illegal activity involving more than $100,000 in a 12-month period shall be fined twice the amount provided in subsection (b)(3) or (c)(3) (as the case may be) of [section 3571 of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3571), imprisoned for not more than 10 years, or both.

*([Pub. L. 106–102, title V, § 523](/us/pl/106/102/tV/s523), Nov. 12, 1999, [113 Stat. 1448](/us/stat/113/1448).)*
