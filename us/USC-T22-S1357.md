# § 1357. Trade agreements with the Philippines

##### § 1357. Trade agreements with the Philippines

Until July 4, 1974, no trade agreement shall be made with the Philippines under [section 1351 of title 19](/us/usc/t19/s1351), unless, prior to such time, the President of the United States has made the proclamation provided for in [section 1347 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1347), or the executive agreement provided for in subchapter III of this chapter has been terminated.

*([Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244](/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244), title V, § 508, [60 Stat. 158](/us/stat/60/158).)*

#### Suspension of Provisions

Section not applicable during such time as the revised agreement between the United States and the Philippines is in effect, see [section 1373 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1373).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The executive agreement provided for in subchapter III of this chapter, referred to in text, expired July 4, 1974.
