# § 3572. Implementation of simplified acquisition procedures

##### § 3572. Implementation of simplified acquisition procedures

The simplified acquisition procedures contained in the Federal Acquisition Regulation pursuant to [section 1901 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s1901) shall apply as provided in such section to the agencies named in [section 3063 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s3063).

*(Added [Pub. L. 103–355, title IV, § 4203(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/355/tIV/s4203/a/1), Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3345](/us/stat/108/3345), § 2302b; amended [Pub. L. 111–350, § 5(b)(10)](/us/pl/111/350/s5/b/10), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3843](/us/stat/124/3843); renumbered § 3572 and amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1823(b)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1823/b), (c)(2), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4205](/us/stat/134/4205).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 3572 was renumbered [section 7222 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s7222).

#### Amendments

2021—[Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283) renumbered [section 2302b of this title](/us/usc/t10/s2302b) as this section and substituted “section 3063” for “section 2303(a)”.

2011—[Pub. L. 111–350](/us/pl/111/350) substituted “[section 1901 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s1901)” for “section 31 of the Office of Federal Procurement Policy Act”.
