# § 674. Short title

##### § 674. Short title

This Act may be cited as the “Agricultural Marketing Agreement Act of 1937”.

*([June 3, 1937, ch. 296, § 6](/us/act/1937-06-03/ch296/s6), [50 Stat. 249](/us/stat/50/249).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, is [act June 3, 1937, ch. 296](/us/act/1937-06-03/ch296), [50 Stat. 246](/us/stat/50/246), which among other things enacted this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.
