# § 1038. Omitted

##### § 1038. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 88–457, § 8](/us/pl/88/457/s8), Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 556](/us/stat/78/556), which authorized prescription of rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
