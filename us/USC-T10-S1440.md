# § 1440. Annuities not subject to legal process

##### § 1440. Annuities not subject to legal process

Except as provided in [section 1437(c)(3)(B) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1437/c/3/B), no annuity payable under this subchapter is assignable or subject to execution, levy, attachment, garnishment, or other legal process.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 111](/us/stat/70A/111); [Pub. L. 92–425, § 1(2)(A)](/us/pl/92/425/s1/2/A), Sept. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 706](/us/stat/86/706); [Pub. L. 98–525, title VI, § 642(a)(2)](/us/pl/98/525/tVI/s642/a/2), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2546](/us/stat/98/2546); [Pub. L. 99–145, title XIII, § 1303(a)(10)](/us/pl/99/145/tXIII/s1303/a/10), Nov. 8, 1985, [99 Stat. 739](/us/stat/99/739).)*

The words “either in law or equity” are omitted as surplusage.
