# §§ 1371 to 1371b. Repealed. [July 21, 1949, ch. 356, § 1(b)](/us/act/1949-07-21/ch356/s1/b), [63 Stat. 475](/us/stat/63/475)

##### §§ 1371 to 1371b. Repealed. [July 21, 1949, ch. 356, § 1(b)](/us/act/1949-07-21/ch356/s1/b), [63 Stat. 475](/us/stat/63/475)

Section 1371, [act Mar. 2, 1931, ch. 375, § 1](/us/act/1931-03-02/ch375/s1), [46 Stat. 1471](/us/stat/46/1471), related to employees entitled to retirement privileges.

Section 1371a, acts [Mar. 2, 1931, ch. 375, § 2](/us/act/1931-03-02/ch375/s2), [46 Stat. 1471](/us/stat/46/1471); [June 19, 1934, ch. 667, § 1](/us/act/1934-06-19/ch667/s1), [48 Stat. 1122](/us/stat/48/1122); [July 29, 1942, ch. 536, § 1](/us/act/1942-07-29/ch536/s1), [56 Stat. 726](/us/stat/56/726), related to automatic separation.

Section 1371b, acts [Mar. 2, 1931, ch. 375, § 3](/us/act/1931-03-02/ch375/s3), [46 Stat. 1472](/us/stat/46/1472); [July 2, 1945, ch. 220](/us/act/1945-07-02/ch220), [59 Stat. 212](/us/stat/59/212), related to voluntary retirement.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Apr. 1, 1948, see [Act July 21, 1949, ch. 356, § 7(a)](/us/act/1949-07-21/ch356/s7/a), [63 Stat. 476](/us/stat/63/476).

#### Additional Repeal

Sections were also repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 648](/us/stat/80/648).
