# § 1067d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–39, title III, § 302](/us/pl/111/39/tIII/s302), July 1, 2009, [123 Stat. 1938](/us/stat/123/1938)

##### § 1067d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–39, title III, § 302](/us/pl/111/39/tIII/s302), July 1, 2009, [123 Stat. 1938](/us/stat/123/1938)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–329, title III](/us/pl/89/329/tIII), formerly title X, § 1024, as added [Pub. L. 99–498, title X, § 1002](/us/pl/99/498/tX/s1002), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1562](/us/stat/100/1562); amended [Pub. L. 102–325, title X, § 1002(b)](/us/pl/102/325/tX/s1002/b), July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 780](/us/stat/106/780); renumbered title III, [Pub. L. 105–244, title III, § 301(a)(5)](/us/pl/105/244/tIII/s301/a/5), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1636](/us/stat/112/1636), required Secretary to submit to President and Congress a report by Jan. 1, 1996, summarizing and evaluating Federal programs to increase minority participation and representation in scientific fields.

Section was formerly classified to [section 1135b–3 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1135b–3) prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 105–244](/us/pl/105/244).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective as if enacted on the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 110–315](/us/pl/110/315) (Aug. 14, 2008), see [section 3 of Pub. L. 111–39](/us/pl/111/39/s3), set out as an Effective Date of 2009 Amendment note under [section 1001 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1001).
