##### § 1a–8. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

Section, [Pub. L. 98–540, § 4](/us/pl/98/540/s4), Oct. 24, 1984, [98 Stat. 2719](/us/stat/98/2719); [Pub. L. 103–437, § 6(d)(1)](/us/pl/103/437/s6/d/1), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4583](/us/stat/108/4583); [Pub. L. 104–333, div. I, title VIII, § 814(d)(1)(F)](/us/pl/104/333/dI/tVIII/s814/d/1/F), Nov. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 4196](/us/stat/110/4196), related to maintenance management system for the National Park System. Subsec. (a) was repealed and restated in [section 101301 of Title 54](/us/usc/t54/s101301), National Park Service and Related Programs. Subsec. (b), which required annual status reports, had been previously repealed by [Pub. L. 104–333](/us/pl/104/333).
