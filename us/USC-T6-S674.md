# § 674. No private right of action

##### § 674. No private right of action

Nothing in this part may be construed to create a private right of action for enforcement of any provision of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 107–296, title XXII, § 2225](/us/pl/107/296/tXXII/s2225), formerly title II, § 215, Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2155](/us/stat/116/2155); renumbered title XXII, § 2225, [Pub. L. 115–278, § 2(g)(2)(H)](/us/pl/115/278/s2/g/2/H), Nov. 16, 2018, [132 Stat. 4178](/us/stat/132/4178).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 107–296](/us/pl/107/296), Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2135](/us/stat/116/2135), known as the Homeland Security Act of 2002, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 101 of this title](/us/usc/t6/s101) and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 134 of this title](/us/usc/t6/s134) prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 115–278](/us/pl/115/278).
