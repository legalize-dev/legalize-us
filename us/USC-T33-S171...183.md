# §§ 171 to 183. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(a)](/us/pl/96/591/s8/a), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435](/us/stat/94/3435)

##### §§ 171 to 183. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(a)](/us/pl/96/591/s8/a), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435](/us/stat/94/3435)

Section 171, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 96](/us/stat/30/96), made general provision for the application of rules regarding lights.

Section 172, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 96](/us/stat/30/96), related to lights of steam vessels underway.

Section 173, acts [June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 97](/us/stat/30/97); [May 20, 1936, ch. 433](/us/act/1936-05-20/ch433), [49 Stat. 1367](/us/stat/49/1367); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 2](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s2), [62 Stat. 249](/us/stat/62/249); Aug. 14, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–635, § 1](/us/pl/85/635/s1), [72 Stat. 590](/us/stat/72/590), related to lights of vessels towing or pushing another vessel.

Section 174, acts [June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 97](/us/stat/30/97); [Mar. 1, 1933, ch. 157](/us/act/1933-03-01/ch157), [47 Stat. 1417](/us/stat/47/1417), related to lights of sailing vessels underway and vessels being towed.

Section 175, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 97](/us/stat/30/97), related to lights of small vessels underway in bad weather.

Section 176, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 98](/us/stat/30/98), related to lights of rowboats.

Section 177, acts [June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 98](/us/stat/30/98); [Feb. 19, 1900, ch. 22, § 1](/us/act/1900-02-19/ch22/s1), [31 Stat. 30](/us/stat/31/30), related to lights of pilot vessels on and off duty.

Section 178, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 98](/us/stat/30/98); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, §§ 101–104, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7875, [60 Stat. 1097](/us/stat/60/1097), related to lights of fishing vessels.

Section 179, acts [June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 98](/us/stat/30/98); Aug. 14, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–635, § 2](/us/pl/85/635/s2), [72 Stat. 590](/us/stat/72/590), related to lights of overtaken vessels.

Section 180, acts [June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 98](/us/stat/30/98); [Apr. 22, 1940, ch. 128, § 1](/us/act/1940-04-22/ch128/s1), [54 Stat. 150](/us/stat/54/150); Aug. 5, 1963, [Pub. L. 88–84, § 1](/us/pl/88/84/s1), [77 Stat. 116](/us/stat/77/116); Oct. 15, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–670, § 6](/us/pl/89/670/s6), [80 Stat. 937](/us/stat/80/937), related to lights of vessels at anchor.

Section 181, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 99](/us/stat/30/99), related to additional lights when necessary.

Section 182, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 99](/us/stat/30/99), related to special lights for ships of war and convoy. See [section 2071 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2071).

Section 183, [act June 7, 1897, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-07/ch4/s1), [30 Stat. 99](/us/stat/30/99), related to day signal of vessels under sail.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Dec. 24, 1981, see [section 7 of Pub. L. 96–591](/us/pl/96/591/s7), set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 1604 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1604).
