# §§ 1500 to 1500b. Omitted

##### §§ 1500 to 1500b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted pursuant to [section 5316 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5316) which terminated the authority to make grants or loans under this chapter after Jan. 1, 1975.
