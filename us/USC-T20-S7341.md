# § 7341. Short title

##### § 7341. Short title

This part may be cited as the “Rural Education Achievement Program”.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 5201](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s5201), formerly title VI, § 6201, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/107/110/tVI/s601), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1891](/us/stat/115/1891); renumbered title V, § 5201, [Pub. L. 114–95, title V, § 5001(a)(2)](/us/pl/114/95/tV/s5001/a/2), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2039](/us/stat/129/2039), 2040.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 5201 of Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10/s5201) was renumbered section 4301 and was classified to [section 7221 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7221), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95).

Another prior [section 5201 of Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10/s5201) was classified to [section 7231 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7231), prior to the general amendment of former subchapter V of this chapter by [Pub. L. 107–110](/us/pl/107/110).
