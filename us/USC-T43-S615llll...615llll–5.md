Section 615*llll*–4, [Pub. L. 94–228, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/94/228/tIV/s405), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 208](/us/stat/90/208), related to interest rates.

Section 615*llll*–5, [Pub. L. 94–228, title IV, § 406](/us/pl/94/228/tIV/s406), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 208](/us/stat/90/208), related to lands held in single ownership.
