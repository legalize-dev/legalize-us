# § 4769. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 4769. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated, without fiscal year limitation, such sums as may be necessary to carry out the programs authorized by this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 91–648, title V, § 509](/us/pl/91/648/tV/s509), Jan. 5, 1971, [84 Stat. 1928](/us/stat/84/1928).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, means the provisions of subchapters I, II, III, and IV of this chapter. See [section 4761 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4761).
