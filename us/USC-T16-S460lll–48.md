# § 460lll–48. Tennessee Valley Authority transfer costs

##### § 460lll–48. Tennessee Valley Authority transfer costs

Any costs incurred by Tennessee Valley Authority associated with the transfer under this part shall be derived from funding described in section 460*lll*–49 of this title.

*([Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(e) [title V, § 548]](/us/pl/105/277/dA/s101/e/tV/s548), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–231](/us/stat/112/2681-231), 2681–325.)*
