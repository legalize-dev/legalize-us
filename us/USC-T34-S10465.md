# § 10465. Definitions and grant conditions

##### § 10465. Definitions and grant conditions

In this subchapter the definitions and grant conditions in [section 12291 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s12291) shall apply.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2106](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2106), formerly § 2105, as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title IV, § 40231(a)(3)](/us/pl/103/322/tIV/s40231/a/3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1933](/us/stat/108/1933); amended [Pub. L. 106–386, div. B, title I, § 1109(a)(2)](/us/pl/106/386/dB/tI/s1109/a/2), Oct. 28, 2000, [114 Stat. 1503](/us/stat/114/1503); [Pub. L. 109–162, § 3(c)(2)](/us/pl/109/162/s3/c/2), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 2972](/us/stat/119/2972); renumbered § 2106, [Pub. L. 117–103, div. W, title XV, § 1506(1)](/us/pl/117/103/dW/tXV/s1506/1), Mar. 15, 2022, [136 Stat. 956](/us/stat/136/956).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3796hh–4 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796hh–4), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 2106 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s2106) was classified to [section 3796hh–5 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796hh–5), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 109–271, § 2(f)(2)](/us/pl/109/271/s2/f/2), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 752](/us/stat/120/752).

#### Amendments

2006—[Pub. L. 109–162](/us/pl/109/162) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section consisted of pars. (1) to (3) defining for purposes of this subchapter “domestic violence”, “protection order”, and “dating violence”.

2000—Par. (3). [Pub. L. 106–386](/us/pl/106/386) added par. (3).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of 2022 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 117–103](/us/pl/117/103) not effective until Oct. 1 of the first fiscal year beginning after Mar. 15, 2022, see [section 4(a) of div. W of Pub. L. 117–103](/us/pl/117/103/dW/s4/a), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 6851 of Title 15](/us/usc/t15/s6851), Commerce and Trade.
