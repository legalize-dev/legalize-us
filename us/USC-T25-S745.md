# § 745. Omitted

##### § 745. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 5](/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1207/s5), [68 Stat. 1100](/us/stat/68/1100), which related to transfer of tribal real property that is under supervision of the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
