# §§ 4231 to 4237. Transferred

##### §§ 4231 to 4237. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 121 to 127 of [Pub. L. 116–224](/us/pl/116/224), formerly classified to sections 4231 to 4237, respectively, of this title, were renumbered sections 121 to 127 of [Pub. L. 109–449](/us/pl/109/449) by [Pub. L. 119–65, § 4(a)](/us/pl/119/65/s4/a), Dec. 26, 2025, [139 Stat. 1989](/us/stat/139/1989), and transferred to sections 1971 to 1977, respectively, of this title.
