#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–438, § 3](/us/pl/92/438/s3), Sept. 29, 1972, [86 Stat. 741](/us/stat/86/741), which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
