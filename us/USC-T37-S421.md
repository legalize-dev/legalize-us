# § 421. Allowances: no increase while dependent is entitled to basic pay

##### § 421. Allowances: no increase while dependent is entitled to basic pay

A member of a uniformed service may not be paid an increased allowance under this chapter, on account of a dependent, for any period during which that dependent is entitled to basic pay under [section 204 of this title](/us/usc/t37/s204).

*([Pub. L. 87–649](/us/pl/87/649), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 479](/us/stat/76/479), § 420; renumbered § 421, [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title VI, § 611(a)(1)(A)](/us/pl/100/180/dA/tVI/s611/a/1/A), Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1093](/us/stat/101/1093).)*

The words “of a uniformed service” are inserted for clarity. The words “claiming a dependent as defined in this subsection” are omitted as surplusage. The words “under [section 204 of this title](/us/usc/t37/s204)” are substituted for the words “for the performance of duty as defined in section 232(e) of this revised title”.
