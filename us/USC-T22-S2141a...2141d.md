# §§ 2141a to 2141d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–288, § 7(a)](/us/pl/104/288/s7/a), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3407](/us/stat/110/3407)

##### §§ 2141a to 2141d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–288, § 7(a)](/us/pl/104/288/s7/a), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3407](/us/stat/110/3407)

Section 2141a, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 3](/us/pl/104/288/s3), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3403](/us/stat/110/3403), related to establishment of United States National Tourism Organization.

Section 2141b, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 4](/us/pl/104/288/s4), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3404](/us/stat/110/3404), related to establishment of United States National Tourism Organization Board.

Section 2141c, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 5](/us/pl/104/288/s5), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3406](/us/stat/110/3406), related to design and use by Organization of symbols, emblems, trademarks, and names.

Section 2141d, [Pub. L. 104–288, § 6](/us/pl/104/288/s6), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3406](/us/stat/110/3406); [Pub. L. 105–277, div. G](/us/pl/105/277/dG), subdiv. A, title XIII, § 1335(*o*), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–789](/us/stat/112/2681-789), related to United States Government cooperation.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 2 years after Oct. 11, 1996, see [section 7 of Pub. L. 104–288](/us/pl/104/288/s7), as described in the Codification note under former [section 2141e of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2141e).
