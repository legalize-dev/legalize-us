# § 431a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

##### § 431a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

Section, [act Sept. 14, 1950, ch. 950, § 1](/us/act/1950-09-14/ch950/s1) (part), [64 Stat. 849](/us/stat/64/849), limited further extension or establishment of national monuments in Wyoming. See [section 320301(d) of Title 54](/us/usc/t54/s320301/d), National Park Service and Related Programs. Section comprised only part of the last sentence of section 1 of act Sept. 14, 1950. The remainder of such section, except that part of the last sentence which repealed sections 406 to 406d of this title, was classified to section 406d–1 and former [section 451a of this title](/us/usc/t16/s451a).
