# § 41303. Transfers of permits

##### § 41303. Transfers of permits

A permit issued under [section 41302 of this title](/us/usc/t49/s41302) may be transferred only when the Secretary of Transportation approves the transfer because the transfer is in the public interest.

*([Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(e)](/us/pl/103/272/s1/e), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1127](/us/stat/108/1127).)*
