##### § 1758. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–134, § 9](/us/pl/98/134/s9), Oct. 18, 1983, [97 Stat. 855](/us/stat/97/855), which related to extension of Federal recognition and privileges, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
