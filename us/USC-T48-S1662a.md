# § 1662a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 118–232, § 1](/us/pl/118/232/s1), Jan. 4, 2025, [138 Stat. 2834](/us/stat/138/2834)

##### § 1662a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 118–232, § 1](/us/pl/118/232/s1), Jan. 4, 2025, [138 Stat. 2834](/us/stat/138/2834)

Section, [Pub. L. 98–213, § 12](/us/pl/98/213/s12), Dec. 8, 1983, [97 Stat. 1462](/us/stat/97/1462), provided that amendments or modifications to the constitution of American Samoa, as approved pursuant to Executive Order 10264 as in effect Jan. 1, 1983, could be made only by Congress.
