# § 300ff–55. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 300ff–55. Authorization of appropriations

For the purpose of making grants under [section 300ff–51 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300ff–51), there are authorized to be appropriated, $218,600,000 for fiscal year 2007, $226,700,000 for fiscal year 2008, $235,100,000 for fiscal year 2009, $246,855,000 for fiscal year 2010, $259,198,000 for fiscal year 2011, $272,158,000 for fiscal year 2012, and $285,766,000 for fiscal year 2013.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2009—[Pub. L. 111–87, § 2(d)](/us/pl/111/87/s2/d), substituted “$235,100,000 for fiscal year 2009, $246,855,000 for fiscal year 2010, $259,198,000 for fiscal year 2011, $272,158,000 for fiscal year 2012, and $285,766,000 for fiscal year 2013” for “and $235,100,000 for fiscal year 2009”.

[Pub. L. 111–87, § 2(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/87/s2/a/1), (3)(A), repealed [Pub. L. 109–415, § 703](/us/pl/109/415/s703), and revived the provisions of this section as in effect on Sept. 30, 2009. See 2006 Amendment note and Effective Date of 2009 Amendment; Revival Section note below.

2006—[Pub. L. 109–415, § 703](/us/pl/109/415/s703), which directed repeal of this section effective Oct. 1, 2009, was itself repealed by [Pub. L. 111–87, § 2(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/87/s2/a/1), effective Sept. 30, 2009.

[Pub. L. 109–415, § 303](/us/pl/109/415/s303), substituted “, $218,600,000 for fiscal year 2007, $226,700,000 for fiscal year 2008, and $235,100,000 for fiscal year 2009” for “such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2001 through 2005”.

2000—[Pub. L. 106–345](/us/pl/106/345) substituted “for each of the fiscal years 2001 through 2005” for “in each of the fiscal years 1996, 1997, 1998, 1999, and 2000”.

1996—[Pub. L. 104–146](/us/pl/104/146) substituted “such sums as may be necessary in each of the fiscal years 1996, 1997, 1998, 1999, and 2000.” for “$75,000,000 for fiscal years 1991, and such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1992 through 1995.”
