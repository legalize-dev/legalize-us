# § 27. Definitions

##### § 27. Definitions

The terms “State” and “States,” as used in this chapter, shall be held to include the District of Columbia.

*([July 9, 1918, ch. 143](/us/act/1918-07-09/ch143), ch. XV, § 8, [40 Stat. 887](/us/stat/40/887).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, means chapter XV of [act July 9, 1918, ch. 143](/us/act/1918-07-09/ch143), [40 Stat. 887](/us/stat/40/887), which, insofar as classified to the Code, enacted sections 24 to 27 of this title and amended [section 28 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s28).
