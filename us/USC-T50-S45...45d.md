# §§ 45 to 45d. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### §§ 45 to 45d. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section 45, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2, § 1](/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2/s1), [52 Stat. 3](/us/stat/52/3), related to photographing of defensive installations. See sections 795 to 797 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 45a, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2, § 2](/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2/s2), [52 Stat. 3](/us/stat/52/3), related to photographing, etc., from aircraft. See [section 796 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s796).

Section 45b, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2, § 3](/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2/s3), [52 Stat. 3](/us/stat/52/3), related to reproducing, publishing, selling uncensored copies. See [section 797 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s797).

Section 45c, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2, § 4](/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2/s4), [52 Stat. 4](/us/stat/52/4), related to definitions of “aircraft”, “post”, “camp”, and “station”. See sections 795 and 796 of Title 18.

Section 45d, [act Jan. 12, 1938, ch. 2, § 5](/us/act/1938-01-12/ch2/s5), [52 Stat. 4](/us/stat/52/4), related to geographical application of law.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal of sections 45 to 45d effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s1), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
