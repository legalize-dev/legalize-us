# § 12212. Prohibition

##### § 12212. Prohibition

None of the funds authorized under this part shall be used to finance the construction of housing.

*([Pub. L. 103–322, title III, § 31133](/us/pl/103/322/tIII/s31133), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1888](/us/stat/108/1888).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 13853 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s13853), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
