# § 486. Omitted

##### § 486. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 14, 1954, ch. 472, § 3](/us/act/1954-07-14/ch472/s3), [68 Stat. 468](/us/stat/68/468), which authorized prescription of regulations to carry out sections 484 to 486 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
