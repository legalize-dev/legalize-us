# § 142g. Copyright Royalty Tribunal; computation and disbursement of pay of Tribunal personnel by Library of Congress

##### § 142g. Copyright Royalty Tribunal; computation and disbursement of pay of Tribunal personnel by Library of Congress

From and after October 1, 1983, the Library of Congress is authorized to compute and disburse basic pay of all personnel of the Copyright Royalty Tribunal pursuant to the provisions of [section 5504 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s5504).

*([Pub. L. 98–51, title II, § 204](/us/pl/98/51/tII/s204), July 14, 1983, [97 Stat. 277](/us/stat/97/277).)*
