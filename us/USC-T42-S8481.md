##### § 8481. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–42, § 1(a)(7)](/us/pl/100/42/s1/a/7), May 21, 1987, [101 Stat. 310](/us/stat/101/310)

Section, [Pub. L. 95–620, title VIII, § 801](/us/pl/95/620/tVIII/s801), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3346](/us/stat/92/3346), required annual disclosure of extent, characteristics, and productive capacity of coal reserves, and of interests held therein, with discretionary exception for small reserves, and publication of such information by Secretary.
