# § 2901. Omitted

##### § 2901. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1491](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1491), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 202](/us/stat/102/202), which contained transition provisions, was omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Prior sections 2901 to 2904 were repealed by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s587/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480](/us/stat/95/480), eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

Section 2901, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 221](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s221), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2204](/us/stat/92/2204), set out statement of purpose for basic skills improvement program.

Section 2902, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 222](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s222), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2205](/us/stat/92/2205); amended [Pub. L. 96–46, § 1(12)](/us/pl/96/46/s1/12), Aug. 6, 1979, [93 Stat. 339](/us/stat/93/339), related to agreements with State educational agencies.

Section 2903, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 223](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s223), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2207](/us/stat/92/2207), related to distribution of grants by the Secretary.

Section 2904, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 224](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s224), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2207](/us/stat/92/2207), provided for a State leadership program.
