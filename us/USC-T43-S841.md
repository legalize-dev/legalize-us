# § 841. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### § 841. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section, [act May 21, 1872, ch. 178](/us/act/1872-05-21/ch178), [17 Stat. 137](/us/stat/17/137), related to offense and punishment of claim agent, attorney or other person for withholding military land bounty warrant. See [section 290 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s290), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 20 of act June 25, 1948.
