# § 574a. Omitted

##### § 574a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–435, § 15](/us/pl/103/435/s15), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4573](/us/stat/108/4573), which related to acquisition of lands for the benefit of individual tribes on the Wind River Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
