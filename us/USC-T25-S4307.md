# § 4307. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 4307. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter, to remain available until expended.

*([Pub. L. 106–464, § 10](/us/pl/106/464/s10), formerly § 8, Nov. 7, 2000, [114 Stat. 2018](/us/stat/114/2018); renumbered § 10, [Pub. L. 116–261, § 3(d)(1)](/us/pl/116/261/s3/d/1), Dec. 30, 2020, [134 Stat. 3308](/us/stat/134/3308).)*
