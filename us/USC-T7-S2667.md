# § 2667. Regulations

##### § 2667. Regulations

The Secretary may issue such regulations as the Secretary determines necessary to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 92–419, title V, § 507](/us/pl/92/419/tV/s507), as added [Pub. L. 97–98, title XIV, § 1444(a)](/us/pl/97/98/tXIV/s1444/a), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1326](/us/stat/95/1326); amended [Pub. L. 115–334, title VI, § 6101(a)(3)(B)](/us/pl/115/334/tVI/s6101/a/3/B), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4727](/us/stat/132/4727).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 2667, [Pub. L. 92–419, title V, § 507](/us/pl/92/419/tV/s507), Aug. 30, 1972, [86 Stat. 674](/us/stat/86/674); [Pub. L. 95–113, title XIV, § 1442](/us/pl/95/113/tXIV/s1442), Sept. 29, 1977, [91 Stat. 1006](/us/stat/91/1006); [Pub. L. 96–355, § 4(4)](/us/pl/96/355/s4/4), Sept. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 1174](/us/stat/94/1174), set forth definitions for purposes of this subchapter, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–98, title XIV, § 1444(a)](/us/pl/97/98/tXIV/s1444/a), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1326](/us/stat/95/1326).

Provisions similar to those comprising this section were contained in former [section 2668 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2668), which was omitted from the Code.

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–334](/us/pl/115/334) substituted “The Secretary may issue” for “The Secretary of Agriculture may issue”.

Section effective Dec. 22, 1981, see [section 1801 of Pub. L. 97–98](/us/pl/97/98/s1801), set out as a note under [section 4301 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s4301).
