# § 16204. Regulations

##### § 16204. Regulations

This chapter shall be administered under regulations prescribed by the Secretary of Defense.

*(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1663(c)(1)](/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI/s1663/c/1), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 3007](/us/stat/108/3007).)*
