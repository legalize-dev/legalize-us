# § 802. Definitions

##### § 802. Definitions

(1) “Association” means the United States Railway Association;

(2) “Commission” means the Interstate Commerce Commission;

(3) “Corporation” means the Consolidated Rail Corporation;

(4) “final system plan” means the final system plan and any additions thereto adopted by the Association pursuant to the Regional Rail Reorganization Act of 1973 ([45 U.S.C. 701](/us/usc/t45/s701) et seq.);

(5) “includes” and variants thereof should be read as if the phrase “but is not limited to” were also set forth;

(6) “Office” means the Rail Services Planning Office of the Commission;

(7) “railroad” has the meaning given that term in [section 20102 of title 49](/us/usc/t49/s20102); and

(8) “Secretary” means the Secretary of Transportation or his designated representative.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, means [Pub. L. 94–210](/us/pl/94/210), Feb. 5, 1976, [90 Stat. 31](/us/stat/90/31), known as the Railroad Revitalization and Regulatory Reform Act of 1976. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 801 of this title](/us/usc/t45/s801) and Tables.

The Regional Rail Reorganization Act of 1973, referred to in par. (4), is [Pub. L. 93–236](/us/pl/93/236), Jan. 2, 1974, [87 Stat. 985](/us/stat/87/985), which is classified principally to chapter 16 (§ 701 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 701 of this title](/us/usc/t45/s701) and Tables.

#### Amendments

2005—Par. (7). [Pub. L. 109–59](/us/pl/109/59) amended par. (7) generally. Prior to amendment, par. (7) read as follows: “ ‘railroad’ means a rail carrier subject to part A of subtitle IV of title 49, and includes the National Railroad Passenger Corporation; and”.

1995—Par. (7). [Pub. L. 104–88](/us/pl/104/88) substituted “rail carrier subject to part A of subtitle IV of title 49” for “common carrier by railroad or express, as defined in section 1(3) of the Interstate Commerce Act ([49 U.S.C. 1(3)](/us/usc/t49/s1/3))”.

1983—Par. (7). [Pub. L. 97–468](/us/pl/97/468) struck out “and the Alaska Railroad” before the semicolon at end.
