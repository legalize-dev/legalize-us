# § 3642. Disposition of property of International Pacific Salmon Fisheries Commission

##### § 3642. Disposition of property of International Pacific Salmon Fisheries Commission

The Secretary of State shall dispose of any United States property held by the International Pacific Salmon Fisheries Commission on the date of its termination in a manner which would further the purposes of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 99–5, § 13](/us/pl/99/5/s13) (part), Mar. 15, 1985, [99 Stat. 15](/us/stat/99/15).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section consists of a part of [section 13 of Pub. L. 99–5](/us/pl/99/5/s13). The remainder of [section 13 of Pub. L. 99–5](/us/pl/99/5/s13) provided for the repeal, effective Dec. 31, 1985, of the Sockeye Salmon or Pink Salmon Fishing Act of 1947, [act July 29, 1947, ch. 345](/us/act/1947-07-29/ch345), [61 Stat. 511](/us/stat/61/511), as amended, which was classified to chapter 10A (§ 776 et seq.) of this title.
