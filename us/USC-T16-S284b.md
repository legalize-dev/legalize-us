# § 284b. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 284b. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary, but not in excess of $5,473,000, to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 89–671, § 3](/us/pl/89/671/s3), Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 951](/us/stat/80/951); [Pub. L. 92–272, title II, § 201(4)](/us/pl/92/272/tII/s201/4), Apr. 11, 1972, [86 Stat. 120](/us/stat/86/120).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1972—[Pub. L. 92–272](/us/pl/92/272) increased maximum sums authorized to be appropriated from not in excess of $600,000 to not in excess of $5,473,000.
