# § 5104A. Binding nature of favorable findings

##### § 5104A. Binding nature of favorable findings

Any finding favorable to the claimant as described in [section 5104(b)(4) of this title](/us/usc/t38/s5104/b/4) shall be binding on all subsequent adjudicators within the Department, unless clear and convincing evidence is shown to the contrary to rebut such favorable finding.

*(Added [Pub. L. 115–55, § 2(f)(1)](/us/pl/115/55/s2/f/1), Aug. 23, 2017, [131 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/131/1107).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

L. 115–55](/us/pl/115/55) and to address appeals of decisions on legacy claims, with provision for early applicability of the new appeals system to certain claims, see [section 2(x) of Pub. L. 115–55](/us/pl/115/55/s2/x), set out as an Effective Date of 2017 Amendment note under [section 101 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s101), and bracketed note thereunder.
