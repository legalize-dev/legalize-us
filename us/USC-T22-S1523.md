# § 1523. Repealed. [Oct. 26, 1951, ch. 575](/us/act/1951-10-26/ch575), title III, § 305, [65 Stat. 647](/us/stat/65/647)

##### § 1523. Repealed. [Oct. 26, 1951, ch. 575](/us/act/1951-10-26/ch575), title III, § 305, [65 Stat. 647](/us/stat/65/647)

Section, [act June 2, 1951, ch. 121](/us/act/1951-06-02/ch121), Ch. XIII, § 1302(a), [65 Stat. 63](/us/stat/65/63), related to assistance denied countries trading with Russia or satellite countries. See section 1611 et seq. of this title. Section 1302(b) of that act repealed section 1304 of [act Sept. 27, 1950, ch. 1052](/us/act/1950-09-27/ch1052), Ch. XIII, [64 Stat. 1066](/us/stat/64/1066), which contained similar provisions.
