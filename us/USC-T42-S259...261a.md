##### §§ 259 to 261a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–310, div. B, title XXXIV, § 3405(a)](/us/pl/106/310/dB/tXXXIV/s3405/a), Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1221](/us/stat/114/1221), as amended by [Pub. L. 114–198, title I, § 110(b)](/us/pl/114/198/tI/s110/b), July 22, 2016, [130 Stat. 710](/us/stat/130/710)

L. 91–513, title I, § 2(a)(2)(A)](/us/pl/91/513/tI/s2/a/2/A), (3), (4), Oct. 27, 1970, [84 Stat. 1240](/us/stat/84/1240); [Pub. Section 260a, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III, § 345](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIII/s345), as added [May 8, 1954, ch. 195, § 2](/us/act/1954-05-08/ch195/s2), [68 Stat. 79](/us/stat/68/79); amended [July 24, 1956, ch. 676](/us/act/1956-07-24/ch676), title III, § 302(c), [70 Stat. 622](/us/stat/70/622); [Pub.
