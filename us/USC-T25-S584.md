# § 584. Omitted

##### § 584. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–206, § 4](/us/pl/92/206/s4), Dec. 18, 1971, [85 Stat. 737](/us/stat/85/737), which related to apportionment of remaining funds between the Shoshone-Bannock Tribes of the Fort Hall Reservation and the Shoshone Tribe of the Wind River Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
