# § 1192. Discharge

##### § 1192. Discharge

(1) on which the last payment is due after the first 3 years of the plan, or such other time not to exceed 5 years fixed by the court; or

(2) of the kind specified in [section 523(a) of this title](/us/usc/t11/s523/a).

*(Added [Pub. L. 116–54, § 2(a)](/us/pl/116/54/s2/a), Aug. 23, 2019, [133 Stat. 1083](/us/stat/133/1083).)*
