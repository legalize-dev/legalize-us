# § 12714. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–99, title IV, § 404(a)](/us/pl/104/99/tIV/s404/a), Jan. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 44](/us/stat/110/44)

##### § 12714. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–99, title IV, § 404(a)](/us/pl/104/99/tIV/s404/a), Jan. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 44](/us/stat/110/44)

Section, [Pub. L. 101–625, title IX, § 957](/us/pl/101/625/tIX/s957), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 4422](/us/stat/104/4422), related to maximum annual limitation on rent increases resulting from employment.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 104–99, title IV, § 404(a)](/us/pl/104/99/tIV/s404/a), Jan. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 44](/us/stat/110/44), provided in part that this section is repealed retroactive to Nov. 28, 1990, and shall be of no effect.

#### Economic Independence

[Pub. L. 102–550, title IX, § 923](/us/pl/102/550/tIX/s923), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 3884](/us/stat/106/3884), which provided that Secretary of Housing and Urban Development was to immediately implement [section 12714 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12714) and that other Federal agencies authorized to assist low-income families were to take similar steps to encourage economic independence and the accumulation of assets, was repealed retroactive to Oct. 28, 1992, by [Pub. L. 104–99, title IV, § 404(b)](/us/pl/104/99/tIV/s404/b), Jan. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 44](/us/stat/110/44), which further provided that [section 923 of Pub. L. 102–550](/us/pl/102/550/s923) was to be of no effect.
