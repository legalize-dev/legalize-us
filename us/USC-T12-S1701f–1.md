##### § 1701f–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–19, § 5(c)](/us/pl/90/19/s5/c), May 25, 1967, [81 Stat. 21](/us/stat/81/21)

Section, [act Aug. 10, 1948, ch. 832, title III, § 304](/us/act/1948-08-10/ch832/tIII/s304), as added [July 15, 1949, ch. 338](/us/act/1949-07-15/ch338), title IV, § 401, [63 Stat. 431](/us/stat/63/431), provided for appointment, powers, and compensation of a Director. Section was previously repealed by [Pub. L. 89–534, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/534/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 655](/us/stat/80/655).
