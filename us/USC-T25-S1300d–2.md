# § 1300d–2. Omitted

##### § 1300d–2. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–555, title I, § 102](/us/pl/92/555/tI/s102), Oct. 25, 1972, [86 Stat. 1168](/us/stat/86/1168), which related to apportionment of funds on the basis of the membership rolls, distribution of per capita shares to tribe members, and use of remaining funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
