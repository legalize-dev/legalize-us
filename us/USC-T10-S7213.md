# § 7213. Corps of Engineers: assignment or transfer of officers to duties involving civil functions

##### § 7213. Corps of Engineers: assignment or transfer of officers to duties involving civil functions

Officers of the Corps of Engineers may be assigned or transferred to and from duties involving the civil functions of the Corps of Engineers only with the approval of the Secretary of the Army.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 201](/us/stat/70A/201), § 3533; [Pub. L. 89–718, § 26](/us/pl/89/718/s26), Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1119](/us/stat/80/1119); renumbered § 7213, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(b)(6)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/b/6), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1838](/us/stat/132/1838).)*

The words “and reassignments” are omitted as surplusage.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 7213, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 445](/us/stat/70A/445), related to relief of contractors and their employees from losses by enemy action, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–160, div. A, title VIII, § 824(a)(3)](/us/pl/103/160/dA/tVIII/s824/a/3), Nov. 30, 1993, [107 Stat. 1707](/us/stat/107/1707).

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–232](/us/pl/115/232) renumbered [section 3533 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s3533) as this section.

1966—[Pub. L. 89–718](/us/pl/89/718) struck out provisions requiring the recommendation of the Chief of Engineers in order to transfer officers of the Corps of Engineers to or from the civil functions of the Corps of Engineers.

#### Effective Date of 2018 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 115–232](/us/pl/115/232) effective Feb. 1, 2019, with provision for the coordination of amendments and special rule for certain redesignations, see [section 800 of Pub. L. 115–232](/us/pl/115/232/s800), set out as a note preceding [section 3001 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s3001).
