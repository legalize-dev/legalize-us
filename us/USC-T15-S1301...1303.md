# §§ 1301 to 1303. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–563, title I, § 117(a)](/us/pl/89/563/tI/s117/a), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 727](/us/stat/80/727)

##### §§ 1301 to 1303. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–563, title I, § 117(a)](/us/pl/89/563/tI/s117/a), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 727](/us/stat/80/727)

Sections, [Pub. L. 87–637](/us/pl/87/637), §§ 1–3, Sept. 5, 1962, [76 Stat. 437](/us/stat/76/437), provided for promulgation of standards for hydraulic brake fluid used in motor vehicles and set the penalty for the unlawful sale, importation, or introduction into commerce of fluid not meeting the published standards. See chapter 38 (§ 1381 et seq.) of this title.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Savings Provision

[Pub. L. 89–563, title I, § 117(b)](/us/pl/89/563/tI/s117/b)–(e), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 727](/us/stat/80/727), provided that persons willfully violating sections 1301 to 1303 and 1321 to 1323 of this title would be punished in accordance with provisions of laws in effect on date of violation, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379).
