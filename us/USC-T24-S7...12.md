# §§ 7 to 12. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

##### §§ 7 to 12. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

Section 7, R.S. § 4806; acts [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 156, § 4](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch156/s4), [18 Stat. 485](/us/stat/18/485); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309); [July 26, 1916, ch. 256](/us/act/1916-07-26/ch256), [39 Stat. 390](/us/stat/39/390); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205(b), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, 2729, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424), 1425, provided for lease and sale of hospitals. See [section 248 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s248), The Public Health and Welfare.

Section 8, acts [Aug. 4, 1894, ch. 213](/us/act/1894-08-04/ch213), [28 Stat. 229](/us/stat/28/229); [Jan. 28, 1915, ch. 20, § 2](/us/act/1915-01-28/ch20/s2), [38 Stat. 801](/us/stat/38/801); [July 30, 1937, ch. 545, § 2](/us/act/1937-07-30/ch545/s2), [50 Stat. 548](/us/stat/50/548); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205(b), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, 2729, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424), 1425, provided for admission of Coast Guard personnel and their families to hospitals. See [section 253 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s253). [Act Jan. 28, 1915, ch. 20, § 2](/us/act/1915-01-28/ch20/s2), [38 Stat. 801](/us/stat/38/801) was also repealed by [act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20), [63 Stat. 561](/us/stat/63/561).

Section 9, [act June 23, 1913, ch. 3, § 1](/us/act/1913-06-23/ch3/s1), [38 Stat. 24](/us/stat/38/24), provided for hospital relief of officers and employees of the Public Health Service. See sections 249 and 253 of Title 42.

Section 10, [act July 1, 1918, ch. 113, § 1](/us/act/1918-07-01/ch113/s1), [40 Stat. 694](/us/stat/40/694), provided for hospital relief for officers and crews of the Fish and Wildlife Service. See [section 249 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s249).

Section 11, acts [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 156, § 6](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch156/s6), [18 Stat. 486](/us/stat/18/486); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205(b), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, 2729, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424), 1425, provided for care of foreign seamen. See [section 249 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s249).

Section 11a, R.S. § 4805; acts [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 156, § 6](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch156/s6), [18 Stat. 486](/us/stat/18/486); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205(b), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, 2729, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424), 1425, provided that foreign vessels were liable for hospital charges for care of their seamen. See [section 249 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s249).

Section 12, R.S. § 4804, provided for exclusion of employees on canal boats in coasting trade from hospital care. See [section 249 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s249).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Renumbering of Repealing Act

For renumbering of act July 1, 1944, which repealed these sections, see note set out under sections 1, 2 of this title.
