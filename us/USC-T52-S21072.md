# § 21072. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 21072. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out the provisions of this part $200,000 for fiscal year 2003 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the 6 succeeding fiscal years.

*([Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 296](/us/pl/107/252/tII/s296), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1704](/us/stat/116/1704).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 15472 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s15472), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
