# [§ 1493. Repealed. [July 28, 1953, ch. 253, § 8](/us/act/1953-07-28/ch253/s8), [67 Stat. 226](/us/stat/67/226)]

##### [§ 1493. Repealed. [July 28, 1953, ch. 253, § 8](/us/act/1953-07-28/ch253/s8), [67 Stat. 226](/us/stat/67/226)]

Section, [act June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 941](/us/stat/62/941), authorized Court of Claims to give legal advice to heads of executive departments in matters referred to it by the heads, if Court had jurisdiction over the matters.
