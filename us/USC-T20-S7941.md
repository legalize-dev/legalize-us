# § 7941. Short title

##### § 7941. Short title

This subpart may be cited as the “Paul D. Coverdell Teacher Protection Act of 2001”.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 8551](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s8551), formerly title II, § 2361, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title II, § 201](/us/pl/107/110/tII/s201), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1667](/us/stat/115/1667); renumbered title IX, § 9541, renumbered title VIII, § 8551, [Pub. L. 114–95, title II, § 2001(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/114/95/tII/s2001/a/3/A), (B), (D), title VIII, § 8001(a)(7), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1913](/us/stat/129/1913), 2088, 2089.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was classified to [section 6731 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6731) prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95).

L. 107–110, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/107/110/tIX/s901), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1985](/us/stat/115/1985), which related to evaluations, was renumbered [section 8601 of title VIII of Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s8601) by [Pub. L. 114–95, title VIII, § 8001(a)(10)](/us/pl/114/95/tVIII/s8001/a/10), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2088](/us/stat/129/2088), 2089, and transferred to [section 7981 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7981).
