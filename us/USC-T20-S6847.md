# § 6847. Civil rights

##### § 6847. Civil rights

Nothing in this part shall be construed in a manner inconsistent with any Federal law guaranteeing a civil right.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 3126](/us/pl/89/10/tIII/s3126), formerly § 3127, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title III, § 301](/us/pl/107/110/tIII/s301), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1705](/us/stat/115/1705); renumbered § 3126, [Pub. L. 114–95, title III, § 3001(2)(B)](/us/pl/114/95/tIII/s3001/2/B), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1953](/us/stat/129/1953).)*
