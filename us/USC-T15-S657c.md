# § 657c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title XVI, § 1699(a)](/us/pl/112/239/dA/tXVI/s1699/a), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2092](/us/stat/126/2092)

##### § 657c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title XVI, § 1699(a)](/us/pl/112/239/dA/tXVI/s1699/a), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2092](/us/stat/126/2092)

Section, [Pub. L. 85–536, § 2[33]](/us/pl/85/536/s2/s33), as added [Pub. L. 106–50, title II, § 202(a)](/us/pl/106/50/tII/s202/a), Aug. 17, 1999, [113 Stat. 236](/us/stat/113/236); amended [Pub. L. 106–554, § 1(a)(9) [title VIII, § 808]](/us/pl/106/554/s1/a/9/tVIII/s808), Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2763](/us/stat/114/2763), 2763A–706; [Pub. L. 108–447, div. B, title VI, § 636](/us/pl/108/447/dB/tVI/s636), div. K, title I, §§ 143(a), 146, Dec. 8, 2004, [118 Stat. 2922](/us/stat/118/2922), 3455; [Pub. L. 110–186, title I, § 103(a)](/us/pl/110/186/tI/s103/a), Feb. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 625](/us/stat/122/625), established the National Veterans Business Development Corporation.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Representation of Authorization

[Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title XVI, § 1699(b)](/us/pl/112/239/dA/tXVI/s1699/b), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2092](/us/stat/126/2092), provided that: “On and after the date of enactment of this Act [Jan. 2, 2013], the National Veterans Business Development Corporation and any successor thereto may not represent that the corporation is federally chartered or in any other manner authorized by the Federal Government.”
