# § 3194. Archeological and paleontological sites

##### § 3194. Archeological and paleontological sites

(1) submit notice of such proposed designation or acquisition to the appropriate committees of the Congress; and

(2) publish notice of such proposed designation or acquisition in the Federal Register.

*([Pub. L. 96–487, title XIII, § 1304](/us/pl/96/487/tXIII/s1304), Dec. 2, 1980, [94 Stat. 2478](/us/stat/94/2478).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in provision preceding par. (1), is [Pub. L. 96–487](/us/pl/96/487), Dec. 2, 1980, [94 Stat. 2371](/us/stat/94/2371), known as the Alaska National Interest Lands Conservation Act. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 3101 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s3101) and Tables.
