# § 590a. Omitted

##### § 590a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–442, § 1](/us/pl/92/442/s1), Sept. 29, 1972, [86 Stat. 743](/us/stat/86/743), which credited funds to the Shoshone-Bannock Tribes of the Fort Hall Reservation for certain claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
