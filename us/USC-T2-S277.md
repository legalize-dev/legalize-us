# § 277. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–191, § 13](/us/pl/93/191/s13), Dec. 18, 1973, [87 Stat. 746](/us/stat/87/746)

##### § 277. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–191, § 13](/us/pl/93/191/s13), Dec. 18, 1973, [87 Stat. 746](/us/stat/87/746)

Section, [act Feb. 24, 1919, ch. 18, title XIII, § 1303(d)](/us/act/1919-02-24/ch18/tXIII/s1303/d), as added [June 2, 1924, ch. 234](/us/act/1924-06-02/ch234), title XI, § 1101, [43 Stat. 353](/us/stat/43/353), provided for free transmission of official mail matter of legislative counsel. Official mail matter of Legislative Counsel of House of Representatives is covered by [section 282d of this title](/us/usc/t2/s282d).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Dec. 18, 1973, see [section 14 of Pub. L. 93–191](/us/pl/93/191/s14), set out as an Effective Date of 1973 Amendment note under [section 3210 of Title 39](/us/usc/t39/s3210), Postal Service.
