# § 983a. Omitted

##### § 983a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–484, § 3](/us/pl/101/484/s3), Oct. 31, 1990, [104 Stat. 1167](/us/stat/104/1167), which related to Federal recognition of the Ponca Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
