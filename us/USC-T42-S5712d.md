# § 5712d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI, § 1172(b)](/us/pl/109/162/tXI/s1172/b), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3123](/us/stat/119/3123)

##### § 5712d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI, § 1172(b)](/us/pl/109/162/tXI/s1172/b), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3123](/us/stat/119/3123)

Section, [Pub. L. 93–415, title III, § 316](/us/pl/93/415/tIII/s316), as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title IV, § 40155](/us/pl/103/322/tIV/s40155), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1922](/us/stat/108/1922), related to grants for prevention of sexual abuse and exploitation.

A prior [section 316 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s316) was renumbered [section 313 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s313) and is classified to [section 11213 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s11213), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

Another prior [section 316 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s316) was renumbered [section 372 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s372) and is classified to [section 11272 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s11272), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

Another prior [section 316 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s316) was renumbered [section 382 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s382) and is classified to [section 11274 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s11274), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
