# § 4801. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 365](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s365), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3975](/us/stat/108/3975)

##### § 4801. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 365](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s365), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3975](/us/stat/108/3975)

Section, [Pub. L. 100–297, title III, § 3202](/us/pl/100/297/tIII/s3202), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 338](/us/stat/102/338), established Fund for the Improvement and Reform of Schools and Teaching.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 100–297, title III, § 3201](/us/pl/100/297/tIII/s3201), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 338](/us/stat/102/338), provided that part B of title III of [Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297) (enacting this chapter) could be cited as the “Fund for the Improvement and Reform of Schools and Teaching Act”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 365](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s365), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3975](/us/stat/108/3975).
