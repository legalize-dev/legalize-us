# § 10511. Grant authorization

##### § 10511. Grant authorization

The Attorney General may make funds available under this subchapter to States and units of local government, or combinations thereof, to carry out all or a substantial part of a program or project intended to develop or improve the capability to analyze deoxyribonucleic acid (referred to in this subchapter as “DNA”) in a forensic laboratory.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2401](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2401), as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXI, § 210302(c)(1)(C)](/us/pl/103/322/tXXI/s210302/c/1/C), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2066](/us/stat/108/2066).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3796kk of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796kk), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 2401 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s2401) was renumbered section 2601 and is classified to [section 10541 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10541).
