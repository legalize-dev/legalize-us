# § 1750. Definitions

##### § 1750. Definitions

As used in this subchapter, the terms “mortgage”, “first mortgage”, “mortgagee”, “mortgagor”, “maturity date”, and “State” shall have the same meaning as in [section 1707 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1707).
