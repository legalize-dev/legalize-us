# §§ 3411 to 3414. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 336(f)(1)](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s336/f/1), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1007](/us/stat/110/1007)

##### §§ 3411 to 3414. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 336(f)(1)](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s336/f/1), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1007](/us/stat/110/1007)

Section 3411, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1502](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1502), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1328](/us/stat/95/1328), set forth findings relating to special areas conservation.

Section 3412, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1503](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1503), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1329](/us/stat/95/1329), related to program formulation and implementation.

Section 3413, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1504](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1504), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1331](/us/stat/95/1331), related to designation of geographic area as special area.

Section 3414, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1505](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1505), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1331](/us/stat/95/1331), set forth limitations on contracts under program.
