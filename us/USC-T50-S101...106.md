# §§ 101 to 106. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### §§ 101 to 106. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section 101, acts [Apr. 20, 1918, ch. 59, § 1](/us/act/1918-04-20/ch59/s1), [40 Stat. 533](/us/stat/40/533); [Nov. 30, 1940, ch. 926](/us/act/1940-11-30/ch926), [54 Stat. 1220](/us/stat/54/1220); [Dec. 24, 1942, ch. 824](/us/act/1942-12-24/ch824), [56 Stat. 1087](/us/stat/56/1087); 1946 Proc. No. 2695, eff. July 4, 1946, 11 F.R. 7517, [60 Stat. 1352](/us/stat/60/1352), related to definition of war terms. See [section 2151 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s2151), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 102, [act Apr. 20, 1918, ch. 59, § 2](/us/act/1918-04-20/ch59/s2), [40 Stat. 534](/us/stat/40/534), related to destruction or injury of war material in time of war. See [section 2153 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s2153).

Section 103, [act Apr. 20, 1918, ch. 59, § 3](/us/act/1918-04-20/ch59/s3), [40 Stat. 534](/us/stat/40/534), related to making or causing to be made defective war material. See [section 2154 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s2154).

Section 104, [act Apr. 20, 1918, ch. 59, § 4](/us/act/1918-04-20/ch59/s4), as added [Nov. 30, 1940, ch. 926](/us/act/1940-11-30/ch926), [54 Stat. 1220](/us/stat/54/1220); amended [Aug. 21, 1941, ch. 388](/us/act/1941-08-21/ch388), [55 Stat. 655](/us/stat/55/655), related to definition of national-defense terms. See [section 2151 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s2151).

Section 105, [act Apr. 20, 1918, ch. 59, § 5](/us/act/1918-04-20/ch59/s5), as added [Nov. 30, 1940, ch. 926](/us/act/1940-11-30/ch926), [54 Stat. 1220](/us/stat/54/1220), related to destruction or injury of national-defense materials. See [section 2155 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s2155).

Section 106, [act Apr. 20, 1918, ch. 59, § 6](/us/act/1918-04-20/ch59/s6), as added [Nov. 30, 1940, ch. 926](/us/act/1940-11-30/ch926), [54 Stat. 1220](/us/stat/54/1220), related to making or causing to be made defective national-defense material. See [section 2156 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s2156).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal of sections 101 to 106 effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s1), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
