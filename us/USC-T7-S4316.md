# § 4316. Regulations

##### § 4316. Regulations

The Secretary may issue such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 97–98, title XVII, § 1717](/us/pl/97/98/tXVII/s1717), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1357](/us/stat/95/1357).)*
