# § 163. Omitted

##### § 163. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 423, § 7](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch423/s7), [43 Stat. 1108](/us/stat/43/1108), which required the Library of Congress Trust Fund Board to submit an annual report to Congress on moneys or securities received and held and operations, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance. See, also, page 10 of House Document No. 103–7.
