# § 307. Application

##### § 307. Application

Chapter 1 applies to actions and proceedings brought under this chapter to the extent chapter 1 is not in conflict with this chapter or the Inter-American Convention as ratified by the United States. This chapter applies to the extent that this chapter is not in conflict with chapter 4.

*(Added [Pub. L. 101–369, § 1](/us/pl/101/369/s1), Aug. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 449](/us/stat/104/449); amended [Pub. L. 117–90, § 2(b)(1)(C)](/us/pl/117/90/s2/b/1/C), Mar. 3, 2022, [136 Stat. 27](/us/stat/136/27).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–90](/us/pl/117/90) substituted “Application” for “Chapter 1; residual application” in section catchline and inserted at end “This chapter applies to the extent that this chapter is not in conflict with chapter 4.”
