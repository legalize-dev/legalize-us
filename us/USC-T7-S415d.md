# § 415d. Omitted

##### § 415d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 17, 1928, ch. 602, § 3](/us/act/1928-05-17/ch602/s3), [45 Stat. 594](/us/stat/45/594), related to rules and regulations for and deposit of receipts under former sections 415b to 415d of this title, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
