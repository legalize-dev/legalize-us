# § 891h. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 891h. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary $93,000,000 for the period of fiscal years 2025 through 2026 to carry out this subchapter and section 302 of the Fisheries Survey Vessel Authorization Act of 2000 (title III of [Public Law 106–450](/us/pl/106/450); [114 Stat. 1945](/us/stat/114/1945); [33 U.S.C. 891b](/us/usc/t33/s891b) note).

(b) Limitation on fleet modernization activities All NOAA fleet modernization, shipbuilding, and conversion shall be conducted in accordance with this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 102–567, title VI, § 610](/us/pl/102/567/tVI/s610), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4303](/us/stat/106/4303); [Pub. L. 119–60, div. H, title LXXXIV, § 8402(a)(5)](/us/pl/119/60/dH/tLXXXIV/s8402/a/5), Dec. 18, 2025, [139 Stat. 1910](/us/stat/139/1910).)*
