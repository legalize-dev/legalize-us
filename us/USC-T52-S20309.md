# § 20309. Effect on certain other laws

##### § 20309. Effect on certain other laws

The exercise of any right under this chapter shall not affect, for purposes of any Federal, State, or local tax, the residence or domicile of a person exercising such right.

*([Pub. L. 99–410, title I, § 106](/us/pl/99/410/tI/s106), Aug. 28, 1986, [100 Stat. 927](/us/stat/100/927).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 1973ff–5 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s1973ff–5), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
