# § 3707. Regulations

##### § 3707. Regulations

The Director of the Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe regulations for the administration of this chapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 107–347, title II, § 209(c)(1)](/us/pl/107/347/tII/s209/c/1), Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 2930](/us/stat/116/2930).)*
