# §§ 1708 to 1715. Omitted

##### §§ 1708 to 1715. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1512](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1512), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3633](/us/stat/104/3633).

Section 1708, [act July 10, 1954, ch. 469, title I, § 108](/us/act/1954-07-10/ch469/tI/s108), as added Dec. 23, 1985, [Pub. L. 99–198, title XI, § 1111(h)](/us/pl/99/198/tXI/s1111/h), [99 Stat. 1476](/us/stat/99/1476); amended Aug. 23, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–418, title II, § 2309](/us/pl/100/418/tII/s2309), [102 Stat. 1346](/us/stat/102/1346), related to agreements with financial intermediaries in developing countries for purchase of commodities.
