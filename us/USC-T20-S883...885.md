#### Codification

Section 883, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 803](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s803), formerly title VI, § 603, Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 57](/us/stat/79/57); renumbered title VII, § 703, and amended [Pub. L. 89–750, title I](/us/pl/89/750/tI), §§ 111(f), 161, Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1196](/us/stat/80/1196), 1204; renumbered title VIII, § 803, [Pub. L. 90–247, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/90/247/tVII/s702), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 816](/us/stat/81/816); amended [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 163](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s163), title IV, § 401(c)(2), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 152](/us/stat/84/152), 173, was comprised of subsecs. (a) to (c). Subsecs. (a) and (b) were repealed by [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV, § 401(c)(2)](/us/pl/91/230/tIV/s401/c/2), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173](/us/stat/84/173). Subsec. (c), which related to federal administration, was renumbered [Pub. L. 89–10, title X, § 1002](/us/pl/89/10/tX/s1002) by [Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII, § 801(1)](/us/pl/95/561/tVIII/s801/1), (2), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2284](/us/stat/92/2284), and transferred to [section 3382 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s3382), and was subsequently omitted in the general amendment of [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10) by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).
