#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 369, § 6(c)](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch369/s6/c) (part), formerly § 6(b), [42 Stat. 1002](/us/stat/42/1002), as amended and renumbered, which related to enforcement powers of Commission, was omitted in the general amendment of section 6(c) of act Sept. 21, 1922, by [Pub. L. 111–203, title VII, § 753(a)](/us/pl/111/203/tVII/s753/a), July 21, 2010, [124 Stat. 1750](/us/stat/124/1750). Section 6(c) is now classified to [section 9 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s9).
