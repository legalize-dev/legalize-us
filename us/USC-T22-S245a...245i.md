# §§ 245a to 245i. Repealed. Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 19](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s19), [54 Stat. 12](/us/stat/54/12)

##### §§ 245a to 245i. Repealed. Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 19](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s19), [54 Stat. 12](/us/stat/54/12)

Sections 245a to 245i, Joint Res. [Aug. 31, 1935, ch. 837](/us/act/1935-08-31/ch837), [49 Stat. 1081](/us/stat/49/1081); acts [Feb. 29, 1936, ch. 106](/us/act/1936-02-29/ch106), [49 Stat. 1153](/us/stat/49/1153); [May 1, 1937, ch. 146](/us/act/1937-05-01/ch146), [50 Stat. 121](/us/stat/50/121), constituted the Neutrality Act of 1935. See the Neutrality Act of 1939, sections 441, 444, 445, 447 to 451, and 453 to 457 of this title.
