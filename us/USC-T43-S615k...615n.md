# §§ 615k to 615n. Omitted

##### §§ 615k to 615n. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 615k, [act Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 981, § 1](/us/act/1956-08-06/ch981/s1), [70 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/70/1059), authorized construction, operation, and maintenance of Little Wood River Project, Idaho.

Section 615*l*, [act Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 981, § 2](/us/act/1956-08-06/ch981/s2), [70 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/70/1059), related to construction, operation, and maintenance of public recreational facilities in connection with this project.

Section 615m, [act Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 981, § 3](/us/act/1956-08-06/ch981/s3), [70 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/70/1059), related to preservation of fish and wildlife in connection with this project and the operation of this project in accordance with water rights.

Section 615n, [act Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 981, § 4](/us/act/1956-08-06/ch981/s4), [70 Stat. 1060](/us/stat/70/1060), related to authorization of appropriations for this project.
