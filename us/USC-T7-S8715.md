# § 8715. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–79, title I, § 1103(a)](/us/pl/113/79/tI/s1103/a), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 658](/us/stat/128/658)

##### § 8715. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–79, title I, § 1103(a)](/us/pl/113/79/tI/s1103/a), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 658](/us/stat/128/658)

Section, [Pub. L. 110–234, title I, § 1105](/us/pl/110/234/tI/s1105), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 945](/us/stat/122/945); [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title I, § 1105, June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 1673, related to the average crop revenue election program available during each of the 2009, 2010, 2011, and 2012 crop years.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Continued Application for 2013 Crop Year

[Pub. L. 113–79, title I, § 1103(b)](/us/pl/113/79/tI/s1103/b), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 658](/us/stat/128/658), provided that: “Section 1105 of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 ([7 U.S.C. 8715](/us/usc/t7/s8715)), as in effect on the day before the date of enactment of this Act [Feb. 7, 2014], shall continue to apply through the 2013 crop year with respect to all covered commodities (as defined in section 1001 of that Act ([7 U.S.C. 8702](/us/usc/t7/s8702))) and peanuts on a farm for which the irrevocable election under section 1105 of that Act was made before the date of enactment of this Act.”
