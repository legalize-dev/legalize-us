# § 10726. Rules and regulations

##### § 10726. Rules and regulations

The Director of the Bureau of Justice Assistance shall promulgate such rules and regulations as are necessary to carry out this subchapter, including rules and regulations for submitting and reviewing applications under [section 10725 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10725).
