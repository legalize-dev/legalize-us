Section, added [Pub. L. 112–239, div. A, title V, § 511(a)](/us/pl/112/239/dA/tV/s511/a), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 1717](/us/stat/126/1717), related to Assistants to the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff for National Guard matters and Reserve matters.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

Provisions similar to those formerly contained in this section were contained in [Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/105/85/dA/tIX/s901), Nov. 18, 1997, [111 Stat. 1853](/us/stat/111/1853), which was set out as a note under [section 155 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s155), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 112–239, § 511(c)](/us/pl/112/239/s511/c).
