# §§ 1901 to 1918. Transferred

##### §§ 1901 to 1918. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 1901, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 1](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s1), [48 Stat. 113](/us/stat/48/113), which related to establishment of United States Employment Service, was transferred to [section 49 of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49), Labor.

Section 1902, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 2](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s2), [48 Stat. 114](/us/stat/48/114); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 203, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424), which related to establishment of officers and employees of the Service, was transferred to [section 49a of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49a).

Section 1903, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 3](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s3), [48 Stat. 114](/us/stat/48/114), which related to promotion and development of national system of employment offices, was transferred to [section 49b of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49b).

Section 1904, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 4](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s4), [48 Stat. 114](/us/stat/48/114), which related to establishment of state agencies, was transferred to [section 49c of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49c).

Section 1905, acts [June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 5](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s5), [48 Stat. 114](/us/stat/48/114); [May 10, 1935, ch. 102](/us/act/1935-05-10/ch102), [49 Stat. 216](/us/stat/49/216); [June 29, 1938, ch. 816](/us/act/1938-06-29/ch816), [52 Stat. 1244](/us/stat/52/1244); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 203, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 4, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7873, [60 Stat. 1095](/us/stat/60/1095), which related to authorization of appropriations, was transferred to [section 49d of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49d).

Section 1906, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 6](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s6), [48 Stat. 115](/us/stat/48/115); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 203, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 4, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7873, [60 Stat. 1095](/us/stat/60/1095), which related to apportionment among States, was transferred to [section 49e of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49e).

Section 1907, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 7](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s7), [48 Stat. 115](/us/stat/48/115); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 203, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 4, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7873, [60 Stat. 1095](/us/stat/60/1095), which related to ascertainment of amount due to States and certification thereof to Secretary of the Treasury, was transferred to [section 49f of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49f).

Section 1908, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 8](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s8), [48 Stat. 115](/us/stat/48/115); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 203, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 4, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7873, [60 Stat. 1095](/us/stat/60/1095), which related to submission of State plans, was transferred to [section 49g of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49g).

Section 1909, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 9](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s9), [48 Stat. 116](/us/stat/48/116); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 203, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 4, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7873, [60 Stat. 1095](/us/stat/60/1095), which related to reports by State agencies, was transferred to [section 49h of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49h).

Section 1910, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 11](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s11), [48 Stat. 116](/us/stat/48/116); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 203, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 4, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7873, [60 Stat. 1095](/us/stat/60/1095), which related to establishment of a Federal Advisory Council, was transferred to [section 49j of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49j).

Section 1911, [act June 6, 1933, ch. 49, § 12](/us/act/1933-06-06/ch49/s12), [48 Stat. 117](/us/stat/48/117); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 203, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 4, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7873, [60 Stat. 1095](/us/stat/60/1095), which related to issuance of rules and regulations, was transferred to [section 49k of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49k).

Section 1912, [act Aug. 11, 1939, ch. 693](/us/act/1939-08-11/ch693), [53 Stat. 1409](/us/stat/53/1409), which related to transfer to States of property used by United States Employment Service, was transferred to [section 49c–1 of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49c–1).

Section 1913, acts [July 26, 1946, ch. 672](/us/act/1946-07-26/ch672), title I, § 101, [60 Stat. 684](/us/stat/60/684); [June 16, 1948, ch. 472](/us/act/1948-06-16/ch472), title I, § 101, [62 Stat. 446](/us/stat/62/446), which related to Federal employees employed in State and local employment service and their conditions of service, was transferred to [section 49c–2 of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49c–2) and subsequently omitted from the Code.

Section 1914, [act July 26, 1946, ch. 672, title I, § 101](/us/act/1946-07-26/ch672/tI/s101), [60 Stat. 685](/us/stat/60/685), which related to refund of contributions to Federal Retirement System, was transferred to [section 49c–3 of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49c–3) and was repealed.

Section 1915, acts [July 26, 1946, ch. 672](/us/act/1946-07-26/ch672), title I, § 101, [60 Stat. 685](/us/stat/60/685); [July 8, 1947, ch. 210](/us/act/1947-07-08/ch210), title I, § 101, [61 Stat. 263](/us/stat/61/263); [June 16, 1948, ch. 472](/us/act/1948-06-16/ch472), title I, § 101, [62 Stat. 446](/us/stat/62/446), which related to establishment and maintenance of personnel standards on merit basis, was transferred to [section 49c–4 of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49c–4) and subsequently omitted from the Code.

Section 1916, acts [July 26, 1946, ch. 672](/us/act/1946-07-26/ch672), title I, § 101, [60 Stat. 686](/us/stat/60/686); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 4, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7873, [60 Stat. 1095](/us/stat/60/1095); [July 8, 1947, ch. 210](/us/act/1947-07-08/ch210), title I, § 101, [61 Stat. 263](/us/stat/61/263); [June 16, 1948, ch. 472](/us/act/1948-06-16/ch472), title I, § 101, [62 Stat. 446](/us/stat/62/446), which related to joint budget for grants under this chapter and for certain grants under the Social Security Act, was transferred to [section 49c–5 of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49c–5) and subsequently omitted from the Code.

Section 1917, [act June 16, 1948, ch. 472, title I, § 101](/us/act/1948-06-16/ch472/tI/s101), [62 Stat. 445](/us/stat/62/445), which related to payment to States for administrative expenses, was transferred to [section 49m of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49m) and subsequently omitted from the Code.

Section 1918, [act June 16, 1948, ch. 472, title I, § 101](/us/act/1948-06-16/ch472/tI/s101), [62 Stat. 445](/us/stat/62/445), which related to personnel standards, was transferred to [section 49n of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s49n) and subsequently omitted from the Code.
