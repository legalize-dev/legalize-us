# § 1234i. Definitions

##### § 1234i. Definitions

(1) The term “recipient” means a recipient of a grant or cooperative agreement under an applicable program.

(2) The term “applicable program” excludes programs authorized by the Higher Education Act of 1965 [[20 U.S.C. 1001](/us/usc/t20/s1001) et seq.] and assistance programs provided under the Act of September 30, 1950 11 See References in Text note below. (Public Law 874, 81st Congress), and the Act of September 23, 1950 1 (Public Law 815, 81st Congress).

*([Pub. L. 90–247, title IV, § 460](/us/pl/90/247/tIV/s460), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title III, § 3501(a)](/us/pl/100/297/tIII/s3501/a), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 357](/us/stat/102/357).)*
