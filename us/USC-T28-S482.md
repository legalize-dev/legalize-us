# § 482. Definitions

##### § 482. Definitions

As used in this chapter, the term “judicial officer” means a United States district court judge or a United States magistrate judge.

*(Added [Pub. L. 101–650, title I, § 103(a)](/us/pl/101/650/tI/s103/a), title III, § 321, Dec. 1, 1990, [104 Stat. 5096](/us/stat/104/5096), 5117.)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Change of Name

“United States magistrate judge” substituted for “United States magistrate” in text pursuant to [section 321 of Pub. L. 101–650](/us/pl/101/650/s321), set out as a note under [section 631 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s631).
