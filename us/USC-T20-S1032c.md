# § 1032c. Evaluation

##### § 1032c. Evaluation

Not less than ten percent of the funds awarded to an eligible consortium to carry out a project under this subpart shall be used to evaluate the effectiveness of such project.

*([Pub. L. 89–329, title II, § 234](/us/pl/89/329/tII/s234), as added [Pub. L. 110–315, title II, § 201(3)](/us/pl/110/315/tII/s201/3), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3157](/us/stat/122/3157).)*
