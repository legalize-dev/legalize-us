##### § 587a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 19, 1934, ch. 648, title II, § 1](/us/act/1934-06-19/ch648/tII/s1), [48 Stat. 1056](/us/stat/48/1056), made the provisions of the Federal Employees Compensation Act applicable to enrollees under this subchapter and became obsolete with the expiration of the other sections of this subchapter.
