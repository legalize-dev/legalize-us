# § 500. Omitted

##### § 500. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 1](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s1), [50 Stat. 900](/us/stat/50/900), which declared purpose of this subchapter for establishment of a reindeer industry, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897), [50 Stat. 900](/us/stat/50/900), which enacted this subchapter, was popularly known as the “Reindeer Industry Act of 1937”.

#### Repeals

[Act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 17](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s17), [50 Stat. 902](/us/stat/50/902), which repealed provisions inconsistent with this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### Authorization of Appropriations

[Act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 16](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s16), [50 Stat. 902](/us/stat/50/902), which authorized the appropriation of $2,000,000 to carry out this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
