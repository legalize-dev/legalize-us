# § 484a. Reciprocal information sharing

##### § 484a. Reciprocal information sharing

Acting in accordance with a bilateral or multilateral arrangement, the Secretary, in the Secretary’s discretion and on the basis of reciprocity, may provide information from the National Sex Offender Registry relating to a conviction for a sex offense against a minor (as such terms are defined in [section 20911 of title 34](/us/usc/t34/s20911)) to a foreign government upon the request of the foreign government, and may receive comparable information from the foreign government.

*([Pub. L. 107–296, title VIII, § 895](/us/pl/107/296/tVIII/s895), as added [Pub. L. 117–347, title III, § 323(a)(1)(B)](/us/pl/117/347/tIII/s323/a/1/B), Jan. 5, 2023, [136 Stat. 6207](/us/stat/136/6207).)*
