# § 1475. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title XII, § 1205(c)](/us/pl/97/35/tXII/s1205/c), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 716](/us/stat/95/716)

##### § 1475. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title XII, § 1205(c)](/us/pl/97/35/tXII/s1205/c), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 716](/us/stat/95/716)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–601, § 6](/us/pl/91/601/s6), Dec. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1672](/us/stat/84/1672), provided for appointment of a technical advisory committee to assist the Secretary in carrying out the purposes of the Poison Prevention Packaging Act of 1970.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Aug. 13, 1981, see [section 1215 of Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35/s1215), set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under [section 2052 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s2052).
