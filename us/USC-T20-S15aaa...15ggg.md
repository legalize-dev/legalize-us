# §§ 15aaa to 15ggg. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–576, title I, § 103](/us/pl/90/576/tI/s103), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091](/us/stat/82/1091)

##### §§ 15aaa to 15ggg. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–576, title I, § 103](/us/pl/90/576/tI/s103), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091](/us/stat/82/1091)

Section 15aaa, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III, § 301](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII/s301), as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/85/864/tVIII/s802), [72 Stat. 1597](/us/stat/72/1597); amended Oct. 3, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–344, title II, § 207](/us/pl/87/344/tII/s207), [75 Stat. 760](/us/stat/75/760); Dec. 18, 1963, [Pub. L. 88–210, § 11(b)](/us/pl/88/210/s11/b), [77 Stat. 411](/us/stat/77/411), authorized an appropriation of $15,000,000 annually for area vocational education programs.

Section 15bbb, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III, § 302](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII/s302), as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/85/864/tVIII/s802), [72 Stat. 1598](/us/stat/72/1598), covered allotment of funds appropriated under [section 15aaa of this title](/us/usc/t20/s15aaa).

Section 15ccc, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III, § 303](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII/s303), as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/85/864/tVIII/s802), [72 Stat. 1598](/us/stat/72/1598), set out conditions which States had to fulfill to qualify for payments.

Section 15ddd, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III, § 304](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII/s304), as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/85/864/tVIII/s802), [72 Stat. 1599](/us/stat/72/1599), set out requirements of programs under sections 15aaa to 15ggg of this title.

Section 15eee, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III, § 305](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII/s305), as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/85/864/tVIII/s802), [72 Stat. 1599](/us/stat/72/1599), covered additional State plan requirements for eligibility under sections 15aaa to 15ggg of this title.

Section 15fff, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III, § 306](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII/s306), as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/85/864/tVIII/s802), [72 Stat. 1600](/us/stat/72/1600), authorized appropriations to administer sections 15aaa to 15ggg of this title.

Section 15ggg, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title III, § 307](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tIII/s307), as added Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–864, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/85/864/tVIII/s802), [72 Stat. 1600](/us/stat/72/1600); amended June 25, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–70, § 18(b)(3)](/us/pl/86/70/s18/b/3), [73 Stat. 144](/us/stat/73/144); July 12, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–624, § 14(b)(4)](/us/pl/86/624/s14/b/4), [74 Stat. 414](/us/stat/74/414), defined terms as used in sections 15aaa to 15ggg of this title.

For general subject matter of sections 15aaa to 15ggg, see section 1241 et seq. of this title.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 90–576, title I, § 103](/us/pl/90/576/tI/s103), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091](/us/stat/82/1091), provided that the repeal is effective July 1, 1969.
