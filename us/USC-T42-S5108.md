# § 5108. Monitoring and oversight

##### § 5108. Monitoring and oversight

(1) shall—

(A) be in addition to the review of the State plan upon its submission under [section 5106a(b)(1)(A) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5106a/b/1/A); and

(B) include monitoring of State policies and procedures required under clauses (ii) and (iii) of [section 5106a(b)(2)(B) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5106a/b/2/B); and

(2) may include—

(A) a comparison of activities carried out by the State to comply with the requirements of [section 5106a(b) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5106a/b) with the State plan most recently approved under [section 629b of this title](/us/usc/t42/s629b);

(B) a review of information available on the website of the State relating to its compliance with the requirements of [section 5106a(b) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5106a/b);

(C) site visits, as may be necessary to carry out such monitoring; and

(D) a review of information available in the State’s Annual Progress and Services Report most recently submitted under section 1357.16 of title 45, Code of Federal Regulations (or successor regulations).
