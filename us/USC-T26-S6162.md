# [§ 6162. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(a)(12)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1906/a/12), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1825](/us/stat/90/1825)]

##### [§ 6162. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(a)(12)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1906/a/12), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1825](/us/stat/90/1825)]

Section, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 763](/us/stat/68A/763), provided for an extension of time for payment of tax on gain attributable to liquidation of personal holding companies.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on first day of month which begins more than 90 days after Oct. 4, 1976, see [section 1906(d)(1) of Pub. L. 94–455](/us/pl/94/455/s1906/d/1), set out as an Effective Date of 1976 Amendment note under [section 6013 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s6013).
