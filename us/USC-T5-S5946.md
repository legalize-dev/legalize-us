# § 5946. Membership fees; expenses of attendance at meetings; limitations

##### § 5946. Membership fees; expenses of attendance at meetings; limitations

(1) membership fees or dues of an employee as defined by [section 2105 of this title](/us/usc/t5/s2105) or an individual employed by the government of the District of Columbia in a society or association; or

(2) expenses of attendance of an individual at meetings or conventions of members of a society or association.

*([Pub. L. 89–554](/us/pl/89/554), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 514](/us/stat/80/514).)*

The words “or by sections 4109 and 4110 of this title” are added on authority of former sections 2309 and 2318(b), which are carried into sections 4109 and 4110, respectively.

In the last sentence, the words “This section does not” are substituted for “That nothing contained in the Act making appropriations to provide for the expenses of the Government of the District of Columbia for the fiscal year ending June thirtieth, nineteen hundred and thirteen, and for other purposes, approved June twenty-sixth, nineteen hundred and twelve, shall be so construed as to” appearing in the Act of Mar. 4, 1913, [37 Stat. 854](/us/stat/37/854).

Standard changes are made to conform with the definitions applicable and the style of this title as outlined in the preface to the report.
