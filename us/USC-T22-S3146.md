# § 3146. Definitions

##### § 3146. Definitions

(1) the terms “foreign”, “direct investment”, “international investment”, “United States”, “business enterprise”, “foreign person”, and “United States person” have the meanings given those terms in [section 3102 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3102); and

(2) the term “foreign direct investment in the United States” means direct investment by foreign persons in any business enterprise that is a United States person.

*([Pub. L. 101–533, § 10](/us/pl/101/533/s10), Nov. 7, 1990, [104 Stat. 2351](/us/stat/104/2351).)*
