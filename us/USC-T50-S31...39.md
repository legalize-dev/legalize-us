# §§ 31 to 39. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### §§ 31 to 39. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section 31, acts [June 15, 1917, ch. 30](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30), title I, § 1, [40 Stat. 217](/us/stat/40/217); [Mar. 28, 1940, ch. 72](/us/act/1940-03-28/ch72), title I, § 1, [54 Stat. 79](/us/stat/54/79), related to unlawful obtaining or permitting to be obtained information affecting national defense. See [section 793 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s793), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 32, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title I, § 2](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tI/s2), [40 Stat. 218](/us/stat/40/218), related to unlawful disclosures affecting national defense. See [section 794 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s794).

Section 33, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title I, § 3](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tI/s3), [40 Stat. 219](/us/stat/40/219), related to seditious or disloyal acts or words in time of war. See [section 2388 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s2388). Section 33 was amended by [act May 16, 1918, ch. 75, § 1](/us/act/1918-05-16/ch75/s1), [40 Stat. 553](/us/stat/40/553), which was repealed and the original section reenacted by [act Mar. 3, 1921, ch. 136](/us/act/1921-03-03/ch136), [41 Stat. 1359](/us/stat/41/1359).

Section 34, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title I, § 4](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tI/s4), [40 Stat. 219](/us/stat/40/219), related to conspiracy to violate sections 32 and 33 of this title. See sections 794 and 2388 of Title 18.

Section 35, acts [June 15, 1917, ch. 30](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30), title I, § 5, [40 Stat. 219](/us/stat/40/219); [Mar. 28, 1940, ch. 72, § 2](/us/act/1940-03-28/ch72/s2), [54 Stat. 79](/us/stat/54/79), related to the harboring or concealing of violators of the law. See sections 792 and 2388 of Title 18.

Section 36, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title I, § 6](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tI/s6), [40 Stat. 219](/us/stat/40/219), related to designation by proclamation of prohibited areas. See [section 793 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s793).

Section 37, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title I, § 8](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tI/s8), [40 Stat. 219](/us/stat/40/219), related to places subject to provisions of sections 31 to 42 of this title. See [section 2388 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s2388).

Section 38, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title I, § 7](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tI/s7), [40 Stat. 219](/us/stat/40/219), related to jurisdiction of courts-martial and military commissions.

Section 39, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title XIII, § 2](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tXIII/s2), [40 Stat. 231](/us/stat/40/231); Proc. No. 2695, eff. July 4, 1946, 11 F.R. 7517, [60 Stat. 1352](/us/stat/60/1352), related to jurisdiction of Canal Zone courts over offenses on high seas. See [section 3241 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3241).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal of sections 31 to 39 effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s1), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
