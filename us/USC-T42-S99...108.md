# §§ 99 to 108. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

##### §§ 99 to 108. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

Section 99, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 12](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s12), as added [Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 836](/us/act/1901-03-03/ch836), [31 Stat. 1087](/us/stat/31/1087), authorized medical officers performing duties as quarantine officers to administer oaths. See [section 272 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s272).

Section 100, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 8](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s8), [27 Stat. 452](/us/stat/27/452), provided compensation for use of State buildings for quarantine purposes.

Section 101, [act June 19, 1906, ch. 3433, § 5](/us/act/1906-06-19/ch3433/s5), [34 Stat. 301](/us/stat/34/301), provided for acquisition of quarantine stations. See [section 267 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s267).

Section 102, [act Feb. 15, 1893, ch. 114, § 10](/us/act/1893-02-15/ch114/s10), as added [Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 836](/us/act/1901-03-03/ch836), [31 Stat. 1086](/us/stat/31/1086); amended [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712), provided for establishment of quarantine grounds. See sections 267 and 271 of this title.

Section 103, [act June 19, 1906, ch. 3433, § 1](/us/act/1906-06-19/ch3433/s1), [34 Stat. 299](/us/stat/34/299), provided for control and management of quarantine grounds. See [section 267 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s267).

Section 104, acts [June 19, 1906, ch. 3433, § 2](/us/act/1906-06-19/ch3433/s2), [34 Stat. 299](/us/stat/34/299); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for transferal of title of land and water from other departments to Service for its use.

Section 105, [act June 19, 1906, ch. 3433, § 3](/us/act/1906-06-19/ch3433/s3), [34 Stat. 300](/us/stat/34/300), provided for notice of selection of places for quarantine stations and anchorages.

Section 106, acts [Aug. 1, 1888, ch. 727](/us/act/1888-08-01/ch727), [25 Stat. 355](/us/stat/25/355); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided penalties for trespass on quarantine stations. See [section 271 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s271).

Section 107, [act Mar. 27, 1890, ch. 51, § 2](/us/act/1890-03-27/ch51/s2), [26 Stat. 31](/us/stat/26/31), provided penalties for the violation of quarantine laws by officers of Service. See [section 271 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s271).

Section 108, [act Mar. 27, 1890, ch. 51, § 3](/us/act/1890-03-27/ch51/s3), [26 Stat. 32](/us/stat/26/32), provided penalties for the violation of quarantine laws by common carriers. See [section 271 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s271).
