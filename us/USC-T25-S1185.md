# § 1185. Omitted

##### § 1185. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–508, § 5](/us/pl/90/508/s5), Sept. 21, 1968, [82 Stat. 862](/us/stat/82/862), which exempted funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
