# § 11. Interest in national banks

##### § 11. Interest in national banks

It shall not be lawful for the Comptroller or the Deputy Comptroller of the Currency, either directly or indirectly, to hold an interest in any national bank or any Federal savings association.

*(R.S. § 329; [Pub. L. 106–569, title XII, § 1233(b)](/us/pl/106/569/tXII/s1233/b), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 3037](/us/stat/114/3037); [Pub. L. 111–203, title III, § 314(c)](/us/pl/111/203/tIII/s314/c), July 21, 2010, [124 Stat. 1524](/us/stat/124/1524).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

R.S. § 329 derived from [act June 3, 1864, ch. 106, § 1](/us/act/1864-06-03/ch106/s1), [13 Stat. 99](/us/stat/13/99), which was the National Bank Act. See [section 38 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s38).

#### Amendments

2010—[Pub. L. 111–203](/us/pl/111/203) inserted “or any Federal savings association” before the period.

2000—[Pub. L. 106–569](/us/pl/106/569) substituted “to hold an interest in any national bank” for “to be interested in any association issuing national currency under the laws of the United States”.
