# § 2105. Placement

##### § 2105. Placement

The Law Revision Counsel is directed to place this Act as chapter 20 of title 48.

*([Pub. L. 114–187, § 6](/us/pl/114/187/s6), June 30, 2016, [130 Stat. 553](/us/stat/130/553).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 114–187](/us/pl/114/187), June 30, 2016, [130 Stat. 549](/us/stat/130/549), known as the Puerto Rico Oversight, Management, and Economic Stability Act and also as PROMESA, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 2101 of this title](/us/usc/t48/s2101) and Tables.
