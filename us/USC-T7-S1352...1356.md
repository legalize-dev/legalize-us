#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 97–98, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/97/98/tVI/s601), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1242](/us/stat/95/1242), provided that the repeal of sections 1352 to 1356 of this title is effective beginning with the 1982 crop of rice.
