# § 6537. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/A), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2775](/us/stat/133/2775)

##### § 6537. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/A), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2775](/us/stat/133/2775)

Section, [Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 105](/us/pl/94/59/tI/s105), July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 275](/us/stat/89/275), related to compensation of Assistants to Majority and Minority in Office of Secretary of Senate.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the later of the first day of the first applicable pay period beginning on or after Jan. 1, 2020, or the first day of the first applicable pay period beginning on or after Dec. 20, 2019, see [section 212(c) of Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94/s212/c), set out as a note under [section 282b of this title](/us/usc/t2/s282b).

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 105](/us/pl/94/59/tI/s105), July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 275](/us/stat/89/275), which provided that this section was effective July 1, 1975, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/A), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2775](/us/stat/133/2775).
