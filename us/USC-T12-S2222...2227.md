# §§ 2222 to 2227. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–399, title IV, § 409(d)](/us/pl/100/399/tIV/s409/d), Aug. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 1003](/us/stat/102/1003)

##### §§ 2222 to 2227. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–399, title IV, § 409(d)](/us/pl/100/399/tIV/s409/d), Aug. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 1003](/us/stat/102/1003)

Sections 2222 to 2227 were directed to be repealed by [Pub. L. 100–233, title IV, § 418(c)](/us/pl/100/233/tIV/s418/c), formerly § 415(c), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1653](/us/stat/101/1653), renumbered § 418(c), [Pub. L. 100–399, title IV, § 409(a)](/us/pl/100/399/tIV/s409/a), Aug. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 1003](/us/stat/102/1003), which was repealed by [section 409(c) of Pub. L. 100–399](/us/pl/100/399/s409/c), title IV, Aug. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 1003](/us/stat/102/1003).

[Section 409(c) of Pub. L. 100–399](/us/pl/100/399/s409/c) provided in part that [section 418(c) of Pub. L. 100–233](/us/pl/100/233/s418/c) is repealed and that this chapter shall be applied and administered, and the amendments by sections 430 and 802(u) of [Pub. L. 100–233](/us/pl/100/233) (amending sections 2226 and 2223, respectively, of this title) shall take effect, as if such section 418(c) had not been enacted.

Section 2222, [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).1, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 614](/us/stat/85/614); [Pub. L. 99–205, title II, § 205(g)(2)](/us/pl/99/205/tII/s205/g/2), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1707](/us/stat/99/1707), related to district boards of directors, membership, eligibility, and terms.

Section 2223, [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).2, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 614](/us/stat/85/614); [Pub. L. 96–592, title V, § 502](/us/pl/96/592/tV/s502), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3449](/us/stat/94/3449); [Pub. L. 99–205, title II, § 205(g)(3)](/us/pl/99/205/tII/s205/g/3), (4), title VI, § 607, Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1707](/us/stat/99/1707), 1712; [Pub. L. 100–233, title VIII, § 802(u)](/us/pl/100/233/tVIII/s802/u), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1712](/us/stat/101/1712), related to nomination and election of district directors.

Section 2224, [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).3, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 615](/us/stat/85/615), related to functions of district directors.

Section 2225, [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).4, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 615](/us/stat/85/615), related to district board officers.

Section 2226, [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).5, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 616](/us/stat/85/616); [Pub. L. 100–233, title IV, § 430](/us/pl/100/233/tIV/s430), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1658](/us/stat/101/1658), related to compensation of district boards.

Section 2227, [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).6, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 616](/us/stat/85/616); 1978 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 102, 43 F.R. 36037, [92 Stat. 3784](/us/stat/92/3784); [Pub. L. 99–205, title II, § 205(g)(5)](/us/pl/99/205/tII/s205/g/5), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1707](/us/stat/99/1707), related to powers of district farm credit board.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective immediately after amendments made by [section 401 of Pub. L. 100–233](/us/pl/100/233/s401), which were effective 6 months after Jan. 6, 1988, see [section 1001(b) of Pub. L. 100–399](/us/pl/100/399/s1001/b), set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 2002 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2002).
