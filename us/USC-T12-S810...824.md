# §§ 810 to 824. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 810 to 824. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 810, [act May 12, 1933, ch. 25, title II, § 29](/us/act/1933-05-12/ch25/tII/s29), [48 Stat. 46](/us/stat/48/46), prohibited making of loans or issuing of bonds after May 12, 1933, by joint-stock land banks.

Section 811, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374), provided for organization of joint-stock banks.

Section 812, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374), provided for individual liability of shareholders in joint-stock land banks.

Section 813, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374), covered powers, duties, and liabilities of joint-stock land banks.

Section 814, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374), placed a limit on amount of issue of bonds and prohibited transacting of unauthorized business.

Section 815, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, provided for minimum allowable capital stock for joint-stock land banks.

Section 816, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374), prohibited issuance of bonds before capital stock of joint-stock land banks was entirely paid up.

Section 817, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, set out required form for bonds issued by joint-stock land banks.

Section 818, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 16, [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374); [Mar. 4, 1931, ch. 518, § 1](/us/act/1931-03-04/ch518/s1), [46 Stat. 1548](/us/stat/46/1548), set out interest rates and restrictions on mortgage loans for joint-stock land banks.

Section 819, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374), set a limitation on interest rates.

Section 820, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374), prohibited unauthorized commissions or charges by joint-stock land banks.

Section 821, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16), [39 Stat. 374](/us/stat/39/374); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, authorized issuance of bonds by joint-stock land banks.

Section 822, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16) (par.), as added [May 29, 1920, ch. 215](/us/act/1920-05-29/ch215), [41 Stat. 691](/us/stat/41/691), and amended Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, provided for voluntary liquidation of joint-stock land banks.

Section 823, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16) (pars.), as added [May 29, 1920, ch. 215](/us/act/1920-05-29/ch215), [41 Stat. 691](/us/stat/41/691); amended [Mar. 4, 1925, ch. 524, § 5](/us/act/1925-03-04/ch524/s5), [43 Stat. 1263](/us/stat/43/1263); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for acquisition of assets of a liquidating joint-stock land bank.

Section 824, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 16](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s16) (par.), as added [Mar. 4, 1931, ch. 518, § 2](/us/act/1931-03-04/ch518/s2), [46 Stat. 1548](/us/stat/46/1548); amended Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, covered insolvency and receivership of joint-stock land banks.
