# § 876. Omitted

##### § 876. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–661](/us/pl/89/661), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 911](/us/stat/80/911), which authorized advances and expenditures from certain judgment funds and exempted distributions to members from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
