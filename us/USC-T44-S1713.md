# § 1713. Documents to be delivered to the Executive Mansion

##### § 1713. Documents to be delivered to the Executive Mansion

The Director of the Government Publishing Office shall deliver to the Executive Mansion two copies of each document, bill, and resolution as soon as printed and ready for distribution.

*([Pub. L. 90–620](/us/pl/90/620), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1281](/us/stat/82/1281); [Pub. L. 113–235, div. H, title I, § 1301(c)(1)](/us/pl/113/235/dH/tI/s1301/c/1), Dec. 16, 2014, [128 Stat. 2537](/us/stat/128/2537).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 80 ([Jan. 12, 1895, ch. 23, § 88](/us/act/1895-01-12/ch23/s88), [28 Stat. 622](/us/stat/28/622)).
