# § 7108. Rejection of applications

##### § 7108. Rejection of applications

An application for review on appeal shall not be entertained unless it is in conformity with this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1243](/us/stat/72/1243), § 4008; renumbered § 7108, [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV, § 402(b)(1)](/us/pl/102/40/tIV/s402/b/1), May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 238](/us/stat/105/238).)*
