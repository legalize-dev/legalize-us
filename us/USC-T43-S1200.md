# § 1200. Omitted

##### § 1200. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 6, 1954, ch. 463, § 3](/us/act/1954-07-06/ch463/s3), [68 Stat. 453](/us/stat/68/453), which related to determination of just compensation for property conveyed under former [section 1199(a) of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1199/a), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
