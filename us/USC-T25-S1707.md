# § 1707. Omitted

##### § 1707. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–395, § 8](/us/pl/95/395/s8), Sept. 30, 1978, [92 Stat. 816](/us/stat/92/816), which related to purchase and transfer of private settlement lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
