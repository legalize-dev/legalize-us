# § 150909. Statement required in audit report

##### § 150909. Statement required in audit report

The corporation shall include in the audit report statement required under [section 10101(b)(1)(B) of this title](/us/usc/t36/s10101/b/1/B) a schedule of all contracts requiring payments greater than $10,000 and all payments of compensation or fees at a rate greater than $10,000 a year.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1397](/us/stat/112/1397).)*

The word “salaries” is omitted as included in “compensation”.
