# § 714c. Omitted

##### § 714c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–481, § 5](/us/pl/98/481/s5), Oct. 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 2252](/us/stat/98/2252), which related to governance of tribe by an Interim Council, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
