# § 612c–5. Section 612c funds for purchase of fruits, vegetables, and nuts to support domestic nutrition assistance programs

##### § 612c–5. Section 612c funds for purchase of fruits, vegetables, and nuts to support domestic nutrition assistance programs

(a) Funding for additional purchases of fruits, vegetables, and nuts In addition to the purchases of fruits, vegetables, and nuts required by [section 612c–4 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s612c–4), the Secretary of Agriculture shall purchase fruits, vegetables, and nuts for the purpose of providing nutritious foods for use in domestic nutrition assistance programs, using, of the funds made available under [section 612c of this title](/us/usc/t7/s612c), the following amounts:

(1) $190,000,000 for fiscal year 2008.

(2) $193,000,000 for fiscal year 2009.

(3) $199,000,000 for fiscal year 2010.

(4) $203,000,000 for fiscal year 2011.

(5) $206,000,000 for fiscal year 2012 and each fiscal year thereafter.

(b) Form of purchases Fruits, vegetables, and nuts may be purchased under this section in the form of frozen, canned, dried, or fresh fruits, vegetables, and nuts.

*([Pub. L. 110–234, title IV, § 4404](/us/pl/110/234/tIV/s4404), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1137](/us/stat/122/1137); [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title IV, § 4404, June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 1899.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) and [Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246) enacted identical sections. [Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a).

Section is comprised of [section 4404 of Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246/s4404). Subsec. (c) of [section 4404 of Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246/s4404) amended [section 612c–4 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s612c–4).

Section was enacted as part of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008, and not as part of the Agricultural Adjustment Act which comprises this chapter.
