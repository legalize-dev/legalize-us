# §§ 1171 to 1195. Transferred

##### §§ 1171 to 1195. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 1171, R.S. § 1689, which related to general application of provisions to consular officers, was transferred to [section 4191 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4191).

Section 1172, R.S. § 4082, which related to solemnization of marriages, was transferred to [section 4192 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4192) and subsequently repealed.

Section 1173, R.S. § 1707; [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), which related to receipt by consuls and vice consuls of protests, was transferred to [section 4193 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4193).

Section 1174, R.S. § 1708; [Feb. 14, 1903, ch. 552, § 10](/us/act/1903-02-14/ch552/s10), [32 Stat. 829](/us/stat/32/829), which related to lists and returns of seamen and vessels, etc., was transferred to [section 4194 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4194).

Section 1175, R.S. § 1709; [Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 223](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch223), [36 Stat. 1083](/us/stat/36/1083); [June 10, 1921, ch. 18, § 304](/us/act/1921-06-10/ch18/s304), [42 Stat. 24](/us/stat/42/24); [July 12, 1940, ch. 618](/us/act/1940-07-12/ch618), [54 Stat. 758](/us/stat/54/758), which related to estates of decedents generally and to General Accounting Office as conservator, was transferred to [section 4195 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4195) and subsequently repealed.

Section 1176, R.S. § 1710; [July 12, 1940, ch. 618](/us/act/1940-07-12/ch618), [54 Stat. 760](/us/stat/54/760), which related to notification of death of decedent and transmission of inventory of effects of decedent, was transferred to [section 4196 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4196).

Section 1177, R.S. § 1711; [July 12, 1940, ch. 618](/us/act/1940-07-12/ch618), [54 Stat. 760](/us/stat/54/760), which related to a consular or diplomatic officer following testamentary directions and assistance to a testamentary appointee, was transferred to [section 4197 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4197).

Section 1178, [act June 30, 1902, ch. 1331, § 1](/us/act/1902-06-30/ch1331/s1), [32 Stat. 546](/us/stat/32/546), which related to execution of a bond as administrator or guardian and to an action on bond, was transferred to [section 4198 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4198).

Section 1179, [act June 30, 1902, ch. 1331, § 2](/us/act/1902-06-30/ch1331/s2), [32 Stat. 547](/us/stat/32/547), which related to penalty for failure to give bond and for embezzlement, was transferred to [section 4199 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4199).

Section 1180, R.S. § 1715, which related to certification by consular officers of invoices generally, was transferred to [section 4200 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4200).

Section 1181, [act Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 9](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s9), [34 Stat. 101](/us/stat/34/101), which related to fees for certification of invoices, was transferred to [section 4201 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4201).

Section 1182, R.S. § 1716, which related to exaction of excessive fees for verification of invoices and penalty therefor, was transferred to [section 4202 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4202).

Section 1183, [act Feb. 24, 1903, ch. 753](/us/act/1903-02-24/ch753), [32 Stat. 854](/us/stat/32/854), which related to destruction of old invoices, was transferred to [section 4203 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4203).

Section 1184, R.S. § 1717, which provided for a restriction as to a certificate for goods from countries adjacent to United States, was transferred to [section 4204 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4204).

Section 1185, R.S. § 1718, which related to retention of papers of American vessels until payment of demands and wages, was transferred to [section 4205 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4205).

Section 1186, [act June 26, 1884, ch. 121, § 12](/us/act/1884-06-26/ch121/s12), [23 Stat. 56](/us/stat/23/56), which related to prohibition of fees for services to American vessels or seamen, was transferred to [section 4206 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4206).

Section 1187, R.S. § 1719; [Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 3](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s3), [34 Stat. 100](/us/stat/34/100), which related to prohibition of profits from dealings with discharged seamen, was transferred to [section 4207 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4207).

Section 1188, R.S. § 1722; [Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 3](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s3), [34 Stat. 100](/us/stat/34/100), which related to valuation of foreign coins in payment of fees, was transferred to [section 4208 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4208).

Section 1189, R.S. § 1723, which related to exaction of excessive fees and a penalty therefor of treble amount, was transferred to [section 4209 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4209).

Section 1190, R.S. § 1724; [Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 3](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s3), [34 Stat. 100](/us/stat/34/100), which related to liability for uncollected fees, was transferred to [section 4210 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4210).

Section 1191, R.S. § 1725; [July 31, 1894, ch. 174, § 5](/us/act/1894-07-31/ch174/s5), [28 Stat. 206](/us/stat/28/206); [Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 3](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s3), [34 Stat. 100](/us/stat/34/100); [June 10, 1921, ch. 18, § 304](/us/act/1921-06-10/ch18/s304), [42 Stat. 24](/us/stat/42/24), which related to returns as to fees by officers compensated by fees, was transferred to [section 4211 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4211).

Section 1192, R.S. §§ 1726, 1727, which related to receipts for fees and numbering of receipts, was transferred to [section 4212 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4212).

Section 1193, R.S. § 1727, which related to registry of fees, was transferred to [section 4213 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4213).

Section 1194, R.S. § 1728; [June 28, 1955, ch. 196](/us/act/1955-06-28/ch196), [69 Stat. 187](/us/stat/69/187), which related to accounting of fees and certification of a transcript of register, was transferred to [section 4214 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4214).

Section 1195, [act Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 7](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s7), [34 Stat. 101](/us/stat/34/101), which related to notarial acts, oaths, affirmations, affidavits, and depositions and fees therefor, was transferred to [section 4215 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4215).
