# § 3797q. Transferred

##### § 3797q. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 3797q was editorially reclassified as [section 10581 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10581), Crime Control and Law Enforcement, which was repealed by [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(g)(2)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s504/g/2), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5234](/us/stat/132/5234).

#### Prior Provisions

Prior sections 3797q–1 to 3797q–6 were repealed by [Pub. L. 114–255, div. B, title XIV, § 14013](/us/pl/114/255/dB/tXIV/s14013), Dec. 13, 2016, [130 Stat. 1298](/us/stat/130/1298).

Section 3797q–1, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2902](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2902), as added [Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 112(a)](/us/pl/110/199/tI/s112/a), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 672](/us/stat/122/672), related to use of grant funds.

Section 3797q–2, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2903](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2903), as added [Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 112(a)](/us/pl/110/199/tI/s112/a), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 673](/us/stat/122/673), related to grant applications.

Section 3797q–4, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2905](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2905), as added [Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 112(a)](/us/pl/110/199/tI/s112/a), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 673](/us/stat/122/673), related to geographic distribution of grants.
