# §§ 1381 to 1398. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569](/us/stat/72/1569)

##### §§ 1381 to 1398. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569](/us/stat/72/1569)

Section 1381, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 601](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s601), [68 Stat. 1183](/us/stat/68/1183), defined terms used in sections 1381 to 1399 of this title. See [section 3731 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3731), Coast Guard.

Section 1382, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 602](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s602), [68 Stat. 1183](/us/stat/68/1183), related to applicability of sections 1381 to 1399 of this title. See [section 3732 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3732).

Section 1383, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 603](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s603), [68 Stat. 1184](/us/stat/68/1184), related to authorized numbers and distribution of officers in the Coast Guard Reserve. See [section 3735 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3735).

Section 1384, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 604](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s604), [68 Stat. 1184](/us/stat/68/1184), related to promotions and selection boards. See [section 3740 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3740).

Section 1385, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 605](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s605), [68 Stat. 1185](/us/stat/68/1185), provided for precedence. See [section 3736 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3736).

Section 1386, acts [Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257), title VI, § 606, [68 Stat. 1185](/us/stat/68/1185); [June 30, 1955, ch. 247, § 5(a)](/us/act/1955-06-30/ch247/s5/a), [69 Stat. 221](/us/stat/69/221), related to running mates. See [section 3737 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3737).

Section 1387, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 607](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s607), [68 Stat. 1186](/us/stat/68/1186), related to promotion zones. See [section 3742 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3742).

Section 1388, acts [Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257), title VI, § 608, [68 Stat. 1186](/us/stat/68/1186); [June 30, 1955, ch. 247, § 5(b)](/us/act/1955-06-30/ch247/s5/b), [69 Stat. 221](/us/stat/69/221), related to date of rank upon promotion. See [section 3747 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3747).

Section 1389, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 609](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s609), [68 Stat. 1186](/us/stat/68/1186), related to minimum points for consideration for promotion.

Section 1390, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 610](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s610), [68 Stat. 1186](/us/stat/68/1186), related to qualifications for promotion. See [section 3745 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3745).

Section 1391, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 611](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s611), [68 Stat. 1186](/us/stat/68/1186), related to failure of selection and elimination. See [section 3751 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3751).

Section 1392, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 612](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s612), [68 Stat. 1187](/us/stat/68/1187), provided for removal from promotion list by the President. See [section 3749 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3749).

Section 1393, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 613](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s613), [68 Stat. 1187](/us/stat/68/1187), related to maximum ages for active status. See [section 3753 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3753).

Section 1394, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 614](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s614), [68 Stat. 1187](/us/stat/68/1187), related to type of promotion. See [section 3748 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3748).

Section 1395, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 615](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s615), [68 Stat. 1188](/us/stat/68/1188), related to promotion of officers serving on active duty. See [section 3739 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3739).

Section 1396, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 616](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s616), [68 Stat. 1188](/us/stat/68/1188), provided for appointment of former Navy and Coast Guard officers. See [section 3755 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3755).

Section 1397, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 617](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s617), [68 Stat. 1188](/us/stat/68/1188), provided for recall of retired officers. See [section 3757 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s3757).

Section 1398, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title VI, § 618](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tVI/s618), [68 Stat. 1188](/us/stat/68/1188), authorized the Secretary to promulgate regulations.
