# § 2716. Budget justification document for funding relating to perfluoroalkyl substances and polyfluoroalkyl substances

##### § 2716. Budget justification document for funding relating to perfluoroalkyl substances and polyfluoroalkyl substances

(1) research and development efforts;

(2) testing;

(3) remediation;

(4) contaminant disposal; and

(5) community outreach.
