# § 1300i–10. Omitted

##### § 1300i–10. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–580, § 11](/us/pl/100/580/s11), Oct. 31, 1988, [102 Stat. 2935](/us/stat/102/2935), which set out special considerations related to certain tribal lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
