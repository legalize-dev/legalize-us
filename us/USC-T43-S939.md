# § 939. Alteration, amendment, or repeal

##### § 939. Alteration, amendment, or repeal

Congress reserves the right at any time to alter, amend, or repeal sections 934 to 939 of this title, or any part thereof.

*([Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 152, § 6](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch152/s6), [18 Stat. 483](/us/stat/18/483).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Repeal; Savings Provision

L. 94–579, title VII, § 706(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s706/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2793](/us/stat/90/2793), effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976, insofar as applicable to the issuance of rights-of-way over, upon, under, and through the public lands and lands in the National Forest System.
