# § 32. Application

##### § 32. Application

The provisions of this chapter shall be held to apply only to cables to which the convention for the time being applies.

*([Feb. 29, 1888, ch. 17, § 12](/us/act/1888-02-29/ch17/s12), [25 Stat. 42](/us/stat/25/42).)*
