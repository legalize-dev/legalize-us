# § 193. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–826, § 3](/us/pl/87/826/s3), Oct. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 953](/us/stat/76/953)

##### § 193. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–826, § 3](/us/pl/87/826/s3), Oct. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 953](/us/stat/76/953)

Section, [act Jan. 5, 1923, ch. 23, § 2](/us/act/1923-01-05/ch23/s2), [42 Stat. 1110](/us/stat/42/1110), related to statements and reports for collectors of customs and Treasury Department. See section 301 et seq. of Title 13, Census.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 180 days after Oct. 15, 1962, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 87–826](/us/pl/87/826/s4), set out as a note under [section 301 of Title 13](/us/usc/t13/s301), Census.

#### Savings Provision

See [section 3 of Pub. L. 87–826](/us/pl/87/826/s3), set out as a note under sections 173 and 174 of this title.
