# §§ 1172 to 1174. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–374, title VI, § 601(c)(2)](/us/pl/96/374/tVI/s601/c/2), Oct. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 1472](/us/stat/94/1472)

##### §§ 1172 to 1174. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–374, title VI, § 601(c)(2)](/us/pl/96/374/tVI/s601/c/2), Oct. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 1472](/us/stat/94/1472)

Section 1172, [Pub. L. 89–698, title I, § 101](/us/pl/89/698/tI/s101), Oct. 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 1066](/us/stat/80/1066), made provision for a program of international studies and research involving operation of centers for advanced international studies. See [section 1123 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1123).

Section 1173, [Pub. L. 89–698, title I, § 102](/us/pl/89/698/tI/s102), Oct. 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 1067](/us/stat/80/1067), provided for undergraduate programs in international studies. See [section 1124 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1124).

Section 1174, [Pub. L. 89–698, title I, § 103](/us/pl/89/698/tI/s103), Oct. 29, 1966, [80 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/80/1068); [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV, § 401(c)(7)](/us/pl/91/230/tIV/s401/c/7), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173](/us/stat/84/173), authorized payment in installments and in advance or by way of reimbursement.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Sections repealed effective Oct. 1, 1980, see [section 1393(a) of Pub. L. 96–374](/us/pl/96/374/s1393/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 1001 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1001).
