# § 5411. Definitions

##### § 5411. Definitions

(1) Board The term “Board” means the Board of Directors of the Foundation.

(2) Bureau The term “Bureau” means the Office of Justice Services of the Bureau of Indian Affairs.

(3) Committee The term “Committee” means the Committee for the Establishment of the Indian Law Enforcement Foundation established under [section 5412(e)(1) of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5412/e/1).

(4) Foundation The term “Foundation” means the Indian Law Enforcement Foundation established under [section 5412 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5412).

(5) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

*([Pub. L. 93–638, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/93/638/tVII/s701), as added [Pub. L. 111–211, title II, § 231(c)](/us/pl/111/211/tII/s231/c), July 29, 2010, [124 Stat. 2274](/us/stat/124/2274).)*
