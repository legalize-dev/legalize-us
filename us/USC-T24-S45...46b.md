# §§ 45 to 46b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title XV, § 1532(b)(1)](/us/pl/101/510/dA/tXV/s1532/b/1), (2), (5), (6), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1733](/us/stat/104/1733)

##### §§ 45 to 46b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title XV, § 1532(b)(1)](/us/pl/101/510/dA/tXV/s1532/b/1), (2), (5), (6), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1733](/us/stat/104/1733)

Section 45, R.S. § 4819; [June 12, 1906, ch. 3078](/us/act/1906-06-12/ch3078), [34 Stat. 242](/us/stat/34/242); [May 11, 1908, ch. 163](/us/act/1908-05-11/ch163), [35 Stat. 110](/us/stat/35/110); Nov. 29, 1989, [Pub. L. 101–189, div. A, title III, § 347(1)](/us/pl/101/189/dA/tIII/s347/1), [103 Stat. 1422](/us/stat/103/1422), authorized commissioners to receive donations for benefit of Soldiers’ Home.

Section 46, acts [Mar. 3, 1883, ch. 130, § 8](/us/act/1883-03-03/ch130/s8), [22 Stat. 565](/us/stat/22/565); [July 26, 1947, ch. 343](/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343), title II, § 205(a), [61 Stat. 501](/us/stat/61/501); Dec. 15, 1973, [Pub. L. 93–185](/us/pl/93/185), [87 Stat. 712](/us/stat/87/712), related to investment and withdrawal of funds of Soldiers’ Home.

Section 46a, [act Apr. 9, 1935, ch. 54, title II](/us/act/1935-04-09/ch54/tII), [49 Stat. 147](/us/stat/49/147), related to deposit and expenditure of interest earned on funds of Soldiers’ Home.

Section 46b, [act July 19, 1937, ch. 511](/us/act/1937-07-19/ch511), [50 Stat. 519](/us/stat/50/519), related to laws governing administration of funds appropriated from Soldiers’ Home Permanent Fund.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective one year after Nov. 5, 1990, see [section 1541(a) of Pub. L. 101–510](/us/pl/101/510/s1541/a), formerly set out as an Effective Date note under [section 401 of this title](/us/usc/t24/s401).
