# § 351. Establishment

##### § 351. Establishment

There is hereby established a commission to be known as the Citizens’ Commission on Public Service and Compensation (hereinafter referred to as the “Commission”).

*([Pub. L. 90–206, title II, § 225(a)](/us/pl/90/206/tII/s225/a), Dec. 16, 1967, [81 Stat. 642](/us/stat/81/642); [Pub. L. 101–194, title VII, § 701(a)](/us/pl/101/194/tVII/s701/a), Nov. 30, 1989, [103 Stat. 1763](/us/stat/103/1763).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1989—[Pub. L. 101–194](/us/pl/101/194) substituted “Citizens’ Commission on Public Service and Compensation” for “Commission on Executive, Legislative, and Judicial Salaries”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective Dec. 16, 1967, see [section 220(a)(1) of Pub. L. 90–206](/us/pl/90/206/s220/a/1), set out as a note under [section 3110 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s3110), Government Organization and Employees.
