# [§ 218. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–600, title I, § 113(a)(1)](/us/pl/95/600/tI/s113/a/1), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2778](/us/stat/92/2778)]

##### [§ 218. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–600, title I, § 113(a)(1)](/us/pl/95/600/tI/s113/a/1), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2778](/us/stat/92/2778)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 92–178, title VII, § 702(a)](/us/pl/92/178/tVII/s702/a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 561](/us/stat/85/561); amended [Pub. L. 93–625](/us/pl/93/625), §§ 11(d), 12(b), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2120](/us/stat/88/2120); [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1906/b/13/A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/90/1834), related to contributions to candidates for public office.

A prior section 218 was renumbered [section 226 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s226).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective with respect to contributions the payment of which is made after Dec. 31, 1978, in taxable years beginning after such date, see [section 113(d) of Pub. L. 95–600](/us/pl/95/600/s113/d), set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 24 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s24).
