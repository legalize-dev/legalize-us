# § 1757a. Omitted

##### § 1757a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 110–228, § 1](/us/pl/110/228/s1), May 8, 2008, [122 Stat. 753](/us/stat/122/753), which related to extensions of leases of certain land by the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
