# § 7614b. Review of plan of work requirements

##### § 7614b. Review of plan of work requirements

(a) Review The Secretary shall work with university partners in extension and research to review and identify measures to streamline the submission, reporting under, and implementation of plan of work requirements, including those requirements under—

(1) sections 3221(d) and 3222(c) of this title;

(2) [section 361g of this title](/us/usc/t7/s361g); and

(3) [section 344 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s344).

(b) Consultation In carrying out the review and formulating and compiling the recommendations, the Secretary shall consult with the land-grant institutions.

*([Pub. L. 110–234, title VII, § 7505](/us/pl/110/234/tVII/s7505), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1258](/us/stat/122/1258); [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title VII, § 7505, June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 2020.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) and [Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246) enacted identical sections. [Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a).

Section was enacted as part of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008, and not as part of the Agricultural Research, Extension, and Education Reform Act of 1998, which in part comprises this chapter.
