# §§ 1131 to 1134. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

##### §§ 1131 to 1134. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

Section 1131, acts [Jan. 18, 1897, ch. 62, § 1](/us/act/1897-01-18/ch62/s1), [29 Stat. 490](/us/stat/29/490); [June 23, 1897, ch. 8](/us/act/1897-06-23/ch8), [30 Stat. 105](/us/stat/30/105); [Mar. 1, 1899, ch. 328](/us/act/1899-03-01/ch328), [30 Stat. 966](/us/stat/30/966), related to homestead settlers on lands in Greer County.

Section 1132, [act Jan. 18, 1897, ch. 62, § 2](/us/act/1897-01-18/ch62/s2), [29 Stat. 490](/us/stat/29/490), related to laws applicable to entries of unoccupied lands.

Section 1133, [act Jan. 18, 1897, ch. 62, § 3](/us/act/1897-01-18/ch62/s3), [29 Stat. 490](/us/stat/29/490), related to laws applicable to entries of town-sites.

Section 1134, [act Jan. 18, 1897, ch. 62, § 7](/us/act/1897-01-18/ch62/s7), [29 Stat. 491](/us/stat/29/491), authorized applicability of sections 1131 to 1134 of this title to Greer County, Oklahoma, and repeal of inconsistent provisions.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789), provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

#### Savings Provision

Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579) not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579/s701), set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1701).
