# § 433m. Authorization of appropriation

##### § 433m. Authorization of appropriation

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of sections 433k and 433*l* of this title.

*([June 29, 1936, ch. 863, § 4](/us/act/1936-06-29/ch863/s4), [49 Stat. 2029](/us/stat/49/2029).)*
