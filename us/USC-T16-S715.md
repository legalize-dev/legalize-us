# § 715. Short title

##### § 715. Short title

This subchapter shall be known by the short title of “Migratory Bird Conservation Act.”

*([Feb. 18, 1929, ch. 257, § 1](/us/act/1929-02-18/ch257/s1), [45 Stat. 1222](/us/stat/45/1222).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of 1976 Amendment

[Pub. L. 94–215, § 1](/us/pl/94/215/s1), Feb 17, 1976, [90 Stat. 189](/us/stat/90/189), provided: “That this Act [amending sections 668dd, 715a, 715k–3, 715k–5, 718a, 718b, and 718d of this title] may be cited as the ‘Wetlands Loan Extension Act of 1976’.”
