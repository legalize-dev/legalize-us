# § 4362. Application to Canada and Mexico

##### § 4362. Application to Canada and Mexico

Pursuant to article 10.10 of the USMCA (as defined in [section 4502 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4502)) and [section 4588 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4588), this subchapter and the amendments made by this title 11 See References in Text note below. shall apply with respect to goods from Canada and Mexico.

*([Pub. L. 114–125, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/114/125/tIV/s403), Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 156](/us/stat/130/156); [Pub. L. 116–260, div. O, title VI, § 602(c)](/us/pl/116/260/dO/tVI/s602/c), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2153](/us/stat/134/2153).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title IV of [Pub. L. 114–125](/us/pl/114/125), Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 155](/us/stat/130/155), which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title IV to the Code, see [section 401 of Pub. L. 114–125](/us/pl/114/125/s401), set out as a Short Title note under [section 4301 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4301) and Tables.

The amendments made by this title, referred to in text, mean the amendments made by title IV of [Pub. L. 114–125](/us/pl/114/125), Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 155](/us/stat/130/155). For complete classification of title IV to the Code, see [section 401 of Pub. L. 114–125](/us/pl/114/125/s401), set out as a Short Title note under [section 4301 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4301) and Tables.

#### Amendments

2020—[Pub. L. 116–260](/us/pl/116/260) substituted “article 10.10 of the USMCA (as defined in [section 4502 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4502)) and [section 4588 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4588)” for “article 1902 of the North American Free Trade Agreement and [section 3438 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s3438)”.
