# § 278b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–426, title VII, § 701(b)](/us/pl/95/426/tVII/s701/b), Oct. 7, 1978, [92 Stat. 991](/us/stat/92/991)

##### § 278b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–426, title VII, § 701(b)](/us/pl/95/426/tVII/s701/b), Oct. 7, 1978, [92 Stat. 991](/us/stat/92/991)

Section, [act June 28, 1944, ch. 294, title I](/us/act/1944-06-28/ch294/tI), [58 Stat. 402](/us/stat/58/402), related to a report to Congress of the operation and work of the laboratory, including the statement of the receipts and expenditures, such a report to cover a fiscal year period ending on June 30 of the calendar year immediately preceding the convening of each such session. Similar provisions were contained in the following prior acts:

[July 1, 1943, ch. 182](/us/act/1943-07-01/ch182), title I, [57 Stat. 277](/us/stat/57/277).

[July 2, 1942, ch. 472](/us/act/1942-07-02/ch472), title I, [56 Stat. 474](/us/stat/56/474).

[June 28, 1941, ch. 258](/us/act/1941-06-28/ch258), title I, [55 Stat. 271](/us/stat/55/271).

[May 14, 1940, ch. 189](/us/act/1940-05-14/ch189), title I, [54 Stat. 187](/us/stat/54/187).

[June 29, 1939, ch. 248](/us/act/1939-06-29/ch248), [53 Stat. 891](/us/stat/53/891).

[Apr. 27, 1938, ch. 180](/us/act/1938-04-27/ch180), title I, [52 Stat. 253](/us/stat/52/253).

[June 16, 1937, ch. 359](/us/act/1937-06-16/ch359), title I, [50 Stat. 267](/us/stat/50/267).
