# [§ 8684a. Omitted]

##### [§ 8684a. Omitted]

#### Codification

Section, added and amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1876(a)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1876/a), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4291](/us/stat/134/4291), related to rate for progress payments in the repair, maintenance, or overhaul of naval vessels, and was to become effective Jan. 1, 2022. [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XVII, § 1701(n)(3)](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tXVII/s1701/n/3), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 2146](/us/stat/135/2146), repealed [section 1876 of Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283/s1876), effective as if included in title XVIII of [Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283), thereby omitting this section before it took effect. Text of [section 2307(g)(1) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s2307/g/1), which had been transferred to this section, was transferred to [section 3808(a) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s3808/a).
