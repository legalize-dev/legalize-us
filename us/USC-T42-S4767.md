# § 4767. Termination of grants

##### § 4767. Termination of grants

(1) that a program or project has been so changed that it no longer complies with the provisions of this chapter; or

(2) that in the operation of the program or project there is a failure to comply substantially with any such provision;

*([Pub. L. 91–648, title V, § 507](/us/pl/91/648/tV/s507), Jan. 5, 1971, [84 Stat. 1928](/us/stat/84/1928); 1978 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 102, eff. Jan. 1, 1979, 43 F.R. 36037, [92 Stat. 3783](/us/stat/92/3783).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, means the provisions of subchapters I, II, III, and IV of this chapter. See [section 4761 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4761).

#### **Executive Documents**
