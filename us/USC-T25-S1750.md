# § 1750. Omitted

##### § 1750. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 105–83, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/105/83/tVII/s702), Nov. 14, 1997, [111 Stat. 1624](/us/stat/111/1624), which set out congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 105–83, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/105/83/tVII/s701), Nov. 14, 1997, [111 Stat. 1624](/us/stat/111/1624), provided that title VII of [Pub. L. 105–83](/us/pl/105/83), enacting this part, could be cited as the “Miccosukee Settlement Act of 1997”.
