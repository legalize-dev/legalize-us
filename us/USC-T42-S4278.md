# § 4278. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 4278. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 86–380, § 8](/us/pl/86/380/s8), Sept. 24, 1959, [73 Stat. 706](/us/stat/73/706).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 2378 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s2378) prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).
