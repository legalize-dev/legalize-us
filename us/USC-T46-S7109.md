# § 7109. Review of criminal records

##### § 7109. Review of criminal records

The Secretary may review the criminal record of each holder of a license or certificate of registry issued under this part who applies for renewal of that license or certificate of registry.

*([Pub. L. 98–89](/us/pl/98/89), Aug. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 540](/us/stat/97/540); [Pub. L. 101–380, title IV, § 4102(e)(1)](/us/pl/101/380/tIV/s4102/e/1), Aug. 18, 1990, [104 Stat. 510](/us/stat/104/510).)*

Section 7109 authorizes the Secretary to renew licenses and certificates of registry for additional 5 year periods.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1990—[Pub. L. 101–380](/us/pl/101/380) substituted “Review of criminal records” for “Renewal of licenses” in section catchline and amended text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “A license issued under this part may be renewed for additional 5-year periods.”
