# § 1928. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–145, § 2(a)(4)(B)(i)](/us/pl/111/145/s2/a/4/B/i), Mar. 4, 2010, [124 Stat. 50](/us/stat/124/50)

##### § 1928. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–145, § 2(a)(4)(B)(i)](/us/pl/111/145/s2/a/4/B/i), Mar. 4, 2010, [124 Stat. 50](/us/stat/124/50)

Section, R.S. § 1823; [Mar. 3, 1921, ch. 124, § 1](/us/act/1921-03-03/ch124/s1), [41 Stat. 1291](/us/stat/41/1291), related to suspension of members of the force.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was classified to section 208 of former Title 40, prior to the enactment of Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works, by [Pub. L. 107–217, § 1](/us/pl/107/217/s1), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1062](/us/stat/116/1062).

R.S. § 1823 derived from acts [Mar. 3, 1873, ch. 226](/us/act/1873-03-03/ch226), [17 Stat. 488](/us/stat/17/488); [June 20, 1874, ch. 328](/us/act/1874-06-20/ch328), [18 Stat. 86](/us/stat/18/86); [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 129](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch129), [18 Stat. 345](/us/stat/18/345).
