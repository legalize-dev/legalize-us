# § 421a. Omitted

##### § 421a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 3, 1926, ch. 769, § 2](/us/act/1926-07-03/ch769/s2), [44 Stat. 840](/us/stat/44/840), appropriated $25,000 to effectuate the purposes of former [section 421 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s421), and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
