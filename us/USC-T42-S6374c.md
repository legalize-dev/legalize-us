##### § 6374c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 94–163, title III, § 400DD](/us/pl/94/163/tIII/s400DD), as added [Pub. L. 100–494, § 4(a)](/us/pl/100/494/s4/a), Oct. 14, 1988, [102 Stat. 2445](/us/stat/102/2445); amended [Pub. L. 102–486, title IV, § 402(2)](/us/pl/102/486/tIV/s402/2), (3), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2876](/us/stat/106/2876), provided for establishment of the Interagency Commission on Alternative Motor Fuels and the United States Alternative Fuels Council, required the Commission to submit interim reports and a final report by Sept. 30, 1992, to Congress, and terminated the Commission and Council upon submission of the final report.
