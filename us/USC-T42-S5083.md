##### § 5083. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–510, § 102(b)](/us/pl/95/510/s102/b), Oct. 24, 1978, [92 Stat. 1781](/us/stat/92/1781)

Section, [Pub. L. 93–113, title V, § 503](/us/pl/93/113/tV/s503), Oct. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 415](/us/stat/87/415); [Pub. L. 94–293, § 6(b)](/us/pl/94/293/s6/b), May 27, 1976, [90 Stat. 526](/us/stat/90/526), authorized appropriations for national volunteer programs to assist small businesses and promote volunteer service by persons with business experience.
