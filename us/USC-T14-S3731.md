# § 3731. Definitions

##### § 3731. Definitions

(1) “Reserve officer” means a commissioned officer in the Reserve, except an officer excluded by [section 3732 of this title](/us/usc/t14/s3732) or a commissioned warrant officer; and

(2) “discharged” means released from an appointment as a Reserve officer.

*(Added [Pub. L. 96–322, § 1](/us/pl/96/322/s1), Aug. 4, 1980, [94 Stat. 1007](/us/stat/94/1007), § 720; renumbered § 3731 and amended [Pub. L. 115–282, title I](/us/pl/115/282/tI), §§ 118(b), 123(b)(2), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4233](/us/stat/132/4233), 4240.)*
