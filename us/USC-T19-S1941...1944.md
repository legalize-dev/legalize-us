# §§ 1941 to 1944. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(e)](/us/pl/93/618/tVI/s602/e), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072](/us/stat/88/2072)

##### §§ 1941 to 1944. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(e)](/us/pl/93/618/tVI/s602/e), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072](/us/stat/88/2072)

Section 1941, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 322](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s322), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 892](/us/stat/76/892), set out qualifying requirements for trade readjustment allowances. See [section 2291 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2291).

Section 1942, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 323](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s323), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 893](/us/stat/76/893), set out provisions covering weekly amount for trade readjustment allowances. See [section 2292 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2292).

Section 1943, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 324](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s324), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 894](/us/stat/76/894), set out time limitations for trade readjustment allowances. See [section 2293 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2293).

Section 1944, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 325](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s325), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 894](/us/stat/76/894), provided for application of State laws. See [section 2294 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2294).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on 90th day following Jan. 3, 1975, see note set out under [section 1901 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1901).
