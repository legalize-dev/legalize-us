# § 1079d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(13)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/13), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160](/us/stat/94/2160)

##### § 1079d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(13)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/13), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160](/us/stat/94/2160)

Section, acts [May 1, 1956, ch. 229, § 5](/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229/s5), [70 Stat. 125](/us/stat/70/125); July 12, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–612, § 2(a)](/us/pl/86/612/s2/a), [74 Stat. 371](/us/stat/74/371), related to grants to needy ineligible widows.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).

#### Effective Date of 1960 Amendment

[Pub. L. 86–612, § 2(b)](/us/pl/86/612/s2/b), July 12, 1960, [74 Stat. 371](/us/stat/74/371), which provided that the amendment made to this section by [section 2(a) of Pub. L. 86–612](/us/pl/86/612/s2/a) take effect on the first day of the first month which begins more than thirty days after July 12, 1960, was repealed by [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(15)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/15), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160](/us/stat/94/2160).
