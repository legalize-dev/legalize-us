# § 7121. Definitions

##### § 7121. Definitions

(1) Participating county The term “participating county” means an eligible county that elects under [section 7112(d) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7112/d) to expend a portion of the Federal funds received under [section 7112 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7112) in accordance with this subchapter.

(2) Project funds The term “project funds” means all funds an eligible county elects under [section 7112(d) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7112/d) to reserve for expenditure in accordance with this subchapter.

(3) Resource advisory committee The term “resource advisory committee” means—

(A) an advisory committee established by the Secretary concerned under [section 7125 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7125); or

(B) an advisory committee determined by the Secretary concerned to meet the requirements of [section 7125 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7125).

(4) Resource management plan The term “resource management plan” means—

(A) a land use plan prepared by the Bureau of Land Management for units of the Federal land described in [section 7102(7)(B) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7102/7/B) pursuant to [section 1712 of title 43](/us/usc/t43/s1712); or

(B) a land and resource management plan prepared by the Forest Service for units of the National Forest System pursuant to [section 1604 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s1604).
