# [§ 2432. Renumbered § 4351]

##### [§ 2432. Renumbered § 4351]

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

As originally enacted, [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1849(b)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1849/b)–(k), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4259–4264](/us/stat/134/4259-4264), transferred individual subsecs. of this section to section 4350 et seq. of this title, and section 1849(*l*) of [Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283) subsequently repealed this section. [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XVII, § 1701](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tXVII/s1701)(*o*)(1)–(5), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 2146](/us/stat/135/2146), 2147, amended [section 1849 of Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283/s1849), effective as if included therein, so that the individual transfers were eliminated and this section was transferred as a whole to [section 4351 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s4351). However, the repeal of this section by section 1849(*l*) (which was redesignated as section 1849(e)) of [Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283) was not eliminated and therefore could not be executed given the transfer of this section to [section 4351 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s4351).
