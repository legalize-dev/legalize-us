# § 1853. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–465, title IV, § 412(c)](/us/pl/103/465/tIV/s412/c), Dec. 8, 1994, [108 Stat. 4964](/us/stat/108/4964)

##### § 1853. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–465, title IV, § 412(c)](/us/pl/103/465/tIV/s412/c), Dec. 8, 1994, [108 Stat. 4964](/us/stat/108/4964)

Section, [act May 28, 1956, ch. 327, title II, § 203](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327/tII/s203), [70 Stat. 199](/us/stat/70/199), provided for an export sales program for cotton.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the date of entry into force of the WTO Agreement with respect to the United States (Jan. 1, 1995), except as otherwise provided, see [section 451 of Pub. L. 103–465](/us/pl/103/465/s451), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3601 of Title 19](/us/usc/t19/s3601), Customs Duties.
