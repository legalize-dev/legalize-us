# § 2676. Contracts in foreign countries

##### § 2676. Contracts in foreign countries

The Secretary of State is authorized to enter into contracts in foreign countries involving expenditures from funds appropriated or otherwise made available to the Department of State, without regard to the provisions of [section 6306 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s6306): *Provided*, That nothing in this section shall be construed to waive the provisions of [section 431 of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s431).

*([Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 841](/us/act/1956-08-01/ch841), title I, § 9, [70 Stat. 891](/us/stat/70/891); renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 97–241, title II, § 202(a)](/us/pl/97/241/tII/s202/a), Aug. 24, 1982, [96 Stat. 282](/us/stat/96/282).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

In text, “[section 6306 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s6306)” substituted for “section 3741 of the Revised Statutes ([41 U.S.C. 22](/us/usc/t41/s22))” on authority of [Pub. L. 111–350, § 6(c)](/us/pl/111/350/s6/c), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3854](/us/stat/124/3854), which Act enacted Title 41, Public Contracts.

Section was formerly classified to [section 170n of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s170n) prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).
