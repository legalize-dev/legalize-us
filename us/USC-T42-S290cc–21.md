# § 290cc–21. Formula grants to States

##### § 290cc–21. Formula grants to States

For the purpose of carrying out [section 290cc–22 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290cc–22), the Secretary, acting through the Director of the Center for Mental Health Services, shall for each of the fiscal years 2023 through 2027 make an allotment for each State in an amount determined in accordance with [section 290cc–24 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290cc–24). The Secretary shall make payments, as grants, each such fiscal year to each State from the allotment for the State if the Secretary approves for the fiscal year involved an application submitted by the State pursuant to [section 290cc–29 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290cc–29).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 521 of act July 1, 1944, was renumbered section 542 by [section 611(2) of Pub. L. 100–77](/us/pl/100/77/s611/2) and is classified to [section 290dd–1 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290dd–1).

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–328](/us/pl/117/328) substituted “2023 through 2027” for “2018 through 2022”.

2016—[Pub. L. 114–255](/us/pl/114/255) substituted “2018 through 2022” for “1991 through 1994”.

1992—[Pub. L. 102–352](/us/pl/102/352) repealed [Pub. L. 102–321, § 163(a)(1)](/us/pl/102/321/s163/a/1), which directed the substitution of “Administrator of the Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration” for “Director of the National Institute of Mental Health”.

[Pub. L. 102–321, § 162(1)](/us/pl/102/321/s162/1), substituted “Center for Mental Health Services” for “National Institute of Mental Health”.

1990—[Pub. L. 101–645](/us/pl/101/645) amended section generally, substituting provisions relating to formula grants to States for provisions relating to establishment of block grant program for services to homeless individuals who are chronically mentally ill.

1989—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 101–93](/us/pl/101/93) directed that subsec. (a) of this section as similarly amended by title VIII of [Pub. L. 100–607](/us/pl/100/607) and title VI of [Pub. L. 100–628](/us/pl/100/628) be amended to read as if the amendments made by title VI of [Pub. L. 100–628](/us/pl/100/628) had not been enacted. See 1988 Amendment note below.

1988—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 100–607](/us/pl/100/607) and [Pub. L. 100–628](/us/pl/100/628) made identical amendments, amending first sentence generally. Prior to amendment, first sentence read as follows: “The Secretary shall for fiscal years 1987 and 1988 allot to each State an amount determined in accordance with sections 290cc–28 and 290cc–29 of this title.”
