# § 290k–1. Governor and Alternate Governor

##### § 290k–1. Governor and Alternate Governor

The Governor and Alternate Governor of the Bank, appointed under [section 286a of this title](/us/usc/t22/s286a), shall serve as Governor and Alternate Governor, respectively, of the Agency.

*([Pub. L. 100–202, § 101(e) [title I]](/us/pl/100/202/s101/e/tI), Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1329–131](/us/stat/101/1329-131), 1329–134.)*

#### Codification
