# § 6576. Rule of construction on equalized spending

##### § 6576. Rule of construction on equalized spending

Nothing in this subchapter shall be construed to mandate equalized spending per pupil for a State, local educational agency, or school.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1605](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1605), formerly § 1906, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title I, § 101](/us/pl/107/110/tI/s101), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1619](/us/stat/115/1619); renumbered § 1605, [Pub. L. 114–95, title I, § 1501(a)(5)](/us/pl/114/95/tI/s1501/a/5), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1906](/us/stat/129/1906).)*
