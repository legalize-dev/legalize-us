# § 7533. Effect on other statutes

##### § 7533. Effect on other statutes

This subchapter does not impair the powers vested in the Atomic Energy Commission by chapter 23 of title 42, or the requirement in [section 2201(d) of title 42](/us/usc/t42/s2201/d) that adequate provision be made for administrative review of a determination to dismiss an employee of the Atomic Energy Commission.

*([Pub. L. 89–554](/us/pl/89/554), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 529](/us/stat/80/529).)*

Standard changes are made to conform with the definitions applicable and the style of this title as outlined in the preface to the report.
