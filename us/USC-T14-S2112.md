# § 2112. Promotion year; defined

##### § 2112. Promotion year; defined

For the purposes of this chapter, “promotion year” means the period which commences on July 1 of each year and ends on June 30 of the following year.

*(Added [Pub. L. 94–546, § 1(17)](/us/pl/94/546/s1/17), Oct. 18, 1976, [90 Stat. 2520](/us/stat/90/2520), § 256a; renumbered § 2112, [Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 112(b)](/us/pl/115/282/tI/s112/b), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4216](/us/stat/132/4216).)*
