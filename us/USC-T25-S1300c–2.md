# § 1300c–2. Omitted

##### § 1300c–2. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–468, § 3](/us/pl/92/468/s3), Oct. 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 782](/us/stat/86/782), which related to preparation of tribal membership roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
