##### § 12821. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–625, title II, § 271](/us/pl/101/625/tII/s271), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 4124](/us/stat/104/4124); [Pub. L. 108–186, title I, § 102](/us/pl/108/186/tI/s102), Dec. 16, 2003, [117 Stat. 2686](/us/stat/117/2686); [Pub. L. 111–8, div. I, title II, § 229(4)](/us/pl/111/8/dI/tII/s229/4), Mar. 11, 2009, [123 Stat. 978](/us/stat/123/978), which related to downpayment assistance toward the purchase of single family housing, was omitted from the Code upon the expiration of Secretary’s authority on Dec. 31, 2011, to make grants to participating jurisdictions to assist low-income families to achieve homeownership.
