# § 715a. Omitted

##### § 715a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, act [Pub. L. 101–42, § 3](/us/pl/101/42/s3), June 28, 1989, [103 Stat. 91](/us/stat/103/91), which related to restoration of Federal recognition, rights, and privileges to the Coquille Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
