# § 1533. Children of Civil War veterans

##### § 1533. Children of Civil War veterans

Whenever there is no surviving spouse entitled to pension under [section 1532 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s1532), the Secretary shall pay to the children of each Civil War veteran who met the service requirements of [section 1532 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s1532) a pension at the monthly rate of $73.13 for one child, plus $8.13 for each additional child, with the total amount equally divided.
