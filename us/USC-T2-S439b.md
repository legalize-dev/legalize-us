# § 439b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–187, title I, § 105(1)](/us/pl/96/187/tI/s105/1), Jan. 8, 1980, [93 Stat. 1354](/us/stat/93/1354)

##### § 439b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–187, title I, § 105(1)](/us/pl/96/187/tI/s105/1), Jan. 8, 1980, [93 Stat. 1354](/us/stat/93/1354)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–225, title III, § 318](/us/pl/92/225/tIII/s318), formerly § 319, as added [Pub. L. 93–443, title II, § 210](/us/pl/93/443/tII/s210), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1289](/us/stat/88/1289); renumbered § 318, [Pub. L. 94–283, title I, § 105](/us/pl/94/283/tI/s105), May 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 481](/us/stat/90/481), set forth prohibitions respecting franked solicitations.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 8, 1980, see [section 301(a) of Pub. L. 96–187](/us/pl/96/187/s301/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 30101 of Title 52](/us/usc/t52/s30101), Voting and Elections.
