# § 12120. Liquified gas tankers

##### § 12120. Liquified gas tankers

(1) is a foreign built vessel that was built before October 19, 1996; or

(2) was documented under this chapter before that date, even if the vessel is placed under a foreign registry and subsequently redocumented under this chapter for operation under this section.

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 5](/us/pl/109/304/s5), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1504](/us/stat/120/1504).)*

The words “Notwithstanding section 27 of the Merchant Marine Act, 1920 (46 App. U.S.C. 883), [section 12106 of title 46](/us/usc/t46/s12106), United States Code, section 506 of the Merchant Marine Act, 1936 (46 App. U.S.C. 1156)” are omitted as unnecessary. The words “the Commonwealth of” are omitted as unnecessary and for consistency in the revised title.
