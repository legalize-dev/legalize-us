# § 61a. Transferred

##### § 61a. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 61a was editorially reclassified as [section 6531 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6531). [Section 6531 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6531) was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(H)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/H), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2776](/us/stat/133/2776).

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 61a, [act Aug. 5, 1955, ch. 568, § 1](/us/act/1955-08-05/ch568/s1), [69 Stat. 499](/us/stat/69/499), prescribed gross annual compensation of Secretary of Senate.
