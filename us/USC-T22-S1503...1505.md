# §§ 1503 to 1505. Repealed. [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), title V, § 542(a)(4), (10), [68 Stat. 861](/us/stat/68/861)

##### §§ 1503 to 1505. Repealed. [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), title V, § 542(a)(4), (10), [68 Stat. 861](/us/stat/68/861)

Section 1503, acts [Apr. 3, 1948, ch. 169](/us/act/1948-04-03/ch169), title I, § 104, [62 Stat. 138](/us/stat/62/138); [Apr. 19, 1949, ch. 77, § 2](/us/act/1949-04-19/ch77/s2), [63 Stat. 50](/us/stat/63/50); [Oct. 10, 1951, ch. 479](/us/act/1951-10-10/ch479), title V, § 503(b)(1), as added [June 20, 1952, ch. 449, § 7(c)](/us/act/1952-06-20/ch449/s7/c), [66 Stat. 144](/us/stat/66/144), related to Economic Cooperation Administration and provided for the creation of a corporation, its powers, duties, and liabilities, termination date, capital stock, employment of personnel and promulgation of rules and regulations.

Section 1504, acts [Apr. 3, 1948, ch. 169](/us/act/1948-04-03/ch169), title I, § 105, [62 Stat. 140](/us/stat/62/140); [Apr. 19, 1949, ch. 77, § 3](/us/act/1949-04-19/ch77/s3), [63 Stat. 50](/us/stat/63/50); [Oct. 10, 1951, ch. 479](/us/act/1951-10-10/ch479), title V, § 503(b)(1), as added [June 20, 1952, ch. 449, § 7(c)](/us/act/1952-06-20/ch449/s7/c), [66 Stat. 144](/us/stat/66/144), related to general functions of Administrator.

Section 1505, [act Apr. 3, 1948, ch. 169, title I, § 107](/us/act/1948-04-03/ch169/tI/s107), [62 Stat. 141](/us/stat/62/141), related to creation of Public Advisory Board; composition; qualifications; appointment; compensation; expenses; meetings; and additional advisory committees.
