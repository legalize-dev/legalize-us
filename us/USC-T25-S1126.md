# § 1126. Omitted

##### § 1126. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–309, § 5](/us/pl/92/309/s5), June 2, 1972, [86 Stat. 200](/us/stat/86/200), which directed that funds be distributed equally to the individuals enrolled, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
