# § 60910. Applicability

##### § 60910. Applicability

This chapter applies in the case of any cold case murder occurring on or after January 1, 1970.

*([Pub. L. 117–164, § 11](/us/pl/117/164/s11), Aug. 3, 2022, [136 Stat. 1361](/us/stat/136/1361).)*
