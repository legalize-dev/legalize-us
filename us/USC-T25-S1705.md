# § 1705. Omitted

##### § 1705. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–395, § 6](/us/pl/95/395/s6), Sept. 30, 1978, [92 Stat. 815](/us/stat/92/815), which related to publication of findings as to whether the State of Rhode Island had satisfied the conditions set forth in [section 1706 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1706) and consequences of such publication, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
