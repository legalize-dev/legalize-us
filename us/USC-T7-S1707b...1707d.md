# §§ 1707b to 1707d. Omitted

##### §§ 1707b to 1707d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted as part of the complete revision of the Agricultural Trade Act of 1978 ([Pub. L. 95–501](/us/pl/95/501)) by [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1531](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1531), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3668](/us/stat/104/3668). See chapter 87 (§ 5601 et seq.) of this title.

Section 1707b, [Pub. L. 95–501, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/501/tII/s201), Oct. 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 1686](/us/stat/92/1686), provided for a program of deferred payment sales of agricultural commodities.

Section 1707c, [Pub. L. 95–501, title II, § 202](/us/pl/95/501/tII/s202), Oct. 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 1687](/us/stat/92/1687), provided for a program of credit sales to People’s Republic of China.

Section 1707d, [Pub. L. 95–501, title II, § 203](/us/pl/95/501/tII/s203), Oct. 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 1687](/us/stat/92/1687), defined “agricultural commodity”.
