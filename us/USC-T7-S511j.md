# § 511j. Publication of violations

##### § 511j. Publication of violations

The Secretary is authorized to publish the facts regarding any violation of this chapter.

*([Aug. 23, 1935, ch. 623, § 11](/us/act/1935-08-23/ch623/s11), [49 Stat. 734](/us/stat/49/734).)*
