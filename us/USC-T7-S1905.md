# § 1905. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–445, § 5(b)](/us/pl/95/445/s5/b), Oct. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 1069](/us/stat/92/1069)

##### § 1905. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–445, § 5(b)](/us/pl/95/445/s5/b), Oct. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 1069](/us/stat/92/1069)

Section, [Pub. L. 85–765, § 5](/us/pl/85/765/s5), Aug. 27, 1958, [72 Stat. 863](/us/stat/72/863), related to establishment, composition, functions, compensation, meetings, and reports of advisory committees.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective one year after Oct. 10, 1978, and nonapplicability during not to exceed additional 18 months in hardship cases, see sec. 7 of [Pub. L. 95–445](/us/pl/95/445) set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 603 of Title 21](/us/usc/t21/s603), Food and Drugs.
