# § 5350. Delivery of reports of disbursements

##### § 5350. Delivery of reports of disbursements

(a) None of the funds made available in any fiscal year may be used to deliver a printed copy of the report of disbursements for the operations of the House of Representatives under [section 5535 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s5535) to the office of a Member of the House of Representatives (including a Delegate or Resident Commissioner to the Congress).

(b) This section shall apply with respect to fiscal year 2019 and each succeeding fiscal year.

*([Pub. L. 115–244, div. B, title I, § 115](/us/pl/115/244/dB/tI/s115), Sept. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 2930](/us/stat/132/2930).)*
