# §§ 219 to 222. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 219 to 222. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 219, R.S. § 4078; [act June 14, 1902, ch. 1088, § 3](/us/act/1902-06-14/ch1088/s3), [32 Stat. 386](/us/stat/32/386), related to issuance of false passports. See [section 1541 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1541), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 220, acts [June 15, 1917, ch. 30](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30), title IX, § 2, [40 Stat. 227](/us/stat/40/227); [Mar. 28, 1940, ch. 72, § 7](/us/act/1940-03-28/ch72/s7), [54 Stat. 80](/us/stat/54/80), related to false statements in application for passports. See [section 1542 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1542).

Section 221, acts [June 15, 1917, ch. 30](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30), title IX, § 3, [40 Stat. 227](/us/stat/40/227); [Mar. 28, 1940, ch. 72, § 7](/us/act/1940-03-28/ch72/s7), [54 Stat. 80](/us/stat/54/80), related to unlawful use of passports. See [section 1544 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1544).

Section 222, acts [June 15, 1917, ch. 30](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30), title IX, § 4, [40 Stat. 227](/us/stat/40/227); [Mar. 28, 1940, ch. 72, § 7](/us/act/1940-03-28/ch72/s7), [54 Stat. 80](/us/stat/54/80), related to forging or altering of passports. See [section 1543 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1543).
