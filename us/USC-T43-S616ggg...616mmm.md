# §§ 616ggg to 616mmm. Omitted

##### §§ 616ggg to 616mmm. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 616ggg, [Pub. L. 89–292, § 1](/us/pl/89/292/s1), Oct. 22, 1965, [79 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/79/1068), authorized construction, operation, and maintenance of the Southern Nevada project, Nevada.

Section 616hhh, [Pub. L. 89–292, § 2](/us/pl/89/292/s2), Oct. 22, 1965, [79 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/79/1068), related to allocation of project costs and to repayment of these allocated project costs.

Section 616iii, [Pub. L. 89–292, § 3](/us/pl/89/292/s3), Oct. 22, 1965, [79 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/79/1068), related to commencement of construction of this project, transfer of the care, operation and maintenance of this project to a State agency and to the permanent use of project facilities by Nevada.

Section 616jjj, [Pub. L. 89–292, § 4](/us/pl/89/292/s4), Oct. 22, 1965, [79 Stat. 1069](/us/stat/79/1069), related to construction costs allocated to defense installations.

Section 616kkk, [Pub. L. 89–292, § 5](/us/pl/89/292/s5), Oct. 22, 1965, [79 Stat. 1069](/us/stat/79/1069), related to control of diverted waters.

Section 616*lll*, [Pub. L. 89–292, § 6](/us/pl/89/292/s6), Oct. 22, 1965, [79 Stat. 1069](/us/stat/79/1069); [Pub. L. 89–510](/us/pl/89/510), July 19, 1966, [80 Stat. 312](/us/stat/80/312), related to contract provisions for subordination of rights of contracting parties to those of Basic Management, Inc. or its assignees.

Section 616mmm, [Pub. L. 89–292, § 7](/us/pl/89/292/s7), Oct. 22, 1965, [79 Stat. 1069](/us/stat/79/1069), authorized appropriations for this project.
