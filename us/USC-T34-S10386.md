# § 10386. Revocation or suspension of funding

##### § 10386. Revocation or suspension of funding

If the Attorney General determines, as a result of the reviews required by [section 10385 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10385), or otherwise, that a grant recipient under this subchapter is not in substantial compliance with the terms and requirements of an approved grant application submitted under [section 10382 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10382), the Attorney General may revoke or suspend funding of that grant, in whole or in part.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 1706](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s1706), as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title I, § 10003(a)(3)](/us/pl/103/322/tI/s10003/a/3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1813](/us/stat/108/1813).)*
