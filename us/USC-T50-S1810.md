# § 1810. Civil liability

##### § 1810. Civil liability

(a) actual damages, but not less than liquidated damages equal to the greater of—

(1) if the aggrieved person is a United States person, $10,000 or $1,000 per day for each day of violation; or

(2) for any other aggrieved person, $1,000 or $100 per day for each day of violation;

(b) punitive damages; and

(c) reasonable attorney’s fees and other investigation and litigation costs reasonably incurred.

*([Pub. L. 95–511, title I, § 110](/us/pl/95/511/tI/s110), Oct. 25, 1978, [92 Stat. 1796](/us/stat/92/1796); [Pub. L. 118–49, § 15(a)](/us/pl/118/49/s15/a), Apr. 20, 2024, [138 Stat. 882](/us/stat/138/882).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2024—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 118–49](/us/pl/118/49) amended subsec. (a) generally. Prior to amendment, subsec. (a) read as follows: “actual damages, but not less than liquidated damages of $1,000 or $100 per day for each day of violation, whichever is greater;”.
