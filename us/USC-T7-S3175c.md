# § 3175c. Program of food, nutrition, and consumer education by State cooperative extension services

##### § 3175c. Program of food, nutrition, and consumer education by State cooperative extension services

(1) provide effective and meaningful food, nutrition, and consumer education services to as many low-income individuals as possible;

(2) employ educational methodologies, including innovative approaches, that accomplish the purpose set forth in [section 3175b of this title](/us/usc/t7/s3175b); and

(3) to the extent practicable, coordinate activities carried out under the program with the delivery to low-income individuals of benefits under food assistance programs.

*([Pub. L. 99–198, title XV, § 1586](/us/pl/99/198/tXV/s1586), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1596](/us/stat/99/1596).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subtitle, referred to in text, is subtitle C (§§ 1581–1589) of title XV of [Pub. L. 99–198](/us/pl/99/198) which is classified as follows: sections 1581 and 1582 are not classified to the Code; section 1583 is set out as a note under [section 2011 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2011); sections 1584 through 1588 are classified, respectively, to sections 3175a to 3175e of this title; and section 1589 is classified to [section 3178a of this title](/us/usc/t7/s3178a).

#### Codification

Section was enacted as part of the Food Security Act of 1985, and not as part of the National Agricultural Research, Extension, and Teaching Policy Act of 1977 which comprises this chapter.
