# § 101. Administrator

##### § 101. Administrator

In this subtitle, the term “Administrator” means the Administrator for Federal Procurement Policy appointed under [section 1102 of this title](/us/usc/t41/s1102).

*([Pub. L. 111–350, § 3](/us/pl/111/350/s3), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3678](/us/stat/124/3678).)*
