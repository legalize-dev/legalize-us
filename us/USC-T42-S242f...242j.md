# §§ 242f to 242j. Transferred

##### §§ 242f to 242j. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 242f, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III, § 308](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIII/s308), as added July 12, 1960, [Pub. L. 93–353](/us/pl/93/353) and transferred to section 242*l* of this title.
