# § 7176. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 7176. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part $1,000,000,000 for fiscal year 2017 and $1,100,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2018 through 2020.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 4206](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s4206), as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/107/110/tIV/s401), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1773](/us/stat/115/1773); amended [Pub. L. 114–95, title IV, § 4201(a)](/us/pl/114/95/tIV/s4201/a), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1993](/us/stat/129/1993).)*
