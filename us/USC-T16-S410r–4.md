# § 410r–4. Authorization of appropriations for reimbursement of revolving fund

##### § 410r–4. Authorization of appropriations for reimbursement of revolving fund

There is hereby authorized to be appropriated to the emergency credit revolving fund, upon the transfer authorized in [section 410r–3 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s410r–3), such sum as may be necessary but not in excess of $452,000 to reimburse the fund for costs incurred by the Farmers Home Administration in connection with the aforesaid property.

*([Pub. L. 88–588, § 2](/us/pl/88/588/s2), Sept. 12, 1964, [78 Stat. 933](/us/stat/78/933).)*
