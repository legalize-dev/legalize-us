# § 1133. Omitted

##### § 1133. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–660, § 3](/us/pl/89/660/s3), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 911](/us/stat/80/911), which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
