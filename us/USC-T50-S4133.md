# § 4133. Transfers and assignments

##### § 4133. Transfers and assignments

The transfer or assignment for value of any property forming the subject matter of a claim under subsection (a) or (b) of [section 4132 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s4132) subsequent to its damage, loss, or destruction shall not operate to extinguish any claim of the transferor otherwise compensable under either of such subsections. If a claim which could otherwise be allowed under subsection (a) or (b) of [section 4132 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s4132) has been assigned for value prior to October 22, 1962, the assignee shall be the party entitled to claim thereunder.

*([July 3, 1948, ch. 826](/us/act/1948-07-03/ch826), title II, § 203, as added [Pub. L. 87–846, title I, § 103](/us/pl/87/846/tI/s103), Oct. 22, 1962, [76 Stat. 1109](/us/stat/76/1109).)*
