# § 677g. Omitted

##### § 677g. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, acts [Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 8](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s8), [68 Stat. 869](/us/stat/68/869); [Aug. 2, 1956, ch. 880, § 2](/us/act/1956-08-02/ch880/s2), [70 Stat. 936](/us/stat/70/936), which related to membership rolls of full-blood and mixed-blood members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
