# §§ 907 to 911. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

##### §§ 907 to 911. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

Section 907, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 512](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV/s512), [60 Stat. 1007](/us/stat/60/1007), related to commission of Foreign Service officers.

Section 908, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 513](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV/s513), [60 Stat. 1007](/us/stat/60/1007), authorized Secretary to define limits of consular districts.

Section 909, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 514](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV/s514), [60 Stat. 1008](/us/stat/60/1008), related to assignment and transfers.

Section 910, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title V, § 515, [60 Stat. 1008](/us/stat/60/1008); Aug. 17, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–105, title IV, § 404](/us/pl/95/105/tIV/s404), [91 Stat. 853](/us/stat/91/853), related to citizenship requirements of Foreign Service officers.

Section 911, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title V, § 516, [60 Stat. 1008](/us/stat/60/1008); [July 28, 1956, ch. 770, § 5](/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770/s5), [70 Stat. 704](/us/stat/70/704); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 8](/us/pl/86/723/s8), [74 Stat. 832](/us/stat/74/832); Aug. 17, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–105, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/95/105/tIV/s405), [91 Stat. 853](/us/stat/91/853), related to admissions to class 6, 7, or 8 for Foreign Service officers.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).
