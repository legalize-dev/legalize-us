##### §§ 1001 to 1006. Repealed. [June 27, 1952, ch. 477](/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477), title IV, § 403(a)(42), [66 Stat. 280](/us/stat/66/280), eff. Dec. 24, 1952

Section 1001, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title III, § 701](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tIII/s701), as added [Mar. 27, 1942, ch. 199](/us/act/1942-03-27/ch199), title X, § 1001, [56 Stat. 182](/us/stat/56/182); amended [Dec. 22, 1944, ch. 662, § 1](/us/act/1944-12-22/ch662/s1), [58 Stat. 886](/us/stat/58/886); [Dec. 28, 1945, ch. 590, § 1(c)(1)](/us/act/1945-12-28/ch590/s1/c/1), [59 Stat. 658](/us/stat/59/658), related to exceptions from certain requirements of naturalization of persons serving in the armed forces during World War II. See [section 1440 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1440).

Section 1002, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title III, § 702](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tIII/s702), as added [Mar. 27, 1942, ch. 199](/us/act/1942-03-27/ch199), title X, § 1001, [56 Stat. 182](/us/stat/56/182); amended [Dec. 22, 1944, ch. 662, § 2](/us/act/1944-12-22/ch662/s2), [58 Stat. 887](/us/stat/58/887), related to alien serving outside of jurisdiction of naturalization court. See [section 1440 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1440).

Section 1003, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title III, § 703](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tIII/s703), as added [Mar. 27, 1942, ch. 199](/us/act/1942-03-27/ch199), title X, § 1001, [56 Stat. 183](/us/stat/56/183), related to waiver of notice to commissioner in case of alien enemy. See [section 1440 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1440).

Section 1004, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title III, § 704](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tIII/s704), as added [Mar. 27, 1942, ch. 199](/us/act/1942-03-27/ch199), title X, § 1001, [56 Stat. 183](/us/stat/56/183), related to persons excepted from former subchapter. See [section 1440 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1440).

Section 1005, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title III, § 705](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tIII/s705), as added [Mar. 27, 1942, ch. 199](/us/act/1942-03-27/ch199), title X, § 1001, [56 Stat. 183](/us/stat/56/183), related to forms, rules and regulations. See [section 1440 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1440).

Section 1006, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title III, § 706](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tIII/s706), as added [Dec. 28, 1945, ch. 590, § 1(c)(2)](/us/act/1945-12-28/ch590/s1/c/2), [59 Stat. 658](/us/stat/59/658), related to time of service limitation. See [section 1440 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1440).
