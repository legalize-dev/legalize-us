# § 3002. Federal Acquisition Regulation

##### § 3002. Federal Acquisition Regulation

In this part, the term “Federal Acquisition Regulation” means the Federal Acquisition Regulation issued pursuant to [section 1303(a)(1) of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s1303/a/1).

*(Added and amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1806(a)(1)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1806/a/1), (5), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4152](/us/stat/134/4152).)*
