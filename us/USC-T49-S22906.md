# § 22906. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 22906. Authorization of appropriations

(1) For fiscal year 2009, $100,000,000.

(2) For fiscal year 2010, $300,000,000.

(3) For fiscal year 2011, $400,000,000.

(4) For fiscal year 2012, $500,000,000.

(5) For fiscal year 2013, $600,000,000.

*(Added [Pub. L. 110–432, div. B, title III, § 301(a)](/us/pl/110/432/dB/tIII/s301/a), Oct. 16, 2008, [122 Stat. 4946](/us/stat/122/4946), § 24406; renumbered § 22906, [Pub. L. 115–420, § 7(a)(1)](/us/pl/115/420/s7/a/1), Jan. 3, 2019, [132 Stat. 5445](/us/stat/132/5445).)*
