# § 50106. Prohibition of land acquisition

##### § 50106. Prohibition of land acquisition

No funds provided under this chapter shall be used for land acquisition.

*([Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609R](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s609R), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2106](/us/stat/98/2106).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 10506 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s10506), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
