# § 675. Omitted

##### § 675. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 12, 1953, ch. 406, § 2](/us/act/1953-08-12/ch406/s2), [67 Stat. 540](/us/stat/67/540), which prohibited use of funds authorized to be expended or advanced pursuant to former [section 674 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s674) for the payment of agents’ or attorneys’ fees, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
