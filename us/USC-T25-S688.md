# § 688. Omitted

##### § 688. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 85–794, § 2](/us/pl/85/794/s2), Aug. 28, 1958, [72 Stat. 958](/us/stat/72/958), which provided that moneys paid under former sections 687 to 689 of this title would not be subject to liens or claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
