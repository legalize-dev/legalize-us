# § 60504. Rule of construction

##### § 60504. Rule of construction

(1) be made as competitive grants 11 So in original. Probably should be “as a competitive grant”. to eligible entities for a 12-month period, except that grants awarded under section 113 22 See References in Text note below., [section 60521 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s60521), [section 60531 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s60531), and section 60532 2 of this title or under [section 10631 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10631) may be made for a 24-month period; and

(2) require that services for participants, when necessary and appropriate, be transferred from programs funded under this Act or the amendment made by this Act, respectively, to State and community-based programs not funded under this Act or the amendment made by this Act, respectively, before the expiration of the grant.

*([Pub. L. 110–199, § 6](/us/pl/110/199/s6), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 660](/us/stat/122/660); [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 502(h)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s502/h), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5231](/us/stat/132/5231).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 110–199](/us/pl/110/199), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 657](/us/stat/122/657), known as the Second Chance Act of 2007: Community Safety Through Recidivism Prevention and also as the Second Chance Act of 2007. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title of 2008 Act note set out under [section 10101 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10101) and Tables.

Section 113, referred to in par. (1), means [section 113 of Pub. L. 110–199](/us/pl/110/199/s113). For complete classification of [section 113 of Pub. L. 110–199](/us/pl/110/199/s113) to the Code, see Tables.

[Section 60532 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s60532), referred to in par. (1), was repealed by [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s504/a), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5233](/us/stat/132/5233).

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 17504 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s17504), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2018—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 115–391](/us/pl/115/391) inserted “or under [section 10631 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10631)” after “[section 60532 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s60532)”.
