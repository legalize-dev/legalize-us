# § 6707. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 6707. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this chapter $15,000,000 for each fiscal year.

(b) Limitation No funds made available under subsection (a) shall be used to pay the costs of constructing any facilities.

*([Pub. L. 108–317, § 8](/us/pl/108/317/s8), Oct. 5, 2004, [118 Stat. 1210](/us/stat/118/1210).)*
