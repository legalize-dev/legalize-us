# § 282a. Governor, executive director, and alternates of Corporation

##### § 282a. Governor, executive director, and alternates of Corporation

The governor and executive director of the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development, and the alternate for each of them, appointed under [section 286a of this title](/us/usc/t22/s286a), shall serve as governor, director and alternates, respectively, of the Corporation.

*([Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 788, § 3](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch788/s3), [69 Stat. 669](/us/stat/69/669).)*
