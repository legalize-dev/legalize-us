# § 1140f. Purpose

##### § 1140f. Purpose

It is the purpose of this subpart to support model demonstration programs that promote the successful transition of students with intellectual disabilities into higher education.

*([Pub. L. 89–329, title VII, § 766](/us/pl/89/329/tVII/s766), as added [Pub. L. 110–315, title VII, § 709(2)](/us/pl/110/315/tVII/s709/2), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3365](/us/stat/122/3365).)*
