# § 724. Omitted

##### § 724. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 23, 1954, ch. 831, § 4](/us/act/1954-08-23/ch831/s4), [68 Stat. 769](/us/stat/68/769), which provided for cancellation of certain indebtedness effective on the date of the proclamation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
