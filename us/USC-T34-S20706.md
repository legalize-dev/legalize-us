# § 20706. Senior Policy Operating Group

##### § 20706. Senior Policy Operating Group

Each Federal department or agency involved in grant activities related to combatting trafficking or providing services to persons subjected to trafficking inside the United States shall apprise the Senior Policy Operating Group established by section 105(f) 11 See References in Text note below. of the Victims of Trafficking and Violence Protection Act of 2000 ([22 U.S.C. 7103(f)](/us/usc/t22/s7103/f)), under the procedures established by the Senior Policy Operating Group, of such activities of the department or agency to ensure that the activities are consistent with the purposes of the Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000 ([22 U.S.C. 7101](/us/usc/t22/s7101) et seq.).

*([Pub. L. 109–164, title II, § 206](/us/pl/109/164/tII/s206), Jan. 10, 2006, [119 Stat. 3571](/us/stat/119/3571); [Pub. L. 110–457, title II, § 233](/us/pl/110/457/tII/s233), Dec. 23, 2008, [122 Stat. 5074](/us/stat/122/5074).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Section 105(f) of the Victims of Trafficking and Violence Protection Act of 2000, referred to in text, was redesignated 105(g) of the Victims of Trafficking and Violence Protection Act of 2000 by [Pub. L. 113–4, title XII, § 1201(3)](/us/pl/113/4/tXII/s1201/3), Mar. 7, 2013, [127 Stat. 136](/us/stat/127/136).

The Trafficking Victims Protection Act of 2000, referred to in text, is div. A of [Pub. L. 106–386](/us/pl/106/386), Oct. 28, 2000, [114 Stat. 1466](/us/stat/114/1466), which is classified principally to chapter 78 (§ 7101 et seq.) of Title 22, Foreign Relations and Intercourse. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 7101 of Title 22](/us/usc/t22/s7101) and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 14044d of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s14044d), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2008—[Pub. L. 110–457](/us/pl/110/457) struck out “, as the department or agency determines appropriate,” before “apprise the Senior Policy Operating Group”.
