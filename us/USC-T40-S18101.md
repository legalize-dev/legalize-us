# § 18101. Definitions

##### § 18101. Definitions

(1) Federal agency.— The term “federal agency” has the same meaning given that term in [section 102 of this title](/us/usc/t40/s102).

(2) TTY.— The term “TTY” means a text-telephone used in the transmission of coded signals through the nationwide telecommunications system.

*([Pub. L. 107–217](/us/pl/107/217), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1290](/us/stat/116/1290).)*
