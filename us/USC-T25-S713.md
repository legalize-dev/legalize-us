# § 713. Omitted

##### § 713. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–165, § 2](/us/pl/98/165/s2), Nov. 22, 1983, [97 Stat. 1064](/us/stat/97/1064), which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of 2023 Amendment

[Pub. L. 118–32, § 1](/us/pl/118/32/s1), Dec. 26, 2023, [137 Stat. 1109](/us/stat/137/1109), provided that: “This Act [enacting and amending provisions set out as notes under [section 713f of this title](/us/usc/t25/s713f)] may be cited as the ‘Grand Ronde Reservation Act Amendment of 2023’.”

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 98–165, § 1](/us/pl/98/165/s1), Nov. 22, 1983, [97 Stat. 1064](/us/stat/97/1064), provided that [Pub. L. 98–165](/us/pl/98/165), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Grand Ronde Restoration Act”.
