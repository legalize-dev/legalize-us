# § 1153. Omitted

##### § 1153. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–114, § 3](/us/pl/90/114/s3), Oct. 24, 1967, [81 Stat. 335](/us/stat/81/335), which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disabilities, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
