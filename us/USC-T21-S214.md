# § 214. Previous laws unaffected

##### § 214. Previous laws unaffected

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as modifying or revoking any of the provisions of sections 191 to 193 11 See References in Text note below. of this title.

*([Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 74, § 13](/us/act/1915-03-03/ch74/s13), [38 Stat. 822](/us/stat/38/822).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Sections 191 to 193 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by [Pub. L. 91–513, title III, § 1101(a)(1)](/us/pl/91/513/tIII/s1101/a/1), Oct. 27, 1970, [84 Stat. 1291](/us/stat/84/1291). See section 801 et seq. of this title.
