# § 7384w. Subpoenas; oaths; examination of witnesses

##### § 7384w. Subpoenas; oaths; examination of witnesses

(1) issue subpoenas for and compel the attendance of witnesses;

(2) administer oaths;

(3) examine witnesses; and

(4) require the production of books, papers, documents, and other evidence.
