# § 81. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–344, title V, § 505(2)](/us/pl/93/344/tV/s505/2), July 12, 1974, [88 Stat. 322](/us/stat/88/322)

##### § 81. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–344, title V, § 505(2)](/us/pl/93/344/tV/s505/2), July 12, 1974, [88 Stat. 322](/us/stat/88/322)

Section, [act July 2, 1954, ch. 455, title I](/us/act/1954-07-02/ch455/tI), [68 Stat. 400](/us/stat/68/400), directed that the fiscal year for the adjustment of the accounts of Sergeant at Arms of House for compensation and mileage of Members, Delegates, and Resident Commissioner extend from July 1 to June 30.
