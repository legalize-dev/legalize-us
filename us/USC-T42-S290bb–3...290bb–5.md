# §§ 290bb–3 to 290bb–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–310, div. B, title XXXIII, § 3301(c)(1)](/us/pl/106/310/dB/tXXXIII/s3301/c/1)–(3), Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1209](/us/stat/114/1209)

##### §§ 290bb–3 to 290bb–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–310, div. B, title XXXIII, § 3301(c)(1)](/us/pl/106/310/dB/tXXXIII/s3301/c/1)–(3), Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1209](/us/stat/114/1209)

Section 290bb–3, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V, § 510](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV/s510), as added [Pub. L. 102–321, title I, § 109](/us/pl/102/321/tI/s109), July 10, 1992, [106 Stat. 342](/us/stat/106/342), related to demonstration projects of national significance.

A prior section 510 of act July 1, 1944, was classified to [section 290bb of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290bb), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–321, § 122(b)(1)](/us/pl/102/321/s122/b/1). Prior to repeal, section 510(b) of act July 1, 1944, was renumbered section 464H(b) by [Pub. L. 102–321](/us/pl/102/321) and transferred to [section 285n(b) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s285n/b).

Another prior section 510 of act July 1, 1944, which was classified to [section 228 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s228), was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238g of this title](/us/usc/t42/s238g).

Section 290bb–4, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V, § 511](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV/s511), as added [Pub. L. 102–321, title I, § 110](/us/pl/102/321/tI/s110), July 10, 1992, [106 Stat. 343](/us/stat/106/343), related to grants for substance abuse treatment in State and local criminal justice systems.

A prior section 511 of act July 1, 1944, which was classified to [section 290bb–1 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290bb–1), was renumbered section 464J of act July 1, 1944, by [Pub. L. 102–321](/us/pl/102/321) and transferred to [section 285n–2 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s285n–2).

Another prior section 511 of act July 1, 1944, which was classified to [section 229 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s229), was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238h of this title](/us/usc/t42/s238h).

Section 290bb–5, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V, § 512](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV/s512), as added [Pub. L. 102–321, title I, § 111](/us/pl/102/321/tI/s111), July 10, 1992, [106 Stat. 344](/us/stat/106/344), related to training in provision of treatment services.

A prior section 512 of act July 1, 1944, which was classified to [section 290bb–1a of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290bb–1a), was renumbered section 506 of act July 1, 1944, by [Pub. L. 102–321](/us/pl/102/321) and transferred to [section 290aa–5 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290aa–5).

Another prior section 512 of act July 1, 1944, was renumbered section 513 by [Pub. L. 98–509](/us/pl/98/509) and classified to [section 290bb–2 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290bb–2), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–321, § 122(d)[(e)]](/us/pl/102/321/s122/d).

Another prior section 512 of act July 1, 1944, which was classified to [section 229a of this title](/us/usc/t42/s229a), was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238i of this title](/us/usc/t42/s238i).
