# §§ 802 to 807. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

##### §§ 802 to 807. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

Section 802, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title I, § 121, [60 Stat. 1000](/us/stat/60/1000); Oct. 7, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–426, title I, § 116](/us/pl/95/426/tI/s116), [92 Stat. 969](/us/stat/92/969), set forth definitions applicable to provisions of this chapter.

Section 803, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title X, § 1001](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tX/s1001), [60 Stat. 1030](/us/stat/60/1030), related to prohibition on wearing of uniforms.

Section 804, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title X, § 1002, [60 Stat. 1030](/us/stat/60/1030); Oct. 15, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–673, § 8(3)](/us/pl/89/673/s8/3), [80 Stat. 953](/us/stat/80/953), related to acceptance of gifts by chief of mission or other principal officer.

Section 805, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title X, § 1003](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tX/s1003), [60 Stat. 1030](/us/stat/60/1030), related to prohibition on engaging in business abroad.

Section 806, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title X, § 1004](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tX/s1004), [60 Stat. 1030](/us/stat/60/1030), related to prohibition on personal correspondence on affairs of foreign governments.

Section 807, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title X, § 1005](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tX/s1005), [60 Stat. 1030](/us/stat/60/1030), related to prohibition on political, racial, religious, or color discrimination.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).
