##### § 983b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–484, § 4](/us/pl/101/484/s4), Oct. 31, 1990, [104 Stat. 1167](/us/stat/104/1167), which related to restoration of rights of the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
