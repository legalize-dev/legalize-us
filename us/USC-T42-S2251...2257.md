##### §§ 2251 to 2257. Repealed. [Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724), title I, § 302(a), as added [Pub. L. 95–110, § 1](/us/pl/95/110/s1), Sept. 20, 1977, [91 Stat. 884](/us/stat/91/884); renumbered title I, Oct. 24, 1992, [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 902(a)(8)](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s902/a/8), [106 Stat. 2944](/us/stat/106/2944)

Section 2251, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 201](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s201), as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 956](/us/stat/68/956), provided for establishment of Joint Committee on Atomic Energy.

Provisions similar to section 2251 were contained in [section 1815(a) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1815/a) prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946 by [act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 919](/us/stat/68/919).

Section 2252, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 202](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s202), as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 956](/us/stat/68/956); amended Sept. 6, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–206, § 17](/us/pl/87/206/s17), [75 Stat. 479](/us/stat/75/479); Mar. 26, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–294](/us/pl/88/294), [78 Stat. 172](/us/stat/78/172); Dec. 6, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–514](/us/pl/93/514), [88 Stat. 1611](/us/stat/88/1611), set out authority and duties of Joint Committee.

Provisions similar to section 2252 were contained in [section 1815(b) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1815/b) prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946 by [act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 919](/us/stat/68/919).

Section 2253, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 203](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s203), as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 956](/us/stat/68/956), provided for a Chairman and a Vice Chairman of Committee.

Provisions similar to section 2253 were contained in [section 1815(c) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1815/c) prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946 by [act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 919](/us/stat/68/919).

Section 2254, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 204](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s204), as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 957](/us/stat/68/957); amended Dec. 27, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–554, title I, § 101(2)](/us/pl/93/554/tI/s101/2), [88 Stat. 1776](/us/stat/88/1776), related to the powers of Committee.

Provisions similar to section 2254 were contained in [section 1815(d) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1815/d) prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946 by [act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 919](/us/stat/68/919).

Section 2255, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 205](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s205), as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 957](/us/stat/68/957), related to staff and assistance for Committee.

Provisions similar to section 2255 were contained in [section 1815(e) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1815/e) prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946 by [act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 919](/us/stat/68/919).

Section 2256, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 206](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s206), as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 957](/us/stat/68/957), related to classification of information originating in Committee.

Section 2257, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 207](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s207), as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 957](/us/stat/68/957), required that Committee keep records of all Committee actions.
