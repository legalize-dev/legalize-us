# § 5663. Cross reference

##### § 5663. Cross reference

For penalties of common application pertaining to liquors, including wines, see part IV.

*(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201](/us/pl/85/859/tII/s201), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1407](/us/stat/72/1407); amended [Pub. L. 96–39, title VIII, § 807(a)(57)](/us/pl/96/39/tVIII/s807/a/57), July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 289](/us/stat/93/289).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 5663, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 695](/us/stat/68A/695), consisted of provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859](/us/pl/85/859).

#### Amendments

1979—[Pub. L. 96–39](/us/pl/96/39) struck out reference to penalties for rectified products under part I of this subchapter.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of 1979 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 96–39](/us/pl/96/39) effective Jan. 1, 1980, see [section 810 of Pub. L. 96–39](/us/pl/96/39/s810), set out as a note under [section 5001 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s5001).
