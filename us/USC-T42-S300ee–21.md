# § 300ee–21. Evaluations

##### § 300ee–21. Evaluations

The Secretary shall, directly or through grants or contracts, evaluate the services provided and activities carried out with payments to States under this part.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XXV, formerly title XV, § 2511, as added [Pub. L. 100–607, title II, § 221](/us/pl/100/607/tII/s221), Nov. 4, 1988, [102 Stat. 3101](/us/stat/102/3101); renumbered title XXV, [Pub. L. 101–93, § 5(e)(1)](/us/pl/101/93/s5/e/1), Aug. 16, 1989, [103 Stat. 612](/us/stat/103/612).)*
