# § 603. Regulations

##### § 603. Regulations

The Secretary concerned and the Secretary of Veterans Affairs shall prescribe regulations necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 87–649](/us/pl/87/649), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 484](/us/stat/76/484); [Pub. L. 101–189, div. A, title XVI, § 1621(b)(2)](/us/pl/101/189/dA/tXVI/s1621/b/2), Nov. 29, 1989, [103 Stat. 1604](/us/stat/103/1604).)*

The words “effectively” and “provisions of” are omitted as surplusage.
