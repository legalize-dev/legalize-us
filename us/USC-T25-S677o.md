# § 677o. Omitted

##### § 677o. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 16](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s16), [68 Stat. 876](/us/stat/68/876), which related to termination of restrictions on individually owned property of the mixed-blood group, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
