# §§ 1596 to 1598. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 1596 to 1598. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 1596, [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497, title IV, § 596](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497/tIV/s596), [46 Stat. 752](/us/stat/46/752), related to buildings on boundary. See [section 547 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s547), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 1597, [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497, title IV, § 597](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497/tIV/s597), [46 Stat. 752](/us/stat/46/752), related to fraudulent treatment of goods in warehouses. See [section 548 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s548).

Section 1598, acts [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 598, [46 Stat. 752](/us/stat/46/752); [June 25, 1938, ch. 679, § 26](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch679/s26), [52 Stat. 1089](/us/stat/52/1089), related to offenses concerning seals and unlawful removal of goods from custom custody. See [section 549 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s549).
