# § 144. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–131, § 3](/us/pl/88/131/s3), Sept. 4, 1963, [77 Stat. 194](/us/stat/77/194)

##### § 144. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–131, § 3](/us/pl/88/131/s3), Sept. 4, 1963, [77 Stat. 194](/us/stat/77/194)

Section, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495, § 6](/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495/s6), Pt. A, [65 Stat. 408](/us/stat/65/408), related to applicability of sections 144 to 147d of this title, provided that rules concerning lights be complied with from sunset to sunrise, and defined terms used in sections 145 to 147d of this title.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1965, see Proc. No. 3632, Dec. 29, 1964, 29 F.R. 19167, and [section 3 of Pub. L. 88–131](/us/pl/88/131/s3), both set out as notes under [section 1051 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1051).
