# §§ 300v to 300v–3. Omitted

##### §§ 300v to 300v–3. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 300v to 300v–3, which provided for the establishment, duties, administration, funding, and termination of the President’s Commission for the Study of Ethical Problems in Medicine and Biomedical and Behavioral Research, were omitted pursuant to section 300v–3, which provided for the Commission’s termination on Dec. 31, 1982. See 48 F.R. 34408.

Section 300v, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XVIII, § 1801](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXVIII/s1801), as added [Pub. L. 95–622, title III, § 301](/us/pl/95/622/tIII/s301), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3437](/us/stat/92/3437); amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)](/us/pl/96/88/tV/s509/b), Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695](/us/stat/93/695); [Pub. L. 100–527, § 10(1)](/us/pl/100/527/s10/1), Oct. 25, 1988, [102 Stat. 2640](/us/stat/102/2640), established the President’s Commission for the Study of Ethical Problems in Medicine and Biomedical and Behavioral Research and directed the President to appoint eleven members.

Section 300v–1, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XVIII, § 1802](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXVIII/s1802), as added [Pub. L. 95–622, title III, § 301](/us/pl/95/622/tIII/s301), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3439](/us/stat/92/3439); amended [Pub. L. 96–32, § 4](/us/pl/96/32/s4), July 10, 1979, [93 Stat. 82](/us/stat/93/82), related to duties of the Commission.

Section 300v–2, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XVIII, § 1803](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXVIII/s1803), as added [Pub. L. 95–622, title III, § 301](/us/pl/95/622/tIII/s301), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3440](/us/stat/92/3440), related to administrative provisions.

Section 300v–3, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XVIII, § 1804](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXVIII/s1804), as added [Pub. L. 95–622, title III, § 301](/us/pl/95/622/tIII/s301), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3441](/us/stat/92/3441), related to authorization of appropriations and termination of the Commission.
