# § 223. Number of Federal reserve cities in district

##### § 223. Number of Federal reserve cities in district

A Federal reserve district shall contain only one Federal reserve city.

*([Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 2](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s2) (part), [38 Stat. 251](/us/stat/38/251).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section is based on part of the first par. of section 2 of act Dec. 23, 1913. Some of the other provisions of the first par. are classified to [section 222 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s222), and some were not included in the Code. For classification of other pars. of section 2 of this Act, see Codification note under [section 222 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s222).
