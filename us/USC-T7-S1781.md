# § 1781. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–130, § 4(a)](/us/pl/103/130/s4/a), Nov. 1, 1993, [107 Stat. 1369](/us/stat/107/1369)

##### § 1781. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–130, § 4(a)](/us/pl/103/130/s4/a), Nov. 1, 1993, [107 Stat. 1369](/us/stat/107/1369)

Section, acts [Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041](/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041), title VII, § 702, [68 Stat. 910](/us/stat/68/910); Sept. 29, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–113, title III, § 301](/us/pl/95/113/tIII/s301), [91 Stat. 921](/us/stat/91/921), declared encouragement of continued domestic production of wool to be policy of Congress.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Act Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041, title VII, § 701](/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041/tVII/s701), [68 Stat. 910](/us/stat/68/910), provided that title VII of act Aug. 28, 1954 (enacting this chapter) could be cited as the “National Wool Act of 1954”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–130, § 3(a)](/us/pl/103/130/s3/a), Nov. 1, 1993, [107 Stat. 1369](/us/stat/107/1369).
