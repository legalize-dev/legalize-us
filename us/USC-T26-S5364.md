# § 5364. Wine imported in bulk

##### § 5364. Wine imported in bulk

Natural wine (as defined in section 5381) imported or brought into the United States in bulk containers may, under such regulations as the Secretary may prescribe, be withdrawn from customs custody and transferred in such bulk containers to the premises of a bonded wine cellar without payment of the internal revenue tax imposed on such wine. The proprietor of a bonded wine cellar to which such wine is transferred shall become liable for the tax on the wine withdrawn from customs custody under this section upon release of the wine from customs custody, and the importer, or the person bringing such wine into the United States, shall thereupon be relieved of the liability for such tax.

*(Added [Pub. L. 105–34, title XIV, § 1422(a)](/us/pl/105/34/tXIV/s1422/a), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 1050](/us/stat/111/1050); amended [Pub. L. 105–206, title VI, § 6014(b)(3)](/us/pl/105/206/tVI/s6014/b/3), July 22, 1998, [112 Stat. 820](/us/stat/112/820).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 5364, added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201](/us/pl/85/859/tII/s201), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1381](/us/stat/72/1381), limited proprietors of bonded wine cellars or taxpaid wine bottling houses to the production, reception, storage, or use of only standard wine, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 96–39, title VIII](/us/pl/96/39/tVIII), §§ 807(a)(46), 810, July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 287](/us/stat/93/287), 292, eff. Jan. 1, 1980.

Another prior section 5364, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 665](/us/stat/68A/665), consisted of provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859](/us/pl/85/859).

#### Amendments

1998—[Pub. L. 105–206](/us/pl/105/206) substituted “Natural wine (as defined in section 5381) imported or brought into” for “Wine imported or brought into” in first sentence.
