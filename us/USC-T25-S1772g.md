# § 1772g. Omitted

##### § 1772g. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–228, § 9](/us/pl/100/228/s9), Dec. 31, 1987, [101 Stat. 1561](/us/stat/101/1561), which described results of revocation of the Settlement Agreement or any part thereof, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
