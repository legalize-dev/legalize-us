# § 4227. Regulations

##### § 4227. Regulations

The Secretary shall issue final regulations necessary to carry out this subchapter not later than October 1, 2001.

*([Pub. L. 104–330, title VIII, § 807](/us/pl/104/330/tVIII/s807), as added [Pub. L. 106–568, title II, § 203](/us/pl/106/568/tII/s203), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2885](/us/stat/114/2885), and [Pub. L. 106–569, title V, § 513](/us/pl/106/569/tV/s513), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2979](/us/stat/114/2979).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 106–568, § 203](/us/pl/106/568/s203), and [Pub. L. 106–569, § 513](/us/pl/106/569/s513), enacted substantially identical sections 807 of [Pub. L. 104–330](/us/pl/104/330). This section is based on the text of [section 807 of Pub. L. 104–330](/us/pl/104/330/s807), as added by [Pub. L. 106–569, § 513](/us/pl/106/569/s513). [Section 807 of Pub. L. 104–330](/us/pl/104/330/s807), as added by [Pub. L. 106–568, § 203](/us/pl/106/568/s203), required the issuance of final regulations not later than October 1, 2000, instead of October 1, 2001.
