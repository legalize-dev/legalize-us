# § 422k. Supplement to Federal reclamation laws; short title

##### § 422k. Supplement to Federal reclamation laws; short title

This subchapter shall be a supplement to the Federal reclamation laws and may be cited as the Small Reclamation Projects Act of 1956.

*([Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 972, § 11](/us/act/1956-08-06/ch972/s11), [70 Stat. 1047](/us/stat/70/1047).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Federal reclamation laws, referred to in text, are defined in [section 422b of this title](/us/usc/t43/s422b).
