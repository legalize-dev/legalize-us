# § 1434. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–60, title II, § 203(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/60/tII/s203/a/1), Aug. 15, 1979, [93 Stat. 398](/us/stat/93/398)

##### § 1434. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–60, title II, § 203(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/60/tII/s203/a/1), Aug. 15, 1979, [93 Stat. 398](/us/stat/93/398)

Section, acts [Jan. 27, 1948, ch. 36](/us/act/1948-01-27/ch36), title X, § 1001, [62 Stat. 13](/us/stat/62/13); [Apr. 5, 1952, ch. 159, § 1](/us/act/1952-04-05/ch159/s1), [66 Stat. 43](/us/stat/66/43); 1977 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 7(a)(1), 42 F.R. 62461, [91 Stat. 1637](/us/stat/91/1637); 1978 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 102, eff. Jan. 1, 1979, 43 F.R. 36037, [92 Stat. 3783](/us/stat/92/3783), provided for loyalty check of personnel. [Pub. L. 87–256, § 111(a)(2)](/us/pl/87/256/s111/a/2), Sept. 21, 1961, [75 Stat. 538](/us/stat/75/538), previously repealed this section insofar as it related to persons employed or assigned to duties under the Mutual Educational and Cultural Exchange Act of 1961.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1979, see [section 209 of Pub. L. 96–60](/us/pl/96/60/s209), set out as an Effective Date of 1979 Amendment note under [section 1471 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1471).
