# § 1471i. Administration

##### § 1471i. Administration

(a) Regulations The Commodity Credit Corporation shall issue regulations to carry out this subchapter.

(b) Processing and decisions to be made as quickly as practicable Such regulations shall establish procedures to ensure that the request for assistance by a Governor or county committee under [section 1471b of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1471b), and individual applications of livestock producers under [section 1471c of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1471c) for assistance, are processed and decisions thereon are made as quickly as practicable.

(c) Indigenous plants not considered feed on hand For purposes of this subchapter, indigenous plants available to a livestock producer but not normally consumed by livestock as feed, such as cactus, may not be considered as feed on hand for such producers.

*([Oct. 31, 1949, ch. 792](/us/act/1949-10-31/ch792), title VI, § 611, as added [Pub. L. 100–387, title I, § 101(a)](/us/pl/100/387/tI/s101/a), Aug. 11, 1988, [102 Stat. 931](/us/stat/102/931).)*
