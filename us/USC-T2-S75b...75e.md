# §§ 75b to 75e. Omitted

##### §§ 75b to 75e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 75b, [act May 1, 1947, ch. 49, title I](/us/act/1947-05-01/ch49/tI), [61 Stat. 58](/us/stat/61/58), accorded Clerk of House the same priority as executive agencies under the Surplus Property Act of 1944, [act Oct. 3, 1944, ch. 479](/us/act/1944-10-03/ch479), [58 Stat. 765](/us/stat/58/765). The Surplus Property Act of 1944, which was formerly classified principally to sections 1611 to 1646 of the former Appendix to Title 50, War and National Defense, was repealed, with certain exceptions, by [act June 30, 1949, ch. 288, title V, § 503](/us/act/1949-06-30/ch288/tV/s503), [63 Stat. 399](/us/stat/63/399), and the priorities thereunder expired Dec. 31, 1949. See References in Text note under [section 352 of Title 30](/us/usc/t30/s352), Mineral Lands and Mining.

Sections 75c to 75e were omitted from the Code for lack of general applicability.

Section 75c, based on H. Res. No. 449, Sept. 21, 1961, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 87–730, § 103](/us/pl/87/730/s103), Oct. 2, 1962, [76 Stat. 693](/us/stat/76/693), related to basic compensation of Assistant Tally Clerks in Office of Clerk of House.

Section 75d, based on H. Res. No. 331, June 7, 1961, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 87–730, § 103](/us/pl/87/730/s103), Oct. 2, 1962, [76 Stat. 693](/us/stat/76/693), related to basic compensation of stationery and assistant stationery clerks.

Section 75e, based on H. Res. Nos. 225, 341, 402 and 773 of the 87th Congress, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 87–130, § 103](/us/pl/87/130/s103), Aug. 10, 1961, [75 Stat. 334](/us/stat/75/334); [Pub. L. 87–730, § 103](/us/pl/87/730/s103), Oct. 2, 1962, [76 Stat. 693](/us/stat/76/693); [Pub. L. 88–248, § 103](/us/pl/88/248/s103), Dec. 30, 1963, [77 Stat. 817](/us/stat/77/817), related to compensation of certain laborers and clerks in offices of Clerk, Doorkeeper and Postmaster of House.
