# § 713c. Omitted

##### § 713c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–165, § 5](/us/pl/98/165/s5), Nov. 22, 1983, [97 Stat. 1065](/us/stat/97/1065), which related to Interim Council to be the governing body of the tribe until the tribal governing body established in accordance with [section 713d of this title](/us/usc/t25/s713d) would first convene, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
