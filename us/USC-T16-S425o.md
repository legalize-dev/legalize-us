# § 425o. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 425o. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of sections 425k to 425*o* of this title.

*([Pub. L. 101–214, § 6](/us/pl/101/214/s6), Dec. 11, 1989, [103 Stat. 1851](/us/stat/103/1851).)*
