# § 134. Simplified acquisition threshold

##### § 134. Simplified acquisition threshold

In division B, the term “simplified acquisition threshold” means $250,000.

*([Pub. L. 111–350, § 3](/us/pl/111/350/s3), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3682](/us/stat/124/3682); [Pub. L. 115–91, div. A, title VIII, § 805](/us/pl/115/91/dA/tVIII/s805), Dec. 12, 2017, [131 Stat. 1456](/us/stat/131/1456).)*
