# § 2008f. Crop insurance requirement

##### § 2008f. Crop insurance requirement

(a) In general As a condition of obtaining any benefit (including a direct loan, loan guarantee, or payment) described in subsection (b), a borrower must obtain at least catastrophic risk protection insurance coverage under [section 1508 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1508) for the crop and crop year for which the benefit is sought, if the coverage is offered by the Corporation.

(b) Applicable benefits Subsection (a) shall apply to—

(1) a farm ownership loan (FO) under [section 1923 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1923);

(2) an operating loan (OL) under [section 1942 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1942); and

(3) an emergency loan (EM) under [section 1961 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1961).

*([Pub. L. 87–128, title III, § 371](/us/pl/87/128/tIII/s371), as added [Pub. L. 103–354, title I, § 119(b)](/us/pl/103/354/tI/s119/b), Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3208](/us/stat/108/3208).)*
