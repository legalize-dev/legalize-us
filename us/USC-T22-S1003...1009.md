# §§ 1003 to 1009. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

##### §§ 1003 to 1009. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

Section 1003, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 633](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s633), as added [Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23, § 7](/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23/s7), [69 Stat. 25](/us/stat/69/25), related to selection-out process and regulations.

A prior section 633 of [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI), [60 Stat. 1015](/us/stat/60/1015), which provided that Secretary prescribe maximum period during which Foreign Service officers in classes 2 or 3 remain in force without promotion and for retirement from Service for any officer who did not receive a promotion within such period, was repealed by [act Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23, § 7](/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23/s7), [69 Stat. 25](/us/stat/69/25).

Section 1004, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 634](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s634), as added [Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23, § 7](/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23/s7), [69 Stat. 25](/us/stat/69/25); amended [July 28, 1956, ch. 770, § 7](/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770/s7), [70 Stat. 704](/us/stat/70/704); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 22](/us/pl/86/723/s22), [74 Stat. 835](/us/stat/74/835); July 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–350, title V, § 520](/us/pl/94/350/tV/s520), [90 Stat. 846](/us/stat/90/846), related to selection-out benefits.

A prior section 634 of [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI), [60 Stat. 1015](/us/stat/60/1015), which provided selection-out benefits to officers in classes 4 and 5 was repealed by [act Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23, § 7](/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23/s7), [69 Stat. 25](/us/stat/69/25).

Section 1005, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title VI, § 635, [60 Stat. 1016](/us/stat/60/1016); [July 28, 1956, ch. 770, § 8](/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770/s8), [70 Stat. 704](/us/stat/70/704); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 23](/us/pl/86/723/s23), [74 Stat. 836](/us/stat/74/836), related to status, etc., of officers in class 7 or 8.

Section 1006, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title VI, § 636, [60 Stat. 1016](/us/stat/60/1016); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 24](/us/pl/86/723/s24), [74 Stat. 836](/us/stat/74/836), related to voluntary retirement.

Section 1007, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title VI, § 637, [60 Stat. 1016](/us/stat/60/1016); [July 28, 1956, ch. 770, § 8](/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770/s8), [70 Stat. 704](/us/stat/70/704); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 25(a)](/us/pl/86/723/s25/a), [74 Stat. 836](/us/stat/74/836); July 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–350, title V, § 521](/us/pl/94/350/tV/s521), [90 Stat. 846](/us/stat/90/846), related to separation for cause of officers and employees.

Section 1008, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title VI, § 638, [60 Stat. 1016](/us/stat/60/1016); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 26](/us/pl/86/723/s26), [74 Stat. 837](/us/stat/74/837), related to termination of limited appointments of Reserve officers, and staff officers and employees.

Section 1009, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 639](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s639), as added Oct. 7, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–426, title IV, § 404](/us/pl/95/426/tIV/s404), [92 Stat. 979](/us/stat/92/979), related to professional career counseling, advice, and placement assistance.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).
