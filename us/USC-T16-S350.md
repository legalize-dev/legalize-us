# § 350. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–429, § 3(b)](/us/pl/94/429/s3/b), Sept. 28, 1976, [90 Stat. 1342](/us/stat/90/1342)

##### § 350. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–429, § 3(b)](/us/pl/94/429/s3/b), Sept. 28, 1976, [90 Stat. 1342](/us/stat/90/1342)

Section, [act Feb. 26, 1917, ch. 121, § 4](/us/act/1917-02-26/ch121/s4), [39 Stat. 938](/us/stat/39/938), provided that nothing in sections 347 to 349, 351 to 353, and 354 of this title was to affect the mineral land laws applicable to lands in the park prior to Feb. 26, 1917.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Mining Rights Existing Prior to September 28, 1976

[Section 3 of Pub. L. 94–429](/us/pl/94/429/s3) provided in part that this section was repealed in order to close area to entry and location under the Mining Law of 1872, subject to valid existing rights.
