# § 3661. Use of information for sentencing

##### § 3661. Use of information for sentencing

No limitation shall be placed on the information concerning the background, character, and conduct of a person convicted of an offense which a court of the United States may receive and consider for the purpose of imposing an appropriate sentence.

*(Added [Pub. L. 91–452, title X, § 1001(a)](/us/pl/91/452/tX/s1001/a), Oct. 15, 1970, [84 Stat. 951](/us/stat/84/951), § 3577; renumbered § 3661, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 212(a)(1)](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s212/a/1), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 1987](/us/stat/98/1987).)*
