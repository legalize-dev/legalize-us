# §§ 3281 to 3283. Omitted

##### §§ 3281 to 3283. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 3281, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7001](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7001), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 274](/us/stat/102/274), provided that this subchapter could be cited as the “Bilingual Education Act”.

A prior section 3281, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 801](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s801), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII, § 801(3)](/us/pl/95/561/tVIII/s801/3), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2284](/us/stat/92/2284), set out short title for program for community schools, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s587/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480](/us/stat/95/480), eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

Section 3282, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7002](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7002), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 274](/us/stat/102/274), stated policy of Congress and authorized appropriations.

A prior section 3282, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s802), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII, § 801(3)](/us/pl/95/561/tVIII/s801/3), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2284](/us/stat/92/2284), provided Congressional statement of findings and purpose, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s587/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480](/us/stat/95/480), eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

Section 3283, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7003](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7003), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 276](/us/stat/102/276), defined terms used in this subchapter and contained provisions relating to regulations.

Prior sections 3283 to 3290 were repealed by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s587/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480](/us/stat/95/480), eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

Section 3283, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 803](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s803), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII, § 801(3)](/us/pl/95/561/tVIII/s801/3), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2285](/us/stat/92/2285), provided a definition of a community education program.

Section 3284, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 804](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s804), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII, § 801(3)](/us/pl/95/561/tVIII/s801/3), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2285](/us/stat/92/2285); amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 301(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/88/tIII/s301/a/1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677](/us/stat/93/677), 692, authorized State programs for community education.

Section 3285, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 805](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s805), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII, § 801(3)](/us/pl/95/561/tVIII/s801/3), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2286](/us/stat/92/2286), related to the use of community education programs for non-Federal contribution in certain Federal programs.
