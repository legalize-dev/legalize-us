# § 10512. Applications

##### § 10512. Applications

To request a grant under this subchapter, the chief executive officer of a State or unit of local government shall submit an application in such form as the Attorney General may require.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2402](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2402), as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXI, § 210302(c)(1)(C)](/us/pl/103/322/tXXI/s210302/c/1/C), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2066](/us/stat/108/2066).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification
