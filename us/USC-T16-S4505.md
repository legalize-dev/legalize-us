# § 4505. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 4505. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 101–513, title VI, § 610](/us/pl/101/513/tVI/s610), formerly § 606, Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 2072](/us/stat/104/2072); renumbered § 610, [Pub. L. 102–574, § 2(a)(1)](/us/pl/102/574/s2/a/1), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4593](/us/stat/106/4593).)*
