# § 2212. Transferred

##### § 2212. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–510, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141](/us/pl/101/510/dC/tXXXI/s3141), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1837](/us/stat/104/1837), which related to contractor liability for injury or loss of property arising out of atomic weapons testing programs, was renumbered [section 4803 of Pub. L. 107–314](/us/pl/107/314/s4803) by [Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(k)(4)(A)](/us/pl/108/136/dC/tXXXI/s3141/k/4/A)–(C), Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1783](/us/stat/117/1783), and transferred to former [section 2783 of Title 50](/us/usc/t50/s2783), War and National Defense. See [section 6324 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s6324), Armed Forces.
