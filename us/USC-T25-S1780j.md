#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 109–286, § 12](/us/pl/109/286/s12), Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1225](/us/stat/120/1225), which related to the conveyance of the Northern Tier Land, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
