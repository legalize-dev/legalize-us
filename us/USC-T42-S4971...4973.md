# § 4971 to 4973. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title II, § 2121](/us/pl/111/13/tII/s2121), Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1584](/us/stat/123/1584)

##### § 4971 to 4973. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title II, § 2121](/us/pl/111/13/tII/s2121), Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1584](/us/stat/123/1584)

Section 4971, [Pub. L. 93–113, title I, § 111](/us/pl/93/113/tI/s111), Oct. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 399](/us/stat/87/399); [Pub. L. 98–288, § 9](/us/pl/98/288/s9), May 21, 1984, [98 Stat. 191](/us/stat/98/191); [Pub. L. 99–551, § 5](/us/pl/99/551/s5), Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3073](/us/stat/100/3073); [Pub. L. 101–204, title II, § 201(2)](/us/pl/101/204/tII/s201/2), Dec. 7, 1989, [103 Stat. 1813](/us/stat/103/1813); [Pub. L. 103–82, title III, § 330(a)(2)](/us/pl/103/82/tIII/s330/a/2), (3), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 902](/us/stat/107/902), set forth the congressional statement of purpose for the University Year for VISTA program of full-time volunteer service by students enrolled in institutions of higher education.

Section 4972, [Pub. L. 93–113, title I, § 112](/us/pl/93/113/tI/s112), Oct. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 399](/us/stat/87/399); [Pub. L. 98–288, § 30(b)(1)](/us/pl/98/288/s30/b/1), May 21, 1984, [98 Stat. 197](/us/stat/98/197); [Pub. Section 4973, [Pub. L. 93–113, title I, § 113](/us/pl/93/113/tI/s113), Oct. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 399](/us/stat/87/399); [Pub. L. 96–143, § 5](/us/pl/96/143/s5), Dec. 13, 1979, [93 Stat. 1075](/us/stat/93/1075); [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 608(f)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s608/f/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 488](/us/stat/95/488); [Pub. L. 103–82, title III, § 330(a)(2)](/us/pl/103/82/tIII/s330/a/2), (3), (b), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 902](/us/stat/107/902), set out special conditions related to the University Year for VISTA program.
