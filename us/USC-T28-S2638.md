# § 2638. New grounds in support of a civil action

##### § 2638. New grounds in support of a civil action

(1) applies to the same merchandise that was the subject of the protest; and

(2) is related to the same administrative decision listed in section 514 of the Tariff Act of 1930 that was contested in the protest.

*(Added [Pub. L. 96–417, title III, § 301](/us/pl/96/417/tIII/s301), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1736](/us/stat/94/1736).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Section 515 of the Tariff Act of 1930, referred to in text, is classified to [section 1515 of Title 19](/us/usc/t19/s1515), Customs Duties.

Section 514 of the Tariff Act of 1930, referred to in par. (2), is classified to [section 1514 of Title 19](/us/usc/t19/s1514).
