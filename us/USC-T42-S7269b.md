# § 7269b. Transfer of unexpended appropriation balances

##### § 7269b. Transfer of unexpended appropriation balances

The unexpended balances of prior appropriations provided for activities in this Act or subsequent Energy and Water Development Appropriations Acts may on and after October 2, 1992, be transferred to appropriation accounts for such activities established pursuant to this title.11 See References in Text note below. Balances so transferred may be merged with funds in the applicable established accounts and thereafter may be accounted for as one fund for the same time period as originally enacted.

*([Pub. L. 102–377, title III, § 303](/us/pl/102/377/tIII/s303), Oct. 2, 1992, [106 Stat. 1339](/us/stat/106/1339).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This title, referred to in text, is title III of [Pub. L. 102–377](/us/pl/102/377), Oct. 2, 1992, [106 Stat. 1332](/us/stat/106/1332). For complete classification of title III to the Code, see Tables.

#### Codification

Section was enacted as part of the Energy and Water Development Appropriations Act, 1993, and not as part of the Department of Energy Organization Act which comprises this chapter.
