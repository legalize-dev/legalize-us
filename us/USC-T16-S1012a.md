# § 1012a. Funding

##### § 1012a. Funding

In addition to any other funds made available by this chapter, of the funds of the Commodity Credit Corporation, the Secretary shall make available to carry out this chapter $150,000,000 for fiscal year 2026 and each fiscal year thereafter, to remain available until expended.

*([Aug. 4, 1954, ch. 656, § 15](/us/act/1954-08-04/ch656/s15), as added [Pub. L. 115–334, title II, § 2401(c)](/us/pl/115/334/tII/s2401/c), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4570](/us/stat/132/4570); amended [Pub. L. 119–21, title I, § 10601(e)](/us/pl/119/21/tI/s10601/e), July 4, 2025, [139 Stat. 108](/us/stat/139/108).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2025—[Pub. L. 119–21](/us/pl/119/21) substituted “$150,000,000 for fiscal year 2026 and each fiscal year thereafter, to remain available until expended” for “$50,000,000 for fiscal year 2019 and each fiscal year thereafter”.
