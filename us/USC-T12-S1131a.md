# § 1131a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### § 1131a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section, [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title I, § 3](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98/tI/s3), [48 Stat. 257](/us/stat/48/257), provided for the granting of a charter to the Production Credit Corporation, and authorized the directors to adopt by-laws. Section 3 of act June 16, 1933, was amended by [act July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, § 105(b)](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741/tI/s105/b), [70 Stat. 665](/us/stat/70/665), to eliminate provisions relating to the Production Credit Corporation. Section 3 of act June 16, 1933, was classified in its entirety to [section 1134a of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1134a).
