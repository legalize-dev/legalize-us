# § 72b–1. Omitted

##### § 72b–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title I, § 134(b)](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tI/s134/b), [60 Stat. 832](/us/stat/60/832), related to reports of committees and subcommittees of the Senate and House of Representatives on employed personnel. See [section 72c of this title](/us/usc/t2/s72c) and the Standing Rules of the Senate. Section 2(a) of Senate Resolution No. 274, Ninety-sixth Congress, Nov. 14, 1979, provided in part that this section, insofar as it relates to the Senate, is repealed.
