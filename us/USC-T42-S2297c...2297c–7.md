# §§ 2297c to 2297c–7. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–134, title III, § 3116(a)(1)](/us/pl/104/134/tIII/s3116/a/1), Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–349](/us/stat/110/1321-349)

##### §§ 2297c to 2297c–7. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–134, title III, § 3116(a)(1)](/us/pl/104/134/tIII/s3116/a/1), Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–349](/us/stat/110/1321-349)

Section 2297c, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1401](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1401), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2934](/us/stat/106/2934); amended [Pub. L. 102–572, title IX, § 902(b)(1)](/us/pl/102/572/tIX/s902/b/1), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4516](/us/stat/106/4516), related to marketing and contracting authority of United States Enrichment Corporation.

Section 2297c–1, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1402](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1402), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2934](/us/stat/106/2934), related to Corporation pricing policy for Department of Energy and other customers.

Section 2297c–2, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1403](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1403), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2935](/us/stat/106/2935), related to leasing of gaseous diffusion facilities of Department of Energy.

Section 2297c–3, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1404](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1404), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2935](/us/stat/106/2935), related to capital structure of Corporation.

Section 2297c–4, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1405](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1405), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2936](/us/stat/106/2936), authorized Corporation to apply for licenses for use of patented inventions and discoveries.

Section 2297c–5, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1406](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1406), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2936](/us/stat/106/2936), related to liability for acts committed before and after transition date.

Section 2297c–6, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1407](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1407), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2937](/us/stat/106/2937), related to transfer of uranium inventories to Corporation.

Section 2297c–7, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1408](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1408), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2937](/us/stat/106/2937), related to purchase of highly enriched uranium from former Soviet Union.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective as of date on which 100 percent of ownership of United States Enrichment Corporation has been transferred to private investors (July 28, 1998), see [section 3116(a)(1) of Pub. L. 104–134](/us/pl/104/134/s3116/a/1), set out as a note under former [section 2297 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2297).
