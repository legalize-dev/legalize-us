# §§ 2881 to 2883. Omitted

##### §§ 2881 to 2883. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 2881, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1461](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1461), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 199](/us/stat/102/199), required report on State and local evaluations.

A prior section 2881, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 201](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s201), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2201](/us/stat/92/2201), set out the purpose of this part, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s587/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480](/us/stat/95/480), eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

Section 2882, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1462](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1462), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 199](/us/stat/102/199), required national study on effect of programs on children.

A prior section 2882, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 202](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s202), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2202](/us/stat/92/2202), related to applications for grants and awards under this part, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s587/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480](/us/stat/95/480), eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

Section 2883, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1463](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1463), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 199](/us/stat/102/199), authorized appropriations.

Prior sections 2883 to 2890 were repealed by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s587/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480](/us/stat/95/480), eff. Oct. 1, 1982.

Section 2883, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 203](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s203), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2202](/us/stat/92/2202), related to acceptance of gifts by the Secretary.

Section 2884, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 204](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s204), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2202](/us/stat/92/2202), authorized Secretary to make grants and contracts under this part.

Section 2885, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 205](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s205), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2202](/us/stat/92/2202), provided for instruction in basic skills.

Section 2886, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 206](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s206), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2203](/us/stat/92/2203), related to parental participation in basic skills instruction.

Section 2887, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 207](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s207), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2203](/us/stat/92/2203), related to use of technology in basic skills instruction.

Section 2888, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 208](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s208), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2204](/us/stat/92/2204), related to involvement of educational agencies and private organizations in improving basic skills.

Section 2889, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 209](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s209), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2204](/us/stat/92/2204), related to collection and dissemination of information relating to basic skills programs.

Section 2890, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 210](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s210), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/561/tII/s201), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2204](/us/stat/92/2204), related to coordination of programs under this part with other Federal programs.
