# § 12111. Support of Federal prisoners in non-Federal institutions

##### § 12111. Support of Federal prisoners in non-Federal institutions

(a) In general The Attorney General may make payments to States and units of local government for the purposes authorized in [section 4013 of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s4013).

(b) Authorization of appropriations Notwithstanding any other provision of this part other than [section 12108(a)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t34/s12108/a/2), there are authorized to be appropriated from amounts authorized under [section 12108 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s12108) for each of fiscal years 1996 through 2000 such sums as may be necessary to carry out this section.
