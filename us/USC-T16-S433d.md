# § 433d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–568, § 3(2)](/us/pl/92/568/s3/2), Oct. 26, 1972, [86 Stat. 1182](/us/stat/86/1182)

##### § 433d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–568, § 3(2)](/us/pl/92/568/s3/2), Oct. 26, 1972, [86 Stat. 1182](/us/stat/86/1182)

Section, [act June 2, 1936, ch. 477, § 4](/us/act/1936-06-02/ch477/s4), [49 Stat. 1394](/us/stat/49/1394), provided that members of Perry’s Victory Memorial Commission created by [act Mar. 3, 1919, ch. 116](/us/act/1919-03-03/ch116), [40 Stat. 1322](/us/stat/40/1322), act as a board of advisors, and also provided for number of members, method of filling vacancies, and travel expenses but no compensation for the members.
