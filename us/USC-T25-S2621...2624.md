##### §§ 2621 to 2624. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 367](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s367), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3976](/us/stat/108/3976)

Section 2621, [Pub. L. 100–297, title V, § 5321](/us/pl/100/297/tV/s5321), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 403](/us/stat/102/403); [Pub. L. 100–427, § 18](/us/pl/100/427/s18), Sept. 9, 1988, [102 Stat. 1612](/us/stat/102/1612), related to improvement of educational opportunities for Indian children.
