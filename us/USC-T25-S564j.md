#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 11](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s11), [68 Stat. 721](/us/stat/68/721), which related to taxation of property distributed under this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
