# § 12252. Eligibility to receive grants

##### § 12252. Eligibility to receive grants

To be eligible to receive a grant under [section 12251 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s12251), a State shall submit to the Attorney General an application at such time, in such form, and containing such information as the Attorney General reasonably may require by rule.

*([Pub. L. 103–322, title III, § 31912](/us/pl/103/322/tIII/s31912), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1895](/us/stat/108/1895).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 13892 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s13892), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
