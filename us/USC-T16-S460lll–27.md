# § 460lll–27. Designation of national recreation trail

##### § 460lll–27. Designation of national recreation trail

Effective on the date of transfer pursuant to section 460*lll*–41 of this title, the North-South Trail is designated as a national recreation trail under [section 1243 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s1243).

*([Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(e) [title V, § 527]](/us/pl/105/277/dA/s101/e/tV/s527), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–231](/us/stat/112/2681-231), 2681–317.)*
