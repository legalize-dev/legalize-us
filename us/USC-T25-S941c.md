# § 941c. Omitted

##### § 941c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–116, § 5](/us/pl/103/116/s5), Oct. 27, 1993, [107 Stat. 1122](/us/stat/107/1122), which authorized appropriation and provided for disbursement of funds in accordance with the settlement agreement, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
