# § 4705. Regulations

##### § 4705. Regulations

The Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe regulations to carry out the purpose of this chapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 95–454, title VI, § 601(a)](/us/pl/95/454/tVI/s601/a), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1188](/us/stat/92/1188), § 4706; renumbered § 4705, [Pub. L. 105–362, title XIII, § 1302(b)(2)(B)(i)](/us/pl/105/362/tXIII/s1302/b/2/B/i), Nov. 10, 1998, [112 Stat. 3293](/us/stat/112/3293).)*
