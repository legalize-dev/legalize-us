# § 564b. Omitted

##### § 564b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 3](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s3), [68 Stat. 718](/us/stat/68/718), which related to membership in tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
