# § 1709b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–364, § 6](/us/pl/85/364/s6), Apr. 1, 1958, [72 Stat. 77](/us/stat/72/77)

##### § 1709b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–364, § 6](/us/pl/85/364/s6), Apr. 1, 1958, [72 Stat. 77](/us/stat/72/77)

Section, [Pub. L. 85–104, title VI, § 605](/us/pl/85/104/tVI/s605), July 12, 1957, [71 Stat. 305](/us/stat/71/305), authorized Federal Housing Commissioner and Administrator of Veterans’ Affairs to fix reasonable limits on charges, fees, and discounts imposed upon builders, sellers, or purchasers.
