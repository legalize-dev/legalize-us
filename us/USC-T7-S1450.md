# § 1450. Repealed. [Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041](/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041), title II, § 203(b), [68 Stat. 899](/us/stat/68/899)

##### § 1450. Repealed. [Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041](/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041), title II, § 203(b), [68 Stat. 899](/us/stat/68/899)

Section, acts [Mar. 31, 1950, ch. 81, § 5](/us/act/1950-03-31/ch81/s5), [64 Stat. 42](/us/stat/64/42); [Jan. 30, 1954, ch. 2, § 5(a)](/us/act/1954-01-30/ch2/s5/a), [68 Stat. 7](/us/stat/68/7), provided that for the crop year of 1951 and thereafter, no price support would be available for Irish potatoes unless marketing quotas were in effect.
