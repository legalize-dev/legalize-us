# §§ 109a to 109d. Transferred

##### §§ 109a to 109d. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 109a to 109c transferred to sections 1353a, 1353b, and 1353d, respectively, of this title.

Section 109d, acts [July 1, 1943, ch. 182](/us/act/1943-07-01/ch182), title II, [57 Stat. 288](/us/stat/57/288); [June 28, 1944, ch. 294](/us/act/1944-06-28/ch294), title II, [58 Stat. 413](/us/stat/58/413); [May 21, 1945, ch. 129](/us/act/1945-05-21/ch129), title II, [59 Stat. 186](/us/stat/59/186); [July 5, 1946, ch. 541](/us/act/1946-07-05/ch541), title II, [60 Stat. 463](/us/stat/60/463); [July 9, 1947, ch. 211](/us/act/1947-07-09/ch211), title II, [61 Stat. 292](/us/stat/61/292); [June 3, 1948, ch. 400](/us/act/1948-06-03/ch400), title II, [62 Stat. 316](/us/stat/62/316); [July 20, 1949, ch. 354](/us/act/1949-07-20/ch354), title II, [63 Stat. 460](/us/stat/63/460); [Sept. 6, 1950, ch. 896](/us/act/1950-09-06/ch896), ch. III, title II, [64 Stat. 618](/us/stat/64/618), which related to employment of interpreters in the Immigration and Naturalization Service, was transferred to section 342f of former Title 5, Executive Departments and Government Officers and Employees, and subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554](/us/pl/89/554), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378), 632, which enacted Title 5, Government Organization and Employees. See [section 1555 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1555).
