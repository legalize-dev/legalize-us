# § 218. Civil penalties

##### § 218. Civil penalties

Any person who violates the provisions of this subchapter shall be subject to a civil penalty of not more than $10,000, and each day shall constitute a separate offense.

*([Aug. 29, 1935, ch. 814](/us/act/1935-08-29/ch814), title II, § 207, as added [Pub. L. 100–690, title VIII, § 8001(a)(3)](/us/pl/100/690/tVIII/s8001/a/3), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4520](/us/stat/102/4520).)*
