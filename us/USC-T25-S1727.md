# § 1727. Omitted

##### § 1727. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–420, § 8](/us/pl/96/420/s8), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1795](/us/stat/94/1795), which provided for implementation of the Indian Child Welfare Act in jurisdiction over Indian child custody proceedings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
