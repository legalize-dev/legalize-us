# § 640. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–531, § 26](/us/pl/93/531/s26), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1723](/us/stat/88/1723)

##### § 640. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–531, § 26](/us/pl/93/531/s26), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1723](/us/stat/88/1723)

Section, [act Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92, § 10](/us/act/1950-04-19/ch92/s10), [64 Stat. 47](/us/stat/64/47), established Joint Committee on Navajo-Hopi Indian Administration, with function of making a continuous study of programs for administration and rehabilitation of Navajo and Hopi Indians.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 93–531, § 26](/us/pl/93/531/s26), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1723](/us/stat/88/1723), provided that the repeal is effective as of the close of business December 31, 1974.
