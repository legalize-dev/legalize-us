# § 3750. Transferred

##### § 3750. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 3750 was editorially reclassified as [section 10151 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10151), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

#### Prior Provisions

Prior sections 3750a to 3750d were repealed by [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI, § 1111(b)(2)](/us/pl/109/162/tXI/s1111/b/2), (d), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3101](/us/stat/119/3101), 3102, applicable with respect to the first fiscal year beginning after Jan. 5, 2006, and each fiscal year thereafter.

Section 3750a, [Pub. L. 102–519, title I, § 130](/us/pl/102/519/tI/s130), Oct. 25, 1992, [106 Stat. 3386](/us/stat/106/3386), related to the purpose of former sections 3750a to 3750d, to supplement the provisions of the Edward Byrne Memorial State and Local Law Enforcement Assistance Program to help States to curb motor vehicle thefts and related violence, and authorization of grants to Anti Car Theft Committees.

Section 3750b, [Pub. L. 102–519, title I, § 131](/us/pl/102/519/tI/s131), Oct. 25, 1992, [106 Stat. 3386](/us/stat/106/3386), related to application for grants.

Section 3750c, [Pub. L. 102–519, title I, § 132](/us/pl/102/519/tI/s132), Oct. 25, 1992, [106 Stat. 3387](/us/stat/106/3387), related to award of grants.

Section 3750d, [Pub. L. 102–519, title I, § 133](/us/pl/102/519/tI/s133), Oct. 25, 1992, [106 Stat. 3387](/us/stat/106/3387), related to authorization of appropriations.

Other prior sections 3750 to 3750d of this title, which comprised a prior part E of title I of [Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351), were omitted in the general amendment of title I of [Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351) by [Pub. L. 96–157, § 2](/us/pl/96/157/s2), Dec. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 1167](/us/stat/93/1167).

L. 93–83, § 2](/us/pl/93/83/s2), Aug. 6, 1973, [87 Stat. 209](/us/stat/87/209), set out Congressional statement of purpose in providing a system of grants for correctional institutions and facilities.
