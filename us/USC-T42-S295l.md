# § 295l. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–392, title I, § 106(a)(2)(C)](/us/pl/105/392/tI/s106/a/2/C), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3557](/us/stat/112/3557)

##### § 295l. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–392, title I, § 106(a)(2)(C)](/us/pl/105/392/tI/s106/a/2/C), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3557](/us/stat/112/3557)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title VII, § 793](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tVII/s793), as added [Pub. L. 102–408, title I, § 102](/us/pl/102/408/tI/s102), Oct. 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 2061](/us/stat/106/2061); amended [Pub. L. 102–531, title III, § 313(a)(6)](/us/pl/102/531/tIII/s313/a/6), Oct. 27, 1992, [106 Stat. 3507](/us/stat/106/3507), required development, publication, dissemination, and biennial report to Congress on statistics respecting public and community health personnel.

A prior section 793 of act July 1, 1944, was classified to [section 295h–1c of this title](/us/usc/t42/s295h–1c) prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 102–408](/us/pl/102/408).

Another prior section 793 of act July 1, 1944, was renumbered section 794 by [Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35) and classified to [section 295h–2 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s295h–2).
