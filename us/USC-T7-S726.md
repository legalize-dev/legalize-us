# § 726. Omitted

##### § 726. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 6, 1934, ch. 409](/us/act/1934-06-06/ch409), [48 Stat. 911](/us/stat/48/911), related to administration of oaths for tax exemption certificates, and was rendered inoperative by repeal of sections 721 to 723 and 725 of this title by [act Feb. 10, 1936, ch. 42](/us/act/1936-02-10/ch42), [49 Stat. 1106](/us/stat/49/1106).
