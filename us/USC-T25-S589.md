# § 589. Omitted

##### § 589. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–206, § 9](/us/pl/92/206/s9), Dec. 18, 1971, [85 Stat. 739](/us/stat/85/739), which exempted distributed funds from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
