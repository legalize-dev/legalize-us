#### Codification

B, title V, § 501](/us/pl/104/208/dB/tV/s501), Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3009–537](/us/stat/110/3009-537); [Pub. L. 115–103, title III, § 301](/us/pl/115/103/tIII/s301), Jan. 8, 2018, [131 Stat. 2258](/us/stat/131/2258), which related to transfer to the Secretary of the Interior of land to be held in trust for the Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
