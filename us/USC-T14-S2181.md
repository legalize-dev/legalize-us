# § 2181. Physical fitness of officers

##### § 2181. Physical fitness of officers

The Secretary shall prescribe regulations under which the physical fitness of officers to perform their duties shall be periodically determined.

*(Added [Pub. L. 88–130, § 1(10)(C)](/us/pl/88/130/s1/10/C), Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 190](/us/stat/77/190), § 335; renumbered § 2181, [Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 112(b)](/us/pl/115/282/tI/s112/b), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4216](/us/stat/132/4216).)*
