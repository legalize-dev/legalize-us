# §§ 225a to 227. Transferred

##### §§ 225a to 227. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 225a, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V, § 507](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV/s507), as added June 24, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–31, § 5](/us/pl/90/31/s5), [81 Stat. 79](/us/stat/81/79); amended Oct. 27, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–513, title I, § 3(c)](/us/pl/91/513/tI/s3/c), [84 Stat. 1241](/us/stat/84/1241); Apr. 22, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–278, title XI, § 1102(b)](/us/pl/94/278/tXI/s1102/b), [90 Stat. 415](/us/stat/90/415); Oct. 7, 1980, [Pub. L. 96–398, title VIII, § 804(b)](/us/pl/96/398/tVIII/s804/b), [94 Stat. 1603](/us/stat/94/1603); Aug. 13, 1981, [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX, § 902(g)(2)](/us/pl/97/35/tIX/s902/g/2), [95 Stat. 560](/us/stat/95/560), which related to availability of appropriations for grants to Federal institutions, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238d of this title](/us/usc/t42/s238d).

A prior section 507 of [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV), providing for settlement of accounts of deceased officers, was classified to [section 225 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s225) and subsequently repealed.

Section 226, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V, § 508](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV/s508), [58 Stat. 711](/us/stat/58/711); 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 1, §§ 5, 8, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631](/us/stat/67/631); 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 102, eff. July 1, 1970, 35 F.R. 7959, [84 Stat. 2085](/us/stat/84/2085), which related to transfer of funds between appropriations, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238e of this title](/us/usc/t42/s238e).

Section 227, acts [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title V, § 509 [58 Stat. 711](/us/stat/58/711); [June 16, 1948, ch. 481, § 6(b)](/us/act/1948-06-16/ch481/s6/b), [62 Stat. 469](/us/stat/62/469); [June 25, 1948, ch. 654, § 7](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch654/s7), [62 Stat. 1018](/us/stat/62/1018); Reorg. Plan No. 1 of 1953 §§ 5, 8, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631](/us/stat/67/631), which related to availability of appropriations for carrying out purposes of this chapter, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238f of this title](/us/usc/t42/s238f).
