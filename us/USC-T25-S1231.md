# § 1231. Omitted

##### § 1231. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–417, § 1](/us/pl/91/417/s1), Sept. 25, 1970, [84 Stat. 868](/us/stat/84/868), which provided for judgment funds to be distributed in equal shares to enrolled members of the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
