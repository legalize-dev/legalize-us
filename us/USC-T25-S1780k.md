# § 1780k. Omitted

##### § 1780k. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 109–286, § 13](/us/pl/109/286/s13), Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1228](/us/stat/120/1228), which provided for boundaries between the Pueblo of Santa Clara and the Pueblo de San Ildefonso, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
