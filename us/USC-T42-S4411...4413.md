##### §§ 4411 to 4413. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–288, title VII, § 703](/us/pl/93/288/tVII/s703), formerly title VI, § 603, May 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 164](/us/stat/88/164); renumbered title VII, § 703, [Pub. L. 103–337, div. C, title XXXIV, § 3411(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/337/dC/tXXXIV/s3411/a/1), (2), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 3100](/us/stat/108/3100)

Section 4411, [Pub. L. 91–606, title II, § 201](/us/pl/91/606/tII/s201), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1746](/us/stat/84/1746), related to cooperation of Federal agencies of a Federal coordinating officer.

Section 4412, [Pub. L. 91–606, title II, § 202](/us/pl/91/606/tII/s202), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1746](/us/stat/84/1746), related to formation of emergency support teams of Federal personnel.

Section 4413, [Pub. L. 91–606, title II, § 203](/us/pl/91/606/tII/s203), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1747](/us/stat/84/1747), related to cooperation of Federal agencies in rendering emergency assistance, the scope of services, non-liability of the Federal government, employment of temporary personnel, rules and regulations, review of programs, and reports to Congress.
