# § 715e–1. Omitted

##### § 715e–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 15, 1935, ch. 261, title III, § 304](/us/act/1935-06-15/ch261/tIII/s304), [49 Stat. 382](/us/stat/49/382), applying [section 715e of this title](/us/usc/t16/s715e) to exchanges effected under former sections 715d–1 and 715d–2, has been omitted due to the repeal of sections 715d–1 and 715d–2 by [Pub. L. 89–669, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/669/s8/a), Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 930](/us/stat/80/930).
