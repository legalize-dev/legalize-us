# § 633. Omitted

##### § 633. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92, § 3](/us/act/1950-04-19/ch92/s3), [64 Stat. 45](/us/stat/64/45), which provided that Navajo and Hopi Indians be given preference in employment and on-the-job training for projects undertaken pursuant to this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
