# §§ 5021 to 5023. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/i), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

##### §§ 5021 to 5023. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/i), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

Section 5021, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6027](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6027), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1478](/us/stat/102/1478), authorized Presidential Awards for Teaching Excellence in Foreign Languages.

Section 5022, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6028](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6028), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1479](/us/stat/102/1479), contained administrative provisions.

Section 5023, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6029](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6029), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1479](/us/stat/102/1479), authorized appropriations.
