# § 575. Omitted

##### § 575. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 27, 1939, ch. 387, § 5](/us/act/1939-07-27/ch387/s5), [53 Stat. 1129](/us/stat/53/1129), which related to restoration of lands to tribal ownership, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
