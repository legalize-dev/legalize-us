# § 367. Short title

##### § 367. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “Railroad Unemployment Insurance Act”.

*([June 25, 1938, ch. 680, § 17](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch680/s17), [52 Stat. 1113](/us/stat/52/1113).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [act June 25, 1938, ch. 680](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch680), [52 Stat. 1094](/us/stat/52/1094), which enacted this chapter and amended sections 503 and 1104 and former [section 1107 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s1107), The Public Health and Welfare. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

#### Codification

Another section 17 of Act June 25, 1938, was classified to [section 368 of this title](/us/usc/t45/s368) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–251, § 5(c)](/us/pl/104/251/s5/c), Oct. 9, 1996, [110 Stat. 3165](/us/stat/110/3165).
