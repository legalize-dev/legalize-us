# § 941n. Omitted

##### § 941n. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–116, § 16](/us/pl/103/116/s16), Oct. 27, 1993, [107 Stat. 1137](/us/stat/107/1137), which related to tax treatment of income and transactions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
