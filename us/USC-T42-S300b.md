# § 300b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title XXI, § 2193(b)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tXXI/s2193/b/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 827](/us/stat/95/827)

##### § 300b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title XXI, § 2193(b)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tXXI/s2193/b/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 827](/us/stat/95/827)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XI, § 1101](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXI/s1101), as added Apr. 22, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–278, title IV, § 403(a)](/us/pl/94/278/tIV/s403/a), [90 Stat. 407](/us/stat/90/407); amended Nov. 10, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–626, title II, § 205(b)](/us/pl/95/626/tII/s205/b), (d)(2), (e), [92 Stat. 3583](/us/stat/92/3583), 3584; [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)](/us/pl/96/88/tV/s509/b), Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695](/us/stat/93/695); Aug. 13, 1981, [Pub. L. 97–35, title XXI, § 2193(a)(1)(B)](/us/pl/97/35/tXXI/s2193/a/1/B), [95 Stat. 826](/us/stat/95/826); Jan. 4, 1983, [Pub. L. 97–414, § 8](/us/pl/97/414/s8)(*o*), [96 Stat. 2061](/us/stat/96/2061), related to testing, counseling, information and education programs.

A prior section 300b, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XI, § 1101](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXI/s1101), as added May 16, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–294, § 3(c)](/us/pl/92/294/s3/c), [86 Stat. 137](/us/stat/86/137); amended Aug. 29, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–414, § 4(2)](/us/pl/92/414/s4/2), [86 Stat. 652](/us/stat/86/652), authorized Secretary to make grants and enter contracts with public and nonprofit private entities with respect to establishment of voluntary sickle cell anemia screening and counseling programs and to develop and disseminate informational and educational materials relating to sickle cell anemia, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–278, title IV, § 403(a)](/us/pl/94/278/tIV/s403/a), Apr. 22, 1976, [90 Stat. 407](/us/stat/90/407).
