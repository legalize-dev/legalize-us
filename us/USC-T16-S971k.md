# § 971k. Savings clause

##### § 971k. Savings clause

Nothing in this chapter shall have the effect of diminishing the rights and obligations of any Nation under Article VIII(3) of the Convention.

*([Pub. L. 94–70, § 11](/us/pl/94/70/s11), formerly § 12, as added [Pub. L. 104–43, title III, § 308](/us/pl/104/43/tIII/s308), Nov. 3, 1995, [109 Stat. 387](/us/stat/109/387); amended [Pub. L. 114–81, title I, § 107(5)](/us/pl/114/81/tI/s107/5), Nov. 5, 2015, [129 Stat. 658](/us/stat/129/658).)*
