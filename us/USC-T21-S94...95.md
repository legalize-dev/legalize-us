# §§ 94 to 95. Transferred

##### §§ 94 to 95. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 94, [act June 30, 1914, ch. 131](/us/act/1914-06-30/ch131), [38 Stat. 420](/us/stat/38/420), which related to inspection of reindeer, was transferred to [section 692 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s692).

Section 94a, [act May 23, 1908, ch. 192](/us/act/1908-05-23/ch192), [35 Stat. 254](/us/stat/35/254), which related to inspection of dairy products for export, was transferred to [section 693 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s693).

Section 95, acts [June 30, 1906, ch. 3913](/us/act/1906-06-30/ch3913), [34 Stat. 679](/us/stat/34/679); [June 26, 1934, ch. 756, § 2](/us/act/1934-06-26/ch756/s2), [48 Stat. 1225](/us/stat/48/1225), which related to authorization of appropriations for expenses of inspection, was transferred to [section 694 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s694).
