# § 2105. Nonliability of United States

##### § 2105. Nonliability of United States

The Government of the United States shall have no liability in connection with any housing unit, or necessary land therefor, or adaptation acquired under the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1169](/us/stat/72/1169), § 805; [Pub. L. 96–385, title III, § 301(d)](/us/pl/96/385/tIII/s301/d), Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1531](/us/stat/94/1531); renumbered § 2105, [Pub. L. 102–83, § 5(a)](/us/pl/102/83/s5/a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406](/us/stat/105/406).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

Prior section 2105, [Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1224](/us/stat/72/1224), related to administration of mustering-out payments provisions, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–50, § 1(a)](/us/pl/89/50/s1/a), June 24, 1965, [79 Stat. 173](/us/stat/79/173), effective July 1, 1966.

#### Amendments

1991—[Pub. L. 102–83](/us/pl/102/83) renumbered [section 805 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s805) as this section.

1980—[Pub. L. 96–385](/us/pl/96/385) made the United States not liable for any adaptation in connection with any housing unit.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of 1980 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 96–385](/us/pl/96/385) effective Oct. 1, 1980, see [section 601(b) of Pub. L. 96–385](/us/pl/96/385/s601/b), set out as a note under [section 1114 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s1114).
