# § 1506. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1506. Authorization of appropriations

Necessary amounts may be appropriated to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 111–350, § 3](/us/pl/111/350/s3), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3700](/us/stat/124/3700).)*
