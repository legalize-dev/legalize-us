# § 1774. Omitted

##### § 1774. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–503, § 2](/us/pl/101/503/s2), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1292](/us/stat/104/1292), which set out congressional findings and purposes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 101–503, § 1](/us/pl/101/503/s1), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1292](/us/stat/104/1292), provided that [Pub. L. 101–503](/us/pl/101/503), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Seneca Nation Settlement Act of 1990”.
