# § 1328. Termination

##### § 1328. Termination

This subchapter shall terminate on December 31, 2033.

*(Added [Pub. L. 115–390, title II, § 202(a)](/us/pl/115/390/tII/s202/a), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5188](/us/stat/132/5188); amended [Pub. L. 117–263, div. E, title LIX, § 5949(k)(1)](/us/pl/117/263/dE/tLIX/s5949/k/1), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 3492](/us/stat/136/3492).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–263](/us/pl/117/263) substituted “December 31, 2033” for “the date that is 5 years after the date of the enactment of the Federal Acquisition Supply Chain Security Act of 2018”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective 90 days after Dec. 21, 2018, and applicable to contracts that are awarded before, on, or after that date, see [section 202(c) of Pub. L. 115–390](/us/pl/115/390/s202/c), set out as a note under [section 1321 of this title](/us/usc/t41/s1321).

Title II of [Pub. L. 115–390](/us/pl/115/390) effective 90 days after Dec. 21, 2018, see [section 205 of Pub. L. 115–390](/us/pl/115/390/s205), set out as a note under [section 1321 of this title](/us/usc/t41/s1321).
