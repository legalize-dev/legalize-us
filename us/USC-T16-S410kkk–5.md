# § 410kkk–5. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 410kkk–5. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 108–387, title I, § 107](/us/pl/108/387/tI/s107), Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2236](/us/stat/118/2236).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 108–387](/us/pl/108/387), Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2234](/us/stat/118/2234), which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 410kkk of this title](/us/usc/t16/s410kkk) and Tables.
