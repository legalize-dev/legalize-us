# §§ 1051 to 1053. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 1051 to 1053. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 1051, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 204(a)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s204/a), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1456](/us/stat/42/1456); amended [Mar. 4, 1925, ch. 524, § 2](/us/act/1925-03-04/ch524/s2), [43 Stat. 1262](/us/stat/43/1262); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 7](/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164/s7), [49 Stat. 316](/us/stat/49/316); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 104(d), [70 Stat. 664](/us/stat/70/664), provided for discount rates to be charged by intermediate credit banks. See [section 2075 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2075).

Section 1052, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 204(b)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s204/b), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1456](/us/stat/42/1456); amended Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, placed a limitation on interest rate charged original borrower on paper discounted with bank.

Section 1053, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 204(c)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s204/c), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1456](/us/stat/42/1456); amended Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 29](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s29), [50 Stat. 715](/us/stat/50/715), covered purchases by intermediate credit banks of debentures or other similar obligations issued by or for benefit of such bank or other intermediate credit banks.
