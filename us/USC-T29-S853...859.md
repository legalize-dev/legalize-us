# §§ 853 to 859. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–300, title I, § 184(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/300/tI/s184/a/1), Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1357](/us/stat/96/1357)

##### §§ 853 to 859. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–300, title I, § 184(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/300/tI/s184/a/1), Oct. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1357](/us/stat/96/1357)

Section 853, [Pub. L. 93–203, title II, § 231](/us/pl/93/203/tII/s231), as added [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2](/us/pl/95/524/s2), Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1957](/us/stat/92/1957), set out the Congressional statement of purpose for transitional employment opportunities for the economically disadvantaged.

Section 854, [Pub. L. 93–203, title II, § 232](/us/pl/93/203/tII/s232), as added [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2](/us/pl/95/524/s2), Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1958](/us/stat/92/1958), related to financial assistance to prime sponsors for transitional public service employment for economically disadvantaged persons who are unemployed.

Section 855, [Pub. L. 93–203, title II, § 233](/us/pl/93/203/tII/s233), as added [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2](/us/pl/95/524/s2), Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1958](/us/stat/92/1958); amended [Pub. L. 96–583, § 3(b)](/us/pl/96/583/s3/b), Dec. 23, 1980, [94 Stat. 3376](/us/stat/94/3376), related to allocation of funds for carrying out of transitional employment opportunities for the economically disadvantaged.

Section 856, [Pub. L. 93–203, title II, § 234](/us/pl/93/203/tII/s234), as added [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2](/us/pl/95/524/s2), Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1959](/us/stat/92/1959), related to expenditure of funds by prime sponsors for purposes of transitional employment opportunities for economically disadvantaged.

Section 857, [Pub. L. 93–203, title II, § 235](/us/pl/93/203/tII/s235), as added [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2](/us/pl/95/524/s2), Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1959](/us/stat/92/1959), related to applicability of [section 966 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s966) to transitional employment opportunities for economically disadvantaged.

Section 858, [Pub. L. 93–203, title II, § 236](/us/pl/93/203/tII/s236), as added [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2](/us/pl/95/524/s2), Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1959](/us/stat/92/1959), related to eligibility for transitional employment opportunities for economically disadvantaged.

Section 859, [Pub. L. 93–203, title II, § 237](/us/pl/93/203/tII/s237), as added [Pub. L. 95–524, § 2](/us/pl/95/524/s2), Oct. 27, 1978, [92 Stat. 1960](/us/stat/92/1960), related to payment of wages to economically disadvantaged individuals employed in transitional public service employment.
