# § 9094. Assignment of payments

##### § 9094. Assignment of payments

(a) In general The provisions of [section 590h(g) of title 16](/us/usc/t16/s590h/g), relating to assignment of payments, shall apply to payments made under this chapter.

(b) Notice The producer making the assignment, or the assignee, shall provide the Secretary with notice, in such manner as the Secretary may require, of any assignment made under this section.

*([Pub. L. 113–79, title I, § 1611](/us/pl/113/79/tI/s1611), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 710](/us/stat/128/710).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in subsec. (a), was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 113–79](/us/pl/113/79), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 649](/us/stat/128/649), which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Tables.
