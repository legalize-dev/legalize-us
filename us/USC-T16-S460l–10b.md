# § 460l–10b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

##### § 460l–10b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

Section, [Pub. L. 88–578, title I, § 10](/us/pl/88/578/tI/s10), formerly § 9, as added [Pub. L. 90–401, § 4](/us/pl/90/401/s4), July 15, 1968, [82 Stat. 355](/us/stat/82/355); renumbered § 10, [Pub. L. 92–347, § 2](/us/pl/92/347/s2), July 11, 1972, [86 Stat. 459](/us/stat/86/459), related to contracts for options to acquire lands and waters in national park system. See [section 200309 of Title 54](/us/usc/t54/s200309), National Park Service and Related Programs.

A prior [section 10 of Pub. L. 88–578](/us/pl/88/578/s10) was renumbered section 11 and was classified to section 460*l*–10c of this title prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 113–287](/us/pl/113/287).
