# § 207. Omitted

##### § 207. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, acts [Jan. 31, 1899, ch. 78, § 7](/us/act/1899-01-31/ch78/s7), [30 Stat. 811](/us/stat/30/811); [July 1, 1916, ch. 209](/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209), [39 Stat. 309](/us/stat/39/309), related to return of nonresident indigent insane in District of Columbia.

Section, which had been set out as [section 17 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s17) of the 1929 edition of the D.C. Code, was omitted from the 1940 edition of the D.C. Code as superseded by section 8 of [act Aug. 9, 1939, ch. 620](/us/act/1939-08-09/ch620), [53 Stat. 1297](/us/stat/53/1297) (D.C. Code, § 21–317). The act of Aug. 9, 1939, was repealed by [Pub. L. 88–597, § 19(a)](/us/pl/88/597/s19/a), Sept. 15, 1964, [78 Stat. 953](/us/stat/78/953).
