# § 20943. Federal assistance in identification and location of sex offenders relocated as a result of a major disaster

##### § 20943. Federal assistance in identification and location of sex offenders relocated as a result of a major disaster

The Attorney General shall provide assistance to jurisdictions in the identification and location of a sex offender relocated as a result of a major disaster.

*([Pub. L. 109–248, title I, § 144](/us/pl/109/248/tI/s144), July 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 606](/us/stat/120/606).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 16943 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s16943), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
