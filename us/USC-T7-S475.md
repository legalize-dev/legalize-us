L. 85–430, § 2](/us/pl/85/430/s2), [72 Stat. 149](/us/stat/72/149); June 30, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–331, § 1](/us/pl/92/331/s1), [86 Stat. 400](/us/stat/86/400), directed Secretary of Agriculture to issue cotton crop reports at certain times during growing and harvesting season.
