# §§ 9863 to 9865. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(w)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/w), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4025](/us/stat/108/4025)

##### §§ 9863 to 9865. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(w)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/w), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4025](/us/stat/108/4025)

Section 9863, [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 664](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s664), as added [Pub. L. 101–501, title II, § 204(a)](/us/pl/101/501/tII/s204/a), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1245](/us/stat/104/1245), authorized financial assistance for research regarding Follow Through programs and approaches.

A prior section 9863, [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 664](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s664), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 509](/us/stat/95/509), related to grants or contracts for research, demonstration, and pilot projects, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 101–501, title II, § 204(a)](/us/pl/101/501/tII/s204/a), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1245](/us/stat/104/1245).

Section 9863a, [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 664A](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s664A), as added [Pub. L. 101–501, title II, § 204(a)](/us/pl/101/501/tII/s204/a), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1245](/us/stat/104/1245), related to technical assistance and training.

Section 9863b, [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 664B](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s664B), as added [Pub. L. 101–501, title II, § 204(a)](/us/pl/101/501/tII/s204/a), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1246](/us/stat/104/1246), authorized grants to entities which operate, or previously operated, effective Follow Through programs.

Section 9863c, [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 664C](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s664C), as added [Pub. L. 101–501, title II, § 204(a)](/us/pl/101/501/tII/s204/a), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1246](/us/stat/104/1246), related to grant to establish national clearinghouse on Follow Through programs.

Section 9864, [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 665](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s665), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 510](/us/stat/95/510); [Pub. L. 101–501, title II, § 205](/us/pl/101/501/tII/s205), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1246](/us/stat/104/1246), related to announcement of research and evaluation contracts.

Section 9865, [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 666](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s666), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 510](/us/stat/95/510); [Pub. L. 101–501, title II, § 206](/us/pl/101/501/tII/s206), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1247](/us/stat/104/1247), related to continuing evaluation of programs under this subchapter.
