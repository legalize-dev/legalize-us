# § 8544. Civilian teachers: number; compensation

##### § 8544. Civilian teachers: number; compensation

The Secretary of the Navy may employ as many civilians as he considers necessary to serve at the Naval Postgraduate School under the direction of the President of the school as senior professors, professors, associate professors, assistant professors, and instructors. The Secretary shall prescribe the compensation of those persons.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 437](/us/stat/70A/437), § 7044; [Pub. L. 108–375, div. A, title V, § 557(a)(4)(A)](/us/pl/108/375/dA/tV/s557/a/4/A), Oct. 28, 2004, [118 Stat. 1915](/us/stat/118/1915); renumbered § 8544, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(c)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/c/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1836](/us/stat/132/1836).)*

The words “as many * * * as he considers necessary” are substituted for the words “such number * * * as in his opinion may be necessary for the proper instruction of students in the theoretical, academic, and scientific subjects pertaining to the technical and practical aspects of the naval profession” for brevity.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 8544, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 527](/us/stat/70A/527), restricted performance of civil functions by commissioned officers of Regular Air Force, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 90–235, § 4(a)(6)](/us/pl/90/235/s4/a/6), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 759](/us/stat/81/759). See [section 973 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s973).

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–232](/us/pl/115/232) renumbered [section 7044 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s7044) as this section.

2004—[Pub. L. 108–375](/us/pl/108/375) substituted “President of the school” for “Superintendent”.
