# § 563. Omitted

##### § 563. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 29, 1953, ch. 86, § 1](/us/act/1953-05-29/ch86/s1), [67 Stat. 40](/us/stat/67/40), which provided for payment of salaries and expenses for Klamath Tribe Officials out of tribal funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 563, acts [June 25, 1938, ch. 710](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch710), [52 Stat. 1207](/us/stat/52/1207); [Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 519](/us/act/1939-08-07/ch519), [53 Stat. 1244](/us/stat/53/1244); [May 15, 1945, ch. 123](/us/act/1945-05-15/ch123), [59 Stat. 167](/us/stat/59/167), provided for payment of salaries and expenses of Klamath Tribe officials out of tribal funds but limited the amount of such expenditures to $15,000 per annum, prior to repeal by [act May 29, 1953, ch. 86, § 2](/us/act/1953-05-29/ch86/s2), [67 Stat. 40](/us/stat/67/40).
