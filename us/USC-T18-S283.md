# [§ 283. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–849, § 2](/us/pl/87/849/s2), Oct. 23, 1962, [76 Stat. 1126](/us/stat/76/1126); [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title VIII, § 822(a)](/us/pl/100/180/dA/tVIII/s822/a), Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1132](/us/stat/101/1132)]

##### [§ 283. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–849, § 2](/us/pl/87/849/s2), Oct. 23, 1962, [76 Stat. 1126](/us/stat/76/1126); [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title VIII, § 822(a)](/us/pl/100/180/dA/tVIII/s822/a), Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1132](/us/stat/101/1132)]

Section, acts [June 25, 1948, ch. 645](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645), [62 Stat. 697](/us/stat/62/697); [June 28, 1949, ch. 268, § 2(b)](/us/act/1949-06-28/ch268/s2/b), [63 Stat. 280](/us/stat/63/280), related to officers or employees interested in claims against the government. [Pub. L. 87–849](/us/pl/87/849) continued limited applicability to retired officers of the Armed Forces of the United States. [Pub. L. 100–180](/us/pl/100/180) repealed section to the extent that it had not been repealed by [section 2 of Pub. L. 87–849](/us/pl/87/849/s2). Section was supplanted by [section 205 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s205).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 90 days after Oct. 23, 1962, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 87–849](/us/pl/87/849/s4), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 201 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s201).
