# §§ 287c–31 to 287c–34. Transferred

##### §§ 287c–31 to 287c–34. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 287c–31, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title IV, § 485E](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIV/s485E), as added [Pub. L. 106–525, title I, § 101(a)](/us/pl/106/525/tI/s101/a), Nov. 22, 2000, [114 Stat. 2497](/us/stat/114/2497); amended [Pub. L. 109–482, title I](/us/pl/109/482/tI), §§ 103(b)(44), 104(b)(1)(N), Jan. 15, 2007, [120 Stat. 3688](/us/stat/120/3688), 3693, which set out the purpose of the National Center on Minority Health and Health Disparities, was renumbered section 464z–3 of act July 1, 1944, and transferred to [section 285t of this title](/us/usc/t42/s285t).

Section 287c–32, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title IV, § 485F](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIV/s485F), as added [Pub. L. 106–525, title I, § 102](/us/pl/106/525/tI/s102), Nov. 22, 2000, [114 Stat. 2501](/us/stat/114/2501); amended [Pub. L. 109–482, title I, § 103(b)(45)](/us/pl/109/482/tI/s103/b/45), Jan. 15, 2007, [120 Stat. 3688](/us/stat/120/3688), which related to centers of excellence for research education and training, was renumbered section 464z–4 of act July 1, 1944, and transferred to [section 285t–1 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s285t–1).

Section 287c–33, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title IV, § 485G](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIV/s485G), as added [Pub. L. 106–525, title I, § 103](/us/pl/106/525/tI/s103), Nov. 22, 2000, [114 Stat. 2503](/us/stat/114/2503); amended [Pub. L. 109–482, title I, § 103(b)(46)](/us/pl/109/482/tI/s103/b/46), Jan. 15, 2007, [120 Stat. 3688](/us/stat/120/3688), which related to loan repayment program for minority health disparities research, was renumbered section 464z–5 of act July 1, 1944, and transferred to [section 285t–2 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s285t–2), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 114–255, div. A, title II, § 2022(c)(1)](/us/pl/114/255/dA/tII/s2022/c/1), Dec. 13, 2016, [130 Stat. 1054](/us/stat/130/1054).

Section 287c–34, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title IV, § 485H](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIV/s485H), as added [Pub. L. 106–525, title I, § 104](/us/pl/106/525/tI/s104), Nov. 22, 2000, [114 Stat. 2503](/us/stat/114/2503); amended [Pub. L. 109–482, title I, § 104(b)(1)(O)](/us/pl/109/482/tI/s104/b/1/O), Jan. 15, 2007, [120 Stat. 3693](/us/stat/120/3693), which related to administrative support and support services, was renumbered section 464z–6 of act July 1, 1944, and transferred to [section 285t–3 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s285t–3).
