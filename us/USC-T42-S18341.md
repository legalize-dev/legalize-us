# § 18341. Commercial Cargo Development program

##### § 18341. Commercial Cargo Development program

(1) efforts to conduct a flight test;

(2) accelerate development; and

(3) develop the ground infrastructure needed for commercial cargo capability.

*([Pub. L. 111–267, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/111/267/tIV/s401), Oct. 11, 2010, [124 Stat. 2820](/us/stat/124/2820); [Pub. L. 115–10, title III, § 302(f)](/us/pl/115/10/tIII/s302/f), Mar. 21, 2017, [131 Stat. 26](/us/stat/131/26).)*
