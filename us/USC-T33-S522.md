# § 522. Existing provisions of law

##### § 522. Existing provisions of law

The first sentence of [section 494 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s494), and [section 502 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s502), shall be inapplicable with respect to any bridge to which the provisions of this subchapter are applicable.

*([June 21, 1940, ch. 409, § 12](/us/act/1940-06-21/ch409/s12), [54 Stat. 501](/us/stat/54/501); [Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8507(b), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4754](/us/stat/134/4754); [Pub. L. 117–263, div. K, title CXVIII, § 11805(a)](/us/pl/117/263/dK/tCXVIII/s11805/a), (c), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 4164](/us/stat/136/4164), 4165.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–263, § 11805(c)](/us/pl/117/263/s11805/c), struck out “, except to the extent provided in this section” before period at end.

[Pub. L. 117–263, § 11805(a)](/us/pl/117/263/s11805/a), reinstated [section 12(a) of the act of June 21, 1940](/us/act/1940-06-21/s12/a), as it appeared before repeal by [Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283), and redesignated it as the sole text of section 12.

2021—[Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283) repealed this section. Text of subsec. (a) was reinstated by [Pub. L. 117–263](/us/pl/117/263) and redesignated as text of entire section. See above.
