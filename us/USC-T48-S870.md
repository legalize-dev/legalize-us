##### § 870. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992)

Section, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145, § 46](/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145/s46), [39 Stat. 966](/us/stat/39/966); [Feb. 26, 1919, ch. 49](/us/act/1919-02-26/ch49), §§ 2, 4, [40 Stat. 1182](/us/stat/40/1182); [Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 501, § 6](/us/act/1939-08-07/ch501/s6), [53 Stat. 1226](/us/stat/53/1226), related to salaries of district court officials. See [section 604 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s604), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
