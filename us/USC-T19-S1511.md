# § 1511. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–410, title I, § 107](/us/pl/95/410/tI/s107), Oct. 3, 1978, [92 Stat. 892](/us/stat/92/892)

##### § 1511. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–410, title I, § 107](/us/pl/95/410/tI/s107), Oct. 3, 1978, [92 Stat. 892](/us/stat/92/892)

Section, acts [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 511, [46 Stat. 733](/us/stat/46/733); June 2, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–271, title III, § 301(p)](/us/pl/91/271/tIII/s301/p), [84 Stat. 290](/us/stat/84/290), provided for inspection of importer’s books. See sections 1508–1510 of this title.

Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [act May 27, 1921, ch. 14, § 405](/us/act/1921-05-27/ch14/s405), [42 Stat. 18](/us/stat/42/18), which was superseded by [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356, title IV, § 511](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356/tIV/s511), [42 Stat. 969](/us/stat/42/969). Section 511 of the 1922 act was superseded by section 511 of act June 17, 1930, comprising this section, and repealed by section 651(a)(1) of the 1930 act.

Earlier provisions for assessment of additional duty for failure or refusal of persons importing merchandise or dealing in imported merchandise to submit their books, records, etc., to inspection, were contained in [act Oct. 3, 1913, ch. 16](/us/act/1913-10-03/ch16), § III, V, [38 Stat. 190](/us/stat/38/190), prior to repeal by [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356, title IV, § 643](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356/tIV/s643), [42 Stat. 989](/us/stat/42/989).
