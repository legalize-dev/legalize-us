##### § 870. Repealed. [Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 837](/us/act/1956-08-01/ch837), title V, § 502(7), [70 Stat. 886](/us/stat/70/886)

Section, acts [Jan. 19, 1942, ch. 6, § 9](/us/act/1942-01-19/ch6/s9), [56 Stat. 8](/us/stat/56/8); [Mar. 29, 1944, ch. 141, § 4](/us/act/1944-03-29/ch141/s4), [58 Stat. 130](/us/stat/58/130); [July 15, 1954, ch. 507, § 15](/us/act/1954-07-15/ch507/s15), [68 Stat. 481](/us/stat/68/481), provided for payment of death gratuity to survivors of commissioned officers of Coast and Geodetic Survey.
