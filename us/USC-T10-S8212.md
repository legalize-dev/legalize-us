# § 8212. Additional regulations for Marine Corps

##### § 8212. Additional regulations for Marine Corps

The President may prescribe military regulations for the discipline of the Marine Corps.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 375](/us/stat/70A/375), § 6012; renumbered § 8212, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(b)(7)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/b/7), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/132/1834).)*

The words “such” and “as he may deem expedient” are omitted as surplusage.
