# § 5825. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–66, title I, § 1021(e)](/us/pl/104/66/tI/s1021/e), Dec. 21, 1995, [109 Stat. 713](/us/stat/109/713)

##### § 5825. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–66, title I, § 1021(e)](/us/pl/104/66/tI/s1021/e), Dec. 21, 1995, [109 Stat. 713](/us/stat/109/713)

Section, [Pub. L. 102–511, title III, § 305](/us/pl/102/511/tIII/s305), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 3335](/us/stat/106/3335), directed Secretary of Commerce to submit to Congress annual reports describing implementation of business and commercial development provisions, analyzing programs of other industrialized countries, and examining trade practices of other Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development nations.
