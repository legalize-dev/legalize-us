# § 300cc. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–482, title I, § 104(b)(2)(C)](/us/pl/109/482/tI/s104/b/2/C), Jan. 15, 2007, [120 Stat. 3693](/us/stat/120/3693)

##### § 300cc. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–482, title I, § 104(b)(2)(C)](/us/pl/109/482/tI/s104/b/2/C), Jan. 15, 2007, [120 Stat. 3693](/us/stat/120/3693)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title XXIII, § 2301](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tXXIII/s2301), as added [Pub. L. 100–607, title II, § 201(4)](/us/pl/100/607/tII/s201/4), Nov. 4, 1988, [102 Stat. 3063](/us/stat/102/3063); amended [Pub. L. 102–531, title III, § 312(d)(16)](/us/pl/102/531/tIII/s312/d/16), Oct. 27, 1992, [106 Stat. 3505](/us/stat/106/3505), required an annual comprehensive report on all expenditures by Secretary with respect to AIDS.

A prior section 300cc, act July 1, 1944, § 2301, was successively renumbered by subsequent acts and transferred, see [section 238 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s238).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable only with respect to amounts appropriated for fiscal year 2007 or subsequent fiscal years, see [section 109 of Pub. L. 109–482](/us/pl/109/482/s109), set out as an Effective Date of 2007 Amendment note under [section 281 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s281).
