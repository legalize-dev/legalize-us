# § 5811. Purpose

##### § 5811. Purpose

The purpose of this subchapter is to establish National Education Goals.

*([Pub. L. 103–227, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/227/tI/s101), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 130](/us/stat/108/130).)*
