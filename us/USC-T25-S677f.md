# § 677f. Omitted

##### § 677f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 7](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s7), [68 Stat. 869](/us/stat/68/869), which related to the employment of legal counsel for mixed-blood members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
