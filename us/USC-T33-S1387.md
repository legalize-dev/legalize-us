# § 1387. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1387. Authorization of appropriations

(1) $2,400,000,000 for fiscal year 2022;

(2) $2,750,000,000 for fiscal year 2023;

(3) $3,000,000,000 for fiscal year 2024; and

(4) $3,250,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2025 and 2026.

*([June 30, 1948, ch. 758](/us/act/1948-06-30/ch758), title VI, § 607, as added [Pub. L. 100–4, title II, § 212(a)](/us/pl/100/4/tII/s212/a), Feb. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 26](/us/stat/101/26); amended [Pub. L. 117–58, div. E, title II, § 50210(b)](/us/pl/117/58/dE/tII/s50210/b), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 1169](/us/stat/135/1169).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2021—[Pub. L. 117–58](/us/pl/117/58) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section related to appropriations for fiscal years 1989 to 1994.
