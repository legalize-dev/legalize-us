# § 4552. Authorization of appropriations for dairy farmer indemnities

##### § 4552. Authorization of appropriations for dairy farmer indemnities

There is hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 90–484, § 2](/us/pl/90/484/s2), Aug. 13, 1968, [82 Stat. 750](/us/stat/82/750).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 450k of this title](/us/usc/t7/s450k) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Section was enacted as part of [Pub. L. 90–484](/us/pl/90/484), and not as part of the Dairy Production Stabilization Act of 1983 which comprises this chapter.
