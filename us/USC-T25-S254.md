# § 254. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### § 254. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section, [act June 27, 1934, ch. 846](/us/act/1934-06-27/ch846), [48 Stat. 1245](/us/stat/48/1245), related to inapplicability of liquor laws to lands outside reservations free from restrictions against alienation. See sections 1154 and 1156 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 20](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s20), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862).
