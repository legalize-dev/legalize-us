# § 7239. Enlisted members: officers not to use as servants

##### § 7239. Enlisted members: officers not to use as servants

No officer of the Army may use an enlisted member of the Army as a servant.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 208](/us/stat/70A/208), § 3639; renumbered § 7239, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(b)(8)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/b/8), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1838](/us/stat/132/1838).)*

The words “in any case whatever” are omitted as surplusage.
