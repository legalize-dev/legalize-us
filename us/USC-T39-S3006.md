# [§ 3006. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–168, title I, § 105(b)(1)](/us/pl/106/168/tI/s105/b/1), Dec. 12, 1999, [113 Stat. 1811](/us/stat/113/1811)]

##### [§ 3006. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–168, title I, § 105(b)(1)](/us/pl/106/168/tI/s105/b/1), Dec. 12, 1999, [113 Stat. 1811](/us/stat/113/1811)]

Section, [Pub. L. 91–375](/us/pl/91/375), Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 747](/us/stat/84/747), prohibited persons from obtaining or attempting to obtain remittances of money or property of any kind through the mail for an obscene, lewd, lascivious, indecent, filthy, or vile thing.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 120 days after Dec. 12, 1999, see [section 111 of Pub. L. 106–168](/us/pl/106/168/s111), set out as an Effective Date of 1999 Amendment note under [section 3001 of this title](/us/usc/t39/s3001).
