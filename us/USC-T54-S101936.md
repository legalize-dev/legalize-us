# § 101936. Regulations

##### § 101936. Regulations

As soon as practicable after the effective date of this subchapter, the Secretary shall promulgate regulations appropriate for its implementation.

*(Added [Pub. L. 114–289, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/114/289/tVII/s701), Dec. 16, 2016, [130 Stat. 1493](/us/stat/130/1493).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The effective date of this subchapter, referred to in text, probably means the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 114–289](/us/pl/114/289), which enacted this subchapter and was approved Dec. 16, 2016.
