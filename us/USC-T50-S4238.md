# § 4238. Compliance with Budget Act

##### § 4238. Compliance with Budget Act

(1) the Fund established under [section 4233 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s4233),

(2) the trust established under [section 4235(b) of this title](/us/usc/t50/s4235/b), and

(3) the provisions of sections 4236 and 4237 of this title,

*([Pub. L. 100–383, title II, § 208](/us/pl/100/383/tII/s208), Aug. 10, 1988, [102 Stat. 916](/us/stat/102/916).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Budget Act, referred to in section catchline, probably means the Congressional Budget Act of 1974, titles I through IX of [Pub. L. 93–344](/us/pl/93/344), July 12, 1974, [88 Stat. 297](/us/stat/88/297). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 621 of Title 2](/us/usc/t2/s621), The Congress, and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to section 1989c–7 of the former Appendix to this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
