# § 856. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–429, § 7(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/429/s7/a/1), Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4388](/us/stat/108/4388)

##### § 856. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–429, § 7(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/429/s7/a/1), Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4388](/us/stat/108/4388)

Section, [Pub. L. 94–210, title VII, § 708](/us/pl/94/210/tVII/s708), as added [Pub. L. 102–533, § 4(a)](/us/pl/102/533/s4/a), Oct. 27, 1992, [106 Stat. 3516](/us/stat/106/3516), directed Secretary to develop and report to Congress within one year after Oct. 27, 1992, on a program master plan for improvements in passenger service between Boston and New York.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 103–429, § 7(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/429/s7/a/1), Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4388](/us/stat/108/4388), provided that the repeal by section 7(a)(1) is effective July 5, 1994.
