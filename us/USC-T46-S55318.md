# § 55318. Effect on other law

##### § 55318. Effect on other law

This subchapter does not affect chapter 5 of title 5.

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 8(c)](/us/pl/109/304/s8/c), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1648](/us/stat/120/1648).)*

The words “[section 1707a(b)(8) of title 7](/us/usc/t7/s1707a/b/8)” are omitted because the provision referred to has been repealed.
