# § 3371. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title VII, § 7614(a)](/us/pl/115/334/tVII/s7614/a), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4833](/us/stat/132/4833)

##### § 3371. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title VII, § 7614(a)](/us/pl/115/334/tVII/s7614/a), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4833](/us/stat/132/4833)

Section, [Pub. L. 95–113, title XIV, § 1492](/us/pl/95/113/tXIV/s1492), as added [Pub. L. 113–79, title VII, § 7128(a)](/us/pl/113/79/tVII/s7128/a), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 877](/us/stat/128/877), related to matching funds requirement.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal of section by [Pub. L. 115–334](/us/pl/115/334) applicable to grants, cooperative agreements, or other awards described in [Pub. L. 115–334, § 7614(b)](/us/pl/115/334/s7614/b) (amending sections 3157, 3322, 3333, 5813, 5924, 5925, 5925b, 7626, 7632, and 8114 of this title) that are made after Dec. 20, 2018, with matching funds requirement in effect on Dec. 20, 2018, to continue to apply to such grant, cooperative agreement, or other award, see [section 7614(c) of Pub. L. 115–334](/us/pl/115/334/s7614/c), set out as an Effective Date of 2018 Amendment note under [section 3151 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s3151).
