# §§ 241d to 241d–2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title I, § 101(c)](/us/pl/95/561/tI/s101/c), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2200](/us/stat/92/2200)

##### §§ 241d to 241d–2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title I, § 101(c)](/us/pl/95/561/tI/s101/c), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2200](/us/stat/92/2200)

Section 241d, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II, § 126](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII/s126), formerly § 121, as added Apr. 13, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 113(b)(6)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s113/b/6), [84 Stat. 126](/us/stat/84/126), renumbered Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(3)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s101/a/3), [88 Stat. 495](/us/stat/88/495), and amended Oct. 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–482, title V, § 501(b)(1)(B)](/us/pl/94/482/tV/s501/b/1/B), [90 Stat. 2236](/us/stat/90/2236), related to maximum entitlement for special incentive grants.

A prior section 241d, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title I, § 104](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tI/s104), formerly § 204, as added by [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 2](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s2), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 30](/us/stat/79/30), and renumbered by [Pub. L. 90–247, title I, § 110](/us/pl/90/247/tI/s110), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 787](/us/stat/81/787), making provision for special incentive grants to local educational agencies, was repealed by [Pub. L. 89–750, title I, § 108(a)](/us/pl/89/750/tI/s108/a), Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1195](/us/stat/80/1195). Such repeal, pursuant to [section 191 of Pub. L. 89–750](/us/pl/89/750/s191), was effective with respect to fiscal years beginning after June 30, 1966.

Provisions (for amount and distribution of special incentive grants) similar to those comprising this section, as added by [Pub. L. 91–230](/us/pl/91/230), were contained in [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title I, § 121(a)](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tI/s121/a), formerly title II, § 221(a), as added and renumbered Jan. 2, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–247, title I](/us/pl/90/247/tI), §§ 108(a)(5), 110, [81 Stat. 786](/us/stat/81/786), 787 (classified to [section 241h–1(a) of this title](/us/usc/t20/s241h–1/a)), prior to repeal thereof by [Pub. L. 91–230, § 113(b)(6)](/us/pl/91/230/s113/b/6).

Section 241d–1, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II, § 127](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII/s127), formerly § 122, as added Apr. 13, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 113(b)(6)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s113/b/6), [84 Stat. 126](/us/stat/84/126), renumbered Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(3)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s101/a/3), [88 Stat. 495](/us/stat/88/495), and amended Oct. 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–482, title V, § 501(a)(1)](/us/pl/94/482/tV/s501/a/1), [90 Stat. 2235](/us/stat/90/2235), related to application made for special incentive grants by States.

Provisions (for application and information therein) similar to those comprising part of this section were contained in [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title I, § 121(b)](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tI/s121/b), formerly title II, § 221(b), as added and renumbered Jan. 2, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–247, title I](/us/pl/90/247/tI), §§ 108(a)(5), 110, [81 Stat. 786](/us/stat/81/786), 787 (formerly classified to former [section 241h–1(b) of this title](/us/usc/t20/s241h–1/b)), prior to repeal thereof by [Pub. L. 91–230, § 113(b)(6)](/us/pl/91/230/s113/b/6).

Section 241d–2, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II, § 128](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII/s128), formerly § 123, as added Apr. 13, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 113(b)(6)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s113/b/6), [84 Stat. 127](/us/stat/84/127), renumbered Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(3)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s101/a/3), [88 Stat. 495](/us/stat/88/495), and amended Oct. 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–482, title V, § 501(a)(1)](/us/pl/94/482/tV/s501/a/1), [90 Stat. 2235](/us/stat/90/2235), defined “effort index”.

Provisions (defining “State effort index” and “national effort index”) similar to those comprising this section were contained in [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title I, § 121(c)](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tI/s121/c), formerly title II, § 221(c), as added and renumbered Jan. 2, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–247, title I](/us/pl/90/247/tI), §§ 108(a)(5), 110, [81 Stat. 786](/us/stat/81/786), 787 (formerly classified to former [section 241h–1(c) of this title](/us/usc/t20/s241h–1/c)), prior to repeal thereof by [Pub. L. 91–230, § 113(b)(6)](/us/pl/91/230/s113/b/6).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 1530(a) of Pub. L. 95–561](/us/pl/95/561/s1530/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 1221e–3 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1221e–3).
