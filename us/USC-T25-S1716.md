#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–395, title II, § 202](/us/pl/95/395/tII/s202), as added [Pub. L. 96–601, § 5(a)](/us/pl/96/601/s5/a), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3499](/us/stat/94/3499); amended [Pub. L. 99–514, § 2](/us/pl/99/514/s2), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2095](/us/stat/100/2095), which related to deferral of capital gains, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
