# § 7642. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 7642. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated such amount, not to exceed $30,000,000, as may be necessary for the purposes of this subchapter.

*([July 14, 1955, ch. 360](/us/act/1955-07-14/ch360), title IV, § 403, as added [Pub. L. 91–604, § 14](/us/pl/91/604/s14), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1710](/us/stat/84/1710).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Another section 403 of act July 14, 1955, as added by [Pub. L. 101–549, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/101/549/tIV/s401), Nov. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 2589](/us/stat/104/2589), is classified to [section 7651b of this title](/us/usc/t42/s7651b).
