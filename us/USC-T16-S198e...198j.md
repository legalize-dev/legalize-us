# §§ 198e to 198j. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 198e to 198j. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 198e, acts [Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 583, § 6](/us/act/1929-03-02/ch583/s6), [45 Stat. 1538](/us/stat/45/1538); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213), related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedures.

Section 198f, [act Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 583, § 7](/us/act/1929-03-02/ch583/s7), [45 Stat. 1538](/us/stat/45/1538), related to issuance of process and arrest. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

Section 198g, [act Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 583, § 8](/us/act/1929-03-02/ch583/s8), [45 Stat. 1538](/us/stat/45/1538), related to whom process is directed and arrest without process. See [section 3053 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3053), Crimes and Criminal Procedure, rule 4 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix, and rule 4 of Federal Rules of Civil Procedure, Title 28, Appendix, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 198h, acts [Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 583, § 9](/us/act/1929-03-02/ch583/s9), [45 Stat. 1539](/us/stat/45/1539); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213), related to salary.

Section 198i, [act Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 583, § 10](/us/act/1929-03-02/ch583/s10), [45 Stat. 1539](/us/stat/45/1539), related to fees, costs, and expenses.

Section 198j, [act Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 583, § 11](/us/act/1929-03-02/ch583/s11), [45 Stat. 1539](/us/stat/45/1539), related to disposition of fines and costs.
