# §§ 1314f to 1315a. Omitted

##### §§ 1314f to 1315a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 1314f to 1315a were omitted as not of general application, and as covered by the Canal Zone Code. The Canal Zone Code was subsequently redesignated the Panama Canal Code by [Pub. L. 96–70, § 3303(b)](/us/pl/96/70/s3303/b), and repealed by [Pub. L. 104–201, § 3549](/us/pl/104/201/s3549).

Section 1314f, [act Oct. 1, 1942, ch. 574](/us/act/1942-10-01/ch574), [56 Stat. 763](/us/stat/56/763), related to prohibition of production, possession, and disposition of marihuana.

Section 1314g, [act Oct. 1, 1942, ch. 574](/us/act/1942-10-01/ch574), [56 Stat. 763](/us/stat/56/763), related to defining terms for purposes of act Oct. 1, 1942.

Section 1314h, acts [Oct. 1, 1942, ch. 574](/us/act/1942-10-01/ch574), [56 Stat. 763](/us/stat/56/763); [Sept. 26, 1950, ch. 1049, § 2(b)](/us/act/1950-09-26/ch1049/s2/b), [64 Stat. 1038](/us/stat/64/1038), related to licenses for certain marihuana uses.

Section 1314i, [act Oct. 1, 1942, ch. 574](/us/act/1942-10-01/ch574), [56 Stat. 763](/us/stat/56/763), related to violations, punishment, and confiscation.

Section 1315, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 390, § 5](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch390/s5), [37 Stat. 562](/us/stat/37/562); [June 15, 1914, ch. 106, § 1](/us/act/1914-06-15/ch106/s1), 2, [38 Stat. 385](/us/stat/38/385), 386; [Aug. 24, 1937, ch. 752](/us/act/1937-08-24/ch752), [50 Stat. 750](/us/stat/50/750); [Sept. 26, 1950, ch. 1049, § 11](/us/act/1950-09-26/ch1049/s11), [64 Stat. 1042](/us/stat/64/1042), related to tolls generally.

Section 1315a, [act Sept. 26, 1950, ch. 1049, § 12](/us/act/1950-09-26/ch1049/s12), [64 Stat. 1042](/us/stat/64/1042), related to bases of tolls.
