# § 10445. Staff of Office on Violence Against Women

##### § 10445. Staff of Office on Violence Against Women

The Attorney General shall ensure that the Director has adequate staff to support the Director in carrying out the Director’s responsibilities under this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2005](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2005), as added [Pub. L. 107–273, div. A, title IV, § 402(3)](/us/pl/107/273/dA/tIV/s402/3), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1791](/us/stat/116/1791); amended [Pub. L. 117–103, div. W, title IX, § 901(d)](/us/pl/117/103/dW/tIX/s901/d), Mar. 15, 2022, [136 Stat. 911](/us/stat/136/911).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3796gg–0c of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796gg–0c), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 2005 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s2005) was renumbered section 2010 and is classified to [section 10449 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10449).

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–103](/us/pl/117/103) substituted “Office on Violence Against Women” for “Violence Against Women Office” in section catchline.
