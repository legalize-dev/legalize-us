# § 1831. Purpose

##### § 1831. Purpose

It is the purpose of this subchapter to provide grants for the encouragement of endowment funds for the operation and improvement of tribally controlled colleges or universities.

*([Pub. L. 95–471, title III, § 301](/us/pl/95/471/tIII/s301), as added [Pub. L. 98–192, § 13](/us/pl/98/192/s13), Dec. 1, 1983, [97 Stat. 1341](/us/stat/97/1341); amended [Pub. L. 105–244, title IX, § 901(b)(6)](/us/pl/105/244/tIX/s901/b/6), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1828](/us/stat/112/1828).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1998—[Pub. L. 105–244](/us/pl/105/244) substituted “colleges or universities” for “community colleges”.
