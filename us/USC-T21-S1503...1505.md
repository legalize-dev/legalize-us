# §§ 1503 to 1505. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1009](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1009), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4188](/us/stat/102/4188), as amended by [Pub. L. 105–20, § 2(b)](/us/pl/105/20/s2/b), June 27, 1997, [111 Stat. 234](/us/stat/111/234)

##### §§ 1503 to 1505. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1009](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1009), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4188](/us/stat/102/4188), as amended by [Pub. L. 105–20, § 2(b)](/us/pl/105/20/s2/b), June 27, 1997, [111 Stat. 234](/us/stat/111/234)

Section 1503, [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1004](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1004), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4184](/us/stat/102/4184), related to coordination between Office of National Drug Control Policy and executive branch departments and agencies. See [section 1704 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s1704).

Section 1504, [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1005](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1005), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4185](/us/stat/102/4185); [Pub. L. 103–322, title IX, § 90203](/us/pl/103/322/tIX/s90203), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1991](/us/stat/108/1991); [Pub. L. 105–20, § 2(b)](/us/pl/105/20/s2/b), June 27, 1997, [111 Stat. 234](/us/stat/111/234), related to annual development and submission of National Drug Control Strategy by President to Congress. See [section 1705 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s1705).

Section 1505, [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1008](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1008), formerly § 1003A, as added [Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title X, § 1011](/us/pl/101/510/dA/tX/s1011), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1633](/us/stat/104/1633); renumbered § 1008 and amended [Pub. L. 103–322, title IX, § 90204(a)](/us/pl/103/322/tIX/s90204/a), (b), (c)(2), (3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1993](/us/stat/108/1993), 1994, established Counter-Drug Technology Assessment Center within Office of National Drug Control Policy.

A prior section 1505, [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1008](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1008), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4188](/us/stat/102/4188), provided for an executive reorganization study and report to Congress and the President no later than Jan. 15, 1990, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–322, § 90204(c)(1)](/us/pl/103/322/s90204/c/1).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 30, 1997, see [section 1009 of Pub. L. 100–690](/us/pl/100/690/s1009), as amended, which was formerly classified to [section 1506 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s1506).
