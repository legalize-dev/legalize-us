# § 666a. Penalties

##### § 666a. Penalties

Any person who shall violate any rule or regulation promulgated in accordance with this Act shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and upon conviction thereof shall be fined not more than $500 or imprisoned for not more than one year, or both.

*([Mar. 10, 1934, ch. 55, § 7](/us/act/1934-03-10/ch55/s7), as added [Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 965](/us/act/1946-08-14/ch965), [60 Stat. 1082](/us/stat/60/1082).)*

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, is [act Mar. 10, 1934, ch. 55](/us/act/1934-03-10/ch55), [48 Stat. 401](/us/stat/48/401), known as the Fish and Wildlife Coordination Act, which is classified generally to sections 661 to 666c–1 of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 661(a) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s661/a), Short Title note set out under [section 661 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s661), and Tables.
