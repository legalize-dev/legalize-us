# §§ 81a to 81c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(27)](/us/pl/104/186/tII/s204/27)–(29), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1734](/us/stat/110/1734)

##### §§ 81a to 81c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(27)](/us/pl/104/186/tII/s204/27)–(29), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1734](/us/stat/110/1734)

Section 81a, [act July 26, 1949, ch. 366](/us/act/1949-07-26/ch366), [63 Stat. 482](/us/stat/63/482), related to audits and reports of fiscal records of Sergeant at Arms of House.

Section 81b, based on H. Res. No. 465, Eighty-fourth Congress, Apr. 11, 1956, enacted into permanent law by [act June 27, 1956, ch. 453, title I, § 103](/us/act/1956-06-27/ch453/tI/s103), [70 Stat. 370](/us/stat/70/370), related to payment from House contingent fund for restoration or adjustment of trust fund account of Sergeant at Arms.

Section 81c, based on H. Res. No. 144, Eighty-fifth Congress, Feb. 7, 1957, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 85–75, title I, § 103](/us/pl/85/75/tI/s103), July 1, 1957, [71 Stat. 256](/us/stat/71/256), related to purchase of and payment for insurance of office funds of Sergeant at Arms of House.
