# § 460hhh. Definitions

##### § 460hhh. Definitions

(1) National Forest lands The term “National Forest lands” means lands included in the National Forest System (as defined in [section 1609(a) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s1609/a)).

(2) Recreation Area The term “Recreation Area” means the Spring Mountains National Recreation Area established by this subchapter.

(3) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Agriculture.

*([Pub. L. 103–63, § 2](/us/pl/103/63/s2), Aug. 4, 1993, [107 Stat. 297](/us/stat/107/297).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 103–63, § 1](/us/pl/103/63/s1), Aug. 4, 1993, [107 Stat. 297](/us/stat/107/297), provided that: “This Act [enacting this subchapter] may be cited as the ‘Spring Mountains National Recreation Area Act’.”
