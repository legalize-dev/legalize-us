# § 1780c. Omitted

##### § 1780c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 109–286, § 5](/us/pl/109/286/s5), Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1220](/us/stat/120/1220), which related to extinguishment of any claims to land and any claims for damages or other remedies, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
