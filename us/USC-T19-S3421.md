# § 3421. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78)

##### § 3421. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78)

Section, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 361](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s361), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2122](/us/stat/107/2122), set out agricultural standards on peanut butter and peanut paste, established a grant for an animal health biocontainment facility, and required annual reports on inspections of certain imported foods.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Prior to repeal, section was comprised of [section 361 of Pub. L. 103–182](/us/pl/103/182/s361), which consisted of subsecs. (a) to (i). Subsecs. (g) to (i) of section 361 were set out as the text of this section. Subsecs. (a) to (f) of section 361 are classified as follows: subsec. (a) amended [section 1582 of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s1582), Agriculture; subsec. (b) amended [section 104 of Title 21](/us/usc/t21/s104), Food and Drugs; subsec. (c) amended [section 105 of Title 21](/us/usc/t21/s105); subsec. (d) amended [section 1306 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1306) and [section 281 of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s281); subsec. (e) amended [section 466 of Title 21](/us/usc/t21/s466); and subsec. (f) amended [section 620 of Title 21](/us/usc/t21/s620).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the date on which the USMCA entered into force (July 1, 2020), see [section 601 of Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113/s601), set out as a note under former [section 3301 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s3301).
