# §§ 731 to 735. Omitted

##### §§ 731 to 735. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 731 and 732 ceased to be effective Apr. 15, 2005, pursuant to [section 765d(4) of this title](/us/usc/t47/s765d/4).

Section 731, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III, § 301](/us/pl/87/624/tIII/s301), as added [Pub. L. 103–414, title III, § 304(b)(2)](/us/pl/103/414/tIII/s304/b/2), Oct. 25, 1994, [108 Stat. 4297](/us/stat/108/4297), related to the creation of a communications satellite corporation.

A prior section 731, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III, § 301](/us/pl/87/624/tIII/s301), Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 423](/us/stat/76/423), authorized creation of a for-profit communications satellite corporation not to be a Government agency, subject to this chapter and the District of Columbia Business Corporation Act, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–414, § 304(b)(2)](/us/pl/103/414/s304/b/2).

Section 732, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III, § 302](/us/pl/87/624/tIII/s302), as added [Pub. L. 103–414, title III, § 304(b)(2)](/us/pl/103/414/tIII/s304/b/2), Oct. 25, 1994, [108 Stat. 4297](/us/stat/108/4297), related to laws applicable to the corporation.

A prior section 732, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III, § 302](/us/pl/87/624/tIII/s302), Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 423](/us/stat/76/423), related to process of organization of the communications satellite corporation, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–414, § 304(b)(2)](/us/pl/103/414/s304/b/2).

Sections 733 and 734 ceased to be effective Mar. 17, 2000, pursuant to [section 765d(1) of this title](/us/usc/t47/s765d/1).

Section 733, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III, § 303](/us/pl/87/624/tIII/s303), Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 423](/us/stat/76/423); [Pub. L. 91–3, § 1](/us/pl/91/3/s1), Mar. 12, 1969, [83 Stat. 4](/us/stat/83/4); [Pub. L. 103–414, title III, § 303(b)(1)](/us/pl/103/414/tIII/s303/b/1), Oct. 25, 1994, [108 Stat. 4296](/us/stat/108/4296), related to the board of directors and officers of the corporation.

Section 734, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III, § 304](/us/pl/87/624/tIII/s304), Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 424](/us/stat/76/424); [Pub. L. 97–410, § 5](/us/pl/97/410/s5), Jan. 3, 1983, [96 Stat. 2045](/us/stat/96/2045); [Pub. L. 103–414, title III](/us/pl/103/414/tIII), §§ 303(b)(2), (3), 304(b)(3), Oct. 25, 1994, [108 Stat. 4296](/us/stat/108/4296), 4297, related to financing of the corporation.

Section 735, [Pub. L. 87–624, title III, § 305](/us/pl/87/624/tIII/s305), Aug. 31, 1962, [76 Stat. 425](/us/stat/76/425), which related to powers of the corporation, ceased to be effective July 18, 2001, pursuant to [section 765d(2) of this title](/us/usc/t47/s765d/2).
