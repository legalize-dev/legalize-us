# §§ 1467 to 1468a. Omitted

##### §§ 1467 to 1468a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted pursuant to [section 5316 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5316) which terminated authority to make grants or loans under this subchapter after Jan. 1, 1975.

Section 1467, [act July 15, 1949, ch. 388, title I, § 116](/us/act/1949-07-15/ch388/tI/s116), as added Aug. 10, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–117, title III, § 311(a)](/us/pl/89/117/tIII/s311/a), [79 Stat. 477](/us/stat/79/477); amended May 25, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–19, § 6(b)](/us/pl/90/19/s6/b), [81 Stat. 21](/us/stat/81/21); Aug. 1, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–448, title V, § 510](/us/pl/90/448/tV/s510), [82 Stat. 524](/us/stat/82/524); Dec. 24, 1969, [Pub. L. 91–152, title II, § 202(b)](/us/pl/91/152/tII/s202/b), [83 Stat. 386](/us/stat/83/386), related to grants to cities, other municipalities, counties, and Indian tribes, etc., for demolition of unsafe structures.

Section 1468, [act July 15, 1949, ch. 338, title I, § 117](/us/act/1949-07-15/ch338/tI/s117), as added Aug. 10, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–117, title III, § 311(a)](/us/pl/89/117/tIII/s311/a), [79 Stat. 478](/us/stat/79/478); amended May 25, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–19, § 6(b)](/us/pl/90/19/s6/b), [81 Stat. 21](/us/stat/81/21); Aug. 1, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–448, title V, § 515](/us/pl/90/448/tV/s515), [82 Stat. 525](/us/stat/82/525); Dec. 24, 1969, [Pub. L. 91–152, title II, § 202(c)](/us/pl/91/152/tII/s202/c), [83 Stat. 386](/us/stat/83/386), related to grants to cities, other municipalities, counties, and Indian tribes, etc., for code enforcement.

Section 1468a, [act July 15, 1949, ch. 338, title I, § 118](/us/act/1949-07-15/ch338/tI/s118), as added Aug. 1, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–448, title V, § 514](/us/pl/90/448/tV/s514), [82 Stat. 525](/us/stat/82/525); amended Dec. 24, 1969, [Pub.
