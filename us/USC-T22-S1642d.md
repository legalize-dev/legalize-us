# § 1642d. Nationality requirements

##### § 1642d. Nationality requirements

A claim under [section 1642c of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1642c) shall not be allowed unless the property upon which the claim is based was owned by a national of the United States on the date of nationalization or other taking thereof and unless the claim has been held by a national of the United States continuously thereafter until the date of filing with the Commission.

*([Mar. 10, 1950, ch. 54](/us/act/1950-03-10/ch54), title IV, § 405, as added [Pub. L. 85–604, § 1](/us/pl/85/604/s1), Aug. 8, 1958, [72 Stat. 528](/us/stat/72/528).)*
