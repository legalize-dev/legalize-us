# §§ 46–1 to 46–3. Omitted

##### §§ 46–1 to 46–3. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 46–1 to 46–3, relating to Territory of Alaska, were omitted in view of admission of Alaska into the Union.

Section 46–1, [act July 28, 1956, ch. 772, title I, § 101](/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772/tI/s101), [70 Stat. 709](/us/stat/70/709), authorized territorial legislature to enact laws on subject of mental health.

Section 46–2, [act July 28, 1956, ch. 772, title I, § 102](/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772/tI/s102), [70 Stat. 709](/us/stat/70/709), related to jurisdiction, functions and duties of commissioners and courts in carrying out [section 46–1 of this title](/us/usc/t48/s46–1).

Section 46–3, [act July 28, 1956, ch. 772, title II, § 202](/us/act/1956-07-28/ch772/tII/s202), [70 Stat. 711](/us/stat/70/711), prescribed a land-grant program for purpose of [section 46–1 of this title](/us/usc/t48/s46–1).
