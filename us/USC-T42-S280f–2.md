# § 280f–2. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 280f–2. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part $12,500,000 for each of fiscal years 2026 through 2030.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title III, § 399J, as added [Pub. L. 119–44, title I, § 104(a)](/us/pl/119/44/tI/s104/a), Dec. 1, 2025, [139 Stat. 673](/us/stat/139/673).)*
