# § 6101. Statement of purpose

##### § 6101. Statement of purpose

It is the purpose of this chapter to prohibit discrimination on the basis of age in programs or activities receiving Federal financial assistance.

*([Pub. L. 94–135, title III, § 302](/us/pl/94/135/tIII/s302), Nov. 28, 1975, [89 Stat. 728](/us/stat/89/728); [Pub. L. 95–478, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/95/478/tIV/s401/a), Oct. 18, 1978, [92 Stat. 1555](/us/stat/92/1555); [Pub. L. 99–272, title XIV, § 14001(b)(4)](/us/pl/99/272/tXIV/s14001/b/4), Apr. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 329](/us/stat/100/329).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1986—[Pub. L. 99–272](/us/pl/99/272) struck out “, including programs or activities receiving funds under the State and Local Fiscal Assistance Act of 1972 ([31 U.S.C. 1221](/us/usc/t31/s1221) et seq.)” after “Federal financial assistance”.

1978—[Pub. L. 95–478](/us/pl/95/478) struck out “unreasonable” before “discrimination”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of 1986 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 99–272](/us/pl/99/272) effective Oct. 18, 1986, see [section 14001(e) of Pub. L. 99–272](/us/pl/99/272/s14001/e), title XIV, Apr. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 329](/us/stat/100/329).

#### Effective Date of 1978 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 95–478](/us/pl/95/478) effective at the close of Sept. 30, 1978, see [section 504 of Pub. L. 95–478](/us/pl/95/478/s504), set out as a note under [section 3001 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3001).

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 94–135, title III, § 301](/us/pl/94/135/tIII/s301), Nov. 28, 1975, [89 Stat. 728](/us/stat/89/728), provided that: “The provisions of this title [enacting this chapter] may be cited as the ‘Age Discrimination Act of 1975’.”
