# § 1300e–2. Omitted

##### § 1300e–2. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–557, § 3](/us/pl/92/557/s3), Oct. 25, 1972, [86 Stat. 1171](/us/stat/86/1171), which related to expenditure of funds withheld from per capita distribution pursuant to former [section 1300e–1 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1300e–1), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
