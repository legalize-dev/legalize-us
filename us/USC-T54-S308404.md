# § 308404. Sunset

##### § 308404. Sunset

This program shall expire on the date that is 7 years after the date of enactment of this chapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 115–104, § 3(a)](/us/pl/115/104/s3/a), Jan. 8, 2018, [131 Stat. 2261](/us/stat/131/2261).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The date of enactment of this chapter, referred to in text, is the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 115–104](/us/pl/115/104), which was approved Jan. 8, 2018.
