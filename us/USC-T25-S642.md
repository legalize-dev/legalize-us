#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–264, § 2](/us/pl/91/264/s2), May 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 260](/us/stat/84/260), which related to powers of the Hopi Tribal Council, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
