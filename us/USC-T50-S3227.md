# § 3227. Transfer date

##### § 3227. Transfer date

In this part, the term “transfer date” means the date on which the National Intelligence University is transferred from the Defense Intelligence Agency to the Director of National Intelligence under [section 3334a(a) of this title](/us/usc/t50/s3334a/a).

*([July 26, 1947, ch. 343](/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343), title X, § 1031, as added [Pub. L. 116–260, div. W, title III, § 305(a)](/us/pl/116/260/dW/tIII/s305/a), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2366](/us/stat/134/2366).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The transfer date, referred to in text, is June 20, 2021.
