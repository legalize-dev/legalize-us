# § 60305. Data availability

##### § 60305. Data availability

The Administrator shall ensure that the results of each of the pilot projects completed under [section 60303 of this title](/us/usc/t51/s60303) shall be retrievable through an electronic, internet-accessible database.

*([Pub. L. 111–314, § 3](/us/pl/111/314/s3), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3424](/us/stat/124/3424).)*
