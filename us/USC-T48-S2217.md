# § 2217. Savings clause

##### § 2217. Savings clause

Nothing in this subchapter is intended to change or alter any Federal legal requirements or laws.

*([Pub. L. 114–187, title V, § 507](/us/pl/114/187/tV/s507), June 30, 2016, [130 Stat. 603](/us/stat/130/603).)*
