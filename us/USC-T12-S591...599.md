# §§ 591 to 599. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 591 to 599. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 591, R.S. § 5208; acts [July 12, 1882, ch. 290, § 13](/us/act/1882-07-12/ch290/s13), [22 Stat. 166](/us/stat/22/166); [Sept. 26, 1918, ch. 177, § 7](/us/act/1918-09-26/ch177/s7), [40 Stat. 972](/us/stat/40/972); [Feb. 25, 1927, ch. 191, § 12](/us/act/1927-02-25/ch191/s12), [44 Stat. 1231](/us/stat/44/1231), related to unlawful certification of checks. See [section 1004 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1004), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 592, R.S. § 5209; acts [Sept. 26, 1918, ch. 177, § 7](/us/act/1918-09-26/ch177/s7), [40 Stat. 972](/us/stat/40/972); [Aug. 23, 1935, ch. 614](/us/act/1935-08-23/ch614), title III, § 316, [49 Stat. 712](/us/stat/49/712), related to embezzlement. See sections 334, 656, and 1005 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 593, acts [Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 22(a)](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s22/a), [38 Stat. 272](/us/stat/38/272); [Sept. 26, 1918, ch. 177, § 5](/us/act/1918-09-26/ch177/s5), [40 Stat. 970](/us/stat/40/970); [Feb. 25, 1927, ch. 191, § 15](/us/act/1927-02-25/ch191/s15), [44 Stat. 1232](/us/stat/44/1232); [Aug. 23, 1935, ch. 614](/us/act/1935-08-23/ch614), title III, § 326(a), [49 Stat. 715](/us/stat/49/715), related to loans and gratuities.

Section 594, acts [Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 22(b)](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s22/b), [38 Stat. 272](/us/stat/38/272); [Sept. 26, 1918, ch. 177, § 5](/us/act/1918-09-26/ch177/s5), [40 Stat. 970](/us/stat/40/970); [Aug. 23, 1935, ch. 614](/us/act/1935-08-23/ch614), title III, § 326(b), [49 Stat. 716](/us/stat/49/716), related to bank examiners performing other services for compensation and disclosure of information. See sections 1906 and 1909 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 595, acts [Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 22(c)](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s22/c), [38 Stat. 272](/us/stat/38/272); [June 21, 1917, ch. 32, § 11](/us/act/1917-06-21/ch32/s11), [40 Stat. 240](/us/stat/40/240); [Sept. 26, 1918, ch. 177, § 5](/us/act/1918-09-26/ch177/s5), [40 Stat. 970](/us/stat/40/970), related to officers and employees accepting commissions and gifts for procuring loans. See [section 215 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s215), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 596, [act Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 22(h)](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s22/h), as added [June 19, 1934, ch. 653, § 3](/us/act/1934-06-19/ch653/s3), [48 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/48/1107), related to false statements or overvaluation of securities to secure loan. See [section 1014 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1014), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 597, [act Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 22(i)](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s22/i), as added [June 19, 1934, ch. 653, § 3](/us/act/1934-06-19/ch653/s3), [48 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/48/1107), related to embezzlement, etc. See sections 655 and 1005 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 598, [act Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 22(j)](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s22/j), as added [June 19, 1934, ch. 653, § 3](/us/act/1934-06-19/ch653/s3), [48 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/48/1107), related to application of former sections 202 to 207 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 599, [act Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 22(k)](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s22/k), as added [June 19, 1934, ch. 653, § 3](/us/act/1934-06-19/ch653/s3), [48 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/48/1107), related to fees, commissions, and bonuses in connection with loans. See [section 214 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s214), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.
