# § 1063. Cross references

##### § 1063. Cross references

(1) For nonrecognition of gain in connection with the transfer of obsolete vessels to the Maritime Administration under chapter 573 of title 46, United States Code, see [section 57307 of title 46](/us/usc/t46/s57307).

(2) For recognition of gain or loss in connection with the construction of new vessels, see chapter 533 of title 46, United States Code.
