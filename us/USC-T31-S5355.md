# § 5355. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 5355. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated the following amounts for the following fiscal years to carry out the purposes of this subchapter: **For fiscal year:****The amount authorized is:** 1999 $5,000,000. 2000 $7,500,000. 2001 $10,000,000. 2002 $12,500,000. 2003 $15,000,000. 2004 $15,000,000. 2005 $15,000,000.

*(Added [Pub. L. 105–310, § 2(a)](/us/pl/105/310/s2/a), Oct. 30, 1998, [112 Stat. 2948](/us/stat/112/2948); amended [Pub. L. 108–458, title VI, § 6102(b)](/us/pl/108/458/tVI/s6102/b), Dec. 17, 2004, [118 Stat. 3745](/us/stat/118/3745).)*
