# § 887a. Art. 87a. Resistance, flight, breach of arrest, and escape

##### § 887a. Art. 87a. Resistance, flight, breach of arrest, and escape

(1) resists apprehension;

(2) flees from apprehension;

(3) breaks arrest; or

(4) escapes from custody or confinement;
