# § 2214a. “Bank” defined

##### § 2214a. “Bank” defined

In this part, the term “bank” includes each association operating under subchapter II.

*([Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28A, as added [Pub. L. 104–105, title II, § 209](/us/pl/104/105/tII/s209), Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 174](/us/stat/110/174).)*
