# § 1382k. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–123, § 2(h)](/us/pl/97/123/s2/h), Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1661](/us/stat/95/1661)

##### § 1382k. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–123, § 2(h)](/us/pl/97/123/s2/h), Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1661](/us/stat/95/1661)

Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XVI, § 1622](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXVI/s1622), as added Aug. 13, 1981, [Pub. L. 97–35, title XXII, § 2201(g)](/us/pl/97/35/tXXII/s2201/g), [95 Stat. 833](/us/stat/95/833), related to benefits for individuals formerly receiving minimum benefits.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective with respect to benefits for months after December 1981, see [section 2(j)(2) of Pub. L. 97–123](/us/pl/97/123/s2/j/2), set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under [section 415 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s415).

[Pub. L. 97–35, title XXII, § 2201(h)](/us/pl/97/35/tXXII/s2201/h), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 834](/us/stat/95/834), which provided for the effective date of this section and the other enactments and amendments made by [section 2201 of Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35/s2201), was repealed by [Pub. L. 97–123, § 2(j)(1)](/us/pl/97/123/s2/j/1), Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1661](/us/stat/95/1661).
