# § 13261. Prohibited acts

##### § 13261. Prohibited acts

It shall be unlawful for any person to violate any provision of section 13251, 13253(b), 13257, or 13263a of this title, or any regulation issued under such sections.

*([Pub. L. 102–486, title V, § 511](/us/pl/102/486/tV/s511), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2899](/us/stat/106/2899); [Pub. L. 109–58, title VII, § 703(b)](/us/pl/109/58/tVII/s703/b), Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 816](/us/stat/119/816).)*
