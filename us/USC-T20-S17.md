# § 17. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 643](/us/stat/80/643)

##### § 17. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 643](/us/stat/80/643)

Section, [act Feb. 23, 1917, ch. 114, § 6](/us/act/1917-02-23/ch114/s6), [39 Stat. 932](/us/stat/39/932), created a Federal Board for Vocational Education, and provided for appointments, salary, powers and duties.
