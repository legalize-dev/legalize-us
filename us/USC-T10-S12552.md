# § 12552. Funeral honors functions at funerals for veterans

##### § 12552. Funeral honors functions at funerals for veterans

Performance by a Reserve of funeral honors functions at the funeral of a veteran (as defined in [section 1491(h) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1491/h)) may not be considered to be a period of drill or training, but may be performed as funeral honors duty under [section 12503 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s12503).

*(Added [Pub. L. 105–261, div. A, title V, § 567(c)(1)](/us/pl/105/261/dA/tV/s567/c/1), Oct. 17, 1998, [112 Stat. 2031](/us/stat/112/2031); amended [Pub. L. 106–65, div. A, title V, § 578(g)(4)](/us/pl/106/65/dA/tV/s578/g/4), Oct. 5, 1999, [113 Stat. 628](/us/stat/113/628); [Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [[div.
