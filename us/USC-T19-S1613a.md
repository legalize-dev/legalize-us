# § 1613a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–514, title XVIII, § 1888(7)](/us/pl/99/514/tXVIII/s1888/7), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2925](/us/stat/100/2925)

##### § 1613a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–514, title XVIII, § 1888(7)](/us/pl/99/514/tXVIII/s1888/7), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2925](/us/stat/100/2925)

Section, [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497, title IV, § 613a](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497/tIV/s613a), as added and amended Oct. 12, 1984, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II](/us/pl/98/473/tII), §§ 317, 2304, [98 Stat. 2054](/us/stat/98/2054), 2193; Oct. 27, 1986, [Pub. L. 99–570, title I, § 1152(b)(1)](/us/pl/99/570/tI/s1152/b/1), [100 Stat. 3207–12](/us/stat/100/3207-12); July 11, 1987, [Pub. L. 100–71, title I, § 101](/us/pl/100/71/tI/s101), [101 Stat. 438](/us/stat/101/438), related to establishment, purpose, etc. of the Customs Forfeiture Fund. See [section 1613b of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1613b).

[Section 1152(b)(1) of Pub. L. 99–570](/us/pl/99/570/s1152/b/1), which amended this section subsequently to repeal by [Pub. L. 99–514](/us/pl/99/514), was repealed by [section 101 of Pub. L. 100–71](/us/pl/100/71/s101), which also provided in part that [section 1152(b) of Pub. L. 99–570](/us/pl/99/570/s1152/b) be treated as though it had never been enacted.
