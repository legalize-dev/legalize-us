# § 747. Omitted

##### § 747. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 7](/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1207/s7), [68 Stat. 1102](/us/stat/68/1102), which related to property of deceased members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
