# § 2225. Reclamation Drought Response Fund

##### § 2225. Reclamation Drought Response Fund

The Secretary shall undertake a study of the need, if any, to establish a Reclamation Drought Response Fund to be available for defraying those expenses which the Secretary determines necessary to implement plans prepared under [section 2222 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s2222) and to make loans for nonstructural and minor structural activities for the prevention or mitigation of the adverse effects of drought.

*([Pub. L. 102–250, title II, § 205](/us/pl/102/250/tII/s205), Mar. 5, 1992, [106 Stat. 58](/us/stat/106/58).)*
