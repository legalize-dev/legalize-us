# § 1836. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1836. Authorization of appropriations

(a) There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out the provisions of this subchapter, $10,000,000 for fiscal year 2009 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the five succeeding fiscal years.

(b) Any funds appropriated pursuant to subsection (a) are authorized to remain available until expended.

1992—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 102–325](/us/pl/102/325) amended subsec. (a) generally, substituting provisions authorizing appropriations for fiscal years 1993 to 1997 for provisions authorizing appropriations for fiscal years 1987 to 1992.

1990—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 101–477](/us/pl/101/477) substituted “1990 and 1991, and for fiscal year 1992, $10,000,000” for “and 1990”.

1986—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 99–428](/us/pl/99/428) substituted “1987, 1988, 1989, and 1990” for “1985, 1986, and 1987”.
