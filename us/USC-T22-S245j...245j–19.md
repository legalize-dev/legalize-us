# §§ 245j to 245j–19. Transferred

##### §§ 245j to 245j–19. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 245j, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 1](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s1), [54 Stat. 4](/us/stat/54/4), which related to a proclamation of a state of war between foreign states, was transferred to [section 441 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s441).

Section 245j–1, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 2](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s2), [54 Stat. 4](/us/stat/54/4), which related to commerce with states engaged in armed conflict, was transferred to [section 442 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s442) and was repealed by Joint Res. [Nov. 17, 1941, ch. 473, § 1](/us/act/1941-11-17/ch473/s1), [55 Stat. 764](/us/stat/55/764).

Section 245j–2, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 3](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s3), [54 Stat. 7](/us/stat/54/7), which related to combat areas, was transferred to [section 443 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s443) and was repealed by Joint Res. [Nov. 17, 1941, ch. 473, § 1](/us/act/1941-11-17/ch473/s1), [55 Stat. 764](/us/stat/55/764).

Section 245j–3, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 4](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s4), [54 Stat. 7](/us/stat/54/7), as amended, which related to American Red Cross vessels, was transferred to [section 444 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s444).

Section 245j–4, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 5](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s5), [54 Stat. 7](/us/stat/54/7), which related to travel on vessels of belligerent states, was transferred to [section 445 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s445).

Section 245j–5, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 6](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s6), [54 Stat. 7](/us/stat/54/7), which related to the arming of American merchant vessels, was transferred to [section 446 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s446) and was repealed by Joint Res. [Nov. 17, 1941, ch. 473, § 2](/us/act/1941-11-17/ch473/s2), [55 Stat. 764](/us/stat/55/764).

Section 245j–6, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 7](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s7), [54 Stat. 7](/us/stat/54/7), as amended, which related to financial transactions, was transferred to [section 447 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s447).

Section 245j–7, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 8](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s8), [54 Stat. 8](/us/stat/54/8), which related to solicitation and collection of funds and contributions, was transferred to [section 448 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s448).

Section 245j–8, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 9](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s9), [54 Stat. 8](/us/stat/54/8), which related to American republics, was transferred to [section 449 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s449).

Section 245j–9, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 10](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s10), [54 Stat. 9](/us/stat/54/9), which related to restrictions on use of American ports, was transferred to [section 450 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s450).

Section 245j–10, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 11](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s11), [54 Stat. 9](/us/stat/54/9), which related to submarines and armed merchant vessels, was transferred to [section 451 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s451).

Section 245j–11, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 12](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s12), [54 Stat. 10](/us/stat/54/10), which related to National Munitions Control Board, was transferred to [section 452 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s452) and was repealed by [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937, title V, § 542(a)(12)](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937/tV/s542/a/12), [68 Stat. 861](/us/stat/68/861).

Section 245j–12, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 13](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s13), [54 Stat. 11](/us/stat/54/11), which related to regulations, was transferred to [section 453 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s453).

Section 245j–13, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2). § 14, [54 Stat. 11](/us/stat/54/11), which related to unlawful use of the American flag, was transferred to [section 454 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s454).

Section 245j–14, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 15](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s15), [54 Stat. 11](/us/stat/54/11), which related to penalties, was transferred to [section 455 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s455).

Section 245j–15, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 16](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s16), [54 Stat. 12](/us/stat/54/12), as amended, which defined terms used in neutrality provisions, was transferred to [section 456 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s456).

Section 245j–16, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 17](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s17), [54 Stat. 12](/us/stat/54/12), which related to separability of provisions, was transferred to a note set out under [section 441 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s441).

Section 245j–17, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 18](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s18), [54 Stat. 12](/us/stat/54/12), which related to appropriations, was transferred to [section 457 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s457).

Section 245j–18, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 19](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s19), [54 Stat. 12](/us/stat/54/12), which related to repeal of earlier Joint Resolutions, was transferred and set out as a note under [section 441 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s441).

Section 245j–19, Joint Res. [Nov. 4, 1939, ch. 2, § 20](/us/act/1939-11-04/ch2/s20), [54 Stat. 12](/us/stat/54/12), which related to short title of this resolution, was transferred and set out as a note under [section 441 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s441).
