# § 115. Vessel

##### § 115. Vessel

In this title, the term “vessel” has the meaning given that term in [section 3 of title 1](/us/usc/t1/s3).

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 4](/us/pl/109/304/s4), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1487](/us/stat/120/1487).)*
