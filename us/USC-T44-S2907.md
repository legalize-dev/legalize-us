# § 2907. Records centers and centralized microfilming or digitization services

##### § 2907. Records centers and centralized microfilming or digitization services

The Archivist may establish, maintain, and operate records centers and centralized microfilming or digitization services for Federal agencies.

*(Added [Pub. L. 94–575, § 2(a)(3)](/us/pl/94/575/s2/a/3), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2726](/us/stat/90/2726); amended [Pub. L. 98–497, title I, § 107(b)(15)(A)](/us/pl/98/497/tI/s107/b/15/A), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2288](/us/stat/98/2288); [Pub. L. 113–187, § 9(b)(1)](/us/pl/113/187/s9/b/1), Nov. 26, 2014, [128 Stat. 2013](/us/stat/128/2013).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 2907, [Pub. L. 90–620](/us/pl/90/620), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1297](/us/stat/82/1297), contained provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–575, § 2(a)(3)](/us/pl/94/575/s2/a/3).

#### Amendments

2014—[Pub. L. 113–187](/us/pl/113/187) inserted “or digitization” after “microfilming” in section catchline and text.

1984—[Pub. L. 98–497](/us/pl/98/497) substituted “Archivist” for “Administrator”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of 1984 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 98–497](/us/pl/98/497) effective Apr. 1, 1985, see [section 301 of Pub. L. 98–497](/us/pl/98/497/s301), set out as a note under [section 2102 of this title](/us/usc/t44/s2102).
