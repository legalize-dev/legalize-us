# § 1775. Omitted

##### § 1775. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–377, § 2](/us/pl/103/377/s2), Oct. 19, 1994, [108 Stat. 3501](/us/stat/108/3501), which set out congressional findings and purposes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 103–377, § 1](/us/pl/103/377/s1), Oct. 19, 1994, [108 Stat. 3501](/us/stat/108/3501), provided that [Pub. L. 103–377](/us/pl/103/377), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Mohegan Nation of Connecticut Land Claims Settlement Act of 1994”.
