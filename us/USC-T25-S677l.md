# § 677l. Omitted

##### § 677l. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 13](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s13), [68 Stat. 874](/us/stat/68/874), which related to distribution of assets to individual members of the mixed-blood group, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
