#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–59, § 7](/us/pl/92/59/s7), July 29, 1971, [85 Stat. 159](/us/stat/85/159), which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
