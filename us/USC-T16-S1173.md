# § 1173. Regulations

##### § 1173. Regulations

The Secretary is authorized to prescribe such regulations as he deems necessary and appropriate to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 89–702, title III, § 303](/us/pl/89/702/tIII/s303), as added [Pub. L. 98–129, § 2](/us/pl/98/129/s2), Oct. 14, 1983, [97 Stat. 845](/us/stat/97/845).)*
