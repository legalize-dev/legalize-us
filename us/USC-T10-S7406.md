# § 7406. Service schools: leaves of absence for instructors

##### § 7406. Service schools: leaves of absence for instructors

The officer in charge of an Army service school may grant a leave of absence for the period of the suspension of the ordinary academic studies, without reduction of pay or allowances, to any officer on duty exclusively as an instructor at the school.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 235](/us/stat/70A/235), § 4306; renumbered § 7406, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(c)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/c/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1839](/us/stat/132/1839).)*

The words “The provisions of [section 1144 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1144), authorizing leaves of absence to certain officers of the Military Academy * * * are hereby, extended to include” are omitted as surplusage.
