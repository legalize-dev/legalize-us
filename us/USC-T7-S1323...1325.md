##### §§ 1323 to 1325. Repealed. [Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041](/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041), title III, § 304, [68 Stat. 902](/us/stat/68/902)

Section 1324, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 324](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s324), [52 Stat. 50](/us/stat/52/50), related to storage amounts.

Section 1325, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 325](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s325), [52 Stat. 51](/us/stat/52/51), related to penalties for marketing corn in excess of quota.
