# § 84b. Omitted

##### § 84b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, acts [July 17, 1947, ch. 262](/us/act/1947-07-17/ch262), [61 Stat. 365](/us/stat/61/365); Oct. 18, 1986, [Pub. L. 99–500, § 101(j)](/us/pl/99/500/s101/j), [100 Stat. 1783–287](/us/stat/100/1783-287), and Oct. 30, 1986, [Pub. L. 99–591, § 101(j)](/us/pl/99/591/s101/j), [100 Stat. 3341–287](/us/stat/100/3341-287); July 11, 1987, [Pub. L. 100–71, title I](/us/pl/100/71/tI), [101 Stat. 425](/us/stat/101/425), provided that on and after July 17, 1947, sums received from the sales of copies of transcripts of hearings of committees reported by such reporters be covered into the Treasury. See [section 5540 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s5540).

Amendment of section by [Pub. L. 99–500](/us/pl/99/500) and 99–591, as amended by [Pub. L. 100–71](/us/pl/100/71), is based on section 104(b) of title I of H.R. 5203 (see House Report 99–805 as filed in the House on Aug. 15, 1986), and incorporated by reference in [section 101(j) of Pub. L. 99–500](/us/pl/99/500/s101/j) and 99–591, to be effective as if enacted into law. [Pub. L. 99–591](/us/pl/99/591) is a corrected version of [Pub. L. 99–500](/us/pl/99/500).

Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in appropriation acts which were classified to [section 117a of this title](/us/usc/t2/s117a).
