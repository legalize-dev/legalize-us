# § 1241. Omitted

##### § 1241. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–59, § 1](/us/pl/92/59/s1), July 29, 1971, [85 Stat. 158](/us/stat/85/158), which related to distribution of judgment funds and payment of attorney fees and expenses, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
