# § 5603. United States export controls

##### § 5603. United States export controls

(1) use the authorities of the Arms Export Control Act [[22 U.S.C. 2751](/us/usc/t22/s2751) et seq.] to control the export of those defense articles and defense services, and

(2) use the authorities of the Export Administration Act of 1979 to control the export of those goods and technology,

*([Pub. L. 102–182, title III, § 304(a)](/us/pl/102/182/tIII/s304/a), Dec. 4, 1991, [105 Stat. 1246](/us/stat/105/1246).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Arms Export Control Act, referred to in par. (1), is [Pub. L. 90–629](/us/pl/90/629), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1320](/us/stat/82/1320), which is classified principally to chapter 39 (§ 2751 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 2751 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2751) and Tables.

The Export Administration Act of 1979, referred to in par. (2), is [Pub. L. 96–72](/us/pl/96/72), Sept. 29, 1979, [93 Stat. 503](/us/stat/93/503), which was classified principally to chapter 56 (§ 4601 et seq.) of Title 50, War and National Defense, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title XVII, § 1766(a)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tXVII/s1766/a), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 2232](/us/stat/132/2232), except for sections 11A, 11B, and 11C thereof ([50 U.S.C. 4611](/us/usc/t50/s4611), 4612, 4613).
