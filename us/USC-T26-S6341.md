# § 6341. Expense of levy and sale

##### § 6341. Expense of levy and sale

The Secretary shall determine the expenses to be allowed in all cases of levy and sale.

*([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 789](/us/stat/68A/789); [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1906/b/13/A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/90/1834).)*
