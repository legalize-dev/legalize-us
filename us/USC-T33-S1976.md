# § 1976. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1976. Authorization of appropriations

Of the amounts authorized under [section 1968(a) of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1968/a), the Secretary of Commerce shall use up to $1,000,000 to carry out this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 109–449, § 126](/us/pl/109/449/s126), formerly [Pub. L. 116–224, title I, § 126](/us/pl/116/224/tI/s126), Dec. 18, 2020, [134 Stat. 1083](/us/stat/134/1083); renumbered [Pub. L. 109–449, § 126](/us/pl/109/449/s126), by [Pub. L. 119–65, § 4(a)](/us/pl/119/65/s4/a), Dec. 26, 2025, [139 Stat. 1989](/us/stat/139/1989).)*
