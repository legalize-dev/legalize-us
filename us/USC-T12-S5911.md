# § 5911. Application of existing insolvency law

##### § 5911. Application of existing insolvency law

(1) A depository institution (as defined in [section 1813 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1813)) shall be resolved by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation, National Credit Union Administration, or State payment stablecoin regulator, as applicable.

(2) A subsidiary of a depository institution (as defined in [section 1813 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1813)) or a nonbank entity may be considered a debtor under title 11.

*([Pub. L. 119–27, § 11(g)](/us/pl/119/27/s11/g), July 18, 2025, [139 Stat. 458](/us/stat/139/458).)*
