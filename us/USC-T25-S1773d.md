# § 1773d. Omitted

##### § 1773d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–41, § 6](/us/pl/101/41/s6), June 21, 1989, [103 Stat. 86](/us/stat/103/86), which related to one-time payment to each enrolled member and establishment of a permanent trust fund to enhance the ability of the tribe to provide services to its members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
