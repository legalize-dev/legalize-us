#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–206, § 1](/us/pl/92/206/s1), Dec. 18, 1971, [85 Stat. 737](/us/stat/85/737), which related to disposition of judgment funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
