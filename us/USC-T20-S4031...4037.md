# §§ 4031 to 4037. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–297, title II, § 2303](/us/pl/100/297/tII/s2303), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 324](/us/stat/102/324)

##### §§ 4031 to 4037. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–297, title II, § 2303](/us/pl/100/297/tII/s2303), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 324](/us/stat/102/324)

Section 4031, [Pub. L. 98–377, title VI, § 602](/us/pl/98/377/tVI/s602), Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1295](/us/stat/98/1295), related to statement of purpose.

Section 4032, [Pub. L. 98–377, title VI, § 603](/us/pl/98/377/tVI/s603), Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1296](/us/stat/98/1296), related to definitions.

Section 4033, [Pub. L. 98–377, title VI, § 604](/us/pl/98/377/tVI/s604), Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1296](/us/stat/98/1296); [Pub. L. 99–159, title II, § 251](/us/pl/99/159/tII/s251), Nov. 22, 1985, [99 Stat. 901](/us/stat/99/901); [Pub. L. 99–425, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/99/425/tVII/s701), Sept. 30, 1986, [100 Stat. 977](/us/stat/100/977), related to school excellence awards.

Section 4034, [Pub. L. 98–377, title VI, § 605](/us/pl/98/377/tVI/s605), Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1296](/us/stat/98/1296), related to selection of schools for awards.

Section 4035, [Pub. L. 98–377, title VI, § 606](/us/pl/98/377/tVI/s606), Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1298](/us/stat/98/1298), related to amount and conditions of awards.

Section 4036, [Pub. L. 98–377, title VI, § 607](/us/pl/98/377/tVI/s607), Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1298](/us/stat/98/1298), related to special school awards.

Section 4037, [Pub. L. 98–377, title VI, § 608](/us/pl/98/377/tVI/s608), Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1298](/us/stat/98/1298), related to research, evaluation, dissemination, and monitoring activities.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective July 1, 1988, see [section 6303 of Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297/s6303), set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 1071 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1071).

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 98–377, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/98/377/tVI/s601), Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1295](/us/stat/98/1295), which provided that title VI of [Pub. L. 98–377](/us/pl/98/377) was to be cited as the “Excellence in Education Act”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 100–297, title II, § 2303](/us/pl/100/297/tII/s2303), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 324](/us/stat/102/324).
