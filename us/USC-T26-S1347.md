#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Savings Provision

[Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1951(b)(12)(B)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1951/b/12/B), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1840](/us/stat/90/1840), provided that: “Notwithstanding subparagraph (A) [repealing this section], if amounts received in a taxable year beginning after December 31, 1976, would have been subject to the provisions of section 1347 if received in a taxable year beginning before such date, the tax imposed by section 1 attributable to such receipt shall be computed as if section 1347 had not been repealed.”
