# § 788c. Omitted

##### § 788c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–504, § 3](/us/pl/90/504/s3), Sept. 21, 1968, [82 Stat. 855](/us/stat/82/855), which related to distribution of judgment funds to heirs of deceased enrollees, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
