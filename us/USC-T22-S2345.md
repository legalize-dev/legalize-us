# § 2345. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–629](/us/pl/90/629), ch. 4, § 45(a), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1327](/us/stat/82/1327)

##### § 2345. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–629](/us/pl/90/629), ch. 4, § 45(a), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1327](/us/stat/82/1327)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. II, § 525, formerly §§ 503(e), 509(b), as added [Pub. L. 88–633](/us/pl/88/633), pt. II, § 201(a), (d), Oct. 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 1011](/us/stat/78/1011); amended [Pub. L. 89–171](/us/pl/89/171), pt. II, § 201(f), Sept. 6, 1965, [79 Stat. 657](/us/stat/79/657); renumbered § 525 and amended [Pub. L. 90–137](/us/pl/90/137), pt. II, § 201(b)(3), (i)(2), Nov. 14, 1967, [81 Stat. 455](/us/stat/81/455), 457, provided for guaranties until June 30, 1968. See [section 2764 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2764).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective July 1, 1968, see [section 41 of Pub. L. 90–629](/us/pl/90/629/s41), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 2751 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2751).

#### Savings Provision

Determinations, authorizations, regulations, orders, contracts, agreements, and other actions issued undertaken, or entered into under authority of any provision of former [section 2345 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2345) as continuing in full force and effect until modified by appropriate authority, see [section 46 of Pub. L. 90–629](/us/pl/90/629/s46), set out as a note under former [section 2341 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2341).
