# § 1123. Omitted

##### § 1123. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–309, § 2](/us/pl/92/309/s2), June 2, 1972, [86 Stat. 199](/us/stat/86/199), which provided for payment of costs incident to carrying out the provisions of former sections 1122 to 1130 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
