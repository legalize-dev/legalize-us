# § 7504. Regulations

##### § 7504. Regulations

The Office of Personnel Management may prescribe regulations to carry out the purpose of this subchapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 95–454, title II, § 204(a)](/us/pl/95/454/tII/s204/a), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1135](/us/stat/92/1135).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective 90 days after Oct. 13, 1978, see [section 907 of Pub. L. 95–454](/us/pl/95/454/s907), set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 1101 of this title](/us/usc/t5/s1101).
