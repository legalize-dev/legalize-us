# §§ 5231 to 5233. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1571](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1571), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3702](/us/stat/104/3702)

##### §§ 5231 to 5233. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1571](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1571), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3702](/us/stat/104/3702)

Section 5231, [Pub. L. 100–418, title IV, § 4211](/us/pl/100/418/tIV/s4211), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1393](/us/stat/102/1393), provided for staffing of Foreign Agricultural Service. See [section 5694 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s5694).

Section 5232, [Pub. L. 100–418, title IV, § 4212](/us/pl/100/418/tIV/s4212), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1394](/us/stat/102/1394), provided for an agricultural attaché educational program. See [section 1749 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1749).

Section 5233, [Pub. L. 100–418, title IV, § 4213](/us/pl/100/418/tIV/s4213), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1394](/us/stat/102/1394), provided for the allocation of agricultural attaché resource time.
