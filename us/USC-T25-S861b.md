# § 861b. Omitted

##### § 861b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–281, § 3](/us/pl/95/281/s3), May 15, 1978, [92 Stat. 247](/us/stat/92/247), which related to restoration of Federal supervision as fulfilling other Federal statutory requirements, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
