# § 305301. Definitions

##### § 305301. Definitions

(1) Board.— The term “Board” means the Preservation Technology and Training Board established pursuant to [section 305303 of this title](/us/usc/t54/s305303).

(2) Center.— The term “Center” means the National Center for Preservation Technology and Training established pursuant to [section 305302 of this title](/us/usc/t54/s305302).

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3220](/us/stat/128/3220).)*
