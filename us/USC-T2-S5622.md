# § 5622. Lump sum allowance for Corrections Calendar Office

##### § 5622. Lump sum allowance for Corrections Calendar Office

There shall be a lump sum allowance of $300,000 per fiscal year for the salaries and expenses of the Corrections Calendar Office, established by [section 5621 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s5621). Such amount shall be allocated between the majority party and the minority party as determined by the Speaker, in consultation with the minority leader.

*([Pub. L. 105–55, title I, § 101](/us/pl/105/55/tI/s101), Oct. 7, 1997, [111 Stat. 1183](/us/stat/111/1183).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 74d–1 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s74d–1) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Section is based on section 1 of House Resolution No. 130, One Hundred Fifth Congress, Apr. 24, 1997, which was enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 105–55](/us/pl/105/55).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Transfer of Allowance

For transfer of lump sum allowance under this section to Offices of Speaker and Minority Leader, see [section 5624 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s5624).
