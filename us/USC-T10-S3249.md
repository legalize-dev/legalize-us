# § 3249. Advocates for competition

##### § 3249. Advocates for competition

Each advocate for competition designated pursuant to [section 1705(a) of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s1705/a) for an agency named in [section 3063 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s3063) shall be a general or flag officer if a member of the armed forces or in a position classified above GS–15 pursuant to [section 5108 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s5108), if a civilian employee and shall be designated to serve for a minimum of two years.
