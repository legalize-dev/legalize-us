# § 8869. Security for costs

##### § 8869. Security for costs

The court may require any party to give security for costs at any stage of the cause and upon filing an appeal.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 480](/us/stat/70A/480), § 7669; renumbered § 8869, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(d)(9)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/d/9), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1837](/us/stat/132/1837).)*

The word “filing” is substituted for the word “claiming”.
