# § 4147. Funding

##### § 4147. Funding

The Secretary shall use not more than $25,000,000 of the amounts made available under [section 4124(a) of this title](/us/usc/t12/s4124/a) for fiscal year 1993, and not more than $25,000,000 of the amounts made available under [section 4124(a) of this title](/us/usc/t12/s4124/a) for fiscal year 1994, to carry out this subchapter.
