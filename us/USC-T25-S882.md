#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–404, § 1](/us/pl/91/404/s1), Sept. 19, 1970, [84 Stat. 845](/us/stat/84/845), which related to disposition of the judgment fund of the Sac and Fox Tribes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
