# § 5213. Withdrawal of distilled spirits from bonded premises on determination of tax

##### § 5213. Withdrawal of distilled spirits from bonded premises on determination of tax

Subject to the provisions of section 5173, distilled spirits may be withdrawn from the bonded premises of a distilled spirits plant on payment or determination of tax thereon, in approved containers, under such regulations as the Secretary shall prescribe.

*(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201](/us/pl/85/859/tII/s201), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1362](/us/stat/72/1362); amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1906/b/13/A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/90/1834); [Pub. L. 96–39, title VIII, § 807(a)(27)](/us/pl/96/39/tVIII/s807/a/27), July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 285](/us/stat/93/285).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 5213, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 639](/us/stat/68A/639), related to return of materials used in the manufacture of distilled spirits, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub.
