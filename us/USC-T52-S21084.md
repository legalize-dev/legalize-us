# § 21084. Minimum requirements

##### § 21084. Minimum requirements

The requirements established by this subchapter are minimum requirements and nothing in this subchapter shall be construed to prevent a State from establishing election technology and administration requirements that are more strict than the requirements established under this subchapter so long as such State requirements are not inconsistent with the Federal requirements under this subchapter or any law described in [section 21145 of this title](/us/usc/t52/s21145).

*([Pub. L. 107–252, title III, § 305](/us/pl/107/252/tIII/s305), formerly § 304, Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1714](/us/stat/116/1714); renumbered § 305, [Pub. L. 118–106, § 2(a)(1)](/us/pl/118/106/s2/a/1), Oct. 4, 2024, [138 Stat. 1591](/us/stat/138/1591).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title III of [Pub. L. 107–252](/us/pl/107/252), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1704](/us/stat/116/1704), which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title III to the Code, see Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 15484 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s15484), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 305 of Pub. L. 107–252](/us/pl/107/252/s305) was renumbered section 306 and is classified to [section 21085 of this title](/us/usc/t52/s21085).
