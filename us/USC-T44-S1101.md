# § 1101. Printing and binding for the President

##### § 1101. Printing and binding for the President

The Director of the Government Publishing Office shall execute such printing and binding for the President as he may order and make requisition for.

*([Pub. L. 90–620](/us/pl/90/620), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1261](/us/stat/82/1261); [Pub. L. 113–235, div. H, title I, § 1301(c)(1)](/us/pl/113/235/dH/tI/s1301/c/1), Dec. 16, 2014, [128 Stat. 2537](/us/stat/128/2537).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 211 ([Jan. 12, 1895, ch. 23, § 88](/us/act/1895-01-12/ch23/s88), [28 Stat. 622](/us/stat/28/622)).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section constitutes part of section 88 of act Jan. 12, 1895. Remainder of section is classified to [section 1713 of this title](/us/usc/t44/s1713).

#### Amendments

2014—[Pub. L. 113–235](/us/pl/113/235) substituted “Director of the Government Publishing Office” for “Public Printer”.
