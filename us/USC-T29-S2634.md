# § 2634. Compensation

##### § 2634. Compensation

(a) Pay Members of the Commission shall serve without compensation.

(b) Travel expenses Members of the Commission shall be allowed reasonable travel expenses, including a per diem allowance, in accordance with [section 5703 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s5703) when performing duties of the Commission.

*([Pub. L. 103–3, title III, § 304](/us/pl/103/3/tIII/s304), Feb. 5, 1993, [107 Stat. 25](/us/stat/107/25).)*
