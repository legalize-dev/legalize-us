#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 109–286, § 7](/us/pl/109/286/s7), Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1221](/us/stat/120/1221), which authorized sale of lands by the Secretary of Agriculture, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
