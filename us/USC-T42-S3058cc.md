# § 3058cc. Administration

##### § 3058cc. Administration

(1) other State agencies;

(2) area agencies on aging;

(3) county governments;

(4) institutions of higher education;

(5) Indian tribes; or

(6) nonprofit service providers or volunteer organizations.

*([Pub. L. 89–73, title VII, § 762](/us/pl/89/73/tVII/s762), as added [Pub. L. 102–375, title VII, § 707](/us/pl/102/375/tVII/s707), Sept. 30, 1992, [106 Stat. 1291](/us/stat/106/1291); amended [Pub. L. 106–501, title VIII, § 801(e)(5)](/us/pl/106/501/tVIII/s801/e/5), Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2293](/us/stat/114/2293).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2000—[Pub. L. 106–501](/us/pl/106/501) struck out “or an entity described in [section 3058aa(c) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3058aa/c)” after “A State agency” in introductory provisions.
