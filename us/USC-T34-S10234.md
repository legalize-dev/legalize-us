# § 10234. Prohibition on use of Central Intelligence Agency services

##### § 10234. Prohibition on use of Central Intelligence Agency services

Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, no use will be made of services, facilities, or personnel of the Central Intelligence Agency.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 815](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s815), formerly § 822, as added [Pub. L. 96–157, § 2](/us/pl/96/157/s2), Dec. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 1214](/us/stat/93/1214); renumbered § 815, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609B(f)](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s609B/f), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2093](/us/stat/98/2093).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3789k of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3789k), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
