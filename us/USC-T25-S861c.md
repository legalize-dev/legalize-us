# § 861c. Omitted

##### § 861c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–281, § 4](/us/pl/95/281/s4), May 15, 1978, [92 Stat. 247](/us/stat/92/247), which related to entitlement to participate in programs and services provided by United States to Indians as result of return to status as Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
