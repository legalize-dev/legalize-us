##### § 1737. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–117, title XI, § 1108(aa)](/us/pl/89/117/tXI/s1108/aa), Aug. 10, 1965, [79 Stat. 507](/us/stat/79/507)

Section, [act June 27, 1934, ch. 847, title VI, § 602](/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847/tVI/s602), as added [Mar. 28, 1941, ch. 31, § 1](/us/act/1941-03-28/ch31/s1), [55 Stat. 55](/us/stat/55/55); amended [May 26, 1942, ch. 319, § 14(b)](/us/act/1942-05-26/ch319/s14/b) [56 Stat. 305](/us/stat/56/305); [June 30, 1947, ch. 166](/us/act/1947-06-30/ch166), title II, § 206(*l*), [61 Stat. 208](/us/stat/61/208); 1947 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 3, eff. July 27, 1947, 12 F.R. 4981, [61 Stat. 954](/us/stat/61/954); [Apr. 20, 1950, ch. 94](/us/act/1950-04-20/ch94), title I, § 122, [64 Stat. 59](/us/stat/64/59), created the War Housing Insurance Fund.

For establishment of the General Insurance Fund, see [section 1735c of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1735c).
