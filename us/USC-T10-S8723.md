# § 8723. Periodic re-examination of production requirements

##### § 8723. Periodic re-examination of production requirements

The Secretary shall from time to time reexamine the need for the production of petroleum from oil shale for national defense when that production is authorized under [section 8722 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s8722). If he finds that the authorized quantity is no longer needed, he shall reduce production to the amount currently needed for national defense.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 8723 was renumbered [section 9263 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s9263).

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–232, § 809(a)](/us/pl/115/232/s809/a), substituted “section 8722” for “section 7422”.

[Pub. L. 115–232, § 807(d)(5)](/us/pl/115/232/s807/d/5), renumbered [section 7423 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s7423) as this section.

1976—[Pub. L. 94–258](/us/pl/94/258) struck out “of the Navy” after “Secretary” and “or products” after “petroleum”.

1962—[Pub. L. 87–796](/us/pl/87/796) directed the Secretary to re­examine, from time to time, the need for production of products from oil shale.
