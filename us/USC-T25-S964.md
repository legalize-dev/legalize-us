# § 964. Omitted

##### § 964. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–235, § 4](/us/pl/87/235/s4), Sept. 14, 1961, [75 Stat. 508](/us/stat/75/508), which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
