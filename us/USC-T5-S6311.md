# § 6311. Regulations

##### § 6311. Regulations

The Office of Personnel Management may prescribe regulations necessary for the administration of this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 89–554](/us/pl/89/554), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 521](/us/stat/80/521); [Pub. L. 95–454, title IX, § 906(a)(2)](/us/pl/95/454/tIX/s906/a/2), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1224](/us/stat/92/1224).)*

Standard changes are made to conform with the definitions applicable and the style of this title as outlined in the preface to the report.
