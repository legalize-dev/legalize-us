#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Feb. 28, 1919, ch. 78](/us/act/1919-02-28/ch78), [40 Stat. 1210](/us/stat/40/1210), granted leave of absence to any entryman who, prior to Feb. 28, 1919, made entry upon land withdrawn under reclamation law, until water became available for irrigation.
