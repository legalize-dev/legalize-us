# § 668. Omitted

##### § 668. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–312, § 1](/us/pl/92/312/s1), June 14, 1972, [86 Stat. 216](/us/stat/86/216), which related to the sale of lands held by the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
