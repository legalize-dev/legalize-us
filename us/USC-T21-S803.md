# § 803. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–137, § 1(b)](/us/pl/95/137/s1/b), Oct. 18, 1977, [91 Stat. 1169](/us/stat/91/1169)

##### § 803. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–137, § 1(b)](/us/pl/95/137/s1/b), Oct. 18, 1977, [91 Stat. 1169](/us/stat/91/1169)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–513, title II, § 103](/us/pl/91/513/tII/s103), Oct. 27, 1970, [84 Stat. 1245](/us/stat/84/1245), authorized Bureau of Narcotics and Dangerous Drugs to add, during fiscal year 1971, 300 agents, together with necessary supporting personnel, and provided for appropriations of $6,000,000 to carry out such addition.
