##### § 1504. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–195, § 1(c)(1)](/us/pl/97/195/s1/c/1), June 16, 1982, [96 Stat. 115](/us/stat/96/115)

Section, acts [Feb. 14, 1903, ch. 552, § 2](/us/act/1903-02-14/ch552/s2), [32 Stat. 826](/us/stat/32/826); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1), [37 Stat. 736](/us/stat/37/736); [Mar. 3, 1917, ch. 163, § 1](/us/act/1917-03-03/ch163/s1), [39 Stat. 1111](/us/stat/39/1111), provided for appointment by President of an Assistant Secretary of Commerce, who would perform such duties as prescribed by Secretary or required by law.
