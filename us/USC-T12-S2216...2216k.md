# §§ 2216 to 2216k. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–233, title II, § 207(a)(3)](/us/pl/100/233/tII/s207/a/3), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1607](/us/stat/101/1607)

##### §§ 2216 to 2216k. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–233, title II, § 207(a)(3)](/us/pl/100/233/tII/s207/a/3), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1607](/us/stat/101/1607)

Section 2216, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28A, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1680](/us/stat/99/1680), provided for chartering of Farm Credit System Capital Corporation by Farm Credit Administration and revoked charter of Farm Credit System Capital Corporation which had been issued under part D of this subchapter.

Section 2216a, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28B, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1680](/us/stat/99/1680), set out purposes of Capital Corporation.

Section 2216b, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28C, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1680](/us/stat/99/1680), provided for Board of Directors of Capital Corporation.

Section 2216c, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28D, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1682](/us/stat/99/1682), provided for compensation of members of Board of Directors of Capital Corporation.

Section 2216d, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28E, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1682](/us/stat/99/1682), authorized Board of Directors of Capital Corporation to adopt rules.

Section 2216e, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28F, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1682](/us/stat/99/1682), provided for selection of chief executive officer of Capital Corporation.

Section 2216f, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28G, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1682](/us/stat/99/1682), enumerated corporate powers of Capital Corporation.

Section 2216g, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28H, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1685](/us/stat/99/1685), provided for succession of Capital Corporation.

Section 2216h, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28I, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1685](/us/stat/99/1685), set out provisions limiting powers of Capital Corporation.

Section 2216i, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28J, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1686](/us/stat/99/1686), set out authority of Secretary of the Treasury.

Section 2216j, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28K, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1687](/us/stat/99/1687), provided for initial capitalization of Capital Corporation.

Section 2216k, [Pub. L. 92–181, title IV, § 4](/us/pl/92/181/tIV/s4).28L, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title I, § 103](/us/pl/99/205/tI/s103), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1687](/us/stat/99/1687), provided for tax status of consolidated obligations.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 15 days after Jan. 6, 1988, see [section 207(b) of Pub. L. 100–233](/us/pl/100/233/s207/b), set out as a note under [section 2152 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2152).
