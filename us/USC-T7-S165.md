# § 165. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–448, title IV, § 402(a)(13)](/us/pl/88/448/tIV/s402/a/13), Aug. 19, 1964, [78 Stat. 493](/us/stat/78/493)

##### § 165. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–448, title IV, § 402(a)(13)](/us/pl/88/448/tIV/s402/a/13), Aug. 19, 1964, [78 Stat. 493](/us/stat/78/493)

Section, [act Aug. 20, 1912, ch. 308, § 12](/us/act/1912-08-20/ch308/s12), [37 Stat. 319](/us/stat/37/319), related to appointment of members of a Federal Horticultural Board from among employees of Department of Agriculture.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on first day of first month which begins later than ninetieth day following Aug. 19, 1964, see [section 403 of Pub. L. 88–448](/us/pl/88/448/s403).
