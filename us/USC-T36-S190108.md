# § 190108. Headquarters

##### § 190108. Headquarters

The headquarters of the corporation shall be in the District of Columbia.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1448](/us/stat/112/1448).)*

The word “national” is omitted as unnecessary.
