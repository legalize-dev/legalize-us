# § 2876. Rental guarantees

##### § 2876. Rental guarantees

(1) the occupancy of such units at levels specified in the agreements; or

(2) rental income derived from rental of such units at levels specified in the agreements.

*(Added [Pub. L. 104–106, div. B, title XXVIII, § 2801(a)(1)](/us/pl/104/106/dB/tXXVIII/s2801/a/1), Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 546](/us/stat/110/546); amended [Pub. L. 106–65, div. B, title XXVIII, § 2803(e)](/us/pl/106/65/dB/tXXVIII/s2803/e), Oct. 5, 1999, [113 Stat. 849](/us/stat/113/849).)*
