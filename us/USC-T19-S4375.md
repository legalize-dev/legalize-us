# § 4375. Trade negotiating objectives

##### § 4375. Trade negotiating objectives

The principal negotiating objectives of the United States shall include obtaining the objectives of the bilateral agreements described under [section 4374(a) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4374/a) for any trade agreements under negotiation as of February 24, 2016, or future trade agreement negotiations.

*([Pub. L. 114–125, title IV, § 415](/us/pl/114/125/tIV/s415), Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 161](/us/stat/130/161).)*
