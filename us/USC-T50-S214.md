# § 214. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### § 214. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section, R.S. § 5310, provided that property taken on inland waters of the United States was not a maritime prize. See [section 7651 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s7651), Armed Forces.
