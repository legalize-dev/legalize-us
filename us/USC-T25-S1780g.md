# § 1780g. Omitted

##### § 1780g. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 109–286, § 9](/us/pl/109/286/s9), Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1224](/us/stat/120/1224), which related to trust status of Settlement Area Land and National Forest boundaries, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
