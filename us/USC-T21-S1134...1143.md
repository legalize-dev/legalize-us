# §§ 1134 to 1143. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–255, title I, § 104](/us/pl/92/255/tI/s104), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 67](/us/stat/86/67)

##### §§ 1134 to 1143. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–255, title I, § 104](/us/pl/92/255/tI/s104), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 67](/us/stat/86/67)

Section 1134, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 224](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s224), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 71](/us/stat/86/71), provided for establishment of research and development programs by Director.

Section 1135, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 225](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s225), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72](/us/stat/86/72), provided for establishment of a single non-Federal share requirement.

Section 1136, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 226](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s226), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72](/us/stat/86/72), provided for Director to make drug prevention function recommendations to President and to consult with responsible agencies with respect to drug prevention functions.

Section 1137, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 227](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s227), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72](/us/stat/86/72), provided for resolution of agency conflicts with regard to drug prevention functions.

Section 1138, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 228](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s228), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72](/us/stat/86/72), provided for communications and liaison with respect to drug prevention functions by one of Assistant Directors.

Section 1139, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 229](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s229), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 72](/us/stat/86/72), provided, with respect to drug prevention functions, coordination of State and local governments, availability of information and other services and activities.

Section 1140, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 230](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s230), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 73](/us/stat/86/73), provided for a management oversight review by a Federal officer.

Section 1141, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 231](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s231), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 73](/us/stat/86/73), provided for convening of a council of officials of Federal agencies responsible for drug prevention functions by Director.

Section 1142, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 232](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s232), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 73](/us/stat/86/73), provided for designation of the Director to represent Government in international negotiations related to drug prevention functions.

Section 1143, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 233](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s233), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 73](/us/stat/86/73), provided for an annual written report to the President by the Director.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Section 1104 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s1104), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–237, § 4(c)(1)](/us/pl/94/237/s4/c/1), Mar. 19, 1976, [90 Stat. 244](/us/stat/90/244), provided for repeal of sections effective June 30, 1975.
