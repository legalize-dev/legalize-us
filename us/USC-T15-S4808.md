# § 4808. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 4808. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated for each of the fiscal years 1991 and 1992 such sums as may be necessary not to exceed $5,000,000 to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 100–418, title V, § 5209](/us/pl/100/418/tV/s5209), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1461](/us/stat/102/1461); [Pub. L. 101–382, title I, § 133(e)](/us/pl/101/382/tI/s133/e), Aug. 20, 1990, [104 Stat. 649](/us/stat/104/649).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1990—[Pub. L. 101–382](/us/pl/101/382) substituted “1991 and 1992” for “1989 and 1990”.
