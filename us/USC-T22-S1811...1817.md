# §§ 1811 to 1817. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 642(a)(2), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460)

##### §§ 1811 to 1817. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 642(a)(2), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460)

Section 1811, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. I, § 101, [68 Stat. 833](/us/stat/68/833), stated the Congressional declaration of purpose for military assistance. See [section 2301 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2301).

Section 1811a, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937, § 2](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937/s2), formerly ch. IV, § 549, as added [July 8, 1955, ch. 301, § 11](/us/act/1955-07-08/ch301/s11), [69 Stat. 289](/us/stat/69/289), renumbered and amended [July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 2](/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s2), [70 Stat. 555](/us/stat/70/555); July 24, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–108, § 2](/us/pl/86/108/s2), [73 Stat. 246](/us/stat/73/246); May 14, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–472, § 2](/us/pl/86/472/s2), [74 Stat. 134](/us/stat/74/134), stated the Congressional declaration of purpose for this chapter. See sections 2151 and 2301 of this title.

Section 1812, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. I, § 102, [68 Stat. 833](/us/stat/68/833), related to the basis and terms of military assistance. See [section 2395 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2395).

Section 1813, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. I, § 103, [68 Stat. 833](/us/stat/68/833); [July 8, 1955, ch. 301, § 2(a)](/us/act/1955-07-08/ch301/s2/a)–(c), [69 Stat. 283](/us/stat/69/283); [July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 3(a)](/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s3/a), [70 Stat. 555](/us/stat/70/555); Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–141, § 2(a)](/us/pl/85/141/s2/a), [71 Stat. 355](/us/stat/71/355); June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. I, § 101, [72 Stat. 261](/us/stat/72/261); July 24, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–108](/us/pl/86/108), ch. I, § 101(a), [73 Stat. 247](/us/stat/73/247); May 14, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–472](/us/pl/86/472), ch. I, § 101(a), [74 Stat. 134](/us/stat/74/134), authorized appropriations for military assistance. See [section 2312 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2312).

Section 1814, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. I, § 104, [68 Stat. 834](/us/stat/68/834); Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–141, § 2(b)](/us/pl/85/141/s2/b), [71 Stat. 356](/us/stat/71/356), authorized NATO infrastructure contributions.

Section 1815, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. I, § 105, [68 Stat. 834](/us/stat/68/834); [July 8, 1955, ch. 301, § 2(d)](/us/act/1955-07-08/ch301/s2/d)–(f), [69 Stat. 284](/us/stat/69/284); [July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 3(b)](/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s3/b), [70 Stat. 555](/us/stat/70/555); Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 86–108](/us/pl/86/108), ch. I, § 101(b), [73 Stat. 247](/us/stat/73/247); May 14, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–472](/us/pl/86/472), ch. I, § 101(b), [74 Stat. 134](/us/stat/74/134), prescribed the conditions and eligibility for military assistance. See [section 2314 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2314).

Section 1817, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. I, § 107, [68 Stat. 836](/us/stat/68/836); Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub.
