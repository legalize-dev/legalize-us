# § 285. Attorney fees

##### § 285. Attorney fees

The court in exceptional cases may award reasonable attorney fees to the prevailing party.

*([July 19, 1952, ch. 950](/us/act/1952-07-19/ch950), [66 Stat. 813](/us/stat/66/813).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on Title 35, U.S.C., 1946 ed., § 70, part (R.S. 4921, amended (1) [Mar. 3, 1897, ch. 391, § 6](/us/act/1897-03-03/ch391/s6), [29 Stat. 694](/us/stat/29/694), (2) [Feb. 18, 1922, ch. 58, § 8](/us/act/1922-02-18/ch58/s8), [42 Stat. 392](/us/stat/42/392), (3) [Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 726, § 1](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch726/s1), [60 Stat. 778](/us/stat/60/778)).

This section is substantially the same as the corresponding provision in R.S. 4921; “in exceptional cases” has been added as expressing the intention of the present statute as shown by its legislative history and as interpreted by the courts.
