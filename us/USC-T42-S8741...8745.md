# §§ 8741 to 8745. Omitted

##### §§ 8741 to 8745. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 8741 to 8745 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under [section 8791 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s8791).

Section 8741, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 141](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s141), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 665](/us/stat/94/665), related to construction and operation of synthetic fuel projects.

Section 8742, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 142](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s142), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 666](/us/stat/94/666), limited construction projects.

Section 8743, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 143](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s143), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 666](/us/stat/94/666), subjected construction projects and joint ventures to certain Federal laws and provided for monitoring of environmental and health related emissions.

Section 8744, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 144](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s144), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 667](/us/stat/94/667), related to construction project reports.

Section 8745, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 145](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s145), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 667](/us/stat/94/667), related to financial record requirements for contract recipients.
