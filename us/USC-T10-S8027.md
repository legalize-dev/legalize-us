# § 8027. Chief of Legislative Affairs

##### § 8027. Chief of Legislative Affairs

(a) There is a Chief of Legislative Affairs in the Department of the Navy.

(b) The Chief of Legislative Affairs shall perform legislative affairs functions as specified for the Office of the Secretary of the Navy by [section 8014(c)(1)(F) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s8014/c/1/F).
