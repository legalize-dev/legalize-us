# § 756. Omitted

##### § 756. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 16](/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1207/s16), [68 Stat. 1103](/us/stat/68/1103), which related to cancellation of debts of the tribe to the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
