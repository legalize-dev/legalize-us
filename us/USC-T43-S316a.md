# § 316a. Definitions

##### § 316a. Definitions

(1) The term “person” means individual, partnership, corporation, or association.

(2) The term “district” means any grazing district established under the provisions of [section 316b of this title](/us/usc/t43/s316b).

(3) The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(4) The term “lessee” means the holder of any lease.

*([Mar. 4, 1927, ch. 513, § 2](/us/act/1927-03-04/ch513/s2), [44 Stat. 1452](/us/stat/44/1452).)*
