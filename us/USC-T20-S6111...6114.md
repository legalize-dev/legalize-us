# §§ 6111 to 6114. Omitted

##### §§ 6111 to 6114. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 6111 to 6114 were omitted pursuant to [section 6251 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6251) which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001.

Section 6111, [Pub. L. 103–239, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/239/tI/s101), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 576](/us/stat/108/576), related to general program requirements.

Section 6112, [Pub. L. 103–239, title I, § 102](/us/pl/103/239/tI/s102), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 576](/us/stat/108/576), related to school-based learning component.

Section 6113, [Pub. L. 103–239, title I, § 103](/us/pl/103/239/tI/s103), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 577](/us/stat/108/577), related to work-based learning component.

Section 6114, [Pub. L. 103–239, title I, § 104](/us/pl/103/239/tI/s104), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 577](/us/stat/108/577), related to connecting activities component.
