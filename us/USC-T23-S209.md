# [§ 209. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–424, title I, § 126(d)](/us/pl/97/424/tI/s126/d), Jan. 6, 1983, [96 Stat. 2115](/us/stat/96/2115)]

##### [§ 209. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–424, title I, § 126(d)](/us/pl/97/424/tI/s126/d), Jan. 6, 1983, [96 Stat. 2115](/us/stat/96/2115)]

Section, [Pub. L. 85–767](/us/pl/85/767), Aug. 27, 1958, [72 Stat. 908](/us/stat/72/908); [Pub. L. 88–423, § 4(b)](/us/pl/88/423/s4/b), Aug. 13, 1964, [78 Stat. 397](/us/stat/78/397), provided for use of funds for construction and maintenance of public lands highways, cooperation with State agencies, the application of [section 112 of this title](/us/usc/t23/s112) to public lands highways, and for use of such funds for adjacent ancillary facilities and services.
