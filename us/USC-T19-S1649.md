# § 1649. Change in designation of customs attachés

##### § 1649. Change in designation of customs attachés

On and after June 17, 1930, customs attachés shall be known as “Treasury attachés.”

*([June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 649, [46 Stat. 762](/us/stat/46/762).)*
