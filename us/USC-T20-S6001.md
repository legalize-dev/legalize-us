# § 6001. Short title

##### § 6001. Short title

This subchapter may be cited as the “Educational Research, Development, Dissemination, and Improvement Act of 1994”.

*([Pub. L. 103–227, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/103/227/tIX/s901), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 212](/us/stat/108/212).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title IX of [Pub. L. 103–227](/us/pl/103/227), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 212](/us/stat/108/212), which enacted this subchapter, amended sections 2422, 3155, 3412, 3419, 3462, and 4085b of this title, repealed [section 1221e of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1221e), and enacted provisions set out as notes under sections 1221e and 3155 of this title.
