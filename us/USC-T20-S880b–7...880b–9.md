# §§ 880b–7 to 880b–9. Omitted

##### §§ 880b–7 to 880b–9. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general revision of title VII of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII](/us/pl/89/10/tVII), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 105(a)(1)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s105/a/1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 511](/us/stat/88/511), by [Pub. L. 95–561, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/95/561/tVII/s701), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2268](/us/stat/92/2268).

Section 880b–7, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 721](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s721), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 105(a)(1)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s105/a/1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 506](/us/stat/88/506), provided for bilingual education programs.

Section 880b–8, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 722](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s722), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, Title I, § 105(a)(1)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s105/a/1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 507](/us/stat/88/507), related to Indian children in schools.

Section 880b–9, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 723](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s723), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 105(a)(1)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s105/a/1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 508](/us/stat/88/508); amended [Pub. L. 94–273, § 3(7)](/us/pl/94/273/s3/7), Apr. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 376](/us/stat/90/376); S. Res. 4, Feb. 4, 1977, related to grants and contract for training.
