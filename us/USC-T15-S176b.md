# § 176b. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### § 176b. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section, [act Jan. 27, 1938, ch. 11, § 2](/us/act/1938-01-27/ch11/s2), [52 Stat. 8](/us/stat/52/8), related to disclosure by employee of information. See [section 1905 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1905), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Section 20 of act June 25, 1948, provided that the repeal of this section shall be effective Sept. 1, 1948.
