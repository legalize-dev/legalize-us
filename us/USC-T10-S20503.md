# § 20503. Removal of officer: action by Secretary upon recommendation of retention board

##### § 20503. Removal of officer: action by Secretary upon recommendation of retention board

The Secretary of the Air Force may remove an officer from space force active status if the removal of such officer from space force active status is recommended by a retention board convened under [section 20502 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s20502).

*(Added [Pub. L. 118–31, div. A, title XVII, § 1719](/us/pl/118/31/dA/tXVII/s1719), Dec. 22, 2023, [137 Stat. 661](/us/stat/137/661).)*
