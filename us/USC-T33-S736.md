# § 736. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20), [63 Stat. 561](/us/stat/63/561)

##### § 736. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20), [63 Stat. 561](/us/stat/63/561)

Section, R.S. § 4676; acts [June 10, 1910, ch. 301, § 6](/us/act/1910-06-10/ch301/s6), [36 Stat. 538](/us/stat/36/538); [Aug. 16, 1937, ch. 665, § 1](/us/act/1937-08-16/ch665/s1), [50 Stat. 666](/us/stat/50/666), related to placement of markers over sunken craft and other obstructions. See [section 545 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s545), Coast Guard.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President [Aug. 4, 1949], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
