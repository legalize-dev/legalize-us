# § 182. Omitted

##### § 182. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Mar. 23, 1874, ch. 62, § 1](/us/act/1874-03-23/ch62/s1), [18 Stat. 23](/us/stat/18/23), related to consular courts in Turkey and Egypt. Such courts in Turkey were abolished Oct. 14, 1949, and such courts in Egypt were abolished by the Treaty of Oct. 28, 1931.
