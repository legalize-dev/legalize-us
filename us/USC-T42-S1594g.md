# § 1594g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–241, title IV, § 406(b)](/us/pl/85/241/tIV/s406/b), Aug. 30, 1957, [71 Stat. 556](/us/stat/71/556)

##### § 1594g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–241, title IV, § 406(b)](/us/pl/85/241/tIV/s406/b), Aug. 30, 1957, [71 Stat. 556](/us/stat/71/556)

Section, [act Aug. 3, 1956, ch. 939, title IV, § 419](/us/act/1956-08-03/ch939/tIV/s419), [70 Stat. 1018](/us/stat/70/1018), related to conditions precedent to entering into contracts for construction or acquisition of family housing units by or for the use of military or civilian personnel of any of the military services.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 85–241, title IV, § 406(b)](/us/pl/85/241/tIV/s406/b), Aug. 30, 1957, [71 Stat. 556](/us/stat/71/556), provided that the repeal of this section is effective July 1, 1958.
