# §§ 981 to 987. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 981 to 987. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 981, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 31, [39 Stat. 382](/us/stat/39/382); [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 21](/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164/s21), [49 Stat. 319](/us/stat/49/319), related to false statements in applications for loans. See [section 1014 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1014), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 982, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 31](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s31), [39 Stat. 382](/us/stat/39/382), related to counterfeiting bonds or coupons. See [section 493 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s493).

Section 983, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 31](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s31), [39 Stat. 382](/us/stat/39/382), related to charging or receiving unauthorized fees or commissions. See sections 216 and 1907 of Title 18.

Section 984, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 31](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s31), [39 Stat. 382](/us/stat/39/382), related to fraud and embezzlement. See sections 657 and 1006 of Title 18.

Section 985, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 31](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s31), [39 Stat. 382](/us/stat/39/382), related to false pretenses as to character of bonds or coupons. See [section 1013 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1013).

Section 986, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 31](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s31), [39 Stat. 382](/us/stat/39/382), related to detection and arrest of violators. See [section 3056 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3056).

Section 987, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 31](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s31) (par.), as added [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VII, § 78, [48 Stat. 272](/us/stat/48/272), related to false statements by mortgagee. See [section 1011 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1011).
