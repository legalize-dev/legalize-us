# § 46a–2. Omitted

##### § 46a–2. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–545, § 101](/us/pl/89/545/s101), Aug. 27, 1966, [80 Stat. 356](/us/stat/80/356), provided, effective fiscal year 1967 and thereafter, for stationery allowance of $3,000 per annum for Senators from States having population of 10 million or more inhabitants. See amendment by [Pub. L. 90–21](/us/pl/90/21) to [section 6117 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6117) providing such an allowance to all Senators effective fiscal year 1967 and thereafter.
