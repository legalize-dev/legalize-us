# § 3787. Transferred

##### § 3787. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 3787 was editorially reclassified as [section 10225 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10225), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 3787, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 807](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s807), as added [Pub. L. 96–157, § 2](/us/pl/96/157/s2), Dec. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 1204](/us/stat/93/1204), contained provisions similar to those in [section 10225 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10225), Crime Control and Law Enforcement, prior to repeal by [section 609B(e) of Pub. L. 98–473](/us/pl/98/473/s609B/e).
