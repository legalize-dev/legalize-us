# § 15115. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 15115. Authorization of appropriations

(a) Scholarships There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out [section 15113 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s15113) $800,000 for fiscal year 2001 and such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 2002 through 2007.

(b) Staff development curriculum There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out [section 15114 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s15114) $800,000 for fiscal year 2001 and such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 2002 and 2003.

*([Pub. L. 106–402, title III, § 305](/us/pl/106/402/tIII/s305), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1737](/us/stat/114/1737).)*
