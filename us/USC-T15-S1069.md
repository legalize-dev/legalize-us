# § 1069. Application of equitable principles in inter partes proceedings

##### § 1069. Application of equitable principles in inter partes proceedings

In all inter partes proceedings equitable principles of laches, estoppel, and acquiescence, where applicable may be considered and applied.

*([July 5, 1946, ch. 540](/us/act/1946-07-05/ch540), title I, § 19, [60 Stat. 434](/us/stat/60/434); [Pub. L. 93–596, § 1](/us/pl/93/596/s1), Jan. 2, 1975, [88 Stat. 1949](/us/stat/88/1949); [Pub. L. 100–667, title I, § 119](/us/pl/100/667/tI/s119), Nov. 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 3941](/us/stat/102/3941).)*
