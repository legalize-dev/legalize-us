# § 2009bb–11. Annual report

##### § 2009bb–11. Annual report

Not later than 180 days after the end of each fiscal year, the Authority shall submit to the President and to Congress a report describing the activities carried out under this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 87–128, title III, § 383M](/us/pl/87/128/tIII/s383M), formerly § 383L, as added [Pub. L. 110–234, title VI, § 6026(c)(1)(A)](/us/pl/110/234/tVI/s6026/c/1/A), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1178](/us/stat/122/1178), and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title VI, § 6026(c)(1)(A), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 1940.)*
