# § 239a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 542(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s542/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 458](/us/stat/95/458)

##### § 239a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 542(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s542/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 458](/us/stat/95/458)

Section, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title I, § 4A](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tI/s4A), as added Oct. 3, 1980, [Pub. L. 96–374, title XIII, § 1341](/us/pl/96/374/tXIII/s1341), [95 Stat. 1500](/us/stat/95/1500), related to payments for special programs for alien children who fled from Cambodia, Vietnam, Laos, Cuba, or Haiti. See provisions set out as notes under [section 1522 of Title 8](/us/usc/t8/s1522), Aliens and Nationality.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 547 of Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35/s547), set out as a note under [section 1522 of Title 8](/us/usc/t8/s1522), Aliens and Nationality.
