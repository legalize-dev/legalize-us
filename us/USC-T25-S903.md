# § 903. Omitted

##### § 903. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–197, § 2](/us/pl/93/197/s2), Dec. 22, 1973, [87 Stat. 770](/us/stat/87/770), which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 93–197, § 1](/us/pl/93/197/s1), Dec. 22, 1973, [87 Stat. 770](/us/stat/87/770), provided that [Pub. L. 93–197](/us/pl/93/197), enacting this subchapter and repealing sections 891 to 902 of this title, could be cited as the “Menominee Restoration Act”.
