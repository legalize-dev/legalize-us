# § 19404. Applicability

##### § 19404. Applicability

This chapter shall apply with respect to a claim that is filed under Federal, State, or Tribal law on or after December 7, 2022.

*([Pub. L. 117–224, § 5](/us/pl/117/224/s5), Dec. 7, 2022, [136 Stat. 2292](/us/stat/136/2292).)*
