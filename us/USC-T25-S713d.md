#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–165, § 6](/us/pl/98/165/s6), Nov. 22, 1983, [97 Stat. 1066](/us/stat/97/1066), which related to the tribal constitution and bylaws and election of the tribal governing body, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
