##### §§ 341 to 351. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(a)](/us/pl/96/591/s8/a), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435](/us/stat/94/3435)

Section 341, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [Mar. 3, 1897, ch. 389, § 12](/us/act/1897-03-03/ch389/s12), [29 Stat. 690](/us/stat/29/690); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 253](/us/stat/62/253), related to rate of speed in fog or bad weather conditions.

Section 341a, R.S. § 4233; [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 254](/us/stat/62/254), related to ascertainment of risk of collision.

Section 342, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [26 Stat. 672](/us/stat/26/672); [Mar. 3, 1897, ch. 389, § 12](/us/act/1897-03-03/ch389/s12), [29 Stat. 690](/us/stat/29/690); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 253](/us/stat/62/253), related to situation in which sailing vessels were approaching one another.

Section 343, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 254](/us/stat/62/254), related to steam vessels meeting end on.

Section 344, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 254](/us/stat/62/254), related to signals to be given in the situation where steam vessels are crossing.

Section 345, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 255](/us/stat/62/255); Nov. 5, 1966, [Pub. Section 346, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 255](/us/stat/62/255), related to duty of steam vessels to slacken speed.

Section 347, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 255](/us/stat/62/255), related to duty of an overtaking vessel to keep out of the way.

Section 348, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 255](/us/stat/62/255), set out rule that a vessel having the right of way was to keep course.

Section 348a, R.S. § 4233, Rule 23(A), as added [Pub. L. 89–764, § 6](/us/pl/89/764/s6), Nov. 5, 1966, [80 Stat. 1313](/us/stat/80/1313), related to rule that a steam vessel could not hamper safe passage of a large vessel or vessel in tow.

Section 349, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 255](/us/stat/62/255), related to danger signals, responding signals, and additional signals.

Section 350, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [Mar. 3, 1897, ch. 389, § 13](/us/act/1897-03-03/ch389/s13), 29 Stat 690; May 21 1948, ch. 328, § 4, [62 Stat. 256](/us/stat/62/256), related to departures from the rules to avoid collision.

Section 351, R.S. § 4233; [Feb. 19, 1895, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-19/ch102/s1), [28 Stat. 672](/us/stat/28/672); [Mar. 3, 1897, ch. 389, § 13](/us/act/1897-03-03/ch389/s13), [29 Stat. 690](/us/stat/29/690); [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 256](/us/stat/62/256), related to usual additional precautions required.
