# § 1034. Omitted

##### § 1034. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 88–457, § 4](/us/pl/88/457/s4), Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 555](/us/stat/78/555), which provided for per capita distributions to members of the Cherokee Band upon completion of roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
