# § 9684. Termination of Overseas Private Investment Corporation and other superceded authorities

##### § 9684. Termination of Overseas Private Investment Corporation and other superceded authorities

(1) the Overseas Private Investment Corporation is terminated; and

(2) Omitted.

*([Pub. L. 115–254, div. F, title VI, § 1464](/us/pl/115/254/dF/tVI/s1464), Oct. 5, 2018, [132 Stat. 3513](/us/stat/132/3513).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

L. 115–254](/us/pl/115/254/s1464) repealed title IV of chapter 2 of part I of the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961 ([22 U.S.C. 2191](/us/usc/t22/s2191) et seq.), other than subsections (g), (*l*), (m), and (n) of section 237 thereof ([22 U.S.C. 2197(g)](/us/usc/t22/s2197/g), (*l*), (m), (n)).
