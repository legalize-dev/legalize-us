# § 10388. General regulatory authority

##### § 10388. General regulatory authority

The Attorney General may promulgate regulations and guidelines to carry out this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 1708](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s1708), as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title I, § 10003(a)(3)](/us/pl/103/322/tI/s10003/a/3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1813](/us/stat/108/1813).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3796dd–7 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796dd–7), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
