# § 1636e. Voluntary reporting

##### § 1636e. Voluntary reporting

The Secretary shall encourage voluntary reporting by packers (as defined in [section 191 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s191)) to which the mandatory reporting requirements of this subchapter do not apply.

*([Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 966](/us/act/1946-08-14/ch966), title II, § 256, as added [Pub. L. 106–78, title IX, § 911(2)](/us/pl/106/78/tIX/s911/2), Oct. 22, 1999, [113 Stat. 1204](/us/stat/113/1204).)*
