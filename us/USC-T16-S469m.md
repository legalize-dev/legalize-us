# § 469m. Repealed or Omitted

##### § 469m. Repealed or Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 111–11, title VII, § 7111](/us/pl/111/11/tVII/s7111), Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1199](/us/stat/123/1199), which related to Women’s Rights National Historical Park, was repealed in part and omitted in part. Subsec. (a), which enacted section 410*ll*–1 of this title, was omitted from the Code. Subsecs. (b) and (c) were repealed and restated in sections 308501 and 308502 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs, by [Pub. L. 113–287](/us/pl/113/287), §§ 3, 7, Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3094](/us/stat/128/3094), 3272, which act enacted Title 54.
