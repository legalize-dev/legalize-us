# § 111e. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 111e. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums, but not more than $193,233 as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of sections 111c to 111e of this title.

*([Pub. L. 88–235, § 3](/us/pl/88/235/s3), Dec. 23, 1963, [77 Stat. 474](/us/stat/77/474); [Pub. L. 94–578, title I, § 101(8)](/us/pl/94/578/tI/s101/8), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2732](/us/stat/90/2732).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1976—[Pub. L. 94–578](/us/pl/94/578) substituted “$193,233” for “$125,000”.
