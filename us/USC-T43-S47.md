# § 47. Repealed. [Aug. 7, 1946, ch. 770, § 1(11)](/us/act/1946-08-07/ch770/s1/11), [60 Stat. 867](/us/stat/60/867)

##### § 47. Repealed. [Aug. 7, 1946, ch. 770, § 1(11)](/us/act/1946-08-07/ch770/s1/11), [60 Stat. 867](/us/stat/60/867)

Section, [act May 10, 1926, ch. 277](/us/act/1926-05-10/ch277), [44 Stat. 487](/us/stat/44/487), required annual statements and reports of expenditures for the benefit of Indians relating to the operation of oil and gas leases, and the mining of other minerals, on Indian lands.
