# § 324. Rule of construction

##### § 324. Rule of construction

Nothing in this subchapter may be construed to diminish or reduce the authority of agency Chief Information Officers for information resources management provided in [section 11315 of title 40](/us/usc/t40/s11315) or those authorities to manage information resources to accomplish agency missions as established in [section 3502 of title 44](/us/usc/t44/s3502).

*([Pub. L. 118–231, § 2(a)](/us/pl/118/231/s2/a), Jan. 4, 2025, [138 Stat. 2831](/us/stat/138/2831).)*
