# § 565. Repealed. [Pub. L. 117–261, § 2](/us/pl/117/261/s2), Dec. 21, 2022, [136 Stat. 2393](/us/stat/136/2393)

##### § 565. Repealed. [Pub. L. 117–261, § 2](/us/pl/117/261/s2), Dec. 21, 2022, [136 Stat. 2393](/us/stat/136/2393)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–224, § 1](/us/pl/89/224/s1), Oct. 1, 1965, [79 Stat. 897](/us/stat/79/897), authorized the Secretary of the Interior to distribute to the Klamath Tribe funds obtained from the Indian Claims Commission from the tribe’s judgment against the United States.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of Repeal

[Pub. L. 117–261, § 1](/us/pl/117/261/s1), Dec. 21, 2022, [136 Stat. 2393](/us/stat/136/2393), provided that: “This Act [repealing this subchapter] may be cited as the ‘Klamath Tribe Judgment Fund Repeal Act’.”
