# § 40903. Definitions

##### § 40903. Definitions

(1) Court order The term “court order” includes a court order (as described in [section 922(g)(8) of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s922/g/8)).

(2) Mental health terms The terms “adjudicated as a mental defective” and “committed to a mental institution” have the same meanings as in [section 922(g)(4) of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s922/g/4).

(3) Misdemeanor crime of domestic violence The term “misdemeanor crime of domestic violence” has the meaning given the term in [section 921(a)(33) of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s921/a/33).

*([Pub. L. 110–180, § 3](/us/pl/110/180/s3), Jan. 8, 2008, [121 Stat. 2560](/us/stat/121/2560).)*
