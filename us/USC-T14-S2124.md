# § 2124. Promotions; pay and allowances

##### § 2124. Promotions; pay and allowances

An officer who is promoted under [section 2121 of this title](/us/usc/t14/s2121) shall be entitled to the pay and allowances of the grade to which promoted from his date of rank in such grade.

*(Added [Pub. L. 88–130, § 1(10)(C)](/us/pl/88/130/s1/10/C), Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 182](/us/stat/77/182), § 274; renumbered § 2124 and amended [Pub. L. 115–282, title I](/us/pl/115/282/tI), §§ 112(b), 123(b)(2), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4216](/us/stat/132/4216), 4240.)*
