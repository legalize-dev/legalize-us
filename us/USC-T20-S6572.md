# § 6572. Agreements and records

##### § 6572. Agreements and records

(a) Agreements In any case in which a negotiated rulemaking process is established under [section 6571(b) of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6571/b), all published proposed regulations shall conform to agreements that result from the rulemaking described in [section 6571 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6571) unless the Secretary reopens the negotiated rulemaking process.

(b) Records The Secretary shall ensure that an accurate and reliable record of agreements reached during the negotiations process is maintained.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 1602 of Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10/s1602) was classified to [section 6512 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6512), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95).

#### Amendments

2015—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 114–95, § 1601(b)](/us/pl/114/95/s1601/b), amended subsec. (a) generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “All published proposed regulations shall conform to agreements that result from negotiated rulemaking described in [section 6571 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6571) unless the Secretary reopens the negotiated rulemaking process or provides a written explanation to the participants involved in the process explaining why the Secretary decided to depart from, and not adhere to, such agreements.”
