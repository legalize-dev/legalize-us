# § 12772. Reserve commissioned officers who have served as Attending Physician to the Congress: grade on transfer to Retired Reserve

##### § 12772. Reserve commissioned officers who have served as Attending Physician to the Congress: grade on transfer to Retired Reserve

Unless entitled to a higher grade under another provision of law, a reserve commissioned officer who is transferred to the Retired Reserve after having served in the position of Attending Physician to the Congress is entitled to be placed on the retired list established by [section 12774(a) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s12774/a) in the grade held by the officer while serving in that position.

*(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1662(k)(1)](/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI/s1662/k/1), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 3005](/us/stat/108/3005).)*
