# § 3796gg–9. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–271, § 3(a)](/us/pl/109/271/s3/a), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 754](/us/stat/120/754)

##### § 3796gg–9. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–271, § 3(a)](/us/pl/109/271/s3/a), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 754](/us/stat/120/754)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2014](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2014), as added [Pub. L. 109–162, title II, § 202](/us/pl/109/162/tII/s202), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 2994](/us/stat/119/2994), related to sexual assault services. See [section 12511 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s12511), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 109–271](/us/pl/109/271), which directed the repeal of section 202 of the Violence Against Women and Department of Justice Reauthorization Act of 2005 ([Pub. L. 109–162](/us/pl/109/162)), was executed by repealing this section, which was section 2014 of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968 as added by [section 202 of Pub. L. 109–162](/us/pl/109/162/s202), to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
