# § 8212. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 8212. Authorization of appropriations

(a) There is authorized to be appropriated $25,000,000 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1973, and a like sum for each of the six succeeding fiscal years. Sums appropriated pursuant to this section shall be used for making grants pursuant to [section 8213 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s8213).

(b) Sums appropriated pursuant to subsection (a) of this section shall remain available until the end of the sixth fiscal year following the fiscal year for which they are appropriated.

*(Added [Pub. L. 92–541, § 2(a)](/us/pl/92/541/s2/a), Oct. 24, 1972, [86 Stat. 1102](/us/stat/86/1102), § 5072; renumbered § 8212 and amended [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV, § 402(b)(2)(B)](/us/pl/102/40/tIV/s402/b/2/B), (d)(1), May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 239](/us/stat/105/239).)*
