# § 4801. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–626, title II, § 208(b)](/us/pl/95/626/tII/s208/b), Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3588](/us/stat/92/3588)

##### § 4801. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–626, title II, § 208(b)](/us/pl/95/626/tII/s208/b), Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3588](/us/stat/92/3588)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–695, title I, § 101](/us/pl/91/695/tI/s101), Jan. 13, 1971, [84 Stat. 2078](/us/stat/84/2078); [Pub. L. 93–151, § 1](/us/pl/93/151/s1), Nov. 9, 1973, [87 Stat. 565](/us/stat/87/565); [Pub. L. 94–317, title II](/us/pl/94/317/tII), 204(a), June 23, 1976, [90 Stat. 705](/us/stat/90/705), related to the development of local programs with respect to detection and treatment of lead-based paint poisoning.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 95–626, title II, § 208(b)](/us/pl/95/626/tII/s208/b), Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3588](/us/stat/92/3588), provided that the repeal of this section is effective Oct. 1, 1979.

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 91–695, § 1](/us/pl/91/695/s1), Jan. 13, 1971, [84 Stat. 2078](/us/stat/84/2078), provided: “That this Act [enacting this chapter] may be cited as the ‘Lead-Based Paint Poisoning Prevention Act’.”
