#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 99–660, title IX, § 931](/us/pl/99/660/tIX/s931), Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3807](/us/stat/100/3807), which provided for awards for biomedical research on Alzheimer’s disease and related dementias, was redesignated section 445B of the Public Health Service Act by [Pub. L. 100–607, title I, § 142(a)](/us/pl/100/607/tI/s142/a), Nov. 4, 1988, [102 Stat. 3057](/us/stat/102/3057), and is classified to [section 285e–4 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s285e–4).
