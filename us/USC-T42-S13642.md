# § 13642. Applicability

##### § 13642. Applicability

Except as otherwise provided in subtitles B through F of this title and the amendments made by such subtitles, such subtitles and the amendments made by such subtitles shall apply upon the expiration of the 6-month period beginning on October 28, 1992.

*([Pub. L. 102–550, title VI, § 684](/us/pl/102/550/tVI/s684), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 3832](/us/stat/106/3832).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Subtitles B through F of this title, referred to in text, mean subtitles B to F of title VI of [Pub. L. 102–550](/us/pl/102/550), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 3812–3830](/us/stat/106/3812-3830), which enacted this chapter, amended sections 1437a, 1437c to 1437g, 1437*l*, 1437*o*, 1438, 8011, 8013, and 12705 of this title and [section 1701q of Title 12](/us/usc/t12/s1701q), Banks and Banking, and enacted provisions set out as notes under [section 1437a of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1437a) and [section 1701q of Title 12](/us/usc/t12/s1701q).
