# §§ 64a to 64c. Omitted

##### §§ 64a to 64c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 64a, [act Feb. 21, 1929, ch. 288](/us/act/1929-02-21/ch288), [45 Stat. 1254](/us/stat/45/1254), defined child and children as used in [section 64 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s64), and was superseded by [section 401 of Title 37](/us/usc/t37/s401), Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.

Section 64b, [act June 24, 1935, ch. 291, § 3](/us/act/1935-06-24/ch291/s3), [49 Stat. 421](/us/stat/49/421), defined permanent change in station as used in [section 64 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s64) and was superseded by [section 411 of Title 37](/us/usc/t37/s411).

Provisions of section 64c, which related to use of government automobiles to transport school children at isolated stations, were enacted as permanent legislation by the 1948 amendment to [section 227 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s227). Section 64c was based upon the following prior appropriation acts:

[June 14, 1948, ch. 465](/us/act/1948-06-14/ch465), title II, [62 Stat. 400](/us/stat/62/400).

[July 8, 1947, ch. 210](/us/act/1947-07-08/ch210), title II, [61 Stat. 267](/us/stat/61/267).

[July 26, 1946, ch. 672](/us/act/1946-07-26/ch672), title II, [60 Stat. 690](/us/stat/60/690).

[July 3, 1945, ch. 263](/us/act/1945-07-03/ch263), title II, [59 Stat. 368](/us/stat/59/368).

[June 28, 1944, ch. 302](/us/act/1944-06-28/ch302), title II, [58 Stat. 559](/us/stat/58/559).

[July 12, 1943, ch. 221](/us/act/1943-07-12/ch221), title II, [57 Stat. 507](/us/stat/57/507).

[July 2, 1942, ch. 475](/us/act/1942-07-02/ch475), title II, [56 Stat. 582](/us/stat/56/582).

[July 1, 1941, ch. 269](/us/act/1941-07-01/ch269), title II, [55 Stat. 483](/us/stat/55/483).

[June 26, 1940, ch. 428](/us/act/1940-06-26/ch428), title II, [54 Stat. 587](/us/stat/54/587).
