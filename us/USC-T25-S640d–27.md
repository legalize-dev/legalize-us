#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 29](/us/pl/93/531/s29), as added [Pub. L. 96–305, § 11](/us/pl/96/305/s11), July 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 934](/us/stat/94/934), which related to attorney fees, costs and expenses for litigation or court action, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
