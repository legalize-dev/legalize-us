# § 2122. Authorization of appropriations for cotton ginning studies

##### § 2122. Authorization of appropriations for cotton ginning studies

For the purposes of [section 2121 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2121) there is authorized to be appropriated, after June 30, 1931, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, such sums as may be necessary.

*([Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 203, § 2](/us/act/1930-04-19/ch203/s2), [46 Stat. 248](/us/stat/46/248).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 425 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s425) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Section was enacted as part of [act Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 203](/us/act/1930-04-19/ch203), and not as part of the Cotton Research and Promotion Act which comprises this chapter.
