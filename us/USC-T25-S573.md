# § 573. Omitted

##### § 573. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 27, 1939, ch. 387, § 3](/us/act/1939-07-27/ch387/s3), [53 Stat. 1129](/us/stat/53/1129), which appropriated from the judgment fund sums for the purchase of lands, the establishment of a loan fund, and the establishment and administration of productive enterprises, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
