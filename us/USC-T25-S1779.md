# § 1779. Omitted

##### § 1779. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 107–331, title VI, § 602](/us/pl/107/331/tVI/s602), Dec. 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 2845](/us/stat/116/2845), which set out congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 107–331, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/107/331/tVI/s601), Dec. 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 2845](/us/stat/116/2845), provided that title VI of [Pub. L. 107–331](/us/pl/107/331), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Cherokee, Choctaw, and Chickasaw Nations Claims Settlement Act”.
