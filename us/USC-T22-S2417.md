# § 2417. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–113, title VII, § 734(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/113/tVII/s734/a/1), Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1560](/us/stat/95/1560)

##### § 2417. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–113, title VII, § 734(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/113/tVII/s734/a/1), Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1560](/us/stat/95/1560)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 657, as added [Pub. L. 95–384, § 14](/us/pl/95/384/s14), Sept. 26, 1978, [92 Stat. 739](/us/stat/92/739), required annual Presidential reports for fiscal year ending previous Sept. 30 respecting military assistance, military education and training assistance, foreign military sales, and commercial military sales. See sections 2394 and 2765 of this title.

A prior section 2417, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 657, as added [Pub. L. 92–226](/us/pl/92/226), pt. III, § 304(b), Feb. 7, 1972, [86 Stat. 30](/us/stat/86/30); amended [Pub. L. 93–189, § 23](/us/pl/93/189/s23), Dec. 17, 1973, [87 Stat. 726](/us/stat/87/726); [Pub. L. 94–273, § 5(2)](/us/pl/94/273/s5/2), Apr. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 377](/us/stat/90/377); [Pub. L. 94–329, title II, § 216](/us/pl/94/329/tII/s216), June 30, 1976, [90 Stat. 747](/us/stat/90/747), related to an annual report by the President to Congress showing the dollar value of foreign assistance including military sales, education and training, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 95–424, title V, § 502(d)(1)](/us/pl/95/424/tV/s502/d/1), Oct. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 959](/us/stat/92/959), effective Oct. 1, 1978.
