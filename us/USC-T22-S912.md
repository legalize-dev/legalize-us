# § 912. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

##### § 912. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

Section, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title V, § 517, [60 Stat. 1008](/us/stat/60/1008); [Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23, § 4](/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23/s4), [69 Stat. 25](/us/stat/69/25); [July 28, 1956, ch. 770, § 6](/us/act/1956-07-28/ch770/s6), [70 Stat. 704](/us/stat/70/704); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 9](/us/pl/86/723/s9), [74 Stat. 832](/us/stat/74/832), related to admission to classes 1 to 7 for Foreign Service officers.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).

#### Transfer of Officers From Old to New Classes

[Act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title XI, § 1102](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tXI/s1102), [60 Stat. 1033](/us/stat/60/1033), which related to transfer of officers from old class to new class, was repealed by [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159).

#### In Class Promotions

[Act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title XI, § 1104](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tXI/s1104), [60 Stat. 1034](/us/stat/60/1034), which related to in class promotions, was repealed by [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159).
