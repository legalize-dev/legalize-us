#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 85–395, § 3](/us/pl/85/395/s3), May 9, 1958, [72 Stat. 106](/us/stat/72/106); [Pub. L. 86–540](/us/pl/86/540), June 29, 1960, [74 Stat. 252](/us/stat/74/252), which related to payments to next of kin or legatees, minors, and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
