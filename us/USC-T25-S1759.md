# § 1759. Omitted

##### § 1759. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–134, § 10](/us/pl/98/134/s10), Oct. 18, 1983, [97 Stat. 856](/us/stat/97/856), which provided for a general discharge and release of the State of Connecticut, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
