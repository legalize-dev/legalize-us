# § 712. Omitted

##### § 712. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 97–391, § 2](/us/pl/97/391/s2), Dec. 29, 1982, [96 Stat. 1960](/us/stat/96/1960); [Pub. L. 100–139, § 5(b)](/us/pl/100/139/s5/b), Oct. 26, 1987, [101 Stat. 827](/us/stat/101/827), which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of 1987 Amendment

[Pub. L. 100–139, § 1](/us/pl/100/139/s1), Oct. 26, 1987, [101 Stat. 822](/us/stat/101/822), provided that [Pub. L. 100–139](/us/pl/100/139) [see Tables for classification] could be cited as the “Cow Creek Band of Umpqua Tribe of Indians Distribution of Judgment Funds Act of 1987”.

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 97–391, § 1](/us/pl/97/391/s1), Dec. 29, 1982, [96 Stat. 1960](/us/stat/96/1960), provided that [Pub. L. 97–391](/us/pl/97/391), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Cow Creek Band of Umpqua Tribe of Indians Recognition Act”.
