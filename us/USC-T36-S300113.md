# § 300113. Reservation of right to amend or repeal

##### § 300113. Reservation of right to amend or repeal

Congress reserves the right to amend or repeal the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1494](/us/stat/112/1494), § 300111; renumbered § 300113, [Pub. L. 110–26, § 11(a)](/us/pl/110/26/s11/a), May 11, 2007, [121 Stat. 109](/us/stat/121/109).)*
