# § 165a. Omitted

##### § 165a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–139, title II](/us/pl/98/139/tII), Oct. 31, 1983, [97 Stat. 881](/us/stat/97/881), which provided that during fiscal year 1984 and thereafter the superintendent of Saint Elizabeths Hospital could reside off the premises of the hospital, notwithstanding former [section 165 of this title](/us/usc/t24/s165), was omitted in light of the transfer of Saint Elizabeths Hospital to the District of Columbia on Oct. 1, 1987, by [Pub. L. 98–621](/us/pl/98/621), Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3369](/us/stat/98/3369), which is classified principally to subchapter III (§ 225 et seq.) of this chapter.
