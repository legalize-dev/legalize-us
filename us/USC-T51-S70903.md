# § 70903. International Space Station research

##### § 70903. International Space Station research

(1) carry out a program of microgravity research consistent with [section 40904 of this title](/us/usc/t51/s40904); and

(2) consider the need for a life sciences centrifuge and any associated holding facilities.

*([Pub. L. 111–314, § 3](/us/pl/111/314/s3), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3436](/us/stat/124/3436).)*
