# § 702. Omitted

##### § 702. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 733, § 12](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch733/s12), [68 Stat. 727](/us/stat/68/727), which related to execution of patents, deeds, and other instruments by the Secretary of the Interior, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
