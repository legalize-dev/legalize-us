# § 379a. Presumption of existence of jurisdiction

##### § 379a. Presumption of existence of jurisdiction

In any action to enforce the requirements of this chapter respecting a device, tobacco product, food, drug, or cosmetic the connection with interstate commerce required for jurisdiction in such action shall be presumed to exist.

*([June 25, 1938, ch. 675, § 709](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch675/s709), as added [Pub. L. 94–295, § 8](/us/pl/94/295/s8), May 28, 1976, [90 Stat. 583](/us/stat/90/583); amended [Pub. L. 105–115, title IV, § 419](/us/pl/105/115/tIV/s419), Nov. 21, 1997, [111 Stat. 2379](/us/stat/111/2379); [Pub. L. 111–31, div. A, title I, § 103(k)](/us/pl/111/31/dA/tI/s103/k), June 22, 2009, [123 Stat. 1837](/us/stat/123/1837).)*

#### Amendments

2009—[Pub. L. 111–31](/us/pl/111/31) inserted “tobacco product,” after “device,”.
