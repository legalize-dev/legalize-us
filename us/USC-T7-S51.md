# § 51. Short title

##### § 51. Short title

This chapter shall be known by the short title of “United States Cotton Standards Act.”

*([Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch288/s1), [42 Stat. 1517](/us/stat/42/1517).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

[Act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 288, § 14](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch288/s14), [42 Stat. 1520](/us/stat/42/1520), provided: “That this Act [enacting this chapter] shall become effective on and after August 1, 1923.”
