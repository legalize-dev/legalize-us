# § 1715. Omitted

##### § 1715. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–395, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/395/tII/s201), as added [Pub. L. 96–601, § 5(a)](/us/pl/96/601/s5/a), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3498](/us/stat/94/3498), which provided for tax exemption for settlement lands, with certain exceptions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
