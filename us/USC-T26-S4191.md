# [§ 4191. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–94, div. N, title I, § 501(a)](/us/pl/116/94/dN/tI/s501/a), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 3118](/us/stat/133/3118)]

##### [§ 4191. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–94, div. N, title I, § 501(a)](/us/pl/116/94/dN/tI/s501/a), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 3118](/us/stat/133/3118)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 111–152, title I, § 1405(a)(1)](/us/pl/111/152/tI/s1405/a/1), Mar. 30, 2010, [124 Stat. 1064](/us/stat/124/1064); amended [Pub. L. 114–113, div. Q, title I, § 174(a)](/us/pl/114/113/dQ/tI/s174/a), Dec. 18, 2015, [129 Stat. 3071](/us/stat/129/3071); [Pub. L. 115–120, div. D, § 4001(a)](/us/pl/115/120/dD/s4001/a), Jan. 22, 2018, [132 Stat. 38](/us/stat/132/38), imposed on the sale of any taxable medical device by the manufacturer, producer, or importer a tax equal to 2.3 percent of the price for which so sold.

For prior sections 4191, 4192, 4201, and 4211, see Prior Provisions note set out preceding this section.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to sales after Dec. 31, 2019, see [section 501(d) of Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94/s501/d), set out as an Effective Date of 2019 Amendment note under [section 4221 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s4221).
