##### § 640d–26. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 28](/us/pl/93/531/s28), as added [Pub. L. 96–305, § 11](/us/pl/96/305/s11), July 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 933](/us/stat/94/933), which related to applicability of environmental impact provisions and sections 1782 and 1752(g) of Title 43, Public Lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
