# § 4503c. Omitted

##### § 4503c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–513, title VI, § 607](/us/pl/101/513/tVI/s607), as added [Pub. L. 102–574, § 2(a)(2)](/us/pl/102/574/s2/a/2), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4595](/us/stat/106/4595), which required the Secretary of Agriculture to make annual reports to Congress, submitting the reports pursuant to [section 1606(c) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s1606/c), on the progress, needs, and long-range plans of the Institutes of Tropical Forestry in meeting the requirements of [section 6706 of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s6706), Agriculture, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance. See, also, page 45 of House Document No. 103–7.

A prior [section 607 of Pub. L. 101–513](/us/pl/101/513/s607), which amended sections 1641, 1643, 2101, and 2109 of this title, was renumbered section 611.
