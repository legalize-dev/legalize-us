# §§ 236 to 245. Transferred

##### §§ 236 to 245. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 236, [act Jan. 31, 1922, ch. 44, § 1](/us/act/1922-01-31/ch44/s1), [42 Stat. 361](/us/stat/42/361), which related to prohibition against exportation of arms to American countries or countries under American jurisdiction in a state of domestic violence, was transferred to [section 409 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s409) and was repealed by [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862).

Section 237, [act Jan. 31, 1922, ch. 44, § 2](/us/act/1922-01-31/ch44/s2), [42 Stat. 361](/us/stat/42/361), which related to penalties, was transferred to [section 410 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s410) and was repealed by [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862).

Section 238, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 1](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s1), [40 Stat. 223](/us/stat/40/223), as amended, which related to illegal exportation of war materials, was transferred to [section 401 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s401).

Section 239, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 2](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s2), [40 Stat. 224](/us/stat/40/224), which related to issuance of warrant for detention of seized property, was transferred to [section 402 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s402) and was repealed by [act Aug. 13, 1953, ch. 434, § 2](/us/act/1953-08-13/ch434/s2), [67 Stat. 577](/us/stat/67/577).

Section 240, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 3](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s3), [40 Stat. 224](/us/stat/40/224), which related to filing petition for restoration of seized property, was transferred to [section 403 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s403) and was repealed by [act Aug. 13, 1953, ch. 434, § 2](/us/act/1953-08-13/ch434/s2), [67 Stat. 577](/us/stat/67/577).

Section 241, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 4](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s4), [40 Stat. 224](/us/stat/40/224), as amended, which related to institution of libel proceedings and sale of seized property, was transferred to [section 404 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s404) and was repealed by [act Aug. 13, 1953, ch. 434, § 2](/us/act/1953-08-13/ch434/s2), [67 Stat. 577](/us/stat/67/577).

Section 242, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 5](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s5), [40 Stat. 224](/us/stat/40/224), which related to method of trial and bond for redelivery, was transferred to [section 405 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s405) and was repealed by [act Aug. 13, 1953, ch. 434, § 2](/us/act/1953-08-13/ch434/s2), [67 Stat. 577](/us/stat/67/577).

Section 243, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 6](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s6), [40 Stat. 225](/us/stat/40/225), which related to interference with foreign trade, was transferred to [section 406 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s406).

Section 244, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 7](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s7), [40 Stat. 225](/us/stat/40/225), which related to the President’s discretion to release seized property, was transferred to [section 407 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s407) and was repealed by act Aug, 13, 1953, ch. 434, § 2, [67 Stat. 577](/us/stat/67/577).

Section 245, [act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title VI, § 8](/us/act/1917-06-15/ch30/tVI/s8), [40 Stat. 225](/us/stat/40/225), which related to use of land and naval forces to prevent exportation, was transferred to [section 408 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s408).
