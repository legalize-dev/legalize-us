# § 1537. Children of Spanish-American War veterans

##### § 1537. Children of Spanish-American War veterans

Whenever there is no surviving spouse entitled to pension under [section 1536 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s1536), the Secretary shall pay to the children of each Spanish-American War veteran who met the service requirements of [section 1512(a) of this title](/us/usc/t38/s1512/a) a pension at the monthly rate of $73.13 for one child, plus $8.13 for each additional child, with the total amount equally divided.

*([Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1138](/us/stat/72/1138), § 537; [Pub.
