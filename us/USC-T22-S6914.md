# § 6914. Votes of the Commission

##### § 6914. Votes of the Commission

Decisions of the Commission, including adoption of reports and recommendations to the executive branch or to the Congress, shall be made by a majority vote of the members of the Commission present and voting. Two-thirds of the Members 11 So in original. Probably should not be capitalized. of the Commission shall constitute a quorum for purposes of conducting business.
