# § 2352. Regulations

##### § 2352. Regulations

The Secretary shall prescribe such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this part.

*([Pub. L. 93–618, title II, § 262](/us/pl/93/618/tII/s262), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2034](/us/stat/88/2034); temporarily renumbered § 260, [Pub. L. 111–5, div. B, title I, § 1864(a)(2)](/us/pl/111/5/dB/tI/s1864/a/2), Feb. 17, 2009, [123 Stat. 397](/us/stat/123/397), and [Pub. L. 112–40, title II, § 201(b)](/us/pl/112/40/tII/s201/b), (c), Oct. 21, 2011, [125 Stat. 403](/us/stat/125/403), and [Pub. L. 114–27, title IV, § 402(b)](/us/pl/114/27/tIV/s402/b), (c), June 29, 2015, [129 Stat. 374](/us/stat/129/374).)*

#### Termination of Assistance

For termination of assistance under this part after June 30, 2022, see Termination Date note below.
