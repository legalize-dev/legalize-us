# § 2112a. Continued operation of University

##### § 2112a. Continued operation of University

The University may not be closed.

*(Added [Pub. L. 104–201, div. A, title IX, § 907(a)(1)](/us/pl/104/201/dA/tIX/s907/a/1), Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2620](/us/stat/110/2620); amended [Pub. L. 114–328, div. A, title VII, § 724(c)](/us/pl/114/328/dA/tVII/s724/c), Dec. 23, 2016, [130 Stat. 2230](/us/stat/130/2230).)*
