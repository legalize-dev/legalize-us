# § 656. Omitted

##### § 656. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 18, 1928, ch. 624, § 6](/us/act/1928-05-18/ch624/s6), [45 Stat. 603](/us/stat/45/603), which provided for judgments to be placed in the Treasury of the United States to the credit of the Indians of California and to accrue interest at the rate of 4 per centum per annum, and which set out authorized uses for the fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
