# §§ 1031 to 1033. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 1031 to 1033. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 1031, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 202(a)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s202/a), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1455](/us/stat/42/1455); amended [Mar. 4, 1925, ch. 524, § 7](/us/act/1925-03-04/ch524/s7), [43 Stat. 1264](/us/stat/43/1264); [June 26, 1930, ch. 616, § 1](/us/act/1930-06-26/ch616/s1), [46 Stat. 816](/us/stat/46/816); [May 19, 1932, ch. 191, § 1](/us/act/1932-05-19/ch191/s1), [47 Stat. 159](/us/stat/47/159); [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VII, § 76(b), (c), [48 Stat. 271](/us/stat/48/271); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 5(a)](/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164/s5/a), (b), [49 Stat. 315](/us/stat/49/315); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 104(b), [70 Stat. 663](/us/stat/70/663); Oct. 4, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–237, § 1(a)](/us/pl/89/237/s1/a), [79 Stat. 922](/us/stat/79/922); Aug. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–525, § 3(a)](/us/pl/89/525/s3/a), [80 Stat. 334](/us/stat/80/334), set out lending powers of intermediate credit banks. See [section 2074 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2074).

Section 1032, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 202(b)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s202/b), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1455](/us/stat/42/1455), placed a limitation on amount of purchases by intermediate credit banks for national banks, State banks, trust companies, or saving institutions.

Section 1033, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 202(c)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s202/c), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1455](/us/stat/42/1455); amended [June 26, 1930, ch. 616, § 2](/us/act/1930-06-26/ch616/s2), [46 Stat. 816](/us/stat/46/816); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 104(c), [70 Stat. 664](/us/stat/70/664); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(f)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/f), [73 Stat. 386](/us/stat/73/386); Oct. 3, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–343, § 1(b)](/us/pl/87/343/s1/b), [75 Stat. 758](/us/stat/75/758), covered maturity, sale, and purchase of loans, advances, and discounts of intermediate credit banks.
