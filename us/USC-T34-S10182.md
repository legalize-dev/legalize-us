# § 10182. Period of award

##### § 10182. Period of award

(1) an evaluation of the program or project indicates that it has been effective in achieving the stated goals or offers the potential for improving the functioning of the criminal justice system; and

(2) the applicant that conducts such program or project agrees to provide at least one-half of the total cost of such program or project from any source of funds, including Federal grants, available to the eligible jurisdiction.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 518](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s518), formerly § 514, as added [Pub. L. 100–690, title VI, § 6091(a)](/us/pl/100/690/tVI/s6091/a), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4336](/us/stat/102/4336); renumbered § 518 and amended [Pub. L. 101–647, title XVIII, § 1801(a)(5)](/us/pl/101/647/tXVIII/s1801/a/5), (6), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4847](/us/stat/104/4847).)*

Section was formerly classified to [section 3764 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3764), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
