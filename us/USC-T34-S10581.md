# § 10581. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(g)(2)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s504/g/2), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5234](/us/stat/132/5234)

##### § 10581. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(g)(2)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s504/g/2), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5234](/us/stat/132/5234)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2901](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2901), as added [Pub. L. 114–255, div. B, title XIV, § 14013](/us/pl/114/255/dB/tXIV/s14013), Dec. 13, 2016, [130 Stat. 1298](/us/stat/130/1298), related to mental health and drug treatment alternatives to incarceration programs.

Section was formerly classified to [section 3797q of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3797q), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

A prior [section 2901 of title I of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2901), as added [Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 112(a)](/us/pl/110/199/tI/s112/a), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 672](/us/stat/122/672), authorized the Attorney General to make grants for qualified drug treatment programs as alternatives to imprisonment, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 114–255, div. B, title XIV, § 14013](/us/pl/114/255/dB/tXIV/s14013), Dec. 13, 2016, [130 Stat. 1298](/us/stat/130/1298).
