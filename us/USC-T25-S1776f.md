#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–444, § 8](/us/pl/103/444/s8), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4640](/us/stat/108/4640), which related to exchanges of land or minerals, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
