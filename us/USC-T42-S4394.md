#### Codification

L. 91–515, title V, § 501(d)](/us/pl/91/515/tV/s501/d), Oct. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1310](/us/stat/84/1310), which required the President, within one year of his transmittal to Congress of the report required by [section 4393 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4393), and annually thereafter, to supplement that report with such new data, evaluations, or recommendations as he may deem appropriate, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance. See, also, item 6 on page 20 of House Document No. 103–7.
