# § 5847. Effect on other laws

##### § 5847. Effect on other laws

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as modifying or affecting the requirements of section 38 of the Arms Export Control Act ([22 U.S.C. 2778](/us/usc/t22/s2778)), as amended, with respect to the manufacture, exportation, and importation of arms, ammunition, and implements of war.

*(Added [Pub. L. 90–618, title II, § 201](/us/pl/90/618/tII/s201), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1232](/us/stat/82/1232); amended [Pub. L. 115–141, div. U, title IV, § 401(a)(252)](/us/pl/115/141/dU/tIV/s401/a/252), Mar. 23, 2018, [132 Stat. 1196](/us/stat/132/1196).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 5847, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 726](/us/stat/68A/726), related to regulations which the Secretary or his delegate may prescribe, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 90–618](/us/pl/90/618).

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–141](/us/pl/115/141) substituted “section 38 of the Arms Export Control Act ([22 U.S.C. 2778](/us/usc/t22/s2778))” for “section 414 of the Mutual Security Act of 1954”.
