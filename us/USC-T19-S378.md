# § 378. Repealed. [Feb. 28, 1933, ch. 131, § 1](/us/act/1933-02-28/ch131/s1), [47 Stat. 1349](/us/stat/47/1349)

##### § 378. Repealed. [Feb. 28, 1933, ch. 131, § 1](/us/act/1933-02-28/ch131/s1), [47 Stat. 1349](/us/stat/47/1349)

Section, R.S. § 2938, related to appraiser as special examiner.

[Act Feb. 28, 1933, ch. 131, § 2](/us/act/1933-02-28/ch131/s2), [47 Stat. 1349](/us/stat/47/1349), provided that rights or liabilities existing under this section on Feb. 28, 1933, should not be affected thereby.
