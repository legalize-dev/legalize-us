# § 1703. Omitted

##### § 1703. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–395, § 4](/us/pl/95/395/s4), Sept. 30, 1978, [92 Stat. 814](/us/stat/92/814), which established the Rhode Island Indian Claims Settlement Fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
