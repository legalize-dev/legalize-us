##### § 60f–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–213, § 1(c)](/us/pl/86/213/s1/c), Sept. 1, 1959, [73 Stat. 444](/us/stat/73/444)

Section, [act June 27, 1956, ch. 453](/us/act/1956-06-27/ch453), [70 Stat. 359](/us/stat/70/359), authorized Senators to fix basic compensation of one employee at a rate not to exceed $8,040 per annum.
