# § 216c. Rules and regulations

##### § 216c. Rules and regulations

The Comptroller may issue rules and regulations necessary or appropriate to carry out this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 96–221, title VII, § 734](/us/pl/96/221/tVII/s734), as added [Pub. L. 97–320, title IV, § 408](/us/pl/97/320/tIV/s408), Oct. 15, 1982, [96 Stat. 1515](/us/stat/96/1515).)*
