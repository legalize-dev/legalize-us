# § 648a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–140, title VI, § 609(e)](/us/pl/102/140/tVI/s609/e), Oct. 28, 1991, [105 Stat. 826](/us/stat/105/826)

##### § 648a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–140, title VI, § 609(e)](/us/pl/102/140/tVI/s609/e), Oct. 28, 1991, [105 Stat. 826](/us/stat/105/826)

Section, [Pub. L. 85–536, § 2[21A]](/us/pl/85/536/s2/s21A), as added [Pub. L. 101–515, title V, § 9(a)](/us/pl/101/515/tV/s9/a), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 2144](/us/stat/104/2144), related to Small Business Development Center Technical Assistance Program.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal; Termination of Funding

[Pub. L. 102–140, title VI, § 609(e)](/us/pl/102/140/tVI/s609/e), Oct. 28, 1991, [105 Stat. 826](/us/stat/105/826), provided that: “Notwithstanding any other law, no funds shall be appropriated to carry out section 21A of the Small Business Act [[15 U.S.C. 648a](/us/usc/t15/s648a)] after September 30, 1991, and such section is repealed October 1, 1992.”
