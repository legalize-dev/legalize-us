# [§ 1342. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1901(a)(147)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1901/a/147), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1788](/us/stat/90/1788)]

##### [§ 1342. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1901(a)(147)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1901/a/147), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1788](/us/stat/90/1788)]

Section, added [Aug. 12, 1955, ch. 870, § 3](/us/act/1955-08-12/ch870/s3), [69 Stat. 717](/us/stat/69/717), related to computation of tax where taxpayer recovers substantial amount held by another under claim of right.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective for taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 1976, see [section 1901(d) of Pub. L. 94–455](/us/pl/94/455/s1901/d), set out as an Effective Date of 1976 Amendment note under [section 2 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s2).
