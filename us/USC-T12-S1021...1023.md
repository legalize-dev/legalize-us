# §§ 1021 to 1023. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 1021 to 1023. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 1021, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 201(a)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s201/a), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1454](/us/stat/42/1454); amended Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, authorized the Farm Credit Administration to grant charters for 12 intermediate credit banks. See [section 2071 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2071).

Section 1022, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 201](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s201) (b), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1454](/us/stat/42/1454); amended [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VII, § 76(a), [48 Stat. 271](/us/stat/48/271); [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 10](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s10), [50 Stat. 707](/us/stat/50/707); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 104(a), [70 Stat. 663](/us/stat/70/663), covered the location, directors, officers, employees, and by-laws of intermediate credit banks. See [section 2072 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2072).

Section 1023, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 201(c)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s201/c), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1454](/us/stat/42/1454); amended [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 26](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s26), [50 Stat. 715](/us/stat/50/715), set out the corporate powers of intermediate credit banks. See [section 2072 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2072).
