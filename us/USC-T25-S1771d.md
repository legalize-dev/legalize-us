# § 1771d. Omitted

##### § 1771d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–95, § 6](/us/pl/100/95/s6), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 706](/us/stat/101/706), which related to the purchase and transfer of settlement lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
