# §§ 129a to 131. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–171, title X, § 10418(a)(1)](/us/pl/107/171/tX/s10418/a/1), (8), (16), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 507](/us/stat/116/507), 508

##### §§ 129a to 131. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–171, title X, § 10418(a)(1)](/us/pl/107/171/tX/s10418/a/1), (8), (16), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 507](/us/stat/116/507), 508

Section 129a, [Pub. L. 97–46, § 1](/us/pl/97/46/s1), Sept. 25, 1981, [95 Stat. 953](/us/stat/95/953); [Pub. L. 106–224, title IV, § 438(b)](/us/pl/106/224/tIV/s438/b), June 20, 2000, [114 Stat. 454](/us/stat/114/454), related to transfer of funds for emergency arrest of animal or poultry diseases. Section was formerly classified to [section 147b of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s147b), Agriculture.

Section 130, [act May 29, 1884, ch. 60, § 8](/us/act/1884-05-29/ch60/s8), [23 Stat. 33](/us/stat/23/33); 1967 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 402(430), eff. Aug. 11, 1967, 32 F.R. 11669, [81 Stat. 948](/us/stat/81/948); [Pub. L. 93–198, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/93/198/tIV/s401), Dec. 24, 1973, [87 Stat. 793](/us/stat/87/793), related to pleuropneumonia in District of Columbia and duties of Council of the District of Columbia.

Section 131, [act May 26, 1910, ch. 256](/us/act/1910-05-26/ch256), [36 Stat. 440](/us/stat/36/440), related to fences along international boundary lines to keep out diseased animals.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 97–46, § 2](/us/pl/97/46/s2), Sept. 25, 1981, [95 Stat. 953](/us/stat/95/953), provided that [section 129a of this title](/us/usc/t21/s129a) was to become effective Sept. 25, 1981, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 107–171, title X, § 10418(a)(1)](/us/pl/107/171/tX/s10418/a/1), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 507](/us/stat/116/507).
