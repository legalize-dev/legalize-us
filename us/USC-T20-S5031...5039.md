# §§ 5031 to 5039. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/i), (j), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

##### §§ 5031 to 5039. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/i), (j), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

Section 5031, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6041](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6041), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1483](/us/stat/102/1483), provided that this part could be cited as the “Educational Partnerships Act of 1988”.

Section 5032, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6042](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6042), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1483](/us/stat/102/1483), stated purpose of this part.

Section 5033, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6043](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6043), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1483](/us/stat/102/1483), authorized grants to eligible partnerships to use public and private resources for various educational purposes.

Section 5034, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6044](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6044), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1483](/us/stat/102/1483), related to authorized activities by eligible partnerships receiving payments.

Section 5035, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6045](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6045), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1484](/us/stat/102/1484), related to applications for grants.

Section 5036, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6046](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6046), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1485](/us/stat/102/1485), related to approval of applications.

Section 5037, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6047](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6047), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1485](/us/stat/102/1485), related to computation of grant amounts.

Section 5038, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6048](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6048), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1485](/us/stat/102/1485), related to evaluation by Secretary of grants and dissemination of information relating to assisted activities.

Section 5039, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6049](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6049), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1486](/us/stat/102/1486), defined terms used in this part.
