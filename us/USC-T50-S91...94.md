# §§ 91 to 94. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### §§ 91 to 94. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 91, [act June 16, 1938, ch. 458, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-16/ch458/s1), [52 Stat. 707](/us/stat/52/707), authorized Secretary of War to place educational orders for munitions of special or technical design.

Section 92, [act June 16, 1938, ch. 458, § 2](/us/act/1938-06-16/ch458/s2), [52 Stat. 708](/us/stat/52/708), related to production equipment.

Section 93, [act June 16, 1938, ch. 458, § 3](/us/act/1938-06-16/ch458/s3), [52 Stat. 708](/us/stat/52/708), placed certain limitations on number of orders.

Section 94, acts [June 16, 1938, ch. 458, § 4](/us/act/1938-06-16/ch458/s4), [52 Stat. 708](/us/stat/52/708); [Apr. 3, 1939, ch. 35, § 13](/us/act/1939-04-03/ch35/s13), [53 Stat. 560](/us/stat/53/560), related to availability of appropriations for purposes of sections 91 to 94 of this title.
