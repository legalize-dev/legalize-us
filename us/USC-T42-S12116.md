# § 12116. Regulations

##### § 12116. Regulations

Not later than 1 year after July 26, 1990, the Commission shall issue regulations in an accessible format to carry out this subchapter in accordance with subchapter II of chapter 5 of title 5.

*([Pub. L. 101–336, title I, § 106](/us/pl/101/336/tI/s106), July 26, 1990, [104 Stat. 336](/us/stat/104/336).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective 24 months after July 26, 1990, see [section 108 of Pub. L. 101–336](/us/pl/101/336/s108), set out as a note under [section 12111 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12111).
