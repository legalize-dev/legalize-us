# §§ 1121 to 1123. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–255, title I, § 104](/us/pl/92/255/tI/s104), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 67](/us/stat/86/67)

##### §§ 1121 to 1123. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–255, title I, § 104](/us/pl/92/255/tI/s104), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 67](/us/stat/86/67)

Section 1121, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 211](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s211), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 69](/us/stat/86/69), provided for appointment of an acting Director until position was initially filled.

Section 1122, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 213](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s213), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 69](/us/stat/86/69), prohibited Director or any other Federal officer from waiving or disregarding any limitation or requirement prescribed by law with respect to any Federal program or activity.

Section 1123, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 214](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s214), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 70](/us/stat/86/70), provided for authorization of appropriations, and fiscal year availability of such appropriations.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Section 1104 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s1104), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 94–237, § 4(c)(1)](/us/pl/94/237/s4/c/1), Mar. 19, 1976, [90 Stat. 244](/us/stat/90/244), provided for repeal of sections effective June 30, 1975.
