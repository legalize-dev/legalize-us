# § 3796a. Transferred

##### § 3796a. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 3796a was editorially reclassified as [section 10282 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10282), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 3796a, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 702](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s702), as added [Pub. L. 94–430, § 2](/us/pl/94/430/s2), Sept. 29, 1976, [90 Stat. 1347](/us/stat/90/1347), contained provisions similar to those in [section 10282 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10282), Crime Control and Law Enforcement, prior to the general amendment of title I of [Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351) by [Pub. L. 96–157](/us/pl/96/157).
