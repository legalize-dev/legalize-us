# § 3211. Relationship to other authority

##### § 3211. Relationship to other authority

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to limit or affect any authority of the Secretary or the Commission under any other provision of law.

*([Pub. L. 95–617, title III, § 311](/us/pl/95/617/tIII/s311), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3154](/us/stat/92/3154).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Definitions

The definitions of Secretary and Commission in [section 2602 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s2602), Conservation, apply to this section.
