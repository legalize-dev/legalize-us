# § 96. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–201, § 18](/us/pl/90/201/s18), Dec. 15, 1967, [81 Stat. 600](/us/stat/81/600)

##### § 96. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–201, § 18](/us/pl/90/201/s18), Dec. 15, 1967, [81 Stat. 600](/us/stat/81/600)

Section, [act July 24, 1919, ch. 26](/us/act/1919-07-24/ch26), [41 Stat. 241](/us/stat/41/241), provided for marking horse meat transported in interstate commerce. See [section 619 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s619).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Dec. 15, 1967, see [section 20 of Pub. L. 90–201](/us/pl/90/201/s20), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 601 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s601).
