# § 715. Omitted

##### § 715. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–42, § 2](/us/pl/101/42/s2), June 28, 1989, [103 Stat. 91](/us/stat/103/91), which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 101–42, § 1](/us/pl/101/42/s1), June 28, 1989, [103 Stat. 91](/us/stat/103/91), provided that [Pub. L. 101–42](/us/pl/101/42), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Coquille Restoration Act”.
