# § 1300g–5. Omitted

##### § 1300g–5. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–89, title I, § 106](/us/pl/100/89/tI/s106), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 668](/us/stat/101/668), which repealed the Tiwa Indians Act ([Pub. L. 90–287](/us/pl/90/287), Apr. 12, 1968, [82 Stat. 93](/us/stat/82/93), providing the tribe’s designation and transferring responsibility for the tribe to the State of Texas), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
