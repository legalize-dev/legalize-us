# § 28505. Rules and regulations

##### § 28505. Rules and regulations

Within 1 year after the date of enactment of this section, the Board shall issue such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out this chapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 110–432, div. B, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/110/432/dB/tIV/s401/a), Oct. 16, 2008, [122 Stat. 4956](/us/stat/122/4956).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The date of enactment of this section, referred to in text, is the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 110–432](/us/pl/110/432), which was approved Oct. 16, 2008.
