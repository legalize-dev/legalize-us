# § 777j. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–348, § 1(14)](/us/pl/89/348/s1/14), Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1311](/us/stat/79/1311)

##### § 777j. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–348, § 1(14)](/us/pl/89/348/s1/14), Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1311](/us/stat/79/1311)

Section, [act Aug. 9, 1950, ch. 658, § 11](/us/act/1950-08-09/ch658/s11), [64 Stat. 434](/us/stat/64/434), required the Secretary of the Interior to make an annual report to the Congress giving detailed information as to the projects established under this chapter and expenditures therefor.
