# § 2662. Definitions

##### § 2662. Definitions

(1) The term “local educational agency” means—

(A) any local educational agency as defined in [section 7801 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s7801);

(B) the owner of any nonprofit elementary or secondary school building; and

(C) the governing authority of any school operated pursuant to [section 241 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s241), as in effect before enactment of the Improving America’s Schools Act of 1994, or successor authority, relating to impact aid for children who reside on Federal property.

(2) The term “nonprofit elementary or secondary school” has the meaning given such term by section 2642(8) 11 So in original. Probably should be section “2642(9)”. of this title.

(3) The term “radon” means the radioactive gaseous element and its short-lived decay products produced by the disintegration of the element radium occurring in air, water, soil, or other media.

(4) The term “school building” has the meaning given such term by [section 2642(13) of this title](/us/usc/t15/s2642/13).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 241 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s241), as in effect before enactment of the Improving America’s Schools Act of 1994, referred to in par. (1)(C), means [section 241 of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s241), Education, prior to its repeal by [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 331(b)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s331/b), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3965](/us/stat/108/3965).

#### Amendments

2015—Par. (1)(A). [Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95) made technical amendment to reference in original act which appears in text as reference to [section 7801 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s7801).

2002—Par. (1)(A). [Pub. L. 107–110](/us/pl/107/110) substituted “7801” for “8801”.

1994—Par. (1)(A). [Pub. L. 103–382, § 391(c)(4)(A)](/us/pl/103/382/s391/c/4/A), made technical amendment to reference to [section 8801 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s8801) to reflect change in reference to corresponding section of original act.

Par. (1)(C). [Pub. L. 103–382](/us/pl/103/382) directed two separate amendments of par. (1)(C), the first, by [section 391(c)(4)(B) of Pub. L. 103–382](/us/pl/103/382/s391/c/4/B), directed the insertion of “or successor authority” immediately after “[section 241 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s241)”, the second, by [section 392(b)(2) of Pub. L. 103–382](/us/pl/103/382/s392/b/2), directed the insertion (without reference to the first amendment) of “as in effect before enactment of the Improving America’s Schools Act of 1994” immediately after “[section 241 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s241),”. Literal execution of the second amendment was not possible, as “[section 241 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s241),” was amended to read “[section 241 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s241) or successor authority,” by the first amendment. Commas were editorially inserted before and after the phrase added by the second amendment and it was inserted immediately after “[section 241 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s241)” to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
