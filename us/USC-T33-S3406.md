# § 3406. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 3406. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration to carry out this subchapter $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2028 11 So in original. Probably should be followed by a period.

*([Pub. L. 111–11, title XII, § 12006](/us/pl/111/11/tXII/s12006), Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1419](/us/stat/123/1419); [Pub. L. 117–263, div. J, title CIII, § 10305(g)](/us/pl/117/263/dJ/tCIII/s10305/g), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 3974](/us/stat/136/3974).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–263](/us/pl/117/263) substituted “this subchapter $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2023 through 2028” for “this subchapter—” and struck out pars. (1) to (7) which authorized appropriations for fiscal years 2009 through 2015.
