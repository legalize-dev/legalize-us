# § 3746. Promotion; acceptance; oath of office

##### § 3746. Promotion; acceptance; oath of office

(a) A Reserve officer who has been appointed under this subchapter is considered to have accepted the appointment unless delivery thereof cannot be effected.

(b) A Reserve officer who has served continuously since taking the oath of office prescribed in [section 3331 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s3331), is not required to take a new oath of office upon appointment in a higher grade.

*(Added [Pub. L. 96–322, § 1](/us/pl/96/322/s1), Aug. 4, 1980, [94 Stat. 1012](/us/stat/94/1012), § 735; renumbered § 3746, [Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 118(b)](/us/pl/115/282/tI/s118/b), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4233](/us/stat/132/4233).)*
