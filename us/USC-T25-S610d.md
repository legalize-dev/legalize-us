# § 610d. Omitted

##### § 610d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–534, § 5](/us/pl/90/534/s5), Sept. 28, 1968, [82 Stat. 884](/us/stat/82/884), which provided that any moneys received by the Swinomish Indian Tribal Community from the sale, exchange, mortgage, or granting of a security interest in tribal land could be used for tribal purposes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
