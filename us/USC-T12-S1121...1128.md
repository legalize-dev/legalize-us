# §§ 1121 to 1128. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 1121 to 1128. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 1121, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 211(a)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s211/a), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1459](/us/stat/42/1459), related to offenses by officers, employees, or agents. See sections 657 and 1006 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 1122, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 211(b)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s211/b), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1459](/us/stat/42/1459), related to false statements to banks. See [section 1014 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1014).

Section 1123, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 211(c)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s211/c), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1459](/us/stat/42/1459), related to overvaluation of property for loan purposes. See [section 1014 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1014).

Section 1124, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 211(d)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s211/d), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1459](/us/stat/42/1459), related to offenses by examiners. See sections 1907 and 1909 of Title 18.

Section 1125, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 211(e)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s211/e), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1459](/us/stat/42/1459), related to offenses by officers, employees, and agents. See [section 215 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s215).

Section 1126, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 211(f)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s211/f), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1459](/us/stat/42/1459), related to forgery and counterfeiting offenses. See [section 493 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s493).

Section 1127, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 211(g)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s211/g), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1459](/us/stat/42/1459), related to false representations as to debentures, etc., of banks. See [section 1013 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1013).

Section 1128, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 211(h)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s211/h), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1459](/us/stat/42/1459), related to use of words “Federal intermediate credit bank”. See [section 709 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s709).
