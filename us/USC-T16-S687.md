# § 687. Grand Canyon Game Preserve included in park

##### § 687. Grand Canyon Game Preserve included in park

Such parts of the Grand Canyon National Game Preserve, designated under authority of [section 684 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s684), as are by this Act included with 11 So in original. Probably should be “within”. the Grand Canyon National Park are excluded and eliminated from said game preserve.

*([Feb. 26, 1919, ch. 44, § 9](/us/act/1919-02-26/ch44/s9), [40 Stat. 1178](/us/stat/40/1178).)*
