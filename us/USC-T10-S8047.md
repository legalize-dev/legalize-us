# § 8047. Legislative Assistant to the Commandant

##### § 8047. Legislative Assistant to the Commandant

There is in the Marine Corps a Legislative Assistant to the Commandant.

*(Added [Pub. L. 107–314, div. A, title V, § 504(d)(3)(A)](/us/pl/107/314/dA/tV/s504/d/3/A), Dec. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 2532](/us/stat/116/2532), § 5047; amended [Pub. L. 114–328, div. A, title V, § 502(y)](/us/pl/114/328/dA/tV/s502/y), Dec. 23, 2016, [130 Stat. 2104](/us/stat/130/2104); renumbered § 8047, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(a)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/a/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/132/1834).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments
