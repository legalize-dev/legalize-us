# §§ 1981 to 1983. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title XV, § 1529](/us/pl/95/561/tXV/s1529), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2380](/us/stat/92/2380)

##### §§ 1981 to 1983. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title XV, § 1529](/us/pl/95/561/tXV/s1529), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2380](/us/stat/92/2380)

Section 1981, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 731](/us/pl/93/380/tVII/s731), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 596](/us/stat/88/596); [Pub. L. 94–194, § 7](/us/pl/94/194/s7), Dec. 31, 1975, [89 Stat. 1105](/us/stat/89/1105); S. Res. 4, Feb. 4, 1977, set forth evaluation requirements for programs.

Section 1982, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 732](/us/pl/93/380/tVII/s732), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 596](/us/stat/88/596); [Pub. L. 94–194](/us/pl/94/194), §§ 1(c), 6(c), 9(b), Dec. 31, 1975, [89 Stat. 1104–1106](/us/stat/89/1104-1106), set forth provisions authorizing appropriations for covered programs.

Section 1983, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 733](/us/pl/93/380/tVII/s733), as added [Pub. L. 94–194, § 8](/us/pl/94/194/s8), Dec. 31, 1975, [89 Stat. 1105](/us/stat/89/1105), set forth provisions relating to acceptance of gifts or donations by the Commissioner.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 1530(a) of Pub. L. 95–561](/us/pl/95/561/s1530/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 1221e–3 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1221e–3).
