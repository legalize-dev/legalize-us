# § 1225. Omitted

##### § 1225. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–364, § 5](/us/pl/91/364/s5), July 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 689](/us/stat/84/689), which exempted funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
