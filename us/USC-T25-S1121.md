# § 1121. Omitted

##### § 1121. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–659, § 11](/us/pl/89/659/s11), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 910](/us/stat/80/910), which authorized the prescription of rules and regulations, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
