# § 4201. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

##### § 4201. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–577, title I](/us/pl/90/577/tI), §§ 101–110, Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1098–1101](/us/stat/82/1098-1101), defined terms used in this chapter. See sections 6501 and 6505(a) of Title 31, Money and Finance.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 90–577, § 1](/us/pl/90/577/s1), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1098](/us/stat/82/1098), which provided that [Pub. L. 90–577](/us/pl/90/577) could be cited as the “Intergovernmental Cooperation Act of 1968”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068), 1080.
