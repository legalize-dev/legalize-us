#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 111–11, title VII, § 7301](/us/pl/111/11/tVII/s7301), Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/123/1213); [Pub. L. 113–76, div. G, title IV, § 429](/us/pl/113/76/dG/tIV/s429), Jan. 17, 2014, [128 Stat. 345](/us/stat/128/345); [Pub. L. 113–235, div. F, title IV, § 421](/us/pl/113/235/dF/tIV/s421), Dec. 16, 2014, [128 Stat. 2449](/us/stat/128/2449), which related to the American Battlefield Protection Program, was transferred in part and repealed in part. Subsec. (a) was transferred and is set out as a note under [section 308101 of Title 54](/us/usc/t54/s308101), National Park Service and Related Programs. Subsecs. (b) and (c) were repealed and restated in sections 308101 to 308103 of Title 54 by [Pub. L. 113–287](/us/pl/113/287), §§ 3, 7, Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3094](/us/stat/128/3094), 3272, which act enacted Title 54.
