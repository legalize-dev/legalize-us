# § 3301. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78)

##### § 3301. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78)

Section, [Pub. L. 103–182, § 2](/us/pl/103/182/s2), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2060](/us/stat/107/2060), defined terms for the North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal of North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act

[Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78), provided that: “The North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act ([Public Law 103–182](/us/pl/103/182); [19 U.S.C. 3301](/us/usc/t19/s3301) et seq.) is repealed, effective on the date on which the USMCA enters into force [July 1, 2020].”

[For definition of “USMCA” as used in [section 601 of Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113/s601), set out above, see [section 4502 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4502).]

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 103–182, § 1(a)](/us/pl/103/182/s1/a), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2057](/us/stat/107/2057), which provided that [Pub. L. 103–182](/us/pl/103/182) could be cited as the “North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78), effective on the date the USMCA entered into force (July 1, 2020).
