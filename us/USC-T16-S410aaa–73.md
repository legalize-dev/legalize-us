# § 410aaa–73. Land disposal

##### § 410aaa–73. Land disposal

Except as provided in [section 410aaa–26 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s410aaa–26), none of the lands within the boundaries of the wilderness or park areas designated under this Act shall be granted to or otherwise made available for use by the Metropolitan Water District or any other agencies or persons pursuant to the Boulder Canyon Project Act ([43 U.S.C. 617–619b](/us/usc/t43/s617–619b)) or any similar Acts.

*([Pub. L. 103–433, title VII, § 703](/us/pl/103/433/tVII/s703), Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4497](/us/stat/108/4497).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, is defined in [section 3 of Pub. L. 103–433](/us/pl/103/433/s3), which is set out as a Definitions note under [section 410aaa of this title](/us/usc/t16/s410aaa).

The Boulder Canyon Project Act, referred to in text, is [act Dec. 21, 1928, ch. 42](/us/act/1928-12-21/ch42), [45 Stat. 1057](/us/stat/45/1057), which is classified generally to subchapter I (§ 617 et seq.) of chapter 12A of Title 43, Public Lands. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 617t of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s617t) and Tables.
