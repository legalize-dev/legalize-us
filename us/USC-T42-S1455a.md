# § 1455a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–383, title II, § 204](/us/pl/93/383/tII/s204), Aug. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 668](/us/stat/88/668)

##### § 1455a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–383, title II, § 204](/us/pl/93/383/tII/s204), Aug. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 668](/us/stat/88/668)

Section, [act Aug. 2, 1954, ch. 649, title VIII, § 815](/us/act/1954-08-02/ch649/tVIII/s815), [68 Stat. 647](/us/stat/68/647), required submission of specifications by applicants prior to award of any contract for construction of a project and submission of data with respect to acquisition of land prior to authorization to purchase such land.
