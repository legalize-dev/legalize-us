# § 644. Omitted

##### § 644. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–264, § 4](/us/pl/91/264/s4), May 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 261](/us/stat/84/261), which affirmed bonds issued pursuant to this subchapter as valid and binding obligations, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
