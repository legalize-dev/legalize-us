# [§§ 831 to 834. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–164, title I, § 122(a)](/us/pl/97/164/tI/s122/a), Apr. 2, 1982, [96 Stat. 36](/us/stat/96/36)]

##### [§§ 831 to 834. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–164, title I, § 122(a)](/us/pl/97/164/tI/s122/a), Apr. 2, 1982, [96 Stat. 36](/us/stat/96/36)]

Section 831, [act June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 924](/us/stat/62/924), authorized Court of Customs and Patent Appeals to appoint a clerk, assistant clerks, stenographic law clerks, clerical assistants, and other necessary employees, and set out duties of clerk.

Section 832, acts [June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 924](/us/stat/62/924); [May 24, 1949, ch. 139, § 76](/us/act/1949-05-24/ch139/s76), [63 Stat. 101](/us/stat/63/101), authorized Court of Customs and Patent Appeals to appoint a marshal and set out duties of that marshal.

Section 833, [act June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 925](/us/stat/62/925), authorized Court of Customs and Patent Appeals to appoint a reporter and set out duties of that reporter.

Section 834, [act June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 925](/us/stat/62/925), authorized Court of Customs and Patent Appeals to appoint necessary bailiffs and messengers and set out duties of those bailiffs and messengers.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1982, see [section 402 of Pub. L. 97–164](/us/pl/97/164/s402), set out as an Effective Date of 1982 Amendment note under [section 171 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s171).
