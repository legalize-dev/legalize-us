# § 1108. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1108. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter, but sums appropriated for any one fiscal year shall not exceed $1,200,000.

*([Pub. L. 89–454, title I, § 9](/us/pl/89/454/tI/s9), June 17, 1966, [80 Stat. 208](/us/stat/80/208); [Pub. L. 89–688, § 2(b)](/us/pl/89/688/s2/b), Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 1001](/us/stat/80/1001); [Pub. L. 91–15, § 2](/us/pl/91/15/s2), May 23, 1969, [83 Stat. 10](/us/stat/83/10).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1969—[Pub. L. 91–15](/us/pl/91/15) substituted “$1,200,000” for “$1,500,000”.

1966—[Pub. L. 89–688](/us/pl/89/688) substituted “this title” for “this Act”, which, for purposes of codification, has been changed to “this subchapter”.
