# § 8045. Deputy Commandants

##### § 8045. Deputy Commandants

There are in the Headquarters, Marine Corps, not more than eight Deputy Commandants, detailed by the Secretary of the Navy from officers on the active-duty list of the Marine Corps.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2024—[Pub. L. 118–159](/us/pl/118/159) substituted “not more than eight Deputy Commandants” for “not more than seven Deputy Commandants”.

2018—[Pub. L. 115–232](/us/pl/115/232) renumbered [section 5045 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s5045) as this section.

2016—[Pub. L. 114–328](/us/pl/114/328) substituted “seven” for “six”.

2002—[Pub. L. 107–314](/us/pl/107/314) substituted “six” for “five”.

2000—[Pub. L. 106–398](/us/pl/106/398) amended section catchline and text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “There are in the Headquarters, Marine Corps, a Chief of Staff, not more than five Deputy Chiefs of Staff, and not more than three Assistant Chiefs of Staff, detailed by the Secretary of the Navy from officers on the active-duty list of the Marine Corps.”
