# § 10595a. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 10595a. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $10,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023.

(b) Use of amounts Of the amount made available to carry out this subchapter in any fiscal year, not less than 5 percent shall be used for grants to Indian Tribes.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2926](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2926), as added [Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 113](/us/pl/110/199/tI/s113), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 676](/us/stat/122/676); amended [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 502(b)(3)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s502/b/3), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5228](/us/stat/132/5228).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3797s–5 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3797s–5), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2018—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 115–391](/us/pl/115/391) added subsec. (a) and struck out former subsec. (a). Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $10,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2009 and 2010.”
