# § 8156. Coordination with health services development activities carried out under the National Health Planning and Resources Development Act of 1974

##### § 8156. Coordination with health services development activities carried out under the National Health Planning and Resources Development Act of 1974

The Secretary and the Secretary of Health and Human Services shall, to the maximum extent practicable, coordinate programs carried out under this subchapter and programs carried out under part F of title XVI of the Public Health Service Act ([42 U.S.C. 300t](/us/usc/t42/s300t) et seq.).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The National Health Planning and Resources Development Act of 1974, referred to in section catchline, is [Pub. L. 93–641](/us/pl/93/641), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2225](/us/stat/88/2225), which is classified generally to subchapters XIII (§ 300k et seq.) and XIV (§ 300*o* et seq.) of chapter 6A of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title of 1975 Amendment note set out under [section 201 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s201) and Tables.

The Public Health Service Act, referred to in text, is [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), [58 Stat. 682](/us/stat/58/682). Part F of title XVI of the Public Health Service Act was added by act Jan. 4, 1975, [Pub. L. 93–641, § 4](/us/pl/93/641/s4), [88 Stat. 2273](/us/stat/88/2273), and was redesignated as Part D by [Pub. L. 96–79, title II, § 202(a)](/us/pl/96/79/tII/s202/a), Oct. 4, 1979, [93 Stat. 632](/us/stat/93/632). Part D of that Act is classified generally to Part D (§ 300t) of subchapter XIV of chapter 6A of Title 42. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 201 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s201) and Tables.

#### Amendments

1991—[Pub. L. 102–40](/us/pl/102/40) renumbered [section 5056 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s5056) as this section.

[Pub. L. 102–83](/us/pl/102/83) substituted “The Secretary” for “The Administrator”.

1982—[Pub. L. 97–295, § 4(95)(A)](/us/pl/97/295/s4/95/A), substituted “Health and Human Services” for “Health, Education, and Welfare”.

[Pub. L. 97–295, § 4(93)](/us/pl/97/295/s4/93), inserted “([42 U.S.C. 300t](/us/usc/t42/s300t) et seq.)” after “part F of title XVI of the Public Health Service Act”.

1976—[Pub. L. 94–581](/us/pl/94/581) substituted “health services development activities carried out under the National Health Planning and Resources Development Act of 1974” for “programs carried out under the Heart Disease, Cancer, and Stroke Amendments of 1965” in section catchline and “part F of title XVI” for “title IX” in text.
