# § 410cccc–2. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 410cccc–2. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums, but not more than $5,422,000, as may be necessary for the acquisition of land and interests in land and for the development of the Golden Spike National Historic Site 11 See Change of Name note below. pursuant to sections 410cccc to 410cccc–2 of this title.

*([Pub. L. 89–102, § 3](/us/pl/89/102/s3), July 30, 1965, [79 Stat. 426](/us/stat/79/426); [Pub. L. 94–578, title II, § 201(5)](/us/pl/94/578/tII/s201/5), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2733](/us/stat/90/2733).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1976—[Pub. L. 94–578](/us/pl/94/578) substituted “$5,422,000” for “$1,168,000”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Change of Name

Golden Spike National Historic Site was redesignated Golden Spike National Historical Park by [Pub. L. 116–9, title II, § 2205(b)](/us/pl/116/9/tII/s2205/b), Mar. 12, 2019, [133 Stat. 738](/us/stat/133/738), which is classified to [section 410cccc–3(b) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s410cccc–3/b).
