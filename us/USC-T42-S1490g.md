# § 1490g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–181, title I](/us/pl/98/181/tI) [title V, § 503(c)], Nov. 30, 1983, [97 Stat. 1241](/us/stat/97/1241)

##### § 1490g. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–181, title I](/us/pl/98/181/tI) [title V, § 503(c)], Nov. 30, 1983, [97 Stat. 1241](/us/stat/97/1241)

Section, [act July 15, 1949, ch. 338, title V, § 527](/us/act/1949-07-15/ch338/tV/s527), as added Aug. 22, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–383, title V, § 518](/us/pl/93/383/tV/s518), [88 Stat. 699](/us/stat/88/699), defined “housing” as including mobile homes and mobile home sites, and authorized the Secretary to prescribe property standards for mobile homes financed under this subchapter.
