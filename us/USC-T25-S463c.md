# § 463c. Omitted

##### § 463c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 28, 1937, ch. 527, § 3](/us/act/1937-07-28/ch527/s3), [50 Stat. 536](/us/stat/50/536), which related to gift of lands by the State of Arizona to the Papago Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
