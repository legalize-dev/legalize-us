# § 590. Omitted

##### § 590. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–206, § 10](/us/pl/92/206/s10), Dec. 18, 1971, [85 Stat. 739](/us/stat/85/739), which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to prescribe rules and regulations, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
