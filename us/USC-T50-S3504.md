# § 3504. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–507, § 21(b)(2)](/us/pl/85/507/s21/b/2), July 7, 1958, [72 Stat. 337](/us/stat/72/337)

##### § 3504. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–507, § 21(b)(2)](/us/pl/85/507/s21/b/2), July 7, 1958, [72 Stat. 337](/us/stat/72/337)

Section, [act June 20, 1949, ch. 227, § 4](/us/act/1949-06-20/ch227/s4), [63 Stat. 208](/us/stat/63/208), related to education and training of officers and employees. See section 4101 et seq. of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 403d of this title](/us/usc/t50/s403d) and repealed prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
