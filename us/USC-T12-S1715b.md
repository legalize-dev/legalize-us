# § 1715b. Rules and regulations

##### § 1715b. Rules and regulations

The Secretary is authorized and directed to make such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

*([June 27, 1934, ch. 847](/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847), title II, § 211, as added [Feb. 3, 1938, ch. 13, § 3](/us/act/1938-02-03/ch13/s3), [52 Stat. 23](/us/stat/52/23); amended [Apr. 20, 1950, ch. 94](/us/act/1950-04-20/ch94), title I, § 122, [64 Stat. 59](/us/stat/64/59); [Pub. L. 90–19, § 1(a)(3)](/us/pl/90/19/s1/a/3), May 25, 1967, [81 Stat. 17](/us/stat/81/17).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1967—[Pub. L. 90–19](/us/pl/90/19) substituted “Secretary” for “Commissioner”.

1950—Act Apr. 20, 1950, substituted “Commissioner” for “Administrator”.
