# § 60102. Definitions

##### § 60102. Definitions

(1) Crime of violence The term “crime of violence” has the same meaning as in [section 924(c)(3) of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s924/c/3).

(2) Private prisoner transport company The term “private prisoner transport company” means any entity, other than the United States, a State, or an inferior political subdivision of a State, which engages in the business of the transporting for compensation, individuals committed to the custody of any State or of an inferior political subdivision of a State, or any attempt thereof.

(3) Violent prisoner The term “violent prisoner” means any individual in the custody of a State or an inferior political subdivision of a State who has previously been convicted of or is currently charged with a crime of violence or any similar statute of a State or the inferior political subdivisions of a State, or any attempt thereof.

*([Pub. L. 106–560, § 3](/us/pl/106/560/s3), Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2784](/us/stat/114/2784).)*
