# § 12101. Definition

##### § 12101. Definition

In this chapter, the term “enlistment” means original enlistment or reenlistment.

*(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1662(b)(1)](/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI/s1662/b/1), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2988](/us/stat/108/2988).)*
