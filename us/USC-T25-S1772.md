# § 1772. Omitted

##### § 1772. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–228, § 2](/us/pl/100/228/s2), Dec. 31, 1987, [101 Stat. 1556](/us/stat/101/1556), which set out congressional findings and policy, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 100–228, § 1](/us/pl/100/228/s1), Dec. 31, 1987, [101 Stat. 1556](/us/stat/101/1556), provided that [Pub. L. 100–228](/us/pl/100/228), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Seminole Indian Land Claims Settlement Act of 1987”.
