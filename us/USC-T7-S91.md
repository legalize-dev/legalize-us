# § 91. Short title

##### § 91. Short title

For convenience of reference, this chapter may be designated and cited as “The Naval Stores Act.”

*([Mar. 3, 1923, ch. 217, § 1](/us/act/1923-03-03/ch217/s1), [42 Stat. 1435](/us/stat/42/1435).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

[Act Mar. 3, 1923, ch. 217, § 10](/us/act/1923-03-03/ch217/s10), [42 Stat. 1437](/us/stat/42/1437), provided: “That this Act [enacting this chapter] shall become effective at the expiration of ninety days next after the date of its approval [Mar. 23, 1923].”
