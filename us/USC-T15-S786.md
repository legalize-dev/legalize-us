# § 786. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–91, title VII, § 709(a)(1)](/us/pl/95/91/tVII/s709/a/1), Aug. 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 607](/us/stat/91/607)

##### § 786. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–91, title VII, § 709(a)(1)](/us/pl/95/91/tVII/s709/a/1), Aug. 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 607](/us/stat/91/607)

Section, [Pub. L. 93–275, § 28](/us/pl/93/275/s28), May 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 115](/us/stat/88/115), provided that upon termination of this chapter, any functions or personnel transferred by this chapter shall revert to the department, agency, or office from which they were transferred.
