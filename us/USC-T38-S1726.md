# § 1726. Reimbursement for loss of personal effects by natural disaster

##### § 1726. Reimbursement for loss of personal effects by natural disaster

The Secretary shall, under regulations which the Secretary shall prescribe, reimburse veterans in Department hospitals and domiciliaries for any loss of personal effects sustained by fire, earthquake, or other natural disaster while such effects were stored in designated locations in Department hospitals or domiciliaries.

*([Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1144](/us/stat/72/1144), § 627; [Pub. L. 93–82, title I, § 105](/us/pl/93/82/tI/s105), Aug. 2, 1973, [87 Stat. 183](/us/stat/87/183); [Pub. L. 94–581, title II, § 210(a)(12)](/us/pl/94/581/tII/s210/a/12), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2863](/us/stat/90/2863); renumbered § 1726 and amended [Pub. L. 102–83](/us/pl/102/83), §§ 4(a)(3), (4), (b)(1), (2)(E), 5(a), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 404–406](/us/stat/105/404-406).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

Prior section 1726, [Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1197](/us/stat/72/1197), provided for control by agencies of United States, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–358, § 3(a)(3)](/us/pl/89/358/s3/a/3), Mar. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 20](/us/stat/80/20). See [section 3689 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s3689).

#### Amendments

1991—[Pub. L. 102–83, § 5(a)](/us/pl/102/83/s5/a), renumbered [section 626 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s626) as this section.

[Pub. L. 102–83, § 4(b)(1)](/us/pl/102/83/s4/b/1), (2)(E), substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator” in two places.

[Pub. L. 102–83, § 4(a)(3)](/us/pl/102/83/s4/a/3), (4), substituted “Department” for “Veterans’ Administration” in two places.

1976—[Pub. L. 94–581](/us/pl/94/581) substituted “the Administrator shall prescribe” for “he shall prescribe”.

1973—[Pub. L. 93–82](/us/pl/93/82) substituted “natural disaster” for “fire” in section catchline and extended reimbursement provisions to earthquake and other natural disasters also.
