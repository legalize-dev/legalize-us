# § 2052. Research by Commission

##### § 2052. Research by Commission

The Commission is authorized and directed to conduct, through its own facilities, activities and studies of the types specified in [section 2051 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2051).

*([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724), title I, § 32, as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 928](/us/stat/68/928); renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 902(a)(8)](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s902/a/8), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2944](/us/stat/106/2944).)*
