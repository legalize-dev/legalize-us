# § 1738h. Disbursement of environmental funds

##### § 1738h. Disbursement of environmental funds

Funds in an Environmental Fund shall be disbursed only pursuant to a framework agreement entered into pursuant to [section 1738f of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1738f).

*([July 10, 1954, ch. 469](/us/act/1954-07-10/ch469), title VI, § 609, as added [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1512](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1512), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3661](/us/stat/104/3661).)*
