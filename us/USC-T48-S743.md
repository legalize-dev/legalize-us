# § 743. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

##### § 743. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

Section, acts [Apr. 12, 1900, ch. 191, § 10](/us/act/1900-04-12/ch191/s10), [31 Stat. 80](/us/stat/31/80); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309); [May 17, 1932, ch. 190](/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190), [47 Stat. 158](/us/stat/47/158), provided for quarantine stations in Puerto Rico. See [section 267 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s267), The Public Health and Welfare.
