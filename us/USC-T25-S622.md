# § 622. Omitted

##### § 622. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1949, ch. 425, § 2](/us/act/1949-08-13/ch425/s2), [63 Stat. 605](/us/stat/63/605), which related to exchange of Pueblo and Navajo tribal lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
