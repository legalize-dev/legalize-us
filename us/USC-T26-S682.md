# [§ 682. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–97, title I, § 11051(b)(1)(C)](/us/pl/115/97/tI/s11051/b/1/C), Dec. 22, 2017, [131 Stat. 2089](/us/stat/131/2089)]

##### [§ 682. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–97, title I, § 11051(b)(1)(C)](/us/pl/115/97/tI/s11051/b/1/C), Dec. 22, 2017, [131 Stat. 2089](/us/stat/131/2089)]

Section, [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 234](/us/stat/68A/234); [Pub. L. 98–369, div. A, title IV, § 422(d)(2)](/us/pl/98/369/dA/tIV/s422/d/2), July 18, 1984, [98 Stat. 798](/us/stat/98/798), related to income of an estate or trust in case of divorce.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to any divorce or separation instrument (as defined in former [section 71(b)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t26/s71/b/2) as in effect before Dec. 22, 2017) executed after Dec. 31, 2018, and to such instruments executed on or before Dec. 31, 2018, and modified after Dec. 31, 2018, if the modification expressly provides that the amendment made by [section 11051 of Pub. L. 115–97](/us/pl/115/97/s11051) applies to such modification, see [section 11051(c) of Pub. L. 115–97](/us/pl/115/97/s11051/c), set out as an Effective Date of 2017 Amendment note under [section 61 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s61).
