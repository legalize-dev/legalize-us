# § 9681. Definitions

##### § 9681. Definitions

(1) Agency The term “agency” includes any entity, organizational unit, program, or function.

(2) Transition period The term “transition period” means the period—

(A) beginning on October 5, 2018; and

(B) ending on the effective date of the reorganization plan required by [section 9682(e) of this title](/us/usc/t22/s9682/e).

*([Pub. L. 115–254, div. F, title VI, § 1461](/us/pl/115/254/dF/tVI/s1461), Oct. 5, 2018, [132 Stat. 3510](/us/stat/132/3510).)*
