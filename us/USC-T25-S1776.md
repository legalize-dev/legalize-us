# § 1776. Omitted

##### § 1776. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–444, § 2](/us/pl/103/444/s2), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4632](/us/stat/108/4632), which set out congressional findings and purposes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 103–444, § 1](/us/pl/103/444/s1), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4632](/us/stat/108/4632), provided that [Pub. L. 103–444](/us/pl/103/444), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Crow Boundary Settlement Act of 1994”.
