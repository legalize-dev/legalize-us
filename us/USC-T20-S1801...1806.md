# §§ 1801 to 1806. Omitted

##### §§ 1801 to 1806. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general revision of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, titles I to IX of [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 27](/us/stat/79/27), by [Pub. L. 95–561](/us/pl/95/561), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2143](/us/stat/92/2143).

Section 1801, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s401), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/93/380/tIV/s401), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 535](/us/stat/88/535); amended [Pub. L. 94–482, title III, § 328](/us/pl/94/482/tIII/s328), Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2220](/us/stat/90/2220); [Pub. L. 95–112, § 2(d)](/us/pl/95/112/s2/d), Sept. 24, 1977, [91 Stat. 911](/us/stat/91/911), authorized appropriations for making grants for libraries and library resources for fiscal years ending prior to Oct. 1, 1979.

Section 1802, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 402](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s402), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/93/380/tIV/s401), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 537](/us/stat/88/537), provided for allotments to States.

Section 1803, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s403), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/93/380/tIV/s401), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 538](/us/stat/88/538); amended [Pub. L. 94–482, title III, § 323(a)(3)](/us/pl/94/482/tIII/s323/a/3), title V, § 501(e)(1), Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2217](/us/stat/90/2217), 2237, provided for structure and function of State plans.

Section 1804, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 404](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s404), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/93/380/tIV/s401), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 540](/us/stat/88/540), directed Commissioner to afford the State educational agency reasonable notice and opportunity for a hearing prior to final disapproval of a State plan.

Section 1805, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s405), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/93/380/tIV/s401), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 541](/us/stat/88/541), related to payments to States.

Section 1806, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 406](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s406), as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/93/380/tIV/s401), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 541](/us/stat/88/541); amended [Pub. L. 94–482, title III, § 324](/us/pl/94/482/tIII/s324), title V, § 501(r), Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2220](/us/stat/90/2220), 2238, related to participation of children enrolled in private schools.
