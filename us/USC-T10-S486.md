# [§ 486. Repealed. [Pub. L. 118–159, div. A, title V, § 566(b)(1)](/us/pl/118/159/dA/tV/s566/b/1), Dec. 23, 2024, [138 Stat. 1905](/us/stat/138/1905)]

##### [§ 486. Repealed. [Pub. L. 118–159, div. A, title V, § 566(b)(1)](/us/pl/118/159/dA/tV/s566/b/1), Dec. 23, 2024, [138 Stat. 1905](/us/stat/138/1905)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title V, § 549G(a)(1)](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tV/s549G/a/1), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 1727](/us/stat/135/1727); amended [Pub. L. 118–159, div. A, title XVII, § 1701(a)(9)](/us/pl/118/159/dA/tXVII/s1701/a/9), Dec. 23, 2024, [138 Stat. 2203](/us/stat/138/2203), required the Secretary of each military department to submit annual reports on racial, ethnic, and sex demographics in the military justice system and required the Secretary of Defense to forward any reports received to Congress.

A prior section 486, added [Pub. L. 106–65, div. A, title II, § 241(a)(1)](/us/pl/106/65/dA/tII/s241/a/1), Oct. 5, 1999, [113 Stat. 549](/us/stat/113/549), related to quadrennial report on emerging operational concepts, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 112–81, div. A, title X, § 1061(8)(A)](/us/pl/112/81/dA/tX/s1061/8/A), Dec. 31, 2011, [125 Stat. 1583](/us/stat/125/1583).
