# § 1300a. Omitted

##### § 1300a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–461, § 2](/us/pl/92/461/s2), Oct. 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 769](/us/stat/86/769), which related to set aside of percentage of funds for the benefit of the Payson Indian Band, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
