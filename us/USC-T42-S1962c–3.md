# § 1962c–3. Noncompliance; curtailing of payments

##### § 1962c–3. Noncompliance; curtailing of payments

(a) the program submitted by such State and approved under [section 1962c–2 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1962c–2) has been so changed that it no longer complies with a requirement of such section; or

(b) in the administration of the program there is a failure to comply substantially with such a requirement,
