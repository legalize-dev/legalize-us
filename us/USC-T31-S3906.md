# [§ 3906. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–362, title XIII, § 1301(c)(1)](/us/pl/105/362/tXIII/s1301/c/1), Nov. 10, 1998, [112 Stat. 3293](/us/stat/112/3293)]

##### [§ 3906. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–362, title XIII, § 1301(c)(1)](/us/pl/105/362/tXIII/s1301/c/1), Nov. 10, 1998, [112 Stat. 3293](/us/stat/112/3293)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 97–452, § 1(18)(A)](/us/pl/97/452/s1/18/A), Jan. 12, 1983, [96 Stat. 2476](/us/stat/96/2476), § 3905; renumbered § 3906 and amended [Pub. L. 100–496](/us/pl/100/496), §§ 9(a)(1), 10, Oct. 17, 1988, [102 Stat. 2460](/us/stat/102/2460), 2463, required submission of reports to Director of the Office of Management and Budget by 60th day after end of fiscal year by head of each agency concerning agency’s payment practices during that fiscal year.

A prior section 3906 was renumbered [section 3907 of this title](/us/usc/t31/s3907).
