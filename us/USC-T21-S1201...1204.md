# §§ 1201 to 1204. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1007(a)(3)](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1007/a/3), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4187](/us/stat/102/4187)

##### §§ 1201 to 1204. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1007(a)(3)](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1007/a/3), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4187](/us/stat/102/4187)

Section 1201, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 1302](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s1302), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2168](/us/stat/98/2168), set forth Congressional findings and declaration of purpose relating to illegal flow of narcotics into United States.

Section 1202, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 1303](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s1303), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2168](/us/stat/98/2168), established National Drug Enforcement Policy Board.

Section 1203, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 1304](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s1304), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2169](/us/stat/98/2169), delineated responsibilities and functions of National Drug Enforcement Policy Board.

Section 1204, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 1305](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s1305), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2170](/us/stat/98/2170), related to reports to Congress.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1007(a)(3)](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1007/a/3), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4187](/us/stat/102/4187), provided that the repeal of this chapter is effective on 30th day after first Director of National Drug Control Policy is confirmed by the Senate.

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 1301](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s1301), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2168](/us/stat/98/2168), which provided that chapter XIII (§§ 1301 to 1307) of title II of [Pub. L. 98–473](/us/pl/98/473) was to be cited as the National Narcotics Act of 1984, was repealed by [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1007(a)(3)](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1007/a/3), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4187](/us/stat/102/4187).

#### **Executive Documents**

#### Executive Order No. 12590

Ex. Ord. No. 12590, Mar. 26, 1987, 52 F.R. 10021, as amended by Ex. Ord. No. 13284, § 11, Jan. 23, 2003, 68 F.R. 4076, provided for the establishment of a National Drug Policy Board, designated its membership and functions, and authorized coordinating groups.
