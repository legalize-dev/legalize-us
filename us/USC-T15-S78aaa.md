# § 78aaa. Short title

##### § 78aaa. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “Securities Investor Protection Act of 1970”.

*([Pub. L. 91–598, § 1(a)](/us/pl/91/598/s1/a), Dec. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1636](/us/stat/84/1636).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “This Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 91–598](/us/pl/91/598), Dec. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1636](/us/stat/84/1636). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of 1978 Amendment

[Pub. L. 95–283, § 1](/us/pl/95/283/s1), May 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 249](/us/stat/92/249), provided that: “This Act [enacting sections 78fff–1 to 78fff–4 of this title, amending sections 77c, 78c, 78k, and 78ccc to 78*lll* of this title and enacting provisions set out as a note under [section 78k of this title](/us/usc/t15/s78k)] may be cited as the ‘Securities Investor Protection Act Amendments of 1978’.”
