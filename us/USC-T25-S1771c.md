# § 1771c. Omitted

##### § 1771c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–95, § 5](/us/pl/100/95/s5), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 705](/us/stat/101/705), which provided for conditions precedent to Federal purchase of settlement lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
