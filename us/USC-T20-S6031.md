# § 6031. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–279, title IV, § 403(2)](/us/pl/107/279/tIV/s403/2), Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1985](/us/stat/116/1985)

##### § 6031. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–279, title IV, § 403(2)](/us/pl/107/279/tIV/s403/2), Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1985](/us/stat/116/1985)

Section, [Pub. L. 103–227, title IX, § 931](/us/pl/103/227/tIX/s931), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 227](/us/stat/108/227), established the National Institute on Student Achievement, Curriculum, and Assessment, the National Institute on the Education of At-Risk Students, the National Institute on Educational Governance, Finance, Policy-Making, and Management, the National Institute on Early Childhood Development and Education, and the National Institute on Postsecondary Education, Libraries, and Lifelong Education.
