# § 6531. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(H)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/H), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2776](/us/stat/133/2776)

##### § 6531. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(H)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/H), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2776](/us/stat/133/2776)

Section, [Pub. L. 88–426, title II, § 203(g)](/us/pl/88/426/tII/s203/g), Aug. 14, 1964, [78 Stat. 415](/us/stat/78/415); [Pub. L. 93–371, § 4](/us/pl/93/371/s4), Aug. 13, 1974, [88 Stat. 429](/us/stat/88/429); [Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 105](/us/pl/94/59/tI/s105), July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 275](/us/stat/89/275); [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/A), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2775](/us/stat/133/2775), related to compensation of Secretary of Senate.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the later of the first day of the first applicable pay period beginning on or after Jan. 1, 2020, or the first day of the first applicable pay period beginning on or after Dec. 20, 2019, see [section 212(c) of Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94/s212/c), set out as a note under [section 282b of this title](/us/usc/t2/s282b).

#### Effective Date of 1975 Amendment

[Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 105](/us/pl/94/59/tI/s105), July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 275](/us/stat/89/275), which provided that the increase in the Secretary’s rate of compensation to $40,000 was effective July 1, 1975, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/A), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2775](/us/stat/133/2775).
