# § 755. Omitted

##### § 755. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 15](/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1207/s15), [68 Stat. 1103](/us/stat/68/1103), which related to execution of patents, deeds, and other instruments by the Secretary of the Interior, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
