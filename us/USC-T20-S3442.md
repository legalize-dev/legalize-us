##### § 3442. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–145, title XII, § 1204(a)(1)](/us/pl/99/145/tXII/s1204/a/1), Nov. 8, 1985, [99 Stat. 720](/us/stat/99/720)

Section, [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 302](/us/pl/96/88/tIII/s302), Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 679](/us/stat/93/679); [Pub. L. 97–252, title XI, § 1118](/us/pl/97/252/tXI/s1118), Sept. 8, 1982, [96 Stat. 753](/us/stat/96/753); [Pub. L. 98–94, title XII, § 1223](/us/pl/98/94/tXII/s1223), Sept. 24, 1983, [97 Stat. 692](/us/stat/97/692), provided for transfers to Department and Secretary of Education from Department and Secretary of Defense of functions relating to operation of overseas schools for and education of dependents and personnel, effectuation of transfers, and operation of overseas institutions of higher education.
