# § 6298. Rules

##### § 6298. Rules

The Commission and the Secretary may each issue such rules as each deems necessary to carry out the provisions of this part.

*([Pub. L. 94–163, title III, § 328](/us/pl/94/163/tIII/s328), Dec. 22, 1975, [89 Stat. 928](/us/stat/89/928); [Pub. L. 95–619, title VI, § 691(b)(2)](/us/pl/95/619/tVI/s691/b/2), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3288](/us/stat/92/3288).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1978—[Pub. L. 95–619](/us/pl/95/619) substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”, meaning Administrator of the Federal Energy Administration.
