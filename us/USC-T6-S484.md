# § 484. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 484. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out [section 482 of this title](/us/usc/t6/s482).

*([Pub. L. 107–296, title VIII, § 894](/us/pl/107/296/tVIII/s894), Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2256](/us/stat/116/2256).)*
