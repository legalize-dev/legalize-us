# [§§ 3315 to 3324. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–187, § 7(a)](/us/pl/113/187/s7/a), Nov. 26, 2014, [128 Stat. 2011](/us/stat/128/2011)]

##### [§§ 3315 to 3324. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–187, § 7(a)](/us/pl/113/187/s7/a), Nov. 26, 2014, [128 Stat. 2011](/us/stat/128/2011)]

Section 3315, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1698](/us/stat/88/1698), related to definitions of certain terms used in sections 3315 to 3324.

Section 3316, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1699](/us/stat/88/1699), related to the establishment of the National Study Commission on Records and Documents of Federal Officials.

Section 3317, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1699](/us/stat/88/1699), related to the duties of the Commission.

Section 3318, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1699](/us/stat/88/1699); amended [Pub. L. 94–261, § 1(a)](/us/pl/94/261/s1/a), Apr. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 326](/us/stat/90/326), related to membership of the Commission.

Section 3319, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1701](/us/stat/88/1701), related to director, staff, experts, and consultants.

Section 3320, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1701](/us/stat/88/1701), related to the powers of the Commission.

Section 3321, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1701](/us/stat/88/1701), related to support services provided to the Commission by the Administrator of General Services and the Archivist of the United States.

Section 3322, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1701](/us/stat/88/1701); amended [Pub. L. 94–261, § 1(b)](/us/pl/94/261/s1/b), Apr. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 326](/us/stat/90/326), related to the report of the Commission.

Section 3323, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1701](/us/stat/88/1701), related to termination of the Commission.

Section 3324, added [Pub. L. 93–526, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/526/tII/s202), Dec. 19, 1974, [88 Stat. 1701](/us/stat/88/1701), related to authorization of appropriations.
