# § 3023. Separation of ensigns found not fully qualified

##### § 3023. Separation of ensigns found not fully qualified

If an officer in the grade of ensign is at any time found not fully qualified, the officer shall be separated from the commissioned service.

*([Pub. L. 107–372, title II, § 223](/us/pl/107/372/tII/s223), Dec. 19, 2002, [116 Stat. 3085](/us/stat/116/3085); [Pub. L. 117–263, div. K, title CXVII, § 11703(a)](/us/pl/117/263/dK/tCXVII/s11703/a), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 4156](/us/stat/136/4156); [Pub. L. 119–60, div. H, title LXXXIV, § 8407](/us/pl/119/60/dH/tLXXXIV/s8407), Dec. 18, 2025, [139 Stat. 1913](/us/stat/139/1913).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [section 853e of this title](/us/usc/t33/s853e) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 107–372](/us/pl/107/372).

#### Amendments

2025—[Pub. L. 119–60](/us/pl/119/60) struck out “permanent” before “grade of ensign” and “the officer’s commission shall be revoked and” before “the officer shall be separated”.

2022—[Pub. L. 117–263](/us/pl/117/263) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section consisted of subsecs. (a) and (b) relating, respectively, to promotion of ensigns to grade of lieutenant (junior grade) and separation of ensigns found not fully qualified.
