# §§ 351 to 355. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–417, § 1](/us/pl/88/417/s1), Aug. 11, 1964, [78 Stat. 389](/us/stat/78/389)

##### §§ 351 to 355. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–417, § 1](/us/pl/88/417/s1), Aug. 11, 1964, [78 Stat. 389](/us/stat/78/389)

Section 351, [act Oct. 22, 1919, ch. 77, § 1](/us/act/1919-10-22/ch77/s1), [41 Stat. 293](/us/stat/41/293), authorized Secretary of the Interior to grant permits to explore for underground water.

Section 352, acts [Oct. 22, 1919, ch. 77, § 2](/us/act/1919-10-22/ch77/s2), [41 Stat. 294](/us/stat/41/294); [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1](/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1), [42 Stat. 208](/us/stat/42/208); [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462), [43 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/43/1145); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100](/us/stat/60/1100), related to designation by Secretary of lands subject to disposal.

Section 353, acts [Oct. 22, 1919, ch. 77, § 3](/us/act/1919-10-22/ch77/s3), [41 Stat. 294](/us/stat/41/294); [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1](/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1), [42 Stat. 208](/us/stat/42/208); [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462), [43 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/43/1145); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100](/us/stat/60/1100), related to application for permit to explore for under-ground water.

Section 354, [act Oct. 22, 1919, ch. 77, § 4](/us/act/1919-10-22/ch77/s4), [41 Stat. 294](/us/stat/41/294), related to conditions of permit and its cancellation for failure to meet them.

Section 355, [act Oct. 22, 1919, ch. 77, § 5](/us/act/1919-10-22/ch77/s5), [41 Stat. 294](/us/stat/41/294), related to issuance of a patent to land on the development of a water supply.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Savings Provision

[Pub. L. 88–417, § 1](/us/pl/88/417/s1), Aug. 11, 1964, [78 Stat. 389](/us/stat/78/389), provided: “That, subject to any valid rights and obligations existing on the date of approval of this Act [Aug. 11, 1964], the Act of October 22, 1919 ([41 Stat. 293](/us/stat/41/293); [43 U.S.C. 351–355](/us/usc/t43/s351–355), 357–360), is hereby repealed.”

#### Processing of Applications Filed Prior to August 11, 1964

[Pub. L. 88–417, § 2](/us/pl/88/417/s2), Aug. 11, 1964, [78 Stat. 389](/us/stat/78/389), provided that: “Any valid application for permit under that Act [this chapter], on file with the Secretary of the Interior on the effective date of this Act [Aug. 11, 1964], may be processed in the same manner as if this Act [repealing sections 351 to 355 and 357 to 360 of this title] had not been enacted.”
