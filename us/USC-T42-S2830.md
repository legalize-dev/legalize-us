# § 2830. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 683(a)](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s683/a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 519](/us/stat/95/519)

##### § 2830. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 683(a)](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s683/a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 519](/us/stat/95/519)

Section, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 236](/us/pl/88/452/tII/s236), formerly § 237, as added [Pub. L. 93–644, § 5(f)](/us/pl/93/644/s5/f), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2297](/us/stat/88/2297); renumbered § 237 and amended [Pub. L. 95–568, § 6(d)](/us/pl/95/568/s6/d), (e), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2428](/us/stat/92/2428), set forth authorization of appropriations.

A prior [section 236 of Pub. L. 88–452](/us/pl/88/452/s236), title II, as added [Pub. L. 93–644, § 5(f)](/us/pl/93/644/s5/f), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2297](/us/stat/88/2297); amended [Pub. L. 94–341, § 2(a)(14)](/us/pl/94/341/s2/a/14), July 6, 1976, [90 Stat. 804](/us/stat/90/804), related to establishment of Intergovernmental Advisory Council on Community Services and was classified to [section 2829 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2829), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 95–568, § 6(d)](/us/pl/95/568/s6/d), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2428](/us/stat/92/2428).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 9912(a) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s9912/a), prior to the general amendment of chapter 106 (§ 9901 et seq.) of this title by [Pub. L. 105–285](/us/pl/105/285).
