# §§ 85 to 88a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(33)](/us/pl/104/186/tII/s204/33), (34)(A), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1734](/us/stat/110/1734)

##### §§ 85 to 88a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(33)](/us/pl/104/186/tII/s204/33), (34)(A), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1734](/us/stat/110/1734)

Section 85, [act Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 830, § 1](/us/act/1901-03-03/ch830/s1), [31 Stat. 968](/us/stat/31/968), related to performance of duties by employees of House.

Section 86, [act Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 830, § 1](/us/act/1901-03-03/ch830/s1), [31 Stat. 968](/us/stat/31/968), related to division of salaries of employees of House.

Section 87, [act Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 830, § 1](/us/act/1901-03-03/ch830/s1), [31 Stat. 968](/us/stat/31/968), related to requiring or permitting employees of House to sublet duties.

Section 88, [act Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 830, § 1](/us/act/1901-03-03/ch830/s1), [31 Stat. 968](/us/stat/31/968), prescribed age limits of twelve and eighteen for service as pages in House of Representatives but made the restriction inapplicable to chief pages, riding pages, and telephone pages. See [section 4901(b) of this title](/us/usc/t2/s4901/b).

Section 88a, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title II, § 243](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tII/s243), [60 Stat. 839](/us/stat/60/839), related to education of Congressional and Supreme Court pages, authorized appropriations, and allowed pages to elect to attend private or parochial schools.
