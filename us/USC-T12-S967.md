# § 967. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### § 967. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 29](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s29) (pars.), as added [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 25(d)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s25/d), [50 Stat. 713](/us/stat/50/713); amended Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(e)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/e), (h), [73 Stat. 386](/us/stat/73/386), 387, provided for a conservator in lieu of a receiver for land bank associations which have failed to meet their outstanding obligations.
