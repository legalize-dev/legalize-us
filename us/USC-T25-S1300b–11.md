# § 1300b–11. Omitted

##### § 1300b–11. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 97–429, § 2](/us/pl/97/429/s2), Jan. 8, 1983, [96 Stat. 2269](/us/stat/96/2269), which set out Congressional findings and declaration of policy, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 97–429, § 1](/us/pl/97/429/s1), Jan. 8, 1983, [96 Stat. 2269](/us/stat/96/2269), provided that [Pub. L. 97–429](/us/pl/97/429), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Texas Band of Kickapoo Act”.
