# § 3113. Restriction on reemployment after conviction of certain crimes

##### § 3113. Restriction on reemployment after conviction of certain crimes

(1) the employee is convicted of a violation of [section 201(b) of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s201/b); and

(2) such violation related to conduct prohibited under section 1010(a) of the Controlled Substances Import and Export Act ([21 U.S.C. 960(a)](/us/usc/t21/s960/a)).

*(Added [Pub. L. 105–61, title VI, § 638(a)](/us/pl/105/61/tVI/s638/a), Oct. 10, 1997, [111 Stat. 1316](/us/stat/111/1316).)*
