# § 251. Transfer of functions

##### § 251. Transfer of functions

(1) The Border Patrol program.

(2) The detention and removal program.

(3) The intelligence program.

(4) The investigations program.

(5) The inspections program.

*([Pub. L. 107–296, title IV, § 441](/us/pl/107/296/tIV/s441), Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2192](/us/stat/116/2192); [Pub. L. 114–125, title VIII, § 802(g)(1)(B)(v)(I)](/us/pl/114/125/tVIII/s802/g/1/B/v/I), Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 212](/us/stat/130/212).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2016—[Pub. L. 114–125](/us/pl/114/125) substituted “Transfer of functions” for “Transfer of functions to Under Secretary for Border and Transportation Security” in section catchline and “Secretary” for “Under Secretary for Border and Transportation Security” in introductory provisions.
