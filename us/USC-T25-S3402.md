# § 3402. Definitions

##### § 3402. Definitions

(1) Federal agency The term “federal 11 So in original. Probably should be capitalized. agency” has the same meaning given the term “agency” in [section 551(1) of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s551/1).

(2) Indian tribe

(A) In general The terms “Indian tribe” and “tribe” have the meaning given the term “Indian tribe” in [section 5304 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5304).

(B) Inclusion The term “Indian tribe” includes tribal organizations (as defined in [section 5304 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5304)).

(3) Indian The term “Indian” shall have the meaning given such term in [section 5304(d) of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5304/d).

(4) Program The term “program” means a program described in [section 3404(a) of this title](/us/usc/t25/s3404/a).

(5) Secretary Except where otherwise provided, the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.
