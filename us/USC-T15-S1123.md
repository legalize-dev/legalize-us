# § 1123. Rules and regulations for conduct of proceedings in Patent and Trademark Office

##### § 1123. Rules and regulations for conduct of proceedings in Patent and Trademark Office

The Director shall make rules and regulations, not inconsistent with law, for the conduct of proceedings in the Patent and Trademark Office under this chapter.

*([July 5, 1946, ch. 540](/us/act/1946-07-05/ch540), title VI, § 41, [60 Stat. 440](/us/stat/60/440); [Pub. L. 93–596, § 1](/us/pl/93/596/s1), Jan. 2, 1975, [88 Stat. 1949](/us/stat/88/1949); [Pub. L. 106–113, div. B, § 1000(a)(9) [title IV, § 4732(b)(1)(B)]](/us/pl/106/113/dB/s1000/a/9/tIV/s4732/b/1/B), Nov. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 1536](/us/stat/113/1536), 1501A–583.)*
