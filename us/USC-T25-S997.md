# § 997. Omitted

##### § 997. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–775, § 7](/us/pl/87/775/s7), Oct. 9, 1962, [76 Stat. 777](/us/stat/76/777), which provided for costs incident to making payments to be paid from the judgment fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
