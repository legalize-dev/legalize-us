# § 872. Omitted

##### § 872. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 86–451, title I, § 301](/us/pl/86/451/tI/s301), May 13, 1960, [74 Stat. 94](/us/stat/74/94), which prescribed the rate of extra compensation for recorders, instrument observers and other Federal employees while making oceanographic observations or tending seismographs, was from an appropriation act. See [section 873 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s873), which authorizes Secretary of Commerce to establish rates of compensation for such personnel.
