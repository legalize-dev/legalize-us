# § 8247. Sale of meals by general messes

##### § 8247. Sale of meals by general messes

Under such regulations as the Secretary of the Navy prescribes, meals may be sold by general messes afloat and ashore.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 381](/us/stat/70A/381), § 6087; renumbered § 8247, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(b)(8)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/b/8), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1835](/us/stat/132/1835).)*
