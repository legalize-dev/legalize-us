# § 1414. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–42, § 6(c)](/us/pl/105/42/s6/c), Aug. 15, 1997, [111 Stat. 1130](/us/stat/111/1130)

##### § 1414. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–42, § 6(c)](/us/pl/105/42/s6/c), Aug. 15, 1997, [111 Stat. 1130](/us/stat/111/1130)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–522, title III, § 304](/us/pl/92/522/tIII/s304), as added [Pub. L. 102–523, § 2(a)](/us/pl/102/523/s2/a), Oct. 26, 1992, [106 Stat. 3428](/us/stat/106/3428), related to reviews, reports, and recommendations by Secretary of Commerce.

A prior [section 304 of Pub. L. 92–522](/us/pl/92/522/s304) was renumbered section 404 and is classified to [section 1421c of this title](/us/usc/t16/s1421c).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

For effective date of repeal, see [section 8 of Pub. L. 105–42](/us/pl/105/42/s8), set out as an Effective Date of 1997 Amendment note under [section 1362 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s1362).
