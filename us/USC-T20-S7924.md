# § 7924. State control over standards

##### § 7924. State control over standards

(a) In general Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to prohibit a State from withdrawing from the Common Core State Standards or from otherwise revising their standards.

(b) Prohibition No officer or employee of the Federal Government shall, directly or indirectly, through grants, contracts or other cooperative agreements, through waiver granted under [section 7861 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7861) or through any other authority, take any action against a State that exercises its rights under subsection (a).
