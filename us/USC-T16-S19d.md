# § 19d. Repealed. [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1076, § 1(25)](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1076/s1/25), [68 Stat. 968](/us/stat/68/968)

##### § 19d. Repealed. [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1076, § 1(25)](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1076/s1/25), [68 Stat. 968](/us/stat/68/968)

Section, [act July 10, 1935, ch. 375, § 6](/us/act/1935-07-10/ch375/s6), [49 Stat. 478](/us/stat/49/478), required the National Park Trust Fund Board to submit an annual report to Congress of the moneys or securities received and held by it, and of its operations.
