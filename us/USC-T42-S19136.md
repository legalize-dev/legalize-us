# § 19136. Rule of construction

##### § 19136. Rule of construction

Nothing in this subchapter may be construed to require public disclosure of information that is exempt from mandatory disclosure under [section 552 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s552).

*([Pub. L. 117–167, div. B, title IV, § 10407](/us/pl/117/167/dB/tIV/s10407), Aug. 9, 2022, [136 Stat. 1609](/us/stat/136/1609).)*
