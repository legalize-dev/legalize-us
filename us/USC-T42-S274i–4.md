# § 274i–4. Definitions

##### § 274i–4. Definitions

(1) Organ The term “organ” has the meaning given such term in section 121.2 of title 42, Code of Federal Regulations.

(2) Organ procurement organization The term “organ procurement organization” means a qualified organ procurement organization described in [section 273(b)(1) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s273/b/1).
