# § 2033. Applicability of antidumping provisions and antitrust laws

##### § 2033. Applicability of antidumping provisions and antitrust laws

Nothing contained in this chapter shall be construed to affect or modify the provisions of subtitle B of title VII of the Tariff Act of 1930 [[19 U.S.C. 1673](/us/usc/t19/s1673) et seq.] or of any of the antitrust laws as designated in [section 12 of title 15](/us/usc/t15/s12).

*([Pub. L. 89–283, title V, § 503](/us/pl/89/283/tV/s503), Oct. 21, 1965, [79 Stat. 1026](/us/stat/79/1026); [Pub. L. 96–39, title I, § 106(b)(2)](/us/pl/96/39/tI/s106/b/2), July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 193](/us/stat/93/193).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 89–283](/us/pl/89/283), Oct. 21, 1965, [79 Stat. 1016](/us/stat/79/1016). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 2001 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2001) and Tables.

The Tariff Act of 1930, referred to in text, is [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), [46 Stat. 590](/us/stat/46/590). Subtitle B of title VII of the Tariff Act of 1930 is classified generally to part II of subtitle IV (§ 1673 et seq.) of chapter 4 of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 1654 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1654) and Tables.

#### Amendments

1979—[Pub. L. 96–39](/us/pl/96/39) substituted “subtitle B of title VII of the Tariff Act of 1930” for “the Anti-Dumping Act, 1921,”.
