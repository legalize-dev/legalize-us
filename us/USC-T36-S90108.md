# § 90108. Annual audit

##### § 90108. Annual audit

The foundation shall enter into a contract with an independent auditor to conduct an annual audit of the foundation.

*(Added [Pub. L. 107–252, title VI, § 601(a)](/us/pl/107/252/tVI/s601/a), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1721](/us/stat/116/1721), § 152608; renumbered § 90108, [Pub. L. 113–237, § 3(c)(6)(B)](/us/pl/113/237/s3/c/6/B), Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 2840](/us/stat/128/2840).)*
