# § 241g. Change in name of Theodore Roosevelt National Memorial Park

##### § 241g. Change in name of Theodore Roosevelt National Memorial Park

The area formerly known as the “Theodore Roosevelt National Memorial Park”, established by the Act of April 25, 1947 ([61 Stat. 52](/us/stat/61/52)) [[16 U.S.C. 241](/us/usc/t16/s241) et seq.], shall henceforth be known as the “Theodore Roosevelt National Park”.

*([Pub. L. 95–625, title VI, § 610](/us/pl/95/625/tVI/s610), Nov. 10, 1978, [92 Stat. 3521](/us/stat/92/3521).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Act of April 25, 1947, referred to in text, is [act Apr. 25, 1947, ch. 41](/us/act/1947-04-25/ch41), [61 Stat. 52](/us/stat/61/52), which is classified generally to this subchapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

#### Codification

Section was not enacted as part of [act Apr. 25, 1947, ch. 41](/us/act/1947-04-25/ch41), [61 Stat. 52](/us/stat/61/52), which comprises this subchapter.
