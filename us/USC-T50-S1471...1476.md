# §§ 1471 to 1476. Transferred

##### §§ 1471 to 1476. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 1471, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III, § 301](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII/s301), [69 Stat. 588](/us/stat/69/588), which related to definitions, was transferred to [section 1973cc–21 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–21), The Public Health and Welfare.

Section 1472, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III, § 302](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII/s302), [69 Stat. 588](/us/stat/69/588), which related to free postage, was transferred to [section 1973cc–22 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–22).

Section 1473, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III, § 303](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII/s303), [69 Stat. 588](/us/stat/69/588), which related to prevention of fraud and coercion, was transferred to [section 1973cc–23 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–23).

Section 1474, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III, § 304](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII/s304), [69 Stat. 589](/us/stat/69/589), which related to acts done in good faith, was transferred to [section 1973cc–24 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–24).

Section 1475, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III, § 305](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII/s305), [69 Stat. 589](/us/stat/69/589), which related to undue influence and free discussion, was transferred to [section 1973cc–25 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–25).

Section 1476, [act Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 656, title III, § 308](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch656/tIII/s308), [69 Stat. 589](/us/stat/69/589), which authorized appropriations, was transferred to [section 1973cc–26 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s1973cc–26).

Sections 1471 to 1476 were formerly classified to sections 2191 to 2196 of Title 5 prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).
