# § 1750f. Rules and regulations

##### § 1750f. Rules and regulations

The Secretary is authorized and directed to make such rules and regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

*([June 27, 1934, ch. 847](/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847), title IX, § 907, as added [Sept. 1, 1951, ch. 378](/us/act/1951-09-01/ch378), title II, § 201, [65 Stat. 301](/us/stat/65/301); amended [Pub. L. 90–19, § 1(a)(3)](/us/pl/90/19/s1/a/3), May 25, 1967, [81 Stat. 17](/us/stat/81/17).)*
