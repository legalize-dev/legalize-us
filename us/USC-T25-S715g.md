# § 715g. Omitted

##### § 715g. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–42, § 9](/us/pl/101/42/s9), June 28, 1989, [103 Stat. 93](/us/stat/103/93), which related to adoption of a tribal constitution and election of tribal officials, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
