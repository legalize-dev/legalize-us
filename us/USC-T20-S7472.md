# § 7472. Peer review

##### § 7472. Peer review

The Secretary may use a peer review process to review applications submitted to the Secretary under subpart 2 or subpart 3 of this part.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6142](/us/pl/89/10/tVI/s6142), formerly title VII, § 7142, as added [Pub. L. 114–95, title VI, § 6001(a)](/us/pl/114/95/tVI/s6001/a), (b)(1), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2046](/us/stat/129/2046).)*
