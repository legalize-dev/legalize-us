# § 473. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 637](/us/stat/80/637)

##### § 473. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 637](/us/stat/80/637)

Section, [act Mar. 2, 1895, ch. 172, § 2](/us/act/1895-03-02/ch172/s2), [28 Stat. 740](/us/stat/28/740), provided that the powers and authority conferred upon the harbor master, District of Columbia, may in his absence or disability be exercised by the pilot of the harbor police boat.
