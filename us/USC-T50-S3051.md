# § 3051. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–259, title III, § 347(c)](/us/pl/111/259/tIII/s347/c), Oct. 7, 2010, [124 Stat. 2698](/us/stat/124/2698)

##### § 3051. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–259, title III, § 347(c)](/us/pl/111/259/tIII/s347/c), Oct. 7, 2010, [124 Stat. 2698](/us/stat/124/2698)

Section, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, title I, § 114A](/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343/tI/s114A), as added [Pub. L. 107–306, title VIII, § 823(a)](/us/pl/107/306/tVIII/s823/a), Nov. 27, 2002, [116 Stat. 2427](/us/stat/116/2427); amended [Pub. L. 108–136, div. A, title IX, § 921(g)](/us/pl/108/136/dA/tIX/s921/g), Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1570](/us/stat/117/1570); [Pub. L. 108–458, title I, § 1071(a)(4)](/us/pl/108/458/tI/s1071/a/4), Dec. 17, 2004, [118 Stat. 3690](/us/stat/118/3690), related to annual report on improvement of financial statements for auditing purposes.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 404i–1 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s404i–1) and repealed prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Correcting Long-Standing Material Weaknesses

[Pub. L. 111–259, title III, § 368](/us/pl/111/259/tIII/s368), Oct. 7, 2010, [124 Stat. 2705](/us/stat/124/2705), which provided that senior intelligence management officials be designated to correct long-standing, correctable material weaknesses of certain intelligence elements, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–92, div. E, title LXVII, § 6726(a)](/us/pl/116/92/dE/tLXVII/s6726/a), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2236](/us/stat/133/2236).
