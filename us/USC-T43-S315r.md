# § 315r. Rental payments in advance in case of withdrawal of lands for war or national defense purposes

##### § 315r. Rental payments in advance in case of withdrawal of lands for war or national defense purposes

In administering the provisions of [section 315q of this title](/us/usc/t43/s315q), payments of rentals may be made in advance.

*([Oct. 29, 1949, ch. 787](/us/act/1949-10-29/ch787), title III, § 301, [63 Stat. 996](/us/stat/63/996).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was not enacted as a part of act June 28, 1934, known as the Taylor Grazing Act, which comprises this subchapter.
