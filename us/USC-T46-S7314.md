# § 7314. Service requirements for qualified members of engine departments

##### § 7314. Service requirements for qualified members of engine departments

To qualify for an endorsement as qualified member of the engine department, an applicant must provide proof that the applicant has 6 months’ service in the related entry rating as described in [section 7313(b) of this title](/us/usc/t46/s7313/b).

*([Pub. L. 98–89](/us/pl/98/89), Aug. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 544](/us/stat/97/544).)*

Section 7314 requires individuals who wish to have their documents endorsed as a qualified member of the engine department must have at least 6 months service as a wiper or coal passer.
