# §§ 4211 to 4219. Transferred

##### §§ 4211 to 4219. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 111 to 119 of [Pub. L. 116–224](/us/pl/116/224), formerly classified to sections 4211 to 4219, respectively, of this title, were renumbered sections 111 to 119 of [Pub. L. 109–449](/us/pl/109/449) by [Pub. L. 119–65, § 3(a)](/us/pl/119/65/s3/a), Dec. 26, 2025, [139 Stat. 1987](/us/stat/139/1987), and transferred to sections 1961 to 1969, respectively, of this title.
