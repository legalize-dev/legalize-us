# § 2241. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 2241. Authorization of appropriations

Except as otherwise provided in [section 2243 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s2243) (relating to temperature control devices at Shasta Dam, California), there is authorized to be appropriated not more than $120,000,000 11 See Extension of Authorization of Appropriations note below. in total for the period of fiscal years 2006 through 2028.
