# § 992. Omitted

##### § 992. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–775, § 2](/us/pl/87/775/s2), Oct. 9, 1962, [76 Stat. 776](/us/stat/76/776), which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
