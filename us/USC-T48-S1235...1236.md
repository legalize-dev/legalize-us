# §§ 1235 to 1236. Transferred

##### §§ 1235 to 1236. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 1235, [act Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 5](/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84/s5), [48 Stat. 459](/us/stat/48/459), related to transfer of property and rights to Philippine Commonwealth, and was transferred to [section 1391 of Title 22](/us/usc/t22/s1391), Foreign Relations and Intercourse.

Section 1235a, [act June 29, 1944, ch. 322, § 2](/us/act/1944-06-29/ch322/s2), [58 Stat. 626](/us/stat/58/626), related to acquisition of military and naval bases by United States, and has been transferred to [section 1392 of Title 22](/us/usc/t22/s1392).

Section 1236, acts [Mar. 24, 1934, ch. 84, § 6](/us/act/1934-03-24/ch84/s6), [48 Stat. 459](/us/stat/48/459); [Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 502, § 1](/us/act/1939-08-07/ch502/s1), [53 Stat. 1226](/us/stat/53/1226); [Apr. 30, 1946, ch. 244](/us/act/1946-04-30/ch244), title V, § 511(2), [60 Stat. 158](/us/stat/60/158); Sept. 22, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–346, title I, § 104(1)](/us/pl/86/346/tI/s104/1), [73 Stat. 622](/us/stat/73/622), related to supplementary sinking fund for bond payments, purchase of bonds by United States, and creation of special trust account, and has been transferred to [section 1393 of Title 22](/us/usc/t22/s1393).
