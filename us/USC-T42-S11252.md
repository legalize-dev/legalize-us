# § 11252. Dissemination

##### § 11252. Dissemination

The Director of the National Institute of Mental Health shall disseminate the results of research conducted under this part to appropriate professional entities and to the public.

*([Pub. L. 99–660, title IX, § 932](/us/pl/99/660/tIX/s932), formerly § 945, Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3810](/us/stat/100/3810); renumbered § 932, [Pub. L. 100–607, title I, § 142(c)(2)(B)](/us/pl/100/607/tI/s142/c/2/B), Nov. 4, 1988, [102 Stat. 3057](/us/stat/102/3057).)*
