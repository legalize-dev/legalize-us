# § 5141. Interest rates and taxes

##### § 5141. Interest rates and taxes

Loans made or insured pursuant to sections 5136 to 5143 of this title will be subject to the interest rate provisions of section 307(a)(3)(B) of the Consolidated Farmers Home Administration Act of 1961, as amended [[7 U.S.C. 1927(a)(3)(B)](/us/usc/t7/s1927/a/3/B)], and to the provisions of subtitle D of that Act [[7 U.S.C. 1981](/us/usc/t7/s1981) et seq.] except sections 340 [[7 U.S.C. 1990](/us/usc/t7/s1990)], 341, 342 [[7 U.S.C. 1013a](/us/usc/t7/s1013a)], and 343 [[7 U.S.C. 1991](/us/usc/t7/s1991)] thereof: *Provided*, That section 334 [[7 U.S.C. 1984](/us/usc/t7/s1984)] thereof shall not be construed to subject to taxation any lands or interests therein while they are held by an Indian tribe or tribal corporation or by the United States in trust for such tribe or tribal corporation pursuant to sections 5136 to 5143 of this title.

*([Pub. L. 91–229, § 5](/us/pl/91/229/s5), Apr. 11, 1970, [84 Stat. 120](/us/stat/84/120); [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVIII, § 1854(a)](/us/pl/101/624/tXVIII/s1854/a), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3837](/us/stat/104/3837).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Consolidated Farmers Home Administration Act of 1961, referred to in text, is now the Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act, [Pub. L. 87–128, title III](/us/pl/87/128/tIII), Aug. 8, 1961, [75 Stat. 307](/us/stat/75/307). Subtitle D of the Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act is classified principally to subchapter IV (§ 1981 et seq.) of chapter 50 of Title 7, Agriculture. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1921 of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s1921) and Tables.

Section 341 of that Act is set out as a note under [section 1921 of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s1921).

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 492 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s492) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

1990—[Pub. L. 101–624](/us/pl/101/624) substituted “section 307(a)(3)(B)” for “section 307(a)”.
