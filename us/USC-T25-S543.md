# § 543. Omitted

##### § 543. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 7, 1939, ch. 552, § 3](/us/act/1939-08-07/ch552/s3), [53 Stat. 1253](/us/stat/53/1253), which related to liability of judgment funds for debts, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
