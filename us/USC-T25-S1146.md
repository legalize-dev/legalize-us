# § 1146. Omitted

##### § 1146. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–93, § 6](/us/pl/90/93/s6), Sept. 27, 1967, [81 Stat. 230](/us/stat/81/230), which exempted funds distributed per capita from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
