# § 1300i–11. Omitted

##### § 1300i–11. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–580, § 14](/us/pl/100/580/s14), Oct. 31, 1988, [102 Stat. 2936](/us/stat/102/2936); [Pub. L. 101–301, § 9(4)](/us/pl/101/301/s9/4), May 24, 1990, [104 Stat. 211](/us/stat/104/211); [Pub. L. 102–572, title IX, § 902(b)(1)](/us/pl/102/572/tIX/s902/b/1), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4516](/us/stat/106/4516), which related to claims challenging the partition of the joint reservation pursuant to former [section 1300i–1 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1300i–1), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
