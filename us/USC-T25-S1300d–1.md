# § 1300d–1. Omitted

##### § 1300d–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–555, title I, § 101](/us/pl/92/555/tI/s101), Oct. 25, 1972, [86 Stat. 1168](/us/stat/86/1168), which related to membership rolls of Lower Council Sioux tribal groups, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
