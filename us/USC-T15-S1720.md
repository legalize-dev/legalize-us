# § 1720. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1720. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 90–448, title XIV, § 1422](/us/pl/90/448/tXIV/s1422), formerly § 1421, Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 599](/us/stat/82/599); renumbered § 1422, [Pub. L. 96–153, title IV, § 409](/us/pl/96/153/tIV/s409), Dec. 21, 1979, [93 Stat. 1132](/us/stat/93/1132).)*
