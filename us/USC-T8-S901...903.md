# §§ 901 to 903. Repealed. [June 27, 1952, ch. 477](/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477), title IV, § 403(a)(42), [66 Stat. 280](/us/stat/66/280), eff. Dec. 24, 1952

##### §§ 901 to 903. Repealed. [June 27, 1952, ch. 477](/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477), title IV, § 403(a)(42), [66 Stat. 280](/us/stat/66/280), eff. Dec. 24, 1952

Section 901, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title I](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tI), subchap. V, § 501, [54 Stat. 1171](/us/stat/54/1171), related to procedure when diplomatic officials believe that persons in foreign state have lost American nationality. See [section 1501 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1501).

Section 902, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title I](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tI), subchap. V, § 502, [54 Stat. 1171](/us/stat/54/1171), related to certificates of nationality for non-naturalized United States nationals. See [section 1502 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1502).

Section 903, [act Oct. 14, 1940, ch. 876, title I](/us/act/1940-10-14/ch876/tI), subchap. V, § 503, [54 Stat. 1171](/us/stat/54/1171), related to judicial proceedings for declaration of United States nationality in event of denial of rights and privileges as national. See [section 1503 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1503).
