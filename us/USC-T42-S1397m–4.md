# § 1397m–4. Report

##### § 1397m–4. Report

(1) compiling, summarizing, and analyzing the information contained in the State reports submitted under subsections (b)(4) and (c)(4) 11 See References in Text note below. of [section 1397m–1 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1397m–1); and

(2) containing such recommendations for legislative or administrative action as the Secretary determines to be appropriate.
