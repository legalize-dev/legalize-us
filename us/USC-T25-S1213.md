# § 1213. Omitted

##### § 1213. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–454, title II, § 203](/us/pl/103/454/tII/s203), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4792](/us/stat/108/4792), which reaffirmed the status of the Central Council of Tlingit and Haida Indian Tribes as a federally recognized Indian tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
