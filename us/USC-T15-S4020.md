# § 4020. Rules and regulations

##### § 4020. Rules and regulations

The Secretary, with the concurrence of the Attorney General, shall promulgate such rules and regulations as are necessary to carry out the purposes of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 97–290, title III, § 310](/us/pl/97/290/tIII/s310), Oct. 8, 1982, [96 Stat. 1245](/us/stat/96/1245).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 97–290](/us/pl/97/290), Oct. 8, 1982, [96 Stat. 1233](/us/stat/96/1233), which enacted this chapter and [section 6a of this title](/us/usc/t15/s6a) and [section 635a–4 of Title 12](/us/usc/t12/s635a–4), Banks and Banking, amended [section 45 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45) and sections 372 and 1843 of Title 12, and enacted provisions set out as notes under sections 1, 4001, and 4011 of this title and sections 1841 and 1843 of Title 12. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective Oct. 8, 1982, see [section 312 of Pub. L. 97–290](/us/pl/97/290/s312), set out as a note under [section 4011 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s4011).
