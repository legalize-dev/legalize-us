# § 8901. Composition

##### § 8901. Composition

The Naval Militia consists of the Naval Militia of the States, the District of Columbia, Guam, and the Virgin Islands.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 486](/us/stat/70A/486), § 7851; [Pub. L. 109–163, div.
