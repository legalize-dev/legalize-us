# § 564q. Omitted

##### § 564q. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 18](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s18), [68 Stat. 722](/us/stat/68/722), which related to termination of Federal trust relationship to the affairs of the tribe and its members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
