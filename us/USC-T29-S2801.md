#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the first day of the first full program year after July 22, 2014 (July 1, 2015), see [section 506 of Pub. L. 113–128](/us/pl/113/128/s506), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3101 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s3101).

#### Short Title of 2007 Amendment

[Pub. L. 110–140, title X, § 1001](/us/pl/110/140/tX/s1001), Dec. 19, 2007, [121 Stat. 1748](/us/stat/121/1748), provided that: “This title [amending [section 2916 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s2916)] may be cited as the ‘Green Jobs Act of 2007’.”

#### Short Title of 2006 Amendment

[Pub. L. 109–281, § 1](/us/pl/109/281/s1), Sept. 22, 2006, [120 Stat. 1173](/us/stat/120/1173), provided that: “This Act [enacting [section 2918a of this title](/us/usc/t29/s2918a), amending [section 2939 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s2939), [section 1701u of Title 12](/us/usc/t12/s1701u), Banks and Banking, [section 4183 of Title 25](/us/usc/t25/s4183), Indians, and [section 12870 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s12870), The Public Health and Welfare, repealing sections 12899 to 12899i of Title 42, and enacting provisions set out as notes under [section 2918a of this title](/us/usc/t29/s2918a) and [section 1701u of Title 12](/us/usc/t12/s1701u)] may be cited as the ‘YouthBuild Transfer Act’.”
