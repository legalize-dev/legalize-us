# § 360ddd–2. Inapplicability of drug fees to designated medical gases

##### § 360ddd–2. Inapplicability of drug fees to designated medical gases

A designated medical gas, alone or in combination with another designated gas or gases (as medically appropriate) deemed under [section 360ddd–1 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s360ddd–1) to have in effect an approved application shall not be assessed fees under section 379h(a) or 379j–12(a) of this title on the basis of such deemed approval.
