# § 203. Postmaster General; Deputy Postmaster General

##### § 203. Postmaster General; Deputy Postmaster General

The chief executive officer of the Postal Service is the Postmaster General appointed under [section 202(c) of this title](/us/usc/t39/s202/c). The alternate chief executive officer of the Postal Service is the Deputy Postmaster General appointed under [section 202(d) of this title](/us/usc/t39/s202/d).

*([Pub. L. 91–375](/us/pl/91/375), Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 721](/us/stat/84/721).)*
