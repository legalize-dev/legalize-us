# § 300316. Secretary

##### § 300316. Secretary

In this division, the term “Secretary” means the Secretary acting through the Director.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3190](/us/stat/128/3190).)*
