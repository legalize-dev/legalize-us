# §§ 231 to 250. Omitted

##### §§ 231 to 250. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 231 to 250, relating to Columbia Institution for the Deaf, were transferred to sections 31–1001 to 31–1020 of the District of Columbia Code and subsequently repealed by acts [Aug. 6, 1946, ch. 770, § 1(61)](/us/act/1946-08-06/ch770/s1/61), [60 Stat. 871](/us/stat/60/871); [June 18, 1954, ch. 324, § 9](/us/act/1954-06-18/ch324/s9), [68 Stat. 267](/us/stat/68/267); Dec. 24, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–587, § 5](/us/pl/91/587/s5), [84 Stat. 1579](/us/stat/84/1579). The Columbia Institution for the Deaf was redesignated Gallaudet College by act June 18, 1954, and thereafter redesignated Gallaudet University by [Pub. L. 99–371, title I, § 101(a)](/us/pl/99/371/tI/s101/a), Aug. 4, 1986, [100 Stat. 781](/us/stat/100/781), which is classified to subchapter I (§ 4301 et seq.) of chapter 55 of Title 20, Education.
