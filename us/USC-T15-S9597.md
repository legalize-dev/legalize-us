# § 9597. Executive Order 11625

##### § 9597. Executive Order 11625

(1) in accordance with this chapter and the requirements of this chapter; and

(2) without regard to Executive Order 11625 (36 Fed. Reg. 19967; relating to prescribing additional arrangements for developing and coordinating a national program for minority business enterprise).

*([Pub. L. 117–58, div. K, title VII, § 100707](/us/pl/117/58/dK/tVII/s100707), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 1467](/us/stat/135/1467).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Executive Order 11625, referred to in section catchline and par. (2), is Ex. Ord. No. 11625, Oct. 13, 1971, 36 F.R. 19967, which is set out as a note under [section 631 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s631).

This chapter, referred to in par. (1), was in the original “this division”, meaning div. K of [Pub. L. 117–58](/us/pl/117/58), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 1445](/us/stat/135/1445), which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of division K to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 9501 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s9501) and Tables.
