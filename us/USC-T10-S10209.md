# § 10209. Regular and reserve components: discrimination prohibited

##### § 10209. Regular and reserve components: discrimination prohibited

(1) among Regulars;

(2) among Reserves; and

(3) between Regulars and Reserves.

*(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1661(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI/s1661/a/1), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2978](/us/stat/108/2978).)*
