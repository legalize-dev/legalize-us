# § 19193. Definition

##### § 19193. Definition

In this part, the term “Director” means the Director of the National Science Foundation.

*([Pub. L. 117–167, div. B, title V, § 10533](/us/pl/117/167/dB/tV/s10533), Aug. 9, 2022, [136 Stat. 1628](/us/stat/136/1628).)*
