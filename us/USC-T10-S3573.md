# § 3573. Micro-purchase threshold

##### § 3573. Micro-purchase threshold

The micro-purchase threshold for the Department of Defense is $10,000.

*(Added [Pub. L. 114–328, div. A, title VIII, § 821(a)](/us/pl/114/328/dA/tVIII/s821/a), Dec. 23, 2016, [130 Stat. 2276](/us/stat/130/2276), § 2338; amended [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 821(a)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s821/a), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1853](/us/stat/132/1853); renumbered § 3573, [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1823(b)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1823/b), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4205](/us/stat/134/4205).)*
