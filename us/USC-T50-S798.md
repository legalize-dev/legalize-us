# § 798. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–199, title VIII, § 803(1)](/us/pl/103/199/tVIII/s803/1), Dec. 17, 1993, [107 Stat. 2329](/us/stat/107/2329)

##### § 798. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–199, title VIII, § 803(1)](/us/pl/103/199/tVIII/s803/1), Dec. 17, 1993, [107 Stat. 2329](/us/stat/107/2329)

Section, [act Sept. 23, 1950, ch. 1024, title I, § 1(b)](/us/act/1950-09-23/ch1024/tI/s1/b), [64 Stat. 987](/us/stat/64/987), provided that the Internal Security Act of 1950 not be construed to authorize, require, or establish military or civilian censorship or in any way to limit or infringe upon freedom of press or of speech as guaranteed by Constitution.
