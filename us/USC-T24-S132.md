# § 132. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–56, title XXII, § 2202(100)](/us/pl/85/56/tXXII/s2202/100), June 17, 1957, [71 Stat. 166](/us/stat/71/166)

##### § 132. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–56, title XXII, § 2202(100)](/us/pl/85/56/tXXII/s2202/100), June 17, 1957, [71 Stat. 166](/us/stat/71/166)

Section, [act June 5, 1920, ch. 235, § 1](/us/act/1920-06-05/ch235/s1), [41 Stat. 905](/us/stat/41/905), authorized Board of Managers of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers to make rules governing assignment of classes eligible for domiciliary care.

#### Effective Date of Repeal
