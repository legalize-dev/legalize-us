# § 5426. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 5426. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 93–383, title VI, § 626](/us/pl/93/383/tVI/s626), formerly § 627, Aug. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 714](/us/stat/88/714); renumbered § 626, [Pub. L. 106–569, title VI, § 611(2)](/us/pl/106/569/tVI/s611/2), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 3012](/us/stat/114/3012).)*
