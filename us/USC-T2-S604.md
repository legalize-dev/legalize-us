# § 604. Omitted

##### § 604. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 94–440, title V, § 500](/us/pl/94/440/tV/s500), Oct. 1, 1976, [90 Stat. 1452](/us/stat/90/1452), the Legislative Appropriation Act, 1977, which authorized the Congressional Budget Office to contract without regard to section 5 of former Title 41, Public Contracts, applied to fiscal year 1977 and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts. Similar provisions were contained in the following prior appropriation act:

[Pub. L. 94–157, title I](/us/pl/94/157/tI), Dec. 18, 1975, [89 Stat. 834](/us/stat/89/834).
