# § 3596. Regulations

##### § 3596. Regulations

The Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe regulations to carry out the purpose of this subchapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 95–454, title IV, § 404(b)](/us/pl/95/454/tIV/s404/b), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1167](/us/stat/92/1167), § 3595; renumbered § 3596, [Pub. L. 97–35, title XVII, § 1704(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tXVII/s1704/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 756](/us/stat/95/756).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1981—[Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35) renumbered [section 3596 of this title](/us/usc/t5/s3596) as this section.
