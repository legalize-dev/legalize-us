# [§ 1293. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–189, § 3](/us/pl/87/189/s3), Aug. 30, 1961, [75 Stat. 417](/us/stat/75/417)]

##### [§ 1293. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–189, § 3](/us/pl/87/189/s3), Aug. 30, 1961, [75 Stat. 417](/us/stat/75/417)]

Section, acts [June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 929](/us/stat/62/929); Mar. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–3, § 14(b)](/us/pl/86/3/s14/b), [73 Stat. 10](/us/stat/73/10), provided for appeal from supreme court of Puerto Rico to court of appeals for first circuit. See [section 1258 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s1258).

A subsequent section 1293, added [Pub. L. 95–598, title II, § 236(a)](/us/pl/95/598/tII/s236/a), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2667](/us/stat/92/2667), which related to bankruptcy appeals, did not become effective pursuant to [section 402(b) of Pub. L. 95–598](/us/pl/95/598/s402/b), as amended, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 101 of Title 11](/us/usc/t11/s101), Bankruptcy.
