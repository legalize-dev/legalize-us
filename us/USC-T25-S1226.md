# § 1226. Omitted

##### § 1226. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–364, § 6](/us/pl/91/364/s6), July 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 689](/us/stat/84/689), which provided for shares not distributed within two years to revert to the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
