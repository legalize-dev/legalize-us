# §§ 215 to 220. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–597, § 19(c)](/us/pl/88/597/s19/c), Sept. 15, 1964, [78 Stat. 953](/us/stat/78/953)

##### §§ 215 to 220. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–597, § 19(c)](/us/pl/88/597/s19/c), Sept. 15, 1964, [78 Stat. 953](/us/stat/78/953)

Section 215, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1618, § 1](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1618/s1), [33 Stat. 316](/us/stat/33/316), related to apprehension and detention of certain insane persons in District of Columbia.

Section 216, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1618, § 2](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1618/s2), [33 Stat. 317](/us/stat/33/317), related to arrest at other than public places in District of Columbia.

Section 217, acts [Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1618, § 3](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1618/s3), [33 Stat. 317](/us/stat/33/317); [July 1, 1916, ch. 209](/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209), [39 Stat. 309](/us/stat/39/309), related to temporary detention of alleged insane persons found in District of Columbia.

Section 218, acts [Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1618, § 4](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1618/s4), [33 Stat. 317](/us/stat/33/317); [July 1, 1916, ch. 209](/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209), [39 Stat. 309](/us/stat/39/309), related to temporary commitment of persons found in District of Columbia to other hospital, or detention in police station.

Section 219, acts [Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1618, § 5](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1618/s5), [33 Stat. 318](/us/stat/33/318); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), related to certificate by physician as to sanity or insanity found in District of Columbia.

Section 220, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1618, § 6](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1618/s6), [33 Stat. 318](/us/stat/33/318), related to making false affidavit or certificate.
