# § 5714–1. Transferred

##### § 5714–1. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 5714–1 was editorially reclassified as [section 11221 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s11221), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Study of Housing Services and Strategies

[Pub. L. 108–96, title I, § 119](/us/pl/108/96/tI/s119), Oct. 10, 2003, [117 Stat. 1170](/us/stat/117/1170), provided that the Secretary of Health and Human Services was to conduct a study of programs funded under part B of the Runaway and Homeless Youth Act ([42 U.S.C. 5714–1](/us/usc/t42/s5714–1) et seq.) to report on long-term housing outcomes for youth after exiting the program.
