# § 4637. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–17, title IV, § 415](/us/pl/100/17/tIV/s415), Apr. 2, 1987, [101 Stat. 255](/us/stat/101/255)

##### § 4637. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–17, title IV, § 415](/us/pl/100/17/tIV/s415), Apr. 2, 1987, [101 Stat. 255](/us/stat/101/255)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–646, title II, § 217](/us/pl/91/646/tII/s217), Jan. 2, 1971, [84 Stat. 1902](/us/stat/84/1902), related to displacement by code enforcement, rehabilitation, and demolition programs receiving Federal assistance.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on effective date provided in regulations promulgated under [section 4633 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4633) (as amended by [section 412 of Pub. L. 100–17](/us/pl/100/17/s412)), but not later than 2 years after Apr. 2, 1987, see [section 418 of Pub. L. 100–17](/us/pl/100/17/s418), set out as an Effective Date of 1987 Amendment note under [section 4601 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4601).
