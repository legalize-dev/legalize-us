# §§ 1040 to 1045. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 1040 to 1045. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 1040, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 39](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s39), [50 Stat. 718](/us/stat/50/718), defined “debenture” and “debentures” as used in purchase, sale, or use as security of debentures issued by or for benefit of intermediate credit banks.

Section 1041, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 203(a)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s203/a), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1456](/us/stat/42/1456); amended Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 6(a)](/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164/s6/a), [49 Stat. 315](/us/stat/49/315); [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 27](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s27), [50 Stat. 715](/us/stat/50/715); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 104(f), [70 Stat. 664](/us/stat/70/664); Oct. 4, 1965. [Pub. L. 89–237, § 1(b)](/us/pl/89/237/s1/b), [79 Stat. 922](/us/stat/79/922); June 18, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–345, § 1(a)](/us/pl/90/345/s1/a), [82 Stat. 182](/us/stat/82/182), empowered intermediate credit banks to borrow money and issue and sell collateral trust debentures. See [section 2074 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2074).

Section 1042, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 203(b)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s203/b), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1456](/us/stat/42/1456); amended Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 28](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s28), [50 Stat. 715](/us/stat/50/715); Sept. 20, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–595](/us/pl/89/595), [80 Stat. 821](/us/stat/80/821), directed Farm Credit Administration to prescribe rules and regulations governing collateral and handling thereof and to fix interest rates.

Section 1043, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 203(c)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s203/c), as added [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title I, § 2, [42 Stat. 1456](/us/stat/42/1456); amended Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, prohibited assumption of liability on debentures or other obligations issued pursuant to former [section 1041 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1041) by the United States.

Section 1044, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 203(d)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s203/d), as added [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 6(b)](/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164/s6/b), [49 Stat. 315](/us/stat/49/315); amended [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 104(f), [70 Stat. 664](/us/stat/70/664), authorized intermediate credit banks to issue and sell consolidated debentures or other similar obligations.

Section 1045, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title II, § 203(e)](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tII/s203/e), as added [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 6(b)](/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164/s6/b), [49 Stat. 316](/us/stat/49/316); amended [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 104(f), [70 Stat. 664](/us/stat/70/664), covered investment of fiduciary and trust funds in debentures and other similar obligations of intermediate credit banks and security for public deposits.
