# §§ 3061 to 3068. Omitted

##### §§ 3061 to 3068. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 3061, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 4101](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s4101), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 237](/us/stat/102/237), provided that this part could be referred to as the “Jacob K. Javits Gifted and Talented Students Education Act of 1988”.

L. 95–561, title III, § 301(a)](/us/pl/95/561/tIII/s301/a), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2227](/us/stat/92/2227), set out short title for program of population education, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 587(a)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s587/a/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 480](/us/stat/95/480), eff. Oct. 1, 1982.
