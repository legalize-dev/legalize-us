# § 10233. Prohibition on land acquisition

##### § 10233. Prohibition on land acquisition

No funds under this chapter shall be used for land acquisition.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 814](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s814), formerly § 821, as added [Pub. L. 96–157, § 2](/us/pl/96/157/s2), Dec. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 1214](/us/stat/93/1214); renumbered § 814, [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609B(f)](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s609B/f), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2093](/us/stat/98/2093).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3789j of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3789j), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 814 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s814) was classified to [section 3789c of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3789c), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to repeal by [section 609B(e) of Pub. L. 98–473](/us/pl/98/473/s609B/e).
