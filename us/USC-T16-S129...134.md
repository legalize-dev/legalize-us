# §§ 129 to 134. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 129 to 134. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 129, acts [Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 368, § 6](/us/act/1916-08-21/ch368/s6), [39 Stat. 523](/us/stat/39/523); [June 25, 1935, ch. 309, § 1](/us/act/1935-06-25/ch309/s1), [49 Stat. 422](/us/stat/49/422); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213), related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges under section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 130, [act Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 368, § 7](/us/act/1916-08-21/ch368/s7), [39 Stat. 523](/us/stat/39/523), related to arrests by commissioner [now magistrate judge].

Section 131, [act Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 368, § 8](/us/act/1916-08-21/ch368/s8), [39 Stat. 523](/us/stat/39/523), related to issuance of process.

Section 132, acts [Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 368, § 9](/us/act/1916-08-21/ch368/s9), [39 Stat. 523](/us/stat/39/523); [June 25, 1935, ch. 309, § 2](/us/act/1935-06-25/ch309/s2), [49 Stat. 422](/us/stat/49/422), related to residence of commissioner [now magistrate judge].

Section 132a, [act June 25, 1935, ch. 309, § 3](/us/act/1935-06-25/ch309/s3), [49 Stat. 422](/us/stat/49/422), related to salary of commissioner [now magistrate judge].

Section 133, [act Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 368, § 11](/us/act/1916-08-21/ch368/s11), [39 Stat. 524](/us/stat/39/524), related to disposition of fines and costs.

Section 134, [act Aug. 21, 1916, ch. 368, § 10](/us/act/1916-08-21/ch368/s10), [39 Stat. 524](/us/stat/39/524), related to accounting for fees, costs, and expenses.
