# § 2497b. Jurisdiction

##### § 2497b. Jurisdiction

Legislation in the Senate of the United States amending section 2497 or 2497a of this title shall be referred to the Committee on Finance of the Senate.

*([Pub. L. 93–618, title IX, § 903](/us/pl/93/618/tIX/s903), as added [Pub. L. 110–234, title XV, § 15101(a)](/us/pl/110/234/tXV/s15101/a), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1499](/us/stat/122/1499), and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title XV, § 15101(a), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 2261.)*
