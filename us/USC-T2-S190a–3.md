# § 190a–3. Repealed. S. Res. 9, § 2, Nov. 5, 1975

##### § 190a–3. Repealed. S. Res. 9, § 2, Nov. 5, 1975

Section, [Pub. L. 93–344, title I, § 102(d)](/us/pl/93/344/tI/s102/d), July 12, 1974, [88 Stat. 301](/us/stat/88/301), provided that meetings of Senate Committee on the Budget or any subcommittee thereof be open to public except in certain specified instances.
