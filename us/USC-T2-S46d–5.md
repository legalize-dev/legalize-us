# § 46d–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–342](/us/pl/92/342), July 10, 1972, [86 Stat. 435](/us/stat/86/435)

##### § 46d–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–342](/us/pl/92/342), July 10, 1972, [86 Stat. 435](/us/stat/86/435)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–382](/us/pl/91/382), Aug. 18, 1970, [84 Stat. 810](/us/stat/84/810), related to reimbursement to Senators and President of Senate of official telephone and telegraph communications charges incurred by them or on their behalf out of contingent fund of Senate up to a maximum of $150 per annum.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 92–342](/us/pl/92/342) provided that the repeal is effective July 1, 1972.
