# §§ 1421 to 1426. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379)

##### §§ 1421 to 1426. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379)

Section 1421, [Pub. L. 89–563, title II, § 201](/us/pl/89/563/tII/s201), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 728](/us/stat/80/728), related to labeling for pneumatic tires and required contents of label.

Section 1422, [Pub. L. 89–563, title II, § 202](/us/pl/89/563/tII/s202), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 729](/us/stat/80/729), related to maximum permissible load standards for original equipment tires. See [section 30123 of Title 49](/us/usc/t49/s30123), Transportation.

Section 1423, [Pub. L. 89–563, title II, § 203](/us/pl/89/563/tII/s203), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 729](/us/stat/80/729), related to uniform quality grading system for motor vehicle tires and elimination of deceptive and confusing tire nomenclature. See [section 30123 of Title 49](/us/usc/t49/s30123).

Section 1424, [Pub. L. 89–563, title II, § 204](/us/pl/89/563/tII/s204), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 729](/us/stat/80/729); [Pub. L. 93–492, title I, § 110(c)](/us/pl/93/492/tI/s110/c), Oct. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1484](/us/stat/88/1484), related to regrooved tires. See sections 30123, 30163, and 30165 of Title 49.

Section 1425, [Pub. L. 89–563, title II, § 205](/us/pl/89/563/tII/s205), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 729](/us/stat/80/729), provided that, in event of conflict, orders and regulations issued by Secretary under this subchapter and subchapter I of this chapter applicable to motor vehicle tires were to prevail over orders and interpretations issued by Federal Trade Commission. See [section 30123 of Title 49](/us/usc/t49/s30123).

Section 1426, [Pub. L. 89–563, title II, § 206](/us/pl/89/563/tII/s206), as added [Pub. L. 91–265, § 6](/us/pl/91/265/s6), May 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 263](/us/stat/84/263), related to safety standards for retreaded tires.
