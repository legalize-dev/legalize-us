# § 4301. Continuation of Gallaudet College as Gallaudet University

##### § 4301. Continuation of Gallaudet College as Gallaudet University

(a) Gallaudet University The Gallaudet College created by an Act entitled “An Act to amend the charter of the Columbia Institution for the Deaf, change its name, define its corporate powers, and provide for its organization and administration, and for other purposes”, approved June 18, 1954, is continued as a body corporate under the name of Gallaudet University. On and after August 4, 1986, Gallaudet College shall be known as Gallaudet University and have perpetual succession and shall have the powers and be subject to the limitations contained in this chapter.

(b) Purpose The purpose of Gallaudet University shall be to provide education and training to individuals who are deaf and otherwise to further the education of individuals who are deaf.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

An Act to amend the charter of the Columbia Institution for the Deaf, change its name, define its corporate powers, and provide for its organization and administration, and for other purposes, referred to in subsec. (a), is [act June 18, 1954, ch. 324](/us/act/1954-06-18/ch324), [68 Stat. 265](/us/stat/68/265), which was classified generally to subchapter I (§ 691 et seq.) of chapter 20B of this title, and was repealed by [Pub. L. 99–371, title IV, § 410(a)](/us/pl/99/371/tIV/s410/a), Aug. 4, 1986, [100 Stat. 794](/us/stat/100/794).

This chapter, referred to in subsec. (a), was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 99–371](/us/pl/99/371), Aug. 4, 1986, [100 Stat. 781](/us/stat/100/781), known as the Education of the Deaf Act of 1986, which is classified generally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note below and Tables.

#### Prior Provisions

Provisions similar to this section were contained in sections 691 and 691a of this title prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 99–371](/us/pl/99/371).

#### Amendments

1993—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 103–73](/us/pl/103/73) inserted comma after “Hereafter” in original, which for purposes of codification had been translated as “On or after August 4, 1986,”, requiring no change in text.

1992—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 102–421](/us/pl/102/421) substituted “individuals who are deaf” for “deaf individuals” after “training to” and for “the deaf” after “education of”.
