# § 8733. Lease of land acquired for park, parkway, or playground purposes

##### § 8733. Lease of land acquired for park, parkway, or playground purposes

(1) subject to the approval of the National Capital Planning Commission;

(2) subject to the need for the immediate use of the land, building, or structure in other ways by the public; and

(3) on terms the Administrator decides.

*([Pub. L. 107–217](/us/pl/107/217), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1225](/us/stat/116/1225).)*

The words “Administrator of General Services” are substituted for “Director of Public Buildings and Public Parks of the National Capital” [subsequently changed to “Director of the National Park Service” because of section 2 of Executive Order No. 6166 (eff. June 10, 1933) and the Act of March 2, 1934 (ch. 38, [48 Stat. 389](/us/stat/48/389)), and “Public Buildings Administrator” because of sections 301 and 303 of Reorganization Plan No. I of 1939 (eff. July 1, 1939, [53 Stat. 1426](/us/stat/53/1426), 1427)] because of section 103(a) of the Federal Property and Administrative Services Act of 1949 (ch. 288, [63 Stat. 380](/us/stat/63/380)), which is restated as section 303(c) [303(b)] of the revised title. The words “National Capital Planning Commission” are substituted for “National Capital Park and Planning Commission” because of [section 9 of the Act of June 6, 1924](/us/act/1924-06-06/s9) (ch. 270), as added by [section 1 of the Act of July 19, 1952](/us/act/1952-07-19/s1) (ch. 949, [66 Stat. 790](/us/stat/66/790)). See section 8711(f) of the revised title.
