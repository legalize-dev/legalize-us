# § 1202. Omitted

##### § 1202. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–283, § 2](/us/pl/91/283/s2), June 19, 1970, [84 Stat. 313](/us/stat/84/313), which related to determination of per capita shares, reversion of denied shares to the tribe, and use of reverted funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
