# § 1503. Set-offs

##### § 1503. Set-offs

The United States Court of Federal Claims shall have jurisdiction to render judgment upon any set-off or demand by the United States against any plaintiff in such court.

*([June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 942](/us/stat/62/942); [Pub. L. 97–164, title I, § 133(e)(1)](/us/pl/97/164/tI/s133/e/1), Apr. 2, 1982, [96 Stat. 40](/us/stat/96/40); [Pub. L. 102–572, title IX, § 902(a)(1)](/us/pl/102/572/tIX/s902/a/1), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4516](/us/stat/106/4516).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 250(2) ([Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231, § 145](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231/s145), [36 Stat. 1136](/us/stat/36/1136)).

The second subsection of [section 250 of title 28](/us/usc/t28/s250), U.S.C., 1940 ed., is incorporated in this section. The proviso, relating to suits for fees due officers of the United States, has been incorporated in [section 2501 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s2501).

Changes were made in phraseology.
