# § 754. Use of existing resources

##### § 754. Use of existing resources

In establishing the national preparedness goal and national preparedness system, the Administrator shall use existing preparedness documents, planning tools, and guidelines to the extent practicable and consistent with this Act.

*([Pub. L. 109–295, title VI, § 654](/us/pl/109/295/tVI/s654), Oct. 4, 2006, [120 Stat. 1432](/us/stat/120/1432).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This Act, referred to in text, means title VI of [Pub. L. 109–295](/us/pl/109/295), Oct. 4, 2006, [120 Stat. 1394](/us/stat/120/1394), known as the Post-Katrina Emergency Management Reform Act of 2006. For complete classification of title VI to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 701 of this title](/us/usc/t6/s701) and Tables.
