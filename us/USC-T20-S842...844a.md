# §§ 842 to 844a. Omitted

##### §§ 842 to 844a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general revision of title III of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III](/us/pl/89/10/tIII), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 39](/us/stat/79/39), by [Pub. L. 95–561, title III, § 301](/us/pl/95/561/tIII/s301), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2210](/us/stat/92/2210).

Section 842, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 302](/us/pl/89/10/tIII/s302), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 40](/us/stat/79/40); [Pub. L. 89–750, title I, § 132](/us/pl/89/750/tI/s132), Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1201](/us/stat/80/1201); [Pub. L. 90–247, title I, § 131](/us/pl/90/247/tI/s131), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 789](/us/stat/81/789); [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 131(a)(1)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s131/a/1), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 131](/us/stat/84/131); [Pub. L. 92–318, title IV, § 421(b)(1)(B)](/us/pl/92/318/tIV/s421/b/1/B), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 341](/us/stat/86/341); [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 103(b)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s103/b), title VIII, § 843(a), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 502](/us/stat/88/502), 611, provided for the allotment of appropriated funds for supplementary educational centers and services.

Section 843, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 303](/us/pl/89/10/tIII/s303), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 40](/us/stat/79/40); [Pub. L. 89–750, title I, § 152(b)](/us/pl/89/750/tI/s152/b), Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1203](/us/stat/80/1203); [Pub. L. 90–247, title I, § 131](/us/pl/90/247/tI/s131), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 790](/us/stat/81/790); [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 131(a)(1)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s131/a/1), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 132](/us/stat/84/132); [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 509(a)(2)](/us/pl/92/318/tV/s509/a/2), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 353](/us/stat/86/353), related to the uses of Federal funds for supplementary educational centers and services.

Section 844, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 304](/us/pl/89/10/tIII/s304), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 41](/us/stat/79/41); [Pub. L. 89–750, title I](/us/pl/89/750/tI), §§ 133, 134, Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1201](/us/stat/80/1201), 1202; [Pub. L. 90–247, title I, § 131](/us/pl/90/247/tI/s131), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 791](/us/stat/81/791); [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 131(a)(1)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s131/a/1), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 133](/us/stat/84/133), related to the content and form of applications for grant funds for supplementary educational centers and services.

Section 844a, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 305](/us/pl/89/10/tIII/s305), as added [Pub. L. 90–247, title I, § 131](/us/pl/90/247/tI/s131), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 792](/us/stat/81/792); amended [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 131(a)(1)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s131/a/1), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 135](/us/stat/84/135); [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 103(c)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s103/c), title IV, § 402(c)(2)(i), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 502](/us/stat/88/502), 546; [Pub. L. 94–273, § 3(8)](/us/pl/94/273/s3/8), Apr. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 376](/us/stat/90/376); [Pub. L. 95–112, § 2(c)(2)](/us/pl/95/112/s2/c/2), Sept. 24, 1977, [91 Stat. 911](/us/stat/91/911), set out the requisites for State plans for supplementary educational centers and services.

A prior [section 305 of Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10/s305) was renumbered section 307 by [section 131 of Pub. L. 90–247](/us/pl/90/247/s131) and was set out as [section 845 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s845).
