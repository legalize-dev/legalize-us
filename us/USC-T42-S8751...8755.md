# §§ 8751 to 8755. Omitted

##### §§ 8751 to 8755. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 8751 to 8755 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under [section 8791 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s8791).

Section 8751, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 151](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s151), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 667](/us/stat/94/667), related to issue by Corporation of obligations purchasable by United States only.

Section 8752, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 152](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s152), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 668](/us/stat/94/668), related to limitations on total amount of obligational authority.

Section 8753, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 153](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s153), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 669](/us/stat/94/669), related to budgetary treatment.

Section 8754, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 154](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s154), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 669](/us/stat/94/669), related to receipts of Corporation.

Section 8755, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 155](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s155), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 669](/us/stat/94/669), related to tax status of Corporation.
