# § 17825. Repealed or Omitted

##### § 17825. Repealed or Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1109](/us/pl/110/422/tXI/s1109), Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4811](/us/stat/122/4811), which related to protection of scientific credibility, integrity, and communication within NASA, was repealed in part and omitted in part. Subsec. (c) was repealed and reenacted as [section 60506 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s60506), National and Commercial Space Programs, by [Pub. L. 111–314](/us/pl/111/314), §§ 3, 6, Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3328](/us/stat/124/3328), 3444, which Act enacted Title 51. Subsec. (a), which provided sense of Congress regarding NASA’s posture toward scientific research, and subsec. (b), which directed Comptroller General to initiate study within 60 days after Oct. 15, 2008, complete it within 270 days, and report to Congress, were omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 51.
