# § 1468a. Advertising

##### § 1468a. Advertising

No savings association shall carry on any sale, plan, or practices, or any advertising, in violation of regulations promulgated by a Federal banking agency.

*([June 13, 1933, ch. 64, § 12](/us/act/1933-06-13/ch64/s12), as added [Pub. L. 101–73, title III, § 301](/us/pl/101/73/tIII/s301), Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 343](/us/stat/103/343); amended [Pub. L. 111–203, title III, § 369(10)](/us/pl/111/203/tIII/s369/10), July 21, 2010, [124 Stat. 1565](/us/stat/124/1565).)*

#### Amendments
