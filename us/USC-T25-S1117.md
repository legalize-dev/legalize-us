# § 1117. Omitted

##### § 1117. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–659, § 7](/us/pl/89/659/s7), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 910](/us/stat/80/910), which provided for distribution of certain judgment funds to persons whose names appeared on the roll prepared pursuant to former [section 1114 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1114), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
