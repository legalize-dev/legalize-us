# § 21053. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 21053. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated for grants under this subpart $10,000,000 for fiscal year 2003.

(b) Availability of funds Amounts appropriated pursuant to the authorization under this section shall remain available, without fiscal year limitation, until expended.

*([Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 283](/us/pl/107/252/tII/s283), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1702](/us/stat/116/1702).)*

#### Codification
