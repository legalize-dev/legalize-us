# § 7923. Limitations on school-based health centers

##### § 7923. Limitations on school-based health centers

Notwithstanding [section 7802 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7802), funds used for activities under this chapter shall be carried out in accordance with the provision of [section 280h–5(a)(3)(C) of title 42](/us/usc/t42/s280h–5/a/3/C).

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 8543](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s8543), as added [Pub. L. 114–95, title VIII, § 8035](/us/pl/114/95/tVIII/s8035), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2119](/us/stat/129/2119).)*
