# §§ 147 to 147d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–131, § 3](/us/pl/88/131/s3), Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 194](/us/stat/77/194)

##### §§ 147 to 147d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–131, § 3](/us/pl/88/131/s3), Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 194](/us/stat/77/194)

Section 147, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495, § 6](/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495/s6), Pt. D, [65 Stat. 419](/us/stat/65/419), related to sound signals of vessels indicating course.

Section 147a, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495, § 6](/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495/s6), Pt. D, [65 Stat. 419](/us/stat/65/419), related to additional precautions.

Section 147b, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495, § 6](/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495/s6), Pt. D, [65 Stat. 420](/us/stat/65/420), related to local rules for harbors and inland waters.

Section 147c, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495, § 6](/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495/s6), Pt. D, [65 Stat. 420](/us/stat/65/420), related to distress signals.

Section 147d, [act Oct. 11, 1951, ch. 495, § 6](/us/act/1951-10-11/ch495/s6), Pt. D, [65 Stat. 420](/us/stat/65/420), related to orders to helmsmen.
