# §§ 3441 to 3445. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 336(f)(1)](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s336/f/1), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1007](/us/stat/110/1007)

##### §§ 3441 to 3445. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 336(f)(1)](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s336/f/1), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1007](/us/stat/110/1007)

Section 3441, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1521](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1521), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1336](/us/stat/95/1336), authorized formulation and implementation of reservoir sedimentation reduction program.

Section 3442, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1522](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1522), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1336](/us/stat/95/1336), related to preparation and contents of plan.

Section 3443, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1523](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1523), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1336](/us/stat/95/1336), related to approval of plans by Congress.

Section 3444, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1524](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1524), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1336](/us/stat/95/1336), authorized appropriations for carrying out provisions of subchapter IV.

Section 3445, [Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1525](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1525), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1337](/us/stat/95/1337), required report to Congress by Jan. 1, 1987, evaluating program.
