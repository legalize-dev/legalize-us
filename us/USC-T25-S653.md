# § 653. Omitted

##### § 653. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 18, 1928, ch. 624, § 3](/us/act/1928-05-18/ch624/s3), [45 Stat. 602](/us/stat/45/602), which related to settlement of claims notwithstanding statutes of limitation, amount of decree, and set-off of payments, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
