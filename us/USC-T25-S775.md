# § 775. Omitted

##### § 775. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1085, § 5](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1085/s5), [68 Stat. 980](/us/stat/68/980), which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to prescribe rules and regulations necessary to carry out the purposes of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
