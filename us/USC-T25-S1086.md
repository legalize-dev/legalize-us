# § 1086. Omitted

##### § 1086. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–655, § 6](/us/pl/89/655/s6), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 905](/us/stat/80/905), which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to advance or expend certain tribal funds of the Hoh tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
