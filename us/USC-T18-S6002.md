# § 6002. Immunity generally

##### § 6002. Immunity generally

(1) a court or grand jury of the United States,

(2) an agency of the United States, or

(3) either House of Congress, a joint committee of the two Houses, or a committee or a subcommittee of either House,

*(Added [Pub. L. 91–452, title II, § 201(a)](/us/pl/91/452/tII/s201/a), Oct. 15, 1970, [84 Stat. 927](/us/stat/84/927); amended [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXXIII, § 330013(4)](/us/pl/103/322/tXXXIII/s330013/4), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2146](/us/stat/108/2146).)*
