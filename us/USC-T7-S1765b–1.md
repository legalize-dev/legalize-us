##### § 1765b–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–501, title VI, § 602](/us/pl/95/501/tVI/s602), Oct. 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 1691](/us/stat/92/1691), which provided for an annual report on export promotion, was omitted as part of the complete revision of [Pub. L. 95–501](/us/pl/95/501) by [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1531](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1531), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3668](/us/stat/104/3668). See chapter 87 (§ 5601 et seq.) of this title.
