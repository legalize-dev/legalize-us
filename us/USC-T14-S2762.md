# § 2762. Shore patrol duty; payment of expenses

##### § 2762. Shore patrol duty; payment of expenses

An officer or cadet of the Coast Guard who is assigned shore patrol duty away from his vessel or other duty station may be paid his actual expenses.

*(Added [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 14(a)](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s14/a), [70A Stat. 624](/us/stat/70A/624), § 510; renumbered § 2762, [Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 116(b)](/us/pl/115/282/tI/s116/b), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4226](/us/stat/132/4226).)*
