# § 17708. Nonapplication

##### § 17708. Nonapplication

This chapter does not modify in any manner the Communications Act of 1934 ([47 U.S.C. 151](/us/usc/t47/s151) et seq.).

*([Pub. L. 107–217](/us/pl/107/217), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1288](/us/stat/116/1288).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Communications Act of 1934, referred to in text, is [act June 19, 1934, ch. 652](/us/act/1934-06-19/ch652), [48 Stat. 1064](/us/stat/48/1064), which is classified principally to chapter 5 (§ 151 et seq.) of Title 47, Telecommunications. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 609 of Title 47](/us/usc/t47/s609) and Tables.
