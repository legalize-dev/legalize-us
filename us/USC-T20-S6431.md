# § 6431. Eligibility

##### § 6431. Eligibility

(1) in institutions for neglected or delinquent children and youth;

(2) attending community day programs for neglected or delinquent children and youth; or

(3) in adult correctional institutions.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1411](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1411), as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title I, § 101](/us/pl/107/110/tI/s101), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1581](/us/stat/115/1581).)*
