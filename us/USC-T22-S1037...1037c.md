# §§ 1037 to 1037c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

##### §§ 1037 to 1037c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

Section 1037, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 691](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s691), as added Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–141, title IV, § 404(a)](/us/pl/94/141/tIV/s404/a), [89 Stat. 765](/us/stat/89/765), set forth Congressional statement of purposes with respect to grievances.

Section 1037a, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 692](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s692), as added Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–141, title IV, § 404(a)](/us/pl/94/141/tIV/s404/a), [89 Stat. 765](/us/stat/89/765); amended 1977 Reorg. Plan No. 2, §§ 5, 7(a)(4), 42 F.R. 62461, [91 Stat. 1636](/us/stat/91/1636), 1637, set forth provisions relating to consideration and resolution of grievances, promulgation of regulations for procedures, and applicable criteria.

Section 1037b, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 693](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s693), as added Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–141, title IV, § 404(a)](/us/pl/94/141/tIV/s404/a), [89 Stat. 770](/us/stat/89/770), related to filing of grievances and election of remedies.

Section 1037c, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 694](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s694), as added Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–141, title IV, § 404(a)](/us/pl/94/141/tIV/s404/a), [89 Stat. 770](/us/stat/89/770), related to judicial review of regulations and final actions.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).
