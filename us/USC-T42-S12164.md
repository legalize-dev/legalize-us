# § 12164. Regulations

##### § 12164. Regulations

Not later than 1 year after July 26, 1990, the Secretary of Transportation shall issue regulations, in an accessible format, necessary for carrying out this subpart.

*([Pub. L. 101–336, title II, § 244](/us/pl/101/336/tII/s244), July 26, 1990, [104 Stat. 352](/us/stat/104/352).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective July 26, 1990, see [section 246(b) of Pub. L. 101–336](/us/pl/101/336/s246/b), set out as a note under [section 12161 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12161).
