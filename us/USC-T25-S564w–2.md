##### § 564w–2. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 29](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s29), as added [Pub. L. 93–102](/us/pl/93/102), Aug. 16, 1973, [87 Stat. 349](/us/stat/87/349), which related to Federal acquisition of tribal land, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
