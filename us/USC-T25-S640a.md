# § 640a. Omitted

##### § 640a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–189, § 2](/us/pl/92/189/s2), Dec. 15, 1971, [85 Stat. 646](/us/stat/85/646); [Pub. L. 110–315, title IX, § 946(a)](/us/pl/110/315/tIX/s946/a), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3468](/us/stat/122/3468), which related to purpose of former sections 640a to 640c–3 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of 2008 Amendment

[Pub. L. 110–315, title IX, § 945](/us/pl/110/315/tIX/s945), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3468](/us/stat/122/3468), provided that subpart 2 (§§ 945, 946) of part E of title IX of [Pub. L. 110–315](/us/pl/110/315) [see Tables for classification] could be cited as the “Navajo Nation Higher Education Act of 2008”.

#### Short Title of 1978 Amendment

[Pub. L. 95–471, title II, § 201](/us/pl/95/471/tII/s201), Oct. 17, 1978, [92 Stat. 1329](/us/stat/92/1329), provided that title II of [Pub. L. 95–471](/us/pl/95/471) [see Tables for classification] could be cited as the “Navajo Community College Assistance Act of 1978”.

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 92–189, § 1](/us/pl/92/189/s1), Dec. 15, 1971, [85 Stat. 646](/us/stat/85/646), provided that [Pub. L. 92–189](/us/pl/92/189), enacting sections 640a to 640c–3 of this title, could be cited as the “Navajo Community College Act”.
