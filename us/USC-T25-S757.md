# § 757. Omitted

##### § 757. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 17](/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1207/s17), [68 Stat. 1103](/us/stat/68/1103), which related to proclamation declaring termination of Federal trust upon removal of Federal restrictions on the property of each tribe and its individual members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
