# §§ 221 to 228. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### §§ 221 to 228. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section 221, [act June 25, 1936, ch. 815, § 1](/us/act/1936-06-25/ch815/s1), [49 Stat. 1928](/us/stat/49/1928), related to citation of this chapter.

Section 222, [act June 25, 1936, ch. 815, § 2](/us/act/1936-06-25/ch815/s2), [49 Stat. 1928](/us/stat/49/1928), related to definitions. See sections 1262 and 3615 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 223, [act June 25, 1936, ch. 815, § 3](/us/act/1936-06-25/ch815/s3), [49 Stat. 1928](/us/stat/49/1928), related to transportation of liquor into States where sale is prohibited. See [section 1262 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1262).

Section 224, [act June 25, 1936, ch. 815, § 4](/us/act/1936-06-25/ch815/s4), [49 Stat. 1928](/us/stat/49/1928), related to searches and seizures. See [section 3615 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3615).

Section 225, [act June 25, 1936, ch. 815, § 5](/us/act/1936-06-25/ch815/s5), [49 Stat. 1929](/us/stat/49/1929), related to enforcement of this chapter. See [section 1261 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1261).

Section 226, [act June 25, 1936, ch. 815, § 10](/us/act/1936-06-25/ch815/s10), [49 Stat. 1929](/us/stat/49/1929), related to effect of this chapter on other laws.

Section 227, [act June 25, 1936, ch. 815, § 11](/us/act/1936-06-25/ch815/s11), [49 Stat. 1930](/us/stat/49/1930), related to separability provisions of this chapter.

Section 228, [act June 25, 1936, ch. 815, § 12](/us/act/1936-06-25/ch815/s12), [49 Stat. 1930](/us/stat/49/1930), related to effective date of this chapter.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal of sections 221 to 228 effective Sept. 1, 1948, see [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 20](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s20), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862).
