# § 4952. Authority to operate VISTA program

##### § 4952. Authority to operate VISTA program

This part shall be administered by one of the Assistant Directors appointed pursuant to [section 12651e(d)(1)(A) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12651e/d/1/A). Such Director may recruit, select, and train persons to serve in full-time volunteer programs consistent with the provisions and to carry out the purpose of this part.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1993—[Pub. L. 103–82](/us/pl/103/82) substituted “This part shall be administered by one of the Assistant Directors appointed pursuant to [section 12651e(d)(1)(A) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12651e/d/1/A). Such Director” for “The Director”.

1989—[Pub. L. 101–204](/us/pl/101/204) struck out subsec. (a) designation before “The Director may”, struck out subsec. (b) which related to replacement of applicants who become unavailable for service, and struck out subsec. (c) which related to age quotas.

1984—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 98–288](/us/pl/98/288) designated existing provisions as subsec. (a) and added subsecs. (b) and (c).
