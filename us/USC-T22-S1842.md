# § 1842. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–141, § 4(b)](/us/pl/85/141/s4/b), Aug. 14, 1957, [71 Stat. 356](/us/stat/71/356)

##### § 1842. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–141, § 4(b)](/us/pl/85/141/s4/b), Aug. 14, 1957, [71 Stat. 356](/us/stat/71/356)

Section, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937, title I, § 132](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937/tI/s132), [68 Stat. 838](/us/stat/68/838), authorized appropriations for defense support, relief and rehabilitation, and other necessary assistance for those parts of Korea which the President determined to be not under Communist control.
