# § 645. Cost

##### § 645. Cost

(a) USF The Commission may not use funds from the universal service programs of the Commission established under [section 254 of this title](/us/usc/t47/s254), and the regulations issued under that section, to pay for any costs associated with this subchapter.

(b) Other funds The Commission may recover costs associated with this subchapter under [section 159 of this title](/us/usc/t47/s159) to the extent provided for in an appropriation Act, as required under subsection (a) of that section.

*([June 19, 1934, ch. 652](/us/act/1934-06-19/ch652), title VIII, § 805, as added [Pub. L. 116–130, § 2](/us/pl/116/130/s2), Mar. 23, 2020, [134 Stat. 238](/us/stat/134/238).)*
