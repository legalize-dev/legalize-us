##### §§ 721a to 724. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20), [63 Stat. 561](/us/stat/63/561)

Section 721a, acts [Aug. 16, 1937, ch. 665, § 2](/us/act/1937-08-16/ch665/s2), [50 Stat. 667](/us/stat/50/667); [July 11, 1941, ch. 290, § 2](/us/act/1941-07-11/ch290/s2), [55 Stat. 585](/us/stat/55/585), related to deposit of damage payments and disbursement. See [section 546 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s546), Coast Guard.

Section 722, acts [July 7, 1884, ch. 332](/us/act/1884-07-07/ch332), [23 Stat. 198](/us/stat/23/198); [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 6](/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s6), [36 Stat. 538](/us/stat/36/538), related to necessity for contract for expenditure of appropriations.

Section 724, acts [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 8](/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s8), [36 Stat. 538](/us/stat/36/538); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1), [37 Stat. 736](/us/stat/37/736), related to contracts for materials and necessity for public letting.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President [Aug. 4, 1949], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
