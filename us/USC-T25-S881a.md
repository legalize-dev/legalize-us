# § 881a. Omitted

##### § 881a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–401, § 2](/us/pl/91/401/s2), Sept. 16, 1970, [84 Stat. 838](/us/stat/84/838), which related to payments to minor members of the Potawatomi Indians and members under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
