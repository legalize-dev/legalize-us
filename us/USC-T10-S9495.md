# § 9495. Funds appropriated for the Civil Air Patrol

##### § 9495. Funds appropriated for the Civil Air Patrol

Funds appropriated for the Civil Air Patrol shall be available only for the exclusive use of the Civil Air Patrol.

*(Added [Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [[div. A]](/us/pl/106/398/s1/dA), title X, § 1090(a)], Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1654](/us/stat/114/1654), 1654A–297, § 9445; renumbered § 9495, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 806(a)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s806/a/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1832](/us/stat/132/1832).)*
