# § 1755. Omitted

##### § 1755. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–134, § 6](/us/pl/98/134/s6), Oct. 18, 1983, [97 Stat. 855](/us/stat/97/855), which related to State jurisdiction over the reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
