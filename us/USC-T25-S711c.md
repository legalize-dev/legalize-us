##### § 711c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–195, § 5](/us/pl/95/195/s5), Nov. 18, 1977, [91 Stat. 1416](/us/stat/91/1416); [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)](/us/pl/96/88/tV/s509/b), Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695](/us/stat/93/695), which related to Interim Council to be the acting tribal governing body until tribal officials would be elected, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
