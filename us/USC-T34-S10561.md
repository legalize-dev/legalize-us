# § 10561. Grant authorization

##### § 10561. Grant authorization

The Attorney General shall award grants to States and units of local government in accordance with this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2801](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2801), as added [Pub. L. 106–561, § 2(c)(1)](/us/pl/106/561/s2/c/1), Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2788](/us/stat/114/2788); amended [Pub. L. 107–273, div. B, title V, § 5001(b)(1)](/us/pl/107/273/dB/tV/s5001/b/1), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1813](/us/stat/116/1813).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3797j of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3797j), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2002—[Pub. L. 107–273](/us/pl/107/273) inserted “and units of local government” after “States”.
