# § 7274r. Transferred

##### § 7274r. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 108–7, div. D, title III, § 308](/us/pl/108/7/dD/tIII/s308), Feb. 20, 2003, [117 Stat. 154](/us/stat/117/154), which related to research, development, and demonstration activities with respect to engineering and manufacturing capabilities at covered nuclear weapons production plants, was transferred and is listed in a similar provisions note under the heading Activities at Covered Nuclear Weapons Facilities under former [section 2812 of Title 50](/us/usc/t50/s2812), War and National Defense.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Engineering and Manufacturing Research, Development, and Demonstration by Plant Managers of Certain Nuclear Weapons Production Plants

[Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [div. C, title XXXI, § 3156]](/us/pl/106/398/s1/dC/tXXXI/s3156), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1654](/us/stat/114/1654), 1654A–467, which was formerly set out as a note under this section, was renumbered [section 4832 of Pub. L. 107–314](/us/pl/107/314/s4832) by [Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(k)(12)](/us/pl/108/136/dC/tXXXI/s3141/k/12), Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1785](/us/stat/117/1785), and classified to [section 2812 of Title 50](/us/usc/t50/s2812), War and National Defense, and was repealed and restated as [section 6352 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s6352), Armed Forces, by [Pub. L. 119–60, div. C, title XXXI, § 3111(a)](/us/pl/119/60/dC/tXXXI/s3111/a), (b)(1), Dec. 18, 2025, [139 Stat. 1454](/us/stat/139/1454), 1458.
