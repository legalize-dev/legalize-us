# §§ 616nn to 616rr. Omitted

##### §§ 616nn to 616rr. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 616nn, [Pub. L. 88–583, § 1](/us/pl/88/583/s1), Sept. 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 925](/us/stat/78/925), authorized construction, operation, and maintenance of Lower Teton Division of Teton Basin project.

Section 616*oo*, [Pub. L. 88–583, § 2](/us/pl/88/583/s2), Sept. 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 925](/us/stat/78/925), related to extension of period of repayment of construction costs.

Section 616pp, [Pub. L. 88–583, § 3](/us/pl/88/583/s3), Sept. 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 925](/us/stat/78/925), authorized construction, operation, and maintenance of public recreation facilities in connection with this project.

Section 616qq, [Pub. L. 88–583, § 4](/us/pl/88/583/s4), Sept. 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 926](/us/stat/78/926); [Pub. L. 96–470, § 108(d)](/us/pl/96/470/s108/d), Oct. 19, 1980, [94 Stat. 2239](/us/stat/94/2239), related to water users contracts and conditions to be met prior to construction of facilities.

Section 616rr, [Pub. L. 88–583, § 5](/us/pl/88/583/s5), Sept. 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 926](/us/stat/78/926), authorized appropriations for this division.
