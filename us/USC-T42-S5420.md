# § 5420. Failure to report violations; penalties

##### § 5420. Failure to report violations; penalties

Any person, other than an officer or employee of the United States, or a person exercising inspection functions under a State plan pursuant to [section 5422 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5422), who knowingly and willfully fails to report a violation of any construction or safety standard established under [section 5403 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5403) may be fined up to $1,000 or imprisoned for up to one year, or both.

*([Pub. L. 93–383, title VI, § 621](/us/pl/93/383/tVI/s621), Aug. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 712](/us/stat/88/712).)*
