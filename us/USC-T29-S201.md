# § 201. Short title

##### § 201. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “Fair Labor Standards Act of 1938”.

*([June 25, 1938, ch. 676, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch676/s1), [52 Stat. 1060](/us/stat/52/1060).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**
