# §§ 2641 to 2643. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 367](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s367), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3976](/us/stat/108/3976)

##### §§ 2641 to 2643. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 367](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s367), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3976](/us/stat/108/3976)

Section 2641, [Pub. L. 100–297, title V, § 5341](/us/pl/100/297/tV/s5341), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 411](/us/stat/102/411); [Pub. L. 100–427, § 21](/us/pl/100/427/s21), Sept. 9, 1988, [102 Stat. 1612](/us/stat/102/1612), related to establishment of Office of Indian Education within Department of Education. See [section 3423c of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s3423c), Education.

Section 2642, [Pub. L. 100–297, title V, § 5342](/us/pl/100/297/tV/s5342), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 412](/us/stat/102/412); [Pub. L. 100–427, § 22](/us/pl/100/427/s22), Sept. 9, 1988, [102 Stat. 1613](/us/stat/102/1613), established National Advisory Council on Indian Education.

Section 2643, [Pub. L. 100–297, title V, § 5343](/us/pl/100/297/tV/s5343), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 413](/us/stat/102/413), authorized appropriations for administration of Indian education programs. See [section 7882 of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s7882), Education.
