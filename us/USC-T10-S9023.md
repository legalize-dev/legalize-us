# § 9023. Chief of Legislative Liaison

##### § 9023. Chief of Legislative Liaison

(a) There is a Chief of Legislative Liaison in the Department of the Air Force.

(b) The Chief of Legislative Liaison shall perform legislative affairs functions as specified for the Office of the Secretary of the Air Force by [section 9014(c)(1)(F) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s9014/c/1/F).
