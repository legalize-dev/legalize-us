# § 21101. Adoption of voluntary guidance by Commission

##### § 21101. Adoption of voluntary guidance by Commission

(a) In general To assist States in meeting the requirements of part A of this subchapter, the Commission shall adopt voluntary guidance consistent with such requirements in accordance with the procedures described in [section 21102 of this title](/us/usc/t52/s21102).

(b) Deadlines The Commission shall adopt the recommendations under this section not later than—

(1) in the case of the recommendations with respect to [section 21081 of this title](/us/usc/t52/s21081), January 1, 2004;

(2) in the case of the recommendations with respect to [section 21082 of this title](/us/usc/t52/s21082), October 1, 2003; and

(3) in the case of the recommendations with respect to [section 21083 of this title](/us/usc/t52/s21083), October 1, 2003.

(c) Quadrennial update The Commission shall review and update recommendations adopted with respect to [section 21081 of this title](/us/usc/t52/s21081) no less frequently than once every 4 years.

*([Pub. L. 107–252, title III, § 311](/us/pl/107/252/tIII/s311), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1715](/us/stat/116/1715).)*
