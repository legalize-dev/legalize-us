# § 96. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–316, title I, § 112(a)](/us/pl/104/316/tI/s112/a), Oct. 19, 1996, [110 Stat. 3833](/us/stat/110/3833)

##### § 96. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–316, title I, § 112(a)](/us/pl/104/316/tI/s112/a), Oct. 19, 1996, [110 Stat. 3833](/us/stat/110/3833)

Section, acts [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 132, § 7](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch132/s7), [18 Stat. 450](/us/stat/18/450); [July 31, 1894, ch. 174](/us/act/1894-07-31/ch174), §§ 3, 7, [28 Stat. 205](/us/stat/28/205), 206; [June 10, 1921, ch. 18](/us/act/1921-06-10/ch18), title III, § 304, [42 Stat. 24](/us/stat/42/24), directed that copies of contracts made by Commissioner of Indian Affairs, or any other officer of Government for the Indian Service, be furnished to General Accounting Office prior to payment.
