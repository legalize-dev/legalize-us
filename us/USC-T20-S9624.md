# § 9624. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 9624. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated—

(1) for fiscal year 2003—

(A) $4,600,000 to carry out [section 9621 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s9621); and

(B) $107,500,000 to carry out [section 9622 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s9622); and

(2) such sums as may be necessary for each of the 5 succeeding fiscal years to carry out sections 9621 and 9622 of this title.

(b) Availability Amounts made available under this section shall remain available until expended.
