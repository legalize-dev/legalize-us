# § 103. Boundary Line

##### § 103. Boundary Line

In this title, the term “Boundary Line” means a line established under [section 2(b) of the Act of February 19, 1895](/us/act/1895-02-19/s2/b) ([33 U.S.C. 151(b)](/us/usc/t33/s151/b)).

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 4](/us/pl/109/304/s4), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1486](/us/stat/120/1486); [Pub. L. 114–120, title III, § 306(a)(1)](/us/pl/114/120/tIII/s306/a/1), Feb. 8, 2016, [130 Stat. 54](/us/stat/130/54).)*
