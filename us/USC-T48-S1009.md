# § 1009. Repealed. [Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 655, § 56(d)](/us/act/1951-10-31/ch655/s56/d), [65 Stat. 729](/us/stat/65/729)

##### § 1009. Repealed. [Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 655, § 56(d)](/us/act/1951-10-31/ch655/s56/d), [65 Stat. 729](/us/stat/65/729)

Section, [act Mar. 8, 1902, ch. 140, § 9](/us/act/1902-03-08/ch140/s9), [32 Stat. 55](/us/stat/32/55), related to requirements as to evidence in treason cases in Philippines.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Savings Provision

[Act Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 655, § 56](/us/act/1951-10-31/ch655/s56)(*l*), [65 Stat. 730](/us/stat/65/730), provided that the repeal of this section should not affect any rights or liabilities existing hereunder on the effective date of that repeal (Oct. 31, 1951).
