# § 6422. Payments for programs under this part

##### § 6422. Payments for programs under this part

(a) Agency subgrants Based on the allocation amount computed under [section 6432 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6432), the Secretary shall allocate to each State educational agency an amount necessary to make subgrants to State agencies under subpart 1 of this part.

(b) Local subgrants Each State shall retain, for the purpose of carrying out subpart 2 of this part, funds generated throughout the State under part A of this subchapter based on children and youth residing in local correctional facilities, or attending community day programs for delinquent children and youth.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1402](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1402), as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title I, § 101](/us/pl/107/110/tI/s101), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1580](/us/stat/115/1580).)*
