# § 9547. Termination

##### § 9547. Termination

The provisions of this subchapter shall terminate on the date that is 5 years after August 2, 2017.

*([Pub. L. 115–44, title II, § 258](/us/pl/115/44/tII/s258), Aug. 2, 2017, [131 Stat. 934](/us/stat/131/934).)*
