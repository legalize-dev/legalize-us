# § 2209. Subsidies

##### § 2209. Subsidies

No funds of the Commission shall be employed in the construction or operation of facilities licensed under section 2133 or 2134 of this title except under contract or other arrangement entered into pursuant to [section 2051 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2051).
