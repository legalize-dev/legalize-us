# § 5352. Taxpaid wine bottling house

##### § 5352. Taxpaid wine bottling house

Any person bottling, packaging, or repackaging taxpaid wines shall, before commencing such operations, make application to the Secretary and receive permission to operate. Such premises shall be known as “tax-paid wine bottling houses.”

*(Added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201](/us/pl/85/859/tII/s201), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1378](/us/stat/72/1378); amended [Pub. #### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 5352, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 663](/us/stat/68A/663), consisted of provisions similar to those comprising this section, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859](/us/pl/85/859).

#### Amendments

1979—[Pub. L. 96–39](/us/pl/96/39) struck out “at premises other than the bottling premises of a distilled spirits plant” after “taxpaid wines”.

1976—[Pub. L. 94–455](/us/pl/94/455) struck out “or his delegate” after “Secretary” wherever appearing.
