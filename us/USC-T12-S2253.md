# § 2253. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title V, § 5411(30)](/us/pl/115/334/tV/s5411/30), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4682](/us/stat/132/4682)

##### § 2253. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title V, § 5411(30)](/us/pl/115/334/tV/s5411/30), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4682](/us/stat/132/4682)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).18, as added [Pub. L. 99–205, title II, § 202(b)](/us/pl/99/205/tII/s202/b), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1693](/us/stat/99/1693), related to continued effectiveness of certain delegations made by the Farm Credit Administration through twelve months after Dec. 23, 1985.

A prior section 2253, [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).19, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 622](/us/stat/85/622), related to delegation of duties and powers to financial institutions, prior to repeal, effective thirty days after Dec. 23, 1985, by [Pub. L. 99–205, title II, § 202(a)](/us/pl/99/205/tII/s202/a), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1693](/us/stat/99/1693).

A prior section 5.18 of [Pub. L. 92–181](/us/pl/92/181) was renumbered section 5.17 and is classified to [section 2252 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2252).
