# § 4103. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–325, title V, § 551(a)](/us/pl/103/325/tV/s551/a), Sept. 23, 1994, [108 Stat. 2269](/us/stat/108/2269)

##### § 4103. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–325, title V, § 551(a)](/us/pl/103/325/tV/s551/a), Sept. 23, 1994, [108 Stat. 2269](/us/stat/108/2269)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–448, title XIII, § 1362](/us/pl/90/448/tXIII/s1362), Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 588](/us/stat/82/588); [Pub. L. 95–128, title VII, § 704(b)](/us/pl/95/128/tVII/s704/b), Oct. 12, 1977, [91 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/91/1145); [Pub. L. 98–181, title I](/us/pl/98/181/tI) [title IV, § 451(d)(1)], Nov. 30, 1983, [97 Stat. 1229](/us/stat/97/1229), related to purchase, by Director, of insured properties damaged substantially beyond repair by flood, for subsequent transfer to State or local agency upon negotiated conditions.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Savings Provision

[Pub. L. 103–325, title V, § 551(c)](/us/pl/103/325/tV/s551/c), Sept. 23, 1994, [108 Stat. 2269](/us/stat/108/2269), required the Director of the Federal Emergency Management Agency to comply with any purchase or loan commitment entered into before the expiration of the 1-year period beginning on Sept. 23, 1994, pursuant to authority under this section or [section 551(b) of Pub. L. 103–325](/us/pl/103/325/s551/b).

#### Transition Phase

[Pub. L. 103–325, title V, § 551(b)](/us/pl/103/325/tV/s551/b), Sept. 23, 1994, [108 Stat. 2269](/us/stat/108/2269), permitted the Director of the Federal Emergency Management Agency to enter into loan and purchase commitments as provided under this section (as in effect immediately before the enactment of [Pub. L. 103–325](/us/pl/103/325)) during the 1-year period beginning on Sept. 23, 1994.
