# [§ 6312. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–5, title I, § 4(a)(2)](/us/pl/92/5/tI/s4/a/2), Mar. 17, 1971, [85 Stat. 5](/us/stat/85/5)]

##### [§ 6312. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–5, title I, § 4(a)(2)](/us/pl/92/5/tI/s4/a/2), Mar. 17, 1971, [85 Stat. 5](/us/stat/85/5)]

Section, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 777](/us/stat/68A/777), permitted the Secretary to receive Treasury bills, notes and certificates of indebtedness issued by the United States in payment of any internal revenue taxes or stamps.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 92–5, title I, § 4(a)](/us/pl/92/5/tI/s4/a), Mar. 17, 1971, [85 Stat. 5](/us/stat/85/5), provided that the repeal of this section is effective with respect to obligations issued after Mar. 3, 1971.

#### Repeals

[Pub. L. 92–5, title I, § 4(a)(2)](/us/pl/92/5/tI/s4/a/2), Mar. 17, 1971, [85 Stat. 5](/us/stat/85/5), which repealed this section and provided for the effective date of that repeal, was itself repealed by [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068), 1081.
