# § 81q. Transfer of grant

##### § 81q. Transfer of grant

The grant shall not be sold, conveyed, transferred, set over, or assigned.

*([June 18, 1934, ch. 590, § 17](/us/act/1934-06-18/ch590/s17), [48 Stat. 1002](/us/stat/48/1002).)*
