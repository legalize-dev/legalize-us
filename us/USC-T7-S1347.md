# § 1347. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–88, § 2](/us/pl/98/88/s2), Aug. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 494](/us/stat/97/494)

##### § 1347. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–88, § 2](/us/pl/98/88/s2), Aug. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 494](/us/stat/97/494)

Section, acts [Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30), title III, § 347, [52 Stat. 59](/us/stat/52/59); [Aug. 29, 1949, ch. 518, § 1](/us/act/1949-08-29/ch518/s1), [63 Stat. 675](/us/stat/63/675); [July 17, 1952, ch. 933, § 4](/us/act/1952-07-17/ch933/s4), [66 Stat. 759](/us/stat/66/759); Aug. 28, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–835, title I](/us/pl/85/835/tI), § 103(3), [72 Stat. 990](/us/stat/72/990); Sept. 21, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–341, title II, § 203](/us/pl/86/341/tII/s203), [73 Stat. 611](/us/stat/73/611); June 30, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–566](/us/pl/86/566), [74 Stat. 295](/us/stat/74/295); Aug. 11, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–475](/us/pl/90/475), §§ 4, 6, [82 Stat. 701](/us/stat/82/701), 702, set out a program for long staple cotton. See [section 1444(h) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1444/h).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 98–88, § 2](/us/pl/98/88/s2), Aug. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 494](/us/stat/97/494), provided that the repeal of this section is effective beginning with the 1984 crop of extra long staple cotton.
