# § 6849. Prohibition

##### § 6849. Prohibition

In carrying out this part, the Secretary shall neither mandate nor preclude the use of a particular curricular or pedagogical approach to educating English learners.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 3128](/us/pl/89/10/tIII/s3128), formerly § 3129, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title III, § 301](/us/pl/107/110/tIII/s301), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1706](/us/stat/115/1706); renumbered § 3128 and amended [Pub. L. 114–95, title III](/us/pl/114/95/tIII), §§ 3001(2)(B), 3003(*l*), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1953](/us/stat/129/1953), 1964.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 3128 of Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10/s3128) was renumbered section 3127 and is classified to [section 6848 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6848).

#### Amendments

2015—[Pub. L. 114–95, § 3003](/us/pl/114/95/s3003)(*l*), substituted “English learners” for “limited English proficient children”.

#### Effective Date of 2015 Amendment
