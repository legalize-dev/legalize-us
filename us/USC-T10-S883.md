# § 883. Art. 83. Malingering

##### § 883. Art. 83. Malingering

(1) feigns illness, physical disablement, mental lapse, or mental derangement; or

(2) intentionally inflicts self-injury;

*(Added [Pub. L. 114–328, div. E, title LX, § 5404](/us/pl/114/328/dE/tLX/s5404), Dec. 23, 2016, [130 Stat. 2940](/us/stat/130/2940).)*
