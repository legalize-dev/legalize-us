##### § 242q–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–482, title I, § 104(b)(3)(B)](/us/pl/109/482/tI/s104/b/3/B), Jan. 15, 2007, [120 Stat. 3694](/us/stat/120/3694)

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable only with respect to amounts appropriated for fiscal year 2007 or subsequent fiscal years, see [section 109 of Pub. L. 109–482](/us/pl/109/482/s109), set out as an Effective Date of 2007 Amendment note under [section 281 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s281).
