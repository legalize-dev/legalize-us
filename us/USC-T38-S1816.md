# § 1816. Regulations

##### § 1816. Regulations

The Secretary shall prescribe regulations for purposes of the administration of this subchapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 106–419, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/106/419/tIV/s401/a), Nov. 1, 2000, [114 Stat. 1859](/us/stat/114/1859).)*
