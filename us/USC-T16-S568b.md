# § 568b. Repealed. [Aug. 25, 1950, ch. 781, § 3](/us/act/1950-08-25/ch781/s3), [64 Stat. 473](/us/stat/64/473), eff. June 30, 1951

##### § 568b. Repealed. [Aug. 25, 1950, ch. 781, § 3](/us/act/1950-08-25/ch781/s3), [64 Stat. 473](/us/stat/64/473), eff. June 30, 1951

Section, [act May 18, 1937, ch. 226](/us/act/1937-05-18/ch226), [50 Stat. 188](/us/stat/50/188), related to cooperation by Secretary of Agriculture in development of farm forestry in States and Territories. This section was known as the “Cooperative Farm Forestry Act”.
