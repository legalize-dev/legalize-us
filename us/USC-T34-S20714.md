# § 20714. Training of tribal law enforcement and prosecutorial personnel

##### § 20714. Training of tribal law enforcement and prosecutorial personnel

The Attorney General, in consultation with the Director of the Office of Tribal Justice, shall carry out a program under which tribal law enforcement officials may receive technical assistance and training to pursue a victim-centered approach to investigating and prosecuting severe forms of trafficking in persons (as defined in [section 7102 of title 22](/us/usc/t22/s7102)).
