# § 7541. Short title

##### § 7541. Short title

This part may be cited as the “Alaska Native Educational Equity, Support, and Assistance Act”.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6301](/us/pl/89/10/tVI/s6301), formerly title VII, § 7301, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/107/110/tVII/s701), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1942](/us/stat/115/1942); renumbered title VI, § 6301, [Pub. L. 114–95, title VI, § 6001(a)](/us/pl/114/95/tVI/s6001/a), (b)(1), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2046](/us/stat/129/2046).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

Provisions naming former part C (§ 7931 et seq.) of subchapter IX of this chapter as the “Alaska Native Educational Equity, Support and Assistance Act” were contained in [section 7931 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7931), prior to the general amendment of former subchapter IX of this chapter by [Pub. L. 107–110](/us/pl/107/110).

A prior section 7541, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7301](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7301), as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3739](/us/stat/108/3739), set forth findings and purpose of emergency immigrant education program, prior to the general amendment of former subchapter VII of this chapter by [Pub. L. 107–110](/us/pl/107/110).

A prior [section 6301 of Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10/s6301) was classified to [section 7371 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7371) prior to the general amendment of part C of subchapter V of this chapter by [Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95).

Another prior [section 6301 of Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10/s6301) was classified to [section 7351 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7351), prior to the general amendment of former subchapter VI of this chapter by [Pub. L. 107–110](/us/pl/107/110).
