# § 640d–17. Omitted

##### § 640d–17. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 18](/us/pl/93/531/s18), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1721](/us/stat/88/1721), which related to actions for accounting, fair value of grazing, and claims for damages to land, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
