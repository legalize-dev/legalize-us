# §§ 551 to 556. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### §§ 551 to 556. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 551, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 108](/us/stat/64/108), defined terms used in this chapter. See sections 101 and 801 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 552, acts [May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 109](/us/stat/64/109); [Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 852, § 23](/us/act/1956-08-01/ch852/s23), [70 Stat. 911](/us/stat/70/911), related to persons subject to Articles of War. See [section 802 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s802). [Act Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 852, § 23](/us/act/1956-08-01/ch852/s23), [70 Stat. 911](/us/stat/70/911) was repealed by [Pub. L. 87–651, title III, § 307A](/us/pl/87/651/tIII/s307A), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 526](/us/stat/76/526).

Section 553, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 109](/us/stat/64/109), related to jurisdiction to try certain personnel. See [section 803 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s803).

Section 554, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 110](/us/stat/64/110), provided for dismissed officer’s right to trial by court-martial. See [section 804 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s804).

Section 555, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 110](/us/stat/64/110), provided that this chapter should be applicable in all places. See [section 805 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s805).

Section 556, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 110](/us/stat/64/110), related to judge advocates and legal officers. See [section 806 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s806).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1957, see [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53) footnote, [70A Stat. 680](/us/stat/70A/680).
