# § 564l. Omitted

##### § 564l. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 13](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s13), [68 Stat. 721](/us/stat/68/721), which related to Klamath irrigation works, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
