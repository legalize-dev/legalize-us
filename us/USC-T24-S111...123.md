# §§ 111 to 123. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–857, § 14(1)](/us/pl/85/857/s14/1), (2), (7), (9), (14)–(16), (22), (25), (27), (34), (46), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1268–1271](/us/stat/72/1268-1271)

##### §§ 111 to 123. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–857, § 14(1)](/us/pl/85/857/s14/1), (2), (7), (9), (14)–(16), (22), (25), (27), (34), (46), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1268–1271](/us/stat/72/1268-1271)

Section 111, R.S. § 4831; [act Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 129, § 1](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch129/s1), [18 Stat. 359](/us/stat/18/359), authorized Board of Managers of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers to receive donations of money or property.

Section 112, [act Aug. 18, 1894, ch. 301, § 1](/us/act/1894-08-18/ch301/s1), [28 Stat. 412](/us/stat/28/412), related to receipts from sales of subsistence stores or other property of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers.

Section 113, [act Mar. 2, 1923, ch. 178, title II](/us/act/1923-03-02/ch178/tII), [42 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/42/1424), related to use of moneys allotted for support of World War veterans.

Acts [June 30, 1922, ch. 253](/us/act/1922-06-30/ch253), title II, [42 Stat. 763](/us/stat/42/763); [Mar. 2, 1923, ch. 178](/us/act/1923-03-02/ch178), title II, [42 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/42/1424), which were set out in notes under [section 113 of this title](/us/usc/t24/s113), related to use of moneys allotted for support of World War veterans, and were repealed by [Pub. L. 85–857, § 14(44)](/us/pl/85/857/s14/44), (46), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1271](/us/stat/72/1271).

Section 114, [act June 6, 1900, ch. 785, § 1](/us/act/1900-06-06/ch785/s1), [31 Stat. 294](/us/stat/31/294), related to availability of appropriations for construction of buildings at any of branches of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers.

Section 115, [act Mar. 3, 1903, ch. 1007, § 1](/us/act/1903-03-03/ch1007/s1), [32 Stat. 1137](/us/stat/32/1137), related to availability of appropriations for branch homes of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers.

Section 116, [act Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 75, § 1](/us/act/1915-03-03/ch75/s1), [38 Stat. 850](/us/stat/38/850), prohibited use of appropriations for repairs for construction of new buildings.

Section 117, [act July 9, 1886, ch. 756, § 2](/us/act/1886-07-09/ch756/s2), [24 Stat. 129](/us/stat/24/129), related to security for deposits of moneys pertaining to National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers.

Section 118, [act Mar. 3, 1893, ch. 210, § 1](/us/act/1893-03-03/ch210/s1), [27 Stat. 653](/us/stat/27/653), related to supervision of accounts of volunteer soldiers’ homes.

Section 119, [act Aug. 18, 1894, ch. 301, § 1](/us/act/1894-08-18/ch301/s1), [28 Stat. 411](/us/stat/28/411), related to disbursements, accounts, supplies and to posthumous fund.

Section 120, acts [Mar. 3, 1887, ch. 362](/us/act/1887-03-03/ch362), [24 Stat. 539](/us/stat/24/539); [June 10, 1921, ch. 18, § 304](/us/act/1921-06-10/ch18/s304), [42 Stat. 24](/us/stat/42/24), related to expenditures of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers.

Section 121, acts [Mar. 3, 1901, ch. 853, § 1](/us/act/1901-03-03/ch853/s1), [31 Stat. 1178](/us/stat/31/1178); [June 10, 1921, ch. 18, § 304](/us/act/1921-06-10/ch18/s304), [42 Stat. 24](/us/stat/42/24), related to auditing and settlement of accounts of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers.

Section 122, [act Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 129, § 1](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch129/s1), [18 Stat. 359](/us/stat/18/359), related to employment of clerks, appropriations, estimates, requisitions, and accounts of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers.

Section 123, acts [Aug. 5, 1892, ch. 380, § 1](/us/act/1892-08-05/ch380/s1), [27 Stat. 384](/us/stat/27/384); [June 10, 1921, ch. 18, § 304](/us/act/1921-06-10/ch18/s304), [42 Stat. 24](/us/stat/42/24), required statement of expenses of Board of Managers of National Home for Disabled Volunteer Soldiers, to be included in annual budget.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1959, see [section 2 of Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857/s2), set out as a note preceding Part I of Title 38, Veterans’ Benefits.
