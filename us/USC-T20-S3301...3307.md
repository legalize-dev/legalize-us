# §§ 3301 to 3307. Omitted

##### §§ 3301 to 3307. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 3301, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7031](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7031), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 285](/us/stat/102/285), related to use of funds for data collection, evaluation, and research.

Section 3302, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7032](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7032), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 286](/us/stat/102/286), related to grants for State programs.

Section 3303, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7033](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7033), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 287](/us/stat/102/287), related to program evaluation requirements.

Section 3304, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7034](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7034), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 287](/us/stat/102/287), related to evaluation assistance centers.

Section 3305, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7035](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7035), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 287](/us/stat/102/287), related to research and development.

Section 3306, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7036](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7036), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 288](/us/stat/102/288), related to coordination of research.

Section 3307, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 7037](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s7037), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 289](/us/stat/102/289), related to education statistics.
