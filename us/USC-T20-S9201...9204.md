#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the first day of the first full program year after July 22, 2014 (July 1, 2015), see [section 506 of Pub. L. 113–128](/us/pl/113/128/s506), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3101 of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s3101), Labor.

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 105–220, title V, § 507](/us/pl/105/220/tV/s507), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1247](/us/stat/112/1247), which provided that [Pub. L. 105–220](/us/pl/105/220), and the amendments made by [Pub. L. 105–220](/us/pl/105/220), would take effect on Aug. 7, 1998, except as otherwise provided, was repealed by [Pub. L. 113–128, title V, § 511(a)](/us/pl/113/128/tV/s511/a), July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1705](/us/stat/128/1705).

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 105–220, § 1(a)](/us/pl/105/220/s1/a), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 936](/us/stat/112/936), which provided that [Pub. L. 105–220](/us/pl/105/220) could be cited as the “Workforce Investment Act of 1998”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 113–128, title V, § 511(a)](/us/pl/113/128/tV/s511/a), July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1705](/us/stat/128/1705).

[Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 201](/us/pl/105/220/tII/s201), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/112/1059), which provided that title II of [Pub. L. 105–220](/us/pl/105/220) could be cited as the “Adult Education and Family Literacy Act”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 113–128, title V, § 511(a)](/us/pl/113/128/tV/s511/a), July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1705](/us/stat/128/1705).
