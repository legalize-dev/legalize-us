# § 576. Omitted

##### § 576. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 27, 1939, ch. 387, § 6](/us/act/1939-07-27/ch387/s6), [53 Stat. 1130](/us/stat/53/1130), which related to purchase of lands and reimbursement of expenditures, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
