# §§ 6231 to 6235. Omitted

##### §§ 6231 to 6235. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 6231 to 6235 were omitted pursuant to [section 6251 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6251) which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001.

Section 6231, [Pub. L. 103–239, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/103/239/tVI/s601), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 603](/us/stat/108/603), related to requirements applicable to programs under this chapter.

Section 6232, [Pub. L. 103–239, title VI, § 602](/us/pl/103/239/tVI/s602), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 604](/us/stat/108/604), related to sanctions.

Section 6233, [Pub. L. 103–239, title VI, § 603](/us/pl/103/239/tVI/s603), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 604](/us/stat/108/604), related to State authority.

Section 6234, [Pub. L. 103–239, title VI, § 604](/us/pl/103/239/tVI/s604), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 605](/us/stat/108/605), related to prohibition on Federal mandates, direction, and control.

Section 6235, [Pub. L. 103–239, title VI, § 605](/us/pl/103/239/tVI/s605), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 605](/us/stat/108/605); [Pub. L. 105–362, title VIII, § 801(c)(6)](/us/pl/105/362/tVIII/s801/c/6), Nov. 10, 1998, [112 Stat. 3288](/us/stat/112/3288), authorized appropriations.
