# § 520b. Applicants for enlistment: authority to use funds for the issue of authorized articles

##### § 520b. Applicants for enlistment: authority to use funds for the issue of authorized articles

Funds appropriated to the Department of Defense may be used for the issue of authorized articles to applicants for enlistment.

*(Added [Pub. L. 98–525, title XIV, § 1401(a)(1)](/us/pl/98/525/tXIV/s1401/a/1), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2614](/us/stat/98/2614); amended [Pub. L. 99–145, title XIII, § 1303(a)(4)(A)](/us/pl/99/145/tXIII/s1303/a/4/A), Nov. 8, 1985, [99 Stat. 738](/us/stat/99/738).)*
