# §§ 2795 to 2797. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 683(a)](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s683/a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 519](/us/stat/95/519)

##### §§ 2795 to 2797. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 683(a)](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s683/a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 519](/us/stat/95/519)

Section 2795, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 212](/us/pl/88/452/tII/s212), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 104](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s104), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 694](/us/stat/81/694), related to specific powers and functions of community action agencies.

A prior [section 212 of Pub. L. 88–452](/us/pl/88/452/s212) was classified to [section 2801 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2801), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–750, title III, § 315](/us/pl/89/750/tIII/s315), Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1222](/us/stat/80/1222).

Section 2796, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 213](/us/pl/88/452/tII/s213), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 104](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s104), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 695](/us/stat/81/695); amended [Pub. L. 95–568](/us/pl/95/568), §§ 4(d), 17(a)(5), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2426](/us/stat/92/2426), 2439, related to administrative standards, rules and regulations, and review and consultative requirements.

A prior [section 213 of Pub. L. 88–452](/us/pl/88/452/s213) was classified to [section 2802 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2802), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–750, title III, § 315](/us/pl/89/750/tIII/s315), Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1222](/us/stat/80/1222).

Section 2797, [Pub. L. 88–452, title II, § 214](/us/pl/88/452/tII/s214), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 104](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s104), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 696](/us/stat/81/696), related to housing development and service organizations.

A prior [section 214 of Pub. L. 88–452](/us/pl/88/452/s214) was classified to [section 2803 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2803), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 89–750, title III, § 315](/us/pl/89/750/tIII/s315), Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1222](/us/stat/80/1222).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 9912(a) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s9912/a), prior to the general amendment of chapter 106 (§ 9901 et seq.) of this title by [Pub. L. 105–285](/us/pl/105/285).
