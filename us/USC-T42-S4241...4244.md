# §§ 4241 to 4244. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

##### §§ 4241 to 4244. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

Section 4241, [Pub. L. 90–577, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/90/577/tVI/s601), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1106](/us/stat/82/1106), related to Congressional review of grant-in-aid programs. See [section 6507 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6507), Money and Finance.

Section 4242, [Pub. L. 90–577, title VI, § 602](/us/pl/90/577/tVI/s602), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/82/1107), related to studies by the Comptroller General of Federal grant-in-aid programs and reports to Congress. See [section 6508(a) of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6508/a).

Section 4243, [Pub. L. 90–577, title VI, § 603](/us/pl/90/577/tVI/s603), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/82/1107), related to studies by the Advisory Commission on Intergovernmental Relations and a report of its findings to Congress. See [section 6508(b) of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6508/b).

Section 4244, [Pub. L. 90–577, title VI, § 604](/us/pl/90/577/tVI/s604), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/82/1107), provided for preservation of House and Senate committee jurisdiction.
