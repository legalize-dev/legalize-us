# § 5374. Effect of circulars, policies, manuals, guidance, and rules

##### § 5374. Effect of circulars, policies, manuals, guidance, and rules

(1) the eligibility provisions of [section 5324(g) of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5324/g); and

(2) regulations promulgated pursuant to [section 5373 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5373).
