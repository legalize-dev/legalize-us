# § 1166. Omitted

##### § 1166. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–117, § 6](/us/pl/90/117/s6), Oct. 31, 1967, [81 Stat. 338](/us/stat/81/338), which provided for costs incident to making the payments authorized by this subchapter to be paid from the judgment fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
