# § 1234A. Gains or losses from certain terminations

##### § 1234A. Gains or losses from certain terminations

(1) a right or obligation (other than a securities futures contract, as defined in section 1234B) with respect to property which is (or on acquisition would be) a capital asset in the hands of the taxpayer, or

(2) a section 1256 contract (as defined in section 1256) not described in paragraph (1) which is a capital asset in the hands of the taxpayer,

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2002—Pars. (1) to (3). [Pub. L. 107–147](/us/pl/107/147) inserted “or” at end of par. (1), struck out “or” at end of par. (2), and struck out par. (3) which read as follows: “a securities futures contract (as so defined) which is a capital asset in the hands of the taxpayer,”.

2000—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 106–554, § 1(a)(7) [title IV, § 401(b)(1)]](/us/pl/106/554/s1/a/7/tIV/s401/b/1), inserted “(other than a securities futures contract, as defined in section 1234B)” after “right or obligation”.

Par. (3). [Pub. L. 106–554, § 1(a)(7) [title IV, § 401(b)(2)–(4)]](/us/pl/106/554/s1/a/7/tIV/s401/b/2), added par. (3).

1997—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 105–34](/us/pl/105/34) substituted “property” for “personal property (as defined in section 1092(d)(1))”.

1984—[Pub. L. 98–369, § 102(e)(9)](/us/pl/98/369/s102/e/9), inserted at end “The preceding sentence shall not apply to the retirement of any debt instrument (whether or not through a trust or other participation arrangement).”

Par. (2). [Pub. L. 98–369, § 102(e)(4)](/us/pl/98/369/s102/e/4), substituted “a section 1256 contract” for “a regulated futures contract”.

1983—[Pub. L. 97–448](/us/pl/97/448) inserted reference to a regulated futures contract (as defined in section 1256) not described in paragraph (1) which is a capital asset in the hands of the taxpayer.
