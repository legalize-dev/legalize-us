# § 30301. Short title

##### § 30301. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “Death on the High Seas Act”.

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 6(c)](/us/pl/109/304/s6/c), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1511](/us/stat/120/1511).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Act Mar. 30, 1920, ch. 111](/us/act/1920-03-30/ch111), [41 Stat. 537](/us/stat/41/537), which enacted chapter 21 (§ 761 et seq.) of the former Appendix to this title, was popularly known as the “Death on the High Seas Act”, prior to being repealed and restated in this chapter by [Pub. L. 109–304](/us/pl/109/304), §§ 6(c), 19, Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1509](/us/stat/120/1509), 1710.
