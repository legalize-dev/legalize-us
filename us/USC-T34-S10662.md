# § 10662. Funding

##### § 10662. Funding

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $99,000,000 for each fiscal year 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, and 2010.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2997](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2997), as added [Pub. L. 109–177, title VII, § 754](/us/pl/109/177/tVII/s754), Mar. 9, 2006, [120 Stat. 274](/us/stat/120/274).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3797cc–1 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3797cc–1), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
