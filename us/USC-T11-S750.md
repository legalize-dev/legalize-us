# § 750. Distribution of securities

##### § 750. Distribution of securities

The trustee may not distribute a security except under [section 751 of this title](/us/usc/t11/s751).

*([Pub. L. 95–598](/us/pl/95/598), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2614](/us/stat/92/2614).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

#### senate report no. 95–989

Section 750 forbids the trustee from distributing a security other than a customer name security. The term “distribution” refers to a distribution to customers in satisfaction of net equity claims and is not intended to preclude the trustee from liquidating securities under proposed [11 U.S.C. 748](/us/usc/t11/s748).
