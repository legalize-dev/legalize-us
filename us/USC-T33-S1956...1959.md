# §§ 1956 to 1959. Transferred

##### §§ 1956 to 1959. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 7 to 10 of [Pub. L. 109–449](/us/pl/109/449), formerly classified to sections 1956 to 1959, respectively, of this title, were renumbered sections 131 to 134 of [Pub. L. 109–449](/us/pl/109/449) by [Pub. L. 119–65, § 4(b)(1)](/us/pl/119/65/s4/b/1), Dec. 26, 2025, [139 Stat. 1989](/us/stat/139/1989), and transferred to sections 1981 to 1984, respectively, of this title.
