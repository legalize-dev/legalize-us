# § 648. Omitted

##### § 648. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–400, § 2](/us/pl/91/400/s2), Sept. 16, 1970, [84 Stat. 838](/us/stat/84/838), which exempted funds distributed to members of the tribe from Federal and State income tax, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
