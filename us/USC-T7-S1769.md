# § 1769. “Agricultural commodity” defined

##### § 1769. “Agricultural commodity” defined

The term “agricultural commodity” as used in this chapter includes any agricultural commodity or product thereof.

*([Aug. 28, 1954, ch. 1041](/us/act/1954-08-28/ch1041), title VI, § 609, as added [Pub. L. 95–501, title IV, § 401(6)](/us/pl/95/501/tIV/s401/6), Oct. 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 1691](/us/stat/92/1691).)*
