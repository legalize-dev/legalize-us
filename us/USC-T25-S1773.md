# § 1773. Omitted

##### § 1773. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–41, § 2](/us/pl/101/41/s2), June 21, 1989, [103 Stat. 83](/us/stat/103/83), which set out congressional findings and purpose, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 101–41, § 1](/us/pl/101/41/s1), June 21, 1989, [103 Stat. 83](/us/stat/103/83), provided that [Pub. L. 101–41](/us/pl/101/41), enacting this subchapter and provisions formerly set out as a note under [section 1773a of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1773a), could be cited as the “Puyallup Tribe of Indians Settlement Act of 1989”.
