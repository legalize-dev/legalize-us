# § 7786. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 7786. Authorization of appropriations

(a) Grants To carry out [section 7783 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s7783), there are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary $7,500,000 for each of fiscal years 2005 through 2009, of which not more than 5 percent of the funds made available for a fiscal year may be used by the Secretary for administrative costs.

(b) Agreements To carry out [section 7784 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s7784), there are authorized to be appropriated to the Secretary $7,500,000 for each of fiscal years 2005 through 2009, of which not more than 5 percent of the funds made available for a fiscal year may be used by the Secretary for administrative costs of Federal agencies.
