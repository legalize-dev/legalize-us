# § 410kkk. Definitions

##### § 410kkk. Definitions

(1) Park The term “park” means the Lewis and Clark National Historical Park designated in [section 410kkk–1 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s410kkk–1).

(2) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

*([Pub. L. 108–387, title I, § 102](/us/pl/108/387/tI/s102), Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2234](/us/stat/118/2234).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in introductory provisions, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 108–387](/us/pl/108/387), Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2234](/us/stat/118/2234), which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Short Title note below and Tables.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 108–387, title I, § 101](/us/pl/108/387/tI/s101), Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2234](/us/stat/118/2234), provided that: “This title [enacting this subchapter and repealing sections 450mm to 450mm–3 of this title] may be cited as the ‘Lewis and Clark National Historical Park Designation Act’.”
