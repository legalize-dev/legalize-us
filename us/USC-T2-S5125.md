# § 5125. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–244, div. B, title I, § 118(a)](/us/pl/115/244/dB/tI/s118/a), (b), Sept. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 2930](/us/stat/132/2930)

##### § 5125. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–244, div. B, title I, § 118(a)](/us/pl/115/244/dB/tI/s118/a), (b), Sept. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 2930](/us/stat/132/2930)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–665](/us/pl/91/665), ch. VIII, Jan. 8, 1971, [84 Stat. 1989](/us/stat/84/1989); [Pub. L. 93–532, § 1](/us/pl/93/532/s1), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1723](/us/stat/88/1723); [Pub. L. 99–225](/us/pl/99/225), Dec. 28, 1985, [99 Stat. 1743](/us/stat/99/1743), related to the right of every former Speaker to retain an office furnished and maintained by the Government in an appropriate condition.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 115–244, div. B, title I, § 118(c)](/us/pl/115/244/dB/tI/s118/c), Sept. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 2930](/us/stat/132/2930), provided that: “The amendments made by this section [repealing this section and sections 5126 to 5129 of this title] shall apply with respect to any individual who serves as a Representative in Congress during the One Hundred Fifteenth Congress or any succeeding Congress.”
