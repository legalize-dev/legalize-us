# § 7152. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 7152. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 106–393, title IV, § 402](/us/pl/106/393/tIV/s402), as added [Pub. L. 110–343, div. C, title VI, § 601(a)](/us/pl/110/343/dC/tVI/s601/a), Oct. 3, 2008, [122 Stat. 3910](/us/stat/122/3910); amended [Pub. L. 112–141, div. F, title I, § 100101(a)(2)](/us/pl/112/141/dF/tI/s100101/a/2), July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 905](/us/stat/126/905); [Pub. L. 113–40, § 10(a)(4)](/us/pl/113/40/s10/a/4), Oct. 2, 2013, [127 Stat. 545](/us/stat/127/545); [Pub. L. 114–10, title V, § 524(e)](/us/pl/114/10/tV/s524/e), Apr. 16, 2015, [129 Stat. 180](/us/stat/129/180).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 106–393](/us/pl/106/393), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1607](/us/stat/114/1607), known as the Secure Rural Schools and Community Self-Determination Act of 2000, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 7101 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7101) and Tables.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 402 of Pub. L. 106–393](/us/pl/106/393/s402) was set out in a note under [section 500 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s500) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 110–343, div. C, title VI, § 601(a)](/us/pl/110/343/dC/tVI/s601/a), Oct. 3, 2008, [122 Stat. 3893](/us/stat/122/3893).

#### Amendments

2015—[Pub. L. 114–10](/us/pl/114/10) struck out before period at end “for each of fiscal years 2008 through 2013”.

2013—[Pub. L. 113–40](/us/pl/113/40) substituted “2013” for “2012”.

2012—[Pub. L. 112–141](/us/pl/112/141) substituted “2012” for “2011”.
