# §§ 901 to 905. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### §§ 901 to 905. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 901, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. I, § 101, [66 Stat. 481](/us/stat/66/481), defined terms used in this chapter. See sections 101 and 10207 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 902, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. VIII, § 809, [66 Stat. 509](/us/stat/66/509), related to savings provisions for laws relating to appointment in reserve components.

Section 903, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. VIII, § 810, [66 Stat. 509](/us/stat/66/509), related to accrued rights.

Section 904, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. VIII, § 811, [66 Stat. 509](/us/stat/66/509), related to authority to order reservists to active duty and the responsibilities and functions of Chief of National Guard Bureau.

Section 905, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. VIII, § 812, [66 Stat. 509](/us/stat/66/509), related to retroactive pay.
