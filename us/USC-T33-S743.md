# § 743. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8512(a)(5), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4759](/us/stat/134/4759)

##### § 743. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8512(a)(5), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4759](/us/stat/134/4759)

Section, acts [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 11](/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s11), [36 Stat. 538](/us/stat/36/538); [May 17, 1932, ch. 190](/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190), [47 Stat. 158](/us/stat/47/158), related to lighthouse districts. Section 11 of act June 17, 1910, also enacted former [section 717 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s717).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

Prior provisions for the arrangement of the various lighthouse districts were made by R.S. § 4670; [act July 26, 1886, ch. 779](/us/act/1886-07-26/ch779), [24 Stat. 148](/us/stat/24/148), which were repealed by section 13 of act June 17, 1910.
