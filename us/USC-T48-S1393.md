# § 1393. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–357, title III, § 308(a)](/us/pl/97/357/tIII/s308/a), Oct. 19, 1982, [96 Stat. 1710](/us/stat/96/1710)

##### § 1393. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–357, title III, § 308(a)](/us/pl/97/357/tIII/s308/a), Oct. 19, 1982, [96 Stat. 1710](/us/stat/96/1710)

Section, [act July 12, 1921, ch. 44, § 1](/us/act/1921-07-12/ch44/s1), [42 Stat. 123](/us/stat/42/123), declared as ineligible to hold office as a member of colonial councils of Virgin Islands or any other public office under Virgin Islands government, anyone owing allegiance to any country other than United States.
