# §§ 4821 to 4823. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 365](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s365), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3975](/us/stat/108/3975)

##### §§ 4821 to 4823. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 365](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s365), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3975](/us/stat/108/3975)

Section 4821, [Pub. L. 100–297, title III, § 3221](/us/pl/100/297/tIII/s3221), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 339](/us/stat/102/339), stated findings of Congress and purposes of this subchapter.

Section 4822, [Pub. L. 100–297, title III, § 3222](/us/pl/100/297/tIII/s3222), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 340](/us/stat/102/340), related to local educational agencies eligible for grants.

Section 4823, [Pub. L. 100–297, title III, § 3223](/us/pl/100/297/tIII/s3223), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 340](/us/stat/102/340), authorized demonstration grants for development of innovative and promising family-school educational partnership activities.
