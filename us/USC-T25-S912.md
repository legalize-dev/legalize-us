# § 912. Omitted

##### § 912. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 86–97, § 2](/us/pl/86/97/s2), July 17, 1959, [73 Stat. 222](/us/stat/73/222), which related to per capita payments to tribal members from funds appropriated for a certain judgment against the United States and exemption of such payments from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
