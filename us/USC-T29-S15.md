# § 15. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 644](/us/stat/80/644)

##### § 15. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 644](/us/stat/80/644)

Section, [act June 5, 1920, ch. 248, § 4](/us/act/1920-06-05/ch248/s4), [41 Stat. 987](/us/stat/41/987), authorized employment by Woman’s Bureau of Department of Labor of such employees at such rates of compensation as Congress may provide by appropriation.
