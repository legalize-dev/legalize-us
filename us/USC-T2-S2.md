#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 8, 1911, ch. 5](/us/act/1911-08-08/ch5), §§ 1, 2, [37 Stat. 13](/us/stat/37/13), 14, fixed composition of House of Representatives at 435 Members, to be apportioned to the States therein enumerated. For provisions dealing with reapportionment of Representatives and manner of election, etc., see sections 2a and 2b of this title.
