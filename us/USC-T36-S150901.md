# § 150901. Organization

##### § 150901. Organization

(a) Federal Charter.— National Council on Radiation Protection and Measurements (in this chapter, the “corporation”) is a federally chartered corporation.

(b) Perpetual Existence.— Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1395](/us/stat/112/1395).)*

This section is substituted for the source provision for consistency in the revised title and to eliminate unnecessary and executed words.
