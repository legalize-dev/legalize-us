# § 1162. Definition

##### § 1162. Definition

In this subchapter, “Board” means the “Surface Transportation Board”.

*(Added [Pub. L. 104–88, title III, § 302(1)](/us/pl/104/88/tIII/s302/1), Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 943](/us/stat/109/943).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 1162, [Pub. L. 95–598](/us/pl/95/598), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2641](/us/stat/92/2641), defined “Commission”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–88, title III, § 302(1)](/us/pl/104/88/tIII/s302/1), Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 943](/us/stat/109/943).
