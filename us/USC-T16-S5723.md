# § 5723. Exemption

##### § 5723. Exemption

Chapter 10 of title 5 shall not apply to the Panel or to an advisory committee established under [section 5722 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s5722).

*([Pub. L. 106–450, title II, § 204](/us/pl/106/450/tII/s204), Nov. 7, 2000, [114 Stat. 1943](/us/stat/114/1943); [Pub. L. 117–286, § 4(a)(127)](/us/pl/117/286/s4/a/127), Dec. 27, 2022, [136 Stat. 4319](/us/stat/136/4319).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–286](/us/pl/117/286) substituted “Chapter 10 of title 5” for “The Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.)”.
