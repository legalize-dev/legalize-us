# § 7802. Applicability of subchapter

##### § 7802. Applicability of subchapter

Parts B, C, D, E, and F of this subchapter do not apply to subchapter VII of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 8102](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s8102), formerly title IX, § 9102, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/107/110/tIX/s901), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1966](/us/stat/115/1966); renumbered title VIII, § 8102, and amended [Pub. L. 114–95, title VIII](/us/pl/114/95/tVIII), §§ 8001(a)(1), 8003, Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2088](/us/stat/129/2088), 2089, 2099.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 7802, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 9102](/us/pl/89/10/tIX/s9102), as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3774](/us/stat/108/3774), set out purpose of provisions relating to Indian education, prior to the general amendment of former subchapter IX of this chapter by [Pub. L. 107–110](/us/pl/107/110).

#### Amendments

2015—[Pub. L. 114–95, § 8003](/us/pl/114/95/s8003), substituted “Parts B, C, D, E, and F of this subchapter do not apply to subchapter VII” for “Parts B, C, D, and E of this subchapter do not apply to subchapter VIII”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of 2015 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95) effective Dec. 10, 2015, except with respect to certain noncompetitive programs and competitive programs, see [section 5 of Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95/s5), set out as a note under [section 6301 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6301).
