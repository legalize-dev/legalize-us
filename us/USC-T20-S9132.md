# § 9132. Administration

##### § 9132. Administration

(a) In general Not more than 4 percent of the total amount of funds received under this subchapter for any fiscal year by a State may be used for administrative costs.

(b) Construction Nothing in this section shall be construed to limit spending for evaluation costs under [section 9134(c) of this title](/us/usc/t20/s9134/c) from sources other than this subchapter.
