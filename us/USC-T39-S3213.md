# § 3213. Seeds and reports from Department of Agriculture

##### § 3213. Seeds and reports from Department of Agriculture

(1) as penalty mail by the Secretary of Agriculture; and

(2) during the 90-day period immediately following the expiration of their terms of office, as franked mail by Members of Congress.

*([Pub. L. 91–375](/us/pl/91/375), Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 754](/us/stat/84/754); [Pub. L. 97–69, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/69/s5/b), Oct. 26, 1981, [95 Stat. 1043](/us/stat/95/1043).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1981—Par. (2). [Pub. L. 97–69](/us/pl/97/69) substituted “during the 90-day period immediately” for “until the thirtieth day of June”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective July 1, 1971, pursuant to Resolution No. 71–9 of the Board of Governors. See [section 15(a) of Pub. L. 91–375](/us/pl/91/375/s15/a), set out as a note preceding [section 101 of this title](/us/usc/t39/s101).
