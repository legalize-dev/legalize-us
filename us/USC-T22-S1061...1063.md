##### §§ 1061 to 1063. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).

[Pub. L. 94–350, title V, § 522(a)](/us/pl/94/350/tV/s522/a)–(c), July 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 846](/us/stat/90/846), which set forth provisions relating to conversion to Foreign Service Retirement System, was repealed by [Pub.
