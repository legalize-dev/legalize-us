# § 500a. Omitted

##### § 500a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 2](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s2), [50 Stat. 900](/us/stat/50/900), which authorized Secretary of the Interior to acquire reindeer and other property, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
