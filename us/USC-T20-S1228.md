# § 1228. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–260, div. H, title III, § 314(a)](/us/pl/116/260/dH/tIII/s314/a), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 1610](/us/stat/134/1610)

##### § 1228. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–260, div. H, title III, § 314(a)](/us/pl/116/260/dH/tIII/s314/a), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 1610](/us/stat/134/1610)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–247, title IV, § 426](/us/pl/90/247/tIV/s426), formerly § 420, as added [Pub. L. 93–380, title II, § 252](/us/pl/93/380/tII/s252), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 519](/us/stat/88/519); renumbered § 426 and amended [Pub. L. 103–382, title II](/us/pl/103/382/tII), §§ 212(b)(1), 261(b), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3913](/us/stat/108/3913), 3927; [Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [[div. A]](/us/pl/106/398/s1/dA), title XVIII, § 1808(b)(2)], Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1654](/us/stat/114/1654), 1654A–382; [Pub. L. 114–95, title IX, § 9215(mm)(2)](/us/pl/114/95/tIX/s9215/mm/2), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2176](/us/stat/129/2176), prohibited use of appropriated funds for busing.

A prior [section 426 of Pub. L. 90–247](/us/pl/90/247/s426) was renumbered section 433, and is classified to [section 1231c of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1231c).

Another prior [section 426 of Pub. L. 90–247](/us/pl/90/247/s426) was renumbered section 435, and is classified to [section 1231e of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1231e).

Another prior [section 426 of Pub. L. 90–247](/us/pl/90/247/s426) was renumbered section 436, and was classified to [section 1232e of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1232e) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 95–561](/us/pl/95/561).
