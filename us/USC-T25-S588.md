# § 588. Omitted

##### § 588. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–206, § 8](/us/pl/92/206/s8), Dec. 18, 1971, [85 Stat. 739](/us/stat/85/739), which related to distribution of funds to members of the Shoshone Tribe of the Wind River Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
