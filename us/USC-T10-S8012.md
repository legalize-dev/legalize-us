# § 8012. Department of the Navy: seal

##### § 8012. Department of the Navy: seal

The Secretary of the Navy shall have a seal for the Department of the Navy. The design of the seal must be approved by the President. Judicial notice shall be taken of the seal.

*(Added [Pub. L. 99–433, title V, § 511(c)(2)](/us/pl/99/433/tV/s511/c/2), Oct. 1, 1986, [100 Stat. 1043](/us/stat/100/1043), § 5012; renumbered § 8012, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(a)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/a/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/132/1834).)*
