# §§ 331 to 337. Repealed. [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 651(a)(1), [46 Stat. 762](/us/stat/46/762)

##### §§ 331 to 337. Repealed. [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 651(a)(1), [46 Stat. 762](/us/stat/46/762)

Sections, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356, title IV](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356/tIV), §§ 481(a)–(c), 482(a)–(d), [42 Stat. 958](/us/stat/42/958), related to contents of invoices, certification in different consular districts and declarations indorsed on invoices.

Provisions of Tariff Act of 1930 corresponding to section 331, see [section 1481(a) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1481/a); section 332, see [section 1481(b) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1481/b); section 333, see [section 1481(c) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1481/c); section 334, see [section 1482(a) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1482/a); section 335, see [section 1482(b) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1482/b); section 336, see [section 1482(c) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1482/c); section 337, see [section 1482(d) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1482/d).

Sections 331 to 333 were repealed effective on day after enactment of repealing act.

Sections 334 to 337 were repealed effective sixty days after enactment of repealing act.
