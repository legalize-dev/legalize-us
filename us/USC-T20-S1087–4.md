# § 1087–4. Discrimination in secondary markets prohibited

##### § 1087–4. Discrimination in secondary markets prohibited

The Student Loan Marketing Association (and, if the Association is privatized under [section 1087–3 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1087–3), any successor entity functioning as a secondary market for loans under this part, including the Holding Company described in such section) shall not engage directly or indirectly in any pattern or practice that results in a denial of a borrower’s access to loans under this part because of the borrower’s race, sex, color, religion, national origin, age, disability status, income, attendance at a particular eligible institution, length of the borrower’s educational program, or the borrower’s academic year at an eligible institution.

*([Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 440A](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s440A), as added [Pub. L. 104–208, div. A, title I, § 101(e) [title VI, § 604]](/us/pl/104/208/dA/tI/s101/e/tVI/s604), Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3009–233](/us/stat/110/3009-233), 3009–293.)*
