##### § 746. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–318, title I, § 161(b)(3)](/us/pl/92/318/tI/s161/b/3), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 303](/us/stat/86/303)

Section, [Pub. L. 88–204, title III, § 306](/us/pl/88/204/tIII/s306), as added [Pub. L. 90–575, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/90/575/tIV/s403), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1060](/us/stat/82/1060), related to annual interest grants, providing in: subsec. (a) for power of Commissioner; subsec. (d) for maximum amount of funds usable in one State; and subsec. (e) certain prerequisites and nature of financing.
