# § 1300a–2. Omitted

##### § 1300a–2. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–461, § 4](/us/pl/92/461/s4), Oct. 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 769](/us/stat/86/769), which related to apportionment of funds not set aside pursuant to former [section 1300a of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1300a) among the groups cited in former [section 1300a–1 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1300a–1) and use of funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
