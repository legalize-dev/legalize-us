# § 7407. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 7407. Authorization of appropriations

(1) for activities under [section 278h of this title](/us/usc/t15/s278h)—

(A) $25,000,000 for fiscal year 2003;

(B) $40,000,000 for fiscal year 2004;

(C) $55,000,000 for fiscal year 2005;

(D) $70,000,000 for fiscal year 2006;

(E) $85,000,000 for fiscal year 2007; and

(2) for activities under section 278g–3(f) 11 See References in Text note below. of this title—

(A) $6,000,000 for fiscal year 2003;

(B) $6,200,000 for fiscal year 2004;

(C) $6,400,000 for fiscal year 2005;

(D) $6,600,000 for fiscal year 2006; and

(E) $6,800,000 for fiscal year 2007.

*([Pub. L. 107–305, § 11](/us/pl/107/305/s11), Nov. 27, 2002, [116 Stat. 2379](/us/stat/116/2379).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 278g–3 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s278g–3), referred to in par. (2), was amended by [Pub. L. 107–347, title III, § 303](/us/pl/107/347/tIII/s303), Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 2957](/us/stat/116/2957), and, as so amended, did not contain a subsec. (f). A later amendment by [Pub. L. 113–274, title II, § 204(1)](/us/pl/113/274/tII/s204/1), Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 2980](/us/stat/128/2980), redesignated subsec. (e) of [section 278g–3 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s278g–3), relating to definitions, as (f).
