# §§ 2601 to 2603. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VII, § 714](/us/pl/93/203/tVII/s714), formerly title VI, § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883); renumbered title VII, § 714, [Pub. L. 93–567, title I, § 101](/us/pl/93/567/tI/s101), Dec. 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 1845](/us/stat/88/1845)

##### §§ 2601 to 2603. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VII, § 714](/us/pl/93/203/tVII/s714), formerly title VI, § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883); renumbered title VII, § 714, [Pub. L. 93–567, title I, § 101](/us/pl/93/567/tI/s101), Dec. 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 1845](/us/stat/88/1845)

Section 2601, [Pub. L. 87–415, title II, § 231](/us/pl/87/415/tII/s231), Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 30](/us/stat/76/30); [Pub. L. 88–214, § 6](/us/pl/88/214/s6), Dec. 19, 1963, [77 Stat. 424](/us/stat/77/424); [Pub. L. 89–15, § 8](/us/pl/89/15/s8), Apr. 26, 1965, [79 Stat. 78](/us/stat/79/78); [Pub. L. 89–792, § 5](/us/pl/89/792/s5), Nov. 7, 1966, [80 Stat. 1436](/us/stat/80/1436); [Pub. L. 90–636](/us/pl/90/636), §§ 3(b), 5(b), 7, Oct. 24, 1968, [82 Stat. 1353](/us/stat/82/1353), set out general responsibility of Secretary of Health, Education, and Welfare in areas of training and skill development programs.

Section 2602, [Pub. L. 87–415, title II, § 232](/us/pl/87/415/tII/s232), Mar. 15, 1962, [76 Stat. 30](/us/stat/76/30), empowered Secretary of Health, Education, and Welfare to promulgate rules and regulations.

Section 2603, [Pub. L. 87–415, title II, § 233](/us/pl/87/415/tII/s233), as added [Pub. L. 89–792, § 8(b)](/us/pl/89/792/s8/b), Nov. 7, 1966, [80 Stat. 1437](/us/stat/80/1437), directed Secretary of Health, Education, and Welfare to make an annual report to Congress.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 93–203, title VII, § 714](/us/pl/93/203/tVII/s714), formerly title VI, § 614, Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883); renumbered title VII, § 714, [Pub. L. 93–567, title I, § 101](/us/pl/93/567/tI/s101), Dec. 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 1845](/us/stat/88/1845), provided that the repeal is effective with respect to fiscal years after June 30, 1974.
