# § 10530. Patrick Leahy Bulletproof Vest Partnership Grant Program

##### § 10530. Patrick Leahy Bulletproof Vest Partnership Grant Program

The program under this subchapter shall be known as the “Patrick Leahy Bulletproof Vest Partnership Grant Program”.
