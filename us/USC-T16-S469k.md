##### § 469k. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–333, div. I, title VI, § 604(e)(1)](/us/pl/104/333/dI/tVI/s604/e/1), Nov. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 4173](/us/stat/110/4173); [Pub. L. 107–359, § 3(4)(A)](/us/pl/107/359/s3/4/A), Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 3016](/us/stat/116/3016); [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

Section, [Pub. L. 104–333, div. I, title VI, § 604](/us/pl/104/333/dI/tVI/s604), Nov. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 4173](/us/stat/110/4173); [Pub. L. 107–359, § 3](/us/pl/107/359/s3), Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 3016](/us/stat/116/3016), known as the American Battlefield Protection Act of 1996, established the American Battlefield Protection Program.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of 2002 Amendment

[Pub. L. 107–359, § 1](/us/pl/107/359/s1), Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 3016](/us/stat/116/3016), provided that: “This Act [amending this section and enacting provisions set out as a note under this section], may be cited as the ‘Civil War Battlefield Preservation Act of 2002’.”
