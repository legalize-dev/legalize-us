# § 1744. Omitted

##### § 1744. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 97–399, § 5](/us/pl/97/399/s5), Dec. 31, 1982, [96 Stat. 2013](/us/stat/96/2013), which related to approval of prior transfers and extinguishment of claims and aboriginal title involving Florida Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
