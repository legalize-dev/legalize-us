# § 11714. Severability

##### § 11714. Severability

If any provision of this chapter, or the application of any such provision to any person or circumstances is held to be invalid, the remainder of this chapter, and the application of such provision or amendment to persons or circumstances other than those to which it is held invalid, shall not be affected thereby.

*([Pub. L. 100–579, § 16](/us/pl/100/579/s16), as added [Pub. L. 102–396, title IX, § 9168](/us/pl/102/396/tIX/s9168), Oct. 6, 1992, [106 Stat. 1948](/us/stat/106/1948).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section enacted by section 1 of S. 2681, One Hundred Second Congress, as passed by the Senate on Aug. 7, 1992, which was enacted into law by [section 9168 of Pub. L. 102–396](/us/pl/102/396/s9168). Section 9168, which referred to S. 2681, as passed by the Senate on “September 12, 1992”, has been treated as referring to S. 2681, as passed by the Senate on Aug. 7, 1992, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
