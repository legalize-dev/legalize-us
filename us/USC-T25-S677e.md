# § 677e. Omitted

##### § 677e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 6](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s6), [68 Stat. 868](/us/stat/68/868), which granted to mixed-blood members of the tribe the right to organize for their common welfare, adopt a constitution and bylaws, and select representatives, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
