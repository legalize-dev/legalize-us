# § 31g. Biennial report

##### § 31g. Biennial report

(1) describes the status of the national geologic mapping program;

(2) describes and evaluates the progress achieved during the preceding 2 years in developing the national geologic map database; and

(3) includes any recommendations that the Secretary may have for legislative or other action to achieve the purposes of sections 31c through 31f of this title.

*([Pub. L. 102–285, § 8](/us/pl/102/285/s8), as added [Pub. L. 106–148, § 8](/us/pl/106/148/s8), Dec. 9, 1999, [113 Stat. 1724](/us/stat/113/1724); amended [Pub. L. 111–11, title XI, § 11001(h)](/us/pl/111/11/tXI/s11001/h), Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1415](/us/stat/123/1415).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 31g, [Pub. L. 102–285, § 8](/us/pl/102/285/s8), May 18, 1992, [106 Stat. 171](/us/stat/106/171); [Pub. L. 103–437, § 16(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/437/s16/a/1), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4594](/us/stat/108/4594); [Pub. L. 105–36, § 3(f)](/us/pl/105/36/s3/f), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 1111](/us/stat/111/1111); [Pub. L. 105–362, title IX, § 902(b)](/us/pl/105/362/tIX/s902/b), Nov. 10, 1998, [112 Stat. 3291](/us/stat/112/3291), directed the Secretary to submit a biennial report to Congress describing the status of the nationwide geologic mapping program and evaluating the progress achieved during the preceding fiscal year in developing the national geologic-map data base, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 106–148, § 8](/us/pl/106/148/s8), Dec. 9, 1999, [113 Stat. 1724](/us/stat/113/1724).

#### Amendments

2009—[Pub. L. 111–11](/us/pl/111/11) substituted “Not later than 3 years after March 30, 2009, and biennially” for “Not later 3 years after December 9, 1999, and biennially”.
