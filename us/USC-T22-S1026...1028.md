# §§ 1026 to 1028. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

##### §§ 1026 to 1028. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

Section 1026, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 661](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s661), [60 Stat. 1017](/us/stat/60/1017), related to promotion for alien clerks and employees.

Section 1027, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 662](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s662), [60 Stat. 1017](/us/stat/60/1017), related to separation from service of alien clerks and employees for unsatisfactory performance of duty.

Section 1028, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 663](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s663), [60 Stat. 1017](/us/stat/60/1017), related to separation from service of alien clerks and employees for misconduct or malfeasance.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).
