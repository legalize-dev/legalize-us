# § 2314. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–608, § 1(22)](/us/pl/93/608/s1/22), Jan. 2, 1975, [88 Stat. 1970](/us/stat/88/1970)

##### § 2314. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–608, § 1(22)](/us/pl/93/608/s1/22), Jan. 2, 1975, [88 Stat. 1970](/us/stat/88/1970)

Section, [act Aug. 4, 1955, ch. 543](/us/act/1955-08-04/ch543), ch. 10, § 102, [69 Stat. 483](/us/stat/69/483), required a triennial report to the Joint Committee on Atomic Energy by the Commission reviewing its activities under this chapter.
