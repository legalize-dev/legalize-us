# §§ 190i to 190k. Repealed. S. Res. 274, § 2(b), Nov. 14, 1979

##### §§ 190i to 190k. Repealed. S. Res. 274, § 2(b), Nov. 14, 1979

Section 190i, [Pub. L. 91–510, title II, § 243](/us/pl/91/510/tII/s243), Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1173](/us/stat/84/1173), related to actions and procedures of the Committee on Appropriations of Senate. See Standing Rules of the Senate.

Section 190j, [Pub. L. 91–510, title II, § 252(a)](/us/pl/91/510/tII/s252/a), Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1173](/us/stat/84/1173); [Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 302(d)](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s302/d), as added [Pub. L. 95–110, § 1](/us/pl/95/110/s1), Sept. 20, 1977, [91 Stat. 884](/us/stat/91/884), related to cost estimates in reports of Senate committees accompanying certain legislative measures and to other Senate committee operations. See Standing Rules of the Senate.

Section 190k, [Pub. L. 91–510, title II, § 253(a)](/us/pl/91/510/tII/s253/a), (b), Oct. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 1174](/us/stat/84/1174), related to appropriations on an annual basis for continuing programs and activities and review by Senate and joint committees. See Standing Rules of the Senate.
