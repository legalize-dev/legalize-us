# §§ 1148a–4 to 1148d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title V, § 5411(60)](/us/pl/115/334/tV/s5411/60)–(63), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4686](/us/stat/132/4686)

##### §§ 1148a–4 to 1148d. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title V, § 5411(60)](/us/pl/115/334/tV/s5411/60)–(63), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4686](/us/stat/132/4686)

Section 1148a–4, [act July 14, 1953, ch. 192, § 2](/us/act/1953-07-14/ch192/s2), [67 Stat. 150](/us/stat/67/150), related to security for economic disaster and special livestock loans.

Section 1148b, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 32](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s32), [50 Stat. 716](/us/stat/50/716); [June 30, 1947, ch. 166](/us/act/1947-06-30/ch166), title II, § 206(s), [61 Stat. 209](/us/stat/61/209), set out additional powers of regional agricultural credit corporations.

Section 1148c, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 33](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s33), [50 Stat. 717](/us/stat/50/717); [June 30, 1947, ch. 166](/us/act/1947-06-30/ch166), title II, § 206(s), [61 Stat. 209](/us/stat/61/209), related to consolidation or merger of two or more regional agricultural credit corporations.

Section 1148d, [act Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 34](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s34), [50 Stat. 717](/us/stat/50/717), declared the rights and powers of regional agricultural credit corporations, the Farm Credit Administration, or the Governor of the Administration to be unaffected by sections 1148b and 1148c of this title.
