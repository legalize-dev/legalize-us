# § 300dd–33. Funding

##### § 300dd–33. Funding

For the purpose of grants under [section 300dd–31 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300dd–31), there are authorized to be appropriated $100,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1989 and 1990.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XXIV, § 2433, as added [Pub. L. 100–607, title II, § 211](/us/pl/100/607/tII/s211), Nov. 4, 1988, [102 Stat. 3091](/us/stat/102/3091).)*
