# § 2701. Omitted

##### § 2701. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1001), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 140](/us/stat/102/140), which provided a declaration of policy and statement of purpose, was omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519). See [section 6301 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6301).

A prior section 2701, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 101](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s101), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title I, § 101(a)](/us/pl/95/561/tI/s101/a), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2153](/us/stat/92/2153), set out Congressional declaration of policy, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297).

A prior section 2702, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 102](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s102), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title I, § 101(a)](/us/pl/95/561/tI/s101/a), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2153](/us/stat/92/2153); amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 301(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/88/tIII/s301/a/1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677](/us/stat/93/677), 692, provided for the duration of assistance on entitlements created under this subchapter, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297).
