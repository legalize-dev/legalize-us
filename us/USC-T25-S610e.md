# § 610e. Omitted

##### § 610e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–534, § 7](/us/pl/90/534/s7), Sept. 28, 1968, [82 Stat. 884](/us/stat/82/884), which authorized the assignment of income by the Swinomish Indian Tribal Community, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
