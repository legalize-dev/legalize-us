# § 11221. Authority for program

##### § 11221. Authority for program

The Secretary is authorized to make grants and to provide technical assistance to public and nonprofit private entities to establish and operate transitional living youth projects for homeless youth.

*([Pub. L. 93–415, title III, § 321](/us/pl/93/415/tIII/s321), as added [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII, § 7273(f)](/us/pl/100/690/tVII/s7273/f), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4455](/us/stat/102/4455); amended [Pub. L. 106–71, § 3(e)](/us/pl/106/71/s3/e), Oct. 12, 1999, [113 Stat. 1038](/us/stat/113/1038).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 5714–1 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s5714–1), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 321 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s321) was renumbered section 363 and is classified to [section 11275 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s11275).

#### Amendments

1999—[Pub. L. 106–71](/us/pl/106/71) struck out “Purpose and” before “Authority” in section catchline and struck out subsec. (a) designation before “The Secretary” and subsec. (b) which defined “homeless youth” and “transitional living youth project”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective Oct. 1, 1988, see [section 7296(a) of Pub. L. 100–690](/us/pl/100/690/s7296/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 11101 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s11101).
