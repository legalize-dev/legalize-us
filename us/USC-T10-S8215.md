# § 8215. Citizenship of officers of vessels

##### § 8215. Citizenship of officers of vessels

The officers of vessels of the United States shall in all cases by citizens of the United States.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 376](/us/stat/70A/376), § 6019; renumbered § 8215, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(b)(7)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/b/7), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/132/1834).)*
