# § 2231. Reference to chapter 1803

##### § 2231. Reference to chapter 1803

Provisions of law relating to facilities for reserve components are set forth in chapter 1803 of this title (beginning with section 18231).

*(Added [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title XVI, § 1664(b)(11)](/us/pl/103/337/dA/tXVI/s1664/b/11), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 3011](/us/stat/108/3011).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

Prior sections 2231 to 2239 were renumbered sections 18231 to 18239 of this title, respectively.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective Dec. 1, 1994, except as otherwise provided, see [section 1691 of Pub. L. 103–337](/us/pl/103/337/s1691), set out as a note under [section 10001 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s10001).
