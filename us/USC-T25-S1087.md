# § 1087. Omitted

##### § 1087. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–655, § 7](/us/pl/89/655/s7), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 906](/us/stat/80/906), which related to tax exemption of funds distributed to individual members of the Quileute and Hoh Tribes under the provisions of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
