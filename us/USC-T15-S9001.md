# § 9001. Definitions

##### § 9001. Definitions

(1) the terms “Administration” and “Administrator” mean the Small Business Administration and the Administrator thereof, respectively; and

(2) the term “small business concern” has the meaning given the term in [section 632 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s632).

*([Pub. L. 116–136, div. A, title I, § 1101](/us/pl/116/136/dA/tI/s1101), Mar. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 286](/us/stat/134/286).)*
