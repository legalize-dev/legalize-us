# § 20. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–588, § 3](/us/pl/101/588/s3), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2880](/us/stat/104/2880)

##### § 20. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–588, § 3](/us/pl/101/588/s3), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2880](/us/stat/104/2880)

Section, [act Oct. 15, 1914, ch. 323, § 10](/us/act/1914-10-15/ch323/s10), [38 Stat. 734](/us/stat/38/734), related to a $50,000 yearly, aggregate limitation on purchases and contracts between a common carrier and any entity with whom such carrier has any form of interlocking directorate, etc., required filing with ICC of a full statement of transactions excluded from such limitation, and set forth fines and penalties for violation of such limitation.
