# § 70033. Limitation on application to foreign vessels

##### § 70033. Limitation on application to foreign vessels

(1) innocent passage through the territorial sea of the United States; or

(2) transit through the navigable waters of the United States that form a part of an international strait.

*(Added [Pub. L. 115–282, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/115/282/tIV/s401/a), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4261](/us/stat/132/4261); amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8507(a)(6), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4753](/us/stat/134/4753).)*
