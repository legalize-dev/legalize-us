# § 766. Definition of covered school

##### § 766. Definition of covered school

In this subchapter, the term “covered school” means an elementary school or secondary school (as such terms are defined in [section 7801 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s7801)) or an institution of higher education.

*([Pub. L. 93–112, title II, § 206](/us/pl/93/112/tII/s206), as added [Pub. L. 113–128, title IV, § 437](/us/pl/113/128/tIV/s437), July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1671](/us/stat/128/1671); amended [Pub. L. 114–95, title IX, § 9215(mmm)(2)](/us/pl/114/95/tIX/s9215/mmm/2), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2188](/us/stat/129/2188).)*
