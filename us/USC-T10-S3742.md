# § 3742. Adjustment of threshold amount of covered contract

##### § 3742. Adjustment of threshold amount of covered contract

Effective on October 1 of each year that is divisible by five, the amount set forth in [section 3741(2) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s3741/2) shall be adjusted in accordance with [section 1908 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s1908).

*(Added and amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1832(a)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1832/a), (b)(4), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4218](/us/stat/134/4218), 4219.)*
