# § 616. Inspectors of carcasses, etc., meat of which is intended for export; certificates of condition

##### § 616. Inspectors of carcasses, etc., meat of which is intended for export; certificates of condition

For the purpose of [section 615 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s615) the Secretary may appoint inspectors who shall be authorized to give an official certificate stating the condition in which said cattle, sheep, swine, goats, horses, mules, or other equines, and the meat thereof, are found.

*([Mar. 4, 1907, ch. 2907](/us/act/1907-03-04/ch2907), title I, § 16, formerly 14th par., [34 Stat. 1263](/us/stat/34/1263); renumbered § 16 and amended [Pub. L. 90–201](/us/pl/90/201), §§ 1, 3(b), 12(a), Dec. 15, 1967, [81 Stat. 584](/us/stat/81/584), 588, 592.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 84 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s84).

#### Amendments

1967—[Pub. L. 90–201](/us/pl/90/201), §§ 3(b), 12(a), struck out “of Agriculture” after “Secretary”, and included horses, mules, or other equines in the list of animals.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**
