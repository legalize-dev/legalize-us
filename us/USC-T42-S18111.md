# § 18111. Definitions

##### § 18111. Definitions

Unless specifically provided for otherwise, the definitions contained in [section 300gg–91 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300gg–91) shall apply with respect to this title.11 See References in Text note below.

*([Pub. L. 111–148, title I, § 1551](/us/pl/111/148/tI/s1551), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 258](/us/stat/124/258).)*
