# §§ 3261 to 3266. Omitted

##### §§ 3261 to 3266. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 3261, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6101](/us/pl/89/10/tVI/s6101), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 270](/us/stat/102/270), provided that this part could be cited as the “Secondary Schools Basic Skills Demonstration Assistance Act of 1988”.

A prior section 3261, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 751](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s751), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/95/561/tVII/s701), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2282](/us/stat/92/2282); amended [Pub. L. 98–511, title II, § 201](/us/pl/98/511/tII/s201), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2385](/us/stat/98/2385), related to Office of Bilingual Education and Minority Languages Affairs, prior to the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297).

Section 3262, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6102](/us/pl/89/10/tVI/s6102), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 270](/us/stat/102/270), stated purpose of this part.

A prior section 3262, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VII, § 752](/us/pl/89/10/tVII/s752), as added [Pub. L. 98–511, title II, § 201](/us/pl/98/511/tII/s201), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2386](/us/stat/98/2386), related to National Advisory and Coordinating Council on Bilingual Education, prior to the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297).

Section 3263, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6103](/us/pl/89/10/tVI/s6103), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 270](/us/stat/102/270); amended [Pub. L. 101–600, § 5](/us/pl/101/600/s5), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3046](/us/stat/104/3046), authorized appropriations.

Section 3264, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6104](/us/pl/89/10/tVI/s6104), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 271](/us/stat/102/271), related to grants to local educational agencies.

Section 3265, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6105](/us/pl/89/10/tVI/s6105), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 271](/us/stat/102/271), related to authorized activities.

Section 3266, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VI, § 6106](/us/pl/89/10/tVI/s6106), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 272](/us/stat/102/272), related to applications for grants.
