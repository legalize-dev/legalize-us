# § 3562. Disposition of recovered funds

##### § 3562. Disposition of recovered funds

(a) Availability of Funds for Recovery Audits and Activities Program.— Funds collected under a program carried out by an executive agency under [section 3352(i) of this title](/us/usc/t31/s3352/i) shall be available to the executive agency to reimburse the actual expenses incurred by the executive agency in the administration of the program.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2020—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 116–117](/us/pl/116/117) substituted “section 3352(i)” for “section 3561” and “agency to reimburse” for “agency for the following purposes: (1) To reimburse” and struck out par. (2) which read as follows: “To pay contractors for services under the program in accordance with the guidance issued under [section 3561(c)(5) of this title](/us/usc/t31/s3561/c/5).”

2010—[Pub. L. 111–204](/us/pl/111/204) repealed section but provided that subsec. (a) was to continue in effect, with certain exceptions related to programs carried out under [section 3561 of this title](/us/usc/t31/s3561). Prior to amendment, in addition to subsec. (a), section contained subsecs. (b) and (c) which related to treatment of funds not used for programs under [section 3561 of this title](/us/usc/t31/s3561) and priority of other authorized dispositions, respectively.
