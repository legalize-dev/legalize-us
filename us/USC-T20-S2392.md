# § 2392. Authority to make payments

##### § 2392. Authority to make payments

Any authority to make payments or to enter into contracts under this chapter shall be available only to such extent or in such amounts as are provided in advance in appropriation Acts.

*([Pub. L. 88–210, title II, § 212](/us/pl/88/210/tII/s212), formerly title III, § 312, as added [Pub. L. 109–270, § 1(b)](/us/pl/109/270/s1/b), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 743](/us/stat/120/743); renumbered title II, § 212, [Pub. L. 115–224, title II, § 201(a)(5)](/us/pl/115/224/tII/s201/a/5), (6), July 31, 2018, [132 Stat. 1621](/us/stat/132/1621).)*
