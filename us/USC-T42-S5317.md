# § 5317. Liquidation of superseded or inactive programs

##### § 5317. Liquidation of superseded or inactive programs

The Secretary is authorized to transfer the assets and liabilities of any program which is superseded or inactive by reason of this chapter to the revolving fund for liquidating programs established pursuant to title II of the Independent Offices Appropriation Act, 1955 ([Public Law 83–428](/us/pl/83/428); [68 Stat. 272](/us/stat/68/272), 295) [[12 U.S.C. 1701g–5](/us/usc/t12/s1701g–5)].

*([Pub. L. 93–383, title I, § 117(b)](/us/pl/93/383/tI/s117/b), Aug. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 653](/us/stat/88/653); [Pub. L. 98–479, title II, § 204(k)(2)](/us/pl/98/479/tII/s204/k/2), Oct. 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 2233](/us/stat/98/2233).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [Pub. L. 93–383](/us/pl/93/383), Aug. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 633](/us/stat/88/633), which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Tables.

#### Amendments

1984—[Pub. L. 98–479](/us/pl/98/479) substituted “title II of the Independent Offices Appropriation Act, 1955 ([Public Law 83–428](/us/pl/83/428); [68 Stat. 272](/us/stat/68/272), 295)” for “title II of the Independent Offices Appropriation Act of 1965 ([Public Law 81–428](/us/pl/81/428); [68 Stat. 272](/us/stat/68/272), 295)”.
