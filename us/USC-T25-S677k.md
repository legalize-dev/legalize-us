# § 677k. Omitted

##### § 677k. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 12](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s12), [68 Stat. 874](/us/stat/68/874), which related to adjustment of debts in making per capita payments to mixed-blood members and execution of mortgages on property, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
