# § 1772b. Omitted

##### § 1772b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–228, § 4](/us/pl/100/228/s4), Dec. 31, 1987, [101 Stat. 1557](/us/stat/101/1557), which set forth provisions relating to the effective date of [section 1772c of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1772c), including the making of certain findings by the Secretary of the Interior, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
