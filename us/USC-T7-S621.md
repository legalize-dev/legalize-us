# § 621. Machinery belting processed from cotton; exemption from tax

##### § 621. Machinery belting processed from cotton; exemption from tax

The provisions of [section 616 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s616), shall not apply to articles of machinery belting processed wholly or in chief value from cotton, if such processing was completed prior to January 1, 1930.

*([June 26, 1934, ch. 753, § 1](/us/act/1934-06-26/ch753/s1), [48 Stat. 1223](/us/stat/48/1223).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was not enacted as part of the Agricultural Adjustment Act which comprises this chapter.

#### Constitutionality

Unconstitutionality of processing and floor stock taxes, see note set out under [section 616 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s616).
