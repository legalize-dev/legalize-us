# § 941k. Omitted

##### § 941k. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–116, § 13](/us/pl/103/116/s13), Oct. 27, 1993, [107 Stat. 1136](/us/stat/107/1136), which related to acquisition or disposal of real estate outside the reservation by the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
