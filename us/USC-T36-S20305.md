# § 20305. Annual meeting

##### § 20305. Annual meeting

The corporation shall hold an annual meeting at a place in the United States as may be designated.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1287](/us/stat/112/1287).)*
