# § 672. Omitted

##### § 672. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 10, 1912, ch. 284](/us/act/1912-08-10/ch284), [37 Stat. 293](/us/stat/37/293), established Wind Cave National Game Preserve. Preserve abolished and property transferred to Wind Cave National Park, to be administered by Secretary of the Interior for purposes expressed in this section, see [section 141b of this title](/us/usc/t16/s141b).
