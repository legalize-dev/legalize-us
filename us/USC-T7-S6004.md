# § 6004. Regulations

##### § 6004. Regulations

The Secretary may issue such regulations as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 101–624, title XIX, § 1909](/us/pl/101/624/tXIX/s1909), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3841](/us/stat/104/3841).)*
