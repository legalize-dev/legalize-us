# § 294h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–4, title V, § 501(b)(2)](/us/pl/113/4/tV/s501/b/2), Mar. 7, 2013, [127 Stat. 101](/us/stat/127/101)

##### § 294h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–4, title V, § 501(b)(2)](/us/pl/113/4/tV/s501/b/2), Mar. 7, 2013, [127 Stat. 101](/us/stat/127/101)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title VII, § 758](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tVII/s758), as added [Pub. L. 109–162, title V, § 503](/us/pl/109/162/tV/s503), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3024](/us/stat/119/3024); amended [Pub. L. 109–271, § 4(c)](/us/pl/109/271/s4/c), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 758](/us/stat/120/758), provided for grants for interdisciplinary training and education on domestic violence and other types of violence and abuse.

A prior section 294h, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title VII, § 735](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tVII/s735), as added Oct. 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–484, title IV, § 401(b)(3)](/us/pl/94/484/tIV/s401/b/3), [90 Stat. 2263](/us/stat/90/2263); amended Aug. 1, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–83, title III, § 307(c)(5)](/us/pl/95/83/tIII/s307/c/5), [91 Stat. 390](/us/stat/91/390); Aug. 13, 1981, [Pub. L. 97–35, title XXVII, § 2709(e)(4)(B)](/us/pl/97/35/tXXVII/s2709/e/4/B), [95 Stat. 911](/us/stat/95/911); Nov. 16, 1990, [Pub. L. 101–597, title IV, § 401(b)[(a)]](/us/pl/101/597/tIV/s401/b), [104 Stat. 3035](/us/stat/104/3035), related to functions, powers, and duties of the Secretary under the Federal student loan insurance program, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 102–408](/us/pl/102/408). See [section 292j of this title](/us/usc/t42/s292j).
