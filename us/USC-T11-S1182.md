# § 1182. Definitions

##### § 1182. Definitions

(1) Debtor.— The term “debtor” means a small business debtor.

(2) Debtor in possession.— The term “debtor in possession” means the debtor, unless removed as debtor in possession under [section 1185(a) of this title](/us/usc/t11/s1185/a).
