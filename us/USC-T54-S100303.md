# § 100303. Effect on other laws

##### § 100303. Effect on other laws

This chapter and sections 100101(a), 100751(a), 100752, 100753, and 102101 of this title do not affect or modify [section 100902(a) of this title](/us/usc/t54/s100902/a).

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3097](/us/stat/128/3097).)*
