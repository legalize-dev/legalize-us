# § 430f–12. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 430f–12. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out sections 430f–6 to 430f–12 of this title, including $3,000,000 for the construction of an interpretive center under [section 430f–5(d) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s430f–5/d).

*([Pub. L. 106–271, § 7](/us/pl/106/271/s7), formerly § 8, Sept. 22, 2000, [114 Stat. 796](/us/stat/114/796); renumbered § 7, [Pub. L. 110–161, div. F, title I, § 127(5)](/us/pl/110/161/dF/tI/s127/5), Dec. 26, 2007, [121 Stat. 2122](/us/stat/121/2122).)*
