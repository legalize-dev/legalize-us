# § 873a. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992)

##### § 873a. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992)

Section, [act Feb. 12, 1940, ch. 25, § 1](/us/act/1940-02-12/ch25/s1), [54 Stat. 22](/us/stat/54/22), which related to rules governing civil cases. See [section 2072 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s2072), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see section 38 of act June 25, 1948, set out as an Effective Date note preceding [section 1 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s1), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
