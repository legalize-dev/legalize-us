# § 3777. Investigation of accident or injury giving rise to claim

##### § 3777. Investigation of accident or injury giving rise to claim

(1) an investigation of the accident or injury giving rise to the claim has been completed, which shall include a hearing by the Board of Local Inspectors of the Commission; and

(2) the basis for the claim has been laid before the Commission.

*([Pub. L. 96–70, title I, § 1417](/us/pl/96/70/tI/s1417), Sept. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 487](/us/stat/93/487); [Pub. L. 104–201, div. C, title XXXV, § 3544](/us/pl/104/201/dC/tXXXV/s3544), Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2867](/us/stat/110/2867).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1996—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 104–201](/us/pl/104/201) amended par. (1) generally. Prior to amendment, par. (1) read as follows: “an investigation by the competent authorities of the accident or injury giving rise to the claim has been completed; and”.
