# § 6814. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 6814. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

(b) Administrative expenses Funds appropriated under subsection (a) may not be used for the payment of the expenses or expenditures of the PromoFlor Council in administering a provision of an order.

*([Pub. L. 103–190, § 15](/us/pl/103/190/s15), Dec. 14, 1993, [107 Stat. 2289](/us/stat/107/2289).)*
