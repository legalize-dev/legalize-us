# § 11048. Regulations

##### § 11048. Regulations

The Administrator may prescribe such regulations as may be necessary to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 99–499, title III, § 328](/us/pl/99/499/tIII/s328), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1757](/us/stat/100/1757).)*
