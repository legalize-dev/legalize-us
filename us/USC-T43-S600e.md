# § 600e. Authorization of appropriations for public recreation facilities

##### § 600e. Authorization of appropriations for public recreation facilities

There are authorized to be appropriated such amounts, but not more than $1,100,000, as may be necessary for the investigation, preparation of plans, construction and acquisition of lands authorized in [section 600d of this title](/us/usc/t43/s600d).

*([Pub. L. 88–536, § 2](/us/pl/88/536/s2), Aug. 31, 1964, [78 Stat. 744](/us/stat/78/744).)*
