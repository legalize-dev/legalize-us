# § 2216. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–310, title II, § 221(a)](/us/pl/92/310/tII/s221/a), June 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 205](/us/stat/86/205)

##### § 2216. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–310, title II, § 221(a)](/us/pl/92/310/tII/s221/a), June 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 205](/us/stat/86/205)

Section, R.S. § 524; acts [Mar. 2, 1895, ch. 177, § 5](/us/act/1895-03-02/ch177/s5), [28 Stat. 807](/us/stat/28/807); [May 10, 1934, ch. 277, § 512(b)](/us/act/1934-05-10/ch277/s512/b), [48 Stat. 759](/us/stat/48/759), related to the bond of the chief clerk of the Department of Agriculture.

Section was formerly classified to [section 520 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s520) prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).
