# §§ 2622 to 2624. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–83, § 10(b)](/us/pl/90/83/s10/b), Sept. 11, 1967, [81 Stat. 224](/us/stat/81/224)

##### §§ 2622 to 2624. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–83, § 10(b)](/us/pl/90/83/s10/b), Sept. 11, 1967, [81 Stat. 224](/us/stat/81/224)

Section 2622, [Pub. L. 89–673, § 3](/us/pl/89/673/s3), Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 952](/us/stat/80/952), prohibited request for or encouragement of a tender of a gift or decoration. See [section 7342(b) of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s7342/b), Government Organization and Employees.

Section 2623, [Pub. L. 89–673, § 4](/us/pl/89/673/s4), Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 952](/us/stat/80/952), permitted acceptance of a gift of minimal value. See [section 7342(c) of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s7342/c).

Section 2624, [Pub. L. 89–673, § 5](/us/pl/89/673/s5), Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 952](/us/stat/80/952), permitted acceptance of decorations tendered in recognition of active field service in time of combat operations. See [section 7342(d) of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s7342/d).
