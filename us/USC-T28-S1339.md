# § 1339. Postal matters

##### § 1339. Postal matters

The district courts shall have original jurisdiction of any civil action arising under any Act of Congress relating to the postal service.

*([June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 932](/us/stat/62/932).)*

Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 41(6) ([Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231, § 24](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231/s24), par. 6, [36 Stat. 1092](/us/stat/36/1092)).
