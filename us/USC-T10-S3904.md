# [§ 3904. Omitted]

##### [§ 3904. Omitted]

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1838(b)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1838/b), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4242](/us/stat/134/4242), initially transferred [section 2362 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s2362) to this section, to become effective Jan. 1, 2022. Subsequently, [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XVII, § 1701(v)(2)(A)](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tXVII/s1701/v/2/A), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 2154](/us/stat/135/2154), amended [section 1838(b) of Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283/s1838/b), effective as if included therein, by striking out the directive to make the transfer, thereby omitting this section before it took effect. Another amendment by [Pub. L. 117–81](/us/pl/117/81) resulted in the transfer of section 2362 to [section 4144 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s4144).
