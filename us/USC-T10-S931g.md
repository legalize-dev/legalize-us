# § 931g. Art. 131g. Wrongful interference with adverse administrative proceeding

##### § 931g. Art. 131g. Wrongful interference with adverse administrative proceeding

(1) to influence, impede, or obstruct the conduct of the proceeding; or

(2) otherwise to obstruct the due administration of justice;
