# §§ 1331 to 1336. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–369, div. B, title VI, § 2663(f)](/us/pl/98/369/dB/tVI/s2663/f), July 18, 1984, [98 Stat. 1168](/us/stat/98/1168)

##### §§ 1331 to 1336. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–369, div. B, title VI, § 2663(f)](/us/pl/98/369/dB/tVI/s2663/f), July 18, 1984, [98 Stat. 1168](/us/stat/98/1168)

Section 1331, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XIII, § 1301](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXIII/s1301), as added [Aug. 10, 1946, ch. 951](/us/act/1946-08-10/ch951), title III, § 306, [60 Stat. 982](/us/stat/60/982); amended 1949 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 1, eff. Aug. 19, 1949, 14 F.R. 5225, [63 Stat. 1065](/us/stat/63/1065), provided for administration of this chapter by Secretary of Labor.

Section 1332, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XIII, § 1302](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXIII/s1302), as added [Aug. 10, 1946, ch. 951](/us/act/1946-08-10/ch951), title III, § 306, [60 Stat. 982](/us/stat/60/982); amended [July 16, 1949, ch. 342](/us/act/1949-07-16/ch342), §§ 1–3, [63 Stat. 445](/us/stat/63/445), defined “reconversion period”, “compensation”, “Federal maritime service”, and “Federal maritime wages”.

Section 1333, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XIII, § 1303](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXIII/s1303), as added [Aug. 10, 1946, ch. 951](/us/act/1946-08-10/ch951), title III, § 306, [60 Stat. 982](/us/stat/60/982); amended 1949 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 1, eff. Aug. 20, 1949, 14 F.R. 5225, [63 Stat. 1065](/us/stat/63/1065), related to compensation for seamen, agreements with states, payments in absence of agreements, wage information, and determination of wages.

Section 1334, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XIII, § 1304](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXIII/s1304), as added [Aug. 10, 1946, ch. 951](/us/act/1946-08-10/ch951), title III, § 306, [60 Stat. 982](/us/stat/60/982); amended 1949 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 1, eff. Aug. 19, 1949, 14 F.R. 5225, [63 Stat. 1065](/us/stat/63/1065), related to review of determinations and reports.

Section 1335, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XIII, § 1305](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXIII/s1305), as added [Aug. 10, 1946, ch. 951](/us/act/1946-08-10/ch951), title III, § 306, [60 Stat. 982](/us/stat/60/982); amended 1949 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 1, eff. Aug. 19, 1949, 14 F.R. 5225, [63 Stat. 1065](/us/stat/63/1065), related to payments to States, certification of such payments by Secretary of Labor to Secretary of the Treasury, and return of unused funds.

Section 1336, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XIII, § 1306](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXIII/s1306), as added [Aug. 10, 1946, ch. 951](/us/act/1946-08-10/ch951), title III, § 306, [60 Stat. 982](/us/stat/60/982); amended 1949 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 1, eff. Aug. 19, 1949, 14 F.R. 5225, [63 Stat. 1065](/us/stat/63/1065), related to penalties.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective July 18, 1984, but such repeal shall not be construed as changing or affecting any right, liability, status, or interpretation which existed before that date, see [section 2664(b) of Pub. L. 98–369](/us/pl/98/369/s2664/b), set out as an Effective Date of 1984 Amendment note under [section 401 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s401).
