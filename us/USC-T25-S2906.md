# § 2906. Use of English

##### § 2906. Use of English

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as precluding the use of Federal funds to teach English to Native Americans.

*([Pub. L. 101–477, title I, § 107](/us/pl/101/477/tI/s107), Oct. 30, 1990, [104 Stat. 1156](/us/stat/104/1156).)*
