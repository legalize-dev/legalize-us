# § 1643. Application of customs reorganization act

##### § 1643. Application of customs reorganization act

The rights, privileges, powers, and duties vested in or imposed upon the Secretary of the Treasury by this chapter shall be subject to the provisions of subdivision (a) of [section 2073 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2073).

*([June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 643, [46 Stat. 761](/us/stat/46/761).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Subdivision (a) of [section 2073 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2073), referred to in text, was repealed by [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1263, § 10](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1263/s10), [68 Stat. 1229](/us/stat/68/1229).
