# §§ 2711 to 2713. Omitted

##### §§ 2711 to 2713. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 2711 and 2712 were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519). See section 6311 et seq. of this title.

Section 2711, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1005](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1005), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 141](/us/stat/102/141); amended [Pub. L. 102–73, title VIII, § 802(a)](/us/pl/102/73/tVIII/s802/a), July 25, 1991, [105 Stat. 360](/us/stat/105/360), related to basic grants.

A prior section 2711, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 111](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s111), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title I, § 101(a)](/us/pl/95/561/tI/s101/a), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2153](/us/stat/92/2153); amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 301(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/88/tIII/s301/a/1), title V, §§ 507, 508(k), Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677](/us/stat/93/677), 692, 694, related to amount and eligibility for grants, prior to the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297).

Section 2712, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1006](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1006), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 146](/us/stat/102/146), related to grants for local educational agencies in counties with especially high concentrations of children from low-income families.

A prior section 2712, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 112](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s112), as added [Pub. L. 95–561, title I, § 101(a)](/us/pl/95/561/tI/s101/a), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2158](/us/stat/92/2158), related to treatment of earnings for purposes of aid to families with dependent children, prior to the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297).

Section 2713, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(10)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s101/a/10), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 501](/us/stat/88/501); [Pub. L. 95–561, title I, § 101(d)](/us/pl/95/561/tI/s101/d), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2200](/us/stat/92/2200); [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 301(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/88/tIII/s301/a/1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677](/us/stat/93/677), 692, which was enacted as part of Education Amendments of 1974, and formerly classified to [section 241b–1 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s241b–1), related to authorization of appropriations to assist otherwise lower fiscal year allotment levels under former provisions of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965.
