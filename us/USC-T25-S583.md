# § 583. Omitted

##### § 583. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–206, § 3](/us/pl/92/206/s3), Dec. 18, 1971, [85 Stat. 737](/us/stat/85/737), which credited funds to the Northwestern Bands of Shoshone Indians for certain claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
