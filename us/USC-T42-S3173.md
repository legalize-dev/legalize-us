# § 3173. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–373, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/108/373/tIV/s401/a), Oct. 27, 2004, [118 Stat. 1767](/us/stat/118/1767)

##### § 3173. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–373, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/108/373/tIV/s401/a), Oct. 27, 2004, [118 Stat. 1767](/us/stat/118/1767)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–136, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/89/136/tIV/s403), as added [Pub. L. 105–393, title I, § 102(a)](/us/pl/105/393/tI/s102/a), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3609](/us/stat/112/3609), related to incentives for projects in economic development districts.

A prior section 3173, [Pub. L. 89–136, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/89/136/tIV/s405), as added [Pub. L. 94–487, title I, § 117](/us/pl/94/487/tI/s117), Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2335](/us/stat/90/2335), directed furnishing of unemployment rate information by either Federal Government or State, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–393, § 102(a)](/us/pl/105/393/s102/a).

A prior [section 403 of Pub. L. 89–136](/us/pl/89/136/s403) was classified to [section 3171 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3171) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–393, § 102(a)](/us/pl/105/393/s102/a).
