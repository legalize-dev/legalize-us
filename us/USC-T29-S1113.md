# § 1113. Limitation of actions

##### § 1113. Limitation of actions

(1) six years after (A) the date of the last action which constituted a part of the breach or violation, or (B) in the case of an omission the latest date on which the fiduciary could have cured the breach or violation, or

(2) three years after the earliest date on which the plaintiff had actual knowledge of the breach or violation;

*([Pub. L. 93–406, title I, § 413](/us/pl/93/406/tI/s413), Sept. 2, 1974, [88 Stat. 889](/us/stat/88/889); [Pub. L. 100–203, title IX, § 9342(b)](/us/pl/100/203/tIX/s9342/b), Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–371](/us/stat/101/1330-371); [Pub. L. 101–239, title VII](/us/pl/101/239/tVII), §§ 7881(j)(4), 7894(e)(5), Dec. 19, 1989, [103 Stat. 2443](/us/stat/103/2443), 2450.)*
