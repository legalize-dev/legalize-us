# § 12724. Authorization

##### § 12724. Authorization

(1) not more than $14,000,000 for fiscal year 1993, and $25,000,000 for fiscal year 1994, shall be for community housing partnership activities authorized under [section 12773 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12773); and

(2) not more than $11,000,000 for fiscal year 1993, and $22,000,000 for fiscal year 1994, shall be for activities in support of State and local housing strategies authorized under part C.

*([Pub. L. 101–625, title II, § 205](/us/pl/101/625/tII/s205), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 4096](/us/stat/104/4096); [Pub. L. 102–550, title II, § 201](/us/pl/102/550/tII/s201), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 3751](/us/stat/106/3751); [Pub. L. 103–120, § 5](/us/pl/103/120/s5), Oct. 27, 1993, [107 Stat. 1148](/us/stat/107/1148).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1993—[Pub. L. 103–120](/us/pl/103/120) substituted “$25,000,000 for fiscal year 1994” for “$14,000,000 for fiscal year 1994” in par. (1) and “$22,000,000 for fiscal year 1994” for “$11,000,000 for fiscal year 1994” in par. (2).

1992—[Pub. L. 102–550](/us/pl/102/550) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section read as follows: “There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $1,000,000,000 for fiscal year 1991, and $2,086,000,000 for fiscal year 1992, of which—

“(1) not more than $14,000,000 for fiscal year 1991, and $14,000,000 for fiscal year 1992, shall be for community housing partnership activities authorized under [section 12773 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12773); and

“(2) not more than $11,000,000 for fiscal year 1991, and $11,000,000 for fiscal year 1992, shall be for activities in support of State and local housing strategies authorized under part C of this subchapter.”
