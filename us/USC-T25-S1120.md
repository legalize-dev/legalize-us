# § 1120. Omitted

##### § 1120. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–659, § 10](/us/pl/89/659/s10), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 910](/us/stat/80/910), which exempted distributed funds from Federal and State income taxes and provided for payment of certain costs, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
