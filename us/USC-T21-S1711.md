# § 1711. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1711. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this chapter except activities otherwise specified, to remain available until expended, $18,400,000 for each of fiscal years 2018 through 2023.

*([Pub. L. 105–277, div. C, title VII](/us/pl/105/277/dC/tVII), §§ 714, 715, Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–693](/us/stat/112/2681-693); [Pub. L. 109–469, title VI](/us/pl/109/469/tVI), §§ 601, 602, Dec. 29, 2006, [120 Stat. 3533](/us/stat/120/3533); [Pub. L. 115–271, title VIII, § 8202](/us/pl/115/271/tVIII/s8202), Oct. 24, 2018, [132 Stat. 4110](/us/stat/132/4110).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title VII of [Pub. L. 105–277, div. C](/us/pl/105/277/dC), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–670](/us/stat/112/2681-670), which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of title VII to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s1701) and Tables.

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–271, § 8202(b)(2)](/us/pl/115/271/s8202/b/2), repealed [Pub. L. 105–277, § 715](/us/pl/105/277/s715). See 1998 Amendment note below.

[Pub. L. 115–271, § 8202(b)(1)](/us/pl/115/271/s8202/b/1), substituted “$18,400,000 for each of fiscal years 2018 through 2023” for “such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 2006 through 2010”.

[Pub. L. 115–271, § 8202(a)](/us/pl/115/271/s8202/a), revived and restored this section as in effect on Sept. 29, 2003, and as amended by [Pub. L. 109–469](/us/pl/109/469) and [Pub. L. 112–166](/us/pl/112/166). See Reauthorization of the Office of National Drug Control Policy note set out under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s1701).

2006—[Pub. L. 109–469, § 602](/us/pl/109/469/s602), amended [Pub. L. 105–277, § 715](/us/pl/105/277/s715), which provided for the repeal of this section. See 1998 Amendment note below.

[Pub. L. 109–469, § 601](/us/pl/109/469/s601), substituted “chapter except activities otherwise specified,” for “chapter,” and “2006 through 2010” for “1999 through 2003”.

1998—[Pub. L. 105–277, § 715](/us/pl/105/277/s715), as amended by [Pub. L. 109–469, § 602](/us/pl/109/469/s602), which provided for the repeal of this section effective Sept. 30, 2010, was repealed by [Pub. L. 115–271, § 8202(b)(2)](/us/pl/115/271/s8202/b/2). See former [section 1712 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s1712).
