# § 11435. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 11435. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part $85,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2017 through 2020.

*([Pub. L. 100–77, title VII, § 726](/us/pl/100/77/tVII/s726), as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title X, § 1032](/us/pl/107/110/tX/s1032), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 2006](/us/stat/115/2006); amended [Pub. L. 110–289, div. B, title IX, § 2901(a)](/us/pl/110/289/dB/tIX/s2901/a), July 30, 2008, [122 Stat. 2875](/us/stat/122/2875); [Pub. L. 114–95, title IX, § 9106](/us/pl/114/95/tIX/s9106), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2137](/us/stat/129/2137).)*

L. 103–382](/us/pl/103/382).

#### Amendments

2015—[Pub. L. 114–95, § 9106](/us/pl/114/95/s9106), amended section generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “For the purpose of carrying out this part, there are authorized to be appropriated $100,000,000 for fiscal year 2009 and such sums as may be necessary for each subsequent fiscal year.”

2008—[Pub. L. 110–289](/us/pl/110/289) substituted “$100,000,000 for fiscal year 2009 and such sums as may be necessary for each subsequent fiscal year” for “$70,000,000 for fiscal year 2002 and such sums as may be necessary for each of fiscal years 2003 through 2007”.
