# § 12521. Increased penalties for drug-dealing in “drug-free” zones

##### § 12521. Increased penalties for drug-dealing in “drug-free” zones

Pursuant to its authority under [section 994 of title 28](/us/usc/t28/s994), the United States Sentencing Commission shall amend its sentencing guidelines to provide an appropriate enhancement for a defendant convicted of violating [section 860 of title 21](/us/usc/t21/s860).

*([Pub. L. 103–322, title IX, § 90102](/us/pl/103/322/tIX/s90102), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1987](/us/stat/108/1987).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 14051 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s14051), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Section is comprised of [section 90102 of Pub. L. 103–322](/us/pl/103/322/s90102) which is also listed in a table relating to sentencing guidelines set out under [section 994 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s994), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
