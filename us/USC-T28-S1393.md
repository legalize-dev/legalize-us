# [§ 1393. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–702, title X, § 1001(a)](/us/pl/100/702/tX/s1001/a), Nov. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 4664](/us/stat/102/4664)]

##### [§ 1393. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–702, title X, § 1001(a)](/us/pl/100/702/tX/s1001/a), Nov. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 4664](/us/stat/102/4664)]

Section, [act June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 935](/us/stat/62/935), related to divisional venue in civil cases of a single defendant or defendants in different divisions.

[Pub. L. 100–702, title X, § 1001(b)](/us/pl/100/702/tX/s1001/b), Nov. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 4664](/us/stat/102/4664), provided that: “The amendments made by this section [repealing this section] take effect 90 days after the date of enactment of this Act [Nov. 19, 1988].”
