#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 5](/us/pl/93/531/s5), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1714](/us/stat/88/1714); [Pub. L. 93–531, § 30(a)](/us/pl/93/531/s30/a), as added [Pub. L. 96–305, § 11](/us/pl/96/305/s11), July 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 934](/us/stat/94/934), which authorized certain recommendations and provided that they be construed as discretionary, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
