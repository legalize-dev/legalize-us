#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the date of the assessment of annual permit fees in accordance with [section 6214(f) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s6214/f) (as certified to Congress by the Secretary of Agriculture), see [section 6214(k) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s6214/k). The tiered fee schedule set out in [section 6214(f) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s6214/f) was implemented effective Jan. 1, 2016.

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 106–291, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/106/291/tVI/s601), Oct. 11, 2000, [114 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/114/1014), which provided that title VI of [Pub. L. 106–291](/us/pl/106/291), enacting this chapter, could be cited as the “Cabin User Fee Fairness Act of 2000”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 113–291, div. B, title XXX, § 3024(k)](/us/pl/113/291/dB/tXXX/s3024/k), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3766](/us/stat/128/3766), effective on the date of the assessment of annual permit fees in accordance with [section 6214(f) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s6214/f) (as certified to Congress by the Secretary of Agriculture).
