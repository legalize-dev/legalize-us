# § 11172. Grants for technical assistance

##### § 11172. Grants for technical assistance

The Administrator may make grants to and contracts with public and private agencies, organizations, and individuals to provide technical assistance to States, units of general local government, Indian tribal governments, local private entities or agencies, or any combination thereof, to carry out the projects for which grants are made under [section 11171 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s11171).

*([Pub. L. 93–415, title II, § 262](/us/pl/93/415/tII/s262), as added [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title II, § 12212](/us/pl/107/273/dC/tII/s12212), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1891](/us/stat/116/1891).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 5666 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s5666), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 262 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s262) was classified to [section 5665a of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s5665a), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 107–273](/us/pl/107/273).

Another prior [section 262 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s262) was renumbered section 299A and is classified to [section 11182 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s11182).
