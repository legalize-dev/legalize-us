# § 713e. Omitted

##### § 713e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–165, § 7](/us/pl/98/165/s7), Nov. 22, 1983, [97 Stat. 1067](/us/stat/97/1067), which related to membership rolls and voting rights of members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
