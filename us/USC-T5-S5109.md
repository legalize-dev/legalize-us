# § 5109. Positions classified by statute

##### § 5109. Positions classified by statute

(a) The position held by an employee of the Department of Agriculture while he, under [section 450d of title 7](/us/usc/t7/s450d),11 See References in Text note below. is designated and vested with a delegated regulatory function or part thereof shall be classified in accordance with this chapter, but not lower than GS–14.

(b)

(1) The position held by a fully experienced and qualified railroad safety inspector of the Department of Transportation shall be classified in accordance with this chapter, but not lower than GS–12.

(2) The position held by a railroad safety specialist of the Department shall be classified in accordance with this chapter, but not lower than GS–13.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 450d of title 7](/us/usc/t7/s450d), referred to in subsec. (a), probably means section 2 of [act Apr. 4, 1940, ch. 75](/us/act/1940-04-04/ch75), which was formerly classified to [section 450d of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s450d), Agriculture, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as [section 2204–2 of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s2204–2).

#### Amendments

1998—Subsecs. (b), (c). [Pub. L. 105–206](/us/pl/105/206) redesignated subsec. (c) as (b) and struck out former subsec. (b) which read as follows: “The position held by the employee appointed under section 7802(b) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 shall be considered a position classified above GS–15 pursuant to section 5108.”

1994—Subsec. (c). [Pub. L. 103–272](/us/pl/103/272) added subsec. (c).

1990—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 101–509](/us/pl/101/509) substituted “shall be considered a position classified above GS–15 pursuant to section 5108” for “is classified at GS–18, and is in addition to the number of positions authorized by [section 5108(a) of this title](/us/usc/t5/s5108/a)”.

1986—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 99–514](/us/pl/99/514) substituted “Internal Revenue Code of 1986” for “Internal Revenue Code of 1954”.

1978—Subsecs. (b), (c). [Pub. L. 95–454, § 906(b)](/us/pl/95/454/s906/b), redesignated subsec. (c) as (b). Former subsec. (b), which related to classification of position held by an employee appointed under [section 1104(a)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t5/s1104/a/2), was struck out.

1974—Subsec. (c). [Pub. L. 93–406](/us/pl/93/406) added subsec. (c). A prior subsec. (c) was repealed by [Pub. L. 91–34](/us/pl/91/34).

1969—Subsec. (c). [Pub. L. 91–34](/us/pl/91/34) repealed subsec. (c) provisions classifying positions on National Zoological Park police force authorized pursuant to [section 193n of title 40](/us/usc/t40/s193n).
