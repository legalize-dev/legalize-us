# § 964. Additional penalties

##### § 964. Additional penalties

Any penalty imposed for violation of this subchapter shall be in addition to, and not in lieu of, any civil or administrative penalty or sanction authorized by law.

*([Pub. L. 91–513, title III, § 1014](/us/pl/91/513/tIII/s1014), Oct. 27, 1970, [84 Stat. 1291](/us/stat/84/1291).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective on first day of seventh calendar month that begins after Oct. 26, 1970, see 1105(a) of [Pub. L. 91–513](/us/pl/91/513), set out as a under [section 951 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s951).
