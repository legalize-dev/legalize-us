# § 4819. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 4819. Authorization of appropriations

(a) There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for the Secretary to carry out this chapter, subject to reimbursement from the Board under [section 4809(c)(3)(B)(iv) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s4809/c/3/B/iv).

(b) Sums appropriated to carry out this chapter shall not be available for payment of an expense or expenditure incurred by the Board in administering an order.

*([Pub. L. 99–198, title XVI, § 1630](/us/pl/99/198/tXVI/s1630), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1621](/us/stat/99/1621).)*
