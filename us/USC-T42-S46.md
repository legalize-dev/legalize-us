# § 46. Omitted

##### § 46. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, which related to relocation of physicians and dentists, was from the First Supplemental National Defense Appropriation Act, 1944, [act Dec. 23, 1943, ch. 380, title I, § 101](/us/act/1943-12-23/ch380/tI/s101), [57 Stat. 617](/us/stat/57/617), and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts.
