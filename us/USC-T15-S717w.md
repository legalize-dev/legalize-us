# § 717w. Short title

##### § 717w. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “Natural Gas Act.”

*([June 21, 1938, ch. 556, § 26](/us/act/1938-06-21/ch556/s26), formerly § 24, [52 Stat. 833](/us/stat/52/833); renumbered § 26, [Pub. L. 109–58, title III, § 314(b)(1)(A)](/us/pl/109/58/tIII/s314/b/1/A), Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 690](/us/stat/119/690).)*
