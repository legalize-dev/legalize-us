#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Mar. 8, 1946, ch. 82, § 11](/us/act/1946-03-08/ch82/s11), [60 Stat. 49](/us/stat/60/49), as amended, was redesignated as and transferred to [section 57100 of Title 46](/us/usc/t46/s57100), Shipping, by [Pub. L. 115–91, div. C, title XXXV, § 3502(a)(3)](/us/pl/115/91/dC/tXXXV/s3502/a/3), Dec. 12, 2017, [131 Stat. 1910](/us/stat/131/1910).

Section was formerly classified to section 1744 of the former Appendix to this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
