# § 230306. Exclusive right to name, seals, emblems, and badges

##### § 230306. Exclusive right to name, seals, emblems, and badges

The corporation and its subordinate divisions have the exclusive right to use the name “Veterans of World War I of the United States of America, Incorporated”. The corporation has the exclusive right to use, and to allow others to use, seals, emblems, and badges the corporation adopts.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1484](/us/stat/112/1484).)*

The words “sole”, “or refuse”, and “and such emblems, seals, and badges as have heretofore been used by the Ohio corporation described in [section 778 of this title](/us/usc/t36/s778)” are omitted as unnecessary. The words “and the right to which may be lawfully transferred to the corporation” are omitted as executed.
