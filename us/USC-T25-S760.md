##### § 760. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 23](/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1207/s23), [68 Stat. 1104](/us/stat/68/1104), which related to education and training program prior to the issuance of the proclamation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
