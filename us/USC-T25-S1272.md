# § 1272. Omitted

##### § 1272. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–295, § 2](/us/pl/92/295/s2), May 16, 1972, [86 Stat. 139](/us/stat/86/139), which related to payment of shares to minors and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
