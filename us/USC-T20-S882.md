##### § 882. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV, § 401(h)(3)](/us/pl/91/230/tIV/s401/h/3), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 174](/us/stat/84/174)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s802), formerly title VI, § 602, Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 57](/us/stat/79/57), renumbered title VII, § 702, [Pub. L. 89–750, title I, § 161](/us/pl/89/750/tI/s161), Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1204](/us/stat/80/1204), renumbered title VIII, § 802, [Pub. L. 90–247, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/90/247/tVII/s702), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 816](/us/stat/81/816), provided for appointment of advisory council and for compensation ($100 per day limitation, including travel time) and travel expenses of the members.
