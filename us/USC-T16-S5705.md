# § 5705. Exemption

##### § 5705. Exemption

Chapter 10 of title 5 shall not apply to the Panel, the Yukon River Joint Technical Committee, or the Advisory Committee created under [section 5704 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s5704).

*([Pub. L. 104–43, title VII, § 706](/us/pl/104/43/tVII/s706), Nov. 3, 1995, [109 Stat. 394](/us/stat/109/394); [Pub. L. 117–286, § 4(a)(126)](/us/pl/117/286/s4/a/126), Dec. 27, 2022, [136 Stat. 4319](/us/stat/136/4319).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–286](/us/pl/117/286) substituted “Chapter 10 of title 5” for “The Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.)”.
