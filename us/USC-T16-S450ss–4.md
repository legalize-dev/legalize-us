# § 450ss–4. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–199, div. F, title V, § 544(e)(1)](/us/pl/108/199/dF/tV/s544/e/1), Jan. 23, 2004, [118 Stat. 348](/us/stat/118/348)

##### § 450ss–4. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–199, div. F, title V, § 544(e)(1)](/us/pl/108/199/dF/tV/s544/e/1), Jan. 23, 2004, [118 Stat. 348](/us/stat/118/348)

Section, [Pub. L. 105–58, § 6](/us/pl/105/58/s6), Oct. 9, 1997, [111 Stat. 1264](/us/stat/111/1264), provided for duties and authorities of Oklahoma City National Memorial Trust.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 108–199, div. F, title V, § 544(e)(2)](/us/pl/108/199/dF/tV/s544/e/2), Jan. 23, 2004, [118 Stat. 348](/us/stat/118/348), provided that: “The repeal under this subsection [repealing this section] shall take effect upon the transfer of the Memorial property, rights, authorities, and duties pursuant to the amendments made by subsection (d) [amending [section 450ss–3 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s450ss–3)].”
