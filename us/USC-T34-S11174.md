# § 11174. Reports

##### § 11174. Reports

Recipients of grants made under this part shall submit to the Administrator such reports as may be reasonably requested by the Administrator to describe progress achieved in carrying out the projects for which such grants are made.

*([Pub. L. 93–415, title II, § 264](/us/pl/93/415/tII/s264), as added [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title II, § 12212](/us/pl/107/273/dC/tII/s12212), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1891](/us/stat/116/1891).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 5668 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s5668), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
