Section, [Pub. L. 95–91, title VI, § 603](/us/pl/95/91/tVI/s603), formerly § 608, Aug. 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 596](/us/stat/91/596); renumbered § 603 and amended, [Pub. L. 103–160, div. C, title XXXI, § 3161(c)(1)(D)](/us/pl/103/160/dC/tXXXI/s3161/c/1/D), (E), Nov. 30, 1993, [107 Stat. 1958](/us/stat/107/1958), related to sanctions.

A prior [section 603 of Pub. L. 95–91](/us/pl/95/91/s603) was classified to [section 7213 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s7213) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–160](/us/pl/103/160).
