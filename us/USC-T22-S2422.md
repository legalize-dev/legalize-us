# § 2422. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–88, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/102/88/tVI/s601), Aug. 14, 1991, [105 Stat. 441](/us/stat/105/441)

##### § 2422. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–88, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/102/88/tVI/s601), Aug. 14, 1991, [105 Stat. 441](/us/stat/105/441)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 662, as added [Pub. L. 93–559, § 32](/us/pl/93/559/s32), Dec. 30, 1974, [88 Stat. 1804](/us/stat/88/1804), and amended [Pub. L. 96–450, title IV, § 407(a)](/us/pl/96/450/tIV/s407/a), Oct. 14, 1980, [94 Stat. 1981](/us/stat/94/1981), prohibited funding of activities of the Central Intelligence Agency, other than intelligence gathering, without Presidential finding of importance to national security of United States and defined such activities as significant anticipated intelligence activities for purpose of [section 413 of Title 50](/us/usc/t50/s413), War and National Defense. See [section 3094 of Title 50](/us/usc/t50/s3094).
