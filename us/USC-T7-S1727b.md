# § 1727b. Grant programs

##### § 1727b. Grant programs

To carry out the policies and accomplish the objectives described in [section 1691 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1691), the Administrator may negotiate and execute agreements with least developed countries to provide commodities to such countries on a grant basis either through the Commodity Credit Corporation or through private trade channels.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 303 of [act July 10, 1954, ch. 469, title III](/us/act/1954-07-10/ch469/tIII), [68 Stat. 459](/us/stat/68/459), which enacted [section 1692 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1692), was renumbered section 310 by [Pub. L. 95–88, title II, § 211(a)(1)](/us/pl/95/88/tII/s211/a/1), Aug. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 548](/us/stat/91/548).

#### Amendments

1990—[Pub. L. 101–624](/us/pl/101/624) amended section generally, substituting present provisions for provisions relating to a multiyear utilization proposal regarding value and amount of commodities proposed to be distributed, integration with other forms of development assistance, and non-replacement of other programs.

1979—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 96–53](/us/pl/96/53) struck out “for each year such funds are to be disbursed” after “on an annual basis”.
