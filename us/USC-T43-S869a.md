# § 869a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–66, § 1](/us/pl/86/66/s1), June 23, 1959, [73 Stat. 110](/us/stat/73/110)

##### § 869a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–66, § 1](/us/pl/86/66/s1), June 23, 1959, [73 Stat. 110](/us/stat/73/110)

Section, [act Apr. 13, 1928, ch. 370](/us/act/1928-04-13/ch370), §§ 1, 2, [45 Stat. 429](/us/stat/45/429), extended provisions of [section 869 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s869) to former Oregon and California Railroad grant lands revested in the United States and to former Coos Bay Wagon Road grant lands reconveyed to the United States.
