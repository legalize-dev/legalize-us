# § 690q. Termination

##### § 690q. Termination

The program under this part shall terminate at the end of the second full fiscal year after the date that the Administrator establishes the program under this part.

*([Pub. L. 85–699, title III, § 398](/us/pl/85/699/tIII/s398), as added [Pub. L. 110–140, title XII, § 1207](/us/pl/110/140/tXII/s1207), Dec. 19, 2007, [121 Stat. 1783](/us/stat/121/1783).)*
