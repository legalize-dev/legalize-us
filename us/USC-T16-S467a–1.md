# § 467a–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–586, § 4(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/586/s4/a/1), (b), Dec. 23, 1980, [94 Stat. 3386](/us/stat/94/3386)

##### § 467a–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–586, § 4(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/586/s4/a/1), (b), Dec. 23, 1980, [94 Stat. 3386](/us/stat/94/3386)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–527, § 2](/us/pl/92/527/s2), Oct. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 1049](/us/stat/86/1049), established the Mar-A-Lago National Historic Site Advisory Commission and provided for its membership, term of office, designation of a Chairman, filling of vacancies, compensation and expenses, consultations on general policies and specific matters related to administration of the site, and voting.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

For effective date of repeal, see [Pub. L. 96–586, § 4](/us/pl/96/586/s4), Dec. 23, 1980, [94 Stat. 3386](/us/stat/94/3386), set out as a note under [section 467a of this title](/us/usc/t16/s467a).
