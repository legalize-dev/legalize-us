# § 773. Omitted

##### § 773. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1085, § 3](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1085/s3), [68 Stat. 979](/us/stat/68/979), which related to payments on behalf of deceased enrollees, minors, and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
