# § 4225. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

##### § 4225. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–577, title III, § 305](/us/pl/90/577/tIII/s305), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1103](/us/stat/82/1103), provided for the reservation of existing authority of Federal departments and agencies with respect to furnishing services to State and local units of government. See [section 6505(d) of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6505/d), Money and Finance.
