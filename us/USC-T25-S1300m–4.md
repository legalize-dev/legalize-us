# § 1300m–4. Omitted

##### § 1300m–4. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–454, title III, § 306](/us/pl/103/454/tIII/s306), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4794](/us/stat/108/4794), which related to tribal membership rolls, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
