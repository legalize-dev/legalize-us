# § 2304. Privacy

##### § 2304. Privacy

(a) GEPA Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to supersede the privacy protections afforded parents and students under section 444 of the General Education Provisions Act ([20 U.S.C. 1232g](/us/usc/t20/s1232g)).

(b) Prohibition on development of national database Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to permit the development of a national database of personally identifiable information on individuals receiving services under this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 88–210, § 5](/us/pl/88/210/s5), as added [Pub. L. 109–270, § 1(b)](/us/pl/109/270/s1/b), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 690](/us/stat/120/690).)*
