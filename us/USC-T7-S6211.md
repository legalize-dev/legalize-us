# § 6211. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 6211. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such funds as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

(b) Administrative expenses The funds so appropriated shall not be available for payment of the expenses or expenditures of the Board in administering any provisions of an order issued under this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 101–624, title XIX, § 1962](/us/pl/101/624/tXIX/s1962), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3880](/us/stat/104/3880).)*
