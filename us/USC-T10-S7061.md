# § 7061. Regulations

##### § 7061. Regulations

The President may prescribe regulations for the government of the Army.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 165](/us/stat/70A/165), § 3061; renumbered § 7061, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(a)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/a), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1838](/us/stat/132/1838).)*

The word “prescribe” is substituted for the words “make and publish”. 10:16 (last 35 words) is omitted as surplusage.
