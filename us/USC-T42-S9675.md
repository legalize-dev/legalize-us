# § 9675. Applicability of securities laws

##### § 9675. Applicability of securities laws

(a) Ownership interests The ownership interests of members of a risk retention group shall be considered to be—

(1) exempted securities for purposes of [section 77e of title 15](/us/usc/t15/s77e) and for purposes of section 78*l* of title 15; and

(2) securities for purposes of the provisions of [section 77q of title 15](/us/usc/t15/s77q) and the provisions of [section 78j of title 15](/us/usc/t15/s78j).

(b) Investment Company Act A risk retention group shall not be considered to be an investment company for purposes of the Investment Company Act of 1940 ([15 U.S.C. 80a–1](/us/usc/t15/s80a–1) et seq.).

(c) Blue sky law The ownership interests of members in a risk retention group shall not be considered securities for purposes of any State blue sky law.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Investment Company Act of 1940, referred to in subsec. (b), is title I of [act Aug. 22, 1940, ch. 686](/us/act/1940-08-22/ch686), [54 Stat. 789](/us/stat/54/789), which is classified generally to subchapter I (§ 80a–1 et seq.) of chapter 2D of Title 15, Commerce and Trade. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 80a–51 of Title 15](/us/usc/t15/s80a–51) and Tables.
