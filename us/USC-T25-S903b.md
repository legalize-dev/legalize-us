# § 903b. Omitted

##### § 903b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–197, § 4](/us/pl/93/197/s4), Dec. 22, 1973, [87 Stat. 771](/us/stat/87/771), which set out provisions relating to the Menominee Restoration Committee, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
