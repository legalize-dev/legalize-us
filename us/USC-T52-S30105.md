# § 30105. Reports on convention financing

##### § 30105. Reports on convention financing

(1) represents a State, or a political subdivision thereof, or any group of persons, in dealing with officials of a national political party with respect to matters involving a convention held in such State or political subdivision to nominate a candidate for the office of President or Vice President, or

(2) represents a national political party in making arrangements for the convention of such party held to nominate a candidate for the office of President or Vice President,

*([Pub. L. 92–225, title III, § 305](/us/pl/92/225/tIII/s305), formerly § 307, Feb. 7, 1972, [86 Stat. 16](/us/stat/86/16); [Pub. L. 93–443, title II, § 208(c)(6)](/us/pl/93/443/tII/s208/c/6), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1286](/us/stat/88/1286); renumbered § 305 and amended [Pub. L. 96–187, title I](/us/pl/96/187/tI), §§ 105(2), 112(a), Jan. 8, 1980, [93 Stat. 1354](/us/stat/93/1354), 1366.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 437 of Title 2](/us/usc/t2/s437), The Congress, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 305 of Pub. L. 92–225](/us/pl/92/225/s305) was classified to [section 435 of Title 2](/us/usc/t2/s435), The Congress, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 96–187](/us/pl/96/187).

#### Amendments

1980—[Pub. L. 96–187](/us/pl/96/187) substituted “60” and “20” for “sixty” and “twenty”, respectively, and struck out “Federal Election” before “Commission”.

1974—[Pub. L. 93–443](/us/pl/93/443) substituted “Federal Election Commission” and “it” for “Comptroller General of the United States” and “he”, respectively.
