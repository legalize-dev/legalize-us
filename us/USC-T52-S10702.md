# § 10702. “State” defined

##### § 10702. “State” defined

As used in this chapter, the term “State” includes the District of Columbia.

*([Pub. L. 89–110, title III, § 302](/us/pl/89/110/tIII/s302), as added [Pub. L. 91–285, § 6](/us/pl/91/285/s6), June 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 318](/us/stat/84/318); amended [Pub. L. 94–73, title IV, § 407](/us/pl/94/73/tIV/s407), Aug. 6, 1975, [89 Stat. 405](/us/stat/89/405).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 1973bb–1 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s1973bb–1), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

1975—[Pub. L. 94–73](/us/pl/94/73) substituted definition of State for provisions prohibiting denial of right to vote because of age.
