# § 1222. Omitted

##### § 1222. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 24, 1954, ch. 900, § 2](/us/act/1954-08-24/ch900/s2), [68 Stat. 790](/us/stat/68/790), which related to notice of opening of lands to purchase under former sections 1221 to 1223 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
