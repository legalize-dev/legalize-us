# § 1123. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–331, § 10](/us/pl/89/331/s10), Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1278](/us/stat/79/1278)

##### § 1123. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–331, § 10](/us/pl/89/331/s10), Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1278](/us/stat/79/1278)

Section, [act Aug. 8, 1947, ch. 519, title II, § 213](/us/act/1947-08-08/ch519/tII/s213), as added July 13, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–535, § 12](/us/pl/87/535/s12), 76 Stat, 163; amended July 19, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–539, § 2(e)](/us/pl/87/539/s2/e), [76 Stat. 170](/us/stat/76/170), made provision for import fees and set the amount and basis for such fees.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1965, pursuant to [section 14 of Pub. L. 89–331](/us/pl/89/331/s14).
