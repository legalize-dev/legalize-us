# §§ 1462 to 1464. Omitted

##### §§ 1462 to 1464. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted pursuant to [section 5316 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5316) which terminated authority to make grants or loans under this subchapter after Jan. 1, 1975.

Section 1462, [act July 15, 1949, ch. 338, title I, § 111](/us/act/1949-07-15/ch338/tI/s111), as added [Aug. 7, 1956, ch. 1029](/us/act/1956-08-07/ch1029), title III, § 307(a), [70 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/70/1101); amended May 25, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–19, § 6(b)](/us/pl/90/19/s6/b), [81 Stat. 21](/us/stat/81/21); Aug. 1, 1968, [Pub.
