# § 305. National tree

##### § 305. National tree

The tree genus Quercus, commonly known as the oak tree, is the national tree.

*(Added [Pub. L. 108–447, div. J, title I, § 109(a)](/us/pl/108/447/dJ/tI/s109/a), Dec. 8, 2004, [118 Stat. 3344](/us/stat/118/3344).)*
