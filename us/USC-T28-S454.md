# § 454. Practice of law by justices and judges

##### § 454. Practice of law by justices and judges

Any justice or judge appointed under the authority of the United States who engages in the practice of law is guilty of a high misdemeanor.

*([June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 908](/us/stat/62/908).)*

Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 373 ([Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231, § 258](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231/s258), [36 Stat. 1161](/us/stat/36/1161)).
