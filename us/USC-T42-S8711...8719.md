# §§ 8711 to 8719. Omitted

##### §§ 8711 to 8719. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 8711 to 8719 were omitted from the Code in view of termination of United States Synthetic Fuels Corporation. See note set out under [section 8791 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s8791).

Section 8711, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 115](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s115), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 636](/us/stat/94/636), created the Corporation, provided for its offices and residence status, and referred to its general powers.

Section 8712, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 116](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s116), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 636](/us/stat/94/636), provided for board of directors of Corporation.

Section 8713, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 117](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s117), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 638](/us/stat/94/638); [Pub. L. 98–473, title I, § 101(c) [title II, § 201]](/us/pl/98/473/tI/s101/c/tII/s201), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 1837](/us/stat/98/1837), 1860, related to officers and employees of Corporation.

Section 8714, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 118](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s118), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 638](/us/stat/94/638), related to conflicts of interest and financial disclosure.

Section 8715, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 119](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s119), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 639](/us/stat/94/639), related to delegation of authority and transfer of functions.

Section 8716, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 120](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s120), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 640](/us/stat/94/640), authorized administrative expenses.

Section 8717, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 121](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s121), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 641](/us/stat/94/641), related to public access to information.

Section 8718, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 122](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s122), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 641](/us/stat/94/641), related to Inspector General of Corporation.

Section 8719, [Pub. L. 96–294, title I, § 123](/us/pl/96/294/tI/s123), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 644](/us/stat/94/644), established an Advisory Committee to the Board of Directors. For continuation of Advisory Committee, see [section 7404(c) of Pub. L. 99–272](/us/pl/99/272/s7404/c), set out as a note under [section 8791 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s8791).
