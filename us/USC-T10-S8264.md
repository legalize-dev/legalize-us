# § 8264. Shore patrol duty: payment of expenses

##### § 8264. Shore patrol duty: payment of expenses

An officer, midshipman, or cadet of the naval service who is assigned to shore patrol duty away from his vessel or other duty station may be paid his actual services.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 386](/us/stat/70A/386), § 6153; renumbered § 8264, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(b)(10)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/b/10), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1835](/us/stat/132/1835).)*
