# § 302. National motto

##### § 302. National motto

“In God we trust” is the national motto.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1263](/us/stat/112/1263); [Pub. L. 107–293, § 3(a)](/us/pl/107/293/s3/a), Nov. 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 2060](/us/stat/116/2060).)*
