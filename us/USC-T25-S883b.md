# § 883b. Omitted

##### § 883b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–586, § 3](/us/pl/92/586/s3), Oct. 27, 1972, [86 Stat. 1296](/us/stat/86/1296), which related to filing of claims by members of the Osage Tribe and reversion of unclaimed shares, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
