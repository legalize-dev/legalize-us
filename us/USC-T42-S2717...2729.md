# §§ 2717 to 2729. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VI, § 614](/us/pl/93/203/tVI/s614), Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883)

##### §§ 2717 to 2729. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VI, § 614](/us/pl/93/203/tVI/s614), Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883)

Section 2717, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 107](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s107), Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 511](/us/stat/78/511); [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 675](/us/stat/81/675), provided for establishment of Job Corps centers.

Section 2718, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 108](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s108), Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 511](/us/stat/78/511); [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 676](/us/stat/81/676), covered Job Corps program activities.

Section 2719, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 109](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s109), Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 511](/us/stat/78/511); [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 676](/us/stat/81/676), covered allowances and support for Job Corps enrollees.

Section 2720, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 110](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s110), Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 511](/us/stat/78/511); [Pub. L. 89–253, § 7](/us/pl/89/253/s7), Oct. 9, 1965, [79 Stat. 974](/us/stat/79/974); [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 677](/us/stat/81/677), set out standards of conduct and discipline for Job Corps enrollees.

Section 2721, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 111](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s111), as added [Pub. L. 89–794, title I, § 110](/us/pl/89/794/tI/s110), Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1453](/us/stat/80/1453); amended [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 677](/us/stat/81/677), covered community participation in Job Corps activities.

Section 2722, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 111–1](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s111–1), as added [Pub. L. 89–794, title I, § 111](/us/pl/89/794/tI/s111), Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1454](/us/stat/80/1454), provided for experimental and demonstration projects and directed Director to report to Congress no later than Mar. 1, 1968.

Section 2723, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 112](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s112), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 678](/us/stat/81/678), provided for counseling and job placement.

Section 2724, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 113](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s113), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 679](/us/stat/81/679); amended [Pub. L. 92–424, § 27(b)(1)](/us/pl/92/424/s27/b/1), Sept. 19, 1972, [86 Stat. 705](/us/stat/86/705), covered experimental and development projects.

Section 2725, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 114](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s114), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 680](/us/stat/81/680), covered advisory boards and committees.

Section 2726, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 115](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s115), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 680](/us/stat/81/680), covered participation of States.

Section 2727, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 116](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s116), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 681](/us/stat/81/681); amended [Pub. L. 90–623, § 5(a)](/us/pl/90/623/s5/a), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1315](/us/stat/82/1315), covered the application of provisions of Federal law.

Section 2728, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 117](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s117), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 682](/us/stat/81/682), set out certain special limitations.

Section 2729, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 118](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s118), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 101](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s101), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 682](/us/stat/81/682), prohibited political discrimination and political activity.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 93–203, title VI, § 614](/us/pl/93/203/tVI/s614), Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883), provided that the repeal by that section is effective with respect to fiscal years after June 30, 1974.
