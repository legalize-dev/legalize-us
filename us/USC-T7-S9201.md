# § 9201. Definitions

##### § 9201. Definitions

(1) Animal The term “animal” has the meaning given the term in [section 8302 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s8302).

(2) Transboundary disease The term “transboundary disease” has the meaning given the term in [section 8914(a) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s8914/a).

(3) Veterinary countermeasure The term “veterinary countermeasure” has the meaning given the term in [section 8302 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s8302).

*([Pub. L. 116–260, div. P, § 2](/us/pl/116/260/dP/s2), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/134/2159).)*
