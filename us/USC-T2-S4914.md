# § 4914. Academic year and summer term for page program

##### § 4914. Academic year and summer term for page program

The page program shall consist of the two semesters of the academic year, plus a non-academic summer term.

*([Pub. L. 98–367, title I, § 103](/us/pl/98/367/tI/s103), July 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 479](/us/stat/98/479); [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(40)(B)](/us/pl/104/186/tII/s204/40/B), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1736](/us/stat/110/1736).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 88c–2 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s88c–2) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Section is based on section 2 of House Resolution No. 234, Ninety-eighth Congress, June 29, 1983, which was enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 98–367](/us/pl/98/367).

#### Amendments

1996—[Pub. L. 104–186](/us/pl/104/186) substituted “semesters of the academic year, plus a non-academic” for “terms of the academic year plus a”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**
