# § 715d. Omitted

##### § 715d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–42, § 6](/us/pl/101/42/s6), June 28, 1989, [103 Stat. 92](/us/stat/103/92), which related to criminal and civil jurisdiction within the reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
