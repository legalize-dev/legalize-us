# §§ 193 to 195. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–456, title III, § 303(c)](/us/pl/87/456/tIII/s303/c), May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 78](/us/stat/76/78)

##### §§ 193 to 195. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–456, title III, § 303(c)](/us/pl/87/456/tIII/s303/c), May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 78](/us/stat/76/78)

Section 193, [act Jan. 9, 1883, ch. 17](/us/act/1883-01-09/ch17), [22 Stat. 402](/us/stat/22/402), related to grain brought from Canada for grinding.

Section 194, [act May 18, 1896, ch. 195](/us/act/1896-05-18/ch195), [29 Stat. 122](/us/stat/29/122), provided for the return free of articles and livestock exported for exhibition.

Section 195, [act Mar. 3, 1899, ch. 454](/us/act/1899-03-03/ch454), [30 Stat. 1372](/us/stat/30/1372), provided for free entry of animals taken abroad with circus or menagerie.
