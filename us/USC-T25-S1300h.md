# § 1300h. Omitted

##### § 1300h. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–420, § 2](/us/pl/100/420/s2), Sept. 8, 1988, [102 Stat. 1577](/us/stat/102/1577), which set out congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 100–420, § 1](/us/pl/100/420/s1), Sept. 8, 1988, [102 Stat. 1577](/us/stat/102/1577), provided that [Pub. L. 100–420](/us/pl/100/420), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Lac Vieux Desert Band of Lake Superior Chippewa Indians Act”.
