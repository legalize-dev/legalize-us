# § 9329. Computation of retired pay: law applicable

##### § 9329. Computation of retired pay: law applicable

A member of the Air Force or the Space Force retired under this chapter is entitled to retired pay computed under chapter 945 of this title.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 554](/us/stat/70A/554), § 8929; renumbered § 9329 and amended [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII), §§ 806(b)(13), 809(a), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1833](/us/stat/132/1833), 1840; [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title IX, § 923(c)(20)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tIX/s923/c/20), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3812](/us/stat/134/3812).)*

The revised section is based on the various retirement provisions in this chapter and is inserted to make explicit the entitlement to retired pay upon retirement.
