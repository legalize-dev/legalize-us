# § 21905. Voluntary participation

##### § 21905. Voluntary participation

The minimum standards established under [section 21904(a) of this title](/us/usc/t34/s21904/a), and any other guidelines and programs established under [section 21903 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s21903), shall be adoptable on a voluntary basis only.
