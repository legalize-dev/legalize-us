# § 2909. Disclaimers

##### § 2909. Disclaimers

(1) the authority, jurisdiction, or responsibility of the States to manage, control, or regulate fish and resident wildlife under State law;

(2) any requirement under State law that lands, waters, and interests therein may only be acquired for conservation purposes if the owner thereof is a willing seller; and

(3) the authority of the Secretary of Agriculture under the Act of March 2, 1931 ([46 Stat. 1468–1469](/us/stat/46/1468-1469), [7 U.S.C. 426–426b](/us/usc/t7/s426–426b)).11 See References in Text note below.

*([Pub. L. 96–366, § 10](/us/pl/96/366/s10), Sept. 29, 1980, [94 Stat. 1329](/us/stat/94/1329).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Act of March 2, 1931, referred to in par. (3), is [act Mar. 2, 1931, ch. 370](/us/act/1931-03-02/ch370), [46 Stat. 1468](/us/stat/46/1468), which was formerly classified to sections 426 to 426b of Title 7, Agriculture. Sections 1 and 3 of the Act, which were formerly classified to sections 426 and 426b, respectively, of Title 7, were editorially reclassified as sections 8351 and 8352, respectively, of Title 7. Section 2 of the Act, which was formerly classified to [section 426a of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s426a), was omitted from the Code as obsolete. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.
