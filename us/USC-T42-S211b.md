# § 211b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–412, title V, § 501(f)](/us/pl/94/412/tV/s501/f), Sept. 14, 1976, [90 Stat. 1258](/us/stat/90/1258)

##### § 211b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–412, title V, § 501(f)](/us/pl/94/412/tV/s501/f), Sept. 14, 1976, [90 Stat. 1258](/us/stat/90/1258)

Section, [act Feb. 28, 1948, ch. 83, § 6(b)](/us/act/1948-02-28/ch83/s6/b)–(f), [62 Stat. 45](/us/stat/62/45), dealt with promotion of Public Health Service officers.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Savings Provision

Repeal not to affect any action taken or proceeding pending at the time of repeal, see [section 501(h) of Pub. L. 94–412](/us/pl/94/412/s501/h), set out as a note under [section 1601 of Title 50](/us/usc/t50/s1601), War and National Defense.
