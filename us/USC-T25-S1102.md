# § 1102. Omitted

##### § 1102. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–656, § 2](/us/pl/89/656/s2), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 906](/us/stat/80/906), which related to distribution of funds to tribe members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
