Repeal effective Oct. 1, 2007, except that section as in effect on the day before Sept. 27, 2005, shall apply to eligible lenders that received a designation under subsec. L. 110–84](/us/pl/110/84/s302/c), set out as an Effective Date of 2007 Amendment note under [section 1078 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1078).
