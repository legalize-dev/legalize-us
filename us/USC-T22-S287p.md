# § 287p. Citizenship of members

##### § 287p. Citizenship of members

Each such member of the National Commission must be an American citizen.

*([July 30, 1946, ch. 700, § 4](/us/act/1946-07-30/ch700/s4), [60 Stat. 713](/us/stat/60/713).)*
