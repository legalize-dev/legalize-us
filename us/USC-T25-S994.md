# § 994. Omitted

##### § 994. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–775, § 4](/us/pl/87/775/s4), Oct. 9, 1962, [76 Stat. 776](/us/stat/76/776), which exempted funds distributed under this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
