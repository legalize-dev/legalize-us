# [§ 4015. Omitted]

##### [§ 4015. Omitted]

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

As enacted, [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1841(c)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1841/c), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4243](/us/stat/134/4243), originally transferred [section 2361 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s2361) to this section, to become effective Jan. 1, 2022. Subsequently, [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XVII, § 1701(u)(2)(D)](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tXVII/s1701/u/2/D), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 2151](/us/stat/135/2151), amended [section 1841(c) of Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283/s1841/c), effective as if included therein, so as to eliminate that transfer, thereby omitting this section before it took effect. Another amendment by [Pub. L. 117–81](/us/pl/117/81) resulted in the transfer of section 2361 to [section 4141 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s4141).
