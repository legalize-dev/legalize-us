# § 8218. Aviation designations: naval flight officer

##### § 8218. Aviation designations: naval flight officer

Any officer of the naval service may be designated a naval flight officer if he has successfully completed the course prescribed for naval flight officers.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 377](/us/stat/70A/377), § 6024; [Pub. L. 91–198, § 1(2)](/us/pl/91/198/s1/2), Feb. 26, 1970, [84 Stat. 15](/us/stat/84/15); renumbered § 8218, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(b)(7)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/b/7), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/132/1834).)*

The phrase “by competent authority” is omitted as surplusage. The definition form of [34 U.S.C. 735](/us/usc/t34/s735) is not followed.
