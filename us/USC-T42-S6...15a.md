# §§ 6 to 15a. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

##### §§ 6 to 15a. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

Section 6, acts [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for care of sick and disabled seamen. See [section 249 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s249).

Section 6a, [act Mar. 31, 1936, ch. 161](/us/act/1936-03-31/ch161), [49 Stat. 1185](/us/stat/49/1185), provided for care of seamen on Government vessels not in Military or Naval Establishments and of cadets on State school ships. See [section 249 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s249).

Section 7, [act Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for investigations by Service into diseases, etc., and publications relating thereto. See [section 241 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s241).

Section 8, acts [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 4](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s4), [32 Stat. 713](/us/stat/32/713); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for use of Service in time of war. See [section 217 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s217).

Section 8a, [act Apr. 9, 1930, ch. 125, § 2(a)](/us/act/1930-04-09/ch125/s2/a), [46 Stat. 150](/us/stat/46/150), provided for extension of facilities of Service to health officials and scientists. See [section 241 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s241).

Section 9, [act Oct. 1, 1918, ch. 179, § 2](/us/act/1918-10-01/ch179/s2), [40 Stat. 1008](/us/stat/40/1008), provided for suppression of Spanish influenza and other communicable diseases. See [section 264 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s264).

Section 9a, [act Apr. 9, 1930, ch. 125, § 3](/us/act/1930-04-09/ch125/s3), [46 Stat. 150](/us/stat/46/150), provided that administrative office and bureau divisions in District of Columbia be a part of departmental organization and scientific offices and research laboratories as part of field services. See [section 203 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s203).

Section 10, acts [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 130](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch130), [18 Stat. 377](/us/stat/18/377); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for appointment of Surgeon General. See [section 205 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s205).

Section 11, R.S. § 4802; acts [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for supervisory powers of Surgeon General. See [section 203 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s203).

Section 11a, [act Apr. 9, 1930, ch. 125, § 10(b)](/us/act/1930-04-09/ch125/s10/b), [46 Stat. 152](/us/stat/46/152), provided for pay and allowances of Surgeon General and for reversion in grade on expiration of term. See sections 205 and 207 of this title.

Section 11b, [act Aug. 9, 1939, ch. 606](/us/act/1939-08-09/ch606), [53 Stat. 1266](/us/stat/53/1266), provided for rank and pay of Assistant to Surgeon General. See sections 206 and 207 of this title.

Section 12, acts [Jan. 4, 1889, ch. 19, § 1](/us/act/1889-01-04/ch19/s1), [25 Stat. 639](/us/stat/25/639); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for appointment of medical officers after examination. See [section 209 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s209).

Section 13, [act Jan. 4, 1889, ch. 19, § 2](/us/act/1889-01-04/ch19/s2), [25 Stat. 639](/us/stat/25/639), provided for original appointments as assistant surgeons and promotion of passed assistant surgeon. See sections 209 and 211 of this title.

Section 14, [act Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 2](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s2), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for help as provided by Congress. See [section 209 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s209).

Section 15, acts [Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 541](/us/act/1891-03-03/ch541), [26 Stat. 923](/us/stat/26/923); [July 31, 1894, ch. 174](/us/act/1894-07-31/ch174), [28 Stat. 179](/us/stat/28/179); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712), provided for details for duty in bureau. See [section 215 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s215).

Section 15a, acts [Mar. 2, 1895, ch. 177, § 1](/us/act/1895-03-02/ch177/s1), [28 Stat. 780](/us/stat/28/780); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309), provided for detail of two hospital attendants for duty in laboratory. See [section 215 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s215).
