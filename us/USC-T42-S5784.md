#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 115–385, § 402(c)(2)](/us/pl/115/385/s402/c/2), which directed the repeal of [section 505 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s505), was executed by repealing this section, which was [section 506 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s506), to reflect the probable intent of Congress and the renumbering of this section from section 505 to section 506 of the Act by [section 305 of Pub. L. 115–385](/us/pl/115/385/s305). A new [section 505 of Pub. L. 93–415](/us/pl/93/415/s505) was enacted by [Pub. L. 115–385](/us/pl/115/385) and is classified to [section 11314 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s11314), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
