# § 61d. Transferred

##### § 61d. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 61d was editorially reclassified as [section 6651 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6651). [Section 6651 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6651) was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(G)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/G), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2776](/us/stat/133/2776).

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 61d, [Pub. L. 93–145](/us/pl/93/145), Nov. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 528](/us/stat/87/528); [Pub. L. 95–26, title I](/us/pl/95/26/tI), May 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 80](/us/stat/91/80); [Pub. L. 96–38, title I, § 103](/us/pl/96/38/tI/s103), July 25, 1979, [93 Stat. 112](/us/stat/93/112); [Pub. L. 97–51, § 121](/us/pl/97/51/s121), Oct. 1, 1981, [95 Stat. 965](/us/stat/95/965), provided that effective October 1, 1981, the compensation of Chaplain of Senate would be $52,750.

Another prior section 61d, acts [Aug. 5, 1955, ch. 568, § 1](/us/act/1955-08-05/ch568/s1), [69 Stat. 499](/us/stat/69/499); July 12, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–628](/us/pl/86/628), [74 Stat. 446](/us/stat/74/446); Aug. 14, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–426, title II, § 203(h)](/us/pl/88/426/tII/s203/h), [78 Stat. 415](/us/stat/78/415); Dec. 12, 1969, [Pub. L. 91–145](/us/pl/91/145), [83 Stat. 340](/us/stat/83/340); Aug. 18, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–382](/us/pl/91/382), [84 Stat. 808](/us/stat/84/808), made provision for the appointment of a Secretary to Chaplain of Senate and prescribed compensation of Chaplain of Senate and Secretary to Chaplain.
