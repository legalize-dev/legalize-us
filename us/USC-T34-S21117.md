# § 21117. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 21117. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter—

(1) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2009;

(2) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2010;

(3) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2011;

(4) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2012;

(5) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2013 11 So in original. Probably should be followed by a semicolon.

(6) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2014;

(7) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2015;

(8) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2016;

(9) $60,000,000 for fiscal year 2017; and

(10) $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2018 through 2024.

(b) Availability Funds appropriated under subsection (a) shall remain available until expended.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 17617 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s17617), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2022—Subsec. (a)(10). [Pub. L. 117–262](/us/pl/117/262) substituted “2024” for “2022”.

2017—Subsec. (a)(10). [Pub. L. 115–82](/us/pl/115/82) substituted “each of fiscal years 2018 through 2022” for “fiscal year 2018”.

2012—Subsec. (a)(6) to (10). [Pub. L. 112–206](/us/pl/112/206) added pars. (6) to (10).
