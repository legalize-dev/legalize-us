# §§ 20709a to 20709c. Transferred

##### §§ 20709a to 20709c. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 20709a, [Pub. L. 115–392, § 7](/us/pl/115/392/s7), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5253](/us/stat/132/5253), which related to holistic training for Federal law enforcement officers and prosecutors, was redesignated as [section 212 of Pub. L. 109–164](/us/pl/109/164/s212) and transferred to [section 20712 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s20712).

Section 20709b, [Pub. L. 115–393, title V, § 501](/us/pl/115/393/tV/s501), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5275](/us/stat/132/5275), which related to encouraging a victim-centered approach to training of Federal law enforcement personnel, was redesignated as [section 213 of Pub. L. 109–164](/us/pl/109/164/s213) and transferred to [section 20713 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s20713).

Section 20709c, [Pub. L. 115–393, title V, § 504](/us/pl/115/393/tV/s504), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5277](/us/stat/132/5277), which related to training of tribal law enforcement and prosecutorial personnel, was redesignated as [section 214 of Pub. L. 109–164](/us/pl/109/164/s214) and transferred to [section 20714 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s20714).
