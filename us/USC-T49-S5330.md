# [§ 5330. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–141, div. B, § 20030(e)](/us/pl/112/141/dB/s20030/e), July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 731](/us/stat/126/731)]

##### [§ 5330. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–141, div. B, § 20030(e)](/us/pl/112/141/dB/s20030/e), July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 731](/us/stat/126/731)]

Section, [Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(d)](/us/pl/103/272/s1/d), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 831](/us/stat/108/831); [Pub. L. 109–59, title III](/us/pl/109/59/tIII), §§ 3002(b)(4), 3029(a), Aug. 10, 2005, [119 Stat. 1545](/us/stat/119/1545), 1625, related to State safety oversight of certain rail fixed guideway public transportation systems.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 112–141, div. B, § 20030(e)](/us/pl/112/141/dB/s20030/e), July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 731](/us/stat/126/731), provided that the repeal of this section is effective 3 years after the effective date of the final rules issued by the Secretary of Transportation under [section 5329(e) of this title](/us/usc/t49/s5329/e). Such effective date is Apr. 15, 2016, see 81 F.R. 14230.
