# § 58. Short title

##### § 58. Short title

This subchapter may be cited as the “Federal Trade Commission Act”.

*([Sept. 26, 1914, ch. 311, § 28](/us/act/1914-09-26/ch311/s28), formerly § 18, as added [Mar. 21, 1938, ch. 49, § 4](/us/act/1938-03-21/ch49/s4), [52 Stat. 114](/us/stat/52/114); renumbered § 21, [Pub. L. 93–637, title II, § 202(a)](/us/pl/93/637/tII/s202/a), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2193](/us/stat/88/2193); renumbered § 25, [Pub. L. 96–252, § 13](/us/pl/96/252/s13), May 28, 1980, [94 Stat. 380](/us/stat/94/380); renumbered § 26, [Pub. L. 103–312, § 2](/us/pl/103/312/s2), Aug. 26, 1994, [108 Stat. 1691](/us/stat/108/1691); renumbered § 28, [Pub. L. 109–455, § 11(1)](/us/pl/109/455/s11/1), Dec. 22, 2006, [120 Stat. 3381](/us/stat/120/3381).)*

#### Amendment of Section

For repeal of amendment renumbering this section by [section 13 of Pub. L. 109–455](/us/pl/109/455/s13), see Termination Date of 2006 Amendment note below.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Termination Date of 2006 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 109–455](/us/pl/109/455) repealed effective Sept. 30, 2027, and provisions amended by [Pub. L. 109–455](/us/pl/109/455) to be amended to read as if [Pub. L. 109–455](/us/pl/109/455) had not been enacted, see [section 13 of Pub. L. 109–455](/us/pl/109/455/s13), as amended, set out as a note under [section 44 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s44).

#### Short Title of 2022 Amendment

[Pub. L. 117–103, div. Q, title I, § 101](/us/pl/117/103/dQ/tI/s101), Mar. 15, 2022, [136 Stat. 809](/us/stat/136/809), provided that: “This title [enacting [section 45e of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45e), amending [section 21711 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s21711), Crime Control and Law Enforcement, and enacting provisions set out as notes under this section and [section 45e of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45e), and [section 21711 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s21711)] may be cited as the ‘Fraud and Scam Reduction Act’.”

[Pub. L. 117–103, div. Q, title I, § 111](/us/pl/117/103/dQ/tI/s111), Mar. 15, 2022, [136 Stat. 809](/us/stat/136/809), provided that: “This subtitle [subtitle A (§§ 111, 112) of title I of div. Q of [Pub. L. 117–103](/us/pl/117/103), amending [section 21711 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s21711), Crime Control and Law Enforcement, and enacting provisions set out as notes under [section 45e of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45e) and [section 21711 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s21711)] may be cited as the ‘Stop Senior Scams Act’.”

[Pub. L. 117–103, div. Q, title I, § 121](/us/pl/117/103/dQ/tI/s121), Mar. 15, 2022, [136 Stat. 811](/us/stat/136/811), provided that: “This subtitle [subtitle B (§§ 121, 122) of title I of div. Q of [Pub. L. 117–103](/us/pl/117/103), enacting [section 45e of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45e)] may be cited as the ‘Seniors Fraud Prevention Act of 2022’.”

#### Short Title of 2021 Amendment

[Pub. L. 116–287, § 1](/us/pl/116/287/s1), Jan. 5, 2021, [134 Stat. 4882](/us/stat/134/4882), provided that: “This Act [enacting provisions set out as a note under [section 52 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s52)] may be cited as the ‘Combating Pandemic Scams Act of 2020’.”

#### Short Title of 2018 Amendment

[Pub. L. 115–271, title VIII, § 8021](/us/pl/115/271/tVIII/s8021), Oct. 24, 2018, [132 Stat. 4082](/us/stat/132/4082), provided that: “This subtitle [subtitle B (§§ 8021–8023) of title VIII of [Pub. L. 115–271](/us/pl/115/271), enacting [section 45d of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45d) and provisions set out as a note under [section 45d of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45d)] may be cited as the ‘Opioid Addiction Recovery Fraud Prevention Act of 2018’.”

#### Short Title of 2016 Amendment

[Pub. L. 114–274, § 1](/us/pl/114/274/s1), Dec. 14, 2016, [130 Stat. 1401](/us/stat/130/1401), provided that: “This Act [enacting [section 45c of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45c) and provisions set out as a note under [section 45c of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45c)] may be cited as the ‘Better Online Ticket Sales Act of 2016’ or the ‘BOTS Act of 2016’.”

[Pub. L. 114–258, § 1](/us/pl/114/258/s1), Dec. 14, 2016, [130 Stat. 1355](/us/stat/130/1355), provided that: “This Act [enacting [section 45b of this title](/us/usc/t15/s45b)] may be cited as the ‘Consumer Review Fairness Act of 2016’.”

#### Short Title of 2006 Amendment

[Pub. L. 109–455, § 1](/us/pl/109/455/s1), Dec. 22, 2006, [120 Stat. 3372](/us/stat/120/3372), provided that: “This Act [enacting sections 57b–2a, 57b–2b, 57c–1, and 57c–2 of this title, amending this section, sections 44, 45, 46, 56, and 57b–2 of this title, and [section 3412 of Title 12](/us/usc/t12/s3412), Banks and Banking, and enacting provisions set out as notes under [section 44 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s44)] may be cited as the ‘Undertaking Spam, Spyware, And Fraud Enforcement With Enforcers beyond Borders Act of 2006’ or the ‘U.S. SAFE WEB Act of 2006’.”

[[Section 1 of Pub. L. 109–455](/us/pl/109/455/s1), set out above, repealed effective Sept. 30, 2027, see [section 13 of Pub. L. 109–455](/us/pl/109/455/s13), as amended, set out as a Termination Date of 2006 Amendment note under [section 44 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s44).]

#### Short Title of 1996 Amendment

[Pub. L. 104–216, § 1](/us/pl/104/216/s1), Oct. 1, 1996, [110 Stat. 3019](/us/stat/110/3019), provided that: “This Act [amending [section 57c of this title](/us/usc/t15/s57c)] may be cited as the ‘Federal Trade Commission Reauthorization Act of 1996’.”

#### Short Title of 1994 Amendment

[Pub. L. 103–312, § 1(a)](/us/pl/103/312/s1/a), Aug. 26, 1994, [108 Stat. 1691](/us/stat/108/1691), provided that: “This Act [enacting [section 57b–5 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s57b–5), amending this section and sections 45, 53, 57a, 57b–1, 57b–2, and 57c of this title, and enacting provisions set out as notes under sections 45 and 57c of this title] may be cited as the ‘Federal Trade Commission Act Amendments of 1994’.”

#### Short Title of 1980 Amendment

[Pub. L. 96–252, § 1](/us/pl/96/252/s1), May 28, 1980, [94 Stat. 374](/us/stat/94/374), provided that “This Act [enacting sections 57a–1 and 57b–1 to 57b–4 of this title, amending this section and sections 45, 46, 50, 57a, and 57c of this title, and enacting provisions set out as notes under sections 45, 46, 57a, 57a–1, and 57c of this title] may be cited as the ‘Federal Trade Commission Improvements Act of 1980’.”
