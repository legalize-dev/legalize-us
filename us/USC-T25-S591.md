# § 591. Omitted

##### § 591. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 8, 1940, ch. 285, § 1](/us/act/1940-06-08/ch285/s1), [54 Stat. 254](/us/stat/54/254), which related to reservation of Chippewa National Forest lands for the Minnesota Chippewa Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
