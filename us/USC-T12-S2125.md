# § 2125. Dividends

##### § 2125. Dividends

Dividends may be payable only on nonvoting investment stock, if declared by the board of directors of the bank, subject to the general direction of the Farm Credit Administration.

*([Pub. L. 92–181, title III, § 3](/us/pl/92/181/tIII/s3).4, Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 604](/us/stat/85/604); [Pub. L. 99–205, title II, § 205(e)(5)](/us/pl/99/205/tII/s205/e/5), title VI, § 606, Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1705](/us/stat/99/1705), 1711; [Pub. L. 100–233, title VIII, § 805](/us/pl/100/233/tVIII/s805)(*l*), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1715](/us/stat/101/1715).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1985—[Pub. L. 99–205](/us/pl/99/205) struck out “the Governor of” before “the Farm Credit Administration” and inserted “, subject to the general direction of the Farm Credit Administration”.
