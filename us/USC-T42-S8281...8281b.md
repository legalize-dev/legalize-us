# §§ 8281 to 8281b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–412, title II, § 201(a)](/us/pl/99/412/tII/s201/a), Aug. 28, 1986, [100 Stat. 943](/us/stat/100/943)

##### §§ 8281 to 8281b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–412, title II, § 201(a)](/us/pl/99/412/tII/s201/a), Aug. 28, 1986, [100 Stat. 943](/us/stat/100/943)

Section 8281, [Pub. L. 95–619, title VII, § 710](/us/pl/95/619/tVII/s710), as added [Pub. L. 96–294, title V, § 565](/us/pl/96/294/tV/s565), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 752](/us/stat/94/752), provided that definitions in [section 8211 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s8211) apply to this subchapter and defined additional terms.

Section 8281a, [Pub. L. 95–619, title VII, § 711](/us/pl/95/619/tVII/s711), as added [Pub. L. 96–294, title V, § 565](/us/pl/96/294/tV/s565), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 754](/us/stat/94/754), provided that this subchapter apply to any public utility for which coverage is provided under [section 8212 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s8212).

Section 8281b, [Pub. L. 95–619, title VII, § 712](/us/pl/95/619/tVII/s712), as added [Pub. L. 96–294, title V, § 565](/us/pl/96/294/tV/s565), June 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 754](/us/stat/94/754), related to rules of the Secretary for submission and approval of plans.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Demonstration Projects for Energy Efficiency in Commercial Buildings

[Pub. L. 99–412, title II, § 202](/us/pl/99/412/tII/s202), Aug. 28, 1986, [100 Stat. 943](/us/stat/100/943), provided that: “The Secretary of Energy shall, using funds appropriated for energy conservation activities of the Department of Energy, carry out demonstration projects by sharing the cost of the construction and development by nongovernmental entities of facilities which demonstrate innovative technologies for utility applications that increase energy efficiency in commercial buildings.”
