# § 1790c. Reward for information leading to recoveries or civil penalties

##### § 1790c. Reward for information leading to recoveries or civil penalties

The Board may pay rewards in connection with an offense affecting an insured credit union, under the same circumstances and subject to the same limitations that a Federal banking agency may pay rewards under [section 1831j of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1831j) in connection with an offense affecting a depository institution insured by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation.
