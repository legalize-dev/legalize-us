# § 564f. Omitted

##### § 564f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 7](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s7), [68 Stat. 720](/us/stat/68/720), which provided for per capita payments to tribe members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
