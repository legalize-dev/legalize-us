# § 500i. Omitted

##### § 500i. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 10](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s10), [50 Stat. 901](/us/stat/50/901), which provided for alienation of reindeer or interests, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
