# [§§ 181 to 190. Renumbered §§ 601 to 610]

##### [§§ 181 to 190. Renumbered §§ 601 to 610]

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Subchapter II heading “INFRASTRUCTURE FINANCE” was struck out and sections 181 to 190, which comprised subchapter II of this chapter, were renumbered sections 601 to 610, respectively, and transferred to follow the analysis of chapter 6 of this title, by [Pub. L. 109–59, title I, § 1602(b)(6)(B)](/us/pl/109/59/tI/s1602/b/6/B), (d), Aug. 10, 2005, [119 Stat. 1247](/us/stat/119/1247), as amended by [Pub. L. 110–244, title I, § 101(f)](/us/pl/110/244/tI/s101/f), June 6, 2008, [122 Stat. 1574](/us/stat/122/1574).
