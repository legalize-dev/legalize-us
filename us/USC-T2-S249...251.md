# §§ 249 to 251. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 249 to 251. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 249, [act Feb. 28, 1925, ch. 368, title III, § 310](/us/act/1925-02-28/ch368/tIII/s310), [43 Stat. 1073](/us/stat/43/1073), related to promises or pledges by candidates. See [section 599 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s599), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 250, [act Feb. 28, 1925, ch. 368, title III, § 311](/us/act/1925-02-28/ch368/tIII/s311), [43 Stat. 1073](/us/stat/43/1073), related to expenditures to influence voting. See [section 597 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s597).

Section 251, acts [Feb. 28, 1925, ch. 368](/us/act/1925-02-28/ch368), title III, § 313, [43 Stat. 1074](/us/stat/43/1074); [June 25, 1943, ch. 144, § 9](/us/act/1943-06-25/ch144/s9), [57 Stat. 167](/us/stat/57/167); [June 23, 1947, ch. 120](/us/act/1947-06-23/ch120), title III, § 304, [61 Stat. 159](/us/stat/61/159), related to political contributions by national banks, corporations, or labor unions. See [section 30118 of Title 52](/us/usc/t52/s30118), Voting and Elections.
