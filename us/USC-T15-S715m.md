# § 715m. Cooperation between Secretary of the Interior and Federal and State authorities

##### § 715m. Cooperation between Secretary of the Interior and Federal and State authorities

The Secretary of the Interior, in carrying out the Act of February 22, 1935, as amended (15 U.S.C., ch. 15A), is authorized to cooperate with Federal and State authorities.

*([June 25, 1946, ch. 472, § 3](/us/act/1946-06-25/ch472/s3), [60 Stat. 307](/us/stat/60/307).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Act of February 22, 1935, referred to in text, is [act Feb. 22, 1935, ch. 18](/us/act/1935-02-22/ch18), [49 Stat. 30](/us/stat/49/30), popularly known as the “Hot Oil Act” and also as the “Connally Hot Oil Act”, which is classified generally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 715 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s715) and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was not enacted as a part of act Feb. 22, 1935, which comprises this chapter.
