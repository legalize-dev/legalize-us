# § 958. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–66, title I, § 1011(d)](/us/pl/104/66/tI/s1011/d), Dec. 21, 1995, [109 Stat. 709](/us/stat/109/709)

##### § 958. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–66, title I, § 1011(d)](/us/pl/104/66/tI/s1011/d), Dec. 21, 1995, [109 Stat. 709](/us/stat/109/709)

Section, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1558](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1558), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3699](/us/stat/104/3699), directed Secretary of Agriculture to collect information contained on peanut export documentation, including country of origin, and submit reports to Congress annually notwithstanding certain confidentiality provisions.
