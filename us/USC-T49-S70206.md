# § 70206. Savings provision

##### § 70206. Savings provision

Nothing in this subtitle provides additional authority to regulate or direct private activity on freight networks designated under this subtitle.

*(Added [Pub. L. 114–94, div. A, title VIII, § 8001(a)](/us/pl/114/94/dA/tVIII/s8001/a), Dec. 4, 2015, [129 Stat. 1612](/us/stat/129/1612), § 70204; renumbered § 70206, [Pub. L. 117–58, div. B, title I, § 21106(a)(1)](/us/pl/117/58/dB/tI/s21106/a/1), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 659](/us/stat/135/659).)*
