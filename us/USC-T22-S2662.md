# § 2662. Transferred

##### § 2662. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 2662, [act Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 841, title I, § 1](/us/act/1956-08-01/ch841/tI/s1) [part], [70 Stat. 890](/us/stat/70/890); as renumbered title I and amended Aug. 24, 1982, [Pub. L. 97–241, title II, § 202(a)](/us/pl/97/241/tII/s202/a), [96 Stat. 282](/us/stat/96/282), which related to establishment, maintenance, and operation of passport and despatch agencies, was amended generally by [Pub. L. 103–236](/us/pl/103/236) and transferred to [section 2651a of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2651a).

Section was formerly classified to [section 170f of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s170f) prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).
