# § 143. Repealed. [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1076, § 1(24)](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1076/s1/24), [68 Stat. 968](/us/stat/68/968)

##### § 143. Repealed. [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1076, § 1(24)](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1076/s1/24), [68 Stat. 968](/us/stat/68/968)

Section, [act Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 210, § 27](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch210/s27), [36 Stat. 1077](/us/stat/36/1077), required Secretary of the Interior to submit an annual report to Speaker of House of Representatives of fiscal affairs of all Indian tribes for whose benefit expenditures from either public or tribal funds were made by any officer, clerk, or employee in Department of the Interior.
