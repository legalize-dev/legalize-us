# § 887b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–380, title IV, § 402(c)(3)](/us/pl/93/380/tIV/s402/c/3), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 546](/us/stat/88/546)

##### § 887b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–380, title IV, § 402(c)(3)](/us/pl/93/380/tIV/s402/c/3), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 546](/us/stat/88/546)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 809](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s809), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 164](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s164), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 154](/us/stat/84/154); amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 109(a)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s109/a), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 513](/us/stat/88/513), related to correction education services, providing in subsecs. (a) for grants for research and demonstration projects; (b) for appointment and functions of advisory committees and furnishing of advice and recommendations of Federal officials and other persons and organizations to the Commissioner; and (c) for authorization of $500,000 for fiscal years ending June 30, 1974, and 1975.

[Section 109(b) of Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380/s109/b) made such authorizations effective on and after July 1, 1974.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective July 1, 1975, see [section 402(c)(3) of Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380/s402/c/3).
