# § 230. Depositions by agents touching depredations

##### § 230. Depositions by agents touching depredations

The superintendents, agents, and subagents within their respective districts are authorized and empowered to take depositions of witnesses touching any depredations, within the purview of sections 227, 228 11 See References in Text note below. and 229 of this title, and to administer oaths to the deponents.

*(R.S. § 2157.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Sections 227 and 228 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), and are covered by [section 1160 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1160), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

#### Codification

R.S. § 2157 derived from [act June 30, 1834, ch. 161, § 18](/us/act/1834-06-30/ch161/s18), [4 Stat. 732](/us/stat/4/732).

#### Indian Agents
