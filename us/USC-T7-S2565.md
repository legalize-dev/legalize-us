# § 2565. Attorney fees

##### § 2565. Attorney fees

The court in exceptional cases may award reasonable attorney fees to the prevailing party.

*([Pub. L. 91–577, title III, § 125](/us/pl/91/577/tIII/s125), Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1556](/us/stat/84/1556).)*
