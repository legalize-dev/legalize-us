# § 231. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 22, 1942, ch. 516](/us/act/1942-07-22/ch516), [56 Stat. 689](/us/stat/56/689), was from the Department of Agriculture Appropriation Act, 1943, and provided for fees for inspection of brands appearing upon livestock.
