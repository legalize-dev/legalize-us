# § 1971. Definitions

##### § 1971. Definitions

(1) Prize competition The term “prize competition” means the competition for the award of the Genius Prize for Save Our Seas Innovations established under [section 1972 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1972).

(2) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Commerce.
