# § 2343. Waiver of applicability of certain laws

##### § 2343. Waiver of applicability of certain laws

Sections 4651, 3201, 3351, 3352(a), 3352(c), 3701 through 3708, and 3841 of this title and [section 6306 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s6306) shall not apply to acquisitions made under the authority of [section 2341 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s2341) or to agreements entered into under [section 2342 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s2342).
