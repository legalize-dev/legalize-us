# § 6806. Statement in annual report

##### § 6806. Statement in annual report

The Secretary shall include in each annual report submitted under [section 7267 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s7267) a statement with respect to activities conducted under this subchapter and recommendations as to the need for and types of further Federal legislation.

*([Pub. L. 94–385, title II, § 206](/us/pl/94/385/tII/s206), Aug. 14, 1976, [90 Stat. 1144](/us/stat/90/1144); [Pub. L. 95–617, title I, § 143](/us/pl/95/617/tI/s143), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3134](/us/stat/92/3134); [Pub. L. 96–470, title II, § 203(g)](/us/pl/96/470/tII/s203/g), Oct. 19, 1980, [94 Stat. 2243](/us/stat/94/2243).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1980—[Pub. L. 96–470](/us/pl/96/470) substituted “The Secretary shall include in each annual report submitted under [section 7267 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s7267) a statement” for “Not later than the last day in December in each year, the Secretary shall transmit to the Congress a report”.

1978—[Pub. L. 95–617](/us/pl/95/617) substituted “Secretary” for “Administrator”, meaning Administrator of the Federal Energy Administration.
