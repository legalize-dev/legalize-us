# § 1417. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–448, title XVII, § 1719(b)](/us/pl/90/448/tXVII/s1719/b), Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 610](/us/stat/82/610)

##### § 1417. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–448, title XVII, § 1719(b)](/us/pl/90/448/tXVII/s1719/b), Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 610](/us/stat/82/610)

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 896, § 17](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch896/s17), [50 Stat. 897](/us/stat/50/897), related to capital stock of the Authority.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Retirement of Capital Stock

[Pub. L. 90–448, title XVII, § 1719(b)](/us/pl/90/448/tXVII/s1719/b), Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 610](/us/stat/82/610), provided in part that the capital stock referred to in this section be retired and the sum of $1,000,000 represented by such stock returned to the Treasury of the United States.
