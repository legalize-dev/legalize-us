# §§ 1882 to 1884. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(d)](/us/pl/93/618/tVI/s602/d), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072](/us/stat/88/2072)

##### §§ 1882 to 1884. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(d)](/us/pl/93/618/tVI/s602/d), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072](/us/stat/88/2072)

Section 1882, [Pub. L. 87–794, title II, § 252](/us/pl/87/794/tII/s252), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 879](/us/stat/76/879), provided for Presidential action in face of foreign import restrictions. See sections 2112 of this title.

Section 1883, [Pub. L. 87–794, title II, § 253](/us/pl/87/794/tII/s253), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 880](/us/stat/76/880), covered area of staging requirements. See [section 2119 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2119).

Section 1884, [Pub. L. 87–794, title II, § 254](/us/pl/87/794/tII/s254), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 880](/us/stat/76/880), provided for rounding authority. See [section 2119 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2119).
