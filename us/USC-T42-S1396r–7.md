# § 1396r–7. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–33, title IV, § 4713(a)](/us/pl/105/33/tIV/s4713/a), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 509](/us/stat/111/509)

##### § 1396r–7. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–33, title IV, § 4713(a)](/us/pl/105/33/tIV/s4713/a), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 509](/us/stat/111/509)

Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XIX, § 1926](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXIX/s1926), as added Dec. 19, 1989, [Pub. L. 101–239, title VI, § 6402(b)](/us/pl/101/239/tVI/s6402/b), [103 Stat. 2260](/us/stat/103/2260), related to adequate payment levels for obstetrical and pediatric services.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 105–33, title IV, § 4713(b)](/us/pl/105/33/tIV/s4713/b), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 509](/us/stat/111/509), provided that: “The repeal made by subsection (a) [repealing this section] shall apply to services furnished on or after October 1, 1997.”
