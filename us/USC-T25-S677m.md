# § 677m. Omitted

##### § 677m. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 14](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s14), [68 Stat. 875](/us/stat/68/875), which related to procedure by Secretary of the Interior if distribution to individual mixed-blood members was not completed within seven years from Aug. 27, 1954, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
