# §§ 5051 to 5057. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/i), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

##### §§ 5051 to 5057. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/i), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

Section 5051, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6061](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6061), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1491](/us/stat/102/1491), provided that this subpart could be cited as the “School Dropout Demonstration Assistance Act of 1988”.

Section 5052, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6062](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6062), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1491](/us/stat/102/1491), stated purpose of this subpart.

Section 5053, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6063](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6063), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1491](/us/stat/102/1491), authorized appropriations.

Section 5054, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6064](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6064), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1491](/us/stat/102/1491), related to allotment of grants to local educational agencies.

Section 5055, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6065](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6065), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1493](/us/stat/102/1493), related to applications for grants.

Section 5056, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6066](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6066), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1494](/us/stat/102/1494), related to activities for which grants could be used.

Section 5057, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6067](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6067), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1495](/us/stat/102/1495), related to distribution of assistance and limitation on costs.
