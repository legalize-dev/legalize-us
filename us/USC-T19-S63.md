# § 63. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 636](/us/stat/80/636)

##### § 63. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 636](/us/stat/80/636)

Section, [act Aug. 28, 1890, ch. 812](/us/act/1890-08-28/ch812), §§ 1, 2, [26 Stat. 362](/us/stat/26/362), provided for leaves of absence of officers and employees in customs service who receive per diem compensation.

Section was additionally repealed by [Pub. L. 91–271, title III, § 321(k)](/us/pl/91/271/tIII/s321/k), June 2, 1970, [84 Stat. 293](/us/stat/84/293).
