# § 8302. Regulations

##### § 8302. Regulations

The President may prescribe regulations for the administration of the preceding sections of this chapter.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 391](/us/stat/70A/391), § 6252; renumbered § 8302, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(b)(13)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/b/13), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1836](/us/stat/132/1836).)*

The words “further”, “from time to time any and all rules”, “orders which he shall deem necessary”, and “to execute full purpose and intention thereof” are omitted as surplusage.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 8302, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 513](/us/stat/70A/513), related to promotion to captain, major, or lieutenant colonel of commissioned medical, dental, or veterinary officers in Regular Air Force upon examination of professional fitness and effect upon failure of promotion, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 96–513, title II, § 204](/us/pl/96/513/tII/s204), Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2880](/us/stat/94/2880), effective Sept. 15, 1981. See sections 631 and 632 of this title.

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–232](/us/pl/115/232) renumbered [section 6252 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s6252) as this section.
