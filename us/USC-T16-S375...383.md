# §§ 375 to 383. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 375 to 383. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 375, [act Apr. 20, 1904, ch. 1400, § 5](/us/act/1904-04-20/ch1400/s5), [33 Stat. 188](/us/stat/33/188), related to offenses under State law. See [section 13 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s13), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 376, acts [Apr. 20, 1904, ch. 1400, § 6](/us/act/1904-04-20/ch1400/s6), [33 Stat. 188](/us/stat/33/188); [Mar. 2, 1907, ch. 2516](/us/act/1907-03-02/ch2516), §§ 1, 2, [34 Stat. 1218](/us/stat/34/1218); [Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 230](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch230), [36 Stat. 1086](/us/stat/36/1086); [June 24, 1946, ch. 463, § 2](/us/act/1946-06-24/ch463/s2), [60 Stat. 303](/us/stat/60/303), related to prosecutions for violations of law or rules and regulations. See [section 636 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s636), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 377, acts [Apr. 20, 1904, ch. 1400, § 7](/us/act/1904-04-20/ch1400/s7), [33 Stat. 188](/us/stat/33/188); [Mar. 2, 1907, ch. 2516, § 2](/us/act/1907-03-02/ch2516/s2), [34 Stat. 1218](/us/stat/34/1218); [June 24, 1946, ch. 463, § 3](/us/act/1946-06-24/ch463/s3), [60 Stat. 303](/us/stat/60/303), related to issuance of process. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

Section 378, acts [Apr. 20, 1904, ch. 1400, § 8](/us/act/1904-04-20/ch1400/s8), [33 Stat. 189](/us/stat/33/189); [Mar. 2, 1907, ch. 2516, § 2](/us/act/1907-03-02/ch2516/s2), [34 Stat. 1218](/us/stat/34/1218); [June 24, 1946, ch. 463, § 4](/us/act/1946-06-24/ch463/s4), [60 Stat. 303](/us/stat/60/303), related to process directed to marshal. See [section 3053 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3053), rule 4 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix, and rule 4 of Federal Rules of Civil Procedure, Title 28, Appendix, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 379, acts [Apr. 20, 1904, ch. 1400, § 9](/us/act/1904-04-20/ch1400/s9), [33 Stat. 189](/us/stat/33/189); [Mar. 2, 1907, ch. 2516, § 2](/us/act/1907-03-02/ch2516/s2), [34 Stat. 1218](/us/stat/34/1218); [June 24, 1946, ch. 463, § 5](/us/act/1946-06-24/ch463/s5), [60 Stat. 303](/us/stat/60/303), related to fees of commissioner [now magistrate judge]. See [section 634 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s634).

Section 380, acts [Apr. 20, 1904, ch. 1400, § 11](/us/act/1904-04-20/ch1400/s11), [33 Stat. 189](/us/stat/33/189); [Mar. 2, 1907, ch. 2516, § 2](/us/act/1907-03-02/ch2516/s2), [34 Stat. 1218](/us/stat/34/1218), related to disposition of fines and costs. See [section 634 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s634).

Section 381, acts [Apr. 20, 1904, ch. 1400, § 13](/us/act/1904-04-20/ch1400/s13), [33 Stat. 189](/us/stat/33/189); [Mar. 2, 1907, ch. 2516, § 2](/us/act/1907-03-02/ch2516/s2), [34 Stat. 1218](/us/stat/34/1218), related to execution of sentence of conviction. See [section 3041 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3041), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 382, acts [Apr. 20, 1904, ch. 1400, § 12](/us/act/1904-04-20/ch1400/s12), [33 Stat. 189](/us/stat/33/189); [June 24, 1946, ch. 463, § 6](/us/act/1946-06-24/ch463/s6), [60 Stat. 303](/us/stat/60/303), related to imprisonment for nonpayment of fines or costs. See [section 3041 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3041).

Section 383, [act Apr. 20, 1904, ch. 1400, § 10](/us/act/1904-04-20/ch1400/s10), [33 Stat. 189](/us/stat/33/189), related to fees chargeable to United States. See [section 604 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s604), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
