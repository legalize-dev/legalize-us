# § 2608. Temporary disposition of materials and articles subject to this chapter

##### § 2608. Temporary disposition of materials and articles subject to this chapter

(1) sufficient safeguards will be taken by the institution for the protection of such material or article; and

(2) sufficient bond is posted by the institution to ensure its return to the Secretary.

*([Pub. L. 97–446, title III, § 309](/us/pl/97/446/tIII/s309), Jan. 12, 1983, [96 Stat. 2360](/us/stat/96/2360).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective on the 90th day after Jan. 12, 1983, or on any date which the President shall prescribe and publish in the Federal Register, with exceptions, see [section 315 of Pub. L. 97–446](/us/pl/97/446/s315), set out as a note under [section 2601 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2601).
