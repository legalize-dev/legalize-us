##### § 864. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–372, title II, § 271(6)](/us/pl/107/372/tII/s271/6), Dec. 19, 2002, [116 Stat. 3094](/us/stat/116/3094)

Section, [act May 18, 1920, ch. 190, § 11](/us/act/1920-05-18/ch190/s11), [41 Stat. 603](/us/stat/41/603); 1965 Reorg. Plan No. 2, eff. July 13, 1965, 30 F.R. 8819, [79 Stat. 1318](/us/stat/79/1318); 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 4, eff. Oct. 3, 1970, 35 F.R. 15627, [84 Stat. 2090](/us/stat/84/2090), provided that laws concerning retirement of commissioned Naval officers would apply to commissioned National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration officers.
