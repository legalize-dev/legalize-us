#### Codification

Section 1252a, [act June 27, 1952, ch. 477, title II](/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477/tII), ch. 5, § 242A, as added Nov. 18, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII, § 7347(a)](/us/pl/100/690/tVII/s7347/a), [102 Stat. 4471](/us/stat/102/4471), as amended, which related to expedited removal of aliens convicted of committing aggravated felonies, was renumbered section 238 of ch. 4 of title II of act June 27, 1952, by [Pub. L. 104–208, div. C, title III, § 308(b)(5)](/us/pl/104/208/dC/tIII/s308/b/5), Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3009–615](/us/stat/110/3009-615), and was transferred to [section 1228 of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1228).
