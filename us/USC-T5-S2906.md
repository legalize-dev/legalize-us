# § 2906. Oath; custody

##### § 2906. Oath; custody

The oath of office taken by an individual under [section 3331 of this title](/us/usc/t5/s3331) shall be delivered by him to, and preserved by, the House of Congress, agency, or court to which the office pertains.

*([Pub. L. 89–554](/us/pl/89/554), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 412](/us/stat/80/412).)*

Standard changes are made to conform with the definitions applicable and the style of this title as outlined in the preface to the report.
