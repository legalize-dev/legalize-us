# § 40503. Biomedical research fellowships

##### § 40503. Biomedical research fellowships

The Administrator and the Director of the National Institutes of Health shall create a joint program of graduate research fellowships in biomedical research described in [section 40501(a) of this title](/us/usc/t51/s40501/a). Fellowships under such program may provide for participation in approved research conferences and symposia.

*([Pub. L. 111–314, § 3](/us/pl/111/314/s3), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3389](/us/stat/124/3389).)*
