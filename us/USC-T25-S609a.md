# § 609a. Omitted

##### § 609a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–278, § 2](/us/pl/90/278/s2), Mar. 30, 1968, [82 Stat. 69](/us/stat/82/69), which exempted funds distributed to tribe members from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
