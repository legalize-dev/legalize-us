# § 3079. Insider threat policy compliance and reporting

##### § 3079. Insider threat policy compliance and reporting

(1) implement the policy established in accordance with [section 3024(f)(8) of this title](/us/usc/t50/s3024/f/8); and

(2) concurrent with the submission to Congress of budget justification materials in support of the budget of the President for a fiscal year that is submitted to Congress under [section 1105(a) of title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1105/a), submit to Congress a certification as to whether the element is in compliance with such policy.
