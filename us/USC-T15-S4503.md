# § 4503. Deposit of remainder of excess amount into Treasury as indirect restitution

##### § 4503. Deposit of remainder of excess amount into Treasury as indirect restitution

The amount that remains from the excess amount described in section 4502(c) 11 See References in Text note below. of this title after all disbursements have been made for a fiscal year under section 4502(d) 1 of this title shall be deposited by the Secretary of the Treasury into the general fund of the Treasury.

*([Pub. L. 99–509, title III, § 3004](/us/pl/99/509/tIII/s3004), Oct. 21, 1986, [100 Stat. 1884](/us/stat/100/1884).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Section 4502(c) and (d) of this title, referred to in text, was repealed by [section 4502(e) of this title](/us/usc/t15/s4502/e).
