# § 179. Repealed. [Aug. 7, 1946, ch. 770, § 1(59)](/us/act/1946-08-07/ch770/s1/59), [60 Stat. 871](/us/stat/60/871)

##### § 179. Repealed. [Aug. 7, 1946, ch. 770, § 1(59)](/us/act/1946-08-07/ch770/s1/59), [60 Stat. 871](/us/stat/60/871)

Section, acts [June 4, 1880, ch. 121](/us/act/1880-06-04/ch121), [21 Stat. 156](/us/stat/21/156); [July 1, 1916, ch. 209](/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209), [39 Stat. 309](/us/stat/39/309), related to annual report of Superintendent to Congress.

Prior to this repeal, 1940 Reorg. Plan No. IV, § 11(a), eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2422, [54 Stat. 1236](/us/stat/54/1236), set out in the Appendix to Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, directed that the annual report required by this section be submitted through the Federal Security Administrator.
