# § 419. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–778, title I, § 103(j)(1)](/us/pl/86/778/tI/s103/j/1), Sept. 13, 1960, [74 Stat. 937](/us/stat/74/937)

##### § 419. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–778, title I, § 103(j)(1)](/us/pl/86/778/tI/s103/j/1), Sept. 13, 1960, [74 Stat. 937](/us/stat/74/937)

Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title II, § 219](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tII/s219), as added [Aug. 28, 1950, ch. 809](/us/act/1950-08-28/ch809), title I, § 107, [64 Stat. 517](/us/stat/64/517), prescribed the effective date of this subchapter in Puerto Rico as January 1 of the first calendar year which begins more than 90 days after the date on which the President received a certification from the Governor of Puerto Rico.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 13, 1960, see section 103(v)(1), (3) of [Pub. L. 86–778](/us/pl/86/778), set out as an Effective Date of 1960 Amendment note under [section 402 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s402).
