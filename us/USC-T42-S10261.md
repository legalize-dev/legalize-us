# § 10261. Definitions

##### § 10261. Definitions

(1) The term “Chairman” means the Chairman of the Nuclear Waste Technical Review Board.

(2) The term “Board” means the Nuclear Waste Technical Review Board established under [section 10262 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s10262).
