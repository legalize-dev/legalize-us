#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to returns filed for partnership taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 2017, with certain exceptions, see [section 1101(g) of Pub. L. 114–74](/us/pl/114/74/s1101/g), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 6221 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s6221).
