# § 283y. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–118, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/95/118/tVII/s702), Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1070](/us/stat/91/1070)

##### § 283y. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–118, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/95/118/tVII/s702), Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1070](/us/stat/91/1070)

Section, [Pub. L. 86–147, § 28](/us/pl/86/147/s28), as added [Pub. L. 94–302, title I, § 103(a)(1)](/us/pl/94/302/tI/s103/a/1), May 31, 1976, [90 Stat. 592](/us/stat/90/592); H. Res. 5, Jan. 4, 1977, set forth provisions relating to United States participation in financial assistance by Inter-American Development Bank to any country engaging in a consistent pattern of gross violations of internationally recognized human rights. See [section 262d of this title](/us/usc/t22/s262d).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 3, 1977, see [section 1001 of Pub. L. 95–118](/us/pl/95/118/s1001), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 282i of this title](/us/usc/t22/s282i).
