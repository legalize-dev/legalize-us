# § 677g. Sunset

##### § 677g. Sunset

The authorities granted to the Secretary or the Director under this part shall expire on the date that is 7 years after November 15, 2021.

*([Pub. L. 107–296, title XXII, § 2238](/us/pl/107/296/tXXII/s2238), as added [Pub. L. 117–58, div. G, title VI, § 70602(a)](/us/pl/117/58/dG/tVI/s70602/a), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 1272](/us/stat/135/1272).)*
