# § 636. Omitted

##### § 636. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92, § 6](/us/act/1950-04-19/ch92/s6), [64 Stat. 46](/us/stat/64/46), which related to the adoption of a constitution by the Navajo Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
