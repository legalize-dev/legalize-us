# §§ 6a to 6d. Repealed. [Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1263, § 13(b)](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1263/s13/b), [68 Stat. 1231](/us/stat/68/1231)

##### §§ 6a to 6d. Repealed. [Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1263, § 13(b)](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1263/s13/b), [68 Stat. 1231](/us/stat/68/1231)

Sections, [act May 29, 1928, ch. 865](/us/act/1928-05-29/ch865), §§ 1–4, [45 Stat. 955](/us/stat/45/955), related to compensation. See sections 5101 et seq. and 5301 et seq. of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

[Act Dec. 12, 1930, ch. 10](/us/act/1930-12-12/ch10), [46 Stat. 1026](/us/stat/46/1026), formerly set out as a credit to these sections, was repealed by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 648](/us/stat/80/648).
