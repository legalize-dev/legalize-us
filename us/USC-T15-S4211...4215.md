# §§ 4211 to 4215. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–555, § 4](/us/pl/102/555/s4), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 4166](/us/stat/106/4166)

##### §§ 4211 to 4215. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–555, § 4](/us/pl/102/555/s4), Oct. 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 4166](/us/stat/106/4166)

Section 4211, [Pub. L. 98–365, title II, § 201](/us/pl/98/365/tII/s201), July 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 453](/us/stat/98/453), related to operation and data marketing of Landsat system by Secretary of Commerce and provided for Secretary’s authority to contract.

Section 4212, [Pub. L. 98–365, title II, § 202](/us/pl/98/365/tII/s202), July 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 454](/us/stat/98/454); [Pub. L. 100–147, title III, § 304](/us/pl/100/147/tIII/s304), Oct. 30, 1987, [101 Stat. 876](/us/stat/101/876), related to Secretary’s authority to contract for marketing of unenhanced data.

Section 4213, [Pub. L. 98–365, title II, § 203](/us/pl/98/365/tII/s203), July 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 454](/us/stat/98/454), related to conditions of competition for contract to market unenhanced data.

Section 4214, [Pub. L. 98–365, title II, § 204](/us/pl/98/365/tII/s204), July 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 455](/us/stat/98/455), related to sale of unenhanced data, entitlement to revenues from such sales, and the permissibility of marketing such data after end of Landsat system space segment.

Section 4215, [Pub. L. 98–365, title II, § 205](/us/pl/98/365/tII/s205), July 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 455](/us/stat/98/455), related to supply of unenhanced data to foreign ground stations and contract provisions relating thereto.
