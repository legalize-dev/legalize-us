# § 3422. Office of Inspector General

##### § 3422. Office of Inspector General

There shall be in the Department an Office of Inspector General, established in accordance with chapter 4 of title 5.

*([Pub. L. 96–88, title II, § 211](/us/pl/96/88/tII/s211), formerly § 212, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 675](/us/stat/93/675); renumbered § 211, [Pub. L. 103–382, title II, § 271(a)(2)](/us/pl/103/382/tII/s271/a/2), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3929](/us/stat/108/3929); amended [Pub. L. 117–286, § 4(b)(41)](/us/pl/117/286/s4/b/41), Dec. 27, 2022, [136 Stat. 4348](/us/stat/136/4348).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 211 of Pub. L. 96–88](/us/pl/96/88/s211) was renumbered section 210 and is classified to [section 3421 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s3421).

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–286](/us/pl/117/286) substituted “chapter 4 of title 5.” for “the Inspector General Act of 1978.”
