# § 13262. Enforcement

##### § 13262. Enforcement

(a) Violation Whoever violates [section 13261 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s13261) shall be subject to a civil penalty of not more than $5,000 for each violation.

(b) Willful violation Whoever willfully violates [section 13261 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s13261) shall be fined not more than $10,000 for each violation.

(c) Knowing and willful violation following prior violation and penalty Any person who knowingly and willfully violates [section 13261 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s13261) after having been subjected to a civil penalty for a prior violation of [section 13261 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s13261) shall be fined not more than $50,000.

*([Pub. L. 102–486, title V, § 512](/us/pl/102/486/tV/s512), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2899](/us/stat/106/2899).)*
