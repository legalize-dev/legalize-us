# § 421. Definition

##### § 421. Definition

In this part, the term “executive agency” has the meaning given that term under [section 133 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s133).

*([Pub. L. 107–296, title VIII, § 851](/us/pl/107/296/tVIII/s851), Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2235](/us/stat/116/2235).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

In text, “[section 133 of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s133)” substituted for “section 4(1) of the Office of Federal Procurement Policy Act ([41 U.S.C. 403(1)](/us/usc/t41/s403/1))” on authority of [Pub. L. 111–350, § 6(c)](/us/pl/111/350/s6/c), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3854](/us/stat/124/3854), which Act enacted Title 41, Public Contracts.
