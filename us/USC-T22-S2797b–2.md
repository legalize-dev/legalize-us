# § 2797b–2. Authority relating to MTCR adherents

##### § 2797b–2. Authority relating to MTCR adherents

Notwithstanding [section 2797b(b) of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2797b/b), the President may take the actions under [section 2797b(a)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2797b/a/2) under the circumstances described in [section 2797c(b)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2797c/b/2).

*([Pub. L. 90–629](/us/pl/90/629), ch. 7, § 73B, as added [Pub. L. 106–113, div. B, § 1000(a)(7) [div. B, title XI, § 1137]](/us/pl/106/113/dB/s1000/a/7/dB/tXI/s1137), Nov. 29, 1999, [113 Stat. 1536](/us/stat/113/1536), 1501A–496).*
