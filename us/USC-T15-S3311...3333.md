# §§ 3311 to 3333. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–60, § 2(b)](/us/pl/101/60/s2/b), July 26, 1989, [103 Stat. 158](/us/stat/103/158)

##### §§ 3311 to 3333. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–60, § 2(b)](/us/pl/101/60/s2/b), July 26, 1989, [103 Stat. 158](/us/stat/103/158)

Sections 3311 to 3320 comprised part A of this subchapter.

Section 3311, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 101](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s101), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3356](/us/stat/92/3356), related to inflation adjustments and other general price ceiling rules to be applied in establishing wellhead price controls.

Section 3312, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 102](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s102), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3358](/us/stat/92/3358); [Pub. L. 102–154, title I](/us/pl/102/154/tI), Nov. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 1000](/us/stat/105/1000), related to ceiling price for new natural gas and certain gas produced from Outer Continental Shelf.

Section 3313, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 103](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s103), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3361](/us/stat/92/3361), related to ceiling price for new, onshore production wells.

Section 3314, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 104](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s104), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3362](/us/stat/92/3362), related to a ceiling price for sales of natural gas dedicated to interstate commerce.

Section 3315, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 105](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s105), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3363](/us/stat/92/3363), related to ceiling price for sales under intrastate contracts existing on Nov. 8, 1978.

Section 3316, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 106](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s106), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3365](/us/stat/92/3365), related to ceiling price for sales under rollover contracts.

Section 3317, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 107](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s107), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3366](/us/stat/92/3366), related to ceiling price for high-cost natural gas.

Section 3318, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 108](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s108), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3367](/us/stat/92/3367), related to ceiling price for stripper well natural gas.

Section 3319, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 109](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s109), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3368](/us/stat/92/3368), related to ceiling price for other categories of natural gas.

Section 3320, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 110](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s110), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3368](/us/stat/92/3368), related to treatment of State severance taxes and certain production-related costs.

Sections 3331 to 3333 comprised part B of this subchapter.

Section 3331, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 121](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s121), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3369](/us/stat/92/3369); [Pub. L. 101–60, § 2(a)](/us/pl/101/60/s2/a), July 26, 1989, [103 Stat. 157](/us/stat/103/157), provided for elimination of price controls for certain natural gas sales.

Section 3332, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 122](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s122), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3370](/us/stat/92/3370), related to standby price control authority.

Section 3333, [Pub. L. 95–621, title I, § 123](/us/pl/95/621/tI/s123), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3371](/us/stat/92/3371), related to reports to Congress by Department of Energy.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Section 2(b) of Pub. L. 101–60](/us/pl/101/60/s2/b) provided that the repeal of sections 3311 to 3320 and 3331 to 3333 is effective Jan. 1, 1993.
