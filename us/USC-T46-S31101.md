# § 31101. Short title

##### § 31101. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “Public Vessels Act”.

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 6(c)](/us/pl/109/304/s6/c), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1521](/us/stat/120/1521).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Act of March 3, 1925, ch. 428](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch428), [43 Stat. 1112](/us/stat/43/1112), which enacted chapter 22 (§ 781 et seq.) of the former Appendix to this title, was popularly known as the “Public Vessels Act”, prior to being repealed and restated in this chapter by [Pub. L. 109–304](/us/pl/109/304), §§ 6(c), 19, Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1509](/us/stat/120/1509), 1710.
