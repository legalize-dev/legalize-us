#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 110–75, § 1](/us/pl/110/75/s1), Aug. 13, 2007, [121 Stat. 724](/us/stat/121/724), which related to conveyances of land and interests after Jan. 1, 2007, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
