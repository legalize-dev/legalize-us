# §§ 406 to 406d. Repealed. [Sept. 14, 1950, ch. 950, § 1](/us/act/1950-09-14/ch950/s1), [64 Stat. 849](/us/stat/64/849)

##### §§ 406 to 406d. Repealed. [Sept. 14, 1950, ch. 950, § 1](/us/act/1950-09-14/ch950/s1), [64 Stat. 849](/us/stat/64/849)

Sections, [act Feb. 26, 1929, ch. 331](/us/act/1929-02-26/ch331), §§ 1–5, [45 Stat. 1314–1316](/us/stat/45/1314-1316), related to Grand Teton National Park of Wyoming. See section 406d–1 et seq. of this title. See, also, sections 482m and 673b, relating to Teton National Forest, and National Elk Refuge, in Wyoming, respectively.

Sections 406 to 406d were not enacted as part of [act Sept. 14, 1950, ch. 950](/us/act/1950-09-14/ch950), [64 Stat. 849](/us/stat/64/849), which comprises this subchapter.
