#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 24, 1954, ch. 900, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-24/ch900/s1), [68 Stat. 789](/us/stat/68/789), which related to sale of certain public lands in Wisconsin, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
