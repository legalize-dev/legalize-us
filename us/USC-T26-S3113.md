# [§ 3113. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1903(a)(2)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1903/a/2), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1806](/us/stat/90/1806)]

##### [§ 3113. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1903(a)(2)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1903/a/2), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1806](/us/stat/90/1806)]

Section, added [Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 836](/us/act/1956-08-01/ch836), title II, § 201(a)(1), [70 Stat. 839](/us/stat/70/839), related to a restriction on exemptions from taxation for District of Columbia credit unions with respect to the tax imposed by [section 3111 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s3111).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable with respect to wages paid after Dec. 31, 1976, see [section 1903(d) of Pub. L. 94–455](/us/pl/94/455/s1903/d), set out as an Effective Date of 1976 Amendment note under [section 3101 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s3101).
