# § 495. Omitted

##### § 495. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 561, § 15](/us/act/1891-03-03/ch561/s15), [26 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/26/1101), which reserved the Annette Islands for the use of the Metlakahtla Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
