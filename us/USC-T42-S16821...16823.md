# §§ 16821 to 16823. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

##### §§ 16821 to 16823. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

Section 16821, [Pub. L. 109–155, title VII, § 707](/us/pl/109/155/tVII/s707), Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2937](/us/stat/119/2937), related to small business contracting. See [section 30306 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s30306), National and Commercial Space Programs.

Section 16822, [Pub. L. 109–155, title VII, § 708](/us/pl/109/155/tVII/s708), Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2938](/us/stat/119/2938), related to NASA healthcare program. See [section 31301 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s31301).

Section 16823, [Pub. L. 109–155, title VII, § 709](/us/pl/109/155/tVII/s709), Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2938](/us/stat/119/2938), related to offshore performance of contracts for the procurement of goods and services. See [section 30704 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s30704).
