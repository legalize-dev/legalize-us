#### **Editorial Note**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) and [Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246) repealed this chapter. [Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a). This chapter was comprised generally of title III (§§ 301–311) of [Pub. L. 106–224](/us/pl/106/224), June 20, 2000, [114 Stat. 428](/us/stat/114/428), as amended. Title III of [Pub. L. 106–224](/us/pl/106/224) was formerly set out as a note under [section 8101 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s8101).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal of this chapter and repeal of [Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) by [Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246) effective May 22, 2008, the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234), see [section 4 of Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246/s4), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 8701 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s8701).
