# § 12834. Uniform recordkeeping and reports to Congress

##### § 12834. Uniform recordkeeping and reports to Congress

(a) Uniform requirements The Secretary shall develop and establish uniform recordkeeping, performance reporting, and auditing requirements for use by participating jurisdictions.

(b) Omitted

*([Pub. L. 101–625, title II, § 284](/us/pl/101/625/tII/s284), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 4126](/us/stat/104/4126).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Subsec. (b) of this section, which required the Secretary to make an annual report to Congress that summarizes and assesses the results of reports provided under this section, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance. See, also, the 3rd item on page 102 of House Document No. 103–7.
