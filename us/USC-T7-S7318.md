# § 7318. Termination of Commission

##### § 7318. Termination of Commission

The Commission shall terminate on submission of the final report required by [section 7314 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s7314).

*([Pub. L. 104–127, title I, § 188](/us/pl/104/127/tI/s188), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 941](/us/stat/110/941).)*
