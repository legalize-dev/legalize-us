# § 415b. Omitted

##### § 415b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 17, 1928, ch. 602, § 1](/us/act/1928-05-17/ch602/s1), [45 Stat. 593](/us/stat/45/593), authorized appropriations of funds for purposes of former [section 415c of this title](/us/usc/t7/s415c), and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
