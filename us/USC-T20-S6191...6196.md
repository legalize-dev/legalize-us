# §§ 6191 to 6196. Omitted

##### §§ 6191 to 6196. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 6191 to 6196 were omitted pursuant to [section 6251 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6251) which provided that the authority under this chapter terminated on Oct. 1, 2001.

Section 6191, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/103/239/tIV/s401), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 594](/us/stat/108/594), authorized projects to further the purposes of this chapter.

Section 6192, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV, § 402](/us/pl/103/239/tIV/s402), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 594](/us/stat/108/594), related to performance outcomes and evaluation.

Section 6193, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/103/239/tIV/s403), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 596](/us/stat/108/596), related to training and technical assistance.

Section 6194, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV, § 404](/us/pl/103/239/tIV/s404), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 596](/us/stat/108/596); [Pub. L. 107–279, title IV, § 404(e)](/us/pl/107/279/tIV/s404/e), Nov. 5, 2002, [116 Stat. 1986](/us/stat/116/1986), related to Capacity Building and Information and Dissemination Network.

Section 6195, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/103/239/tIV/s405), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 597](/us/stat/108/597), required reports to Congress.

Section 6196, [Pub. L. 103–239, title IV, § 406](/us/pl/103/239/tIV/s406), May 4, 1994, [108 Stat. 597](/us/stat/108/597), related to funding.
