# § 3415. Regulations

##### § 3415. Regulations

The Secretary is authorized to issue such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 95–113, title XVII, § 1716](/us/pl/95/113/tXVII/s1716), Sept. 29, 1977, [91 Stat. 1040](/us/stat/91/1040).)*
