# §§ 1020i to 1020n. Repealed. [Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 964, § 2(a)(2)](/us/act/1946-08-14/ch964/s2/a/2), [60 Stat. 1062](/us/stat/60/1062)

##### §§ 1020i to 1020n. Repealed. [Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 964, § 2(a)(2)](/us/act/1946-08-14/ch964/s2/a/2), [60 Stat. 1062](/us/stat/60/1062)

Sections 1020i to 1020n, [act Jan. 29, 1937, ch. 7](/us/act/1937-01-29/ch7), §§ 1–6, [50 Stat. 5](/us/stat/50/5), provided for loans to farmers by the Governor of the Farm Credit Administration for production and harvesting of crops, feed for livestock and other related purposes.
