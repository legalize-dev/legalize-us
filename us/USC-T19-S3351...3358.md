# §§ 3351 to 3358. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78)

##### §§ 3351 to 3358. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78)

Section 3351, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 301](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s301), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2100](/us/stat/107/2100), provided definitions for this subpart.

Section 3352, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 302](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s302), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2100](/us/stat/107/2100), related to commencing of action for relief.

Section 3353, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 303](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s303), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2101](/us/stat/107/2101), related to required International Trade Commission actions on petitions.

Section 3354, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 304](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s304), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2102](/us/stat/107/2102), related to provision of relief from certain imports.

Section 3355, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 305](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s305), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2103](/us/stat/107/2103), related to termination of relief authority for certain articles.

Section 3356, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 306](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s306), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2104](/us/stat/107/2104), related to compensation authority.

Section 3357, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 307](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s307), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2104](/us/stat/107/2104), related to submission of petitions to the International Trade Commission.

Section 3358, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 309](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s309), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2105](/us/stat/107/2105); [Pub. L. 104–295, § 21(b)(4)](/us/pl/104/295/s21/b/4), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3530](/us/stat/110/3530); [Pub. L. 114–125, title VIII, § 802(d)(2)](/us/pl/114/125/tVIII/s802/d/2), Feb. 24, 2016, [130 Stat. 210](/us/stat/130/210), related to price-based snapback for frozen concentrated orange juice.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the date on which the USMCA entered into force (July 1, 2020), see [section 601 of Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113/s601), set out as a note under former [section 3301 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s3301).

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 318](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s318), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2108](/us/stat/107/2108), which provided that the provisions of subtitle A of title III of [Pub. L. 103–182](/us/pl/103/182) would take effect on the date the North American Free Trade Agreement entered into force with respect to the United States, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78), effective on the date the USMCA entered into force (July 1, 2020).
