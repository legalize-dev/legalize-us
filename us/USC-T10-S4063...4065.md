# [§§ 4063 to 4065. Omitted]

##### [§§ 4063 to 4065. Omitted]

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

As enacted, [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1842(b)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1842/b), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4244](/us/stat/134/4244), originally transferred sections 2361a, 2358b, and 2374a of this title to sections 4063 to 4065, respectively, to become effective Jan. 1, 2022. Subsequently, [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XVII, § 1701(q)(1)](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tXVII/s1701/q/1), (u)(3)(B), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 2148](/us/stat/135/2148), 2152, amended [section 1842(b) of Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283/s1842/b), effective as if included therein, so as to eliminate those transfers, thereby omitting these sections before they took effect. Other amendments by [Pub. L. 117–81](/us/pl/117/81) resulted in transfers of sections 2361a, 2358b, and 2374a to sections 4142, 1766, and 4025 of this title, respectively.
