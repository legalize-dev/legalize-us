# § 215. “Consul” defined

##### § 215. “Consul” defined

The word “consul” as used in this chapter shall mean the consular officer in charge of the district concerned.

*([Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 74, § 12](/us/act/1915-03-03/ch74/s12), [38 Stat. 822](/us/stat/38/822).)*
