# §§ 911 to 915. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 911 to 915. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 911, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 24, [39 Stat. 379](/us/stat/39/379); [Jan. 23, 1932, ch. 9, § 4](/us/act/1932-01-23/ch9/s4), [47 Stat. 13](/us/stat/47/13); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title III, § 306(b), [69 Stat. 665](/us/stat/69/665); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(d)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/d), (h), [73 Stat. 386](/us/stat/73/386), 387, provided for amount to be carried in reserve account by land bank associations. See [section 2052 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2052).

Section 912, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 24, [39 Stat. 379](/us/stat/39/379); [Jan. 23, 1932, ch. 9, § 4](/us/act/1932-01-23/ch9/s4), [47 Stat. 13](/us/stat/47/13), covered making good of any impairment of reserve before payment of any dividends.

Section 913, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 24, [39 Stat. 379](/us/stat/39/379); [Jan. 23, 1932, ch. 9, § 4](/us/act/1932-01-23/ch9/s4), [47 Stat. 13](/us/stat/47/13); [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, § 4](/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164/s4), [49 Stat. 315](/us/stat/49/315), provided for payment of dividends by land bank associations on balance of net earnings. See [section 2052 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2052).

Section 914, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 24](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s24), [39 Stat. 379](/us/stat/39/379); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, covered investment of reserves of land bank associations.

Section 915, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 24](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s24), [39 Stat. 379](/us/stat/39/379), provided for disposition of reserves on liquidation of land bank associations.
