# §§ 5801 to 5809. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title II, § 2813](/us/pl/115/334/tII/s2813), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4602](/us/stat/132/4602)

##### §§ 5801 to 5809. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title II, § 2813](/us/pl/115/334/tII/s2813), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4602](/us/stat/132/4602)

Section 5801, [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 352](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s352), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1010](/us/stat/110/1010), defined terms in this chapter.

Section 5802, [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 353](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s353), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1010](/us/stat/110/1010), established National Natural Resources Conservation Foundation as a charitable and nonprofit corporation to promote innovative solutions to the problems associated with the conservation of natural resources on private lands.

Section 5803, [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 354](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s354), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1011](/us/stat/110/1011), directed administration of the Foundation by a Board of Trustees and set forth provisions relating to its composition and operation.

Section 5804, [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 355](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s355), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1012](/us/stat/110/1012), related to appointment and hiring of officers and employees by the Board.

Section 5805, [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 356](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s356), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1013](/us/stat/110/1013); [Pub. L. 107–171, title II, § 2506](/us/pl/107/171/tII/s2506), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 274](/us/stat/116/274), related to corporate powers and obligations of the Foundation.

Section 5806, [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 357](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s357), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/110/1014), authorized provision of administrative services and support by the Department of Agriculture.

Section 5807, [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 358](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s358), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/110/1014); [Pub. L. 105–225, § 6(b)](/us/pl/105/225/s6/b), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1499](/us/stat/112/1499), directed auditing of Foundation accounts by the Attorney General.

Section 5808, [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 359](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s359), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/110/1014), related to release of the United States from liability for any debt, default, act, or omission of the Foundation.

Section 5809, [Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 360](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s360), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/110/1014), authorized appropriations.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 104–127, title III, § 351](/us/pl/104/127/tIII/s351), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1010](/us/stat/110/1010), which provided that subtitle F (§§ 351–360) of title III of [Pub. L. 104–127](/us/pl/104/127) could be cited as the “National Natural Resources Conservation Foundation Act”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 115–334, title II, § 2813](/us/pl/115/334/tII/s2813), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4602](/us/stat/132/4602).
