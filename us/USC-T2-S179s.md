# § 179s. Limitations of remedies

##### § 179s. Limitations of remedies

The remedies provided in [section 179r of this title](/us/usc/t2/s179r) shall be the exclusive remedies under sections 179*l* to 179w of this title, or any other Federal or State law, regarding the use of the seal described in [section 179m(a)(3) of this title](/us/usc/t2/s179m/a/3).

*([Pub. L. 104–285, title I, § 109](/us/pl/104/285/tI/s109), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3381](/us/stat/110/3381).)*
