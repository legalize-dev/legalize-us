# § 2503. Administration by Office of Career Education

##### § 2503. Administration by Office of Career Education

The provisions of this subchapter shall be carried out by the Secretary of Education through the Office of Career Education established pursuant to [section 2612a(c) of this title](/us/usc/t20/s2612a/c).

*([Pub. L. 94–482, title III, § 333](/us/pl/94/482/tIII/s333), Oct. 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 2222](/us/stat/90/2222); [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 301(a)(1)](/us/pl/96/88/tIII/s301/a/1), title V, § 507, Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 677](/us/stat/93/677), 692.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 2612a of this title](/us/usc/t20/s2612a), referred to in text, has been omitted from the Code.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective 30 days after Oct. 12, 1976, except either as specifically otherwise provided or, if not so specifically otherwise provided, effective July 1, 1976, where section provides for authorization of appropriations, see [section 532 of Pub. L. 94–482](/us/pl/94/482/s532), set out as an Effective Date of 1976 Amendment note under [section 1001 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1001).

#### Transfer of Functions

“Secretary of Education” substituted in text for “Commissioner” pursuant to sections 301(a)(1) and 507 of [Pub. L. 96–88](/us/pl/96/88), which are classified to sections 3441(a)(1) and 3507 of this title and which transferred functions of Commissioner of Education to Secretary of Education.

For authority of Secretary of Education to consolidate, alter, or discontinue the Office of Career Education, or to reallocate any functions vested by statute in the Office, see [section 413 of Pub. L. 96–88](/us/pl/96/88/s413), which is classified to [section 3473 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s3473).
