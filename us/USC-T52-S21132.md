# § 21132. Transfer of functions

##### § 21132. Transfer of functions

There are transferred to the Election Assistance Commission established under [section 20921 of this title](/us/usc/t52/s20921) all functions which the Federal Election Commission exercised under [section 20508(a) of this title](/us/usc/t52/s20508/a) before October 29, 2002.

*([Pub. L. 107–252, title VIII, § 802(a)](/us/pl/107/252/tVIII/s802/a), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1726](/us/stat/116/1726).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 15532 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s15532), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
