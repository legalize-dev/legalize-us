# § 9481. Establishment: purpose

##### § 9481. Establishment: purpose

(1) appointment as reserve officers, or enlistment as reserve noncommissioned officers, for service in the Air Force Reserve; or

(2) appointment as officers, or enlistment as noncommissioned officers, for service in the Space Force in a space force active status.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 571](/us/stat/70A/571), § 9411; renumbered § 9481, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 806(a)(2)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s806/a/2), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1832](/us/stat/132/1832); [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title IX, § 923(d)(16)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tIX/s923/d/16), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3815](/us/stat/134/3815); [Pub. L. 118–31, div. A, title XVII, § 1721(d)(1)](/us/pl/118/31/dA/tXVII/s1721/d/1), Dec. 22, 2023, [137 Stat. 667](/us/stat/137/667).)*

The words “upon military reservations or elsewhere” are omitted as surplusage. The words “of the Air Force” are inserted for clarity. The words “or enlistment as” are inserted for clarity.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2023—[Pub. L. 118–31](/us/pl/118/31) inserted dash after “to qualify them for” and par. (1) designation before “appointment”, substituted “; or” for “or the Space Force Reserve.”, and added par. (2).

2021—[Pub. L. 116–283, § 923(d)(16)(B)](/us/pl/116/283/s923/d/16/B), inserted “or the Space Force Reserve” after “the Air Force Reserve”.

[Pub. L. 116–283, § 923(d)(16)(A)](/us/pl/116/283/s923/d/16/A), which directed amendment of this section by inserting “, the Space Force,” after “members of the Air Force,”, was executed by making the insertion after “members of the Air Force” to reflect the probable intent of Congress.

2018—[Pub. L. 115–232](/us/pl/115/232) renumbered [section 9411 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s9411) as this section.
