#### **Editorial Notes**

Section 1181f was editorially reclassified as [section 2605 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s2605).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Sharing of Bureau of Land Management Timber Sale Receipts

[Pub. L. 103–66, title XIII, § 13983](/us/pl/103/66/tXIII/s13983), Aug. 10, 1993, [107 Stat. 682](/us/stat/107/682), as amended by [Pub. L. 103–443, § 1(b)](/us/pl/103/443/s1/b), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4631](/us/stat/108/4631), authorized the Secretary of the Treasury to make defined special payments in certain fiscal years to counties in Oregon and California in lieu of certain other payments under former sections 1181f and 1181f–1 et seq. of this title, prior to repeal by [Pub.
