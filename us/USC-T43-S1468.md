# § 1468. Working capital fund; availability for uniforms or allowances therefor

##### § 1468. Working capital fund; availability for uniforms or allowances therefor

The working capital fund, established by [section 1467 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1467), shall on and after June 13, 1956 be available for uniforms or allowances therefor, as authorized by [section 5901 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s5901).

*([June 13, 1956, ch. 380](/us/act/1956-06-13/ch380), title I, § 101, [70 Stat. 266](/us/stat/70/266).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 503 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s503) prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).
