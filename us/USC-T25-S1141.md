# § 1141. Omitted

##### § 1141. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–93, § 1](/us/pl/90/93/s1), Sept. 27, 1967, [81 Stat. 229](/us/stat/81/229), which related to distribution of judgment funds and payment of attorney’s fees and expenses, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
