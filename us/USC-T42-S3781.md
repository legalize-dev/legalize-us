##### § 3781. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609B(a)](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s609B/a), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2091](/us/stat/98/2091)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 801](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s801), as added [Pub. L. 96–157, § 2](/us/pl/96/157/s2), Dec. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 1201](/us/stat/93/1201), related to establishment of Office of Justice Assistance, Research, and Statistics.

A prior section 3781, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 601](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s601), June 19, 1968, [82 Stat. 209](/us/stat/82/209); 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 2, eff. July 1, 1970, 35 F.R. 7959, [84 Stat. 2085](/us/stat/84/2085); [Pub. L. 91–644, title I](/us/pl/91/644/tI), §§ 6(b), 9, Jan. 2, 1971, [84 Stat. 1887](/us/stat/84/1887), 1888; [Pub. L. 93–83, § 2](/us/pl/93/83/s2), Aug. 6, 1973, [87 Stat. 216](/us/stat/87/216); [Pub. L. 94–503, title I, § 129](/us/pl/94/503/tI/s129), Oct. 15, 1976, [90 Stat. 2424](/us/stat/90/2424), defined the terms used in this subchapter, prior to the general amendment of title I of [Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351) by [Pub. L. 96–157](/us/pl/96/157). See [section 10251 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10251), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 12, 1984, see [section 609AA(a) of Pub. L. 98–473](/us/pl/98/473/s609AA/a), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 10101 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10101), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
