# § 150304. Annual meeting

##### § 150304. Annual meeting

The corporation shall hold an annual meeting at a place designated by the corporation.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1389](/us/stat/112/1389).)*
