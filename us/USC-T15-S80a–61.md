# § 80a–61. Loans

##### § 80a–61. Loans

(1) any loan to a director, officer, or employee of, or general partner in, a business development company for the purpose of purchasing securities of such company as part of an executive compensation plan, if such loan meets the requirements of [section 80a–56(j) of this title](/us/usc/t15/s80a–56/j); or

(2) any loan to a company controlled by a business development company, which companies could be deemed to be under common control solely because a third person controls such business development company.

*([Aug. 22, 1940, ch. 686](/us/act/1940-08-22/ch686), title I, § 62, as added [Pub. L. 96–477, title I, § 105](/us/pl/96/477/tI/s105), Oct. 21, 1980, [94 Stat. 2287](/us/stat/94/2287).)*
