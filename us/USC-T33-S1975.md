# § 1975. Report to Congress

##### § 1975. Report to Congress

(1) if the Secretary has entered into an agreement under [section 1973 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1973), a statement by the Marine Debris Foundation that describes the activities carried out by the Marine Debris Foundation relating to the duties described in [section 1973 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1973); and

(2) a statement by 1 or more of the judges appointed under [section 1974 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1974) that explains the basis on which the winner of the cash prize was selected.

*([Pub. L. 109–449, § 125](/us/pl/109/449/s125), formerly [Pub. L. 116–224, title I, § 125](/us/pl/116/224/tI/s125), Dec. 18, 2020, [134 Stat. 1083](/us/stat/134/1083); renumbered [Pub. L. 109–449, § 125](/us/pl/109/449/s125), by [Pub. L. 119–65, § 4(a)](/us/pl/119/65/s4/a), Dec. 26, 2025, [139 Stat. 1989](/us/stat/139/1989).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This title, referred to in text, means title I of [Pub. L. 116–224](/us/pl/116/224), Dec. 18, 2020, [134 Stat. 1074](/us/stat/134/1074), which was classified principally to subchapter I (§ 4211 et seq.) of chapter 55 of this title. Subtitles B and C of title I of [Pub. L. 116–224](/us/pl/116/224) were transferred to subtitles B and C of [Pub. L. 109–449](/us/pl/109/449) by [Pub. L. 119–65](/us/pl/119/65), §§ 3(a), 4(a), Dec. 26, 2025, [139 Stat. 1987](/us/stat/139/1987), 1989, and are classified to subchapter II of this chapter and this subchapter, respectively. Subtitle A of title I of [Pub. L. 116–224](/us/pl/116/224) amended sections 1951, 1958, and 1959 of this title, and subtitle D of title I of [Pub. L. 116–224](/us/pl/116/224) is classified to part C (§ 4251) of subchapter I of chapter 55 of this title.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 4235 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s4235) prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 119–65](/us/pl/119/65).
