# § 1113. Attempt to commit murder or manslaughter

##### § 1113. Attempt to commit murder or manslaughter

Except as provided in [section 113 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s113), whoever, within the special maritime and territorial jurisdiction of the United States, attempts to commit murder or manslaughter, shall, for an attempt to commit murder be imprisoned not more than twenty years or fined under this title, or both, and for an attempt to commit manslaughter be imprisoned not more than seven years or fined under this title, or both.

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on title 18, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 456 ([Mar. 4, 1909, ch. 321, § 277](/us/act/1909-03-04/ch321/s277), [35 Stat. 1143](/us/stat/35/1143)).

Words “within the special maritime and territorial jurisdiction of the United States” were added in view of definitive [section 7 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s7), and section was rearranged to more clearly express intent of existing law.

Mandatory punishment provision was rephrased in the alternative.

#### Amendments
