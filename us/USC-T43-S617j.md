# § 617j. Omitted

##### § 617j. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Dec. 21, 1928, ch. 42, § 11](/us/act/1928-12-21/ch42/s11), [45 Stat. 1063](/us/stat/45/1063), authorized Secretary of the Interior to make surveys and investigations to determine what lands in Arizona should be included in Parker-Gila Valley reclamation project and required him to make a report to Congress not later than Dec. 10, 1931.
