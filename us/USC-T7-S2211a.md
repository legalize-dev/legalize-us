# § 2211a. Omitted

##### § 2211a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–501, title V, § 501(a)](/us/pl/95/501/tV/s501/a), Oct. 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 1691](/us/stat/92/1691), which related to establishment in Department of Agriculture of position of Under Secretary of Agriculture for International Affairs and Commodity Programs, was omitted in the complete revision of [Pub. L. 95–501](/us/pl/95/501) by [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1531](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1531), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3668](/us/stat/104/3668). See [section 5691 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s5691).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Redesignation of Assistant Secretary of Agriculture for International Affairs and Commodity Programs

[Pub. L. 95–501, title V, § 501(c)](/us/pl/95/501/tV/s501/c), Oct. 21, 1978, [92 Stat. 1691](/us/stat/92/1691), which prohibited the use of the designation of “Assistant Secretary of Agriculture for International Affairs and Commodity Programs” after Oct. 21, 1978, was omitted in the complete revision of [Pub. L. 95–501](/us/pl/95/501) by [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1531](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1531), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3668](/us/stat/104/3668).
