# § 963. Courts defined

##### § 963. Courts defined

As used in this chapter, unless the context indicates otherwise, the words “court” and “courts” include the Supreme Court of the United States and the courts enumerated in [section 610 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s610).

*([June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 927](/us/stat/62/927).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

This section was included to embrace the Supreme Court and all courts under the supervision of the Administrative Office of the United States Courts. See [section 610 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s610) and reviser’s note thereunder.
