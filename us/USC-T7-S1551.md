# § 1551. Short title

##### § 1551. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “Federal Seed Act.”

*([Aug. 9, 1939, ch. 615, § 1](/us/act/1939-08-09/ch615/s1), [53 Stat. 1275](/us/stat/53/1275).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

See [section 1610 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1610).

#### Short Title of 1983 Amendment

[Pub. L. 97–439, § 1](/us/pl/97/439/s1), Jan. 8, 1983, [96 Stat. 2287](/us/stat/96/2287), provided that: “This Act [amending sections 1561, 1571, 1581, 1582, 1585, and 1586 of this title and repealing sections 1583 and 1584 of this title] may be cited as the ‘Federal Seed Act Amendments of 1982’.”
