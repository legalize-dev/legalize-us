# § 229E. Requests for military assistance to enforce prohibition in certain emergencies

##### § 229E. Requests for military assistance to enforce prohibition in certain emergencies

The Attorney General may request the Secretary of Defense to provide assistance under [section 382 of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s382) 11 See References in Text note below. in support of Department of Justice activities relating to the enforcement of [section 229 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s229) in an emergency situation involving a chemical weapon. The authority to make such a request may be exercised by another official of the Department of Justice in accordance with [section 382(f)(2) of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s382/f/2).1

*(Added [Pub. L. 105–277, div. I, title II, § 201(a)](/us/pl/105/277/dI/tII/s201/a), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–869](/us/stat/112/2681-869).)*
