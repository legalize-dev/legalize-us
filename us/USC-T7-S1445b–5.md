##### § 1445b–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–624, title XI, § 1161(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/624/tXI/s1161/a/2), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3520](/us/stat/104/3520)

Section, [act Oct. 31, 1949, ch. 792, title I, § 107F](/us/act/1949-10-31/ch792/tI/s107F), as added Dec. 23, 1985, [Pub. L. 99–198, title X, § 1006](/us/pl/99/198/tX/s1006), [99 Stat. 1448](/us/stat/99/1448), related to export certificate programs for 1986 through 1990 crops of wheat and feed grains.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective beginning with 1991 crop of an agricultural commodity, with provision for prior crops, see [section 1171 of Pub. L. 101–624](/us/pl/101/624/s1171), set out as an Effective Date of 1990 Amendment note under [section 1421 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1421).
