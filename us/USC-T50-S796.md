# § 796. Effect of subchapter on other criminal laws

##### § 796. Effect of subchapter on other criminal laws

The foregoing provisions of this subchapter shall be construed as being in addition to and not in modification of existing criminal statutes.

*([Sept. 23, 1950, ch. 1024](/us/act/1950-09-23/ch1024), title I, § 17, [64 Stat. 1003](/us/stat/64/1003).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [act Sept. 23, 1950, ch. 1024](/us/act/1950-09-23/ch1024), [64 Stat. 987](/us/stat/64/987), known as the Subversive Activities Control Act of 1950, which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title I of such act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 781 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s781) and Tables.
