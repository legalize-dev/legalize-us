# § 467h. Reports

##### § 467h. Reports

(1) describes the status of the Program;

(2) describes the progress achieved by Federal agencies during the 2 preceding fiscal years in implementing the Federal Guidelines for Dam Safety;

(3) describes the progress achieved in dam safety by States participating in the Program; and

(4) includes any recommendations for legislative and other action that the Administrator considers necessary.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 467h, [Pub. L. 92–367, § 9](/us/pl/92/367/s9), as added [Pub. L. 99–662, title XII, § 1201(b)](/us/pl/99/662/tXII/s1201/b), Nov. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 4262](/us/stat/100/4262), provided for creation of National Dam Safety Review Board, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–303, title II, § 215(c)(2)](/us/pl/104/303/tII/s215/c/2), Oct. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 3685](/us/stat/110/3685).

A prior [section 12 of Pub. L. 92–367](/us/pl/92/367/s12) was renumbered section 13, and is classified to [section 467i of this title](/us/usc/t33/s467i).

Another prior [section 12 of Pub. L. 92–367](/us/pl/92/367/s12) was classified to [section 467k of this title](/us/usc/t33/s467k) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–303](/us/pl/104/303).
