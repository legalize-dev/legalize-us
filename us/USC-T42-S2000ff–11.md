# § 2000ff–11. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 2000ff–11. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this chapter (except for [section 2000ff–7 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2000ff–7)).

*([Pub. L. 110–233, title II, § 212](/us/pl/110/233/tII/s212), May 21, 2008, [122 Stat. 920](/us/stat/122/920).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective 18 months after May 21, 2008, see [section 213 of Pub. L. 110–233](/us/pl/110/233/s213), set out as a note under [section 2000ff of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2000ff).
