##### §§ 268 to 272. Repealed. [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 651(a)(1), [46 Stat. 762](/us/stat/46/762), eff. June 18, 1930

Sections, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356, title IV](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356/tIV), §§ 454–458, [42 Stat. 955](/us/stat/42/955), 956, related to duties and compensation of boarding and discharging inspectors, custody of cargo not unladen promptly, unlading at risk of consignee and time for unlading bulk cargo.

Provisions of Tariff Act of 1930 corresponding to section 268, see [section 1455 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1455); section 269, see [section 1456 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1456); section 270, see [section 1457 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1457); section 271, none; section 272, see [section 1458 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1458).
