# § 410ww–8. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 410ww–8. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this part.

*([Pub. L. 102–419, title I, § 109](/us/pl/102/419/tI/s109), Oct. 16, 1992, [106 Stat. 2143](/us/stat/106/2143); [Pub. L. 106–356, § 2(b)](/us/pl/106/356/s2/b), Oct. 24, 2000, [114 Stat. 1391](/us/stat/114/1391).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2000—[Pub. L. 106–356](/us/pl/106/356) struck out “: *Provided*, That the amount to be appropriated for the operation, development or restoration of non-federally owned properties within the boundaries of the park shall not exceed $200,000” after “this part”.
