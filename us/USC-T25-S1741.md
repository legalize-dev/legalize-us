# § 1741. Omitted

##### § 1741. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 97–399, § 2](/us/pl/97/399/s2), Dec. 31, 1982, [96 Stat. 2012](/us/stat/96/2012), which set out congressional findings and declaration of policy, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 97–399, § 1](/us/pl/97/399/s1), Dec. 31, 1982, [96 Stat. 2012](/us/stat/96/2012), provided that [Pub. L. 97–399](/us/pl/97/399), enacting this part A (§ 1741 et seq.) of this subchapter, could be cited as the “Florida Indian Land Claims Settlement Act of 1982”.
