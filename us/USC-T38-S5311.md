# § 5311. Prohibition of certain benefit payments

##### § 5311. Prohibition of certain benefit payments

There shall be no payment of dependency and indemnity compensation, death compensation, or death pension which, because of a widow’s relationship with another man before enactment of [Public Law 87–674](/us/pl/87/674), would not have been payable by the Veterans’ Administration under the standard for determining remarriage applied by that agency before said enactment.

*(Added [Pub. L. 91–376, § 8(b)](/us/pl/91/376/s8/b), Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 790](/us/stat/84/790), § 3111; renumbered § 5311, [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV, § 402(b)(1)](/us/pl/102/40/tIV/s402/b/1), May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 238](/us/stat/105/238).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Public Law 87–674](/us/pl/87/674), referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 87–674](/us/pl/87/674), Sept. 19, 1962, [76 Stat. 558](/us/stat/76/558), which was enacted Sept. 19, 1962, and amended sections 101, 103, and 3010 [now 5110] of this title to provide for the restoration of certain widows and children to the benefit rolls upon annulment of their marriages or remarriages.

#### Amendments

1991—[Pub. L. 102–40](/us/pl/102/40) renumbered [section 3111 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s3111) as this section.
