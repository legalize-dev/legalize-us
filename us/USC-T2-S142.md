# § 142. Omitted

##### § 142. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, acts [June 29, 1922, ch. 251, § 1](/us/act/1922-06-29/ch251/s1), [42 Stat. 715](/us/stat/42/715); Nov. 5, 1990, [Pub. L. 101–520, title III, § 307](/us/pl/101/520/tIII/s307), [104 Stat. 2277](/us/stat/104/2277); [Pub. L. 108–7, div. H, title I, § 1208(a)](/us/pl/108/7/dH/tI/s1208/a), Feb. 20, 2003, [117 Stat. 375](/us/stat/117/375), established office of administrative assistant and disbursing officer of Library of Congress which was abolished by [section 142a of this title](/us/usc/t2/s142a). As amended generally by [Pub. L. 108–7](/us/pl/108/7), section no longer relates to office of administrative assistant and disbursing officer. See [section 141 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s141).
