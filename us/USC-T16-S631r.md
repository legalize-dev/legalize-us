# § 631r. Repealed. [July 25, 1947, ch. 327, § 1](/us/act/1947-07-25/ch327/s1), [61 Stat. 449](/us/stat/61/449)

##### § 631r. Repealed. [July 25, 1947, ch. 327, § 1](/us/act/1947-07-25/ch327/s1), [61 Stat. 449](/us/stat/61/449)

Section, [act Feb. 26, 1944, ch. 65, § 19](/us/act/1944-02-26/ch65/s19), [58 Stat. 104](/us/stat/58/104), limited the duration of the provisions of sections 631a–631q of this title which implement the Provisional Fur-Seal Agreement of 1942 to twelve months after the cessation of hostilities of World War II.
