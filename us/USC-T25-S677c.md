# § 677c. Omitted

##### § 677c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 4](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s4), [68 Stat. 868](/us/stat/68/868), which related to transfer of members from full-blood roll to mixed-blood group, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
