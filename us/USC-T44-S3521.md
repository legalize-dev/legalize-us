# § 3521. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 3521. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to the Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs to carry out the provisions of this subchapter, and for no other purpose, $8,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 1996, 1997, 1998, 1999, 2000, and 2001.

*(Added [Pub. L. 104–13, § 2](/us/pl/104/13/s2), May 22, 1995, [109 Stat. 184](/us/stat/109/184), § 3520; amended [Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [[div. A]](/us/pl/106/398/s1/dA), title X, § 1064(b)], Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1654](/us/stat/114/1654), 1654A–275; renumbered § 3521, [Pub. L. 107–198, § 3(a)(1)](/us/pl/107/198/s3/a/1), June 28, 2002, [116 Stat. 730](/us/stat/116/730).)*
