# § 148. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 704(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s704/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792](/us/stat/90/2792)

##### § 148. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 704(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s704/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792](/us/stat/90/2792)

Section, acts [June 25, 1910, ch. 431, § 13](/us/act/1910-06-25/ch431/s13), [36 Stat. 858](/us/stat/36/858); June 29, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–533, § 1(13)](/us/pl/86/533/s1/13), [74 Stat. 248](/us/stat/74/248), authorized withdrawal of lands in Indian reservations for power or reservation sites.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 704(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s704/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792](/us/stat/90/2792), provided that the repeal made by section 704(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

#### Savings Provision

Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579) not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579/s701), set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1701).
