# § 423l–1. Short title; definitions

##### § 423l–1. Short title; definitions

(a) Short title Sections 423*l*–1 to 423*l*–6 of this title may be cited as the “Richmond National Battlefield Park Act of 2000”.

(b) Definitions In sections 423*l*–1 to 423*l*–6 of this title:

(1) Battlefield park The term “battlefield park” means the Richmond National Battlefield Park.

(2) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

*([Pub. L. 106–511, title V, § 501](/us/pl/106/511/tV/s501), Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2373](/us/stat/114/2373).)*
