# § 541e. Availability of funds for acquisition of lands, etc., within added area

##### § 541e. Availability of funds for acquisition of lands, etc., within added area

Notwithstanding the provisions of [section 200306(a)(2) of title 54](/us/usc/t54/s200306/a/2), moneys appropriated from the Land and Water Conservation Fund shall be available for the acquisition of any lands, waters, or interests therein within the area added to the Siuslaw National Forest by this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 93–535, § 6](/us/pl/93/535/s6), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1733](/us/stat/88/1733); [Pub. L. 113–287, § 5(d)(15)](/us/pl/113/287/s5/d/15), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3265](/us/stat/128/3265).)*
