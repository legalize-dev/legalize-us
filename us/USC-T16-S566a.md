# § 566a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–288, § 3(b)](/us/pl/92/288/s3/b), May 5, 1972, [86 Stat. 134](/us/stat/86/134)

##### § 566a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–288, § 3(b)](/us/pl/92/288/s3/b), May 5, 1972, [86 Stat. 134](/us/stat/86/134)

Section, [act Oct. 26, 1949, ch. 735, § 1](/us/act/1949-10-26/ch735/s1), [63 Stat. 909](/us/stat/63/909), provided for annual authorization of appropriations for carrying out sections 564, 565, and 566 of this title, with maximum limits for the fiscal years ending June 30, 1950 through June 30, 1954.
