# § 402. Providing business data to Designated Statistical Agencies

##### § 402. Providing business data to Designated Statistical Agencies

The Bureau of the Census may provide business data to the Bureau of Economic Analysis and the Bureau of Labor Statistics (“Designated Statistical Agencies”) if such information is required for an authorized statistical purpose and the provision is the subject of a written agreement with that Designated Statistical Agency, or their successors, as defined in [section 3576(e) of title 44](/us/usc/t44/s3576/e).

*(Added [Pub. L. 107–347, title V, § 526(b)(1)](/us/pl/107/347/tV/s526/b/1), Dec. 17, 2002, [116 Stat. 2969](/us/stat/116/2969); amended [Pub. L. 115–435, title III, § 302(c)(2)](/us/pl/115/435/tIII/s302/c/2), Jan. 14, 2019, [132 Stat. 5553](/us/stat/132/5553).)*
