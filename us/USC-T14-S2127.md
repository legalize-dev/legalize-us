# § 2127. Recall to active duty during war or national emergency

##### § 2127. Recall to active duty during war or national emergency

In time of war or national emergency, the Secretary may order any regular officer on the retired list to active duty.

*(Added [Pub. L. 88–130, § 1(10)(C)](/us/pl/88/130/s1/10/C), Sept. 24, 1963, [77 Stat. 189](/us/stat/77/189), § 331; renumbered § 2127, [Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 112(b)](/us/pl/115/282/tI/s112/b), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4216](/us/stat/132/4216).)*
