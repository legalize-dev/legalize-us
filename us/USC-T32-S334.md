# [§ 334. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–124, § 3](/us/pl/97/124/s3), Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1666](/us/stat/95/1666)]

##### [§ 334. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–124, § 3](/us/pl/97/124/s3), Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1666](/us/stat/95/1666)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 94–464, § 2(b)](/us/pl/94/464/s2/b), Oct. 8, 1976, [90 Stat. 1986](/us/stat/90/1986); amended [Pub. L. 96–513, title V, § 515(3)](/us/pl/96/513/tV/s515/3), Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2937](/us/stat/94/2937), provided for the payment of malpractice liability of National Guard Medical personnel. See sections 1089(a) of Title 10, Armed Forces, and 2671 of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Amendment After Repeal

[Pub. L. 97–258, § 3(h)(1)](/us/pl/97/258/s3/h/1), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1065](/us/stat/96/1065), purported to substitute “[section 1304 of title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1304)” for “[section 1302 of the Act of July 27, 1956](/us/act/1956-07-27/s1302), ([31 U.S.C. 724a](/us/usc/t31/s724a))” in subsec. (a) of [section 334 of this title](/us/usc/t32/s334), without reference to the earlier repeal of that section by [Pub. L. 97–124, § 3](/us/pl/97/124/s3), Dec. 29, 1981, [95 Stat. 1666](/us/stat/95/1666).

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective only with respect to claims arising on or after Dec. 29, 1981, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 97–124](/us/pl/97/124/s4), set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under [section 1089 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s1089), Armed Forces.
