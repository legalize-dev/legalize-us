# § 1947. Assignment of personnel as instructors

##### § 1947. Assignment of personnel as instructors

The Commandant may assign any member to appropriate instruction duty at the Academy.

*([Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393), [63 Stat. 510](/us/stat/63/510), § 192; [Pub. L. 98–557, § 15(a)(3)(H)](/us/pl/98/557/s15/a/3/H), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2865](/us/stat/98/2865); renumbered § 1947, [Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 110(b)](/us/pl/115/282/tI/s110/b), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4212](/us/stat/132/4212).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on title 14, U.S.C., 1946 ed., § 15i ([Apr. 16, 1937, ch. 107, § 8](/us/act/1937-04-16/ch107/s8), [50 Stat. 67](/us/stat/50/67)).
