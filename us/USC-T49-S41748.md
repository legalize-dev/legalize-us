# [§ 41748. Repealed. [Pub. L. 118–63, title V, § 561](/us/pl/118/63/tV/s561)(*l*), May 16, 2024, [138 Stat. 1217](/us/stat/138/1217)]

##### [§ 41748. Repealed. [Pub. L. 118–63, title V, § 561](/us/pl/118/63/tV/s561)(*l*), May 16, 2024, [138 Stat. 1217](/us/stat/138/1217)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 108–176, title IV, § 410(b)](/us/pl/108/176/tIV/s410/b), Dec. 12, 2003, [117 Stat. 2548](/us/stat/117/2548), established a marketing incentive program for eligible places that received subsidized service by an air carrier under section 41733.

Another [section 410(b) of Pub. L. 108–176](/us/pl/108/176/s410/b) amended the table of sections at the beginning of this chapter.
