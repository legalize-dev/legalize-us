# § 303102. Funding

##### § 303102. Funding

For each of fiscal years 2012 to 2023,11 See Application of Section note below. $150,000,000 shall be deposited in the Historic Preservation Fund from revenues due and payable to the United States under section 9 of the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act ([43 U.S.C. 1338](/us/usc/t43/s1338)), [section 8733(b) of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s8733/b), or both, notwithstanding any provision of law that those proceeds shall be credited to miscellaneous receipts of the Treasury.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3206](/us/stat/128/3206); [Pub. L. 114–289, title VIII, § 802(a)](/us/pl/114/289/tVIII/s802/a), (c)(1), Dec. 16, 2016, [130 Stat. 1494](/us/stat/130/1494), 1495; [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 809(s)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s809/s), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1844](/us/stat/132/1844).)*

Reference to fiscal years 1977–2011 is omitted as obsolete.
