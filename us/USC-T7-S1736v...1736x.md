# §§ 1736v to 1736x. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1572(3)](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1572/3), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3702](/us/stat/104/3702)

##### §§ 1736v to 1736x. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–624, title XV, § 1572(3)](/us/pl/101/624/tXV/s1572/3), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3702](/us/stat/104/3702)

Section 1736v, [Pub. L. 99–198, title XI, § 1127](/us/pl/99/198/tXI/s1127), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1483](/us/stat/99/1483); [Pub. L. 99–260, § 6](/us/pl/99/260/s6), Mar. 20, 1986, [100 Stat. 50](/us/stat/100/50); [Pub. L. 100–418, title IV, § 4306](/us/pl/100/418/tIV/s4306), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1398](/us/stat/102/1398), provided for program to develop and expand markets for United States agricultural commodities.

Section 1736w, [Pub. L. 99–198, title XI, § 1128](/us/pl/99/198/tXI/s1128), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1485](/us/stat/99/1485), provided for program of export sales of poultry, beef and pork meats and meat-food products for 1986 through 1989.

Section 1736x, [Pub. L. 99–198, title XI, § 1132](/us/pl/99/198/tXI/s1132), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1488](/us/stat/99/1488); [Pub. L. 100–418, title IV, § 4307](/us/pl/100/418/tIV/s4307), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1398](/us/stat/102/1398), provided for reports by agricultural attachés. See [section 1748 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1748).
