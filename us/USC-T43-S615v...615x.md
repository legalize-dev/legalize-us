# §§ 615v to 615x. Omitted

##### §§ 615v to 615x. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 615v, [Pub. L. 86–745, § 1](/us/pl/86/745/s1), Sept. 13, 1960, [74 Stat. 882](/us/stat/74/882), authorized construction, operation, and maintenance of Dallas Project, Oregon.

Section 615w, [Pub. L. 86–745, § 2](/us/pl/86/745/s2), Sept. 13, 1960, [74 Stat. 882](/us/stat/74/882), related to provisions concerning construction, operation, and maintenance of this project and to rates for power and energy.

Section 615x, [Pub. L. 86–745, § 3](/us/pl/86/745/s3), Sept. 13, 1960, [74 Stat. 883](/us/stat/74/883), authorized appropriations for this project.
