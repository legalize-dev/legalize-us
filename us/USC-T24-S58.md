# § 58. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### § 58. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section, acts [June 4, 1897, ch. 2, § 1](/us/act/1897-06-04/ch2/s1), [30 Stat. 54](/us/stat/30/54); [June 28, 1950, ch. 383](/us/act/1950-06-28/ch383), title IV, § 402(d), [64 Stat. 272](/us/stat/64/272), provided for sale of medical and hospital supplies to the Soldiers’ Home [now United States Soldiers’ and Airmen’s Home] in the District of Columbia. See sections 4624 and 9624 of Title 10, Armed Forces.
