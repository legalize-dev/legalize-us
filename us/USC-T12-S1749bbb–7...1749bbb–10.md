# §§ 1749bbb–7 to 1749bbb–10. Omitted

##### §§ 1749bbb–7 to 1749bbb–10. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 1749bbb–7 to 1749bbb–10, comprising part B of this subchapter, were omitted in view of termination of powers of Director under this part on Nov. 30, 1983, pursuant to former [section 1749bbb(b) of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1749bbb/b).
