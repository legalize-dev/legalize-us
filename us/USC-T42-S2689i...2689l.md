# §§ 2689i to 2689l. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX, § 902(e)(2)(B)](/us/pl/97/35/tIX/s902/e/2/B), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 560](/us/stat/95/560)

##### §§ 2689i to 2689l. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX, § 902(e)(2)(B)](/us/pl/97/35/tIX/s902/e/2/B), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 560](/us/stat/95/560)

Section 2689i, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II, § 221](/us/pl/88/164/tII/s221), as added [Pub. L. 94–63, title III, § 303](/us/pl/94/63/tIII/s303), July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 322](/us/stat/89/322); amended [Pub. L. 94–273, § 44](/us/pl/94/273/s44), Apr. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 381](/us/stat/90/381), set forth provisions respecting Federal share, etc., for facilities assistance.

A prior [section 221 of Pub. L. 88–164](/us/pl/88/164/s221), title II, was classified to [section 2688a of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2688a).

Section 2689j, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II, § 222](/us/pl/88/164/tII/s222), as added [Pub. L. 94–63, title III, § 303](/us/pl/94/63/tIII/s303), July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 323](/us/stat/89/323), related to approval of projects, and procedures applicable.

A prior [section 222 of Pub. L. 88–164](/us/pl/88/164/s222), title II, was classified to [section 2688b of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2688b).

Section 2689k, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II, § 223](/us/pl/88/164/tII/s223), as added [Pub. L. 94–63, title III, § 303](/us/pl/94/63/tIII/s303), July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 325](/us/stat/89/325), related to payments for projects.

A prior [section 223 of Pub. L. 88–164](/us/pl/88/164/s223), title II, was classified to [section 2688c of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2688c).

Section 2689*l*, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II, § 224](/us/pl/88/164/tII/s224), as added [Pub. L. 94–63, title III, § 303](/us/pl/94/63/tIII/s303), July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 326](/us/stat/89/326), related to judicial review of projects.

A prior [section 224 of Pub. L. 88–164](/us/pl/88/164/s224), title II, was classified to [section 2688d of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2688d).
