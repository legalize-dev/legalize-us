# § 7111. Purpose

##### § 7111. Purpose

(1) provide all students with access to a well-rounded education;

(2) improve school conditions for student learning; and

(3) improve the use of technology in order to improve the academic achievement and digital literacy of all students.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 4101](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s4101), as added [Pub. L. 114–95, title IV, § 4101](/us/pl/114/95/tIV/s4101), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1968](/us/stat/129/1968).)*
