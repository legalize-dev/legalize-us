# [§ 2517. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 811(c)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s811/c/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1845](/us/stat/132/1845)]

##### [§ 2517. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 811(c)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s811/c/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1845](/us/stat/132/1845)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 102–190, div. A, title VIII, § 821(a)](/us/pl/102/190/dA/tVIII/s821/a), Dec. 5, 1991, [105 Stat. 1430](/us/stat/105/1430), § 2525; renumbered § 2517 and amended [Pub. L. 102–484, div. D, title XLII, § 4227](/us/pl/102/484/dD/tXLII/s4227), Oct. 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 2685](/us/stat/106/2685); [Pub. L. 111–383, div. A, title IX, § 901(j)(4)](/us/pl/111/383/dA/tIX/s901/j/4), Jan. 7, 2011, [124 Stat. 4324](/us/stat/124/4324), established the Office for Foreign Defense Critical Technology Monitoring and Assessment and set out its responsibilities.

A prior section 2517 was renumbered [section 2523 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s2523) and subsequently repealed.
