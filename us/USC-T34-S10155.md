# § 10155. Rules

##### § 10155. Rules

The Attorney General shall issue rules to carry out this part. The first such rules shall be issued not later than one year after the date on which amounts are first made available to carry out this part.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 504](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s504), as added [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI, § 1111(a)(2)(C)](/us/pl/109/162/tXI/s1111/a/2/C), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3097](/us/stat/119/3097).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3754 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3754), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
