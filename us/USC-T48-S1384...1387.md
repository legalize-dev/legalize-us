# §§ 1384 to 1387. Transferred

##### §§ 1384 to 1387. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 1384, [act July 2, 1940, ch. 516, § 4](/us/act/1940-07-02/ch516/s4), [54 Stat. 724](/us/stat/54/724); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 801, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7877, [60 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/60/1101), related to functions of Smithsonian Institution, and was transferred to [section 79b of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s79b), Education.

Section 1385, [act July 2, 1940, ch. 516, § 5](/us/act/1940-07-02/ch516/s5), [54 Stat. 725](/us/stat/54/725); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 801, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7877, [60 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/60/1101), related to resident manager, and was transferred to [section 79c of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s79c).

Section 1386, [act July 2, 1940, ch. 516, § 6](/us/act/1940-07-02/ch516/s6), [54 Stat. 725](/us/stat/54/725); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 801, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7877, [60 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/60/1101), related to deposit of receipts into treasury, and was transferred to [section 79d of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s79d).

Section 1387, [act July 2, 1940, ch. 516, § 7](/us/act/1940-07-02/ch516/s7), [54 Stat. 725](/us/stat/54/725), related to authorization of appropriations, and was transferred to [section 79e of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s79e).
