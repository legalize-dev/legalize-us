# § 55115. Supplies on fish processing vessels

##### § 55115. Supplies on fish processing vessels

[Section 55102 of this title](/us/usc/t46/s55102) does not apply to supplies aboard a United States documented fish processing vessel that are necessary and used for processing or assembling fishery products aboard such a vessel.

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 8(c)](/us/pl/109/304/s8/c), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1640](/us/stat/120/1640).)*

The words “[Section 55102 of this title](/us/usc/t46/s55102) does not apply to” are substituted for “for the purposes of this section” and “shall be considered ship’s equipment and not merchandise” for consistency in the chapter.
