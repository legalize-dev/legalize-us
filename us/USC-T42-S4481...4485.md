##### §§ 4481 to 4485. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–288, title VII, § 703](/us/pl/93/288/tVII/s703), formerly title VI, § 603, May 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 164](/us/stat/88/164); renumbered title VII, § 703, [Pub. L. 103–337, div. C, title XXXIV, § 3411(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/337/dC/tXXXIV/s3411/a/1), (2), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 3100](/us/stat/108/3100)

Section 4481, [Pub. L. 91–606, title II, § 251](/us/pl/91/606/tII/s251), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1757](/us/stat/84/1757), related to repair and restoration of damaged United States facilities.

Section 4482, [Pub. L. 91–606, title II, § 252](/us/pl/91/606/tII/s252), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1757](/us/stat/84/1757); [Pub. L. 93–251](/us/pl/93/251) title I, § 45(a), Mar. 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 24](/us/stat/88/24), related to restoration of State and local public facilities.

Section 4483, [Pub. L. 91–606, title II, § 253](/us/pl/91/606/tII/s253), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1758](/us/stat/84/1758), related to priority to applications for public facility and public housing assistance in major disaster areas.

Section 4484, [Pub. L. 91–606, title II, § 254](/us/pl/91/606/tII/s254), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1758](/us/stat/84/1758), related to relocation assistance.

Section 4485, [Pub. L. 91–606, title II, § 255](/us/pl/91/606/tII/s255), as added [Pub. L. 92–209, § 1](/us/pl/92/209/s1), Dec. 18, 1971, [85 Stat. 742](/us/stat/85/742), related to private medical care facilities grants for repair, reconstruction, or replacement of damaged or destroyed facilities.
