# § 610c. Omitted

##### § 610c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–534, § 4](/us/pl/90/534/s4), Sept. 28, 1968, [82 Stat. 884](/us/stat/82/884), which related to mortgages and deeds of trust, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
