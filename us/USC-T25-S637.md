# § 637. Omitted

##### § 637. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92, § 7](/us/act/1950-04-19/ch92/s7), [64 Stat. 46](/us/stat/64/46), which related to use of Navajo tribal funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
