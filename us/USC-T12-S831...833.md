# §§ 831 to 833. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 831 to 833. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 831, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 17, [39 Stat. 375](/us/stat/39/375); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 9](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s9), [50 Stat. 707](/us/stat/50/707); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), contained an enumeration of the powers of Farm Credit Administration. See [section 2252 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2252).

Section 832, acts [Sept. 21, 1944, ch. 412](/us/act/1944-09-21/ch412), title VI, § 601(a) to (c), [58 Stat. 740](/us/stat/58/740); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title III, § 307, [69 Stat. 665](/us/stat/69/665); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 108, [70 Stat. 667](/us/stat/70/667); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for allocation of administrative expenses rendered each year by Farm Credit Administration. See [section 2250 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2250).

Section 833, [act Sept. 21, 1944, ch. 412, title VI, § 602](/us/act/1944-09-21/ch412/tVI/s602), [58 Stat. 741](/us/stat/58/741), authorized purchase of manuscripts, data, and special reports by Farm Credit Administration.
