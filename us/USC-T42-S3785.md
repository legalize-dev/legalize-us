# § 3785. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI, § 1155(3)](/us/pl/109/162/tXI/s1155/3), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3114](/us/stat/119/3114)

##### § 3785. Repealed. [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI, § 1155(3)](/us/pl/109/162/tXI/s1155/3), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3114](/us/stat/119/3114)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 804](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s804), formerly § 805, as added [Pub. L. 96–157, § 2](/us/pl/96/157/s2), Dec. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 1203](/us/stat/93/1203); renumbered § 804 and amended [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609B(d)](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s609B/d), (f), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2093](/us/stat/98/2093); [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXXIII, § 330001(h)(9)](/us/pl/103/322/tXXXIII/s330001/h/9), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2139](/us/stat/108/2139), related to appellate court review.

Another [section 804 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s804), title III, June 19, 1968, [82 Stat. 223](/us/stat/82/223), is set out as a note under [section 2510 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s2510), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

A prior [section 804 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s804) was renumbered section 803 and is classified to [section 10223 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s10223), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
