# § 587. Omitted

##### § 587. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–206, § 7](/us/pl/92/206/s7), Dec. 18, 1971, [85 Stat. 738](/us/stat/85/738), which related to distribution of funds to members of the Shoshone-Bannock Tribes of the Fort Hall Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
