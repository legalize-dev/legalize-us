# § 1194. Omitted

##### § 1194. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–259, § 4](/us/pl/91/259/s4), May 21, 1970, [84 Stat. 254](/us/stat/84/254), which exempted per capita distributions of judgment funds from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
