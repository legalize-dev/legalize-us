# § 1300b–15. Omitted

##### § 1300b–15. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 97–429, § 6](/us/pl/97/429/s6), Jan. 8, 1983, [96 Stat. 2270](/us/stat/96/2270), which related to jurisdiction over civil causes of action and criminal offenses arising on the Band’s trust lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
