# [§ 3345. Omitted]

##### [§ 3345. Omitted]

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1818(c)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1818/c), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4188](/us/stat/134/4188), initially transferred [section 2302e of this title](/us/usc/t10/s2302e) to this section, to become effective Jan. 1, 2022. [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XVII, § 1701(v)(1)(A)](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tXVII/s1701/v/1/A), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 2154](/us/stat/135/2154), repealed [section 1818(c) of Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283/s1818/c), effective as if included therein, which eliminated that transfer, thereby omitting this section before it took effect. Another amendment by [Pub. L. 117–81](/us/pl/117/81) resulted in the transfer of section 2302e to [section 4004 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s4004).
