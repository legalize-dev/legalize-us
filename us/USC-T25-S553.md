# § 553. Omitted

##### § 553. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 1, 1938, ch. 310, § 3](/us/act/1938-06-01/ch310/s3), [52 Stat. 605](/us/stat/52/605), which related to deposit and expenditure of payments, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
