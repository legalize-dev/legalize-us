# § 2654. Regulations

##### § 2654. Regulations

The Secretary of Labor shall prescribe such regulations as are necessary to carry out subchapter I and this subchapter not later than 120 days after February 5, 1993.

*([Pub. L. 103–3, title IV, § 404](/us/pl/103/3/tIV/s404), Feb. 5, 1993, [107 Stat. 26](/us/stat/107/26).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective 6 months after Feb. 5, 1993, see [section 405(b)(1) of Pub. L. 103–3](/us/pl/103/3/s405/b/1), set out as a note under [section 2601 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s2601).
