# § 1184. Removal of officer: action by Secretary upon recommendation of board of inquiry

##### § 1184. Removal of officer: action by Secretary upon recommendation of board of inquiry

The Secretary of the military department concerned may remove an officer from active duty if the removal of such officer from active duty is recommended by a board of inquiry convened under [section 1182 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1182).

*(Added [Pub. L. 96–513, title I, § 110](/us/pl/96/513/tI/s110), Dec. 12, 1980, [94 Stat. 2874](/us/stat/94/2874); amended [Pub. L. 105–261, div. A, title V, § 503(b)(2)](/us/pl/105/261/dA/tV/s503/b/2), (c)(1), Oct. 17, 1998, [112 Stat. 2003](/us/stat/112/2003).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1998—[Pub. L. 105–261](/us/pl/105/261) substituted “inquiry” for “review” in section catchline and “board of inquiry convened under [section 1182 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1182)” for “board of review convened under [section 1183 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1183)” in text.
