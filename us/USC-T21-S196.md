##### § 196. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

Section, [act June 14, 1930, ch. 488, § 4(b)](/us/act/1930-06-14/ch488/s4/b), (c), [46 Stat. 587](/us/stat/46/587); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff.
