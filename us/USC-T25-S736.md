# § 736. Omitted

##### § 736. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–89, title II, § 206](/us/pl/100/89/tII/s206), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 671](/us/stat/101/671), which established a Federal reservation and set out provisions relating to conveyances of title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
