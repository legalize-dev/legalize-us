# §§ 17827 to 17829. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

##### §§ 17827 to 17829. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

Section 17827, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1112](/us/pl/110/422/tXI/s1112), Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4811](/us/stat/122/4811), provided an exception to alternative fuel procurement requirement. See [section 30310 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s30310), National and Commercial Space Programs.

Section 17828, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1116](/us/pl/110/422/tXI/s1116), Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4813](/us/stat/122/4813), related to cooperative unmanned aerial vehicle activities. See [section 31504 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s31504).

Section 17829, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1117](/us/pl/110/422/tXI/s1117), Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4813](/us/stat/122/4813), related to development of enhanced-use lease policy. See [section 31505 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s31505).
