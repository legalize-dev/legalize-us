# §§ 1271 to 1279. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569](/us/stat/72/1569)

##### §§ 1271 to 1279. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569](/us/stat/72/1569)

Section 1271, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 331](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s331), [68 Stat. 1164](/us/stat/68/1164), related to applicability of sections 1221 to 1227, 1231 to 1238, and 1261 to 1267 of this title. See section 14301 et seq. of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 1272, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 332](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s332), [68 Stat. 1164](/us/stat/68/1164), related to officers eligible for vacancy promotion entering on active duty. See section 14301 et seq. of Title 10.

Section 1273, acts [Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257), title III, § 333, [68 Stat. 1164](/us/stat/68/1164); [June 30, 1955, ch. 247](/us/act/1955-06-30/ch247), §§ 2, 10, [69 Stat. 218](/us/stat/69/218), 222, provided for promotion to higher grade while on active duty. See [section 14311 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s14311).

Section 1274, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 334](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s334), [68 Stat. 1164](/us/stat/68/1164), provided for promotion under mandatory consideration of officers with higher temporary grade. See section 14301 et seq. of Title 10.

Section 1275, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 335](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s335), [68 Stat. 1165](/us/stat/68/1165), related to appointment in appropriate higher grade after temporary appointment. See section 14301 et seq. of Title 10.

Section 1276, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 336](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s336), [68 Stat. 1165](/us/stat/68/1165), related to officers of the National Guard of the United States. See section 14301 et seq. of Title 10.

Section 1277, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 337](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s337), [68 Stat. 1165](/us/stat/68/1165), which related to withholding promotion on release from active duty, was also repealed by [act June 30, 1955, ch. 247, § 11](/us/act/1955-06-30/ch247/s11), [69 Stat. 222](/us/stat/69/222).

Section 1278, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 338](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s338), [68 Stat. 1165](/us/stat/68/1165), related to promotion upon release from active duty. See section 14301 et seq. of Title 10.

Section 1279, acts [Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257), title III, § 339, [68 Stat. 1165](/us/stat/68/1165); [June 30, 1955, ch. 247, § 1(c)](/us/act/1955-06-30/ch247/s1/c), [69 Stat. 218](/us/stat/69/218), related to retention for additional service.
