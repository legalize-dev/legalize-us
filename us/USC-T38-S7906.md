# § 7906. Regulations

##### § 7906. Regulations

The Secretary shall prescribe regulations for the administration of this chapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 109–461, title IX, § 903(a)(1)](/us/pl/109/461/tIX/s903/a/1), Dec. 22, 2006, [120 Stat. 3464](/us/stat/120/3464).)*
