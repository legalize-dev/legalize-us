# §§ 867 to 867c. Omitted

##### §§ 867 to 867c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general revision of title V of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V](/us/pl/89/10/tV), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 47](/us/stat/79/47), by [Pub. L. 95–561, title V, § 501](/us/pl/95/561/tV/s501), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2240](/us/stat/92/2240).

Section 867, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 531](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s531), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(D)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 145](/us/stat/84/145); amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 104(c)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s104/c), title VIII, § 843(a)(2), (3), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 503](/us/stat/88/503), 611; [Pub. L. 95–112, § 2(e)(3)](/us/pl/95/112/s2/e/3), Sept. 24, 1977, [91 Stat. 911](/us/stat/91/911), authorized the appropriation of funds for comprehensive planning and evaluation grants for fiscal years ending prior to Oct. 1, 1979.

A prior section 867, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 507](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s507), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 51](/us/stat/79/51), as amended, was renumbered § 553 by [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(3)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/3), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 142](/us/stat/84/142), and was classified to [section 869b of this title](/us/usc/t20/s869b) which was repealed by [Pub. L. 91–648, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/91/648/tIV/s403), Jan. 5, 1971, [84 Stat. 1925](/us/stat/84/1925).

Section 867a, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 532](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s532), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(D)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 146](/us/stat/84/146), related to comprehensive planning and evaluation grants and to the establishment and functioning of State planning and evaluation agencies.

Section 867b, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 533](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s533), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(D)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 147](/us/stat/84/147), related to the form and content of grant applications.

Section 867c, [Pub. L. 89–10, title V, § 534](/us/pl/89/10/tV/s534), as added [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 143(a)(4)(D)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s143/a/4/D), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 148](/us/stat/84/148), related to the required annual reports.
