Amendment by [Pub. L. 115–271](/us/pl/115/271), which added another exception for sections 1021 to 1035 of [Pub. L. 100–690](/us/pl/100/690) ([21 U.S.C. 1521](/us/usc/t21/s1521) to 1535), and which was not given effect because those sections had not been treated as repealed in light of [Pub.
