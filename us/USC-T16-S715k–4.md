# § 715k–4. Accounting and use of appropriations

##### § 715k–4. Accounting and use of appropriations

Funds appropriated each fiscal year pursuant to sections 715k–3 to 715k–5 of this title shall be accounted for, added to, and used for purposes of the migratory bird conservation fund 11 So in original. Probably should be capitalized. established pursuant to [section 718d of this title](/us/usc/t16/s718d).

*([Pub. L. 87–383, § 2](/us/pl/87/383/s2), Oct. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 813](/us/stat/75/813).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was not enacted as part of the Migratory Bird Conservation Act which comprises this subchapter.
