# § 1116. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1007(c)(1)](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1007/c/1), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4187](/us/stat/102/4187)

##### § 1116. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title I, § 1007(c)(1)](/us/pl/100/690/tI/s1007/c/1), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4187](/us/stat/102/4187)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 206](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s206), as added [Pub. L. 96–181, § 4](/us/pl/96/181/s4), Jan. 2, 1980, [93 Stat. 1311](/us/stat/93/1311), provided that this subchapter not be construed as limiting the statutory authority of the Secretary of Defense or the Administrator of Veterans’ Affairs.

A prior section 1116, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 206](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s206), as added [Pub. L. 94–237, § 4(b)](/us/pl/94/237/s4/b), Mar. 19, 1976, [90 Stat. 242](/us/stat/90/242), which provided for the employment and compensation of experts and consultants, was omitted in the general revision of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 96–181, § 4](/us/pl/96/181/s4), Jan. 2, 1980, [93 Stat. 1309](/us/stat/93/1309).

Another prior section 1116, [Pub. L. 92–255, title II, § 206](/us/pl/92/255/tII/s206), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 68](/us/stat/86/68), which provided for the employment and prescription of functions for officers and employees by the Director, was repealed by [Pub. L. 92–255, title I, § 104](/us/pl/92/255/tI/s104), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 67](/us/stat/86/67), eff. June 30, 1975.

Provisions similar to this section were contained in [section 1119 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s1119) prior to the general revision of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 96–181, § 4](/us/pl/96/181/s4), Jan. 2, 1980, [93 Stat. 1309](/us/stat/93/1309).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 21, 1989, see [section 1012 of Pub. L. 100–690](/us/pl/100/690/s1012).
