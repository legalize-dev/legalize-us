##### § 916. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–88, title III, § 328](/us/pl/104/88/tIII/s328), Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 952](/us/stat/109/952)

Section, [Pub. L. 96–101, § 18](/us/pl/96/101/s18), Nov. 4, 1979, [93 Stat. 746](/us/stat/93/746); [Pub. L. 96–254, title I, § 116](/us/pl/96/254/tI/s116), May 30, 1980, [94 Stat. 405](/us/stat/94/405), provided that this chapter be in lieu of directed service on any line of the Milwaukee Railroad with certain exceptions.
