# §§ 3841 to 3844. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–201, div. C, title XXXV, § 3546(a)(4)](/us/pl/104/201/dC/tXXXV/s3546/a/4), Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2868](/us/stat/110/2868)

##### §§ 3841 to 3844. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–201, div. C, title XXXV, § 3546(a)(4)](/us/pl/104/201/dC/tXXXV/s3546/a/4), Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2868](/us/stat/110/2868)

Section 3841, [Pub. L. 96–70, title II, § 2201](/us/pl/96/70/tII/s2201), Sept. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 493](/us/stat/93/493), related to continuing jurisdiction during transition period of United States District Court for the District of the Canal Zone and magistrates’ courts under the Canal Zone Code.

Section 3842, [Pub. L. 96–70, title II, § 2202](/us/pl/96/70/tII/s2202), Sept. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 494](/us/stat/93/494), related to division and terms of United States District Court for the District of the Canal Zone.

Section 3843, [Pub. L. 96–70, title II, § 2203(a)](/us/pl/96/70/tII/s2203/a), Sept. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 494](/us/stat/93/494), related to extension of terms of office of district judge, magistrate, United States attorney, and United States marshal.

Section 3844, [Pub. L. 96–70, title II, § 2206](/us/pl/96/70/tII/s2206), Sept. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 494](/us/stat/93/494), related to continuing operation during transition period of two magistrates’ courts established under Canal Zone Code.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 96–70, title II, § 2203(b)](/us/pl/96/70/tII/s2203/b), Sept. 27, 1979, [93 Stat. 494](/us/stat/93/494), provided that [section 3843 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3843) was to take effect on Sept. 27, 1979, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–201, div. C, title XXXV, § 3546(a)(4)](/us/pl/104/201/dC/tXXXV/s3546/a/4), Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2868](/us/stat/110/2868).
