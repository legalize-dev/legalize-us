# § 7509. Relationship to other programs

##### § 7509. Relationship to other programs

(a) [Section 2013(b) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2013/b) shall not apply with respect to the distribution of commodities under this chapter.

(b) Except as otherwise provided in [section 7504 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s7504), none of the commodities distributed under this chapter shall be sold or otherwise disposed of in commercial channels in any form.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) and [Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246) made identical amendments to this section. The amendments by [Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) were repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a).

#### Amendments

2008—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4002(b)(1)(B)](/us/pl/110/246/s4002/b/1/B), (2)(I), made technical amendment to reference in original act which appears in text as reference to [section 2013(b) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2013/b).

1983—[Pub. L. 98–92](/us/pl/98/92) substituted “Relationship to other programs” for “Relationships to food stamps” in section catchline, designated existing provisions as subsec. (a), and added subsec. (b).
