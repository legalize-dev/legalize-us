# § 1300n. Omitted

##### § 1300n. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 106–568, title XIV, § 1402](/us/pl/106/568/tXIV/s1402), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2939](/us/stat/114/2939), which set out congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 106–568, title XIV, § 1401](/us/pl/106/568/tXIV/s1401), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2939](/us/stat/114/2939), provided that title XIV of [Pub. L. 106–568](/us/pl/106/568), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Graton Rancheria Restoration Act”.
