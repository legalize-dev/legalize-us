# § 5116i. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 5116i. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter $80,000,000 for fiscal year 2010 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2011 through 2015.

*([Pub. L. 93–247, title II, § 209](/us/pl/93/247/tII/s209), formerly § 210, as added [Pub. L. 104–235, title I, § 121](/us/pl/104/235/tI/s121), Oct. 3, 1996, [110 Stat. 3088](/us/stat/110/3088); amended [Pub. L. 108–36, title I, § 130](/us/pl/108/36/tI/s130), June 25, 2003, [117 Stat. 818](/us/stat/117/818); renumbered § 209 and amended [Pub. L. 111–320, title I](/us/pl/111/320/tI), §§ 140, 141, Dec. 20, 2010, [124 Stat. 3482](/us/stat/124/3482).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 209 of Pub. L. 93–247](/us/pl/93/247/s209) was renumbered section 208 and is classified to [section 5116h of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5116h).

#### Amendments

2010—[Pub. L. 111–320, § 140](/us/pl/111/320/s140), substituted “2010” for “2004” and “2011 through 2015” for “2005 through 2008”.

2003—[Pub. L. 108–36](/us/pl/108/36) amended section catchline and text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subchapter, $66,000,000 for fiscal year 1997 and such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1998 through 2001.”
