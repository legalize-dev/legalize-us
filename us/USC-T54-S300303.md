# § 300303. Council

##### § 300303. Council

In this division, the term “Council” means the Advisory Council on Historic Preservation established by [section 304101 of this title](/us/usc/t54/s304101).

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3188](/us/stat/128/3188).)*
