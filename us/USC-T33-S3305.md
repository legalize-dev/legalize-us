# § 3305. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 3305. Authorization of appropriations

(1) to carry out sections 3302, 3303a(c), 3303a(d), 3303a(e), and 3303a(f) of this title, $4,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023;

(2) to carry out [section 3303 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3303), $20,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023; and

(3) to carry out [section 3303a(h) of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3303a/h), $30,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023.

*([Pub. L. 110–114, title IX, § 9008](/us/pl/110/114/tIX/s9008), formerly § 9006, Nov. 8, 2007, [121 Stat. 1291](/us/stat/121/1291); renumbered § 9008 and amended [Pub. L. 113–121, title III, § 3016(e)(1)](/us/pl/113/121/tIII/s3016/e/1), (f), June 10, 2014, [128 Stat. 1293](/us/stat/128/1293), 1300; [Pub. L. 115–270, title I, § 1144(2)](/us/pl/115/270/tI/s1144/2), Oct. 23, 2018, [132 Stat. 3785](/us/stat/132/3785).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–270](/us/pl/115/270) substituted “2019 through 2023” for “2015 through 2019” in pars. (1) to (3).

2014—[Pub. L. 113–121, § 3016(f)(2)](/us/pl/113/121/s3016/f/2), substituted “Secretary—” for “Secretary to carry out this chapter $20,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2008 through 2013.” and added pars. (1) to (3).

[Pub. L. 113–121, § 3016(f)(1)](/us/pl/113/121/s3016/f/1), which directed the substitution of “is” for “are”, could not be executed because “are” does not appear in text.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### “Secretary” Defined

Secretary means the Secretary of the Army, see [section 2 of Pub. L. 110–114](/us/pl/110/114/s2), set out as a note under [section 2201 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2201).
