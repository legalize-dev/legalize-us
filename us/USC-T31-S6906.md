# § 6906. Funding

##### § 6906. Funding

(1) each county or other eligible unit of local government shall be entitled to payment under this chapter; and

(2) sums shall be made available to the Secretary of the Interior for obligation or expenditure in accordance with this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 97–258](/us/pl/97/258), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1035](/us/stat/96/1035); [Pub. L. 110–343, div. F, title I, § 100111](/us/pl/112/141/dF/tI/s100111), July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 906](/us/stat/126/906); [Pub. L. 113–79, title XII, § 12312](/us/pl/113/79/tXII/s12312), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 992](/us/stat/128/992); [Pub. L. 115–141, div. G, title I, § 118](/us/pl/115/141/dG/tI/s118), Mar. 23, 2018, [132 Stat. 661](/us/stat/132/661); [Pub. L. 116–6, div. E, title I, § 116](/us/pl/116/6/dE/tI/s116), Feb. 15, 2019, [133 Stat. 232](/us/stat/133/232).)*

The words “to the Secretary of the Interior” are added for clarity. The words “*Provided*, That, notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter” and “in advance” are omitted as unnecessary.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2019—[Pub. L. 116–6](/us/pl/116/6) substituted “fiscal year 2019” for “fiscal year 2018” in introductory provisions.

2018—[Pub. L. 115–141](/us/pl/115/141) substituted “fiscal year 2018” for “each of fiscal years 2008 through 2014” in introductory provisions.

2014—[Pub. L. 113–79](/us/pl/113/79) substituted “2014” for “2013” in introductory provisions.

2012—[Pub. L. 112–141](/us/pl/112/141) substituted “2013” for “2012” in introductory provisions.

2008—[Pub. L. 110–343](/us/pl/110/343) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section read as follows: “Necessary amounts may be appropriated to the Secretary of the Interior to carry out this chapter. Amounts are available only as provided in appropriation laws.”
