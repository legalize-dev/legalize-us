# § 239l–3. Funding

##### § 239l–3. Funding

Beginning with fiscal year 2010, the Secretary shall transfer from the Public Health and Social Services Emergency Fund such sums as may be necessary to carry out this part.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title II, § 274, as added [Pub. L. 111–148, title V, § 5315](/us/pl/111/148/tV/s5315), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 642](/us/stat/124/642).)*
