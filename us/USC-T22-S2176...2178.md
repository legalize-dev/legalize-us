# §§ 2176 to 2178. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–424, title I, § 102(g)(1)(A)](/us/pl/95/424/tI/s102/g/1/A), Oct. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 942](/us/stat/92/942)

##### §§ 2176 to 2178. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–424, title I, § 102(g)(1)(A)](/us/pl/95/424/tI/s102/g/1/A), Oct. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 942](/us/stat/92/942)

Section 2176, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. I, § 216, Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 429](/us/stat/75/429); [Pub. L. 88–633](/us/pl/88/633), pt. I, § 102(d), Oct. 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 1009](/us/stat/78/1009), related to payment by the United States of transportation charges of the American Red Cross and United States voluntary nonprofit relief agencies.

Section 2177, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. I, § 217, as added [Pub. L. 88–633](/us/pl/88/633), pt. I, § 102(e), Oct. 7, 1964, [78 Stat. 1009](/us/stat/78/1009), related to a determination of the feasibility of establishing programs for the furnishing to less developed countries of used tools, machinery, etc., to be donated by private enterprise.

Section 2178, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. I, § 218, as added [Pub. L. 90–137](/us/pl/90/137), pt. I, § 103(a), Nov. 14, 1967, [81 Stat. 450](/us/stat/81/450), related to the demonstration of the use of fish and other protein concentrates as a means of reducing nutritional deficiencies in less developed countries.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 605 of Pub. L. 95–424](/us/pl/95/424/s605), set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 2151 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2151).
