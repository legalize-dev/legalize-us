# § 1300b–16. Omitted

##### § 1300b–16. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 97–429, § 7](/us/pl/97/429/s7), Jan. 8, 1983, [96 Stat. 2270](/us/stat/96/2270), which related to provision of Federal Indian services, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
