#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–377, § 5](/us/pl/103/377/s5), Oct. 19, 1994, [108 Stat. 3504](/us/stat/108/3504), which provided for conveyance of lands described in exhibits A and B of the State Agreement, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
