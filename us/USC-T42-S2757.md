# § 2757. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–575, title I, § 131(a)](/us/pl/90/575/tI/s131/a), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1028](/us/stat/82/1028)

##### § 2757. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–575, title I, § 131(a)](/us/pl/90/575/tI/s131/a), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1028](/us/stat/82/1028)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 442](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s442), Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1251](/us/stat/79/1251), authorized appropriations for the fiscal years ending June 30, 1966, 1967, and 1968, to carry out the provisions of part C of title I of [Pub. L. 88–452](/us/pl/88/452), which was classified to this part.

In addition to repealing this section, [section 131 of Pub. L. 90–575](/us/pl/90/575/s131) redesignated part C of title I of [Pub. L. 88–452](/us/pl/88/452) as part C of title IV of [Pub. L. 89–329](/us/pl/89/329) and renumbered the sections within that part. As a result, a new [section 442 of Pub. L. 89–329](/us/pl/89/329/s442) was created at the same time this section 442 was repealed. The new part C of title IV of [Pub. L. 89–329](/us/pl/89/329) has been editorially transferred in the Code from this part to part C (§ 1087–51 et seq.) of subchapter IV of chapter 28 of Title 20, Education, and the new section 442 is classified to [section 1087–52 of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s1087–52).
