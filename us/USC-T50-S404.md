# § 404. Transferred

##### § 404. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 404, comprising section 107 of the National Security Act of 1947, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343](/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343), was editorially reclassified as [section 3042 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s3042). [Section 3042 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s3042) was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 116–92, div. E, title LXVII, § 6742(b)(3)](/us/pl/116/92/dE/tLXVII/s6742/b/3), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2240](/us/stat/133/2240).
