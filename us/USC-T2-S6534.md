# § 6534. Compensation of Assistant Secretary of Senate

##### § 6534. Compensation of Assistant Secretary of Senate

The Assistant Secretary of the Senate may be paid at a maximum annual rate of compensation not to exceed $39,000.

*([Pub. L. 91–145](/us/pl/91/145), Dec. 12, 1969, [83 Stat. 340](/us/stat/83/340); [Pub. L. 93–371, § 4](/us/pl/93/371/s4), Aug. 13, 1974, [88 Stat. 429](/us/stat/88/429); [Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 105](/us/pl/94/59/tI/s105), July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 275](/us/stat/89/275); [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/A), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2775](/us/stat/133/2775).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 61a–3 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s61a–3) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2019—[Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94) repealed [Pub. L. 94–59, § 105](/us/pl/94/59/s105). See 1975 Amendment note below.

1975—[Pub. L. 94–59](/us/pl/94/59), which substituted “$39,000” for “$37,620”, effective July 1, 1975, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94). See above.

1974—[Pub. L. 93–371](/us/pl/93/371) substituted provision setting maximum annual rate of compensation of Assistant Secretary at not to exceed $37,620, for provisions authorizing Secretary of Senate to fix the compensation of Assistant Secretary at not to exceed $11,826 per annum, effective July 1, 1974.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Change of Name

Assistant Secretary of the Senate deemed successor in references to Chief Clerk of Senate in all laws, rules, resolutions, and orders, effective July 1, 1971, under provisions of [Pub. L. 92–51](/us/pl/92/51), July 9, 1971, [85 Stat. 125](/us/stat/85/125).

#### Effective Date of 2019 Amendment

Amendment by [Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94) effective on the later of the first day of the first applicable pay period beginning on or after Jan. 1, 2020, or the first day of the first applicable pay period beginning on or after Dec. 20, 2019, see [section 212(c) of Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94/s212/c), set out as a note under [section 282b of this title](/us/usc/t2/s282b).

#### 1974 Adjustment in Compensation Not To Supersede Adjustments in Compensation or Limitations by President Pro Tempore of the Senate

Adjustment in compensation by [Pub. L. 93–371](/us/pl/93/371) not to supersede order of President pro tempore of the Senate authorizing higher rate of compensation or any authority of the President pro tempore to adjust rates of compensation or limitations under section 4 of the Federal Pay Comparability Act of 1970, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 93–371](/us/pl/93/371/s4), set out in part as a note under [section 273 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s273).

#### Increases in Compensation

Increases in compensation of Assistant Secretary of the Senate under authority of Federal Salary Act of 1967 ([Pub. L. 90–206](/us/pl/90/206)) and Federal Pay Comparability Act of 1970 ([Pub. L. 91–656](/us/pl/91/656)), see [section 4571 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s4571), and Salary Directives of President pro tempore of the Senate, set out as notes under that section.
