# §§ 1971 to 1978. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(e)](/us/pl/93/618/tVI/s602/e), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072](/us/stat/88/2072)

##### §§ 1971 to 1978. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(e)](/us/pl/93/618/tVI/s602/e), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072](/us/stat/88/2072)

Section 1971, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 331](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s331), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 896](/us/stat/76/896), made provision for agreements with States. See section 2311 et seq. of this title.

Section 1972, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 332](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s332), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 896](/us/stat/76/896), made provision for payments to States. See [section 2313 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2313).

Section 1973, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 333](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s333), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 897](/us/stat/76/897), provided for liabilities of certifying and disbursing officers. See [section 2314 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2314).

Section 1974, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 334](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s334), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 897](/us/stat/76/897), provided for recovery of overpayments. See [section 2315 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2315).

Section 1975, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 335](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s335), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 897](/us/stat/76/897), set out penalties for making false statements or failing to disclose material facts. See [section 2316 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2316).

Section 1976, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 336](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s336), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 897](/us/stat/76/897), provided for review of determinations as to entitlement for adjustment assistance. See [section 2322 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2322).

Section 1977, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 337](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s337), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 897](/us/stat/76/897), authorized appropriations for adjustment assistance to workers.

Section 1978, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 338](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s338), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 897](/us/stat/76/897), defined terms. See [section 2319 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2319).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on 90th day following Jan. 3, 1975, see note set out under [section 1901 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1901).
