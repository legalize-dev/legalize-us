#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 20, 1921, ch. 9](/us/act/1921-05-20/ch9), [42 Stat. 7](/us/stat/42/7), gave ex-service men, successful at drawing held March 5, 1920, but unable to make entry because of reinstatement of conflicting claims, preference for not less than thirty days before next opening of lands to other entry.
