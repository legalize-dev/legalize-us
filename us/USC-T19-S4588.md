# § 4588. Treatment of amendments to antidumping and countervailing duty law

##### § 4588. Treatment of amendments to antidumping and countervailing duty law

(1) section 303 11 See References in Text note below. or title VII of the Tariff Act of 1930 [[19 U.S.C. 1671](/us/usc/t19/s1671) et seq.], or any successor statute, or

(2) any other statute which—

(A) provides for judicial review of final determinations under such section, title, or successor statute, or

(B) indicates the standard of review to be applied,

*([Pub. L. 116–113, title IV, § 418](/us/pl/116/113/tIV/s418), formerly [Pub. L. 103–182, title IV, § 408](/us/pl/103/182/tIV/s408), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2140](/us/stat/107/2140); renumbered [§ 418 of Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113/s418) and amended [Pub. L. 116–113, title V, § 504(i)](/us/pl/116/113/tV/s504/i), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 76](/us/stat/134/76).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Tariff Act of 1930, referred to in par. (1), is [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), [46 Stat. 590](/us/stat/46/590). Title VII of the Act is classified generally to subtitle IV (§ 1671 et seq.) of chapter 4 of this title. Section 303 of the Act was classified to [section 1303 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1303) and was repealed, effective Jan. 1, 1995, by [Pub. L. 103–465, title II, § 261(a)](/us/pl/103/465/tII/s261/a), Dec. 8, 1994, [108 Stat. 4908](/us/stat/108/4908). For savings provisions and treatment of references to section 1303 in other laws, see section 261(b), (d)(1)(C) of [Pub. L. 103–465](/us/pl/103/465), set out as notes under former [section 1303 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1303). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 1654 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1654) and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3438 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s3438) prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113).

#### Amendments

2020—[Pub. L. 116–113, § 504(i)(3)](/us/pl/116/113/s504/i/3), substituted “the USMCA” for “the Agreement enters into force with respect to the United States” in introductory provisions and “USMCA country” for “NAFTA country” in concluding provisions.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of 2020 Amendment

Transfer to and amendment of this section by [Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113) effective on the date on which the USMCA enters into force (July 1, 2020), but not applicable to certain determinations under [section 1516a of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1516a) or binational panel reviews under NAFTA, see [section 504(k) of Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113/s504/k), set out as a note under [section 4581 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4581).

#### Application of Amendments by [Public Law 114–27](/us/pl/114/27) to Goods From Canada and Mexico

[Pub. L. 114–27, title V, § 507](/us/pl/114/27/tV/s507), June 29, 2015, [129 Stat. 387](/us/stat/129/387), provided that: “Pursuant to article 1902 of the North American Free Trade Agreement and section 408 of the North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act ([former] [19 U.S.C. 3438](/us/usc/t19/s3438)), the amendments made by this title [see Short Title of 2015 Amendment note set out under [section 1654 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1654)] shall apply with respect to goods from Canada and Mexico.”

#### Application of Amendments by [Public Law 103–465](/us/pl/103/465) to Goods From Canada and Mexico

[Pub. L. 103–465, title II, § 234](/us/pl/103/465/tII/s234), Dec. 8, 1994, [108 Stat. 4901](/us/stat/108/4901), provided that: “Pursuant to article 1902 of the North American Free Trade Agreement and section 408 of the North American Free Trade Agreement Implementation Act [former [19 U.S.C. 3438](/us/usc/t19/s3438)], the amendments made by this title [see Tables for classification] shall apply with respect to goods from Canada and Mexico.”
