#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 108–148, title III, § 301](/us/pl/108/148/tIII/s301), Dec. 3, 2003, [117 Stat. 1902](/us/stat/117/1902), which provided congressional findings and purposes of title III of [Pub. L. 108–148](/us/pl/108/148) (enacting this subchapter and [section 2103b of this title](/us/usc/t16/s2103b)), was omitted in view of the repeal of sections 2103b and 6542 of this title.
