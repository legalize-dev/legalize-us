# § 2163. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–189, § 3(b)](/us/pl/93/189/s3/b), Dec. 17, 1973, [87 Stat. 717](/us/stat/87/717)

##### § 2163. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–189, § 3(b)](/us/pl/93/189/s3/b), Dec. 17, 1973, [87 Stat. 717](/us/stat/87/717)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. I, § 203, Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 427](/us/stat/75/427); [Pub. L. 91–175](/us/pl/91/175), pt. I, § 101(b), Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 805](/us/stat/83/805); [Pub. L. 92–226](/us/pl/92/226), pt. I, § 101(b), Feb. 7, 1972, [86 Stat. 21](/us/stat/86/21); [Pub. L. 93–189, § 3(a)](/us/pl/93/189/s3/a), Dec. 17, 1973, [87 Stat. 717](/us/stat/87/717); [Pub. L. 93–559, § 6](/us/pl/93/559/s6), Dec. 30, 1974, [88 Stat. 1796](/us/stat/88/1796), authorized use of not more than 50 per centum of dollar receipts scheduled to be paid during each of the fiscal years 1974 and 1975 from loans made under this subchapter and predecessor foreign assistance legislation for making loans under part I of this subchapter for each such fiscal year, and disposition of dollar receipts paid on and after July 1, 1975.

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective July 1, 1975, see [section 3(b) of Pub. L. 93–189](/us/pl/93/189/s3/b).
