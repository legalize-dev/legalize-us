# § 714. Omitted

##### § 714. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–481, § 2](/us/pl/98/481/s2), Oct. 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 2250](/us/stat/98/2250), which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 98–481, § 1](/us/pl/98/481/s1), Oct. 17, 1984, [98 Stat. 2250](/us/stat/98/2250), provided that [Pub. L. 98–481](/us/pl/98/481), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Coos, Lower Umpqua, and Siuslaw Restoration Act”.
