# § 101922. Use of nonmonetary consideration in concession contracts

##### § 101922. Use of nonmonetary consideration in concession contracts

[Section 1302 of title 40](/us/usc/t40/s1302) shall not apply to concession contracts awarded by the Secretary pursuant to this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3150](/us/stat/128/3150).)*

The words relating to the leasing of buildings and properties of the United States” are omitted as unnecessary.
