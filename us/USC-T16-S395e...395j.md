# §§ 395e to 395j. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 395e to 395j. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 395e, acts [Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 200, § 6](/us/act/1930-04-19/ch200/s6), [46 Stat. 228](/us/stat/46/228); [June 25, 1938, ch. 684, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch684/s1), [52 Stat. 1164](/us/stat/52/1164); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213); [Apr. 21, 1948, ch. 223, § 2](/us/act/1948-04-21/ch223/s2), [62 Stat. 196](/us/stat/62/196), related to appointment and jurisdiction of United States commissioners. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 395f, [act Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 200, § 7](/us/act/1930-04-19/ch200/s7), [46 Stat. 228](/us/stat/46/228), related to issuance of process. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

Section 395g, [act Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 200, § 8](/us/act/1930-04-19/ch200/s8), [46 Stat. 229](/us/stat/46/229), related to whom process issued. See [section 3053 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3053), rule 4 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix, and rule 4 of Federal Rules of Civil Procedure, Title 28, Appendix, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 395h, acts [Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 200, § 9](/us/act/1930-04-19/ch200/s9), [46 Stat. 229](/us/stat/46/229); [June 25, 1938, ch. 684, § 2](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch684/s2), [52 Stat. 1164](/us/stat/52/1164); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213), relating to commissioner’s [now magistrate judge’s] salary. See [section 633 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s633).

Section 395i, [act Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 200, § 10](/us/act/1930-04-19/ch200/s10), [46 Stat. 229](/us/stat/46/229), related to fees and costs chargeable to United States. See [section 604 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s604).

Section 395j, [act Apr. 19, 1930, ch. 200, § 11](/us/act/1930-04-19/ch200/s11), [46 Stat. 229](/us/stat/46/229), related to disposition of fines and costs. See [section 634 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s634).
