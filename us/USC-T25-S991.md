# § 991. Omitted

##### § 991. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–775, § 1](/us/pl/87/775/s1), Oct. 9, 1962, [76 Stat. 776](/us/stat/76/776), which provided for per capita payments to tribal members from funds appropriated for a certain judgment against the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
