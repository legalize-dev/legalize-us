# § 907. Applicability of other laws

##### § 907. Applicability of other laws

(1) is performing services as a teacher at the close of a school year,

(2) agrees in writing to serve as a teacher for the next school year, and

(3) is employed in another position in the recess period immediately preceding such next school year, or, during such recess period, receives quarters, allowances, or additional compensation referred to in sections 905 and 906 of this title, or both, as the case may be,

*([Pub. L. 86–91, § 10(b)](/us/pl/86/91/s10/b), July 17, 1959, [73 Stat. 217](/us/stat/73/217); [Pub. L. 88–448, title IV, § 401(n)](/us/pl/88/448/tIV/s401/n), Aug. 19, 1964, [78 Stat. 492](/us/stat/78/492).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

“[Section 5533 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s5533)” substituted in text for “section 301 of the Dual Compensation Act” on authority of [Pub. L. 89–554, § 7(b)](/us/pl/89/554/s7/b), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 631](/us/stat/80/631), the first section of which enacted Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.

Section was formerly classified to [section 2358(b) of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s2358/b) period to the general revision and enactment of Title 5 by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).

#### Amendments

1964—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 88–448](/us/pl/88/448) made section 301 of the Dual Compensation Act inapplicable and struck out provisions which referred to former [section 62 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s62) and [section 6 of the act of May 10, 1916](/us/act/1916-05-10/s6).
