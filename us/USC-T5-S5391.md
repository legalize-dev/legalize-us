# § 5391. Definitions

##### § 5391. Definitions

For the purposes of this subchapter, “agency”, “employee”, and “position” have the meanings given them by section 5102.

*(Added [Pub. L. 101–509, title V, § 529 [title I, § 105(a)(1)]](/us/pl/101/509/tV/s529/tI/s105/a/1), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1427](/us/stat/104/1427), 1447.)*
