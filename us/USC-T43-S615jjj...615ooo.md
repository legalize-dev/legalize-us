# §§ 615jjj to 615ooo. Omitted

##### §§ 615jjj to 615ooo. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 615jjj, [Pub. L. 92–514, title II, § 201](/us/pl/92/514/tII/s201), Oct. 20, 1972, [86 Stat. 966](/us/stat/86/966), authorized construction, operation, and maintenance of Brantley project, Pecos River Basin, New Mexico.

Section 615kkk, [Pub. L. 92–514, title II, § 202](/us/pl/92/514/tII/s202), Oct. 20, 1972, [86 Stat. 966](/us/stat/86/966), related to conservation and development of fish and wildlife resources and the enhancement of recreational opportunities in connection with this project.

Section 615*lll*, [Pub. L. 92–514, title II, § 203](/us/pl/92/514/tII/s203), Oct. 20, 1972, [86 Stat. 967](/us/stat/86/967), provided that nothing in [Pub. L. 92–514](/us/pl/92/514), enacting sections 615aaa to 615jjjj of this title, amend, repeal, or modify the Pecos River Compact, 1948.

Section 615mmm, [Pub. L. 92–514, title II, § 204](/us/pl/92/514/tII/s204), Oct. 20, 1972, [86 Stat. 967](/us/stat/86/967), related to repayment of costs for flood control, dam safety, recreation, and fish and wildlife enhancement.

Section 615nnn, [Pub. L. 92–514, title II, § 205](/us/pl/92/514/tII/s205), Oct. 20, 1972, [86 Stat. 967](/us/stat/86/967), related to interest rates.

Section 615*ooo*, [Pub. L. 92–514, title II, § 206](/us/pl/92/514/tII/s206), Oct. 20, 1972, [86 Stat. 967](/us/stat/86/967); [Pub. L. 96–375, § 11](/us/pl/96/375/s11), Oct. 3, 1975, [94 Stat. 1507](/us/stat/94/1507), authorized appropriations for this project.
