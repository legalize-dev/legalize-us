# §§ 1405s to 1405t. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 649](/us/stat/80/649), 651, 655

##### §§ 1405s to 1405t. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 649](/us/stat/80/649), 651, 655

Section 1405s, acts [June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 20](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s20), [49 Stat. 1812](/us/stat/49/1812); [Dec. 26, 1941, ch. 637](/us/act/1941-12-26/ch637), [55 Stat. 872](/us/stat/55/872), related to executive branch of Government, and to appointment, powers and duties of Governor.

Section 1405s–1, [act Oct. 15, 1949, ch. 695, § 5(a)](/us/act/1949-10-15/ch695/s5/a), [63 Stat. 880](/us/stat/63/880), prescribed compensation of Governor.

This section was not enacted as part of the Organic Act of the Virgin Islands of the United States which comprises this subchapter.

Section 1405t, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 21](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s21), [49 Stat. 1812](/us/stat/49/1812), related to appointment, powers and duties of Government Secretary.
