# § 2297g–4. Reports to Congress

##### § 2297g–4. Reports to Congress

Within 3 years after October 24, 1992, and at least once every 3 years thereafter, the Secretary shall report to the Congress on progress under this subchapter. The 5th report submitted under this section shall contain recommendations of the Secretary for the reauthorization of the program and Fund under this division.

*([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724), title II, § 1805, as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title XI, § 1101](/us/pl/102/486/tXI/s1101), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2955](/us/stat/106/2955).)*

#### Termination of Reporting Requirements

For termination, effective May 15, 2000, of provisions of law requiring submittal to Congress of any annual, semiannual, or other regular periodic report listed in House Document No. 103–7 (in which a report required under this section is listed in item 7 on page 83), see [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance.
