# § 30712. Distribution of assets on dissolution or final liquidation

##### § 30712. Distribution of assets on dissolution or final liquidation

On dissolution or final liquidation of the corporation, any assets remaining after the discharge of all liabilities shall be used by the board of directors for the purpose stated in [section 30702 of this title](/us/usc/t36/s30702) or be transferred to a recognized educational foundation.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1324](/us/stat/112/1324).)*

The word “satisfaction” is omitted as included in “discharge”, and the word “obligations” is omitted as included in “liabilities”. The word “outstanding” is omitted as unnecessary.
