# § 1300k. Omitted

##### § 1300k. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–324, § 2](/us/pl/103/324/s2), Sept. 21, 1994, [108 Stat. 2156](/us/stat/108/2156), which set out congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 103–324, § 1](/us/pl/103/324/s1), Sept. 21, 1994, [108 Stat. 2156](/us/stat/108/2156), provided that [Pub. L. 103–324](/us/pl/103/324), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Little Traverse Bay Bands of Odawa Indians and the Little River Band of Ottawa Indians Act”.
