# § 7124. Cross references

##### § 7124. Cross references

For criminal penalties for concealment of property, false statement, or falsifying and destroying records, in connection with any closing agreement, compromise, or offer of compromise, see section 7206.

*([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 850](/us/stat/68A/850), § 7123; [Pub. L. 97–258, § 3(f)(12)](/us/pl/97/258/s3/f/12), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1065](/us/stat/96/1065); renumbered § 7124, [Pub. L. 105–206, title III, § 3465(a)(1)](/us/pl/105/206/tIII/s3465/a/1), July 22, 1998, [112 Stat. 767](/us/stat/112/767).)*
