# § 295. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(b)](/us/pl/96/591/s8/b), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435](/us/stat/94/3435)

##### § 295. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(b)](/us/pl/96/591/s8/b), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435](/us/stat/94/3435)

Section, [act Feb. 8, 1895, ch. 64, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-08/ch64/s1), as added [Mar. 18, 1948, ch. 138, § 5](/us/act/1948-03-18/ch138/s5), [62 Stat. 82](/us/stat/62/82), related to day and night signals for vessels anchored, not under command, or aground.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal
