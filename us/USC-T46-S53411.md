# § 53411. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 53411. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated for payments under section 53406, $60,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2022 and 2023, and $120,000,000 for fiscal years 2024 through 2035, to remain available until expended.

*(Added [Pub. L. 116–283, div. C, title XXXV, § 3511(a)](/us/pl/116/283/dC/tXXXV/s3511/a), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4418](/us/stat/134/4418); amended [Pub. L. 117–263, div. C, title XXXV, § 3501(b)(1)](/us/pl/117/263/dC/tXXXV/s3501/b/1), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 3064](/us/stat/136/3064).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–263](/us/pl/117/263) substituted “and 2023, and $120,000,000 for fiscal years 2024 through 2035” for “through 2035”.
