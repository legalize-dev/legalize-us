# § 776. Applicability of chapter

##### § 776. Applicability of chapter

(1) the United States;

(2) the territories, commonwealths, and possessions of the United States; and

(3) all other places under the jurisdiction of the United States.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 36](/us/stat/70A/36), § 774; [Pub. L. 99–661, div. A, title XIII, § 1343(a)(1)](/us/pl/99/661/dA/tXIII/s1343/a/1), Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3992](/us/stat/100/3992); [Pub. L. 100–26, § 3(6)](/us/pl/100/26/s3/6), Apr. 21, 1987, [101 Stat. 273](/us/stat/101/273); renumbered § 775, [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title V, § 508(a)(1)](/us/pl/100/180/dA/tV/s508/a/1), Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1086](/us/stat/101/1086); renumbered § 776, [Pub. L. 102–484, div. A, title III, § 377(a)(1)](/us/pl/102/484/dA/tIII/s377/a/1), Oct. 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 2386](/us/stat/106/2386).)*

The words “the Canal Zone, Guam, American Samoa, and the Virgin Islands as well as to * * * other” are omitted as covered by the words “possessions, and all other places under its jurisdiction”.

#### Amendments

1992—[Pub. L. 102–484](/us/pl/102/484) renumbered [section 775 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s775) as this section.

1987—[Pub. L. 100–180](/us/pl/100/180) renumbered [section 774 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s774) as this section.

[Pub. L. 100–26](/us/pl/100/26) amended directory language of [Pub. L. 99–661](/us/pl/99/661). See 1986 Amendment note below.

1986—[Pub. L. 99–661](/us/pl/99/661), as amended by [Pub. L. 100–26](/us/pl/100/26), amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section read as follows: “This chapter applies in the United States, the Territories, Commonwealths, and possessions, and all other places under its jurisdiction.”
