# § 1300h–5. Omitted

##### § 1300h–5. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–420, § 7](/us/pl/100/420/s7), Sept. 8, 1988, [102 Stat. 1579](/us/stat/102/1579), which related to land acquisition and establishment of Federal reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
