# § 106. Commandant defined

##### § 106. Commandant defined

In this title, the term “Commandant” means the Commandant of the Coast Guard.

*(Added [Pub. L. 115–232, div. C, title XXXV, § 3531(a)](/us/pl/115/232/dC/tXXXV/s3531/a), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 2320](/us/stat/132/2320), § 5; renumbered § 106, [Pub. L. 115–282, title I, § 103(b)](/us/pl/115/282/tI/s103/b), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4195](/us/stat/132/4195).)*

L. 119–60](/us/pl/119/60), see Tables for classification]: “(1) Commandant.—The term ‘Commandant’ means the Commandant of the Coast Guard. “(2) Secretary.—Unless otherwise specified, the term ‘Secretary’ means the Secretary of the department in which the Coast Guard is operating.”
