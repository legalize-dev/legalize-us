# § 6430. Treatment of tax imposed at Leaking Underground Storage Tank Trust Fund financing rate

##### § 6430. Treatment of tax imposed at Leaking Underground Storage Tank Trust Fund financing rate

(1) which are exempt from tax under section 4081(a) by reason of section 4082(f)(2),

(2) which are exempt from tax under section 4041(d) by reason of the last sentence of paragraph (5) thereof,

(3) with respect to which the rate increase under section 4081(a)(2)(B) is zero by reason of section 4082(e)(2), or

(4) which are removed as eligible indelibly dyed diesel fuel or kerosene under section 6435.

*(Added [Pub. L. 109–58, title XIII, § 1362(b)(3)(A)](/us/pl/109/58/tXIII/s1362/b/3/A), Aug. 8, 2005, [119 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/119/1059); amended [Pub. L. 110–172, § 6(d)(2)(D)](/us/pl/110/172/s6/d/2/D), Dec. 29, 2007, [121 Stat. 2481](/us/stat/121/2481); [Pub. L. 119–21, title VII, § 70525(b)(2)](/us/pl/119/21/tVII/s70525/b/2), July 4, 2025, [139 Stat. 282](/us/stat/139/282).)*

#### Amendments

2025—Par. (4). [Pub. L. 119–21](/us/pl/119/21) added par. (4).

2007—[Pub. L. 110–172](/us/pl/110/172) reenacted section catchline without change and amended text generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “No refunds, credits, or payments shall be made under this subchapter for any tax imposed at the Leaking Underground Storage Tank Trust Fund financing rate, except in the case of fuels destined for export.”
