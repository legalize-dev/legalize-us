# §§ 853p to 853r. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–372, title II, § 271(1)](/us/pl/107/372/tII/s271/1), Dec. 19, 2002, [116 Stat. 3094](/us/stat/116/3094)

##### §§ 853p to 853r. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–372, title II, § 271(1)](/us/pl/107/372/tII/s271/1), Dec. 19, 2002, [116 Stat. 3094](/us/stat/116/3094)

Section 853p, [act June 3, 1948, ch. 390, § 17](/us/act/1948-06-03/ch390/s17), [62 Stat. 300](/us/stat/62/300), provided that retired pay be based on highest rank held. See [section 3046 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3046).

Section 853q, [act June 3, 1948, ch. 390, § 18](/us/act/1948-06-03/ch390/s18), [62 Stat. 300](/us/stat/62/300), provided that Coast and Geodetic Survey Commissioned Officers’ Act of 1948 would not affect retired rank and pay held pursuant to other laws. See [section 3047 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3047).

Section 853r, [act June 3, 1948, ch. 390, § 19](/us/act/1948-06-03/ch390/s19), [62 Stat. 300](/us/stat/62/300), related to personnel board. See [section 3022 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3022).
