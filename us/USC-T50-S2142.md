# § 2142. Thrift Savings Plan participation

##### § 2142. Thrift Savings Plan participation

(a) Eligibility for Thrift Savings Plan Participants in the system shall be deemed to be employees for the purposes of [section 8351 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s8351).

(b) Management of Thrift Savings Plan accounts by Director Subsections (k) and (m) of [section 8461 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s8461) shall apply with respect to contributions made by participants to the Thrift Savings Fund under section 8351 of such title and to earnings attributable to the investment of such contributions.
