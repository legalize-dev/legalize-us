# § 379d. Automation of Food and Drug Administration

##### § 379d. Automation of Food and Drug Administration

(a) In general The Secretary, acting through the Commissioner of Food and Drugs, shall automate appropriate activities of the Food and Drug Administration to ensure timely review of activities regulated under this chapter.

(b) Authorization of appropriations There are authorized to be appropriated each fiscal year such sums as are necessary to carry out this section.
