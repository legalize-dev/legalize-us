# § 49. Omitted

##### § 49. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Oct. 2, 1888, ch. 1069](/us/act/1888-10-02/ch1069), [25 Stat. 529](/us/stat/25/529), which required the Secretary of the Smithsonian Institution to submit to Congress annually a detailed statement of expenditures under appropriations for “International Exchanges,” “North American Ethnology,” and the “National Museum,”, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance. See, also, page 192 of House Document No. 103–7.
