# §§ 404c–5 to 404c–9. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 404c–5 to 404c–9. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 404c–5, acts [June 5, 1942, ch. 341, § 5](/us/act/1942-06-05/ch341/s5), [56 Stat. 318](/us/stat/56/318); [Apr. 21, 1948, ch. 223, § 3](/us/act/1948-04-21/ch223/s3), [62 Stat. 196](/us/stat/62/196), related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 404c–6, [act June 5, 1942, ch. 341, § 6](/us/act/1942-06-05/ch341/s6), [56 Stat. 319](/us/stat/56/319), related to issuance of process. See sections 3041 and 3141 of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

Section 404c–7, [act June 5, 1942, ch. 341, § 7](/us/act/1942-06-05/ch341/s7), [56 Stat. 319](/us/stat/56/319), related to commissioner’s [now magistrate judge’s] salary. See [section 634 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s634), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 404c–8, [act June 5, 1942, ch. 341, § 8](/us/act/1942-06-05/ch341/s8), [56 Stat. 319](/us/stat/56/319), related to fees, costs, and expenses against United States. See [section 604 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s604).

Section 404c–9, [act June 5, 1942, ch. 341, § 9](/us/act/1942-06-05/ch341/s9), [56 Stat. 319](/us/stat/56/319), related to disposition of fines and costs. See [section 634 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s634).
