# § 8217. Aviation training facilities

##### § 8217. Aviation training facilities

The President may maintain facilities to provide flight training for 16,000 members of the naval service.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 376](/us/stat/70A/376), § 6022; renumbered § 8217, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(b)(7)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/b/7), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/132/1834).)*

The proviso to the effect that the section does not affect the responsibility of the Secretary of the Navy under [34 U.S.C. 732](/us/usc/t34/s732) is omitted as unnecessary. The words “as may, in his judgment, be necessary” are omitted as surplusage. The words “members of the naval service” are substituted for “naval aviators” to avoid the implication that trainees are naval aviators while undergoing the training. The designation depends on successful completion of flight training.
