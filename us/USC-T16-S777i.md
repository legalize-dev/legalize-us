# § 777i. Rules and regulations

##### § 777i. Rules and regulations

The Secretary of the Interior is authorized to make rules and regulations for carrying out the provisions of this chapter.

*([Aug. 9, 1950, ch. 658, § 10](/us/act/1950-08-09/ch658/s10), [64 Stat. 434](/us/stat/64/434).)*

#### **Executive Documents**

#### Transfer of Functions

Transfer of functions to Secretary of Commerce from Secretary of the Interior by Reorg. Plan No. 4 of 1970, eff. Oct. 3, 1970, 35 F.R. 15627, [84 Stat. 2090](/us/stat/84/2090), see note set out under [section 777 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s777).
