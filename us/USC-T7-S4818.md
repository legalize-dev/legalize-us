# § 4818. Administrative provision

##### § 4818. Administrative provision

The provisions of this chapter applicable to orders shall be applicable to amendments to orders.

*([Pub. L. 99–198, title XVI, § 1629](/us/pl/99/198/tXVI/s1629), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1621](/us/stat/99/1621).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective Jan. 1, 1986, see [section 1631 of Pub. L. 99–198](/us/pl/99/198/s1631), set out as a note under [section 4801 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s4801).
