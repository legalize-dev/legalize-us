# § 7122. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 7122. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subpart $1,650,000,000 for fiscal year 2017 and $1,600,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2018 through 2020.

(b) Forward funding [Section 1223 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1223) shall apply to this subpart.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 4112](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s4112), as added [Pub. L. 114–95, title IV, § 4101](/us/pl/114/95/tIV/s4101), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1982](/us/stat/129/1982).)*
