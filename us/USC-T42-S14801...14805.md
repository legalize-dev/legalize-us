# §§ 14801 to 14805. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–194, § 4](/us/pl/108/194/s4), Dec. 19, 2003, [117 Stat. 2891](/us/stat/117/2891)

##### §§ 14801 to 14805. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–194, § 4](/us/pl/108/194/s4), Dec. 19, 2003, [117 Stat. 2891](/us/stat/117/2891)

Section 14801, [Pub. L. 106–174, § 2](/us/pl/106/174/s2), Feb. 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 18](/us/stat/114/18), related to congressional findings regarding poison control centers. See provisions set out as a note under [section 300d–71 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300d–71).

Section 14802, [Pub. L. 106–174, § 3](/us/pl/106/174/s3), Feb. 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 18](/us/stat/114/18), defined “Secretary”.

Section 14803, [Pub. L. 106–174, § 4](/us/pl/106/174/s4), Feb. 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 18](/us/stat/114/18), established a national toll-free number to be used to access regional poison control centers. See [section 300d–71 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300d–71).

Section 14804, [Pub. L. 106–174, § 5](/us/pl/106/174/s5), Feb. 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 19](/us/stat/114/19), established a nationwide media campaign to educate the public and health care providers about poison prevention and the availability of poison control resources in local communities. See [section 300d–72 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300d–72).

Section 14805, [Pub. L. 106–174, § 6](/us/pl/106/174/s6), Feb. 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 19](/us/stat/114/19), related to the award of grants to certified regional poison control centers. See [section 300d–73 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300d–73).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 106–174, § 1](/us/pl/106/174/s1), Feb. 25, 2000, [114 Stat. 18](/us/stat/114/18), which provided that [Pub. L. 106–174](/us/pl/106/174), enacting this chapter, could be cited as the “Poison Control Center Enhancement and Awareness Act”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 108–194, § 4](/us/pl/108/194/s4), Dec. 19, 2003, [117 Stat. 2891](/us/stat/117/2891).
