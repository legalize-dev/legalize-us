# § 1140o. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1140o. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this subpart such sums as may be necessary for fiscal year 2009 and each of the five succeeding fiscal years.

(b) Priority For the first fiscal year for which funds are made available under this section, the Secretary shall give priority to allocating funding for the purposes of section 1140*l* of this title.
