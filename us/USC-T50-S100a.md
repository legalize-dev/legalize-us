# § 100a. Omitted

##### § 100a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, which was from the Department of Defense Appropriation Act, 1983, [Pub. L. 97–377, title I, § 101(c) [title VII, § 712]](/us/pl/97/377/tI/s101/c/tVII/s712), Dec. 21, 1982, [96 Stat. 1833](/us/stat/96/1833), 1851, prohibited use of funds available to Department of Defense agencies for acquisition, construction, or operation of certain scrap-processing facilities, and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts.
