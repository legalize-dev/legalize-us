# §§ 80e to 80h. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 80e to 80h. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 80e, [act Apr. 23, 1946, ch. 202, § 1](/us/act/1946-04-23/ch202/s1), [60 Stat. 119](/us/stat/60/119), related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 80f, [act Apr. 23, 1946, ch. 202, § 2](/us/act/1946-04-23/ch202/s2), [60 Stat. 119](/us/stat/60/119), related to arrests for violations of rules and petty offenses.

Section 80g, [act Apr. 23, 1946, ch. 202, § 3](/us/act/1946-04-23/ch202/s3), [60 Stat. 120](/us/stat/60/120), related to arrests for criminal offenses, and is now covered by [section 3041 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3041), Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and rules 4, 5(c), and 9 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix.

Section 80h, [act Apr. 23, 1946, ch. 202, § 4](/us/act/1946-04-23/ch202/s4), [60 Stat. 120](/us/stat/60/120), related to payment and disposition of fees, costs, and expenses.
