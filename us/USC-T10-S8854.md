# § 8854. Effect of failure to start proceedings

##### § 8854. Effect of failure to start proceedings

(1) move for a monition to show cause why such proceedings shall not be started; or

(2) bring an original suit for restitution.

*([Aug. 10, 1956. ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 475](/us/stat/70A/475), § 7654; renumbered § 8854, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(d)(9)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/d/9), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1837](/us/stat/132/1837).)*
