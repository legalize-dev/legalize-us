# § 410eee–1. Definitions

##### § 410eee–1. Definitions

(1) Historical park The term “historical park” means the Adams National Historical Park established in [section 410eee–2 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s410eee–2).

(2) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

*([Pub. L. 105–342, § 3](/us/pl/105/342/s3), Nov. 2, 1998, [112 Stat. 3201](/us/stat/112/3201).)*
