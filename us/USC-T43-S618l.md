##### § 618l. Repealed. [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1076, § 1(22)](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1076/s1/22), [68 Stat. 968](/us/stat/68/968)

Section, [act July 19, 1940, ch. 643, § 13](/us/act/1940-07-19/ch643/s13), [54 Stat. 779](/us/stat/54/779), required Secretary of the Interior to submit an annual financial statement and report to Congress of operations under this subchapter.
