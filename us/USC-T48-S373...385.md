# §§ 373 to 385. Transferred

##### §§ 373 to 385. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 373, acts [July 8, 1916, ch. 228, § 1](/us/act/1916-07-08/ch228/s1), [39 Stat. 352](/us/stat/39/352); [June 28, 1918, ch. 110](/us/act/1918-06-28/ch110), [40 Stat. 632](/us/stat/40/632), which set a limit on the amount of homestead entries, was transferred to [section 270–8 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s270–8), Public Lands, and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 374, acts [July 8, 1916, ch. 228, § 1](/us/act/1916-07-08/ch228/s1), [39 Stat. 352](/us/stat/39/352); [June 28, 1918, ch. 110](/us/act/1918-06-28/ch110), [40 Stat. 632](/us/stat/40/632), which permitted a homestead entry in Alaska notwithstanding a former homestead entry in another state or territory, was transferred to [section 270–9 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s270–9), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 375, [act July 8, 1916, ch. 228, § 2](/us/act/1916-07-08/ch228/s2), as added [June 28, 1918, ch. 110](/us/act/1918-06-28/ch110), [40 Stat. 633](/us/stat/40/633); amended [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1](/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1), [42 Stat. 208](/us/stat/42/208); [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462), [43 Stat. 1144](/us/stat/43/1144); [July 11, 1956, ch. 571, § 1](/us/act/1956-07-11/ch571/s1), [70 Stat. 528](/us/stat/70/528), which made provision for proof of entry on unsurveyed lands, was transferred to [section 270–10 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s270–10), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 376, acts [Mar. 8, 1922, ch. 96, § 1](/us/act/1922-03-08/ch96/s1), [42 Stat. 415](/us/stat/42/415); Aug. 23, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–725, § 1](/us/pl/85/725/s1), [72 Stat. 730](/us/stat/72/730), which covered claims on land containing coal, oil, and gas, was transferred to [section 270–11 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s270–11), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 377, acts [Mar. 8, 1922, ch. 96, § 2](/us/act/1922-03-08/ch96/s2), [42 Stat. 416](/us/stat/42/416); Aug. 23, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–725, § 2](/us/pl/85/725/s2), [72 Stat. 730](/us/stat/72/730), which called for the inclusion, in the patent for lands containing coal, oil, and gas, of a reservation to the United States of such minerals together with the right to prospect for, mine, and remove the same, was transferred to [section 270–12 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s270–12).

Section 377a, [act Mar. 8, 1922, ch. 96, § 3](/us/act/1922-03-08/ch96/s3), as added Aug. 17, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–147](/us/pl/87/147), [75 Stat. 384](/us/stat/75/384); amended Oct. 3, 1962, [Pub. Section 378, [act July 8, 1916, ch. 228, § 3](/us/act/1916-07-08/ch228/s3), formerly § 2, [39 Stat. 352](/us/stat/39/352), renumbered [June 28, 1918, ch. 110](/us/act/1918-06-28/ch110), [40 Stat. 633](/us/stat/40/633), which excepted from homestead settlement and entry the lands in the Annette and Pribilof Islands, islands leased or occupied for the propagation of foxes, and other islands reserved or withdrawn from settlement or entry, was transferred to [section 270–14 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s270–14), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 381, acts [June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 26](/us/act/1900-06-06/ch786/s26), [31 Stat. 329](/us/stat/31/329); [May 31, 1938, ch. 297](/us/act/1938-05-31/ch297), [52 Stat. 588](/us/stat/52/588); [Aug. 8, 1947, ch. 514, § 1](/us/act/1947-08-08/ch514/s1), [61 Stat. 916](/us/stat/61/916); Aug. 14, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–662](/us/pl/85/662), [72 Stat. 615](/us/stat/72/615), which extended the mining laws of the United States to the Territory of Alaska, was transferred to [section 49a of Title 30](/us/usc/t30/s49a), Mineral Lands and Mining.

Section 382, [act June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 15](/us/act/1900-06-06/ch786/s15), [31 Stat. 327](/us/stat/31/327), which required recording notices of location of mining claims, was transferred to [section 49c of Title 30](/us/usc/t30/s49c).

Section 383, [act June 6, 1900, ch. 786, § 16](/us/act/1900-06-06/ch786/s16), [31 Stat. 328](/us/stat/31/328), which authorized regulations for recording notices of location of mining claims, and legalized certain records, was transferred to [section 49d of Title 30](/us/usc/t30/s49d).

Section 384, [act Mar. 2, 1907, ch. 2559, § 1](/us/act/1907-03-02/ch2559/s1), [34 Stat. 1243](/us/stat/34/1243), which required annual labor or improvements on mining claims, was transferred to [section 49e of Title 30](/us/usc/t30/s49e).
