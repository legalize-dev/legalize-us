# § 634. Assistance relating to water supply

##### § 634. Assistance relating to water supply

The Secretary may provide assistance to municipalities the water supply of which is adversely affected by construction carried out by the Corps of Engineers.

*([Pub. L. 115–270, title I, § 1114](/us/pl/115/270/tI/s1114), Oct. 23, 2018, [132 Stat. 3776](/us/stat/132/3776).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### “Secretary” Defined

Secretary means the Secretary of the Army, see [section 102 of Pub. L. 115–270](/us/pl/115/270/s102), set out as a note under [section 2201 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2201).
