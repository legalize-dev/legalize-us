# §§ 1766a to 1766c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 642(a)(2), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460)

##### §§ 1766a to 1766c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 642(a)(2), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460)

Section 1766a, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. IV, § 515, as added [July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 9(e)](/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s9/e), [70 Stat. 560](/us/stat/70/560), related to authorization for grant of contract authority. See [section 2365 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2365).

Section 1766b, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. IV, § 516, as added June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. IV, § 401(d), [72 Stat. 269](/us/stat/72/269), prohibited use of funds for debt retirement.

Section 1766c, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. IV, § 517, as added June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. IV, § 401(d), [72 Stat. 269](/us/stat/72/269); amended July 24, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–108](/us/pl/86/108), ch. IV, § 401(e), [73 Stat. 253](/us/stat/73/253); May 14, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–472](/us/pl/86/472), ch. IV, § 401(e), [74 Stat. 139](/us/stat/74/139), related to completion of plans and cost estimates, and to computation of benefits and costs. See [section 2361 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2361).
