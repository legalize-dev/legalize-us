# § 1184. Omitted

##### § 1184. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–508, § 4](/us/pl/90/508/s4), Sept. 21, 1968, [82 Stat. 862](/us/stat/82/862), which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and enrollees who are less than twenty-one years of age or under a legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
