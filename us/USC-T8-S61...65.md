# §§ 61 to 65. Omitted

##### §§ 61 to 65. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 61, R.S. 2032, related to continuation of laws then in force.

Section 62, R.S. 2033, related to enforcement of laws by former Secretary of War.

Section 63, acts [Mar. 3, 1879, ch. 182, § 2](/us/act/1879-03-03/ch182/s2), [20 Stat. 402](/us/stat/20/402); [Feb. 1, 1888, ch. 4, § 1](/us/act/1888-02-01/ch4/s1), [25 Stat. 9](/us/stat/25/9); [July 1, 1898, ch. 546](/us/act/1898-07-01/ch546), [30 Stat. 640](/us/stat/30/640), related to claims for pay or bounty.

Section 64, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1351](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1351), [32 Stat. 556](/us/stat/32/556), related to retained bounty fund.

Section 65, R.S. § 2037, related to wives and children of colored soldiers.
