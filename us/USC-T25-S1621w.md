# § 1621w. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–148, title X, § 10221(a)](/us/pl/111/148/tX/s10221/a), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 935](/us/stat/124/935)

##### § 1621w. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–148, title X, § 10221(a)](/us/pl/111/148/tX/s10221/a), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 935](/us/stat/124/935)

Section, [Pub. L. 94–437, title II, § 224](/us/pl/94/437/tII/s224), as added [Pub. L. 102–573, title II, § 217(a)](/us/pl/102/573/tII/s217/a), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4559](/us/stat/106/4559), authorized appropriations to carry out this subchapter through fiscal year 2000.

The repeal is based on section 101(b)(5) of title I of S. 1790, One Hundred Eleventh Congress, as reported by the Committee on Indian Affairs of the Senate in Dec. 2009, which was enacted into law by [section 10221(a) of Pub. L. 111–148](/us/pl/111/148/s10221/a).
