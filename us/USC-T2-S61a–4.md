# § 61a–4. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–145](/us/pl/93/145), Nov. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 531](/us/stat/87/531)

##### § 61a–4. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–145](/us/pl/93/145), Nov. 1, 1973, [87 Stat. 531](/us/stat/87/531)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–145](/us/pl/91/145), Dec. 12, 1969, [83 Stat. 340](/us/stat/83/340); [Pub. L. 91–382](/us/pl/91/382), Aug. 18, 1970, [84 Stat. 807](/us/stat/84/807), provided for appointment and salary of a Comptroller of the Senate and a secretary to the Comptroller.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 93–145](/us/pl/93/145) provided that the repeal is effective July 1, 1973.
