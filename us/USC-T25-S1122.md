# § 1122. Omitted

##### § 1122. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–309, § 1](/us/pl/92/309/s1), June 2, 1972, [86 Stat. 199](/us/stat/86/199), which directed the distribution of certain judgment funds as provided in former sections 1122 to 1130 of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
