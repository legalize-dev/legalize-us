# § 855a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–857, § 14(79)](/us/pl/85/857/s14/79), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1272](/us/stat/72/1272)

##### § 855a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–857, § 14(79)](/us/pl/85/857/s14/79), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1272](/us/stat/72/1272)

Section, acts [Dec. 3, 1942, ch. 670, § 2](/us/act/1942-12-03/ch670/s2), [56 Stat. 1038](/us/stat/56/1038); [Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 738](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch738), [68 Stat. 730](/us/stat/68/730), related to rights and benefits of commissioned officers of Coast and Geodetic Survey assigned to projects of Army and Navy outside continental United States or in hazardous military areas.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal by [Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857) effective Jan. 1, 1959, see [section 2 of Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857/s2), set out as an Effective Date note preceding Part I of Title 38, Veterans’ Benefits.
