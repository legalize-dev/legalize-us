# §§ 1251 to 1255. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569](/us/stat/72/1569)

##### §§ 1251 to 1255. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569](/us/stat/72/1569)

Section 1251, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 319](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s319), [68 Stat. 1160](/us/stat/68/1160), related to examination for Federal recognition upon unit vacancy promotion. See [section 309 of Title 32](/us/usc/t32/s309), National Guard.

Section 1252, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 320](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s320), [68 Stat. 1160](/us/stat/68/1160), related to extension of automatic Federal recognition to higher grade. See [section 310 of Title 32](/us/usc/t32/s310).

Section 1253, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 321](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s321), [68 Stat. 1160](/us/stat/68/1160), provided for promotion to higher grade upon recognition. See [section 14308(f) of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s14308/f), Armed Forces.

Section 1254, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 322](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s322), [68 Stat. 1161](/us/stat/68/1161), provided for promotion upon transfer to Army Reserve. See [section 12213 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12213).

Section 1255, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 323](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s323), [68 Stat. 1161](/us/stat/68/1161), provided for appointment of adjutants general and assistant adjutants general as Reserve officers. See [section 12215(a) of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12215/a).
