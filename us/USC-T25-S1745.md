# § 1745. Omitted

##### § 1745. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 97–399, § 6](/us/pl/97/399/s6), Dec. 31, 1982, [96 Stat. 2014](/us/stat/96/2014), which provided for special provisions for the Miccosukee Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
