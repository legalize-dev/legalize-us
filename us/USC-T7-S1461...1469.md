# §§ 1461 to 1469. Omitted

##### §§ 1461 to 1469. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 1461 to 1469 were omitted pursuant to section 1469 which provided that this subchapter was to be effective only for the 1991 through 1997 program crops.
