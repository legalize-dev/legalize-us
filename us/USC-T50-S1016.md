# § 1016. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–651, title III, § 307A](/us/pl/87/651/tIII/s307A), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 526](/us/stat/76/526)

##### § 1016. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–651, title III, § 307A](/us/pl/87/651/tIII/s307A), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 526](/us/stat/76/526)

Section, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 265, as added [July 9, 1956, ch. 534](/us/act/1956-07-09/ch534), [70 Stat. 517](/us/stat/70/517); amended Sept. 2, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–857, § 13(r)](/us/pl/85/857/s13/r), [72 Stat. 1266](/us/stat/72/1266); Sept. 21, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–324, § 1](/us/pl/86/324/s1), [73 Stat. 596](/us/stat/73/596); June 28, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–509, § 1](/us/pl/87/509/s1), [76 Stat. 120](/us/stat/76/120), provided for a lump-sum readjustment payment for involuntary release from active duty. See [section 12686 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12686), Armed Forces.

[Pub. L. 87–509, § 1](/us/pl/87/509/s1), June 28, 1962, [76 Stat. 120](/us/stat/76/120), was repealed by [Pub. L. 89–718, § 75(4)](/us/pl/89/718/s75/4), Nov. 2, 1966, [80 Stat. 1124](/us/stat/80/1124).
