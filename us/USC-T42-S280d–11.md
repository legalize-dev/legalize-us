#### Codification

L. 106–310, div. A, title V, § 502(3)](/us/pl/106/310/dA/tV/s502/3), Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1115](/us/stat/114/1115), and transferred to [section 280e–11 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s280e–11).
