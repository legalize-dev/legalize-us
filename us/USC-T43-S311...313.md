# §§ 311 to 313. Repealed. [Aug. 1, 1955, ch. 448](/us/act/1955-08-01/ch448), [69 Stat. 434](/us/stat/69/434)

##### §§ 311 to 313. Repealed. [Aug. 1, 1955, ch. 448](/us/act/1955-08-01/ch448), [69 Stat. 434](/us/stat/69/434)

Section 311, acts [June 3, 1878, ch. 151, § 1](/us/act/1878-06-03/ch151/s1), [20 Stat. 89](/us/stat/20/89); [Aug. 4, 1892, ch. 375, § 2](/us/act/1892-08-04/ch375/s2), [27 Stat. 348](/us/stat/27/348); [May 18, 1898, ch. 344, § 1](/us/act/1898-05-18/ch344/s1), [30 Stat. 418](/us/stat/30/418), authorized sale of public lands valuable chiefly for timber or stone.

Section 312, acts [June 3, 1878, ch. 151, § 2](/us/act/1878-06-03/ch151/s2), [20 Stat. 89](/us/stat/20/89); [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1](/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1), [42 Stat. 208](/us/stat/42/208); [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462), [43 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/43/1145), provided for applications for purchase of timber and stone lands.

Section 313, [act June 3, 1878, ch. 151, § 3](/us/act/1878-06-03/ch151/s3), [20 Stat. 90](/us/stat/20/90), provided for publication of applications to purchase timber and stone lands, patent and entry.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Existing Rights and Claims

[Act Aug. 1, 1955, ch. 448](/us/act/1955-08-01/ch448), [69 Stat. 434](/us/stat/69/434), provided that the repeal of sections 311 to 313 is subject to valid existing rights and claims.
