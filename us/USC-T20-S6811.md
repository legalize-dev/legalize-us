# § 6811. Short title

##### § 6811. Short title

This part may be cited as the “English Language Acquisition, Language Enhancement, and Academic Achievement Act”.

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 3101](/us/pl/89/10/tIII/s3101), as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title III, § 301](/us/pl/107/110/tIII/s301), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1690](/us/stat/115/1690).)*
