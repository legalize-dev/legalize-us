# § 10681. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 502(c)(1)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s502/c/1), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5228](/us/stat/132/5228)

##### § 10681. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 502(c)(1)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s502/c/1), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5228](/us/stat/132/5228)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 3001](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s3001), as added [Pub. L. 110–199, title I, § 114(2)](/us/pl/110/199/tI/s114/2), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 677](/us/stat/122/677), related to grant program to evaluate and improve educational methods at prisons, jails, and juvenile facilities. See [section 10741 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10741).

Section was formerly classified to [section 3797dd of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3797dd), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Another [section 3001 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s3001) is classified to [section 10671 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10671).
