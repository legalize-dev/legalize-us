# § 460nnn–122. Use of land and water conservation fund

##### § 460nnn–122. Use of land and water conservation fund

(a) Availability of fund There are authorized to be appropriated $25,000,000 from the land and water conservation fund established under [section 200302 of title 54](/us/usc/t54/s200302) to provide funds for the acquisition of land and interests in land under [section 460nnn–24 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s460nnn–24) and to enter into nondevelopment easements and conservation easements under subsections (b) and (c) of [section 460nnn–42 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s460nnn–42).

(b) Term of use Amounts appropriated pursuant to the authorization of appropriations in subsection (a) shall remain available until expended.

*([Pub. L. 106–399, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/106/399/tVII/s702), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1673](/us/stat/114/1673); [Pub. L. 113–287, § 5(d)(5)](/us/pl/113/287/s5/d/5), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3264](/us/stat/128/3264).)*
