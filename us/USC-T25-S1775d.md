##### § 1775d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–377, § 6](/us/pl/103/377/s6), Oct. 19, 1994, [108 Stat. 3505](/us/stat/108/3505), which related to consent of the United States to State assumption of criminal jurisdiction, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
