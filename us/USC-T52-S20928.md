# § 20928. Requiring majority approval for actions

##### § 20928. Requiring majority approval for actions

Any action which the Commission is authorized to carry out under this chapter may be carried out only with the approval of at least three of its members.

*([Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 208](/us/pl/107/252/tII/s208), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1678](/us/stat/116/1678).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 107–252](/us/pl/107/252), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1666](/us/stat/116/1666), known as the Help America Vote Act of 2002, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 15328 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s15328), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
