# § 566. Omitted

##### § 566. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 99–398, § 2](/us/pl/99/398/s2), Aug. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 849](/us/stat/100/849); [Pub. L. 99–514, § 2](/us/pl/99/514/s2), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2095](/us/stat/100/2095), which related to restoration of Federal recognition, rights, and privileges to the tribe and tribe members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 99–398, § 1](/us/pl/99/398/s1), Aug. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 849](/us/stat/100/849), provided that [Pub. L. 99–398](/us/pl/99/398), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Klamath Indian Tribe Restoration Act”.
