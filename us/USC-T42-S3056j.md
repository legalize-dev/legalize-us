# § 3056j. Treatment of assistance

##### § 3056j. Treatment of assistance

Assistance provided under this subchapter shall not be considered to be financial assistance described in [section 1255a(h)(1)(A) of title 8](/us/usc/t8/s1255a/h/1/A).

*([Pub. L. 89–73, title V, § 512](/us/pl/89/73/tV/s512), as added [Pub. L. 109–365, title V, § 501](/us/pl/109/365/tV/s501), Oct. 17, 2006, [120 Stat. 2580](/us/stat/120/2580).)*
