# §§ 1311 to 1318. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 1311 to 1318. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 1311, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252, title II, § 216(a)](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252/tII/s216/a), [42 Stat. 1471](/us/stat/42/1471), related to various criminal acts of officers, employees, or agents. See [section 709 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s709), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 1312, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252, title II, § 216(b)](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252/tII/s216/b), [42 Stat. 1472](/us/stat/42/1472), related to false statements. See [section 1014 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1014).

Section 1313, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252, title II, § 216(c)](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252/tII/s216/c), [42 Stat. 1472](/us/stat/42/1472), related to overvaluation of property offered as security. See [section 1014 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1014).

Section 1314, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252, title II, § 216(d)](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252/tII/s216/d), [42 Stat. 1472](/us/stat/42/1472), related to offenses by examiners. See sections 1908 and 1909 of Title 18.

Section 1315, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252, title II, § 216(e)](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252/tII/s216/e), [42 Stat. 1472](/us/stat/42/1472), related to acceptance of gifts by officers. See [section 215 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s215).

Section 1316, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252, title II, § 216(f)](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252/tII/s216/f), [42 Stat. 1473](/us/stat/42/1473), related to forgery, etc. See [section 493 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s493).

Section 1317, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252, title II, § 216(g)](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252/tII/s216/g), [42 Stat. 1473](/us/stat/42/1473), related to false representations as to debentures. See [section 1013 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1013).

Section 1318, [act Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252, title II, § 216(h)](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252/tII/s216/h), [42 Stat. 1473](/us/stat/42/1473), related to unlawful use of words “National Agricultural Credit Corporation”. See [section 709 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s709).
