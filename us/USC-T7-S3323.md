# § 3323. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–185, title III, § 302(a)](/us/pl/105/185/tIII/s302/a), June 23, 1998, [112 Stat. 563](/us/stat/112/563)

##### § 3323. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–185, title III, § 302(a)](/us/pl/105/185/tIII/s302/a), June 23, 1998, [112 Stat. 563](/us/stat/112/563)

Section, [Pub. L. 95–113, title XIV, § 1476](/us/pl/95/113/tXIV/s1476), as added [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1614(b)(1)](/us/pl/101/624/tXVI/s1614/b/1), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3728](/us/stat/104/3728); amended [Pub. L. 104–127, title VIII, § 820(c)](/us/pl/104/127/tVIII/s820/c), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1168](/us/stat/110/1168), authorized grants and appropriations for acquaculture research facilities.

A prior section 3323, [Pub. L. 95–113, title XIV, § 1476](/us/pl/95/113/tXIV/s1476), as added [Pub. L. 97–98, title XIV, § 1440(a)](/us/pl/97/98/tXIV/s1440/a), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1317](/us/stat/95/1317), related to establishment, duration, and meetings of Aquaculture Advisory Board, and appointment and compensation of Board members, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 99–198, title XIV, § 1429(b)](/us/pl/99/198/tXIV/s1429/b), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1556](/us/stat/99/1556).
