# § 2205. Regulations for implementation of provisions

##### § 2205. Regulations for implementation of provisions

The Secretary is authorized to prescribe such regulations as the Secretary determines necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 95–334, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/95/334/tIV/s405), Aug. 4, 1978, [92 Stat. 434](/us/stat/92/434); [Pub. L. 115–334, title II, § 2403(a)(2)(A)](/us/pl/115/334/tII/s2403/a/2/A), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4571](/us/stat/132/4571).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–334](/us/pl/115/334) substituted “The Secretary is” for “The Secretary of Agriculture is”.
