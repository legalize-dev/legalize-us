# § 460mmm–2. McInnis Canyons National Conservation Area

##### § 460mmm–2. McInnis Canyons National Conservation Area

(a) In general There is established the McInnis Canyons National Conservation Area in the State of Colorado and the State of Utah.

(b) Areas included The Conservation Area shall consist of approximately 122,300 acres of public land as generally depicted on the Map.

*([Pub. L. 106–353, § 4](/us/pl/106/353/s4), Oct. 24, 2000, [114 Stat. 1375](/us/stat/114/1375); [Pub. L. 108–400, § 1(c)](/us/pl/108/400/s1/c), Oct. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2254](/us/stat/118/2254).)*
