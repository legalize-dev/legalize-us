# § 20261. Selection of officers for continuation on the Space Force officer list

##### § 20261. Selection of officers for continuation on the Space Force officer list

[Section 14701 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s14701) shall apply in continuation or retention on the Space Force officer in the same manner as to continuation on the reserve active-status list.
