# § 1771. Omitted

##### § 1771. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–95, § 2](/us/pl/100/95/s2), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 704](/us/stat/101/704), which set out congressional findings and declaration of policy, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 100–95, § 1](/us/pl/100/95/s1), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 704](/us/stat/101/704), provided that [Pub. L. 100–95](/us/pl/100/95), enacting this subchapter and provisions formerly set out as a note under this section, could be cited as the “Wampanoag Tribal Council of Gay Head, Inc., Indian Claims Settlement Act of 1987”.
