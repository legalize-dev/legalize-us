# § 3175. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–177, title III, § 313(a)](/us/pl/108/177/tIII/s313/a), Dec. 13, 2003, [117 Stat. 2610](/us/stat/117/2610)

Section, [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, title IX, § 905](/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343/tIX/s905), as added [Pub.
