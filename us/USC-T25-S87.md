# § 87. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### § 87. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section, [act June 22, 1874, ch. 389, § 10](/us/act/1874-06-22/ch389/s10), [18 Stat. 177](/us/stat/18/177), related to interest of agents and employees in Indian contracts. See [section 437 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s437), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 20](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s20), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862).
