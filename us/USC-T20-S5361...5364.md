# §§ 5361 to 5364. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(l)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/l), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

##### §§ 5361 to 5364. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(l)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/l), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

Section 5361, [Pub. L. 101–589, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/101/589/tIV/s401), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2897](/us/stat/104/2897), stated purpose of this subchapter to increase participation of women and minorities in mathematics, science, and engineering.

Section 5362, [Pub. L. 101–589, title IV, § 402](/us/pl/101/589/tIV/s402), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2897](/us/stat/104/2897), related to Distinguished Visiting Professors.

Section 5363, [Pub. L. 101–589, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/101/589/tIV/s403), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2897](/us/stat/104/2897), related to faculty awards for women and minorities.

Section 5364, [Pub. L. 101–589, title IV, § 404](/us/pl/101/589/tIV/s404), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2897](/us/stat/104/2897), related to alliances for minority participation.
