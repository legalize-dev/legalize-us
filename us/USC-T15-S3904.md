# § 3904. Securities laws

##### § 3904. Securities laws

(a) Ownership interest of members in risk retention groups The ownership interests of members in a risk retention group shall be—

(1) considered to be exempted securities for purposes of section 5 of the Securities Act of 1933 [[15 U.S.C. 77e](/us/usc/t15/s77e)] and for purposes of section 12 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 [[15 U.S.C. 78](/us/usc/t15/s78)*l*]; and

(2) considered to be securities for purposes of the provisions of section 17 of the Securities Act of 1933 [[15 U.S.C. 77q](/us/usc/t15/s77q)] and the provisions of section 10 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 [[15 U.S.C. 78j](/us/usc/t15/s78j)].

(b) Investment companies A risk retention group shall not be considered to be an investment company for purposes of the Investment Company Act of 1940 ([15 U.S.C. 80a–1](/us/usc/t15/s80a–1) et seq.).

(c) State blue sky laws The ownership interests of members in a risk retention group shall not be considered securities for purposes of any State blue sky law.

*([Pub. L. 97–45, § 5](/us/pl/97/45/s5), Sept. 25, 1981, [95 Stat. 952](/us/stat/95/952).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

(b), is title I of [act Aug. 22, 1940, ch. 686](/us/act/1940-08-22/ch686), [54 Stat. 789](/us/stat/54/789), which is classified generally to subchapter I (§ 80a–1 et seq.) of chapter 2D of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 80a–51 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s80a–51) and Tables.
