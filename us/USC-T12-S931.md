# § 931. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### § 931. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 26, [39 Stat. 380](/us/stat/39/380); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), exempted land banks and land bank associations from taxation and directed that mortgages and bonds be deemed instrumentalities of government. See [section 2055 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2055).
