##### §§ 8241 to 8247. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–110, title X, § 1011(5)(A)](/us/pl/107/110/tX/s1011/5/A), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1986](/us/stat/115/1986)

Section 8242, [Pub. L. 89–10, title X, § 10902](/us/pl/89/10/tX/s10902), as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3844](/us/stat/108/3844), set forth findings.

Section 8246, [Pub. L. 89–10, title X, § 10906](/us/pl/89/10/tX/s10906), as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3846](/us/stat/108/3846), defined “Community learning center”.

Section 8247, [Pub. L. 89–10, title X, § 10907](/us/pl/89/10/tX/s10907), as added [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3846](/us/stat/108/3846), authorized appropriations.
