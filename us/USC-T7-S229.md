# § 229. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–234, title XI](/us/pl/110/234/tXI), 11004(b), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1356](/us/stat/122/1356), and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title XI, § 11004(b), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 2118

##### § 229. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–234, title XI](/us/pl/110/234/tXI), 11004(b), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1356](/us/stat/122/1356), and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title XI, § 11004(b), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 2118

Section, [act Aug. 15, 1921, ch. 64, title IV, § 416](/us/act/1921-08-15/ch64/tIV/s416), as added [Pub. L. 110–234, title XI, § 11004(a)(2)](/us/pl/110/234/tXI/s11004/a/2), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1355](/us/stat/122/1355), and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title XI, § 11004(a)(2), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 2117, related to annual reports about investigations and enforcement actions.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) and [Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246) repealed this section. [Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) was repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a).

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 416 of act Aug. 15, 1921, was renumbered section 417 and is classified to [section 229c of this title](/us/usc/t7/s229c).

Another prior section 416 of act Aug. 15, 1921, was classified to [section 229a of this title](/us/usc/t7/s229a), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 106–78](/us/pl/106/78).
