# § 9598. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 9598. Authorization of appropriations

(1) a majority shall be used in each such fiscal year to carry out the MBDA Business Center Program under part B of subchapter I, including the component of that program relating to specialty centers; and

(2) $20,000,000 shall be used in each such fiscal year to carry out subchapter III.

*([Pub. L. 117–58, div. K, title VII, § 100708](/us/pl/117/58/dK/tVII/s100708), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 1467](/us/stat/135/1467).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this division”, meaning div. K of [Pub. L. 117–58](/us/pl/117/58), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 1445](/us/stat/135/1445), which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of division K to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 9501 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s9501) and Tables.
