# § 2399. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–161, title I, § 101(6)](/us/pl/94/161/tI/s101/6), Dec. 20, 1975, [89 Stat. 850](/us/stat/89/850)

##### § 2399. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–161, title I, § 101(6)](/us/pl/94/161/tI/s101/6), Dec. 20, 1975, [89 Stat. 850](/us/stat/89/850)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 639, as added [Pub. L. 80–171](/us/pl/80/171), pt. III, § 302(k), Sept. 6, 1965, [79 Stat. 661](/us/stat/79/661); amended [Pub. L. 93–559, § 28(a)](/us/pl/93/559/s28/a), Dec. 30, 1974, [88 Stat. 1803](/us/stat/88/1803), provided for famine or disaster relief, authorized appropriation of $40,000,000 for fiscal year 1975, and required Presidential reports to Committees of the Senate and Speaker of the House. See sections 2292 and 2292a of this title.
