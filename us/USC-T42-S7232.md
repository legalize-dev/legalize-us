# § 7232. Senior positions

##### § 7232. Senior positions

In addition to those positions created by subchapter II of this chapter, there shall be within the Department fourteen additional officers in positions authorized by [section 5316 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s5316) who shall be appointed by the Secretary and who shall perform such functions as the Secretary shall prescribe from time to time.

*([Pub. L. 95–91, title VI, § 622](/us/pl/95/91/tVI/s622), Aug. 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 597](/us/stat/91/597).)*
