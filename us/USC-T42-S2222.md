# § 2222. Condemnation of real property

##### § 2222. Condemnation of real property

Proceedings for condemnation shall be instituted pursuant to the provisions of [section 3113 of title 40](/us/usc/t40/s3113), and [section 1403 of title 28](/us/usc/t28/s1403). Sections 3114 to 3116 and 3118 of title 40 shall be applicable to any such proceedings.

*([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724), title I, § 172, as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 953](/us/stat/68/953); renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 902(a)(8)](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s902/a/8), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2944](/us/stat/106/2944).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

In text, “[section 3113 of title 40](/us/usc/t40/s3113)” substituted for “the Act approved August 1, 1888, as amended,” and “Sections 3114 to 3116 and 3118 of title 40” substituted for “The Act approved February 26, 1931, as amended,”, on authority of [Pub. L. 107–217, § 5(c)](/us/pl/107/217/s5/c), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1303](/us/stat/116/1303), the first section of which enacted Title 40, Public Buildings, Property, and Works.

#### Prior Provisions

Provisions similar to this section were contained in [section 1813(b) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1813/b), prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946, by act Aug. 30, 1954.
