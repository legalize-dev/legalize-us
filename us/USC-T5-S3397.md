# § 3397. Regulations

##### § 3397. Regulations

The Office of Personnel Management shall prescribe regulations to carry out the purpose of this subchapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 95–454, title IV, § 403(a)](/us/pl/95/454/tIV/s403/a), Oct. 13, 1978, [92 Stat. 1164](/us/stat/92/1164).)*
