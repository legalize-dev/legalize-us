# § 1773e. Omitted

##### § 1773e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–41, § 7](/us/pl/101/41/s7), June 21, 1989, [103 Stat. 87](/us/stat/103/87), which appropriated $100,000 for navigation equipment at Commencement Bay as part of the fisheries aspect of the Settlement Agreement, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
