# §§ 2961 to 2966. Omitted

##### §§ 2961 to 2966. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 2961, [Pub. L. 89–10, title I, § 1561](/us/pl/89/10/tI/s1561), as added [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1001](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1001), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 210](/us/stat/102/210), provided general authority for national programs and activities.
