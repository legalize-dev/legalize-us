# § 8305. Annual report

##### § 8305. Annual report

(1) a reciprocal defense procurement memorandum of understanding described in [section 8304(a) of this title](/us/usc/t41/s8304/a);

(2) the Trade Agreements Act of 1979 ([19 U.S.C. 2501](/us/usc/t19/s2501) et seq.); or

(3) an international agreement to which the United States is a party.

*([Pub. L. 111–350, § 3](/us/pl/111/350/s3), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3833](/us/stat/124/3833).)*
