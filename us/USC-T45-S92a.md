# § 92a. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### § 92a. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 92a, acts [Feb. 14, 1933, ch. 51](/us/act/1933-02-14/ch51), [47 Stat. 800](/us/stat/47/800); [Mar. 2, 1933, ch. 187](/us/act/1933-03-02/ch187), [47 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/47/1424), related to compensation of certain railroads for transportation of property and troops of United States. See [section 10721 of Title 49](/us/usc/t49/s10721), Transportation.

Prior to repeal, section was classified to section 1375b of former Title 10, Army and Air Force.
