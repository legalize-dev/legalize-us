# § 1667. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–576, title I, § 103](/us/pl/90/576/tI/s103), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091](/us/stat/82/1091)

##### § 1667. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–576, title I, § 103](/us/pl/90/576/tI/s103), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091](/us/stat/82/1091)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–688, § 2](/us/pl/87/688/s2), Sept. 25, 1962, [76 Stat. 586](/us/stat/76/586), extended to American Samoa the benefits of the Vocational Education Act of 1946 and authorized an annual appropriation of $80,000 therefor.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 90–576, title I, § 103](/us/pl/90/576/tI/s103), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091](/us/stat/82/1091), provided that the repeal of this section is effective July 1, 1969.
