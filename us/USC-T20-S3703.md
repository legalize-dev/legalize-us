# § 3703. Omitted

##### § 3703. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–430, title II, § 204](/us/pl/96/430/tII/s204), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1848](/us/stat/94/1848), which required the National Afro-American History and Culture Commission to submit to appropriate committees of Congress a report containing a detailed statement of the financial transactions of the Commission and the activities undertaken by the Commission during the previous year, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance. See, also, page 179 of House Document No. 103–7.
