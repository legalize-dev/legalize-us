# § 20306. Annual report

##### § 20306. Annual report

The corporation shall make an annual report to Congress, to be filed with the Librarian of Congress.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1287](/us/stat/112/1287).)*
