# §§ 293n to 293p. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–392, title I, § 102(4)](/us/pl/105/392/tI/s102/4), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3539](/us/stat/112/3539)

##### §§ 293n to 293p. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–392, title I, § 102(4)](/us/pl/105/392/tI/s102/4), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3539](/us/stat/112/3539)

Section 293n, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title VII, § 750](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tVII/s750), as added [Pub. L. 102–408, title I, § 102](/us/pl/102/408/tI/s102), Oct. 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 2044](/us/stat/106/2044), authorized grants and contracts for development of training programs for physician assistants.

Section 293*o*, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title VII, § 751](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tVII/s751), as added [Pub. L. 102–408, title I, § 102](/us/pl/102/408/tI/s102), Oct. 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 2044](/us/stat/106/2044), authorized grants and contracts for implementation of training projects for podiatric physicians.

Section 293p, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title VII, § 752](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tVII/s752), as added [Pub. L. 102–408, title I, § 102](/us/pl/102/408/tI/s102), Oct. 13, 1992, [106 Stat. 2045](/us/stat/106/2045), set forth general provisions relating to traineeships and fellowships.
