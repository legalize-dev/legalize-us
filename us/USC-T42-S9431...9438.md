# §§ 9431 to 9438. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX, § 902(e)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tIX/s902/e/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 560](/us/stat/95/560)

##### §§ 9431 to 9438. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX, § 902(e)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tIX/s902/e/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 560](/us/stat/95/560)

Section 9431, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II, § 201](/us/pl/96/398/tII/s201), Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1571](/us/stat/94/1571), set forth authority, etc., for grants for community mental health centers.

Section 9432, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II, § 202](/us/pl/96/398/tII/s202), Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1573](/us/stat/94/1573), set forth authority, etc., for grants for services for chronically, mentally ill individuals.

Section 9433, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II, § 203](/us/pl/96/398/tII/s203), Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1575](/us/stat/94/1575), set forth authority, etc., for grants for services for severely, mentally disturbed children and adults.

Section 9434, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II, § 204](/us/pl/96/398/tII/s204), Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1577](/us/stat/94/1577), set forth authority, etc., for grants for mental health services for elderly individuals and other priority populations.

Section 9435, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II, § 205](/us/pl/96/398/tII/s205), Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1581](/us/stat/94/1581), set forth authority, etc., for grants for non-revenue producing services.

Section 9436, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II, § 206](/us/pl/96/398/tII/s206), Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1582](/us/stat/94/1582), set forth authority, etc., for grants for mental health services in health care centers.

Section 9437, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II, § 207](/us/pl/96/398/tII/s207), Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1583](/us/stat/94/1583), set forth authority, etc., for grants and contracts for innovative projects.

Section 9438, [Pub. L. 96–398, title II, § 208](/us/pl/96/398/tII/s208), Oct. 7, 1980, [94 Stat. 1584](/us/stat/94/1584), set forth authority, etc., for grants for prevention of mental illness and promotion of mental health.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 902(h) of Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35/s902/h), set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under section 238*l* of this title.
