# § 9491. Status as federally chartered corporation; purposes

##### § 9491. Status as federally chartered corporation; purposes

(a) Status.—

(1) The Civil Air Patrol is a nonprofit corporation that is federally chartered under [section 40301 of title 36](/us/usc/t36/s40301).

(2) Except as provided in [section 9492(b)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s9492/b/2), the Civil Air Patrol is not an instrumentality of the Federal Government for any purpose.

(b) Purposes.— The purposes of the Civil Air Patrol are set forth in [section 40302 of title 36](/us/usc/t36/s40302).
