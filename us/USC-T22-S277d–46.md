# § 277d–46. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 277d–46. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out sections 277d–43 to 277d–46 of this title. Such sums shall remain available until expended.

*([Pub. L. 106–457, title VIII, § 806](/us/pl/106/457/tVIII/s806), Nov. 7, 2000, [114 Stat. 1981](/us/stat/114/1981); [Pub. L. 108–425, § 3](/us/pl/108/425/s3), Nov. 30, 2004, [118 Stat. 2421](/us/stat/118/2421).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2004—[Pub. L. 108–425](/us/pl/108/425) substituted “such sums as may be necessary” for “a total of $156,000,000 for fiscal years 2001 through 2005”.
