# [§ 9775. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–145, title V, § 509(b)](/us/pl/92/145/tV/s509/b), Oct. 27, 1971, [85 Stat. 408](/us/stat/85/408)]

##### [§ 9775. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–145, title V, § 509(b)](/us/pl/92/145/tV/s509/b), Oct. 27, 1971, [85 Stat. 408](/us/stat/85/408)]

Section, [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 590](/us/stat/70A/590), authorized assignment of quarters belonging to United States at an air base or other Air Force installation to officers, grade lieutenant general down to second lieutenant, 10 to 2 rooms, respectively, and prohibited other assignment where quarters existed.
