# § 1195. Transactions with professionals

##### § 1195. Transactions with professionals

Notwithstanding [section 327(a) of this title](/us/usc/t11/s327/a), a person is not disqualified for employment under [section 327 of this title](/us/usc/t11/s327), by a debtor solely because that person holds a claim of less than $10,000 that arose prior to commencement of the case.

*(Added [Pub. L. 116–54, § 2(a)](/us/pl/116/54/s2/a), Aug. 23, 2019, [133 Stat. 1084](/us/stat/133/1084).)*
