##### [§ 280D. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–418, title I, § 1941(b)(4)(A)](/us/pl/100/418/tI/s1941/b/4/A), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1324](/us/stat/102/1324)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 96–499, title XI, § 1131(d)(1)](/us/pl/96/499/tXI/s1131/d/1), Dec. 5, 1980, [94 Stat. 2693](/us/stat/94/2693), related to portion of chapter 45 windfall profit tax on domestic crude oil for which credit or refund was allowable under section 6429.

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to crude oil removed from the premises on or after Aug. 23, 1988, see [section 1941(c) of Pub. L. 100–418](/us/pl/100/418/s1941/c), set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 164 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s164).
