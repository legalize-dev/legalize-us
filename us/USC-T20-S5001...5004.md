# §§ 5001 to 5004. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/i), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

##### §§ 5001 to 5004. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(i)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/i), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

Section 5001, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6001](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6001), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1469](/us/stat/102/1469), provided that this chapter could be cited as the “Education and Training for a Competitive America Act of 1988”.

Section 5002, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6002](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6002), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1469](/us/stat/102/1469), stated findings of Congress and purpose.

Section 5003, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6003](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6003), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1470](/us/stat/102/1470), defined terms used in this chapter.

Section 5004, [Pub. L. 100–418, title VI, § 6004](/us/pl/100/418/tVI/s6004), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1470](/us/stat/102/1470), contained general restrictions relating to grants and contracts under this chapter.
