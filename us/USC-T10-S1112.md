# § 1112. Assets of Fund

##### § 1112. Assets of Fund

(1) Amounts paid into the Fund under [section 1116 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1116).

(2) Any amount appropriated to the Fund.

(3) Any return on investment of the assets of the Fund.

(4) Amounts paid into the Fund pursuant to [section 1111(c) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1111/c).

*(Added [Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [[div. A]](/us/pl/106/398/s1/dA), title VII, § 713(a)(1)], Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1654](/us/stat/114/1654), 1654A–180; amended [Pub. L. 107–107, div. A, title VII, § 711(b)(2)](/us/pl/107/107/dA/tVII/s711/b/2), Dec. 28, 2001, [115 Stat. 1165](/us/stat/115/1165).)*
