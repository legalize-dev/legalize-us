#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of 1991 Amendment

[Pub. L. 102–73, § 1](/us/pl/102/73/s1), July 25, 1991, [105 Stat. 333](/us/stat/105/333), provided that [Pub. L. 102–73](/us/pl/102/73) was to be cited as the “National Literacy Act of 1991”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 251(a)(2)](/us/pl/105/220/tII/s251/a/2), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1079](/us/stat/112/1079).

#### Short Title of 1988 Amendment

[Pub. L. 100–297, title II, § 2101](/us/pl/100/297/tII/s2101), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 302](/us/stat/102/302), provided that part B (§§ 2101, 2102) of title II of [Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297), enacting this chapter, was to be cited as the “Adult Education Amendments of 1988”.

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 89–750, title III, § 301](/us/pl/89/750/tIII/s301), as added by [Pub. L. 100–297, title II, § 2102](/us/pl/100/297/tII/s2102), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 302](/us/stat/102/302), provided that title III of [Pub. L. 89–750](/us/pl/89/750), enacting this chapter, was to be cited as the “Adult Education Act”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 251(a)(1)](/us/pl/105/220/tII/s251/a/1), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1079](/us/stat/112/1079).

#### Congressional Findings Regarding National Literacy Act of 1991

[Pub. L. 102–73, § 2](/us/pl/102/73/s2), July 25, 1991, [105 Stat. 333](/us/stat/105/333), stated findings of Congress regarding the National Literacy Act of 1991, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 251(a)(2)](/us/pl/105/220/tII/s251/a/2), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1079](/us/stat/112/1079).

#### Definitions

[Pub. L. 102–73, § 3](/us/pl/102/73/s3), July 25, 1991, [105 Stat. 334](/us/stat/105/334), defined the term “literacy” for purposes of [Pub. L. 102–73](/us/pl/102/73), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 105–220, title II, § 251(a)(2)](/us/pl/105/220/tII/s251/a/2), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1079](/us/stat/112/1079).
