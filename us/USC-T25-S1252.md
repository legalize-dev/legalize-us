# § 1252. Omitted

##### § 1252. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–253, § 2](/us/pl/92/253/s2), Mar. 17, 1972, [86 Stat. 64](/us/stat/86/64), which exempted funds distributed to members of the tribes from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
