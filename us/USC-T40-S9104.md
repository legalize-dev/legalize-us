# § 9104. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 9104. Authorization of appropriations

Necessary amounts may be appropriated to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 107–217](/us/pl/107/217), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1232](/us/stat/116/1232).)*
