# § 9653. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–499, title V, § 511(b)](/us/pl/99/499/tV/s511/b), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1761](/us/stat/100/1761)

##### § 9653. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–499, title V, § 511(b)](/us/pl/99/499/tV/s511/b), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1761](/us/stat/100/1761)

Section, [Pub. L. 96–510, title III, § 303](/us/pl/96/510/tIII/s303), Dec. 11, 1980, [94 Stat. 2808](/us/stat/94/2808), provided for termination of authority to collect taxes under this chapter.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1987, see [section 511(c) of Pub. L. 99–499](/us/pl/99/499/s511/c), set out as an Effective Date of 1986 Amendment note under [section 4611 of Title 26](/us/usc/t26/s4611), Internal Revenue Code.
