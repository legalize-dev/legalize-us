# § 7121. Rule of construction

##### § 7121. Rule of construction

(1) authorize activities or programming that encourages teenage sexual activity; or

(2) prohibit effective activities or programming that meet the requirements of [section 7906 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7906).

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title IV, § 4111](/us/pl/89/10/tIV/s4111), as added [Pub. L. 114–95, title IV, § 4101](/us/pl/114/95/tIV/s4101), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1982](/us/stat/129/1982).)*
