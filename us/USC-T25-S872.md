# § 872. Omitted

##### § 872. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 85–395, § 2](/us/pl/85/395/s2), May 9, 1958, [72 Stat. 105](/us/stat/72/105), which authorized per capita distributions to tribal members from funds appropriated for a certain judgment against the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
