# §§ 364 to 365. Transferred

##### §§ 364 to 365. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 364, [act July 24, 1947, ch. 305](/us/act/1947-07-24/ch305), [61 Stat. 414](/us/stat/61/414), which authorized the legislature to provide for the exercise of zoning power in town sites, was transferred to [section 738 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s738), Public Lands, and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 364a, [act Aug. 30, 1949, ch. 521, § 1](/us/act/1949-08-30/ch521/s1), [63 Stat. 679](/us/stat/63/679), which authorized the sale of certain public lands and set out the requirements of public auction, notice, and proof of the buyer’s intention, was transferred to [section 687b of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s687b), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 364b, [act Aug. 30, 1949, ch. 521, § 2](/us/act/1949-08-30/ch521/s2), [63 Stat. 679](/us/stat/63/679), which prohibited the sale of land for less than the appraised value and the cost of making any survey to properly describe the land sold, was transferred to [section 687b–1 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s687b–1), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 364c, [act Aug. 30, 1949, ch. 521, § 3](/us/act/1949-08-30/ch521/s3), [63 Stat. 679](/us/stat/63/679), which called for issuance of a certificate of purchase to buyers of public lands and made provision for patent in fee and issuance and contents thereof and placed the liability for mining damage upon persons prospecting for and removing minerals, was transferred to [section 687b–2 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s687b–2), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 364d, [act Aug. 30, 1949, ch. 521, § 4](/us/act/1949-08-30/ch521/s4), [63 Stat. 679](/us/stat/63/679), which saved existing rights and limited the application of sections 364a–364e of this title, was transferred to [section 687b–3 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s687b–3), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 364e, [act Aug. 30, 1949, ch. 521, § 5](/us/act/1949-08-30/ch521/s5), [63 Stat. 679](/us/stat/63/679), which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to make rules and regulations to carry out the purposes of section 364a to 364e of this title, was transferred to [section 687b–4 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s687b–4), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 364f, [Pub. L. 88–66](/us/pl/88/66), July 19, 1963, [77 Stat. 80](/us/stat/77/80), which called for the application of equitable principles upon submission of proof of compliance with use requirements after prescribed period, was transferred to [section 687b–5 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s687b–5), and was subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789).

Section 365, [act Oct. 9, 1942, ch. 584, § 6](/us/act/1942-10-09/ch584/s6), [56 Stat. 779](/us/stat/56/779), which provided for continuation of existing land districts and offices and made provision for making of changes in district boundaries, discontinuance of districts, and designation of land offices, was transferred to [section 123a of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s123a).
