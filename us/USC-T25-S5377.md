# § 5377. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 5377. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 93–638, title IV, § 417](/us/pl/93/638/tIV/s417), as added [Pub. L. 116–180, title I, § 101(e)](/us/pl/116/180/tI/s101/e), Oct. 21, 2020, [134 Stat. 878](/us/stat/134/878).)*
