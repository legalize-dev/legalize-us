# § 788g. Omitted

##### § 788g. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–506, § 3](/us/pl/90/506/s3), Sept. 21, 1968, [82 Stat. 859](/us/stat/82/859), which related to distribution of judgment funds to heirs of deceased enrollees, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
