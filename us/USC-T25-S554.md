# § 554. Omitted

##### § 554. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 1, 1938, ch. 310, § 4](/us/act/1938-06-01/ch310/s4), [52 Stat. 606](/us/stat/52/606), which related to disposition of payment upon the death of an Indian, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
