# § 2688p. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–296, title IV, § 401(b)(2)](/us/pl/91/296/tIV/s401/b/2), June 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 352](/us/stat/84/352)

##### § 2688p. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–296, title IV, § 401(b)(2)](/us/pl/91/296/tIV/s401/b/2), June 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 352](/us/stat/84/352)

Section, [Pub. L. 88–164, title II, § 262](/us/pl/88/164/tII/s262), as added [Pub. L. 90–574, title III, § 303(a)](/us/pl/90/574/tIII/s303/a), Oct. 15, 1968, [82 Stat. 1010](/us/stat/82/1010), authorized use of up to 1 per centum of appropriated funds by Secretary for evaluation of programs authorized by this subchapter.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 91–296, title IV, § 401(b)(2)](/us/pl/91/296/tIV/s401/b/2), June 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 352](/us/stat/84/352), provided that the repeal by that section is effective with respect to appropriations for fiscal years beginning after June 30, 1970.
