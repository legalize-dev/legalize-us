# § 7131. Definition

##### § 7131. Definition

In this chapter, the term “enlistment” means original enlistment or reenlistment.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 177](/us/stat/70A/177), § 3251; [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title XII, § 1231(19)(A)](/us/pl/100/180/dA/tXII/s1231/19/A), Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1161](/us/stat/101/1161); renumbered § 7131, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(b)(2)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/b/2), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1838](/us/stat/132/1838).)*
