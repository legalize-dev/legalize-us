# § 6434. Applicability of other laws

##### § 6434. Applicability of other laws

Chapter 10 of title 5 shall not apply to the Commission.

*([Pub. L. 105–292, title II, § 206](/us/pl/105/292/tII/s206), formerly § 204, Oct. 27, 1998, [112 Stat. 2799](/us/stat/112/2799); renumbered § 206, [Pub. L. 106–55, § 1(b)(2)](/us/pl/106/55/s1/b/2), Aug. 17, 1999, [113 Stat. 401](/us/stat/113/401); [Pub. L. 117–286, § 4(a)(172)](/us/pl/117/286/s4/a/172), Dec. 27, 2022, [136 Stat. 4324](/us/stat/136/4324).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 206 of Pub. L. 105–292](/us/pl/105/292/s206) was renumbered section 209 and is classified to [section 6436 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s6436).

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–286](/us/pl/117/286) substituted “Chapter 10 of title 5” for “The Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App.)”.
