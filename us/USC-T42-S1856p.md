# § 1856p. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–11, § 2](/us/pl/101/11/s2), Apr. 7, 1989, [103 Stat. 15](/us/stat/103/15)

##### § 1856p. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–11, § 2](/us/pl/101/11/s2), Apr. 7, 1989, [103 Stat. 15](/us/stat/103/15)

Section, [Pub. L. 100–428, § 5](/us/pl/100/428/s5), Sept. 9, 1988, [102 Stat. 1616](/us/stat/102/1616), provided that authority to enter into agreements, to furnish or accept emergency wildfire protection resources, or to incur obligations for reimbursement under [section 1856n of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1856n) was to terminate Dec. 31, 1988.
