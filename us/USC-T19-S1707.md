# § 1707. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–295, § 3(a)(2)](/us/pl/104/295/s3/a/2), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3515](/us/stat/110/3515)

##### § 1707. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–295, § 3(a)(2)](/us/pl/104/295/s3/a/2), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3515](/us/stat/110/3515)

Section, [act Aug. 5, 1935, ch. 438, title I, § 7](/us/act/1935-08-05/ch438/tI/s7), [49 Stat. 520](/us/stat/49/520), required certificate for importation of alcoholic liquors in small vessels, provided for issuance of bond where liquor was destined for foreign port, and authorized penalties for failure to carry certificate unless lost, mislaid without fraud, defaced by accident, or incorrect by reason of clerical error or other mistake.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal of section applicable as of Dec. 8, 1993, see [section 3(b) of Pub. L. 104–295](/us/pl/104/295/s3/b), set out as an Effective Date of 1996 Amendment note under [section 1321 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1321).
