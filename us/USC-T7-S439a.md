# § 439a. Omitted

##### § 439a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [July 2, 1948, ch. 818, § 2](/us/act/1948-07-02/ch818/s2), [62 Stat. 1234](/us/stat/62/1234), authorized Secretary of Agriculture to operate plants under former sections 439 to 439e of this title, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
