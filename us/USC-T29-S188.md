# § 188. Repealed. [Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 690, § 4(3)](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch690/s4/3), [69 Stat. 625](/us/stat/69/625)

##### § 188. Repealed. [Aug. 9, 1955, ch. 690, § 4(3)](/us/act/1955-08-09/ch690/s4/3), [69 Stat. 625](/us/stat/69/625)

Section, [act June 23, 1947, ch. 120, title III, § 305](/us/act/1947-06-23/ch120/tIII/s305), [61 Stat. 160](/us/stat/61/160), forbade striking by Government employees, required discharge of striking employee and forfeiture of his civil-service status, and made him ineligible for employment for three years. See sections 3333 and 7311 of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, and [section 1918 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1918), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.
