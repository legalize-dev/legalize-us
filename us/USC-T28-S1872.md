# § 1872. Issues of fact in Supreme Court

##### § 1872. Issues of fact in Supreme Court

In all original actions at law in the Supreme Court against citizens of the United States, issues of fact shall be tried by a jury.

*([June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 953](/us/stat/62/953).)*

Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 343 ([Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231, § 235](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231/s235), [36 Stat. 1156](/us/stat/36/1156)).
