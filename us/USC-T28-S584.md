# § 584. Official stations

##### § 584. Official stations

The Attorney General may determine the official stations of the United States trustees and assistant United States trustees within the regions for which they were appointed.

*(Added [Pub. L. 95–598, title II, § 224(a)](/us/pl/95/598/tII/s224/a), Nov. 6, 1978, [92 Stat. 2663](/us/stat/92/2663); amended [Pub. L. 99–554, title I, § 144(d)](/us/pl/99/554/tI/s144/d), Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3096](/us/stat/100/3096).)*
