# § 640d–18. Omitted

##### § 640d–18. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 19](/us/pl/93/531/s19), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1721](/us/stat/88/1721); [Pub. L. 96–305, § 8](/us/pl/96/305/s8), July 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 932](/us/stat/94/932), which provided for a reduction of livestock within the joint use area, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
