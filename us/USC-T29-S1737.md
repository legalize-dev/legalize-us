# § 1737. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 199(b)(2)](/us/pl/105/220/tI/s199/b/2), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/112/1059)

##### § 1737. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–220, title I, § 199(b)(2)](/us/pl/105/220/tI/s199/b/2), Aug. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/112/1059)

Section, [Pub. L. 97–300, title IV, § 456](/us/pl/97/300/tIV/s456), formerly § 457, as added [Pub. L. 102–235, § 9](/us/pl/102/235/s9), Dec. 12, 1991, [105 Stat. 1809](/us/stat/105/1809); renumbered § 456 and amended [Pub. L. 102–367, title IV, § 403(a)(5)](/us/pl/102/367/tIV/s403/a/5), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1084](/us/stat/106/1084), related to nontraditional employment demonstration program.

A prior [section 456 of Pub. L. 97–300](/us/pl/97/300/s456) was classified to [section 1736 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s1736), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–367, title IV, § 403(a)(4)](/us/pl/102/367/tIV/s403/a/4), Sept. 7, 1992, [106 Stat. 1084](/us/stat/106/1084).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective July 1, 2000, see [section 199(c)(2)(B) of Pub. L. 105–220](/us/pl/105/220/s199/c/2/B), set out as a note under [section 1501 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s1501).
