# § 1365. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–442, § 1](/us/pl/86/442/s1), Apr. 22, 1960, [74 Stat. 81](/us/stat/74/81)

##### § 1365. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–442, § 1](/us/pl/86/442/s1), Apr. 22, 1960, [74 Stat. 81](/us/stat/74/81)

Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title XV, § 1505](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tXV/s1505), as added [Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1212, § 4(a)](/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1212/s4/a), [68 Stat. 1133](/us/stat/68/1133), related to status of a Federal employee who was performing Federal service at time of separation from employment by the United States.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective only with respect to benefit years which began more than thirty days after Apr. 22, 1960, see [Pub. L. 86–442, § 1](/us/pl/86/442/s1), Apr. 22, 1960, [74 Stat. 81](/us/stat/74/81).
