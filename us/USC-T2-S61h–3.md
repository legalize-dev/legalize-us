# § 61h–3. Omitted

##### § 61h–3. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 61h–3, [Pub. L. 94–59, title I](/us/pl/94/59/tI), July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 269](/us/stat/89/269), authorized Senate Majority and Minority Leaders to appoint and fix compensation of an executive secretary and a clerical assistant effective July 1, 1975. Positions established by Legislative Branch Appropriation Act, 1970, for Offices of Senate Majority and Minority Leaders, which Act, [Pub. L. 91–145](/us/pl/91/145), Dec. 12, 1969, [83 Stat. 339](/us/stat/83/339), classified to former [section 61h–1 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s61h–1), authorized the respective leaders to appoint an executive secretary, and a clerical assistant, were abolished, see [Pub. L. 95–26, title I](/us/pl/95/26/tI), May 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 80](/us/stat/91/80), set out as a note under former [section 61h–1 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s61h–1). See, also, [section 6131 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6131).
