# §§ 828 to 852. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 649](/us/stat/80/649), 651

##### §§ 828 to 852. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 649](/us/stat/80/649), 651

Sections, [act Apr. 26, 1937, ch. 127](/us/act/1937-04-26/ch127), §§ 1–22, [50 Stat. 75–91](/us/stat/50/75-91), related to the regulation of interstate commerce of bituminous coal.

[Section 849 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s849) was amended by acts [Apr. 11, 1941, ch. 64, § 1(a)](/us/act/1941-04-11/ch64/s1/a), [55 Stat. 134](/us/stat/55/134); [Apr. 24, 1943, ch. 68](/us/act/1943-04-24/ch68), [57 Stat. 68](/us/stat/57/68); [May 21, 1943, ch. 97](/us/act/1943-05-21/ch97), [57 Stat. 82](/us/stat/57/82).

[Section 852 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s852) was added by [act Apr. 11, 1941, ch. 64, § 2](/us/act/1941-04-11/ch64/s2), [55 Stat. 134](/us/stat/55/134).
