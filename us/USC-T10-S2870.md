# [§ 2870. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title VIII, § 818(a)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tVIII/s818/a), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3751](/us/stat/134/3751)]

##### [§ 2870. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title VIII, § 818(a)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tVIII/s818/a), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3751](/us/stat/134/3751)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 116–92, div. A, title VIII, § 865(a)(1)](/us/pl/116/92/dA/tVIII/s865/a/1), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 1523](/us/stat/133/1523), related to use of qualified apprentices by military construction contractors.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of 2019 Amendment

[Pub. L. 116–92, div. A, title VIII, § 865(b)](/us/pl/116/92/dA/tVIII/s865/b), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 1524](/us/stat/133/1524), which provided that the amendments made by [section 865 of Pub. L. 116–92](/us/pl/116/92/s865) (enacting this section) would apply with respect to contracts awarded on or after 180 days after Dec. 20, 2019, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title VIII, § 818(b)(2)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tVIII/s818/b/2), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3751](/us/stat/134/3751).
