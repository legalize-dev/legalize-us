# § 4506. Exemptions

##### § 4506. Exemptions

(1) good cause exists for granting an exemption; and

(2) the safety of the vessel and those on board will not be adversely affected.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2016—[Pub. L. 114–120](/us/pl/114/120) struck out subsec. (a) designation before “The Secretary may”.

2010—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 111–281](/us/pl/111/281) struck out subsec. (b) which read as follows: “A vessel to which this chapter applies is exempt from [section 4502(b)(2)(B) of this title](/us/usc/t46/s4502/b/2/B) if it—

“(1) is less than 36 feet in length; and

“(2) is operating—

“(A) in internal waters of the United States; or

“(B) within 3 nautical miles from the baselines from which the territorial sea of the United States is measured.”

1998—Subsec. (b)(2). [Pub. L. 105–383](/us/pl/105/383) added par. (2) and struck out former par. (2) which read as follows: “is not operating on the high seas.”

1992—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 102–587](/us/pl/102/587) substituted “4502(b)(2)(B)” for “4502(b)(2)”.

#### **Executive Documents**

#### Territorial Sea of United States

For extension of territorial sea of United States, see Proc. No. 5928, set out as a note under [section 1331 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s1331), Public Lands.
