##### § 2771. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3131(u)(1)](/us/pl/112/239/dC/tXXXI/s3131/u/1), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2184](/us/stat/126/2184)

Section, [Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVII, § 4731](/us/pl/107/314/dD/tXLVII/s4731), formerly [Pub. L. 95–509, title II, § 208](/us/pl/95/509/tII/s208), Oct. 24, 1978, [92 Stat. 1779](/us/stat/92/1779); renumbered [Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLVII, § 4731](/us/pl/107/314/dD/tXLVII/s4731), and amended [Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(j)(7)](/us/pl/108/136/dC/tXXXI/s3141/j/7), Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1782](/us/stat/117/1782), provided that the Secretary was to submit to the Congress for fiscal year 1980, and for each subsequent fiscal year, a single request for authorization of appropriations for common defense and security programs.
