# § 296f. Technical assistance

##### § 296f. Technical assistance

Funds appropriated under this subchapter may be used by the Secretary to provide technical assistance in relation to any of the authorities under this subchapter.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title VIII, § 808, formerly § 807, as added [Pub. L. 105–392, title I, § 123(4)](/us/pl/105/392/tI/s123/4), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3566](/us/stat/112/3566); renumbered § 808, [Pub. L. 106–525, title IV, § 401(b)(1)](/us/pl/106/525/tIV/s401/b/1), Nov. 22, 2000, [114 Stat. 2508](/us/stat/114/2508).)*
