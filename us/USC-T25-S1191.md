# § 1191. Omitted

##### § 1191. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–259, § 1](/us/pl/91/259/s1), May 21, 1970, [84 Stat. 253](/us/stat/84/253), which provided for per capita distribution of certain judgment funds to eligible members of the Confederated Tribes and set out provisions relating to the payment of shares of deceased members and minors or incompetents, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
