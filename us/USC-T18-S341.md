# § 341. Definitions

##### § 341. Definitions

As used in this chapter, the term “common carrier” means a locomotive, a rail carrier, a sleeping car carrier, a bus transporting passengers in interstate commerce, a water common carrier, and an air common carrier.

*(Added [Pub. L. 99–570, title I, § 1971(a)](/us/pl/99/570/tI/s1971/a), Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–59](/us/stat/100/3207-59); amended [Pub. L. 100–690, title VI, § 6482(a)](/us/pl/100/690/tVI/s6482/a), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4382](/us/stat/102/4382).)*
