# § 1639s. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1639s. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this subchapter.

*([Aug. 14, 1946, ch. 966](/us/act/1946-08-14/ch966), title II, § 297E, as added [Pub. L. 115–334, title X, § 10113](/us/pl/115/334/tX/s10113), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4914](/us/stat/132/4914).)*
