##### § 2217. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–218, title I, § 107](/us/pl/103/218/tI/s107), Mar. 9, 1994, [108 Stat. 85](/us/stat/108/85)

Section, [Pub. L. 100–407, title I, § 107](/us/pl/100/407/tI/s107), Aug. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 1058](/us/stat/102/1058), directed Secretary of Education to conduct national evaluation of program of grants to States authorized by this subchapter and to report to Congress on results not later than Oct. 1, 1992.
