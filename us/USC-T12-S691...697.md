# §§ 691 to 697. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 691 to 697. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 691, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 5](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s5), [39 Stat. 364](/us/stat/39/364); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, provided for minimum amount of original capital for Federal land banks. See [section 2013 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2013).

Section 692, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 5](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s5), [39 Stat. 364](/us/stat/39/364), provided for distribution and value of stock for Federal land banks. See [section 2013 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2013).

Section 693, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 5, [39 Stat. 364](/us/stat/39/364); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), covered transfer of stock held by land bank associations. See [section 2013 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2013).

Section 694, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 5, [39 Stat. 364](/us/stat/39/364); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VIII, § 80(a), [48 Stat. 273](/us/stat/48/273); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), covered dividends for land bank stock. See [section 2013 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2013).

Section 695, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 5, [39 Stat. 364](/us/stat/39/364); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 5(a)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s5/a), [50 Stat. 704](/us/stat/50/704), covered subscription by United States for unsubscribed balance of original capital stock of Federal land banks. See section 2001 et seq. of this title.

Section 696, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 5, [39 Stat. 364](/us/stat/39/364); [Jan. 23, 1932, ch. 9, § 1](/us/act/1932-01-23/ch9/s1), [47 Stat. 12](/us/stat/47/12); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), covered retirement of original stock of Federal land banks. See [section 2051 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2051).

Section 697, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 5, [39 Stat. 364](/us/stat/39/364); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, Pub L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), set out required proportion of capital of Federal land banks that had to be held in form of quick assets. See [section 2051 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2051).
