# § 315m–2. Administration of leased lands

##### § 315m–2. Administration of leased lands

The lands leased under sections 315m–1 to 315m–4 of this title shall be administered under the provisions of the Act of June 28, 1934 ([48 Stat. 1269](/us/stat/48/1269)), as amended June 26, 1936 ([49 Stat. 1976](/us/stat/49/1976)), commonly known as the Taylor Grazing Act.

*([June 23, 1938, ch. 603, § 2](/us/act/1938-06-23/ch603/s2), [52 Stat. 1033](/us/stat/52/1033).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Act of June 28, 1934 ([48 Stat. 1269](/us/stat/48/1269)), as amended June 26, 1936 ([49 Stat. 1976](/us/stat/49/1976)), referred to in text, is [act June 28, 1934, ch. 865](/us/act/1934-06-28/ch865), [48 Stat. 1269](/us/stat/48/1269), known as the Taylor Grazing Act, which is classified principally to this subchapter (§ 315 et seq.). For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 315 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s315) and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was not enacted as a part of act June 28, 1934, known as the Taylor Grazing Act, which comprises this subchapter.
