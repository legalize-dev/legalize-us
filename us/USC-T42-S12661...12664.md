# §§ 12661 to 12664. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title I, § 1831(a)](/us/pl/111/13/tI/s1831/a), Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1578](/us/stat/123/1578)

##### §§ 12661 to 12664. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–13, title I, § 1831(a)](/us/pl/111/13/tI/s1831/a), Apr. 21, 2009, [123 Stat. 1578](/us/stat/123/1578)

Section 12661, [Pub. L. 101–610, title III, § 302](/us/pl/101/610/tIII/s302), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3180](/us/stat/104/3180), related to findings and purpose of the Points of Light Foundation.

Section 12662, [Pub. L. 101–610, title III, § 303](/us/pl/101/610/tIII/s303), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3181](/us/stat/104/3181); [Pub. L. 103–82, title IV, § 402(b)(4)](/us/pl/103/82/tIV/s402/b/4), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 919](/us/stat/107/919), granted Presidential authority to designate for funding a private, nonprofit organization known as the Points of Light Foundation.

Section 12663, [Pub. L. 101–610, title III, § 304](/us/pl/101/610/tIII/s304), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3181](/us/stat/104/3181), related to grants to the Foundation.

Section 12664, [Pub. L. 101–610, title III, § 305](/us/pl/101/610/tIII/s305), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3181](/us/stat/104/3181), related to eligibility of the Foundation for grants.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 2009, see [section 6101(a) of Pub. L. 111–13](/us/pl/111/13/s6101/a), set out as an Effective Date of 2009 Amendment note under [section 4950 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4950).
