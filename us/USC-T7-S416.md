# § 416. Omitted

##### § 416. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, from [act May 11, 1922, ch. 185](/us/act/1922-05-11/ch185), [42 Stat. 517](/us/stat/42/517), known as the Agriculture Department Appropriation Act, 1923, authorized awarding a contract for up to five years for distribution of plants, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
