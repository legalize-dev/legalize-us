# § 3108. Prohibition against circulation privilege

##### § 3108. Prohibition against circulation privilege

An obligation issued under sections 3102–3104(a)(1) and 3105–3107 of this title may not bear the circulation privilege.

*([Pub. L. 97–258](/us/pl/97/258), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 942](/us/stat/96/942).)*

The reference in 31:758 to certificates authorized under 31:757 is omitted because the authority under 31:757 was ended by section 2(b)(3) of the Public Debt Act of 1941 (ch. 7, [55 Stat. 7](/us/stat/55/7)).
