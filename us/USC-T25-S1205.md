# § 1205. Omitted

##### § 1205. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–283, § 5](/us/pl/91/283/s5), June 19, 1970, [84 Stat. 313](/us/stat/84/313), which provided for certain moneys withheld from per capita distribution to be credited to a joint account for expenditure for official salaries and expenses of Fort Peck Tribes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
