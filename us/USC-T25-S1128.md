# § 1128. Omitted

##### § 1128. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–309, § 7](/us/pl/92/309/s7), June 2, 1972, [86 Stat. 200](/us/stat/86/200), which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
