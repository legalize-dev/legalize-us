# § 60554. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(d)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s504/d), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5233](/us/stat/132/5233)

##### § 60554. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(d)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s504/d), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5233](/us/stat/132/5233)

Section, [Pub. L. 110–199, title II, § 244](/us/pl/110/199/tII/s244), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 692](/us/stat/122/692), related to study of effectiveness of depot naltrexone for heroin addiction.

Section was formerly classified to [section 17554 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s17554), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
