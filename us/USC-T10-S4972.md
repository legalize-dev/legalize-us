# § 4972. Transferability

##### § 4972. Transferability

A guarantee issued under this subchapter shall be fully and freely transferable.

*(Added [Pub. L. 104–106, div. A, title XIII, § 1321(a)(1)](/us/pl/104/106/dA/tXIII/s1321/a/1), Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 476](/us/stat/110/476), § 2540a; renumbered § 4972, [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1873(b)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1873/b), (c)(1), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4290](/us/stat/134/4290).)*
