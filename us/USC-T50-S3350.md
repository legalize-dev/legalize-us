# § 3350. Maximum amount charged for declassification reviews

##### § 3350. Maximum amount charged for declassification reviews

(1) may not charge the person reproduction fees in excess of the amount of fees that the head would charge the person for reproduction required in the course of processing a request for information under [section 552 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s552) (commonly referred to as the “Freedom of Information Act”); and

(2) may waive or reduce any processing fees in the same manner as the head waives or reduces fees under such section 552.

*([Pub. L. 115–31, div. N, title III, § 313](/us/pl/115/31/dN/tIII/s313), May 5, 2017, [131 Stat. 816](/us/stat/131/816).)*
