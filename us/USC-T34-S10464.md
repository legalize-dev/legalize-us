# § 10464. Regulations or guidelines

##### § 10464. Regulations or guidelines

Not later than 120 days after September 13, 1994, the Attorney General shall publish proposed regulations or guidelines implementing this subchapter. Not later than 180 days after September 13, 1994, the Attorney General shall publish final regulations or guidelines implementing this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2105](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2105), formerly § 2104, as added [Pub. L. 103–322, title IV, § 40231(a)(3)](/us/pl/103/322/tIV/s40231/a/3), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1933](/us/stat/108/1933); renumbered § 2105, [Pub. L. 117–103, div. W, title XV, § 1506(1)](/us/pl/117/103/dW/tXV/s1506/1), Mar. 15, 2022, [136 Stat. 956](/us/stat/136/956).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3796hh–3 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796hh–3), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 2105 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s2105) was renumbered section 2106 and is classified to [section 10465 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10465).

Amendment by [Pub. L. 117–103](/us/pl/117/103) not effective until Oct. 1 of the first fiscal year beginning after Mar. 15, 2022, see [section 4(a) of div. W of Pub. L. 117–103](/us/pl/117/103/dW/s4/a), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 6851 of Title 15](/us/usc/t15/s6851), Commerce and Trade.
