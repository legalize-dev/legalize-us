# § 54. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title XV, § 1532(b)(1)](/us/pl/101/510/dA/tXV/s1532/b/1), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1733](/us/stat/104/1733)

##### § 54. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–510, div. A, title XV, § 1532(b)(1)](/us/pl/101/510/dA/tXV/s1532/b/1), Nov. 5, 1990, [104 Stat. 1733](/us/stat/104/1733)

Section, R.S. § 4824, related to coverage of inmates by Articles of War. See section 801 et seq. of Title 10, Armed Forces.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective one year after Nov. 5, 1990, see [section 1541(a) of Pub. L. 101–510](/us/pl/101/510/s1541/a), formerly set out as an Effective Date note under [section 401 of this title](/us/usc/t24/s401).
