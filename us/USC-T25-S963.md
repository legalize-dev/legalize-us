# § 963. Omitted

##### § 963. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–235, § 3](/us/pl/87/235/s3), Sept. 14, 1961, [75 Stat. 508](/us/stat/75/508), which related to per capita distribution to tribal members out of funds appropriated to pay a judgment dated Feb. 11, 1960, and provided for tax exemption of the funds so distributed, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
