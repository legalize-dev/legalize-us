# § 60555. Authorization of appropriations for research

##### § 60555. Authorization of appropriations for research

There are authorized to be appropriated to the Attorney General to carry out sections 60551, 60552, and 60553 of this title, $5,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, and 2023.

*([Pub. L. 110–199, title II, § 245](/us/pl/110/199/tII/s245), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 692](/us/stat/122/692); [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(e)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s504/e), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5233](/us/stat/132/5233).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 17555 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s17555), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–391](/us/pl/115/391) substituted “and 60553 of this title, $5,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, and 2023” for “60553, and 60554 of this title, $10,000,000 for each of the fiscal years 2009 and 2010”.
