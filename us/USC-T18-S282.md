##### [§ 282. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–849, § 2](/us/pl/87/849/s2), Oct. 23, 1962, [76 Stat. 1126](/us/stat/76/1126)]

Section, [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645), [62 Stat. 697](/us/stat/62/697), related to practice in Court of Claims by Members of Congress. Section was supplanted by [section 204 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s204).

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 90 days after Oct. 23, 1962, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 87–849](/us/pl/87/849/s4), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 201 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s201).
