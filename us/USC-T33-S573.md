# § 573. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 656](/us/stat/80/656)

##### § 573. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 656](/us/stat/80/656)

Section, [act May 17, 1950, ch. 188, title I, § 111](/us/act/1950-05-17/ch188/tI/s111), [64 Stat. 170](/us/stat/64/170), provided that section 607 of the Federal Employees Pay Act of 1945, as amended [former [5 U.S.C. 947](/us/usc/t5/s947)], should not be construed to prevent employment of additional personnel.
