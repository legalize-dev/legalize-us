# § 1300j–7a. Omitted

##### § 1300j–7a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–323, § 9](/us/pl/103/323/s9), as added [Pub. L. 103–435, § 20(2)](/us/pl/103/435/s20/2), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4574](/us/stat/108/4574); amended [Pub. L. 104–109, § 1](/us/pl/104/109/s1), Feb. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 763](/us/stat/110/763), which required submission of a list of members of the band as of Sept. 21, 1994, to the Secretary of the Interior not later than 120 days after such date, and submission of a membership roll to the Secretary not later than 18 months after such date, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
