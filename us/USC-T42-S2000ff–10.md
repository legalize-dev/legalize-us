# § 2000ff–10. Regulations

##### § 2000ff–10. Regulations

Not later than 1 year after May 21, 2008, the Commission shall issue final regulations to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 110–233, title II, § 211](/us/pl/110/233/tII/s211), May 21, 2008, [122 Stat. 920](/us/stat/122/920).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective 18 months after May 21, 2008, see [section 213 of Pub. L. 110–233](/us/pl/110/233/s213), set out as a note under [section 2000ff of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2000ff).
