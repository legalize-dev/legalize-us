# § 7012. Repealed. [Pub. L. 114–95, title III, § 3001(5)(B)](/us/pl/114/95/tIII/s3001/5/B), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1953](/us/stat/129/1953)

##### § 7012. Repealed. [Pub. L. 114–95, title III, § 3001(5)(B)](/us/pl/114/95/tIII/s3001/5/B), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1953](/us/stat/129/1953)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title III, § 3302](/us/pl/89/10/tIII/s3302), as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title III, § 301](/us/pl/107/110/tIII/s301), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1732](/us/stat/115/1732), related to parental notification.

A prior [section 3302 of Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10/s3302) was classified to [section 6922 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6922), prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 107–110](/us/pl/107/110).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Dec. 10, 2015, except with respect to certain noncompetitive programs and competitive programs, see [section 5 of Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95/s5), set out as an Effective Date of 2015 Amendment note under [section 6301 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6301).
