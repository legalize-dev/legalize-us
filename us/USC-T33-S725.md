##### § 725. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8512(a)(1), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4759](/us/stat/134/4759)

Section, R.S. § 4680; acts [June 17, 1910, ch. 301](/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301), §§ 6, 13, [36 Stat. 538](/us/stat/36/538), 539; [June 20, 1918, ch. 103, § 7](/us/act/1918-06-20/ch103/s7), [40 Stat. 608](/us/stat/40/608); 1939 Reorg. #### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

R.S. § 4680 derived from [act Aug. 31, 1852, ch. 112, § 17](/us/act/1852-08-31/ch112/s17), [10 Stat. 120](/us/stat/10/120).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Savings

[Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8512(b)(1), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4759](/us/stat/134/4759), provided that: “Notwithstanding any repeals made by this section [repealing this section and sections 717, 727, 728, 730a, 743, 745a, 747b, 748, 754a, 763–1, 763–2, 763a–1, and 770 to 772 of this title], any individual beneficiary currently receiving payments under the authority of any provisions repealed in this section shall continue to receive such benefits.”
