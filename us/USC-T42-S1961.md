# § 1961. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–467, title IV, § 410(a)](/us/pl/95/467/tIV/s410/a), Oct. 17, 1978, [92 Stat. 1316](/us/stat/92/1316)

##### § 1961. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–467, title IV, § 410(a)](/us/pl/95/467/tIV/s410/a), Oct. 17, 1978, [92 Stat. 1316](/us/stat/92/1316)

Section, [Pub. L. 88–379, § 1(b)](/us/pl/88/379/s1/b), July 17, 1964, [78 Stat. 329](/us/stat/78/329), set out Congressional purpose in enacting water resources research program legislation. See [section 7802 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s7802).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 88–379, § 1(a)](/us/pl/88/379/s1/a), July 17, 1964, [78 Stat. 329](/us/stat/78/329), provided that [Pub. L. 88–379](/us/pl/88/379), which enacted sections 1961 to 1961c–8, may be cited as the “Water Resources Research Act of 1964”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 95–467, title IV, § 410(a)](/us/pl/95/467/tIV/s410/a), Oct. 17, 1978, [92 Stat. 1316](/us/stat/92/1316).
