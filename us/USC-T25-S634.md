# § 634. Omitted

##### § 634. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92, § 4](/us/act/1950-04-19/ch92/s4), [64 Stat. 45](/us/stat/64/45), which related to loans to Tribes or individual members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
