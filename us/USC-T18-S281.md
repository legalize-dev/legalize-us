# [§ 281. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–106, div. D, title XLIII, § 4304(b)(3)](/us/pl/104/106/dD/tXLIII/s4304/b/3), Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 664](/us/stat/110/664); [Pub. L. 104–294, title VI, § 602(d)](/us/pl/104/294/tVI/s602/d), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3503](/us/stat/110/3503)]

##### [§ 281. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–106, div. D, title XLIII, § 4304(b)(3)](/us/pl/104/106/dD/tXLIII/s4304/b/3), Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 664](/us/stat/110/664); [Pub. L. 104–294, title VI, § 602(d)](/us/pl/104/294/tVI/s602/d), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3503](/us/stat/110/3503)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title VIII, § 822(b)(1)](/us/pl/100/180/dA/tVIII/s822/b/1), Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1132](/us/stat/101/1132), related to restrictions on retired military officers regarding certain matters affecting the Government.

[Pub. L. 104–294, title VI, § 602(d)](/us/pl/104/294/tVI/s602/d), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3503](/us/stat/110/3503), which repealed this section, was repealed by [Pub. L. 107–273, div. B, title IV, § 4002(c)(1)](/us/pl/107/273/dB/tIV/s4002/c/1), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1808](/us/stat/116/1808), effective Oct. 11, 1996.

A prior section 281, acts [June 25, 1948, ch. 645](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645), [62 Stat. 697](/us/stat/62/697); [May 24, 1949, ch. 139, § 6](/us/act/1949-05-24/ch139/s6), [63 Stat. 90](/us/stat/63/90), which related to compensation to Members of Congress, officers and others in matters affecting the Government, was repealed by [Pub. L. 87–849](/us/pl/87/849), §§ 2, 4, Oct. 23, 1962, [76 Stat. 1126](/us/stat/76/1126), eff. 90 days after Oct. 23, 1962, which repeal continued limited applicability to retired officers of the Armed Forces of the United States. [Pub. L. 100–180, div. A, title VIII, § 822(a)](/us/pl/100/180/dA/tVIII/s822/a), Dec. 4, 1987, [101 Stat. 1132](/us/stat/101/1132), repealed such prior section 281 to the extent that it had not been repealed by [section 2 of Pub. L. 87–849](/us/pl/87/849/s2). See [section 203 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s203).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

For effective date and applicability of repeal by [Pub. L. 104–106](/us/pl/104/106), see [section 4401 of Pub. L. 104–106](/us/pl/104/106/s4401), set out as an Effective Date of 1996 Amendment note under [section 2220 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s2220), Armed Forces.
