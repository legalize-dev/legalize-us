# § 615llll–6. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–516, § 12(a)](/us/pl/100/516/s12/a), Oct. 24, 1988, [102 Stat. 2572](/us/stat/102/2572)

##### § 615llll–6. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–516, § 12(a)](/us/pl/100/516/s12/a), Oct. 24, 1988, [102 Stat. 2572](/us/stat/102/2572)

Section, [Pub. L. 94–228, title IV, § 407](/us/pl/94/228/tIV/s407), Mar. 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 209](/us/stat/90/209), authorized appropriations for Pollock-Herreid Unit. [Section 12(a) of Pub. L. 100–516](/us/pl/100/516/s12/a) provided in part that: “The Pollock-Herreid Unit shall remain an authorized feature of the Pick-Sloan Missouri Basin Program.”
