# § 3156. Mission readiness exercises

##### § 3156. Mission readiness exercises

The joint policy required by [section 3151 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s3151) shall also provide for the incorporation of contractors and contract operations in mission readiness exercises for operations that will include contracting and contractor support.

*(Added and amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1810(a)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1810/a), (b)(6), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4162](/us/stat/134/4162), 4164.)*
