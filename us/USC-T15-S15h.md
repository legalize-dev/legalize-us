# § 15h. Applicability of parens patriae actions

##### § 15h. Applicability of parens patriae actions

Sections 15c, 15d, 15e, 15f, and 15g of this title shall apply in any State, unless such State provides by law for its nonapplicability in such State.

*([Oct. 15, 1914, ch. 323, § 4H](/us/act/1914-10-15/ch323/s4H), as added [Pub. L. 94–435, title III, § 301](/us/pl/94/435/tIII/s301), Sept. 30, 1976, [90 Stat. 1396](/us/stat/90/1396).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Injuries sustained prior to Sept. 30, 1976, not covered by this section, see [section 304 of Pub. L. 94–435](/us/pl/94/435/s304), set out as a note under [section 15c of this title](/us/usc/t15/s15c).
