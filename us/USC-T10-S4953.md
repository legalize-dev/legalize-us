# § 4953. Regulations

##### § 4953. Regulations

The Secretary of Defense shall prescribe regulations to carry out this chapter, and shall consult with an association recognized under section 4954(f) 11 See References in Text note below. regarding any revisions to such regulations.

*(Added and amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1872(a)(1)(B)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1872/a/1/B), (4), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4287](/us/stat/134/4287), 4288; [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title XVII, § 1701(m)(1)(C)](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tXVII/s1701/m/1/C), (3), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 2144](/us/stat/135/2144), 2145; [Pub. L. 117–263, div.
