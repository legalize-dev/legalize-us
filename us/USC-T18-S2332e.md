# § 2332e. Requests for military assistance to enforce prohibition in certain emergencies

##### § 2332e. Requests for military assistance to enforce prohibition in certain emergencies

The Attorney General may request the Secretary of Defense to provide assistance under [section 382 of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s382) 11 See References in Text note below. in support of Department of Justice activities relating to the enforcement of [section 2332a of this title](/us/usc/t18/s2332a) during an emergency situation involving a weapon of mass destruction. The authority to make such a request may be exercised by another official of the Department of Justice in accordance with [section 382(f)(2) of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s382/f/2).1

*(Added [Pub. L. 104–201, title XIV, § 1416(c)(2)(A)](/us/pl/104/201/tXIV/s1416/c/2/A), Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2723](/us/stat/110/2723), § 2332d; renumbered § 2332e, [Pub. L. 104–294, title VI, § 605(q)](/us/pl/104/294/tVI/s605/q), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3510](/us/stat/110/3510); amended [Pub. L. 107–56, title I, § 104](/us/pl/107/56/tI/s104), Oct. 26, 2001, [115 Stat. 277](/us/stat/115/277).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 382 of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s382), referred to in text, was renumbered [section 282 of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s282), Armed Forces, by [Pub. L. 114–328, div. A, title XII, § 1241(a)(2)](/us/pl/114/328/dA/tXII/s1241/a/2), Dec. 23, 2016, [130 Stat. 2497](/us/stat/130/2497).

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 104–201, § 1416(c)(2)(A)](/us/pl/104/201/s1416/c/2/A), which directed amendment of the chapter 133B of this title that relates to terrorism by adding this section, was executed by adding this section to this chapter to reflect the probable intent of Congress. This title does not contain a chapter 133B.

#### Amendments

2001—[Pub. L. 107–56](/us/pl/107/56) substituted “2332a of this title” for “2332c of this title” and struck out “chemical” before “weapon of”.

1996—[Pub. L. 104–294](/us/pl/104/294) renumbered [section 2332d of this title](/us/usc/t18/s2332d), relating to requests for military assistance to enforce prohibition in certain emergencies, as this section.
