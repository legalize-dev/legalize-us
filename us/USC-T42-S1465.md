##### § 1465. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–646, title II, § 220(a)(5)](/us/pl/91/646/tII/s220/a/5), Jan. 2, 1971, [84 Stat. 1903](/us/stat/84/1903)

Section, [act July 15, 1949, ch. 338, title I, § 114](/us/act/1949-07-15/ch338/tI/s114), as added Sept. 2, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–560, title III, § 310(a)](/us/pl/88/560/tIII/s310/a), [78 Stat. 788](/us/stat/78/788); amended Aug. 10, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–117, title I, § 101(i)](/us/pl/89/117/tI/s101/i), title IV, § 404(b), (c)(1), [79 Stat. 453](/us/stat/79/453), 486; May 25, 1967, [Pub. (a), financial assistance to displaced individuals, families, businesses, and nonprofit organizations; subsec. (b), payments to business concerns or nonprofit organizations, considerations, and maximum amounts; subsec. (c), payments to individuals and families, considerations, computation of amount, maximum amounts, and restrictions; subsec. (d), payments to individuals, families, business concerns, and nonprofit organizations for recording fees, transfer taxes, incidental expenses, penalty costs, and pro rata taxes; and subsec. (e), rules and regulations, finality of administrative decisions, and promptness of payments. See chapter 61 (section 4601 et seq.) of this title.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal not applicable to any State so long as sections 4630 and 4655 of this title are not applicable in such State; but such sections completely applicable to all States after July 1, 1972, but until such date applicable to a State to extent the State is able under its laws to comply with such sections, see [section 221 of Pub. L. 91–646](/us/pl/91/646/s221), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 4601 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4601).

#### Savings Provision

Any rights or liabilities existing under provisions repealed by [section 220(a) of Pub. L. 91–646](/us/pl/91/646/s220/a) as not affected by such repeal, see [section 220(b) of Pub. L. 91–646](/us/pl/91/646/s220/b), set out as a note under [section 4621 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4621).
