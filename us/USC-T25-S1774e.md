# § 1774e. Omitted

##### § 1774e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–503, § 7](/us/pl/101/503/s7), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1296](/us/stat/104/1296), which related to conditions precedent to payment of United States and State funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
