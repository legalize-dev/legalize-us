# § 572. Omitted

##### § 572. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 27, 1939, ch. 387, § 2](/us/act/1939-07-27/ch387/s2), [53 Stat. 1128](/us/stat/53/1128), which authorized payments to individual tribe members, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
