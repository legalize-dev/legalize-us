# § 423g. Rules and regulations

##### § 423g. Rules and regulations

The Secretary of the Interior, subject to the approval of the President, shall have the power to make and shall make all needful rules and regulations for the care of the battlefield, and for the establishment and marking of lines of battle and other historical features of the battlefield.

*([July 3, 1926, ch. 746, § 8](/us/act/1926-07-03/ch746/s8), [44 Stat. 823](/us/stat/44/823); Ex. Ord. No. 6166, § 2, June 10, 1933; Ex. Ord. No. 6228, § 1, July 28, 1933; [Pub. L. 87–603, § 1](/us/pl/87/603/s1), Aug. 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 403](/us/stat/76/403).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Change of Name

“Battlefield” substituted in text for “park” in view of redesignation of Petersburg National Military Park as Petersburg National Military Battlefield by [Pub. L. 87–603](/us/pl/87/603). See [section 423h–1 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s423h–1).
