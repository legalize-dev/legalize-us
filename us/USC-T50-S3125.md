# § 3125. Providing information to Congress

##### § 3125. Providing information to Congress

Nothing in this subchapter may be construed as authority to withhold information from the Congress or from a committee of either House of Congress.

*([July 26, 1947, ch. 343](/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343), title VI, § 604, formerly § 605, as added [Pub. L. 97–200, § 2(a)](/us/pl/97/200/s2/a), June 23, 1982, [96 Stat. 123](/us/stat/96/123); renumbered § 604, [Pub. L. 112–277, title III, § 310(a)(4)(B)](/us/pl/112/277/tIII/s310/a/4/B), Jan. 14, 2013, [126 Stat. 2475](/us/stat/126/2475).)*
