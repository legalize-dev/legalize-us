# § 252. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

##### § 252. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862)

Section, [act May 18, 1916, ch. 125, § 1](/us/act/1916-05-18/ch125/s1), [39 Stat. 124](/us/stat/39/124), related to application of former sections 246 and 251 of this title to search and seizure and setting up a distillery. See [section 3113 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3113), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 1, 1948, see [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 20](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s20), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862).
