# § 3912. GAO report

##### § 3912. GAO report

Not later than 24 months after the effective date of this chapter, the Comptroller General of the United States shall transmit a report to the Congress on the effectiveness of the provisions of this chapter in preventing the importation or exportation of rough diamonds that is prohibited under [section 3903 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s3903). The Comptroller General shall include in the report any recommendations on any modifications to this chapter that may be necessary.

*([Pub. L. 108–19, § 13](/us/pl/108/19/s13), Apr. 25, 2003, [117 Stat. 636](/us/stat/117/636).)*
