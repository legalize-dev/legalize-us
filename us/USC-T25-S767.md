# § 767. Omitted

##### § 767. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–227, § 8](/us/pl/96/227/s8), Apr. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 322](/us/stat/94/322), which barred legal claims for lands lost through tax or other sales since Sept. 1, 1954, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
