# §§ 11 to 14. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–33, title VI, § 6201](/us/pl/105/33/tVI/s6201), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 653](/us/stat/111/653)

##### §§ 11 to 14. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–33, title VI, § 6201](/us/pl/105/33/tVI/s6201), Aug. 5, 1997, [111 Stat. 653](/us/stat/111/653)

Section 11, acts [Feb. 23, 1917, ch. 114, § 1](/us/act/1917-02-23/ch114/s1), [39 Stat. 929](/us/stat/39/929); Ex. Ord. No. 6166, § 15, June 10, 1933; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 204, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424); 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 1, §§ 5, 8, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631](/us/stat/67/631); Oct. 17, 1979, [Pub. L. 96–88, title III, § 301](/us/pl/96/88/tIII/s301), title V, § 507, [93 Stat. 677](/us/stat/93/677), 692, related to annual appropriations for payments to States for teaching agricultural, trade, home economics, and industrial subjects.

Section 12, acts [Feb. 23, 1917, ch. 114, § 2](/us/act/1917-02-23/ch114/s2), [39 Stat. 930](/us/stat/39/930); July 12, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–624, § 14(b)(2)](/us/pl/86/624/s14/b/2), [74 Stat. 414](/us/stat/74/414), appropriated money for salaries of teachers, supervisors, and directors of agricultural subjects.

Section 13, [act Feb. 23, 1917, ch. 114, § 3](/us/act/1917-02-23/ch114/s3), [39 Stat. 930](/us/stat/39/930), appropriated money for salaries of teachers of trade, home economics, and industrial subjects.

Section 14, acts [Feb. 23, 1917, ch. 114, § 4](/us/act/1917-02-23/ch114/s4), [39 Stat. 931](/us/stat/39/931); June 25, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–70, § 18(b)(1)](/us/pl/86/70/s18/b/1), [73 Stat. 144](/us/stat/73/144); July 12, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–624, § 14(b)(2)](/us/pl/86/624/s14/b/2), [74 Stat. 414](/us/stat/74/414), appropriated money for preparation of teachers, supervisors, and directors of agricultural subjects and teachers of trade, industrial, and home economics.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Act June 8, 1936, ch. 541, § 2](/us/act/1936-06-08/ch541/s2), [49 Stat. 1488](/us/stat/49/1488), as amended by [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 725, title I, § 101](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch725/tI/s101), [60 Stat. 775](/us/stat/60/775), provided that the [act of Feb. 23, 1917, ch. 114](/us/act/1917-02-23/ch114), [39 Stat. 929](/us/stat/39/929) (enacting sections 11 to 15 and 16 to 28 of this title), was to be known as the Smith-Hughes Vocational Education Act. The 1917 act was also known as the Vocational Education Act of 1917.

The [act of June 8, 1936, ch. 541](/us/act/1936-06-08/ch541), [49 Stat. 1488](/us/stat/49/1488) (enacting sections 15i to 15ggg of this title), which was repealed by [Pub. L. 90–576, title I, § 103](/us/pl/90/576/tI/s103), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1091](/us/stat/82/1091), was known as the Vocational Education Act of 1946.

[Pub. L. 87–22, § 1](/us/pl/87/22/s1), Apr. 24, 1961, [75 Stat. 44](/us/stat/75/44), provided that [Pub. L. 87–22](/us/pl/87/22) (amending sections 15aa, 15bb, 15cc, and 15jj of this title) could be cited as “The Practical Nurse Training Extension Act of 1961”.
