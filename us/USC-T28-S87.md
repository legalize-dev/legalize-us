# § 87. Delaware

##### § 87. Delaware

Delaware constitutes one judicial district.

Court shall be held at Wilmington.

*([June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 875](/us/stat/62/875).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 148 ([Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231, § 75](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231/s75), [36 Stat. 1108](/us/stat/36/1108)).

Minor changes in phraseology were made.
