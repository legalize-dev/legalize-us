##### § 1300g–3. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–89, title I, § 104](/us/pl/100/89/tI/s104), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 667](/us/stat/101/667), which related to State and tribal authority, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
