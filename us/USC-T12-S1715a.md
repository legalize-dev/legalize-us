# § 1715a. Repealed. [June 3, 1939, ch. 175, § 13](/us/act/1939-06-03/ch175/s13), [53 Stat. 807](/us/stat/53/807)

##### § 1715a. Repealed. [June 3, 1939, ch. 175, § 13](/us/act/1939-06-03/ch175/s13), [53 Stat. 807](/us/stat/53/807)

Section, [act June 27, 1934, ch. 847, title II, § 210](/us/act/1934-06-27/ch847/tII/s210), as added by [act Feb. 3, 1938, ch. 13, § 3](/us/act/1938-02-03/ch13/s3), [52 Stat. 22](/us/stat/52/22), related to additional housing insurance.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Applications Prior to Repeal

[Act June 3, 1939, ch. 175, § 13](/us/act/1939-06-03/ch175/s13), [53 Stat. 807](/us/stat/53/807), which repealed this section, also provided: “That the Administrator is authorized to insure under said section [this section] any mortgage for the insurance of which an application has been filed with him prior to the effective date of this act [June 3, 1939].”
