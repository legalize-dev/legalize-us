# § 45304. Maximum fees for private person services

##### § 45304. Maximum fees for private person services

The Administrator of the Federal Aviation Administration may establish maximum fees that private persons may charge for services performed under a delegation to the person under [section 44702(d) of this title](/us/usc/t49/s44702/d).

*([Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(e)](/us/pl/103/272/s1/e), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1225](/us/stat/108/1225), § 45303; renumbered § 45304, [Pub. L. 104–264, title II, § 276(a)(1)](/us/pl/104/264/tII/s276/a/1), Oct. 9, 1996, [110 Stat. 3247](/us/stat/110/3247).)*

In this section, the word “Administrator” in section 314(a) of the Federal Aviation Act of 1958 ([Public Law 85–726](/us/pl/85/726), [72 Stat. 754](/us/stat/72/754)) is retained on authority of 49:106(g). The words “services performed under a delegation to the person under [section 44702(d) of this title](/us/usc/t49/s44702/d)” are substituted for “their services” because of the restatement.
