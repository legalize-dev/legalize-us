# § 1300b–3. Omitted

##### § 1300b–3. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–467, § 4](/us/pl/92/467/s4), Oct. 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 781](/us/stat/86/781), which related to payments to minors and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
