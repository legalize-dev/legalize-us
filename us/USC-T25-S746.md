# § 746. Omitted

##### § 746. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1954, ch. 1207, § 6](/us/act/1954-09-01/ch1207/s6), [68 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/68/1101), which related to transfer of funds or other personal property held in trust by the United States, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
