# § 12655a. Limitation on purchase of capital equipment

##### § 12655a. Limitation on purchase of capital equipment

Not to exceed 10 percent of the amount of assistance made available to a program agency under this division shall be used for the purchase of major capital equipment.

*([Pub. L. 101–610, title I, § 199B](/us/pl/101/610/tI/s199B), formerly § 122, Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 3140](/us/stat/104/3140); [Pub. L. 102–10, § 5(2)](/us/pl/102/10/s5/2), Mar. 12, 1991, [105 Stat. 30](/us/stat/105/30); renumbered § 199B and amended [Pub. L. 103–82, title I, § 101(a)](/us/pl/103/82/tI/s101/a), (e)(3), Sept. 21, 1993, [107 Stat. 788](/us/stat/107/788), 815.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 12542 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s12542) prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 103–82, § 101(a)](/us/pl/103/82/s101/a).

#### Amendments

1993—[Pub. L. 103–82, § 101(e)(3)](/us/pl/103/82/s101/e/3), amended section generally, substituting provisions relating to limitation on purchase of capital equipment for provisions relating to allocation of funds.

1991—Subsec. (e). [Pub. L. 102–10](/us/pl/102/10) inserted “service” after “youth”.
