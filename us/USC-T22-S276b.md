# § 276b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–45, § 4(d)(4)](/us/pl/95/45/s4/d/4), June 15, 1977, [91 Stat. 223](/us/stat/91/223)

##### § 276b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–45, § 4(d)(4)](/us/pl/95/45/s4/d/4), June 15, 1977, [91 Stat. 223](/us/stat/91/223)

Section, [act Aug. 25, 1937, ch. 757](/us/act/1937-08-25/ch757), [50 Stat. 770](/us/stat/50/770), provided that, on and after Aug. 25, 1937, the certificate of the president and executive secretary of the American Group of the Interparliamentary Union be final and conclusive upon the accounting officers in the auditing of all accounts of the Group.
