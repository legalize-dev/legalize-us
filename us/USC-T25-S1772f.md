# § 1772f. Omitted

##### § 1772f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–228, § 8](/us/pl/100/228/s8), Dec. 31, 1987, [101 Stat. 1561](/us/stat/101/1561), which related to judicial review of provisions of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
