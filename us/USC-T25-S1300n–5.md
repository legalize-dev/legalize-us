# § 1300n–5. Omitted

##### § 1300n–5. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 106–568, title XIV, § 1407](/us/pl/106/568/tXIV/s1407), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2941](/us/stat/114/2941), which provided for governance of the tribe by an Interim Tribal Council until ratification of a final tribal constitution, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
