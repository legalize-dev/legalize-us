# § 1253. Omitted

##### § 1253. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–253, § 3](/us/pl/92/253/s3), Mar. 17, 1972, [86 Stat. 64](/us/stat/86/64), which related to payments to minors and persons under a legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
