# § 6013. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 6013. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There are authorized to be appropriated for each fiscal year such sums as are necessary to carry out this chapter.

(b) Administrative expenses Funds appropriated to carry out this chapter shall not be available for payment of the expenses or expenditures of the Board in administering any provision of any plan issued under this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 101–624, title XIX, § 1918](/us/pl/101/624/tXIX/s1918), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3854](/us/stat/104/3854).)*
