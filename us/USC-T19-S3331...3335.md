# §§ 3331 to 3335. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78)

##### §§ 3331 to 3335. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78)

Section 3331, [Pub. L. 103–182, title II, § 201](/us/pl/103/182/tII/s201), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2068](/us/stat/107/2068); [Pub. L. 104–188, title I, § 1954(a)(5)](/us/pl/104/188/tI/s1954/a/5), Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1927](/us/stat/110/1927), provided for various tariff modifications.

Section 3332, [Pub. L. 103–182, title II, § 202](/us/pl/103/182/tII/s202), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2069](/us/stat/107/2069); [Pub. L. 104–295, § 21(a)(2)](/us/pl/104/295/s21/a/2), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3529](/us/stat/110/3529); [Pub. L. 105–206, title V, § 5003(b)(4)](/us/pl/105/206/tV/s5003/b/4), July 22, 1998, [112 Stat. 790](/us/stat/112/790), related to rules of origin for goods.

Section 3333, [Pub. L. 103–182, title II, § 203](/us/pl/103/182/tII/s203), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2086](/us/stat/107/2086); [Pub. L. 116–113, title V, § 501(b)(1)](/us/pl/116/113/tV/s501/b/1), (2), (d)(1)–(3), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 67](/us/stat/134/67), 68, consisted of subsecs. (a) to (e) relating to drawback. Subsecs. (b) and (c) had amended sections 81c, 1311 to 1313, and 1562 of this title. Subsecs. (a), (d), and (e) were transferred to [section 4534 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4534) by [Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113) prior to repeal of section 3333.

Section 3334, [Pub. L. 103–182, title II, § 210](/us/pl/103/182/tII/s210), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2099](/us/stat/107/2099), prohibited drawback for television picture tubes.

Section 3335, [Pub. L. 103–182, title II, § 211](/us/pl/103/182/tII/s211), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2099](/us/stat/107/2099), related to monitoring of television and picture tube imports and reports on results.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the date on which the USMCA entered into force (July 1, 2020), see [section 601 of Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113/s601), set out as a note under former [section 3301 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s3301).

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 103–182, title II, § 213](/us/pl/103/182/tII/s213), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2099](/us/stat/107/2099), which set out various effective dates for provisions in title II of [Pub. L. 103–182](/us/pl/103/182), was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78), effective on the date the USMCA entered into force (July 1, 2020).
