# § 1293. Omitted

##### § 1293. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–456, § 3](/us/pl/92/456/s3), Oct. 3, 1972, [86 Stat. 762](/us/stat/86/762), which related to applications for enrollment in tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
