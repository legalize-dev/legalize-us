# § 8236a. Technical assistance

##### § 8236a. Technical assistance

Not later than 2 years after October 24, 1992, the Secretary shall establish a program to provide technical assistance to State and local organizations to encourage the adoption of and use of residential energy efficiency rating systems consistent with the voluntary guidelines issued under [section 8236 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s8236).
