# § 5605. Contracts for transportation of mail by vessel

##### § 5605. Contracts for transportation of mail by vessel

The Postal Service may contract for the transportation of mail by vessel without advertising for bids.

*([Pub. L. 91–375](/us/pl/91/375), Aug. 12, 1970, [84 Stat. 773](/us/stat/84/773); [Pub. L. 109–435, title X, § 1002(b)(3)](/us/pl/109/435/tX/s1002/b/3), Dec. 20, 2006, [120 Stat. 3255](/us/stat/120/3255).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2006—[Pub. L. 109–438](/us/pl/109/438) struck out “for periods of not in excess of 4 years” before period at end.
