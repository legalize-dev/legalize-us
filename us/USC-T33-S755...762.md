# §§ 755 to 762. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20), [63 Stat. 561](/us/stat/63/561)

##### §§ 755 to 762. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20), [63 Stat. 561](/us/stat/63/561)

Section 755, [act June 16, 1880, ch. 235](/us/act/1880-06-16/ch235), [21 Stat. 263](/us/stat/21/263), related to police powers of masters of Lighthouse Service. See [section 522 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s522), Coast Guard.

Section 756, [act Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 81, § 6](/us/act/1915-03-03/ch81/s6), [38 Stat. 928](/us/stat/38/928), related to use of appropriations with Forest Service. See [section 701 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s701).

Section 757, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 417](/us/act/1916-08-29/ch417), [39 Stat. 602](/us/stat/39/602), related to cooperation with Army and Navy Departments in time of or in preparation for war. See sections 103, 501, 504, 505, 521, 701, 704, and 705 of Title 14.

Section 758, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 417](/us/act/1916-08-29/ch417), [39 Stat. 602](/us/stat/39/602), related to transfer of vessels, equipment, etc., to Navy or Army Departments in case of vocational emergency. See sections 103, 501, 504, 505, 521, 701, 704, and 705 of Title 14.

Section 759, acts [June 20, 1906, ch. 3447, § 3](/us/act/1906-06-20/ch3447/s3), [34 Stat. 324](/us/stat/34/324); [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 6](/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s6), [36 Stat. 538](/us/stat/36/538), related to unauthorized establishment of lights and penalty. See [section 542 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s542).

Section 760, [act May 14, 1908, ch. 168, § 5](/us/act/1908-05-14/ch168/s5), [35 Stat. 162](/us/stat/35/162), related to failure to maintain lights on bridges and penalty. See [section 544 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s544).

Section 761, acts [May 14, 1908, ch. 168, § 6](/us/act/1908-05-14/ch168/s6), [35 Stat. 162](/us/stat/35/162); [June 17, 1910, ch. 301, § 6](/us/act/1910-06-17/ch301/s6), [36 Stat. 538](/us/stat/36/538), related to wrongful interference with Government aids to navigation and penalty. See [section 543 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s543).

Section 762, [act Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 81, § 8](/us/act/1915-03-03/ch81/s8), [38 Stat. 928](/us/stat/38/928), related to wrongful interference with private aids to navigation and penalty. See [section 543 of Title 14](/us/usc/t14/s543).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President [Aug. 4, 1949], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
