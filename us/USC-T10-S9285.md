# § 9285. Medal of honor: presentation of Medal of Honor Flag

##### § 9285. Medal of honor: presentation of Medal of Honor Flag

The President shall provide for the presentation of the Medal of Honor Flag designated under [section 903 of title 36](/us/usc/t36/s903) to each person to whom a medal of honor is awarded under [section 9271 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s9271). Presentation of the flag shall be made at the same time as the presentation of the medal under section 9271 or 9282(a) of this title. In the case of a posthumous presentation of the medal, the flag shall be presented to the person to whom the medal is presented.
