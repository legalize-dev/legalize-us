# § 5178. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–390, title II, § 206(c)](/us/pl/106/390/tII/s206/c), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1571](/us/stat/114/1571)

##### § 5178. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–390, title II, § 206(c)](/us/pl/106/390/tII/s206/c), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1571](/us/stat/114/1571)

Section, [Pub. L. 93–288, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/93/288/tIV/s411), as added [Pub. L. 100–707, title I, § 106(g)](/us/pl/100/707/tI/s106/g), Nov. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 4704](/us/stat/102/4704), related to individual and family grant programs.

A prior section 5178, [Pub. L. 93–288, title IV, § 408](/us/pl/93/288/tIV/s408), May 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 156](/us/stat/88/156), related to individual and family grant programs, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 100–707, § 106(g)](/us/pl/100/707/s106/g).

A prior [section 411 of Pub. L. 93–288](/us/pl/93/288/s411) was renumbered section 414 by [Pub. L. 100–707](/us/pl/100/707) and is classified to [section 5181 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5181).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 18 months after Oct. 30, 2000, see [section 206(d) of Pub. L. 106–390](/us/pl/106/390/s206/d), set out as an Effective Date of 2000 Amendment note under [section 5174 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5174).
