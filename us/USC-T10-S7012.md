# § 7012. Department of the Army: seal

##### § 7012. Department of the Army: seal

The Secretary of the Army shall have a seal for the Department of the Army. The design of the seal must be approved by the President. Judicial notice shall be taken of the seal.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 157](/us/stat/70A/157), § 3011; renumbered § 3012, [Pub. L. 99–433, title V, § 501(a)(2)](/us/pl/99/433/tV/s501/a/2), Oct. 1, 1986, [100 Stat. 1034](/us/stat/100/1034); renumbered § 7012, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(a)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/a), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1838](/us/stat/132/1838).)*

The words “of office” are omitted as surplusage.
