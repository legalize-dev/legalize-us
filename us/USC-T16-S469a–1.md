# § 469a–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

##### § 469a–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

Section, [Pub. L. 86–523, § 3](/us/pl/86/523/s3), as added [Pub. L. 93–291, § 1(3)](/us/pl/93/291/s1/3), May 24, 1974, [88 Stat. 174](/us/stat/88/174), related to threats of irreparable loss or destruction of significant scientific, prehistorical, historical, or archeological data by Federal construction projects. See [section 312502 of Title 54](/us/usc/t54/s312502), National Park Service and Related Programs.

A prior [section 3 of Pub. L. 86–523](/us/pl/86/523/s3) was renumbered section 6 and was classified to [section 469b of this title](/us/usc/t16/s469b) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 113–287](/us/pl/113/287).
