#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to taxable years beginning after Dec. 31, 2017, see [section 13001(c)(1) of Pub. L. 115–97](/us/pl/115/97/s13001/c/1), set out as an Effective Date of 2017 Amendment note under [section 11 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s11).

#### Extension of Special Rule Relating to Qualified Timber Gain

[Pub. L. 115–123, div. D, title I, § 40310](/us/pl/115/123/dD/tI/s40310), Feb. 9, 2018, [132 Stat. 147](/us/stat/132/147), provided that: “For purposes of applying section 1201(b) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 with respect to taxable years beginning during 2017, such section shall be applied by substituting ‘2016 or 2017’ for ‘2016’.”
