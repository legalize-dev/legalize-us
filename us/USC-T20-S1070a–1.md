#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 401A](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s401A), as added [Pub. L. 109–171, title VIII, § 8003](/us/pl/109/171/tVIII/s8003), Feb. 8, 2006, [120 Stat. 155](/us/stat/120/155); amended [Pub. L. 110–227, § 10(a)](/us/pl/110/227/s10/a), May 7, 2008, [122 Stat. 748](/us/stat/122/748); [Pub. L. 110–315, title IV, § 402(a)(1)](/us/pl/110/315/tIV/s402/a/1), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3190](/us/stat/122/3190), which authorized the Secretary to make academic competitiveness grants to eligible students, was omitted from the Code upon the expiration of Secretary’s authority to make such grants at the end of award year 2010–2011.

L. 100–369, § 7(c)](/us/pl/100/369/s7/c), July 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 837](/us/stat/102/837), related to family contribution schedule for Pell Grants and data elements, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–325, title IV, § 401(i)](/us/pl/102/325/tIV/s401/i), July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 482](/us/stat/106/482).
