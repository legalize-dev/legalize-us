# § 2103b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–79, title VIII, § 8002](/us/pl/113/79/tVIII/s8002), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 913](/us/stat/128/913)

##### § 2103b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–79, title VIII, § 8002](/us/pl/113/79/tVIII/s8002), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 913](/us/stat/128/913)

Section, [Pub. L. 95–313, § 6](/us/pl/95/313/s6), as added [Pub. L. 108–148, title III, § 302](/us/pl/108/148/tIII/s302), Dec. 3, 2003, [117 Stat. 1903](/us/stat/117/1903); amended [Pub. L. 110–234, title VII, § 7511(c)(35)](/us/pl/110/234/tVII/s7511/c/35), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1270](/us/stat/122/1270); [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title VII, § 7511(c)(35), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 2032, related to the establishment and implementation of watershed forestry assistance programs.

A prior section 2103b, [Pub. L. 95–313, § 6](/us/pl/95/313/s6), as added [Pub. L. 101–624, title XII, § 1216](/us/pl/101/624/tXII/s1216), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3526](/us/stat/104/3526), established the Stewardship Incentive Program, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 107–171, title VIII, § 8001(a)](/us/pl/107/171/tVIII/s8001/a), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 468](/us/stat/116/468).

A prior [section 6 of Pub. L. 95–313](/us/pl/95/313/s6) was renumbered section 9 and is classified to [section 2105 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s2105).
