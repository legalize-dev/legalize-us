# § 22508. Meetings

##### § 22508. Meetings

The corporation may hold its meetings at any place the corporation decides.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1305](/us/stat/112/1305).)*
