# § 1701. Omitted

##### § 1701. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–395, § 2](/us/pl/95/395/s2), Sept. 30, 1978, [92 Stat. 813](/us/stat/92/813), which set out congressional findings and declaration of policy, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 95–395, § 1](/us/pl/95/395/s1), Sept. 30, 1978, [92 Stat. 813](/us/stat/92/813), provided that [Pub. L. 95–395](/us/pl/95/395), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Rhode Island Indian Claims Settlement Act”.
