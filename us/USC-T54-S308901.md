# § 308901. Definitions

##### § 308901. Definitions

(1) Collection.— The term “collection” means a collection of intellectual and cultural artifacts, including documents, sculpture, and works of art.

(2) Eligible entity.— The term “eligible entity” means a Federal entity, State, local, or tribal government, educational institution, or nonprofit organization.

(3) Historic property.— The term “historic property” has the meaning given the term in [section 300308 of this title](/us/usc/t54/s300308).

(4) Nationally significant.— The term “nationally significant”, in reference to a collection or historic property, means a collection or historic property that meets the applicable criteria for national significance, in accordance with regulations promulgated by the Secretary pursuant to [section 302103 of this title](/us/usc/t54/s302103).

(5) Program.— The term “program” means the Save America’s Treasures Program established under [section 308902(a) of this title](/us/usc/t54/s308902/a).

(6) Secretary.— The term “Secretary” means the Secretary, acting through the Director.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3243](/us/stat/128/3243).)*

In paragraph (4), the words “in reference to a collection or historic property” are added for clarity.
