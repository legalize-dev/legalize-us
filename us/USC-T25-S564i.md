# § 564i. Omitted

##### § 564i. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 10](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s10), [68 Stat. 721](/us/stat/68/721), which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to transfer to the tribe or to a public or nonprofit body any federally owned property deemed necessary for Indian use, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
