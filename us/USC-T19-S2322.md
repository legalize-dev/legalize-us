# § 2322. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–210, div. A, title I](/us/pl/107/210/dA/tI), §123(b)(2), Aug. 6, 2002, [116 Stat. 944](/us/stat/116/944)

##### § 2322. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–210, div. A, title I](/us/pl/107/210/dA/tI), §123(b)(2), Aug. 6, 2002, [116 Stat. 944](/us/stat/116/944)

Section, [Pub. L. 93–618, title II, § 249A](/us/pl/93/618/tII/s249A), as added [Pub. L. 103–182, title V, § 503(c)](/us/pl/103/182/tV/s503/c), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2151](/us/stat/107/2151), prohibited assistance relating to a separation pursuant to certifications under both subparts A and D of this part.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 2322, [Pub. L. 93–618, title II, § 250](/us/pl/93/618/tII/s250), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2029](/us/stat/88/2029), provided for judicial review for workers or groups aggrieved by a final determination by the Secretary under [section 2273 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2273), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 96–417, title VI, § 612](/us/pl/96/417/tVI/s612), title VII, § 701(a), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1746](/us/stat/94/1746), 1747, effective Nov. 1, 1980, and applicable with respect to civil actions pending on or commenced on or after such date. See [section 2395 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2395).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable with respect to petitions filed under this part on or after the date that is 90 days after Aug. 6, 2002, except with respect to certain workers, see [section 123(c) of Pub. L. 107–210](/us/pl/107/210/s123/c), set out as a note under [section 2331 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2331).
