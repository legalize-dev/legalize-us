##### § 9. Repealed. [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title XI, § 1131(25), [60 Stat. 1037](/us/stat/60/1037)

Section, acts [Apr. 5, 1906, ch. 1366, § 4](/us/act/1906-04-05/ch1366/s4), [34 Stat. 100](/us/stat/34/100); [May 24, 1924, ch. 182, § 17](/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182/s17), formerly § 10, [43 Stat. 142](/us/stat/43/142), renumbered § 17 and amended [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276, § 7](/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276/s7), [46 Stat. 1209](/us/stat/46/1209), related to inspection of diplomatic and consular offices.
