# § 421h. Procedural and substantive requirements applicable to works financed by loans pursuant to sections 421a to 421h of this title

##### § 421h. Procedural and substantive requirements applicable to works financed by loans pursuant to sections 421a to 421h of this title

Works financed by loans made under sections 421a to 421h of this title shall be subject to all procedural and substantive requirements of the Fish and Wildlife Coordination Act [[16 U.S.C. 661](/us/usc/t16/s661) et seq.], the Federal Water Pollution Control Act, as amended [[33 U.S.C. 1251](/us/usc/t33/s1251) et seq.], and the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969 [[42 U.S.C. 4321](/us/usc/t42/s4321) et seq.].

*([July 4, 1955, ch. 271, § 8](/us/act/1955-07-04/ch271/s8), as added [Pub. L. 92–487](/us/pl/92/487), Oct. 13, 1972, [86 Stat. 806](/us/stat/86/806).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Fish and Wildlife Coordination Act, referred to in text, is [act Mar. 10, 1934, ch. 55](/us/act/1934-03-10/ch55), [48 Stat. 401](/us/stat/48/401), which is classified generally to sections 661 to 666c–1 of Title 16, Conservation. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 661(a) of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s661/a), Short Title note set out under [section 661 of Title 16](/us/usc/t16/s661), and Tables.

The Federal Water Pollution Control Act, as amended, referred to in text, is [act June 30, 1948, ch. 758](/us/act/1948-06-30/ch758), as amended generally by [Pub. L. 92–500, § 2](/us/pl/92/500/s2), Oct. 18, 1972, [86 Stat. 816](/us/stat/86/816), which is classified generally to chapter 26 (§ 1251 et seq.) of Title 33, Navigation and Navigable Waters. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1251 of Title 33](/us/usc/t33/s1251) and Tables.

The National Environmental Policy Act of 1969, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 91–190](/us/pl/91/190), Jan. 1, 1970, [83 Stat. 852](/us/stat/83/852), which is classified generally to chapter 55 (§ 4321 et seq.) of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 4321 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s4321) and Tables.
