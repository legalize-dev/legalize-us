# § 862. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–649, § 14b](/us/pl/87/649/s14b), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 498](/us/stat/76/498)

##### § 862. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–649, § 14b](/us/pl/87/649/s14b), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 498](/us/stat/76/498)

Section, acts [Mar. 4, 1907, ch. 2918, § 1](/us/act/1907-03-04/ch2918/s1), [34 Stat. 1322](/us/stat/34/1322); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1), [37 Stat. 736](/us/stat/37/736); [June 21, 1955, ch. 172, § 1](/us/act/1955-06-21/ch172/s1), [69 Stat. 169](/us/stat/69/169); Sept. 26, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–304, § 9(b)](/us/pl/87/304/s9/b), [75 Stat. 665](/us/stat/75/665), authorized commissioned officers of the Coast and Geodetic Survey to make assignments or allotments of their pay. See [section 706 of Title 37](/us/usc/t37/s706), Pay and Allowances of the Uniformed Services.
