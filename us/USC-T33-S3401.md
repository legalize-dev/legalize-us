# § 3401. Purpose

##### § 3401. Purpose

The purpose of this subchapter is to establish the national ocean exploration program within the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.

*([Pub. L. 111–11, title XII, § 12001](/us/pl/111/11/tXII/s12001), Mar. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 1417](/us/stat/123/1417); [Pub. L. 117–263, div. J, title CIII, § 10305(a)](/us/pl/117/263/dJ/tCIII/s10305/a), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 3971](/us/stat/136/3971).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–263](/us/pl/117/263) struck out “and the national undersea research program” after “national ocean exploration program”.
