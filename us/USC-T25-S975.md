##### § 975. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–629, § 5](/us/pl/87/629/s5), Sept. 5, 1962, [76 Stat. 430](/us/stat/76/430), which related to land surveys and execution of conveyances by the Secretary of the Interior, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
