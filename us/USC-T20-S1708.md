# § 1708. Jurisdiction of district courts

##### § 1708. Jurisdiction of district courts

The appropriate district court of the United States shall have and exercise jurisdiction of proceedings instituted under [section 1706 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1706).

*([Pub. L. 93–380, title II, § 209](/us/pl/93/380/tII/s209), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 516](/us/stat/88/516).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective on and after sixtieth day after Aug. 21, 1974, see [section 2(c) of Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380/s2/c), set out as a note under [section 1221–1 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1221–1).
