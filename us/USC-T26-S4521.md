# [§ 4521. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–456, title III, § 302(d)](/us/pl/87/456/tIII/s302/d), May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 77](/us/stat/76/77)]

##### [§ 4521. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–456, title III, § 302(d)](/us/pl/87/456/tIII/s302/d), May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 77](/us/stat/76/77)]

Section, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 539](/us/stat/68A/539), imposed a tax on petroleum products imported into the United States.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective with respect to articles entered, or withdrawn from warehouse, for consumption on or after Aug. 31, 1963, see [section 501(a) of Pub. L. 87–456](/us/pl/87/456/s501/a), title V, May 24, 1962, [76 Stat. 78](/us/stat/76/78).
