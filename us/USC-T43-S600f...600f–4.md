#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 600f, [Pub. L. 93–493, title II, § 201](/us/pl/93/493/tII/s201), Oct. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1491](/us/stat/88/1491), related to authorization for construction, operation, and maintenance of Cibolo project.

Section 600f–1, [Pub. L. 93–493, title II, § 202](/us/pl/93/493/tII/s202), Oct. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1491](/us/stat/88/1491), related to basis of interest rate on unpaid balance of reimbursable costs of Cibolo project.

Section 600f–2, [Pub. L. 93–493, title II, § 203](/us/pl/93/493/tII/s203), Oct. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1491](/us/stat/88/1491), related to water delivery and reimbursable construction cost repayment contracts.

Section 600f–3, [Pub. L. 93–493, title II, § 204](/us/pl/93/493/tII/s204), Oct. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1492](/us/stat/88/1492), related to conservation and development of fish and wildlife resources and enhancement of recreation.

Section 600f–4, [Pub. L. 93–493, title II, § 205](/us/pl/93/493/tII/s205), Oct. 27, 1974, [88 Stat. 1492](/us/stat/88/1492), related to authorization of appropriations to defray construction costs of Cibolo project.
