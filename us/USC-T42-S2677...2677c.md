# §§ 2677 to 2677c. Transferred

##### §§ 2677 to 2677c. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 2677, [Pub. L. 88–164, title I, § 137](/us/pl/88/164/tI/s137), as added [Pub. L. 91–517, title I, § 101(b)](/us/pl/91/517/tI/s101/b), Oct. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1323](/us/stat/84/1323), which related to payments to States for planning, administration, and services, was transferred to former [section 6064 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s6064).

A prior section 2677, [Pub. L. 88–164, title I, § 137](/us/pl/88/164/tI/s137), Oct. 31, 1963, [77 Stat. 289](/us/stat/77/289); [Pub. L. 90–170, § 3(b)](/us/pl/90/170/s3/b), Dec. 4, 1967, [81 Stat. 528](/us/stat/81/528), prohibited duplication of grants for the construction of any facility for the mentally retarded under the Public Health Service Act. See section 201 et seq. of this title.

Section 2677a, [Pub. L. 88–164, title I, § 138](/us/pl/88/164/tI/s138), as added [Pub. L. 91–517, title I, § 101(b)](/us/pl/91/517/tI/s101/b), Oct. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1323](/us/stat/84/1323), which related to withholding of payments for planning, administration, and services, was transferred to former [section 6065 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s6065).

Section 2677b, [Pub. L. 88–164, title I, § 139](/us/pl/88/164/tI/s139), as added [Pub. L. 91–517, title I, § 101(b)](/us/pl/91/517/tI/s101/b), Oct. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1323](/us/stat/84/1323), which related to promulgation of regulations, was transferred to former [section 6008 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s6008).

Section 2677c, [Pub. L. 88–164, title I, § 140](/us/pl/88/164/tI/s140), as added [Pub. L. 91–517, title I, § 101(b)](/us/pl/91/517/tI/s101/b), Oct. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1324](/us/stat/84/1324), which related to nonduplication of payments, was transferred to former [section 6066 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s6066).
