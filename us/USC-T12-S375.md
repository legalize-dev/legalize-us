#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2010—[Pub. L. 111–203](/us/pl/111/203) substituted “[Reserved]” for text, which related to purchases from directors and sales to directors.

[Pub. L. 111–203, title VI, § 615(c)](/us/pl/111/203/tVI/s615/c), July 21, 2010, [124 Stat. 1615](/us/stat/124/1615), provided that: “The amendments made by this section [amending this section and [section 1828 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1828)] shall take effect on the transfer date.”

[For definition of “transfer date” as used in [section 615(c) of Pub. L. 111–203](/us/pl/111/203/s615/c), set out above, see [section 5301 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s5301).]
