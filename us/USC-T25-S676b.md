# § 676b. Omitted

##### § 676b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–420, § 1](/us/pl/91/420/s1), Sept. 25, 1970, [84 Stat. 871](/us/stat/84/871), which related to uses of unexpended balance of fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
