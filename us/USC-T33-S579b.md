#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 113–121, title VI, § 6001](/us/pl/113/121/tVI/s6001), June 10, 2014, [128 Stat. 1345](/us/stat/128/1345); [Pub. L. 114–322, title I, § 1301(g)](/us/pl/114/322/tI/s1301/g), Dec. 16, 2016, [130 Stat. 1690](/us/stat/130/1690), consisted of subsecs. (a) to (f) relating to deauthorization of inactive projects authorized for construction before Nov. 8, 2007. Subsecs. (a) and (c) to (f) were repealed by [Pub. L. 114–322, title I, § 1301(g)](/us/pl/114/322/tI/s1301/g), Dec. 16, 2016, [130 Stat. 1690](/us/stat/130/1690). Subsec. (b) of [section 6001 of Pub. L. 113–121](/us/pl/113/121/s6001) amended [section 579a of this title](/us/usc/t33/s579a).
