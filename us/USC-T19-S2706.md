# § 2706. Effective date

##### § 2706. Effective date

(a) This chapter shall take effect on August 5, 1983.

(b) Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–382, title II, § 211](/us/pl/101/382/tII/s211), Aug. 20, 1990, [104 Stat. 655](/us/stat/104/655).

*([Pub. L. 98–67, title II, § 218](/us/pl/98/67/tII/s218), Aug. 5, 1983, [97 Stat. 395](/us/stat/97/395); [Pub. L. 101–382, title II, § 211](/us/pl/101/382/tII/s211), Aug. 20, 1990, [104 Stat. 655](/us/stat/104/655).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in subsec. (a), was in the original “this subtitle” meaning subtitle A (§§ 211–218) of title II of [Pub. L. 98–67](/us/pl/98/67) which enacted this chapter, amended [section 1202 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1202), repealed [section 2582 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2582), and enacted provisions set out as notes under sections 1202, 1319, 2251, and 2703 of this title and [section 1311 of Title 33](/us/usc/t33/s1311), Navigation and Navigable Waters. For complete classification of subtitle A to the Code, see Tables.

#### Amendments

1990—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 101–382](/us/pl/101/382) struck out subsec. (b) which related to termination of duty-free treatment. Notwithstanding directory language repealing “section 218 of the Caribbean Basin Economic Recovery Act ([19 U.S.C. 2706(b)](/us/usc/t19/s2706/b))”, amendment was executed by repealing subsec. (b) to reflect the probable intent of Congress in view of catchline for [section 211 of Pub. L. 101–382](/us/pl/101/382/s211) which read “Repeal of termination date on duty-free treatment under the Act”.
