# § 4243. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–111, § 6(a)(3)](/us/pl/107/111/s6/a/3), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 2096](/us/stat/115/2096)

##### § 4243. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–111, § 6(a)(3)](/us/pl/107/111/s6/a/3), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 2096](/us/stat/115/2096)

Section, [Pub. L. 100–478, title II, § 2304](/us/pl/100/478/tII/s2304), Oct. 7, 1988, [102 Stat. 2322](/us/stat/102/2322), directed Secretary to determine within 3 months after the 8th Conference of the Parties to CITES whether the importation of illegally harvested ivory into the United States had been substantially stopped, and upon a determination that it had not been substantially stopped, to recommend to Congress necessary legislation or other actions to achieve the purposes of this chapter.
