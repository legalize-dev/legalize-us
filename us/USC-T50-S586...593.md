# §§ 586 to 593. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### §§ 586 to 593. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 586, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 115](/us/stat/64/115), prescribed persons who may convene general courts-martial. See [section 822 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s822), Armed Forces.

Section 587, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 115](/us/stat/64/115), prescribed persons who may convene special courts-martial. See [section 823 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s823).

Section 588, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 116](/us/stat/64/116), prescribed persons who may convene summary courts-martial. See [section 824 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s824).

Section 589, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 116](/us/stat/64/116), related to persons who may serve on courts-martial. See [section 825 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s825).

Section 590, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 117](/us/stat/64/117), provided for law officer of a general court-martial. See [section 826 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s826).

Section 591, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 117](/us/stat/64/117), related to appointment of trial counsel and defense counsel. See [section 827 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s827).

Section 592, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 117](/us/stat/64/117), provided for appointment of reporters and interpreters. See [section 828 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s828).

Section 593, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 117](/us/stat/64/117), related to absent and additional members of courts-martial. See [section 829 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s829).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1957, see [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53) footnote, [70A Stat. 680](/us/stat/70A/680).
