# § 2528. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3133(e)(1)(A)](/us/pl/112/239/dC/tXXXI/s3133/e/1/A), (2), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2192](/us/stat/126/2192), 2193

##### § 2528. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–239, div. C, title XXXI, § 3133(e)(1)(A)](/us/pl/112/239/dC/tXXXI/s3133/e/1/A), (2), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2192](/us/stat/126/2192), 2193

Section, [Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLII, § 4208](/us/pl/107/314/dD/tXLII/s4208), formerly [Pub. L. 104–106, div. C, title XXXI, § 3152](/us/pl/104/106/dC/tXXXI/s3152), Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 623](/us/stat/110/623); [Pub. L. 106–398, § 1 [div. C, title XXXI, § 3192]](/us/pl/106/398/s1/dC/tXXXI/s3192), Oct. 30, 2000, [114 Stat. 1654](/us/stat/114/1654), 1654A–480; renumbered [Pub. L. 107–314, div. D, title XLII, § 4208](/us/pl/107/314/dD/tXLII/s4208), by [Pub. L. 108–136, div. C, title XXXI, § 3141(e)(9)](/us/pl/108/136/dC/tXXXI/s3141/e/9), Nov. 24, 2003, [117 Stat. 1759](/us/stat/117/1759); [Pub. L. 110–181, div. C, title XXXI, § 3112(b)(1)](/us/pl/110/181/dC/tXXXI/s3112/b/1), Jan. 28, 2008, [122 Stat. 577](/us/stat/122/577), required the Secretary of Energy to submit to Congress in odd-numbered years reports on the nuclear test readiness of the United States.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Section 3133(e)(1)(A) of Pub. L. 112–239](/us/pl/112/239/s3133/e/1/A) repealed [section 4208 of Pub. L. 107–314](/us/pl/107/314/s4208), which was classified to this section. [Section 3133(e)(2) of Pub. L. 112–239](/us/pl/112/239/s3133/e/2) repealed [section 3152 of Pub. L. 104–106](/us/pl/104/106/s3152), which was transferred and redesignated as [section 4208 of Pub. L. 107–314](/us/pl/107/314/s4208), and section 3133(e)(2) is treated as also repealing this section, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
