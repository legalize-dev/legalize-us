# § 500f. Omitted

##### § 500f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 7](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s7), [50 Stat. 900](/us/stat/50/900), which related to management of reindeer industry, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
