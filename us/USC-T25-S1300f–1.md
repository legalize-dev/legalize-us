# § 1300f–1. Omitted

##### § 1300f–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–375, § 2](/us/pl/95/375/s2), Sept. 18, 1978, [92 Stat. 712](/us/stat/92/712), which related to adoption of tribal constitution and bylaws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
