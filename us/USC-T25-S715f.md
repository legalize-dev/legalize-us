# § 715f. Omitted

##### § 715f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–42, § 8](/us/pl/101/42/s8), June 28, 1989, [103 Stat. 93](/us/stat/103/93), which provided for governance of the Tribe by an Interim Council until the adoption of a new tribal constitution and bylaws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
