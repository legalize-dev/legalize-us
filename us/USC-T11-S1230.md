# § 1230. Revocation of an order of confirmation

##### § 1230. Revocation of an order of confirmation

(a) On request of a party in interest at any time within 180 days after the date of the entry of an order of confirmation under [section 1225 of this title](/us/usc/t11/s1225), and after notice and a hearing, the court may revoke such order if such order was procured by fraud.

(b) If the court revokes an order of confirmation under subsection (a) of this section, the court shall dispose of the case under [section 1207 of this title](/us/usc/t11/s1207), unless, within the time fixed by the court, the debtor proposes and the court confirms a modification of the plan under [section 1229 of this title](/us/usc/t11/s1229).
