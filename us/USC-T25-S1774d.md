# § 1774d. Omitted

##### § 1774d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–503, § 6](/us/pl/101/503/s6), Nov. 3, 1990, [104 Stat. 1295](/us/stat/104/1295), which related to sources of settlement funds and distribution and use of amounts, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
