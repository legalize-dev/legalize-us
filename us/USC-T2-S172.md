# § 172. Definitions

##### § 172. Definitions

(1) the term Center means the Center for the Book;

(2) the term Librarian means the Librarian of Congress.

*([Pub. L. 95–129, § 2](/us/pl/95/129/s2), Oct. 13, 1977, [91 Stat. 1151](/us/stat/91/1151).)*
