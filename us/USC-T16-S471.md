# § 471. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 704(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s704/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792](/us/stat/90/2792)

##### § 471. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 704(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s704/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792](/us/stat/90/2792)

Section, acts [Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 561, § 24](/us/act/1891-03-03/ch561/s24), [26 Stat. 1103](/us/stat/26/1103); [Mar. 4, 1907, ch. 2907](/us/act/1907-03-04/ch2907), [34 Stat. 1271](/us/stat/34/1271); [June 25, 1910, ch. 421, § 2](/us/act/1910-06-25/ch421/s2), [36 Stat. 847](/us/stat/36/847); [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 369](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch369), [37 Stat. 497](/us/stat/37/497); [June 7, 1924, ch. 348, § 9](/us/act/1924-06-07/ch348/s9) (first and fifth sentences), [43 Stat. 655](/us/stat/43/655), provided for establishment of national forests by the President, limited inclusion of lands in certain States, and authorized addition of lands suitable for production of timber.

Act Mar. 4, 1907, cited above, was not repealed by [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Section 704(a) of Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579/s704/a) provided that this section is repealed effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

#### Savings Provision

Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579) not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see note set out under [section 1701 of Title 43](/us/usc/t43/s1701), Public Lands.
