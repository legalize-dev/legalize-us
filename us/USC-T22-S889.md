#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).

#### Employment of Family Members Overseas

[Pub. L. 95–426, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/95/426/tIV/s401/a), Oct. 7, 1978, [92 Stat. 977](/us/stat/92/977), which related to employment of family members overseas, was repealed by [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(2)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/2), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160](/us/stat/94/2160). See [section 3951 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3951).

#### Report to Congress on Employment of Family Members of Government Personnel Overseas

L. 95–426, title IV, § 401(c)](/us/pl/95/426/tIV/s401/c), Oct. 7, 1978, [92 Stat. 978](/us/stat/92/978), which related to report by Secretary of State respecting overseas employment of family members of Government personnel, was repealed by [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(2)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/2), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160](/us/stat/94/2160).
