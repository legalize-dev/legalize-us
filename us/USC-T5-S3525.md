# § 3525. Regulations

##### § 3525. Regulations

The Office of Personnel Management may prescribe regulations to carry out this subchapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 107–296, title XIII, § 1313(a)(1)(A)](/us/pl/107/296/tXIII/s1313/a/1/A), Nov. 25, 2002, [116 Stat. 2294](/us/stat/116/2294).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**
