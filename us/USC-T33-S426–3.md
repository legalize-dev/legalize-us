# § 426–3. Transfer of functions of Beach Erosion Board

##### § 426–3. Transfer of functions of Beach Erosion Board

All functions of the Beach Erosion Board pertaining to review of reports of investigations made concerning erosion of the shores of coastal and lake waters, and the protection of such shores, are hereby transferred to the Board established by [section 541 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s541), referred to as the Board of Engineers for Rivers and Harbors.

*([Pub. L. 88–172, § 3](/us/pl/88/172/s3), Nov. 7, 1963, [77 Stat. 305](/us/stat/77/305).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Termination of Board of Engineers for Rivers and Harbors and Reassignment of Duties and Responsibilities

For termination of Board of Engineers for Rivers and Harbors 180 days after Oct. 31, 1992, and reassignment of duties and responsibilities by Secretary of Army, see [section 223 of Pub. L. 102–580](/us/pl/102/580/s223), set out as a note under [section 541 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s541).

#### Abolition of Beach Erosion Board

[Section 1 of Pub. L. 88–172](/us/pl/88/172/s1) abolished Beach Erosion Board, and is set out as a note under [section 426 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s426). For transfer of certain functions of Board to Coastal Engineering Research Center, see [section 426–1 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s426–1).
