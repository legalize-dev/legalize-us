# § 12208. Transvestites

##### § 12208. Transvestites

For the purposes of this chapter, the term “disabled” or “disability” shall not apply to an individual solely because that individual is a transvestite.

*([Pub. L. 101–336, title V, § 509](/us/pl/101/336/tV/s509), formerly § 508, July 26, 1990, [104 Stat. 373](/us/stat/104/373); renumbered § 509, [Pub. L. 110–325, § 6(a)(2)](/us/pl/110/325/s6/a/2), Sept. 25, 2008, [122 Stat. 3558](/us/stat/122/3558).)*
