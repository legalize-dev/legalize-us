# § 305c–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–23, § 2](/us/pl/87/23/s2), Apr. 24, 1961, [75 Stat. 45](/us/stat/75/45)

##### § 305c–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–23, § 2](/us/pl/87/23/s2), Apr. 24, 1961, [75 Stat. 45](/us/stat/75/45)

Section, [act May 10, 1939, ch. 119, § 1](/us/act/1939-05-10/ch119/s1), [53 Stat. 699](/us/stat/53/699), provided for a limitation of $10 per diem in lieu of subsistence on amount that may be paid to members of the Indian Arts and Crafts Board. See [section 305 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s305).
