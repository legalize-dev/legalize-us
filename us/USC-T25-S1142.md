#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–93, § 2](/us/pl/90/93/s2), Sept. 27, 1967, [81 Stat. 229](/us/stat/81/229), which related to preparation of membership rolls, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
