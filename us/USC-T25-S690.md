# § 690. Omitted

##### § 690. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 88–663](/us/pl/88/663), Oct. 13, 1964, [78 Stat. 1093](/us/stat/78/1093), which related to distribution of judgment fund and tax exemption of per capita payments, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
