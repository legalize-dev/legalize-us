# § 23. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–649, title II, § 202(b)](/us/pl/86/649/tII/s202/b), July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 507](/us/stat/74/507)

##### § 23. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–649, title II, § 202(b)](/us/pl/86/649/tII/s202/b), July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 507](/us/stat/74/507)

Section, [act Feb. 14, 1931, ch. 187](/us/act/1931-02-14/ch187), [46 Stat. 1118](/us/stat/46/1118), prescribed fees for depositions in hearings in Bureau of Land Management. See [section 1371 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1371). Similar provisions were contained in the following prior appropriation acts:

[Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462), [43 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/43/1145).

[June 5, 1924, ch. 264](/us/act/1924-06-05/ch264), [43 Stat. 395](/us/stat/43/395).

[Jan. 24, 1923, ch. 42](/us/act/1923-01-24/ch42), [42 Stat. 1179](/us/stat/42/1179).

[May 24, 1922, ch. 199](/us/act/1922-05-24/ch199), [42 Stat. 558](/us/stat/42/558).

[Mar. 3, 1915, ch. 75](/us/act/1915-03-03/ch75), [38 Stat. 855](/us/stat/38/855).
