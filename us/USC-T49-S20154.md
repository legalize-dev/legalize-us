# [§ 20154. Repealed. [Pub. L. 114–94, div. A, title XI, § 11301(c)(1)](/us/pl/114/94/dA/tXI/s11301/c/1), Dec. 4, 2015, [129 Stat. 1648](/us/stat/129/1648)]

##### [§ 20154. Repealed. [Pub. L. 114–94, div. A, title XI, § 11301(c)(1)](/us/pl/114/94/dA/tXI/s11301/c/1), Dec. 4, 2015, [129 Stat. 1648](/us/stat/129/1648)]

Section, [Pub. L. 109–59, title IX, § 9002(a)(1)](/us/pl/109/59/tIX/s9002/a/1), Aug. 10, 2005, [119 Stat. 1919](/us/stat/119/1919), related to capital grants for rail line relocation projects.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal by [Pub. L. 114–94](/us/pl/114/94) effective Oct. 1, 2015, see [section 1003 of Pub. L. 114–94](/us/pl/114/94/s1003), set out as an Effective Date of 2015 Amendment note under [section 5313 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s5313), Government Organization and Employees.

#### Regulations

[Pub. L. 109–59, title IX, § 9002(b)](/us/pl/109/59/tIX/s9002/b), Aug. 10, 2005, [119 Stat. 1921](/us/stat/119/1921), required Secretary of Transportation to issue temporary regulations to implement grant program under this section by April 1, 2006, and to issue final regulations by October 1, 2006.
