# § 269. Permanent International Association of Road Congresses; authorization of membership

##### § 269. Permanent International Association of Road Congresses; authorization of membership

The President is authorized to maintain membership of the United States in the Permanent International Association of Road Congresses.

*([Pub. L. 102–138, title I, § 164(b)](/us/pl/102/138/tI/s164/b), Oct. 28, 1991, [105 Stat. 676](/us/stat/105/676).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 269, [act June 18, 1926, ch. 623](/us/act/1926-06-18/ch623), [44 Stat. 754](/us/stat/44/754), authorized appropriations of $3,000 per annum to enable United States to accept membership in Permanent Association of International Road Congresses, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–138, title I, § 164(a)](/us/pl/102/138/tI/s164/a), Oct. 28, 1991, [105 Stat. 676](/us/stat/105/676).
