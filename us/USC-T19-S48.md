# § 48. Repealed. [Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 744, § 2](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch744/s2), [60 Stat. 807](/us/stat/60/807), eff. Nov. 1, 1946

##### § 48. Repealed. [Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 744, § 2](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch744/s2), [60 Stat. 807](/us/stat/60/807), eff. Nov. 1, 1946

Section, acts [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 251, § 5](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch251/s5), [42 Stat. 1454](/us/stat/42/1454); [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 645(b), [46 Stat. 761](/us/stat/46/761), related to travel, subsistence, and transportation expenses of customs officers and employees. These provisions are covered generally in chapter 57 of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
