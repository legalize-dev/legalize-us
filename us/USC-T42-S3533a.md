# § 3533a. Transferred

##### § 3533a. Transferred

Section, [Pub. L. 90–448, title XI, § 1105(a)](/us/pl/90/448/tXI/s1105/a), Aug. 1, 1968, [82 Stat. 567](/us/stat/82/567); [Pub. L. 96–153, title VI, § 603(a)](/us/pl/96/153/tVI/s603/a), Dec. 21, 1979, [93 Stat. 1138](/us/stat/93/1138), which established in Federal Emergency Management Agency position of Federal Insurance Administrator, was transferred to [section 4129 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s4129).
