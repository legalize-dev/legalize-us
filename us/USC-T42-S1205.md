# § 1205. Omitted

##### § 1205. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title X, § 1005](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tX/s1005), [49 Stat. 647](/us/stat/49/647), made available $30,000 for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1936, for expenses in administering sections 1201 to 1204 of this title.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Repeals

[Pub. L. 92–603, title III, § 303(a)](/us/pl/92/603/tIII/s303/a), (b), Oct. 30, 1972, [86 Stat. 1484](/us/stat/86/1484), provided that this section was repealed effective Jan. 1, 1974, except with respect to Puerto Rico, Guam, and the Virgin Islands.
