# §§ 1941 to 1944. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title XV, § 1529](/us/pl/95/561/tXV/s1529), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2380](/us/stat/92/2380)

##### §§ 1941 to 1944. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title XV, § 1529](/us/pl/95/561/tXV/s1529), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2380](/us/stat/92/2380)

Section 1941, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 711](/us/pl/93/380/tVII/s711), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 591](/us/stat/88/591), set forth provisions respecting statement of purpose for State reading improvement programs.

Section 1942, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 712](/us/pl/93/380/tVII/s712), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 591](/us/stat/88/591), set forth applicability and effective date of provisions respecting State reading improvement programs.

Section 1943, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 713](/us/pl/93/380/tVII/s713), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 591](/us/stat/88/591), set forth requirements for allotments and reallotments for States.

Section 1944, [Pub. L. 93–380, title VII, § 714](/us/pl/93/380/tVII/s714), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 592](/us/stat/88/592); [Pub. L. 94–194, § 4](/us/pl/94/194/s4), Dec. 31, 1975, [89 Stat. 1104](/us/stat/89/1104), set forth provisions respecting requirements for grants for State reading improvement programs.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 1530(a) of Pub. L. 95–561](/us/pl/95/561/s1530/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 1221e–3 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1221e–3).
