# § 1834. Criminal forfeiture

##### § 1834. Criminal forfeiture

Forfeiture, destruction, and restitution relating to this chapter shall be subject to section 2323, to the extent provided in that section, in addition to any other similar remedies provided by law.

*(Added [Pub. L. 104–294, title I, § 101(a)](/us/pl/104/294/tI/s101/a), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3489](/us/stat/110/3489); amended [Pub. L. 110–403, title II, § 207](/us/pl/110/403/tII/s207), Oct. 13, 2008, [122 Stat. 4263](/us/stat/122/4263).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2008—[Pub. L. 110–403](/us/pl/110/403) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section related to forfeiture of property either derived from or used to commit a violation of this chapter.
