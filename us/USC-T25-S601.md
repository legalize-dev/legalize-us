# § 601. Omitted

##### § 601. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 9, 1946, ch. 933, § 1](/us/act/1946-08-09/ch933/s1), [60 Stat. 968](/us/stat/60/968), which related to preparation of a tribal membership roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
