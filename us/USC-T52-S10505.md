# § 10505. Penalty

##### § 10505. Penalty

Whoever shall deprive or attempt to deprive any person of any right secured by section 10501, 10502, or 10503 of this title shall be fined not more than $5,000, or imprisoned not more than five years, or both.

*([Pub. L. 89–110, title II, § 205](/us/pl/89/110/tII/s205), formerly § 204, as added [Pub. L. 91–285, § 6](/us/pl/91/285/s6), June 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 317](/us/stat/84/317); renumbered § 205 and amended [Pub. L. 94–73, title III](/us/pl/94/73/tIII), §§ 302, 304, Aug. 6, 1975, [89 Stat. 403](/us/stat/89/403).)*
