# §§ 861 to 864. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 861 to 864. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 861, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 20, [39 Stat. 377](/us/stat/39/377); [Apr. 20, 1920, ch. 154, § 5](/us/act/1920-04-20/ch154/s5), [41 Stat. 571](/us/stat/41/571); [Mar. 4, 1921, ch. 151](/us/act/1921-03-04/ch151), [41 Stat. 1362](/us/stat/41/1362); [Aug. 13, 1921, ch. 63](/us/act/1921-08-13/ch63), [42 Stat. 159](/us/stat/42/159); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [June 30, 1945, ch. 204, § 9](/us/act/1945-06-30/ch204/s9), [59 Stat. 268](/us/stat/59/268); Aug. 18, 1958, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(b)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/b), [73 Stat. 386](/us/stat/73/386), authorized issuance of farm loan bonds in amount, denominations, and terms as Farm Credit Administration authorized. See [section 2153 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2153).

Section 862, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 20](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s20), [39 Stat. 377](/us/stat/39/377); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, directed the Farm Credit Administration to prescribe rules and regulations concerning circumstances and manner in which farm loan bonds shall be paid and retired. See [section 2153 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2153).

Section 863, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 20](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s20), [39 Stat. 377](/us/stat/39/377), directed that farm loan bonds be delivered through registrar of the district to bank applying for them.

Section 864, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 20, [39 Stat. 377](/us/stat/39/377); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(b)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/b), [73 Stat. 386](/us/stat/73/386), covered preparation of bonds, custody of plates and dies, exchange for registered bonds, and reexchange for coupons.
