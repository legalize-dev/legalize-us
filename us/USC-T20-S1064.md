# § 1064. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–325, title III, § 304(a)(2)](/us/pl/102/325/tIII/s304/a/2), July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 476](/us/stat/106/476)

##### § 1064. Repealed. [Pub. L. 102–325, title III, § 304(a)(2)](/us/pl/102/325/tIII/s304/a/2), July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 476](/us/stat/106/476)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–329, title III, § 331](/us/pl/89/329/tIII/s331), as added [Pub. L. 99–498, title III, § 301(a)](/us/pl/99/498/tIII/s301/a), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1298](/us/stat/100/1298), related to establishment of challenge grant program.

A prior section 1064, [Pub. L. 89–329, title III, § 331](/us/pl/89/329/tIII/s331), as added [Pub. L. 96–374, title III, § 301](/us/pl/96/374/tIII/s301), Oct. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 1395](/us/stat/94/1395); amended [Pub. L. 97–35, title V, § 516(c)(2)](/us/pl/97/35/tV/s516/c/2), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 447](/us/stat/95/447), established a challenge grant program, prior to the general revision of this subchapter by [Pub. L. 99–498](/us/pl/99/498).

Another prior section 1064, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 404](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s404), Nov. 8, 1965, [79 Stat. 1233](/us/stat/79/1233), related to time and manner of making application for grant, selection of recipients and conditions precedent to award, prior to the general revision of part A of subchapter IV of this chapter by [Pub. L. 92–318, title I, § 131(b)(1)](/us/pl/92/318/tI/s131/b/1), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 247](/us/stat/86/247).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1992, see [section 2 of Pub. L. 102–325](/us/pl/102/325/s2), set out as an Effective Date of 1992 Amendment note under [section 1001 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1001).
