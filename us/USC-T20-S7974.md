# § 7974. Preemption

##### § 7974. Preemption

Nothing in this subpart 11 See References in Text note below. is intended to preempt any provision of law of a State or political subdivision of a State that is more restrictive than a provision of this subpart.1

*([Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 8574](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s8574), formerly title IV, § 4304, as added [Pub. L. 107–110, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/107/110/tIV/s401), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1776](/us/stat/115/1776); renumbered title IX, § 9564, renumbered title VIII, § 8574, [Pub. L. 114–95, title IV, § 4001(a)(4)(A)](/us/pl/114/95/tIV/s4001/a/4/A), (B), (C)(ii), title VIII, § 8001(a)(9), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 1966](/us/stat/129/1966), 2088, 2089.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subpart, referred to in text, was in the original “this part”, and was translated as reading “this subpart”, to reflect the probable intent of Congress. [Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95) redesignated part C of title IV of [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10) as subpart 5 of part F of title VIII of [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), which is classified to this subpart.

#### Codification

Section was classified to [section 7184 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s7184) prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95).
