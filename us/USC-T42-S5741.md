# § 5741. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 656](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s656), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2124](/us/stat/98/2124)

##### § 5741. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 656](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s656), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2124](/us/stat/98/2124)

Section, [Pub. L. 93–415, title III, § 331](/us/pl/93/415/tIII/s331), as added [Pub. L. 95–115, § 7(c)](/us/pl/95/115/s7/c), Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/91/1059); amended [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)](/us/pl/96/88/tV/s509/b), Oct. 17, 1979, [93 Stat. 695](/us/stat/93/695), authorized President to submit to Congress after April 30, 1978, a reorganization plan for establishment of an Office of Youth Assistance, subject to Congressional resolution of disapproval.

Prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 98–473](/us/pl/98/473), [section 5741 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s5741) comprised part C of this subchapter. [Section 657(e) of Pub. L. 98–473](/us/pl/98/473/s657/e) redesignated former part D, consisting of section 5751 (now [34 U.S.C. 11280](/us/usc/t34/s11280)) of this title, as part C. Previously, part C was redesignated part D by [Pub. L. 95–115, § 7(c)](/us/pl/95/115/s7/c), Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1059](/us/stat/91/1059).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 12, 1984, see [section 670(a) of Pub. L. 98–473](/us/pl/98/473/s670/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1984 Amendment note under [section 11101 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s11101), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
