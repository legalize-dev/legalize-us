# § 3774. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 3774. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this chapter not more than $75,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2029.

*([Pub. L. 109–294, § 5](/us/pl/109/294/s5), Oct. 3, 2006, [120 Stat. 1354](/us/stat/120/1354); [Pub. L. 116–9, title VII, § 7001(a)](/us/pl/116/9/tVII/s7001/a), Mar. 12, 2019, [133 Stat. 779](/us/stat/133/779); [Pub. L. 118–159, div. E, title LIII, § 5302](/us/pl/118/159/dE/tLIII/s5302), Dec. 23, 2024, [138 Stat. 2445](/us/stat/138/2445).)*
