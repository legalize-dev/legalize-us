# § 30509. Principal office

##### § 30509. Principal office

The principal office of the corporation shall be in the District of Columbia.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1321](/us/stat/112/1321).)*
