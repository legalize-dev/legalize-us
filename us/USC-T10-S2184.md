# § 2184. Termination of assistance

##### § 2184. Termination of assistance

(1) shall be discontinued for any person whose conduct or progress is unsatisfactory under standards consistent with those established under [section 3524 of title 38](/us/usc/t38/s3524); and

(2) may not be provided for any person for more than 45 months (or the equivalent in other than full-time education or training).

*(Added [Pub. L. 99–399, title VIII, § 806(d)(1)](/us/pl/99/399/tVIII/s806/d/1), Aug. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 888](/us/stat/100/888); amended [Pub. L. 103–337, div. A, title X, § 1070(e)(7)](/us/pl/103/337/dA/tX/s1070/e/7), Oct. 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 2859](/us/stat/108/2859).)*
