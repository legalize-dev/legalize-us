# § 577. Arbitrators

##### § 577. Arbitrators

(a) The parties to an arbitration proceeding shall be entitled to participate in the selection of the arbitrator.

(b) The arbitrator shall be a neutral who meets the criteria of [section 573 of this title](/us/usc/t5/s573).

*(Added [Pub. L. 101–552, § 4(b)](/us/pl/101/552/s4/b), Nov. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 2742](/us/stat/104/2742), § 587; renumbered § 577 and amended [Pub. L. 102–354, § 3(b)(2)](/us/pl/102/354/s3/b/2), (3), Aug. 26, 1992, [106 Stat. 944](/us/stat/106/944), 945.)*
