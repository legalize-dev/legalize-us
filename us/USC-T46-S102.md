# § 102. Barge

##### § 102. Barge

In this title, the term “barge” means a non-self-propelled vessel.

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 4](/us/pl/109/304/s4), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1486](/us/stat/120/1486).)*
