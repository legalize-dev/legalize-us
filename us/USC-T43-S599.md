# § 599. Omitted

##### § 599. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 17, 1916, ch. 349](/us/act/1916-08-17/ch349), [39 Stat. 516](/us/stat/39/516), provided that any person who established residence and made improvements on land within Yuma reclamation project for two years prior to August 17, 1916, should have right to make entry for the farm unit and have residence and improvements credited on his final proof.
