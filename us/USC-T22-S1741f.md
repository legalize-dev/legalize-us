# § 1741f. Rule of construction

##### § 1741f. Rule of construction

Nothing in this subchapter may be construed to authorize a private right of action.

*([Pub. L. 116–260, div. FF, title III, § 308](/us/pl/116/260/dFF/tIII/s308), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 3099](/us/stat/134/3099).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act” and was translated as reading “this subtitle”, meaning subtitle A (§§ 301–308) of title III of div. FF of [Pub. L. 116–260](/us/pl/116/260), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 3091](/us/stat/134/3091), known as the Robert Levinson Hostage Recovery and Hostage-Taking Accountability Act, which enacted this subchapter, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
