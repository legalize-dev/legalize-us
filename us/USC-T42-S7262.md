# § 7262. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–206, title V, § 502](/us/pl/104/206/tV/s502), Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3002](/us/stat/110/3002)

##### § 7262. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–206, title V, § 502](/us/pl/104/206/tV/s502), Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3002](/us/stat/110/3002)

Section, [Pub. L. 95–91, title VI, § 652](/us/pl/95/91/tVI/s652), Aug. 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 601](/us/stat/91/601), authorized Secretary to accept gifts, bequests, and devises of property for purpose of aiding or facilitating work of Department.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 104–206](/us/pl/104/206), which directed the repeal of [42 U.S.C. 7262](/us/usc/t42/s7262), was executed by repealing [section 652 of Pub. L. 95–91](/us/pl/95/91/s652), which was classified to this section, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
