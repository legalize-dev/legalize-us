# § 3873. Exemption from Metric Conversion Act of 1975

##### § 3873. Exemption from Metric Conversion Act of 1975

The Commission is exempt from the provisions of the Metric Conversion Act of 1975 ([15 U.S.C. 205a](/us/usc/t15/s205a) et seq.).

*([Pub. L. 96–70, title III, § 3302](/us/pl/96/70/tIII/s3302), as added [Pub. L. 104–201, div. C, title XXXV, § 3547](/us/pl/104/201/dC/tXXXV/s3547), Sept. 23, 1996, [110 Stat. 2868](/us/stat/110/2868).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Metric Conversion Act of 1975, referred to in section catchline and text, is [Pub. L. 94–168](/us/pl/94/168), Dec. 23, 1975, [89 Stat. 1007](/us/stat/89/1007), which is classified generally to subchapter II (§ 205a et seq.) of chapter 6 of Title 15, Commerce and Trade. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 205a of Title 15](/us/usc/t15/s205a) and Tables.
