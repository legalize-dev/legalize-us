# § 1477a. Omitted

##### § 1477a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Jan. 27, 1948, ch. 36, title VII, § 703](/us/act/1948-01-27/ch36/tVII/s703), as added Mar. 30, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–264](/us/pl/92/264), [86 Stat. 114](/us/stat/86/114); amended Aug. 20, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–394](/us/pl/92/394), [86 Stat. 577](/us/stat/86/577); July 6, 1973, [Pub. L. 93–59](/us/pl/93/59), [87 Stat. 142](/us/stat/87/142); 1977 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 7(a)(1), 42 F.R. 62461, [91 Stat. 1637](/us/stat/91/1637), which authorized appropriations for Radio Free Europe and Radio Liberty for fiscal year 1973 in an amount of $38,520,000, and also authorized an additional $1,150,000 for nondiscretionary costs, has been omitted in view of the authorization not being extended later than fiscal year 1973.
