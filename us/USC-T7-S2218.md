# § 2218. Fee for administering or taking oaths, affirmations, and affidavits

##### § 2218. Fee for administering or taking oaths, affirmations, and affidavits

No officer, agent, or employee of the Department of Agriculture shall demand or accept any fee or compensation whatsoever for administering or taking any oath, affirmation, or affidavit under the authority conferred by [section 2217 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2217).

*([Jan. 31, 1925, ch. 124, § 2](/us/act/1925-01-31/ch124/s2), [43 Stat. 803](/us/stat/43/803).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 522 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s522) prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Department of the Interior Personnel

Application to Department of the Interior employees, see note under [section 2217 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s2217).
