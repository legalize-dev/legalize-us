# §§ 95 to 98a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–649, title II, § 204(b)](/us/pl/86/649/tII/s204/b), July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 507](/us/stat/74/507)

##### §§ 95 to 98a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–649, title II, § 204(b)](/us/pl/86/649/tII/s204/b), July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 507](/us/stat/74/507)

Section 95, acts [Mar. 26, 1908, ch. 102, § 1](/us/act/1908-03-26/ch102/s1), [35 Stat. 48](/us/stat/35/48); [Dec. 11, 1919, ch. 5](/us/act/1919-12-11/ch5), [41 Stat. 366](/us/stat/41/366), related to repayment of purchase moneys paid under applications rejected.

Section 96, acts [Mar. 26, 1908, ch. 102, § 2](/us/act/1908-03-26/ch102/s2), [35 Stat. 48](/us/stat/35/48); [Dec. 11, 1919, ch. 5](/us/act/1919-12-11/ch5), [41 Stat. 366](/us/stat/41/366), related to repayment of excess payments.

Section 97, acts [Mar. 26, 1908, ch. 102, § 3](/us/act/1908-03-26/ch102/s3), [35 Stat. 48](/us/stat/35/48); [Dec. 11, 1919, ch. 5](/us/act/1919-12-11/ch5), [41 Stat. 366](/us/stat/41/366); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, § 403, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7876, [60 Stat. 1100](/us/stat/60/1100), related to certification of amount of excess moneys and repayment.

Section 98, [act Mar. 26, 1908, ch. 102, § 4](/us/act/1908-03-26/ch102/s4), as added [Dec. 11, 1919, ch. 5](/us/act/1919-12-11/ch5), [41 Stat. 367](/us/stat/41/367), related to rules and regulations.

Section 98a, [act June 27, 1930, ch. 642](/us/act/1930-06-27/ch642), [46 Stat. 822](/us/stat/46/822), made sections 95 to 98 of this title applicable to all payments in excess of lawful requirements made under statutes relating to disposition of public lands.
