# §§ 114d–2 to 114d–6. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–152, § 3](/us/pl/92/152/s3), Nov. 5, 1971, [85 Stat. 419](/us/stat/85/419)

##### §§ 114d–2 to 114d–6. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–152, § 3](/us/pl/92/152/s3), Nov. 5, 1971, [85 Stat. 419](/us/stat/85/419)

Section 114d–2, [Pub. L. 90–388, § 1](/us/pl/90/388/s1), July 6, 1968, [82 Stat. 294](/us/stat/82/294), provided for cooperation with Central America in control and eradication of foot-and-mouth disease or rinderpest.

Section 114d–3, [Pub. L. 90–388, § 2](/us/pl/90/388/s2), July 6, 1968, [82 Stat. 294](/us/stat/82/294), provided for uses of funds.

Section 114d–4, [Pub. L. 90–388, § 3](/us/pl/90/388/s3), July 6, 1968, [82 Stat. 294](/us/stat/82/294), defined governments of Central America.

Section 114d–5, [Pub. L. 90–388, § 4](/us/pl/90/388/s4), July 6, 1968, [82 Stat. 294](/us/stat/82/294), provided for cooperation with public and private organizations and individuals.

Section 114d–6, [Pub. L. 90–388, § 5](/us/pl/90/388/s5), July 6, 1968, [82 Stat. 294](/us/stat/82/294), provided for authorization of appropriations.
