# §§ 636 to 639. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### §§ 636 to 639. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 636, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 126](/us/stat/64/126), prohibited cruel and unusual punishments. See [section 855 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s855), Armed Forces.

Section 637, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 126](/us/stat/64/126), related to maximum limits of punishment. See [section 856 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s856).

Section 638, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 126](/us/stat/64/126), provided for effective date of sentences. See [section 857 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s857).

Section 639, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 126](/us/stat/64/126), related to execution of confinement. See [section 854 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s854).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1957, see [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53) footnote, [70A Stat. 680](/us/stat/70A/680).
