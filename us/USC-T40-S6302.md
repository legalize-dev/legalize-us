# § 6302. Public use of grounds

##### § 6302. Public use of grounds

Public travel in, and occupancy of, the grounds specified under [section 6301 of this title](/us/usc/t40/s6301) are restricted to the sidewalks and other paved surfaces, except in the National Zoological Park.

*([Pub. L. 107–217](/us/pl/107/217), Aug. 21, 2002, [116 Stat. 1185](/us/stat/116/1185).)*
