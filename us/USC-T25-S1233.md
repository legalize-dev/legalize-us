#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–417, § 3](/us/pl/91/417/s3), Sept. 25, 1970, [84 Stat. 868](/us/stat/84/868), which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
