# § 8802. Sales: members of Army, Air Force, and Space Force; prices

##### § 8802. Sales: members of Army, Air Force, and Space Force; prices

The Navy and the Marine Corps shall sell subsistence supplies to any member of the Army, the Air Force, or the Space Force at prices charged members of the naval service.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 471](/us/stat/70A/471), § 7602; renumbered § 8802, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(d)(9)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/d/9), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1837](/us/stat/132/1837); [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title IX, § 924(d)(2)(A)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tIX/s924/d/2/A), (B), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3826](/us/stat/134/3826).)*

This section is expanded to cover sales to members of the Air Force, as authorized by § 305(a) of the National Security Act of 1947, as amended ([5 U.S.C. 171](/us/usc/t5/s171)*l*).
