# §§ 1070f to 1070f–6. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–315, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/110/315/tIV/s411), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3226](/us/stat/122/3226)

##### §§ 1070f to 1070f–6. Repealed. [Pub. L. 110–315, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/110/315/tIV/s411), Aug. 14, 2008, [122 Stat. 3226](/us/stat/122/3226)

Section 1070f, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 420D](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s420D), as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/105/244/tIV/s411), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1671](/us/stat/112/1671), set out congressional findings.

A prior section 1070f, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 420B](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s420B), as added [Pub. L. 99–498, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/99/498/tIV/s401/a), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1352](/us/stat/100/1352); amended [Pub. L. 100–50, § 9](/us/pl/100/50/s9), June 3, 1987, [101 Stat. 341](/us/stat/101/341); [Pub. L. 102–325, title IV, § 409](/us/pl/102/325/tIV/s409), July 23, 1992, [106 Stat. 510](/us/stat/106/510), related to special child care services for disadvantaged college students, prior to the general amendment of this subpart by [Pub. L. 105–244](/us/pl/105/244).

Section 1070f–1, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 420E](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s420E), as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/105/244/tIV/s411), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1672](/us/stat/112/1672), related to the purpose of this subpart and the program authorized.

Section 1070f–2, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 420F](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s420F), as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/105/244/tIV/s411), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1672](/us/stat/112/1672), related to grant application.

Section 1070f–3, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 420G](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s420G), as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/105/244/tIV/s411), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1672](/us/stat/112/1672), set out authorized activities for funding.

Section 1070f–4, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 420H](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s420H), as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/105/244/tIV/s411), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1673](/us/stat/112/1673), provided for a matching requirement limiting Federal funds to not more than 50 percent of the cost of a project.

Section 1070f–5, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 420I](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s420I), as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/105/244/tIV/s411), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1673](/us/stat/112/1673), required that the Secretary use a peer review process to review applications and make recommendations for funding.

Section 1070f–6, [Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 420J](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s420J), as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/105/244/tIV/s411), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1673](/us/stat/112/1673), authorized appropriations for fiscal year 1999 and each of the 4 succeeding fiscal years.
