# § 4234. Repayment

##### § 4234. Repayment

(1) reduce future grant payments on behalf of the Department by an amount equal to the grant amounts used for that housing (under the authority of [section 4238(a)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t25/s4238/a/2)); or

(2) require repayment to the Secretary of any amount equal to those grant amounts.

*([Pub. L. 104–330, title VIII, § 815](/us/pl/104/330/tVIII/s815), as added [Pub. L. 106–568, title II, § 203](/us/pl/106/568/tII/s203), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2890](/us/stat/114/2890), and [Pub. L. 106–569, title V, § 513](/us/pl/106/569/tV/s513), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2984](/us/stat/114/2984).)*
