# §§ 646 to 663. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### §§ 646 to 663. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 646, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 127](/us/stat/64/127), related to errors of law and to lesser included offense. See [section 859 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s859), Armed Forces.

Section 647, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 127](/us/stat/64/127), provided for initial action on record. See [section 860 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s860).

Section 648, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 127](/us/stat/64/127), provided for initial action on general court-martial records. See [section 861 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s861).

Section 649, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 127](/us/stat/64/127), provided for reconsideration and revision. See [section 862 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s862).

Section 650, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 127](/us/stat/64/127), related to rehearings. See [section 863 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s863).

Section 651, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 128](/us/stat/64/128), related to approval by convening authority. See [section 864 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s864).

Section 652, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 128](/us/stat/64/128), provided for disposition of records after review by convening authority. See [section 865 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s865).

Section 653, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 128](/us/stat/64/128), provided for review by board of review. See [section 866 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s866).

Section 654, acts [May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 130](/us/stat/64/130); [Mar. 2, 1955, ch. 9, § 1(i)](/us/act/1955-03-02/ch9/s1/i), [69 Stat. 10](/us/stat/69/10), provided for review by the Court of Military Appeals. See [section 867 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s867).

Section 655, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 130](/us/stat/64/130), authorized establishment of branch offices of The Judge Advocate General. See [section 868 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s868).

Section 656, [act May 30, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-30/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 130](/us/stat/64/130), provided for review in office of The Judge Advocate General. See [section 869 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s869).

Section 657, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 130](/us/stat/64/130), provided for appellate counsel. See [section 870 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s870).

Section 658, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 131](/us/stat/64/131), provided for execution of sentence and suspension of sentence. See [section 871 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s871).

Section 659, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 131](/us/stat/64/131), related to vacation of suspension of a court-martial sentence. See [section 872 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s872).

Section 660, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 132](/us/stat/64/132), provided for a petition for a new trial. See [section 873 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s873).

Section 661, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 132](/us/stat/64/132), authorized remission and suspension of sentences. See [section 874 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s874).

Section 662, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 132](/us/stat/64/132), related to restoration of rights, privileges, and property. See [section 875 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s875).

Section 663, [act May 5, 1950, ch. 169, § 1](/us/act/1950-05-05/ch169/s1), [64 Stat. 132](/us/stat/64/132), related to finality of court-martial judgments. See [section 876 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s876).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1957, see [act Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53) footnote, [70A Stat. 680](/us/stat/70A/680).
