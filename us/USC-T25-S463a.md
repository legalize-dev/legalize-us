# § 463a. Omitted

##### § 463a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 28, 1937, ch. 527, § 1](/us/act/1937-07-28/ch527/s1), [50 Stat. 536](/us/stat/50/536), which related to extension of boundaries of Papago Indian Reservation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
