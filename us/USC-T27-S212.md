# § 212. Omitted

##### § 212. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 26, 1936, ch. 830, title V, § 504](/us/act/1936-06-26/ch830/tV/s504), [49 Stat. 1965](/us/stat/49/1965), proposed a transfer of the appropriations authorized for the Federal Alcohol Administration created by [section 202 of this title](/us/usc/t27/s202) to the Administration created by [section 202b of this title](/us/usc/t27/s202b). It was to have taken effect when a majority of the members authorized to be appointed under [section 202b of this title](/us/usc/t27/s202b) took office. The members, however, were never appointed.
