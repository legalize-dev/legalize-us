# § 1471d. Availability of appropriations for transportation, reprint, telephone, and library membership expenses

##### § 1471d. Availability of appropriations for transportation, reprint, telephone, and library membership expenses

Appropriations in this title 11 See References in Text note below. or appropriations made under this title 1 in subsequent Energy and Water Development Appropriations Acts shall on and after October 2, 1992, be available for hire, maintenance, and operation of aircraft; hire of passenger motor vehicles; purchases of reprints; payment for telephone services in private residences in the field, when authorized under regulations approved by the Secretary; and the payment of dues, when authorized by the Secretary, for library memberships in societies or associations which issue publications to members only or at a price to members lower than to subscribers who are not members.

*([Pub. L. 102–377, title II, § 204](/us/pl/102/377/tII/s204), Oct. 2, 1992, [106 Stat. 1332](/us/stat/106/1332).)*
