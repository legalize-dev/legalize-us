# § 1041. Omitted

##### § 1041. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 106–568, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/106/568/tVII/s702), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2913](/us/stat/114/2913), which set out Congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 106–568, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/106/568/tVII/s701), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2913](/us/stat/114/2913), provided that title VII of [Pub. L. 106–568](/us/pl/106/568), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Shawnee Tribe Status Act of 2000”.
