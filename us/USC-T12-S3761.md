# § 3761. Foreclosure costs

##### § 3761. Foreclosure costs

(1) Advertising and postage Necessary advertising costs and postage incurred in giving notice pursuant to sections 3758 and 3760 of this title.

(2) Mileage Mileage (determined by the most reasonable road distance) for posting notices and for the foreclosure commissioner’s or auctioneer’s attendance at the sale, as provided in [section 1821 of title 28](/us/usc/t28/s1821).

(3) Title and lien search Reasonable and necessary costs incurred in connection with any search of title and lien records.

(4) Recordation fees Costs incurred to record documents.

(5) Commission A commission for the foreclosure commissioner (if the foreclosure commissioner is not an employee of the United States) for the conduct of the foreclosure, to the extent such a commission is authorized by the Secretary.

*([Pub. L. 103–327, title II](/us/pl/103/327/tII), Sept. 28, 1994, [108 Stat. 2316](/us/stat/108/2316).)*
