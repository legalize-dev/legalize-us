# § 1627. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII, § 7367(c)(6)](/us/pl/100/690/tVII/s7367/c/6), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4480](/us/stat/102/4480)

##### § 1627. Repealed. [Pub. L. 100–690, title VII, § 7367(c)(6)](/us/pl/100/690/tVII/s7367/c/6), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4480](/us/stat/102/4480)

Section, [act June 17, 1930, ch. 497, title IV, § 627](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497/tIV/s627), as added Oct. 25, 1984, [Pub. L. 98–547, title III, § 302](/us/pl/98/547/tIII/s302), [98 Stat. 2771](/us/stat/98/2771), related to unlawful importation or exportation of certain vehicles and equipment.

Another section 627 of act June 17, 1930, as added by [Pub. L. 98–573, title II, § 205](/us/pl/98/573/tII/s205), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2974](/us/stat/98/2974), is classified to [section 1627a of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1627a).
