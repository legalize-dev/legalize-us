# § 104. Commercially available off-the-shelf item

##### § 104. Commercially available off-the-shelf item

(1) means an item that—

(A) is a commercial product (as described in [section 103(1) of this title](/us/usc/t41/s103/1));

(B) is sold in substantial quantities in the commercial marketplace; and

(C) is offered to the Federal Government, without modification, in the same form in which it is sold in the commercial marketplace; but

(2) does not include bulk cargo, as defined in [section 40102(4) of title 46](/us/usc/t46/s40102/4), such as agricultural products and petroleum products.

*([Pub. L. 111–350, § 3](/us/pl/111/350/s3), Jan. 4, 2011, [124 Stat. 3679](/us/stat/124/3679); [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 836(a)(2)(B)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s836/a/2/B), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1860](/us/stat/132/1860); [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title X, § 1081(d)(4)(A)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tX/s1081/d/4/A), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3874](/us/stat/134/3874).)*

In paragraph (2), the words “[section 40102(4) of title 46](/us/usc/t46/s40102/4)” are substituted for “section 3 of the Shipping Act of 1984 (46 U.S.C. App. 1702)” because of [section 18(c) of Public Law 109–304](/us/pl/109/304/s18/c) (46 U.S.C. note prec. 101).
