# § 4207. Reporting requirement

##### § 4207. Reporting requirement

(1) the effects, if any, of Federal programs, authorities, and administrative activities with respect to the protection of United States farmland; and

(2) the results of the reviews of existing policies and procedures required under [section 4203(a) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s4203/a).

*([Pub. L. 97–98, title XV, § 1546](/us/pl/97/98/tXV/s1546), Dec. 22, 1981, [95 Stat. 1343](/us/stat/95/1343); [Pub. L. 99–198, title XII, § 1255(a)](/us/pl/99/198/tXII/s1255/a), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1518](/us/stat/99/1518).)*
