# §§ 1191 to 1202. Omitted

##### §§ 1191 to 1202. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 1191 to 1202 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.

Section 1191, acts [Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 11](/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s11), [39 Stat. 548](/us/stat/39/548); [July 21, 1921, ch. 51](/us/act/1921-07-21/ch51), [42 Stat. 145](/us/stat/42/145); [May 31, 1922, ch. 203](/us/act/1922-05-31/ch203), [42 Stat. 599](/us/stat/42/599), related to bond issues to anticipate taxes and revenue.

Section 1192, [act Feb. 6, 1905, ch. 453, § 2](/us/act/1905-02-06/ch453/s2), [33 Stat. 689](/us/stat/33/689), related to bond issues for public improvements.

Section 1193, [act Feb. 6, 1905, ch. 453, § 1](/us/act/1905-02-06/ch453/s1), [33 Stat. 689](/us/stat/33/689), related to government bonds as exempt from taxation.

Section 1194, acts [July 1, 1902, ch. 1369, § 66](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369/s66), [32 Stat. 707](/us/stat/32/707); [Feb. 6, 1905, ch. 453, § 3](/us/act/1905-02-06/ch453/s3), [33 Stat. 690](/us/stat/33/690), related to municipal indebtedness for improvements.

Section 1195, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369, § 67](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369/s67), [32 Stat. 707](/us/stat/32/707), related to denominations of bonds.

Section 1196, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369, § 68](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369/s68), [32 Stat. 708](/us/stat/32/708), related to use of funds from sale of bonds.

Section 1197, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369, § 69](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369/s69), [32 Stat. 708](/us/stat/32/708), related to taxes to pay bonds and creation of a sinking fund.

Section 1198, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369), §§ 70, 71, [32 Stat. 708](/us/stat/32/708), related to bonds for sewers and water supply in Manila.

Section 1199, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369, § 72](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369/s72), [32 Stat. 708](/us/stat/32/708), related to use of funds from sale of bonds for sewers and water supply in Manila.

Section 1200, [act July 1, 1902, ch. 1369, § 73](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369/s73), [32 Stat. 708](/us/stat/32/708), related to taxes and sinking fund for payment of bonds for sewers and water supply in Manila.

Section 1201, [act Feb. 6, 1905, ch. 453, § 4](/us/act/1905-02-06/ch453/s4), [33 Stat. 690](/us/stat/33/690), related to guarantee of railroad bonds and the contract of guaranty.

Section 1202, [act Feb. 6, 1905, ch. 453, § 4](/us/act/1905-02-06/ch453/s4), [33 Stat. 690](/us/stat/33/690), related to jurisdiction of Supreme Court of Philippines over actions brought under [section 1201 of this title](/us/usc/t48/s1201).
