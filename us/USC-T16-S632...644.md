# §§ 632 to 644. Repealed. [Feb. 26, 1944, ch. 65, § 18](/us/act/1944-02-26/ch65/s18), [58 Stat. 104](/us/stat/58/104)

##### §§ 632 to 644. Repealed. [Feb. 26, 1944, ch. 65, § 18](/us/act/1944-02-26/ch65/s18), [58 Stat. 104](/us/stat/58/104)

Section 632, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s1), [37 Stat. 499](/us/stat/37/499), prohibited killing seal or sea otter in certain waters of North Pacific.

Section 633, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 2](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s2), [37 Stat. 500](/us/stat/37/500), forbade equipping vessels for pelagic sealing or sea-otter hunting and use of ports of United States by such vessels.

Section 634, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 3](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s3), [37 Stat. 500](/us/stat/37/500), permitted natives to carry on pelagic fishing.

Section 635, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 4](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s4), [37 Stat. 500](/us/stat/37/500), prohibited importing illegally taken skins, and forfeiture thereof.

Section 636, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 5](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s5), [37 Stat. 500](/us/stat/37/500); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1), [37 Stat. 736](/us/stat/37/736); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. II, § 4(e) eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2731, [53 Stat. 1433](/us/stat/53/1433), related to regulations made by President, and enforcement of law and regulations.

Section 637, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 6](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s6), [37 Stat. 501](/us/stat/37/501), related to punishment for violation of law, and forfeitures.

Section 638, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 7](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s7), [37 Stat. 501](/us/stat/37/501), related to presumption as to violations.

Section 639, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 8](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s8), [37 Stat. 501](/us/stat/37/501), related to venue of prosecutions.

Section 640, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 9](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s9), [37 Stat. 501](/us/stat/37/501), related to guard or patrol of waters, and seizure and search of certain vessels.

Section 641, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 10](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s10), [37 Stat. 501](/us/stat/37/501), related to seizure of vessels outside of jurisdiction of signatory powers, and procedure.

Section 642, acts [Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 11](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s11), [37 Stat. 502](/us/stat/37/502); [June 22, 1916, ch. 171](/us/act/1916-06-22/ch171), [39 Stat. 236](/us/stat/39/236); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1), [37 Stat. 736](/us/stat/37/736); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. II, § 4(e), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2731, [53 Stat. 1433](/us/stat/53/1433), which related to delivery by, receipt and disposal of skins by the United States under convention of July 7, 1911, and performance of articles thereunder.

Section 643, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 12](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s12), [37 Stat. 502](/us/stat/37/502), related to definition of “Pelagic sealing”, and construction of “person”.

Section 643a, [act Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 373, § 13](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch373/s13), [37 Stat. 502](/us/stat/37/502), related to continuance in force of sections 632–643.

Section 644, R.S. § 1956; acts [Apr. 21, 1910, ch. 183, § 4](/us/act/1910-04-21/ch183/s4), [36 Stat. 327](/us/stat/36/327); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1), [37 Stat. 736](/us/stat/37/736); [Jan. 13, 1925, ch. 75](/us/act/1925-01-13/ch75), §§ 2, 14, [43 Stat. 739](/us/stat/43/739), 747; 1939 Reorg. Plan No. II, § 4(e), eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2731, [53 Stat. 1433](/us/stat/53/1433), forbade killing of certain fur-bearing animals in Alaska, punishment and forfeiture, power of Secretary of the Interior to authorize killing, and duty to execute law.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Termination of Sections

Sections 632 to 643a were terminated on the authority of former [section 643a of this title](/us/usc/t16/s643a) when Japan abrogated the treaty on Oct. 23, 1940, eff. Oct. 23, 1941. Sections 632 to 644 were later specifically repealed by [act Feb. 26, 1944, ch. 65, § 18](/us/act/1944-02-26/ch65/s18), [58 Stat. 104](/us/stat/58/104).
