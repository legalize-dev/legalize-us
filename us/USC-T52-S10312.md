# § 10312. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 10312. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out the provisions of chapters 103 to 107 of this title.

*([Pub. L. 89–110, title I, § 18](/us/pl/89/110/tI/s18), Aug. 6, 1965, [79 Stat. 446](/us/stat/79/446); renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 91–285, § 2](/us/pl/91/285/s2), June 22, 1970, [84 Stat. 314](/us/stat/84/314).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to section 1973*o* of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
