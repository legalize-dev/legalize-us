# §§ 660 to 665. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 660 to 665. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 660, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 3](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s3), [39 Stat. 361](/us/stat/39/361); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, provided for submission of statements covering salaries paid by land banks.

Section 661, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 3](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s3), [39 Stat. 361](/us/stat/39/361); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, required filing of an annual report to Congress by Farm Credit Administration. See section 2221 et seq. of this title.

Section 662, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 3, [39 Stat. 361](/us/stat/39/361); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 101(d)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s101/d), [73 Stat. 384](/us/stat/73/384), provided for examinations and reports. See [section 2254 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2254).

Section 663, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 3, [39 Stat. 361](/us/stat/39/361); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title III, § 301(a), [69 Stat. 664](/us/stat/69/664); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for statements of condition of land bank associations and land banks. See [section 2254 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2254).

Section 664, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 3](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s3), [39 Stat. 361](/us/stat/39/361); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, authorized preparation of bulletins and circulars by Farm Credit Administration. See [section 2252 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2252).

Section 665, acts [Jan. 23, 1932, ch. 9, § 6](/us/act/1932-01-23/ch9/s6), [47 Stat. 14](/us/stat/47/14); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title I, § 110(d), [69 Stat. 662](/us/stat/69/662), authorized Farm Credit Administration to make rules and regulations. See [section 2252 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2252).
