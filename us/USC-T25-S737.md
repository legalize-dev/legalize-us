# § 737. Omitted

##### § 737. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–89, title II, § 207](/us/pl/100/89/tII/s207), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 672](/us/stat/101/672), which related to gaming activities on the reservation and on lands of the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
