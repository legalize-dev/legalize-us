# § 390h–22. Omitted

##### § 390h–22. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 102–575, title XVI, § 16](/us/pl/102/575/tXVI/s16)——, as added [Pub. L. 110–161, div. C, title II, § 210](/us/pl/110/161/dC/tII/s210), Dec. 26, 2007, [121 Stat. 1955](/us/stat/121/1955), which related to the Cucamonga Valley water recycling project, was omitted pursuant to subsec. (e) of section, which provided that the authority of the Secretary of the Interior to carry out any provisions of this section terminated 10 years after Dec. 26, 2007.

Section was based on section “16——” of [Pub. L. 102–575](/us/pl/102/575). Two other sections “16——” of [Pub. L. 102–575](/us/pl/102/575) have been enacted and are classified to sections 390h–21 and 390h–23 of this title.
