# § 3058. Establishment

##### § 3058. Establishment

The Assistant Secretary, acting through the Administration, shall establish and carry out a program for making allotments to States to pay for the cost of carrying out vulnerable elder rights protection activities.

*([Pub. L. 89–73, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/89/73/tVII/s701), as added [Pub. L. 102–375, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/102/375/tVII/s701), Sept. 30, 1992, [106 Stat. 1271](/us/stat/106/1271); amended [Pub. L. 103–171, § 3(a)(13)](/us/pl/103/171/s3/a/13), Dec. 2, 1993, [107 Stat. 1990](/us/stat/107/1990).)*
