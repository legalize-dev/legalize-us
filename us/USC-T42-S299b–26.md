# § 299b–26. Severability

##### § 299b–26. Severability

If any provision of this part is held to be unconstitutional, the remainder of this part shall not be affected.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title IX, § 927, formerly § 926, as added [Pub. L. 109–41, § 2(a)(5)](/us/pl/109/41/s2/a/5), July 29, 2005, [119 Stat. 434](/us/stat/119/434); renumbered § 927, [Pub. L. 111–148, title III, § 3509(e)(1)](/us/pl/111/148/tIII/s3509/e/1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 534](/us/stat/124/534).)*
