# § 701h–2. No adverse effect on processes

##### § 701h–2. No adverse effect on processes

(1) the process or timeline for development and implementation of other water resources development projects by other non-Federal entities that do not use such authorities; or

(2) the process for including such projects in the President’s annual budget submission to Congress under [section 1105(a) of title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1105/a).

*([Pub. L. 115–270, title I, § 1166(b)](/us/pl/115/270/tI/s1166/b), Oct. 23, 2018, [132 Stat. 3797](/us/stat/132/3797).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### “Secretary” Defined

Secretary means the Secretary of the Army, see [section 102 of Pub. L. 115–270](/us/pl/115/270/s102), set out as a note under [section 2201 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2201).
