# §§ 102 to 104. Repealed. [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 651(a)(1), [46 Stat. 762](/us/stat/46/762)

##### §§ 102 to 104. Repealed. [June 17, 1930, ch. 497](/us/act/1930-06-17/ch497), title IV, § 651(a)(1), [46 Stat. 762](/us/stat/46/762)

Section 102, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356, title III, § 318(a)](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356/tIII/s318/a), [42 Stat. 948](/us/stat/42/948), related to conveyance of conversion and production cost information to the President and Congress. See [section 1332(d) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1332/d).

Section 103, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356, title III, § 318(c)](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356/tIII/s318/c), [42 Stat. 947](/us/stat/42/947), related to powers of the commission under title VII of the Revenue Act of 1916 and its power to require statements by importers and any American grower, producer, manufacturer, or seller as to their selling prices in the United States.

Section 104, [act Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 356, title III, § 318(b)](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch356/tIII/s318/b), [42 Stat. 947](/us/stat/42/947), defined the terms “article” and “import costs”. See [section 1332(e) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1332/e).
