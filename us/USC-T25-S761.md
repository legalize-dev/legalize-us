# § 761. Omitted

##### § 761. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–227, § 2](/us/pl/96/227/s2), Apr. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 317](/us/stat/94/317); [Pub. L. 109–126, § 4](/us/pl/109/126/s4), Dec. 7, 2005, [119 Stat. 2547](/us/stat/119/2547), which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 96–227, § 1](/us/pl/96/227/s1), Apr. 3, 1980, [94 Stat. 317](/us/stat/94/317), provided that [Pub. L. 96–227](/us/pl/96/227), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Paiute Indian Tribe of Utah Restoration Act”.
