# § 2372. Date of transfer of utilities

##### § 2372. Date of transfer of utilities

Transfers of utilities shall be made as soon as possible, but in any event, not later than five years after August 4, 1955, in the case of Oak Ridge and Richland, or, in the case of Los Alamos, not later than June 30, 1998.

*([Aug. 4, 1955, ch. 543](/us/act/1955-08-04/ch543), ch. 7, § 72, [69 Stat. 480](/us/stat/69/480); [Pub. L. 87–719, § 20](/us/pl/87/719/s20), Sept. 28, 1962, [76 Stat. 666](/us/stat/76/666); [Pub. L. 104–106, div. C, title XXXI, § 3161(a)](/us/pl/104/106/dC/tXXXI/s3161/a), Feb. 10, 1996, [110 Stat. 627](/us/stat/110/627).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments
