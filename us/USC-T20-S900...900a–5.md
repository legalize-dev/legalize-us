# §§ 900 to 900a–5. Omitted

##### §§ 900 to 900a–5. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general revision of title IX of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX](/us/pl/89/10/tIX), as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/92/318/tV/s504/a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347](/us/stat/86/347), by [Pub. L. 95–561, title VIII, § 802](/us/pl/95/561/tVIII/s802), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2292](/us/stat/92/2292).

Section 900, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/89/10/tIX/s901), as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/92/318/tV/s504/a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 346](/us/stat/86/346), contained the Congressional statement of policy with regard to the ethnic heritage program.

Section 900a, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 902](/us/pl/89/10/tIX/s902), as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/92/318/tV/s504/a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347](/us/stat/86/347), authorized grants for the ethnic heritage studies program.

Section 900a–1, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 903](/us/pl/89/10/tIX/s903), as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/92/318/tV/s504/a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347](/us/stat/86/347); amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 111(b)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s111/b), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 513](/us/stat/88/513), described the authorized activities for the ethnic heritage program.

Section 900a–2, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 904](/us/pl/89/10/tIX/s904), as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/92/318/tV/s504/a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347](/us/stat/86/347), related to the form and content of applications for assistance.

Section 900a–3, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 905](/us/pl/89/10/tIX/s905), as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/92/318/tV/s504/a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 347](/us/stat/86/347), related to the administration of the program.

Section 900a–4, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 906](/us/pl/89/10/tIX/s906), as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/92/318/tV/s504/a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 348](/us/stat/86/348), established the National Advisory Council on Ethnic Heritage Studies.

Section 900a–5, [Pub. L. 89–10, title IX, § 907](/us/pl/89/10/tIX/s907), as added [Pub. L. 92–318, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/92/318/tV/s504/a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 348](/us/stat/86/348); amended [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 111(a)(1)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s111/a/1), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 513](/us/stat/88/513), authorized appropriations for fiscal years through fiscal year 1978.
