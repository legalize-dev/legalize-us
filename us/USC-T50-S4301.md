# § 4301. Designation of chapter

##### § 4301. Designation of chapter

This chapter shall be known as the “Trading with the enemy 11 So in original. Act.”

*([Oct. 6, 1917, ch. 106, § 1](/us/act/1917-10-06/ch106/s1), [40 Stat. 411](/us/stat/40/411).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [act Oct. 6, 1917, ch. 106](/us/act/1917-10-06/ch106), [40 Stat. 411](/us/stat/40/411), known as the Trading with the enemy Act, also known as the Trading with the Enemy Act, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to section 1 of the former Appendix to this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
