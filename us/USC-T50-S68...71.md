# §§ 68 to 71. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### §§ 68 to 71. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 68, acts [Mar. 4, 1909, ch. 319, § 47](/us/act/1909-03-04/ch319/s47), [35 Stat. 1075](/us/stat/35/1075); [June 28, 1950, ch. 383](/us/act/1950-06-28/ch383), title IV, § 402(i), [64 Stat. 273](/us/stat/64/273); [Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 654, § 2(28)](/us/act/1951-10-31/ch654/s2/28), [65 Stat. 708](/us/stat/65/708), authorized sale of obsolete ordnance for public parks, public buildings and soldiers’ monuments purposes. See sections 4684 and 9684 of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 69, [act Mar. 2, 1905, ch. 1307](/us/act/1905-03-02/ch1307), [33 Stat. 841](/us/stat/33/841), authorized sale of individual pieces of armament. See [section 2574 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s2574).

Section 70, acts [Mar. 3, 1909, ch. 252](/us/act/1909-03-03/ch252), [35 Stat. 751](/us/stat/35/751); [June 28, 1950, ch. 383](/us/act/1950-06-28/ch383), title IV, § 402(h), [64 Stat. 273](/us/stat/64/273), authorized sale of ordnance property to officers of the Navy and Marine Corps. See section 4625 and 9625 of Title 10.

Section 71, [act Mar. 3, 1909, ch. 252](/us/act/1909-03-03/ch252), [35 Stat. 750](/us/stat/35/750), authorized sale of ordnance stores to civilian employees of Army and to American National Red Cross. See sections 4625 and 9625 of Title 10.
