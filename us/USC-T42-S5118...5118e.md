# §§ 5118 to 5118e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–235, title I, § 131](/us/pl/104/235/tI/s131), Oct. 3, 1996, [110 Stat. 3088](/us/stat/110/3088)

##### §§ 5118 to 5118e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–235, title I, § 131](/us/pl/104/235/tI/s131), Oct. 3, 1996, [110 Stat. 3088](/us/stat/110/3088)

Section 5118, [Pub. L. 93–247, title III, § 301](/us/pl/93/247/tIII/s301), as added [Pub. L. 101–645, title VI, § 661(b)](/us/pl/101/645/tVI/s661/b), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4755](/us/stat/104/4755), related to demonstration grants for prevention of inappropriate separation from family and for prevention of child abuse and neglect.

Section 5118a, [Pub. L. 93–247, title III, § 302](/us/pl/93/247/tIII/s302), as added [Pub. L. 101–645, title VI, § 661(b)](/us/pl/101/645/tVI/s661/b), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4757](/us/stat/104/4757), related to joint training of appropriate service personnel with respect to certain subjects and additional authorized activities for which a grantee may expend grant funds.

Section 5118b, [Pub. L. 93–247, title III, § 303](/us/pl/93/247/tIII/s303), as added [Pub. L. 101–645, title VI, § 661(b)](/us/pl/101/645/tVI/s661/b), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4757](/us/stat/104/4757), related to additional agreements required of agencies, evaluations of effectiveness of demonstration programs, report to Congress, and restriction on use of grant to purchase or improve real property.

Section 5118c, [Pub. L. 93–247, title III, § 304](/us/pl/93/247/tIII/s304), as added [Pub. L. 101–645, title VI, § 661(b)](/us/pl/101/645/tVI/s661/b), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4759](/us/stat/104/4759), related to required submission of description of intended uses of grant.

Section 5118d, [Pub. L. 93–247, title III, § 305](/us/pl/93/247/tIII/s305), as added [Pub. L. 101–645, title VI, § 661(b)](/us/pl/101/645/tVI/s661/b), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4759](/us/stat/104/4759), related to requirement of submission of application for grant.

Section 5118e, [Pub. L. 93–247, title III, § 306](/us/pl/93/247/tIII/s306), as added [Pub. L. 101–645, title VI, § 661(b)](/us/pl/101/645/tVI/s661/b), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4760](/us/stat/104/4760); amended [Pub. L. 102–295, title I, § 131](/us/pl/102/295/tI/s131), May 28, 1992, [106 Stat. 199](/us/stat/106/199), related to authorization of appropriations for carrying out this subchapter.
