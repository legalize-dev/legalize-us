# § 1704. Omitted

##### § 1704. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–395, § 5](/us/pl/95/395/s5), Sept. 30, 1978, [92 Stat. 814](/us/stat/92/814), which related to option agreements to purchase private settlement lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
