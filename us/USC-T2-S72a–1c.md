# § 72a–1c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–26, title I, § 106(f)](/us/pl/95/26/tI/s106/f), May 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 84](/us/stat/91/84)

##### § 72a–1c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–26, title I, § 106(f)](/us/pl/95/26/tI/s106/f), May 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 84](/us/stat/91/84)

Section, [Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 108](/us/pl/94/59/tI/s108), July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 276](/us/stat/89/276); [Pub. L. 94–440, title I, § 102](/us/pl/94/440/tI/s102), Oct. 1, 1976, [90 Stat. 1443](/us/stat/90/1443), authorized Senators to hire staff assistance in connection with their committee memberships. See [section 4332 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s4332).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 95–26, title I, § 106(g)(1)](/us/pl/95/26/tI/s106/g/1), May 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 84](/us/stat/91/84), which provided that the repeal is effective Mar. 1, 1977, was repealed by [Pub. L. 95–94, title I, § 111(e)(1)](/us/pl/95/94/tI/s111/e/1), Aug. 5, 1977, [91 Stat. 663](/us/stat/91/663).
