# §§ 212 to 214. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–621, § 10(q)](/us/pl/98/621/s10/q), Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3381](/us/stat/98/3381)

##### §§ 212 to 214. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–621, § 10(q)](/us/pl/98/621/s10/q), Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3381](/us/stat/98/3381)

Section 212, acts [June 23, 1874, ch. 465, § 1](/us/act/1874-06-23/ch465/s1), [18 Stat. 251](/us/stat/18/251); [Aug. 7, 1882, ch. 433](/us/act/1882-08-07/ch433), [22 Stat. 330](/us/stat/22/330); [July 1, 1916, ch. 209](/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209), [39 Stat. 309](/us/stat/39/309); 1940 Reorg. Plan No. IV, § 11(a), eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2422, [54 Stat. 1236](/us/stat/54/1236); 1953 Reorg. Plan No. 1, § 5, eff. Apr. 11, 1953, 18 F.R. 2053, [67 Stat. 631](/us/stat/67/631); Oct. 17, 1979, [Pub. L. 96–88, title V, § 509(b)](/us/pl/96/88/tV/s509/b), [93 Stat. 695](/us/stat/93/695), related to transfer to Saint Elizabeths Hospital of insane prisoners.

Section 213, acts [June 23, 1874, ch. 465, § 2](/us/act/1874-06-23/ch465/s2), [18 Stat. 251](/us/stat/18/251); [July 1, 1916, ch. 209](/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209), [39 Stat. 309](/us/stat/39/309), related to accommodation of insane convicts in State asylums and compensation therefor.

Section 214, [act June 23, 1874, ch. 465, § 3](/us/act/1874-06-23/ch465/s3), [18 Stat. 252](/us/stat/18/252), related to return of transferred insane convicts and prisoners to prison on restoration of their sanity.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1987, see [section 12(b) of Pub. L. 98–621](/us/pl/98/621/s12/b), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 225 of this title](/us/usc/t24/s225).
