# § 701. Omitted

##### § 701. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 733, § 11](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch733/s11), [68 Stat. 726](/us/stat/68/726), which related to advances and expenditures from tribal funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
