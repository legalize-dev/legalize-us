# § 711. Omitted

##### § 711. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–195, § 2](/us/pl/95/195/s2), Nov. 18, 1977, [91 Stat. 1415](/us/stat/91/1415), which set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 95–195, § 1](/us/pl/95/195/s1), Nov. 18, 1977, [91 Stat. 1415](/us/stat/91/1415), provided that [Pub. L. 95–195](/us/pl/95/195), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Siletz Indian Tribe Restoration Act”.
