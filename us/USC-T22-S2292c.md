##### § 2292c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–74, div. I, title VII, § 7034(l)](/us/pl/112/74/dI/tVII/s7034/l), Dec. 23, 2011, [125 Stat. 1216](/us/stat/125/1216)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. I, § 494, formerly § 452, as added [Pub. L. 93–333, § 2(2)](/us/pl/93/333/s2/2), July 8, 1974, [88 Stat. 290](/us/stat/88/290); renumbered § 494, [Pub. L. 94–161, title I, § 101(4)](/us/pl/94/161/tI/s101/4), Dec. 20, 1975, [89 Stat. 850](/us/stat/89/850), authorized appropriations for disaster relief and emergency recovery needs in Pakistan and Nicaragua.

Section was formerly classified to [section 2262 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2262).
