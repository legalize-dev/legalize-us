# § 686. Transferred

##### § 686. Transferred

Section, [Pub. L. 95–355, title I, § 100](/us/pl/95/355/tI/s100), Sept. 8, 1978, [92 Stat. 531](/us/stat/92/531), which authorized National Technical Institute for the Deaf to make purchases through General Services Administration, was transferred to [section 4362 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s4362).
