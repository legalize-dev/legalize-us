# § 390h–15. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 390h–15. Authorization of appropriations

There is authorized to be appropriated for fiscal years beginning after September 30, 1992, $4,000,000 to carry out the study authorized by [section 390h–14 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s390h–14).

*([Pub. L. 102–575, title XVI, § 1633](/us/pl/102/575/tXVI/s1633), formerly § 1617, Oct. 30, 1992, [106 Stat. 4669](/us/stat/106/4669); renumbered § 1633 and amended [Pub. L. 104–266, § 2(a)(1)](/us/pl/104/266/s2/a/1), (b)(3), Oct. 9, 1996, [110 Stat. 3290](/us/stat/110/3290), 3294.)*
