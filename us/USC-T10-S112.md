# § 112. Department of Defense: seal

##### § 112. Department of Defense: seal

The Secretary of Defense shall have a seal for the Department of Defense. The design of the seal is subject to approval by the President. Judicial notice shall be taken of the seal.

*(Added [Pub. L. 87–651, title II, § 202](/us/pl/87/651/tII/s202), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 517](/us/stat/76/517), § 132; renumbered § 112 and amended [Pub. L. 99–433, title I](/us/pl/99/433/tI), §§ 101(a)(2), 110(d)(1), Oct. 1, 1986, [100 Stat. 994](/us/stat/100/994), 1002.)*
