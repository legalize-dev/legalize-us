# § 1102. Definitions

##### § 1102. Definitions

(1) The term “Center” means the John C. Stennis Center for Public Service Training and Development established under [section 1103(a) of this title](/us/usc/t2/s1103/a).

(2) The term “Board” means the Board of Trustees of the John C. Stennis Center for Public Service Training and Development established under [section 1103(b) of this title](/us/usc/t2/s1103/b).

(3) The term “fund” means the John C. Stennis Center for Public Service Training and Development Trust Fund provided for under [section 1105 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s1105).

*([Pub. L. 100–458, title I, § 113](/us/pl/100/458/tI/s113), Oct. 1, 1988, [102 Stat. 2172](/us/stat/102/2172).)*
