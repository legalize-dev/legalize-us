# § 290bb–23. Repealed. [Pub. L. 114–255, div. B, title IX, § 9017](/us/pl/114/255/dB/tIX/s9017), Dec. 13, 2016, [130 Stat. 1248](/us/stat/130/1248)

##### § 290bb–23. Repealed. [Pub. L. 114–255, div. B, title IX, § 9017](/us/pl/114/255/dB/tIX/s9017), Dec. 13, 2016, [130 Stat. 1248](/us/stat/130/1248)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V, § 517](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV/s517), formerly § 509A, as added [Pub. L. 99–570, title IV, § 4005(a)](/us/pl/99/570/tIV/s4005/a), Oct. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 3207–113](/us/stat/100/3207-113); amended [Pub. L. 100–690, title II, § 2051(d)](/us/pl/100/690/tII/s2051/d), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4206](/us/stat/102/4206); renumbered § 517 and amended [Pub. L. 102–321, title I, § 114](/us/pl/102/321/tI/s114), July 10, 1992, [106 Stat. 346](/us/stat/106/346); [Pub. L. 106–310, div. B, title XXXI, § 3103](/us/pl/106/310/dB/tXXXI/s3103), Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1171](/us/stat/114/1171), related to prevention, treatment, and rehabilitation model projects for high risk youth.

A prior section 517 of act July 1, 1944, was classified to [section 290cc–2 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290cc–2), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–321, title I, § 123(c)](/us/pl/102/321/tI/s123/c), July 10, 1992, [106 Stat. 363](/us/stat/106/363).
