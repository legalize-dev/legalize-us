# § 677j. Omitted

##### § 677j. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 11](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s11), [68 Stat. 873](/us/stat/68/873), which set out permitted uses of tribal funds and imposed restrictions on the use of tribal funds by the mixed-blood group until its adoption of a plan for termination of Federal supervision of the group, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
