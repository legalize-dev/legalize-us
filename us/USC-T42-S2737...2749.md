# §§ 2737 to 2749. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VI, § 614](/us/pl/93/203/tVI/s614), Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883)

##### §§ 2737 to 2749. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–203, title VI, § 614](/us/pl/93/203/tVI/s614), Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883)

Section 2737, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 120](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s120), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 683](/us/stat/81/683), set out the Congressional statement of purpose in enacting provisions for a program of work and training for youth and adults.

Section 2738, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 121](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s121), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 683](/us/stat/81/683), covered community program areas and comprehensive work and training programs.

Section 2739, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 122](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s122), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 683](/us/stat/81/683), set out provisions covering prime sponsors and delegate agencies. See [section 812 of Title 29](/us/usc/t29/s812), Labor.

Section 2740, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 123](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s123), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 684](/us/stat/81/684), set out the eligible activities for work and training programs.

Section 2741, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 124](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s124), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 686](/us/stat/81/686); amended [Pub. L. 90–575, title V, § 503](/us/pl/90/575/tV/s503), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1062](/us/stat/82/1062), set out special conditions for programs.

Section 2742, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 125](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s125), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 686](/us/stat/81/686); amended [Pub. L. 92–424, § 5](/us/pl/92/424/s5), Sept. 19, 1972, [86 Stat. 690](/us/stat/86/690), covered program participants.

Section 2743, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 126](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s126), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 686](/us/stat/81/686), provided for programs dealing with long-term unemployment of persons fifty-five years and older.

Section 2744, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 127](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s127), as added, [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 687](/us/stat/81/687), provided for pilot projects.

Section 2745, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 128](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s128), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 687](/us/stat/81/687), covered technical assistance and training.

Section 2746, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 129](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s129), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 687](/us/stat/81/687), provided for role to be played by States.

Section 2747, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 130](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s130), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 687](/us/stat/81/687), provided for equitable distribution of assistance.

Section 2748, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 131](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s131), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 687](/us/stat/81/687), set limitation on Federal assistance.

Section 2749, [Pub. L. 88–452, title I, § 132](/us/pl/88/452/tI/s132), as added [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 102](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s102), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 688](/us/stat/81/688); amended [Pub. L. 92–424, § 27(b)(1)](/us/pl/92/424/s27/b/1), (3), Sept. 19, 1972, [86 Stat. 705](/us/stat/86/705), provided for development and implementation of program data.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 93–203, title VI, § 614](/us/pl/93/203/tVI/s614), Dec. 28, 1973, [87 Stat. 883](/us/stat/87/883), provided that the repeal by that section is effective with respect to fiscal years after June 30, 1974.
