# § 182d. Audits by Comptroller General

##### § 182d. Audits by Comptroller General

Each of the revolving funds established under sections 182a to 182d of this title shall be subject to audit by the Comptroller General at the Comptroller General’s discretion.

*([Pub. L. 106–481, title I, § 104](/us/pl/106/481/tI/s104), Nov. 9, 2000, [114 Stat. 2190](/us/stat/114/2190).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section applicable with respect to fiscal year 2002 and each succeeding fiscal year, see [section 105 of Pub. L. 106–481](/us/pl/106/481/s105), set out as a note under [section 182a of this title](/us/usc/t2/s182a).
