# § 10447. Definitions and grant conditions

##### § 10447. Definitions and grant conditions

In this subchapter the definitions and grant conditions in [section 12291 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s12291) shall apply.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 2008](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s2008), as added [Pub. L. 109–162, § 3(c)(1)](/us/pl/109/162/s3/c/1), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 2971](/us/stat/119/2971).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3796gg–2 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796gg–2), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Another [section 2008 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s2008) was renumbered section 2016 and is classified to [section 10453 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10453).
