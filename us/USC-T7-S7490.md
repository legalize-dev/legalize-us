# § 7490. Regulations

##### § 7490. Regulations

The Secretary may issue such regulations as are necessary to carry out this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 104–127, title V, § 581](/us/pl/104/127/tV/s581), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1083](/us/stat/110/1083).)*
