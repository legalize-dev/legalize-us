# § 2103. National artificial reef plan

##### § 2103. National artificial reef plan

(1) geographic, hydrographic, geologic, biological, ecological, social, economic, and other criteria for siting artificial reefs;

(2) design, material, and other criteria for constructing artificial reefs;

(3) mechanisms and methodologies for monitoring the compliance of artificial reefs with the requirements of permits issued under [section 2104 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2104);

(4) mechanisms and methodologies for managing the use of artificial reefs;

(5) a synopsis of existing information on artificial reefs and needs for further research on artificial reef technology and management strategies; and

(6) an evaluation of alternatives for facilitating the transfer of artificial reef construction materials to persons holding permits issued pursuant to [section 2104 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2104), including, but not limited to, credits for environmental mitigation and modified tax obligations.

*([Pub. L. 98–623, title II, § 204](/us/pl/98/623/tII/s204), Nov. 8, 1984, [98 Stat. 3395](/us/stat/98/3395).)*
