# §§ 17822 to 17824. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

##### §§ 17822 to 17824. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

Section 17822, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1103](/us/pl/110/422/tXI/s1103), Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4808](/us/stat/122/4808), related to astronaut health care. See [section 31302 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s31302), National and Commercial Space Programs.

Section 17823, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1104](/us/pl/110/422/tXI/s1104), Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4809](/us/stat/122/4809), related to National Academies decadal surveys. See [section 20305 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s20305).

Section 17824, [Pub. L. 110–422, title XI, § 1107](/us/pl/110/422/tXI/s1107), Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4810](/us/stat/122/4810), related to NASA outreach program to promote business competitiveness through space and aeronautics technologies. See [section 30305 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s30305).
