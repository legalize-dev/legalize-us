# § 1193. Omitted

##### § 1193. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–259, § 3](/us/pl/91/259/s3), May 21, 1970, [84 Stat. 254](/us/stat/84/254), which provided that judgment funds would remain tribal funds until distributed, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
