# § 10268. Report

##### § 10268. Report

The Board shall report not less than 2 times per year to Congress and the Secretary its findings, conclusions, and recommendations. The first such report shall be submitted not later than 12 months after December 22, 1987.

*([Pub. L. 97–425, title V, § 508](/us/pl/97/425/tV/s508), as added [Pub. L. 100–202, § 101(d) [title III]](/us/pl/100/202/s101/d/tIII), Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1329–104](/us/stat/101/1329-104), 1329–121; [Pub. L. 100–203, title V, § 5051](/us/pl/100/203/tV/s5051), Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–250](/us/stat/101/1330-250).)*

For termination, effective May 15, 2000, of provisions of this section relating to reporting to Congress 2 times per year, see [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance, and the last item on page 186 of House Document No. 103–7.
