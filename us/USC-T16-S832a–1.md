# § 832a–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–426, title III, § 305(36)](/us/pl/88/426/tIII/s305/36), Aug. 14, 1964, [78 Stat. 427](/us/stat/78/427)

##### § 832a–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 88–426, title III, § 305(36)](/us/pl/88/426/tIII/s305/36), Aug. 14, 1964, [78 Stat. 427](/us/stat/78/427)

Section, [Pub. L. 87–330, title II, § 200](/us/pl/87/330/tII/s200), Sept. 30, 1961, [75 Stat. 728](/us/stat/75/728), prescribed the compensation of the Administrator, Bonneville Power Administration, and is prescribed by [section 5316 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s5316), Government Organization and Employees.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on first day of first pay period which begins on or after July 1, 1964, see [section 501 of Pub. L. 88–426](/us/pl/88/426/s501).
