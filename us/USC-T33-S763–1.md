# § 763–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8512(a)(11), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4759](/us/stat/134/4759)

##### § 763–1. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8512(a)(11), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4759](/us/stat/134/4759)

Section, [act Oct. 29, 1949, ch. 788](/us/act/1949-10-29/ch788), [63 Stat. 1026](/us/stat/63/1026), related to increase of retired pay for those retired on or before June 29, 1949.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Certain Pay Increases Unaffected by Repeal

[Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV](/us/pl/116/283/dG/tLVXXXV) [LXXXV], § 8512(b)(2), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4760](/us/stat/134/4760), provided that: “Notwithstanding the repeals made under paragraphs (10) and (11) of subsection (a) [repealing this section and [section 763a–1 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s763a–1)], any pay increases made under chapter 788 of the Act of October 29, 1949 [former [33 U.S.C. 763–1](/us/usc/t33/s763–1)], and chapter 524 of the Act of July 9, 1956 [former [33 U.S.C. 763–2](/us/usc/t33/s763–2)], as in effect prior to their repeal shall remain in effect.”
