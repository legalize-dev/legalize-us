# § 500g. Omitted

##### § 500g. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 8](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s8), [50 Stat. 901](/us/stat/50/901); [Pub. L. 99–514, title XVII, § 1709(a)](/us/pl/99/514/tXVII/s1709/a), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2783](/us/stat/100/2783), which empowered Secretary to distribute reindeer, property, and profits to natives, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
