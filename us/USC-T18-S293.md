# [§ 293. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–123, § 3(a)](/us/pl/101/123/s3/a), Oct. 23, 1989, [103 Stat. 760](/us/stat/103/760)]

##### [§ 293. Repealed. [Pub. L. 101–123, § 3(a)](/us/pl/101/123/s3/a), Oct. 23, 1989, [103 Stat. 760](/us/stat/103/760)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 100–700, § 3(a)](/us/pl/100/700/s3/a), Nov. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 4632](/us/stat/102/4632), related to limitation on Government contract costs.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 101–123, § 3(b)](/us/pl/101/123/s3/b), Oct. 23, 1989, [103 Stat. 760](/us/stat/103/760), provided that: “The repeal made by this section [repealing this section and provisions formerly set out as a note below] shall be deemed to be effective on the date of enactment of [Public Law 100–700](/us/pl/100/700) [Nov. 19, 1988].”

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 100–700, § 3(c)](/us/pl/100/700/s3/c), Nov. 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 4633](/us/stat/102/4633), which provided that this section was to apply to contracts entered into after Nov. 19, 1988, was repealed by [Pub. L. 101–123, § 3(a)](/us/pl/101/123/s3/a), Oct. 23, 1989, [103 Stat. 760](/us/stat/103/760).
