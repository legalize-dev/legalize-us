# § 17791. Repealed or Omitted

##### § 17791. Repealed or Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 110–422, title VIII, § 801](/us/pl/110/422/tVIII/s801), Oct. 15, 2008, [122 Stat. 4803](/us/stat/122/4803), which reaffirmed policy on surveying near-Earth asteroids and comets, was repealed in part and omitted in part. Subsec. (a) was repealed and reenacted as [section 71101 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s71101), National and Commercial Space Programs, by [Pub. L. 111–314](/us/pl/111/314), §§ 3, 6, Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3328](/us/stat/124/3328), 3444, which Act enacted Title 51. Subsec. (b), which provided sense of Congress regarding the policy and its benefits, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 51.
