# § 1772d. Omitted

##### § 1772d. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–228, § 6](/us/pl/100/228/s6), Dec. 31, 1987, [101 Stat. 1559](/us/stat/101/1559), which related to acceptance of land by the Secretary of the Interior in trust as a reservation for use and benefit of the Seminole Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
