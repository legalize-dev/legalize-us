# § 726. Omitted

##### § 726. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 23, 1954, ch. 831, § 6](/us/act/1954-08-23/ch831/s6), [68 Stat. 769](/us/stat/68/769), which related to applicability of Federal and State laws on and after the date of the proclamation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
