# § 2014. Farm Credit Bank capitalization

##### § 2014. Farm Credit Bank capitalization

In accordance with [section 2154a of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2154a), the Farm Credit Banks shall provide, through bylaws and subject to Farm Credit Administration regulations, for the capitalization of the bank and the manner in which bank stock shall be issued, held, transferred, and retired and bank earnings distributed.
