# § 19158. Definition

##### § 19158. Definition

In this part, the term “Director” means the Director of the Office of Science and Technology Policy.

*([Pub. L. 117–167, div. B, title V, § 10510](/us/pl/117/167/dB/tV/s10510), Aug. 9, 2022, [136 Stat. 1613](/us/stat/136/1613).)*
