# § 1134. Omitted

##### § 1134. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–660, § 4](/us/pl/89/660/s4), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 911](/us/stat/80/911), which exempted funds distributed under the provisions of this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
