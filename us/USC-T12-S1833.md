# § 1833. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–208, div. A, title II, § 2224(b)](/us/pl/104/208/dA/tII/s2224/b), Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3009–415](/us/stat/110/3009-415)

##### § 1833. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–208, div. A, title II, § 2224(b)](/us/pl/104/208/dA/tII/s2224/b), Sept. 30, 1996, [110 Stat. 3009–415](/us/stat/110/3009-415)

Section, [Pub. L. 101–73, title IX, § 918](/us/pl/101/73/tIX/s918), Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 487](/us/stat/103/487), required certain agencies to annually report to Congress detailing civil and criminal actions and investigations undertaken during preceding 12-month period.
