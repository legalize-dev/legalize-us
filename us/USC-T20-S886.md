# § 886. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV, § 401(d)(4)](/us/pl/91/230/tIV/s401/d/4), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173](/us/stat/84/173)

##### § 886. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV, § 401(d)(4)](/us/pl/91/230/tIV/s401/d/4), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173](/us/stat/84/173)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–10, title VIII, § 806](/us/pl/89/10/tVIII/s806), formerly title VII, § 706, as added [Pub. L. 89–750, title I, § 171](/us/pl/89/750/tI/s171), Nov. 3, 1966, [80 Stat. 1209](/us/stat/80/1209); renumbered § 806 and amended [Pub. L. 90–247, title I, § 171](/us/pl/90/247/tI/s171), title VII, § 702, Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 805](/us/stat/81/805), 816, provided for dissemination of information and authorization of appropriations of $1,500,000; $3,500,000; $3,700,000; and $4,000,000 for fiscal years ending June 30, 1967, 1968, 1969, and 1970.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Termination of Section

[Pub. L. 90–576, title III, § 303(e)](/us/pl/90/576/tIII/s303/e), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1096](/us/stat/82/1096), which provided that this section shall become ineffective the first fiscal year for which funds are appropriated to carry out the provisions of [section 6 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s6), was repealed by [section 401(d)(3) of Pub. L. 91–230](/us/pl/91/230/s401/d/3).
