# § 90109. Civil action by Attorney General for equitable relief

##### § 90109. Civil action by Attorney General for equitable relief

(1) engages or threatens to engage in any act, practice, or policy that is inconsistent with the purposes in [section 90102 of this title](/us/usc/t36/s90102); or

(2) refuses, fails, or neglects to carry out its obligations under this chapter or threatens to do so.

*(Added [Pub. L. 107–252, title VI, § 601(a)](/us/pl/107/252/tVI/s601/a), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1721](/us/stat/116/1721), § 152609; renumbered § 90109 and amended [Pub. L. 113–237, § 3(c)(6)(B)](/us/pl/113/237/s3/c/6/B), (C), Dec. 18, 2014, [128 Stat. 2840](/us/stat/128/2840).)*
