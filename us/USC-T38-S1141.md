# § 1141. Basic entitlement

##### § 1141. Basic entitlement

The surviving spouse, child or children, and dependent parent or parents of any veteran who died before January 1, 1957, as the result of injury or disease incurred in or aggravated by active military, naval, air, or space service, in line of duty, during other than a period of war, shall be entitled to receive compensation as hereinafter provided in this subchapter.

L. 94–433, title IV, § 404(18)](/us/pl/94/433/tIV/s404/18), Sept. 30, 1976, [90 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/90/1379); renumbered § 1141, [Pub. #### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2021—[Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283) substituted “air, or space service” for “or air service”.

1991—[Pub. L. 102–83](/us/pl/102/83) renumbered [section 341 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s341) as this section.

1976—[Pub. L. 94–433](/us/pl/94/433) substituted “spouse” for “widow”.

1971—[Pub. L. 92–197](/us/pl/92/197) struck out eligibility clause when the veteran died after April 30, 1957, under circumstances described in [section 417(a) of this title](/us/usc/t38/s417/a).
