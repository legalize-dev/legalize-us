# § 1024. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–471, title I, § 108(c)](/us/pl/92/471/tI/s108/c), Oct. 9, 1972, [86 Stat. 787](/us/stat/86/787)

##### § 1024. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–471, title I, § 108(c)](/us/pl/92/471/tI/s108/c), Oct. 9, 1972, [86 Stat. 787](/us/stat/86/787)

Section, [act Aug. 12, 1954, ch. 669, § 5](/us/act/1954-08-12/ch669/s5), [68 Stat. 698](/us/stat/68/698), provided that service of individuals appointed as United States Commissioners shall not be treated as service for purposes of certain sections of Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure, and Title 5, Government Organization and Employees.
