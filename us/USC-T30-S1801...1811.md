# §§ 1801 to 1811. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–260, div. Z, title VII, § 7002(n)(1)](/us/pl/116/260/dZ/tVII/s7002/n/1), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2576](/us/stat/134/2576)

##### §§ 1801 to 1811. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–260, div. Z, title VII, § 7002(n)(1)](/us/pl/116/260/dZ/tVII/s7002/n/1), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2576](/us/stat/134/2576)

Section 1801, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 202](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s202), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1249](/us/stat/98/1249), related to Congressional findings and declaration of purposes.

Section 1802, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 203](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s203), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1250](/us/stat/98/1250), related to establishment of National Critical Materials Council.

Section 1803, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 204](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s204), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1250](/us/stat/98/1250), related to responsibilities and authorities of Council.

Section 1804, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 205](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s205), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1251](/us/stat/98/1251), related to program and policy for advanced materials research and technology.

Section 1805, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 206](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s206), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1252](/us/stat/98/1252), related to innovation in basic and advanced materials industries.

Section 1806, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 207](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s207), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1252](/us/stat/98/1252), related to compensation of members and reimbursement.

Section 1807, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 208](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s208), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1253](/us/stat/98/1253), related to Executive Director.

Section 1808, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 209](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s209), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1253](/us/stat/98/1253), related to responsibilities and duties of Director.

Section 1809, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 210](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s210), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1253](/us/stat/98/1253); [Pub. L. 100–418, title V, § 5183](/us/pl/100/418/tV/s5183), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1454](/us/stat/102/1454), related to general authority of Council.

Section 1810, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 211](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s211), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1254](/us/stat/98/1254); [Pub. L. 100–418, title V, § 5184](/us/pl/100/418/tV/s5184), Aug. 23, 1988, [102 Stat. 1454](/us/stat/102/1454), related to authorization of appropriations.

Section 1811, [Pub. L. 98–373, title II, § 212](/us/pl/98/373/tII/s212), July 31, 1984, [98 Stat. 1254](/us/stat/98/1254), defined the term “materials”.
