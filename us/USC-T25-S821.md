# § 821. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–281, § 1(b)(2)](/us/pl/95/281/s1/b/2), May 15, 1978, [92 Stat. 246](/us/stat/92/246)

##### § 821. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–281, § 1(b)(2)](/us/pl/95/281/s1/b/2), May 15, 1978, [92 Stat. 246](/us/stat/92/246)

Section, [act Aug. 2, 1956, ch. 881, § 1](/us/act/1956-08-02/ch881/s1), [70 Stat. 937](/us/stat/70/937), set out purpose of former sections 821 to 826 of this title as termination of Federal supervision and services for tribe.
