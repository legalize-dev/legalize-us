# §§ 672 to 677a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 672 to 677a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 672, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 362](/us/stat/39/362); [Feb. 27, 1921, ch. 78](/us/act/1921-02-27/ch78), [41 Stat. 1148](/us/stat/41/1148); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title III, § 303, [42 Stat. 1474](/us/stat/42/1474); [Mar. 4, 1929, ch. 700](/us/act/1929-03-04/ch700), [45 Stat. 1558](/us/stat/45/1558); [May 17, 1932, ch. 190](/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190), [47 Stat. 158](/us/stat/47/158); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 5(a)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s5/a), [50 Stat. 704](/us/stat/50/704); [June 30, 1945, ch. 204, § 3](/us/act/1945-06-30/ch204/s3), [59 Stat. 267](/us/stat/59/267); [Oct. 29, 1949, ch. 786, § 1(a)](/us/act/1949-10-29/ch786/s1/a), [63 Stat. 985](/us/stat/63/985); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for establishment of Federal land banks. See [section 2011 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2011).

Section 672a, acts [Mar. 10, 1924, ch. 46, § 2](/us/act/1924-03-10/ch46/s2), [43 Stat. 17](/us/stat/43/17); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 5(a)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s5/a), [50 Stat. 704](/us/stat/50/704), provided for extension of farm credit system under Farm Credit Administration to Hawaii.

Section 673, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 4](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s4), [39 Stat. 362](/us/stat/39/362); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, provided for temporary management of Federal land banks.

Section 674, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 362](/us/stat/39/362); [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VIII, § 80(a), [48 Stat. 273](/us/stat/48/273); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), set out requisite contents of organization certificates for land banks.

Section 675, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 4](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s4), [39 Stat. 363](/us/stat/39/363); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, authorized changes in organization certificates on direction of Farm Credit Administration.

Section 676, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 4](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s4), [39 Stat. 363](/us/stat/39/363); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, set time for commencement of corporate existence of land banks and enumerated their powers. See [section 2012 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2012).

Section 677, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 363](/us/stat/39/363); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), set time for termination of temporary management of land banks.

Section 677a, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 4, [39 Stat. 363](/us/stat/39/363); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 252](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch252), title III, § 304, [42 Stat. 1474](/us/stat/42/1474); [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VII, § 70a(a)(1)–(4), title VIII, § 80(a), [48 Stat. 269](/us/stat/48/269), 273; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 7(b)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s7/b), [50 Stat. 707](/us/stat/50/707), provided for compensation of director of farm credit boards of each farm credit district. See [section 2226 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2226).
