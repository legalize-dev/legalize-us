# § 379dd–1. Location of Foundation

##### § 379dd–1. Location of Foundation

The Foundation shall, if practicable, be located not more than 20 miles from the District of Columbia.

*([June 25, 1938, ch. 675, § 771](/us/act/1938-06-25/ch675/s771), as added [Pub. L. 110–85, title VI, § 601(b)](/us/pl/110/85/tVI/s601/b), Sept. 27, 2007, [121 Stat. 897](/us/stat/121/897).)*
