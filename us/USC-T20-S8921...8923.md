##### §§ 8921 to 8923. Repealed. [Pub. L. 107–110, title X, § 1011(5)(C)](/us/pl/107/110/tX/s1011/5/C), Jan. 8, 2002, [115 Stat. 1986](/us/stat/115/1986)
