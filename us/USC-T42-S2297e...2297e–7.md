# §§ 2297e to 2297e–7. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–134, title III, § 3116(a)(1)](/us/pl/104/134/tIII/s3116/a/1), Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–349](/us/stat/110/1321-349)

##### §§ 2297e to 2297e–7. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–134, title III, § 3116(a)(1)](/us/pl/104/134/tIII/s3116/a/1), Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–349](/us/stat/110/1321-349)

Section 2297e, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1601](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1601), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2939](/us/stat/106/2939), related to assessment by United States Enrichment Corporation of viability of commercialization of AVLIS (atomic vapor laser isotope separation technology) and alternative uranium enrichment technologies.

Section 2297e–1, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1602](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1602), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2939](/us/stat/106/2939), related to transfer of rights and property to Corporation.

Section 2297e–2, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1603](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1603), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2940](/us/stat/106/2940), related to predeployment activities by Corporation.

Section 2297e–3, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1604](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1604), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2940](/us/stat/106/2940); amended [Pub. L. 102–572, title IX, § 902(b)(2)](/us/pl/102/572/tIX/s902/b/2), Oct. 29, 1992, [106 Stat. 4516](/us/stat/106/4516), related to Corporation sponsorship of private for-profit corporation to construct AVLIS and alternative technologies.

Section 2297e–4, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1605](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1605), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2942](/us/stat/106/2942), related to AVLIS Commercialization Fund within Corporation.

Section 2297e–5, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1606](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1606), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2942](/us/stat/106/2942), related to Department research and development assistance.

Section 2297e–6, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1607](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1607), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2942](/us/stat/106/2942), related to site selection.

Section 2297e–7, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, title II, § 1608](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/tII/s1608), as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/102/486/tIX/s901), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2942](/us/stat/106/2942), related to exclusion from Price-Anderson coverage.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective as of date on which 100 percent of ownership of United States Enrichment Corporation has been transferred to private investors (July 28, 1998), see [section 3116(a)(1) of Pub. L. 104–134](/us/pl/104/134/s3116/a/1), set out as a note under former [section 2297 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2297).
