# § 285e–6. Dissemination of research results

##### § 285e–6. Dissemination of research results

The Director of the Institute shall disseminate the results of research conducted under [section 285e–5 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s285e–5) and this section to appropriate professional entities and to the public.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title IV, § 445D, formerly [Pub. L. 99–660, title IX, § 942](/us/pl/99/660/tIX/s942), Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3809](/us/stat/100/3809); renumbered § 445D of act July 1, 1944, and amended [Pub. L. 100–607, title I, § 142(a)](/us/pl/100/607/tI/s142/a), (d)(3), Nov. 4, 1988, [102 Stat. 3057](/us/stat/102/3057), 3058.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 11242 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s11242) prior to renumbering by [Pub. L. 100–607](/us/pl/100/607).

#### Amendments

1988—[Pub. L. 100–607, § 142(a)](/us/pl/100/607/s142/a), renumbered [section 11242 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s11242) as this section.

[Pub. L. 100–607, § 142(d)(3)](/us/pl/100/607/s142/d/3), substituted “the Institute” for “the National Institute on Aging” and “[section 285e–5 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s285e–5) and this section” for “this part”.
