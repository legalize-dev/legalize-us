# § 9806. Implementation

##### § 9806. Implementation

(1) the Global Fragility Strategy required under [section 9803 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s9803), including each of the country plans developed under [section 9805 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s9805), is implemented, updated, and coordinated on a regular basis; and

(2) the strategy is used to guide United States Government policy at a senior level and incorporated into relevant strategies and plans across the United States Government such that the activities of all relevant Federal departments and agencies are consistent with the strategy.

*([Pub. L. 116–94, div. J, title V, § 507](/us/pl/116/94/dJ/tV/s507), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 3065](/us/stat/133/3065).)*
