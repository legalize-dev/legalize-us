# § 713a. Omitted

##### § 713a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–165, § 3](/us/pl/98/165/s3), Nov. 22, 1983, [97 Stat. 1064](/us/stat/97/1064), which related to consideration of Confederated Tribes of Grand Ronde Community as one tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
