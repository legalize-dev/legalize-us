# § 1301. Alien seeking entry; contents

##### § 1301. Alien seeking entry; contents

No visa shall be issued to any alien seeking to enter the United States until such alien has been registered in accordance with [section 1201(b) of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1201/b).

*([June 27, 1952, ch. 477](/us/act/1952-06-27/ch477), title II, ch. 7, § 261, [66 Stat. 223](/us/stat/66/223); [Pub. L. 99–653, § 8](/us/pl/99/653/s8), Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3657](/us/stat/100/3657); [Pub. L. 100–525, § 8(g)](/us/pl/100/525/s8/g), Oct. 24, 1988, [102 Stat. 2617](/us/stat/102/2617).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1988—[Pub. L. 100–525](/us/pl/100/525) made technical correction to [Pub. L. 99–653](/us/pl/99/653). See 1986 Amendment note below.

1986—[Pub. L. 99–653](/us/pl/99/653), as amended by [Pub. L. 100–525](/us/pl/100/525), amended section generally, striking out “and fingerprinted” after “has been registered” and substituting “[section 1201(b) of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1201/b)” for “[section 1201(b) of this title](/us/usc/t8/s1201/b), unless such alien has been exempted from being fingerprinted as provided in that section”.
