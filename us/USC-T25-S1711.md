#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–395, § 12](/us/pl/95/395/s12), Sept. 30, 1978, [92 Stat. 817](/us/stat/92/817), which related to limitation of actions and jurisdiction, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
