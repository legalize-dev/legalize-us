# § 671. Omitted

##### § 671. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, acts [Aug. 21, 1951, ch. 338, § 1](/us/act/1951-08-21/ch338/s1), [65 Stat. 193](/us/stat/65/193); [June 29, 1954, ch. 412](/us/act/1954-06-29/ch412), [68 Stat. 321](/us/stat/68/321), which related to use of funds of the Ute Indian Tribe of the Uintah and Ouray Reservation for expenditure and per capita payments, regulations applicable to loans, and restrictions on attorney fees, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
