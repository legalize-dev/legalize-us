# § 9095. Tracking of benefits

##### § 9095. Tracking of benefits

As soon as practicable after February 7, 2014, the Secretary may track the benefits provided, directly or indirectly, to individuals and entities under titles I and II and the amendments made by those titles.

*([Pub. L. 113–79, title I, § 1612](/us/pl/113/79/tI/s1612), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 710](/us/stat/128/710).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Titles I and II, referred to in text, are titles I and II of [Pub. L. 113–79](/us/pl/113/79), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 658](/us/stat/128/658), 713. Title I of the Act is classified principally to this chapter. Title II of the Act enacted, amended, and repealed numerous sections and provisions set out as notes in Title 16, Conservation, and Title 43, Public Lands. For complete classification of titles I and II to the Code, see Tables.
