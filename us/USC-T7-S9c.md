# § 9c. Notice of investigations and enforcement actions

##### § 9c. Notice of investigations and enforcement actions

The Commission shall provide the Securities and Exchange Commission with notice of the commencement of any proceeding and a copy of any order entered by the Commission pursuant to section 9 and 13b of this title against any futures commission merchant or introducing broker registered pursuant to [section 6f(a)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s6f/a/2), any floor broker or floor trader exempt from registration pursuant to [section 6f(a)(3) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s6f/a/3), any associated person exempt from registration pursuant to [section 6k(6) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s6k/6), or any board of trade designated as a contract market pursuant to [section 7b–1 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s7b–1).

*([Sept. 21, 1922, ch. 369, § 6(g)](/us/act/1922-09-21/ch369/s6/g), as added [Pub. L. 106–554, § 1(a)(5) [title II, § 253(b)]](/us/pl/106/554/s1/a/5/tII/s253/b), Dec. 21, 2000, [114 Stat. 2763](/us/stat/114/2763), 2763A–449.)*

#### Codification
