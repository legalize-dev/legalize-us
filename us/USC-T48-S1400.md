# § 1400. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–454, title VII, § 709](/us/pl/98/454/tVII/s709), Oct. 5, 1984, [98 Stat. 1741](/us/stat/98/1741)

##### § 1400. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–454, title VII, § 709](/us/pl/98/454/tVII/s709), Oct. 5, 1984, [98 Stat. 1741](/us/stat/98/1741)

Section, [act May 20, 1932, ch. 194](/us/act/1932-05-20/ch194), [47 Stat. 160](/us/stat/47/160), related to extension of admiralty laws of the United States to Virgin Islands.

Repeal effective on ninetieth day following Oct. 5, 1984, see [section 1005 of Pub. L. 98–454](/us/pl/98/454/s1005), set out as an Effective Date of 1984 Amendment note under [section 1424 of this title](/us/usc/t48/s1424).
