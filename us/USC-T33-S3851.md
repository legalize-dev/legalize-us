# § 3851. Criminal enforcement

##### § 3851. Criminal enforcement

Any person who knowingly violates paragraph (2), (3), (4), or (5) of [section 3841(a) of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3841/a) or [section 3841(b) of this title](/us/usc/t33/s3841/b) shall be fined under title 18 or imprisoned not more than 6 years, or both.

*([Pub. L. 111–281, title X, § 1041](/us/pl/111/281/tX/s1041), Oct. 15, 2010, [124 Stat. 3029](/us/stat/124/3029).)*
