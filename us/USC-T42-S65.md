# § 65. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### § 65. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section, acts [Mar. 2, 1923, ch. 178](/us/act/1923-03-02/ch178), title I, [42 Stat. 1385](/us/stat/42/1385); [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 11](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s11), [63 Stat. 559](/us/stat/63/559), prohibited issuance of heat or light in kind to any person in Public Health Service while such person is receiving an allowance for rental of quarters.
