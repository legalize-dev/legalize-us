# § 7201. Definitions

##### § 7201. Definitions

(1) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

(2) System The term “system” means the National Landscape Conservation System established by [section 7202(a) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s7202/a).
