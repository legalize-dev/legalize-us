# § 702m. Interest of Members of Congress in contracts for acquisition of land

##### § 702m. Interest of Members of Congress in contracts for acquisition of land

In every contract or agreement to be made or entered into for the acquisition of land either by private sale or condemnation as in sections 702a, 702b to 702d, 702e to 702g, 702h, 702i, 702j, 702k, 702*l*, 702m, and 704 of this title provided the provisions contained in [section 6306(a) of title 41](/us/usc/t41/s6306/a) shall be applicable.

*([May 15, 1928, ch. 569, § 14](/us/act/1928-05-15/ch569/s14), [45 Stat. 539](/us/stat/45/539).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification
