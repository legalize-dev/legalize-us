# §§ 1381 to 1383. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 705(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s705/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792](/us/stat/90/2792)

##### §§ 1381 to 1383. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 705(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s705/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792](/us/stat/90/2792)

Section 1381, [Pub. L. 86–649, title III, § 301](/us/pl/86/649/tIII/s301), July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 507](/us/stat/74/507), related to forfeiture of bond or deposit by timber purchaser or permittee.

Section 1382, [Pub. L. 86–649, title III, § 302](/us/pl/86/649/tIII/s302), July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 507](/us/stat/74/507), related to maintenance of roads and trails under jurisdiction of Bureau of Land Management and deposit of funds to insure maintenance.

Section 1383, [Pub. L. 86–649, title III, § 303](/us/pl/86/649/tIII/s303), July 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 508](/us/stat/74/508), related to Oregon and California Railroad and Coos Bay Wagon Road Grant lands.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 705(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s705/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2792](/us/stat/90/2792), provided that the repeal made by section 705(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

#### Savings Provision

Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579) not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579/s701), set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1701).
