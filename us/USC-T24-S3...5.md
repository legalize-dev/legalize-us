##### §§ 3 to 5. Repealed. [June 15, 1943, ch. 125, § 3](/us/act/1943-06-15/ch125/s3), [57 Stat. 153](/us/stat/57/153), eff. July 1, 1943

Section 3, R.S. §§ 1614, 4808, related to deduction from pay of seamen for Navy hospital fund.

Section 4, R.S. § 4809, related to fines on seamen appropriated for Navy hospitals.

Section 5, [act June 7, 1900, ch. 859](/us/act/1900-06-07/ch859), [31 Stat. 697](/us/stat/31/697), related to forfeitures from desertion for naval hospital fund.
