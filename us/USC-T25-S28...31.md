##### §§ 28 to 31. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 632](/us/stat/80/632), 634

Section 28, R.S. § 2056; [act May 17, 1882, ch. 163, § 1](/us/act/1882-05-17/ch163/s1), [22 Stat. 87](/us/stat/22/87), fixed term of office for Indian Agents.

Section 29, R.S. § 2057, provided for a bond by Indian Agents.

Section 30, R.S. § 2060, prescribed limits of residence of Indian Agents.

Section 31, R.S. § 2058, related to duties of Indian Agents.
