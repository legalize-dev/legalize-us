##### § 1131b. Repealed. [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(c), [70 Stat. 665](/us/stat/70/665)

Section, [act June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title I, § 4](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98/tI/s4), [48 Stat. 257](/us/stat/48/257), provided for capital stock of Production Credit Corporations, value of shares, amount and subscription for initial stock, and payments for stock subscribed on behalf of the United States.
