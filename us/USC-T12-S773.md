# § 773. Omitted

##### § 773. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 15, 1922, ch. 190, § 3](/us/act/1922-05-15/ch190/s3), [42 Stat. 542](/us/stat/42/542), which related to mortgages on farm lands under United States reclamation projects, and contained a definition of “first mortgage” as used in [section 771 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s771), was omitted because of the repeal of section 771 and other sections of this title that were based on the Federal Farm Loan Act.
