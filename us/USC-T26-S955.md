#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to taxable years of foreign corporations beginning after Dec. 31, 2017, and to taxable years of United States shareholders in which or with which such taxable years of foreign corporations end, see [section 14212(c) of Pub. L. 115–97](/us/pl/115/97/s14212/c), set out as an Effective Date of 2017 Amendment note under [section 851 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s851).
