# § 460yyy. Definitions

##### § 460yyy. Definitions

(1) Conservation Area The term “Conservation Area” means the Fort Stanton-Snowy River Cave National Conservation Area established by [section 460yyy–1(a) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s460yyy–1/a).

(2) Management plan The term “management plan” means the management plan developed for the Conservation Area under [section 460yyy–2(c) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s460yyy–2/c).

(3) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior, acting through the Director of the Bureau of Land Management.
