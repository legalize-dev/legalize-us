# § 1780. Omitted

##### § 1780. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 109–286, § 2](/us/pl/109/286/s2), Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1218](/us/stat/120/1218), which set out definitions of terms and purposes of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 109–286, § 1](/us/pl/109/286/s1), Sept. 27, 2006, [120 Stat. 1218](/us/stat/120/1218), provided that [Pub. L. 109–286](/us/pl/109/286), enacting this subchapter, could be cited as the “Pueblo de San Ildefonso Claims Settlement Act of 2005”.
