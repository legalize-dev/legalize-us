##### § 290bb–24. Repealed. [Pub. L. 106–310, div. B, title XXXIII, § 3302(b)](/us/pl/106/310/dB/tXXXIII/s3302/b), Oct. 17, 2000, [114 Stat. 1210](/us/stat/114/1210)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title V, § 518](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tV/s518), as added [Pub. L. 102–321, title I, § 171](/us/pl/102/321/tI/s171), July 10, 1992, [106 Stat. 377](/us/stat/106/377), related to employee assistance programs.

A prior section 518 of act July 1, 1944, was classified to [section 290cc–11 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s290cc–11), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 102–321, § 120(b)(3)](/us/pl/102/321/s120/b/3).
