# § 28302. Penalties

##### § 28302. Penalties

A person violating [section 28301 of this title](/us/usc/t49/s28301) shall be fined under title 18, imprisoned not more than one year, or both.

*(Added [Pub. L. 104–287, § 5(56)(A)](/us/pl/104/287/s5/56/A), Oct. 11, 1996, [110 Stat. 3394](/us/stat/110/3394).)*

The words “shall be guilty of a misdemeanor” are omitted, and the words “shall be fined under title 18” are substituted for “shall be fined not less than $100 and not more than $1,000”, for consistency with title 18. The words “upon conviction” are omitted as surplus.
