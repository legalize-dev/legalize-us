# §§ 172 to 177. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 172 to 177. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 172, acts [Aug. 22, 1914, ch. 264, § 6](/us/act/1914-08-22/ch264/s6), [38 Stat. 700](/us/stat/38/700); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213), related to appointment and jurisdiction of commissioner. See provisions covering United States magistrate judges in section 631 et seq. of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 173, [act Aug. 22, 1914, ch. 264, § 7](/us/act/1914-08-22/ch264/s7), [38 Stat. 701](/us/stat/38/701), related to arrest, confinement, and bail.

Section 174, [act Aug. 22, 1914, ch. 264, § 8](/us/act/1914-08-22/ch264/s8), [38 Stat. 701](/us/stat/38/701), related to process. See [section 3053 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s3053), Crimes and Criminal Procedure, rule 4 of Federal Rules of Criminal Procedure, Title 18, Appendix, and rule 4 of Federal Rules of Civil Procedure, Title 28, Appendix, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 175, acts [Aug. 22, 1914, ch. 264, § 9](/us/act/1914-08-22/ch264/s9), [38 Stat. 701](/us/stat/38/701); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213), related to commissioner’s [now magistrate judge] salary.

Section 176, [act Aug. 22, 1914, ch. 264, § 11](/us/act/1914-08-22/ch264/s11), [38 Stat. 701](/us/stat/38/701), related to fines and costs.

Section 177, [act Aug. 22, 1914, ch. 264, § 10](/us/act/1914-08-22/ch264/s10), [38 Stat. 701](/us/stat/38/701), related to certification and payment of fees, costs, and expenses.
