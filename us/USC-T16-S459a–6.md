# § 459a–6. Acquisition of non-Federal land within boundaries of recreational area

##### § 459a–6. Acquisition of non-Federal land within boundaries of recreational area

[Section 101102 of title 54](/us/usc/t54/s101102) is amended to extend the authority of the Secretary of the Interior, contained therein, to the Cape Hatteras National Seashore Recreational Area.

*([Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 988, § 1](/us/act/1956-08-06/ch988/s1), [70 Stat. 1066](/us/stat/70/1066).)*
