# § 803. Commissioned officers of the Army, Air Force, or Space Force: forfeiture of pay when dropped from rolls

##### § 803. Commissioned officers of the Army, Air Force, or Space Force: forfeiture of pay when dropped from rolls

A commissioned officer of the Army, the Air Force, or the Space Force who is dropped from the rolls under [section 1161(b) of title 10](/us/usc/t10/s1161/b) for absence without authority for three months forfeits all pay due or to become due.

*([Pub. L. 87–649](/us/pl/87/649), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 486](/us/stat/76/486); [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title IX, § 925(i)(2)(A)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tIX/s925/i/2/A), (B), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3828](/us/stat/134/3828).)*
