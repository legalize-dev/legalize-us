# §§ 4211 to 4214. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

##### §§ 4211 to 4214. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

Section 4211, [Pub. L. 90–577, title II, § 201](/us/pl/90/577/tII/s201), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/82/1101), required the Federal Government to provide to States full information on grant-in-aid funds. See [section 6502 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6502), Money and Finance.

Section 4212, [Pub. L. 90–577, title II, § 202](/us/pl/90/577/tII/s202), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/82/1101), related to deposit of grants-in-aid. See [section 6503(b) of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6503/b).

Section 4213, [Pub. L. 90–577, title II, § 203](/us/pl/90/577/tII/s203), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/82/1101), related to scheduling of Federal grant-in-aid transfers to States. See [section 6503(a) of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6503/a).

Section 4214, [Pub. L. 90–577, title II, § 204](/us/pl/90/577/tII/s204), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/82/1101), related to eligibility of State agencies to administer a grant-in-aid program. See [section 6504 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6504).
