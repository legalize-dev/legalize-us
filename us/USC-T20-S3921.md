# § 3921. Prohibition against the Federal control of education

##### § 3921. Prohibition against the Federal control of education

The provisions of section 1232a 11 See References in Text note below. of this title, relating to prohibition against Federal control of education, shall apply to each program and award authorized by this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 98–377, title I, § 111](/us/pl/98/377/tI/s111), as added [Pub. L. 99–159, title II, § 201](/us/pl/99/159/tII/s201), Nov. 22, 1985, [99 Stat. 897](/us/stat/99/897).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 1232a of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1232a), referred to in text, was in the original a reference to section 432 of the General Education Provisions Act. Sections 425 and 432 of that Act were renumbered as sections 432 and 438, respectively, by [Pub. L. 103–382, title II, § 212(b)(1)](/us/pl/103/382/tII/s212/b/1), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3913](/us/stat/108/3913), and are classified to sections 1231b–2 and 1232a, respectively, of this title.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 3921, [Pub. L. 98–377, title I, § 121](/us/pl/98/377/tI/s121), Aug. 11, 1984, [98 Stat. 1269](/us/stat/98/1269), related to programs authorized for mathematics and science education development, prior to the general revision of this subchapter by [section 201 of Pub. L. 99–159](/us/pl/99/159/s201). See [section 3914 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s3914).
