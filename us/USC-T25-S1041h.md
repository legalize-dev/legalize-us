# § 1041h. Omitted

##### § 1041h. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 106–568, title VII, § 710](/us/pl/106/568/tVII/s710), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2916](/us/stat/114/2916), which provided that no provision of this subchapter would be construed to affect any treaty to which a tribe referred to in this subchapter was a party, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
