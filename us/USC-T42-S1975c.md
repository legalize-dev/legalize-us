# § 1975c. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 1975c. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated,11 So in original. The comma probably should not appear. to carry out this chapter $9,500,000 for fiscal year 1995. None of the sums authorized to be appropriated for fiscal year 1995 may be used to create additional regional offices.

*([Pub. L. 98–183, § 5](/us/pl/98/183/s5), Nov. 30, 1983, [97 Stat. 1304](/us/stat/97/1304); [Pub. L. 102–167, § 2](/us/pl/102/167/s2), Nov. 26, 1991, [105 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/105/1101); [Pub. L. 103–419, § 2](/us/pl/103/419/s2), Oct. 25, 1994, [108 Stat. 4341](/us/stat/108/4341).)*
