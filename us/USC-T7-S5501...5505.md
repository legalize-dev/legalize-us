# §§ 5501 to 5505. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–185, title III, § 302(c)](/us/pl/105/185/tIII/s302/c), June 23, 1998, [112 Stat. 563](/us/stat/112/563)

##### §§ 5501 to 5505. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–185, title III, § 302(c)](/us/pl/105/185/tIII/s302/c), June 23, 1998, [112 Stat. 563](/us/stat/112/563)

Section 5501, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XIV, § 1481](/us/pl/101/624/tXIV/s1481), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3622](/us/stat/104/3622); [Pub. L. 104–127, title VIII, § 831](/us/pl/104/127/tVIII/s831), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1168](/us/stat/110/1168), set forth short title and purpose of chapter, provided definitions, and authorized appropriations.

Section 5502, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XIV, § 1482](/us/pl/101/624/tXIV/s1482), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3622](/us/stat/104/3622), related to soil and water activities.

Section 5503, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XIV, § 1483](/us/pl/101/624/tXIV/s1483), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3623](/us/stat/104/3623); [Pub. L. 102–237, title II, § 201(e)](/us/pl/102/237/tII/s201/e), Dec. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 1847](/us/stat/105/1847), required establishment of water quality coordination program within each State.

Section 5504, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XIV, § 1484](/us/pl/101/624/tXIV/s1484), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3624](/us/stat/104/3624), established water quality and nutrient management research program.

Section 5505, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XIV, § 1485](/us/pl/101/624/tXIV/s1485), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3626](/us/stat/104/3626); [Pub. L. 102–237, title II, § 201(f)](/us/pl/102/237/tII/s201/f), Dec. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 1847](/us/stat/105/1847); [Pub. L. 104–66, title I, § 1011(r)](/us/pl/104/66/tI/s1011/r), Dec. 21, 1995, [109 Stat. 710](/us/stat/109/710), directed establishment of repository of agriculture and ground water quality planning information.
