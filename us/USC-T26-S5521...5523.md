# [§§ 5521 to 5523. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–39, title VIII, § 807(a)(50)](/us/pl/96/39/tVIII/s807/a/50), July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 288](/us/stat/93/288)]

##### [§§ 5521 to 5523. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–39, title VIII, § 807(a)(50)](/us/pl/96/39/tVIII/s807/a/50), July 26, 1979, [93 Stat. 288](/us/stat/93/288)]

Section 5521, added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201](/us/pl/85/859/tII/s201), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1392](/us/stat/72/1392); amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1906/b/13/A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/90/1834), related to establishment and operation of manufacturing bonded warehouses.

A prior section 5521, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 678](/us/stat/68A/678), related to establishment and operation of manufacturing bonded warehouses, prior to the general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859](/us/pl/85/859).

Section 5522, added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201](/us/pl/85/859/tII/s201), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1393](/us/stat/72/1393); amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1906/b/13/A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/90/1834), related to withdrawal of distilled spirits to manufacturing bonded warehouses.

A prior section 5522, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 679](/us/stat/68A/679), related to withdrawal of distilled spirits to manufacturing bonded warehouses, prior to general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859](/us/pl/85/859). See sections 5008(f)(2) and 5214(a) of this title.

Section 5523, added [Pub. L. 85–859, title II, § 201](/us/pl/85/859/tII/s201), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1394](/us/stat/72/1394); amended [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1906(b)(13)(A)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1906/b/13/A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/90/1834), made special provision for distilled spirits and wines rectified in manufacturing bonded warehouses.

A prior section 5523, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 679](/us/stat/68A/679), made special provision for distilled spirits and wines rectified in manufacturing bonded warehouses, prior to general revision of this chapter by [Pub. L. 85–859](/us/pl/85/859).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1980, see [section 810 of Pub. L. 96–39](/us/pl/96/39/s810), set out as an Effective Date of 1979 Amendment note under [section 5001 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s5001).
