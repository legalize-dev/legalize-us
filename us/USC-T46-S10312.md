# § 10312. Settlements on discharge

##### § 10312. Settlements on discharge

When discharge and settlement are completed, the master, individual in charge, or owner and each seaman shall sign the agreement required by [section 10302 of this title](/us/usc/t46/s10302).

*([Pub. L. 98–89](/us/pl/98/89), Aug. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 565](/us/stat/97/565); [Pub. L. 103–206, title IV, § 410](/us/pl/103/206/tIV/s410), Dec. 20, 1993, [107 Stat. 2437](/us/stat/107/2437).)*

Section 10312 outlines the procedures for settlements upon discharge and for settling disputes over seamen’s wages and discharges.
