# § 120. Rhode Island

##### § 120. Rhode Island

Rhode Island constitutes one judicial district.

Court shall be held at Providence.

*([June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 889](/us/stat/62/889).)*
