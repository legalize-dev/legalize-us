# § 42307. Review of regulations and orders

##### § 42307. Review of regulations and orders

A regulation or final order of the Federal Maritime Commission under this chapter is reviewable exclusively in the same forum and in the same manner as provided in [section 2342(3)(B) of title 28](/us/usc/t28/s2342/3/B).

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 7](/us/pl/109/304/s7), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1554](/us/stat/120/1554).)*
