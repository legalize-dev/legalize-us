# §§ 8121 to 8124. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title III, § 313(a)](/us/pl/97/35/tIII/s313/a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 398](/us/stat/95/398)

##### §§ 8121 to 8124. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title III, § 313(a)](/us/pl/97/35/tIII/s313/a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 398](/us/stat/95/398)

Section 8121, [Pub. L. 95–557, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/95/557/tVII/s702), Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2119](/us/stat/92/2119), set forth congressional findings and statement of purposes for neighborhood self-help development programs.

Section 8122, [Pub. L. 95–557, title VII, § 703](/us/pl/95/557/tVII/s703), Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2120](/us/stat/92/2120), defined terms applicable to subchapter.

Section 8123, [Pub. L. 95–557, title VII, § 704](/us/pl/95/557/tVII/s704), Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2120](/us/stat/92/2120); [Pub. L. 96–153, title I, § 107(b)](/us/pl/96/153/tI/s107/b), Dec. 21, 1979, [93 Stat. 1104](/us/stat/93/1104), set forth provisions respecting grants and other forms of assistance.

Section 8124, [Pub. L. 95–557, title VII, § 705](/us/pl/95/557/tVII/s705), Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2121](/us/stat/92/2121); [Pub. L. 96–153, title I, § 107(a)](/us/pl/96/153/tI/s107/a), Dec. 21, 1979, [93 Stat. 1104](/us/stat/93/1104); [Pub. L. 96–399, title I, § 115](/us/pl/96/399/tI/s115), Oct. 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 1623](/us/stat/94/1623), related to authorization of appropriations for grants.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Sections 8121 to 8124 repealed effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 371 of Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35/s371), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3701 of Title 12](/us/usc/t12/s3701), Banks and Banking.
