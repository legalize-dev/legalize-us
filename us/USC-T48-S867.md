# § 867. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–274, § 103(g)](/us/pl/90/274/s103/g), Mar. 27, 1968, [82 Stat. 63](/us/stat/82/63)

##### § 867. Repealed. [Pub. L. 90–274, § 103(g)](/us/pl/90/274/s103/g), Mar. 27, 1968, [82 Stat. 63](/us/stat/82/63)

Section, acts [Mar. 2, 1917, ch. 145, § 44](/us/act/1917-03-02/ch145/s44), [39 Stat. 966](/us/stat/39/966); [May 17, 1932, ch. 190](/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190), [47 Stat. 158](/us/stat/47/158), set out qualifications for jurors in District Court of United States for Puerto Rico as different from those set by local law and directed that juries be selected, drawn, and subject to exemption in accordance with laws of Congress insofar as locally applicable.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 270 days after Mar. 27, 1968, except as to cases in which an indictment is returned or petit jury is empaneled prior to such effective date, see [section 104 of Pub. L. 90–274](/us/pl/90/274/s104), set out as an Effective Date of 1968 Amendment note under [section 1861 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s1861), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
