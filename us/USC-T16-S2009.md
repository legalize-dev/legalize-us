# § 2009. Termination of program

##### § 2009. Termination of program

The provisions of this chapter shall terminate on December 31, 2023.

*([Pub. L. 95–192, § 10](/us/pl/95/192/s10), Nov. 18, 1977, [91 Stat. 1411](/us/stat/91/1411); [Pub. L. 99–198, title XII, § 1252(d)](/us/pl/99/198/tXII/s1252/d), Dec. 23, 1985, [99 Stat. 1517](/us/stat/99/1517); [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title II, § 2804(e), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 1816; [Pub. #### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) and [Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246) made identical amendments to this section. The amendments by [Pub. L. 110–234](/us/pl/110/234) were repealed by [section 4(a) of Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a).

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–334](/us/pl/115/334) substituted “2023” for “2018”.

2008—[Pub. L. 110–246, § 2804(e)](/us/pl/110/246/s2804/e), substituted “2018” for “2008”.

1985—[Pub. L. 99–198](/us/pl/99/198) substituted “2008” for “1985”.
