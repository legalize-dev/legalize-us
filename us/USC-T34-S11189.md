# § 11189. Leasing surplus Federal property

##### § 11189. Leasing surplus Federal property

The Administrator may receive surplus Federal property (including facilities) and may lease such property to States and units of general local government for use in or as facilities for juvenile offenders, or for use in or as facilities for delinquency prevention and treatment activities.

*([Pub. L. 93–415, title II, § 299H](/us/pl/93/415/tII/s299H), as added [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title II, § 12218](/us/pl/107/273/dC/tII/s12218), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1893](/us/stat/116/1893).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 5679 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s5679), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
