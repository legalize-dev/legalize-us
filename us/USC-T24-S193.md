# § 193. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

##### § 193. Repealed. [July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XIII, § 1313, [58 Stat. 714](/us/stat/58/714)

Section, acts [Mar. 3, 1875, ch. 156, § 5](/us/act/1875-03-03/ch156/s5), [18 Stat. 486](/us/stat/18/486); [July 1, 1902, ch. 1370, § 1](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1370/s1), [32 Stat. 712](/us/stat/32/712); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309); [July 1, 1916, ch. 209](/us/act/1916-07-01/ch209), [39 Stat. 309](/us/stat/39/309); [July 1, 1918, ch. 113](/us/act/1918-07-01/ch113), [40 Stat. 644](/us/stat/40/644); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, §§ 201, 205, eff. July 1, 1939, 4 F.R. 2728, 2729, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424), 1425; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. IV, § 11(a), eff. June 30, 1940, 5 F.R. 2422, [54 Stat. 1236](/us/stat/54/1236), provided for admission of insane patients of Public Health Service to Saint Elizabeths Hospital. See [section 222 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s222), The Public Health and Welfare.
