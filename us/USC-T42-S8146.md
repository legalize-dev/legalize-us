# § 8146. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 8146. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated for carrying out the purposes of this subchapter not to exceed $5,000,000 for fiscal year 1979, and not to exceed $5,000,000 for fiscal year 1980. Any amounts appropriated under this section shall remain available until expended.

*([Pub. L. 95–557, title VIII, § 807](/us/pl/95/557/tVIII/s807), Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2124](/us/stat/92/2124); [Pub. L. 96–153, title I, § 108](/us/pl/96/153/tI/s108), Dec. 21, 1979, [93 Stat. 1105](/us/stat/93/1105).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1979—[Pub. L. 96–153](/us/pl/96/153) reduced authorization of appropriation for fiscal year 1980 from “$10,000,000” to “$5,000,000”.
