#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Termination of Conservation Stewardship Program

[Pub. L. 115–334, title II, § 2301(c)(2)](/us/pl/115/334/tII/s2301/c/2), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4552](/us/stat/132/4552), provided that: “Effective on the date of enactment of this Act [Dec. 20, 2018], the conservation stewardship program under subchapter B of chapter 2 of subtitle D of title XII of the Food Security Act of 1985 ([16 U.S.C. 3838d](/us/usc/t16/s3838d) et seq.) (as in effect on the day before the date of enactment of this Act) shall cease to be effective.”
