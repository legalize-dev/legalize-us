#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 12](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s12), [50 Stat. 902](/us/stat/50/902), which authorized Secretary to promulgate rules and regulations, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
