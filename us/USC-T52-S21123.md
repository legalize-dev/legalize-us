# § 21123. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 21123. Authorization of appropriations

(1) $5,000,000 for fiscal year 2003; and

(2) such sums as may be necessary for each succeeding fiscal year.

*([Pub. L. 107–252, title V, § 503](/us/pl/107/252/tV/s503), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1717](/us/stat/116/1717).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 15523 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s15523), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
