# § 7441a. Cadets: appointment by the President

##### § 7441a. Cadets: appointment by the President

Cadets at the Academy shall be appointed by the President alone. An appointment is conditional until the cadet is admitted.

*(Added [Pub. L. 97–60, title II, § 203(a)(2)(A)](/us/pl/97/60/tII/s203/a/2/A), Oct. 14, 1981, [95 Stat. 1006](/us/stat/95/1006), § 4341a; renumbered § 7441a, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(c)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/c/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1839](/us/stat/132/1839).)*
