# § 5391. Civil actions

##### § 5391. Civil actions

(a) Contract defined For the purposes of [section 5331 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5331), the term “contract” shall include compacts and funding agreements entered into under this subchapter.

(b) Applicability of certain laws [Section 81 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s81) and [section 5123 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5123), shall not apply to attorney and other professional contracts entered into by Indian tribes participating in self-governance under this subchapter.

(c) References All references in this chapter to [section 5201 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5201) are hereby deemed to include [section 82a of this title](/us/usc/t25/s82a).

*([Pub. L. 93–638, title V, § 511](/us/pl/93/638/tV/s511), as added [Pub. L. 106–260, § 4](/us/pl/106/260/s4), Aug. 18, 2000, [114 Stat. 726](/us/stat/114/726).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in subsec. (c), was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 93–638](/us/pl/93/638), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2203](/us/stat/88/2203), known as the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 5301 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5301) and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 458aaa–10 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s458aaa–10) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
