# § 70124. Regulations

##### § 70124. Regulations

Unless otherwise provided, the Secretary may issue regulations necessary to implement this chapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 111–281, title VIII, § 820(a)](/us/pl/111/281/tVIII/s820/a), Oct. 15, 2010, [124 Stat. 3001](/us/stat/124/3001).)*
