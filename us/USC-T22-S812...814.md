# §§ 812 to 814. Transferred

##### §§ 812 to 814. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 812, acts [May 24, 1924, ch. 182, § 18](/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182/s18), formerly § 11, [43 Stat. 142](/us/stat/43/142); renumbered § 18 and amended [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276, § 7](/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276/s7), [46 Stat. 1209](/us/stat/46/1209), which related to fees, accounting, and stamps, was transferred to [section 4224 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4224).

Section 813, [act May 24, 1924, ch. 182, § 35](/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182/s35), as added [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276, § 7](/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276/s7), [46 Stat. 1216](/us/stat/46/1216) and amended, which related to establishment of fiscal districts and district accounting and disbursing offices, was transferred to [section 4225 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4225).

Section 814, [act May 24, 1924, ch. 182, § 36](/us/act/1924-05-24/ch182/s36), as added [Feb. 23, 1931, ch. 276, § 7](/us/act/1931-02-23/ch276/s7), [46 Stat. 1216](/us/stat/46/1216), which related to disposition of fees and official monies from diplomatic missions, consular offices and district accounting and disbursing offices, was transferred to [section 4226 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4226).
