# § 556. Omitted

##### § 556. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act June 1, 1938, ch. 310, § 6](/us/act/1938-06-01/ch310/s6), [52 Stat. 606](/us/stat/52/606), which provided for reversion of interest in property upon the death of an enrolled member without heirs or devisees, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
