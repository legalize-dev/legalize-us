# § 1778f. Omitted

##### § 1778f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 106–568, title VI, § 608](/us/pl/106/568/tVI/s608), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2912](/us/stat/114/2912), which related to satisfaction of claims regarding lack of drainage of tribal and allottee lands and approval of releases and waivers required by the Settlement Agreement and this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
