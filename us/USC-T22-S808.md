# § 808. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–310, title II, § 227(c)](/us/pl/92/310/tII/s227/c), June 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 207](/us/stat/86/207)

##### § 808. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–310, title II, § 227(c)](/us/pl/92/310/tII/s227/c), June 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 207](/us/stat/86/207)

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title X, § 1011](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tX/s1011), [60 Stat. 1030](/us/stat/60/1030), related to bonds of officers and employees.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Impairment of Existing Bonds

[Act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title XI, § 1121](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tXI/s1121), [60 Stat. 1035](/us/stat/60/1035), which related to impairment of existing bonds by enactment of this chapter, was repealed by [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159).
