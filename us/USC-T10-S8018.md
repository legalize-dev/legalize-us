# § 8018. Administrative Assistant

##### § 8018. Administrative Assistant

The Secretary of the Navy may appoint an Administrative Assistant in the Office of the Secretary of the Navy. The Administrative Assistant shall perform such duties as the Secretary may prescribe.

*(Added [Pub. L. 99–433, title V, § 511(c)(4)](/us/pl/99/433/tV/s511/c/4), Oct. 1, 1986, [100 Stat. 1047](/us/stat/100/1047), § 5018; renumbered § 8018, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 807(a)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s807/a/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1834](/us/stat/132/1834).)*
