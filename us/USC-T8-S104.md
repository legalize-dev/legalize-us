##### § 104. Repealed. [Dec. 17, 1943, ch. 344, § 1](/us/act/1943-12-17/ch344/s1), [57 Stat. 600](/us/stat/57/600)

Section, acts [June 6, 1900, ch. 791, § 1](/us/act/1900-06-06/ch791/s1), [31 Stat. 611](/us/stat/31/611); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 3](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s3), [37 Stat. 737](/us/stat/37/737); Ex. Ord. No. 6166, § 14, June 10, 1933; 1940 Reorg. Plan No. V, eff. June 14, 1940, 5 F.R. 2423, [54 Stat. 1238](/us/stat/54/1238), provided that Commissioner of Immigration and Naturalization should have charge, under supervision of Attorney General, of administration of Chinese exclusion laws.
