# §§ 343 to 347. Repealed. [Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 96](/us/stat/60/96)

##### §§ 343 to 347. Repealed. [Apr. 19, 1946, ch. 142](/us/act/1946-04-19/ch142), [60 Stat. 96](/us/stat/60/96)

Section 343, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title III, § 313](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIII/s313), as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150](/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150), [58 Stat. 146](/us/stat/58/146), related to penalties under sections 341 to 347 of this title.

Section 344, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title III, § 314](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIII/s314), as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150](/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150), [58 Stat. 146](/us/stat/58/146), related to prohibition on taking polls. See [section 596 of Title 18](/us/usc/t18/s596), Crimes and Criminal Procedure.

Section 344 was also repealed by [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645, § 21](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645/s21), [62 Stat. 862](/us/stat/62/862).

Section 345, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title III, § 315](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIII/s315), as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150](/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150), [58 Stat. 147](/us/stat/58/147), related to certain State officials.

Section 346, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title III, § 316](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIII/s316), as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150](/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150), [58 Stat. 147](/us/stat/58/147), related to agencies acting for the Secretary of State.

Section 347, [act Sept. 16, 1942, ch. 561, title III, § 317](/us/act/1942-09-16/ch561/tIII/s317), as added [Apr. 1, 1944, ch. 150](/us/act/1944-04-01/ch150), [58 Stat. 147](/us/stat/58/147), related to construction of chapter.
