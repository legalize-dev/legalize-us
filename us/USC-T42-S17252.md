# § 17252. Review of large-scale programs

##### § 17252. Review of large-scale programs

The Secretary shall enter into an arrangement with the National Academy of Sciences for an independent review and oversight, beginning in 2011, of the programs under [section 16293(c) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s16293/c) and under [section 17251 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s17251), to ensure that the benefits of such programs are maximized. Not later than January 1, 2012, the Secretary shall transmit to the Congress a report on the results of such review and oversight.

*([Pub. L. 110–140, title VII, § 704](/us/pl/110/140/tVII/s704), Dec. 19, 2007, [121 Stat. 1709](/us/stat/121/1709); [Pub. L. 116–260, div. Z, title IV, § 4003(c)(2)](/us/pl/116/260/dZ/tIV/s4003/c/2), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2539](/us/stat/134/2539).)*
