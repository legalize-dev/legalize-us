# § 2427. Publication

##### § 2427. Publication

The Secretary may establish regulations for the publication of information regarding any pending application when publication is requested by the owner.

*([Pub. L. 91–577, title II, § 57](/us/pl/91/577/tII/s57), Dec. 24, 1970, [84 Stat. 1549](/us/stat/84/1549); [Pub. L. 96–574, § 13](/us/pl/96/574/s13), Dec. 22, 1980, [94 Stat. 3350](/us/stat/94/3350).)*

#### Amendments
