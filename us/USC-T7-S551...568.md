# §§ 551 to 568. Transferred

##### §§ 551 to 568. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 551 to 568 of this title, which were comprised of [act June 10, 1930, ch. 436](/us/act/1930-06-10/ch436), §§ 1–18, [46 Stat. 531](/us/stat/46/531), as amended, known as the Perishable Agricultural Commodities Act, 1930, were transferred to sections 499a to 499r of chapter 20A of this title.
