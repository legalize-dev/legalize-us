# §§ 4201 to 4206. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–498, title V, § 501(b)](/us/pl/99/498/tV/s501/b), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1514](/us/stat/100/1514)

##### §§ 4201 to 4206. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–498, title V, § 501(b)](/us/pl/99/498/tV/s501/b), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1514](/us/stat/100/1514)

Section 4201, [Pub. L. 98–558, title IX, § 901](/us/pl/98/558/tIX/s901), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2902](/us/stat/98/2902), provided for citation of chapter as “Leadership on Educational Administration Development Act of 1984” and contained statement of purpose and declaration of Congressional intent in enacting chapter.

Section 4202, [Pub. L. 98–558, title IX, § 902](/us/pl/98/558/tIX/s902), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2903](/us/stat/98/2903), authorized appropriations for fiscal years 1985 to 1990 to carry out chapter.

Section 4203, [Pub. L. 98–558, title IX, § 903](/us/pl/98/558/tIX/s903), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2903](/us/stat/98/2903), provided for establishment of technical assistance centers.

Section 4204, [Pub. L. 98–558, title IX, § 904](/us/pl/98/558/tIX/s904), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2904](/us/stat/98/2904), set out general criteria for contracts to establish and operate technical assistance centers.

Section 4205, [Pub. L. 98–558, title IX, § 905](/us/pl/98/558/tIX/s905), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2904](/us/stat/98/2904), authorized Secretary to prescribe regulations to carry out chapter.

Section 4206, [Pub. L. 98–558, title IX, § 906](/us/pl/98/558/tIX/s906), Oct. 30, 1984, [98 Stat. 2904](/us/stat/98/2904); [Pub. L. 99–500, § 101(d) [title I, § 134(a)]](/us/pl/99/500/s101/d/tI/s134/a), Oct. 18, 1986, [100 Stat. 1783–180](/us/stat/100/1783-180), 1783–192, and [Pub. L. 99–591, § 101(d) [title I, § 134(a)]](/us/pl/99/591/s101/d/tI/s134/a), Oct. 30, 1986, [100 Stat. 3341–180](/us/stat/100/3341-180), 3341–192, defined terms used in chapter.
