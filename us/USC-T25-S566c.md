# § 566c. Omitted

##### § 566c. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 99–398, § 5](/us/pl/99/398/s5), Aug. 27, 1986, [100 Stat. 850](/us/stat/100/850), which related to hunting, fishing, trapping, and water rights, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
