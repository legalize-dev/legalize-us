# § 677w. Omitted

##### § 677w. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 24](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s24), [68 Stat. 877](/us/stat/68/877); [Pub. L. 93–608, § 1(15)](/us/pl/93/608/s1/15), Jan. 2, 1975, [88 Stat. 1969](/us/stat/88/1969), which related to presentation of a development program for the full-blood group to eventually terminate Federal supervision, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
