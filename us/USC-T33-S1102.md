#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–454, title I, § 3](/us/pl/89/454/tI/s3), June 17, 1966, [80 Stat. 204](/us/stat/80/204); [Pub. L. 89–670, § 8(j)](/us/pl/89/670/s8/j), Oct. 15, 1966, [80 Stat. 943](/us/stat/80/943); [Pub. L. 90–242, § 2](/us/pl/90/242/s2), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 780](/us/stat/81/780); [Pub. L. 91–15, § 1](/us/pl/91/15/s1), May 23, 1969, [83 Stat. 10](/us/stat/83/10); [Pub. L. 91–414](/us/pl/91/414), Sept. 25, 1970, [84 Stat. 865](/us/stat/84/865), established the National Council on Marine Resources and Engineering Development. The provisions of this chapter with respect to the Council expired on June 30, 1971, and the Council terminated on Apr. 30, 1971, for lack of funds.
