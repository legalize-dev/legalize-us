# § 565a. Cooperation by Secretary of Agriculture with Territories

##### § 565a. Cooperation by Secretary of Agriculture with Territories

The Secretary of Agriculture is authorized to cooperate with the Territories of the United States on the same terms and conditions as with States under sections 564 and 565 11 See References in Text note below. of this title.

*([Feb. 20, 1931, ch. 249](/us/act/1931-02-20/ch249), [46 Stat. 1200](/us/stat/46/1200).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Sections 564 and 565 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by [Pub. L. 95–313, § 13(a)(1)](/us/pl/95/313/s13/a/1), July 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 374](/us/stat/92/374).
