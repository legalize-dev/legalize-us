# §§ 5081 to 5083. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–149, § 4(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/149/s4/a/1), Nov. 23, 1993, [107 Stat. 1504](/us/stat/107/1504)

##### §§ 5081 to 5083. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–149, § 4(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/149/s4/a/1), Nov. 23, 1993, [107 Stat. 1504](/us/stat/107/1504)

Section 5081, [Pub. L. 99–440, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/99/440/tIV/s401), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1106](/us/stat/100/1106); [Pub. L. 99–631, § 1(a)(24)](/us/pl/99/631/s1/a/24), Nov. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 3518](/us/stat/100/3518), related to negotiating authority of President to reach agreements on multilateral measures to undermine apartheid.

Section 5082, [Pub. L. 99–440, title IV, § 402](/us/pl/99/440/tIV/s402), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/100/1107); [Pub. L. 99–631, § 1(a)(25)](/us/pl/99/631/s1/a/25), Nov. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 3518](/us/stat/100/3518), authorized President to limit imports from foreign countries benefitting from sanctions or prohibitions imposed under this chapter.

Section 5083, [Pub. L. 99–440, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/99/440/tIV/s403), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1107](/us/stat/100/1107), related to private right of action.
