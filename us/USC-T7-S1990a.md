# § 1990a. Refinancing of certain rural hospital debt

##### § 1990a. Refinancing of certain rural hospital debt

Assistance under [section 1926(a) of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1926/a) for a community facility, or under [section 1932 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1932), may include the refinancing of a debt obligation of a rural hospital as an eligible loan or loan guarantee purpose if the assistance would help preserve access to a health service in a rural community, meaningfully improve the financial position of the hospital, and otherwise meet the financial feasibility and adequacy of security requirements of the Rural Development Agency.

*([Pub. L. 87–128, title III, § 342](/us/pl/87/128/tIII/s342), as added [Pub. L. 115–334, title VI, § 6103](/us/pl/115/334/tVI/s6103), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4728](/us/stat/132/4728).)*
