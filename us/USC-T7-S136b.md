L. 94–140](/us/pl/94/140), §§ 5, 11, [89 Stat. 753](/us/stat/89/753), 754; Sept. 30, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–396, § 9](/us/pl/95/396/s9), [92 Stat. 827](/us/stat/92/827); Oct. 25, 1988, [Pub. L. 100–532, title VIII, § 801(c)](/us/pl/100/532/tVIII/s801/c), (q)(1)(A), (B), [102 Stat. 2681](/us/stat/102/2681), 2683, which related to use of restricted use pesticides and certification of applicators, was transferred to subsecs. (a) to (c) of section 11 of act June 25, 1947, by [section 801(q)(1)(A) of Pub. L. 100–532](/us/pl/100/532/s801/q/1/A) and is classified to section 136i(a) to (c) of this title.
