# §§ 501 to 502. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379)

##### §§ 501 to 502. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379)

Section 501, [Pub. L. 91–518, title I, § 101](/us/pl/91/518/tI/s101), Oct. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1328](/us/stat/84/1328); [Pub. L. 96–73, title I, § 102](/us/pl/96/73/tI/s102), Sept. 29, 1979, [93 Stat. 537](/us/stat/93/537); [Pub. L. 97–35, title XI, § 1171](/us/pl/97/35/tXI/s1171), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 687](/us/stat/95/687), related to congressional findings on needs for cost- and energy-efficient intercity rail passenger service. See [section 24101 of Title 49](/us/usc/t49/s24101), Transportation.

Section 501a, [Pub. L. 91–518, title I, § 102](/us/pl/91/518/tI/s102), as added [Pub. L. 96–73, title I, § 103(a)](/us/pl/96/73/tI/s103/a), Sept. 29, 1979, [93 Stat. 537](/us/stat/93/537); amended [Pub. L. 97–35, title XI, § 1172](/us/pl/97/35/tXI/s1172), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 688](/us/stat/95/688), enumerated goals established by Congress for Amtrak. See [section 24101 of Title 49](/us/usc/t49/s24101).

A prior [section 102 of Pub. L. 91–518](/us/pl/91/518/s102) was renumbered section 103 and classified to [section 502 of this title](/us/usc/t45/s502), prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b).

Section 502, [Pub. L. 91–518, title I, § 103](/us/pl/91/518/tI/s103), formerly § 102, Oct. 30, 1970, [84 Stat. 1328](/us/stat/84/1328); [Pub. L. 93–146, § 2](/us/pl/93/146/s2), Nov. 3, 1973, [87 Stat. 548](/us/stat/87/548); renumbered § 103 and amended [Pub. L. 96–73, title I](/us/pl/96/73/tI), §§ 103(a), 104, Sept. 29, 1979, [93 Stat. 537](/us/stat/93/537), 538; [Pub. L. 97–35, title XI, § 1173](/us/pl/97/35/tXI/s1173), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 689](/us/stat/95/689); [Pub. L. 99–272, title IV, § 4012](/us/pl/99/272/tIV/s4012), Apr. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 109](/us/stat/100/109); [Pub. L. 102–533, § 8](/us/pl/102/533/s8), Oct. 27, 1992, [106 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/106/3519), defined terms for purposes of this chapter. See [section 24102 of Title 49](/us/usc/t49/s24102), Transportation.
