# §§ 2994 to 2994g. Omitted

##### §§ 2994 to 2994g. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections were omitted in the general amendment of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), by [Pub. L. 103–382, title I, § 101](/us/pl/103/382/tI/s101), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 3519](/us/stat/108/3519).

Section 2994, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2016](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s2016), as added [Pub. L. 101–589, title II, § 205(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/589/tII/s205/a/2), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2886](/us/stat/104/2886), authorized award of grants and contracts for establishment and operation of regional mathematics and science education consortia.

Section 2994a, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2017](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s2017), as added [Pub. L. 101–589, title II, § 205(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/589/tII/s205/a/2), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2887](/us/stat/104/2887), related to use of funds.

Section 2994b, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2018](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s2018), as added [Pub. L. 101–589, title II, § 205(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/589/tII/s205/a/2), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2888](/us/stat/104/2888), related to application and review.

Section 2994c, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2019](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s2019), as added [Pub. L. 101–589, title II, § 205(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/589/tII/s205/a/2), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2889](/us/stat/104/2889), related to regional boards.

Section 2994d, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2020](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s2020), as added [Pub. L. 101–589, title II, § 205(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/589/tII/s205/a/2), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2889](/us/stat/104/2889), related to payments, Federal share, and non-Federal share.

Section 2994e, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2021](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s2021), as added [Pub. L. 101–589, title II, § 205(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/589/tII/s205/a/2), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2889](/us/stat/104/2889), related to evaluations of regional consortia.

Section 2994f, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2022](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s2022), as added [Pub. L. 101–589, title II, § 205(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/589/tII/s205/a/2), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2890](/us/stat/104/2890), defined terms used in subpart.

Section 2994g, [Pub. L. 89–10, title II, § 2023](/us/pl/89/10/tII/s2023), as added [Pub. L. 101–589, title II, § 205(a)(2)](/us/pl/101/589/tII/s205/a/2), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2890](/us/stat/104/2890), authorized appropriations.
