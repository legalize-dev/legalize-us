# § 152409. Civil action by Attorney General for equitable relief

##### § 152409. Civil action by Attorney General for equitable relief

(1) engages or threatens to engage in any act, practice, or policy that is inconsistent with the purposes in [section 152402 of this title](/us/usc/t36/s152402); or

(2) refuses, fails, or neglects to carry out its obligations under this chapter or threatens to do so.

*(Added [Pub. L. 106–474, title II, § 201(a)](/us/pl/106/474/tII/s201/a), Nov. 9, 2000, [114 Stat. 2094](/us/stat/114/2094).)*
