# § 9441a. Cadets: appointment by the President

##### § 9441a. Cadets: appointment by the President

Cadets at the Academy shall be appointed by the President alone. An appointment is conditional until the cadet is admitted.

*(Added [Pub. L. 97–60, title II, § 203(c)(2)(A)](/us/pl/97/60/tII/s203/c/2/A), Oct. 14, 1981, [95 Stat. 1006](/us/stat/95/1006), § 9341a; renumbered § 9441a, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 806(a)(3)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s806/a/3), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1832](/us/stat/132/1832).)*
