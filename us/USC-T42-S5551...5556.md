# §§ 5551 to 5556. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–260, div. Z, title III, § 3006(e)(1)](/us/pl/116/260/dZ/tIII/s3006/e/1), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2513](/us/stat/134/2513)

##### §§ 5551 to 5556. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–260, div. Z, title III, § 3006(e)(1)](/us/pl/116/260/dZ/tIII/s3006/e/1), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2513](/us/stat/134/2513)

Section 5551, [Pub. L. 93–473, § 2](/us/pl/93/473/s2), Oct. 26, 1974, [88 Stat. 1431](/us/stat/88/1431), related to congressional declaration of findings and policy.

Section 5552, [Pub. L. 93–473, § 3](/us/pl/93/473/s3), Oct. 26, 1974, [88 Stat. 1431](/us/stat/88/1431), defined terms for this subchapter.

Section 5553, [Pub. L. 93–473, § 4](/us/pl/93/473/s4), Oct. 26, 1974, [88 Stat. 1432](/us/stat/88/1432), related to the Solar Energy Coordination and Management Project.

Section 5554, [Pub. L. 93–473, § 5](/us/pl/93/473/s5), Oct. 26, 1974, [88 Stat. 1433](/us/stat/88/1433), related to solar energy resource determination and assessment program, objectives, and implementation.

Section 5555, [Pub. L. 93–473, § 6](/us/pl/93/473/s6), Oct. 26, 1974, [88 Stat. 1433](/us/stat/88/1433), related to research and development program.

Section 5556, [Pub. L. 93–473, § 7](/us/pl/93/473/s7), Oct. 26, 1974, [88 Stat. 1434](/us/stat/88/1434), related to solar energy demonstration facilities program.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Savings Provision

[Pub. L. 116–260, div. Z, title III, § 3006(e)(2)](/us/pl/116/260/dZ/tIII/s3006/e/2), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2513](/us/stat/134/2513), provided that: “The repeal of the Solar Energy Research, Development, and Demonstration Act of 1974 ([42 U.S.C. 5551](/us/usc/t42/s5551) et seq.) under paragraph (1) shall not affect the authority of the Secretary of Energy to conduct research and development on solar energy.”
