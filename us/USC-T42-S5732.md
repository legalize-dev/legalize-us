##### § 5732. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 5732 was editorially reclassified as [section 11277 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s11277), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

A prior section 5732, [Pub. L. 93–415, title III, § 322](/us/pl/93/415/tIII/s322), Sept. 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 1132](/us/stat/88/1132), set forth restrictions on disclosure and transfer of records, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 95–115, § 7(b)](/us/pl/95/115/s7/b), Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1058](/us/stat/91/1058), eff. Oct. 1, 1977.
