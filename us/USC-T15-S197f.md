##### § 197f. Repealed. [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title XI, § 1131(54), [60 Stat. 1039](/us/stat/60/1039)

Section, [act Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 365, § 7](/us/act/1927-03-03/ch365/s7), [44 Stat. 1396](/us/stat/44/1396), related to availability of appropriation during fiscal year ending June 30, 1926, and thereafter for the Department of Commerce for commercial attachés in Europe, South or Central America and elsewhere.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective three months from Aug. 13, 1946, see section 1141 of act Aug. 13, 1946.
