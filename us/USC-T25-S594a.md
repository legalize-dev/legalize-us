# § 594a. Omitted

##### § 594a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–94, § 2](/us/pl/90/94/s2), Sept. 27, 1967, [81 Stat. 230](/us/stat/81/230), which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to prescribe rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of former sections 594 and 594a of this title, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
