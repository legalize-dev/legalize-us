# § 2307. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 2307. Authorization of appropriations

(1) $1,229,568,538 for fiscal year 2019;

(2) $1,246,782,498 for fiscal year 2020;

(3) $1,264,237,452 for fiscal year 2021;

(4) $1,281,936,777 for fiscal year 2022;

(5) $1,299,883,892 for fiscal year 2023; and

(6) $1,318,082,266 for fiscal year 2024.

*([Pub. L. 88–210, § 9](/us/pl/88/210/s9), as added [Pub. L. 109–270, § 1(b)](/us/pl/109/270/s1/b), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 691](/us/stat/120/691); amended [Pub. L. 115–224, § 10](/us/pl/115/224/s10), July 31, 2018, [132 Stat. 1576](/us/stat/132/1576).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 2307, [Pub. L. 88–210, § 8](/us/pl/88/210/s8), as added [Pub. L. 105–332, § 1(b)](/us/pl/105/332/s1/b), Oct. 31, 1998, [112 Stat. 3083](/us/stat/112/3083), related to authorization of appropriations, prior to the general amendment of this chapter by [Pub. L. 109–270](/us/pl/109/270).

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–224](/us/pl/115/224) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “There is authorized to be appropriated to carry out this chapter (other than sections 2324, 2327, and 2328 of this title, and subchapter II) such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 2007 through 2012.”
