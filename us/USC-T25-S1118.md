# § 1118. Omitted

##### § 1118. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–659, § 8](/us/pl/89/659/s8), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 910](/us/stat/80/910), which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
