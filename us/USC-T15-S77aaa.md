# § 77aaa. Short title

##### § 77aaa. Short title

This subchapter may be cited as the “Trust Indenture Act of 1939.”

*([May 27, 1933, ch. 38](/us/act/1933-05-27/ch38), title III, § 301, as added [Aug. 3, 1939, ch. 411](/us/act/1939-08-03/ch411), [53 Stat. 1149](/us/stat/53/1149).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title of 1990 Amendment

[Pub. L. 101–550, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/101/550/tIV/s401), Nov. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 2721](/us/stat/104/2721), provided that: “This title [amending sections 77ccc to 77eee, 77iii to 77rrr, and 77vvv of this title] may be cited as the ‘Trust Indenture Reform Act of 1990’.”
