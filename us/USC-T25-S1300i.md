# § 1300i. Omitted

##### § 1300i. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–580, § 1](/us/pl/100/580/s1), Oct. 31, 1988, [102 Stat. 2924](/us/stat/102/2924), which provided that this subchapter could be cited as the “Hoopa-Yurok Settlement Act” and set out definitions, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
