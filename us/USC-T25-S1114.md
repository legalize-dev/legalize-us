# § 1114. Omitted

##### § 1114. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–659, § 4](/us/pl/89/659/s4), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 909](/us/stat/80/909), which related to membership roll of Miami Indians of Oklahoma, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
