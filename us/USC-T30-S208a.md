# § 208a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–468, title VI, § 615(a)(3)](/us/pl/97/468/tVI/s615/a/3), Jan. 14, 1983, [96 Stat. 2578](/us/stat/96/2578)

##### § 208a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–468, title VI, § 615(a)(3)](/us/pl/97/468/tVI/s615/a/3), Jan. 14, 1983, [96 Stat. 2578](/us/stat/96/2578)

Section, [act July 19, 1932, ch. 513](/us/act/1932-07-19/ch513), [47 Stat. 707](/us/stat/47/707), authorized general manager of Alaska Railroad to purchase coal annually for railroad from two or more operating companies in areas adjacent to railroad.

Section was formerly classified to [section 445a of Title 48](/us/usc/t48/s445a), Territories and Insular Possessions.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal by [Pub. L. 97–468](/us/pl/97/468) became effective on date of transfer of Alaska Railroad to the State [Jan. 5, 1985], pursuant to [section 1203 of Title 45](/us/usc/t45/s1203), Railroads, see [Pub. L. 97–468, title VI, § 615(a)](/us/pl/97/468/tVI/s615/a), Jan. 14, 1983, [96 Stat. 2577](/us/stat/96/2577).
