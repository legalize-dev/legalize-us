# § 465b. Omitted

##### § 465b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Feb. 24, 1942, ch. 113, § 2](/us/act/1942-02-24/ch113/s2), [56 Stat. 121](/us/stat/56/121), which defined “Klamath Tribe of Indians”, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
