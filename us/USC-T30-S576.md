# § 576. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 576. Authorization of appropriations

There is hereby authorized to be appropriated such amounts as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

*([July 15, 1955, ch. 369, § 5](/us/act/1955-07-15/ch369/s5), formerly § 6, [69 Stat. 353](/us/stat/69/353); renumbered § 5, [Pub. L. 105–362, title IX, § 901(i)(2)](/us/pl/105/362/tIX/s901/i/2), Nov. 10, 1998, [112 Stat. 3290](/us/stat/112/3290).)*
