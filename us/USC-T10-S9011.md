# § 9011. Organization

##### § 9011. Organization

The Department of the Air Force is separately organized under the Secretary of the Air Force. It operates under the authority, direction, and control of the Secretary of Defense.

*(Added [Pub. L. 87–651, title II, § 213(a)](/us/pl/87/651/tII/s213/a), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 524](/us/stat/76/524), § 8010; renumbered § 8011, [Pub. L. 99–433, title V, § 521(a)(1)](/us/pl/99/433/tV/s521/a/1), Oct. 1, 1986, [100 Stat. 1055](/us/stat/100/1055); renumbered § 9011, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 806(c)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s806/c), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1833](/us/stat/132/1833).)*

The word “operates” is substituted for the words “shall function”.
