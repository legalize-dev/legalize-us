# § 743. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–280, § 1(2)](/us/pl/93/280/s1/2), May 10, 1974, [88 Stat. 123](/us/stat/88/123)

##### § 743. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–280, § 1(2)](/us/pl/93/280/s1/2), May 10, 1974, [88 Stat. 123](/us/stat/88/123)

Section, [act Mar. 3, 1885, ch. 360, § 1(1)](/us/act/1885-03-03/ch360/s1/1), [23 Stat. 494](/us/stat/23/494), renumbered by [Pub. L. 93–280, § 1(1)](/us/pl/93/280/s1/1), May 10, 1974, [88 Stat. 123](/us/stat/88/123), was part of a paragraph entitled: “Propagation of Food Fishes” in the Sundry Civil Expenses Appropriation Act, 1886. It authorized the Secretary of the Treasury to detail Coast Guard personnel to the Fish and Wildlife Services for duty. See [section 743a of this title](/us/usc/t16/s743a).
