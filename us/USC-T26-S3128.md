# § 3128. Short title

##### § 3128. Short title

This chapter may be cited as the “Federal Insurance Contributions Act.”

*([Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 429](/us/stat/68A/429), § 3125; renumbered § 3126, [Pub.
