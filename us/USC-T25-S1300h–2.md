# § 1300h–2. Omitted

##### § 1300h–2. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–420, § 4](/us/pl/100/420/s4), Sept. 8, 1988, [102 Stat. 1577](/us/stat/102/1577), which reaffirmed the Federal recognition of the band and the trust relationship between the United States and the band and provided for the band and its members to be eligible for all special programs and services provided by the United States to Indians because of their status as Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
