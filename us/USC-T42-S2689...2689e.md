#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 902(h) of Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35/s902/h), set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under section 238*l* of this title.

#### Short Title

[Section 245 of title II of Pub. L. 88–164](/us/pl/88/164/tII/s245), as added by [section 303 of Pub. L. 94–63](/us/pl/94/63/s303), title III, July 29, 1975, [89 Stat. 333](/us/stat/89/333), which provided that title II of [Pub. L. 88–164](/us/pl/88/164) [this subchapter] was to be cited as the “Community Mental Health Centers Act”, was repealed by [section 902(e)(2)(B) of Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35/s902/e/2/B).
