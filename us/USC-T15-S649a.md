# § 649a. Omitted

##### § 649a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–481, title III, § 301(a)](/us/pl/96/481/tIII/s301/a)–(d), Oct. 21, 1980, [94 Stat. 2330](/us/stat/94/2330), which related to establishment, staffing, functions, evaluation, and reporting requirements of export promotion centers, terminated Oct. 1, 1983.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective and Termination Dates

[Pub. L. 96–481, title III, § 301(e)](/us/pl/96/481/tIII/s301/e), Oct. 21, 1980, [94 Stat. 2331](/us/stat/94/2331), provided that: “This section shall take effect on October 1, 1980, or on the date of the enactment of this section [Oct. 21, 1980] whichever occurs later and shall expire on October 1, 1983.”
