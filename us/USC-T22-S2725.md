# § 2725. Fees for use of the George P. Shultz National Foreign Affairs Training Center

##### § 2725. Fees for use of the George P. Shultz National Foreign Affairs Training Center

The Secretary, acting through the Under Secretary for Management 11 So in original. Probably should be followed by a comma. is authorized to charge a fee for use of the George P. Shultz National Foreign Affairs Training Center of the Department of State. Amounts collected under this section (including reimbursements and surcharges) shall be deposited as an offsetting collection to any Department of State appropriation to recover the costs of such use and shall remain available for obligation until expended.

*([Aug. 1, 1956, ch. 841](/us/act/1956-08-01/ch841), title I, § 53, as added [Pub. L. 105–277, div. G](/us/pl/105/277/dG), subdiv. #### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2025—[Pub. L. 119–60](/us/pl/119/60) substituted “The Secretary, acting through the Under Secretary for Management” for “The Secretary”.

2002—[Pub. L. 107–132](/us/pl/107/132) inserted “George P. Shultz” before “National Foreign Affairs Training Center” in section catchline and in text.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Reporting on Pilot Program

[Pub. L. 105–277, div. G](/us/pl/105/277/dG), subdiv. B, title XXII, § 2205(c), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–809](/us/stat/112/2681-809), required the Secretary of State to submit, two years after Oct. 21, 1998, a report to Congress on the number of persons taking advantage of the pilot program established under [section 4021 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s4021) and this section, their business or government affiliations, the amount of fees collected, and the impact of the program on the primary mission of the National Foreign Affairs Training Center, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 107–228, div. A, title III, § 318(3)](/us/pl/107/228/dA/tIII/s318/3), Sept. 30, 2002, [116 Stat. 1380](/us/stat/116/1380).
