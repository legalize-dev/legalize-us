# § 181. Repealed. [Dec. 16, 1930, ch. 14, § 1](/us/act/1930-12-16/ch14/s1), [46 Stat. 1029](/us/stat/46/1029)

##### § 181. Repealed. [Dec. 16, 1930, ch. 14, § 1](/us/act/1930-12-16/ch14/s1), [46 Stat. 1029](/us/stat/46/1029)

Section, [act June 5, 1900, ch. 716, § 3](/us/act/1900-06-05/ch716/s3), [31 Stat. 270](/us/stat/31/270), provided that a person making an entry which was lost or forfeited should be entitled to benefits of homestead laws as though the former entry had not been made. See [section 182 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s182).
