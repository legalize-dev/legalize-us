# § 2661a. Foreign contracts or arrangements; discrimination

##### § 2661a. Foreign contracts or arrangements; discrimination

(1) any United States person (as defined in [section 7701(a)(30) of title 26](/us/usc/t26/s7701/a/30)) is prohibited from entering into such contract or arrangement, or

(2) such contract or arrangement requires that any such person be excluded from participating in the implementation of such contract or arrangement,

*([Pub. L. 94–350, title I, § 121](/us/pl/94/350/tI/s121), July 12, 1976, [90 Stat. 829](/us/stat/90/829); [Pub. L. 99–514, § 2](/us/pl/99/514/s2), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2095](/us/stat/100/2095).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1986—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 99–514](/us/pl/99/514) substituted “Internal Revenue Code of 1986” for “Internal Revenue Code of 1954”, which for purposes of codification was translated as “title 26” thus requiring no change in text.
