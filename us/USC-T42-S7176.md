# § 7176. Reorganization

##### § 7176. Reorganization

For the purposes of chapter 9 of title 5 the Commission shall be deemed to be an independent regulatory agency.

*([Pub. L. 95–91, title IV, § 406](/us/pl/95/91/tIV/s406), Aug. 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 586](/us/stat/91/586).)*
