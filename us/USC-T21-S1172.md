# § 1172. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–24, § 2(c)(2)](/us/pl/98/24/s2/c/2), Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 182](/us/stat/97/182)

##### § 1172. Repealed. [Pub. L. 98–24, § 2(c)(2)](/us/pl/98/24/s2/c/2), Apr. 26, 1983, [97 Stat. 182](/us/stat/97/182)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–255, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/92/255/tIV/s405), Mar. 21, 1972, [86 Stat. 77](/us/stat/86/77); [Pub. L. 95–461, § 3(b)](/us/pl/95/461/s3/b), Oct. 14, 1978, [92 Stat. 1268](/us/stat/92/1268); [Pub. L. 97–35, title IX, § 973(c)(1)](/us/pl/97/35/tIX/s973/c/1), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 598](/us/stat/95/598), required that the Secretary of Health and Human Services make periodic reports to the Congress and to the President on drug abuse in the United States. See [section 290aa–4 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s290aa–4), The Public Health and Welfare.
