# §§ 1939 to 1941. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 642(a)(2), (7), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460)

##### §§ 1939 to 1941. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 642(a)(2), (7), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460)

Section 1939, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. II, § 419, as added Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–141, § 8(n)](/us/pl/85/141/s8/n), [71 Stat. 362](/us/stat/71/362); amended June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. II, § 205(*l*), [72 Stat. 267](/us/stat/72/267); July 24, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–108](/us/pl/86/108), ch. II, § 205(n), [73 Stat. 251](/us/stat/73/251); May 14, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–472](/us/pl/86/472), ch. II, § 204(*l*), [74 Stat. 138](/us/stat/74/138), related to atoms for peace. See [section 2171(c) of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2171/c).

[Act July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 12](/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s12), [70 Stat. 565](/us/stat/70/565), which was formerly classified to [section 1939 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1939) and authorized appropriations for atoms for peace, was repealed by [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 642(a)(4) Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460).

Section 1940, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. II, § 420, as added Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–141, § 8(n)](/us/pl/85/141/s8/n), [71 Stat. 362](/us/stat/71/362); amended June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. II, § 205(m), [72 Stat. 267](/us/stat/72/267), provided for a program of malaria eradication.

Section 1940a, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. II, § 421, as added May 14, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–472](/us/pl/86/472), ch. II, § 204(m), [74 Stat. 138](/us/stat/74/138), related to loans to small farmers. See [section 2175 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2175).

Section 1941, [Pub. L. 86–108](/us/pl/86/108), ch. V, § 501, July 24, 1959, [73 Stat. 255](/us/stat/73/255), provided for international cooperation in health. Subsec. (b) of [section 501 of Pub. L. 86–108](/us/pl/86/108/s501) was previously repealed by [Pub. L. 86–472](/us/pl/86/472), ch. IV, § 602, May 14, 1960, [74 Stat. 141](/us/stat/74/141).
