#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–420, § 3](/us/pl/96/420/s3), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1786](/us/stat/94/1786), which set out definitions for this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
