# § 3717. Regulations

##### § 3717. Regulations

The Secretary is authorized to issue such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the provisions of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 97–35, title III, § 369I](/us/pl/97/35/tIII/s369I), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 431](/us/stat/95/431).)*
