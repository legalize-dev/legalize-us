# § 677v. Omitted

##### § 677v. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 23](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s23), [68 Stat. 877](/us/stat/68/877), which related to termination of Federal trust relationship to mixed-blood member of the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
