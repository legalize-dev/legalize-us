#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 103–130, § 3(a)](/us/pl/103/130/s3/a), (b), Nov. 1, 1993, [107 Stat. 1369](/us/stat/107/1369), provided that: “(a) In General.—Effective December 31, 1995, the National Wool Act of 1954 ([7 U.S.C. 1781](/us/usc/t7/s1781) et seq.) is repealed. “(b) Application.—The repeal made by subsection (a) [repealing this chapter and provisions set out as notes under sections 2, 1446, and 1781 of this title] shall apply to both the wool and mohair programs.”

#### Liability of Producers

[Pub. L. 103–130, § 5](/us/pl/103/130/s5), Nov. 1, 1993, [107 Stat. 1369](/us/stat/107/1369), provided that: “A provision of this Act [amending sections 1782, 1783, and 1785 of this title, repealing sections 1781 to 1787 of this title, enacting provisions set out as notes under this section and [section 1447 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1447), and repealing provisions set out as notes under sections 2, 1446, and 1781 of this title] may not affect the liability of any person under any provision of law as in effect before the effective date of the provision.”
