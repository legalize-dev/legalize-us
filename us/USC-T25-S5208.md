# § 5208. Application of provisions to Osage County

##### § 5208. Application of provisions to Osage County

This chapter shall not relate to or affect Osage County, Oklahoma.

*([June 26, 1936, ch. 831, § 8](/us/act/1936-06-26/ch831/s8), [49 Stat. 1968](/us/stat/49/1968).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 508 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s508) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
