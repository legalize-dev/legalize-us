# § 12262. Requirements

##### § 12262. Requirements

For the purpose of placing Federal participants in a family unity demonstration project under [section 12261 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s12261), the Attorney General shall consult with the Secretary of Health and Human Services regarding the development and operation of the project.

*([Pub. L. 103–322, title III, § 31922](/us/pl/103/322/tIII/s31922), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1896](/us/stat/108/1896).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 13902 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s13902), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
