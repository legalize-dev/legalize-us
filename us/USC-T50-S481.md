##### § 481. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section, [act June 29, 1948, ch. 715, § 1](/us/act/1948-06-29/ch715/s1), [62 Stat. 1095](/us/stat/62/1095), provided for arming of American vessels during a war or national emergency. See [section 261 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s261), Armed Forces.
