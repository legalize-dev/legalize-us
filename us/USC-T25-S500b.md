# § 500b. Omitted

##### § 500b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 3](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s3), [50 Stat. 900](/us/stat/50/900), which required filing of claims to title to reindeer by nonnatives, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
