# § 677u. Omitted

##### § 677u. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 22](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s22), [68 Stat. 877](/us/stat/68/877), which related to protection of minors, persons non compos mentis, and other members needing assistance, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
