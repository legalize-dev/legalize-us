# § 6684. Assessable penalties with respect to liability for tax under chapter 42

##### § 6684. Assessable penalties with respect to liability for tax under chapter 42

(1) such person has theretofore been liable for tax under such chapter, or

(2) such act or failure to act is both willful and flagrant,

*(Added [Pub. L. 91–172, title I, § 101(c)](/us/pl/91/172/tI/s101/c), Dec. 30, 1969, [83 Stat. 519](/us/stat/83/519); amended [Pub. L. 100–203, title X, § 10712(c)(4)](/us/pl/100/203/tX/s10712/c/4), Dec. 22, 1987, [101 Stat. 1330–467](/us/stat/101/1330-467).)*
