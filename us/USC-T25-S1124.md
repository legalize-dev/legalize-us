# § 1124. Omitted

##### § 1124. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–309, § 3](/us/pl/92/309/s3), June 2, 1972, [86 Stat. 200](/us/stat/86/200), which related to revision of roll prepared pursuant to former [section 1114 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1114), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
