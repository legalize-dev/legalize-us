# § 1778. Omitted

##### § 1778. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 106–568, title VI, § 602](/us/pl/106/568/tVI/s602), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2906](/us/stat/114/2906), which set out congressional findings and purpose, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 106–568, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/106/568/tVI/s601), Dec. 27, 2000, [114 Stat. 2906](/us/stat/114/2906), provided that title VI of [Pub. L. 106–568](/us/pl/106/568), enacting this subchapter and provisions formerly set out as a note under this section, could be cited as the “Torres-Martinez Desert Cahuilla Indians Claims Settlement Act”.
