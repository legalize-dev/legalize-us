# § 793a. Repealed. [June 30, 1954, ch. 428, § 1](/us/act/1954-06-30/ch428/s1), [68 Stat. 336](/us/stat/68/336)

##### § 793a. Repealed. [June 30, 1954, ch. 428, § 1](/us/act/1954-06-30/ch428/s1), [68 Stat. 336](/us/stat/68/336)

Section, [act Mar. 2, 1934, ch. 37, § 4](/us/act/1934-03-02/ch37/s4), [48 Stat. 361](/us/stat/48/361), created a Model Housing Board, and provided for construction and sale of model houses and for creation of a revolving “model housing fund”.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Disposition of Moneys in Revolving Fund

[Act June 30, 1954, ch. 428, § 2](/us/act/1954-06-30/ch428/s2), [68 Stat. 336](/us/stat/68/336), authorized transfer of any moneys remaining in revolving model housing fund under this section to treasury of Commonwealth of Puerto Rico.
