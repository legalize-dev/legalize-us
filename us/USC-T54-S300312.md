# § 300312. National Trust

##### § 300312. National Trust

In this division, the term “National Trust” means the National Trust for Historic Preservation in the United States established under [section 312102 of this title](/us/usc/t54/s312102).

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3189](/us/stat/128/3189).)*
