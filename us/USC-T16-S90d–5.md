# § 90d–5. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 90d–5. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this subchapter, but not more than $4,500,000 shall be appropriated for the acquisition of lands or interest in lands.

*([Pub. L. 90–544, title V, § 506](/us/pl/90/544/tV/s506), Oct. 2, 1968, [82 Stat. 930](/us/stat/82/930); [Pub. L. 94–578, title I, § 101(9)](/us/pl/94/578/tI/s101/9), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2732](/us/stat/90/2732).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1976—[Pub. L. 94–578](/us/pl/94/578) substituted “$4,500,000” for “$3,500,000”.
