# § 941. Omitted

##### § 941. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–116, § 2](/us/pl/103/116/s2), Oct. 27, 1993, [107 Stat. 1118](/us/stat/107/1118), which set out declaration of policy, Congressional findings, and purpose of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 103–116, § 17](/us/pl/103/116/s17), Oct. 27, 1993, [107 Stat. 1138](/us/stat/107/1138), which generally provided that the provisions of [Pub. L. 103–116](/us/pl/103/116) were to become effective upon the transfer of the Existing Reservation to the Secretary, was editorially reclassified and is set out as a note under former [section 931 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s931).

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 103–116, § 1](/us/pl/103/116/s1), Oct. 27, 1993, [107 Stat. 1118](/us/stat/107/1118), provided that [Pub. L. 103–116](/us/pl/103/116), enacting this subchapter and provisions set out as a note under [section 931 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s931) and repealing subchapter XLIII (§ 931 et seq.) of this chapter, could be cited as the “Catawba Indian Tribe of South Carolina Land Claims Settlement Act of 1993”.
