# § 506. Amendment and repeal of chapter

##### § 506. Amendment and repeal of chapter

The right to alter, amend, or repeal this chapter is expressly reserved.

*([June 10, 1930, ch. 440, § 6](/us/act/1930-06-10/ch440/s6), [46 Stat. 540](/us/stat/46/540).)*
