# §§ 5091 to 5099. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–149, § 4(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/149/s4/a/1), Nov. 23, 1993, [107 Stat. 1504](/us/stat/107/1504)

##### §§ 5091 to 5099. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–149, § 4(a)(1)](/us/pl/103/149/s4/a/1), Nov. 23, 1993, [107 Stat. 1504](/us/stat/107/1504)

Section 5091, [Pub. L. 99–440, title V, § 501](/us/pl/99/440/tV/s501), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1108](/us/stat/100/1108); [Pub. L. 99–631, § 1(a)(26)](/us/pl/99/631/s1/a/26), Nov. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 3518](/us/stat/100/3518), related to imposition of additional measures against Government of South Africa.

Section 5092, [Pub. L. 99–440, title V, § 502](/us/pl/99/440/tV/s502), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1109](/us/stat/100/1109); [Pub. L. 99–631, § 1(a)(27)](/us/pl/99/631/s1/a/27), Nov. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 3518](/us/stat/100/3518), related to lifting of prohibitions contained in this chapter.

Section 5093, [Pub. L. 99–440, title V, § 503](/us/pl/99/440/tV/s503), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1109](/us/stat/100/1109), related to study of health conditions in “homelands” areas of South Africa.

Section 5094, [Pub. L. 99–440, title V, § 504](/us/pl/99/440/tV/s504), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1109](/us/stat/100/1109), related to report on imports of strategic and critical materials from South Africa.

Section 5095, [Pub. L. 99–440, title V, § 505](/us/pl/99/440/tV/s505), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1109](/us/stat/100/1109); [Pub. L. 99–631, § 1(a)(28)](/us/pl/99/631/s1/a/28), Nov. 7, 1986, [100 Stat. 3518](/us/stat/100/3518), related to study and report on economy of southern Africa.

Section 5096, [Pub. L. 99–440, title V, § 506](/us/pl/99/440/tV/s506), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1110](/us/stat/100/1110), related to report on relations between other industrialized democracies and South Africa.

Section 5097, [Pub. L. 99–440, title V, § 507](/us/pl/99/440/tV/s507), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1110](/us/stat/100/1110), related to study and report on deposit accounts of South African nationals in United States banks.

Section 5098, [Pub. L. 99–440, title V, § 508](/us/pl/99/440/tV/s508), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1110](/us/stat/100/1110), related to study and report on violation of international embargo on sale and export of military articles to South Africa.

Section 5099, [Pub. L. 99–440, title V, § 509](/us/pl/99/440/tV/s509), Oct. 2, 1986, [100 Stat. 1111](/us/stat/100/1111), related to report on Communist activities in South Africa.
