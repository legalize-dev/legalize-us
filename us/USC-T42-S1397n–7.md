# § 1397n–7. Limitation on use of funds

##### § 1397n–7. Limitation on use of funds

(1) the Federal Interagency Council on Social Impact Partnerships; and

(2) any other agency consulted by the Secretary before approving a social impact partnership project or a feasibility study under [section 1397n–3 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1397n–3).
