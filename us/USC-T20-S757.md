# § 757. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV, § 401(f)(4)](/us/pl/91/230/tIV/s401/f/4), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173](/us/stat/84/173)

##### § 757. Repealed. [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV, § 401(f)(4)](/us/pl/91/230/tIV/s401/f/4), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 173](/us/stat/84/173)

Section, [Pub. L. 88–204, title IV, § 407](/us/pl/88/204/tIV/s407), Dec. 16, 1963, [77 Stat. 379](/us/stat/77/379), prohibited Federal control of education (personnel, curriculum, methods of instruction, or administration), and was superseded by [section 1232a of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1232a).
