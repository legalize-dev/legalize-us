##### [§ 1056. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–357, title VIII, § 886(b)(1)(A)](/us/pl/108/357/tVIII/s886/b/1/A), Oct. 22, 2004, [118 Stat. 1641](/us/stat/118/1641)]
