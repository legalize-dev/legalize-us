# § 2396. Federal laws guaranteeing civil rights

##### § 2396. Federal laws guaranteeing civil rights

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed to be inconsistent with applicable Federal law prohibiting discrimination on the basis of race, color, sex, national origin, age, or disability in the provision of Federal programs or services.

*([Pub. L. 88–210, title II, § 216](/us/pl/88/210/tII/s216), formerly title III, § 316, as added [Pub. L. 109–270, § 1(b)](/us/pl/109/270/s1/b), Aug. 12, 2006, [120 Stat. 744](/us/stat/120/744); renumbered title II, § 216, [Pub. L. 115–224, title II, § 201(a)(5)](/us/pl/115/224/tII/s201/a/5), (6), July 31, 2018, [132 Stat. 1621](/us/stat/132/1621).)*
