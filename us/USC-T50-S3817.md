# § 3817. Savings provision

##### § 3817. Savings provision

Nothing in this chapter shall be deemed to amend any provision of the National Security Act of 1947 ([61 Stat. 495](/us/stat/61/495)) [[50 U.S.C. 3001](/us/usc/t50/s3001) et seq.].

*([June 24, 1948, ch. 625](/us/act/1948-06-24/ch625), title I, § 19, [62 Stat. 627](/us/stat/62/627).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title I of [act June 24, 1948, ch. 625](/us/act/1948-06-24/ch625), [62 Stat. 604](/us/stat/62/604), which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of title I to the Code, see Tables.

The National Security Act of 1947 ([61 Stat. 495](/us/stat/61/495)), referred to in text, is [act July 26, 1947, ch. 343](/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343), [61 Stat. 495](/us/stat/61/495), which is classified principally to chapter 44 (§ 3001 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to section 469 of the former Appendix to this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
