# § 602. Omitted

##### § 602. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 9, 1946, ch. 933, § 2](/us/act/1946-08-09/ch933/s2), [60 Stat. 969](/us/stat/60/969), which related to applications for tribal membership by persons excluded from enrollment, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
