# §§ 941 to 956. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

##### §§ 941 to 956. Repealed. [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041, § 53](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041/s53), [70A Stat. 641](/us/stat/70A/641)

Section 941, acts [July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 217, [66 Stat. 486](/us/stat/66/486); [July 30, 1956, ch. 789, § 4(a)](/us/act/1956-07-30/ch789/s4/a), [70 Stat. 729](/us/stat/70/729), related to qualifications for appointments and enlistments in the Reserves. See sections 12102, 12201, and 12204 of Title 10, Armed Forces. [Act July 30, 1956, ch. 789, § 4(a)](/us/act/1956-07-30/ch789/s4/a), [70 Stat. 729](/us/stat/70/729), was repealed by [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1570](/us/stat/72/1570).

Section 942, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 218, [66 Stat. 487](/us/stat/66/487), authorized the President, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate, to make appointments to general or flag officer grade. See [section 12203 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12203).

Section 943, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 219, [66 Stat. 487](/us/stat/66/487), authorized the President to make appointments in commissioned grades below general or flag officer grades. See [section 12203 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12203).

Section 944, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 220, [66 Stat. 487](/us/stat/66/487), related to appointments in warrant officer grades. See [section 12241 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12241).

Section 945, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 221, [66 Stat. 487](/us/stat/66/487), related to tenure of appointments of commissioned officers. See [section 12203 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12203).

Section 946, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 222, [66 Stat. 487](/us/stat/66/487), provided for a common Federal appointment for officers. See [section 12201(a) of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12201/a).

Section 947, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 223, [66 Stat. 487](/us/stat/66/487), prescribed tenure of appointments of warrant officers. See [section 12241 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12241).

Section 948, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 224, [66 Stat. 487](/us/stat/66/487), provided for indefinite term of appointment, conversion of appointments and enlistments. See sections 12203 and 12241 of Title 10 and note set out under [section 12203 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12203).

Section 949, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 225, [66 Stat. 488](/us/stat/66/488), provided for physical examinations. See [section 12644 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12644).

Section 950, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 226, [66 Stat. 488](/us/stat/66/488), provided for discharge or transfer of physically disqualified personnel. See [section 12644 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12644).

Section 951, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 227, [66 Stat. 488](/us/stat/66/488), related to term of enlistment. See [section 12103 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12103).

Section 952, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 228, [66 Stat. 488](/us/stat/66/488), related to common Federal enlistments. See sections 12102 and 12107 of Title 10.

Section 953, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 229, [66 Stat. 488](/us/stat/66/488), prohibited dual membership in reserve components. See [section 10213 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s10213).

Section 954, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 230, [66 Stat. 489](/us/stat/66/489), related to enlisted personnel as officer candidates. See [section 12209 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12209).

Section 955, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 231, [66 Stat. 489](/us/stat/66/489), related to age limitation for officers.

Section 956, [act July 9, 1952, ch. 608](/us/act/1952-07-09/ch608), pt. II, § 232, [66 Stat. 489](/us/stat/66/489), authorized appointment or enlistment of limited-service personnel. See sections 12102 and 12201 of Title 10.
