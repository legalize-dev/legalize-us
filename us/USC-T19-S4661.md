# § 4661. Establishment

##### § 4661. Establishment

(1) hire and fix the compensation of up to 5 additional full-time officers or employees of the Department of Labor; and

(2) detail or assign such officers or employees to the United States Embassy or a United States Consulate in Mexico to carry out the duties described in [section 4662 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4662).

*([Pub. L. 116–113, title VII, § 721](/us/pl/116/113/tVII/s721), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 86](/us/stat/134/86).)*
