# § 1553. Assistant Commissioners and one District Director; compensation and salary grade

##### § 1553. Assistant Commissioners and one District Director; compensation and salary grade

The compensation of the five assistant commissioners and one district director shall be at the rate of grade GS–16.

*([June 20, 1956, ch. 414](/us/act/1956-06-20/ch414), title II, § 201, [70 Stat. 307](/us/stat/70/307).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 342b–1 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s342b–1) prior to the general revision and enactment of Title 5, Government Organization and Employees, by [Pub. L. 89–554, § 1](/us/pl/89/554/s1), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 378](/us/stat/80/378).

#### Prior Provisions

Provisions similar to those in this section were contained in [act July 7, 1955, ch. 279, title II, § 201](/us/act/1955-07-07/ch279/tII/s201), [69 Stat. 272](/us/stat/69/272).
