# § 3293. Financial institution offenses

##### § 3293. Financial institution offenses

(1) section 215, 656, 657, 1005, 1006, 1007, 1014, 1033, or 1344;

(2) section 1341 or 1343, if the offense affects a financial institution; or

(3) section 1963, to the extent that the racketeering activity involves a violation of section 1344;

*(Added [Pub. L. 101–73, title IX, § 961](/us/pl/101/73/tIX/s961)(*l*)(1), Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 501](/us/stat/103/501); amended [Pub. L. 101–647, title XXV, § 2505(a)](/us/pl/101/647/tXXV/s2505/a), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4862](/us/stat/104/4862); [Pub. L. 103–322, title XXXII, § 320604(b)](/us/pl/103/322/tXXXII/s320604/b), title XXXIII, § 330002(e), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 2119](/us/stat/108/2119), 2140.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1994—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 103–322](/us/pl/103/322) struck out “1008,” after “1007,” and inserted “1033,” after “1014,”.

1990—Par. (3). [Pub. L. 101–647](/us/pl/101/647) added par. (3).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of 1990 Amendment

[Pub. L. 101–647, title XXV, § 2505(b)](/us/pl/101/647/tXXV/s2505/b), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4862](/us/stat/104/4862), provided that: “The amendments made by subsection (a) [amending this section] shall apply to any offense committed before the date of the enactment of this section [Nov. 29, 1990], if the statute of limitations applicable to that offense had not run as of such date.”

#### Effect of This Section on Offenses for Which Prior Period of Limitations Had Not Run

[Pub. L. 101–73, title IX, § 961](/us/pl/101/73/tIX/s961)(*l*)(3), Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 501](/us/stat/103/501), provided that: “The amendments made by this subsection [enacting this section] shall apply to an offense committed before the effective date of this section [Aug. 9, 1989], if the statute of limitations applicable to that offense under this chapter had not run as of such date.”
