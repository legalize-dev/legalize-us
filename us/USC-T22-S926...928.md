# §§ 926 to 928. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

##### §§ 926 to 928. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

Section 926, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 526](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV/s526), [60 Stat. 1010](/us/stat/60/1010), related to benefits of Reserve officers.

Section 927, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 527](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV/s527), [60 Stat. 1010](/us/stat/60/1010), related to reappointment or reassignment of Reserve officers.

Section 928, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title V, § 528, [60 Stat. 1010](/us/stat/60/1010); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 11](/us/pl/86/723/s11), [74 Stat. 833](/us/stat/74/833), related to reinstatement of Reserve officers.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).
