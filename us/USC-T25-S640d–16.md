##### § 640d–16. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 17](/us/pl/93/531/s17), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1720](/us/stat/88/1720), which related to title, possession, and enjoyment of lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
