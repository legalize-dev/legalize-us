# § 1381. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379)

##### § 1381. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379)

Section, [Pub. L. 89–563, § 1](/us/pl/89/563/s1), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 718](/us/stat/80/718), provided congressional declaration of purpose of this chapter. See [section 30101 of Title 49](/us/usc/t49/s30101), Transportation.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 89–563, title I, § 101](/us/pl/89/563/tI/s101), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 718](/us/stat/80/718), provided that [Pub. L. 89–563](/us/pl/89/563) could be cited as the “National Traffic and Motor Vehicle Safety Act of 1966”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379).
