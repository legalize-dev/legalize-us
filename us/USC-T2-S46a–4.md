#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–145](/us/pl/91/145), Dec. 12, 1969, [83 Stat. 342](/us/stat/83/342), made [section 46a–3 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s46a–3) applicable to President of Senate, and was omitted from the Code in view of the repeal of section 46a–3.
