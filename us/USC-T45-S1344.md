# § 1344. Status of Conrail after sale

##### § 1344. Status of Conrail after sale

The Corporation shall be a rail carrier as defined in [section 10102 of title 49](/us/usc/t49/s10102), notwithstanding this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 99–509, title IV, § 4036](/us/pl/99/509/tIV/s4036), Oct. 21, 1986, [100 Stat. 1909](/us/stat/100/1909); [Pub. L. 104–88, title III, § 332](/us/pl/104/88/tIII/s332), Dec. 29, 1995, [109 Stat. 953](/us/stat/109/953).)*
