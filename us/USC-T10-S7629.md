# § 7629. Proceeds: disposition

##### § 7629. Proceeds: disposition

(1) Exterior articles of uniform sold under [section 7621 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s7621).

(2) Supplies and military publications sold to educational institutions under [section 7627 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s7627).

(3) Fuel, oil, other supplies, and services for aircraft of a foreign military or air attache sold under [section 7626 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s7626).
