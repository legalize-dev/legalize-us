# § 1445. Succession of Federal Home Loan Banks

##### § 1445. Succession of Federal Home Loan Banks

Each Federal Home Loan Bank shall have succession until dissolved by the Director under this chapter or by further act of Congress.

*([July 22, 1932, ch. 522, § 25](/us/act/1932-07-22/ch522/s25), [47 Stat. 740](/us/stat/47/740); [Pub. L. 101–73, title VII, § 701(b)(1)](/us/pl/101/73/tVII/s701/b/1), (3)(A), Aug. 9, 1989, [103 Stat. 412](/us/stat/103/412); [Pub. L. 110–289, div. A, title II, § 1204(8)](/us/pl/110/289/dA/tII/s1204/8), July 30, 2008, [122 Stat. 2786](/us/stat/122/2786).)*
