# § 1733. Qualifications for Office of Delegate

##### § 1733. Qualifications for Office of Delegate

(a) be at least twenty-five years of age on the date of the election;

(b) owe allegiance to the United States;

(c) be an inhabitant of the Territory of American Samoa; and

(d) not be, on the date of the election, a candidate for any other office.

*([Pub. L. 95–556, § 3](/us/pl/95/556/s3), Oct. 31, 1978, [92 Stat. 2078](/us/stat/92/2078); [Pub. L. 95–584, § 3](/us/pl/95/584/s3), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2483](/us/stat/92/2483).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1978—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 95–584](/us/pl/95/584) substituted allegiance requirement for provision requiring United States citizenship for at least seven years prior to the date of the election.
