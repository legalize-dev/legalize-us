# § 913. Omitted

##### § 913. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 86–97, § 3](/us/pl/86/97/s3), July 17, 1959, [73 Stat. 222](/us/stat/73/222), which related to payment of shares of deceased enrollees, minors, and persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
