# §§ 261 to 270. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–65, § 11(a)](/us/pl/104/65/s11/a), Dec. 19, 1995, [109 Stat. 701](/us/stat/109/701)

##### §§ 261 to 270. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–65, § 11(a)](/us/pl/104/65/s11/a), Dec. 19, 1995, [109 Stat. 701](/us/stat/109/701)

Section 261, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 302](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s302), [60 Stat. 839](/us/stat/60/839), defined terms used in this chapter.

Section 262, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 303](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s303), [60 Stat. 840](/us/stat/60/840), related to detailed accounts of contributions and retention of receipted bills of expenditures.

Section 263, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 304](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s304), [60 Stat. 840](/us/stat/60/840), required receipts for contributions.

Section 264, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 305](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s305), [60 Stat. 840](/us/stat/60/840), required filing of statements of accounts with Clerk of House.

Section 265, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 306](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s306), [60 Stat. 841](/us/stat/60/841), related to proper filing and preservation of statements filed with Clerk of House.

Section 266, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 307](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s307), [60 Stat. 841](/us/stat/60/841), related to persons to whom chapter was applicable.

Section 267, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 308](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s308), [60 Stat. 841](/us/stat/60/841), related to registration of lobbyists with Secretary of Senate and Clerk of House and required compilation of information required.

Section 268, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 309](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s309), [60 Stat. 842](/us/stat/60/842), required that reports and statements be made under oath.

Section 269, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 310](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s310), [60 Stat. 842](/us/stat/60/842), related to penalties and prohibitions for violations of this chapter.

Section 270, [act Aug. 2, 1946, ch. 753, title III, § 311](/us/act/1946-08-02/ch753/tIII/s311), [60 Stat. 842](/us/stat/60/842), related to exemptions from this chapter.

For provisions relating to disclosure of lobbying activities to influence the Federal Government, see section 1601 et seq. of this title.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Jan. 1, 1996, except as otherwise provided, see [section 24 of Pub. L. 104–65](/us/pl/104/65/s24), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 1601 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s1601).

#### Short Title

Act Aug. 2, 1946, title III, § 301, [60 Stat. 839](/us/stat/60/839), provided that title III of act Aug. 2, 1946 (enacting this chapter), could be cited as the “Federal Regulation of Lobbying Act”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–65, § 11(a)](/us/pl/104/65/s11/a), Dec. 19, 1995, [109 Stat. 701](/us/stat/109/701).
