# § 9017. Secretary of the Air Force: successors to duties

##### § 9017. Secretary of the Air Force: successors to duties

(1) The Under Secretary of the Air Force.

(2) The Assistant Secretaries of the Air Force, in the order prescribed by the Secretary of the Air Force and approved by the Secretary of Defense.

(3) The General Counsel of the Department of the Air Force.

(4) The Chief of Staff.

(5) The Chief of Space Operations.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 3347 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s3347), referred to in text, was repealed and a new section 3347 was enacted by [Pub. L. 105–277, div. C, title I, § 151(b)](/us/pl/105/277/dC/tI/s151/b), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–611](/us/stat/112/2681-611), and, as so enacted, no longer contains provisions authorizing the President to direct temporary successors to duties. See [section 3345 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s3345), Government Organization and Employees.

#### Amendments

2021—Par. (5). [Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283) added par. (5).

2018—[Pub. L. 115–232](/us/pl/115/232) renumbered [section 8017 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s8017) as this section.

1994—Pars. (3), (4). [Pub. L. 103–337](/us/pl/103/337) added par. (3) and redesignated former par. (3) as (4).

1986—[Pub. L. 99–433](/us/pl/99/433) struck out subsec. (a) designation, substituted in par. (2) “, in the order prescribed by the Secretary of the Air Force and approved by the Secretary of Defense” for “in order of their length of service as such”, and struck out subsec. (b) which read as follows: “Performance of the duties of the Secretary by the Chief of Staff or any officer of the Air Force designated under [section 3347 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s3347) shall not be considered as the holding of a civil office within the meaning of [section 973(b) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s973/b).”

1968—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 90–235](/us/pl/90/235) substituted “[section 973(b) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s973/b)” for “[section 8544(b) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s8544/b)”.

1966—[Pub. L. 89–718](/us/pl/89/718) substituted “[section 3347 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s3347)” for “[section 6 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s6)” wherever appearing.
