# § 1753. Uniform rules of evidence requirement

##### § 1753. Uniform rules of evidence requirement

The rules of evidence required to prove that State or local authorities are practicing racial discrimination in assigning students to public schools shall be uniform throughout the United States.

*([Pub. L. 93–380, title II, § 254](/us/pl/93/380/tII/s254), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 520](/us/stat/88/520).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective on and after sixtieth day after Aug. 21, 1974, see [section 2(c) of Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380/s2/c), set out as a note under [section 1221–1 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1221–1).
