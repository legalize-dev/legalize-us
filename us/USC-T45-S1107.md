# § 1107. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–509, title IV, § 4033(c)(1)(C)(i)(I)](/us/pl/99/509/tIV/s4033/c/1/C/i/I), Oct. 21, 1986, [100 Stat. 1908](/us/stat/100/1908)

##### § 1107. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–509, title IV, § 4033(c)(1)(C)(i)(I)](/us/pl/99/509/tIV/s4033/c/1/C/i/I), Oct. 21, 1986, [100 Stat. 1908](/us/stat/100/1908)

Section, [Pub. L. 97–35, title XI, § 1154](/us/pl/97/35/tXI/s1154), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 677](/us/stat/95/677), provided that no distribution of assets of Conrail could be made with respect to any claims of United States until all other valid claims against Conrail were satisfied or until arrangements had been made for their satisfaction.
