# § 7507. Funding

##### § 7507. Funding

There is authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary for fiscal years 2017, 2018, and 2019 for this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 114–113, div. O, title IX, § 908](/us/pl/114/113/dO/tIX/s908), Dec. 18, 2015, [129 Stat. 3034](/us/stat/129/3034).)*
