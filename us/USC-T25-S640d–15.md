# § 640d–15. Omitted

##### § 640d–15. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 16](/us/pl/93/531/s16), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1720](/us/stat/88/1720), which provided for payment of fair rental value for use of lands subsequent to date of partition, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
