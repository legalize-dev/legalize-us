# § 1709. Omitted

##### § 1709. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–395, § 10](/us/pl/95/395/s10), Sept. 30, 1978, [92 Stat. 817](/us/stat/92/817), which provided for preservation of certain other Federal benefits, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
