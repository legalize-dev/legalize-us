# § 3058h. Regulations

##### § 3058h. Regulations

(1) conflicts of interest by persons described in subparagraphs (A) and (B) of [section 3058g(f)(1) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3058g/f/1); and

(2) the relationships described in clauses (i) through (vi) of [section 3058g(f)(1)(C) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3058g/f/1/C).

L. 103–171, § 3(a)(13)](/us/pl/103/171/s3/a/13), Dec. 2, 1993, [107 Stat. 1990](/us/stat/107/1990); [Pub. #### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2016—Par. (1). [Pub. L. 114–144, § 8(d)(1)](/us/pl/114/144/s8/d/1), substituted “subparagraphs (A) and (B) of section 3058g(f)(1)” for “paragraphs (1) and (2) of section 3058g(f)”.

Par. (2). [Pub. L. 114–144, § 8(d)(2)](/us/pl/114/144/s8/d/2), substituted “clauses (i) through (vi) of section 3058g(f)(1)(C)” for “subparagraphs (A) through (D) of section 3058g(f)(3)”.

1993—[Pub. L. 103–171](/us/pl/103/171) substituted “Assistant Secretary” for “Commissioner”.
