# § 111. Repeals as evidence of prior effectiveness

##### § 111. Repeals as evidence of prior effectiveness

No inference shall be raised by the enactment of the Act of March 3, 1933 (ch. 202, [47 Stat. 1431](/us/stat/47/1431)), that the sections of the Revised Statutes repealed by such Act were in force or effect at the time of such enactment: *Provided, however*, That any rights or liabilities existing under such repealed sections shall not be affected by their repeal.

*([July 30, 1947, ch. 388](/us/act/1947-07-30/ch388), [61 Stat. 635](/us/stat/61/635).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Act of March 3, 1933, referred to in text, was repealed by section 2 of act July 30, 1947, section 1 of which enacted this title.
