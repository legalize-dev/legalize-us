# § 606. Omitted

##### § 606. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 9, 1946, ch. 933, § 6](/us/act/1946-08-09/ch933/s6), [60 Stat. 969](/us/stat/60/969), which provided that new members enrolled after Aug. 9, 1946, be ineligible for back pay and annuities out of tribal funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
