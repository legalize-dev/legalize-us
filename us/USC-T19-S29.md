# § 29. Repealed. [Aug. 26, 1935, ch. 689, § 2](/us/act/1935-08-26/ch689/s2), [49 Stat. 864](/us/stat/49/864)

##### § 29. Repealed. [Aug. 26, 1935, ch. 689, § 2](/us/act/1935-08-26/ch689/s2), [49 Stat. 864](/us/stat/49/864)

Section, [act Mar. 15, 1898, ch. 68, § 1](/us/act/1898-03-15/ch68/s1), [30 Stat. 286](/us/stat/30/286), as supplemented by acts [Jan. 28, 1915, ch. 20, § 1](/us/act/1915-01-28/ch20/s1), [38 Stat. 800](/us/stat/38/800); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 251, § 3](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch251/s3), [42 Stat. 1453](/us/stat/42/1453), related to administration of oaths by collectors and assistants.

[Act Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20), [63 Stat. 561](/us/stat/63/561), also repealed [act Jan. 28, 1915, ch. 20, § 1](/us/act/1915-01-28/ch20/s1), [38 Stat. 800](/us/stat/38/800), formerly credited to this section.
