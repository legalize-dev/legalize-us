# § 564t. Omitted

##### § 564t. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 21](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s21), [68 Stat. 723](/us/stat/68/723), which provided that nothing contained in this subchapter would affect the rights of the tribe under the act of August 13, 1946 ([60 Stat. 1049](/us/stat/60/1049)), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
