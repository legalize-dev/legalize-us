# § 6066. Public schools

##### § 6066. Public schools

Except as provided in section 5890 11 See References in Text note below. of this title, nothing in this chapter shall be construed to authorize the use of funds under subchapter III 1 of this chapter to directly or indirectly benefit any school other than a public school.

*([Pub. L. 103–227, title X, § 1020](/us/pl/103/227/tX/s1020), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 269](/us/stat/108/269).)*
