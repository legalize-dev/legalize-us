# § 651. Omitted

##### § 651. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 18, 1928, ch. 624, § 1](/us/act/1928-05-18/ch624/s1), [45 Stat. 602](/us/stat/45/602), which defined “Indians of California”, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
