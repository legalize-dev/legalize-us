# § 639. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–193, title I, § 110(u)](/us/pl/104/193/tI/s110/u), Aug. 22, 1996, [110 Stat. 2175](/us/stat/110/2175)

Section, acts [Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92, § 9](/us/act/1950-04-19/ch92/s9), [64 Stat. 47](/us/stat/64/47); Oct. 30, 1972, [Pub. L. 92–603, title III, § 303(c)](/us/pl/92/603/tIII/s303/c), [86 Stat. 1484](/us/stat/86/1484); Dec. 31, 1973, [Pub. Repeal effective July 1, 1997, with transition rules relating to State options to accelerate such date, rules relating to claims, actions, and proceedings commenced before such date, rules relating to closing out of accounts for terminated or substantially modified programs and continuance in office of Assistant Secretary for Family Support, and provisions relating to termination of entitlement under AFDC program, see [section 116 of Pub. L. 104–193](/us/pl/104/193/s116), as amended, set out as an Effective Date note under [section 601 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s601), The Public Health and Welfare.
