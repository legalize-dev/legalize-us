# § 4548. Regulations for use of credit scores

##### § 4548. Regulations for use of credit scores

(1) by regulation, establish standards and criteria for any process used by an enterprise to validate and approve credit scoring models pursuant to [section 1717(b)(7) of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1717/b/7) and [section 1454(d) of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1454/d); and

(2) ensure that any credit scoring model that is validated and approved by an enterprise under [section 1717(b)(7) of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1717/b/7) or [section 1454(d) of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1454/d) meets the requirements of clauses (i), (ii), and (iii) of [section 1717(b)(7)(C) of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1717/b/7/C) and subparagraphs (A), (B), and (C) of [section 1454(d) of this title](/us/usc/t12/s1454/d), respectively.

*([Pub. L. 102–550, title XIII, § 1328](/us/pl/102/550/tXIII/s1328), as added [Pub. L. 115–174, title III, § 310(c)](/us/pl/115/174/tIII/s310/c), May 24, 2018, [132 Stat. 1355](/us/stat/132/1355).)*
