# [§ 284. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–849, § 2](/us/pl/87/849/s2), Oct. 23, 1962, [76 Stat. 1126](/us/stat/76/1126)]

##### [§ 284. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–849, § 2](/us/pl/87/849/s2), Oct. 23, 1962, [76 Stat. 1126](/us/stat/76/1126)]

Section, acts [June 25, 1948, ch. 645](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645), [62 Stat. 698](/us/stat/62/698); [May 24, 1949, ch. 139, § 7](/us/act/1949-05-24/ch139/s7), [63 Stat. 90](/us/stat/63/90), related to disqualifications of former officers and employees in matters connected with former duties. Section was supplanted by [section 207 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s207).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective 90 days after Oct. 23, 1962, see [section 4 of Pub. L. 87–849](/us/pl/87/849/s4), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 201 of this title](/us/usc/t18/s201).
