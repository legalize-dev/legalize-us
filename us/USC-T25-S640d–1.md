# § 640d–1. Omitted

##### § 640d–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 2](/us/pl/93/531/s2), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1712](/us/stat/88/1712), which related to negotiating teams for the Navajo and Hopi Tribes and negotations between the tribes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
