# §§ 353 to 356. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(a)](/us/pl/96/591/s8/a), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435](/us/stat/94/3435)

##### §§ 353 to 356. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(a)](/us/pl/96/591/s8/a), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435](/us/stat/94/3435)

Section 353, R.S. § 4233A, as added [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 256](/us/stat/62/256); amended Aug. 14, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–656, § 2](/us/pl/85/656/s2), [72 Stat. 612](/us/stat/72/612), related to establishment of special rules.

Section 354, R.S. § 4233B, as added [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 256](/us/stat/62/256), related to penalties for violations by pilots, engineers, mates, or masters. See [section 2072 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2072).

Section 355, R.S. § 4233C, as added [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 4](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s4), [62 Stat. 257](/us/stat/62/257), related to penalties for violation of the rules by a vessel. See [section 2072 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2072).

Section 356, acts [May 21, 1948, ch. 328, § 5](/us/act/1948-05-21/ch328/s5), [62 Stat. 257](/us/stat/62/257); Oct. 15, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–670, § 6(b)(1)](/us/pl/89/670/s6/b/1), [80 Stat. 938](/us/stat/80/938), related to exempted status of Navy and Coast Guard vessels from complying with International Rules of the Road.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Dec. 24, 1981, see [section 7 of Pub. L. 96–591](/us/pl/96/591/s7), set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 1604 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1604).
