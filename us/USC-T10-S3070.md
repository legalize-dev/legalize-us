# [§ 3070. Repealed. [Pub. L. 119–60, div. A, title VIII, § 811(a)(2)](/us/pl/119/60/dA/tVIII/s811/a/2), Dec. 18, 2025, [139 Stat. 948](/us/stat/139/948)]

##### [§ 3070. Repealed. [Pub. L. 119–60, div. A, title VIII, § 811(a)(2)](/us/pl/119/60/dA/tVIII/s811/a/2), Dec. 18, 2025, [139 Stat. 948](/us/stat/139/948)]

Section, added [Pub. L. 102–190, div. A, title III, § 317(a)](/us/pl/102/190/dA/tIII/s317/a), Dec. 5, 1991, [105 Stat. 1338](/us/stat/105/1338), § 2213; renumbered § 3070, [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title XVIII, § 1807(g)(1)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tXVIII/s1807/g/1), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 4159](/us/stat/134/4159); amended [Pub. L. 118–159, div. A, title VIII, § 812](/us/pl/118/159/dA/tVIII/s812), Dec. 23, 2024, [138 Stat. 1979](/us/stat/138/1979), related to limitation on acquisition of excess supplies.

A prior section 3070 was renumbered [section 7070 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s7070).

A prior section 3071, acts [Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 169](/us/stat/70A/169); Sept. 7, 1962, [Pub. L. 87–649, § 6(a)(2)](/us/pl/87/649/s6/a/2), (3), [76 Stat. 494](/us/stat/76/494); Nov. 8, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–130, § 1(8)(C)](/us/pl/90/130/s1/8/C), [81 Stat. 374](/us/stat/81/374), prescribed composition of Women’s Army Corps and provided for a Director, a Deputy Director, and other positions for Women’s Army Corps, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 95–485, title VIII, § 820(b)](/us/pl/95/485/tVIII/s820/b), Oct. 20, 1978, [92 Stat. 1627](/us/stat/92/1627).
