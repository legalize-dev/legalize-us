# § 463b. Omitted

##### § 463b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 28, 1937, ch. 527, § 2](/us/act/1937-07-28/ch527/s2), [50 Stat. 536](/us/stat/50/536), which related to purchase of private lands for the use and benefit of the Papago Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
