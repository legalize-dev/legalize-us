# § 4425. Definition of weapon system component

##### § 4425. Definition of weapon system component

In this subchapter, the term “weapon system component” has the meaning given the term “major system component” in [section 4401 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s4401).

*(Added [Pub. L. 114–328, div. A, title VIII, § 806(a)(1)](/us/pl/114/328/dA/tVIII/s806/a/1), Dec. 23, 2016, [130 Stat. 2259](/us/stat/130/2259), § 2447e; renumbered § 4425 and amended [Pub.
