# §§ 731 to 734. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 731 to 734. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 731, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 8, [39 Stat. 367](/us/stat/39/367); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), set a $5 par value for shares of land bank association stock. See [section 2031 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2031).

Section 732, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 8, [39 Stat. 367](/us/stat/39/367); [Aug. 9, 1937, ch. 704, § 23](/us/act/1937-08-09/ch704/s23), [50 Stat. 710](/us/stat/50/710), set out voting privileges for land bank association stock. See [section 2034 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2034).

Section 733, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 8, [39 Stat. 367](/us/stat/39/367); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), limited borrowers to members. See [section 2034 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2034).

Section 734, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 8, [39 Stat. 367](/us/stat/39/367); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title III, § 301(c), [69 Stat. 664](/us/stat/69/664); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for increases in stock. See [section 2034 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2034).
