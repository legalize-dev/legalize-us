# § 2297f–2. Regulation of Restricted Data

##### § 2297f–2. Regulation of Restricted Data

The Corporation shall be subject to this chapter with respect to the use of, or access to, Restricted Data to the same extent as any private corporation.

*([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724), title II, § 1703, as added [Pub. L. 102–486, title XI, § 1101](/us/pl/102/486/tXI/s1101), Oct. 24, 1992, [106 Stat. 2953](/us/stat/106/2953).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724), as added by [act Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 919](/us/stat/68/919), known as the Atomic Energy Act of 1954, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 2011 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2011) and Tables.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### References to United States Enrichment Corporation

References to the Corporation, meaning the United States Enrichment Corporation, deemed, as of the privatization date (July 28, 1998), to be references to the private corporation, see [section 3116(e) of Pub. L. 104–134](/us/pl/104/134/s3116/e), set out as a note under former [section 2297 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2297).
