# § 1449. Reservation of right to amend or repeal chapter

##### § 1449. Reservation of right to amend or repeal chapter

The right to alter, amend, or repeal this chapter is expressly reserved.

*([July 22, 1932, ch. 522, § 30](/us/act/1932-07-22/ch522/s30), [47 Stat. 741](/us/stat/47/741).)*
