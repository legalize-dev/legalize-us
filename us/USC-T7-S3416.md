# § 3416. Amendments to orders

##### § 3416. Amendments to orders

The provisions of this chapter applicable to orders shall be applicable to amendments to orders.

*([Pub. L. 95–113, title XVII, § 1717](/us/pl/95/113/tXVII/s1717), Sept. 29, 1977, [91 Stat. 1040](/us/stat/91/1040).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective Oct. 1, 1977, see [section 1901 of Pub. L. 95–113](/us/pl/95/113/s1901), set out as an Effective Date of 1977 Amendment note under [section 1307 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1307).
