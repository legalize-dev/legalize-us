# § 191a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–571, § 10(a)](/us/pl/86/571/s10/a), July 5, 1960, [74 Stat. 310](/us/stat/74/310)

##### § 191a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 86–571, § 10(a)](/us/pl/86/571/s10/a), July 5, 1960, [74 Stat. 310](/us/stat/74/310)

Section, [act Oct. 29, 1941, ch. 462](/us/act/1941-10-29/ch462), [55 Stat. 756](/us/stat/55/756), authorized admission to Saint Elizabeths Hospital of Foreign Service personnel adjudged insane in a foreign country. See section 321 et seq. of this title.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 86–571, § 10](/us/pl/86/571/s10), July 5, 1960, [74 Stat. 310](/us/stat/74/310), provided that the repeal of this section and [section 196a of this title](/us/usc/t24/s196a) is effective upon the date of enactment of legislation appropriating funds for carrying out section 321 et seq. of this title.
