# § 649i. Continuation on active duty: officers in certain military specialties and career tracks

##### § 649i. Continuation on active duty: officers in certain military specialties and career tracks

In addition to continuation on active duty provided for in [section 649h of this title](/us/usc/t10/s649h), an officer to whom [section 637a of this title](/us/usc/t10/s637a) applies may be continued on active duty in accordance with the provisions of such section 637a.

*(Added [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title V, § 507(a)(1)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tV/s507/a/1), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1748](/us/stat/132/1748).)*
