# § 612. Omitted

##### § 612. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, acts [May 19, 1947, ch. 80, § 2](/us/act/1947-05-19/ch80/s2), [61 Stat. 102](/us/stat/61/102); [Aug. 30, 1951, ch. 367, § 1](/us/act/1951-08-30/ch367/s1), [65 Stat. 208](/us/stat/65/208); [Pub. L. 85–610, § 1](/us/pl/85/610/s1), Aug. 8, 1958, [72 Stat. 541](/us/stat/72/541), which related to the establishment of a trust fund for each tribe into which the Secretary of the Treasury was directed to transfer funds as necessary to effect the purpose of former [section 611 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s611), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
