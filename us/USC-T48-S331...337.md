# §§ 331 to 337. Repealed. [Dec. 16, 1930, ch. 14, § 1](/us/act/1930-12-16/ch14/s1), [46 Stat. 1029](/us/stat/46/1029)

##### §§ 331 to 337. Repealed. [Dec. 16, 1930, ch. 14, § 1](/us/act/1930-12-16/ch14/s1), [46 Stat. 1029](/us/stat/46/1029)

Section 331, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1629](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1629), [33 Stat. 391](/us/stat/33/391), related to road overseers and to creation of road districts.

Section 332, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1629](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1629), [33 Stat. 391](/us/stat/33/391), related to term of office and qualification of road overseers.

Section 333, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1629](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1629), [33 Stat. 392](/us/stat/33/392), related to duties of overseers.

Section 334, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1629](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1629), [33 Stat. 392](/us/stat/33/392), related to work on roads.

Section 335, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1629](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1629), [33 Stat. 393](/us/stat/33/393), related to an annual report.

Section 336, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1629](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1629), [33 Stat. 393](/us/stat/33/393), related to neglect or refusal of road overseers to perform their duties.

Section 337, [act Apr. 27, 1904, ch. 1629](/us/act/1904-04-27/ch1629), [33 Stat. 393](/us/stat/33/393), related to compensation of road overseers.
