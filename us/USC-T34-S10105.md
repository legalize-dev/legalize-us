# § 10105. Inclusion of Indian tribes

##### § 10105. Inclusion of Indian tribes

For purposes of sections 10103 and 10104 of this title, the term “State” includes an Indian tribal government.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 105](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s105), as added [Pub. L. 109–162, title XI, § 1121(a)](/us/pl/109/162/tXI/s1121/a), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 3107](/us/stat/119/3107).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3712c of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3712c), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

Another [section 105 of Pub. L. 90–351](/us/pl/90/351/s105) was renumbered section 109 and is classified to [section 10109 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10109).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective with respect to appropriations for fiscal year 2007 and for each fiscal year thereafter, see [section 1121(c) of Pub. L. 109–162](/us/pl/109/162/s1121/c), set out as a note under [section 10103 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10103).
