# § 21007. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 21007. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general In addition to amounts transferred under [section 20904(c) of this title](/us/usc/t52/s20904/c), there are authorized to be appropriated for requirements payments under this subpart the following amounts:

(1) For fiscal year 2003, $1,400,000,000.

(2) For fiscal year 2004, $1,000,000,000.

(3) For fiscal year 2005, $600,000,000.

(4) For fiscal year 2010 and subsequent fiscal years, such sums as are necessary for purposes of making requirements payments to States to carry out the activities described in [section 21001(b)(3) of this title](/us/usc/t52/s21001/b/3).

(b) Availability Any amounts appropriated pursuant to the authority of subsection (a) shall remain available without fiscal year limitation until expended.

*([Pub. L. 107–252, title II, § 257](/us/pl/107/252/tII/s257), Oct. 29, 2002, [116 Stat. 1697](/us/stat/116/1697); [Pub. L. 111–84, div. A, title V, § 588(c)](/us/pl/111/84/dA/tV/s588/c), Oct. 28, 2009, [123 Stat. 2334](/us/stat/123/2334).)*
