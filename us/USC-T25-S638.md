# § 638. Omitted

##### § 638. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Apr. 19, 1950, ch. 92, § 8](/us/act/1950-04-19/ch92/s8), [64 Stat. 46](/us/stat/64/46), which related to participation by Tribal Councils in the administration of the program authorized by this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
