# § 1055. Omitted

##### § 1055. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 88–506, § 5](/us/pl/88/506/s5), Aug. 30, 1964, [78 Stat. 639](/us/stat/78/639), which authorized prescription of rules and regulations to carry out the provisions of this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
