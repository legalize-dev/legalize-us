# §§ 741 to 747. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 741 to 747. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 741, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 9, [39 Stat. 368](/us/stat/39/368); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), covered members’ rights to loans. See [section 2201 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2201).

Section 742, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 9, [39 Stat. 368](/us/stat/39/368); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 102(a)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s102/a), [73 Stat. 384](/us/stat/73/384), covered payments for stock from proceeds of member’s mortgage loan. See [section 2033 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2033).

Section 743, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 9, [39 Stat. 368](/us/stat/39/368); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), authorized retention of commissions on interest payments. See [section 2033 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2033).

Section 744, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 9, [39 Stat. 368](/us/stat/39/368); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for individual liability of shareholders.

Section 744a, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VII, § 72, [48 Stat. 271](/us/stat/48/271); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), relieved shareholders of land bank associations of personal liability for contracts, debts, and engagements entered into after June 16, 1933.

Section 745, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245), title I, § 9, [39 Stat. 368](/us/stat/39/368); [June 3, 1935, ch. 164](/us/act/1935-06-03/ch164), 20, [49 Stat. 319](/us/stat/49/319); [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 24](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s24), [50 Stat. 710](/us/stat/50/710); [June 30, 1945, ch. 204, § 1(b)](/us/act/1945-06-30/ch204/s1/b), [59 Stat. 266](/us/stat/59/266); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), (i), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for new members for land bank associations. See [section 2033 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2033).

Section 746, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 9](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s9) (par.) as added [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 25(a)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s25/a), [50 Stat. 710](/us/stat/50/710); amended Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for a common board of directors for two or more associations. See [section 2032 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2032).

Section 747, [act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, § 9](/us/act/1916-07-17/ch245/tI/s9) (par.), as added [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 25(a)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s25/a), [50 Stat. 711](/us/stat/50/711); amended Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, § 104(h)](/us/pl/86/168/tI/s104/h), [73 Stat. 387](/us/stat/73/387), provided for transfer of powers to a loan committee. See [section 2033 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2033).
