# § 535c. Approval of Secretary; commencement and completion requirements; extension of time limits

##### § 535c. Approval of Secretary; commencement and completion requirements; extension of time limits

The approval of the Secretary of the department in which the Coast Guard is operating, as required by [section 491 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s491), shall be given only subsequent to the President’s approval, as provided for in [section 535b of this title](/us/usc/t33/s535b), and shall be null and void unless the construction of the bridge is commenced within two years and completed within five years from the date of the Secretary’s approval: *Provided, however*, That the Secretary, for good cause shown, may extend for a reasonable time either or both of the time limits herein provided.

*([Pub. L. 92–434, § 5](/us/pl/92/434/s5), Sept. 26, 1972, [86 Stat. 732](/us/stat/86/732); [Pub. L. 114–120, title III, § 306(b)(6)(A)](/us/pl/114/120/tIII/s306/b/6/A), Feb. 8, 2016, [130 Stat. 56](/us/stat/130/56).)*
