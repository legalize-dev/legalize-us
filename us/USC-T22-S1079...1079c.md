# §§ 1079 to 1079c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(13)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/13), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160](/us/stat/94/2160)

##### §§ 1079 to 1079c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(13)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/13), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2160](/us/stat/94/2160)

Section 1079, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229, § 1](/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229/s1), [70 Stat. 125](/us/stat/70/125), related to increase of annuities of annuitants retired prior to July 1, 1949.

Section 1079a, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229, § 2](/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229/s2), [70 Stat. 125](/us/stat/70/125), related to availability of increases provided for by [section 1079 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s1079) for officers retired before July 1, 1979, who exercised reduced annuity and restoration clause.

Section 1079b, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229, § 3](/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229/s3), [70 Stat. 125](/us/stat/70/125), related to increases for widowed annuitants.

Section 1079c, [act May 1, 1956, ch. 229, § 4](/us/act/1956-05-01/ch229/s4), [70 Stat. 125](/us/stat/70/125), related to increases for officers’ wives who become annuitants subsequent to the effective date of sections 1079 to 1079f of this title.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).
