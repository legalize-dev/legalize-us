# § 1244. Omitted

##### § 1244. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–59, § 4](/us/pl/92/59/s4), July 29, 1971, [85 Stat. 158](/us/stat/85/158), which related to apportionment of funds between the Minnesota Chippewa Tribe, the Turtle Mountain Band of Chippewas of North Dakota, and the Chippewa-Cree Tribe of Montana, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
