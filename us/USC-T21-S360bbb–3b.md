# § 360bbb–3b. Products held for emergency use

##### § 360bbb–3b. Products held for emergency use

(1) is intended to be held and not used; and

(2) is held and not used, unless and until that product—

(A) is approved, cleared, or licensed under section 355, 360(k), 360b, or 360e of this title or section 351 of the Public Health Service Act [[42 U.S.C. 262](/us/usc/t42/s262)] or conditionally approved under [section 360ccc of this title](/us/usc/t21/s360ccc);

(B) is authorized for investigational use under section 355, 360b, or 360j of this title or section 351 of the Public Health Service Act [[42 U.S.C. 262](/us/usc/t42/s262)]; or

(C) is authorized for use under [section 360bbb–3 of this title](/us/usc/t21/s360bbb–3) or [section 360bbb–3a of this title](/us/usc/t21/s360bbb–3a).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Public Health Service Act, referred to in text, is [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), [58 Stat. 682](/us/stat/58/682), which is classified generally to chapter 6A (§ 201 et seq.) of Title 42, The Public Health and Welfare. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 201 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s201) and Tables.
