# §§ 1138 to 1138c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

##### §§ 1138 to 1138c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section 1138, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VI, § 60, [48 Stat. 266](/us/stat/48/266); [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, § 5(a)](/us/act/1937-08-19/ch704/s5/a), [50 Stat. 704](/us/stat/50/704); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(*l*), [70 Stat. 666](/us/stat/70/666), set out general corporate powers common to banks for cooperatives and production credit associations. See sections 2093 and 2122 of this title.

Section 1138a, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VI, § 61, [48 Stat. 267](/us/stat/48/267); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(m), [70 Stat. 666](/us/stat/70/666), provided for examination of parts of farm credit system by examiners of Farm Credit Administration. See [section 2254 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2254).

Section 1138b, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VI, § 62, [48 Stat. 267](/us/stat/48/267); [Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7, § 11](/us/act/1934-01-31/ch7/s11), [48 Stat. 347](/us/stat/48/347); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(n), [70 Stat. 666](/us/stat/70/666); Oct. 4, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–353, § 3(j)](/us/pl/87/353/s3/j), [75 Stat. 774](/us/stat/75/774), gave designated parts of farm credit system authority to act as fiscal agents of United States.

Section 1138c, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title VI, § 63, [48 Stat. 267](/us/stat/48/267), [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch785), title II, § 205, [69 Stat. 663](/us/stat/69/663); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(*o*), [70 Stat. 666](/us/stat/70/666), provided that obligations of banks for cooperatives and production credit associations be deemed instrumentalities of United States and provided for termination of tax exemption after retirement of government-owned stock. See sections 2098 and 2134 of this title.
