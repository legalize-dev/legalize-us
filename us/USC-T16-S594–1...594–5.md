# §§ 594–1 to 594–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–313, § 16(a)(3)](/us/pl/95/313/s16/a/3), formerly § 13(a)(3), July 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 374](/us/stat/92/374); renumbered § 16(a)(3), [Pub. L. 101–624, title XII, § 1215(1)](/us/pl/101/624/tXII/s1215/1), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3525](/us/stat/104/3525)

##### §§ 594–1 to 594–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–313, § 16(a)(3)](/us/pl/95/313/s16/a/3), formerly § 13(a)(3), July 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 374](/us/stat/92/374); renumbered § 16(a)(3), [Pub. L. 101–624, title XII, § 1215(1)](/us/pl/101/624/tXII/s1215/1), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3525](/us/stat/104/3525)

Section 594–1, [act June 25, 1947, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1947-06-25/ch141/s1), [61 Stat. 177](/us/stat/61/177), set forth policy of Federal Government respecting protection of all forest lands from insects and diseases.

Section 594–2, [act June 25, 1947, ch. 141, § 2](/us/act/1947-06-25/ch141/s2), [61 Stat. 177](/us/stat/61/177), related to conduct of surveys by Secretary of Agriculture to detect infestation by forest insect pests and tree diseases.

Section 594–3, [act June 25, 1947, ch. 141, § 3](/us/act/1947-06-25/ch141/s3), [61 Stat. 177](/us/stat/61/177), related to allocation of funds for pest and plant disease control.

Section 594–4, [act June 25, 1947, ch. 141, § 4](/us/act/1947-06-25/ch141/s4), [61 Stat. 177](/us/stat/61/177), related to contributions for insect or disease control carried out on non-federally owned forest land.

Section 594–5, acts [June 25, 1947, ch. 141, § 5](/us/act/1947-06-25/ch141/s5), [61 Stat. 177](/us/stat/61/177); June 20, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–40](/us/pl/94/40), [89 Stat. 224](/us/stat/89/224), set forth funding requirements for Federal program.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 17 of Pub. L. 95–313](/us/pl/95/313/s17), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 2101 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s2101).

#### Short Title

[Act June 25, 1947, ch. 141, § 7](/us/act/1947-06-25/ch141/s7), [61 Stat. 177](/us/stat/61/177), providing that sections 594–1 to 594–5 of this title be known as the “Forest Pest Control Act”, was repealed by [Pub. L. 95–313, § 16(a)(3)](/us/pl/95/313/s16/a/3), formerly § 13(a)(3), July 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 374](/us/stat/92/374), renumbered § 16(a)(3), [Pub. L. 101–624, title XII, § 1215(1)](/us/pl/101/624/tXII/s1215/1), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3525](/us/stat/104/3525).

#### Construction

[Act June 25, 1947, ch. 141, § 6](/us/act/1947-06-25/ch141/s6), [61 Stat. 177](/us/stat/61/177), providing that sections 594–1 to 594–5 of this title not be construed as limiting or repealing existing legislation, was repealed by [Pub. L. 95–313, § 16(a)(3)](/us/pl/95/313/s16/a/3), formerly § 13(a)(3), July 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 374](/us/stat/92/374), renumbered § 16(a)(3), [Pub. L. 101–624, title XII, § 1215(1)](/us/pl/101/624/tXII/s1215/1), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3525](/us/stat/104/3525).

#### Plan for Control of Dutch Elm Disease

[Pub. L. 94–588, § 20](/us/pl/94/588/s20), Oct. 22, 1976, [90 Stat. 2963](/us/stat/90/2963), provided that the Secretary of Agriculture, in consultation with officials of both the States and political subdivisions thereof, conduct a study of the incidence of Dutch elm disease and evaluate methods for controlling the spread of such disease and submit a report on the results of such study to the President and the Congress on or before Mar. 1, 1977.
