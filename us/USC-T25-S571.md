# § 571. Omitted

##### § 571. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 27, 1939, ch. 387, § 1](/us/act/1939-07-27/ch387/s1), [53 Stat. 1128](/us/stat/53/1128), which directed the Secretary of the Interior to prepare a membership roll of the Shoshone Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
