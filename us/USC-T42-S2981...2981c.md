# §§ 2981 to 2981c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 683(a)](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s683/a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 519](/us/stat/95/519)

##### §§ 2981 to 2981c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title VI, § 683(a)](/us/pl/97/35/tVI/s683/a), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 519](/us/stat/95/519)

Section 2981, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/88/452/tVII/s701), as added [Pub. L. 92–424, § 25(a)](/us/pl/92/424/s25/a), Sept. 19, 1972, [86 Stat. 697](/us/stat/86/697); amended [Pub. L. 93–644, § 10(a)](/us/pl/93/644/s10/a), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2315](/us/stat/88/2315), set forth Congressional statement of purpose with respect to community economic development programs.

A prior section 2981, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/88/452/tVII/s701), Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 534](/us/stat/78/534); [Pub. L. 89–794, title VII, § 701(a)](/us/pl/89/794/tVII/s701/a), Nov. 8, 1966, [80 Stat. 1472](/us/stat/80/1472); [Pub. L. 90–222, title I, § 109](/us/pl/90/222/tI/s109), Dec. 23, 1967, [81 Stat. 722](/us/stat/81/722), respecting treatment of income for certain public assistance purposes, related to provisions of State plans and withholding of payments.

Section 2981a, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/88/452/tVII/s702), as added [Pub. L. 93–644, § 10(a)](/us/pl/93/644/s10/a), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2315](/us/stat/88/2315), defined “community development corporation”.

Section 2981b, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII, § 703](/us/pl/88/452/tVII/s703), as added [Pub. L. 93–644, § 10(a)](/us/pl/93/644/s10/a), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2316](/us/stat/88/2316); amended [Pub. L. 95–568, § 14(a)](/us/pl/95/568/s14/a), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2436](/us/stat/92/2436), related to authorization of appropriations.

Section 2981c, [Pub. L. 88–452, title VII, § 704](/us/pl/88/452/tVII/s704), as added [Pub. L. 95–568, § 14(b)](/us/pl/95/568/s14/b), Nov. 2, 1978, [92 Stat. 2436](/us/stat/92/2436), related to establishment, etc., of advisory community investment boards, and the National Board.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1981, see [section 9912(a) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s9912/a), prior to the general amendment of chapter 106 (§ 9901 et seq.) of this title by [Pub. L. 105–285](/us/pl/105/285).
