# §§ 1380a to 1380p. Omitted

##### §§ 1380a to 1380p. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 1380a to 1380p of this title were effective only with respect to 1957 and 1958 rice crops.

Section 1380a, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380a](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380a), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 208](/us/stat/70/208), provided legislative findings for this part.

Section 1380b, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380b](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380b), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 208](/us/stat/70/208), related to effective date and termination of program.

Section 1380c, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380c](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380c), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 208](/us/stat/70/208), related to determination of primary market quota for rice.

Section 1380d, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380d](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380d), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 209](/us/stat/70/209), related to apportionment of the primary market quota by the Secretary among the States and among farms.

Section 1380e, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380e](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380e), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 209](/us/stat/70/209), provided that a farm operator to which a primary market quota applied could have such quota reviewed.

Section 1380f, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380f](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380f), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 209](/us/stat/70/209), related to price supports made available to cooperators on crops of rice.

Section 1380g, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380g](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380g), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 209](/us/stat/70/209), related to certificates issued to cooperators.

Section 1380h, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380h](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380h), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 210](/us/stat/70/210), related to inventory adjustment payments to persons owning rough rice located in continental United States, for purpose of facilitating transition from price support program formerly in effect.

Section 1380i, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380i](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380i), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 210](/us/stat/70/210), related to set-aside of certain rough and processed rice.

Section 1380j, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380j](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380j), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 210](/us/stat/70/210), related to exemptions from provisions of this part.

Section 1380k, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380k](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380k), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 210](/us/stat/70/210), related to rice processing restrictions.

Section 1380*l*, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380)*l*, as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 211](/us/stat/70/211), related to rice import restrictions.

Section 1380m, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380m](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380m), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 211](/us/stat/70/211), directed the Secretary to prescribe regulations governing the issuance, redemption, acquisition, use, transfer, and disposition of certificates.

Section 1380n, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380n](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380n), as added May 28, 1956, ch 327, title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 211](/us/stat/70/211), related to penalties for violations of import and processing restrictions of this part or regulations prescribed by the Secretary for enforcing such provisions.

Section 1380*o*, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380)*o*, as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 211](/us/stat/70/211), related to reports and records.

Section 1380p, [act Feb. 16, 1938, ch. 30, title III, § 380p](/us/act/1938-02-16/ch30/tIII/s380p), as added [May 28, 1956, ch. 327](/us/act/1956-05-28/ch327), title V, § 501(3), [70 Stat. 211](/us/stat/70/211), defined “cooperator”, “processing of rough rice”, “processed rice”, “United States”, “exporter”, “rough rice equivalent”, and “import”, for purposes of this part.
