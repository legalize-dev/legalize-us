# §§ 450b to 450e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–578, title III, § 308(e)](/us/pl/94/578/tIII/s308/e), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2736](/us/stat/90/2736)

##### §§ 450b to 450e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–578, title III, § 308(e)](/us/pl/94/578/tIII/s308/e), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2736](/us/stat/90/2736)

Section 450b, acts [June 18, 1930, ch. 520, § 1](/us/act/1930-06-18/ch520/s1), [46 Stat. 777](/us/stat/46/777); [Aug. 13, 1935, ch. 520, § 1](/us/act/1935-08-13/ch520/s1), [49 Stat. 613](/us/stat/49/613); [Apr. 15, 1954, ch. 142](/us/act/1954-04-15/ch142), [68 Stat. 54](/us/stat/68/54), provided for creation of the Appomattox Court House National Historical Park.

Section 450c, acts [June 18, 1930, ch. 520, § 2](/us/act/1930-06-18/ch520/s2), [46 Stat. 777](/us/stat/46/777); [Aug. 13, 1935, ch. 520, § 1](/us/act/1935-08-13/ch520/s1), [49 Stat. 613](/us/stat/49/613), authorized appropriation of $100,000 for the Appomattox Court House National Historical Park.

Section 450d, acts [June 18, 1930, ch. 520, § 3](/us/act/1930-06-18/ch520/s3), [46 Stat. 777](/us/stat/46/777); [Aug. 13, 1935, ch. 520, § 1](/us/act/1935-08-13/ch520/s1), [49 Stat. 613](/us/stat/49/613), authorized Secretary of the Interior to accept donations of land or buildings within boundaries of the park.

Section 450d–1, acts [July 17, 1953, ch. 227](/us/act/1953-07-17/ch227), [67 Stat. 181](/us/stat/67/181); [Apr. 15, 1954, ch. 142](/us/act/1954-04-15/ch142), [68 Stat. 54](/us/stat/68/54), authorized exchange of land in park for adjacent non-Federal land.

Section 450e, [act June 18, 1930, ch. 520, § 4](/us/act/1930-06-18/ch520/s4), as added [Aug. 13, 1935, ch. 520, § 2](/us/act/1935-08-13/ch520/s2), [49 Stat. 614](/us/stat/49/614); amended [Apr. 15, 1954, ch. 142](/us/act/1954-04-15/ch142), [68 Stat. 54](/us/stat/68/54), provided for administration of park by National Park Service under the direction of Secretary of the Interior.
