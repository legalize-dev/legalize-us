# § 312106. Consultation with National Park System Advisory Board

##### § 312106. Consultation with National Park System Advisory Board

In carrying out its functions under this chapter, the National Trust may consult with the National Park System Advisory Board on matters relating to the selection of sites, buildings, and objects to be preserved and protected pursuant to this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3251](/us/stat/128/3251).)*

The words “National Park System Advisory Board” are substituted for “Advisory Board on National Parks, Historic Sites, Buildings, and Museums” because the Board was renamed when [section 3 of the Act of August 21, 1935](/us/act/1935-08-21/s3) (known as the Historic Sites, Buildings, and Antiquities Act (ch. 593, [49 Stat. 667](/us/stat/49/667))) was amended by [section 9 of Public Law 91–383](/us/pl/91/383/s9), as added by [section 2 of Public Law 94–458](/us/pl/94/458/s2) ([90 Stat. 1940](/us/stat/90/1940)).
