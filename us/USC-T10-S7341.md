# § 7341. General rule

##### § 7341. General rule

(a)

(1) The retired grade of a regular commissioned officer of the Army who retires other than for physical disability is determined under [section 1370 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1370).

(2) The retired grade of a reserve commissioned officer of the Army who retires other than for physical disability is determined under [section 1370a of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1370a).

(b) Unless entitled to a higher retired grade under some other provision of law, a Regular or Reserve of the Army not covered by subsection (a) who retires other than for physical disability retires in the regular or reserve grade that the member holds on the date of the member’s retirement.

#### Amendments

2021—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 116–283, § 508(b)(2)(A)](/us/pl/116/283/s508/b/2/A), added subsec. (a) and struck out former subsec. (a) which read as follows: “The retired grade of a regular commissioned officer of the Army who retires other than for physical disability, and the retired grade of a reserve commissioned officer of the Army who retires other than for physical disability, is determined under [section 1370 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1370).”

2000—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 106–398](/us/pl/106/398) struck out “or for nonregular service under chapter 1223 of this title” before “, is determined”.

1994—Subsec. (a). [Pub. L. 103–337](/us/pl/103/337) substituted “chapter 1223” for “chapter 67”.

1980—[Pub. L. 96–513](/us/pl/96/513) added subsec. (a), designated existing provisions as subsec. (b), and inserted “not covered by subsection (a)” after “Regular or Reserve of the Army”.
