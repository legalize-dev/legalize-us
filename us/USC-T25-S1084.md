# § 1084. Omitted

##### § 1084. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–655, § 4](/us/pl/89/655/s4), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 905](/us/stat/80/905), which related to inclusion of names in the Hoh base roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
