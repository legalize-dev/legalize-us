# § 1102. Statement of purpose

##### § 1102. Statement of purpose

(1) the removal by a date certain of the Federal Government’s obligation to subsidize the freight operations of Conrail;

(2) transfer of Conrail commuter service responsibilities to one or more entities whose principal purpose is the provision of commuter service; and

(3) an orderly return of Conrail freight service to the private sector.

*([Pub. L. 97–35, title XI, § 1133](/us/pl/97/35/tXI/s1133), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 644](/us/stat/95/644).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subtitle, referred to in text, is subtitle E (§§ 1131–1169) of title XI of [Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 643](/us/stat/95/643), known as the Northeast Rail Service Act of 1981. For complete classification of this subtitle to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1101 of this title](/us/usc/t45/s1101) and Tables.
