# § 1219. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–331, § 5(a)](/us/pl/104/331/s5/a), Oct. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 4072](/us/stat/110/4072)

##### § 1219. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–331, § 5(a)](/us/pl/104/331/s5/a), Oct. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 4072](/us/stat/110/4072)

Section, [Pub. L. 102–166, title III, § 303](/us/pl/102/166/tIII/s303), formerly § 320, Nov. 21, 1991, [105 Stat. 1096](/us/stat/105/1096); renumbered § 303 and amended [Pub. L. 104–1, title V, § 504(a)(3)](/us/pl/104/1/tV/s504/a/3), (4), Jan. 23, 1995, [109 Stat. 41](/us/stat/109/41), provided protection from discriminatory practices with respect to employment of Presidential appointees.

A prior [section 303 of Pub. L. 102–166](/us/pl/102/166/s303) was classified to [section 1203 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s1203) prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 104–1](/us/pl/104/1).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 104–331, § 5(b)](/us/pl/104/331/s5/b), Oct. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 4072](/us/stat/110/4072), provided that: “This section [repealing this section and enacting provisions set out as a note below] shall take effect on October 1, 1997.”

#### Savings Provision

[Pub. L. 104–331, § 5(c)](/us/pl/104/331/s5/c), Oct. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 4072](/us/stat/110/4072), provided that: “The repeal under this section [repealing this section] shall not affect proceedings under such section 303 in which a complaint was filed before the effective date of this section [Oct. 1, 1997], and orders shall be issued in such proceedings and appeals shall be taken therefrom as if this section had not been enacted.”
