# § 2036. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–91, title VII, § 709(c)(1)](/us/pl/95/91/tVII/s709/c/1), Aug. 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 608](/us/stat/91/608)

##### § 2036. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–91, title VII, § 709(c)(1)](/us/pl/95/91/tVII/s709/c/1), Aug. 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 608](/us/stat/91/608)

Section, [act Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724, § 26](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724/s26), as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 926](/us/stat/68/926), established a General Advisory Committee to advise the Atomic Energy Commission on scientific and technical matters relating to materials, production, and research and development.

Provisions similar to this section were contained in [section 1802(b) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1802/b) prior to the general amendment and renumbering of act Aug. 1, 1946, by act Aug. 30, 1954.
