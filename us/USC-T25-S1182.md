# § 1182. Omitted

##### § 1182. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 90–508, § 2](/us/pl/90/508/s2), Sept. 21, 1968, [82 Stat. 861](/us/stat/82/861), which provided for judgment funds to be divided equally between the Cherokee Delawares and the Delaware Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
