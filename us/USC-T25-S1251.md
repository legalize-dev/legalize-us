# § 1251. Omitted

##### § 1251. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–253, § 1](/us/pl/92/253/s1), Mar. 17, 1972, [86 Stat. 64](/us/stat/86/64), which related to distribution of judgment funds and payment of attorney fees and expenses, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
