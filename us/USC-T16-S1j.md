L. 110–229, title III, § 301](/us/pl/110/229/tIII/s301), May 8, 2008, [122 Stat. 768](/us/stat/122/768), related to cooperative agreements for national park natural resource protection. Subsecs. (a) to (c) were repealed and restated in [section 101702(d) of Title 54](/us/usc/t54/s101702/d), National Park Service and Related Programs. Subsec.
