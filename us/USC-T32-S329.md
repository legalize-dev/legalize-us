# § 329. Prohibition on private funding for interstate deployment

##### § 329. Prohibition on private funding for interstate deployment

A member of the National Guard may not be ordered to cross a border of a State to perform duty (under this title or title 10) if such duty is paid for with private funds, unless such duty is in response to a major disaster or emergency under section 401 of the Robert T. Stafford Disaster Relief and Emergency Assistance Act ([42 U.S.C. 5170](/us/usc/t42/s5170)).

*(Added [Pub. L. 117–81, div. A, title V, § 512(a)](/us/pl/117/81/dA/tV/s512/a), Dec. 27, 2021, [135 Stat. 1682](/us/stat/135/1682).)*
