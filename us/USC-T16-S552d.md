# § 552d. Punishment of violations of regulations

##### § 552d. Punishment of violations of regulations

Any violation of the regulations issued under sections 552a to 552d of this title shall be punished as is provided in [section 1853 of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1853).

*([May 28, 1940, ch. 220, § 4](/us/act/1940-05-28/ch220/s4), [54 Stat. 225](/us/stat/54/225).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

“[Section 1853 of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s1853)” substituted in text for “[section 104 of title 18](/us/usc/t18/s104)” on authority of [act June 25, 1948, ch. 645](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645), [62 Stat. 683](/us/stat/62/683), the first section of which enacted Title 18, Crimes and Criminal Procedure.
