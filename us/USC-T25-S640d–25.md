#### Codification

L. 100–666](/us/pl/100/666), §§ 3, 4(b), Nov. 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 3929](/us/stat/102/3929), 3930, which related to discretionary fund to expedite relocation efforts, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
