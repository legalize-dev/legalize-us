# § 1087–54. Sources of matching funds

##### § 1087–54. Sources of matching funds

Nothing in this part shall be construed as restricting the source (other than this part) from which the institution may pay its share of the compensation of a student employed under a work-study program covered by an agreement under this part, and such share may be paid to such student in the form of services and equipment (including tuition, room, board, and books) furnished by such institution.

*([Pub. L. 89–329, title IV, § 444](/us/pl/89/329/tIV/s444), as added [Pub. L. 99–498, title IV, § 403(a)](/us/pl/99/498/tIV/s403/a), Oct. 17, 1986, [100 Stat. 1435](/us/stat/100/1435).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 2754 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s2754), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to transfer to this section. See note below.

Section was originally enacted as section 124 (and later renumbered section 144) of the Economic Opportunity Act of 1964, [Pub. L. 88–452](/us/pl/88/452), at which time it was classified to [section 2754 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s2754), The Public Health and Welfare. It was renumbered as section 444 of title IV of the Higher Education Act of 1965, [Pub. L. 89–329](/us/pl/89/329), by [Pub. L. 90–575, § 131(a)](/us/pl/90/575/s131/a), (b)(1), and later editorially transferred to this section to merge with the rest of the Act, which is classified to this chapter.
