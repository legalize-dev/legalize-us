# §§ 27 to 29. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

##### §§ 27 to 29. Repealed. [June 25, 1948, ch. 646, § 39](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646/s39), [62 Stat. 992](/us/stat/62/992), eff. Sept. 1, 1948

Section 27, acts [May 7, 1894, ch. 72, § 5](/us/act/1894-05-07/ch72/s5), [28 Stat. 74](/us/stat/28/74); [Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231, § 291](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231/s291), [36 Stat. 1167](/us/stat/36/1167); [June 28, 1938, ch. 778, § 1](/us/act/1938-06-28/ch778/s1), [52 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/52/1213), related to jurisdiction and powers of commissioner [now magistrate judges]. See sections 131, 631, and 632 of Title 28, Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.

Section 28, [act May 7, 1894, ch. 72, § 6](/us/act/1894-05-07/ch72/s6), [28 Stat. 75](/us/stat/28/75), related to deputy marshals. See [section 562 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s562).

Section 29, acts [May 7, 1894, ch. 72, § 7](/us/act/1894-05-07/ch72/s7), [28 Stat. 75](/us/stat/28/75); [Apr. 17, 1900, ch. 192, § 1](/us/act/1900-04-17/ch192/s1), [31 Stat. 133](/us/stat/31/133); [Mar. 4, 1923, ch. 295](/us/act/1923-03-04/ch295), [42 Stat. 1560](/us/stat/42/1560), related to compensation of commissioners [now magistrate judges], marshals, and United States attorneys. See sections 548, 571, 572, and 634 of Title 28.
