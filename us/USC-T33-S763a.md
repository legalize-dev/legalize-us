# § 763a. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20), [63 Stat. 561](/us/stat/63/561)

##### § 763a. Repealed. [Aug. 4, 1949, ch. 393, § 20](/us/act/1949-08-04/ch393/s20), [63 Stat. 561](/us/stat/63/561)

Section, [act May 22, 1926, ch. 371, § 7](/us/act/1926-05-22/ch371/s7), [44 Stat. 626](/us/stat/44/626), related to retirement of certain officers and employees of the Lighthouse Service.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective first day of third month after approval by President [Aug. 4, 1949], see section 19 of act Aug. 4, 1949, set out as an Effective Date note preceding chapter 1 of Title 14, Coast Guard.
