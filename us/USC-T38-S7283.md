# § 7283. Disposition of fees

##### § 7283. Disposition of fees

Except for amounts received pursuant to [section 7285 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s7285), all fees received by the Court of Appeals for Veterans Claims shall be covered into the Treasury as miscellaneous receipts.

*(Added [Pub. L. 100–687, div. A, title III, § 301(a)](/us/pl/100/687/dA/tIII/s301/a), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4118](/us/stat/102/4118), § 4083; renumbered § 7283 and amended [Pub. L. 102–40, title IV, § 402(b)(1)](/us/pl/102/40/tIV/s402/b/1), (d)(1), May 7, 1991, [105 Stat. 238](/us/stat/105/238), 239; [Pub. L. 105–368, title V, § 512(a)(1)](/us/pl/105/368/tV/s512/a/1), Nov. 11, 1998, [112 Stat. 3341](/us/stat/112/3341).)*

#### Amendments
