# § 1169. Regulations

##### § 1169. Regulations

The Secretary is authorized to prescribe such regulations as he deems necessary to carry out the provisions of this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 89–702, title II, § 211](/us/pl/89/702/tII/s211), as added [Pub. L. 98–129, § 2](/us/pl/98/129/s2), Oct. 14, 1983, [97 Stat. 844](/us/stat/97/844).)*
