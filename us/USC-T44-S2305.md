# § 2305. Acceptance of gifts

##### § 2305. Acceptance of gifts

The Board may solicit and accept gifts or bequests of money, securities, or other personal property, for the benefit of or in connection with the national archival and records activities administered by the National Archives and Records Administration. Moneys that are for deposit into the trust fund shall be deposited within 10 working days of the receipt thereof.

*([Pub. L. 90–620](/us/pl/90/620), Oct. 22, 1968, [82 Stat. 1292](/us/stat/82/1292); [Pub. L. 98–497, title II, § 202(a)](/us/pl/98/497/tII/s202/a), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2293](/us/stat/98/2293).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on 44 U.S. Code, 1964 ed., § 300cc ([July 9, 1941, ch. 284, § 3](/us/act/1941-07-09/ch284/s3), [55 Stat. 581](/us/stat/55/581)).

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1984—[Pub. L. 98–497](/us/pl/98/497) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, section read as follows: “The Board may accept, receive, hold, and administer gifts or bequests of money, securities, or other personal property, for the benefit of or in connection with the national archival and records activities administered by the General Services Administration as may be approved by the Board.”
