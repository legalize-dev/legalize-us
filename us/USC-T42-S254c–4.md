# § 254c–4. Repealed. [Pub. L. 117–328, div. FF, title II, § 2201(a)(2)](/us/pl/117/328/dFF/tII/s2201/a/2), Dec. 29, 2022, [136 Stat. 5729](/us/stat/136/5729)

##### § 254c–4. Repealed. [Pub. L. 117–328, div. FF, title II, § 2201(a)(2)](/us/pl/117/328/dFF/tII/s2201/a/2), Dec. 29, 2022, [136 Stat. 5729](/us/stat/136/5729)

Section, [act July 1, 1944, ch. 373, title III, § 330D](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373/tIII/s330D), as added [Pub. L. 106–129, § 3](/us/pl/106/129/s3), Dec. 6, 1999, [113 Stat. 1670](/us/stat/113/1670), authorized grants for establishment and operation of regional centers to develop, evaluate, and disseminate effective strategies on facilitating utilization of preventive health services among various populations.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

[Pub. L. 117–328](/us/pl/117/328), which directed amendment of Part B of title III of the Public Health Service Act by striking section 330D, was executed by striking section 330D (this section) of Part D of title III of the Public Health Service Act, to reflect the probable intent of Congress.
