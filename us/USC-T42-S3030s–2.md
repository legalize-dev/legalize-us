# § 3030s–2. Maintenance of effort

##### § 3030s–2. Maintenance of effort

Funds made available under this part shall supplement, and not supplant, any Federal, State, or local funds expended by a State or unit of general purpose local government (including an area agency on aging) to provide services described in [section 3030s–1 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s3030s–1).

*([Pub. L. 89–73, title III, § 374](/us/pl/89/73/tIII/s374), as added [Pub. L. 106–501, title III, § 316(2)](/us/pl/106/501/tIII/s316/2), Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2256](/us/stat/114/2256); amended [Pub. L. 114–144, § 4(m)](/us/pl/114/144/s4/m), Apr. 19, 2016, [130 Stat. 341](/us/stat/130/341).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

Prior sections 3030s–11 and 3030s–12, which comprised subpart II of this part, were repealed by [Pub. L. 109–365, title III, § 322](/us/pl/109/365/tIII/s322), Oct. 17, 2006, [120 Stat. 2552](/us/stat/120/2552).

Section 3030s–11, [Pub. L. 89–73, title III, § 375](/us/pl/89/73/tIII/s375), as added [Pub. L. 106–501, title III, § 316(2)](/us/pl/106/501/tIII/s316/2), Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2256](/us/stat/114/2256), provided for an innovation grant program.

Section 3030s–12, [Pub. L. 89–73, title III, § 376](/us/pl/89/73/tIII/s376), as added [Pub. L. 106–501, title III, § 316(2)](/us/pl/106/501/tIII/s316/2), Nov. 13, 2000, [114 Stat. 2257](/us/stat/114/2257), directed Assistant Secretary to carry out activities of national significance to promote quality and continuous improvement in the support provided to family and other informal caregivers of older individuals.

#### Amendments

2016—[Pub. L. 114–144](/us/pl/114/144) substituted “this part” for “this subpart”.
