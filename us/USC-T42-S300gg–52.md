# § 300gg–52. Required coverage for reconstructive surgery following mastectomies

##### § 300gg–52. Required coverage for reconstructive surgery following mastectomies

The provisions of section 2706 11 See References in Text note below. shall apply to health insurance coverage offered by a health insurance issuer in the individual market in the same manner as they apply to health insurance coverage offered by a health insurance issuer in connection with a group health plan in the small or large group market.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XXVII, § 2752, as added [Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(f) [title IX, § 903(b)]](/us/pl/105/277/dA/s101/f/tIX/s903/b), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–337](/us/stat/112/2681-337), 2681–438.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

Section 2706, referred to in text, is a reference to section 2706 of act July 1, 1944. Section 2706, which was classified to [section 300gg–6 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300gg–6), was renumbered section 2727 and amended by [Pub. L. 111–148, title I](/us/pl/111/148/tI), §§ 1001(2), 1563(c)(5), formerly § 1562(c)(5), title X, § 10107(b)(1), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 130](/us/stat/124/130), 266, 911, and was transferred to [section 300gg–27 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300gg–27). A new section 2706 of act July 1, 1944, related to non-discrimination in health care, was added, effective for plan years beginning on or after Jan. 1, 2014, by [Pub. L. 111–148, title I, § 1201(4)](/us/pl/111/148/tI/s1201/4), Mar. 23, 2010, [124 Stat. 160](/us/stat/124/160), and is classified to [section 300gg–5 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300gg–5).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(f) [title IX, § 903(c)(2)]](/us/pl/105/277/dA/s101/f/tIX/s903/c/2), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–337](/us/stat/112/2681-337), 2681–438, provided that: “The amendment made by subsection (b) [enacting this section] shall apply with respect to health insurance coverage offered, sold, issued, renewed, in effect, or operated in the individual market on or after the date of enactment of this Act [Oct. 21, 1998].”
