# § 254c–17. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–163, § 2(e)(2)](/us/pl/108/163/s2/e/2), Dec. 6, 2003, [117 Stat. 2021](/us/stat/117/2021)

##### § 254c–17. Repealed. [Pub. L. 108–163, § 2(e)(2)](/us/pl/108/163/s2/e/2), Dec. 6, 2003, [117 Stat. 2021](/us/stat/117/2021)

Section, [Pub. L. 107–251, title I, § 102](/us/pl/107/251/tI/s102), Oct. 26, 2002, [116 Stat. 1627](/us/stat/116/1627), provided for grants to State professional licensing boards to develop and implement State policies to promote telemedicine.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal deemed to have taken effect immediately after the enactment of [Pub. L. 107–251](/us/pl/107/251), see [section 3 of Pub. L. 108–163](/us/pl/108/163/s3), set out as an Effective Date of 2003 Amendments note under [section 233 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s233).
