# § 10304. Regulations

##### § 10304. Regulations

(a) In general The Attorney General may promulgate reasonable and necessary regulations to implement this part.

(b) Sliding scale Notwithstanding [section 10303(b) of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10303/b), the Attorney General shall issue regulations regarding the use of a sliding scale based on financial need to ensure that an eligible person who is in financial need receives priority in receiving funds under this part.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3796d–3 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796d–3), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2013—Subsec. (b). [Pub. L. 112–239](/us/pl/112/239) substituted “person” for “dependent”.

1998—[Pub. L. 105–390](/us/pl/105/390) designated existing provisions as subsec. (a), inserted heading, and added subsec. (b).
