# § 8711. Amendments to orders

##### § 8711. Amendments to orders

The provisions of this chapter applicable to the order shall be applicable to any amendment to the order, except that [section 8707 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s8707) shall not apply to an amendment.

*([Pub. L. 115–254, div. E, § 1312](/us/pl/115/254/dE/s1312), Oct. 5, 2018, [132 Stat. 3483](/us/stat/132/3483).)*
