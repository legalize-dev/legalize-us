# § 80101. Organization

##### § 80101. Organization

(a) Federal Charter.— General Federation of Women’s Clubs (in this chapter, the “corporation”) is a body corporate and politic of the District of Columbia.

(b) Perpetual Existence.— Except as otherwise provided, the corporation has perpetual existence.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1360](/us/stat/112/1360).)*

This section is substituted for the source provisions for consistency in the revised title and to eliminate executed and unnecessary words.
