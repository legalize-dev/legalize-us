# § 1109. Federal Employees Compensation Account

##### § 1109. Federal Employees Compensation Account

There is hereby established in the Unemployment Trust Fund a Federal Employees Compensation Account which shall be used for the purposes specified in [section 8509 of title 5](/us/usc/t5/s8509). For the purposes provided for in [section 1104(e) of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1104/e), such account shall be maintained as a separate book account.

*([Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531), title IX, § 909, as added [Pub. L. 96–499, title X, § 1023(a)](/us/pl/96/499/tX/s1023/a), Dec. 5, 1980, [94 Stat. 2657](/us/stat/94/2657).)*
