##### § 1300i–9. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–580, § 10](/us/pl/100/580/s10), Oct. 31, 1988, [102 Stat. 2934](/us/stat/102/2934), which related to establishment of a plan for economic development for the Yurok Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
