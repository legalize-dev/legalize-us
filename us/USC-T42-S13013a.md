# § 13013a. Omitted

##### § 13013a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–647, title II, § 218](/us/pl/101/647/tII/s218), as added [Pub. L. 109–162, title I, § 112(d)(2)](/us/pl/109/162/tI/s112/d/2), Jan. 5, 2006, [119 Stat. 2986](/us/stat/119/2986), which required the Inspector General of the Department of Justice to submit a report to Congress not later than Dec. 31, 2006, on activities funded by the National Court-Appointed Special Advocate Association, was omitted as obsolete.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 218 of Pub. L. 101–647](/us/pl/101/647/s218) was renumbered section 219 and is classified to [section 20324 of Title 34](/us/usc/t34/s20324), Crime Control and Law Enforcement.
