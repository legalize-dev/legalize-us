# § 6536. Compensation of Financial Clerk of Senate

##### § 6536. Compensation of Financial Clerk of Senate

The Financial Clerk of the Senate may be paid at a maximum annual rate of compensation not to exceed $39,000.

*([Pub. L. 93–371, § 4](/us/pl/93/371/s4), Aug. 13, 1974, [88 Stat. 429](/us/stat/88/429); [Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 105](/us/pl/94/59/tI/s105), July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 275](/us/stat/89/275); [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/A), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2775](/us/stat/133/2775).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 64a–1 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s64a–1) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2019—[Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94) repealed [Pub. L. 94–59, § 105](/us/pl/94/59/s105). See 1975 Amendment note below.

1975—[Pub. L. 94–59](/us/pl/94/59), which substituted “$39,000” for “$37,620”, effective July 1, 1975, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94). See above.
