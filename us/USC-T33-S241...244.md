# §§ 241 to 244. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(b)](/us/pl/96/591/s8/b), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435](/us/stat/94/3435)

##### §§ 241 to 244. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–591, § 8(b)](/us/pl/96/591/s8/b), Dec. 24, 1980, [94 Stat. 3435](/us/stat/94/3435)

Section 241, acts [Feb. 8, 1895, ch. 64, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-08/ch64/s1), [28 Stat. 645](/us/stat/28/645); Mar. 28, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–350, § 1](/us/pl/85/350/s1), [72 Stat. 49](/us/stat/72/49), required adoption of rules for navigation of Great Lakes and connecting and tributary waters.

Section 242, acts [Feb. 8, 1895, ch. 64, § 1](/us/act/1895-02-08/ch64/s1), [28 Stat. 645](/us/stat/28/645), defined “sailing vessel”, “steam vessel” and “under way”.

Section 243, acts [Feb. 8, 1895, ch. 64, § 3](/us/act/1895-02-08/ch64/s3), [28 Stat. 649](/us/stat/28/649); [Feb. 14, 1903, ch. 552, § 10](/us/act/1903-02-14/ch552/s10), [32 Stat. 829](/us/stat/32/829); [Mar. 4, 1913, ch. 141, § 1](/us/act/1913-03-04/ch141/s1), [37 Stat. 736](/us/stat/37/736); 1946 Reorg. Plan No. 3, §§ 101–104, eff. July 16, 1946, 11 F.R. 7875, [60 Stat. 1097](/us/stat/60/1097), related to authority of Commandant of the Coast Guard to establish necessary regulations. See [section 2071 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2071).

Section 244, acts [Feb. 8, 1895, ch. 64, § 2](/us/act/1895-02-08/ch64/s2), [28 Stat. 649](/us/stat/28/649); Mar. 28, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–350, § 2](/us/pl/85/350/s2), [72 Stat. 49](/us/stat/72/49), related to liability of persons and private vessels for violation of provisions. See [section 2072 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2072).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Mar. 1, 1983, pursuant to 47 F.R. 15135, Apr. 8, 1982, see [section 7 of Pub. L. 96–591](/us/pl/96/591/s7), set out as an Effective Date of 1980 Amendment note under [section 1604 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1604).
