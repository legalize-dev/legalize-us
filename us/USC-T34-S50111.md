# § 50111. Issuance of rules

##### § 50111. Issuance of rules

The Attorney General, after consultation with appropriate members of the law enforcement community and with State and local officials, shall issue rules to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 609X](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s609X), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2107](/us/stat/98/2107).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 10512 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s10512), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
