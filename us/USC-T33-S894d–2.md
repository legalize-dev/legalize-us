# § 894d–2. Criminal referral

##### § 894d–2. Criminal referral

If the Secretary of Commerce finds, pursuant to an investigation under [section 894d–1 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s894d–1), evidence that a crime may have been committed, the Secretary shall refer the matter to the appropriate law enforcement authorities, including the appropriate United States Attorney.

*([Pub. L. 114–328, div. C, title XXXV, § 3547](/us/pl/114/328/dC/tXXXV/s3547), as added [Pub. L. 116–259, title V, § 504(b)(2)](/us/pl/116/259/tV/s504/b/2), Dec. 23, 2020, [134 Stat. 1180](/us/stat/134/1180).)*
