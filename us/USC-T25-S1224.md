##### § 1224. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–364, § 4](/us/pl/91/364/s4), July 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 688](/us/stat/84/688), which related to disposition of subsequent judgment funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
