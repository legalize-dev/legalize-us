# § 10407. Utilization of private sector

##### § 10407. Utilization of private sector

Funds or a portion of funds allocated under this subchapter may be used by a State or unit of local government that receives a grant under this subchapter to contract with private, nonprofit entities, or community-based organizations to carry out the purposes specified under [section 10401(b) of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10401/b).

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 1806](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s1806), as added [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title II, § 12102(a)](/us/pl/107/273/dC/tII/s12102/a), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1866](/us/stat/116/1866).)*
