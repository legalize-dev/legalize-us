# § 1701g–4. Omitted

##### § 1701g–4. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, which placed restrictions on loans, was from the Independent Offices Appropriation Act, 1953, [act July 5, 1952, ch. 578, title III, § 301](/us/act/1952-07-05/ch578/tIII/s301), [66 Stat. 415](/us/stat/66/415), and was not repeated in subsequent appropriation acts.
