# § 1234. Omitted

##### § 1234. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 91–417, § 4](/us/pl/91/417/s4), Sept. 25, 1970, [84 Stat. 868](/us/stat/84/868), which exempted funds distributed under this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
