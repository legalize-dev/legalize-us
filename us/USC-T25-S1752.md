# § 1752. Omitted

##### § 1752. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–134, § 3](/us/pl/98/134/s3), Oct. 18, 1983, [97 Stat. 852](/us/stat/97/852), which set out definitions for this subchapter, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
