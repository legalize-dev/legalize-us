# § 12225. Award of grants

##### § 12225. Award of grants

(1) Demonstrated need and evidence of the ability to provide the services described in the plan required under [section 12223 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s12223).

(2) The Attorney General shall attempt, to the extent practicable, to achieve an equitable geographic distribution of grant awards.

*([Pub. L. 103–322, title III, § 31705](/us/pl/103/322/tIII/s31705), Sept. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 1891](/us/stat/108/1891).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 13865 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s13865), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
