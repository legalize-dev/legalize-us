# § 7562. Clothing

##### § 7562. Clothing

The President may prescribe the quantity and kind of clothing to be issued annually to members of the Army.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 256](/us/stat/70A/256), § 4562; renumbered § 7562, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(d)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/d), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1839](/us/stat/132/1839).)*

The words “members of the Army” are substituted for the words “troops of the United States”.
