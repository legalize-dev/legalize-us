# § 1300e. Omitted

##### § 1300e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–557, § 1](/us/pl/92/557/s1), Oct. 25, 1972, [86 Stat. 1171](/us/stat/86/1171), which related to division of judgment funds between tribal groups and payment of attorney fees and expenses, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
