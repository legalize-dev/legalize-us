##### § 1764. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–105, § 22](/us/pl/94/105/s22), Oct. 7, 1975, [89 Stat. 528](/us/stat/89/528)

Section, [act June 4, 1946, ch. 281, § 15](/us/act/1946-06-04/ch281/s15), as added June 30, 1971, [Pub. L. 92–32, § 1](/us/pl/92/32/s1), [85 Stat. 85](/us/stat/85/85), authorized use, during fiscal 1971, of not to exceed $35,000,000 from [section 612c of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s612c), and not to exceed $100,000,000 during fiscal 1972 to carry out provisions of this chapter, with unexpended funds to remain available in accordance with last sentence of [section 1752 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1752).
