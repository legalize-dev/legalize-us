#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective July 1, 1988, see [section 6303 of Pub. L. 100–297](/us/pl/100/297/s6303), set out as an Effective Date of 1988 Amendment note under [section 1071 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1071).

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 98–511, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/98/511/tVI/s601), Oct. 19, 1984, [98 Stat. 2401](/us/stat/98/2401), provided that title VI of [Pub. L. 98–511](/us/pl/98/511) was to be cited as the “Emergency Immigrant Education Act of 1984”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 100–297, title I, § 1003(c)](/us/pl/100/297/tI/s1003/c), Apr. 28, 1988, [102 Stat. 293](/us/stat/102/293).
