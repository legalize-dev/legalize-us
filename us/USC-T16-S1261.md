# § 1261. Repealed. [Pub. L. 105–178, title I, § 1112(c)](/us/pl/105/178/tI/s1112/c), June 9, 1998, [112 Stat. 151](/us/stat/112/151)

Section, [Pub. L. 102–240, title I, § 1302](/us/pl/102/240/tI/s1302), Dec. 18, 1991, [105 Stat. 2064](/us/stat/105/2064); [Pub. L. 104–59, title III, § 337(a)](/us/pl/104/59/tIII/s337/a)–(d), Nov. 28, 1995, [109 Stat. 602](/us/stat/109/602), 603; [Pub. [Pub. L. 102–240, title I, § 1301](/us/pl/102/240/tI/s1301), Dec. 18, 1991, [105 Stat. 2064](/us/stat/105/2064), provided that: “This part [part B (§§ 1301–1303) of title I of [Pub. L. 102–240](/us/pl/102/240), enacting this chapter] may be cited as the ‘Symms National Recreational Trails Act of 1991’.”
