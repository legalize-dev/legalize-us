# § 150501. Definition

##### § 150501. Definition

For purposes of this chapter, “State” includes the District of Columbia.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1389](/us/stat/112/1389).)*
