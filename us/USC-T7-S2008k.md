# § 2008k. Making and servicing of loans by personnel of State, county, or area committees

##### § 2008k. Making and servicing of loans by personnel of State, county, or area committees

The Secretary shall use personnel of a State, county or area committee established under [section 590h(b)(5) of title 16](/us/usc/t16/s590h/b/5) to make and service loans under this chapter to the extent the personnel have been trained to do so.

*([Pub. L. 87–128, title III, § 376](/us/pl/87/128/tIII/s376), as added [Pub. L. 107–171, title V, § 5320](/us/pl/107/171/tV/s5320), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 349](/us/stat/116/349).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this title”, meaning title III of [Pub. L. 87–128](/us/pl/87/128), Aug. 8, 1961, [75 Stat. 307](/us/stat/75/307), known as the Consolidated Farm and Rural Development Act, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of title III to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 1921 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s1921) and Tables.
