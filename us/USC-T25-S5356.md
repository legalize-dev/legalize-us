# § 5356. Supplemental assistance to funds provided to local educational agencies

##### § 5356. Supplemental assistance to funds provided to local educational agencies

The assistance provided in this chapter for the education of Indians in the public schools of any State is in addition and supplemental to assistance provided under title VI of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 [[20 U.S.C. 7401](/us/usc/t20/s7401) et seq.].

*([Pub. L. 93–638, title II, § 209](/us/pl/93/638/tII/s209), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2217](/us/stat/88/2217); [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 393(c)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s393/c), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4027](/us/stat/108/4027); [Pub. L. 114–95, title IX, § 9215(rr)](/us/pl/114/95/tIX/s9215/rr), Dec. 10, 2015, [129 Stat. 2181](/us/stat/129/2181).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 93–638](/us/pl/93/638), Jan. 4, 1975, [88 Stat. 2203](/us/stat/88/2203), known as the Indian Self-Determination and Education Assistance Act, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 5301 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5301) and Tables.

The Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965, referred to in text, is [Pub. L. 89–10](/us/pl/89/10), Apr. 11, 1965, [79 Stat. 27](/us/stat/79/27). Title VI of the Act is classified generally to subchapter VI (§ 7401 et seq.) of chapter 70 of Title 20, Education. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 6301 of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s6301) and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 458e of this title](/us/usc/t25/s458e) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2015—[Pub. L. 114–95](/us/pl/114/95) substituted “assistance provided under title VI of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965.” for “assistance provided under title IX of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965.”

1994—[Pub. L. 103–382](/us/pl/103/382) substituted “title IX of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965” for “title IV of the Act of June 23, 1972 ([86 Stat. 235](/us/stat/86/235))”.
