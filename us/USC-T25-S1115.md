##### § 1115. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–659, § 5](/us/pl/89/659/s5), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 909](/us/stat/80/909), which related to applications for enrollment, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
