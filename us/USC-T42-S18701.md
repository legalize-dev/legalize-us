# § 18701. Definitions

##### § 18701. Definitions

(1) Department The term “Department” means the Department of Energy.

(2) Indian Tribe The term “Indian Tribe” has the meaning given the term in [section 5304 of title 25](/us/usc/t25/s5304).

(3) Secretary The term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Energy.

*([Pub. L. 117–58, div. D, § 40001](/us/pl/117/58/dD/s40001), Nov. 15, 2021, [135 Stat. 923](/us/stat/135/923).)*
