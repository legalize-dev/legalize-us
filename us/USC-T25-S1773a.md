# § 1773a. Omitted

##### § 1773a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–41, § 3](/us/pl/101/41/s3), June 21, 1989, [103 Stat. 84](/us/stat/103/84), which provided for resolution of Puyallup tribal land claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
