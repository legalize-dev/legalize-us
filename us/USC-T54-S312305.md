# § 312305. Reports

##### § 312305. Reports

(1) a detailed statement of the activities and accomplishments of the Commission during the fiscal year; and

(2) any recommendations of the Commission for legislation and administrative actions.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3253](/us/stat/128/3253).)*

The words “and to each House of Congress” are omitted pursuant to section 3003 of the Federal Reports Elimination and Sunset Act of 1995 ([31 U.S.C. 1113](/us/usc/t31/s1113) note). See the 4th item on page 174 of House Document No. 103–7.
