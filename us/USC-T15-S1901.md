# § 1901. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379)

##### § 1901. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–513, § 2](/us/pl/92/513/s2), Oct. 20, 1972, [86 Stat. 947](/us/stat/86/947); [Pub. L. 94–163, title III, § 301](/us/pl/94/163/tIII/s301), Dec. 22, 1975, [89 Stat. 901](/us/stat/89/901); [Pub. L. 96–425, § 8(a)(2)](/us/pl/96/425/s8/a/2), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1828](/us/stat/94/1828); [Pub. L. 98–547, title I, § 101(b)](/us/pl/98/547/tI/s101/b), Oct. 25, 1984, [98 Stat. 2767](/us/stat/98/2767), defined terms for purposes of this chapter, except subchapter V of this chapter. See sections 32101, 32301, and 32502 of Title 49, Transportation.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 92–513, § 1](/us/pl/92/513/s1), Oct. 20, 1972, [86 Stat. 947](/us/stat/86/947), provided that [Pub. L. 92–513](/us/pl/92/513) (enacting this chapter) could be cited as the “Motor Vehicle Information and Cost Savings Act”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–272, § 7(b)](/us/pl/103/272/s7/b), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 1379](/us/stat/108/1379).
