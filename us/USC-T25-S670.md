# § 670. Omitted

##### § 670. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–312, § 3](/us/pl/92/312/s3), June 14, 1972, [86 Stat. 216](/us/stat/86/216), which authorized encumbrance by mortgage or deed of trust of lands sold pursuant to [section 668 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s668) and directed that the United States be a party to any foreclosure or sale proceedings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
