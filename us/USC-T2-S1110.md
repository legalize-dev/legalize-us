# § 1110. Appropriations

##### § 1110. Appropriations

There is appropriated to the fund the sum of $7,500,000 to carry out this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 100–458, title I, § 121](/us/pl/100/458/tI/s121), Oct. 1, 1988, [102 Stat. 2176](/us/stat/102/2176).)*
