# §§ 1071 to 1073. Repealed. [Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 654, § 1(114)](/us/act/1951-10-31/ch654/s1/114), [65 Stat. 706](/us/stat/65/706)

##### §§ 1071 to 1073. Repealed. [Oct. 31, 1951, ch. 654, § 1(114)](/us/act/1951-10-31/ch654/s1/114), [65 Stat. 706](/us/stat/65/706)

Section 1071, [act July 5, 1884, ch. 214, § 1](/us/act/1884-07-05/ch214/s1), [23 Stat. 103](/us/stat/23/103), provided for designation by President of abandoned military reservations for disposition by Secretary of the Interior.

Section 1072, [act July 5, 1884, ch. 214, § 2](/us/act/1884-07-05/ch214/s2), [23 Stat. 103](/us/stat/23/103), related to survey or subdivision of those lands and appraisal, advertisement and sale and rights of settlers.

Section 1073, [act July 5, 1884, ch. 214, § 3](/us/act/1884-07-05/ch214/s3), [23 Stat. 103](/us/stat/23/103), related to sale of improvements or other property on those reservations.
