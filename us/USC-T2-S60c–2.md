##### § 60c–2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–136, § 4](/us/pl/92/136/s4), Oct. 11, 1971, [85 Stat. 377](/us/stat/85/377), authorized and directed Secretary of Senate, if requested by an individual paid by Secretary, to pay compensation by sending a check to a financial organization designated by the individual. See [section 3332 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s3332), Money and Finance.
