# § 10308. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 10308. Authorization of appropriations

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part such sums as may be necessary.

*([Pub. L. 90–351, title I, § 1218](/us/pl/90/351/tI/s1218), as added [Pub. L. 104–238, § 2(2)](/us/pl/104/238/s2/2), Oct. 3, 1996, [110 Stat. 3117](/us/stat/110/3117).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 3796d–7 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s3796d–7), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
