Plan No. 3, § 3, eff. July 27, 1947, 12 F.R. 4981, [61 Stat. 954](/us/stat/61/954); [Apr. 20, 1950, ch. 94](/us/act/1950-04-20/ch94), title I, § 122, [64 Stat. 59](/us/stat/64/59), provided for disposition and investment of surplus money in the War Housing Insurance Fund and for credit and payment of charges and fees.
