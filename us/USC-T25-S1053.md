# § 1053. Omitted

##### § 1053. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 88–506, § 3](/us/pl/88/506/s3), Aug. 30, 1964, [78 Stat. 639](/us/stat/78/639), which exempted funds distributed in accordance with this subchapter from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
