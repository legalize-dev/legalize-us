# § 242q–5. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 242q–5. Authorization of appropriations

For the purpose of carrying out sections 242q to 242q–5 of this title, there are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1991 through 1993.

*([Pub. L. 101–557, title III, § 305](/us/pl/101/557/tIII/s305), formerly § 306, Nov. 15, 1990, [104 Stat. 2770](/us/stat/104/2770); renumbered § 305, [Pub. L. 109–482, title I, § 104(b)(3)(B)](/us/pl/109/482/tI/s104/b/3/B), Jan. 15, 2007, [120 Stat. 3694](/us/stat/120/3694).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was enacted as part of the Home Health Care and Alzheimer’s Disease Amendments of 1990, and not as part of the Public Health Service Act which comprises this chapter.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior [section 305 of Pub. L. 101–557](/us/pl/101/557/s305) was renumbered section 304 and is classified to [section 242q–4 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s242q–4).
