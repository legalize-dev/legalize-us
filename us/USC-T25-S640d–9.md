# § 640d–9. Omitted

##### § 640d–9. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 10](/us/pl/93/531/s10), Dec. 22, 1974, [88 Stat. 1716](/us/stat/88/1716); [Pub. L. 96–305, § 3](/us/pl/96/305/s3), July 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 929](/us/stat/94/929); [Pub. L. 100–666, § 6](/us/pl/100/666/s6), Nov. 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 3932](/us/stat/102/3932); [Pub. L. 111–18, § 1](/us/pl/111/18/s1), May 8, 2009, [123 Stat. 1611](/us/stat/123/1611), which related to treatment of lands partitioned to the Navajo Tribe or to the Hopi Tribe and protection of rights and property of individuals, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
