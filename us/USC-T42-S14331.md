# § 14331. Purpose

##### § 14331. Purpose

The purpose of this subchapter is to phase out the use of batteries containing mercury.

*([Pub. L. 104–142, title II, § 202](/us/pl/104/142/tII/s202), May 13, 1996, [110 Stat. 1336](/us/stat/110/1336).)*
