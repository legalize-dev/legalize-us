# [§ 7263. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1952(n)(3)(A)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1952/n/3/A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1846](/us/stat/90/1846)]

##### [§ 7263. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–455, title XIX, § 1952(n)(3)(A)](/us/pl/94/455/tXIX/s1952/n/3/A), Oct. 4, 1976, [90 Stat. 1846](/us/stat/90/1846)]

Section, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 862](/us/stat/68A/862), provided penalties for violations related to cotton futures.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on 90th day after Oct. 4, 1976, see [section 1952(o) of Pub. L. 94–455](/us/pl/94/455/s1952/o), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 15b of Title 7](/us/usc/t7/s15b), Agriculture.
