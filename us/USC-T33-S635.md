# § 635. Coast Guard anchorages

##### § 635. Coast Guard anchorages

The Secretary may perform dredging at Federal expense within and adjacent to anchorages established by the Coast Guard pursuant to existing authorities.

*([Pub. L. 116–260, div. AA, title I, § 106](/us/pl/116/260/dAA/tI/s106), Dec. 27, 2020, [134 Stat. 2623](/us/stat/134/2623).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### “Secretary” Defined

Secretary means the Secretary of the Army, see [section 2 of div. AA of Pub. L. 116–260](/us/pl/116/260/dAA/s2), set out as a note under [section 2201 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s2201).
