# § 3401. Transferred

##### § 3401. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 341(a)](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s341/a), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2116](/us/stat/107/2116), which related to nonimmigrant traders and investors, was renumbered as section 311(a) of subtitle B of title III of [Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113) by [Pub. L. 116–113, title V, § 503(b)(1)](/us/pl/116/113/tV/s503/b/1)–(3), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 71](/us/stat/134/71), and transferred to [section 4561(a) of this title](/us/usc/t19/s4561/a). After the renumbering and transfer of [section 341 of Pub. L. 103–182](/us/pl/103/182/s341) to [section 311 of Pub. L. 116–113](/us/pl/116/113/s311), the subsec. (a) designation of this section was struck out, and subsecs. (b) and (c), which had amended [section 1184 of Title 8](/us/usc/t8/s1184), Immigration and Nationality, were repealed.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

[Pub. L. 103–182, title III, § 342](/us/pl/103/182/tIII/s342), Dec. 8, 1993, [107 Stat. 2118](/us/stat/107/2118), which provided that subtitle D of title III of [Pub. L. 103–182](/us/pl/103/182) took effect on the date the North American Free Trade Agreement entered into force with respect to the United States, was repealed by [Pub. L. 116–113, title VI, § 601](/us/pl/116/113/tVI/s601), Jan. 29, 2020, [134 Stat. 78](/us/stat/134/78), effective on the date the USMCA entered into force (July 1, 2020).
