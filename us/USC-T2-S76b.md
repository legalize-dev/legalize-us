# § 76b. Omitted

##### § 76b. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section 76b, based on H. Res. No. 603, §§ 2, 3, Eighty-seventh Congress, Apr. 16, 1962, enacted into permanent law by [Pub. L. 88–248, § 103](/us/pl/88/248/s103), Dec. 30, 1963, [77 Stat. 817](/us/stat/77/817), related to compensation of telephone clerks in Office of Doorkeeper, was omitted from Code in view of repeal of section 2 of H. Res. No. 603 by [Pub. L. 104–186, title II, § 204(24)](/us/pl/104/186/tII/s204/24) Aug. 20, 1996, [110 Stat. 1733](/us/stat/110/1733).
