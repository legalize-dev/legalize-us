# § 783. Omitted

##### § 783. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 1, 1955, ch. 444, § 4](/us/act/1955-08-01/ch444/s4), [69 Stat. 432](/us/stat/69/432), which related to payments to minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
