# § 1142. Rates of peacetime death compensation

##### § 1142. Rates of peacetime death compensation

For the purposes of [section 1141 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s1141), the monthly rates of death compensation payable shall be those specified in [section 1122 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s1122).

*([Pub. L. 85–857](/us/pl/85/857), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1124](/us/stat/72/1124), § 342; [Pub. L. 93–295, title II, § 206(a)](/us/pl/93/295/tII/s206/a), May 31, 1974, [88 Stat. 183](/us/stat/88/183); renumbered § 1142 and amended [Pub. L. 102–83, § 5(a)](/us/pl/102/83/s5/a), (c)(1), Aug. 6, 1991, [105 Stat. 406](/us/stat/105/406).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1991—[Pub. L. 102–83, § 5(a)](/us/pl/102/83/s5/a), renumbered [section 342 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s342) as this section.

[Pub. L. 102–83, § 5(c)(1)](/us/pl/102/83/s5/c/1), substituted “1141” for “341” and “1122” for “322”.

1974—[Pub. L. 93–295](/us/pl/93/295) substituted “those specified in [section 322 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s322)” for “equal to 80 per centum of the rates prescribed by [section 322 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s322), adjusted upward or downward to the nearest dollar, counting fifty cents and over as a whole dollar”.
