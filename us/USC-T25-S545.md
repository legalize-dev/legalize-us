# § 545. Omitted

##### § 545. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Mar. 29, 1948, ch. 160, § 3](/us/act/1948-03-29/ch160/s3), [62 Stat. 93](/us/stat/62/93), which related to liability of judgment funds for debts contracted prior to the passage of this section and former [section 544 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s544), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
