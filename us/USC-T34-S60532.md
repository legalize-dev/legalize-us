# § 60532. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s504/a), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5233](/us/stat/132/5233)

##### § 60532. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–391, title V, § 504(a)](/us/pl/115/391/tV/s504/a), Dec. 21, 2018, [132 Stat. 5233](/us/stat/132/5233)

Section, [Pub. L. 110–199, title II, § 212](/us/pl/110/199/tII/s212), Apr. 9, 2008, [122 Stat. 680](/us/stat/122/680); [Pub. L. 113–128, title V, § 512(bb)(1)](/us/pl/113/128/tV/s512/bb/1), July 22, 2014, [128 Stat. 1717](/us/stat/128/1717), related to responsible reintegration of offenders.

Section was formerly classified to [section 17532 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s17532), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
