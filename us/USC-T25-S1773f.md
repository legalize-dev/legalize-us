# § 1773f. Omitted

##### § 1773f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–41, § 8](/us/pl/101/41/s8), June 21, 1989, [103 Stat. 87](/us/stat/103/87), which related to funds for economic development and land acquisition, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
