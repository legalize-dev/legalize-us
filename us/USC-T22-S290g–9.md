# § 290g–9. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–118, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/95/118/tVII/s702), Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1070](/us/stat/91/1070)

##### § 290g–9. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–118, title VII, § 702](/us/pl/95/118/tVII/s702), Oct. 3, 1977, [91 Stat. 1070](/us/stat/91/1070)

Section, [Pub. L. 94–302, title II, § 211](/us/pl/94/302/tII/s211), May 31, 1976, [90 Stat. 595](/us/stat/90/595); H. Res. 5, Jan. 4, 1977, set forth provisions relating to United States participation in financial assistance by the African Development Fund to any country engaging in a consistent pattern of gross violations of internationally recognized human rights. See [section 262d of this title](/us/usc/t22/s262d).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 3, 1977, see [section 1001 of Pub. L. 95–118](/us/pl/95/118/s1001), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 282i of this title](/us/usc/t22/s282i).
