#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

E, title LX, § 5401(10)](/us/pl/114/328/dE/tLX/s5401/10), Dec. 23, 2016, [130 Stat. 2939](/us/stat/130/2939), which related to the offense of housebreaking, was omitted in the general amendment of sections 929 and 929a of this title by [Pub. L. 114–328, div. E, title LX, § 5442](/us/pl/114/328/dE/tLX/s5442), Dec. 23, 2016, [130 Stat. 2954](/us/stat/130/2954). See [section 929(b) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s929/b).
