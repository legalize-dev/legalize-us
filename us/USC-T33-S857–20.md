# § 857–20. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–221, § 10](/us/pl/116/221/s10), Dec. 18, 2020, [134 Stat. 1061](/us/stat/134/1061)

##### § 857–20. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–221, § 10](/us/pl/116/221/s10), Dec. 18, 2020, [134 Stat. 1061](/us/stat/134/1061)

Section, [Pub. L. 107–299, § 9](/us/pl/107/299/s9), Nov. 26, 2002, [116 Stat. 2348](/us/stat/116/2348), provided for coordination between the Under Secretary of Commerce for Oceans and Atmosphere and the Director of the National Science Foundation to jointly submit a report on the oceans and coastal research activities of the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration and of the National Science Foundation.
