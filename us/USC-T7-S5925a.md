#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Study of Nutrient Banking

[Pub. L. 107–171, title VII, § 7411](/us/pl/107/171/tVII/s7411), May 13, 2002, [116 Stat. 462](/us/stat/116/462), provided that the Secretary of Agriculture may conduct study to evaluate nutrient banking for purpose of enhancing health and viability of watersheds in areas with large concentrations of animal producing units, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 113–79, title VII, § 7408(c)](/us/pl/113/79/tVII/s7408/c), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 898](/us/stat/128/898).
