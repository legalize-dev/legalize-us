# § 2413. Saving provisions

##### § 2413. Saving provisions

(a) Regulations All regulations promulgated under this chapter prior to October 2, 1996, shall remain in effect until superseding regulations are promulgated under [section 2405 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s2405).

(b) Permits All permits issued under this chapter shall remain in effect until they expire in accordance with the terms of those permits.

*([Pub. L. 95–541, § 14](/us/pl/95/541/s14), as added [Pub. L. 104–227, title I, § 107](/us/pl/104/227/tI/s107), Oct. 2, 1996, [110 Stat. 3042](/us/stat/110/3042).)*
