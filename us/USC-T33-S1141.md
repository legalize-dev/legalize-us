# § 1141. Definitions

##### § 1141. Definitions

(1) Sea Grant Institution The term “Sea Grant Institution” means a sea grant college or sea grant institute, as those terms are defined in [section 1122 of this title](/us/usc/t33/s1122).

(2) Tribal organization The term “Tribal organization” has the meaning given the term “tribal organization” in [section 5304 of title 25](/us/usc/t25/s5304).

(3) Young fisherman The term “young fisherman” means an individual who—

(A) desires to participate in the commercial fisheries of the United States, including the Great Lakes fisheries;

(B) has worked as a captain, crew member, or deckhand on a commercial fishing vessel for not more than 10 years of cumulative service; or

(C) is a beginning commercial fisherman.

*([Pub. L. 116–289, § 2](/us/pl/116/289/s2), Jan. 5, 2021, [134 Stat. 4886](/us/stat/134/4886).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was enacted as part of the Young Fishermen’s Development Act, and not as part of titles I and II of [Pub. L. 89–454](/us/pl/89/454) which comprise this chapter.
