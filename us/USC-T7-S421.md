#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 3, 1926, ch. 769, § 1](/us/act/1926-07-03/ch769/s1), [44 Stat. 840](/us/stat/44/840), directed Secretary of Agriculture to establish a dairying and livestock experiment station in Mandan, North Dakota, and was omitted from the Code as obsolete.
