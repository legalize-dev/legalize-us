# § 2279. Applicability of other laws

##### § 2279. Applicability of other laws

Sections 2274 to 2278b of this title shall not exclude the applicable provisions of any other laws.

*([Aug. 1, 1946, ch. 724](/us/act/1946-08-01/ch724), title I, § 231, formerly § 229, as added [Aug. 30, 1954, ch. 1073, § 1](/us/act/1954-08-30/ch1073/s1), [68 Stat. 959](/us/stat/68/959); renumbered § 231 and amended [Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 1015](/us/act/1956-08-06/ch1015), §§ 6, 7, [70 Stat. 1070](/us/stat/70/1070); renumbered title I, [Pub.
