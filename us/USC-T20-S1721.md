# § 1721. Separability

##### § 1721. Separability

If any provision of this subchapter or of any amendment made by this subchapter, or the application of any such provision to any person or circumstance, is held invalid, the remainder of the provisions of this subchapter and of the amendments made by this subchapter and the application of such provision to other persons or circumstances shall not be affected thereby.

*([Pub. L. 93–380, title II, § 223](/us/pl/93/380/tII/s223), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 519](/us/stat/88/519).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this part”, meaning part A of title II of [Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 519](/us/stat/88/519), which is classified generally to this subchapter.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective on and after sixtieth day after Aug. 21, 1974, see [section 2(c) of Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380/s2/c), set out as a note under [section 1221–1 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1221–1).
