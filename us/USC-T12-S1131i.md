##### § 1131i. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, § 5](/us/pl/92/181/tV/s5).26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 624](/us/stat/85/624)

Section, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98](/us/act/1933-06-16/ch98), title I, § 5, [48 Stat. 258](/us/stat/48/258); [Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7](/us/act/1934-01-31/ch7), §§ 14, 15(a), [48 Stat. 348](/us/stat/48/348); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741](/us/act/1956-07-26/ch741), title I, § 105(d), [70 Stat. 665](/us/stat/70/665); Oct. 3, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–343, § 2(1)](/us/pl/87/343/s2/1), [75 Stat. 758](/us/stat/75/758), provided for a revolving fund for investment in production credit association and intermediate credit banks. See Revolving Funds note under [section 2152 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s2152).
