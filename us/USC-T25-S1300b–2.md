# § 1300b–2. Omitted

##### § 1300b–2. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–467, § 3](/us/pl/92/467/s3), Oct. 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 781](/us/stat/86/781), which prohibited the Secretary from approving plans for the use of money specified in former [section 1300b–1 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s1300b–1) until at least thirty days after the plans have been submitted by the Secretary to Congressional committees, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
