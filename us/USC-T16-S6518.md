# § 6518. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 6518. Authorization of appropriations

(1) activities authorized by this subchapter; and

(2) other hazardous fuel reduction activities of the Secretary, including making grants to States, local governments, Indian tribes, and other eligible recipients for activities authorized by law.

*([Pub. L. 108–148, title I, § 108](/us/pl/108/148/tI/s108), Dec. 3, 2003, [117 Stat. 1901](/us/stat/117/1901); [Pub. L. 115–334, title VIII, § 8402](/us/pl/115/334/tVIII/s8402), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4841](/us/stat/132/4841).)*

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–334](/us/pl/115/334) substituted “$660,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2019 through 2023” for “$760,000,000 for each fiscal year” in introductory provisions.
