# [§ 4503a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 117–263, div. K, title CXV, § 11509(a)(3)](/us/pl/117/263/dK/tCXV/s11509/a/3), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 4137](/us/stat/136/4137)]

##### [§ 4503a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 117–263, div. K, title CXV, § 11509(a)(3)](/us/pl/117/263/dK/tCXV/s11509/a/3), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 4137](/us/stat/136/4137)]

Section, added and amended [Pub. L. 115–282, title V, § 508(a)](/us/pl/115/282/tV/s508/a), (c), Dec. 4, 2018, [132 Stat. 4272](/us/stat/132/4272), provided for an alternate safety compliance program for certain fishing vessels.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Construction of Repeal

Nothing in repeal by [Pub. L. 117–263](/us/pl/117/263) to be construed to affect or apply to any alternative compliance and safety agreement entered into by the Coast Guard that is in effect on Dec. 23, 2022, see [section 11509(b) of Pub. L. 117–263](/us/pl/117/263/s11509/b), set out as a Construction of 2022 Amendment note under [section 4502 of this title](/us/usc/t46/s4502).
