#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 89–655, § 5](/us/pl/89/655/s5), Oct. 14, 1966, [80 Stat. 905](/us/stat/80/905), which related to development and adoption of a tribal organizational document upon completion of Hoh base roll, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
