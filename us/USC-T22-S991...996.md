##### §§ 991 to 996. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

Section 991, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title VI, § 621, [60 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/60/1014); Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–141, title IV, § 402](/us/pl/94/141/tIV/s402), [89 Stat. 764](/us/stat/89/764), related to promotion by selection of Foreign Service officers.

Section 992, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 622](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s622), [60 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/60/1014), related to eligibility for promotion.

Section 993, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title VI, § 623, [60 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/60/1014); Oct. 18, 1973, [Pub. L. 93–126, § 10](/us/pl/93/126/s10), [87 Stat. 453](/us/stat/87/453), related to recommendations for promotion.

Section 994, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 956, title VI, § 624](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch956/tVI/s624), [60 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/60/1014), related to promotion of Reserve officers.

Section 995, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title VI, § 625, [60 Stat. 1014](/us/stat/60/1014); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 17](/us/pl/86/723/s17), [74 Stat. 834](/us/stat/74/834); Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–141, title IV, § 403](/us/pl/94/141/tIV/s403), [89 Stat. 764](/us/stat/89/764), related to computation, etc., of within-class salary increases.

Section 996, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title VI, § 626](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tVI/s626), as added Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 18](/us/pl/86/723/s18), [74 Stat. 834](/us/stat/74/834), related to effect on promotion of functional and geographical area specialization.

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).
