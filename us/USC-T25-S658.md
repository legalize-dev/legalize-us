# § 658. Omitted

##### § 658. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act May 24, 1950, ch. 196, § 2](/us/act/1950-05-24/ch196/s2), [64 Stat. 190](/us/stat/64/190), which provided for a distribution of $150 from the judgment fund to each enrolled Indian, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
