# § 355. Administrative support services

##### § 355. Administrative support services

The Administrator of General Services shall provide administrative support services for the Commission on a reimbursable basis.

*([Pub. L. 90–206, title II, § 225(e)](/us/pl/90/206/tII/s225/e), Dec. 16, 1967, [81 Stat. 643](/us/stat/81/643).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective Dec. 16, 1967, see [section 220(a)(1) of Pub. L. 90–206](/us/pl/90/206/s220/a/1), set out as a note under [section 3110 of Title 5](/us/usc/t5/s3110), Government Organization and Employees.
