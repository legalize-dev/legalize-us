# §§ 14713 to 14715. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

##### §§ 14713 to 14715. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

Section 14713, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I, § 105](/us/pl/105/303/tI/s105), Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2852](/us/stat/112/2852), related to acquisition of space science data. See [section 50113 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s50113), National and Commercial Space Programs.

Section 14714, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I, § 106](/us/pl/105/303/tI/s106), Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2853](/us/stat/112/2853), related to administration of commercial space centers. See [section 50114 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s50114).

Section 14715, [Pub. L. 105–303, title I, § 107](/us/pl/105/303/tI/s107), Oct. 28, 1998, [112 Stat. 2853](/us/stat/112/2853), related to sources of Earth Science data. Subsecs. (a), (b), (d), and (e) were repealed and reenacted as subsecs. (a), (b), (c) and (d) of [section 50115 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s50115). Subsec (c), which required the Administrator to submit certain study results to Congress within six months after Oct. 28, 1998, was repealed as obsolete.
