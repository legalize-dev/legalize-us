# §§ 5931 to 5939. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 509(a)](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s509/a), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 200](/us/stat/108/200)

##### §§ 5931 to 5939. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 509(a)](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s509/a), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 200](/us/stat/108/200)

Section 5931, [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 501](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s501), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 191](/us/stat/108/191), set forth short title of subchapter as National Skill Standards Act of 1994.

Section 5932, [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 502](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s502), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 191](/us/stat/108/191), stated purpose of subchapter.

Section 5933, [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 503](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s503), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 192](/us/stat/108/192); [Pub. L. 104–134, title I, § 101(d) [title VII, § 703(a)(17)]](/us/pl/104/134/tI/s101/d/tVII/s703/a/17), Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–211](/us/stat/110/1321-211), 1321–254; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 104–140, § 1(a)](/us/pl/104/140/s1/a), May 2, 1996, [110 Stat. 1327](/us/stat/110/1327), established National Skill Standards Board.

Section 5934, [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 504](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s504), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 195](/us/stat/108/195); [Pub. L. 104–134, title I, § 101(d) [title VII, § 703(a)(18)]](/us/pl/104/134/tI/s101/d/tVII/s703/a/18), Apr. 26, 1996, [110 Stat. 1321–211](/us/stat/110/1321-211), 1321–254; renumbered title I, [Pub. L. 104–140, § 1(a)](/us/pl/104/140/s1/a), May 2, 1996, [110 Stat. 1327](/us/stat/110/1327); [Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(f) [title VIII, § 405(d)(17)(A)]](/us/pl/105/277/dA/s101/f/tVIII/s405/d/17/A), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–337](/us/stat/112/2681-337), 2681–422, related to functions of National Skill Standards Board.

Section 5935, [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 505](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s505), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 199](/us/stat/108/199), related to deadline for identifying occupational clusters and promoting development of skill standards for the clusters.

Section 5936, [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 506](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s506), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 199](/us/stat/108/199), related to reports to the President and Congress.

Section 5937, [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 507](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s507), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 199](/us/stat/108/199), authorized appropriations.

Section 5938, [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 508](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s508), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 200](/us/stat/108/200); [Pub. L. 105–277, div. A, § 101(f) [title VIII, § 405(d)(17)(B)]](/us/pl/105/277/dA/s101/f/tVIII/s405/d/17/B), Oct. 21, 1998, [112 Stat. 2681–337](/us/stat/112/2681-337), 2681–422, related to definitions for subchapter.

Section 5939, [Pub. L. 103–227, title V, § 509](/us/pl/103/227/tV/s509), Mar. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 200](/us/stat/108/200), repealed this subchapter effective Sept. 30, 1999.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Sept. 30, 1999, see [section 509(a) of Pub. L. 103–227](/us/pl/103/227/s509/a), which was formerly classified to [section 5939(a) of this title](/us/usc/t20/s5939/a).
