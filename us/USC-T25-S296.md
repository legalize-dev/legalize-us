# § 296. Repealed. [Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 576](/us/act/1929-03-02/ch576), [45 Stat. 1534](/us/stat/45/1534)

##### § 296. Repealed. [Mar. 2, 1929, ch. 576](/us/act/1929-03-02/ch576), [45 Stat. 1534](/us/stat/45/1534)
