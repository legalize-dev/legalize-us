# § 1300g–1. Omitted

##### § 1300g–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–89, title I, § 102](/us/pl/100/89/tI/s102), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 666](/us/stat/101/666), which redesignated the Tiwa Indians of Ysleta, Texas, as the Ysleta del Sur Pueblo, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
