# § 4636. Payments not to be considered as income for revenue purposes or for eligibility for assistance under Social Security Act or other Federal law

##### § 4636. Payments not to be considered as income for revenue purposes or for eligibility for assistance under Social Security Act or other Federal law

No payment received under this subchapter shall be considered as income for the purposes of title 26; or for the purposes of determining the eligibility or the extent of eligibility of any person for assistance under the Social Security Act [[42 U.S.C. 301](/us/usc/t42/s301) et seq.] or any other Federal law (except for any Federal law providing low-income housing assistance).

*([Pub. L. 91–646, title II, § 216](/us/pl/91/646/tII/s216), Jan. 2, 1971, [84 Stat. 1902](/us/stat/84/1902); [Pub. L. 99–514, § 2](/us/pl/99/514/s2), Oct. 22, 1986, [100 Stat. 2095](/us/stat/100/2095); [Pub. L. 100–17, title IV, § 413](/us/pl/100/17/tIV/s413), Apr. 2, 1987, [101 Stat. 255](/us/stat/101/255).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Social Security Act, referred to in text, is [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531), [49 Stat. 620](/us/stat/49/620), which is classified generally to chapter 7 (§ 301 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see [section 1305 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s1305) and Tables.

#### Amendments

1987—[Pub. L. 100–17](/us/pl/100/17) inserted “(except for any Federal law providing low-income housing assistance)” before period at end.

1986—[Pub. L. 99–514](/us/pl/99/514) substituted “Internal Revenue Code of 1986” for “Internal Revenue Code of 1954”, which for purposes of codification was translated as “title 26” thus requiring no change in text.
