#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 2212, added [Pub. L. 100–370, § 1(d)(2)(A)](/us/pl/100/370/s1/d/2/A), July 19, 1988, [102 Stat. 842](/us/stat/102/842), directed Secretary of Defense to maintain within each military department an accounting procedure to aid in identification and control of expenditures for contracted advisory and assistance services, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–355, title II, § 2454(c)(1)](/us/pl/103/355/tII/s2454/c/1), Oct. 13, 1994, [108 Stat. 3326](/us/stat/108/3326).

Another prior section 2212, added [Pub. L. 95–356, title VIII, § 802(a)(1)](/us/pl/95/356/tVIII/s802/a/1), Sept. 8, 1978, [92 Stat. 585](/us/stat/92/585); amended [Pub. L. 97–258, § 3(b)(5)](/us/pl/97/258/s3/b/5), Sept. 18, 1982, [96 Stat. 1063](/us/stat/96/1063), related to transmission of annual military construction authorization request, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 97–214, § 7(1)](/us/pl/97/214/s7/1), July 12, 1982, [96 Stat. 173](/us/stat/96/173), eff. Oct. 1, 1982, applicable to military construction projects, and to construction and acquisition of military family housing authorized before, on, or after such date. See [section 2859 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s2859).
