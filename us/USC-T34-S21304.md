# § 21304. Application

##### § 21304. Application

A State seeking an increase in the amount provided to the State under the covered formula grants shall include in the application of the State for each covered formula grant such information as the Attorney General may reasonably require, including information about the law described in [section 21303 of this title](/us/usc/t34/s21303).

*([Pub. L. 114–22, title IV, § 405](/us/pl/114/22/tIV/s405), May 29, 2015, [129 Stat. 257](/us/stat/129/257).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 14043h–3 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s14043h–3), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
