# § 711a. Omitted

##### § 711a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 95–195, § 3](/us/pl/95/195/s3), Nov. 18, 1977, [91 Stat. 1415](/us/stat/91/1415), which related to Federal recognition of the Siletz Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
