# § 282c. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 282c. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums, but not more than $13,575,000 for the acquisition of lands and interests therein and for the development of the San Juan National Historical Park.11 So in original. Probably should be “San Juan Island National Historical Park.”

*([Pub. L. 89–565, § 4](/us/pl/89/565/s4), Sept. 9, 1966, [80 Stat. 737](/us/stat/80/737); [Pub. A, title I, § 118](/us/pl/111/88/dA/tI/s118), Oct. 30, 2009, [123 Stat. 2929](/us/stat/123/2929).)*
