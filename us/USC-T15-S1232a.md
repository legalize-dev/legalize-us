# § 1232a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–178, title IV, § 401(g)(7)(A)](/us/pl/92/178/tIV/s401/g/7/A), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 534](/us/stat/85/534)

##### § 1232a. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–178, title IV, § 401(g)(7)(A)](/us/pl/92/178/tIV/s401/g/7/A), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 534](/us/stat/85/534)

Section, [Pub. L. 91–614, title III, § 304](/us/pl/91/614/tIII/s304), Dec. 31, 1970, [84 Stat. 1845](/us/stat/84/1845), related to Federal manufacturers excise tax on labels and provided for violations and penalties. It was not a part of the Automobile Information Disclosure Act, which comprises this chapter generally.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 92–178, title IV, § 401(g)(7)(B)](/us/pl/92/178/tIV/s401/g/7/B), Dec. 10, 1971, [85 Stat. 534](/us/stat/85/534), provided that: “Subparagraph (A) [repealing this section] shall apply to acts (or failures to act) after the date of the enactment of this Act [Dec. 10, 1971].”
