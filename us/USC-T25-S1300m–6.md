##### § 1300m–6. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–454, title III, § 308](/us/pl/103/454/tIII/s308), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4795](/us/stat/108/4795), which related to adoption of a tribal constitution and bylaws and election of tribal officials, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
