# § 983f. Omitted

##### § 983f. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 101–484, § 8](/us/pl/101/484/s8), Oct. 31, 1990, [104 Stat. 1169](/us/stat/104/1169), which related to adoption of a tribal constitution and election of tribal officials, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
