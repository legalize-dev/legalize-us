# § 2353. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title XXV, § 2526](/us/pl/97/35/tXXV/s2526), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 893](/us/stat/95/893)

##### § 2353. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title XXV, § 2526](/us/pl/97/35/tXXV/s2526), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 893](/us/stat/95/893)

Section, [Pub. L. 93–618, title II, § 263](/us/pl/93/618/tII/s263), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2034](/us/stat/88/2034), contained transitional provisions for certain events occurring prior to the effective date of this part.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Aug. 13, 1981, except as otherwise provided with respect to applications for adjustment assistance, see [section 2529 of Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35/s2529), set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under [section 2343 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s2343).
