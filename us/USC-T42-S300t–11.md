# § 300t–11. Grants and assistance for establishment of program

##### § 300t–11. Grants and assistance for establishment of program

(1) grants and technical assistance may be provided to hospitals in operation on October 4, 1979, (A) for the discontinuance of unneeded hospital services, and (B) for the conversion of unneeded hospital services to other health services needed by the community; and

(2) grants may be provided to State Agencies designated under section 300m(b)(3) 11 See References in Text note below. of this title for reducing excesses in resources and facilities of hospitals.

*([July 1, 1944, ch. 373](/us/act/1944-07-01/ch373), title XVI, § 1641, as added [Pub. L. 96–79, title III, § 301(a)](/us/pl/96/79/tIII/s301/a), Oct. 4, 1979, [93 Stat. 636](/us/stat/93/636).)*

#### References in Text

L. 99–660, title VII, § 701(a)](/us/pl/99/660/tVII/s701/a), Nov. 14, 1986, [100 Stat. 3799](/us/stat/100/3799). [Pub. L. 101–354, § 2](/us/pl/101/354/s2), Aug. 10, 1990, [104 Stat. 410](/us/stat/104/410), enacted section 1503 of act July 1, 1944, which is classified to [section 300m of this title](/us/usc/t42/s300m).
