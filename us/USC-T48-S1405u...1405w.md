# §§ 1405u to 1405w. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–357, title III, § 307](/us/pl/97/357/tIII/s307), Oct. 19, 1982, [96 Stat. 1709](/us/stat/96/1709)

##### §§ 1405u to 1405w. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–357, title III, § 307](/us/pl/97/357/tIII/s307), Oct. 19, 1982, [96 Stat. 1709](/us/stat/96/1709)

Section 1405u, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 22](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s22), [49 Stat. 1812](/us/stat/49/1812), related to appointment of an Administrator for Saint Croix to act for Governor.

Section 1405v, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 23](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s23), [49 Stat. 1813](/us/stat/49/1813), related to appointment of such other executive and administrative officers as may be required in discretion of Secretary of the Interior.

Section 1405w, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 24](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s24), [49 Stat. 1813](/us/stat/49/1813), related to appointment of all salaried officers and employees of municipal governments by Governor with advice and consent of municipal council having jurisdiction.
