# § 7218. Duties: warrant officers; limitations

##### § 7218. Duties: warrant officers; limitations

Under regulations prescribed by the President, a warrant officer may be assigned to perform duties that necessarily include those normally performed by a commissioned officer.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 203](/us/stat/70A/203), § 3548; renumbered § 7218, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(b)(6)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/b/6), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1838](/us/stat/132/1838).)*

10:593 (1st sentence, less provisos) is omitted as superseded by [section 3012(e) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s3012/e). 10:593 (last proviso) is omitted as covered by [section 936(a)(4) of this title](/us/usc/t10/s936/a/4) (article 136(a)(4) of the Uniform Code of Military Justice). The words “may be assigned” are substituted for the words “shall be vested with power to”.
