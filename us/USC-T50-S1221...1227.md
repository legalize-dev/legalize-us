# §§ 1221 to 1227. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569](/us/stat/72/1569)

##### §§ 1221 to 1227. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1569](/us/stat/72/1569)

Section 1221, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 301](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s301), [68 Stat. 1153](/us/stat/68/1153), provided that sections 1221 to 1281 should apply only to Reserve officers of the Army.

Section 1222, acts [Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257), title III, § 302, [68 Stat. 1153](/us/stat/68/1153); [June 30, 1955, ch. 247, § 6](/us/act/1955-06-30/ch247/s6), [69 Stat. 221](/us/stat/69/221), defined terms used in sections 1221 to 1281 of this title. See [section 12007 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s12007), Armed Forces.

Section 1223, acts [Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257), title III, § 303, [68 Stat. 1154](/us/stat/68/1154); [June 30, 1955, ch. 247, § 7](/us/act/1955-06-30/ch247/s7), [69 Stat. 221](/us/stat/69/221), provided for promotion procedures. See sections 14101 et seq. and 14301 et seq. of Title 10.

Section 1224, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 304](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s304), [68 Stat. 1154](/us/stat/68/1154), related to maximum grades for female officers.

Section 1225, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 305](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s305), [68 Stat. 1154](/us/stat/68/1154), related to constructive service credit. See section 12201 et seq. of Title 10, Armed Forces.

Section 1226, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 306](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s306), [68 Stat. 1155](/us/stat/68/1155), related to minimum service in grade. See [section 14303 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s14303).

Section 1227, [act Sept. 3, 1954, ch. 1257, title III, § 307](/us/act/1954-09-03/ch1257/tIII/s307), [68 Stat. 1155](/us/stat/68/1155), prescribed the authorized number of officers and for distribution in grade. See sections 12003 to 12005, 12007, and 12646 of Title 10.
