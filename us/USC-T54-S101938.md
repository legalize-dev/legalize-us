# § 101938. Sunset

##### § 101938. Sunset

The authority given to the Secretary under this subchapter shall expire 9 years after the date of the enactment of this subchapter.

*(Added [Pub. L. 114–289, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/114/289/tVII/s701), Dec. 16, 2016, [130 Stat. 1494](/us/stat/130/1494); amended [Pub. L. 117–328, div. G, title I, § 123](/us/pl/117/328/dG/tI/s123), Dec. 29, 2022, [136 Stat. 4789](/us/stat/136/4789).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The date of the enactment of this subchapter, referred to in text, is the date of enactment of [Pub. L. 114–289](/us/pl/114/289), which was approved Dec. 16, 2016.

#### Amendments

2022—[Pub. L. 117–328](/us/pl/117/328) substituted “9” for “7”.
