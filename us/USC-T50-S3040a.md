# § 3040a. Prohibition on collection and maintenance of information of United States persons based on first amendment-protected activities

##### § 3040a. Prohibition on collection and maintenance of information of United States persons based on first amendment-protected activities

No element of the intelligence community may collect or maintain information concerning a United States person (as defined in [section 3039 of this title](/us/usc/t50/s3039)) solely for the purpose of monitoring an activity protected by the first amendment to the Constitution of the United States.

*([July 26, 1947, ch. 343](/us/act/1947-07-26/ch343), title I, § 105C, as added [Pub. L. 117–103, div. X, title III, § 303](/us/pl/117/103/dX/tIII/s303), Mar. 15, 2022, [136 Stat. 965](/us/stat/136/965).)*
