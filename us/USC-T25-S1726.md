# § 1726. Omitted

##### § 1726. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–420, § 7](/us/pl/96/420/s7), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1795](/us/stat/94/1795), which related to the tribal organization of the Passamaquoddy Tribe, the Penobscot Nation, and the Houlton Band of Maliseet Indians, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
