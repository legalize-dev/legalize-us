# § 430uu–4. Authorization of appropriation

##### § 430uu–4. Authorization of appropriation

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums not exceeding $42,500 as are necessary for the acquisition of lands and interests in land pursuant to sections 430uu to 430uu–4 of this title.

*([Pub. L. 88–24, § 5](/us/pl/88/24/s5), May 17, 1963, [77 Stat. 19](/us/stat/77/19); [Pub. L. 92–272, title I, § 101(2)](/us/pl/92/272/tI/s101/2), Apr. 11, 1972, [86 Stat. 120](/us/stat/86/120).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1972—[Pub. L. 92–272](/us/pl/92/272) substituted “$42,500” for “$20,000”.
