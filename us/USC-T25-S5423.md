# § 5423. Definitions

##### § 5423. Definitions

(1) the term “Bureau funded school” has the meaning given that term in title XI of the Education Amendments of 1978 [[25 U.S.C. 2000](/us/usc/t25/s2000) et seq.];

(2) the term “Foundation” means the Foundation established by the Secretary pursuant to [section 5421 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s5421); and

(3) the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

The Education Amendments of 1978, referred to in par. (1), is [Pub. L. 95–561](/us/pl/95/561), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2143](/us/stat/92/2143). Title XI of the Act is classified principally to chapter 22 (§ 2000 et seq.) of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 6301 of Title 20](/us/usc/t20/s6301), Education, and Tables.

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 458ddd–2 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s458ddd–2) prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
