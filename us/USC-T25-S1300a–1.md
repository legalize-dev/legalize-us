# § 1300a–1. Omitted

##### § 1300a–1. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–461, § 3](/us/pl/92/461/s3), Oct. 6, 1972, [86 Stat. 769](/us/stat/86/769), which related to preparation of membership rolls by the Yavapai Apache Indian Community of the Camp Verde Reservation, the Fort McDowell Mohave-Apache Community, and the Yavapai-Prescott Community, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
