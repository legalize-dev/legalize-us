# § 98i. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1570](/us/stat/72/1570)

##### § 98i. Repealed. [Pub. L. 85–861, § 36A](/us/pl/85/861/s36A), Sept. 2, 1958, [72 Stat. 1570](/us/stat/72/1570)

Section, [act Aug. 3, 1956, ch. 939, title IV, § 416](/us/act/1956-08-03/ch939/tIV/s416), [70 Stat. 1018](/us/stat/70/1018), related to contracts for storage, handling, and distribution of liquid fuels. See [section 2922 of Title 10](/us/usc/t10/s2922), Armed Forces.

Section was not enacted as part of the Strategic and Critical Materials Stock Piling Act which comprises this subchapter.
