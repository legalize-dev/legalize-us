# [§ 522. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–834, § 17(b)(2)](/us/pl/87/834/s17/b/2), Oct. 16, 1962, [76 Stat. 1051](/us/stat/76/1051)]

##### [§ 522. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–834, § 17(b)(2)](/us/pl/87/834/s17/b/2), Oct. 16, 1962, [76 Stat. 1051](/us/stat/76/1051)]

Section, [act Aug. 16, 1954, ch. 736](/us/act/1954-08-16/ch736), [68A Stat. 177](/us/stat/68A/177), related to tax on farmers’ cooperatives.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable, except as otherwise provided, to taxable years of organizations described in [section 1381(a) of this title](/us/usc/t26/s1381/a) beginning after Dec. 31, 1962, see [section 17(c) of Pub. L. 87–834](/us/pl/87/834/s17/c), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 1381 of this title](/us/usc/t26/s1381).
