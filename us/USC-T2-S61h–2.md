# § 61h–2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/A), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2775](/us/stat/133/2775)

##### § 61h–2. Repealed. [Pub. L. 116–94, div. E, title II, § 212(a)(3)(A)](/us/pl/116/94/dE/tII/s212/a/3/A), Dec. 20, 2019, [133 Stat. 2775](/us/stat/133/2775)

Section, [Pub. L. 94–59, title I, § 105](/us/pl/94/59/tI/s105), July 25, 1975, [89 Stat. 275](/us/stat/89/275), set a maximum annual rate of compensation of $36,500 for legislative assistants in Offices of Senate Majority and Minority Leaders. Positions established by Legislative Branch Appropriation Act, 1970, for Offices of Senate Majority and Minority Leaders, which Act, [Pub. L. 91–145](/us/pl/91/145), Dec. 12, 1969, [83 Stat. 339](/us/stat/83/339), classified to former [section 61h–1 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s61h–1), authorized the respective leaders to appoint a legislative assistant, were abolished, see [Pub. L. 95–26, title I](/us/pl/95/26/tI), May 4, 1977, [91 Stat. 80](/us/stat/91/80), set out as a note under former [section 61h–1 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s61h–1). See, also, [section 6131 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6131).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on the later of the first day of the first applicable pay period beginning on or after Jan. 1, 2020, or the first day of the first applicable pay period beginning on or after Dec. 20, 2019, see [section 212(c) of Pub. L. 116–94](/us/pl/116/94/s212/c), set out as a note under [section 282b of this title](/us/usc/t2/s282b).
