# § 7382b. Definitions

##### § 7382b. Definitions

(1) the term “elementary school” has the meaning provided by [section 7801 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s7801);

(2) the term “local educational agency” has the meaning provided by [section 7801 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s7801);

(3) the term “secondary school” has the meaning provided by [section 7801 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s7801);

(4) the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of Energy.
