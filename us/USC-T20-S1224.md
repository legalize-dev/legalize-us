# § 1224. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–380, title V, § 506(a)(1)(B)](/us/pl/93/380/tV/s506/a/1/B), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 562](/us/stat/88/562)

##### § 1224. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–380, title V, § 506(a)(1)(B)](/us/pl/93/380/tV/s506/a/1/B), Aug. 21, 1974, [88 Stat. 562](/us/stat/88/562)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–247, title IV, § 413](/us/pl/90/247/tIV/s413), formerly § 404, Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 814](/us/stat/81/814); amended [Pub. L. 91–230, title IV, § 401(a)(5)](/us/pl/91/230/tIV/s401/a/5), (6), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 165](/us/stat/84/165); renumbered § 413, [Pub. L. 92–318, title III, § 301(a)(1)](/us/pl/92/318/tIII/s301/a/1), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 326](/us/stat/86/326), provided for annual evaluation reports to Congressional committees, penultimate fiscal year reports, and contingent extension of expiring appropriation authority.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Aug. 21, 1974, see [section 506(b) of Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380/s506/b), set out as an Effective Date of 1974 Amendment note under [section 1225 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1225).
