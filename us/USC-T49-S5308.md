##### [§ 5308. Repealed. [Pub. L. 112–141, div. B, § 20002(a)](/us/pl/112/141/dB/s20002/a), July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 622](/us/stat/126/622)]

Section, [Pub. L. 103–272, § 1(d)](/us/pl/103/272/s1/d), July 5, 1994, [108 Stat. 800](/us/stat/108/800); [Pub. L. 105–178, title III, § 3008(a)](/us/pl/105/178/tIII/s3008/a), (c), June 9, 1998, [112 Stat. 348](/us/stat/112/348); [Pub. #### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 2012, see [section 3(a) of Pub. L. 112–141](/us/pl/112/141/s3/a), set out as an Effective and Termination Dates of 2012 Amendment note under [section 101 of Title 23](/us/usc/t23/s101), Highways.

#### National Fuel Cell Bus Technology Development Program

[Pub. L. 109–59, title III, § 3045](/us/pl/109/59/tIII/s3045), Aug. 10, 2005, [119 Stat. 1705](/us/stat/119/1705), which provided for the establishment of a national fuel cell bus technology development program, was repealed by [Pub. L. 112–141, div. B, § 20002(c)(4)](/us/pl/112/141/dB/s20002/c/4), July 6, 2012, [126 Stat. 622](/us/stat/126/622).
