# § 1697. Service in multiparty, multiforum actions

##### § 1697. Service in multiparty, multiforum actions

When the jurisdiction of the district court is based in whole or in part upon [section 1369 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s1369), process, other than subpoenas, may be served at any place within the United States, or anywhere outside the United States if otherwise permitted by law.

*(Added [Pub. L. 107–273, div. C, title I, § 11020(b)(4)(A)(i)](/us/pl/107/273/dC/tI/s11020/b/4/A/i), Nov. 2, 2002, [116 Stat. 1828](/us/stat/116/1828).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section applicable to a civil action if the accident giving rise to the cause of action occurred on or after the 90th day after Nov. 2, 2002, see [section 11020(c) of Pub. L. 107–273](/us/pl/107/273/s11020/c), set out as a note under [section 1369 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s1369).
