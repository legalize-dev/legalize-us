# § 883a. Omitted

##### § 883a. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 92–586, § 2](/us/pl/92/586/s2), Oct. 27, 1972, [86 Stat. 1295](/us/stat/86/1295), which related to payments to members of the Osage Tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
