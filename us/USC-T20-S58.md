# § 58. Omitted

##### § 58. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Mar. 3, 1899, ch. 424, § 1](/us/act/1899-03-03/ch424/s1), [30 Stat. 1085](/us/stat/30/1085), which required that the salaries of officers and employees paid from appropriations under the Smithsonian Institution be reported to Congress annually, terminated, effective May 15, 2000, pursuant to [section 3003 of Pub. L. 104–66](/us/pl/104/66/s3003), as amended, set out as a note under [section 1113 of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1113), Money and Finance. See, also, page 192 of House Document No. 103–7.
