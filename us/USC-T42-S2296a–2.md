# § 2296a–2. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 2296a–2. Authorization of appropriations

(a) In general There is authorized to be appropriated $715,000,000 to carry out this part. The aggregate amount authorized in the preceding sentence shall be increased annually as provided in [section 2296a of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2296a), based upon an inflation index to be determined by the Secretary.

(b) Source Funds described in subsection (a) shall be provided from the Fund established under [section 2297g of this title](/us/usc/t42/s2297g).
