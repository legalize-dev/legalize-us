# § 564k. Omitted

##### § 564k. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 12](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s12), [68 Stat. 721](/us/stat/68/721), which transferred to the tribe for collection all loans made from the reimbursable loan fund established by former [section 531 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s531), was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
