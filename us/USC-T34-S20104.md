# § 20104. Child abuse prevention and treatment grants

##### § 20104. Child abuse prevention and treatment grants

Amounts made available by [section 20101(d)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t34/s20101/d/2) for the purposes of this section shall be obligated and expended by the Secretary of Health and Human Services for grants under section 5106c 11 See References in Text note below. of title 42. Any portion of an amount which is not obligated by the Secretary by the end of the fiscal year in which funds are made available for allocation, shall be reallocated for award under [section 20103(a) of this title](/us/usc/t34/s20103/a), except that with respect to funds deposited during fiscal year 1986 and made available for obligation during fiscal year 1987, any unobligated portion of such amount shall remain available for obligation until September 30, 1988.

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 5106c of title 42](/us/usc/t42/s5106c), referred to in text, was in the original “section 109 of the Child Abuse Prevention and Treatment Act”, meaning [section 109 of Pub. L. 93–247](/us/pl/93/247/s109), and was translated as reading section 107 of that act to reflect the probable intent of Congress and the renumbering of section 109 as section 107 by [section 113(a)(1)(B) of Pub. L. 104–235](/us/pl/104/235/s113/a/1/B), title I, Oct. 3, 1996, [110 Stat. 3079](/us/stat/110/3079).

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 10603a of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s10603a), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section. Some section numbers or references in amendment notes below reflect the classification of such sections or references prior to editorial reclassification.

#### Amendments

1996—[Pub. L. 104–235](/us/pl/104/235) substituted “[section 10601(d)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10601/d/2)” for “section 10601(d)(2)(D) and (d)(3) of this title.” and “section 5106c” for “section 5103(d)”.

1993—[Pub. L. 103–121](/us/pl/103/121) substituted “section 10601(d)(2)(D) and (d)(3) of this title.” for “[section 10601(d)(2) of this title](/us/usc/t34/s10601/d/2)”.
