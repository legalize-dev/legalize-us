# § 55119. Yukon River

##### § 55119. Yukon River

[Section 55102 of this title](/us/usc/t46/s55102) does not apply to the transportation of merchandise on the Yukon River until the Alaska Railroad is completed and the Secretary of Transportation finds that proper facilities will be available for transportation by citizens of the United States to properly handle the traffic.

*([Pub. L. 109–304, § 8(c)](/us/pl/109/304/s8/c), Oct. 6, 2006, [120 Stat. 1640](/us/stat/120/1640).)*
