# [§§ 612 to 617. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–283, title II, § 201(a)](/us/pl/94/283/tII/s201/a), May 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 496](/us/stat/90/496)]

##### [§§ 612 to 617. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–283, title II, § 201(a)](/us/pl/94/283/tII/s201/a), May 11, 1976, [90 Stat. 496](/us/stat/90/496)]

Section 612, acts [June 25, 1948, ch. 645](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch645), [62 Stat. 724](/us/stat/62/724); [Aug. 25, 1950, ch. 784, § 2](/us/act/1950-08-25/ch784/s2), [64 Stat. 475](/us/stat/64/475); Aug. 12, 1970, [Pub. L. 91–375, § 6(j)(7)](/us/pl/91/375/s6/j/7), [84 Stat. 777](/us/stat/84/777), regulated publication and distribution of political statements. See [section 30120 of Title 52](/us/usc/t52/s30120), Voting and Elections.

Section 613, added [Pub. L. 89–486, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/486/s8/a), July 4, 1966, [80 Stat. 248](/us/stat/80/248); amended [Pub. L. 93–443, title I, § 101(d)(1)](/us/pl/93/443/tI/s101/d/1)–(3), (4)(A), (e)(3), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1267](/us/stat/88/1267), prohibited campaign contributions by foreign nationals. See [section 30121 of Title 52](/us/usc/t52/s30121), Voting and Elections.

Section 614, added [Pub. L. 93–443, title I, § 101(f)(1)](/us/pl/93/443/tI/s101/f/1), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1268](/us/stat/88/1268), prohibited making of campaign contributions in the name of another. See [section 30122 of Title 52](/us/usc/t52/s30122), Voting and Elections.

Section 615, added [Pub. L. 93–443, title I, § 101(f)(1)](/us/pl/93/443/tI/s101/f/1), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1268](/us/stat/88/1268), placed limitations on contributions of currency. See [section 30123 of Title 52](/us/usc/t52/s30123), Voting and Elections.

Section 616, added [Pub. L. 93–443, title I, § 101(f)(1)](/us/pl/93/443/tI/s101/f/1), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1268](/us/stat/88/1268), prohibited acceptance of excessive honorariums.

Section 617, added [Pub. L. 93–443, title I, § 101(f)(1)](/us/pl/93/443/tI/s101/f/1), Oct. 15, 1974, [88 Stat. 1268](/us/stat/88/1268), prohibited fraudulent misrepresentation of campaign authority. See [section 30124 of Title 52](/us/usc/t52/s30124), Voting and Elections.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Savings Provision

Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–283](/us/pl/94/283) not to release or extinguish any penalty, forfeiture, or liability incurred under such sections, with each section to be treated as remaining in force for the purpose of sustaining any proper action or prosecution for the enforcement of any penalty, forfeiture, or liability, see [section 114 of Pub. L. 94–283](/us/pl/94/283/s114), set out as a note under [section 441 of Title 2](/us/usc/t2/s441), The Congress.
