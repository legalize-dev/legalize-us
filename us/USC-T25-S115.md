# § 115. Payment of annuities in goods

##### § 115. Payment of annuities in goods

The President may, at the request of any Indian tribe, to which an annuity is payable in money, cause the same to be paid in goods, purchased as provided in [section 91 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s91).

*(R.S. § 2082.)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

[Section 91 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s91), referred to in text, was omitted from the Code.

#### Codification

R.S. § 2082 derived from [act June 30, 1834, ch. 162, § 12](/us/act/1834-06-30/ch162/s12), [4 Stat. 737](/us/stat/4/737).
