# § 1816. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–603, title I, § 101(b)(1)(C)](/us/pl/99/603/tI/s101/b/1/C), Nov. 6, 1986, [100 Stat. 3372](/us/stat/100/3372)

##### § 1816. Repealed. [Pub. L. 99–603, title I, § 101(b)(1)(C)](/us/pl/99/603/tI/s101/b/1/C), Nov. 6, 1986, [100 Stat. 3372](/us/stat/100/3372)

Section, [Pub. L. 97–470, title I, § 106](/us/pl/97/470/tI/s106), Jan. 14, 1983, [96 Stat. 2589](/us/stat/96/2589), prohibited employment of illegal aliens.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal applicable to employment, recruitment, referral, or utilization of services of an individual occurring on or after first day of seventh month beginning after Nov. 6, 1986, see [section 101(b)(2) of Pub. L. 99–603](/us/pl/99/603/s101/b/2), as amended, set out as an Effective Date of 1986 Amendment note under [section 1802 of this title](/us/usc/t29/s1802).
