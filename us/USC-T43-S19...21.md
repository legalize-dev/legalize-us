# §§ 19 to 21. Repealed. [Dec. 16, 1930, ch. 14, § 1](/us/act/1930-12-16/ch14/s1), [46 Stat. 1029](/us/stat/46/1029)

##### §§ 19 to 21. Repealed. [Dec. 16, 1930, ch. 14, § 1](/us/act/1930-12-16/ch14/s1), [46 Stat. 1029](/us/stat/46/1029)

Section 19, R.S. § 2469, related to certified copies of records of the General Land Office.

Section 20, R.S. § 2470, related to exemplification of the records of the General Land Office as evidence.

Section 21, R.S. § 461; acts [Apr. 2, 1888, ch. 54](/us/act/1888-04-02/ch54), [25 Stat. 76](/us/stat/25/76); [Oct. 12, 1888, ch. 1098](/us/act/1888-10-12/ch1098), [25 Stat. 557](/us/stat/25/557); [May 29, 1908, ch. 220, § 15](/us/act/1908-05-29/ch220/s15), [35 Stat. 469](/us/stat/35/469); [June 5, 1920, ch. 235, § 1](/us/act/1920-06-05/ch235/s1), [41 Stat. 908](/us/stat/41/908), related to fees for exemplifications.

For Department of the Interior record provisions and Government records and papers, see section 1460 et seq. of this title, and [section 1733 of Title 28](/us/usc/t28/s1733), Judiciary and Judicial Procedure.
