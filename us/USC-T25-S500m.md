#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Sept. 1, 1937, ch. 897, § 14](/us/act/1937-09-01/ch897/s14), [50 Stat. 902](/us/stat/50/902), which related to use of public lands, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
