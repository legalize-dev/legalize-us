# §§ 1405z to 1406e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–357, title III, § 307](/us/pl/97/357/tIII/s307), Oct. 19, 1982, [96 Stat. 1709](/us/stat/96/1709)

##### §§ 1405z to 1406e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–357, title III, § 307](/us/pl/97/357/tIII/s307), Oct. 19, 1982, [96 Stat. 1709](/us/stat/96/1709)

Section 1405z, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 27](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s27), [49 Stat. 1813](/us/stat/49/1813), related to two divisions of District Court of Virgin Islands, terms of court, rules of practice, and process.

Section 1406, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 28](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s28), [49 Stat. 1814](/us/stat/49/1814), related to jurisdiction of district court generally.

Section 1406a, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 29](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s29), [49 Stat. 1814](/us/stat/49/1814), related to jurisdiction of district court over crimes committed on the high seas.

Section 1406b, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 30](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s30), [49 Stat. 1814](/us/stat/49/1814), related to appeals from District Court of Virgin Islands.

Section 1406c, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 31](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s31), [49 Stat. 1814](/us/stat/49/1814), related to jury trials in criminal cases.

Section 1406d, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 32](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s32), [49 Stat. 1814](/us/stat/49/1814), related to jurisdiction of inferior courts.

Section 1406e, [act June 22, 1936, ch. 699, § 33](/us/act/1936-06-22/ch699/s33), [49 Stat. 1815](/us/stat/49/1815), related to appeals from inferior courts to district court.
