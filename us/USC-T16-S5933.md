# § 5933. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

##### § 5933. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–287, § 7](/us/pl/113/287/s7), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3272](/us/stat/128/3272)

Section, [Pub. L. 105–391, title II, § 203](/us/pl/105/391/tII/s203), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3500](/us/stat/112/3500), related to cooperative agreements with colleges and universities. Subsec. (a) was repealed and restated in [section 100703 of Title 54](/us/usc/t54/s100703), National Park Service and Related Programs. Subsec. (b), requiring a report within one year of Nov. 13, 1998, was repealed as obsolete.
