# § 506. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–507, § 1](/us/pl/96/507/s1), Dec. 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 2747](/us/stat/94/2747)

##### § 506. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–507, § 1](/us/pl/96/507/s1), Dec. 8, 1980, [94 Stat. 2747](/us/stat/94/2747)

Section, [act June 19, 1934, ch. 652, title V, § 506](/us/act/1934-06-19/ch652/tV/s506), as added [Apr. 16, 1946, ch. 138](/us/act/1946-04-16/ch138), [60 Stat. 89](/us/stat/60/89), prohibited certain coercive practices affecting broadcasting and provided penalties for violations.
