# § 677aa. Omitted

##### § 677aa. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 27, 1954, ch. 1009, § 28](/us/act/1954-08-27/ch1009/s28), [68 Stat. 878](/us/stat/68/878), which authorized the Secretary of the Interior to proceed in any manner in any action upon non-agreement between mixed-blood and full-blood groups, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
