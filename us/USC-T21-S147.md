# § 147. Repeal of inconsistent laws

##### § 147. Repeal of inconsistent laws

Any laws or parts of laws inconsistent with this subchapter are repealed.

*([Feb. 15, 1927, ch. 155, § 7](/us/act/1927-02-15/ch155/s7), [44 Stat. 1103](/us/stat/44/1103).)*
