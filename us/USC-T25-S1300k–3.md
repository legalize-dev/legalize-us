# § 1300k–3. Omitted

##### § 1300k–3. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–324, § 5](/us/pl/103/324/s5), Sept. 21, 1994, [108 Stat. 2158](/us/stat/108/2158); [Pub. L. 104–109, § 2(a)](/us/pl/104/109/s2/a), Feb. 12, 1996, [110 Stat. 763](/us/stat/110/763), which reaffirmed all rights and privileges of the bands and members thereof which might have been abrogated or diminished before Sept. 21, 1994, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
