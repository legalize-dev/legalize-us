# § 640d–30. Omitted

##### § 640d–30. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 93–531, § 32](/us/pl/93/531/s32), as added [Pub. L. 100–666, § 7](/us/pl/100/666/s7), Nov. 16, 1988, [102 Stat. 3932](/us/stat/102/3932); amended [Pub. L. 101–121, title I, § 120](/us/pl/101/121/tI/s120), Oct. 23, 1989, [103 Stat. 722](/us/stat/103/722), which related to the Navajo Rehabilitation Trust Fund, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

Another [section 32 of Pub. L. 93–531](/us/pl/93/531/s32) was enacted by [Pub. L. 100–696, title IV, § 407](/us/pl/100/696/tIV/s407), Nov. 18, 1988, [102 Stat. 4593](/us/stat/102/4593), and was classified to [section 640d–31 of this title](/us/usc/t25/s640d–31) prior to omission from the Code.
