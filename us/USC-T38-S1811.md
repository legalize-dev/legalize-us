# § 1811. Definitions

##### § 1811. Definitions

(1) The term “eligible child” means an individual who—

(A) is the child (as defined in [section 1831(1) of this title](/us/usc/t38/s1831/1)) of a woman Vietnam veteran; and

(B) was born with one or more covered birth defects.

(2) The term “covered birth defect” means a birth defect identified by the Secretary under [section 1812 of this title](/us/usc/t38/s1812).

*(Added [Pub. L. 106–419, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/106/419/tIV/s401/a), Nov. 1, 2000, [114 Stat. 1857](/us/stat/114/1857); amended [Pub. L. 108–183, title I, § 102(d)(1)](/us/pl/108/183/tI/s102/d/1), Dec. 16, 2003, [117 Stat. 2654](/us/stat/117/2654).)*
