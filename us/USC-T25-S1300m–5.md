# § 1300m–5. Omitted

##### § 1300m–5. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–454, title III, § 307](/us/pl/103/454/tIII/s307), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4795](/us/stat/108/4795), which provided for governance of the tribe by an Interim Council until adoption of a new tribal constitution and bylaws, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
