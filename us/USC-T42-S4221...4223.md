# §§ 4221 to 4223. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

##### §§ 4221 to 4223. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–258, § 5(b)](/us/pl/97/258/s5/b), Sept. 13, 1982, [96 Stat. 1068](/us/stat/96/1068)

Section 4221, [Pub. L. 90–577, title III, § 301](/us/pl/90/577/tIII/s301), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1102](/us/stat/82/1102), set out the statement of purpose for the provision of special or technical services to State and local units of government by Federal departments and agencies.

Section 4222, [Pub. L. 90–577, title III, § 302](/us/pl/90/577/tIII/s302), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1102](/us/stat/82/1102); 1970 Reorg. Plan No. 2, § 102, eff. July 1, 1970, 35 F.R. 7959, [84 Stat. 2085](/us/stat/84/2085), authorized Federal departments and agencies to provide specialized or technical services to States or their political subdivisions. See section 6505(a), (b) of Title 31, Money and Finance.

Section 4223, [Pub. L. 90–577, title III, § 303](/us/pl/90/577/tIII/s303), Oct. 16, 1968, [82 Stat. 1102](/us/stat/82/1102), related to reimbursement of appropriations to Federal departments and agencies. See [section 6505(c) of Title 31](/us/usc/t31/s6505/c).
