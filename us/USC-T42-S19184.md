# § 19184. Definitions

##### § 19184. Definitions

(1) Director The term “Director” means the Director of the National Science Foundation.

(2) HBCU The term “HBCU” has the meaning given the term “part B institution” in [section 1061 of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s1061).

(3) Minority serving institution The term “minority serving institution” or “MSI” means Hispanic-Serving Institutions as defined in section 1101a) of title 20; Alaska Native Serving Institutions and Native Hawaiian-Serving Institutions as defined in [section 1059d of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s1059d); and Predominantly Black Institutions, Asian American and Native American Pacific Islander-Serving Institutions, and Native American-Serving Nontribal Institutions as defined in [section 1067q of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s1067q).

(4) TCU The term “TCU” has the meaning given the term “Tribal College or University” in [section 1059c of title 20](/us/usc/t20/s1059c).
