# § 426a. Qualifications of members of commission

##### § 426a. Qualifications of members of commission

In appointing the members of the commission created by [section 426 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s426) the Secretary of the Interior shall, as far as practicable, select persons familiar with the terrain of the battlefield of Stones River, Tennessee, and the historical events associated therewith.

*([Mar. 3, 1927, ch. 374, § 2](/us/act/1927-03-03/ch374/s2), [44 Stat. 1399](/us/stat/44/1399); Ex. Ord. No. 6166, § 2, June 10, 1933; Ex. Ord. No. 6228, § 1, July 28, 1933.)*
