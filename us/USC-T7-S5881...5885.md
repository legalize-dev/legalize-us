# §§ 5881 to 5885. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–127, title VIII, § 862(a)](/us/pl/104/127/tVIII/s862/a), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1174](/us/stat/110/1174)

##### §§ 5881 to 5885. Repealed. [Pub. L. 104–127, title VIII, § 862(a)](/us/pl/104/127/tVIII/s862/a), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 1174](/us/stat/110/1174)

Section 5881, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1650](/us/pl/101/624/tXVI/s1650), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3754](/us/stat/104/3754), related to plant and animal pest and disease control program.

Section 5882, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1651](/us/pl/101/624/tXVI/s1651), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3754](/us/stat/104/3754), related to pest and disease control data base and pesticide resistance monitoring.

Section 5883, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1652](/us/pl/101/624/tXVI/s1652), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3755](/us/stat/104/3755); [Pub. L. 102–237, title IV, § 407(8)](/us/pl/102/237/tIV/s407/8), Dec. 13, 1991, [105 Stat. 1865](/us/stat/105/1865), related to research on control and eradication of exotic pests.

Section 5884, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1653](/us/pl/101/624/tXVI/s1653), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3755](/us/stat/104/3755), provided for study of biology and behavior of chinch bugs.

Section 5885, [Pub. L. 101–624, title XVI, § 1654](/us/pl/101/624/tXVI/s1654), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 3756](/us/stat/104/3756), authorized appropriations for plant, pest, and disease control program.
