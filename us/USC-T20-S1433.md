# § 1433. General authority

##### § 1433. General authority

The Secretary shall, in accordance with this subchapter, make grants to States (from their allotments under [section 1443 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1443)) to assist each State to maintain and implement a statewide, comprehensive, coordinated, multidisciplinary, interagency system to provide early intervention services for infants and toddlers with disabilities and their families.

*([Pub. L. 91–230, title VI, § 633](/us/pl/91/230/tVI/s633), as added [Pub. L. 108–446, title I, § 101](/us/pl/108/446/tI/s101), Dec. 3, 2004, [118 Stat. 2746](/us/stat/118/2746).)*
