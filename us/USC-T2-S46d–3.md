# § 46d–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–184](/us/pl/92/184), ch. IV, Dec. 15, 1971, [85 Stat. 635](/us/stat/85/635)

##### § 46d–3. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–184](/us/pl/92/184), ch. IV, Dec. 15, 1971, [85 Stat. 635](/us/stat/85/635)

Section, [Pub. L. 90–21, title I](/us/pl/90/21/tI), May 29, 1967, [81 Stat. 38](/us/stat/81/38), made contingent fund of Senate available for reimbursement of each Senator of strictly official telephone service charges incurred outside District of Columbia up to $300 in each fiscal quarter. See [section 6314 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s6314).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 92–184](/us/pl/92/184) provided that the repeal is effective Jan. 1, 1972.
