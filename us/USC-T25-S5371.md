# § 5371. Annual budget list

##### § 5371. Annual budget list

The Secretary shall list, in the annual budget request submitted to Congress under [section 1105 of title 31](/us/usc/t31/s1105), any funds proposed to be included in funding agreements authorized under this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 93–638, title IV, § 411](/us/pl/93/638/tIV/s411), as added [Pub. L. 116–180, title I, § 101(e)](/us/pl/116/180/tI/s101/e), Oct. 21, 2020, [134 Stat. 876](/us/stat/134/876).)*
