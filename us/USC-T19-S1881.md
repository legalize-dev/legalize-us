# § 1881. Normal trade relations

##### § 1881. Normal trade relations

Except as otherwise provided in this subchapter, in [section 1351 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1351), or in section 401(a) of the Tariff Classification Act of 1962, any duty or other import restriction or duty-free treatment proclaimed in carrying out any trade agreement under this subchapter or [section 1351 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1351) shall apply to products of all foreign countries, whether imported directly or indirectly.

*([Pub. L. 87–794, title II, § 251](/us/pl/87/794/tII/s251), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 879](/us/stat/76/879); [Pub. L. 105–206, title V, § 5003(b)(1)](/us/pl/105/206/tV/s5003/b/1), July 22, 1998, [112 Stat. 789](/us/stat/112/789).)*

#### References in Text
