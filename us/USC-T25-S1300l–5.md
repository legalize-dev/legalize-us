##### § 1300l–5. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–434, title II, § 207](/us/pl/103/434/tII/s207), Oct. 31, 1994, [108 Stat. 4535](/us/stat/108/4535), which related to adoption of a tribal constitution and bylaws and election of tribal officials, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
