# § 695. Omitted

##### § 695. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 733, § 5](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch733/s5), [68 Stat. 725](/us/stat/68/725), which related to transfer of tribal property by the Secretary of the Interior to a corporation or other legal entity organized by the tribe, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
