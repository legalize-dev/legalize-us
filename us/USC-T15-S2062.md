# § 2062. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title XII, § 1211(b)](/us/pl/97/35/tXII/s1211/b), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 721](/us/stat/95/721)

##### § 2062. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–35, title XII, § 1211(b)](/us/pl/97/35/tXII/s1211/b), Aug. 13, 1981, [95 Stat. 721](/us/stat/95/721)

Section, [Pub. L. 92–573, § 13](/us/pl/92/573/s13), Oct. 27, 1972, [86 Stat. 1219](/us/stat/86/1219), provided that Commission could prescribe procedures to insure that manufacturer of a new consumer product notify Commission of new product prior to its distribution.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Aug. 13, 1981, see [section 1215 of Pub. L. 97–35](/us/pl/97/35/s1215), set out as an Effective Date of 1981 Amendment note under [section 2052 of this title](/us/usc/t15/s2052).
