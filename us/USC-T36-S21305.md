# § 21305. Principal office

##### § 21305. Principal office

The principal office of the corporation shall be in the District of Columbia.

*([Pub. L. 105–225](/us/pl/105/225), Aug. 12, 1998, [112 Stat. 1295](/us/stat/112/1295).)*
