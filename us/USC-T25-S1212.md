# § 1212. Omitted

##### § 1212. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 103–454, title II, § 202](/us/pl/103/454/tII/s202), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4792](/us/stat/108/4792), which set out Congressional findings, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 103–454, title II, § 201](/us/pl/103/454/tII/s201), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4792](/us/stat/108/4792), provided that title II of [Pub. L. 103–454](/us/pl/103/454), enacting sections 1212 to 1215 of this title, could be cited as the “Tlingit and Haida Status Clarification Act”.
