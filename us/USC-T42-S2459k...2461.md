# §§ 2459k to 2461. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

##### §§ 2459k to 2461. Repealed. [Pub. L. 111–314, § 6](/us/pl/111/314/s6), Dec. 18, 2010, [124 Stat. 3444](/us/stat/124/3444)

Section 2459k, [Pub. L. 85–568, title III, § 316](/us/pl/85/568/tIII/s316), as added [Pub. L. 109–155, title VII, § 701](/us/pl/109/155/tVII/s701), Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2935](/us/stat/119/2935), related to retrocession of jurisdiction. See [section 20146 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s20146), National and Commercial Space Programs.

Section 2459*l*, [Pub. L. 85–568, title III, § 317](/us/pl/85/568/tIII/s317), as added [Pub. L. 109–155, title VII, § 705](/us/pl/109/155/tVII/s705), Dec. 30, 2005, [119 Stat. 2936](/us/stat/119/2936), related to recovery and disposition authority. See [section 20147 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s20147).

Section 2460, [Pub. L. 86–45, § 4](/us/pl/86/45/s4), June 15, 1959, [73 Stat. 75](/us/stat/73/75), required that appropriations have prior authorization by Congress. See [section 30101 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s30101).

Section 2461, [Pub. L. 91–76](/us/pl/91/76), §§ 1, 2, Sept. 29, 1969, [83 Stat. 124](/us/stat/83/124), related to Congressional Space Medal of Honor and appropriation. See [section 30901 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s30901).
