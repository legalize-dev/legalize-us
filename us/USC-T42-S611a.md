# § 611a. State required to provide certain information

##### § 611a. State required to provide certain information

Each State to which a grant is made under [section 603 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s603) shall, at least 4 times annually and upon request of the Immigration and Naturalization Service, furnish the Immigration and Naturalization Service with the name and address of, and other identifying information on, any individual who the State knows is unlawfully in the United States.

*([Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531), title IV, § 411A, as added [Pub. L. 104–193, title IV, § 404(b)](/us/pl/104/193/tIV/s404/b), Aug. 22, 1996, [110 Stat. 2267](/us/stat/110/2267).)*
