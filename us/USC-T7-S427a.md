# § 427a. Repealed. [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 790, § 2](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch790/s2), [69 Stat. 674](/us/stat/69/674)

##### § 427a. Repealed. [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 790, § 2](/us/act/1955-08-11/ch790/s2), [69 Stat. 674](/us/stat/69/674)

Section, [act June 29, 1935, ch. 338, title I, § 2](/us/act/1935-06-29/ch338/tI/s2), [49 Stat. 437](/us/stat/49/437), authorized research by experiment stations. See [section 361a of this title](/us/usc/t7/s361a).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Existing Rights and Liabilities

Any rights or liabilities existing under this section unaffected by repeal, see section 2 of act Aug. 11, 1955, set out as a note under former [section 361 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s361).
