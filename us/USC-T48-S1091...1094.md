# §§ 1091 to 1094. Omitted

##### §§ 1091 to 1094. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Sections 1091 to 1094 were omitted in view of recognition of Philippine independence.

Section 1091, acts [Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 20](/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s20), [39 Stat. 552](/us/stat/39/552); [June 5, 1934, ch. 390, § 4](/us/act/1934-06-05/ch390/s4), [48 Stat. 879](/us/stat/48/879), related to appointment and qualifications of Resident Commissioners.

Section 1092, acts [July 1, 1902, ch. 1369, § 8](/us/act/1902-07-01/ch1369/s8), [32 Stat. 694](/us/stat/32/694); [Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 20](/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s20), [39 Stat. 552](/us/stat/39/552), related to temporary appointment of Resident Commissioners.

Section 1093, acts [May 22, 1908, ch. 186, § 1](/us/act/1908-05-22/ch186/s1), [35 Stat. 188](/us/stat/35/188); [May 17, 1932, ch. 190](/us/act/1932-05-17/ch190), [47 Stat. 158](/us/stat/47/158), related to salary of Resident Commissioners.

Section 1094, [act Aug. 29, 1916, ch. 416, § 20](/us/act/1916-08-29/ch416/s20), [39 Stat. 552](/us/stat/39/552), related to allowance for stationery and clerk hire.
