# § 1071. Omitted

##### § 1071. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 88–551](/us/pl/88/551), Aug. 31, 1964, [78 Stat. 755](/us/stat/78/755), which provided for per capita payments to enrolled tribal members from funds appropriated for a judgment dated Mar. 1, 1960, and exemption of such payments from Federal and State income taxes, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
