# §§ 183 to 186. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–325, title VI, § 602(e)(32)](/us/pl/103/325/tVI/s602/e/32)–(35), Sept. 23, 1994, [108 Stat. 2292](/us/stat/108/2292)

##### §§ 183 to 186. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–325, title VI, § 602(e)(32)](/us/pl/103/325/tVI/s602/e/32)–(35), Sept. 23, 1994, [108 Stat. 2292](/us/stat/108/2292)

Section 183, R.S. § 5222, provided that, within six months of voting to liquidate, an association was to deposit with Treasurer of United States money sufficient to redeem all outstanding circulation.

Section 184, R.S. § 5223, exempted associations which wound up business for purpose of consolidating with another association from requirement to deposit money to redeem all outstanding circulation.

Section 185, R.S. § 5224; [Feb. 18, 1875, ch. 80, § 1](/us/act/1875-02-18/ch80/s1), [18 Stat. 320](/us/stat/18/320), related to reassignment of bonds to association and redemption of notes.

Section 186, R.S. § 5225; [Feb. 27, 1877, ch. 69, § 1](/us/act/1877-02-27/ch69/s1), [19 Stat. 252](/us/stat/19/252), related to destruction of redeemed notes by Treasurer.
