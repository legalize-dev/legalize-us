# § 2022. Liens on stock

##### § 2022. Liens on stock

The Farm Credit Banks shall have a first lien on the stock or participation certificates it issues for the payment of any liability of the stockholders to the bank.

*([Pub. L. 92–181, title I, § 1](/us/pl/92/181/tI/s1).14, as added [Pub. L. 100–233, title IV, § 401](/us/pl/100/233/tIV/s401), Jan. 6, 1988, [101 Stat. 1629](/us/stat/101/1629).)*
