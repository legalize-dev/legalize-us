# § 7011. Organization

##### § 7011. Organization

The Department of the Army is separately organized under the Secretary of the Army. It operates under the authority, direction, and control of the Secretary of Defense.

*(Added [Pub. L. 87–651, title II, § 210(a)](/us/pl/87/651/tII/s210/a), Sept. 7, 1962, [76 Stat. 524](/us/stat/76/524), § 3010; renumbered § 3011, [Pub. L. 99–433, title V, § 501(a)(2)](/us/pl/99/433/tV/s501/a/2), Oct. 1, 1986, [100 Stat. 1034](/us/stat/100/1034); renumbered § 7011, [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII, § 808(a)](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII/s808/a), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1838](/us/stat/132/1838).)*

The word “operates” is substituted for the words “shall function”.
