# §§ 75a to 79b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 632](/us/stat/80/632), 645, 646, 652

##### §§ 75a to 79b. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 632](/us/stat/80/632), 645, 646, 652

Section 75a, [act May 17, 1926, ch. 303](/us/act/1926-05-17/ch303), [44 Stat. 558](/us/stat/44/558), authorized administration of oaths by an employee of Department of the Interior designated to act as register.

Section 76, R.S. § 2244; acts [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1](/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1), [42 Stat. 208](/us/stat/42/208); [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462), [43 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/43/1145), related to term of office of registers.

Section 77, R.S. § 2222; acts [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1](/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1), [42 Stat. 208](/us/stat/42/208); [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462), [43 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/43/1145), provided for continuation of duties and bond of register after expiration of his commission.

Section 78, R.S. § 2235; acts [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1](/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1), [42 Stat. 208](/us/stat/42/208); [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462), [43 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/43/1145), required register to reside at place where land office was located.

Section 79, R.S. § 2236; acts [Oct. 28, 1921, ch. 114, § 1](/us/act/1921-10-28/ch114/s1), [42 Stat. 208](/us/stat/42/208); [Mar. 3, 1925, ch. 462](/us/act/1925-03-03/ch462), [43 Stat. 1145](/us/stat/43/1145), required registers to give bond in the penal sum of $10,000.

Section 79a, [act Apr. 24, 1944, ch. 177, § 1](/us/act/1944-04-24/ch177/s1), [58 Stat. 215](/us/stat/58/215), related to bond for registers.

Section 79b, [act Apr. 24, 1944, ch. 177, § 2](/us/act/1944-04-24/ch177/s2), [58 Stat. 215](/us/stat/58/215), related to compensation for registers.
