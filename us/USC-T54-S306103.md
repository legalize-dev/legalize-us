# § 306103. Recordation of historic property prior to alteration or demolition

##### § 306103. Recordation of historic property prior to alteration or demolition

(1) timely steps are taken to make or have made appropriate records; and

(2) the records are deposited, in accordance with [section 302107 of this title](/us/usc/t54/s302107), in the Library of Congress or with such other appropriate agency as the Secretary may designate, for future use and reference.

*([Pub. L. 113–287, § 3](/us/pl/113/287/s3), Dec. 19, 2014, [128 Stat. 3226](/us/stat/128/3226).)*
