# § 302. Regulations

##### § 302. Regulations

The committee is authorized to prescribe such regulations as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this chapter.

*([Pub. L. 88–652, § 13](/us/pl/88/652/s13), Oct. 13, 1964, [78 Stat. 1084](/us/stat/78/1084).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### References in Text

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original “this Act”, meaning [Pub. L. 88–652](/us/pl/88/652), Oct. 13, 1964, [78 Stat. 1079](/us/stat/78/1079), known as the House Employees Position Classification Act, which is classified principally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Short Title note set out under [section 291 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s291) and Tables.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date

Section effective Jan. 1, 1965, see [section 17 of Pub. L. 88–652](/us/pl/88/652/s17), set out as a note under [section 291 of this title](/us/usc/t2/s291).
