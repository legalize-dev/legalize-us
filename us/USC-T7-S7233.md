# § 7233. Term of loans

##### § 7233. Term of loans

(a) Term of loan In the case of each loan commodity (other than upland cotton or extra long staple cotton), a marketing assistance loan under [section 7231 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s7231) shall have a term of 9 months beginning on the first day of the first month after the month in which the loan is made.

(b) Special rule for cotton A marketing assistance loan for upland cotton or extra long staple cotton shall have a term of 10 months beginning on the first day of the month in which the loan is made.

(c) Extensions prohibited The Secretary may not extend the term of a marketing assistance loan for any loan commodity.

*([Pub. L. 104–127, title I, § 133](/us/pl/104/127/tI/s133), Apr. 4, 1996, [110 Stat. 907](/us/stat/110/907).)*
