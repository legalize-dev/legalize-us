# § 1462. Attestation of copies by official seal

##### § 1462. Attestation of copies by official seal

All officers who furnish authenticated copies under [section 1460 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1460) shall attest their authentication by the use of an official seal, which is authorized for that purpose.

*([Aug. 24, 1912, ch. 370, § 4](/us/act/1912-08-24/ch370/s4), [37 Stat. 498](/us/stat/37/498).)*
