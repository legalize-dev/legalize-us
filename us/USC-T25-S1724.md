# § 1724. Omitted

##### § 1724. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 96–420, § 5](/us/pl/96/420/s5), Oct. 10, 1980, [94 Stat. 1788](/us/stat/94/1788), which related to the Maine Indian Claims Settlement and Land Acquisition Funds, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
