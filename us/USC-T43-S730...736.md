# §§ 730 to 736. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

##### §§ 730 to 736. Repealed. [Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789)

Section 730, [act July 9, 1914, ch. 138](/us/act/1914-07-09/ch138), [38 Stat. 454](/us/stat/38/454), provided for issuance of patents to transferees of town lots purchased at public sale and transferred prior to October 11, 1911, where patent had not been issued to original purchaser who had since died.

Section 731, [act Feb. 9, 1903, ch. 531](/us/act/1903-02-09/ch531), [32 Stat. 820](/us/stat/32/820), extended town-site laws to ceded Indian lands in the State of Minnesota.

Section 732, [act Mar. 3, 1891, ch. 561, § 11](/us/act/1891-03-03/ch561/s11), [26 Stat. 1099](/us/stat/26/1099), related to town-site entries in Alaska. Section was formerly classified to [section 355 of Title 48](/us/usc/t48/s355), Territories and Insular Possessions.

Section 733, [act May 25, 1926, ch. 379, § 1](/us/act/1926-05-25/ch379/s1), [44 Stat. 629](/us/stat/44/629), related to Indian or Eskimo lands in Alaska set aside on survey of town site. Section was formerly classified to [section 355a of Title 48](/us/usc/t48/s355a).

Section 734, [act May 25, 1926, ch. 379, § 2](/us/act/1926-05-25/ch379/s2), [44 Stat. 630](/us/stat/44/630), related to extension of streets or alleys across Indian or Eskimo land in Alaska. Section was formerly classified to [section 355b of Title 48](/us/usc/t48/s355b).

Section 735, [act May 25, 1926, ch. 379, § 3](/us/act/1926-05-25/ch379/s3), [44 Stat. 630](/us/stat/44/630), related to a survey of nonmineral public lands in Alaska into lots and blocks. Section was formerly classified to [section 355c of Title 48](/us/usc/t48/s355c).

Section 736, [act May 25, 1926, ch. 379, § 4](/us/act/1926-05-25/ch379/s4), [44 Stat. 630](/us/stat/44/630), related to authority of Secretary to prescribe regulations for sections 733 to 735 of this title. Section was formerly classified to [section 355d of Title 48](/us/usc/t48/s355d).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 94–579, title VII, § 703(a)](/us/pl/94/579/tVII/s703/a), Oct. 21, 1976, [90 Stat. 2789](/us/stat/90/2789), provided that the repeal made by section 703(a) is effective on and after Oct. 21, 1976.

#### Savings Provision

Repeal by [Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579) not to be construed as terminating any valid lease, permit, patent, etc., existing on Oct. 21, 1976, see [section 701 of Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579/s701), set out as a note under [section 1701 of this title](/us/usc/t43/s1701).

#### Juneau Indian Village Townsite

[Pub. L. 88–34](/us/pl/88/34), May 29, 1963, [77 Stat. 52](/us/stat/77/52), which provided that sections 733 to 736 of this title were extended and made applicable to all lands of the Juneau Indian Village of Alaska, including uplands and filled in tidelands occupied on May 29, 1963, was repealed by [section 703(a) of Pub. L. 94–579](/us/pl/94/579/s703/a).
