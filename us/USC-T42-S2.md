# § 2. Omitted

##### § 2. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, acts [Feb. 3, 1905, ch. 297](/us/act/1905-02-03/ch297), [33 Stat. 650](/us/stat/33/650); [Aug. 14, 1912, ch. 288, § 1](/us/act/1912-08-14/ch288/s1), [37 Stat. 309](/us/stat/37/309); 1939 Reorg. Plan No. I, eff. July 1, 1939, § 201, 4 F.R. 2728, [53 Stat. 1424](/us/stat/53/1424), which provided for jurisdiction of Federal Security Agency over the Service, was superseded by [section 202 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s202).
