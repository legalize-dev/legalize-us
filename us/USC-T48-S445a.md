# § 445a. Transferred

##### § 445a. Transferred

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act July 19, 1932, ch. 513](/us/act/1932-07-19/ch513), [47 Stat. 707](/us/stat/47/707), which permitted purchase of coal from two or more mines adjacent to the Alaska Railroad, was transferred to [section 208a of Title 30](/us/usc/t30/s208a), Mineral Lands and Mining, and subsequently repealed by [Pub. L. 97–468, title VI, § 615(a)(3)](/us/pl/97/468/tVI/s615/a/3), Jan. 14, 1983, [96 Stat. 2578](/us/stat/96/2578).
