# § 12855. Regulations

##### § 12855. Regulations

The Board of Directors shall issue any regulations necessary to carry out this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 101–625, title III, § 306](/us/pl/101/625/tIII/s306), Nov. 28, 1990, [104 Stat. 4133](/us/stat/104/4133).)*
