# § 906. Funding for administrative expenses

##### § 906. Funding for administrative expenses

For the purpose of administering this chapter and for the purpose of making the studies, investigations, publications, and reports herein provided for, there is authorized to be appropriated, out of any money in the Treasury not otherwise appropriated, such sums as shall be necessary.

*([May 20, 1936, ch. 432](/us/act/1936-05-20/ch432), title I, § 6, [49 Stat. 1365](/us/stat/49/1365); [Oct. 28, 1949, ch. 776, § 2](/us/act/1949-10-28/ch776/s2), [63 Stat. 948](/us/stat/63/948); [Pub. L. 94–124, § 3](/us/pl/94/124/s3), Nov. 4, 1975, [89 Stat. 677](/us/stat/89/677); [Pub. L. 103–437, § 4(a)(3)](/us/pl/103/437/s4/a/3), Nov. 2, 1994, [108 Stat. 4581](/us/stat/108/4581); [Pub. #### Amendments

1996—[Pub. L. 104–127](/us/pl/104/127) struck out at end “On or before February 15, of each calendar year beginning with calendar year 1976, or such other date as may be specified by the appropriate committee, the Secretary of Agriculture shall testify before the House Committee on Agriculture and the Senate Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry and provide justification in detail of the amount requested in the budget to be appropriated for the next fiscal year for the purpose of administering this chapter and for the purpose of making the studies, investigations, publications, and reports herein authorized.”

1949—Act Oct. 28, 1949, inserted “title I,” in credit of act May 20, 1936.
