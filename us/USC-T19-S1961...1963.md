# §§ 1961 to 1963. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(e)](/us/pl/93/618/tVI/s602/e), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072](/us/stat/88/2072)

##### §§ 1961 to 1963. Repealed. [Pub. L. 93–618, title VI, § 602(e)](/us/pl/93/618/tVI/s602/e), Jan. 3, 1975, [88 Stat. 2072](/us/stat/88/2072)

Section 1961, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 328](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s328), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 895](/us/stat/76/895), provided for application for a relocation allowance. See sections 2297 and 2298 of this title.

Section 1962, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 329](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s329), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 895](/us/stat/76/895), set out qualifying requirements for training. See sections 2297 and 2298 of this title.

Section 1963, [Pub. L. 87–794, title III, § 330](/us/pl/87/794/tIII/s330), Oct. 11, 1962, [76 Stat. 896](/us/stat/76/896), defined “relocation allowance”. See sections 2297 and 2298 of this title.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective on 90th day following Jan. 3, 1975, see note set out under [section 1901 of this title](/us/usc/t19/s1901).
