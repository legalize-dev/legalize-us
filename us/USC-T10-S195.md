# § 195. Defense Automated Printing Service: applicability of Federal printing requirements

##### § 195. Defense Automated Printing Service: applicability of Federal printing requirements

The Defense Automated Printing Service shall comply fully with the requirements of [section 501 of title 44](/us/usc/t44/s501) relating to the production and procurement of printing, binding, and blank-book work.

*(Added [Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title III, § 383(a)](/us/pl/105/85/dA/tIII/s383/a), Nov. 18, 1997, [111 Stat. 1711](/us/stat/111/1711).)*

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Authority To Procure Services From Government Publishing Office

[Pub. L. 105–85, div. A, title III, § 387(c)](/us/pl/105/85/dA/tIII/s387/c), Nov. 18, 1997, [111 Stat. 1713](/us/stat/111/1713), as amended by [Pub. L. 113–235, div. H, title I, § 1301(b)](/us/pl/113/235/dH/tI/s1301/b), Dec. 16, 2014, [128 Stat. 2537](/us/stat/128/2537), provided that: “Consistent with [section 501 of title 44](/us/usc/t44/s501), United States Code, the Secretary of a military department or head of a Defense Agency may contract directly with the Government Publishing Office for printing and duplication services otherwise available through the Defense Automated Printing Service.”
