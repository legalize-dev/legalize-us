# § 5042. Revocation of probation

##### § 5042. Revocation of probation

Any juvenile probationer shall be accorded notice and a hearing with counsel before his probation can be revoked.

*(Added [Pub. L. 93–415, title V, § 512](/us/pl/93/415/tV/s512), Sept. 7, 1974, [88 Stat. 1138](/us/stat/88/1138); amended [Pub. L. 98–473, title II, § 214(c)](/us/pl/98/473/tII/s214/c), Oct. 12, 1984, [98 Stat. 2014](/us/stat/98/2014).)*
