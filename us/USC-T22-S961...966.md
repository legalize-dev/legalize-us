# §§ 961 to 966. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

##### §§ 961 to 966. Repealed. [Pub. L. 96–465, title II, § 2205(1)](/us/pl/96/465/tII/s2205/1), Oct. 17, 1980, [94 Stat. 2159](/us/stat/94/2159)

Section 961, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title V, § 571, [60 Stat. 1011](/us/stat/60/1011); [May 26, 1949, ch. 143, § 5](/us/act/1949-05-26/ch143/s5), [63 Stat. 111](/us/stat/63/111); [Apr. 5, 1955, ch. 23, § 6](/us/act/1955-04-05/ch23/s6), [69 Stat. 25](/us/stat/69/25); June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. V, § 502(h), [72 Stat. 273](/us/stat/72/273); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 14](/us/pl/86/723/s14), [74 Stat. 833](/us/stat/74/833); Dec. 16, 1963, [Pub. L. 88–205](/us/pl/88/205), pt. IV, § 404(a), [77 Stat. 391](/us/stat/77/391); Aug. 17, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–105, title IV, § 408](/us/pl/95/105/tIV/s408), [91 Stat. 854](/us/stat/91/854), set forth provisions relating to assignments of officers or employees to any government agency or international organization.

Section 962, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 572](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV/s572), [60 Stat. 1012](/us/stat/60/1012), related to compulsory service in the United States.

Section 963, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 573](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV/s573), [60 Stat. 1012](/us/stat/60/1012), related to assignment for consultation or instruction.

Section 964, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 574](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV/s574), [60 Stat. 1012](/us/stat/60/1012), related to assignment to trade, labor, agricultural, scientific, or other conferences.

Section 965, acts [Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957), title V, § 575, [60 Stat. 1013](/us/stat/60/1013); Sept. 8, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–723, § 15](/us/pl/86/723/s15), [74 Stat. 834](/us/stat/74/834), related to assignments to foreign governments of officers and employees.

Section 966, [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V, § 576](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV/s576), as added Oct. 26, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–475, § 9(a)](/us/pl/93/475/s9/a), [88 Stat. 1440](/us/stat/88/1440); amended Nov. 29, 1975, [Pub. L. 94–141, title IV, § 401(a)](/us/pl/94/141/tIV/s401/a), [89 Stat. 763](/us/stat/89/763); Oct. 7, 1978, [Pub. L. 95–426, title IV, § 403(a)(2)](/us/pl/95/426/tIV/s403/a/2)–(4), [92 Stat. 978](/us/stat/92/978), related to assignments to public or private nonprofit organizations and Members and officers of Congress.

A prior section 576 of [act Aug. 13, 1946, ch. 957, title V](/us/act/1946-08-13/ch957/tV), [60 Stat. 1013](/us/stat/60/1013), which provided for assignments to international organizations, was repealed by [Pub. L. 86–723, § 52(3)](/us/pl/86/723/s52/3), Sept. 8, 1960, [74 Stat. 847](/us/stat/74/847).

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Feb. 15, 1981, except as otherwise provided, see [section 2403 of Pub. L. 96–465](/us/pl/96/465/s2403), set out as an Effective Date note under [section 3901 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s3901).
