# § 667e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–79, § 9(b)(2)](/us/pl/97/79/s9/b/2), Nov. 16, 1981, [95 Stat. 1079](/us/stat/95/1079)

##### § 667e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 97–79, § 9(b)(2)](/us/pl/97/79/s9/b/2), Nov. 16, 1981, [95 Stat. 1079](/us/stat/95/1079)

Section, [act May 25, 1900, ch. 553, § 5](/us/act/1900-05-25/ch553/s5), [31 Stat. 188](/us/stat/31/188), provided that the dead bodies of game animals or game or song birds be subject to the laws of the State into which they are transported. See [section 3378(a) of this title](/us/usc/t16/s3378/a).
