# § 1771e. Omitted

##### § 1771e. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 100–95, § 7](/us/pl/100/95/s7), Aug. 18, 1987, [101 Stat. 707](/us/stat/101/707), which related to jurisdiction over settlement lands and restraint on alienation, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
