# § 1059a. Applications

##### § 1059a. Applications

Each eligible institution desiring to receive assistance under this part shall submit an application in accordance with the requirements of [section 1068 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1068).

*([Pub. L. 89–329, title III, § 314](/us/pl/89/329/tIII/s314), as added [Pub. L. 105–244, title III, § 303(d)](/us/pl/105/244/tIII/s303/d), Oct. 7, 1998, [112 Stat. 1639](/us/stat/112/1639).)*
