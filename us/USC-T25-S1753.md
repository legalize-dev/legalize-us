# § 1753. Omitted

##### § 1753. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 98–134, § 4](/us/pl/98/134/s4), Oct. 18, 1983, [97 Stat. 852](/us/stat/97/852), which related to extinguishment of aboriginal titles and Indian claims, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
