# § 6371f. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 6371f. Authorization of appropriations

For the purpose of carrying out this part, there are authorized to be appropriated for fiscal years 1999 through 2003 such sums as may be necessary.

*([Pub. L. 94–163, title III, § 397](/us/pl/94/163/tIII/s397), as added [Pub. L. 95–619, title III, § 302(a)](/us/pl/95/619/tIII/s302/a), Nov. 9, 1978, [92 Stat. 3246](/us/stat/92/3246); amended [Pub. L. 101–440, § 8(b)](/us/pl/101/440/s8/b), Oct. 18, 1990, [104 Stat. 1015](/us/stat/104/1015); [Pub. L. 105–388, § 2(b)](/us/pl/105/388/s2/b), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3477](/us/stat/112/3477).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

1998—[Pub. L. 105–388](/us/pl/105/388) amended section generally, substituting provisions authorizing appropriations for fiscal years 1999 through 2003 for provisions authorizing appropriations for fiscal years 1991 through 1993.

1990—[Pub. L. 101–440](/us/pl/101/440) amended section generally, substituting provisions authorizing appropriations for fiscal years 1991 through 1993 for provisions authorizing appropriations for fiscal years ending Sept. 30, 1978, Sept. 30, 1979, and Sept. 30, 1980.
