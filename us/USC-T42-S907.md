# § 907. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–296, title I, § 108(a)(2)](/us/pl/103/296/tI/s108/a/2), Aug. 15, 1994, [108 Stat. 1481](/us/stat/108/1481)

##### § 907. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–296, title I, § 108(a)(2)](/us/pl/103/296/tI/s108/a/2), Aug. 15, 1994, [108 Stat. 1481](/us/stat/108/1481)

Section, [act Aug. 14, 1935, ch. 531, title VII, § 706](/us/act/1935-08-14/ch531/tVII/s706), as added July 30, 1965, [Pub. L. 89–97, title I, § 109(a)](/us/pl/89/97/tI/s109/a), [79 Stat. 339](/us/stat/79/339); amended Jan. 2, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–248, title I, § 165](/us/pl/90/248/tI/s165), title IV, § 403(d), [81 Stat. 874](/us/stat/81/874), 932; July 18, 1984, [Pub. L. 98–369, div. B, title VI, § 2663(j)(2)(C)(ii)](/us/pl/98/369/dB/tVI/s2663/j/2/C/ii), [98 Stat. 1170](/us/stat/98/1170); Apr. 7, 1986, [Pub. L. 99–272, title XII, § 12102(g)(1)](/us/pl/99/272/tXII/s12102/g/1), [100 Stat. 285](/us/stat/100/285); Oct. 22, 1986, [Pub. L. 99–514, § 2](/us/pl/99/514/s2), [100 Stat. 2095](/us/stat/100/2095), provided for appointment by Secretary every four years of an Advisory Council on Social Security and functions of Council.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Mar. 31, 1995, see [section 110(a) of Pub. L. 103–296](/us/pl/103/296/s110/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1994 Amendment note under [section 401 of this title](/us/usc/t42/s401).

#### Applicability of Repeal to 1994 Council

[Pub. L. 103–296, title I, § 108(a)(2)](/us/pl/103/296/tI/s108/a/2), Aug. 15, 1994, [108 Stat. 1481](/us/stat/108/1481), provided in part that: “This paragraph [repealing this section] shall not apply with respect to the Advisory Council for Social Security appointed in 1994.”
