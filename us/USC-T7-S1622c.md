# § 1622c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–79, title X, § 10002](/us/pl/113/79/tX/s10002), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 940](/us/stat/128/940)

##### § 1622c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 113–79, title X, § 10002](/us/pl/113/79/tX/s10002), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 940](/us/stat/128/940)

Section, [Pub. L. 110–234, title X, § 10403](/us/pl/110/234/tX/s10403), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1349](/us/stat/122/1349); [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title X, § 10403, June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 2111, related to a grant program to improve movement of specialty crops.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

[Pub. L. 113–79, title X, § 10002](/us/pl/113/79/tX/s10002), Feb. 7, 2014, [128 Stat. 940](/us/stat/128/940), provided that: “Effective October 1, 2013, section 10403 of the Food, Conservation, and Energy Act of 2008 ([7 U.S.C. 1622c](/us/usc/t7/s1622c)) is repealed.”
