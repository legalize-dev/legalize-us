# § 5301. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(l)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/l), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

##### § 5301. Repealed. [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391(l)](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391/l), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023)

Section, [Pub. L. 101–589, title I, § 101](/us/pl/101/589/tI/s101), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2882](/us/stat/104/2882), stated findings of Congress and national objectives relating to mathematics, science, and engineering education.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Short Title

[Pub. L. 101–589, § 1](/us/pl/101/589/s1), Nov. 16, 1990, [104 Stat. 2881](/us/stat/104/2881), provided that [Pub. L. 101–589](/us/pl/101/589) (enacting this chapter and sections 2994 to 2994g of this title, redesignating [section 2993 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s2993) as [section 2996 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s2996), amending sections 237, 240, 1201a, 1221e–1, 2983 to 2992, and 4512 of this title and sections 1869 and 1873 of Title 42, the Public Health and Welfare, and enacting provisions set out as notes under this section and sections 240 and 2982 of this title) could be cited as the “Excellence in Mathematics, Science and Engineering Education Act of 1990”, prior to repeal by [Pub. L. 103–382, title III, § 391](/us/pl/103/382/tIII/s391)(*l*), Oct. 20, 1994, [108 Stat. 4023](/us/stat/108/4023).
