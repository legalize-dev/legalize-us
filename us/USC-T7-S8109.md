# § 8109. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title IX, § 9008](/us/pl/115/334/tIX/s9008), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4886](/us/stat/132/4886)

##### § 8109. Repealed. [Pub. L. 115–334, title IX, § 9008](/us/pl/115/334/tIX/s9008), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4886](/us/stat/132/4886)

Section, [Pub. L. 107–171, title IX, § 9009](/us/pl/107/171/tIX/s9009), as added [Pub. L. 110–234, title IX, § 9001(a)](/us/pl/110/234/tIX/s9001/a), May 22, 2008, [122 Stat. 1324](/us/stat/122/1324), and [Pub. L. 110–246, § 4(a)](/us/pl/110/246/s4/a), title IX, § 9001(a), June 18, 2008, [122 Stat. 1664](/us/stat/122/1664), 2085; amended [Pub. L. 112–240, title VII, § 701(f)(8)](/us/pl/112/240/tVII/s701/f/8), Jan. 2, 2013, [126 Stat. 2365](/us/stat/126/2365), established the Rural Energy Self-Sufficiency Initiative.

A prior section 8109, [Pub. L. 107–171, title IX, § 9011](/us/pl/107/171/tIX/s9011), as added [Pub. L. 108–199, div. A, title VII, § 778(b)](/us/pl/108/199/dA/tVII/s778/b), Jan. 23, 2004, [118 Stat. 41](/us/stat/118/41), related to research, extension, and educational programs on biobased energy technologies and products, prior to the general amendment of this chapter by [Pub. L. 110–246](/us/pl/110/246). See [section 8114 of this title](/us/usc/t7/s8114).
