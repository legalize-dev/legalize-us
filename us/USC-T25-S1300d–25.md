# § 1300d–25. Omitted

##### § 1300d–25. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 105–387, § 6](/us/pl/105/387/s6), Nov. 13, 1998, [112 Stat. 3473](/us/stat/112/3473), which provided that a payment made to a tribe or individual would not effect eligibility for a Federal service or program or result in the reduction or denial of any service or program, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
