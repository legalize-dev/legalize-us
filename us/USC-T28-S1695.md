# § 1695. Stockholder’s derivative action

##### § 1695. Stockholder’s derivative action

Process in a stockholder’s action in behalf of his corporation may be served upon such corporation in any district where it is organized or licensed to do business or is doing business.

*([June 25, 1948, ch. 646](/us/act/1948-06-25/ch646), [62 Stat. 945](/us/stat/62/945).)*

#### Historical and Revision Notes

Based on title 28, U.S.C., 1940 ed., § 112 ([Mar. 3, 1911, ch. 231, § 51](/us/act/1911-03-03/ch231/s51), [36 Stat. 1101](/us/stat/36/1101); [Sept. 19, 1922, ch. 345](/us/act/1922-09-19/ch345), [42 Stat. 849](/us/stat/42/849); [Mar. 4, 1925, ch. 526, § 1](/us/act/1925-03-04/ch526/s1), [43 Stat. 1264](/us/stat/43/1264); [Apr. 16, 1936, ch. 230](/us/act/1936-04-16/ch230), [49 Stat. 1213](/us/stat/49/1213)).

The phrase “is organized or licensed to do business or is doing business” was substituted for the words “resides or is found,” as more specific and to conform to [section 1391 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s1391).

Venue provisions of [section 112 of title 28](/us/usc/t28/s112), U.S.C., 1940 ed., appear in section 1391 and 1401 of this title. Other provisions are incorporated in [section 1693 of this title](/us/usc/t28/s1693).

Changes were made in phraseology.
