# §§ 241c–1 to 241c–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title I, § 101(c)](/us/pl/95/561/tI/s101/c), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2200](/us/stat/92/2200)

##### §§ 241c–1 to 241c–5. Repealed. [Pub. L. 95–561, title I, § 101(c)](/us/pl/95/561/tI/s101/c), Nov. 1, 1978, [92 Stat. 2200](/us/stat/92/2200)

Section 241c–1, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II, § 121](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII/s121), as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(2)(E)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s101/a/2/E), [88 Stat. 491](/us/stat/88/491), provided for programs for handicapped children.

Another section 121 of act Sept. 30, 1950, as added by [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 113(b)(6)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s113/b/6), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 126](/us/stat/84/126), and renumbered section “126” by [Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380), was classified to [section 241d of this title](/us/usc/t20/s241d).

Section 241c–2, act Sept. 30, 1950, title II, § 122, as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(2)(E)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s101/a/2/E), [88 Stat. 492](/us/stat/88/492), provided for programs for migratory children.

Another section 122 of act Sept. 30, 1950, as added by [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 113(b)(6)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s113/b/6), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 126](/us/stat/84/126), and renumbered section “127” by [Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380), was classified to [section 241d–1 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s241d–1).

Section 241c–3, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II, § 123](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII/s123), as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(2)(E)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s101/a/2/E), [88 Stat. 494](/us/stat/88/494), provided for programs for neglected or delinquent children.

Another section 123 of act Sept. 30, 1950, as added by [Pub. L. 91–230, title I, § 113(b)(6)](/us/pl/91/230/tI/s113/b/6), Apr. 13, 1970, [84 Stat. 127](/us/stat/84/127), and renumbered section “128” by [Pub. L. 93–380](/us/pl/93/380), was classified to [section 241d–2 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s241d–2).

Section 241c–4, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II, § 124](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII/s124), as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(2)(E)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s101/a/2/E), [88 Stat. 494](/us/stat/88/494), provided for the reservation of funds for territories.

Section 241c–5, [act Sept. 30, 1950, ch. 1124, title II, § 125](/us/act/1950-09-30/ch1124/tII/s125), as added Aug. 21, 1974, [Pub. L. 93–380, title I, § 101(a)(2)(E)](/us/pl/93/380/tI/s101/a/2/E), [88 Stat. 495](/us/stat/88/495), and amended Apr. 21, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–273, § 3(8)](/us/pl/94/273/s3/8), [90 Stat. 376](/us/stat/90/376); Oct. 12, 1976, [Pub. L. 94–482, title V, § 501(b)(1)(A)](/us/pl/94/482/tV/s501/b/1/A), (*o*), [90 Stat. 2236](/us/stat/90/2236), 2238; Sept. 24, 1977, [Pub. L. 95–112, § 2(a)(1)](/us/pl/95/112/s2/a/1), [91 Stat. 911](/us/stat/91/911), related to minimum payments for State operated programs.

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries**

#### Effective Date of Repeal

Repeal effective Oct. 1, 1978, see [section 1530(a) of Pub. L. 95–561](/us/pl/95/561/s1530/a), set out as an Effective Date of 1978 Amendment note under [section 1221e–3 of this title](/us/usc/t20/s1221e–3).
