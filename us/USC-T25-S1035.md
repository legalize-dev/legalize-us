# § 1035. Omitted

##### § 1035. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 88–457, § 5](/us/pl/88/457/s5), Aug. 20, 1964, [78 Stat. 555](/us/stat/78/555), which related to distribution of shares of deceased enrollees and minors or persons under legal disability, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
