# §§ 1761 to 1765. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 642(a)(2), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460)

##### §§ 1761 to 1765. Repealed. [Pub. L. 87–195](/us/pl/87/195), pt. III, § 642(a)(2), Sept. 4, 1961, [75 Stat. 460](/us/stat/75/460)

Section 1761, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. IV, § 509, [68 Stat. 853](/us/stat/68/853); [July 8, 1955, ch. 301, § 9(d)](/us/act/1955-07-08/ch301/s9/d), [69 Stat. 288](/us/stat/69/288); [July 18, 1956, ch. 627, § 9(d)](/us/act/1956-07-18/ch627/s9/d), [70 Stat. 560](/us/stat/70/560); Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–141, § 9(d)](/us/pl/85/141/s9/d), [71 Stat. 363](/us/stat/71/363); June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. IV, § 401(b), [72 Stat. 269](/us/stat/72/269), related to shipping on United States vessels. See [section 2353 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2353).

Section 1762, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. IV, § 510, [68 Stat. 853](/us/stat/68/853); June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. IV, § 401(c), ch. V, § 501(19), [72 Stat. 269](/us/stat/72/269), 271; July 24, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–108](/us/pl/86/108), ch. IV, § 401(d), [73 Stat. 253](/us/stat/73/253), related to purchase of commodities in bulk and to surplus agricultural commodities. See [section 2354 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2354).

Section 1763, acts [Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. IV, § 511, [68 Stat. 853](/us/stat/68/853); Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–141, § 9(e)](/us/pl/85/141/s9/e), [71 Stat. 363](/us/stat/71/363); June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. V, § 501(20), (21), [72 Stat. 271](/us/stat/72/271), provided for preservation of military stock and retention and return of equipment. See [section 2355 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2355).

Section 1764, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. IV, § 512, [68 Stat. 854](/us/stat/68/854), prescribed penalties for accepting commissions, etc., for procurement services by United States officers and employees.

Section 1765, [act Aug. 26, 1954, ch. 937](/us/act/1954-08-26/ch937), ch. IV, § 513, as added Aug. 14, 1957, [Pub. L. 85–141, § 9(f)](/us/pl/85/141/s9/f), [71 Stat. 363](/us/stat/71/363); amended June 30, 1958, [Pub. L. 85–477](/us/pl/85/477), ch. V, § 501(22), [72 Stat. 271](/us/stat/72/271); May 14, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–472](/us/pl/86/472), ch. IV, § 401(d), [74 Stat. 139](/us/stat/74/139), provided for notice to legislative committees. See [section 2394(d) of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2394/d).
