# § 3618. Authorization of appropriations

##### § 3618. Authorization of appropriations

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out the purposes of this subchapter.

*([Pub. L. 90–284, title VIII, § 819](/us/pl/90/284/tVIII/s819), formerly § 818, Apr. 11, 1968, [82 Stat. 89](/us/stat/82/89); renumbered § 819, [Pub. L. 100–430, § 8(1)](/us/pl/100/430/s8/1), Sept. 13, 1988, [102 Stat. 1625](/us/stat/102/1625).)*
