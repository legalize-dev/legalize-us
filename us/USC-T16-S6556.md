# § 6556. Termination of effectiveness

##### § 6556. Termination of effectiveness

The authority provided by this subchapter terminates effective October 1, 2023.

*([Pub. L. 108–148, title IV, § 406](/us/pl/108/148/tIV/s406), Dec. 3, 2003, [117 Stat. 1911](/us/stat/117/1911); [Pub. L. 115–334, title VIII, § 8406(a)](/us/pl/115/334/tVIII/s8406/a), Dec. 20, 2018, [132 Stat. 4845](/us/stat/132/4845).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Amendments

2018—[Pub. L. 115–334](/us/pl/115/334) amended section generally. Prior to amendment, text read as follows: “There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as are necessary to carry out this subchapter for each of fiscal years 2004 through 2008.”
