# § 564p. Omitted

##### § 564p. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [act Aug. 13, 1954, ch. 732, § 17](/us/act/1954-08-13/ch732/s17), [68 Stat. 722](/us/stat/68/722), which related to execution of patents, deeds, and other instruments by the Secretary of the Interior, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
