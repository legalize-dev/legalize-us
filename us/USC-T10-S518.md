# § 518. Temporary enlistments

##### § 518. Temporary enlistments

Temporary enlistments may be made only in the Army, Navy, Air Force, Marine Corps, Space Force, or Coast Guard, as the case may be, without specification of component.

*(Added [Pub. L. 90–235, § 2(a)(1)(B)](/us/pl/90/235/s2/a/1/B), Jan. 2, 1968, [81 Stat. 755](/us/stat/81/755); amended [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title IX, § 924(b)(2)(A)(iii)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tIX/s924/b/2/A/iii), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3821](/us/stat/134/3821).)*
