# § 410hhhh–5. Definitions

##### § 410hhhh–5. Definitions

(1) the term “preservation district” means the Jimmy Carter National Preservation District established under [section 410hhhh–1 of this title](/us/usc/t16/s410hhhh–1);

(2) the term “historical park” means the Jimmy Carter National Historical Park established under [section 410hhhh of this title](/us/usc/t16/s410hhhh); and

(3) the term “Secretary” means the Secretary of the Interior.

*([Pub. L. 100–206, § 6](/us/pl/100/206/s6), Dec. 23, 1987, [101 Stat. 1437](/us/stat/101/1437); [Pub. L. 116–341, § 2(b)(3)](/us/pl/116/341/s2/b/3)–(5), Jan. 13, 2021, [134 Stat. 5132](/us/stat/134/5132).)*
