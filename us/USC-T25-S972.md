# § 972. Omitted

##### § 972. Omitted

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section, [Pub. L. 87–629, § 2](/us/pl/87/629/s2), Sept. 5, 1962, [76 Stat. 429](/us/stat/76/429), which related to personal property rights of tribe members and restrictions thereon, was omitted from the Code as being of special and not general application.
