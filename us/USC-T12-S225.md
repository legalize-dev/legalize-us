# § 225. Federal reserve banks; title

##### § 225. Federal reserve banks; title

A Federal reserve bank shall include in its title the name of the city in which it is situated, as “Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago.”

*([Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 2](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s2) (part), [38 Stat. 252](/us/stat/38/252).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section is based on part of the second par. of section 2 of act Dec. 23, 1913. The rest of the second par. was not included in the Code. For classification of other pars. of section 2 of this Act, see Codification note set out under [section 222 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s222).
