# § 20322. Purpose

##### § 20322. Purpose

The purpose of this subchapter is to ensure that by January 1, 2015, a court-appointed special advocate shall be available to every victim of child abuse or neglect in the United States that needs such an advocate.

*([Pub. L. 101–647, title II, § 216](/us/pl/101/647/tII/s216), Nov. 29, 1990, [104 Stat. 4794](/us/stat/104/4794); [Pub. #### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section was formerly classified to [section 13012 of Title 42](/us/usc/t42/s13012), The Public Health and Welfare, prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.

#### Amendments

2013—[Pub. L. 113–4](/us/pl/113/4) substituted “January 1, 2015” for “January 1, 2010”.

2006—[Pub. L. 109–162](/us/pl/109/162) substituted “January 1, 2010” for “January 1, 1995”.

1994—[Pub. L. 103–322](/us/pl/103/322) made technical amendment to reference to this subchapter to correct reference to corresponding provision of original act.
