Section 2452, [Pub. L. 85–568, title I, § 103](/us/pl/85/568/tI/s103), July 29, 1958, [72 Stat. 427](/us/stat/72/427); [Pub. L. 98–52, title I, § 108](/us/pl/98/52/tI/s108), July 15, 1983, [97 Stat. 285](/us/stat/97/285), related to definitions. See [section 20103 of Title 51](/us/usc/t51/s20103).
