# § 2000c–9. Classification and assignment

##### § 2000c–9. Classification and assignment

Nothing in this subchapter shall prohibit classification and assignment for reasons other than race, color, religion, sex or national origin.

*([Pub. L. 88–352, title IV, § 410](/us/pl/88/352/tIV/s410), July 2, 1964, [78 Stat. 249](/us/stat/78/249); [Pub. L. 92–318, title IX, § 906(a)](/us/pl/92/318/tIX/s906/a), June 23, 1972, [86 Stat. 375](/us/stat/86/375).)*
