# § 285. Nonvoting stock

##### § 285. Nonvoting stock

Stock not held by member banks shall not be entitled to voting power.

*([Dec. 23, 1913, ch. 6, § 2](/us/act/1913-12-23/ch6/s2) (par.), [38 Stat. 253](/us/stat/38/253).)*

#### **Editorial Notes**

#### Codification

Section is comprised of the eleventh par. of section 2 of act Dec. 23, 1913. For classification of other pars. of section 2 of this Act, see Codification note set out under [section 222 of this title](/us/usc/t12/s222).
