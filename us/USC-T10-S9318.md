# § 9318. Thirty years or more: regular commissioned officers

##### § 9318. Thirty years or more: regular commissioned officers

A regular commissioned officer of the Air Force or the Space Force who has at least 30 years of service computed under [section 9326 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s9326) may be retired upon his request, in the discretion of the President.

*([Aug. 10, 1956, ch. 1041](/us/act/1956-08-10/ch1041), [70A Stat. 550](/us/stat/70A/550), § 8918; renumbered § 9318 and amended [Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title VIII](/us/pl/115/232/dA/tVIII), §§ 806(b)(13), 809(a), Aug. 13, 2018, [132 Stat. 1833](/us/stat/132/1833), 1840; [Pub. L. 116–283, div. A, title IX, § 923(c)(16)](/us/pl/116/283/dA/tIX/s923/c/16), Jan. 1, 2021, [134 Stat. 3812](/us/stat/134/3812).)*

The word “commissioned” is inserted, since the retirement of warrant officers for length of service is covered by [section 1293 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s1293). The word “regular” is inserted, since 10:943 is applicable historically only to officers of a regular component. The words “and placed on the retired list” are omitted as surplusage. The words “computed under [section 8926 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s8926)” are inserted for clarity.

#### Prior Provisions

A prior section 9319 was renumbered [section 9419 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s9419).

#### Amendments

2021—[Pub. L. 116–283](/us/pl/116/283) inserted “or the Space Force” after “Air Force”.

2018—[Pub. L. 115–232](/us/pl/115/232) renumbered [section 8918 of this title](/us/usc/t10/s8918) as this section and substituted “section 9326” for “section 8926”.

#### **Executive Documents**

#### Delegation of Functions

Functions of President under this section to approve request of a regular commissioned officer of Air Force to retire after at least 30 years of service delegated to Secretary of Defense to perform, without approval, ratification, or other action by President, and with authority for Secretary to redelegate, see Ex. Ord. No. 12396, §§ 1(f), 3, Dec. 9, 1982, 47 F.R. 55897, 55898, set out as a note under [section 301 of Title 3](/us/usc/t3/s301), The President.
