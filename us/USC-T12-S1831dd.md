# § 1831dd. Open data publication

##### § 1831dd. Open data publication

(1) made available as an open Government data asset (as defined in [section 3502 of title 44](/us/usc/t44/s3502));

(2) freely available for download;

(3) rendered in a human-readable format; and

(4) accessible via application programming interface where appropriate.

*([Sept. 21, 1950, ch. 967, § 2](/us/act/1950-09-21/ch967/s2)[53], as added [Pub. L. 117–263, div. E, title LVIII, § 5832](/us/pl/117/263/dE/tLVIII/s5832), Dec. 23, 2022, [136 Stat. 3431](/us/stat/136/3431).)*
