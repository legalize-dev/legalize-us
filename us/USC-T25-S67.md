##### § 67. Repealed. [Pub. L. 89–554, § 8(a)](/us/pl/89/554/s8/a), Sept. 6, 1966, [80 Stat. 636](/us/stat/80/636)

Section, [act Mar. 3, 1893, ch. 209, § 1](/us/act/1893-03-03/ch209/s1), [27 Stat. 614](/us/stat/27/614), provided that the superintendent of the training school at Cherokee Agency was to act as an agent.
