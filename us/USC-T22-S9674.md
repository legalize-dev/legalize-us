# § 9674. Applicability of certain provisions of law

##### § 9674. Applicability of certain provisions of law

Subsections (g), (*l*), (m), and (n) of [section 2197 of this title](/us/usc/t22/s2197) shall apply with respect to the Corporation to the same extent and in the same manner as such subsections applied with respect to the Overseas Private Investment Corporation on the day before October 5, 2018.

*([Pub. L. 115–254, div. F, title V, § 1454](/us/pl/115/254/dF/tV/s1454), Oct. 5, 2018, [132 Stat. 3510](/us/stat/132/3510).)*
