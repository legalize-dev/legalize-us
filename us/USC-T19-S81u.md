# § 81u. Right to alter, amend, or repeal chapter

##### § 81u. Right to alter, amend, or repeal chapter

The right to alter, amend, or repeal this chapter is reserved.

*([June 18, 1934, ch. 590, § 21](/us/act/1934-06-18/ch590/s21), [48 Stat. 1003](/us/stat/48/1003).)*
